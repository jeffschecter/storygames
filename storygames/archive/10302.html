\n For those of you in Vancouver, or coming to Vancouver in September:

The Vancouver Gaming Guild is having game day meet-ups every Sunday in September (6th, 13th, 20th, 27th), at Drexoll Games in Kits (4th + MacDonald).

Times are approx. 12 noon until 4pm. There is no formal sign-up. If you want to run something, show up and pitch it. If you want to play something, show up and see what people are willing to run.

I will bring Don't Rest Your Head, In a Wicked Age, and Lacuna. Not sure what other people are going to bring.

For those of you not in Vancouver, you can feel enthusiastic or jealous, as you like.
