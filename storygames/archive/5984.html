    Like I needed another game I want to play.
    \nMan, I swear to you that thing wrote itself. I read the hack, and pretty suddenly shit in may head started to snap together.

    -"Stress" is reversed from hit points so that you can do "fallout" tricks with it, if the mood takes you. I think about fallout tricks constantly, and had that chunk of rules just sitting there in my brainpan. It slotted in while I was reading RBH.

    -The change to universal 2d6 is because I wanted it to be something everyone will have dice for. Which came from my attempts to write a game called "Old Skool", all of which sucked.

    -Changes to combat maneuvers were from a Pokethulhu Hack floating around in my head, from a ways back.

    -The Mastermind Assets? Go look at the End Boss fights in video games. They're direct rips. 'Cuz I was playing Sly Cooper two nights ago.

    I didn't engineer that re-hack. I just tinkertoyed it together, and IT ALL FIT. So fucking strange.
