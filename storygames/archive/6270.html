    \n There's a message up on CRN Games' website. I'm pasting for posterity's sake:

    --

    News
    9 April 2008

    I recently closed my long-standing forum at The Forge, and it\u2019s been quite a while since I put out any news or support for my games, so I thought it might be time for an update. If you\u2019ve enjoyed any of my games, you\u2019ll want to know about my future plans.

    I\u2019ve always been a hobbyist at heart, and I think my low-profile business and loving approach to games has borne that out. As a hobbyist, my interest ebbs and wanes, and I have other engaging goals in my life, such as my family and my contributions to open-source software.

    I\u2019m looking to move to being even more of a hobbyist, and looking to have a better presence for fans of my games at the same time. That\u2019s a hard set of goals, though, so I\u2019m taking a little while to reevaluate my approach. There will definitely be more news soon. What I can promise is that my games will continue to be in print, and that I\u2019m working hard to make sure they remain supported and even expanded upon.

    If you want to keep up with CRN Games news, or want a forum to talk about my games, please feel free to join the CRN Games e-mail group.

    --
