The BPG Owlbear Challenge

Jason_Morningstar
July 2006 edited July 2006 in Story Games
It's time to put your design skillz where your Owlbear-talkin' mouth is.

THE BULLY PULPIT GAMES OWLBEAR CHALLENGE

1. Choose three of the following four ingredients. You will be judged by your skill at incorporating them, as well as the usual stuff like speling.
2. Make your game in 24 hours or, you know, less.
2. Submit your design to 1km1kt by 9 August* and send me the URL.
3. BPG staff will judge all entries and select a winner. A non-gamer librarian** will break all ties.
4. The winner gets a free Roach Fun-Pak.

Your Ingredients are:

Owlbears
Mark Twain
Apiculture
Owlbears

*If you are coming to Gen Con, bring us a copy or two. All the judges will be there.
**Non-gamer librarian will not know what an owlbear is.
