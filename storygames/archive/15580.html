    \n There used to be more game stores in Boston, but several died, including the Games Workshop store across the street from Harvard. Your Move Games was bought by Pandemonium and then eventually closed. Pandemonium was (and, as far as I know, still is) the home of the SG Boston meetup and may have the best indie games selection, though it was always kinda hit or miss, especially when it came to reordering or knowing what to order.

    \n Depending on what route you're taking to get there, a stop in Northampton (roughly 90 minutes due West of Boston) is where my shop is, and we carry a lot of Indie stuff due to our very lucky proximity to a local nexus of very talented game designers:

    Modern Myths

    In general, you can do worse than to check out the Bits & Mortar store locator, as those will be shops who are proactive enough to seek out the program (free PDFs at retail with purchase of hardcopy RPGs) and are the most likely to be aware of RPGs outside the usual trad heavy hitters:

    Bits & Mortar Store Locator
