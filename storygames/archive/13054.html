\n The first playtest of Never to Die took place last night; I went for a bag of 90ish dice, which worked perfectly in the 2-3 hour timeslot we had, but feedback suggested the game would be better if it was a bit shorter, so I\u2019ll go with 75ish dice next time.
\n
\nThe line up for the game was:
\nAnthony as the Fist
\nJef as the Fixer
\nSam as the Brain
\nNeil as the Alpha
\nScott as the Ferret and
\nRobin as the Nutter
\n
\nThe lads started out in their local boozer but the Nutter was so unhappy over the quality of the drinks that he set the bar on fire! As the police and fire brigade arrived, the Brain tried to stick up for his mates but had to be saved by the Ferret, allowing the Nutter to steal a police car that they all drove to the clubs. More mayhem followed, with the Fist breaking open a fire door to skip the queue and the Nutter & Alpha running into a girl they had both shagged who was now pregnant... which didn\u2019t stop the Brain from copping off with her too!
\n
\nThe mechanics worked brilliantly: despite not rolling any dice, the Alpha was the first one to clock one his Moods up to 6 and take the Pain. He managed it purely by manipulating his mates, just what the Alpha is meant to do. Most of the archetypes worked like they were intended to, so just a little tweaking of Habits is required to get rid of some deadweight.
\n
\nThe night out heated up and ended with the lads scarpering from a stolen bus that had had a narrow miss with Dixon\u2019s in the high street, while coppers blocked off each end of the road. The Ferret distracted the coppers by hurling electrical goods at them, but his pissed ex-girlfriend Sharon (yeah, and the pregnant girl was called Tracy, of course) decided she wanted a turn at bus-driving and reversed it over him! He went splat right in front of his mates, as a result of which:
\nthe Brain went on a permanent bender, dancing his lonely nights away in the corner of the club, shunned by all
\nthe Fist and the Nutter both got religion and joined a Christian Evangelical movement that had been plaguing them all night
\nthe Fixer kept using until he finally OD\u2019ed
\nand the Alpha ended up looking after the Ferret\u2019s loved ones as they mourned his death.
\n
\nIt was a top game and Scott has volunteered to run it for the Pompey Crew at Indiecon!

    \n Another game of Never to Die occurred at Consequences over the weekend, run by the inestimable Julian Hayley; the AP is on my website, but I warn, it is nor for the faint-hearted. As a result of a short on-line discussion with Julian and one of the players from that game, Duncan, I can issue the following amendment:
    \n
    \nBrain Patch 1.0: If you're finding that the Brain tends to sit on the sidelines in your games, not really doing very much, try replacing their Habits with the following amended versions.\n
      \n
    • Geek: When a mate tries to suss, you can let them use your Pride instead of their Mood; if they do, add 1 to your Nuts.
      \nHorny: When a mate tries to chat to any woman, you have to either give them 1 die and gain 1 Buzz or take 1 die away and lose 1 Buzz.
      \nYou alright mate? When a mate Loses It, you must ask them if need any help; if they refuse, you get +1 die on your next roll and they gain 1 Nuts. If they accept, they get +1 die on their next roll and you gain 1 Pride.
