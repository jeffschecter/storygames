    \n
    \nIt wound up being a much longer and more arduous task than I would have thought, full of starts and stops and promises unfulfilled and projects dropped by people who just up and disappeared. The problem of having no related skills of my own and needing to depend on others to do even the simplest tasks.
    \n
    \nSo in the end I gave up my initial plan to do a fully modern, relayed out effectively hyperlinked with triple screen, print, and ink friendly layouts and just went with a basic no frills, get it done approach.
    \n
    \nIts only thanks to the efforts of Jeffry Fuller that it got done at all.
    \n
    \nPerhaps next year I'll find someone wanting to tackle the project more completely and release a 10 year anniversary edition with a pdf that actually takes advantage of being a pdf.

    \n The problem we ran into was that the original designer lost the layout file. So, all we had to work with was the final print production pdf. All things considered, I think we got a pretty descent document together, it is fully searchable, has bookmarks, and a nice small footprint.

    If sales were to justify it, I could see going back to the manuscript and doing a new digital layout for it. I could also see putting this together as an epub for use on mobile readers as well.
