\n So, I know there are a few people here who have played Archipelago by now, and I know some of you are like me and don't feel like posting a zillion pages about their experiences. However, inspired by the bite-sized Actual Play thread, I thought this might be a good way for me to get a little feedback:
\n
\nIf you've played the game, write three sentences about your play experience with Archipelago.
\n
\nI don't care if you just write three short sentences about your awesome character, or long-winded sub-claused paragraphs analyzing the interplay between social dynamics and subtleties of card interpretation. I'd just love to hear something about how you played the game.
