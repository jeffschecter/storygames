\n Decided to share a solo player Quiet Year game I'm running through Google Drive's Drawing app. The map can be viewed here.

The concept is that my community of 65 people have lived their lives entirely on board the fourth level of a massive seven level spacecraft (think a ship the size of the moon). They are not highly educated and are the sixth generation to live in this place, thus a lot of the knowledge their great-great-great-grandparents knew has been diluted, distorted, or lost completely. To the current generation the ship is all that is and what lies beyond its reinforced walls is a mystery and the stuff of late night scary stories. The particular area of the ship our community is confined to is the ship's landfill. Here is where the waste from daily operations is deposited and eventually flushed out into the beyond after being sorted by automatons and pushed into pneumatic tubes. The automatons have no personalities and never interact with the community and the community sees them as moving tools. The resources currently available to the community are as follows:

Organic Waste Matter (Abundant, the large misshapen mass in the top left corner)
Inorganic Waste Matter (Scarce)
Environmental Condensation (Scarce)
Ship's Oil Leakage (Scarce)
Electricity (Scarce, requires electrician knowledge to fully harness)

Week 1 - There is a disquieting legend about this place. What is it? or...Alarming weather patterns destroy something. How and what?

Waste Shaman Korddry gathers the children around a fire (started with some of the organic waste, and it burns incredibly bright) and tells them of the creature Fenbian. It is said that four generations ago Fenbian rose from the waste mountain and devoured dozens of children. After he digested them they were excreted as his minions and plagued our community for months. Fenbian was vanquished when the automatons tore him apart and flushed him in the tubes. We still fear that he will rise again and everyday that the waste arrives and sloughs onto the top of the mountain we hold our breaths and wait to see his glowering eyes.

Action: Discover something new - A previously undiscovered (or forgotten generations ago) access tunnel is discovered after Digger Brenzow tunnels his way through a waste pile that has been forgotten by the automatons. It's it not known what this tunnel leads to and the utility lights are dimmed or broken. If a trip down this tunnel were to occur, the explorers would need some of the high burning waste to light their way. One observer who was watching the tunnel late that night claimed they heard bestial cries echoing from somewhere far away.

    \n Week 2 - A charismatic young girl convinces many to help her with an elaborate scheme. What is it? Who joins her endeavors? Start a project to reflect. or...A charismatic young girl tries to tempt many into sinful or dangerous activity. Why does she do this? How does the community respond?

    Young Myrtis steps forward during one of the weekly council meetings drawing looks of disgust from the four elders. She states that the gods of the waste have shown them this access tunnel as a message that it is time to leave this place. She manages to convince about 20 of the younger community members to embark on an expedition down the tunnel. She promises that they will find a land of plenty, it will be the place the waste comes from before it is waste. Myrtis holds up the rotted skins of fruits and exclaims that the tunnel will take them to a place where the fruit is fresh, something the community has experienced rarely. The elders, led by Shaman Korddry attempt to blockade Myrtis and her people, but inevitably give in knowing that the desire of youth seem to always overpower the warnings of the old.

    Project - Explore the Access Tunnel: Est time to completion 4 weeks.

    Action: Discover something new - Years of leaking fluids from the ship's engines have caused a panel to the south to begin to rot away. Gunner Mauriz leads a group of five through the hole in the dense wall and finds a large barrel like container with a series of pipes coming in and out of it that disappear into the walls of the ship. The barrel is approx. 12m high and diameter of approx. 80m. The barrel is constructed of metal, but the type is unknown to the community. Gunner Mauriz fires on the barrel to test its strength and finds his energy weapon barely leaves a scorch mark. The atmosphere in the new chamber is equally as humid as the waste room but much cooler and many of the group relax for a bit in the cool air. Upon returning to the community, Gunner Mauriz produces four sluglike organisms that he found clinging to the tubing in the room. The slugs are fried up and devoured quickly. It has been a long time since they have eaten fresh meat.

    \n Week 3 - There\u2019s a large body of water on the map. Where is it? What does it look like? or...There\u2019s a giant, man-made structure on the map. Where is it? Why is it abandoned?

    Myrtis and her group of 20 discover that the access tunnel is connected a series of many more. By sheer luck they discover the reservoir room, where liquid waste is sent and is processed into clean water. To reach the clean water the group must ascend a ladder to the top of the processing vat. The vat is covered but there are hatches spread out across the vat. Myrtis tells her people to be careful because they do not understand the volatility of the machinery inside the vat. One young man is foolish enough to open a hatch and attempt to descend inside. The whirlpool of liquid swirling about in the vat pulls him quickly to the bottom and it is apparent to all that he is lost.

    Action: Start a project - Go on a slug hunt: Est time to completion 2 weeks

    Gunner Mauriz rallies up a group of 10 armed with handmade spears, crude melee weapons, and about three barely operational energy weapons to return the wet cold room and hunt down some more of those slugs. Unsure if they originally encountered babies or full grown adults, Mauriz is overly cautious about returning. He also wonders if some other community may hunt the slugs as well and wants to well armed in case a territorial dispute arises.

    Spring Week 4 - Where does everyone sleep? Who is unhappy with this arrangement, and why? or...What natural predators roam this area? Are you safe?

    Since the wall rusted away something has gotten into the waste room. One of the guards was found torn apart when Sentry Filix came to relieve him from duty. Medic Zhulah said the body looked like sharp blades had torn it apart. A day later, one of the children, Gawan ran to his mother shouting about the wet monster biting the garbage man. Sentry Rollan and his crew ran out to the spot and found one of automatons being attacked by a four legged creature whose skin glistened with a perpetual dampness. It appeared to have a tongue that wrapped around the torso of the automaton. The machine, being mindless and unaware of the attack simply tried to carry on with its duties. Sentry Rollan took careful aim, knowing his energy pistol had about a 50/50 chance of working when he needed it to. The beast was struck and yelped while shuffling off into the depths of the waste mountains. Sentry Rollan knows that where there is one pest you find many others and now that the creature has been hurt it will return for vengeance.

    \n Spring Week 5 - There\u2019s another community somewhere on the map. Where are they? What sets them apart from you? or...What belief or practice helps to unify your community?

    For many generations the people of the waste have had to deal with the occasional raid by the Oilmen. This rival tribe covers themselves in the black sludge that will drip from the inner guts of the ship and it is said they can be found in the levels above, living in Engine. Their weapons are cobbled together from broken Engine parts and they wear armor composed of cogs and bolts. A few of the more vicious lot have filed their teeth to points and then, in ritual performed on adolescents, had their teeth covered in hot liquid metal from the spare parts. In battle, they charge forward with a frightening grin, bearing shiny metallic fangs. The waste people do not know how the Oilmen survive up there in Engine. When they raid the wastes, they are only looking for inorganic materials and they seem to come at the same part of the cycle, just as new inorganic waste is dumped, as if they somehow know it is there. The community has never seen the Oilmen's children, only savage young men and women.

    Project Completed - Going on a Slug Hunt

    Gunner Mauriz and his hunting party return from the cold wet room with bundles of juicy slugs. A great feast is held and luckily enough in the last day a few pieces of fresh fruit were tossed into the wastes and these are used in the preparation of the slugs. The meals must be prepared over a fire built from waste so the food is always tainted with a certain sourness. But the community is used to this unique spin that is forced upon all their meals. Because of their life lived amongst waste the community's immune system is amazingly hearty and they are able to hold down this rather exotic food. One note, Gunner Whiloh has complained of stomach cramps an hour after the meal and has gone to lie down.

    Action: Start a project - Explore the wet cold room completely and look for access to other chambers with possibly more food. Est. time to completion: 3 weeks

    \n Spring Week 6 - Someone new arrives. Who? or...Two of the community\u2019s younger members get into a fight. What provoked them?

    He calls himself Konducter. He is dressed in a tattered navy blue uniform with a cap sitting askew on his head. There is a name badge on his left pocket that is smudged or scratched away. Konducter was first seen by Scav Psyll who ran back to the village center and gathered up Gunner Mauriz and Sentry Fillix. The two, armed and ready, easily captured and bound Konducter who put up no fight at all. He simply grinned wide and allowed himself to be placed in a disposed food cargo crate. The only words he utters through his grimacing smile are his name and "You all don't have tickets."

    Project Completed - Construct weapons to fight the wet beast if it returns

    Sentry Rollan proudly wields her new dual blade staff. Tinker Mozz hands out the newly constructed weapons and Gunner Mauriz leads training exercises for those not well versed in melee combat.

    Project Completed - Myrtis' group returns from their exploration of the access tunnel.

    Through the almost forgotten access tunnel emerges Myrtis with six less community members than she had when she left. Shaman Korddry gathers up the elders to meet here and there is a heated exchange of words. From what bystanders can gather, Myrtis and her people encountered a tribe that was heavily armed. She describes them as being covered head to toe in black garb, reflective visors across their eyes. They carried large black energy weapons that worked much better than the third hand salvage the Gunners use. Five her people were killed as they fled the Reservoir Chamber. Myrtis emphasizes that this tribe has not followed them back to the waste room. Shaman Korddry is extremely angry with Myrtis, chastising her for her foolish act of exploring the tunnel. Korddry takes 1 Contempt.

    Action: Discover something new - While exploring the wet cold room, Sentry Laxin opens a door into a new chamber where its discovered the automatons are repaired and constructed by large robotic arms with various tool attachments.

    \n Spring Week 7 - An old piece of machinery is discovered, broken but perhaps repairable. What is it? What would it be useful for? or... An old piece of machinery is discovered, cursed and dangerous. How does the community destroy it?

    While exploring the automaton repair room, Sentry Laxin discovers a model of automaton heretofore unseen by the community. It is bulkier than the waste models they normally encounter. Laxin has always leaned more towards being a Tinker and has some ideas about getting this machine up and running again. He is curious to see what its purpose is, knowing that it must be something different than the wastebots. It's size implies it will haul large bundles and if a cache of valuable resources are discovered somewhere in this vessel it could aid carrying them back to the waste room.

    Action: Start a project - Sentry Laxin has convinced Tinker Mozz and Shaman Korddry to lend him the resources to get the large automaton operational. Korddry is inspired by the young Sentry's drive to aid the community. Est. time to completion: 5 weeks.

    \n Spring Week 8 - Are there children in your community? If there are, what is their role in the community? or...How old are the eldest members of the community? What special needs do they have?

    Of the now 58 community members, there are 25 people that are below the age of 17. The children are taught by Shaman Korddry and told the stories of the waste room and basic survival. Most of the children's time is devoted to scavenging with the adult Scavs. Children are very useful for squeezing into small pockets of the waste mountains to get valuables that have been buried deep. At birth, Shaman Korddry exposes the infant to a series of corked bottles he keeps hidden away. He says that the bottles contain the essence of evil spirits that can cause sickness. If the child is able to fight off the evil spirits then they have been proven strong enough to survive in the community.

    Project completed - Explore the cold wet room/ Action - Discover something new

    The exploration of the cold wet room has led the community to discovery of a place of great death. With the unlatching of the door the explorers were hit by a great wave of heat. Inside the roar of churning fire filled the chamber. A metal belt grows out of a hole in the upper wall to the east and there are large black sacks on it, that fall into a massive metal cauldron and glows with fire. The explorers observed automatons much like the ones from the waste room gathering up these same black sacks that line the walls in 4m high piles and loading them into a chute that appears to place them on the belt above. Upon returning to the waste room and explaining this to Shaman Korddry his face turned bleak and he tells of the death room which his own shaman told him about when he was a young one. Shaman Korddry becomes adamant at warning against returning to this place. Myrtis, in all her rebellious glory, takes 1 contempt.

    \n Spring Week 9 - You see a good omen. What is it? or...You see a bad omen. What is it?

    While there is tension between Myrtis and Shaman Korddry, life is fairly quiet in the community. The slug hunts have become a regular thing and it appears things are okay. Then through the waste chutes comes a human body. It's discovered by Scav Giam and the elders inspect the corpse. It is female, wearing a one piece uniform. She appears to be in her late 20s-early 30s and has had her neck slashed open. Strips of skin are hooked on her fingernails. Shaman Korddry suspects something is wrong in the levels above and quickly places the body where an automaton can dispose of it. However, the automaton has no protocols to deal with this and ignores her. Shaman Korddry orders a couple Sentries to aid him in disposing of the body themselves in the out chutes. Never before has a community member interfered with the sorting and dispersion of waste. After the body is safely tucked away and voided into the beyond, the secretive trio return to the community without a word of what has happened. As Shaman Korddry passes the makeshift holding cell of the eerily still-grinning Konducter he glances in. Emerging from the shadows Konducter looks him dead in the eye and, speaking through the clenched teeth of his smile, says, "She didn't have a ticket." Korddry shudders and hurries back to his hut.

    Action: Start a project - Expand the community into the cold wet room. Est. time to completion: 2 weeks.

    Because of the bounty coming from the cold wet room, Shaman Korddry suggests an expansion of the community, particularly the Gunners to set up permanently in this chamber. Gunner Mauriz agrees and make plans to expand the structures to the south.

    \n Spring Week 10 - What important and basic tools does the community lack? or...Where are you storing your food? Why is this a risky place to store things?

    What passes for food in the community is gathered from the waste that falls every few days or so. What has been deemed edible by the community are fruit skins and vegetable scraps. Protein comes in the form of the insects that inhabit the waste (Roaches, beetles, etc.) The amount of waste processed on board has led to nice plump roaches bursting with nutrition. Some of the more industrious community members have begun raising the insects in buckets that they will with waste to be devoured. Recently they have also had the slug hunts to gather a new form of protein rich nutrition. The most labor intensive form of food production is mold, which is grown on certain rotten organics. One of the difficulties with storing and cultivating these foods is short lifespan (for the bugs) and quick spoilage (for all the non-living organics). If the food is not hidden away well, it will be detected by the waste automatons and disposed of, a fact the early generations learned quickly and taught to their children. Through close monitoring, the paths of the automatons were mapped and the foodstuffs were stored outside of these ranges.

    Action: Discover something new - Gunners spot people resembling those Myrtis described attacking her group. Decked out their head to toe black outfits and armed with large energy weapons they have been seen bringing more of the large black sacks into the room Shaman Korddry has referred to as "the place of death".

    \n Spring Week 11 - A young boy starts digging in the ground, and discovers something unexpected. What is it? or...An old man confesses to past crimes and atrocities. What has he done?

    Young Velo is digging near the east waste hills, an area not frequented by the community in generations due to it being where the automatons normally place plastic goods. Plastic is toxic if burnt and not very reliable for shelters, though it lasts for a long time. Velo forms a pretty strong tunnel into the side of the hill and is about 15 feet in and finds a skeleton. Returning to the village he does as he has been taught and alerts one of the Sentries. Sentry Fillix takes a group of his men out to the hills and pulls out the skeleton. The remains are dressed in a dark blue uniform, not unlike Konducter's. After closer examination and cleaning, they find a name tag in on the left breast pocket that reads "Captain Korso". Sentry Fillix knows from the stories of the ship that the Captain is the holy person chosen to steer the world through the beyond and eventually he will land them all in the Promised Place. So, if this is the Captain and he is long dead...who is steering the ship?

    Project Completed - Expand the community into the cold, wet room

    Gunner Mauriz has set up a wall of defense that allows some of the community to set up permanent residence in the cold, wet room (The community boundaries are now marked with the red line)

    Action: Discover something new - Another chamber is found to the north of the automaton repair chamber. This space appears to be in disuse and its walls are lined with dead consoles. The center of the room is made up of large tubes, large enough to fit a person, that have been cracked and shattered. There are also a series of tables that appear to have consoles built into their tops but are also dead. It appears that the room has been either abandoned or forgotten.

    \n Spring Week 12 - What\u2019s the most beautiful thing in this area? or...What\u2019s the most hideous thing in this area?

    The most beautiful thing in the waste room is also the most deadly. Far to the north on the northside of the tallest waste mountain is a place where the mold grows in bright vibrant colors that the community does not even have names for. In the early times, this mold was thought to have magical properties and was harvested and ingested by a small sect of community members. Over time they became incredibly sick and their bodies deformed. At night the mold would glow a bright green blue that could be quite hypnotic. The Shamans quickly came to talk ill of this place explaining that while the ship was good it sometimes produced a poison and that poison would find its way into the Waste. The poison was an aspect of the ship that was to be respected and feared. It should not be ingested and members should keep their distance while admiring its beauty.

    Project Completed - Sentry Laxin completes rebuilding and reactivating the large automaton.

    Laxin has nicknamed the large automaton Stark, a word he saw in ancient papers in the wastes. Stark is put to use helping lift some large pieces of inorganic waste that the community has had to work around. Laxin is applauded for his initiative and Myrtis feels anger, as her curiosity and energy were labeled as dangerous.

    Action: Hold a Discussion - Myrtis calls a meeting, upsetting the elders, to discuss the vast reservoir of water her group discovered. She explains that the community struggles to collect condensation from the walls to just squeak by for its needs. Sending a groups, with some Gunners, to the reservoir could provide the community with and adequate supply of water. Shaman Korddry gets into an argument with Myrtis and tells her that if she continues with her rebellious talk she could exiled. Myrtis draws 1 Contempt (2 total).

    \n Spring Week 13 - What group has the highest status in the community? What must people do to gain inclusion in this group? or...Are there distinct family units in the community? If so, what family structures are common?

    The highest status group in the community are the elders. Led by Shaman Korddry, the elders rise to their position first based on age. The old are revered by the community because all history is passed down orally so you rely on the eldest members to teach almost everything. The second step to becoming an elder is to pass the current Shaman's test of the spirits. As mentioned earlier, the Shaman has a collection of bottles that contain, in his words, "evil spirits". If a community member can withstand all of these and not become the slightest bit ill they are eligible to become an elder. The final step to become one of the elders is to be selected by them after performing some great task that was of benefit to the community. There has been controversy in the past about the politics behind certain selections, but a Shaman's word is law so the community usually quiets down.

    Action: Start a project - Use Stark, the heavy automaton to move large inorganic material and construct a village center, a place to commune. Est. time to completion: 4 weeks.

    Summer Week 2 - A project fails. Which one? Why? or... Something goes foul and supplies are ruined. Add a new Scarcity.

    Myrtis leads her crew of six gunners through the access tunnel. She is happily surprised at how well she remembers the path to the reservoir. However, when her crew arrives there they are ambushed by the black uniformed men. Glowing restraints are slapped around their wrists and Myrtis feels a solid boot heel to the back of her knees. She clenches her jaw not wanting these men to see her weep, wanting to die with pride in herself for not breaking. Then she feels the butt of one of their energy weapons connect with her cheek and everything goes black...

    Action: Hold a Discussion - During the weekly community meeting, Gunner Mauriz brings up expanding the territory further into the cold, wet room.
