\n For the past few months I've been building a totally web-based virtual tabletop, and have just released a preview video.
\n
\nTabletop Quest - Beta Preview - YouTube
\n
\nThe main difference between this and other VTT's is that it has a single built-in "lite" ruleset that is integrated with the main interface. The focus is on simple, quick games (mainly free-form cinematic style), that you can jump into and run with little to no prep. It's general enough to support most genres, taking inspiration from WEG D6, Mini Six, Risus, and Lady Blackbird. The idea is that free-form games are a better fit for a chat-based medium than more crunchy systems, and having a single ruleset will hopefully make matchmaking within the community much easier.
\n
\nAnyway, I'd welcome any feedback on this approach, and ideas for a better online roleplaying experience in general.
