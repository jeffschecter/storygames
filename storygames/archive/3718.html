\n This past Saturday, July 21st, saw the first actual game-play session of our new campaign. Aside from the goal to have a buttload of fun, the purposes for this campaign are two: to give the Verses RPG rules a working progression towards completion through extended playtest, and to work as a unit to create a new campaign setting in which all involved can be personally invested. So far, after a character and setting jam session and one actual play session, the results are better than satisfactory. With this post, I\xe2\u20ac\u2122ll kick off a series of post-session Actual Play reports.

    \n Hahah, yes, the galleries are quite amusing. We used to run this LARP, see... but that's another story.

    For me, I think the coolest part of the game session was the 12%/15%/17% deal. The tone set down with that moment really set the feel for the four hours of gameplay that followed. it's been a long time since I've seen players do that so quickly, effectively, and possibly even unconsciously.

    My least-enjoyed moment at the time would be the end. I was sad to see the session conclude. In hindsight, I wish I had given more opportunities for one or two of the players to shine a little more.
