    \n The plusses count. If someone uses an Oath to get you to help with an ability you have a +2 on, they get that +2 (just like if you helped yourself with that ability). The bonus applies to the highest die rolled.

    You can help with whatever ability you can explain, based on what help the person asked for. Smart askers will try to phrase the request so you help with the ability they want.
