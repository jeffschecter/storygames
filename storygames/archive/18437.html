\n Here's a fun one for people: What story systems do you like to do when you want to game, but don't have a game in mind?

Personally, our group lends itself towards Wu-Shu, because when improvising, we like a bit more of a chaotic and silly story in mind. Case in point: At our last Skype session, we spent about an hour designing a world with each person putting in two elements. Our list ended up looking like this: Runes, Dunes, Three different Races of people, The Shadow, Portals, Underground Cities, etc.

So, basically, we were all refugees hiding on a desert planet from some alien entity called The Shadow, that can't come to this planet. Because of reasons.

It was tremendous fun, and our game ran a little later than my poor old self can handle, normally. So, do other people do this sort of thing, and if so, what system do you use? Or alternatively, what's the best scenario you've come up with on the fly?
