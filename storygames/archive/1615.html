After some conversations with the National Park Service and others, we've boiled down the options for Nerdly One to two weekends - 27-30 April, 2007 and 11-13 May 2007.

I'm interested in getting some feedback on these dates, to see if there is a clear consensus or even a preference.

April 27-30:
The Average High Temperature is 68 F with a historical range of 59 F to 79 F
The Average Low Temperature is 47 F with a historical range of 39 F to 59 F
First weekend of operation for the cabins we want, still a little chilly.

May 11-13:
The Average High Temperature is 75 F with a historical range of 55 F to 88 F
The Average Low Temperature is 56 F with a historical range of 41 F to 68 F
Close to Dex Con

If you have a preference, or information that would impact these dates, here's the place to express it. Note that "Do it on May 25!" or "Do it in Philadelphia!" is not helpful.
