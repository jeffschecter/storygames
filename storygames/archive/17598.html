\n Following Hey, So Did Fiasco Sell In Target?, I thought we should talk about how other roleplaying games have sold in large stores.

As you know, Apocalypse World appeared in this year's Argos catalogue, on page 293 just below the Furby. I'm interested to know how it sold: it was clearly aimed mainly at children, with the pictures of the boy and the girl laughing over the Angel playbook.

There was a pallet of Monsterhearts in the Finsbury Park Aldi this week. They were down past the gherkins, beside the bin of snow shovels that didn't sell at all. They seemed to go quickly: by the time I arrived, there were only a couple of creased copies at the bottom.

Has anyone else seen roleplaying games in major stores?
