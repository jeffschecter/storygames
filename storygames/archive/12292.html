    \n If I had two players I'd be really tempted to make them have more than 1 PC each. (I'm not saying they play more than 1 PC at a time, but having two PCs each means you'd ideally have one from each Nation and as GM you could mix and match the PCs being paired up for adventures. I really think that players would "get" having one of their PCs working against the hidden agenda of their other PC too. It's funny like that.)

    The Demos for Cold City and Hot War are here. The new Hot War one, in particular, is rad.

    With three or more players (or PCs) you get interesting trust dynamics going on, which can be flatter with just two players.
