    \n Thelon's Rift is a good place to start but might be a bit more filled-in than the usual Dungeon World adventure can be. That said, you can look at what the Rift gives you and then find the empty spaces - the world around it, for example, and the reasons the PCs are there. Make a Front and create some urgency (the Rift is pretty agency-free as-written, it's just sort of there to be plundered) around it and then ask the PCs during chargen what they are doing there, what brought them to this place and what might happen if they fail in their goals.
