    \n I did A6 character sheets that I printed on to different coloured paper that had all the rules condensed on to them (unfortunately that was a HD crash ago and I can't run the DTP software that they were written in any more). I think one side was a cheat sheet and the other was character gen with all the options there so you could circle what you'd chosen. Using different colours meant that you could easily remember which characters were in which groups (the octogenarian mall walkers are yellow, the third rate football team is red, etc...)

    I keep meaning to see if I have a hard copy of one of the sheets somewhere, if I do I'll copy it up somewhere.
