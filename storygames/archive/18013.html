\n I have free time today, and I'm putting other things on hold to brainstorm a little. The idea is a grown up version of a Thundercat, He-man, Bravestarr type setting. So out goes the neat little morals and bloodless battles for something a bit more mature. What stays is the fantastical mix of fantasy and tech and the sense that the characters are new strandees on an exotic and dangerous planet!

I use Popplet for my brainstorming, and while I let other ideas simmer and congeal I'm playing around with the archetypes that would make good AW playbooks. Whether or not this becomes a full hack, something different, or nothing at all I figured I'd let anyone who wanted to to view what I'm doing and comment here.

    \n I don't see that as mixing Genre's really - all of those adventure cartoons are really the same Genre. But besides semantics that's exactly what I'm feeling.

    I've just seen such little Voltron! But I think that are some of the other more spacey action cartoons clearly have the same feel.

    I think Space Travel is clearly possible in this game setting, but for whatever reason all of the players/characters/races on the planet are stuck there for now. So there are lots of different tech levels, but not enough resources to have any one completely take over.
