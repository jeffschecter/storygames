\n We\u2019re super-excited to announce HammerCon II, Southern Ontario\u2019s largest game convention by gamers for gamers, on November 6, 2010 at the Plaza Hamilton. HammerCon is an all-day convention packed with roleplaying (including lots of indie/story games), board games, miniature games, tournaments and more from registration at 8am until the gaming winds down at 3am.

Please check our website for additional information: http://www.hammercon.ca

New events are being added all the time so check back often. Make sure you sign up for our newsletter! You can also follow us on Twitter and join us on Facebook.

We\u2019re looking for more events to add to our schedule, so if you\u2019re keen on organizing a game or two for your fellow gamers, you can let us know at events@hammercon.ca or submit your proposal online. Organize 8+ hours of games and get a free convention pass!

You can pre-register for the convention online today for only $15 (discount valid until September 30th). Pre-registered attendees can sign up for games in advance and avoid being trampled at the game registration desk!

Questions? Suggestions? Comments? Concerns? Contact us!

We look forward to seeing you in November!
