    \n
    \n
    \n Hi James,
    \n
    \nThe website does not rock. But we do!
    \n
    \nThe con is located at:
    \n
    \nHilton East Brunswick
    \n3 Tower Center Boulevard
    \nEast Brunswick, New Jersey 08816
    \nTel: 1-732-828-2000
    \n
    \nIt is about 45 minutes from NYC via:
    \n
    \nOperator Name: Suburban Transit
    \nSchedule Details: Line 100
    \nFrom: Port Authority Bus Terminal, New York, NY
    \nTo: Neilson Parking Plaza, East Brunswick, NJ
    \nFare: I believe $7.55 one way or $14.70 two ways
    \nSchedule.
    \n
    \nThe dealer hall is very, very bare bones.
    \n
    \nDreamation treats us really well. If you GM a certain number of events, you will get free admission and I believe part of your hotel paid for. They also have a GM only room with food and snacks.
    \n
    \nDreamation also lets IPR set up a booth right when you walk into the main con area. It's the most visible part of the con. It's even before registration! They also gives us plenty of space for running games.
    \n
    \nHope that helps!
