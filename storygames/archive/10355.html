\n Dear Story Games,

As you may or may not already know, the best thing in the world is Moo Cards.
The second best thing in the world is an event I'm organizing called Story Games Thursday.

I'm doing the logical thing: using the best thing in the world to promote the second best thing in the world. Creating Moo Cards for Story Games Thursday.

The look I'm going for is slick & semi-hipster (Design Matters has had an infectious impact on me). For each card, I want one or two pieces of gaming ephemeral, on a solid background (of black, brown, burgundy/wine, grey or white).

A demo:


Except! That's not the right size/format (it's currently sized to be a banner).
The final versions need to be 874 by 378 pixels (wider than tall). JPG is most preferred.

So, like, I'm interested in having everyone here help me out by submitting images. Moo Cards lets you submit 100 unique images, and I'd love 100!
On each, I'd like to see a few pieces of paper, or a character sheet and two dice, or some pencils and a game book, or whatever. On a nice, rich background.

Whoever submits the most that I in the end use (ie, that fit my vision and are well done) will win a free copy of Ribbon Drive. If there's a tie, I'll do a draw.
Just post to this thread, or post a link. Whisper me if you don't know how to do that but have the pics.
