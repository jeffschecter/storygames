\nCuz that's where I'm moving!

My wife has taken a teaching and research position at Northeastern University, so the whole family is uprooting from the capital of Canada to Beantown. She starts in January 2015, but we'll be making the move in late November or December. Not sure where we'll be living, going to be scouting locations in September.

Anyway, I'm an avid story-gamer, particularly fond of AW-engine games, but I've still got other interests. Met a lot of good people here in Ottawa and hope to do the same in Lovecraft Country.

Hope to hear from some of you soon!
