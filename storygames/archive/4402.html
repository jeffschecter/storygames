\n Okay, we're going to try this again.

Elements are defined by: three resources, three relationships, and one "hook." The hook is a way that a PC can later "plug in" to that element. Stuff like "somebody stole our holy book!" or "just hired a mercenary to take care of our pirate problem."

We're going to try this as a space opera this time. Mike, Ryan, Caesar, Rich, take it away with a first element with three relationships to new elements!

    \n An interesting effect:

    In my last post, I made up a new element in my hook: "The Sect of Harmony." But since that wasn't a relationship, unless someone turns that into a relationship there won't be a written element about it. This could be hot or lame:
    Hot = No one knows much about this Sect, so that players have a mystery on their hands
    Lame = If no one grabs it for a relationship, it might just be dropped by the wayside.

    Interesting, though.
