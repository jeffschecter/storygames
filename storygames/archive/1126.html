Alright, so while there's still some sawdust floating around go-play.org, it's up and running. One of the prime things I want to do with that site is collect the various go-play designs as they come out. I don't so much want the site to be a destination as an archive. So this weekend (hopefully) I'll be setting that up.

In the mean time, the other thing we need are pictures of people displaying their go-play pride. If Rob thrust a sticker in your hands and it ended up on your laptop or car bumper or window or forehead, send us a picture. If you got one of Jason or Rob's teeshirts, send us a picture. If you painted your face green with a big white arrow in the middle, seek help, but send us a picture first. I'm turning any new users that I recognize into Authors, so they can just start up a new post to display their go-play awesomatude.

I'd love to see everybody with their little green flags flying!
