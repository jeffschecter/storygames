    \n In celebration of Fallout 3 coming out, I would also like to contribute a quote that is very appropriate for 3:16.

    "War. War never changes."

    Wolfe,

    Well, the other game we were set to play next was Misspent Youth. We decided we wanted some kind of space between two rpg's about youth against an uncaring world. 3:16 seemed to fit the bill. Sure, the basic theme of "War is hell and strips away your humanity" is still there in both games, but the tacts they go about showing you such things is drastically and refreshingly different.

    I'm fully looking forward to how past expierences contaminate future ones, I just wanted to get away from the whole "youth in trouble" theme for at least a bit. And kill bugs. Lots of bugs!
