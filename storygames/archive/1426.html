  • ScottM

    I went last year, and I urge anyone who actually wants to meet other Story Gamers to make sure you distribute cell phone numbers, check your email at the con, or something similar. I know that the Thursday pickup game of DitV I tried to run had advance interest, but without coordination no one found me. During the convention, it's very easy to miss the people at the booth-- I kept picking the times they were away, giving seminars or running a game. If you've booked several other games, it can be hard to hit the booth frequently enough to ensure that you meet the authors.

    Our story had a semi-happy ending; we did get to meet Luke on Sunday afternoon-- too late to coordinate even a demo. Be proactive-- gather phone numbers or other on-site contact info before you leave for the con.

    Scott
