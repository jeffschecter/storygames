RPG 'zine survey

Matt Snyder
September 2006 edited September 2006 in Story Games
Some of you may know I'm working to create a periodical very much like Daedalus in spirit if not also in name. It would take the form of online content in many forms and also print format via POD sources like Lulu. The sole emphasis is actual play, or "Make Play Better."

I'd like to collect some feedback from you to help shape my thinking on a some of this, so here are some questions for you:
  • Have you read at least 1 article of Daedalus or a similar 'zine? More than 1?
  • Would you pay for a softcover "annual" (1-3 issues per year) of articles, even if they were freely available online? If not, what would you expect in a print volume to be worth paying for? Would you SUBSCRIBE to such an issue?
  • What do you think of advertisements? On the web? In print?
  • If you are a publisher, would you purchase ads on a website and/or print publication? Assuming a reasonable level of audience, would you be willing to pay up to $30 per ad per issue?
  • Would you be willing to write articles and assign rights to such a publication for a small fee ($15)?
  • Can you provide any other ideas or feedback?
