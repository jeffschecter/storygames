    \n "I start digging out the bricks."

    "The gold bricks in the road."

    "Er, okay, why are you doing that?"

    "I'm a thief, aren't I?"

    "Well... well, yes, exactly, only you specialize in stealing fashion items."

    "Oh! That's cool. I'm going to pickpocket that giant lollipop."



    These subverted tropes only get cooler when you unsubvert them again. They take the skin with them.
