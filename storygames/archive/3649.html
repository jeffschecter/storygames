\n So a few years back I handed the awards over to John Kim, who has been doing a good job of keeping up with the mass of yearly indie releases.

Here's the list of last year's independently-produced games.

Here's the list of last year's independently-produced supplements.

Is it just me, or is there not an orgy of awesome games that totally blindsided me?

Sure, I knew about Zorcerer of Zo, Spirit of the Century, Burning Empires, etc. But there's quite a few that caught my attention that I've never even heard of!
In Harm's Way (I knew Clash was working on it a while ago, didn't know it came out)
'45 - Psychobilly Retropocalypse (never heard of it)
Unistat (didn't know Andrew put it online)

Those are just a few that caught my attention. I had already picked up a few others earlier, like Hearts and Souls.

Anyway, this last year looks to have been a really good year for people getting their works out in print.
