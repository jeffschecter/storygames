\n Listening to This Modern Death the other day in their show about religon got me thinking about what if the only way your character could interact with the game world was through their religous beliefs. Within hours I had the core of an idea of a game about beseaching the gods for help to do anything.



I already had another game idea rolling around in my head but I couldn't stop thinking about gods and petitioners. I was finally convenced to push this game out first and so "The Gods Must be Crazy" was born. It took me most of this week to write and rewrite the game and get it posted up over at At the Table.



You and three of your friends play villagers using their faith to fight a dragon! Let me know what you think and what questions/suggestions you have.
