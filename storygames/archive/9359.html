23 pages...

23 pages of notes (10pt font) from my players, regarding their player characters.

Our campaign has just finished "Book I" of our saga. With that, we're skipping ahead 8 years in-game time.

I asked for "updates" on:
- spending any xp, and what the PC spends it on
- updates on magic item notes and backgrounds
- a year-to-year idea of the basics of what their PC does in those 8 years.


Combined ... I now have 23 pages to read!!
I'm not complaining.
I'm flaberghasted at the excitement level, the interest level and the dedication each of my players have for the campaign.


Now... I only hope I can digest and return that enthusiasm!
Does this ever happen to you?
-kev-
