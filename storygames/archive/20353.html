\n Hello Gamers,

AnonyCon 2015 is taking place in Stamford CT on Dec 4th-6th and we're looking for GM's eager to run their favorite games!

AnonyCon is an annual game convention that brings people together to have fun, help charity and showcase new games. Our schedule welcomes games of all types, and we try to feature games each year with opportunities for creative storytelling and problem solving. So if you're interested in running or demoing a board game, card game, rpg, or storytelling game just let us know.

We'd love to have you join us (whether you're looking to run a game, or just play one)!

Check us out online at - http://anonycon.com/
You can submit info on what you're looking to run here - http://anonycon.com/submission/

If you're willing to run three or more games at AnonyCon we'll waive your registration fees (for all 3 days)!

Thanks!

James Unick
AnonyCon 2015
Games So Good They Turn Undead!
