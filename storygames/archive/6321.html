\n Hey all,

August 22nd-24th marks Vancouver's 6th annual Anime Evolution convention. Last year saw 4,200 people gathered together for cosplay, fandom and a convention experience. This year estimates a 5,000 person attendance.

Running alongside Anime Evolution is Cloud City, a gaming convention/event hosted by the Vancouver Gaming Guild. I think it's going to be really awesome, for a bunch of reasons:\n
  • Jake Richmond and Matt Schlotte (authors of Panty Explosion and Classroom Deathmatch, and author of Sea Dracula) are going to be coming as Guests of Honour.
    • \n
  • As it's a gaming con within an anime con, there's going to be a great cross-section of seasoned veterans and people new to gaming (but already invested in some very similar geek subcultures).
    • \n
  • Anime cons are quite a spectacle to witness. Especially as Anime Evolution has a huge cosplay focus.
    • \n
  • Vancouver is a beautiful city, worth exploring (if you find a moment to explore it in).
    • \n
  • Personally, I'm going to be bringing a lot of energy and enthusiasm to the table. I'm working to invite others who'll bring just as much to the plate. I think that this con can and will rock because people will make it rock. Go Play NW and Conquest NW let me see exactly how much west coast gamers rock, and how intensely they do so.
\nIf you're on the west coast, or even if you're not, I hope there's room in August's busy con schedule. Cloud City and Anime Evolution are going to be a treat - a huge amount of brilliant energy and an interesting cross-section of people at the gaming tables. August 22nd-2th, Vancouver BC.

And finally, if you have anything you'd really like to see there, talk to me about it! I'm a member of the Vancouver Gaming Guild, and I'm quite invested in making this con rock.
