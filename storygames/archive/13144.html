\n WHEN?

Saturday, November 13th; 9:30 am - 6:00 pm

WHERE?
The Episcopal Church of the Redeemer, 6211 NE 182nd Street, Kenmore, WA.

WHAT?
Emerald City Gamefest 5! The Seattle area's family-friendly game day! With roleplaying, board games, miniatures, card games, prize drawings, and fun for everyone!

HOW MUCH?
As always the Gamefest is FREE!
