\n Hello all! Nurse your Gen Con hang-over (even if you didn't go) with us in downtown Brooklyn at cozy BRIC Studios for a day of gaming! RPG, Board and Card games will be on the menu!
\n
\nRecess will be held at BRICSTUDIO from 11am to 10pm. On-site event registration begins at 11:00am. Come inside and play some games!
\n
\nWe also host a little nerdy book/comics/DVD/game swap too (just please take home with you anything that hasn't been claimed).
\n
\nFor more info go to http://www.nerdnyc.com/recess/ or check out the "Recess" section of our boards.
\n
\nRecess
\nSaturday, Sept 6th \u2022 11am-10pm \u2022 $15 ($7 children)
\n
\nBRIC Arts
\n647 Fulton Street
\nBrooklyn, NY 11217
\n718 855-7882
\n
\nHope to see some of you there next Saturday!
