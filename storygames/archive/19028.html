\n Pirate World does quite a few new things for Dungeon World/ Fate Core, and the one I reckon you guys might like most is called Luncheon World.

Luncheon World is a guide aimed at streamlining Dungeon World to play online/ over your lunchbreak in 20 mins. It modifies several GM principles, and a lot of the core moves. It runs fairly damn quickly! Most adventures are deliberately short, but can grow and easily form the start of a campaign, or fit into an existing one.

Pirate World has tons of other stuff! Like chapters on the new Hirelings, new Backgrounds (customise your character at level 1), playbooks (9! Including the Brute, Pirate, Fanatic, Reefmonger...). I'd love to try a PBP game here testing all the new stuff.

Here's the kickstarter, currently at 400%:
