\n I've taken a break from some of my major projects to work on something that I toyed with a few years ago.

The project is called Voidstone Chronicles.

At the moment it's in the form of 5 pocketmod books and a few separate decks of cards, one defining character actions (combat techniques/spells/etc.), one for weapons and equipment, another for specific scenes to encounter along the way...the general idea is to pull a lot of the concepts found in computer RPGs back to the table, where they can be expanded in ways that computer's don't really handle well (yet).

Figures, strategic combat, storyline that builds through episodic conflict scenes...it's not going to be for everyone around here, but I know there are a few folks in this part of the online world who enjoy that sort of thing in their games.

Here's where the project currently sits.
