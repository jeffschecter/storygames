\n I am thinking about organizing an informal "Go Play" style day of gaming in Morgantown WV and am looking at event planning sites. Features I'm interested in include:\n
    \n
  • Free
  • Forums to discuss planned events, location, dates
  • Attendee tracking
  • Customizable website appearance (for pictures, maps, etc...)
\nI considered Meetup.com, but their prices are a little steep. Another possibility is Setdot.com. Any advice would be appreciated.
