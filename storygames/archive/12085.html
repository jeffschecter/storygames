    \n A few questions:

    Height: where do GMless or rotating GM or shared worldbuilding games fit into this? Are they a square instead of a cube?

    Width: where would you put Apocalypse World on that scale? It has very codified rules for certain things, but it's the opposite of "if there's no rules it can't be done" and the GM certainly doesn't just tell the players which of their stats to roll for any particular action.

    Depth: this really just says "Positioning" to me.
