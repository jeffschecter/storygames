    \n Hi!

    There are a bunch of story gaming and related activities going on in Portland. Check out Ready, Set, Game PDX (https://www.facebook.com/groups/rsgpdx/) and Play Out Loud (https://www.facebook.com/playoutloudpdx). If you're not on facebook, or if you have any questions, just PM me - I'm the guy you want to talk to about that stuff.

    If you are looking to get an in-home game going, it seems that the best way to do that is to go to a couple of those meetups and meet folks in person. You'll figure out who has similar tastes to yours very quickly. At least, I've had better luck meeting like minded gamers like that than through other means.

    Hope to see you around soon!

    Tayler
