Starting this Friday 3-21-12 @ 8pm - Midnight (Eastern Time) we will be starting a Bi-Weekly Fate based game Online over at Infrno.net (voice required, video preferred).

Genre:
Space Opera (Psionics, cybernetics, androids, and politics)

System:
Fate (a hack, pulling bits and pieces from different Fate based games, and good bit of influence from Apocalypse World)

Style:
Low level and gritty with some deadly combat, and hard choices.
