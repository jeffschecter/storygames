\nhttp://starwars.wikia.com/wiki/Rule_of_Two
"Always two there are, no more, no less: a master and an apprentice." - Yoda

Essentially, I'm looking for a two-player game (GM may be included or not) that explores these themes of ambition, deception and zealousness. The PCs (the master and the apprentice) may have some kind of an endgame where upon one kills the other after accumulating just enough strength and power. Dazzling sword fighting and magical philosophizing are optional but always welcomed. Is there something that doesn't require a ton of hacking?
