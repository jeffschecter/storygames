\n
\nAtomic Array is one of the most popular RPG podcasts on Itunes (Wizards of the Coast flew them to Seattle to cover PHB 2 a few months back) and tends to cover traditional games. But I ran Dogs in the Vineyard for Rone, one of the hosts, and he absolutely loved it. He loved playing so much that Atomic Array interviewed Vincent and myself about Dogs in the Vineyard.
\n
\nI'm listening to the interview now. I'm very excited for Vincent. I suspect many of Atomic Array's listeners haven't heard of or played Dogs so it's a great opportunity to expose Dogs to a wider audience.
\n
\nI hope you guys like the interview!
\n
\nBetween this and our MonkeyDome interview, I'm on a podcast whirlwind tour this week!
\n
\nI don't have much planned for Gencon this year but I'm hoping to run Dogs and a few other indie games for people who have never played. I'm probably attending a few paizo and d20 parties where I'm going to offer to run.
\n
\nSomeone asked me why I talk up Dogs so much. Hell, I talk about it way more than anything I'm working on or am involved in and I don't benefit from promoting Dogs in any way. I think it is an amazing game and I want as many people to know about it as possible. It's one of the few games that delivers consistent fun every time I run it. And I've run it a lot!
\n
\nRock,
\nJohn
