\nshinoBIshonen is a game I made as a birthday present for a friend. It's a RPG for two players centered around two iconic protagonists: the Japanese feudal lord (the daimyo) and his master of spies (the shinobi). It uses a two-character sheet that is called the scroll (or makimono) and a set of five 4-sided dice that are called the caltrops (or makibishi). It's a simple game about love and duty during a time of great turmoil.

The current version that I'm working on is available as a google doc here.
I also have a hand-drawn character sheet here.

I'm putting together the final version that I'll be giving to my friend by the end of January, but meanwhile I'll just share it as a free game and, given the time constraints, I also would love to have some feedback. Please tell me what it made you feel as you've read shinoBIshonen and perhaps played it.
