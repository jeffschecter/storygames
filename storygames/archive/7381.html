\n
\n So I was soft-selling Bullseye pretty good (if you can even call it that). I ended up giving a lot of them away, intentionally. I also had a stack of about 10 or so left on the last day when I had to leave early. I told the Fronters to get them out there, e.g., give one to anyone who buys another ashcan, give one to anyone involved in one of the indie booths who was interested, etc.

Problem is, I have no idea who has them now. If you got one, please let me know so I can keep tabs. To be clear, I will hold open the offer to reserve a setting in the open license if you got the ashcan even if you didn't pay for it.

Tangentially, I should have the wiki updated in a couple of weeks.
