\n Hi!

My new website is up! Permit me to go on for just a bit:

We Are Lost in Play is focused on thoughtful discussion of roleplaying and story game play experiences.

The most visible way we do this is through game reviews. What is a \u201creview\u201d on We Are Lost in Play? It\u2019s exactly that: looking back on the experience of play of a game, re-seeing it in order to understand and communicate that experience. If you want to submit a review, see our Submission Guidelines.

We also have a forum, and just as our reviews are all based on actual play, our forum discussions are structured around talking about specific games\u2014no theory in the air, no pointless chatter. Everything revolves around a game.

We're launching with a review of Witch: the Road to Lindesfarne by Jason Morningstar (there are a few of my old reviews up there, as well).

We plan to put up at least one review every week, on Tuesdays.

There's also a forum with a unique vision moderated by Jesse Burneko. Stop by and let's see what happens.

Thanks folks. If you have any questions I'd be happy to answer them.

(Thanks to Ross Cowman for designing the site and Joe Mcdaldno for formulating the final name)
