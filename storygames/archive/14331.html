    \n Isn't the rapture as the term is being used these days basically an evangelical christian invention from the seventies, based on some weird reading of the english King James bible? I kinda remember reading something about it.

    It sure ISN'T in the catholic doctrine as taught to me in Italy: no people being bodily moved to the sky with tractor beams here. The rise of the Antichrist, sure. The big final war of Armageddon (Al Meghiddo, IIRC)

    It is also true that the apocalypse is a very, very minor part of catholic doctrine and almost never spoken of: it's the various protestant denominations that seem to have latched strongly to that (tiny) part of the bible.

    All this to say... those are failed "end of times" predictions Lester, but not strictly rapture, right?
