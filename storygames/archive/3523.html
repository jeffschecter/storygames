\nNext Top Challenge!
The game of competitive reality television shows
By Kynn Bartlett
Copyright 2007 by Kiva Games

Next Top Challenge! is a roleplaying game for three or more players without a gamemaster. The players take the roles of competitors in an elimination-style reality television show, focused on some quality all the Challengers share. Game mechanics are resolved using bead-threaded cords, based on the Bone White, Blood Red game system.

NTC is currently a draft 0.1 version, without extensive examples of play yet; it's more of a mini-game to be fleshed out later than a complete game, but it's playable now. If you'd like to take a look at eight-page 0.1 version, download a copy from the Kiva Games website (197K pdf).
