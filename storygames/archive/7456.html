\n When I played Bliss Stage for the first time, face to face with someone, I prepared by buying a dollar-store pack of sixers that included a deck of cards and many cheapo poker chips.

The chips? Were very useful.

We also used index cards to build a mech (pilot cards were safety and the head, Anchor relationship was the chassis, legs were Mission Objective, and rels for weapons as needed ): I gave out white chips to indicate Terror, placed Red chips to indicate Threatening, and blue chips for priveliged interludes.

Next time I'm thinking of bringing a little toy anchor or printing out Pilot, Anchor and Authority insignia for people to wear during missions...

What non-essential props have you found useful in your favorite story-games?
