So, @Mcdaldno tweeted thusly earlier: "Getting a tattoo within the hour." In honor of the occasion, I present an attempt at a custom Monsterhearts move. I'm sure you can do better, why don't you?

When you show someone your new tattoo, roll with Hot. On a 10+, they're impressed with how badass/cool/cute it is - gain a string on them and add 2 to your next roll to turn them on. On a 7-9, choose one.
