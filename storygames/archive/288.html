Stupid Up My Text

Jason_Morningstar
March 2006 edited March 2006 in Story Games
Hey everybody, here's a challenge - I want to make the introduction to Drowning and Falling as painful and heartbreaky as possible. At the same time, I want it to sound fun and not mean-spirited toward its lineal progenitors, you know? I'm going for a "thirteen year old Korn fan re-writing D&D" vibe. This is what I came up with while tripping balls on DMT - any suggestions for making it worse?


There is a world far from here - a world like our own, but completely different. A gritty medieval world of magic and mystery with enchanted creatures, drowning, and falling! Are you brave enough to face the danger? Are you not scared of monsters? Do you like acquiring treasure? Do you have good balance and a decent Australian crawl? If so, prepare to test your mettle against the sorts of devilish challenges only your friends could create, because the most amazing heroes will be the only ones to emerge victorious in the savage world of Drowning and Falling!


Best edit gets a pony!
