    \n You put little ones even smaller next to the events. There is one scene shown above. It's very tiny. You can have as many scenes per period as you want, because you can make them tiny and then zoom way in.

    In this Microscope game, we redid the history for Shadowrun, but specified no magic or Awakening. It was really fun and we had crazy ideas.

    Then we picked a likely period and started the campaign right in the middle of it. It was cool knowing that some things might happen in the future. It added relevance, plus, there is a great deal of buy-in when the players helped create the world.
