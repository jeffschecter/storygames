Both editions of TSOY, and the Wiki, disagree about whether the Sky Fire was 100 or 300 years before play starts.

\n

Page 2: "This game is set one hundred years after the Sky Fire"

\n

Page 92: "Three hundred years have passed."

\n

I can see value to either one. If it's 100 years ago, people's great grandparents lived before the Sky Fire and the destruction is fresh in everyone's minds... which is awesome... for Maldor. But that doesn't give the Ammenite culture long to split off from the ruins of Maldor and evolve into its current degenerate state. 300 seems more appropriate for a civilized, if degenerate and insular, culture to evolve from that wreckage.

\n

I was curious whether other folk had chosen one or the other number and why.
