    A topic close to my heart: I'm setting up just such a game using Sorcerer and a variation on Judd's Blood Simple one-sheet.

    However, I'd point out that there's some variation within Leonard's crime novels: the game I'm prepping draws mostly from his harder-edged novels from the 1970s and 80s, Fifty-Two Pickup and City Primeval and Killshot (with Humanity having something to do with honor and "face").

    However, I'm not sure I'd use Sorcerer if I wanted to play a game based on his comic caper/shaggy-dog-story novels like Tishomingo Blues, Mr. Paradise, Get Shorty, or Out of Sight. But I don't know exactly what system I would use: probably Prime Time Adventures.
