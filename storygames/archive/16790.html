\n Here is a tear sheet for a Fighter character. Brood will be the default fantasy setting for Tropez. The final version will be much more polished and have original artwork, but maintains the iconic style of Eyvind Earle / Mary Blair. Of course, there will be user-friendly sheets and detailed rules.

We're working through the rules and soon will have a playtest document ready.

For now, I hope you enjoy!
