    \nOK, is *that* an April Fool? 'Cause seriously, I could see why some people might be offended by this particular joke. Personally I just didn't find it very funny, but there are very few April Fool pranks that I do find funny to be honest. Mostly it's just people trying to come up with ideas that are just believable enough for people to fall for them, but just outlandish enough that they can laugh at anyone that does. Alas, cruelty is the heart of comedy. At least, for some people.
