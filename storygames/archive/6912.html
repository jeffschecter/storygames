\n Hey Folks,

For those of you who always wanted a copy of Drowning & Falling, "The Fantasy Role-Playing Game of Adventure and Death from Drowning and Falling," and were disappointed to find that it was out of print, your time has come! Drowning & Falling is available once again through Lulu.com.

As before, this is a charity project, and profits from sales of the game will be donated to ORBIS, which fights blindness in developing countries. The game is also Creative Commons licensed, so feel free to grab a copy of the text from the Bully Pulpit Games Downloads page and do whatever you like with it!
