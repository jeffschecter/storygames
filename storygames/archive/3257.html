\n My FLGS finally got its IPR order in last Friday, so I picked up my pre-order copy of Michael Hammes and Phil Reed's Vs. Outlaws. Yay! It's so cuuuute! ^_^ I've been showing it around to various people I game with, and I have received several imperious requests to run it soon. I'm scheduled to run a game on July 16 at the local club (our weekly games are booked weeks in advance.) Hearty congratulations to Ronin Arts for creating such a handy, attractive, affordable little game. Sure, it's gimmicky -- but it's the good side of gimmicky!
