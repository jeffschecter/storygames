    That PDF does need some updating. There have been a bunch of minor tweaks and changes since I published it. Enough changes so that it's hard to keep track of them all. I'll see what I can do about getting a 2.2 release out there in the next week or so.

    \n Hi all,

    I just made a compilation of all the changes and usefull bits I could find.
    If you have somethings else I didn't include, feel free to complete.


    Awesome tokens : the bonus you can earn from them is now either +1 to the roll or +1 damage.

    Yari Master : the talent should read :
    "When you attack with a reach weapon you may make two attacks instead of one."
    (From Eric's blog).

    Very heavy weapons :
    "Very Heavy weapons do 2 damage on a regular attack and get +2 damage when they hit you In The Face."
    (From Eric in this post)

    Mighty Grip (Bear talent) : no more "very very heavy weapon". And see very heavy weapons above.

    Armor class :
    "+1 Armor Class does not mean add 1 to the target number for you to be hit. I means your armor class goes up one category. If you aren't wearing any armor and you're weilding a Reach weapon, then your Armor Class is Light. Meaning the TN to hit you is 10, not 9.

    Same thing with the monster powers of Thick Skin and Hard to See.

    That's how you can get an armor class of Uber. But Uber +1 AC still equals Uber. 16 is the best possible TN to be hit."
    (From Eric's blog)

    A new monster power :
    "for a monster who can become a swarm of something:

    Swarm - This monster can turn itself into a tight knit swarm of smaller beasts. While in swarm-form the monster has +1 Armor Class and treats any arena like a favored arena. The monster may not assume swarm form when it has 5 HP or less. The monster automatically fails all Eloquence rolls when in swarm form."
    (From Eric's blog)

    A GMing advice (from this thread) : during a fight, if you feel that your monster is too weak, don't hesitate to give it another power. The 5 xp value bonus compensate the power bump.
    You can find some other good GMing advices in the same thread.

    For adventure construction advices, see this post.

    I hope it will help.
    Now, go playtest : it the best way to help speed the release
    Antoine
