    \n Those would work for static hexagrams, but I'd prefer a custom d8 that generates just one line (old yin on 1, yin on a 2-4, yang on a 5-7, and old yang on an 8). Ideally, each die would be marked to show which of the six lines it generates. To create the first hexagram you roll 6d8 and line them up, and then you reverse any dice that rolled old yin or old yang to create the second hexagram.
