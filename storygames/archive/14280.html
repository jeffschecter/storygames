\n It's that time again! Saturday, 4 June at GameKastle (1350 Coleman Ave, Santa Clara), 11AM-9PM, we'll have the third installment of South Bay Story Games Day, which narrowly avoided being subtitled "3: The Marsupials" because Nightmare on Elm Street 3's name was so much more apropos. Anyway, as usual, it's a totally decentralized event, organized by this spreadsheet here. If you want to run/facilitate a game, add it to the spreadsheet; if you see a game you want to play, add yourself to the list underneath. Looking forward to seeing everyone there!

    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n No worries. We had four and we had fun - last time we had 20 and we had fun. And next time we'll have 30 and we'll still have fun!

    So we started with Grant, John, Oliver and me, braving a most uncharacteristic June downpour. We had a fun Fiasco using the Objective: Zebra playset, and then moved on to Happy Birthday, Robot! (Robot was, oddly enough, on a diet!) Grant left and so Oliver and John and I played a pretty boisterous game of Nasocorn that Vince would have enjoyed, insofar as there were boxes and clouds (and a circle - that was the portfolio, the boxes were processes, the cloud was the Cloud, and they were integrated, as this slide deck communicates...). Then John had to leave, so Oliver and I played Showdown, in the court of fthe Sun King, with his unlettered provincial butting heads with my coarse and disagreeable Parisian. This all happened in about six hours. I might go home and play The Smoke Dream, just to complete the look.

    Gentlemen, thank you for coming. It was a genuine pleasure.
