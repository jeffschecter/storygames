\n [Supercrew] Does anyone know of any future for this awesome little indie supers RPG?


I've just loved reading Supercrew. Simple and smart.
I've found all the (current) online reviews, player aids, character builds, etc... that my Google-Fu can muster.

What I cannot find is ANYTHING having to do with the original creator, or if he would ever plan on adding to the Supercrew core books with supplements or adventures. Or if anyone's planning (or even legally can) publish 3rd party material.

Clearly this is an indie game, so I understand that the support won't be wide-spread. However, I'd love to know if anyone knows anything about the future of this little gem.
Or even how to contact the author to ask any of the above questions.

Any thoughts?

    What is stopping people from creating new stuff for the game? The mechanics are simple and you don't need a license to use them. If you want to create "official" Supercrew supplements than you need the permission of the designer. But what's stopping people from calling it Superteam. Or to create sister-games, like Horrorcrew, Aliencrew or Herocrew.

    I would be surprised if the designer has a problem with people expanding his game.
