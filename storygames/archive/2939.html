I don't know when this started, but Lulu appear to be doing a promotion on shipping to the EU right now. They haven't said when this will end. This is a substantial saving for heavy items (Just to give you an idea, I just ordered both core Earthdawn books in hardback. That's over 1000 pages combined. Shipping was \xc2\xa32). I imagine this will take a while to arrive, but that's OK. It gives me time to find somewhere to keep the books.

From http://www.lulu.com/help/index.php?fSymbol=shipping_faq\n

    \n Lulu (as far as I understand it) have a facility in Spain and one in the UK. But apparently these issues have been resolved and the print quality from the EU production lines is pretty much on a par with the stuff that comes from the US. I haven't ever received anything from the EU sites though, so I'm probably not the best person to ask.
