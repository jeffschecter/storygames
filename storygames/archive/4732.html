Idea: The Carpool Design Contest (One for the Road)

\n
\n
\n
\n\nRobert_Bohl \n\n\n
\n
\n\nNovember 2007 \n edited November 2007 in Story Games
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n So I'm GTalking with Nathan Herrold about possibly carpooling to JiffyCon and I got an idea:

Design a roleplaying game with the people you are carpooling to a convention or event. Restrict the design work to the duration of the drive and any pit-stops you make. When you arrive to the con/event, the judging begins, maybe with a brief playtest/demo as part of the track.

It seems like the situational constraints might be enough, but we might also consider the constraint that it must be something that could be played in a car.

What do you think? It might be something we could even do for Dreamation if people are excited about the idea.
