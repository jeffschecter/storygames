\n Best laid plans and all that, we had to move up our Numenera game. In this first episode, we go through character creation and talk a bit on where we want to start exploring. We look at the pretty innovative "descriptor," "type," and "focus" format, differences in glaives, nanos, and jacks as well as the general conflict resolution mechanic.
