\n Hey there,

We're getting together around the gaming table in western MA again! JiffyCon is our local con that's been going strong since 2006. We have a tradition of running indie and small press games, many run by their designers, and an equally beloved tradition of making sure to have plenty of kick-back hangout time together before and after the gaming.

Event:
JiffyCon Northampton
Sunday, January 23, 2011

Schedule:
Doors open at 9:00 a.m.
Sign up for Games : 9 a.m. to 9:30 a.m.
Session 1: 9:30 a.m. to 1:00 p.m.
Lunch Break: 1:00 p.m. to 2:30 p.m.
Session 2: 2:45 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.

Entry Fee:
$20 adults, $5 kids 6-17, 5 and under free

The lineup of games and GMs as of today is:

The Anthropophagy Society (J. Ellingboe)
In a Wicked Age (E. Torner)
Annalise (K. Jones)
Psi-Run (M. Baker)
Slayer of Dragon RPG (G. Gigee)
Dread House (E. Boss)
Intro to Apocalypse World (J. Crocker)
The White Lotus (E. Shoemaker)
Misspent Youth (R. Bohl)
Time & Temp (Tresi)
Swords Without Master (E. Ravachol)
Harry Potter: The Missing Students (A. Barktok)
Rock of Tahamaat (V. Baker)
Esoterrorists -- Profane Miracles (T. Russell)

We'll also have a miniature painting workshop running throughout the day, courtesy of Pete Serio at Modern Myths, one of our wonderful FLGSs.

We've got a beautiful new space in the heart of Northampton. Conveniently located in Thornes Market near many fine eating establishments with everything from vegan & vegetarian delights bust your gut barbecue within walkable distance for lunch. If you have questions about travel or accomodations, post here or contact us at jiffycon-at-gmail-dot-com.

Sign ups are open for running games through Jan 1. To sign up to run a game fill out our online form:
JiffyCon GM Signup

Hope to see you there!

Best,
Emily
