EVOLUTION PULSE
Evolution Pulse is a setting, compatible with Fate Core and Fate Accelerated. In the handbook you\u2019ll find all the information necessary to imagine and play adventures in a dark dystopian future, where you will move unique characters able to manipulate reality. Here too you\u2019ll find information on not just the Executors, those beings linked to various AI, but also the Heakth, the deadly interdimensional monsters devouring the world.

THE CONCEPT OF REALITY
The whole universe of Evolution Pulse is based on the concept of reality. You will explore this concept as protagonists. Each character have their own way of interacting with and changing reality around them. The Hekath are able to assimilate and destroy reality itself. During a game, players will answer seven key questions in order to define their reality and sketch a new and specific
game of Evolution Pulse.

CHARACTER ARCHETYPES
Each archetype is described in its own chapter, containing all the information needed for the creation of characters, description of Approaches, exclusive Stunts and examples of Aspects, in addition to all the information necessary to start playing. In the handbook there will be 4 main archetypes.

THE PROJECT
Evolution Pulse is a project that starts from the idea to create an obscure world strongly characterized in his essence, but at the same time it leaves a lot of blank spaces in which players can put their ideas. Fully embracing the Fate style, the collaboration and proactivity of the players will contribute to define the concept of reality in their claustrophobic and dark game session!

Evolution Pulse will be available in english in the first quarter of 2016.
