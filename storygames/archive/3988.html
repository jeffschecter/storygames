    \nMutants Down Under was one of the After The Bomb books, which was the post-apocalyptic campaign setting for TMNT, originally sold as a series of 48-page books for $7.95 each. The earlier books had art by Eastman and Laird (or one of them, can't remember which), and in a lot of ways (like the Empire of Humanity that employs squads of mutant dogs) it was one of the precursors of Rifts. It's hard to say whether it's worth it just for the art; it's been at least 5 or 6 years since I last laid eyes on one of those.

    They also did a new version, a single stand-alone game book with all of the TMNT-ness removed since they'd lost the license.
