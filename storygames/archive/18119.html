\n I played Durance twice at HolmCon. It's one of those games that has simple rules, but you still want to play it once or twice just to get under its skin.

These things are in the rulebook, but we didn't realize how important they were first time around:
- Make sure to take some time to visualize and detail each character before you move on to the next. Some games work well if you just go "we have a name and a function, let's see how the rest emerges during play", but with Durance you have a lot of characters, and the game works better if you know who's who before you play.
- Definitely make sure there are relationships between the characters.

We tried out another thing that worked well for us:
- When detailing the planet, go around the table once or twice and let everyone ask the next player a question to answer. "What happens in the 1-week-long dry season?" "What sound do those stampeding animals make?"

What are your tips?
