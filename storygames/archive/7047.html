\n This is the first public announcement for Nerdly Beach Party III, scheduled for September 19-21, once again at San Simeon state park.

NBP2 in early April went really well, with lots of quality gaming and relatively few hitches. To help iron those wrinkles out, we're going to use the budget surplus from last time to print up a huge banner and buy a bit of portable shade. Other than that, it will be mostly business as usual: Awesome gaming, good food, and nice weather*!

Registration will probably open up some time in August. Stay tuned to this channel for more details!


(*According to Weather Underground, September 19th tends to be a bit cooler than NBP2 was, but it doesn't look too bad)
