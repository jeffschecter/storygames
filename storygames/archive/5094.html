    \n Playdebt is the cumulative effect of not getting enough play. Symptoms of playdebt include writing convoluted, unplayable games, forgetting about social dynamics, mental fatigue, and grumpiness. There is debate in the roleplaying community over the specifics causes and cures for playdebt, but it is widely considered caustic and even dangerous to the gaming community as a whole. Go Play is the most widely known popular campaign against the effects of playdebt on our community, and attempt to reduce playdebt across the community by addressing such important issues as play poverty and player shortages in various regions.
