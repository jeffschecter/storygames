    \n Keep in mind, NCGD started as a purely ENWorld thing. This was way back when I was like 13 years old, and living in Raleigh, and had never played any game but D&D.; A few times a year, people who talked to one another frequently on ENWorld got together and played games together. The first events were, like, 3 tables in the AM, 3 tables in the PM, pretty tightly scheduled. People would drive up from South Carolina and down from Virginia for it, so everyone wanted to know what they'd be playing and running so nobody ended up wasting his time. And the predominant style of game required quite a bit of prep, so off the cuff pickup games weren't so feasible.
    \n
    \nMost of a decade after the inception, a lot of that character still colors things, I guess.
