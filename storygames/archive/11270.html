\n I'm hoping to run a game of Full Light, Full Steam soon, and have a few niggling questions about rules. I hope someone here can provide answers!
\n
\nScrip sheets: At the start of a session, each player lists their character's thematic batteries, and as they do so, the other players note them on their own scrip sheet. Do players note their own character's thematic batteries on their own scrip sheet? For instance, if Alice, Bob, and Charlie are players (with Dennis as GM), are there three scrip sheets each with three sets of batteries, or are there three sheets each with two sets of batteries?
\n
\nDice rolling: If a character is using Coordination 3 and Weightless 3 to perform some task, I take it they roll four dice and count the third-smallest die twice to make their total. So if they rolled 1 3 5 6, their total would be 10.
\n
\nPromotions and demotions: Can a promotion of +2 be split into two +1 promotions?
