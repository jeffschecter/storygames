    \n Virtually all of John Wick's small-form games clock in under 25 pages. Just counting the ones that are undeniably standalone games (and not arguably add-ons like Sexcraft or The Flux):

    Cat
    Eldritch High
    Wilderness of Mirrors
    Byron Falls
    Shotgun Diaries
    All The Days of My Children's Hospital
    Yesterday's Tomorrow


    Others that I have include:

    Do: Pilgrims of the Flying Temple
    Adventures in Oz
    Annalise
    Ocean
    Black Seven (and Animus)
    Hollowpoint (minus handouts)
    Broken Gears
