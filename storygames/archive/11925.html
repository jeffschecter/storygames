\n In Sweden the Easter holiday is GothCon weekend. Sweden's biggest and oldest con with 1500-2000 players. I was involved in the Indie gaming lounge this year as usual.

I did a short writeup on my blog about the event and the games played, check it out if you are interested.

http://wilper.wordpress.com/2010/04/09/indierummet-at-gothcon-xxxiv/
