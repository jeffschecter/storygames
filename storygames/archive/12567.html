\nSo here's a little thing I did:






This all needs to be tightened up a bit more, but my thinking was that they would fit on both sides of a half-letter sheet, and could be printed and available at all the booths and event tables.

I'm interested in your feedback, of course- do they seem useful, did I miss anything, are there mistakes, etc. I'm also interested in thoughts on how to get them printed and made available at the convention.

Vendors: if you'd like to send me a better version of your respective logos, feel free- I was working with what I had, but I'm happy to make it look better.
