    \n There's a couple of things I want to try first.
    When I ran that con game where both the insp was flying and the positioning for adv/disadv with scene elements where flying, it was great. They didn't get positional adv every roll, so that's when they used their insp.

    I introduced my own houserules and since then the insp has been flowing. Almost to the point of annoying players.

    So now I need to crank up the volume on scene positioning. Thinking of taking a couple of leafs from AW's "read the sitch" (or the house rules at the end of The Seclusium of Orphone of the Three Visions)... or something else... but I got the phrase "How can I get advantage in this situation" in my head. Maybe that's too explicit.

    Also I need to put interesting elements INTO the scenes, riffing of "Cool Things That Could Happen" in the old Feng Shui game, and making NPCs do them so the players get jealous and also try to do it.
