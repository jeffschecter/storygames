\n So I went overboard playing with my daughter and her littlest pets - one was possessed by a ghost and they needed the help of an exorcist littlest pet to get her back. Although my daughter seemed fine at the time, that night - scared & nightmares. Yay me. Great dad. In my defense, she's almost finished book 5 of the Harry Potter series, so I thought she could handle it.

Anyhow, for when next we play, I'm going to dial back to kid's show level threats and dangers. Things like:

* purple cat doesn't want to share her cake, blue cat is sad
* pink cat cheats when she plays hide & seek, how do the other animals confront her
* yellow cat never wins and is sad
* a new animal comes to littlest pet land and accidentally offends people ...

And I had to wrack my brain just to come up with those. Anybody got more for me?
