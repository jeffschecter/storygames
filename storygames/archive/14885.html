    \nfulminating - Protesting vehemently. Flashing like lightning.

    syzygy - a conjunction or opposition, especially of planetary bodies

    illusory - based on illusion

    demesne - lands of an estate retained for the owner's use

    contrivance - something created inventively to serve a particular purpose. Use of same.

    chicanery - use of trickery to achieve a political, financial, or legal purpose

    machinations - plots or schemes

    embower - surround or shelter (a place or a person), especially with trees or climbing plants

    ephemera - things that exist only briefly

    phlogiston - a non-existant substance supposedly released by combustion

    endeavor - to try hard to do or achieve something. An attempt to achieve a goal. (I have a soft spot for words that are both noun and verb.)
