\n I've started up a new forum for talking theory and stuff. (Gawd, that took longer than I thought it would, but now it's all tweaked out.) My goal is to "move beyond" the Big Model, while not ignoring it. I'm also very interested in discussing how we learn, teach, and practice "what we do" (aka role-playing).

Story 'Prov > Community

I'm not finished writing up all the stickies and such, but you can go check it out. Two notes:\n
  • I'm stealing a page from Knife Fight. Applying for memberships gives you the status of "New Member", with only limited posting access to the site. To qualify for full membership, you need to write a "Reflection on Play" about a play experience where you learned something, particularly something about yourself or another person. After that, your status will be upgraded to regular "Member".
    • \n
  • You're required to use your real name, either as your username or posted in your signature.
\nThanks!
