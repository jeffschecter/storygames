    So all the classes are[edit]negative[/edit] female archetypes. The halfling is Lolita, the Magic User is the Crack Whore, the Cleric is the Nurse. I want to add a Paladin aka the Tight Bunned Librarian. The Elf is the Corporate Ice Queen. The fighter is a Biker Chick. Actually they're all biker chicks on their way to Sturgis. They stop at a Monster Truck rally. Make that an Orc-ridden Monster Truck Rally.

    Magic is all drugs. Magic users get illegal drugs, Clerics use prescription drugs.

    Bargle is "Barbara".

    That's about all I have on it, so it might not actually happen, but it sure was exciting to talk about Emily and Vincent last night.
