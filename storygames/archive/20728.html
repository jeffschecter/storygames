\n I was at barns & noble a while back and saw that they had a car guest book you could buy. This would be like a guestbook in a bed and breakfast or something, usually they just have lines in them.

But this had choices, pick the type of music that was listened to, what did the driver do wrong. It was mildly clever, but it reminded me of the game Space Alert New Frontiers which included achievements you could earn when playing the boardagame, and each achievement gave you points to leveling up a character.

It was stuff like "Everyone agrees your leadership was great," or more specific stuff like "Make it to each room in the ship during one game." If you haven't played this boardgame you should its a ton of fun.

But yeah It made me think that you could make a cool set of achievements for a roadtrip and maybe even leverage that into a sort of passive roadtrip or car RPG.
