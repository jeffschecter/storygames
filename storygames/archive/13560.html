\n Hey folks,

So I have a nice little giglet with Voice of the Revolution where I will be doing some interviews they need done now and then. In addition, I'll be doing short, 5-10 minute interviews on spec for consideration on the show. My first spec piece ran already: it was Kevin Weiser of The Walking Eye talking about making street teams for indie RPGs.

Anyway, I'm wondering if you can suggest people you'd like to hear interviewed, and also indicate what subject you'd like to hear them talk about.

(Keep in mind the time limit and the nature of the show: Indie Press Revolution's house podcast.)
