\n There appears to be a new convention starting up in Long Island, NY. RetCon claims to be dedicated to gaming (all kinds), with the first con being held in August. From the look of it, they are still piecing their web site together, and the rpg portions are pretty slim; however, they just sent out a call for GM's to run games and such.

Who knows how this will turn out, but it seems like there is an opportunity to "set the tone" for independent games coming out of the gate for anyone inclined to do so.
