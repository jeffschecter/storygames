\n So I finally got to play Fiasco at Dreamation this year. Never been a big GMless game fan but Fiasco really changed my opinion and I had a great time playing Jen Wong's Golden Panda Playset.

I bind books as a hobby so I thought I would share my Fiasco tribute with the rest of you. I took the core rules and all the available playsets at the time and stitched them into a single book.
If you want to check out any of my other books you can see them on my flicker account. RPG Book Bindings

    \n Thanks Everyone, To answer your questions...

    Wightbred, basic book binding is not too difficult but working with leather is a little more challenging. I eventually took a class to learn some actual technique but for the most part I learned most of it through trial and error. If you have any general questions I would be happy to try and answer them here. If you got detailed questions feel free to email me at thebookguy32@gmail.com and I will try to answer them.

    John, Thanks my Burning Wheel books are some of my favorites. The Dresden Files is cool because the glowing runes but it is the least playable of my creations. Looks good on a shelf but hard to actually use as a book.

    jessecoombs, I have done a commission or two but I don't like talking business on an open forum if you are interested shoot me an email (see above) and we can talk in more detail.
