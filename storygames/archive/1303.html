    Max,

    Memoir '44 is one of a kind in the Days of Wonder line, but the system (Commands and Colors) is available in Civil War and Rome vs. Carthage flavors as well.

    Battlelore is supposed to be an upgrade to these rules, as well as containing rules for creating your own, individual armies. It's supposed to be expanded upon by both boxed sets and blister packs. Play time is also supposed to be around the 60 minute mark. There will be an online scenario creator that anyone owning the game can use, and your scenarios will be saved for others to play.

    If the game is supported even half as well as Memoir '44, it will be absolutely amazing.

    BattleLord is the fourth Command & Colors game from Richard Borg. The first, Battle Cry, is an American Civil War, published by Avalon Hill. The second, Memoir '44, is set in WWII, published by Days of Wonder. The third, Command and Colors: Ancients, features Rome and Carthage (Greeks coming soon), and was published by GMT Games. Days of Wonder is indicating that BattleLord will be "even bigger and different" from M44.
