    hm, okay.

    \n

    I don't understand what the art is doing here. On the one hand there is this texture-collage thing going, which is reminiscent of your ashcan cover approach, but on the other hand everything is lined in these heavy -almost- black lines. Looking at it up close, the textures are all pleasingly rich; from a distance the richness becomes visual chaos.

    \n

    It's living in a very weird space between collage and cartoon.

    \n

    So my feeling is that the text needs to at least match with this treatment; if it doesn't look like paper cutouts it should look like hand-lettering in ink. I might consider moving it outside the bounds of the image, or using the title and attribution as the text in the wall pattern.
