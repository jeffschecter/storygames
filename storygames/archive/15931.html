\n I know a few of you are in my neck of the woods - that Bellevue / Renton / Redmond part of Washington - and a trek across the water to get in your indie one-shot gaming is sometimes too much.

Well, now, every other week we're playing on the Eastside as well - at Dragon's Lair in Bellevue on alternate Tuesday evenings.

The next one is March 6th: http://www.storygamesseattle.com/events/53360792/

Oh yeah.
