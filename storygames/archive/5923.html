\n Alrighty, folks, registrations are open for Nerdly Beach Party II: The Wrath of Tevis! We'll be heading out to San Simeon State Beach Park for the weekend of April 11-13 for a ton of gaming, socializing, and camping.

Registration this year is just $35, and we have a parent-and-child deal for $60 (although check the Kid Plan if you're bringing kids).

The registration price gets you:

* Campsite at San Simeon State Beach Park
* Food: Saturday breakfast and lunch, Sunday breakfast and lunch (Friday and Saturday dinner is BYOM)
* All the gaming you can shake a stick at

Register here.

We had an excellent time last year, and we're looking forward to a great time this year, as well. Looking forward to seeing you there!
