\n For those interested, I just posted up images of the play deck for my ashcan game, Know Thyself. It's a hybrid of an RPG & card game.

Here're two, to give you an idea of what I'm doing here:


Check them all out at: http://www.tomorrowtheworldgames.com/knowthyself/ashcan/cards/

Right now, it'll only be available at the Ashcan Front next week. I don't have any plans on releasing it in ashcan form outside of that, unless I have so many left over after GenCon that I feel like offering some via mail order.

Specifics for the game design geeks in the audience: Full color (technically on both sides, though the back doesn't use much color) on 110 wt. card stock, printed on with an HP 4700. Printed out as 3x3 sheets of the same card, cut at Kinkos. Shot with two different digital cameras (the specs I don't have on-hand, since my photographer handles that) using a homemade light box. Processed in Photoshop, mostly by me. Final dimensions are just shy of 3.5" by 2.5".

    \n Jackson, that playtest really helped me understand the various problems with the game, and I'm really glad you were able to pop in. I changed a lot of it around after the GPNW playtest, and the feedback you gave at lunch on Sunday was really helpful.

    to give you an idea of the sheer weight of the changes: there's only one Amnesiac now (and effectively everyone else co-GMs the game, so it's a one-player, many-GM experiment), so that instead of telling four mediocre stories, you're telling one strong one. And I took out the "one conflict per scene" idea. It's funny how feedback works!
