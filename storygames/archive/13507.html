\n Heya,

I broke this off from the old thread which was getting really long and cumbersome. And I wanted to keep comments about the new site separated and highlighted so I could better respond to them. But I will say, that the previous thread on the neighborhood is one of the most functional large-scale collaborative efforts I\u2019ve seen in a while. Thank you to everyone who participated.

So, here it is: The RPG Crossroads

Thank you so much Emily for the idea and for the name, and to Luke for solidifying it in my mind. All feedback about the look, usability, and arrangement of the site is welcome. I appreciate all the great feedback I\u2019ve gotten already. Please report any old references to the previous names of the site. I'm trying to catch them all, but I might miss a few. Thanks everyone!

I\u2019ll meet you there, at the crossroads!

Peace,

-Troy
