      \n I play very few games that I *don't* want to play again. There are many many awesome games out there.

      That said, some specific wants:

      Mouse Guard - My one short-lived campaign was super fun, and I want to learn the rules better.
      Lady Blackbird - Campaign-style, like five or six sessions.
      Spellbound Kingdoms - It seems like a game that would reward rules mastery. Plus there are multiple types of peg-legs on the equipment list. You need long-term play to use different peg-legs effectively, I think.
      Storming the Wizard's Tower - Because I love it and can't wait to see the new/finished version.
      Mountain Witch - I only played a single campaign, but I think back to it a lot.
