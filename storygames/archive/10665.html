    \n There's Odyssee 2009 in Berlin, Germany.
    Friday 30th October - Sunday 1st November 2009
    http://odyssee-con.de

    Since its "thing" is to exclude the three most-played RPGs in Germany (D&D, Shadowrun and Das Schwarze Auge), it's usually a bit different from other conventions. It also has slightly more of a story-games emphasis.

    And then there's Burg-Con 19 in Berlin.
    Saturday 15th May - Sunday 16th May 2010.
    http://nexus-berlin.de/Burg-Con_19

    By default, the more traditional of the two RPG conventions. And since it doesn't have any constraints on what kind of RPGs are played there, the location it is named after provides the convention with some character.
