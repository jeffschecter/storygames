On its own thread, as it's a bit left-field for Shreyas's thread. (Let me know if I err about basic HQ details.)

What I find cool about the HQ resolution system: you have a bunch of traits rated on the same scale, and resolution is dominated by one of the traits. You are encouraged to bring in the others to augment your primary trait, but the effect of these augments is slightly muted, compared to the primary traits.

The new mechanic uses a die pool of d6s and d4s. Traits are rated between 1 and 3, with most being 1. By default, you roll (rating)d4, and if any dice come up with a "1", that's a success. Augments working by rolling (rating)d6 for each trait you bring in, and any dice coming up "1" count as an "mastery" for you - pushing your successes up or your opponent's successes down.
