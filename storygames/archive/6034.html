\n Milton Keynes RPG Club are holding another of their games days on 27 September 2008.
\n
\nWHO?
\nWe're Milton Keynes RPG Club. Find out more about us at http://www.mk-rpg.org.uk.
\n
\nWHEN?
\nSaturday 27 September 2008. Doors open at 9.00am, first games start at 10.00am. We'll finish at 11.00pm.
\n
\n(We'll be doing another on 14 March 2009 at the same times.)
\n
\nWHAT?
\nAny roleplaying or board game. You want to play it, bring it along and run it. Previously we've had on offer Amber, Call of Cthulhu, Cold City, Dogs in the Vineyard, GURPS Banestorm, Gwenthia, HeroQuest, Hollow Earth Expedition, Mythic Russia, Primetime Adventures, Savage Worlds, Spycraft, Tekumel, Transhuman Space, True20, Vampire, and some more that I've forgotten. Latest updates will be posted on the Concrete Cow website.
\n
\nA full list of games on offer is on the Concrete Cow games page.
\n
\nWHO'LL BE THERE?
\nWe'll be inviting some Guests of Honour. Details when we get them.
\n
\nSome members of the Collective Endeavour, a coterie of UK Indie RPG designers, will be coming along too. That means you can expect all sorts of indie RPG goodness, if that's the sort of thing that floats your boat.
\n
\nAnd, of course, there's all the fantastic games you'll bring along and run. If you let us know what you're planning to run, we'll put it on the Concrete Cow games page.
\n
\nWHERE?
\n
\nThe Old Bath House, Wolverton, in the north-west corner of Milton Keynes. The full address is
\n
\nThe Old Bath House
\n205 Stratford Road
\nWolverton
\nMilton Keynes
\nMK12 5RL
\n
\nMaps are available on MK Web, Google Maps, and Streetmap.co.uk.
\n
\nDirections for drivers are on the directions page.
\n
\nRegular trains to Milton Keynes run from London Euston station. The nearest station to the con is Wolverton (one stop north of Milton Keynes Central), about five minutes' walk away.
\n
\nHOW MUCH?
\n\xa35.00, payable on the door. This'll cover the cost of hiring the hall.
\n
\nFor more information, visit our Yahoo group or email concrete-cow-owner@yahoogroups.com

    \n\nJuly 2008 \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n A quick update on the preparations for Concrete Cow 08\xbd. Wood Ingham, our Guest of Honour, has confirmed the games he'll be running on the day. He'll be running two games, previewing the Vampire clanbooks, and the supplements for Hunter: the Vigil. Both of them tell the same story, one from the viewpoint of some vampire hunters, and one from the viewpoint of the vampires.
    \n
    \nHunter: The Vigil - The Revenge of the Cainite Heresy.
    \nCrazed fanatics come from nowhere, thrall to an ancient secret they don't even understand. They don't let the innocent get in the way of a millennia-old crusade. Can our heroes stop them before something awful happens? Or are the fanatics' targets even worse? And more importantly: *Who is Cain?*
    \n
    \nVampire: The Requiem - The Reflection of Alison Gregory.
    \nA dead woman's reflection walks away and wages a vendetta against her, and the entire society of the dead stands to lose. Can the characters find it before it delivers everything they have left straight into the hands of a cult of fanatical vampire hunters?
    \n
    \nJan and Joe, official demo people from Cubicle 7 Entertainment, will be there demoing Cubicle 7 games. They'll definitely be doing demos of the forthcoming Doctor Who game and Starblazer Adventures. They'll also be offering demos of a selection from Victoriana, SLA Industries, and Qin: Warring States, but exactly which games will run isn't yet decided. If you have a preference, let me know at concrete-cow-owner@yahoogroups.com!
    \n
    \nIf you're planning to run a game at Concrete Cow, please send me an email at concrete-cow-owner@yahoogroups.com and I'll put the details on the Concrete Cow games page.
    \n
    \nFinally, in a bid to spread the gaming goodness around, I've put together a Concrete Cow howto. It's just a list of hints and tips for running your own one-day gaming mini-con. It's not difficult to do, so give it a go!
    \n
    \nSee you all on 27 September!
    \n
    \nNeil.

    \nConcrete Cow is growing. This is good, as it means more games, more gamers, and more fun for all. The problem is that space is getting a bit tight. Luckily, the Old Bath House, our usual location, has an extra room we can use. It's a rather nice, light, and airy basement room. But we can't let that stop us! Bring your cuddly toys along (I know you've got them) and we'll see how many Dragons we can get in our Dungeon.
    \n
    \nWe'll even refund the entry fee for the owner of the best Dragon.

    \n More updates! First, about the trader stall.
    \n
    \nCubicle 7 Entertainment and Leisure Games will have a combined stall at Concrete Cow. There will be all sorts of Cubicle 7 games for sale, such as Victoriana, StarBlazer, SLA Industries, and Qin. There may even be some news on the forthcoming Doctor Who game.
    \n
    \nThe Leisure Games end of the stall will have a variety of stock, but will concentrate on things such as the Indie Games, the HP Lovecraft Historical Society T-Shirts, DVDs and various paraphernalia.
    \n
    \nIf you have something in particular you'd like to buy at Concrete Cow, either contact Leisure Games directly, or PM me and I'll pass the message on.
    \n
    \nMake sure you bring plenty of cash to give to the nice gentlemen with the shiny books!
    \n
    \nThe other update is on the Guest of Honour front. We're pleased to announce that Andy Peregrine will be joining Wood Ingham as a Guest of Honour at Concrete Cow. Andy is the Line Developer for Victoriana. He'll be chained to a table and forced to run games of Victoriana all day.

    \n Just over a week to go, and a couple of updates for Concrete Cow.
    \n
    \nFor those of you interested in the forthcoming Doctor Who RPG, these are the demo games that will be offered by Jan and Joe, the Cubicle 7 demo people:
    \n
    \nA Day Like No Other
    \nSix ordinary people, one very extraordinary day.
    \n(Yes, that's all the blurb you get!)
    \n
    \nSix Little Greyhounds
    \nTrap 4 is a top security UNIT facility intended for the study and examination of alien artefacts. In the ordinary course of things duty at Trap 4 is a quiet and efficient hub of science. But since the arrival of the latest batch of Artefacts, there have been a number of unfortunate..... accidents. Can the men and women of UNIT Security Detachment 3 get to the root of the problem or will the dangers of Trap 4 get them first?
    \n
    \nUnfortunately, Shane Mclean, the official Savage Worlds demo guy, won't be able to make Concrete Cow: Real Life (TM) got in the way.
    \n
    \nOn the plus side, I'll be offering a few 10-20 minute demos of various Indie games, to supplement all the exciting things on sale with Cubicle 7. Come and grab me in the gaps between gaming sessions if you want to try something out.
    \n
    \nThe list of other pre-announced games is on the Concrete Cow games page. It's not too late to tell me about your wonderful game! Let me know and I'll put it on the list.
    \n
    \nLook forward to seeing you all on Saturday week!
