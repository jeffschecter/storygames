\n Hey folks, I saw this mentioned once or twice but not issued its own thread yet. Figured it was important enough to get some eyes on it:

http://indieplus.org/doku.php

And their G+ page here:
https://plus.google.com/104356358088070229382/posts

Basically it's an online convention between the 9th and the 15th, with games and gaming panels running mostly over G+.

It's kinda disorganized at the moment, but there's a lot of solid enthusiasm behind it, it's likely to tighten up in the next week or so leading up to the event.

If you're interested in running something (or playing something), make sure to post your event here so that other S-Gers can find you!
