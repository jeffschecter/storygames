\n Would it be possible to do away with all the non-d6 dice in the game? They're mostly just used for damage. 1d8 can be replaced by 1d6+1. 1d6+1 will give the same average result as 1d8, but with a smaller range of values. It's really frustrating to be a fighter and roll a 1 for damage, which is why I wanted to go with something that will give higher minimums, as well as lower maximums to avoid cheap shots.A +1d4 bonus could be replaced by +2. Why hold onto this artifact of old-school D&D?
