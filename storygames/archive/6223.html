    \n Jarrod, I can't do another project right now without my eyes bleeding. This is a 2 stat system, essentially; you're always rolling 1d20+COOL+AWE or 1d20+COOL+CML

    Treat COOL = Level, but Cool Skillz = COOL + AWE.

    Everything and its dog is based on COOL+AWE in this game, as you can see (Skillz, Senses, defenses)

    Weapons have a Determined Probability to Strike (DPS) which is AWE + COOL.

    Leetness defense should be there on the character sheet but only used in Leetness vs. Leetness challenges, where each contender rolls 1d20 and adds Leetness to show who is more Leet.

    With all the skillz and such being equal, flow of play is clearly built around resource management, with these resources:

    * Mana,
    * Properties,
    * Lewtz (incl. Phat),
    * Contacts,
    * Hit Points,
    * Death,
    * Race Features,
    * Minions, and
    * Feats.

    Actions include Sensing, Fighting, Skillzing, and using a resource.

    In any game like this though the biggest important resource is spotlight time - hence the Movement and Initiative systems are crucial.

    Knowing what Alignment to be at what time is probably also important.

    (I seriously wonder what this would be like as a competitive game... like, everyone bets $1 at the start of the game and the one with the most Victory points at the end of the game wins.)

    There, so that's my not designing this, OK?
