    Note that every printing of Polaris looks different:

    1st -- White Cover, blue page, signed, stamped, named, "All Men Are Brothers" edition, slightly different formatting.

    2nd -- Brown Cover, white page.

    3rd -- European edition, slightly different size, darker blue pages, more matte cover, shinier ink, slightly higher image quality.

    4th -- White cover, white page.

    5th -- White cover, blue page, standard guts.

    Expect this trend to continue.
