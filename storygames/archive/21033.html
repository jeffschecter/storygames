    \n I thought this was just going to be a silly April Fool's joke of a game, but it's surprisingly complete. Beyond just reading it as a satire of RPG design, I think you could actually play it. Although I'm not sure what happens when your character playtests their game -- do you actually roleplay that, with Inception-like gaming within the game, complete with pretending to use the sketched-out features/mechanics of the in-game game? The mind boggles.
