\n Over on the RPG masters thread, I jokingly mentioned combining Jared Sorensen's Color Wheel with James West's the Pool and its variants. Well, I was up stupid-early one night and couldn't go back to sleep. I've been needing to finalize the first draft of some kind of playtestable system for Do, so I decided to go ahead and mash-up this unholy remix of the damned.

So here's the Watercolor Wheel, literally cut and pasted together from the original documents with a few edits to bridge the two systems together.

It was an odd experience, like the Edgar Rice William S. Burroughs school of game design. I recommend it to anyone who has a bit of designer's block (or if you're a shameless hack like me ^_^ ).

Just as classical painters trained by copying the works of their predecessors, cutting and pasting bits of other systems sparks some fruitful voids that can spur original concepts.
