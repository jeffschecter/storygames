    There is room in this world for a September con. Someone could totally do that.

    Also, a spiritually satisfying "I'm going here instead of Gen Con con". Remember, tough choices are what make Story Game worthwhile!

    The spread between FabReal and GPNW was nice last year (although I wasn't able to make FabReal myself). Con dates are pretty limited, especially when you start getting a bit bigger, or have other specific space needs. For GPNW, we were already limited to four possible weekends when we started discussing it in August, and that was before we had confirmed a venue. By the time we set our location and started looking at their availability, we had basically one option left.
