      Posted By: Colin_Fredericks
      I love you now.
      \nGlad you like it.

      My main goal with the site is to collect again in one place the amazing set of fonts that Lord Kyl had put together (his site died a couple years ago), and then add more. He had the biggest collection of cipher fonts I've seen to date, along with many historical lettering fonts he made from scans of medieval and early print texts. But he only had a tiny bit of sci fi cipher fonts, and I have collected many of those so far as well.

      More than anything, I want the site to be the best place to go when you are looking to do a cipher font clue for a game. I want you to be able to choose from dozens of excellent choices.

      And as a secondary focus, I want to collect other fonts that you would use for a game prop. Like the fonts S. John Ross put together for making receipts. Which is why, for fantasy type fonts, I'm focusing a little more on fonts that look good for cartography notations or journal entries. That sort of stuff.
