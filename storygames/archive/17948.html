\n In a previous thread about gamers in Ann Arbor, it was revealed that there were a number of story gamers in the Toledo area.
BASHcon is the small gaming convention hosted by the University of Toledo. I have my problems with the con, but I still go each year out of lingering loyalty to my local con.
I don't have any games on the schedule, but I'll be at BASHcon the entire weekend, and I'll be prepared to run pick-up games of Dungeon-World, Fiasco, and/or Dogs in the Vineyard for friends and interested parties.
If anyone's heading to BASHcon next weekend, drop me a post!
