    Hey, guys, there's an important thing about these threads: links.

    \n

    This is how indie publicity works. We rely on these threads for communication with players. Enthusiasm is great! But link people up so that enthusiasm gets communicated to the creators, as well. It also helps turn that enthusiasm into sales, which means more people to play with, which means more feedback, which means better games.

    \n

    It's really important that indie creators be in contact with the players of the games. It used to be obvious, since games were all bought directly from the designer, but since there's IPR and our stuff is on game store shelves, that contact is harder to come by. Links help with that. It means that we can see when our stuff is being discussed, which means we can drop in and answer questions or give recommendations. It means that we can talk about someone's awesome ideas in public and give props back on our blogs or whatever.

    \n

    This is citizen media. You are the producer, publicist, and consumer. You don't just buy, you participate.

In every case, the scuplting is pretty nice and done in very high relief, which makes them very touchable. The backs (you can see them on the 'more info' pages for each coin) don't really match the fronts in style - they're abstract interlaced or concentric line patterns - but they're really pretty.
