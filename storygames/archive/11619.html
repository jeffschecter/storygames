    Besides this does open discussion in a matter I have been thinking a lot lately. What to include in "traditional" playing session. Is scaring the players by yelling in their ears a appropriate way of leading into atmosphere? For my games it is. As is touching, hovering around in darkness and other things that increase the sense of being there in the story.

    Giving a lap dance would be perfectly appropriate in my games, if it suited the story, were performed by a GM that knew what he/she was doing with out braking the atmosphere and made some sense.
