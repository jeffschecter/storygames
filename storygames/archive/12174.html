\n Allow me to introduce myself: I'm the Dessert Czarina for GPNW. This year, for the dinner on Friday, I will be making all the desserts. To do this, I need your nerdly help. I would like my desserts to be game related, think Dust Devils Food Cake, etc. I love me some puns, but will also accept other wordplay and desserts that thematically fit into a certain game ("I totally think that they eat a lot of pavlova in Apocaypse World"). Even just alliteration. I'll be making all manner of desserts: cake, cupcakes, pie, tartes, truffles, cookies...probably some other crazy stuff I haven't thought of yet that defies categorization (probably not anything frozen or with similar complications).
\n
\nAs food-snobbish or plain-jane as you can think of, whatever game you can think of.
\n
\nGo.
