\n So, the news concerning the terrorist(?) attack on furries a few days ago has got me thinking and feeling about those thoughts. It's a big thinking-feeling furpile. I feel for these people. I think Mika Brzezinski is a giant douche(or possibly a turd sandwich?)for not being able to keep her composure during the MSNBC news thing. I'm not even going to call what news is now a news story, because I think those are rare, like a brown unicorn.

Anyway, I was struck with the drama and also the comedy of the thing. It's horrible, but it's beautiful. Please don't misunderstand me, I hope the 19 who went to the hospital are healthy, and I hope they find out who did it and why.

This would make a great story, or I'm guessing a great many stories told from many angles.

It should center on the 19 that were hospitalized, because for whatever reasons I view them as more important then the others who weren't.

Were those 19 people in one wing of a hospital? I want to know more!

So, I imagine that the majority of play takes place in the hospital.

Possible character roles would be 1. patient(furry), 2. friend of, 3. relative of, 4. hospital staff, 5. law enforcement, 6. media(reporters)
