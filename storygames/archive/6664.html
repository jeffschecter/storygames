\n For the last few weeks I've been working on an idea that has been fermenting in my brain for some time. Now the first draft of Perfect Fantasy is available for download.

Perfect Fantasy is a streamlined fantasy game based on the 2006 release of Levi Kornelsen's Perfect 20 and is designed to emphasize quick setup and fast, easy play.

Currently, Perfect Fantasy is only available in a draft form available for playtesting and I'm still plugging away at it.

Click this link to download the playtest file now.
