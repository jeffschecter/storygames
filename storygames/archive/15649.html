\n Howdy all.
I've been running around country Australia on my holidays, and getting interested in all the little town histories and progress. I was wondering, does anyone know of some kind of skeletal system for gaming the growth of a small community? Thinking of something like a group of colonists in a hostile land? Norman invaders settling a town, Vikings in the new world, etc?

I think pendragon had something like it, but never really read too much of those rules ( and don't have hem handy on my iPod!

Anyone tried something like this?

    \nStreets Tell Stories supplement for the Underground RPG had rules for influencing socio-economic conditions in a neighborhood. Increasing the score in any one area (ie making improvements) caused scores to drop in other areas, so as soon as you help out by feeding the homeless you have to scramble to deal with crime or property values or sanitation.
