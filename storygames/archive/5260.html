    Hiya Darcy,

    \n

    Thanks for asking. The plans for the Infected haven't changed recently. At least not in any dramatic way. Over the past few months I've intentionally not looked at it or even thought about it. I wanted to have a nice, clear head about it before I sat down to write the final document.

    \n

    I'm hoping to have the thing off to Lulu around the end of January. I'll be doing all the layout work and I'll probably harness a few local fans for a quick (very quick) bit of editing.

    \n

    oops, I'm out of time. I'll post more later.

    Joe,

    \n

    No, I'm not rushing a dealine. The quickness of the editing is due to my desire to avoid lingering over the thing. Once I've typed the thing out and done a bit of layout work, I want it to be not-confusing and not full of typos. What I don't want to do is to spend any time second-guessing myself or kicking myself for not paying closer attention in English class.

    \n

    In other words, I'll be happy to score a B on this thing. I'm not holding my breath on an A+.
