    \n Sorry that was very brief. The initial thing that caught her was the cover. It didn't look like an rpg so she cracked it open thinking it was a "normal" book. Like most people she skipped the intro and went to chapter 1 and was giddy about the picture and quote of the queen. She flipped through the setting info a bit and tossed the book to the side waiting for me to get home. When I did she demanded I give her the run down; so, I explained that it was like the movie Equilibrium in Steam Punk Victorian England with a criminal underground trying to push culture on the oppressed masses who blindly eat their pudding as their told...some times you just want ice cream dammit!
    \n
    \nI really think it came down to a concept that really just struck her fancy.
