\nI am a recycler. I have been gifted two pieces of technology:
\n
\nCultivated Tech: Pumpkin 2 (Ephemera, Orange, Gourd-like Squash)
\n
\nPrinted Tech: Pie Crust 1 (Cultivation, Tender and Flaky, Baked Goods)
\n
\nWhat do I do with them?
\n
\nRecycling challenge: Dumb material source (0 flow) to Dumb material product (1 flow) = Risk 1 flow.
\n
\nMargin of Victory 3: effect 3, no efficiency bonus.
\n
\nEffect 3: Merge two pieces of tech, take highest rating and three tags.
\n
\nFinal flow cost: 1 (no efficiency)
\n
\nI make pie:
\n
\nResult: Pumpkin Pie 2 (Ephemera, Baked Goods, Orange)
