So, for those folks that like fantasy games and Anime: Here's a great place to pick up private-use character sketches. Over 400 artists, and thousands upon thousands of pictures. It's basically a link to the fan illustration gallery for the largest play-by-post RP website on the planet: Infinite Fantasia:

ht tp://t-walker.jp/mugefan/illust/ (assemble the link in your browser).

The go to the VERY BOTTOM of the page. You will see this menu:


So, check that box on the right for "avoid small icon pics", and select "50" (per page), then click any of the above. The real reason I linked to this is the "BUST PICTURES" gallery: There are THOUSANDS of fantasy (and a few modern) chracter types of various quality (from "Pretty Good" to "HOLY SHIT"). It's basically built for this fantasy world, so there are some demihuman races, and funky classes, but for the most part if you want a "dark swordsman/woman", a "ninja", a "cute elf", a "child thief", you can find a few hundred of each if you look long enough.

When you want to go back and try another search, either relink to the original location, or click on the blue icon in the bottom center of every page to go back to the illustration search page.

Again, if you replicate the link anywhere, please break up the link so that folks have to reassemble it, and try not to img-src link directly to pics on that site. Out of respect. Or, more accurately, fear that they'll find out and block outside non-registered viewers.
