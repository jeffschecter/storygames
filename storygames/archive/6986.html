    \n Ah... Go Play Southeast...

    My life has been a bit chaotic this year. I've started a new job that is 4 hours away from where I live. I drive there and live in an apartment for the week and then drive back home on the weekends. I've volunteered to head up the IGE and GoD tables at GenCon. I'm remodeling my house trying to get it ready to sell. There's several more things I've got going at the moment too that you likely have no interest in. The point being that GPSE will either be pushed back to the winter or will be put off until next year. I just don't have the time, energy or finances to head this thing up right now.

    Someone else is welcome to do so, of course.
