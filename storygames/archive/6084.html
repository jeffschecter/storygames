    \n Right. I hear you definitely. I tried something new this time which I'm going to continue: I tried keeping the interview to an hour and fifteen minutes or so. I didn't fully succeed (I went to an hour and twenty), but it was extremely helpful to me in editing. I had been killing myself lately, editing 2+ hours of recording into an hour-long show.

    So anyway, I had a bit of a knotted stomach the entire interview, nervous about keeping to that time limit. The needs of giving two people "screen time" definitely cut into the kinds of digressions I like to engage in. You can count on 2-on-1s being a rarity, and only happening when a game is truly more than one person's thing (and sometimes not even then).
