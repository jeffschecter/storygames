    \n I don't give out the book during character creation. I just give some quick descriptions of the elements/stats and tell people, "Tell me what powers you want and we'll make it work." I've played a few times, primarily using it as a system rather than a setting (e.g. running Planescape with it) or springboarding from it into other things. I used it as the first game of a multi-system fantasy campaign, where people traveled from one game system to another.

    If you've played or GMed Amber, that helps a little for grokking conflict resolution in Everway.
