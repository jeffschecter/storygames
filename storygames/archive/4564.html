\n So one of the things I've been promising to do for awhile with Passages is "A Cast of Thousands". These are going to be free mini-supplements with descriptions of famous literary characters, stat blocks, new advantages/disadvantages, and an adventure seed or two. The first one I'm doing is Count Dracula (new advantage: Shapeshifting).

What other literary characters should I stat up? Note: They do NOT need to be villains -- just characters that could be used interestingly in an rpg.
