\n I have 3 invitation codes to the beta of http://www.die2nite.com/ if anyone is interested.

Most interesting thing to happen to us so far is the first night someone left our gates open. The zombies attacked and killed all and I mean ALL of our experienced players, leaving the most outspoken newbies to step up and take charge based on what little we knew about the game. I was one of them.

But now I'm stranded in the middle of the desert because I miscalcalated and there's zombies between me and town. I can either risk it and try to run through them but potentially leave everything I scavenged in the middle of a clump of zombies, or get just far enough that if I get stranded outside tonight the rest of the town can get the stuff off my corpse.

Last night one of our better players got stranded in the middle of the wastes and cornered by zombies. It was too late to save him, and a uh overzealous player shut the gate before we could organize an expedition before nightfall. He said goodbye to us on the forum and we said good bye to him. Maybe we'll see him in another game. It was a downer though.

    \n How construction projects get done is sort of weird. In my experience (tiny), a bunch of people will argue on the forums about what should be built when. The only thing anyone agrees on is that a Watchtower is generally the first thing that should go up; other than that, it's just people trying to exert their opinions over each other and threatening complaints (which can get you kicked out of the town) for using resources to do anything other than what they want. Usually, I just end up checking the construction page, and if there's something that (a) we have the materials in the bank for and (b) other people have already started putting AP into building, then I will contribute AP to building it as well.

    Otherwise, I just scavenge and then drop everything I get that isn't food or drugs that I need off in the bank.
