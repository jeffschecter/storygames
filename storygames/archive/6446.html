\n
\n
\n
\n\nSimon_Rogers \n\n\n
\n
\n\nMay 2008 \n edited May 2008 in Directed Promotion
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n I'm pleased to say that with the exception of some mail order copies, Trail of Cthulhu has sold through. That's just short of 2000 copies. As you might imagine I am chuffed.
\n
\nHowever, that leaves me with a problem - an enviable one, I grant you - but a problem nonetheless.
\n
\nHow many to reprint? 1000, 1500 and 2000 are really the choices. There's a range of about $2.00 per copy between the cheapest and most expensive options. Past figures suggest that the first quarter's sales are between 30%-80% of the lifetime of the product, so that's not much help. I'm so tempted to invest my takings into 2000 copies, but I still have Dying Earths left over from the free second print run we got.
\n
\nA wildcard in the mix is that I've released the PDF now. Not sure how that will affect sales.
