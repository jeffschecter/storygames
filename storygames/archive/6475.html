    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n It's about the mystique of the dance club, and of Miami. It's about going to these places, running into famous, important people and doing the big things that give your life meaning. Or at least scoring with them.
    \n
    \nIt's a conventional RPG with a game master who describes the setting and the events. It focuses on resource allocation more than task resolution. It's diceless but it can be random. It has a combat system but focuses more on the social mechanics. Character creation is done as a group with a draft and trade of attributes. It doesn't have much in the way of crunchy bits and the special effects budget is very low.
    \n
    \n-Grant
