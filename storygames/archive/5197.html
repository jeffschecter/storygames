    \n Yep, Raphael ran us a great, tight game of Dread. Maybe one demon too few in the climax--we basically housed them--but the gore was flowing, the stunts were wicked cool, and--BONUS!--I got a free copy of the new edition. Signed by The Man Hisself (I'm such a fanboy).

    And it's a rare, misprinted edition! Raphael, how may proofs did you order that had the over-cropped cover?

    I read all of the player rules Sunday (and enough of the GM rules to know... well, DAMN, dude. METAL!); and I'll post an RPG.net Review within a few weeks. I am in Game Chef, I am helping to run the follow-up Layout Competition there, and I wanna get For Mature Audiences laid out in parallel with the comp. Oh, yeah, and I have some work to do at my job, the next few days. (Whew!)
    ----
    I also got in a game of In A Wicked Age, and now I see what all the fuss is about (e.g. the April Fool's site change). It's another Baker masterpiece: very slick integration of mechanics, narration, and awesome. I didn't see ALL of that awesome at the table, though, because (I found later) we weren't quite playing the conflict rules right. In part, this was to smooth play for new players (i.e. all of us but the GM), but I think some of it was flat-out misunderstanding of round order. No biggie--still fun as hell--but there was a time or two when we missed out on the Firepower of the Fully Armed and Operational Battle Station.

    Oh, and I got a free copy of THAT game, too, by being on the top of the We Owe list at game end. I know, I know--that's pretty easy to manipulate, and I confess that, once I hit the top, I wasn't gonna take that advantage die for love or money--I wanted that copy! In retrospect, I know I kinda "cheated" or "metagamed," and the GM prolly should have given the game away based on a vote for best role playing or a random draw or something.

    But, hell, everyone seemed to enjoy it anyway... excepting MAYBE one dude, who never quite grokked the narrative power in his hands, nor seemed best pleased when my character offed his, to prevent him from interfering with my plans to attain Great Power from Baal--but that is another story....

    Great Game Day, for me; and I will be sure to make it to the next one! Might even be running ASCII @HACK, by then...
