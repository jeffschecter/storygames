A total of six, one for each attribute so I can make a tiny bit of niche protection. If you were making a kitty, or are a kitty, what might some of your goals be? What might some of your problems be?

    I've never played Cat. But I've owned a few over the years. I had a tabby that had an apparently uncurable stomach parasite, meaning that he couldn't get nutrition from about half of what he ate. In other words, he was always thin as a rail. He understood that the louder he begged for food, the more people would give him--and so he played up the "I'm starving" card every chance he got, to the point where everyone in the neighborhood was feeding him. (No, I was not starving the cat. We fed him all the time.) He was an excellent hunter, had mutant paws with extra toes, and enjoyed being fawned over. He was raised in a pound, which is probably where he got the parasite. I'm not sure if "Remove my fuckin' stomach parasite" is an appropriate goal for Cat or not.

    My dad doesn't like cats, and used to kick one that got too close to him. A Cat with the goal, "Make my masters divorce, so that this oaf doesn't kick me any more" might be interesting too.

    MY experience with cats suggests that if you want to give them "goals", you might do best to treat said goals like Beliefs in BW. Something they believe to be true about themselves, others, or the world, and will work hard to prove.
    These are some beliefs my cats have.

    "I am the toughest cat on this block."
    "When the humans boil water, then there will be fish-with-rice."
    "If you sit on their head and lick them, they will submit."
    "There is something wonderful at the back of the pot cupboard."
    "Food doesn't taste as good if you have to share. Get there first."
    "If they catch you being naughty, purr really loud." (More of an Instinct, really)
    "Someday, I will find a way to get that bird out that wire box, and then... Game On!"

    You take really basic feline beliefs like that and mix them up with the magical conceits of Cat, and I think you could get some good stuff.
