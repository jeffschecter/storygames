Okay, I'm posting the character's aspects and you guys (hopefully) will help me come up with some awesome ways to poke at these characters. I'm basically looking for Bangs that would tie into their Aspects/Flags. Even if you don't totally understand what's going on with a particular Aspect or character don't worry about it too much--although I think most of them are pretty self explanatory. Any suggestions will be appreciated!

The campaign is an "Amethyst" style Amber campaign where we've come up our own Elder Amberites although Oberon, Dworkin, the Pattern, Trumps, Castle Amber, The Courts of Chaos are all still part of the setting.

    \n We're using a customized variant of Rob Donoghue's Wheel of Fate system (which is available for free at the Fate Yahoo Group). I've drawn in some things from the Amber diceless roleplaying as well. This lets me have a nice baseline for how the powers work. I've also tied the powers entirely to their attributes as in Amber Diceless. I also gave them 10 aspects (and 10 Fate points) as in SoTC as opposed to Wheel of Fate's starting five aspects. This helps reflect the age and power of the characters. Although I've called them Skills--I'm using the Pools from Wheel of Fate. They're basically broad areas of skill such as Cavalier, Courtier, Noble, Scholar, Sailor etc. I'm not using stunts as I've gone in a more rules light direction for this campaign.

    I'm running the game (as this thread might suggest) by just preparing a bunch of bangs pointed at the PCs. Its funny but this is in many ways a further extension of how I always used to run Amber. Now though I have a way to talk about what I'm doing.

    I have to say that I haven't seen my players this committed to a group of PCs--maybe ever. We're three sessions in and things are churning along quite nicely.
