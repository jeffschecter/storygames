"So there's micropayments these days":http://mashable.com/2006/05/09/the-indiekarma-experiment/, via a service called "indieKarma":http://indiekarma.com/. I think it's impossible awesome. The idea: if you have an account, for every hour in which you visit the site[1], one penny of credit is PayPal'd to the site owner, and a minor "Thank you!" flashes on the bottom of the screen, IMHO unobtrusively. If you have no account, the same message pops up briefly, asking you to try indieKarma.

(If you think this could find application in games, please do start another thread!)

I think this could be a decent way of paying for StoryGames, if need be.

[1] So by this I mean: when you visit the site, $0.01 is transferred and you are NOT charged for any further visits to the site in the next hour. So if you're on the site EVERY second of EVER day, you're paying $0.24 for that day.
