\n Story Games very own Andrew Kenrick is doing the final editing for SA. I have to keep resisting the urge to write another page or two for it - not that it needs anything, just that I occasionally come up with "this might be cool" stuff.

Nearly all the art is in - I'm only waiting for a higher-res version of one piece, and to see whether I can buy some last-minute pieces that I love.

I've tentatively chosen Lulu for the printing, though I'm still open to other possibilities.

I'm so psyched. I can't wait for Genericon.

I'll stop shilling now.

--Colin
