\n Recently, my favorite gaming community has been quite alive with discussion (and snarky debate) of the subject of Character Death - to kill, or to coddle?

This flood of focus on the subject led to me having a random idea today at lunch. Let me know if someone's already done this. In this game, the only outcomes of any conflict-resolution mechanic are life or death. Your character sheet is just a set of three lists. List #1 contains a few things which, if any are present in a scene, will lead to your definite and final death. List #2 contains a few things which, if any are present in a scene containing any items from List #1, have the potential to prevent your death through a mechanic of some kind. List #3 contains a few things which, if present in a scene, will guarantee your not-death.

Haven't really taken it any further than that, because this delicious curry has demanded more of my personal attention.
