    \n Tom, what's the problem you're having, exactly? Is the text sounding dull? Is it sounding convoluted?

    It sounds as though you're talking directly to the GM as "you". That can create problems, because you end up describing the players in the third person a lot. In Bleakworlds, I've started saying "If you're the GM, [do this]" and "If you're a Criminal, [do this]" (Criminal is my codeword for "player").

    \n Decide who you're talking to, and talk to them. Are you talking to the GMs (most people buying games end up GMing them)? To everyone in the group? To people who have never, ever, ever heard of role-playing before?

    If you're talking to GMs, I wouldn't say "the GM does this" - I'd probably say "you do this".
