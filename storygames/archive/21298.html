The Menagerie is an anthology of tools, tips, short games and scenarios for -- and inspired by -- the surreal roleplaying game we launched in English in 2012. 40+ international creators, foreword by Emily Care Boss.

The book is primarily a collection of supplements for Itras By, but I think anyone with an interest in freeform, improv and dream/nightmare dichotomies could find something of interests. Free sample downloads & trailer via the link.
