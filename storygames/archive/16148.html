\nThe Mountain Witch - Sperm Edition
\n
\nYou are a band of spermatozoa. Before you lies the Uterus and the Fallopian Tubes and, somewhere near the summit, the fabled Ovum. Only by co-operating can you reach your target... but ultimately, only one of you can penetrate the coat of the Ovum and achieve Fertilization.

\nInspired by the notion of sperm competition and my own terribly warped brain.
