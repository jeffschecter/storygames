\n As the name implies, at Story Games Seattle we play story games every single week. I am constantly looking for more games to feed our ever-growing demand. Maybe you can help. Can you think of games I might have overlooked that would be nice additions to our repertoire?

THE CRITERIA

Fair warning: I have pretty specific requirements. If a game doesn't hit every one of these it's not much use to me.

- No GM

- Works as a One-Shot

- No Prep: no one makes stuff for this session before everyone sits at the table

- Replayable: teaching people new games takes effort. I want games someone can learn and be interested in playing at least three times without feeling like they're treading old ground.

- Easy to Learn: simple enough to teach people and have fun at the first play (no, I'm not requiring it to be so simple that it doesn't need someone to learn the rules and teach it)

- Excellent: yeah, call me crazy, but I only want good games, not just a big list of random games. Give me games you've played and liked or at least really willing to vet.

I know there's an urge to say "sure X is written with a GM, but you could play without a GM!" Sometimes that's true. Much more often you really would need to insert/create a new set of rules to make it work. For my purposes I'm not interested hearing about GMed games that could be made GMless unless you can point to actual rules for doing it. And even then I'm highly skeptical.

    \n I've had fun with all of these and they appear to meet your criteria.

    Ocean - survival horror
    You will need a lot of dice, like 9d6 per player. Commercial, print. Free, PDF.
    http://atarashigames.wordpress.com/teachers-lounge/ocean/

    Panty Explosion Perfect - psychic school girls (very hackable)
    Only 1d6 required, while there is a Superintendent role, the rules say they can play as well. So, that person is more like a facilitator than a GM. Commercial, print.
    http://atarashigames.wordpress.com/teachers-lounge/panty-explosion-perfect/

    The Trouble with Rose - my own character driven story game (with play sets!)
    A set of double six dominoes and a deck of cards required. Rules, play sets, and extras all in Google Docs. Free, PDF
