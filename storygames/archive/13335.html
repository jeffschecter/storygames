    Can you give me more information, Damian? Specific dates, a real link to the contest?
    \nThe official site is this. The site is pretty much inactive most of the time, thou.

    The premise is simple. February is the "Mes de Hierro" ("iron month"). The first week we usually present the secret ingredients (the possible constrains), and by the end we choose the winners via public surveys. The winning entries are the ones used for the contest (plus a couple more other minor constrains). The second week the contest per se takes place: you have about 7 days (the exact time usually changes from year to year) to design a complete game. The third week people read and post reviews about the games. The fourth week people get to vote for their favorites, and the winners are announced afterwards.

    We just had the third one this year, and we are still experimenting with the rules and learning from our mistakes. The "special ingredients" change from year to year, and the results have been super interesting (to say the least).

    El Rolero de Hierro, el concurso rolero m\xe1s letal del planeta. ("The Iron Roleplayer, the most lethal roleplaying contest there is in the planet.")
