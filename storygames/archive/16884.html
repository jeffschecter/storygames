    Be zen and buy 5 things you didn't know about! A fun T-shirt, maybe some art. There will probably be a few designers there with betas and ashcans. Don't just treat it like you were ordering online and flying to Indianapolis yourself to pick it up rather than having them ship it. Get in there and SHOP! : )
    \nI dedicate much time to shopping! But, the last two or three years, I've found I do need to do my homework. There's enough out there that I can't assume I won't miss something by doing an up and down the aisles. I make a list of which booths to check out No Matter What, and which things to look at. Betas and ashcans are great!

    It's very much not like ordering online. I can touch the books and flip through them. I can ask questions. I can get into demos. And the art show -- I love that it's right there in the exhibit hall.

    I have a lovely amber necklace and something that could be considered either a long vest or an open sleeveless overdress from merchants at GenCon. And the vest / overdress has pockets!

    Last year, on whim, I picked up two pairs of cheap steampunky green lensed glasses which fit over my glasses. Josh and I discovered that these made great sunglasses when we went outside. Of course, last year, I also made the mistake of drinking some of that five hour energy drink that was being handed out.
