\n At Gen Con The Ashcan Front moved 265 ashcans for feedback and playtesting, across 11 different games. A daily break-down:
\n
\nThursday: 86
\nFriday: 40
\nSaturday: 50
\nSunday: 89
\n
\nFriday was slow purchase-wise, as expected, and felt slow traffic-wise. Saturday was surprisingly slow, as other booths have reported. But overall, Gen Con broke open the can! And I'm seeing great post-convention momentum for playtesting and feedback. I can't wait to see how the next few months play out for these games. I couldn't be more geeked.
\n
\nPaul

    \n For Know Thyself, I bought 50 copies, sold 14, and gave away around the same number on Sunday, since I didn't want to lug them all back.

    I had a fear that people would be interested in the game for the cards and not for the actual game, thus I wouldn't get feedback. I had a couple Forge people in fact confirm this by saying they were thinking about buying it for the cards but didn't think they'd get a chance to play it. So, I found myself in a strange situation, and priced the game accordingly. (That, and hand-making decks of cards is a royal bitch.)
