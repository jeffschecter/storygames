    Posted By: renatoramDavid, what's the plus of SMF? I've asked around and did not find much.
    As compared to phpbb? I couldn't say; I'm not a phpbb person.

    For me, the pluses were:
    * Click, fill form, click installation.
    * Click, tweak a tiny bit of PHP, click to customize a template to what I wanted (trivial--MUCH more to come).
    * Very comrehensive Admin panel for forum, section, sub-forum, member, permissions, and post management.
    * The footer information is fabulous, if you're curious about performance, statistics, or new members.
    * All the Profile info you could ever want.
    * Integrated Calendar--this might become a BIG deal on my site, as I hope for it to become a portal for folks using GLASS and planning GLASS games or building troupes.
    * Extensive thread management tools for admins: split, move, edit, sticky, merge, lock, delete.
    * One-click maintenance, from the Admin panel.
    * Customize, customize, tweak, plug-in, tune....

    Now, I imagine all that is possible in phpbb. I imagine it's even easy with a few clicks. But the handful of phpbb boards I've seen in my time have been WAY ugly, with apparently minimal functionality. And that's with "admins" who were net- and computer-savvy, so that leads me to suspect that there's something tricky, kludgy, or "back end" about configuring and beautifying phpbb. That said, I know that phpbb is MUCH more supported in terms of plug-ins, apps, and such (might be the cause of so much vulnerability).
