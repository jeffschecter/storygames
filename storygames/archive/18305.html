\n Just found this... http://arsludi.lamemage.com/index.php/218/our-ground-is-level-and-our-table-is-round/

Which makes me wonder - what GMless, prepless titles are out there?

I know of Microscope, The Quiet Year (I believe fits, but haven't read it so not sure), Happy Birthday Robot, Fiasco, Grey Ranks, Polaris, A Penny for My Thoughts, The Committee for the Exploration of Mysteries, Shock: Social Science Fiction, and Remember Tomorrow.

Honorable Mentions to Inspectres for being similar in spirit despite a GM, and Mars Colony for kind of fitting depending how you define GMless

I know I'm forgetting / don't know a lot of titles - what are they?
