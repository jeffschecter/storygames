So, I haven't been a real big fan of the (idiotically-named) Podcast as a medium. I'd really rather just be able to read a transcript or something, actually listening to these things in real-time seems soooo tedious and an inefficient use of time.

Which means I've basically been ignoring anything anybody's ever posted about "Ooh! In this week's installment of X, they talked about cool and interesting thing Y."

However, I've been thinking about shuffling my lifestyle around in a way which would give me a block of otherwise boring, unused mental attention, and I've got Podcast-to-SDcard syncronization working nicely on Linux and I'm just itching to test it out. So now I'm looking for a roundup of gaming's "must-hear audio." Weekly shows? Best actual play recordings?

(Non-gaming ones are maybe okay, but only if they're really awesome and worth discussing on Story Games in some context.)

    One's that haven't been listed already are:

    Fear the Boot. They're of a traditional bent but the show has been very interesting especially if you like somewhat caustic, bitter, sarcastic banter. I do so I usually laugh. Their show has been very focused, starting with how to run a game and has now moved into how to design a game. I disagree with some of their advice, but there is more worthwile stuff than not.

    Another one is Gamer the Podcasting. Their banter is really funny. They also have some discussions that get me almost irritated enough to give feedback. This is a good thing as it means their discussions are causing me to think. It's hard to overcome my lazy. They focus on LARP quite a bit but also do some stuff on table top. Definately an essential cast.

    If you don't listen with headphones Pulp Gamer, can be good. They have a really good sound but their freaking self identification piece is recorded way higher than their regular show and blows my freaking ears out. I keep meaning to take the time to send them feedback but haven't as of yet. Besides that it's a good show.

    Lastly there is Theory From the Closet. It's probrably not essential but since it's mine and I've added these other fine podcasts... anyway. It has no clever banter. It's about teaching folks about design and theory until we get to the point where we can discuss the stuff going on in real time. I'm not satisfied with it yet, but I'm only on show 3 this week , I've been delayed due to illness.
