\n So, something I've been working on recently is a slim companion volume or Cold City. In the year (almost) that the game has been out, I've discovered a lot about different ways to run and play the game, methods for encouraging quality play and so forth. In an effort to bring all of this together, I've written the Companion. It also includes information on various shadowy intelligence agencies operating in Berlin and offer a sample hidden agenda for each, something that may be useful when people are creating characters. There are also a few locations outside of Berlin, such as Krasnoyarsk-14 in Russia and Porton Down in rural England. These all tie in to events in Berlin in some way and have been illustrated with story hooks to bring them all together.

So in anticipation of the release in August (at Gen Con), there's a now a short preview up on the Contested Ground Studios website. In this, there's also an example of the fantastically disturbing artwork that Paul Bourne has produced for the book.

My hope is that the Companion is see as a valuable and worthwhile addition to Cold City. I certainly feel that the game has benefitted from my experience (and the experiences a feedback of others, for which I am very grateful) since it was released and the Companion attempts to distil this information and make it useful for others.

Any feedback/questions/random taunting gratefully received!

Cheers
Malcolm
