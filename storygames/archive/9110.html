\nLast year, when Gary Gygax left us, I heard a good idea for a tribute.

I don't remember if it was in the Accidental Survavors Podcast or Dragon's Landing Podcast.

But the point was to name something; be it a character, a place, a city, an organization or whatever; in your current campaign after Gary Gygax.

I, myself, created the Gary Gygax institute, in which the PCs now work.

I intend on doing the same (in the same game too, since this campaign is still going on) for Dave Arneson. Don't know yet what it'll be. but I'll come up with something and share it later on.

Did you do anything like this when Gary Gygax died? will you now that Dave Arneson did?
