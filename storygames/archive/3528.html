    \n I might as well mention Wellington's upcoming conventions, too:

    Confusion: 4 August, one day (three rounds of gaming). Confusion thread
    Fright Night: 27 October, one evening Halloween horror (two rounds, limited numbers) Fright Night page

    and the big one:
    Kapcon: 19-20 January 2008 (Wellington anniversary weekend). Kapcon forum (Their homepage is still being built for next year, I believe).

    Kapcon is the city's official annual con, and has the most people and games (about 110 and six rounds plus one larp, respectively). The quality of games is very high, it's usually 100% awesome from start to finish. It's also got a strong history of homebrewed and story games there.
    Confusion is smaller, usually getting 40-50 people. It's a bit more relaxed than Kapcon.
    Fright Night is new this year, so I don't know what it will be like... but knowing the people involved in organising it, I am confident it will be good.

    Mike (and anyone else in range), if you can manage a trip down to Wellington for gaming, Kapcon or Confusion would be good ones to aim for.
