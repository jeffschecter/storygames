\n A new forum for discussing the mechanics (and only those) of board games/rpgs.

Why? Simple. I want to discuss this general topic on a vanilla board.

If others do as well, then it'll all work out. Otherwise, I waste nothing but a minimal amount of setup effort. So here it goes.

Here it is: Spin Forum
(Note: The rules and everything are currently up for discussion, since like, its just born.)
(Note: I also want to design some dice roll analysis software and such into the forum itself, but I won't do that unless things get moving.)

    \n Here's what I *want* to see, in vanilla terms. Very, very much.

    Imagine that there were several different "views" listed as clicky buttons on the side navigation panel (one of which would be "everything!!", and one of which would be "personal"). Each view would block and unblock certain categories; you'd build your own "personal" view. Until you rebuilt a personal view, the only category in that view would be a "welcome" category that explained this whole mess.

    Then, give everyone the capacity to create a category of their own, which they could make public, or not.

    Hey, presto. Multiple forums in one.
