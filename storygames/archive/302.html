Ok, so in Drowning and Falling you can play a warrior, a wizard, a cleric, an elf, or, currently, a little person.

I thought that last one (in lieu of halfling, etc) might be funny in an offensive way, but I'm reconsidering. Here's the current description:

Little Person: The stout and jolly little people enjoy a good pipe and a flagon of ale at the end of the day! They are lucky and adorable. Change any single harmful trait to 1, regardless of what you rolled, and add one prayer, based on your alignment. Being little, little people are resistant to falling. When playing a little person, you get to re-roll any one die per falling challenge. It can be a test you failed, or it can be a damage die. Play a little person if you are a huge Led Zeppelin fan.

I'm thinking that every heartbreaker has a custom race, the Chor'pok or whatever, and they are almost always a traditional fantasy trope. Should I do that here? I mean, they are definitely hobbits (I already have art) but what to call them? Basically, what's funniest? I think the others are solid but shout out if they ring false somehow.
