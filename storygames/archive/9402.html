\n So... a couple of months ago my friend Colin ran a Mouse Guard one-shot. We were going to start pretty late in the evening so he sent an email asking us what kind of mouse we would like to play so he could work up some characters. I (jokingly) responded: Can my mouse be visiting from the far east? He wears a black cloak and wields a katana.

At that game Colin pointed out to me how amused he was by the character creation question under Circles asking if your mouse is "a loner, tough and cool."

Flash forward some time later when I found this response from Luke to someone talking about the Independent Trait:

"Playing MR BAD ASS LONER MIRRORSHADES KATANA LONER MOUSE LONER is difficult in a game about team work? It's a roleplaying challenge? I am SHOCKED to hear this. Truly shocked."

Which I shared with Colin and other people I play with. This lead to a whole discussion about.... well Mirrorshaded Mice, I guess.

Question: "Who are your parents?"
Answer: "They're dead."

Question: "Who is your mentor?"
Answer: "I killed him."

Question: "Who is your friend?"
Answer: "My sword"

Question: "Who is your enemy?"
Answer: "My conscience" or alternatively, "I don't know... what's YOUR character's name?"

Just thought I'd share.

Jesse
