\n Hello folks !
I made a game named Explo[nar]rateurs, which is a kind of mash-up between Fu, InSpectres and tagclouds. You can download it there : http://www.explonarrateurs.com

People are thrown into characters belonging to stories, much as in Quantum Leap, and must create a full story to go into another, hoping to return home one day... I thought of an english name, Storysliders. What do you think of it ? Or is Explo[nar]rators a better english name ?
