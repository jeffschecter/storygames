\n For those following at home. Caesar_X had a lot of good feedback and I am incorporating that into Book 1. Book 3 (Cities and Adventure) is still just notes and somewhat usable. Book 2 is well, a pile of paper. For those that expressed interest in the thing I'll be sending out copies as soon as I get the changes made.

But before all that some questions for folks.

There are six paths in Book 1. Horse Rider, Solider, Traveler, Rogue, Privateer, Merchant.
Instead of the Advanced Education Table each path has a specialized skill table based on a core ability (like Str, Dex, Ins, etc..)

For each path what would be skills and/or benefits available only to that group?

The Social ability doesn't get used enough. So right now I have the following rules for Social:
It allow your character access to certain parts of the city
If your character Social ability is high enough the Law tends to look the other way
To maintain a given Social level your character has to cough up Soc x 10 in coins per month

What other fun things can we do with the Social ability in a S&S setting?
