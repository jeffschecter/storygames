\n We are running Games on Demand at the Emerald City Comic Con. This convention is pretty much sold out, but you can get in for free just by volunteering to play games with strangers.

For those of you who haven\u2019t done Games on Demand before, our volunteers prepare a few indie/story/rpg-ish games to run and then we match them up with convention attendees for up to four hours of gaming right then and there.

If you are interested in volunteering, whisper me your email address and I'll hit you up with the sign up sheet and a big block of text that is full of deets.

Morgan (Mathalus)
