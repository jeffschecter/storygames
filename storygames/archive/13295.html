\n Hey guys,
\n
\nI've been working on a game for awhile, I guess ever since The Gamestas bit the dust. I've finished a stable version of it after subjecting my crew to playtests, and I would like to get it out in the wild a little bit. I hope for it to emulate messianic fantasy stories, the game being my way of playing stuff like Dune or Wheel of Time. It focuses on possible destinies for characters, and how the players push and pull the story in order to reach them. Its meant to be a long form game, and me being so deep in the recesses of it, I haven't figured out how to build it for one shot play. I've you'd like to read it and give me feedback or playtest it and relate your experiences, shoot me an email at sinistraphobia AT gmail DOT com. Oh and if you didn't already, tell me about your group and whether you guys would like to be interviewed for a podcast as I travel about the country as talked about here
\n
\nThanks,
