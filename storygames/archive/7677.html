Glyphpress and 2SP announce: The Diesel Sweeties RPG

\n
\n
\n
\n\nElizabeth \n\n\n
\n
\n\nSeptember 2008 \n edited September 2008 in Directed Promotion
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n That's right, Mr. Joshua A.C. Newman and myself (Elizabeth Shoemaker) just got back from a very important business meeting with webcomic superstar and merch mogul R Stephens III, of Diesel Sweeties fame. We're securing the rights to write the official Diesel Sweeties RPG!

A lot of the groundwork has been laid, and we're all super excited about the project. We're aiming for a GenCon 2009 release, although after talking over the scope of the game and thinking about what we've already got, there's a good chance for a Dreamation 2009 release instead. We can make the time for it in our busy schedules, after all.

See more on our respective websites! Eventually! (Who's excited? I'm excited.)
