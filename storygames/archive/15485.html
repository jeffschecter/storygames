    \n Dev:

    When you print one, try throwing a staple in the spine, and another in the bottom of the (front? Back?) page. This makes that 'cover' into a kind of pocket - which works well for holding business cards.

    Then, if you have power / item / reminder cards, print 'em on business cards stock, and it's a handy little pack.
