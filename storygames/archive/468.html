The Forum Blues, or why I don't care...

ross
April 2006 edited April 2006 in Story Games
As a columnist with a few thousand hits a month on rpg.net I am distressed to learn that the forum has become completely boring and useless to me. It may have to do with not having the ability to browse only new topics, but I just can't get excited about anything.

My subscription to Pyramid lapsed last week. Both the SJG newsgroups and the Pyramid boards are boring, boring, boring. Why pay money for an increasingly self-selecting set of not funny jokes.

Likewise JTAS and COTI for Traveller, White Wolf, yadda yadda yadda...

Why is it that I am so disinterested in forums? I used to read and comment sometimes a hundred posts a day...

Ideas, thoughts, wiseracks?
