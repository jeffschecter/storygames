\n OK, this is for my Exalted game, which I have an AP thread on rpg.net here: http://forum.rpg.net/showthread.php?t=395720

It's a heroic mortal game, that's going to transition into a Solar game.

Everything has been to easy for the PCs. The players have been complaining about it. So, I'm setting up the next encounter to actually try and "kill" them. Or rather, set it up so they would die under the system, but then they Exalt as Solars and kick the asses of the "bad guys".

This is almost giving me a feeling of schadenfreude, except I know the players have basically asked for this. Still, it feels wrong so how, yet all so good.

I can't wait to finish stating up the NPCs tonight.
