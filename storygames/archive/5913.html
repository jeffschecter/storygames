    \n A medium is... ah, hell, I'll do the search for you:
    http://wordnet.princeton.edu/perl/webwn?s=medium

    Thinned with paint thinner, basically. That ought to work, actually, presuming plastic dice.

    Now the obvious question: why can't you go buy dice in the color(s) that you want? The colors you list aren't particularly uncommon (excepting maybe orange). Are you coloring some kind of already-custom dice that aren't available in several colors?

    (And as a brief aside: having just bought 100-odd dice to play Mechaton--due to cost savings by buying in bulk, NOT because I really need that many--let me warn you that requiring a lot of custom dice or dice colors could create a barrier to entry, for your game. For ASCII @HACK, I seriously considered allowing Gear or Traits with d16 rank, because such dice are available and it would bridge the "gap" between d12 and d20. I quickly dismissed that notion, as d16s are not found in the average FLGS--necessitating a special (or online) order.

    Rambling a bit, but I think of dice a lot these days (ASCII @HACK, playing DitV a lot, almost got involved in the Burning Empires custom dice group buy);
