\n Wow, this has taken a long time! I just sent off for my Lulu proof, so assuming everything is reasonably excellent, the book (the 280 page book) will soon be available for purchase. It'll cost $11, which is cost plus a few cents to round it off, which I will use to buy drugs to make me forget how long this took. It raised $450 for the Carter Center, too. Here's the wonderful Leo Lingas cover:

It's going to be genuinely useful and it looks great - Josh BishopRoby's layout is top notch and the interior art is also lovely. The really cool thing from my perspective is that this whole thing emerged from this community and now it is real. All the tools are in place for us to make the things we want and need. I love that!

    \n It occurs to me that not everybody who hangs out here knows what this is all about. A long while ago I started a project to collect game-worthy names from all over this and other worlds. It sort of spiralled into madness and I got over a hundred sets of lists, which you can check out here. It's good stuff to have at the game table! We organized it as a charity for the Carter Center by selling ads, and got great artists to volunteer their talents.
