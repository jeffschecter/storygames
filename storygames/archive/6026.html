    \n Don, Here are three brainstorming ideas:
    1) Prisoners\u2019 Dilemma
    Captured by the Spider, the Sorcerer and her retinue are trussed in cocoons of silk.
    The Sorcerer has entrusted one orc and one roden with knowing the gestures of power, and one orc and one roden with the words of power. Together, this knowledge can open the Chamber of Infinity, at whose door the Spider waits.
    Each prisoner possesses knowledge the Spider wants, and could buy their freedom, if they use it first!

    2) Last one standing.
    Deep in a subterranean labyrinth, six figures confront each other in the midst of a Spider\u2019s lair.
    The Orcs each need a mount, the Roden need silk, the Sorcerer needs a compound eye, and the spider needs to eat.
    The Orcs have come together, as have the Roden, each duo working together to make it this far. The Sorcerer has made her way undetected thanks to powerful magics. And the Spider has been waiting\u2026

    3) Mediation
    The Orcs and the Roden have been at war. Now, two representatives from each side are meeting to negotiate a truce. The Sorcerer is the mediator, the Spider\u2019s lair is the meeting location. The war is about access to resources: water, the surface, tunnels; and tribute. The Sorcerer wants the war to end in order to use the united orcs and roden for\u2026something. The Spider wants the war to continue in order to feed on the wounded left on the battlefield (I imagine the lair is full of cocooned orc and roden bodies).
