\n So I've been talking with some folks about GMing some Dungeon World, only I haven't really done much dungeon crawling in a while.

I figured it would be a good idea to grab some good dungeon maps/adventures to use for inspiration or just plunk down somewhere in the game to explore. Question is, where do I find some awesome dungeons? I've been looking over Tony Dowler's microdungeons, but they're mostly too small and vague for me. I've also got For the Love of Dungeons, which is just about right. I've also got the old D&D random dungeon generator tables that I plan on looking over/

Anyone else have a favourite dungeon map, or place to find dungeon maps?

    \n Check out D1-2, Descent into the Depths of the Earth. You'll find a PDF floating around online. The first part of the 'dungeon' is a natural cave system that's pretty huge (it's a hex map with, I think, 1 mile per hex). Possibly too encounter-dense for AW (I'm not sure - certainly it would be for BW) especially at the major encounter areas. But classic and fun.
