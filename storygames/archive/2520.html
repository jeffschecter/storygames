\n We were talking about Dictionary of Mu the other night at the bar, and that drifted to Fred's cool Fate-based thing. Which then led us to imagining a TSOY conversion of Mu. I suppose it was inevitable for me to say "the Dictionary of Zu."

We all kind of stared in silence for a moment. "Damn."

So.. yeah. There it is. The Dictionary of Zu. I know your minds are racing now. Go ahead and pile on here.
