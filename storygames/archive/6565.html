\n Yay! Finally, the mini-con I've been planning for it seems like ages is happening today and over the next few days. Fredenscon (named after my street, which was in turn named Fredensgade ("Peace Street") celebrating the peace after WWI) has been in the works for quite some time - and the idea is even older.
We'll be 8 persons in all, two groups throughout the weekend, gaming, eating, sleeping, laughing and talking in my house. On the gaming menu is: In a Wicked Age, Dirty Secrets, Montsegur 1244, Mechaton, Polaris, InSpectres (with Hellboy flavour), Red Box Hack and A Penny for My Thoughts. So much goodness!
I can't wait!
Bring it on!
