\n I haven't read so many yet but so far I'm really excited to play...

My Daughter, The Queen of France - I love the concept of replaying the same scenes with a different twist each time, I love how the mechanics perfectly reinforce the feeling of going over memories of the past, trying to figure out what went wrong, lots of lovely stuff here.

Durance - the layout, the concept, the locking dice mechanics, everything looks so cool and polished. Lots of fresh ideas here.

how about y'all?
