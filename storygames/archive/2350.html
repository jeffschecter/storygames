    Well, I am.

    Pick a corner of (say) the StoryGames Wiki.

    Make a first page of a Choose Your Own Adventure story. Toss in a few links at the bottom ("Click here if you piss on the burning control panel," "Click here if you open both airlock doors"). Don't bother making the pages that those links lead to ... that's the job of the next guy to hit the site.

    When you come to the site, check out what people have done. Play the game until you hit a link that hasn't been defined. Make a page for that link to go to, and create some links of your own. Maybe, if you've explored enough, go and link some open links into existing pages, to turn the tree into more of a labyrinth.

    That sounds like a fun community project.
