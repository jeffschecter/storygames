\n So, I start this for the third time because I suck at mouse precision.
\n
\nBorn out of a quite strange discussion about games allowed at a non-mainstream-con, I had this idea:
\n
\nRed Sun Hack
\n
\nThe Heartbreaker that TSOY never was.
\n
\nPools
\n
\nAll Characters start with two Pools, Class and Race. They divide 1 point between these Pools. The sum of these pools is a characters Level.
\n
\nSecrets
\n
\nEach Character chooses one of the following Secrets:
\n
\nSecret of the Fighter
\nOpens up a third Pool, called Valor. Also gives access to the warrior abilities.
\n
\nSecret of the Cleric
\nOpens up a third Pool, called Investiture. Also gives access to the cleric abilities.
\n
\nSecret of the Thief
\nOpens up a third Pool, called Gold. Also gives access to the thief abilities.
\n
\nSecret of the Wizard
\nOpens up a third Pool, called Mana. Also gives access to the wizard abilities.
\n
\nAnd one of the following Secrets:
\n
\nSecret of the Human
\nGives you one extra point in your Class Pool. Gives you one extra Secret.
\n
\nSecret of the Elf
\nYou can see in the night and get a bonus die against sleep spells.
\n
\nSecret of the Dwarf
\nYou can see in the dark and get a bonus die defending with race skills.
\n
\nSecret of the Halfling
\nYou get a bonus die defending against Mana skills. Also, you have hairy feet.
\n
\nAbilities
\n
\n\u2026
\n
\nand so on.
\n
\nI'm aware it's badwrongfun. But somehow it does sing to me (like a twentysomething boy after a bar crawl, that's true).
