    \n\nGeorge_Cotronis \n\n\n
    \n
    \n\nNovember 2010 \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n Sorry for the delay! I think I experienced some minor blackouts during Lucca, because everything seems to have blended together into a 4-day maelstrom of con goodness.

    So. I took a plane from Gothenburg and landed in Pisa on the 28th, the day before the con. The plane arrived a half hour earlier (around 23:00), so I called up Flavio to see if he was close. He informed me he was just around the corner, parking the car. Flavio and Paola came out to meet me and took me back to the car, where Giulia was waiting. We drove around looking for a place to eat, which turned out to be an awesome sandwich shop.
    ''Janus people take their sandwiches very seriously'' I was informed by someone, and it was certainly true. At this point I met Joshua, who actually speaks Italian.
    I quickly realized I'd be the only touristy cliche guy of the group (until I met Jason, who thankfully did not speak Italian either).

    While munching on the awesome sandwich, I cornered Joshua and we discussed my latest Diaspora game, what I didn't like about it, how awesome Apocalypse World is, how happy I was that the Kickstarter project for Human Contact took off and stuff like that.
    We got back to the cars and drove to the Villa/Bed and Breakfast we were going to be staying at. I was shown to my room, which I shared with Luca, Noa and later, Rino. The room was on the last floor and was pretty big.
    There was a very strange sound every night we slept there, usually occurring when we were about to go to sleep. It didn't bother us too much, but we never solved the mystery.

    I went back downstairs and found everyone drinking tea. I think I met Joshua's wife, Carrie, who was very sweet. Unfortunately, I was too tired to remember anything else about this night. I slept like a log.

    1st Con Day!
    We piled into the van after a short breakfast where people shuffled around like zombies. We hit some traffic on the way to Lucca, but our driver expertly navigated the Italian streets, also known as ''The Killing Fields''. We were dropped off at one of the side gates, reserved for con personnel, thus avoiding the huge ass line that was already forming at the main gate. I checked out the booth, that everyone else had helped set up the day before, while I was still in Sweden. Met a bunch of people, including most of the Janus people, the Coyote and Narrativa guys and some unrelated people in the nearby booths.

    Then, me and Joshua checked out the whole gaming area. Joshua tried to play some Formula 1 game and failed miserably, ultimately blaming the controller for his failure to not only win, or place, but to simply follow the racetrack.
    We went on and arrived at a booth where a guy was setting up Wings of War. Drawn in by the model of a blimp, we were tricked into playing a round of aerial combat where no blimp was actually present. In this, Joshua excelled and destroyed my plane while hardly getting hit himself.

    Then we went and got some lunch with Jason, where I also met Joe Prince and his wife. I don't remember what the discussing was about, as I was distracted by something at the time. After lunch, I think me, Joshua, Arielle, Giulia and Alessandro went to check out the comics (located in a different part of the city) and to get some coffee. Me and Alessandro had a discussion about Italy, Greece and Sweden, work and education differences. Later, me and Joshua headed out to find the elusive Dylan Dog booth. We couldn't find anything remotely connected to it though, so I had a crepe and we discussed Shock and Vincent's games.

    Don't remember anything else after that, probably just looked around the different stalls. Around 19:30, the Janus folk closed the booth and we headed to a pub/restaurant for dinner. Me and Jason tried to take out some money at an ATM while avoiding getting hit by a car, but were thwarted by a door our credit cards could not open.
    I ordered some kind of pizza and I remember some huge steak-like objects ordered by the people to my right, but not much else. We watched a surreal Italian TV show that featured old music stars singing live. The consensus at the table was that most of them were drunk at the time. The only discussing I remember was with Joshua and it was about the Unstore and how he moved Shock there. Probably forgetting a bunch of stuff.

    Back at the B&B, it was tea and sleep. I don't remember if this is when the nerf combat took place, but it could be. I distinctly remember being promised alcohol, but all I got was some left over whiskey that I didn't even touch. This backfired when Braulio was introduced. But more on that later...


    That concludes day one.
