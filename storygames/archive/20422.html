\n "The Sword and the Flame" was a break through miniatures game in the late 70's about British battles in the colonies 1870-1900. It spurred a lot of Colonial wargaming and included role playing with combat. It was a direct influence on the game "Space 1889" and their companion miniatures game "The soldiers companion."

They are worth checking out.
