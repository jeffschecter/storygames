\n I was looking over all my various Munchkin games,like regular munchkin and it's million supplements, munchkin bites, munchkin cthulhu, super munchkin, munchkin booty, munchkin fu, etc. and I got to thinking: what would a story games munchkin variant be like? I imagine the base structure might be changed (although then again, maybe not).

I'm thinking monster cards like angry pit bulls in the vineyard or spidermen from Lacuna, and things like "Kill puppies for Satan: Go Up a Level"

So yeah. Post cards you think would fit into a Story Games Munchkin.

    \n Czege Principle - On your turn, play a monster card from your hand and then your munchkin immediately enters combat with it. If you defeat it, do not gain any levels (you can still gain its treasures).

    Game Design Contest - Take one item from the player to your left and one item from the player to your right and immediately put them into play on your munchkin, even if they are incompatible or your munchkin can not normally use them.

    "But *I'm* British" - During a combat, temporarily increase your munchkin's level to one higher than one of the monsters that it is in combat with.
