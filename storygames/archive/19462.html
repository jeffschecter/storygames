    \n Yeah, we actually had Team Books as well, but they were proving pretty tricky and a bit complicated. We're thinking of doing a setting that'll have some extra stuff called Avalon City, Jason's homebrew setting he's been playing in for over 15 years. Team Books will end up being a thing for sure at some point I think - we had stuff like: Hand Picked, Family, United Against a Common Threat, Forced Together by Fate, In a World that Hates and Fears Us, Heroes for Hire, On the Run, etc. We're going to explore it, but it definitely needed more time to gestate and get playtested, not to mention some getting used to the main stuff already, with there already being the two mini books and all.

    That's a cool idea to create some ties to characters - especially a move turning a villain into a nemesis over time or something would be cool, let alone adding interesting dynamics between other heroes. Definitely something to think about!
