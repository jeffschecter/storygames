\n With great anxiety and trepidation, I left my game to be playtested by folks without being present. It almost killed me, but I did it and I hope it survives. I left it in the capable hands of AgentFresh, but a great deal of the rules have changed since the first playtest and all he has is my possibly broken text. Anybody else experiencing letting people you know playtest your game without you there to protect it? (Yes, alot of this is tongue in cheek)
