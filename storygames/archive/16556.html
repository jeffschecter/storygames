\n The fly in my Camp Nerdly ointment was the bitter jealousy I felt toward all of the old Nerdly hands wearing their Camp Nerdly t-shirts from years past, and the absence of new t-shirts for this year. I mentioned this seething resentment to Dave Younce, who suggested that I had just volunteered to be schwagmeister for next year's Camp Nerdly. "Money is not an issue," Dave told me. "It just takes someone to step up and take people's shirt sizes."

So I have two questions:

(1) What kinds of swag have been gratifying in the past, either at Nerdly or elsewhere?

(2) What do people think of this proposal from another thread?\n
Marshall (mease19) says:You very much need a shirt that reads:

"Hi, my name is __________,
one cool thing I saw at __________
was__________."

(you could write in the con's name in washable marker)

It'd be cool to also sell them and let people fill in the blanks and wear them at the con.
