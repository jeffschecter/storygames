\n Hey folks!

I'm part of a (relatively) new organization called "Indie+." The goal is to bring people together online to play awesome online indie games.

Our plan for 2013 is to host #Gamenights. Each event has a few games and a few panels on a specific theme. Last January we did a sci-fi #Gamenight that featured 6d6 Bots, Our Last Best Hope: The Core, and a panel featuring Ryan Macklin, Filamena Young, Jared Sorenson, and Chris Tregenza. You can check out our video archives here: http://www.indieplus.org/doku.php?id=gamenight:january2013

The Indie+ #Gamenight for this month is Fantasy-themed. Come and check it out: http://www.indieplus.org/doku.php

We'd love to have you participate!
