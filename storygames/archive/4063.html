    \n I'm trying to create a system that allows a group to replicate any kind of superhero story. I'm trying to give as much freedom as possible to allow the group to use the storytelling conventions of any of the main kinds of superhero storytelling, from Golden Age to date. I also give a lot of hints and tips as to what makes each individual type distinct and how to incorporate them at the gaming table. The book is a love letter to the genre and is aimed at more at the superhero fan than the gamer with just a passing interest in superheroes.
