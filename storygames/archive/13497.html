    \n Not gaming related, but Boing Boing just posted a link to these science based nerd insults from actual peer reviews of papers:

    "The biggest problem with this manuscript, which has nearly sucked the will to live out of me, is the terrible writing style.
    The abstract and results read much like a laundry list.
    It is sad to see so much enthusiasm and effort go into analyzing a dataset that is just not big enough.
    The presentation is of a standard that I would reject from an undergraduate student.
    The finding is not novel and the solution induces despair.
    The writing style is flowery and has an air of Oscar Wilde about it.
    The writing and data presentation are so bad that I had to leave work and go home early and then spend time to wonder what life is about.    "
