\n Okay, so the deal is we need a boss playset for Chapter 5 of our ongoing Roach saga at Go Play NW this year. And because I can't help myself, I've made the seeds of this Homeowner's Association thing.

I figured I'd try to crowdsource the rest. Anyone want to kick in events / NPCs / card names? How would do you assign personal dice in these things?

Setting: Pinnacle Hope Terrace, an upper-middle-class development in an exurb of a major American city.

The unroached player with the most status manages to escape the development.
The Roached player with the most status inherits the chairmanship of the HOA.
