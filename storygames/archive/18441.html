    The beta of Geiger Counter is available here. I recommend printing it as a stapled booklet at your local Kinkos and it'll be exactly the same as the other copies that exist: http://bleedingplay.files.wordpress.com/2008/08/geiger-beta1.pdf

    I have a bunch of amazing ashcans and ashcan-like things that I won't part with, unfortunately, including:
    - Narcissist v 0.5
    - Revolucion! (Sage's first game!)
    - Sign in Stranger
    - Primitive and Manual of Threats
    - Kingdom of Nothing (now available in a full version)
    - Poison'd
    - The Smoke Dream (currently loaned out)
    - Silver & White
    - Ammo and Ammo2: Deus vs. Machina
    - Dungeon & Warrior
    - a bunch of others

    Despite the sarcastic hatorade in this thread, ashcans are awesome, and I'm happy to set anybody straight about that if need be! Nothing is better than a handcrafted or photocopied game you bought at a rockshow or traded one of your own games for. If that's wrong, I don't want to be right. Many of them are substantially more successful as games than some "finished" games that I've bought, or offer unique types of play experiences that are otherwise impossible to find. I treasure them!
