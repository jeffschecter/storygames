\n So I'm stuck at work.. because I have a job. And I have tons of these little things I have to do that require 5-10 minutes of waiting once they're running. I usually glaze over but I tap endlessly on a keyboard, do a stack of crosswords, or spam Story Games with my insightful prose.

But I'm bored bored bored.

What can I do? I'd love to do some kind of game or do a game supporting activity that makes my Wednesday IAWA game better, but making pictures with the GIMP is likely to be noticed and passive-agressively discouraged while typing into a blank text field is unnoticed / A-OK.
