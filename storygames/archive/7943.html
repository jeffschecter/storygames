\n I've been thinking about using the world of the BPRD as a Darkpages imprint. Any ideas/suggestions for Spaces and Elements would be appreciated.
\n
\nHere's my rough take on Hellboy. Again, comments/critiques would be helpful.
\n
\nHellboy (Concept: The Beast)
\n
\nAwareness (2):
\n-Understands Ancient/Magical languages (0)
\nDestruction (5):
\n-Right Hand of Doom (+1)
\n-Super-Human Strength (+1)
\nInstinct (2):
\n-"Anung un Rama" (0)
\nSurvival (3):
\n-Resistant to physical injury (+1)
\n-Good Samaritan (0)
\n
\nArchetype: The Warrior (7)
\nShadow: Rage
\nSpirit: Righteousness (or Morality?)
\n
\nBonds:
\nLoyalty: Members of the BPRD (5)
\nEnmity: Ogdru Jahad (2)
\nMentor: Professor Trevor Bruttenholm (3)
\n
\nCodes:
\n1. Never let my rage control me.
\n2. Protect the world from the occult, paranormal and supernatural.
\n3. Live up to the expectations of my 'Father'.
\n
\nShould I switch the powers Super-Human Strength to the Survival Motif and Good Samaritan to Destruction?
