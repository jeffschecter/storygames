\n My son has announced lately that he's "always wanted to run a game".
Now, for him "always" is a fairly short time - but that's beside the point.
The statement is also likely being made in response to the fact that due to long hours at work followed by illness, I've not been up to running anything myself of late - but that's also beside the point.

When I was about his age, I got my first DMG (the brown cover one) and I never looked back.
Thus, I toyed briefly with 4th edition - but given it's release is a good 6-9 months off, that idea is now on the back burner.

So I look to you good people. What is a good first game for a would-be game-runner?
Particularly one who is on the young side....

    \n I agree with the others, most kids are smarter than we give them credit for. But I would like to add, its easier to run a system you have played, than one you are unfamiliar with. Finally, here is some actual suggestions:
    \nReal young: Shadows (also, there is a KOTOR hack for Shadows, FYI)
    \nYoung: ...InSpaaace!, TrollBabe, Toon, PTA, Otherkind, BESM (Tri-Stat)
    \nTeen-ish: SoY, D&D (any edition), Buffy/WitchCraft, InSpectres, ShadowRun or Abberrant
    \n Good luck man!
