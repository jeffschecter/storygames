How To Get A Go Play Sticker for your Car

Rob Donoghue
August 2006 edited August 2006 in Story Games
Ok, so the Vinyl stickers I ordered came in while I was at Gencon, and I was very happy with the outcome. I;m goign to run through how I got them made, and let folks sort it out for themselves, unless Brennan decides that adding $1 stickers to the IPR catalog is worth the pure madness that they represent.

Anyway, the steps.

1) go to http://www.vinzdecals.com/create.html
2) Scroll down and select "Color Printed" from the options.
3) Select a size of 3" by 3", and upload one of the bigger Go Play Images.
4) Look at the preview and make sure it looks ok. If it looks too big, that's fine.
5) Select how many you want. The first big price drop occours when you buy 10, so I'd generally suggest picking at least 10, even if you don't have 10 people in mind yet.
6) Pay the nice men
7) Wait a few days, maybe a week, and Voila.

I ordered 10, and they ended up sending me 12 (they fit 12 on a sheet) and with shipping and such, it ran me about $20. Only drawback was I had to cut the sheet up myself, but once that was done, they're pretty darn good stickers, and one now proudly adorns my rear bumper.

It's pretty easy, but if anyone has any questions, I'll try to answer them.

-Rob D.
