    \n The first time i ever played Smoke Dream...

    My friend, the Wizard, said i must try it. I was hesitant, i forget why (foolishness, most likely). So in order to fulfill all righteousness, the Wizard sat me at a table, draped in red, and lit a single candle. This was the only light in the room.

    He put a deck of cards in front of me. And i learned to play this solitaire game by having my friend, the Wizard, read the book aloud to me as i made each decision. As i would search for my heart he would watch me, the candlelight reflected in his eyes, and wait for my decisions.

    it. was. awesome.
