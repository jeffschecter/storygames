    \n I only really know Ann Arbor, but Vault of Midnight on Main St and Liberty is pretty excellent. Not a lot of roleplaying materials, though I have spotted a copy of Fiasco on one of their shelves, so there's hope for them yet. But if you're a comic book or board game type of person, you'll have a great time in there.

    Get Your Game On on S State St between Liberty and North University is also pretty good - a bit more roleplaying game oriented than Vault of Midnight (they have a decent shelf of games, a downstairs play area, and even a bulletin board for organizing groups).

    Neither of them are the most amazing game store I've been in (then again, once you've been to Cafe Mox/Card Kingdom, you get a little spoiled), but they're both worth stopping in.
