\n It's that time again! The Go Play Peoria Summer Minicon will be held on June 6, 2008 at the Samaritan Ministries building. The doors open at 9 a.m., and we wrap up around 9 p.m. Please bring a $5 donation to help defray the cost of food.

Bring your own games, plus a willingness to meet new people and try new things.

I'm experimenting a little with the room setup to allow more quiet space for roleplaying games. Of course, this means that we need people there to try it out!

Got questions? Ask away!
