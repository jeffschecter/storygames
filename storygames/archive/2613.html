\n Finally, at long last, I've got off my arse and got the quick-play demo for Cold City up on our website. It's primarily intended for those who know CC to demo at cons, stores, etc, but could be used within a group to have a go at trying out the mechanics and getting a feel for the game.

The demo itself is based on the one I used at Gen Con Indy 2006, tweaked and modified a bit based on demoing experiences and feedback since then. There's also four abbreviated character sheets for use with the demo.

It could probably be tweaked and improved further, but I think it's pretty satisfactory. However, there's always room for feedback and critique, so if there are any comments you have, I'd be delighted to hear them. I'm planning on providing a 1- 2 hour game demo pack at some point in the near future as well, once I've translated notes and scribbles into something legible!

Cheers
Malcolm
