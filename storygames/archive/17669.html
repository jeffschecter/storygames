\n Hey! Do you live in or near Washington DC? Do you like fantasy? Do you also like westerns?

If so, I am looking for you. I am going to be in DC for six weeks, from the last week of January to the first week of March, and I want to run a game during that time. Here's the params:

* We'll play on a week night inside the Beltway. Tuesday or Wednesday night is most likely. I'm not sure where we'll be playing yet, but I hope to find a night Labyrinth is open late.
* We'll be playing Owl Hoot Trail, a game I co-wrote and is being published by Pelgrane later in 2013. It is finished and this isn't a playtest, just playing for fun. It is small on the rules, familiar to anyone who's played D&D, and high on adventure.
* I will be recording the game sessions. I'm not sure what we'll do with these, but Simon at Pelgrane mentioned that having a recorded session of play would be nice.
* Max is 6 people + me.

I am super-excited to be in DC for six weeks and hope to find people excited for adventure! Reply here or hit me up via email (crnixon@gmail.com).
