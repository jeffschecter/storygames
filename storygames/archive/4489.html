\n So, out of the blue, this guy drops me a line and tells me how much fun he is having with Passages. Better yet, he starts sending me recaps of his game. Best of all, they're positively amazing: journeying to the center of the Earth, hopping aboard the Pequod, etc., etc. He even keeps a running "bibliography" of literary characters the group has encountered. It's so amazing, in fact, that I wish I could play in this game. Basically, his actual play is exactly the kind of campaign I envisioned when I wrote the game.

He's sent me about nine reports so far, and I've posted them to my own message boards. You can take a look at them HERE. (I thought they'd be a little long to repost here.)
