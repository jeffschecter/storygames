    Blogs
    Jim Butcher's LJ - Jim mostly just posts writing stuff.
    Complications Ensue: The Crafty TV and Screenwriting Blog

    Steven Barnes' blog discusses a lot of cultural and physical issues, but he also has a lot of writing advice (much of it DIRECTLY applicable to RPGs.) One of the things I really connected with was the idea of writing just the physical actions of a scene, the blocking and the things happening on a level that really visualizes the environment versus paying so much attention to the dialogue.
