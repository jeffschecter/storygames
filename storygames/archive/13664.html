\n We've made a mockup of what Playground will probably look like. You can see it over at playgroundmagazine.net.

Playground is a role-playing magazine for curious people. If you want to read about weird, artsy and sexy approaches to role-playing, this is for you. We're putting together the first two issues now; you'll recognize names from Story Games, have trouble pronouncing names from the Finnish larping scene, and probably have no way of identifying what gaming scene "The Creation of a Killer" comes from.

If you're interested in writing for the magazine, or just want some info, contact us at contact@playgroundmagazine.net.
If you're thinking about getting a subscription, we're at subscriptions@playgroundmagazine.net.
