  • Ben_Lehman
    April 2006 edited April 2006
    Here's the game plan.

    Roast or fry several corn tortillas until totally dry and brittle. In a small baking dish, lay one tortilla. Put a small amount of shrimp (or other seafood), roasted red peppers, and cheese on top of it. Layer with another tortilla, then put more on top of that, then top with a third, then top that with a layer of cheese.

    Optionally, add refried beans to your layers.

    Bake until cheese is thoroughly melted. Pour your favorite salsa (mole or green recommended) on top and eat with a knife and fork.
