\n I'm interested in more like this:\n
Key of Bringing Food for the Other Gamers - You get 1XP for every complete, unopened food item that you bring to gaming (Jar of Salsa, Bag of Chips, Bag of Candy, 6 Pack of Soda/Beer, etc. PRE-OPENED items do not count). 3XP for bringing in special food items only available in another state/country. You get 3XP for bringing in a homemade food item that at least one other player makes favorable comments towards ("Oh, hot Damn! It's Maurice's special recipe Mississippi Mud!!"). Buyoff Don't bring in food. (JAK)
\nThere are a handful over here: http://random-average.com/TSOY/MetaGamingKeys

I think it would be great if the creative geniuses around here could throw a few more into the pot.

Specifically, I have a problem with people texting at the table. I'd like a Key to solve this problem.

Also, I was thinking that instead of awarding xp for hitting these keys, I'd award bonus dice. Does anyone have any experience doing such a thing?
