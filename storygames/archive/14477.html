    \n I don't know this particular game but the idea of passing narrative control around the room to tell a story is old - probably ancient.

    I doesn't tend to work well. If there are no perameters players don't know what to do. I tried the idea out twenty plus years ago in the early days of Matrix gaming and people kept having the Martians attack. Now I suspect people would keep brining up zombie apocalypse. There needs to be a check on sillyness.

    Chris Engle
