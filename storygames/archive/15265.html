    \n Might fall into the interesting set-up/mechanics... The Trouble with Rose uses dominoes to guide the characters in their storytelling and one page play sets to help players create scenes and characters if they don't want to make their own. Every game of it that I have seen or heard about as gone pretty well. It's also got audience scoring for epilogue control.
