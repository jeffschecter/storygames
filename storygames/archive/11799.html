\n#1
Large-scale body control. Reaction speed. Used for dodging, jumping, climbing, and factors into attacking.

#2
Fine motor coordination. Finger and tool manipulation. Used for picking locks and shooting a bow. ("Dexterity" is the obvious dictionary choice, but D&D casts too long a shadow.)

Just for context, I have two other attributes called Strength and Toughness.
