\nSo, with my illnesses and the like, I've been distracted when it comes to Fastlane 2nd edition and getting it to layout, then printed and out the door. However, a long time ago, I whipped up a small single page that described the changes that were coming in the 2nd edition (and attempted to give a rationale for each change) - that way, people who owned the first edition could have access to the rule changes.
\n
\nThe original plan was to release it when the 2nd edition of Fastlane was "ready." But that doesn't look like it's happening any time soon. And time marches on.
\n
\nSo here it is. Please use these changes when you play.

I'd just... given myself a goal ("one page!") that made examples difficult, at least my kinds of examples.
