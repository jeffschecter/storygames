    \n There was only one such player, and he was in the Ramlar game. I'm a bit saddened that he was introduced with such a horrible system, but thankfully, he had heard enough to understand that there were better games out there.

    The T&T adventure we ran was the second one in that demo, involving a boat called The Drunken Dwarf. Man, it was loads of fun, and I'm glad I got to run it twice.

    I'm kicking myself for not getting everyone's email addresses, though. These were actually my first real official demos, so I wasn't entirely used to the setup and process, but I think I adapted pretty quickly. I sure would love to contact those awesome players, though.
