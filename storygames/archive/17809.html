\n Hi there,

(I didn't see any thread about introduction - sorry if this is a duplicate)

I'm a French guy living in South-Western France and I'm getting more and more interested into Story Games, such as Lady Blackbird, Danger Patrol and some AW hacks I could harvest while browsing the web...
I'm also quite curious about solo RPGs, diceless or GM-less games...

I used to do roleplaying games a lot when I was a teenager : lots of MERP, Rolemaster and other derivatives, Star Wars, Shadowrun ; a bit of Chtulhu, a tiny bit of AD&D... And not enough Toon.

Now you may understand why I'm so curious about simple RPG - minimum rules, less dice rolling, more fun.

For now, I can't actually play RPGs (no table around me, no players, etc), but I'm playing with the idea of getting into it again... When I'll have the time.

Yours,
