This web page, out of England, is dedicated to republishing all the original documents of the wargaming/miniatures gaming hobby. The base that role playing grew out of. I don't know if people are interested in this but it is a treasure of books published in the 60's and 70's (before D+D) as well as older works (such as the Victorian Kriegspiel rules). If you want to know the books that Gygax and Arneson read and were influenced by, these are them.

I strongly recommend them. I remember some of them from my early days of gaming.

Chris Engle
