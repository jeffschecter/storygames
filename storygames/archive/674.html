    It was a "Game Design thread":http://www.indie-rpgs.com/forum/index.php?topic=19594.0 where I discuss the tabletop versio of the game; the LARP version basically a little bit of the themes and structures but my friend Warren innovated it greatly.

    The lowdown: Each Lady is secretly paired with one Lord, and in between dancing and talking with various Lords they try to figure out who they're secretly paired with; at the end of the dance, each player has one chance to pick someone to be marry, and they get happy/unhappy endings based on whether or not they guessed right. Also, the Ice Queen and Duke of Ash can reward players with certain powerups, but usually only if they engage in destructive powergames. This much comes from my game; beyond this, Warren wrote up some elaborate backstories for each character (adding more hints about who matches whom, as well as giving each Lords their own motivations). Warren also set up a mechanic for the Ladies to ask their Lords to duel for them, basically to make this game play out even more like Utena.
