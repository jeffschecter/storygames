\n I'm interested in games that have purposes other than entertainment. Once, for example, I played a browser game about a pandemic spreading. You could stop it by building hospitals and quarantining people. That was fun.

However, I often find they're impractical to play. They either:\n
  • Are too expensive or
    • \n
  • Are located in New York or
    • \n
  • Require a download or
    • \n
  • Take too much time.
\nWhich games can I play, like, now, pretty much? Specific recommendations would be good.

(If you know about business and roleplay simulations, I'm interested in them, so they can be a bit more impractical. Don't say "This company does business simulations, check them out" but do say "Here's a business simulation game, have a look".)
