7 + 7 days of creativity
RPGenesis is an international event for the creation and the promotion of roleplaying games. Without depending on publishers, every year participants have a week to develop their games \u2013 and this year, just like in 2012, we take things a step forward.
There will not only be the traditional Author\u2019s Week, where a new game is created with, at least, 5.000 words, but also the Editor\u2019s Week, where participants can further develop past or present projects.
Like in the previous years, there will be no prizes or official judges \u2013 instead, there will be the power of a strong community, always there to supply copious amounts of motivation and help, in order to both create something new, and push forward projects that draw our personal interest.

Editor\u2019s Week
From the 5th to the 11th August, each participant will have 7 days to create something for an already existing RPG \u2013 be it reviewing, translating or expanding an existing game, or even creating new adventures, settings, rules or demonstration material for it \u2013 whatever it takes to further the cause of your RPG of choice. Note that if you chose a game created by someone else, you will need to have the author\u2019s permission to do so. It\u2019s possible to simply write, but you can also draw, take pictures, program, promote, engage in performing arts, or even sing \u2013 whatever it takes for an editor to develop or promote an RPG.

Author\u2019s Week
From the 19th to the 25th of August, each participant will have 7 days to write at least 5.000 words for a new RPG. This is a challenge that many have beaten before since 2010. The goal is to write, write, write \u2013 getting fresh ideas to paper now, so you can review and clean up later; playing with words, experimenting new systems and entering alternative universes \u2013 leaving doubts and hesitations for next year\u2019s Editor\u2019s Week, and dare to write without fear, i.e. to do anything an author would do to get a finished first draft of a game.

What's the Deal Behind This
This is not a contest, this an open event held by the community for the community. All the files can be found in this google docs folder:
https://drive.google.com/folderview?id=0B0bUHr-C_JJ0VTdvaUQtLUNWZHc&usp=sharing
Any promotional material that anyone creates can be put into this folder for everyone to use. The links and the information submitted for each project will also be publicly available.
RPGenesis has no centralized source that everyone has to link to. Any blog, forum, site, podcast, channel, etc. can take all this stuff and help promote the event. If possible, just try to use the #RPGenesis2013 hashtag for cross-reference.

Writing Games Since 2010
The first RPGenesis was organized by just a couple of people in Portugal. We wanted to do a week long NaNoWriMo for RPGs and so we did. On the next year, the Brazilian community joined with the Portuguese and the event grew. Last year, we added the Editor's Week as a way to open the event to all kinds of talent. This year, we would like to welcome non-Portuguese-speaking game designers to see how far RPGenesis can spread its wings. Let's go global!
