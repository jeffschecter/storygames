So I went into Pandemonium for the first time today. Pandemonium is a sci-fi/fantasy book and game store in Boston - for those who know the area, it used to live in Harvard Square, but it's moved to Central Square a couple of months ago, and this is the first time I've gone in since the move.

So I go downstairs to where the gaming stuff's at, and I'm looking through - Burning Wheel, nice...Artesia...oh crap, Timestream!

I thanked the lady working, who is the general manager as well, I think, for carrying my game and for supporting independent publishing. We chatted a bit about games and such and I showed her the proof for carry, and she had complimentary things to say about me taking on the subject. Overall, a good experience.

Oh, and I bought yellow and blue dice for Mechaton.

Anyway, I hope to get my act together enough to organize some indie events there - they have an awesome play space downstairs. If anyone has experience with running successful store-level events, or is in the area and would like to get in on it, I sure would appreciate any help and support.
