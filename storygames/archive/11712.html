    Posted By: Mike SandsMaybe it works better if Walter and Maude swap places?
    \nNah. What you need to do is move the Dude out of True Neutral. The Dude is A) concerned for the well-being of a woman he met for a mere moment, which is to say Good and anti-establishment, unconcerned by the law or proper procedure and generally disorganized, which is to say Chaotic.

    Walter is enraged by violations of the rules, including violating his Free Speech in a coffee shop and Smokey going over the line while bowling. So he's Lawful. While Walter is not really concerned with Bunny's well-being, he's not exactly looking to exploit anone except when it's really convenient (eg, the money drop), so he is Neutral on the Good/Evil axis.


    Maude believes both the Good/Evil dichotomy and the Lawful/Chaotic are constructs of the male patriarchal phallocentric hegemony, and doesn't buy into any of it. Plug her into True Neutral.
