\n Post-Apocalyptic Indianapolis is about to wind up, and Bulldogs! is next, for my small group (4 total). While mostly traditional, story-type-play is slowly starting to take root. They want to try their hands at Class D Freight (low-rent, high-risk delivery), and I need some inspiration.

Being a full-time dad, I've got time to watch movies, and I mine those for story ideas, specific scenarios, locations, etc.

What movies about 'transporting things' would you all recommend?
