Hiya all.

I'm in the midst of working on my monster movie/zombie movie game Infected and I'm trying to fine-tune something. I'm hoping I can get a little help from the creative brains here.

During the course of the game players will author different locations for their characters to hole up in, avoid the ravages of the monsters for a little bit, and maybe get some stuff done. Each location gets authored with four statistics or attributes. Those four are;

Hardware - Available weapons & technology & such.
Comfort - How easy it is to catch a breath while on the run from the horrors.
Danger - The chances of this place being over run by the beasties or of someone just plain getting hurt while here.

and...

Creepiness - How unsettling this place can be, or how likely someone is to have a bit of a breakdown here.

It's that last one that seems to be playing the "One of these things is not like the others"-song in my head.

So, what I'm hoping for, are some ideas on a label to replace Creepiness. It should capture the essence of 'Just how scary is this place?', while fitting in with the other three, and not getting too concrete. I'll appreciate any thoughts that jump out of your brain.

-Eric
