\n Hi everyone.
\nThe wonderful people of Double Exposure who bring us Dreamation each winter. Are gearing up for their Summer con - DEXCON
\n
\nThis year Dexcon will be held from Wednesday, July 8th to Sunday, July 12th, 2009 At the Morristown Hyatt & Conference Center in Morristown, New Jersey You can call the Hyatt (973-647-1234) 9-5 weekdays. Be sure to mention DEXCON 12 and/or Double Exposure to get the $109. event rate
\n
\nIGE NEEDS YOU!
\nto Run Games
\n
\nDexcon would like all event submissions into them by June 12th.
\nIn order to get the games run at the times you want them, and see whos running what when
\nplease visit the IGE Wiki to post your events. I need these events by July 10th so that I can compile and submit them for the good people at Double Exposure.
\n
\nLooking forward to playing with you.
\n
\n-Kat Miller
