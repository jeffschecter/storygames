    Part 2:
    After they returned, they learned that Johnathan's next in command, Maximillian had been captured by Captain Bremer and was probably up for torture. So they planned to get inside and save him before it was too late. Johnathan's soft hearted issue played into it nicely. They tried to get inside, but their ordinary contact wasn't on duty so they had to get in by other means. They mangade to get inside, but the dungeon was watched over by loyal guards. It was established in scene 2 that Florian ahd seen his former Personal Servant reduced to a lackey when they sneaked into the library through the servants quarters. Outside the outer entrance to the dungeon, Florian saw his personal servant and spoke to him. The servant was willing to help him on teh condition that he would try to rebel against his father and if he became king he would be just towards the common people. They mangaed to get clothes and one of the players narrated how another servant told them that they should have been serving drinks to the guests half an hour ago! So an interlude where they played servants and Florian hanaged to speak with his loved one under the guise of serving her a drink. She said she would shelter them and they should meet her maid in the gardens. They managed to get out and sneak through the kitchen to the servant's entrance to the dungeon. It was guarded only by the brutish but stupid Bolg. (When I told them the name of the guard I had forgotten the reference but when a player said "Yes I gather he was brutish enough!" I suddenly remembered my Tolkien.) They managed to get past Bolg, and face the torturer. They saved Maximillian and left the torturer on the Rack. Maximillian couldn't resist giving the wheel a couple of extra turns.

    The last scene they met in the garden a little while later and met Amadelia's maid. She should lead them to shelter, but suddenly they heard an alarm and the bloodhounds.
    It was the last scene so I pushed the players towards conclusion. Probably too much, but the ending wasn't bad. They fled the hounds towards the shield and Johnathan recognized the black coat of Captain Bremer. They reached the shield and couldn't get through. It was then that Prince Florian who had the sphere, fell through the shield. He helped his friends and the maid through as well and they ridiculed the Guard that was trapped behind the shield.
