    \n One of the best systems for emulating a book would likely be Annalise. The claims give you a perfect tool for bringing in consistent imagery and set pieces. The main character certainly has a Vulnerability that you can draw traits from "Orphaned since birth." And it would give you an excuse to explore some of the side characters and their stories. What Secrets do they possess?
