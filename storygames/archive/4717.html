\nSign up for ENWorld Chicago Gameday XVIII

ENWorld Chicago Gameday XVIII is a day of FREE gaming held at earth's finest game store, Games Plus in Mt. Prospect, IL. Gameday XVIII will be Saturday, Nov 10th, from 9:30am to 9:00pm.

We've still got a bunch of seats left in various events. Those of particular interest to those of the hippie persuasion include: Dread (the Jenga one, not the Pandemonium one), Dogs in the Vineyard, and It Was A mutual Decision.
