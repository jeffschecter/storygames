\n I'm not exactly married to the title of this category*.

Dunno what it is about the title. It just doesn't seem to match up with what I'm thinking of with the description. "Play Craft" doesn't seem right as well, it's hard to know it at a glance.
"Improving the Play Experience" seems... overkill? It gets it right more or less, but is kind of long.

The description still stands, and it goes:
"This is discussion geared towards improving the gaming experience as a player (or GM), independent of system. In short, Making Gaming More Fun. Tips, tricks, processes, 'craft', spotty ideas and random musings are the order of the day. System-specific tips and tools are kosher.
Get advice for your game! Get ideas for an upcoming game you're going to run or play in! Give advice to others! "

I don't know a good way to say that.

Any ideas? I'll eventually go with whatever catches my fancy. Wordsmiths unite!

-Andy
