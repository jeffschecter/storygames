    \n Yes, I see now.

    In general, Strife converts directly into Glory. It's not a closed system, though. Extra Glory appears from the aether as needed (like when the heroes defeat a monster... sometimes their total Glory earned is more than the Strife cost of the creature). So maybe a kind of PTA Budget loop would work, with the addition of a bottomless pool of Glory for basic contests and the odd situation. Actually, the SotC Fate point economy kind of works like that, and it seems to hum along just fine.
