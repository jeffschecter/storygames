    Tell us more about colouring in Jake!
    \nYou know how when you go to Denny's or somewhere they'll give you a kids menu and a pack of 4 crayons? How much fun is that? sea Dracula brings all the fin of affordable dining to indie gaming!

    Packed in with every copy of Sea Dracula is an authentic set of 4 crappy crayons! Each color represents one of Sea Dracula's action stats: Investigation, Fighting, Partying and Dancing. Color your lawyer animal mostly blue with yellow stripes to min-max for fighting with a side of investigation. Have your creature attorney major in red with a minor in green for a dancing machine who knows hoy to party!
