\n I personally know very very few people who are "serious" roleplayers, and very few chances to get people together to play. I was wondering if anyone was interested in getting a gaming group going over Skype. I'm interested in playing games like Dogs in the Vineyard, Primetime Adventures, Mouse Guard, and pretty much any other indie RPG. I know that for me it's hard to carve out a chunk of time to game, so we wouldn't have to meet very often, and we can talk schedule after each game session.
\n Number-wise, I'm shooting for four players and a GM. I'd be willing to GM, but I think it would be fun to take turns. Right now the only games I own are DitV and In A Wicked Age, but I'm up to getting more.
\n Anyway, I hope a few people are interested, because I really want to play!
