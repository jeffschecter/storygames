    \n If you like Indian Food, Breads of India just down the block from Endgame isn't open on Sundays so that's too bad.     \n Hey guys,

    I actually have an improv class on Sunday from 2:30 to 5, so that early afternoon meeting out in Oakland doesn't really work for me.

    However, if you guys felt like meeting on Sunday a little after 5 for dinner in the area of Fort Mason/Fisherman's Wharf, I could do that easily. Musee Mecanique, a working arcade full of steampunk-ish turn-of-the-20th-century amusements, on Pier 45 is about the only thing on the wharf worth seeing, and I'd recommend checking it out at some point on your trip one way or another. Or, if I knew how many to expect, I could try and reserve a table at the Slanted Door in the Ferry Building at the Embarcadero and meet up sort of half-way between Endgame and where I'd be coming from.
