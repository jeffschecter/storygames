\n Middle of the night now, Oslo, Norway. Not often a man has the whole house to himself, so I'm staying up late, listening to The Cure.

Love Cats and Lullaby are some of the best songs ever written, and so is Close To Me.

The Cure has music with it's own special mood. I'd like to challenge you to make a roleplaying game on this music, recreating some of the mood you find in their music.

Now, that's a challenge! Go game!
