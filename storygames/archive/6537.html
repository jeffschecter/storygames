\n Hi folks,

I know some of you have been following my foray into card games with some interest so I thought you would like to know that the first half of my order has arrived with the rest on their way:

Opening the box


Unpacking


Tuck Box


Constructed Box

Some sample cards


Yes the cards do fit!


Ryan at Guild of Blades has been fantastic through the whole process, talking me through every step and reassuring me when I was getting a bit stressed. The cards look fantastic, are nice to shuffle and hold and the printing is top notch.

I am very excited about the game and will be launching at 'games expo' in Birmingham, England on the 31st May. I will be opening the shop on my website on the 2nd of June once I am back from the convention. Ryan will also be retailing the game through the GOB site and I will let you folks know when that is up and running but hopefully it should be in time for my release date.

I will be happy to answer any questions.

Cheers

Iain
