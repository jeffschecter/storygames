\n So, I wonder, how many games really sell themselves in a sentence (fragment)?

Monsterhearts: Play sexy queer teenage monster heartbreakers dealing with angst, horror, and heartbreak.
Dogs in the Vineyard: Inexperienced virgin teenagers with guns try to keep the peace and figure out what's right in the Wild West.
Lady Blackbird: Steampunk firefly.

I'd play all those games!

So let's see if we can't build a library of pitches. Let's keep it to twenty words, and don't be afraid to give a different pitch for a game already specified.

    \n There is a home page dedicated for this kind of stuff. Can't remember the address though. I've done a pamphlet for the Indie Room in Sweden - a collection of game masters that attends conventions to show and pitch Forge games and homebrews. Here are the translations of the pitches but they are usually backed up with keywords. For example, InSpectres has the keywords Ghostbusters, McDonalds, Big Brother, paranormal.

    LESS THAN TWO HOURS
    The Cat of Excellence: you are nominated for the glorious Golden Cat.

    Drakar & bananer: reach the final stage to fight the boss.

    GxB: who is Momoko's final pick out of her three dates?

    InSpectres: action-packed mission to capture ghosts.

    Keep It Sunny: The TV series about a bunch of idiots that owns a bar.

    Mumins v\xe4rld: something unpredictable happens when we're about to pick some apples.

    Null State: something goes wrong. No one takes responsibility. You have to clean it up.


    TWO TO FOUR HOURS
    Bad Family: play a family similar to the Simpsons or Family Guy.

    Best Friends: the group of girls that loves outwards but hates inwards.

    Det sj\xe4tte inseglet: every hero hides a dark secret.

    Dungeon World: an elf, a dwarf and a hobbit walks down into a dungeon...

    Hollowpoint: bad people doing bad things for the bad reasons.

    Kagematsu: get Kagematsu to love you in order to save the village.

    Microscope: choose a world and explore it's history!

    Prosopop\xe9e: In a surreal world with it's statue quo removed.

    The Quiet Year: what happens in the village that survived the war?

    Utpost: hell is other people.


    FOUR TO SIX HOURS
    Apocalypse World: the world is broken. What do you do?

    Don't Rest Your Head: no matter what you do: don't fall asleep!

    Lady Blackbird: you're in a cell on the airship The Hand.

    Monsterhearts. inspired by Buffy, Twilight, and similar.

    Psychodrame: "How could this happen? Oh, my God! How?!"

    Remember Tomorrow: mega-corps stands before your goal.

    Svart av kval, vit av lust: what are you willing to sacrifice?
