The Best of Gaming Podcasts

Matthew D. Gandy
August 2006 edited August 2006 in Play Advice
Persuant to my comment at the Podcasting panel at GenCon, I am looking to compile a small CD of the Best of Gaming Podcasts. Currently, I only listen to three (Have Games, Sons of Kryos, and Durham 3), mainly because I have to manage my podcast consumption.

For Judd, Paul, Clinton, Jason, and Remi: what is, in your opinion, your best episode? The one that hits on all cylinders and gets you (and hopefully your listeners) fired up about gaming?

For everyone else: what other gaming podcasts out there are really worth the time? What is the best episode of the podcast you're recommending.

I'm not looking to fill an audio CD, because that would be one each of the ones I listen to, nor am I looking to fill a data CD, because that would be too many hours of content. This should be a sampler, a teaser for the shows themselves, that can be copied and given willy-nilly to my players and others, to give them a heads-up on the whole gaming-podcast groove.

Thank you for your time and attention.
