\n
\n
\n

\n
\n\nSeptember 2009 \n edited September 2009 in Directed Promotion
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n Guys, you may have heard that John Harper, designer of Lady Blackbird, is now asking strangers on the Internet to buy him drinks.

Whatever has driven him to do this, I'm sure you join me in wishing John well. Meanwhile, I'm struggling over what to do. After all, I've enjoyed John's games, and want to give something back. And, like many talented writers, perhaps drinks are what keeps him going. If he needs drinks to keep writing, then perhaps we should buy him some.

Luckily, PayPal allows you to leave comments when you pay. So, when I pay John money, I'll implore him to spend it on healthy fruit juice, reminding him that he doesn't need to drink alcohol to have a good time. I may also remind him about Jesus.

It's a difficult, and sad, situation, and I'd be interested to hear your thoughts on this matter. Do also feel free to pass on your support for John.

Graham
