OK, so Burning Rubber just had its first playtest. Problems were recognized and solved (I think) with the driving system, and I think it's good to go.

\n

If anyone wants to playtest for me, writing down questions and asking me when you've played a couple of corners, I'd be most grateful. You can ask here or by clicking the giant Q on the upper left of my blog or, of course, email me at joshua@glyphpress.com . If I use your feedback, you'll get credit and a custom car for the game built by me when it's published!

\n

I recommend the Lego Racers sets to play with. They're about $5 at Target (actually, oddly, they're $5.44) apiece, they look cool, they're the right size, and they break apart really nicely when you take hits.

\n

Make a race course out of tape on the floor, making sure it's usually juuust wide enough for two cars to get by. We played with a kidney bean shaped track about 6' wide.

\n

Start your engines!
