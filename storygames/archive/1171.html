    I'll post the character creation mechanic soon. For the time being, the quick version is:

    Everyone brings a deck of cards. Spend an hour or so as a group trading cards with each other. Each trade is a pair of little stories, told in the form of remembrance. "Remember when you beat Julian at his favorite game?" (offering a diamond) "Why yes. It was almost as thrilling as the time you spent all day fencing with Random." (accepting, offering a heart)

    In a large group, segregate the chaos-siders from the amber-siders.

    2-10 you know.

    Jacks are "Influence". Mostly, this is access to armies, but it can represent other forms of influence.

    Queens are "allies". Each queen represents one minor sibling. In a "canon" game these are Deirdre (spades), Flora (clubs), Llewella (hearts) and Fiona (diamonds). One queen means she (or he) will trade information with you. Two means she'll help you more actively.

    Kings are minor powers. Sorcery, Trump, Shapeshift, Artifacts. Having one gives you impersonal access; for example, having one king of clubs gives you a personal trump deck. Two kings of clubs might give you the ability to make trump sketches.

    Aces are aspects of the major power (either Pattern or Logrus). Having one gives you "basic" access. Two or more gives you extra "tricks" you can do with them.
