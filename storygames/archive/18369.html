\n DEXCON 16 is an amazing game convention being held from Wednesday, July 3 to Sunday, July 7, 2013 at the Morristown Hyatt & Conference Center in Morristown, New Jersey. From the same great people who organize Dreamation and METATOPIA, DEXCON is always loads of fun and packed with gaming of all types. Here's the direct link: http://www.dexposure.com/dexcon16.html

Kat and I are putting together an Indie Games Explosion and want YOU to be a part of the fun! If you run two sessions of games, you'll earn a free badge to the convention, as well as free meals in the staff suite.

If you're interested in making the convention as fun as possible by running games, please send an e-mail to Kat Miller at her Gmail account: katswhim at gmail dot com Kat will then share a Google Doc with you, where you can describe your game, choose your timeslot, and the like.

Deadline for submission is Friday, May 31, so get your games in early! Hope to see you at the con.
