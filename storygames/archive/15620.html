    Posted By: JBMannonThe problem is there is no way I know of to play it over the net (the system is dominos based).
    \nChallenge accepted! :-)

    Go to The Trouble with Rose folder here (Google docs TTwR folder) and grab the Xtra_TheTroubleWithRose_Taebl.zip file. Inside is a taebl file. Go to Taebl (www.taebl.com), select the 'Play a blank game' link. Once the Silverlight page loads, click on the folder icon in the tool bar and select the taebl file that you unzipped. Viola! Two decks to play with, one is a deck of cards with jokers and the other is a 'deck' of double six dominoes that you can play face down, face up, or deal to your hand (hidden.)

    Have fun and play around with the interface. Lots of drag and drop options, right click menus, etc. To share the room with friends, just post the URL in the lower right corner. Too easy.

    \n We've been hacked!

    Someone went to The Five's website and uploaded the latest Behind The Scenes playtest rules!

    And by latest we of course mean 3 years ago! It's loaded with playtest comments and optional rules. I forgot how much fun I had creating those domino graphics and play examples.

    The File is both usable and readable but also raw with notes and misspellings included! Although that's also strangely appropriate for the game's theme! My only regret is that I no longer have the version where the basic rules were written out as graffiti on the side of torn cardboard box!
