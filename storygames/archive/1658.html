    It's a pretty, pretty game. Clearly alot of thought went into the components. I only got to play for about 15 minutes and haven't played the related games, so some of it is hard to judge. The magic is very battle-geared and has a whole other level of tactical play that was very cool. My only complaint: Too many damn reference cards. A cheat sheet would have been fine, actually.

    OKay, one more complaint: The plastic chosen for the figures is going to make those suckers really endurable, but it also has a probleem with the casting of the horses. The ones I saw will likely need the boil'n'ice treatment to be properly upright. MInd you, they weren't bent so badly they fell over, but they did look a bit like they were fighting for position around a turn in a horse race.
