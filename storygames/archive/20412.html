\n Hey all, Silver Screen Nightmares is a side project of mine that I've finally finished!. Its available to download straight from my blog and its the perfect game to play for Halloween that isn't Dread. Here is a quick overview:

Silver Screen Nightmares is a one-shot rules light horror system meant to emulate the scares and thrills of modern and classic horror movies. Character and scenario generation is quick and simple. Each player selects an archetype and role and the GM makes a couple of quick decisions or rolls randomly to decide what horrors everyone must face. Setup takes no more than ten minutes for everybody!

Have a happy Halloween everyone.
