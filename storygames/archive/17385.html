    \n February in San Ramon (East Bay, on the east side of the hills): DunDraCon

    Memorial Day weekend in Burlingame (near San Francisco airport): KublaCon

    Labor Day weekend in Fremont (East Bay halfway between Berkeley and San Jose): CelestiCon

    October, location up in the air: PacifiCon

    As mcellis linked above, It's to be hoped BigBadCon returns to its location at an Oakland airport hotel in October

    The first Convolution hasn't happened yet; it's a general sf con, but there'll be LARPs and Steve Jackson is a guest and I'd guess there'll be other gaming. The first one is next month, November in Burlingame; the same might be true of a next one if there is a next one...

    \n KublaCon really requires you to book a full slate of games in advance. Drop-in gaming doesn't result in you getting many games in (I got in two games over the weekend, I think?). Of the two cons I've been to in the area, I was less impressed by this one.

    I had a lot of fun at Big Bad Con. It just happened at the start of October. A++ would recommend. It's well run, medium-sized (200-300 people, maybe??), full of great people, full of great games, puts a focus on quality GMs & games, had some cool indie guests (Jason Morningstar, Luke Crane), and was just generally friendly.
