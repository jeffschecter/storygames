    \n My favorite online shooters for the PS3 are Metal Gear Online (part of Metal Gear Solid 4) and Call of Duty 4. The latter is a bit less tactical than MGO, in which you can have a Solid Snake player sneaking and trying to take out members of two other warring factions, for example.

    As for racing, Motorstorm and Burnout: Paradise are both fun. Motorstorm is cheap now at $30, and something about the controls and physics just makes it good fun for me, but it's lacking in features to promote long play sessions. Burnout, on the other hand, is a great game with challenges for hours on end, also in the multiplayer mode.

    And as I said above, as soon as LBP comes out (October 21), you'll have a hard time finding me playing anything else for a good long while.
