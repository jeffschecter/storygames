\n Hello! Indie+ would like to invite you to play some games over Google+ Hangouts. We have two show-up-and-play events happening this Saturday the 27th, where we'll be playing Remember Tomorrow, Lady Blackbird, Kagematsu (modern setting), and Apocalypse World. These are teaching games, you don't have to know anything about them to play. We'll teach you the system and give you everything you need. Here's how you get in:

1) Check out the event pages. You can see which games are being played at what times.
First session: https://plus.google.com/events/c6vdp2kcvndm4nme9l2ns1ppanc
Second session: https://plus.google.com/events/caerihqp9sfb4f7o1bf9iol07n4
2) Click "Yes" under "Are you going?"
3) Sign in to Google (your Gmail or Youtube account should work for Google+ as well), or create a free Google account
4) This is optional: leave a comment on the event saying which game you want to play!
5) When the time comes, you'll receive a notification (the top right bell icon). Click "Join Hangout" on the notification or on the event page. You're in!

If you can't make it this time, there's a second game day on October 18th. We're always looking for people to run indie games for these events: message me (+Aaron Feild) or +Indie+ if you're interested. Thanks, and happy gaming!
