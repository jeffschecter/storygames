    \n I don't wanna sound like a broken record but I strongly would discourage getting a cab from Newark Airport to Dreamation. According to lazy Googling, the cab ride is $40-$50 one-way and the train (which runs twice an hour on weekdays and once or twice an hour on weekends) is $12.75 one-way. That drops you into New Brunswick which is a relatively cheap cab ride to the hotel if you can't get someone to pick you up, which is a big if.

    \n It's sub-New-York bad in New Brunswick. But the fact is that you're really in East Brunswick. The hotel is on the intersection of 3 major highways (Route 1, Route 18, and the New Jersey Turnpike) so there are no stores very near by you can camp out in and it's not simple to walk to the con from anywhere other than maybe the other hotel on the same road and the one attached to the back of the one the con is at. You pretty much have to drive there and pay for parking unless you stay in the hotel.
