\nThis week we continue the discussion of our Burning Empires game. We talk about our use of the scene economy, the scope of scenes, and what is and is not appropriate to accomplish with scene verses maneuver intent. We also revisit the Firefight and Duel of Wits subsystems, where we talk about our growing familiarity with Burning Empires' various moving parts. Finally we cover the loss of the Vaylen FoN, Garret Smiley and what that means for the GM
