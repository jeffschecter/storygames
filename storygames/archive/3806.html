    \n Well, (Physical) tells me it's a skill based off some degree of athleticism...probably whatever the Dexterity equivalent is in that system. For all I know, there's a Stealth (Psionic) skill as well, to conceal your brainwaves from passing psychics.

    [Evergreen] doesn't tell me a goddamn thing; it is completely without meaning in this context. And even if I knew the context, I'd probably still think it was a poor choice of word, and be irritated by it. I can be picky about things like that.


    Edit: Upon further reflection, I think I know what it means now. This is a skill for a game in which the PCs can take on the form of trees. Stealth (Physical) is the skill used when trying to conceal oneself as an actual, real-life tree (as opposed to a strictly mental tree, sort of an illusionary tree-form). [Evergreen] denotes that in this case, the character's preferred tree-form is an evergreen...a bonus when in, say, a stand of fir, but a serious drawback when you're trying to conceal yourself among the maples. If the rest of the game were as gleefully nonsensical and surreal as this, I could be very interested in it.
