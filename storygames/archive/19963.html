\nJohn Hill Died yesterday. I know he wasn't a story gamer but he was a major designer of games and a great guy. He designed Squad Leader for Avalon Hill in the 70's and later made Johnnie Reb Civil War miniatures game for GDW. I knew John from historical miniatures cons. A great fun guy. I'm sorry I'll never run into him again.

We should always honor our hobby's greats. Please tip your hat for John.

Chris Engle
