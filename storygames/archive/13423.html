    Posted By: David ArtmanMake sure that those you love know it, brothers. You never know when you might run out of time to tell them.
    \nAnd that's exactly it.

    Death hurts, but it's a pain that heals with time, love, and support. The best thing you can do, for yourself and your friend, is to surround yourself with the people you love and grieve with them. Then, when the you're all out of grief, continue to surround yourself with those people and celebrate life with them. It's a long process, and it's okay to feel terrible for a while, but trust that things will get better.
