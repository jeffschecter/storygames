\n What is a good name for a pool of points players can use to help their characters be more effective?

Something like Drive, Conviction, Inner Fire, maybe?

Ideally, it would make sense for characters who are weaker/less experienced to have more of this, uh, stuff.

I really like "Drive" and "Conviction", but I don't like the implication that someone who is more experienced and powerful has less drive or determination.

Any ideas?

    \n Potential.

    EDIT: Because it's something the young and inexperienced have which the person at the apex of their career does not, and because it's a half step from that word to the words "energy" and "potence".

    EDIT 2: You could use "Potence" or "Potency" themselves:

    potency, n.

    1.\tthe state or quality of being potent.
    2.\tpower; authority.
    3.\tefficacy; effectiveness; strength.
    4.\tcapacity to be, become, or develop; potentiality.
    5.\ta person or thing exerting power or influence.

    \n This could also depend on the genre/tone of game.

    In an Arabian Adventures game for example, it might be Kismet or Fate ~ you succeeded despite odds because we have no Fate but the Fate we are given and it was given you would do this.

    In a Swashbuckling game, "Flair, Panache"

    For more of this if you are less experienced, the conceit could be that as time passes you become jaded and lose Flair, Fate favours the young and innocent and as time passes you are on the road Fate has set and it does not steer you as much.
