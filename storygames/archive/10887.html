\n The Baja Arizona Science Fiction Association is having Tuscon 36 this weekend. TusCon is a nice little SF relaxacon held in Tucson, AZ.. They have a bit of everything and a nice con suite and they claim that Southern Arizona Gamers Association is running their game room this year. While I realize it's not that long after RinCon, I was wondering if any of the Tuscon or Phoenix story gamers (or from anywhere, but TusCon is very local usually) are going to be there this time? I almost certainly will.
