\n
\n
\n
\n\nDenys \n\n\n
\n
\n\nJanuary 2010 \n edited January 2010 in Story Games
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n My story-game gang is looking to play a game that will support the idea of playing angels and/or demons in a cold-war that exists between them and their minions (probably modern day focused overall). We're looking for something more story gamish and looking for a system that will give us opportunity for social conflict and intrigue (not necessarily between PCs though maybe) more than action stabby-stabby though bringing physical conflict into it wouldn't be unheard of either. We're open for troupe-style play or whatever.

No one cares for the old In Nomine system as it's too sim though if there's a story-game hack to add to it, I'm open to hearing about it.

Probably looking for something with a GM though doesn't have to be if we're happy.

We're looking for long-term play (or at least extended) not just a session thing.

Right now we're thinking Dogs in the Vineyard or In a Wicked Age. We'd done a Mortal Coil game in the past with a similar angel/demon cosmology invented at the table but everyone thought the game was a mess; we liked the fluff we came up with but didn't care for the system support.

What might you suggest?

We're fine with adding hacks to whatever.
