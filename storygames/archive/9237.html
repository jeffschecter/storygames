    \n I've studied a number of story games at length, but have only played a couple (My Life with Master and Universalis). I've even tried my hand at designing one or two, but I can't say I've been too successful at it. The exercise has certainly helped me out in designing my next edition of Legendary Quest, though, which will have some story-game influences even though it will still be fairly traditional at its core.

    I'd like to try some other story games (most especially DitV), but my regular gaming group is pretty hard-core traditional and I have a difficult time getting them to try indie games. I live in Albuquerque as well.
