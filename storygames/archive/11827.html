\n So I'm playing a Time & Temp game with some friends. One of them decides to make a temp who's from the near-ish future. Somewhere in the next century or so (as compared to my character who's from so far in the future that he's illiterate) just after the cyborg uprising of, let's say 2091. He says the character is a cyborg. I'm like, cool, what's his deal? Bionic limbs? Human brain in a robot body? Gun arm?

And my friend's like, no, no, no. No cybernetics.

But he's a cyborg, right?

Right. Both his parents were cyborgs, so he culturally identifies as a cyborg.

...

Brilliant.

What character concepts have you seen that you wish you thought of?
