    \n Hi, I hope this is the best place for this question. I'm looking for suggestions for a story game that might evolve into a wacky or wild adventure for the players. We are big fans of Fiasco but sometimes we're in the mood for something that doesn't go disastrously wrong; it's just wild hi-jinx that might even end positively. We prefer GMless games and have tried Fiasco with a variety of genres already. I'm looking for something that could be run as both a one shot or even a longer episodic game. Something that could be for all ages; depending on the characters and other elements inserted in the game. I think one of the things I have identified I'd like in the game is some kind of "mission" the characters are trying to accomplish while overcoming various obstacles (both internal and external). There could be various missions to accomplish in a longer game and only 1 in a one shot, but this would be slightly different than the characters mostly working against each other in something like Fiasco, to instead the characters mostly working together in this game. However, I don't want a GM having to throw things at the players either, and I would like to see the scenes flow similarly to the way they flow so naturally for Fiasco. I have considered something like "Do: Pilgrim's of the Flying Temple" but I'd prefer a different genre. Basically I'd like something along the lines of "Adventures in Babysitting", or "30 Minutes or Less", something very cinematic and sort of rules light. I'd appreciate any suggestions, thanks.
