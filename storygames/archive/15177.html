i have been working on the paper dice again and i wanted some feedback on this design.

functionally the die is made to work well with fudge systems and is colored to help with games that split 1,2,3 and 4,5,6 as different results.

The goal of the design is to evoke a space ship hud or radar, treating the pips as radar blips. i like the colors but i can have strange tastes what do you think?

I also changed the design from using tabs and slots to a tuck box like construction. it's super easy to assemble and rather strong to boot (these should live just fine in a dice bag with normal dice.

i had some blank space left on the page so i designed a little life counter to go along with the dice. any good ideas as to what i could put on the optional wheels?

    \n The design is brilliant, in that it allows you to use it for any number of games, and yet lends itself to BW and Fudge, as you said. Very, very cool. Unfortunate, from my perspective, is the fact that they are paper. I would personally never use dice like that, but only because I like the heft of a solid die and I feel like the bowed edges make it less random, due to those sides having a bit wider of an area.
