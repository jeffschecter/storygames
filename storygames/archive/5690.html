In the Red Box Hack, there's this combat action called Show Off. The mechanical bits of the action work pretty darned well, but I'm having some issues with the name of the thing and what it evokes. I considered trying to just come up with a new name that would evoke cooler narration, but instead I think I'd like to come up with a nice big list of cool narration and see about figuring out what kind of heading all those things might fit under.

\n

If that makes any sense to you.

\n

So, what I'm asking is, what might your character do to gain himself or herself an immediate advantage in a combat situation? It should be something that doesn't require you to change where you're fighting at and it should be something that characters who are more daring are going to be better at than characters who are more timid.

\n

Hit me with your ideas in the first person, as if you were right now playing the character. Or, as if you were the GM narrating a monster or NPC. Feel free to get creative. Push the limits.

\n

I'll start:

\n

I throw sand in his eyes!
\nThe dragon rears up, cranes his neck about, and starts bellowing fire in the air in a display of rage.
\nI thrust my sword in the air in time for lightning to strike it!
\nI begin chaning the 17 deadly words and give my enemy a look that lets him know I mean business
\nI raise my arms up and let the fire consume me!
\nI let him get in close, letting my defense down far enough to give him a kiss on the cheek
