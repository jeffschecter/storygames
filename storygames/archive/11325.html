    \n The place is getting hammered with downloads. Good fucking times. This is awesome. Here's my list of cool things that haven't been mentioned yet or which I want to mention again:

    Damnation Decade - if you liked Interstate 76 or Vigilante 8, faux postapocalyptic 1970s stuff, for True20 and D20 Modern...fucking awesome.

    Spycraft - yes, the original d20 Modern espionage game. Very, very slick, especially for the mastermind design system. And their "over the top" psychic craziness setting Shadowforce Archer. Ran these for over a year. Great stuff.

    Blood Games II, a horror game engine that will lead you into some excellent and odd supplements.

    Basic Action SuperHeroes (BASH!) Recently a second edition came out for this game, but the first edition is still damn cool.

    Apocalypse Prevention, Inc Do you like apocalypses? Do you like preventing them? There is nobody who does not like preventing apocalypses.

    Diana, Warrior Princess. The high concept nature of this game alone is worth picking it up for - what if our modern day was as badly mangled as we badly mangle the time of the ancients?

    Mars: Savage Worlds Edition. I have probably pimped this one enough. No, just kidding, it's still fucking awesome.

    The Kerberos Club - Victorian suparheroes, I mean superheroes. The costume choices alone should make you want this book.


    There's also a lot of corebooks here for various game systems: Cortex, Karma, MADS, Magpie Codex, QAGS, etc. Those are always worth getting something new in your toolbox.
