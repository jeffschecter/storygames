Posted By: Jason MorningstarThanks.

You know what this reminds me of? Playing D&D with a bunch of pre-teens at the Durham public library. There were twelve of them and it was utter chaos, and they were making guys with "King" and "Unicorn" and "Angel" as their character class. And my first impulse was to correct them, but I realized that it was actually pretty cool, and they'd be happier and have more fun if I kept my mouth shut. In context, it didn't break anything.
\nDUDE, those are the coolest classes I've heard of in a while.

Interesting - just a simple change can show so much.

Character Creation Step 4b. Change your class' name. Be sure to name your class something other than what we use in the book. It'll tell your group what you're thinking about with the character and add color to your game.

This could work in any game where classes are used, obviously, not just D&D.
