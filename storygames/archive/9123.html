    \n on further thought and reading, i think if i tried to play Agora through RPoL, the thing to do would be to either find someone willing to referee the game and not play, or make all the players GMs. if everyone is set as a GM it would insure transparency, especially since GMs are capable of fudging dice rolls, but can also see that rolls were fudged. Plus, it would probably make it easier to post as different characters that might be under the control of different people from time to time, like the Lieutenants and various Obstacles. Though, I suppose the same thing there could be accomplished by just having one name to post as and always specifying in each post who you are at that moment.
