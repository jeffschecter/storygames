    You might be able to do this with theatrical lighting terms - they're not going to be Elizabethan, but...

    \n

    For the "switch" you could use "spotlight" - is the spot on Swordfight? Find Love? You can certainly have multiple spots in one scene.

    \n

    For the "dial" you could use "dimmer."

    \n

    For "knob" (assuming this means a multi-position switch) you could use... I dunno, "gel" (that's the different colored filters you put in front of a lighting instrument to change the color of the light). "Focus," maybe?
