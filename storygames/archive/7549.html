    \n Welcome Simon!

    I'm pretty sure the "in crowd" is an Internet illusion. There's no in crowd here, but there is an intersection of groups of people who know each other.

    Being a Story Gamer is about enjoying a certain kind of play. Tell us about what you're playing these days. Give it a bit of time.

    Regarding Gen Con, I wouldn't say it's actually the best way to meet and get to know the folks on this forum. It's too frenetic for that. That said, show up at the Embassy Suites, say hi to some folks with familiar looking games, and let it be known you want to play. You'll get into something. If worst comes to worst, run around waving a copy of Dogs in the Vineyard over your head. Someone's sure to notice.

    If you really want to get to know folks, go to the nearest "Go Play" event. Failing that, hang at your local game store and see if you can build up local interest in story games.

    So what are you playing these days? What's your game about?
