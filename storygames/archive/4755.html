    \n During the last gamechef, I believe there was a braggadocio thread that bent itself in a battle rap direction until the haters shouted it down.

    The answer is, yes, you could do this, but it would require buy-in from all the players. It's going to self-select. I mean, The Blossoms Are Falling has a duel of verse that gives a bonus if one of the participants actually composed something, but does not require it for normal play.

    Perhaps a hiphop themed game where the stats are the four elements, so all contested actions must be resolved through emcee-ing, djing, breaking, or graff.
