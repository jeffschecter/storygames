    \n Just played my first session tonight Ross. (The game arrived a couple of weeks ago but life sometimes trumps gaming).

    We only just made it to Mars in 1.5 hours of play because everyone was having so much fun. It's amazing how what seems like so little structure in LOM can guide both the telling of great stories and the creation of really vivid characters.

    My character chose to be on the mission at the cost of abandoning his wife and young child. We narrated a scene where he spoke to his little boy for the last time by view screen and the child was wearing a cardboard box space helmet and asking when Daddy would be home. There was a long pause when that scene ended and everyone just kind of looked at each other. It was the most emotionally impactful RPG scene I have ever been in.

    This game just clicked for us. Thanks so much for making it!
