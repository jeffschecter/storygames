Dice roller (prettier than before)

Graham
November 2006 edited November 2006 in Directed Promotion
I posted about this a while ago, when it was a prototype. It looks nicer, now, and it's much better behaved.

Catch your hare: Dice roller

It pretty much does what it says. It rolls dice. But, if people in different locations sign in with the same password, they see the same dice. So it's a tool to use with online play: play the game by IM or Skype and use this to roll the dice.

Also, you can move the dice, and the people you're playing with will see the dice move in the same way.

And the dice are very pretty now. They're different colours and, on Firefox, they're translucent.

Although I'm posting this under Directed Promotion, I'd genuinely welcome any feedback. Specifically, what I want to know is this: how would I need to tweak this tool to let you play a particular game online?

If you do find any faults, then, of course, I need to know about that too: whisper them to me.

Graham
