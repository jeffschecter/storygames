\n I'm working on a game inspired by family therapy and horror movies and I'm compiling a list of movies and tv shows where the evil entity is a toy or doll (like Dolls, which I just finished watching), a child (The Omen), or an adult (can't think of one, and Friday the 13th and a Nightmare on Elm Street aren't what I have in mind).

Here's the list I have so far. Help me add to it, and tell me about other creepy (or campy) horror movies with scary children, dolls, or adults.

Scary Children
The Brood
The Omen
Children of the Corn
Little Oatik
Carrie
The Exorcist
The Shining
The Good Son
Children of the Damned
The Bad Seed
The Ring
Poltergeist
the X-Files, "Eve".

Scary Dolls (this is the easiest!)
Someone already has a good list!
There's an X-Files episode with a murderous doll.

Scary Adults
Dolls also has scary adults who create evil dolls
The Goonies??? The way the movie pits the children against the adults is the type of tension I'm aiming for in the game.

    \nPin sounds like it's exactly the sort of movie you're looking for: weird family dynamic and a creepy toy:

    "A doctor has a lifelike, anatomically-correct medical dummy, with muscles and organs visible through its clear skin, named Pin (after Pinocchio). Via ventriloquism, Pin explains bodily functions in a way kids can relate to. When the over-strict doctor and his wife are killed in a car crash, his son (Leon) transfers his alter-ego into Pin, whom he always believed was alive. He starts using Pin as an excuse to over-protect his sister (Ursula) from admirers and deflect unwanted intrusions, even to the extent of committing murder."

    The Supernatural (Season 2) episode "Playthings" features family secrets, ghosts and creepy dolls.

    The X-Files episode referenced earlier is "Chinga," from Season 5.
