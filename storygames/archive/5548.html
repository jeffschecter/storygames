I just happened on a really neat variant on Luke's three imps while I was pounding out the first proper long-form draft of Black Caddies.

\n

It's this evil mind-controlling Commissar's hat.

\n

When I wear it, I get all shouty and mean. It's hella-fun.

\n

I really, really love those moments when you hit on a voice for writing. I've found my exhorting one, which will see occasional (but punchy and hopefully fun) use. I'm still fiddling, trying to find the "hey, sidle up here, and let me show you how this slippery beast works" voice. It'll come, but it's tricky.

\n

"One section a night" Burgess
