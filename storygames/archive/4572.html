\n So, here's the pitch.

6 GM's running InSpectres game all at the same time with different players.The kicker is that they're all members of the same company. Characters are pre-made and handed out. Teams of agents are picked by the GM's.

The session starts with orientation and a meet-and-greet, followed by a staff meeting. Then the games begin. Because they're all working for the same company, that's right. ONE SET OF FRANCHISE DICE.

Anyone interested in the madness?
