    \n I can answer question 1: you sign up for stuff after the schedule goes up by sending an email to the convention staff with the games you'd like to sign up for in it. They will post the address (and the schedule) at a later date.

    DexCon is partly about the atmosphere! On Fridays, there's an informal black tie night. So if you want to participate, pack some duds. Also, the larp costumes tend to run the gamut from normal clothing to elaborate. Personally, I'd err on the side of elaborate (or at least well-thought-out)

    I am running some freeform games, to which you're heartily invited.

    \n It's easy to get to Newark from Morristown by train.

    And yes, people generally stay at the con hotel, and split their rooming cost with other con-goers. (I am unavailable for such this time out, for reasons I'll detail in a later post.)

    My general advice regarding sign-ups is: pre-register, and then the *instant* the schedule goes up, send in your game preferences for each time slot. Give a 2nd and even 3rd choice for each time-slot, to maximize your chances of getting into games you want. Also\u2014I made this mistake last time\u2014don't send in your preferences to the email from which Vinny announces the schedule is up Send it to the email address listed on the site above the schedule itself.

    Matt
