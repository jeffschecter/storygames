\n Here's a stack of pics from our first run of my tactical experiment. A hoot was had!


Figure 1. That guy up there with the big stack of chips is fucked.


Figure 2. A closer frame of fucked guy after being hammered by accurate M-60 fire.


Figure 3. Meanwhile, on the west side, a stand-off: an officer on the hill commands two units hiding in the marsh by a burned out jeep while the American FNG tries desperately to spot them.


Figure 4. Finished with the east pair (you may be able to make them out, drawn on the map now), the M-60 team crawls down the ridge line to flank the remaining bad guys. The M-60 gunner has just let off a burst, wounding one of the enemy there.

At this point the enemy fled and our heroes regrouped. The M-60 gunner burned through a lot of ammunition but no one was injured. Three of the enemy were badly wounded but no one was killed outright. Break out the smokes.
