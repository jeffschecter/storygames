    Posted By: Jason MorningstarAll joking aside, I'm with Mark - it'd be fun to take very constrained pieces of ephemera (like a character sheet) and playstorm a game around them. Sort of like the reverse engineering challenge but at the table, with friends.
    I haven't done any playstorming yet, but I just had a brainstorm, based on the implied focus of the character sheet.

    See, "What they took away" and "What do you believe in" seem to be too close for my liking. I prefer "What they took away" and "When will it end," as it gives a more gritty and urgent feel to the character.

    I would also change "Name" to "Who were you?"

    I envision two columns. Left-hand column, top-to-bottom, reads: "Ammo," "What is your weapon?", and "When will it end?" The right-hand column reads "Rage," "Who were you," "What did they take away?"

    Just a thought. It brings a bit more gonzo drive to the situation, I think.
