\n I'm at the Seven Years War Association Con in South Bend Indiana. Yesterday put on a miniatures game story game of "Last of the Mohicans". It used the action cards previously described in threads. That game went off well with all kinds of weird things happening (very true to James Fenemore Coopers turgid writing style - you can't be more unrealistic than he was.) That game went fast, two hours, so we had time to play a second game. I trotted out Fairy Tale Assassin League.

So, I laid out the locations (Restaurant with stage show) picked the Wicked Witch as my bad guy, because the host is always the bad guy. Good guys like red riding hood, and tinker bell were out there trying to stop my coerced porn ring (the with was very wicked). We tore through action cards with lots of challenges. An innovation I added was that all the actions happened unless the winner of a challenge specifically says it didn't happen. That only happened twice. Backs were stabbed left and right and in then end most of us were gunned down in the witches hospital room. Because the game uses victory points we had winners and losers and I used the standings to give a kind of Fiasco outcome. The winners got to appear in the movies sequel with better roles (even if they died in the first game).

The game was a Gas and I got some video of player comments that I will put up soon. Everyone had a good time and the game gave us another hour and half of play. I'm feeling very positive about the sales potential of the game. Time to start raising kick starter funds. But first got to do a campaign for Mongolian Goat Rodeo.
