The Indie Gaming Passport is coming to GenCon 2007!

Grab a passport at the Forge on Thursday and get started collecting 11 stamps from around the convention, touring the best that the small press has to offer.

- The Forge Booth
- The Play Collective
- Exile Studios
- The Ashcan Front
- The Art of Keith Senkowski
- Rogue Games
- Games on Demand/Indie Games Explosion
- Grey Ghost Games
- Burning Wheel & Wicked Dead
- Hamster Press
- Cubicle 7

Drop your stamped passport off in the purple prize entry box back at the Forge Booth before Saturday afternoon!

The prize drawing is Saturday, at 3pm, at the Forge booth, and features (at least) the following possible prizes:
