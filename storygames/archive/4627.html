\n Okay, so I am REALLY excited. I see that Sabertooth Games put up a demo adventure for the upcoming Warhammer 40K RPG, Dark Heresy.

I am going to run this and I need four players. Who's in?

Don't know what Warhammer 40K is? It's a grimm and gritty space fantasy where the PCs are part of a team of inquisitors hunting down chaos cultists in the reaches of Imperial Space. Your god, the Emperor of Humanity, is ever vigilant for the stain of chaos and you are sent out to purge a world when the warp rears it's ugly head. Apart from being gritty it's also a larger-than-life setting with giant slug throwers, massive power armor, space elves and orc gangs.

Let me know if your interested and we'll work out the details of when and where. I think the game will probably run two or three sessions. I plan on playing it straight with one or two Story Game techniques that will bring it all together.

Of course, it dawned on me that once I run this I should use the setting material and see how this runs with Dogs because it sounds like they could share terribly similar in themes.

Give me a holla,
- Don
