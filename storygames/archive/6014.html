    \n Hey guys,

    We were wavering about attending but if you guys are available then we're definately coming up!!!

    The symposium is on Friday (we will be on the raod most of the evening), Saturday 8:30am - 6pm thus we'd be availabe AT LEAST past 6pm (although gaming with my peeps always will trump stuffy chatter about comics), and Sunday 9am - 12:45pm. I think we're considering taking off on Monday so we'd be available most all afternoon on Sunday (I think Mark Smylie may want to have lunch together before he heads back to NJ).

    Why are we going? We like comics. We like religion. Mark Smylie (Archai Studios Press & Artesia comic) and David Lewis (Lone and Level Sands) will be heading up and Joanna has relatives in the Boston area. Although it's filling up an already packed April you guys are totally making it worth it.

    I'm dying to get together with you fine people!
