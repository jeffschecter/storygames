\n Greetings,

I just finished creating my Lady Blackbird remix game!

This is a proof of concept game to see if a baseball RPG will actually gather some interest. If not, I still made the game I wanted to play!

The elevator pitch is that you play a minor league baseball player in a yesteryear America as he struggles to make it to the pros on a team that's on a serious losing streak.

I remixed Lady Blackbird, so it is a very recognizable thing. That's good for you Lady Blackbird fans, of course!

Please check out the game (which I've recently updated based on feedback), play it, give me feedback if it is fun, what could make it more fun, what wasn't in there that was needed!

Thanks for your time!
Rich Rogers

(Link to the game: http://www.canonpuncture.com/wp-content/uploads/mtb-lady_b_style_122409.pdf)
