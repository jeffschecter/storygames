\n Hey everyone, so this is cheating a little bit. I have a a thread on Praxis already, but I know S-G gets more traffic, plus this seems appropriate to S-G (sometimes the spheres of S-G & Prax overlap in my head).

Anyway,

I wrote this game that's trying to be about doubt, in some way.

salient details if you don't want to read the 3.25 pages:

-3 to 5 players.
-about an hour, I think.
-we deal with the real doubt of someone at the table.
-a single, shared character.
-scene framing/"what is this scene about?"/"this scene is over"/"I'm the main character" -- these four responsibilities are split up and given to different players.
-ritualistic elements.
-roleplaying poem-ish.

If this sounds interesting to you, I'd be super grateful for any thoughts after you read it. I will playtest it soon. Thanks!

Here it is
