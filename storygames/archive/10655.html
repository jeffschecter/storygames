    \n Chances are good that my wife and I will be going to a Halloween party somewhere. If that doesn't happen, I have two options:

    1. Every year, my FLGS stays open late and hosts a midnight zombie-survival game using hacked Mordheim rules. They get about 20 players together, and each person is expected to bring 10 or so painted zombie mini's (Warhammer scale) and a hero to use in the game.

    2. I am thinking about getting a decent size group together and hosting an open-invite game of Shotgun Diaries.
