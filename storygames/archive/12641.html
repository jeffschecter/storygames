\n There have been two things I want to game lately, which are (as the title says) the themes and concepts of Inception and just some good old-fashioned Dungeon Crawling fun.

So I want to combine the two.

The concept is this; The king has either fallen into an unwaking sleep or some sort of waking-sleep of madness. You, as adventurers, are to enter the castle of his mind, fight your way to the throne room and put the crown back on his head. As you climb higher in the castle, you go deeper into his subconscious and things get stranger. Different areas are different parts of his mind, and so on.
