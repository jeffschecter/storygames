\n I've started a kickstarter project to publish three 16th Century city maps as posters. I have maps of London, Paris and Rome from the 1580's. They are really pretty. I will include a simple court intrigue Matrix game on the edge of the map. The London game will be about Shakespeare, the Paris game will be about the three musketeers. The Roman game will be about Cesare Borgia and his dad, the Pope.

I hope you will check out the project! Here is the URL http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1743621402/16th-century-city-map-matrix-game-posters

Chris Engle
