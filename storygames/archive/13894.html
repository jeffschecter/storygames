\n It's time to announce GnoccoCON III, the convention "Reggiana" of Modern Roleplaying.

For the third year Biasola, a little place near Reggio Emilia, will host us! For 2 days!

10th-11th September 2011

Many games, many fans to know and play with. Good food to taste.

Give yourself the "chizze" (a tipical local dish in the images). Let the lard and the game runs strong in you!

Here some pics of the previous editions:
- 2009 Edition
- 2010 Edition

For questions please refer to me or to Mr. Mario (he'll be soon here).

Also Mr. Graham Walmsley can tell you how was the CON last year (he was our International Guest). ^^
