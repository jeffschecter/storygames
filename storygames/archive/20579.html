    \n Surely you're familiar with The Extraordinary Adventures of Baron Munchausen, 1001 Nights or A Penny for My Toughts? Those are all examples of games with framing devices where the players play characters in both the frame story and the inner stories. There are others, but those came to mind immediately.

    It's not the most common thing out there, but it's not super rare, either.
