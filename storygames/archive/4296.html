\n Just posted this on my LJ:\n
I'm running a 5k race to raise funds for this year's Race for the Cure. One in eight women will be stricken with breast cancer in her lifetime. Chances are, you already know or have met someone affected by the disease.

Please join me by pledging in support of my participation in the Race or contributing to the 2007 Komen Oklahoma City Race for the Cure\xc2\xae. Your tax-deductible contribution will fund national breast cancer research as well as innovative outreach, screening and awareness programs for medically under-served communities in Central Oklahoma.

You can make a donation online, or if you would prefer, you can also send your tax-deductible contribution to the address listed below. Whatever you can give will help! I truly appreciate your support and will keep you posted on my progress.

Because I registered a bit late, I've got a modest goal of raising $125 dollars this year. I hope we can meet and surpass that goal quickly. Thank you so much for your time and support!

Susan G. Komen for the Cure
Central Oklahoma Affiliate
1900 N.W. Expressway, Suite R325
Oklahoma City, OK 73118
