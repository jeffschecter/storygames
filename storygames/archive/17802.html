    \n If you want just a reskin it's really easy.
    1. Rename all guns and modern weapons to ranged weapons and sorceries.
    2. Rename/refluff the playbooks: Gunlugger = Barbarian, Battlebabe = Swashbuckler, Angel = Priest, Skinner = Bard, Brainer = Warlock, Driver = Beast/Dragon Rider, Hardholder = Borderlands Baron etc.

    Done. I don't think anyone has written it up because it's really no bother at all.

    Alternatively there's Apocalypse D&D, the precursor to Dungeon World.

    Does that cover what you're looking for?
