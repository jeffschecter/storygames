\nEpisode 33 The Dresden Files RPG: Character Creation
\n
\nIn this episode we continue our series on the Dresden Files RPG from Evil Hat Productions. This week we cover character creation. We talk about the process for creating characters, how we decided on our characters, and how the GM takes the characters the players made and comes up with a game.
\n
\nIf you are interested in listening to a recording of people going through the character creation process, check out our friends over at The Walking Eye Podcast.
