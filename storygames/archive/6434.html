\n Thanks to a small design contest on the Forge, I find myself in possession of a mostly functioning RPG called Showdown. It's a two-player game, designed to be played in 60-90 minutes.

Here's the basic idea. The two players choose a genre then set up a duel between two characters. While playing through the duel, they simultaneously play through a series of flashbacks, developing the characters and their past. Eventually, one player wins and gets to choose which one will live and which will die.

I'd be really happy if someone could give this a try and then post about it. Thanks!

    \n Hey, everyone! I was playtesting Showdown tonight, and I realized that I left out a fairly significant rule.

    You may only use a Stance that has two Qualities written next to it.

    Therefore, at the beginning of the game, you can only use the +/-1 Stance.
