\n Hi there everyone.

I've gathered several of my small games into two collections -

"Duet" contains
\u2022 Temper (a game played on the edges of your life about working through your real-life anger), and
\u2022 Anna, the Miller's Daughter (a one-or-two player map-drawing story game about a girl in a storybook village).

Duet is a "Passerine" collection, which is to say that it pushes the edges of what I understand games to be,
and the spaces that they are played in. And then\u2026

"Baguette" contains
\u2022 Slammin' (a turn-taking story game about sex-choked hipster assholes and their annoying lives),
\u2022 Tower of Hats (a deep-head dungeon crawl that ignores SAN checks in favor of the real thing),
\u2022 Superhero (a rules-be-damned action extravaganza with many guns and even more bullets),
plus
\u2022 The Slammin Token Master's Guide (to make your game of Slammin' all the more hip and douchey),
\u2022 8 Slammin' character sheets (so you can get playing as soon as you receive this in the mail).

Baguette is a "Batshit" collection, which is to say that it's totally ridiculous - and though the games are, like, actual games
that are, like, actually fun and work good and so on, they're also founded on hilarious jokes poked right into the eyes and
hearts of games we know and love.

Here's the direct link to the store, which has the lovely new photos i took today.

Please leave questions, applause, thoughts, and NON SPOILER anecdotes below.

Once again, Joel, those would be NON SPOILER anecdotes and commentary.


Thank you!
