    \n Ok so we're now on v1.3, and I can see this is going to get very annoying for folks very quickly so, instead of posting about each and every revision, I'll simply say that SFBK is changing almost by the minute, so the best thing if anyone's interested is to check here every now and then. Already two of our playtesters have expressed an interest in either running and/or writing new scenarios, with new opportunities for those pesky delinquent aliens to annoy the Dean and tie up the already over-stretched Terran Watch. That means more stuff for Drunzi to steal, more electronic equipment for Zlith to fiddle with, and a host of powerful new sights, sounds and aromas to overwhelm Harok's super senses. Mind you, he's so ston- I mean, slightly, er, not all there most of the time, so it probably won't make much difference to him. So guys, join the party! The Alien House party, where, to quote Madness' Our House, 'There's always something happening and it's usually very loud'. Oh, and from the latest playtest, Kraag, the heavy lifting guy, got really angry at the unmanly pap pop that the juke box was pumping out so he aimed a punch at it, but rolled about 6DPs and so ended up destroying it completely, at which one of the other players gasped 'He's crushed Celine Dion!'
