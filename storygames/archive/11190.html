\n Hey folks,

For the first time in a long time I'm spending time at my parents house, which means I have a couple bookshelves worth of my old gaming stuff (mostly older D&D, Games Workshop and White Wolf stuff) that I really probably don't need to keep in my life. If anyone is interested in any of these books, whisper me or email me (n-dot-d-dot-paoletta-at-gmail-dot-com). They're free to good homes for only the cost of shipping! I will also entertain offers to trade if you are looking to divest of some of your materials (non-gaming materials are acceptable for trading purposes.)

Everything is in lovingly used condition unless otherwise noted (dinged corners, worn pages, etc).
