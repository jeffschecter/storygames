\n I'll be running BH on Saturday, June 23, at Go Play NW. Several people have expressed interest in playing, and also in preparing characters to have a custom scenario set up rather than the generic demo. This thread is for anyone who'd like to do that, so that we can plan this out.

So far I've heard from Ryan, Collin and Lukas. Anyone else, feel free to jump in. We can have several separate groups, and split off threads if necessary.

To start things off:
1) Do you guys all want to play together?
2) I need a play time estimate; prep will vary depending on whether we'll play for 2 hours, 4 hours, etc.
3) We can create characters together in here, throw ideas around, etc.

I'm okay with a bunch of players at once, as long as it's not over 4 per game. More players means more taking turns and more complex challenges (which is fine, but takes longer). For the purposes of a short one-shot game, we'll keep negotiation to a minimum and focus on letting you guys tear through a couple Conflict Resolution challenges.

The length of the game will very much depend on how many players are in each group, so we'll figure that out then. Anyone not planned for can still get a spontaneous demo, if time permits.

As for the characters, I'm thinking of having the characters return from earning their first tattoo at a Beast Hunters gathering, so you can create the characters according to the book, pick one of the four or five lower-level tattoos, and get 50 reward points to spend on your character. The basic character creation is also available on our web sitefor free, as the sample chapter from the book.
