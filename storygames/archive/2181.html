So, I discovered Bibliodyssey just before the holidays, and it is just fantastic.

It's a blog that is dedicated to posting images from old documents, mostly in the public domain, I believe. So many of them would be appropriate for so many kinds of games, from maps to portraits to weird animals.

Also, I'm sure so many of them would be good for the right game-in-development needing some graphic goodness...

Anyway, thought it would be a good resource that y'all may not know about.
