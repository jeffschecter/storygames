    Something that's more permanent & follow-able than hashtags is Lists.

    Find some awesome RPG folks. Find out what Lists they've created, and what Lists they follow.
    Follow those Lists.

    For example, Judd_of_Kryos has two Lists you might be interested in: Geek News, and Glorious Geeks. Both here.
