    \n Perfect is the enemy of done.

    Sketch large, broad strokes. Fill in the details as you have time.

    Don't forget the basics: food, water, sleep.

    Music helps me be creative. Sometimes, a change of scenery does, too. A shower, a short walk, ten minutes on DDR.

    No one cares how good it looks if the game sucks.

    Character sheets help solidify a lot of things.
