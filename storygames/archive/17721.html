L.A. area gamers, I'm pleased to announce The Indie RPG Project, a Meetup focused on the world of indie roleplaying games. Once we go live, in January of 2013, we'll have monthly events scheduled, but it'll also be a network of local gamers interested in indie rpgs. Stayed tuned for announcements and updates. To learn more about The Indie RPG Project, visit our site at...
