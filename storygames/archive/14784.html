    \n I used to loath eating at Gencon, filling my backpack with meals on the go, but I've found the right mix of edible joy to fuel my weekend of gaming rapture. And being vegetarian and having many vegan friends, not just any place will do.
    \n
    \nCurrito
    \nCircle Center Mall, 49 West Maryland St
    \nBurritos: cheap, quick, healthy, filling, and close by.
    \n
    \nPatachou
    \n225 West Washington St
    \nMy absolute favorite place for breakfast. They close around 3pm so no dinner :-(
    \n
    \nNoodles & Co
    \n121 West Maryland St
    \nJapanese, Mediterranean, and American style Noodles. Fast, large variety, reasonably priced (in the $7 range) and during Gencon open till 3am!
    \n
    \nIndian Garden Restaurant
    \n207 North Delaware St
    \nPretty good North Indian food (not my favorite Indian place but I'm spoiled with my amazing local options). Slightly pricey.
    \n
    \nAdobo Grill
    \n110 East Washington St
    \nSlightly pricey but worth it! Some of the best Mexican desserts and Guacamole I've had outside of the west coast. I was very surprised. I can't even begin to describe the Chocolate tamal. But after finishing it I ordered a second one.
