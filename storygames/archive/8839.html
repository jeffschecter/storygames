\n Heya,

I've managed to streamline, reorganize, and otherwise improve the Anima Prime beta document in time for Gamestorm. In case you haven't encountered AP yet, it's an anime-inspired, group-focused (as opposed to Beast Hunters' 2-player focus), lighthearted, spontaneous game that I initially wrote as an ode to Final Fantasy X, growing out of the core of Beast Hunters. It's become quite something else (and much more) since then, and now the new version is even better and includes sample enemies, the complete new skill system, and more. I've taken out a few things that weren't ready and will leave those for later (or for a supplement). Since the whole thing is Creative Commons licensed, i.e. freely available even after I publish it, I feel pretty good about that approach. Here it is:

Anima Prime Beta 1.2

You can also go to AnimaPrimeRPG.com to get it. That's where the whole text will eventually be available in html format. Actually, it is now, but it's the last version; I'll have to spend some considerable amount of time updating all of those pages, so please just rely on the PDF for now.

Anyway, there you go, enjoy!
