\n So, there's this really cool road trip story that some of you might have heard of.
It's called Firefly.

And there's this new road trip story game that I recently published.
It's called Ribbon Drive.

However, Firefly is an action adventure serial that takes place in space and rotates between a few distinct sets (space, alliance sets, western sets). It has action sequences, and when characters fail they get trapped.

And so I hacked my own game, to best support recreating Firefly.
If the published Serenity game doesn't do it for you (because it's crunchy and has a heavily-defined set of rules and setting info), and you're looking for something more evocative and less definitive, then you'll likely be interested in Radion-Accelerator Drive.

In tangentially-related news, I signed up to twitter just so I could tweet the news to Nathan Fillion.
You can follow me at @McDaldno, though I doubt I'll use it much.
