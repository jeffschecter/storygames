\n I just put my Twin Peaks-inspired story game Tall Pines up on Kickstarter! Take a look and see what you think:

Tall Pines: A Surreal Murder Mystery Roleplaying Game

In Tall Pines you collaboratively build a murder mystery using prompts and weird metaphorical symbols from several decks of cards. In game nerd jargon it's a freeform, no prep, GM-less, one-shot game that accommodates 3 to 6 players with a play time of about 30 minutes per player.

There's a bunch more details and an actual play video on the Kickstarter page. Hit me up here or over there if you have any questions. I am, as you might imagine, super excited to talk about my game!
