    \n Admittedly, I did pop into this thread entirely with the idea of checking out get that I might be able to modify for minis use!

    I didn't really have too much good stuff to suggest, simply because I've been tempted to go the other direction of this for those sorts of minis using games: have simple books that can be read, digested, then never really referenced in play, and have no character sheets at all.

    The closest I've managed that was using Mythic Gm Emulator for a game and just using the quick reference sheets. Technically, MGME does have character sheets, and we had them available, but about ten minutes into play, my buddy and I dropped the character sheets entirely as largely unnecessary for our one-shot scenario.

    I do have a number of minis games that you can basically get away with a single 3x5 note card, but they didn't have any rules on them, and usually cheat sheets of some sort were needed or at least desirable.

    Edited to add:
    I do know of one minis skirmish game that has a synopsis/cheat sheet for the bulk of the combat rules on a movement/range band tool that is only slightly larger than a 3x 5 card ( Songs of Blades and Heroes, although it can be used for most Ganesha Games skirmish games, because most of them use the same core system).
