    \n Well, if the death is accidental, there are only a few scenarios that come to mind. One doesn't accidentally get a belt around one's neck in normal situations. So that leaves autoerotic asphyxiation, as Logos7 suggested, and fooling around with belts (perhaps for a movie scene about hanging or choking, is all I can imagine).

    The simpler answer is suicide. Suicide is often (usually!) tragic and inexplicable.

    I read that Carradine had faced problems with drugs and depression as recently as a few years ago, but reportedly he had overcome those and was doing well. He was in Bangkok to shoot a film, so he had something to look forward to, but who knows what he was really going through.
