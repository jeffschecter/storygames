\n I want to make trifold playbooks for Dogs in the Vineyard. I'm looking for help both on the art / layout front, and for brainstorming elements to put on them. I'm thinking the 5 backgrounds map to the 5 playbooks in this list - Man of the Faith, Woman of the Faith, The Scholar, The Convert, The Runaway. Circle choices for name, eyes, body. Choose from examples or create your own for Traits, Items, Relationships. Traits should mostly be specific and unique to playbook, but relationships could have some overlap to create a more connected group.

The playbooks could even come with different snippets of setting material to help teach / sell the setting. (Convert talks about back east and mountain folk, Woman of the Faith talks about gender roles / expectations, etc...)

I think this could really help Dogs games hit the ground running - any help making this happen is appreciated!
