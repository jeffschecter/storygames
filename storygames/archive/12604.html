    \n I've played GC online only once, but I can say that if I'm ever to play again I would look into a convenient way of drawing maps together. We used Skype + OpenRPG and while the map drawing kinda worked in OpenRPG it was a bit of a mess. Things that could help would be pre-prepared stuff such as player character tokens etc.

    You could also make the map drawing collaborative by encouraging players to search the web for suitable images to use in the map - floorplans, tokens for different things. That could be really cool with the right set of players (able to find stuff quickly, not concentrating only on the searching part).
