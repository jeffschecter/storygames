Who's going to Solmukohta? (Knutepunkt)

\n
\n
\n
\n\nMatthijs \n\n\n
\n
\n\nJanuary 2012 \n edited January 2012 in Story Games
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n This year, Solmukohta/Knutepunkt is in Finland. It's in April. It is probably different from most cons you've been to. Personally, I think it's the nexus of roleplaying as an art form, and a good place to get drunk, meet amazing people and be incredibly jealous of what they're doing with their roleplay.

Are you going?

    \n A guy who's organizing it has asked Finnish game designers to run their games as part of the pre-event "Week in Finland". I see that the event is in Helsinki, so I guess it's not that far-fetched to be in town in early April. Not really my scene, though - I often feel personally frustrated by these people and their games, which would be an entirely unfair attitude to bring to the event. In any case, the actual Solmukohta is definitely too strong a brew for me. I hear only praise from the people who have actually been to Solmukohta, though, and this is certainly an unique roleplaying culture, so it's easy to recommend for anybody interested, even people coming from further away than Nordic countries.
