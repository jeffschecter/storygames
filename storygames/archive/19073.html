\n I've bene readin the GSS pdf thinking...there needs to be more critters.

What about Spiders, Snakes, Frogs, Crickets, etc.

I was thinking about creepier, but still helpful animal Henge. Like the spooky spider girl who lives in the attic who watches over the little boy who has trouble with letters. (Yes ripping off Charolette's Web)

Or the Frog Boy who's always sticky but saves kids from the river and loves to play with them and go fishing.

The lucky Cricket Kid who sings pretty songs and helps out those down on their fortune?

etc. Has anyone homebrewed their own critter kids?
