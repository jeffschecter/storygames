\n I'll be frank - the turnout for AP Week isn't as great as we had hoped. We need more reviews. So I turn to the Story-Games community to help make that happen. Many of you have played dozens of story-games, and I'm sure you have thoughts on them. Take a little while, write up a review (short or long), and submit it for our event. The small press RPG community thrives on feedback and fan-based promotion, and this is your chance to help out.

Here's the event post.
