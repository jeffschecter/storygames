\n As many of you know, I'm one of the RPG coordinators for the Strategicons, a series of three conventions held each year in Los Angeles. I've been putting the schedule together (event submissions close tomorrow), and I just realized how awesome it looks. For reference, these are all of the different game systems we have scheduled:

7th Sea
Battlestations
Buffy the Vampire Slayer
Burning Wheel
Call of Cthulhu
Classic Dungeons & Dragons
Crimson Cutlass
Cthulhu Live

Grey Ranks
GURPS 3e
Hero System
HeroQuest

InSpectres
It Was A Mutual Decision

Not Poker

Polaris

Sensical Roleplaying

Stargate SG-1
Stranglehold!
The Adventures of Baron Munchausen
The Shab-al-Hiri Roach
Toon
Tunnels & Trolls
unWritten
World of Darkness

Keep in mind, this is a con that two years ago was (almost) nothing but GURPS and Call of Cthulhu. So, if you're in the LA area over Labor Day weekend, come on out. I'll also be running an IPR booth in the dealer's room. If you're not in LA, give some serious thought to coming out over President's Day Weekend next February.

--Paul
