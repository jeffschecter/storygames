    Posted By: hoogwhy don't you make players pull a number of blocks equal to ( 1 + number of players who have been removed from the game so far ) ?
    \nThere is a similar rule in the game. I think it is hinted at somewhere in the podcast. Off-hand, I can't remember the exact rule--which is a bit ridiculous on my part--but it is something like pulling three blocks for every player you have less than five. These get pulled even at the very beginning of the game, before you start playing, if you are playing with four or less players. The pulls are live if they happen during the game after the tower has fallen, which means another character can bite it if folks aren't careful.\n
    Posted By: johnstoneI don't get the vegan position on honey. What's up with that?
    \nI find that veganism is more about where you draw the line than anything else--and often those lines are very, very thin indeed. In broad strokes, it is easy to nail down. Vegans don't eat meat, eggs, or dairy products. They don't wear leather or fur. But things like wool and honey muddy the waters. There are some arguments about how the bees are mistreated, but to be honest, I'm not swayed by them. Largely because bees are insects and I've treated other insects far worse (I'm looking at your mosquitoes). And because the trucks that deliver my potatoes and kale probably kill more bees en route than beekeepers do. And because the orange juice I drink is made possible by the very same bees making the honey I'm not purchasing. The whole topic is just as confusing to me as it is to you, but in the end I was never a big fan of honey, so it isn't that hard for me to just avoid it.\n
