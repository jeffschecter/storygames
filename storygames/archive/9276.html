    \n I read through heaps of blogs, generally link surfing between them.

    One of the regulars is Tabletop Manifesto, but I also like keeping up to date with what Jason Godesky is doing with his Fifth World, and a few other individual game designers to see where their game ideas are heading.

    My own blog is Observations of the Fox, where I post comments on gaming and general observations about philosophy and other matters of interest to me. I've been working through a "Game Mechani(sm) of the week" series since the beginning of the year, where I pose a simple game mechanism and how it could be used to reflect a genre, a theme or something a bit different within the context of roleplaying. It also gets used as a platform for discussing other ideas I have about roleplaying.
