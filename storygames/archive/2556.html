\n As a Sacramento local, it's my duty to point out our little game convention. Conquest Sac runs April 13th through the 15th, and was a fun little con last year.

http://www.avalonconventions.com/conquestsac/

I just noticed that the gaming schedule is up. In an effort to pimp out indie gaming as much as possible, I'll be running a couple games:

Friday, April 13th, 8:00pm - 12:00 midnight
"River City Supers versus Mechator!"
Truth & Justice
In a world crammed with super-villains, disaster can strike anywhere! Sacramento's no exception, and while the heroes of the city are underdogs, they'll show the world that they're ready to take on anything -- even an insane robotic overlord!

Saturday, April 14th, 6:00pm - 10:00pm
"Revenge of the Gorilla King!"
Spirit of the Century
During the Great War, you and your friends defeated the great villain, Gorilla Khan. Now he has come for his revenge! He has stolen the Countdown Device: a machine that could undo time itself! Can you stop him before he erases you from history?

I'm looking forward to seeing some S-Gers at the con!
