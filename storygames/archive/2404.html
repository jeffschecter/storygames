Indie Games Explosion and Gen Con 07

Kat Miller
February 2007 edited February 2007 in Meetups / Conventions
Gen Con has opened its registration sunday.

Now is a good time to start talking the Indie Games Explosion for Gen Con07.
Last Year The IGE offered Nearly 35 Game Events, not counting the Games on Demand Tables.
It made a difference in sales and helped the indie comunity as whole.
This year lets Explode with even more registered events!

For the Games on Demand Events I'd love to offer: 5 tables running 12 hours with 2 Table Custodians per 4 hour shift.
I'll need volenteers for this to happen.

I have a Live Journal set up for submissions and suggestions:
http://community.livejournal.com/ige/

and a New page Dedicated to the IGE:
http://www.enter.net/~whim/ige.html

Running games and taking shifts of table Custodian can get you a Free 4 day GM Badge.

-kat Miller
