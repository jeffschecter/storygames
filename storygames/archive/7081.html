    \n I pre-ordered the LE version and had the pdf made available to me. I just love the game and can't wait to run my first game but to answer your question IMHO it makes a much better campaign game than it does a one shot. The use of season actions (which allow you to create works of art, improve your lands, build additions to your castles etc) really only work in a long term game.

    \n You could run it as a one-shot, but only with a lot of prep from the Narrator. She's have to come up with all the characters and the players would have to be familiar with the setting. The preview PDF does a good job of that. If you understand that, you could probably run a quick (4-6 hour) game.
