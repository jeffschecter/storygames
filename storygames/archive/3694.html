\n So, while I'm waiting for art to trickle in for Sufficiently Advanced (and slaving away teaching summer school), I decided I needed a good creative outlet. So I wrote The Princess Game.

The Princess Game is a game about a little girl - the only little girl in the whole wide world. The only person there is, really. It's a game about her dreams and her imagination, about make-believe and magic, and the world of stories. But mostly it\xe2\u20ac\u2122s about the girl.

You play different parts of her \xe2\u20ac\u201d her love, her fear, her curiosity, and more. Together with her Imagination, you go out to see what there is in the world, and invent some of it yourself.

Includes screen-ready, ink-friendly, and booklet-style layouts. Just sixteen pages long. It's for sale at RPGNow (and other places Real Soon Now) for three dollars.
