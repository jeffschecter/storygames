ENWorld Chicago Gameday XVI is Feb 24th - Now with added indie goodness!

buzz
January 2007 edited January 2007 in Meetups / Conventions
ENWorld Chicago Gameday is back once again. Feb 24th, from 9:30am until 9pm, at Games Plus in Mt. Prospect, IL.

The ENWorld sponsorship might make those interested in story games hesitant, but this time around we've got:

Agon
Spirit of the Century
Don't Rest Your Head
...and Ars Magica for those of you kckin' it old-story-school.

And, of course, the mainstream stuff, including D&D 3.5, Moldvay Basic D&D, M&M2e, Iron Heroes, Call of Cthulhu, and some 1st edition Gamma World.

If this piques your interest, go to the sign-up thread on ENWorld and reserve yourself a seat.

Cheers!
