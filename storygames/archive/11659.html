\n Hi, folks!

So this weekend I'm going to be going to a mini-con/reunion of the old gaming group from Ohio. I've agreed to run Misery Bubblegum for them, since they've been curious. I played the heck out of this with Tony at GenCon in 2008 and 2009, so I feel pretty okay having read through the rules as well as Tony Dowler's guide and the Story Games Codex revised rules. However, I just have two questions on things I'm a bit fuzzier on...

1) The GM gets cards to play, but how exactly do they play them? Do they create a pool of NPCs who can use these cards? Or is it better to create one NPC affiliated with the PCs and use them to play the cards?

2) Forces - forcing people to say yes/no/flee to paired questions. I'd like to have a good list of examples for my players. Does anyone have any good examples of brutal forces?

Thanks!
