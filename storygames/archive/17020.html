\n Hello Burners!
Registration for Burning Con 2012 is open!
October 26-28th.
Weekend registration is $50. Daily is $30. Beginners add $10.
http://www.burningwheel.org/registration/

Burning Con will be hosted by Luke Crane, Vincent Baker and John Harper in New York City on October 26-28.

The event space is once again at 440 Studios (but no 4th floor rooms!).

The Friday party space will be held in a beautiful Soho loft a few blocks from the event space.

Sunday afterparty space will be at Bar 82 on Second Ave at 8th Street.

Play Games, Run a Game
As is tradition, all attendees will run a game. Even beginners! You will be contacted after registration by Chief Enforcer Shervyn (on loan from PAX!) who will collect information about the game you wish to run. Ignore him at your peril.

We have some new registration developments this year: single days and beginners track!

Single Day Schedules
You can register for single days. Single day registration is going to make our lives hell, but we want to make your Burning Con the best it can be. We're also running a Beginner Track for the first time.

Saturday: Play two games, run one.
Sunday: Play one game, run one.

Burning Wheel Beginner Track
The Beginner Track is for Burning Wheel only.

Saturday Morning Session
GM tips, tricks and training. I'll personally walk you through a discussion of Burning Wheel and effective GMing skills. Bring scenario ideas and I'll critique them.

Afternoon Session
We'll break into groups and run The Sword for each other. We'll stop play and discuss our decisions as we go along.

Evening Session
Go play! Get first priority and play in a game during this session

Sunday Morning Debut
Your debut! Run a full four hour session (of your critiqued scenario) of Burning Wheel for sleepy con-goers!

Sunday Afternoon Session
Play more!
Beginner's Track requirements: You must be a beginner! If you're not, I'll kick you out into the regular gaming track.

Games
Burning Con is a curated event. As such, acceptable games are limited to those authored by the hosts. This year the list of approved games is: Burning Wheel, Burning Empires, Mouse Guard, FreeMarket, Kill Puppies for Satan, Otherkind, Dogs in the Vineyard, Poisoned, In Wicked Age, Rock of Tahmaat, Apocalypse World, Murderous Ghosts, Agon, Lady Blackbird and Danger Patrol.

Short Form Slots
We will incorporate 2-hour slots into the schedule so you can get the most out of Lady Blackbird, Danger Patrol, Kill Puppies or challenge yourself to run a tight session of Mouse Guard.

Hacks
We work very hard designing our games for your enjoyment. We'd like to see you enjoy them in their pure form, but we know some of you are hackers. If you'd like to run your hack or variant of any of our titles, you must submit your materials in the form of rules, handouts and characters sheets to me 30 days before the con. The hosts will review your materials and either approve them or ask you to run the straight version of the game. If you do not submit hack materials for review, you will be asked to run the straight version of the game (and I will sit in your game and complain if you don't).

Refunds
I will refund registration and move games due to scheduling conflicts up to two weeks before the con (Oct 12th). After October 12th I will not refund registration and will not acknowledge schedule change requests.
