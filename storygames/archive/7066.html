Or if Black Metal is more your cup of tea, there's always Skaldic Curse: http://www.myspace.com/skaldiccurse

David illustrates children's books and has promised to sneak some dinosaurs in to his Defiance illustrations (if I'm not paying attention one may even be riding a tricycle).
