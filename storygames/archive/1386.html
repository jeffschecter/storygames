Chicken Master

Jason_Morningstar
September 2006 edited September 2006 in Story Games
I have gotten into the habit of sifting through our paper recycling bin every time I take out the trash - we live among international grad students who tend to throw out stuff like, say, stacks of MUFFIN, an insane Japanese lifestyle and fashion magazine, or marked-up issues of Am. J. Pathology, or whatever. You just never know.

Today I found a board game.

It is called Chicken Master and it is the best thing I have ever found, ever. I should point out that a lot of care went into Chicken Master - the playing pieces came in a meticulously crafted envelope wrapped in a tractor-feed pull-off ribbon. I welcome ideas on how this game is supposed to be played - the fact that there is a DEATH playing piece intrigues and thrills me.
