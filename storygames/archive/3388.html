\n For those who haven't seen it, today's Order of the Stick.

(For those who've never read it, this is not the place to start. Go back to the beginning.)

Has anyone seen Insult of Opportunity type actions in social combat? You know, when someone else has already scored a point against your foe, and you, without spending an action, chime in with "Yeah, dumbass," just to piss him off? Because if not, I might have to write one.

    \n Burning Wheel has this, during the Duel of Wits. You can add helping dice to one of the primary duelists, but only if you chime in with something appropriate to the skill you're using to help. Using the skill Ugly Truth would be a "Yeah, Dumbass" comment for sure.
