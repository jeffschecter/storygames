Dread: The First Book of Pandemonium art preview

Rafael
October 2006 edited October 2006 in Directed Promotion
The artwork for the revised edition of Dread: The First Book of Pandemonium is starting to trickle in. I've got a small group of artists working on the project this time.

For each illustration, I sent a lengthy description of the demon, along with a brief outline of its modus operandi (possession, consumption of human flesh, yada yada), and reference material where appropriate. I was really happy with the way these turned out.
