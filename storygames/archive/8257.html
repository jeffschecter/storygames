\n Suddenly, I find that I'm nearing completion of a prototype for my next crime RPG, which currently has the working title of Dirty Cities. At this point, I think that I'm going to use an Oracle system to generate situation content. But what should it be?

One way I've described my design goal for this game is that I want to make an RPG that would let me play The Wire. So, fellow Story Gamers, I ask for your assistance. If you were going to make an Oracle for The Wire, what elements would you want to see on it?

If I use your idea, I'll be sure to put you in the credits.

Thanks for your help!

Seth Ben-Ezra
Great Wolf

    \n ...a contract killer, who sources claim was trying to finish his last job owed to the syndicate...

    ...a young prostitute who was witness to the events in question, but who was reticent to co-operate...

    ...creating a situation in the local area where two gangs are converging on the same turf...

    ...the undercover agent failed to report several times, but is known to still be operating the sting...

    ...our insider is a minor soldier in the organized crime syndicate...

    ...the witness to the event was killed as discussed in case #418-CF...

    ...the Capo is offering to turn State's Evidence if we can guarantee one thing...

    ...is being held in a safe house in town (disguised as a pawn shop)...

    ...escaped from custody and fled to a brothel in the red light district...

    ...shots fired, ended with one innocent bystander dead, and their child wounded...

    ...seems like some sort of revenge plot against the detective in the case...

    ...closed file on this may need to be reopened. Suggest we contact the witness from that file...

    ...squad car was fired upon by gang-banger while on routine patrol...

    ...the killings seem to have pattern that points to a male with the attached abnormal psych profile...

    ...the factory worker's union foreman seems to have become involved...

    ...the suspect has been sighted near the strip of bars in that region...

    ...these economically distressed areas, according to the witness, "are a powerkeg - with those hooligans running loose"...

    These sorta what you are looking for in tone, etc?
