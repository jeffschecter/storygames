\n What was originally envisioned at a weekend-long camping/gaming extravaganza at beautiful Chewacla State Park has reduced down to a day in my thift-store moderne living room in reasonably attractive Opelika, Alabama.

In addition to air conditioning and no mosquitoes, we'll have at least two tables of gaming, pot-luck refreshments, possibly some intermittent live music, and if requested, impromptu workshops on character illustration and cartography.

If you are interested in attending, please whisper your contact information and I'll email directions and my phone number.
