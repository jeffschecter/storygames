    \n I'd like to try out a play aid I made for In a Wicked Age when we get together this weekend.

    I came up with an idea for mapping Best Interests to help focus play preparation. I thought it might help the players and GM get a quick visual on possible central conflicts.

    You can download the Best Interest worksheet here:

    To comment or discuss the sheet, please visit the post on Vincent's forum at the forge:
