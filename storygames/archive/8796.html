\n I just started watching Season 1 of MacGuyver on Netflix and I realized the plot summaries are like a really bad/awesome oracle. Here is a taste:\n
    \n
  • In far-off Budapest, MacGyver arranges to meet a Russian double agent who holds some very sensitive information. When the agent ends up dead and the information goes missing, MacGyver enlists the aid of Gypsies to retrieve it.
    • \n
  • When an American photographer snaps photos of a Latin American dictator with an international arms dealer, her life is suddenly on the line, and it's up to MacGyver to get her safely across the border and out of harm's way.
    • \n
  • A larcenous casino owner makes off with a fortune in diamonds intended for a charity auction, and MacGyver's got a plan to get them back -- with a little help from a senator's daughter.
    • \n
  • MacGyver gets more than he bargained for when he plays protector to a man who intends to testify against his mob-connected brother. It turns out that the witness's mother is dying, and he wants MacGyver to help him reach her before it's too late.
    • \n
  • When a mad bomber targets a luxury cruise ship, MacGyver teams up with fellow Vietnam vet Charlie Robinson to save the day. But are Mac's skills any match for a madman who's planted multiple explosives?
    • \n
  • An ill-fated visit to an old professor leaves MacGyver looking after Kate, a teenage hacker with inside information about a certain missile defense system. Now, she's wanted for questioning by the feds and, worse, by some unsavory arms dealers.
    • \n
