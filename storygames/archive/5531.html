\n Hello,

If you're working, or worked, on a game which features both Competition and Story, I'd love for you to write an article about how those two things interact and relate to one another in your game(s). If you also have an end point in your game, a "victory point", I'd also love to hear about that.

If you'd write a short piece or even a long article on the matter I'd love to post it up on my Competitive RPGs Blog, perhaps with some editorial notes or comments.

I am doing this because I came to realise it is lacking in the way of concrete design advice, and I figure the best way to do that right now is by way of example; of making an example case from a design solution/method and from generalizing it.

I will send PMs to certain people to ensure they see this thread and email people who don't frequent Story-Games, if you know such people, please forward this request to them and/or forward me their emails. My email is tundranocaps@gmail.com
