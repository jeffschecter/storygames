\n Salve, citizens!
I'm trying to make a game for Game Chef '09, and I want to start playtesting what I've got so far, to give me further direction in design. If anyone is interested, please let me know by whatever means you wish.

The game itself is a fast-and-loose, possibly somewhat fantastical, historical-fiction game, set at least ostensibly in post-Norman Invasion England. It centers around clan politics and the ironclad law of the Norman conquerors. The mechanics derive from Otherkind dice, with a few flourishes I've devised in order to focus play on the squabbling, allegiances, and rivalries of the clans, plus some stuff in there to focus on life under tyranny.

Thanks!
-- Zac
