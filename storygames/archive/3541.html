    \n So, we have a quick pick-up fighty game. As you gain levels in (stuff), you make stacks of Pogs for your different aptitudes - like a STR stack and a DEX stack and so on. When you want to do something awesome, you throw your slammer at your own stack, and get a number of successes equivalent to the caps flipped over; but, those flipped-over caps stay flipped over until you take a turn to restack your used up caps. When you do attacks, you throw your slammer at the opponent's attribute which you're trying to damage. (Kinda the same thing.)

    To add more powers, you shuffle in Special Pogs (TM) to your attribute stacks, and these are triggered and activated when they get flipped over. So I have "Counter: Parry-Riposte" and "Stunt: Huge Lifting!" special pogs on my STR stack. If an opponent attacks my stack and reveals the "Counter Attack" pog, I get a free attack on them; if I'm trying to do some major strength stunt and flip my "Stunt!" pog, I suceed and get to narrate an impressive feats.
