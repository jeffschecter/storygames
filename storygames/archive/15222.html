\n I have the opportunity to head over to the States to visit some friends in New York and Boston early-ish next year. I'll be spending a little bit of time with them, but also travelling around a bit. My provisional travel plans are:

- moving between New York, Boston and Chicago (with the possibility of heading down to Florida)
- I'll probably spend a month and a half in the States, sometime between March to June 2012

It would be cool to get some gaming in while I'm over there, and I was wondering if anyone could give me a general sense of what's happening around that part of the country during those times. The Story Games calendar doesn't have events posted for that far ahead yet. I'm interested in cons, mini-cons, and the legendary Beach Parties and Camp Nerdlys I've been reading about.

I know there's NerdNYC, and possibly Recess during that time.

I think there's a Story Games Boston group, but I don't know how active they are.

Maybe there'll be a Jiffycon.

I don't know what the story gaming situation is like in Chicago (although I think there's a regular meetup at the Dice Dojo).


Thanks for any info you can give me - I have to make a decision about this in the next 48 hours and it's pretty exciting!
