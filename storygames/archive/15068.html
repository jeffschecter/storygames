    \n Sounds like the cards would be interesting. I like the idea of indirect strategies.

    So many games come with build in optimal strategies. If you play them long enough you get good at min maxing. This can be fun but it isn't like life. From what I've gathered by living is that most of us are dealt sub-optimal hands, given no clear goals to achieve, and have to work very creatively to accomplish anything. Indirect approaches are often the only ones available because we lack the resources to be direct. And when we have the resources we find that the direct approaches often don't work anyway.

    The Vietnam war comes to mind...

    Chris Engle
