    \nWhat is doesn't mention is the narrative elements of the game: the nature of the Understar and the crime the Exile committed are sketched out at the beginning of the game, and are expanded upon in the same way as the backstory in Summerland (my other game). Play revolves around confronting your crimes and expanding on the true nature of the Understar as a route to redemption, at the risk of psychosis as the link between the Exiled mind and the Host breaks down.
