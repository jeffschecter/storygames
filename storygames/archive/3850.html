What games do you regret not buying?

I'll start by mentioning The Mountain Witch. I wish I bought this at GenCon. I ended up buying it in PDF just to get it, and that so totally isn't satisfying. I'm looking forward to getting my hands on it next week. But, for now, that's one game I regret not buying (in hardcopy).

What about you?

    \n Well, to this day i continue to hunt for a copy of Shattered Sky. It was near the end of Gen Con, and i was running low on funds, and had to judge what to buy, and what would be available later (i.e., through distribution/retail). I judged wrong--more than half the stuff i bought that year showed up in the store within a few months; Shattered Sky never did.

    I really wish i'd gotten Paladin in hardcopy. While there've been several other small-press games over the years that i failed to buy when i had the chance, and now can't, none of them are really big regrets--as evidenced by the fact that they don't come immediately to mind.

    Luckily, i've mostly judged right on which other things to snag, and, at least within my funding limitations, gotten the stuff i wanted, including a whole bunch of games that have since turned out to be hard to find. So the other regrets are simply running out of money, and thus not being able to buy everything i want, rather than specific regrets about specific games missed--plus, most of them are still available, so, at some point...

    Oh, and why all the regret over OtE? Did it go out ofprint when i wasn't watching?
