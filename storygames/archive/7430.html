\n I'd like to play a multiplayer online game of How to Host a Dungeon in the vein of the Dwarf Fortress online games.

Here's how it works. One of us starts a game of How to Host a Dungeon, making an electronic version of the map.
After a turn or two, they write a short in-character summary (maybe in the voice of a historian or achivist?) and pass the map on to the next player in the list.
Either it devolved into total chaos, or we get a dungeon. Either way, everyone wins!

Let's talk about how to do it here, and if there's enough interest, we can think about moving the actual game to another forum.
