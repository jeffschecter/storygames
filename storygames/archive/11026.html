Hey, folks, the Game Design Studio has just opened its doors up again. If you've got a game text that needs serious, focused critique, something you've developed to the greatest degree you can without outside crit, then please come by and trade help with others.

\n

The first open crit went quite well, I think. There was no bullshit and everyone got good feedback from somebody. I look forward to seeing how things have progressed and what new stuff you're working on.

\n

Please keep in mind that the Studio will again close its doors in a couple of weeks. The earlier you get in, the more likely you'll get good crit.
