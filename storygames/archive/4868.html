    \n Once you're logged in with your admin account, when you've got the thread open, there's a list of things on the upper left hand side:

    * Bookmark this discussion
    * Delete this discussion
    * Close this discussion
    * Make this discussion sticky
    * Sink this discussion

    It's right under "Start a new discussion."

    ETA: Unless you've added things like the who's online window and the likes, then it's further down. It's the Options category.
