    Hello, Tad! A fellow Iowan. Glad to hear from you. I live near Des Moines, and rarely make it to the Sioux City area.

    There is very little information regarding Dust Devils Revenged on my website. I just revamped the site and have ported over very little information to date.

    Revenged will be available before the end of the year. It keeps getting delayed, unfortunately, but I believe it will be well worth the wait. I'd be happy to answer any questions about the game, if you like.

    Dust Devils is among the "grandpas" of the current indie gaming scene, and was voted Indie Game of the Year for 2002. It's a straight-shooting Western game that captures the grit and drama of the Western films. Think of it as the unofficial Unforgiven RPG. If your'e not familiar with Unforgiven (and you should be -- what a movie!) there are many other film influences besides. You pretty much can't go wrong if you think "Clint Eastwood."

    The game uses poker mechanics for resolution and emphasizes each character's personal "Devil." This is some ugly history or vice that troubles the characters personality and colors all his big choices in the game.

    Revenged will be a much more attractively bound collection, including a completely rewritten and revised rule set from the 2002 original.
