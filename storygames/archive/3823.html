\n Hi folks,
Two of the three design journals for the licensed new edition of Og: Unearthed Edition by author Robin D. Laws are up at Firefly-Games.com. The first covers the goals & parameters of redesigning Og, which Robin & I consider a 'prehistoric' indie game, and the second - just posted - goes into updating the combat rules & bestiary, plus tweaking the lethality. The third journal goes up next Monday.

So those might be of interest to folks.
