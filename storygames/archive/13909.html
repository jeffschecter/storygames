    \n Interesting thought, no one's mentioned that before.

    Let me think about this. I figure I can do something where we keep people in the loop through mail and phone calls, at a cost of about $5.00 per year. That would be the cost to go out of our way to reach out to folks.

    Currently the model is: "We post, you draw to us." The reverse, which is all "WE draw you to us" (through calls, mail etc) is common in other meets like religious retreats, doctor meetups etc. But since there's nothing real at stake, we'd probably ask for $5.00 in order to drum up enough care to go out of our way to call and mail you. It would be a tiny bit like a chore, but it's certainly a chore that we'd be happy to do for you, at a small fee for the person responsible for that chore. I think that $5.00 or so is reasonable for such a thing if someone wanted all direct communication and avoidance of the internet entirely.
