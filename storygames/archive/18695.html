\n While we can't attend Gen Con Indy 2013 in person, All Rolled Up will be available at the event from Chronicle City.

Gen Con Indy 2013 All Rolled Up will each have a themed charm, like these ones - so, if you're shopping around for something to hold all your gaming gear and dice, then these'll be the ones for you.





You can find Chronicle City in the Exhibit Hall at Booth 1751 - far right side of the Exhibit Hall and toward the back.

    \n Velcro, yes.

    I'd probably get one if I didn't have a similar bag already, some sort of toiletry affair I got from somewhere. Been using it for the last decade - it makes a lot of sense for a certain profile of gaming equipment to be put into a wrap-around parcel such as this. I went through 2003-2008 playing just about everything off a bundle and rule texts; I generally had everything one needed for those pesky Forge games in my bundle, whether it be cards, dice, tokens or ice cubes (well, not ice cubes - admittedly Jonathan made that game a bit too difficult to prep for).

    Interestingly I found with D&D over the last two years that the bundle doesn't cut it: too many adventure modules, rules references and such. An appropriately sized cardboard box has been my weapon of choice for that; not that good for long distance carrying, but easy enough to take to the gaming place by car. Enough room to lug e.g. all Fight On! issues and whatever else.

    \n Lisa - Glad to here that you picked up some All Rolled Ups at Gen Con. I'm pleased to report that you can use Crayola Dry Erase Crayons <-- Amazon link. We picked up a packet at the weekend on spec and tested them out on some chalkcloth. You get a nice clear line, the stuff doesn't rub off easily by chance, and it came off easily - without staining or marking the material - after sitting out for 24 hours. No chalk residue, and relatively cheap with plenty of colour options.
