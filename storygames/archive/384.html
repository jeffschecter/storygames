    I think it depends how much of the ruleset the rewrite changes.

    If it's a major rules change, then I'd rewrite it: because if you didn't, you'd effectively be testing rules that you know you'd change anyway.

    If it's a minor change, don't bother: test the solid version you've got already.

    Graham

    I wouldn't "re-write" personally.
    I would do an edit of what you've done, for spelling errors, grammar and such.

    Once you've had a chance to playtest, take notes and ask questions of your players immediately after. Take notes from this, and then re-write and re-edit.

    This is approximately my own preparation for convention events, and it's served me well.

    Good luck with it!
