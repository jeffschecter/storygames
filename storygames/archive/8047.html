\n I just posted the fourth helping of the actual play recording of Primetime Adventures - Star Wars to our Canon Puncture Actual Play podcast feed (http://feeds.feedburner.com/canonpunctureactualplay). The fourth helping completes the first episode of two that we played at GenCon 2008 together.

The players are Paul Tevis, Daniel Perez, myself and Mick Bradley. The Producer is none other than Judd Karlman. I'm very proud of this episode. It was great fun to play and I've enjoyed listening to it, too.

If you're interested in PTA, you really should give this a listen. We hit all the highlights of the game system and have a great time doing it.

If you're a Star Wars junkie, you should check it out, too. We throw out tons of references to the original trilogy and have a romp of a time paying homage to the flicks.

If you want to hear a very talented GM using Story Game techniques, you really should check this out. Judd consistently framed strong scenes and pushed to conflict without forcing it. Its a beautiful thing.
