    \n I got my daughter "Baker's Dozen" \u2014 a rethemed version of Reiner Knizia's "Poison" \u2014 because she loves donuts. And because we didn't have any other card games that have circular cards with holes in the middle. (I've already got the iPhone app of "Poison" and like it.)

    She got me "Jishaku", which is a dexterity game that consists of a bunch of irregularly shaped hematite magnets and a block of foam with that wobbly surface. You take turns putting down magnets but if you make any of them stick together you have to take them back. Really simple, but surprisingly fun because the magnets are good and strong and start wobbling alarmingly as more of them fill up the board.

    And tomorrow I'm off to play Burning Wheel with my group...
