    Here are some resources:

    www.indie-rpgs.com <-- I defer all design help to them directly. There are a lot of people into stuff like that there.
    http://forum.rpg.net/forumdisplay.php?f=11 <-- I love RPGNet, but the Art of Game Design forum is kind of a wheel-spinners lunch zone. Not very helpful, but every once in a long while it will surprise you.

    Otherwise, if you have somethign specific (not just "look at my game and give me feedback", but rather "hey, I've got this one element, how do you think it'll work?") then we've got some decent resources here. Chris Chinn, Ben Lehman and others are Masters of the Game-y Crunch.

    Ex: As far as I can tell, Ben's loosely working on a game that's gonna make Exalted look like Polaris. Before he started his hippie games career, he was the Ultimate Grognard Number One for The Riddle of Steel, etc.
