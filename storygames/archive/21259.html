    \nIn AGON you play badass Greek heroes explicitly competing with one another for glory and the favor of the gods.

    The second edition, which is in progress, modernizes the game mechanics and tweaks the system based on a decade of lessons learned. Anyone who has played AGON will notice the changes, but the core will remain intact. It is a revision, not a redesign.
