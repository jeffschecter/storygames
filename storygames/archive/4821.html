\n Guys,
\n
\nI just acquired a tablet PC. Not terribly fast, but since I bought it for reading PDFs and operating the various web-enabled gizmos around my house that shouldn't be too big a problem.
\n
\nApart from the obvious stuff (like having PDF gaming stuff available right at the table), what other uses have people come up with for tablets and similar devices? How well did they work? Post your experiences!
