Genre crossovers

Graham
October 2006 edited October 2006 in Story Games
I've been asked to run a LARP for GenCon UK next year and I need ideas. Specifically, I need ideas of two genres which I can combine to interesting effect.

For example, my first LARP was a Paranoia / Cthulhu crossover. It was set in Alpha Complex and involved an R&D project which dug too far beneath the sea bed. It ended up with the Troubleshooters being chased by Cthulhu, who was green and therefore of higher clearance.

My second was an All Creatures Great And Small / American Werewolf In London crossover. ACG&S is a British vet programme, set in the Yorkshire Dales. The game started sedately, with the vetenarians meeting in a pub, and then a werewolf burst in and started ripping people to pieces.

Then I nearly did a Terry Pratchett / Mage: The Awakening crossover. And a Mr Man / Cthulhu crossover. And a game called Grand Theft Auto: Narnia. And a Paranoia / Jedi crossover.

But I'm stuck for ideas. Can you think two genres I can jam together, to comic effect, but which make some bizarre sense together?

Graham
