The submissions for the second edition of the Vieux Pots, Nouvelle soupes contest are closed.

For those who don't know/remember, here is the previous discussions about that.

http://www.story-games.com/forums/discussion/comment/362727#Comment_362727
http://www.story-games.com/forums/discussion/14218#Item_55

There year, there are 5 submissions, based on: Bunnies & Burrows (1976), Whog Shrog (france 1988), Amber, Laborinthus (a swiss game told to be the most expansive ever) and more or less Strombringer (based on Sorcerer)








You can now see and download these remakes (in french) online: http://www.narrativiste.eu/2012/vieux-pots-5-jeux-a-telecharger/

BUT for those who don't understand french, the game which won the last year has been translated to english, thanks to Xipehuz. You can read it here:

https://docs.google.com/document/d/1sgrbVgibCj45-Vms-P6VXBrf02t85pmc6qH_ZGfM3lY/edit

It's a remake of Alienoid, a french game from the early 90's, from Gregopor (that one who made the french version of Dungeon World). It's a beta, but it's worth a look in my opinion. Here is the teaser.

You're an Alienoid

Your species comes from outer space Your world is dead, incinerated by its own sun when it became a supernova. Since then, you and your kind have been travelling across interstellar space aboard the Ark, your generation ship, raiding each and every planet on your path.

You are a killing machine. You are 8 feet tall. Your body is covered with a bullet-proof chitin armor, can sustain the vacuum of space, and has enough strength to punch its way through a concrete wall. You are a weapon of mass destruction.

Your body is versatile. You can be grafted extra body parts, like biomechanical weapons, memory banks, and various accessories. You can also assume human form with the help of your Mimetic bioprocessor.

You are telepaths. Evolution has turned you into the ultimate predator: you can smell your prey's fear, anger or confusion from afar. The longer you stay with another species, the better you can understand and hunt it.

You have been infiltrating the Earth's population for many years now. Your species has sent scouts to pave the way and you have since since replaced key personalities in top positions, thanks to your shapeshifting abilities and your enhanced empathy. But whatever you decide to do on Earth, you will have to remain inconspicuous.

Your plan: turn the Earth into your pantry. Slowly but surely, your species has been replacing nuclear warheads all over the world by cryogenic weapons. Alienoid scientists are currently developing warheads that will freeze the planet, leaving its resources and all human flesh for the space robbers to take. It's a just matter of time.

The danger: betrayal of racial purity. Humans harbor a strange disease called feelings. This shit is contagious and forces you to see them as something more than just meat. Thankfully, inquisitors from section VYPER are keeping an eye out and treat the sick, generally with a plasma shot in the face. Literally.



That. We will know at the end of the month who's the winner this year.
