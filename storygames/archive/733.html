Games which must never be written

Graham
May 2006 edited July 2017 in Story Games
All my best game ideas are the ones that make me think "This game must never be written". And then I write them and they're bizarre and fun.

For example, a LARP based on Meat Loaf is clearly a game which must never be written. But, now it's written, I think it's going to work.

Other stupid ideas I've had are:

- The slash game, where you choose a well-known TV series and the action consists of all the characters having sex with each other. Christ knows how the mechanics for that would work.
- The Childhood Whimsy game, in which all the things you believed as a child are true (sheep are just clouds with legs, if you close your eyes you can teleport yourself, there's a lion at the bottom of the stairs which waits there late at night to kill you). I bet someone's done this already.
- Narrativist Snap
- The game where all the conflict resolution is done by the playground game of Mercy (or perhaps Slaps).

and it's pretty obvious that some of those are terrible but some of them have something good in them.

Does anyone else have ideas for games which must never be written?

Graham
