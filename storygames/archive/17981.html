\n I'm gearing up to start a 2nd season of my Monsterhearts game. One of the NPCs is Winston Q. Black, a kindly old man, owner and proprietor of Something For Everyone, a strange curio shop that reminds people of Needful Things.

Anyway, Winston Q. Black has a special move: When you accept a gift from Winston Q. Black, he gains a string on you. Mr. Black loves giving things away. Zola the ghoul, who works at his shop wonders how he stays in business.

Right now he's got a total of 15 Strings on the player characters, and a string on the Chosen's NPC little sister.

So, what's he need all those strings for? What nasty things should he be doing with them? Any ideas for fun custom moves, for when Mr. Black comes calling in a favor?

    \n Some ideas (though Praion's is already really good!):

    * He can spend a string to make you reveal a secret at an inopportune moment.
    * He can spend a string to use one of your powers once as if he possessed that power.
    * He has a creature he is creating in the basement, a la Frankenstein. He can spend a string to give something about you (perhaps a mechanical game function like a Skin Move or something less rooted in the game mechanics like a memory or personality quirk) to the creature.
    * He can spend a string to see through your eyes and/or control you for a limited amount of time.

    \n This is a really interesting--and tricky--question!

    Although all the suggestions so far are interesting (except for the move, because how would an NPC roll the dice?), but I would recommend to step back for a minute: look through all the existing Skins and make a list of interesting String-powered moves (including the basic options).

    Make some of them available to the NPC as options.

    The most basic one, of course, is "make them hold steady if they want to do something" and "mark experience if you do what he wants". Those already pretty well represent the sway he's got over the characters, and give him a pretty creepy vibe when he starts spending those Strings.
