\n Hey Kids, Chicago Gameday 33 is happening on November 10th, starting at 9:00 a.m. (or 8:00 a.m. if you want to join us for breakfast).

Chicago Gameday is a one-day RPG con that runs from 9:00 a.m. until 9:00 p.m. and is held at Games Plus in Mt. Prospect, IL.

It costs absolutely nothing to sign up and play. All you need to do is post in the sign-up thread over on ENWorld and let me know in which games you'd like to play. If there's an open seat, it's yours!

Right now, we have seats available in the following games:

Morning Events
Monster of the Week
Trollbabe
Reign
Feng Shui
Marvel Heroic

Afternoon Events
Fiasco
Over the Edge
Savage Worlds
Heroes Fall
Marvel Heroic

Sign up to play

You can learn more about Chicago Gameday at the Gameday website or on our Facebook page.
