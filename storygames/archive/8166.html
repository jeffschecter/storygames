\n As we head into the home stretch on Diaspora, which will be replacing our ill-considered Traveller/Fate hybrid Spirit of the Far Future, we find an unexpected bonus: it generates cool diagrams.



Honestly, a fun and cool looking diagram is sometimes enough to fire my enthusiasm for a whole project. That's probably a little sick.

Anyway, here's the output from a play session consisting solely of cluster, system, and character generation. Now, we have always had a lot of fun just making characters, so it's not necessarily a valuable observation that this was really, really fun. But it was.

And, hey, cool diagrams.
