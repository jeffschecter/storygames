\n Thanks to Pete for rattling my cage about this...

Matthijs and I decided when we wrote Love in the Time of Sei\xf0 that we'd donate the profits to charity, and I'm really happy to report that we've made our first donation - 212.09 USD to the Mines Advisory Group. That represents our profit from 31 print and 33 .pdf sales. I'm looking forward to sending MAG more money in the future.

Pete also asked about downloadable play materials. We didn't rush to share these because they represent half the game, or all the game if you are an enterprising Archipelago II enthusiast. Anyway, Matthijs and I are happy with the game's reception and very proud of our work. Here are the play materials in .pdf form for easy printing. If you like what you see, buy the game or kick some money MAG's way. Or both!
