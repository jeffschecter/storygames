It's Complicated: How can we make it more complicated?

\n
\n
\n
\n\nGraham \n\n\n
\n
\n\nJuly 2008 \n edited July 2008 in Story Games
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n One thing that bothers me about It's Complicated is that it isn't complicated enough. A game called "It's Complicated" should definitely be really complicated, otherwise I feel cheated.

What else can we add to It's Complicated to make it more complicated?

We might start with tables. It definitely needs loads of random encounter tables, so you can randomly generate someone you meet, and the relationship you have with them.

I also want it to have Spell Lists. I'm not quite sure how they'd work, but they'd all be based around relationships and making them better or worse.

What else can we add to It's Complicated, to make it needlessly complicated?

Graham
