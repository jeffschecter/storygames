\n Alrighty, folks.

Nerdly Beach Party is just weeks away, on the weekend of March 30th - April 1st. We have campsites reserved. We have a menu of tasty food prepared. We have a slate of games planned.

Nerdly Beach Party registration will run you $50, which will get you the aforementioned campsite, tasty food, and slate of games. It will also get you a keen camp teeshirt, cause really: is there any reason not to mimic everything that Dave Younce does? Any money above and beyond the cost for the weekend will go into the next Nerdly Beach Party.

Registration will open later this week. If you want to nail down a slot at the party, shoot an email to 2007prereg@nerdlybeachparty.org. Otherwise, once registration opens, it will be all first-come, first-served.
