\n Hello story-gamers!

I'm recently moved to Ottawa, following my wife on her post-doc adventurers. Had to leave my great crew of gamers behind and am hoping to be able to find some more in Canada's capital! My main interest is Apocalypse World, and various hacks, but I'm interested in all kinds of gaming... been having a lot of fun with Dread lately.

I've put up some posters, found a few interested souls, but was wondering if there's anyone else out there...

Hope to hear from you!
