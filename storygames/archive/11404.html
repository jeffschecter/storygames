\n That's all. Played it today for the first time, despite having owned it (in one form or another) since October. Used the "* Identity" playstyle. Had my world rocked.

Amazing kudos to my group, too, who made "reintegration" the word of the day. By the time we were all into our second memories, the level of crossover between our seemingly-unrelated plots was incredible, and by the end, when we all decided to remember ourselves, it was with the unspoken certainty that our characters were now united against a common enemy who knew our every weakness.
