    \n I have a feeing 101 Zombies fluctuates slightly too. And that's no bad thing, it's like the fable about the frog in the well, which climbs up three steps only to fall down one, or some such. Point being, simply whittling down doesn't provide enough adversity or uncertainty; in fact, I'd be happy to see a game where you could whittle almost all the way down, then through a combo of circs (including luck and/or poor choices) go all the way back up again, so you end the game where you started.
