\n My goal: get my new app spiffy & beautiful. It's already available, but merely functional and quite homely. Once prettied up, it's available for free to all and sundry.

Your rewards: oooohh, cool stuff - like a whole progrock music album, miniatures, a spiffy new rulebook, stories, some on-line activities ... each one for $25, choose as you like.

All comments and questions welcome!

    \n Thanks John. I'm trying something a little new with this - the rewards are pretty much a shelf of cool stuff to buy, and buying one or more mainly contributes toward my goal, which is a bit too pricey for me to do all by myself, and which is going to be available to the world. It's closer to Greg Stolze's "ransom" model than to most RPG kickstarts. (And for those inclined toward the typical RPG kickstart, the usual option is still there - you can indeed pledge and get the spiffy upgraded book.)
