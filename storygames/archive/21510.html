\n Recently, I have started a Canadian based RPG marketplace. Since I know there are a number of designers who frequent story-games, I thought starting a thread might be a good way to let folks know about it.

So far the Marketplace is comprised primarily of Canadian indie RPG, but that was always meant as a starting place.

The Marketplace is here. The basics are that the Marketplace takes a 10% cut (after transaction processing ~3% +30cents).
We do both PDF and Print+PDF sales, and we do sell to Canadian FLGS.

We take care of all the setup for the publisher, all they need to do is drop PDF's\xa0 of their titles in a google drive folder and confirm $CAD pricepoint. We payout quarterly.

If you interested please reach out, and I would be happy to answer any questions from anyone.

I'd also be happy to hear any feedback on the site.
