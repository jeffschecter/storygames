    \n It sounds like any team or collaborative game could work. For board games, Escape from the Temple and Spaceteam are fully collaborative. Team games like One Night Werewolf, the Resistance, or Two Rooms and a Boom involve deduction and critical thinking. For an rpg, World of Dungeons is simple, but does require a GM/story guide.
