    \nFunny you mentioned that - it's the third time today someone tells me the same thing.

    Ropecon is big in Finnish scale, drawing 3500 to 4000 people in - or that's been the trend for the last four years or so. The magical 4000 visitor limit might just break this year. Of course, some 500 people of those are volunteer organisers, lecturers, panelists, GM's and other people who get a free entrance.
