    \n I think zombie protagonists (a la Game Chef 09) are acceptable, as is World War Z. But seriously - zombies are/were played out ten years ago as a meme.

    @Andy: I wouldn't say that the scene fragmented so much as the middle chunk got ripped out of the Forge and smushed into story-games. Nowadays, it seems like story-games is more for the chatty gamer, and the Forge is more for the brand-new-designer gamer; this process has put new (new to me) faces in the scene, which has been great!

    Oh: the Forge is also for people to ask about/debate GNS. Having recently realized how simple a definition of the CAs really is, I'm glad to no longer lurk on a forum where every second or third topic is "EXPLAIN GNS PLZ". GNS discussion is like heat death, to return to the astronomy metaphor. Yeek.
