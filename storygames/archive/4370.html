\n So when Joshua Bishop-Roby was working on Sons of Liberty earlier in the year, I was paying attention to his content creation at the table because I had similar ideas and thought it would be fun. But to be honest, I never really thought roleplaying the Revolutionary War would be very fun, and I wasn't digging the steampunk aesthetic that was attached to it.

Fast-forward to this past weekend and I'm laying on the couch reading my printed out playtest copy and going F- YEAH! This ROCKS!

Joshua, your game may still be in playtest, but it reads great and I'm really looking forward to playing it. The content creation tool is brilliant, I love all the patriots and tories you added, and I'm really glad you added in a Versus Mode. It reminds me in some small way of a Duel of Wits in BWR which is a high complement).

Even the techy possibilities are taking me over. I wanna drop artillery shells over the British lines with a remote-controlled balloon. I wanna send guys in diving bells from my steam-powered submarine to blow up the British flagship. I wanna watch Ben Franklin hack a telescope and some dangerous chemicals into a surveillance camera to catch Tory spies.

Damn you Joshua!!
