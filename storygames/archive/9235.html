\n I have someone who wants to play as a giant-scale character, otherwise human. It'd be a solo game so I don't have to worry about party balance, so whatever, but here's my question: what sort of challenges, quests, scenarios, game seeds would be good for a giant? There's always "find clothing/equipment/basic supplies in your size", but I don't want to beat that to death; there's always "zounds, there's a huge-scale monster that wants to eat you", but I don't want this to turn into a Godzilla freak-of-the-week motif. Any ideas or resources for this?
