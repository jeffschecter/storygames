\n So I've been really wanting to find a system that can emulate a last stand scenario. You know the ones from the movies where the competent soldiers are surrounded by overwhelming enemy forces and they go down in a blaze of glory? I've been watching movies like Blackhawk Down, 13 Hours, Tears of the Sun and really wanted to find a one-shot system where the enemy body count is high, the macho bravado is oozing, and the good guys all die on the hill under the tattered and torn stars and stripes banner. It's a scenario that has trascended even war films, finding its way into genres such as westerns and horror.

Is there a game that already does this? Something that can be hacked? Or any concepts from other games that could be cobbled into this scenario?
