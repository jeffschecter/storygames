    \n Wild West gunslinger? A Battlebabe with the eye contact hypnotizing power and Ice Cold. When you enter a charged situation with an enemy, you stare them down. Both of you, staring at each other as beads of sweat drip down your face, each gauging the other... who will shoot first? The Battlebabe uses this time to read the situation. Oh, look, a +1. Then he rolls+cool to go aggro as they both draw and shoot!

    Or do you mean two guns like in a John Woo movie?

    The Killer: When you go aggro or seize by force with two guns, you can make an area attack, just as if your guns had the autofire tag. Just remember to reload!

    Add that to the Gunlugger and you're pretty solid.
