\n Right, I'm finally getting serious about my design workshop. I've set up a G+ community to talk about, and get feedback on, my games-in-progress, but I welcome more general contributions from folks about game mechanics and other game design-related issues particularly pertinent to the games I'm creating (these are mainly narrative games currently- over time, I'll start working on more traditional adventuring or fantasy games). Over the coming weeks I'll be adding info about all the games that are currently in the process of being teased out of my brain and onto Word documents on my laptop. The community is public, so if you want to join, go right ahead! There's an indyhippo website coming soon also, and a G+ community for projects already completed and published. Hope to see some o' y'all there
