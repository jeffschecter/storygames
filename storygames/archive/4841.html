    \n I used to play with the random dungeon creator in the 1st ed AD&D DMG. I mostly used it for playing by myself and it worked pretty well for that purpose. But when you ran into another adventuring party and had to roll all those people up...geez. I do remember getting way too many magic items from the various encounters.

    I don't think I ever used that for playing with others. There were just took many modules around to use instead.
