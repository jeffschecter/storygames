\n Hi everyone,
\nI am looking for new players for an IRC game group. We are already a small group of players, but the more the merrier! We use a very efficient set of rules for dice rolling, advantages and disadvantages. If you all would like to play, we'll do storytelling games in any setting, with any purpose, objective and characters. Just post or send me an email : )
\n
\nSome campaigns we've played in this manner:
\n-Adapted from the game "The French Invade Texas" by Jaap de Goede. French explorers do battle with the Spanish while colonizing the New World. (Google it!!!)
\n-The missions of a CIA Special Activities Division agent in Afghanistan.
\n-A Dungeons and Dragons world where there are no good or neutral creatures or gods, only evil.
\n-The intrigues, loves and battles of a pantheon of gods.
