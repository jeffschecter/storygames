\n After Chris Hanrahan unfortunately hopped on the sick train last week, Brian Isikoff was kind enough to let me sit in on their 2d6 Feet in a Random Direction podcast. And with Ken Hite as his guest, no less! I had a really fun time and probably will never see a microphone again in my life

Highlights for me were discussing improv classes and their effect on my gaming as well as talking about after-game debriefs and how they can help both players and GMs. Oh, and Brian's 28 person SotC game at DunDraCon!!

If anyone gets a chance to listen, I'd be interested to hear feedback on the show.
