\n Lately I've noticed a significant increase in linkage to my site from weird spammy Web sites. The sites look like online directories, but they're obviously just a bunch of search results with a ton of ads wrapped around them. Is anyone else seeing this? What are you opinions?

Also, I'm getting a lot of hits from porn Web cam sites. Is this happeneing to anyone else? Is there some kind of linkage scam here, or are people just talking about my games on their nude Web cams a lot?

I realize that the received wisdom is that any link is a good link, but I'm a little annoyed that they're diluting my Google results. After all, the obvious goal of these sites is to take someone who's looking for my games, and get them to click on an ad instead.
