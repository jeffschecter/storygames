\n So, last week I left Spirit of the Century and Grey Ranks in the back of my car which was parked undisturbed in front of my Sunny Southern California Home for an entire day. Upon return to my car I discovered that the binding of Spirit of the Century appeared to have MELTED and large chunks of the book had fallen out and indeed what was left of the interior had slide over about half an inch before the binding apparently re-solidified.

My Grey Ranks book however was in fine shape.

A heads up to those planning on baking your SotC books anytime soon.

Jesse

P.S. I am laboring under a curse regarding SotC and my car. The last copy I owned I accidentally left ON TOP of my car and I can only hope was rescued by a passing stranger from the parking lot of a medical center.
