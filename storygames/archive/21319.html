\n This week You Don't Meet In An Inn begins to explore the arcane mysteries surrounding Vassar college and the Knights Templar!

Today we are playing Greg Stolze and John Tynes' Unknown Armies Third Edition!

We join Abigail Archer, Galatea West, and Mark Sinclair in the midst of a magick ritual that will change their lives forever.

You can find the podcast on itunes here, on not-itunes here, and our RSS.

You can follow us on twitter at Notaninn or email us at YouDontMeetInAnInn@gmail.com

You can also like our facebook page or subreddit for regular updates.

If you'd like to support the podcast please rate and review us on itunes, or share us with your friends on social media!

We hope you enjoy listening!
