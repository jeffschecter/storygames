\n I'm progressing with my novel Gu, which I'm writing on a blog.

Today's post/chapter is all about how gaming is different, and how it's the same, in a world where you have an artificial substance that can morph into any shape you like.

There's a tribute to story-games.

The previous post used TonyLB's idea in the Gu brainstorming thread of making somebody a theme park for their birthday.

I'm having fun, but so far I'm not having plot. Since it's told in the form of a digital-experience documentary which you, the reader, are experiencing, I'm not especially worried about that.
