Folks,

A bunch of us have been discussing the possibility of a semi Camp Nerdly style gathering in the UK. In the Midlands to be precise. People are getting together to discuss games and gaming. So far Malcolm Craig, Matt Machell, Andrew Kenrick, and Graham Walmsley have expressed interest in bringing their in-development games (as well as me, obviously and I've almost definitely forgotten someone). That's not to say that this is only for people developing games but people will be bringing games in various stages of completeness and there's no guarantee that they won't suck. If you're OK with that, then awesome! There will be some tried and true published games run as well, just be prepared to do some playtesting.

I'm currently looking into booking this, and the idea so far is:

It's a weekend in mid-late May. I will book 3 nights somewhere (Fri/Sat/Sun)
My idea of roughing it is having to drink instant coffee instead of filter and not getting a decent 3g signal. So there will no actual camping involved. It'll be either lodge/chalet or rented cottage, possibly big old caravans.
I'm trying to get somewhere in Warwickshire/Northamptonshire/Leicestershire.
Cost TBA, but somewhere around the Â£50-Â£75 per person. The more people are there, the less the cost per person.
It'll be self catering. BYO Food/beer/whatever. I dare say we'll end up going out for a curry at some point.
Almost everywhere appears to be non-smoking.
The higher the cost, the better the facilities.
I'll need money up front. I cannot afford pay for booking unless everyone pays up front.

Everyone is welcome, but please bear in mind that we're not looking at somewhere with any facilities for anything beyond gaming space. Please don't bring people who aren't going to dig the game stuff that's going on because the chances are, they'll be pretty bored. Don't bring young kids. Don't bring your dog, because most places won't allow them. Don't bring Chris Tarrant, because that doesn't make any sense. Basically, don't bring anyone who's not going to enjoy the gaming.

That's it so far. Anyone interested? I need to get this off the ground pretty soon.
