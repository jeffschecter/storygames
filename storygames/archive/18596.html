\n A friend was having birthday drinks on Saturday, and mentioned in the round-robin e-mail that someone might be bringing Microscope. Fantastic! I thought, I'd be happy to play it. But then my mind started to wander... and I came up with a hack called Stethoscope. Well, not really a hack, just a tweaking of the name. Anybody got any other similar ideas? Here's my pitch for Stethoscope:

Stethoscope
A collaborative story game where characters develop the power to see into the people around them\u2019s innermost thoughts, the pulsating dark heart of the city. But can they convince other people to help them intervene to nip evil and wickedness in the bud, or are they doomed to watch helplessly as apparently decent and honourable individuals carry out their dastardly schemes and machinations\u2026?

    Star Wars: Hedge of Empire

    The writers are currently working out how to incorporate fetches, freeholds, and Seasonal Courts in the realms of Deep Sapce outside the Empire's borders. (George Lucas is believed to have called these writers a 'phantom menace' for tampering with his creation).
