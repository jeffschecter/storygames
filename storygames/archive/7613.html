\n Hey all, I just noticed that I have $9.00 in credit from RPGNow. I'm looking for product recs from S-G folks.

I'm down with just about anything that meets the following criteria:

1) Not available in print. I'm looking for straight PDF.

2) As mentioned elsewhere, I'm interested in the slightly unique/unusual. "Trad Fantasy Game With Elves and Armor Points #14" isn't really of interest to me, but "Trad Fantasy Game with Really Unique Background and Sorta Unique Combat System" might be.

3) Not too interested in Supers, unless it addresses the material in a totally different way.

4) I'm also interested in stuff that might be used as D&D 4e material (minis, floorplans, etc).

Note: I already have OVA, which is IMO one of the finest products offered there (that's MY recommendation to others looking for something from RPGNow).

Here's a chance to plug stuff you've bought from RPGNow and have liked!

-Andy
