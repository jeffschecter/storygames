\n No doubt you've been busy playing your copies of While the World Ends until the books have started falling to pieces (or the pixels have started fading, for you guys who bought the treesaver version). And as you've told your friends about it you've gotten requests to join the game, which you've had to refuse. Woe the fifth wheels of the gaming community! Alas, While the World Ends is only for four players. Fret not, I have now built a game that you not only can - but must - play with five participants.

Ladies and gentlemen, let me introduce you to The Daughters of Verona!

The Daughters of Verona is my game from Game Chef 2011, now polished and pretty. Go ahead! Download this free and awesome game of Shakespearean comedy.

In four hours the five participants play a comedy (ends with a marriage) containing staples of the genre like Shipwrecks, Crossdressing heroines, Bedtricks and many other hilarious events. No prep*! Just fun!

--

On a more serious note, we have now played the game and it was very fun**. I wonder if I should go ahead and try to source actual real printing of the cards, to sell on the Internet. I would appreciate your comments and feedback on the game. The rules are just four pages long, and there's a strong emergent quality to the game.


* It's quite a bit of work to print and cut all the cards that are needed. Let the fifth player do that while you play another awesome session of While the World Ends.

** By act 3 I laughed so hard my jaw ached.

    So yeah, check this out, it's fun and not too long to play, plus the 5 player thing is interesting for those times when you're too many or too few for other games.
