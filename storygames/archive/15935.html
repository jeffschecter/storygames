    Posted By: nunboiNamely passion, regret, and a very bleak world. Possibly something that mechanically simulated the struggle against the Shadow.
    \nHeya, welcome to Story Games!

    Hmmm. There's some interesting games out there for this mix. Two off the top of my head, not "new and hot", but still might satisfy your exact request:

    The Collectors: The Burning House. This game uses the Fudge system (not FATE, just original Fudge), and features the PCs all as soul collectors working for the Devil. Very bleak, lots of regret, pretty dark but also a little hopeful. No mechanical rules for the struggle against anything, but it's really anchored in the setting (although you don't have a Wraith "Shadow" analogue):

    http://rpg.drivethrustuff.com/product_info.php?products_id=16518

    VOX: Mike F created this basically to recreate the feel of Wraith. Every character has one (or eventually more) "Shadow" characters, each controlled by another player. Eventually, the character either has to synthesize or totally reject the Shadow aspect. Now mind, unlike Wraith the Shadow does not have to be (but can be) a polar opposite of the PC: It could be anything from "the word of God... maybe?" to "Odin!" to "an insect creature that whispers to me" to "a different, darker version of myself". Very surreal setting, it's basically half Wraith, half Twin Peaks with possibly a little Silent Hill 2 thrown in.

    http://rpg.drivethrustuff.com/product_info.php?products_id=63301
