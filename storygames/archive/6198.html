\n Hey, since a few folks asked me in private. Click on your "Account" tab above, and play around with some settings!

Specifically, what you're looking for is the "Change Stylesheet" option. From there, there's about a dozen styles/looks to choose from. See if there's one you like. My personal faves are Jazzman (duh), Soulscape, Clockwork Green and OSGM. See if you can squeeze more use out of perhaps a different look or style.

In "Forum Prefernces", you can change the look of your control panel, or control whisper notification (the box that appears at the top of the forum when someone whispers you a comment).

In "Personal Information", you can set/change your icon, or add custom information to your profile.

Hope that helps!
