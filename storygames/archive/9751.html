    \n OK, so as I'm cleaning up my house over here, I have that tightening in my stomach as I approach the game shelf. Yep, it's time for another culling. Time to sell or trade off the games that I don't have any upcoming (read: 18 months) plans to play or run. I'm reaching into the core of my hippie collection and, in some cases, tearing out its beating heart. In many cases, I'm just selling a second or third copy of an RPG that I have multiple copies of (Bliss Stage, CoS, etc). Anyway, in these economic troubled times, now's your chance to get some of these classic small-press games for cheap!

    Currently, I'm in "Clean up my collection" phase, so I'm not actively looking at trades or swaps, just selling. However, if you have something that you think I might be interested in, whisper me and we'll work it out. Also, if you want to haggle re price, I'm game, but in most cases I've already posted the "as low as I go" price for the item. Also, I'll totally ship overseas, no problem.

    Stuff that I Might Be Interested in Trading For
    Anything that's kinda out there, that I haven't read yet.

    Again, anything RPG-wise that's kinda out there.
