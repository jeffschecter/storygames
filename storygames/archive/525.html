Sek Si - HK Style Cinema Action Game

JasonP
April 2006 edited April 2006 in Story Games
Ok, so I'm pretty clear on what I want:

1.) Ass-kicking, more ass-kicking, with some ass-kicking sprinkled on top and a side o attitude.
2.) Some drama that rears its head up on occasion, ala the identity/amnesia theme in the movie version of Resident Evil.
3.) A big finish, high-budget and emotionally intense.

Take all this and mix well with:

1.) HK Style action cinema, like old John Woo-Chow Yun Fat stuff.
2.) Guns, guns, and more guns with a dash or splash of Kung Fu.
3.) Whatever else the players think is cool.

The titles is a Cantonese swear I found online for roughly 'Eat Shit'. I have some idea on the mechanics and startup:

Startup:
All the characters start in a board room inside a skyscraper of the Nebula Corporation, located in the Hong Kong of tomorrow-land. They are all bad ass agents of the corporation and have been suddenly summoned to a meeting late at night. Only, when they get there the room is empty except for a note on the desk. The note regretfully informs them that their services are no longer needed. Then the first wave of bastards enters the room to try and kill them. Can they get out of the building or will they die trying? What is going on here and why are they being eliminated? All this will be answered in the unfolding drama moments of play, being created by the players as they kick ass.

Mechanics:
Each roll is the tossing of 2 to 9 six-sided dice. Pairs are victories for the character, but whenever a 6 is rolled things have gotten dirty. How bad do you want the victory? Willing to risk some dirt?

This is where I stand right now, open to both ideas and general opinions. The idea really has gotten me pumped at this moment and I thought I'd just toss it out into the world right now.
