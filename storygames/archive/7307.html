\n So, I just got done tonight playing a full three sessions of Grey Ranks with three other people here in NYC, including one of the other playtesters. Finally! Man, was that awesome\u2014everything that I currently look for in a gaming experience: tragedy and emotional catharsis, connection to real-life issues, and of course GM-less-ness.

I was a little worried going in that the group wouldn't gel\u2014I know the two guys reasonably well at this point, and the woman *very* well, as she and I have been good friends and gaming buddies for some eight years, but she didn't know them. But, while chargen and the first half of session one were a little slow, later Chapters just flew by. Indeed, midway through tonight I thought to myself something along the lines of, "Wait, there's only one more Mission scene and zero more Personal scenes in Chapter Eight! And we haven't even been playing very long! Holy Shit!"

The ending was, of course, really sad and poignant, and we had a really interesting and wide-ranging discussion afterwards.

I still really want to play this with the teens I work with sometime.

Only two more weeks before one of the guys is leaving for a long time. We're going to play the Roach. :-)

So, thanks again Jason.

Matt
