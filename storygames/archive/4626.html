\n Looking for a table-top rpg that has the following properties, in order of importance:

(1) Characters collect runes to gain the ability to cast spells.
(2) Characters gain experience as they quest for runes and when they get enough XP they level up.
(3) Combat is effectively limited to Boss Battles and Duels.

(For those familiar with the product, the question is based on the "live-action video game", MagiQuest.)

The most recent edition of RuneQuest does the first effectively.
D20/OGl does the second very effectively.
I'm struggling a bit with the third and finding something that merges the three.

I'm wondering if maybe there is an OGL product out there that would fit the bill?

Thanks in advance.

    \n This might not help you much unless you speak German and find a rare copy of this, but the German bestselling RPG Das Schwarze Auge had an expansion set (two boxes, actually) that did all of this, called Schwertmeister. It was meant for characters level 15 and up.

    Runes are gathered over time, you use three in a triangle to create your own spells (body/path/effect, figure out the details with the GM). Characters gain XP in a pretty standard way and level up with the standard HP/attack/defense/skill benefits. The combat system is based on dueling, with attack series and turnovers, and a fitting setting where dueling (and fighting big scary things one at a time) is the norm.

    So I'm not sure how you could use all this, unless you find an English translation on the net somewhere. I know there are still fans of this discontinued line who publish stuff for it online.
