    \n I applaud the idea, but might have a couple suggestions.

    1) What is the practical difference between "Perfect", "Distinction" and "Success"? They make sense narratively, but without the mechanical underpinnings it seems wasteful.

    2) Any way to merge your two dice rolls? Fewer rolls, the better imo.

    3) The key words seem a little unintuitive as written. You might want to relate things to your task resolution. "And, immediately gain a success on one additional task without rolling".
