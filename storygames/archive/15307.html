We've decided to translate and publish this fantastic game form Eric J. Boyd

http://www.ulule.com/le-comite/

It has a complete new layout ( in full color for the same price, if we reach 140 % of the pledge) , with vector/Art D\xe9co illustrations from Rodolfo Reyes, a mexican artist, and illustrations from the first editions of Jules Vernes novels.

We have included some goodies (a member card of the club for the players), and most important, a new kind of board for storygaming, an erasable on (a velleda surface), that you can see in the video.

Did i said that i love this game ? A lot.
