\n The Amber roleplaying community has a tradition of adapting other RPGs for playing Amber games. (Events at The Black Road in 2004 included adaptations of My Life with Master, Kobolds Ate My Baby, and Kill Puppies for Satan.) I really want to see actual play using Poison'd for an Amber Throne War.

How about you? What feats of actual play would you like to see?

Paul
