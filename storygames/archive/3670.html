\n I know there are several people here who draw cartoons - Remi, johnzo, and I'm sure I've seen others.

I'm thinking about getting a cartoonist to draw me something for a (non-RPG) project of mine, so I'd be interested in finding out what talent there is around here. To make the thread more generally useful, I won't restrict it by talking about exactly what I'm looking for; if you're a cartoonist, speak up, give us a link to your online portfolio or some stuff you've done, say a few words perhaps about what sort of stuff you do, and if I see what I'm looking for I'll whisper you for your rates.

If I don't whisper you, it doesn't mean I don't like your stuff, just that it's not what I'm after for this project. But you may be just what someone else has been looking for.
