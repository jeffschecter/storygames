\n Hey guys and gals! I'm sure many of you have already enjoyed what is the hilariously chaotic and yet dangerously fun game that is group play of ACTION CASTLE! Well I have put a Play-by-Post game of it up for any randomer to enter commands over at Giant in the Playground, for your viewing/gaming pleasure. Of course, I slightly modified the rules to fit the medium, which was discussed in the opening post, but per one of the player's suggestion, I also am posting cheeky responses to incorrect actions regardless of whether they make it through consensus. This has naturally resulted in more fun for all. Without further ado, enjoy (and by all means join in the fun)!

P.S. If people seem to be enjoying themselves enough by the end, I plan to bust out ALL of the Parsely games in this fashion, either in order, or by polling players for their preference of next game.

P.P.S. If Jared is watching, and would prefer I not post the actual gameplay in a public forum (I am sensitive to the needs of the indie designer and I could see where that might inhibit OR increase sales), I can just stop after finishing with ACTION CASTLE, or even cut it off mid-game if necessary. Just let me know.
