\n Hey all!

I've already been thinking about doing more with my entry to Game Chef this year, Our Last Best Hope. In the game, the players undertake an extremely dangerous mission to save the world, hoping to save Humanity from a terrible Crisis. I was really inspired by the movie Sunshine, but the game also draws from other sources like Armageddon, 12 Monkeys, Dawn of the Dead, Children of Men, and Mad Max.

I've got some mechanics I'm happy with, but the game is still in the early stages. I'm really interested in hearing what people would want to get from this kind of story.

If you're playing a group trying to save the world, what would your ideal session include?
