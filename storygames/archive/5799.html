\n I'm repeating this information so that anyone interested in these games can ask questions, create characters, etc....

DATE: March 14th through 16th, 2008
LOCATION: Sheraton Hotel, Birmingham, Alabama

EVENT NAMES:
1. Don't Rest Your Head: Friday 9pm till late?
"The Asylum"
The players (all teens) have been sent by the government to a converted military complex deep underneath a mountain. You were brought here after developing insomnia and weird power. Most of your abilities were severely dampened with meds but two days ago the meds stopped. Your abilities are coming back and the nightmares are sure to follow.

2. Saturday 9 till 1pm and 1 till 5 pm
"Indie Games Now"
Come try out a new game! Preference will be given to players interested in a full game but short pickup games will also be offered as available. Some possibilities include: In a Wicked Age, Spirit of the Century, Dreden File RPG, Don't Rest Your Head, Zorcerer of Zo, Truth and Justice, Mortal Coil, Amber Diceless RPG, Al Shabiri Roach, Fairy's Tale, Og, The Secret Lives of Gingerbread Men, Steal Away Jordan, Dead of Night, Donjon, Dogs in the Vineyard, Inspectres, Everway, OctaNe, Risus, Wushu (and others)

3. Dreden Files RPG (alpha playtest rules): Saturday 5 till 9pm
"Life in the Magic City"
You are the supernatural denizens of Birmingham aka. The Magic City. Birmingham has long been a lodestone of sorts with many magical inhabitants. Unfortunately, UAB researchers have been tapping into an unknown magical force beneath Iron Mountain. Something that should have remained buried\u2026
