\n In this episode I talk with Kat and Michael Miller about their upcoming game, Serial. Serial, which will be published by Incarnadine Press, is a game based on true crime investigations of serial killers. The game is careful to focus most of the interest on the victims of these crimes rather than the cops or the killer. In fact, no one plays the killer. It's an exciting and interesting game that I enjoyed playing.
