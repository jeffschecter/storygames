    Interesting read. Apart from mythos etc., the idea of taking the "only one player needs to succeed with that in a scene for it to matter" skills (investigative) and rationing character creation so there is a standard level of those skills in a group whether 3 or 5 PCs, very neat. Physical hazhards etc. can be scaled to number of PCs but spotting the art clue etc. doesn't scale to # so the level of ability on that pool of things fixed in the group. Very cool design approach.

    Also liked small mixing of social and rules. How to break this tie? Player who showed up first for the game session.
