\n I am going to a small convention the first weekend of November and I need to get my events in ASAP. But I will be running fewer games so I want to make certain they come off. Here is a sample of what I will be sending as the description. Let me know what you think, what I should change and if you were there what would draw you to it.

Slot: B (9pm to 1am) and F (7pm to 11pm)
Title: The Wall
System: Artesia Tales
Notes: mature audiences only, no experience neccessary, this is a playtest session
Description: Two leaders face off at the gates of an ancient fortress. Throne and destiny drives them to spill the blood of their minions across the cursed ashen wastes. Take up the mantle of the Mad Earl seeking to rebuild a long dead dynasty. Grab the reigns of the Duke of Barrows as he desperately tries to unlock the secrets of the very same throne that the earl seeks. Each employs soldiers, mystics and eldritch forces to bend the other to their will. What hand will you play in the conflict across the brutal stone wall? Come try out a new way to play in the world of Mark Smylie's acclaimed Artesia comic and roleplaying game. In the narrative tradition of "My Life With Master" and "The Mountain Witch," Artesia Tales centers this bloody story around the conflict between two groups. Using the Known World's version of the tarot, The Book of Dooms, we spin a yarn of betrayal, magic, love and fear.

Thanks,
- Don
