\n I'm really glad my friend Bailywolf got me into Dungeon World. I didn't think much of it at first glance, but the more I get into it the more I LOVE how I can customize the heck out the game. The most fun I think will come form building my own classes and powers. But other things like tinking up world based fronts and moves will be extra awesome.

I've got a few to-do's on my list but I'd love some feedback and ideas:

1. New Classes - I'm open to suggestions on classes I should attempt to build. I've got ideas for the following: Artificer, Alchemist, Warlock, Witch, Shadow Dancer, etc. Heck, I might even dream up Monster Classes like Dragon, etc. What do you think? What classes would you like to eventually see?

2. System tweeks - It's going to take a while to get my players use to the idea of no initiave in combat. Until I can ween them off of it, I might use a Dex+d6 roll off mechanic. Later down the line I'd love to cook up new Moves for the classes. Like Quick Draw for the Rogue/Ranger, Tactical Leadership for the Fighter, Arcane Alertness for the Wizard, etc, etc. Basically letting the players declare these 'positioning' powers to trigger if/when they go in a combat order. especially if these powers grant unqiue bonues if they roll well. LIke Tactical Leadership allowing the Fighter to 'place' the Rogue in a scene, etc. Thoughts?

3. World Emulation to World Building - Right off to bat I'm going to start building Fronts and Moves associated with Eberron. Just as my practice playground. Eventually I want to dream up unique world powers of my own setting. Is there any settings you want to build eventually?

A few oddball ideas I have:

Powerful Races get plugged into a class by trading out a primay move of the class for a racial move. Then puting that 'traded out' move into the pool of level 2-5 moves to be picked up. Also, adding more racial moves assoicated with the race to the pool if there are multiple.

An expanded Signature Weapon (gear) move list that enhances the Signature Weapon move of a fighter. (Flaming, Frost, Intellegent, etc.)

Wild Talent Moves for Psionics. Again with the idea of a move swap at 1st level or just adding it to the pool as you level up.

Epic Moves for level 10+ characters. Planar Shifting, Calling upon armies. Etc. Only really used for climax events in a campaign.

I'd love to hear some of your future hacking plans for Dungeon World.

    \n Oh forgot a few cool ideas:

    Plot Paths! A madlib style choose 4 event rolls in the character background. Pick Pirates! and get on a 10: You own a ship and a small loyal crew. 9-7: A dread pirate owes you a favor. 6 or Less: You are on the run from Capt. _________.

    Mix in options like Romance, Lost Cities, etc. And let the playes pick 4 and roll up their "history" before the events of the game.

    Plot Moves: Playes pick a domain they get to control in a game. Once a session they get to attempt to change the active plot by invoking this Move. And they either get a Yes: And to a No: And result. Be careful if your Plot Move controls relationships...the next think you know the Duke might be trying to force you to marry his daughter!

    \n Here's some stuff I wrote a while back, similar to your plot paths:

    Cleric! You have fallen in with a band of sell-swords, cut-purses, and diabolists!

    \u2022Yes, for I have been defrocked for the crime of ________ and sent into exile, though the god ________ still heeds my prayers and must have some plan for me.
    \u2022Yes, for I am the last surviving keeper of the ancient and noble faith of ________, now lost to (violence, time, cataclysm, fissure, or conversion).
    \u2022Yes, for I am a dark templar of my faith, charged to seek out lost secrets, sources of power, and ________, all for the greater glory of ________.

    Fighter! You have fallen in with a band of apostates, cut-purses, and diabolists!

    \u2022Yes, for I was born and raised among the wretched and outcast in ________, living hand to mouth, dreaming of future riches and ________.
    \u2022Yes, for I am the black sheep (or exiled scion) of the noble house of ________, and this is the ignoble company I keep, obscuring and protecting me from ________, who has \u201cgreat plans\u201d for me.
    \u2022Yes, for I am through with the wars and battles of ________, and will live out the rest of my days as a servant to my own interests, seeking ________.

    Rogue! You have fallen in with a band of sell-swords, apostates, and diabolists!

    \u2022Yes, and what else is new? I used to ________ with ________, and they were no better than this lot, that\u2019s for certain.
    \u2022Yes, but it\u2019s only a temporary situation. You see, I have grand plans for ________, if only ________ would stop blocking my path to true greatness.
    \u2022Yes, but how else would these idiots know where to find big piles of loot? They\u2019re the unfortunate cost of doing business and I have this great job in mind where we can get ________ from ________.

    Wizard! You have fallen in with a band of sell-swords, apostates, and cut-purses!

    \u2022Yes, and such is the lot of a wizard, to be shunned and feared by pleasant society. Luckily these unsavory types don\u2019t ask too many questions about my strange and unsavory practices, such as ________ and ________.
    \u2022Yes, but once the wizards of ________ acknowledge the true earth-shattering potential of my new line of research into ________, they will beg me to teach them my methods.
    \u2022Yes, but I\u2019m not your average hide-behind-the-armored-dude hedge-mage who crawls head-first into a hole in the ground wearing fancy robes. I like to get my hands dirty. How else do you learn the real stuff like ________ and ________?
