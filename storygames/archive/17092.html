\n My name is Alan Silva, am the author of the Samurai Dogs RPG / Cachorros Samurai. The game tells about conduct: treason, loyalty and their consequences. The players are a pack of dogs led by a samurai master, and the rest of a veteran newcomers. They must aim to complete the samurai code of conduct.

The game has a mechanical-based conduits and biscuits. Yes, you need to eat crackers to gain benefits in the game. We use a six-sided die for conflict resolution mathematicians. For example attributes: Honour, Discipline and technique.

My English is not so good, but I would like your opinion about the two playtests I have done here in my homeland Brazil. I hope you enjoy. The game took part in the contest - DIY game creation 2012 - Publisher of the Secular Games, but was disqualified for the first step by not completing the minimum requirements.
