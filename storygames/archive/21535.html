    \n I don't know if this is what you're asking about, but @Adam_Dray has this amazing thing going on where he's got richly graphic/detailed colour maps of a dungeon imported into Roll20, so you can "crawl" through them, with the map being revealed through "fog of war". Very cool, and seems to work very well.

    When we played, we stumbled into an ambush with some giant spiders. Turned out he had placed them on the map much earlier, and simply had to hit some kind of "reveal layer" option to show them when they attacked. Pretty cool, since it means moving your little miniature/token on the map actually means something.
