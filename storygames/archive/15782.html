\n Hey folks!

In Raleigh, NC, April 13-15th.

And now you've been invited.

We have a Google group for discussion. Ask to join and someone will add you.
MoatCon 2012

Its starts Friday April 13th and goes through Sunday morning April 15th, in Raleigh NC at the Wake Forest Holiday Inn Express.

We got an excellent room rate. Doubles/King are $70/night and hot breakfast is included in the room price.

MoatCon Reservations

We have the option of gaming in the suites and we're renting a large conference room with 6-8 tables. It was awesome sauce last year. We'll need folks to throw in between $15 and $20 dollars each for the conference room space.

We're simply using Google Doc to organize games. Right now we're limiting each person to only running 1 game during the Con. The ideas is to have the GMs bring their top game. And we really want people playing excellent games.

Attendance last year was around two dozen. We had between two and four games per slot.

Last year we had:
Panty Explosion Perfect
Tenra Bansho Zero
Carry: A game about war
Swords without Masters
Fiasco
a Dominion (Card Game) Tournament
All Flesh Must Be Eaten
Unending River
... and lots of other stuff I can't rightly recall at the moment. We even very nearly had a game of The Mountain Witch. ... nearly... (yes, I'm obsessed) (but I'm trying to get better) (typing to myself isn't helping...)

Beyond games we have other activities planned, including Friday dinner out together, and a book/game swap on Saturday night. (Yes, the return of Operation: Blind Drop!)

Drop me a line/whisper here, or just go join the group. But please let us know if you plan on coming. We need to estimate attendance so we can provide appropriate space and refreshments.
