\nJiffyCon East is coming back to the Boston area this fall. Come play indie (and other) games, have some excellent food in the Davis Square area of Somerville, and maybe catch a relaxing dinner with friends, new and old, after the festivities. It will be held on Saturday, November 9, 2013, at a site easily accessible by the T.

JiffyCon is a Massachusetts tradition, run periodically out east and out west since 2006. Mostly role playing games, but board games, too. It's a quick day of gaming, and is a great place to meet other folks interested in playing small press games. There's a discussion and some rsvp'ing going on at Google+.

A super shout-out to @David_Berg who made our mascot image for Jiffy. Thank you, David!

GM signup has just opened, so if you'd like to run something just say the word. Feel free to post here, too, about your game, or to make requests--but be sure to sign up on the google form for it to get on the schedule officially.

There's information about the schedule (two game sessions with a break for lunch), how to sign up for games, and directions to the site on the JiffyCon Community blog.

Hope to see you there!
