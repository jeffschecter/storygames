\n Work on my next title, Velocity, is kicking on a pace and I've had a few interested queries on these boards so I thought I'd try creating a blog about it, just as an experiment.

Velocity is a Graffiti Punk Sci-Fi Dystopian game of loud music, free running and rebellion in a future where imagination is illegal.

Check out the Blog for a full synopsis, ongoing notes on the game's development and, as we get closer to the release date, a few sneaky peeks at what to expect inside the final product!
