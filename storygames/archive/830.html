    You could try some Carissa's Wierd, like "It Was Probably Green." I'd also suggest looking into God Speed You Black Emperor. Parts of the "Run Lola Run" soundtrack would also be awesome for this.

    Depending on the flavor of your game, La Mystere de Voix Bulgares could be perfect. Listen to "Dragana I Slavei" and tell me if it is not creepy! Fado could be good too - Afro-Portuguese blues, basically. Try "Cancao Del Mar" by Amalia Rodrigues. These will give a more "world" feel to your game though instead of having it be British/American-flavored.

    For classical, you could try Faure's "Pavane pour orchestre avec choeur" which is light but disturbingly melancholy. I'd also suggest (though I'm sure you've already thought of it) "O Fortuna" by Orff, and just about anything by Tallis (though my favorite is Spem in Alium).

    --Jess
