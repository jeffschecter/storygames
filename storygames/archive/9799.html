\n Hey,

After five years, I decided it was time to revise my game, Slayer of Dragon RPG. Plus, you know, I was laid-off. Over time, my patience with the narrative elements wore out, so I completely revamped the whole kit-and-caboodle. Second Edition is now a mighty juggernaut of kungfu powers, ready to knock down the evil reign of Overlords everywhere. For justice!

Anyway, if you have a thing for short, boardgame-like RPGs with strong narrative elements, check it out and let me know where I goofed up or just plain make no sense. The draft I am posting is just the text with minimal formatting. Any thoughtful commentary is appreciated; I will return the favor, if I can.

http://standarddeviation.cx/slayer_2_draft_090711.pdf

Best,
Grant

edit: Oh, I forgot to mention, I plan to make it available via IPR or RPGnow or something. I also have three supplements in the works. But first things first.
