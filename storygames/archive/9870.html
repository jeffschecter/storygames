\n Hey all.

Just curious, do folks use the Story Games Wiki/Codex?

If so, what sorts of things do you use it for?

I've seen excellent and mediocre Wiki implementations on the web, and even at my workplace. The excellent ones are actual reference zones, used to help people with problems. The mediocre ones tend to be "organization and publication of information just for information's sake", without actually being referenced/used much.

So, what does the Wiki do for you?

Also:
Anything you'd do (or like to see) to make the Wiki more useful?
Anything you'd suggest in general?
Also, do you have Wiki experience (upgrading, spam prevention etc) and interest in helping out either myself or the wiki admin with the wiki?

Thanks!
