    \n
    \n Call Graph

    http://scribie.com/free-skype-recorder

    It records your section as one channel and everyone else as another. Low kh rate though (16,000 instead of the usual 48,000) and you can only increase the rate by recording in MP3 format. If you are willing to use an MP3 editor (where it directly edits the file), that'll be your best bet for great sound quality.

    Since Ep 40 of Mom's Basement, we have used Call Graph and if you like our sound quality, you'll enjoy it.

    \n I currently use Call Recorder for Macs with Skype. It works fine. It records the conversation in a .mov file (audio only) that then requires conversion to .mp3 to use in editing software. I have to think there is a better way but I'm used to the process by now.
    With Windows I have used Skylook, which is great but expensive and only works within MSOutlook. I eventually switched to Call Burner, which is by the same company and does not require MSOutlook. It's also easy to use and, if I remember correctly, records as an mp3 file.
    Keep us posted regarding your search!

    \n I'll second Call Graph as an economical solution. If you're planning to post online, many people will compress the two channels to one and make it mono at a lower bit rate. That saves space and shortens download times. I use another freebie called Levelator to clean up the audio levels as well.
