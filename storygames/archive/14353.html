\n(This is a playset that I would like to offer for Go Play NW 2011. Comments and suggestions welcomed!)

Are you willing to swallow a soul-eating telepathic insect bent on destroying human civilization?

No?

Even if it will make Crane Books read your manuscript?

Setting
A suburban hotel, crammed full of neophyte writers from every corner of the continent.

Victory
The unroached character with the most status receives an $850 one-book deal with Crane Books.
The roached character with the most status is hired as an editorial assistant by Crane Books.
Everyone else continues to labor in obscurity.

Personal Die (Writerly Status / Everything Else)
Convention Volunteer / Self-Published Writer: d4 / d10
Unpublished Writer: d6 / d8
Published Author: d8/d6
Luminary: Celebrity / Bestseller / Agent / Editor: d10 / d4
