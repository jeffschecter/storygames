\n I know m20 [microlite20] might not be up everyone\u2019s ally here but I thought some folks might be interested in giving feedback on the rules presentation. Because it attracts people new to gaming I want to find a better way to present the m20 rules. What I have come up with is the following idea.

Text Color
Colors are proven to help with memory retention so I want to use different font colors for rules and fluff. By using the different colors I am hoping to help this text stand out to the reader, and by doing them in separate colors I am hoping that it will help people distinguish them apart from one another easier.

Flooooow Charts
Pretty much all rules could be broken down into separate flow charts which could help more easily connect the relationships and steps for new players.

What I would like to do is get some folks together who would not mind me emailing them a few sample pages as I finish them up so I could get some closed circle opinions on the layout before I solicit brooder audiences. If anyone would be interested in helping out you can whisper me your email and I\u2019ll make sure to pop you over a pdf of the test pages.
