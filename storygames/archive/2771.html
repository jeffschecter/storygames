    \n To remove any spaces after tables, remove any carriage returns / line breaks within the table. The forum puts in "br" tags any place there's a return, and when they're within the structure of a table, it puts them all after it (that's more a theory than certainty). It'll look like hell when you're editing the table, but it'll look nicer when displayed.
