\n I want my players to sometimes help bring old elements back into the game. Some event, some NPC, some location... Maybe couple this with some incentive.

I'm thinking of maybe having little cards saying stuff like "Reincorporate: What happened to X's mother? Bonus: Get one extra spell use".

However, "reincorporate" is such a lousy word for regular use. Any good tips for other words or terms? Preferably one-word terms...?
