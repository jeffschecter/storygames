\nI have this wacky idea for Happy Birthday, Robot! at Dreamation.

Normally, the players write the story in a little notebook or on a sheet of paper. But with this being a special occasion, I thought it would be fun to actually have a big white pad of paper on an easel in front of the table. The story would be written in big letters for the whole room to see as it develops. Then I'd take a picture to put online and give the actual sheet of paper to one lucky player as a souvenir.

HBR plays in about 30 minutes, so we could fit several sessions of play into a single 4-hour slot with different players. Before each session, I could just pitch the easel idea to the players on a case-by-case basis.

What do you think? Would that make you self-conscious?
