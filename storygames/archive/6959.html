\n So I've been working on my yet-to-be-named black ops angel game, and I'm having a serious problem coming up with a term. I'm working on the team-versus-team part of the game in which teams maneuver for advantage against each other in accomplishing opposed missions. So without going into gory details of the system (which I'm still nailing down anyway), the basic situation is this...

There are basically three pairs of opposed tactics that teams have access to in their conflicting: Truth v. Spin, Pacify v. Incite, and Violence v. [I've got no F'ing clue].

I need to figure out what the logical opposition for Violence is. And please don't say more violence, because that's already going to be a rule. (Hell, it wouldn't be black ops if you couldn't respond to violence with more violence.) I've been beating my head against a wall, but I can't shake loose any ideas on this one.

So... anyone have any suggestions? Or even any questions? I'd appreciate some feedback.

Thanks!
