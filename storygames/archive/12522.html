\n I was just curious, with the free/low cost publishing threads, of what free games you actually play: I was curious as to the modal distribution.

So think about from the first time you picked up a game until now, which free game out there do you think you have played the most?

Think hard. I'm not looking for "which is your favorite", "which do you think is the coolest/most interesting", etc (although you can follow up with that). Just "the one you played the most".

-Andy

    Posted By: migoHow do you have 0.5 sessions?
    \nI can speak to that!

    A half session can, from my experience, be any of:
    -everyone showing up, starting to play, and then realizing they weren't into it. Cut to drinks & laid-back conversation as a back-up plan.
    -getting through character creation then calling it a night.
    -getting into a rules or story dispute, and having the session devolve into a standstill, thus feeling like you didn't really do what you showed up to do.
    -having someone bail halfway through, abruptly ending the session.
    -doing a complete session, but feeling like there wasn't any magic, and like it didn't really do anything for you.
