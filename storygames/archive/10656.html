\n Hey everyone, just writing to let you know that registrations for JiffyCon Fall 09 are now up. You can find the registration page at: http://jiffycon.com/registration.html

This time, we have it set up so you can pay your entry fees online. Children under 16 enter free and you can opt to kick in for a group lunch that's vegan-, vegetarian-, and omnivore-friendly.

In case you're not familiar with what JiffyCon is (it's an RPG convention in Greenfield, MA), I'll repost the "what is this?" bit from the JiffyCon main site:

WHAT: JiffyCon is a one day mini role-playing convention. Focusing on small-press, independently published and story-oriented games, JiffyCon is a whole day devoted to nothing but having fun playing games.

Snacks and beverages will be provided. Local game designers and authors will be on hand to run and play their games. Many games will be available for sale.

Games to be run include Blowback, Labyrinths and Lycanthropes, Misspent Youth and shock: social science fiction. A full list and descriptions of the games can be found at the games page.

Fit some quality gaming into your busy schedule!
