\n We are looking for playtesters for a new game system with multiple settings in based on the Dying Earth RPG, Blurb follows. You only have to commit to one session. Each setting is predefined, but if you are brave, you can define your own for the playtest. Anyone involved will get a copy of Robin's lovely playtest guidelines. Opinions on the blurb are also welcome. If you want to playtest, either post or email simon@dyingearth.com.

SKULDUGGERY
The Roleplaying Game Of Verbal Fireworks & Sudden Reversals
by Robin D. Laws
Join Your Friends For an Evening Of Scheming, Betrayal and Temptation

Need a break from one inch squares and attacks of opportunity? Looking for an alternative when half the group bails on your in-depth saga of super-serious emoting? Seek no further than Skulduggery, Pelgrane Press\u2019 hilarious new roleplaying game of doing unto others because they\u2019re sure as heck planning to do unto you.

Skulduggery streamlines the acclaimed core rules of the Dying Earth RPG, allowing you to play quick, uproarious games of treachery and oneupmanship in any setting. Fine-tuned for one-shot and impromptu sessions, it can be adjusted for longer-running series as players demand.

\u2022\tThe tongue is sharper than the sword. Skulduggery makes verbal persuasion as suspenseful and decisive as physical combat.
\u2022\tThere\u2019s always another comeback. Don\u2019t like the results when you roll a die? Skulduggery\u2019s reversals-based resolution system lets you roll again\u2014if you can afford to pay the price...
\u2022\tCharacters in minutes. The GM hands out stacks of cards. The players trade for a few minutes. Presto: instant characters, ready to play!
\u2022\tWit wins out. Strategic deployment of dialogue snippets recharges your supply of the points you need to succeed.
\u2022\tThink you\u2019ve got it all under control? Just when they think they\u2019re ahead, characters must face their own worst enemies\u2014the all-consuming temptations driving them to distraction and doom.

Skulduggery comes with all you need to create your own settings and adventures, plus five ready-to-play scenarios:

\u2022\tThe Yes Wing: Ambition and scandal in the White House.
\u2022\tIf Space Permits: Galactic traders corner the market on psychotropic wine.
\u2022\tSkulduggery and Crossbones: Battle for succession aboard a becalmed pirate ship
\u2022\tOff With His Head: Court intrigue in a fantastical kingdom.
\u2022\tCasting Call: I\u2019ve got a barn! You\u2019ve got some old costumes! Let\u2019s put on a show!
