\n Hi, folks;

My little mash-up project, ASCII @HACK, is ready for playtesting:
Version The Latest, Now With Actual Numbers!

Basically, if you understand narration-rights economy (Universalis), trait invocation (DitV), and freeform scene setting (Red Box Hack) then you have the gist of the game and can be up and running after a few minutes of reading. There are a couple of questions at the end, for your consideration post-play (the third set of questions might be moot--I just can't think of a way to do "scoped" traits in a way that doesn't break due to either never being relevant or being too cheap or both).

Thanks!
David
