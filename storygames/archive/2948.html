\n Apropos of the recent Game Chef thoughts...

The Indie RPG Awards have come to the end of their official registration period. There are 32 games (and 11 supplements) registered -- compared to 39 games in 2003, 41 games in 2004, and 29 games in 2005. However, when I review the list, we're a bit short on free games. I think it's a bit natural that people aren't as pushy to promote free games since, well, they don't make money on them. But the Indie RPG Awards do have a special award for Free RPG, so I'd really like to get some more of them onto the list. So I'm extending things a bit to look for more qualified free entries.

So, what were the best Free RPGs of 2006?


P.S. You can post suggestion on the Indie RPG Awards Registration Form
