    \n Yeah, I was initially excited, and then read the rules. Bleh. I just posted this comment, so we'll see if there's any response:\n
    I suspect that there are a bunch of indie rpg designers that might have been excited to participate in this contest (I certainly was, originally), except the rules needlessly exclude a lot of more progressive rpg concepts and seem designed to only allow fairly traditional styles of play. That seems like a missed opportunity, since a lot of the folks who'd be most interested in designing an rpg as a board/card game are perhaps the ones least likely to adhere to super conservative ideas about what an rpg is. Oh well, I guess.
