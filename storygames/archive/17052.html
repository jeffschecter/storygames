\n We're coming back to Dragon*Con! And we even have our own space-ish, can you believe it? We've also got a ton of cool stuff coming in for giveaways from the authors of all your favorite games, both worn in and undiscovered.

You can find us in the basement of the Hilton Atlanta Hotel, or at the bottom of the elevator of the lower level.

Our website is here: https://sites.google.com/site/dcigod/home
You can see the games we'll be offering, see what GMs will be on duty when, and even volunteer if you feel so inclined. You can do this by emailing us at dragoncon.god@gmail.com

Come! Visit! Play with us!
Follow us on Twitter: https://twitter.com/DCon_GoD
We'll be posting games about to be run during the con!
