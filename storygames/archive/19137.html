    \n GM Improv idea:
    Four players, x GMs. The players stay and the GMs form a queue around the table. Every 5 minutes, the next GM in the queue takes over. This way everyone has the same table of players and the same adventure seed but they have to follow the fiction and work with what the other GMs insert into the fiction.

    Perhaps start with an elimination round where everyone present must take a turn at GMing and let the players vote on the top 4 to go on to the championship round.

      \n\nMay 2014 \n
      \n
      \n
      \n
      \n
      \n T minus One Week

      T-shirts have arrived, and they look totally awesome.

      Registration continues, but please get it done by Wednesday to avoid confusion.

      REMINDER:
      You will need to bring:
      - Bring pencils, dice, and a GAME YOU ARE READY TO RUN
      - Something to eat on (plates, bowls)
      - Something to eat with (spoons, chopsticks)
      - Bedding (you will be provided with a bare, raised mattress)
      - Something to bathe yourself with (you get access to the showers and sinks and such, but we don't provide soap or towels, etc)

      A good idea:
      - Shower shoes
      - Adaptable clothing (there's no A/C, and the weather can go either way this time of year)
      - A flashlight

      ALSO:
      - NERDLY NEEDS name-tags. If you have:
      1) something recycled and/or biodegradable
      2) something with (2) holes to hang from
      3) something about 3 inches to a side
      let me know. If you are local and wouldn't mind shopping for me and getting reimbursed, let me know.

      - OPERATION BLIND DROP. If you have one (or more) boardgames, RPG books, gamerly type things you wouldn't mind shuffling around in a pseudo-random fashion for fun, learn more here:

      http://campnerdly.wikispaces.com/Operation+Blind+Drop
