\n Hi all.

This post works just to shamelessly plug our new invention ( new = Dogui and Me ), the "Salgan Al Sol" (translation: "come out to the sun"? ) forums. They\xc2\xb4re intended as a sort of forge+storygames+something ours, a place in the net to gather and empower all the lose and lone roleplaying games designers spread through the world, be it Spain, Argentina, M\xc3\xa9xico, and anywhere else where someone be using a keyboard with an "\xc3\u2018" to write his games.

So i bring the news to you with two purposes:

One, so when later you be swamped by the flood of "el awesomo" and be willing to pay parts of your body for at least an ashcan version of the latest big thing coming from Madrid, Bs. As. or M\xc3\xa9xico ( or Chile or wherever ); when you be rushing first hour in the morning to spanish courses just to unravel the subtleties of the prose and design wisdom of our collective production; so then, you be able to point to this post and say "well, that\xc2\xb4s where it all begun".

And the more down-to-earth two, so you can pass the buzz to any other suspect of speaking our language that comes around here ( or course, now seriously, without any intention to divert members out: SG is awesome, and we hope cross-polinization to work 110% ). We already have Ren\xc3\xa9 ( elmago79 ), so we already got a chunk of that, but the more the merrier they say.


So you know now: every SG-er is welcome, even any of you gringos if you want to hone your linguistics skill an drop a few lines of your game-savvy.

Spread the word, and we\xc2\xb4ll see you when the games start flowing...

( End of plug. You may now continue your forum-ing )
