    Kuma, how do you want to do this? Do you want to wait for more recruits before some possible games are hashed out?
    Well, if everyone in this thread actually wants to participate, that's plenty for a start on PbP. Let's start talking subject matter and game system.

    I'm game for just about anything, but I was considering either a re-run of my Gods of St. Bartholomew game, which is a system-light port of Peter Adkison's Primal Order. All the players are gods of various cultures/societies that have mingled on a central archipelago. You invest in people, places and objects. Much politicking.

    I also have a game where everyone plays the head of a merchant house - building trade routes and competing for the attention of the local lords. It's a bit like Settlers of Catan meets Birthright.

    In that same vein, there's always Champions of the Gods that was just done for Game Chef. I think that'd work well for PbP, assuming that we work the kinks out of the system.

    I'd also be game for something like Dogs, PTA ... just about anything, really. If Shock: were out (and in my sweaty hands) I'd suggest that. We could also do a sort of PbP meets Frankengame thing where we start with subject matter and everyone throws out a mechanic. Really, with this crowd, we're looking at an embarassment of riches in terms of what we can do with PbP - up to and including crafting a PbP game tailored to the medium.

    Or if you don't want to do much thinking, we can always just use Storyteller or play Buffy or Angel - (no Unisystem, please).

    So if everyone wants to pitch out some rough concepts for preferences, we can get to deciding. I'll let y'all know when the forum is set up so we can transfer the conversation there. It'll be tonight at some point.
