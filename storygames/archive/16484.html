    \nGHOSTS

    Heroic Threats has four versions of ghosts: the Poltergeist, a level 2 Elite Artillery, the Banshee, a level 7 Elite Controller, the Hanged Man, a level 8 Elite Skirmisher, and the Burning Man, a level 9 elite Brute.

    The Poltergeist uses Flurry of Debris, which only does 1d4 +4 damage, but creates a zone. The Poltergeist can spend a minor action to do 1d4 damage to all creatures in its Flurry of Debris zone, no attack roll needed. Better move quick!

    The Heroic Threats Banshee is a ghost of a vengeful woman, and has powers to suit. Whenever she's marked, the marker takes 5 damage. If she's not marked, she can force enemies to mark her when they hit her- causing them to take damage. And she can teleport 10 at will, as long as she ends next to someone who is marking her.

    The Hanged Man is an innocent man, hung for crimes he did not commit. His Choking Grip grabs a target, after which he can follow it up with his more damaging Crushing Grip, or worse, tie you up with his Hangman's Noose and leave you restrained and taking ongoing 5 damage until escape while he goes and fights the rest of the party. Also he can pull his grabbed targets with him.

    Finally, the Burned Man, who was burned alive, comes back with his powerful burning fists, and scorching enemies with its Body of Fire aura, which deals 5 damage to adjacent enemies each turn. Luckily, the Burning Man is vulnerable to cold damage, and cold damage also shuts off his Body of Fire aura for a turn. Unluckily, when reduced to 0 hp, he blows up.
