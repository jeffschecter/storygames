\n Forge Midwest 2014 will be held April 4th, 5th, and 6th, at the Best Western East Towne Suites in Madison, WI.

To reserve your room, call 608-244-2020. Room rates are $69 a night.

As always, con attendance is FREE! If you enjoy your time, please consider a donation to help pay for the meeting room rental space.

To find out more about Forge Midwest, visit the Forge Midwest blog: http://forgemidwest.wordpress.com
