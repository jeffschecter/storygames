    Well, most files get uploaded to one place, and have a limited number of trackers, but those trackers make it to many sites. Having never uploaded anything, I don't know how it works. Regardless, you'll want to send people the file, the tracker, and have as many people as possible upload (seed) the file, so when people look for a file to download, this will be the one they download...

    Also, uploading to megaupload/mediafire/rapidshare will help, as there are search engines for those sites.

    I will upload, but being being a firewall, you'll need other people to actually seed it for you after, when I send you guys the tracker address.

    Alternatives, if you do need to upload it on several sites: The Pirate Bay, IsoHunt, MiniNova, there are new sites all the time, TorrentReactor... lists are endless, go to a couple of the big ones and trust it to happen.

    Edit: I see you linked to the file, I'll work on uploading. Demonoid opens for registration once every month/years.
