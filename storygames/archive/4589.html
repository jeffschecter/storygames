\n Hey all you folks who have mad Japan skills,

I'm starting a Blossoms Are Falling game soon, and my players want to cast their characters. However, so far we're having trouble finding good pictures of Japanese stars with our weak search-Japanese skills. We've found lots of names, and even some faces that fit, but we're looking for pictures that aren't clearly modern (if portraits) or for the bonus, have period garb on (if full shot).

So do any of you have some good sources for pictures of Japanese stars?

    \n It's unfortunately not easy. There'd not really a good place to find all sorts of jidaigeki (historical drama) pictures in one place.

    What I would have to do (as a Japanese speaker) is go to the website of a channel, find the page for a samurai drama, and look at the page from there.

    Ex:

    http://www3.nhk.or.jp/taiga/cast/cast.html# (click on names to see pics)

    http://www.nhk.or.jp/jidaigeki/kazenohate/html_kz_cast.html

    http://www.nhk.or.jp/drama/archives/natsukumo/html_nk_cast.html

    http://www.nhk.or.jp/drama/archives/keijirou/html_kei_cast.html

    http://www.nhk.or.jp/drama/archives/kagerou/html_kg_cast.html

    http://www.tv-asahi.co.jp/kinsan/

    http://www3.nhk.or.jp/asadora/cast/cast.html#

    If you find these kinds of pages, look for the kanji:
    \xe7\u2122\xbb\xe5\xa0\xb4\xe4\xba\xba\xe7\u2030\xa9
    Which essentially means "Cast of Characters"

    Also, with the increase of satellite TV, there's now a "Jidaigeki channel", All Samurai. All The Time.

    http://www.jidaigeki.com/

    From this page, pretty much any pictured object you click on leads to an individual drama page.
    Also, if you click on \xe3\u201a\xaa\xe3\u201a\xb9\xe3\u201a\xb9\xe3\u0192\xa1 (at the top it says HOME, the link to the right of that) it pulls up a menu of selected programs, each with their own page with pics.

    Also, the October/Nov schedule (again, wtih pics that lead to pages) is here:
    http://www.jidaigeki.com/itiran/0710.html
    http://www.jidaigeki.com/itiran/0711.html

    Hope that helps to start out with.
