    \n Josh Roby,

    Fribblefrabble is not something that can be inserted into a design. It must be teased subtly out of the woodwork, only felt in the lingering aftermath. Fribblefrabble is more than just the sum of its parts (fribble + frabble) and cannot be achieved merely by having both in the same design. Look to RIFTS or Blue Rose for examples of this method being approached and failing.

    To achieve a sense of full and cathartic Fribblefrabble, I suggest taking all of that "kewl" out of your current design. Replace that "clever" die mechanic with something simple and unimpressive. Instead of explicit stakes, angle towards visible-but-implicit intent. Play it thoroughly, and see what's lacking. And then strip away what remains so that those things are lacking to a greater extent. And in that fruitful void, you will see fribblefrabble emerge.
