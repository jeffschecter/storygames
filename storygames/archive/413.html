I plan on getting Stardeck's 5-Suit deck for use in my CRPG card game that I'm designing. However, if it were to ever go to print or pdf, I'd want to include some ideas on how to cannibalize a deck to add in a fifth suit. How best to do this?

My current imaginings would be the cards would be used PTA style, drawing for the conflict, then discarding when the conflict was resolved.
  • shreyas

    You can make four additional suits!

    Two permanent markers, black and red.

    Give the Heart a curling stem to make a Leaf.
    Add a handle to the Diamond to make a Dagger.
    Round the bottom of the Club to make a Clover.
    Spike the lobes of the Spade to make an Arrowhead.
