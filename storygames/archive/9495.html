\n So, I recently felt the need to come up with some good meaty place-names for a pulp fantasy game I'm hoping to run someday, and I came up with the idea of a name generator. It's far from complete, and I haven't figured out -exactly- how I'm going to work it yet, but this is what I have so far.

The Oracle of Bloody, Filthy, Pulpy Places

Table 1: adjective
Ruined
Haunted
Lost
Forbidden
Cursed
Forsaken
Decadent
Mysterious
Storm-wracked

Table 2: noun
Cathedral
Temple
Tower
Citadel
Fortress
Isle/Island
Palace
Prison
Cemetery
Graveyard
Crypt
Tomb
Sepulchre
Desert
Necropolis
City
Crossroads

Table 3: more description, please!
of Blood
of Beasts
of Swords
of Daggers
of the Night
of the God-King/Queen/Emperor
of the Sorcerer
of the Stars

I'm looking for suggestions for new entries (hopefully the entries I've got are suggestive of what I'm going for) and ideas as to structure.

Specific questions:
1) Should Crypt, Tomb, and Sepulchre be one entry as an option like Isle/Island, or are they distinct enough to have their own entries?
2) Should the final table include [nothing] as a possible result for Tables 1 and 3, or is the excess of descriptors appropriate for the feel of pulp fantasy?
