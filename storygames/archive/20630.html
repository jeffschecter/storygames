\n Hello everyone!
The four chapter demo is available at http://www.charlesparkes.com

\u2018The Aegis Saga - Blood\u2019 is the first in an intended series of three fantasy stories playing out on the bayeux tapestry-like world of Eris.

The Aegis is the comet above Eris, upon whose passage the turn of time is marked for those on the world below. It has religious significance too for those who wield magic \u2013 or \u2018glyf\u2019, though be warned that such power is not used freely on Eris. \u2018Glyf\u2019 risks your life and the lives of those you love around you by threatening the fabric of the world of Eris itself. So tread lightly.

The tale is composed as a delineated choice-driven life story told in the first person. Death is not around every corner on Eris \u2013 \u2018The Aegis Saga\u2019 is intended to be enjoyed as a form of interactive novel that you reach the end of. I hope you will find a deep story fabric to fall into and enjoy, filled with danger, beauty, choice and friendships. I\u2019ve certainly had much joy writing it . . .
