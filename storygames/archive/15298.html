\n Hey all!



The Kickstarter for The Play's The Thing, my entry into the Game Chef 2011 competition, is now live. In fact, it's already at 200%!

The Play's The Thing is a storytelling game about actors who want to rewrite the Shakespearean roles they've been given. You and your friends tell the story of an acting troupe that is scheduled to perform a new play, but has a lot of disagreements about how that play should go.

At this point, we're looking at trying to get up to 300% funding, the place where we start shipping copies of the game to public high school teachers for use in the classroom. If you like Shakespeare (or story games about actors), please check it out!
