    \n No Cons or big events in Mexico. No regular gaming clubs or the like either.

    There are plenty of gamers here and there (mainly in the big important cities, such as Monterrey, Mexico city or Guadalajara), and sometimes there are small gaming events. Most Mexican gamers are pretty much into trad games, and very few are even aware of the existance of the indie stuff.

    Some stores do sponsor the D-Day too (although I can't recall when will it be).

    \n Uruguay: There are gaming pubs and clubs. Yes, Pundit is here and runs some excellent games. I'm here too. ;-)

    Argentina: I know there are some clubs, but I haven't visited them. Try your luck at hobby stores like Entelequia or El Club del Comic.

    Brazil: Back when I visited Curitiba in 2001, there was an excellent store selling RPGs, CCGs, and board games. They also had space for LAN parties, IIRC.
