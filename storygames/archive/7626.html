    \n Doing a full day Saturday:

    In A Wicked Age - Day/Time: Saturday 09:00 AM - 01:00 PM In Game Room 1 (Colonial)
    Adventure Description: Bring nothing but your dice, a pencil, and your imagination! In A Wicked Age is an independently published RPG by Vincent Baker in which we all, as players and GM, come up with four "Oracles" which, in turn, create the principle characters, settings, and initial situation of the game. The system is highly narrative, rather than simulationist--it resolves who has narrative control NOT how well a given action succeeds or fails (though it eventually works that out as well, as part of telling the story). Though IAWA supports on-going play, we will probably only have time for one Chapter.
    In a Wicked Age involves pulling cards to establish not only the scene and (some of) the situation, but also to determine what characters are in it. Character generation will take place at the table.

    ASCII @HACK Official Playtest - Day/Time: Saturday 01:30 PM - 05:30 PM In Game Room 1 (Colonial)
    Adventure Description: ASCII @HACK is an RPG that simulates roguelike game play, but using player empowerment, resource allocation, bidding, and instantiation to create the "random" adventuring zones for a single PC. All other players represent that Adventurer`s Opposition, and they are in a push-pull of competition and cooperation to be the one whose Obstacle kills the Adventurer before the Adventurer completes the Final Zone. There`s no GM--everyone is in on the game and has as much control over the setting and situation as their Tokens allow.
    Note that this is a playtest of a work-in-progress. That means there might be some bumps in the road... but the up-side is that YOU could help fill in those bumps, making the game better with your ideas (and receiving credit for such, upon final publication). To become familiar with the game in advance (not necessary), refer to http://davidartman.com/games/ascii-hack. Some character generation will take place at the table.

    Long Live the King The king is dead... - Day/Time: Saturday 08:00 PM - 12:00 AM In Hotel Bar (woot!)
    Long Live the King: The king is dead... Finally! Through a skillful combination of live-action intrigue and strategic use of resources, you might become the next ruler of the land. Long Live the King is a live-action strategy game for 5 or more players. Long Live the King is a dynamic game that marries the diabolical treachery and scheming of the best strategic board and card games with the pomp and circumstance of the most elaborate live-action games. In the role of an ailing king, the gamemaster participates in the roleplaying while moderating the rules. Up to seven other major players, including a young queen, a haughty archbishop and a wily ambassador, vie to place themselves in positions to rule the kingdom when the king finally keels over. Dozens of minor roles allow the game to be as large and elaborate as you desire. Or just keep it small... and cut-throat.

    Hope to see y'all there. I'll be hunting down something to do Friday night and Sunday (might bail Sun, though).
