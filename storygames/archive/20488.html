\n Hi all. Is there anybody interested in playing an hangout game of Pride and Prejudice for two players?
http://www.lulu.com/shop/roberto-grassi/pride-and-prejudice-a-narration-game/hardcover/product-21755169.html
A narration game for two players building a story around two characters, trying to push it in favour of one or another character by only using "pride and prejudice" statements.
A nice and funny game of around 60 minutes (in case the players decide to narrate six scenes) or less.
No predefined setting or characters. The story builds up through the players narration.
Rob
