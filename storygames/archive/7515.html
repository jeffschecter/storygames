    \n It's probably not the specific thing you were thinking of - but I played in "Tricolor d'Amour", a game by Malik Hyltoft. In that game, which was set in the time of the French revolution, each player was a married man or woman - with a lover. The whole game was about writing letters to your spouse, and to your lover, thus keeping two conversations going at the same time - essentially, leading two lives. It was great fun, and I'd love to play it (or something similar) again.
