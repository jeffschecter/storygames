    \n Mostly I'm thinking "get a handful of players together and play something vaguely HotB-shaped." Given how the game plays, most of it can just be freeplay of people tweeting whenever, throughout the day. When rolls are called for... I dunno how we'll jigger it, but there's tons of options.

    Given my predilections, I'll probably run it by introducing conflicts and then shoving PCs onto both sides and watching what happens (rather than creating and running a lot of heavy-handed NPC antagonists).

    I'd like to run with a sort of family & local feudal politics slant to it, but I'm happy to be overruled by the players there.
