Out of a love for the classic, iconic style of art used inside RPGs that I have had for almost my entire life, I have begun a project to help independent game developers afford high quality CUSTOM illustrations.

These are monthly compendiums of 20 black and white character illustrations created from character submissions and the results of the input of those that support it. Additionally, they come with an indefinite license to use them on your projects.

I am trying a new type of business model with these illustrations and would love your input on them. Check out the details HERE.
