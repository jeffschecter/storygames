So, blogging people of SG, can you help me? Any software that you can recommend? Any that I should absolutely avoid?

    A lot depends on what you need. Do you just need software to simplify turning your writing into dated posts and keeping them organized? Do you need to be able to do this remotely, on any computer available? Do you need a comment system?

    \n

    If you just need the first, uploading a TiddlyWiki might be fine. Another option would be client-side blogging software, like BlogMax (an Emacs extension I used for a while.) I've thought about just installing Markdown as a text translator, then writing a simple CMS in PerlHP, which I enjoy for no good reason other than it seems easy to code.

    \n

    The second and third options usually require more elaborate software, and I'm not up on all the variations, because I haven't needed them. I know you could add comments to TiddlyWiki and to a PerlHP-based solution, and TiddlyWiki also has some server-side adaptations that let you update the site remotely.
