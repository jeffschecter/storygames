\n Hi! I'm Rafael! I started posting here about 8 years ago. I designed an RPG called Pandemonio (formerly released as two separate games -- Dread: The First Book of Pandemonium and Spite: The Second Book of Pandemonium), and a video-chat larp called ViewScream. I also made a dark sci-fi Fiasco playset called The White Hole (it's a lot of fun, you should play it).

I got a new thing in the oven. It's a bestiary for OSR games, and it's called Lusus Naturae (Latin for "freaks of nature"). It's pretty nasty!

For this monstrous manual, I'm delving into the myths of my Peruvian ancestors:

* Machukuna, the ancient ones who saw their doom when the Sun was created, and fled to deep waters (where they wait);
* Sarara, a feathered horror summoned from the darkest pits of the underworld by foolhardy sorcerers;
* Chuyanchaki, shapeshifters who always forget one important detail;
* Supay, lord of death, who rules over all demons from his throne in Ukhupacha;

The project funded on day 3, and we're heading for the stretch goal of full-color illustration. If you like horror in your dungeon-crawling, and you're cool with old-school stats like hit points and armor class, then you'll want to check Lusus Naturae out!

https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/rafaelchandler/lusus-naturae-a-gruesome-old-school-bestiary

Questions? Ask me. I like to chatter.
