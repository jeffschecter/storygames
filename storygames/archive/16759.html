\n Hi all: Andy here.

I'm tightening up the usability on this site, and that requires a few things:

1) Usernames will have an underscore.
Usernames with spaces in them ("Andy K") will not really function with the system; you can't Ignore those users with the Ignore feature, you can't quote them properly, etc.
So, over the next few weeks I'll be changing your userids, adding an underscore ("_") where I see spaces: "Andy_K", etc.
Currently, you don't have the ability to change your userid. If you would rather have another userid, or simply have no space in your userid ("AndyK"), whisper or email me and I'll make it happen.
But if you suddenly can't log in, it may be that. Try your email or username with an underscore if you suddenly cannot log in.
As always, if you have login issues email me at storygames ATMARK gmail.

2) Email changes
In your account ("Edit Account"), there is an email associated with your account.
Unfortunately, due to a bug, you can't change your email at this time (looking into why). If you change your email account and hit "Save", the little "Loading" wheel will roll forever and you will explode in fire.
So if you check your account, and you want to use another email address than the one that is there, again whisper me here or email me at above.

3) First and Last Names: You need to add them.
Click on your userid on the top menu, and on the left side will be an "Edit My Account" link.
Go there and add your First and Last names.
As always, if you have privacy or safety concerns, you can use a pseudonym or alias there and there's nothing wrong with that. But please put something there for First and Last name. Many of us (me included) like to be able to call people by their Real Name, or their internet equivalent of that.
NOTE: When changing your First/Last Names, then hitting Save, JUST change your name; if you try to change your email address too you'll get the freeze mentioned in step 2)
In about a week or two, the folks who have not updated theirs manually will have them updated by us if we know who you are (or left blank if we don't).

4) Gender
For some reason, the Vanilla 2 developers decided that asking people what their real names are is dumb, but confirming their gender is very relevant.
What? That's bullshit. (note: Very fond of the Van2 developers, but there was definitely some misfires)
Currently there's a radio button in your account which confirms you as Male or Female. New users have to pick one (or "Undeclared").
Don't sweat this, this will change. Although instead of getting rid of that radio button, I'm thinking of changing it to something else instead:
"Native English Speaker; Non-Native English Speaker"
"Coffee; Mountain Dew"
"Milli; Vanilli"
"Gamist; Narrativist" (Simulationists don't really exist and thus will not be an option)
"Butter side up; Butter side down"
"Luke Crane; Jared Sorensen"
"Krispy Kreme; Dunkin Donuts" (and regularly the database will purge all users who selected Krispy Kremes over Dunkin Donuts)
If you have another suggestion, feel free to hit me up.

-Andy
