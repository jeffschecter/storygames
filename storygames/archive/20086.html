\n "Once upon a time..." Telling stories with image cubes by Rory's Story Cubes. Have you ever looked at one of the die and said to yourself "what is that?" Maybe you look at the image and keep coming up with the same idea each time or even draw a blank as to what it could mean. I have.

The below link is a unoffical pdf that I made to help me and you come up with ideas for the images on Rory's Story Cubes Original Set. This is just a starting point. DO NOT use this document if you don't need it or can come up with a meaning of the image on your own. My intention is not to define them but create a resource of possibilities JUST in case one draws a blank or keeps using the same old idea over and over again. I left space to write in more ideas when you come up with them during a session.
https://innerandout.files.wordpress.com/2015/03/rorys-story-cubes-meaning-original-set.pdf

I want your help. Look at the images. Read my interpretation. Then if you have a new one please let me know so I may include it in originial set document version update.

I will be working on Rory's Story Cubes Meanings - Enchanted Set.
