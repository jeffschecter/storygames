\n Hi folks, over on RPG.net I asked the question, 'Lovecraft's Mountains of Madness would be awesome if it was statted in....' with a poll. Interesting to see the big response for Fate and Savage Worlds, Trail of Cthulhu of course and many more.
\n
\nWhilst Chaosium did a thorough job on the Lovecraft book, I'm planning to do a dark and pulpy WW2 take on the story with an expedition hot on the heel's of a Nazi group. I wondered what you felt would be a strong system. Of course CoC has been there and done it but some people don't like that system so which other's should I consider?
