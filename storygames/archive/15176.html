\n I have a limited schedule for BigBadCon (in Oakland) this weekend. So instead of signing up for games, I'm going to spend time in the Open Gaming room when I am there.

The con is relatively story-gaming focused, so I'll assume there won't be much trouble finding 2-5 people for games.

I'm bringing Dungeon World Red Book, and Fiasco with as many playsets as I can carry. I'll bring Archipelago 2, but not sure how well it will shine in two hours. If I can find a version of Danger Patrol online I'll definitely bring that.

But what else should I bring? Bonuses if:
-It can be played in under two hours.
-The rules are available online
-Little or no prep-time
-It's either GM-less or very easy to pick up and run.

I'm usually very open to play testing new rules when I have time to prepare. But for this weekend I'd like something I can just pull out and run or play as my time is limited. The more rules-light the better for me.

Ideas? Feel free to add links.
