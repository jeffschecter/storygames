\n There's a Bliss Stage computer game in the works. There are only a few days left in the Kickstarter. If you contribute at a level to get a copy of the tabletop game, you get a copy of the upcoming second edition, not a pdf of the old one, so that's a nice way to get it for people who weren't around for the original Bliss Stage push. The computer game is a "visual novel", which is like a first-person CYOA with way more dialog and description, though it's naturally spliced in the tabletop game's rules.
