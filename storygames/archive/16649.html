    \n J. Walt - the book is going to have Vincent's "Powered by the Apocalypse" mark in it and we're definitely mentioning Tony in the Special Thanks section of the final game (obviously). Originally, Vincent was going to provide us with a custom AW class to go with the kickstarter but scheduling limitations ended up making that not a feasible project for him. If we'd gone that route, obviously the tie-in would have been more obvious. Without it, we've actually found that trying to explain the connection tends to really confuse people more than help them understand the game. They start to think it's an expansion for Apocalypse World, etc or that they need AW to "make it work". We shied away from over-explaining things in the Kickstarter. We're definitely all about giving credit where it's due (any interview with us we make a point of talking it up, the stuff I said above about the book, etc etc) but for people new to the concept of the game, it's been more a hindrance to understanding than a help.

    That said, no reason not to chuck a blurb about it in the FAQ so as to prevent such confusion if it comes up!

    \n I'm open to most or all of the text of "Dark Heart" being CC-BY, though the PDF itself will only be made available to backers, at least initially. The layout will stay mine and the cover image is copyright Jenn Rodgers. Since this is a special thing for backers, I just want to avoid a situation where somebody takes the full text and immediately posts it on the internet, since that makes it less of a reward. But a few months down the road, that seems less of a problem and something I could easily do. I'm drafting layout for "Dark Heart" specifically with a future small (200 copy?) print run in mind, but that won't happen until I'm satisfied that any kinks have been worked out in playtesting (plus I'd have to get Vincent's permission for that, since "Dark Heart" borrows some from AW in addition to DW).
