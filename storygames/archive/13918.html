    Posted By: jim pintoWhat's the recommend method on story-games for recruiting RPG playtesters?
    \nJim, it's my understanding that it's totally fine to ask for playtesters here.
    Do so in the "Directed Promotion" category.

    It's best to start off with 2-3 paragraphs of description, including: what your game is about, what the system is like, what you're looking for from playtesters, how far along in development it is.
