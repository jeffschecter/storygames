\n Council of Five Nations is the SWA's annual gaming convention, held every year on Columbus Day Weekend in October. It is one of the longest-running adventure gaming conventions in the world. We'll be sponsoring our 36th Council on October 11-13, 2013.

We have a full slate of roleplaying, boardgames, and miniatures games scheduled. For more information (and our pre-registration booklet listing offered games), please check out:

http://www.swa-gaming.org/node/26
