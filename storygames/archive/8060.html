\n I promised a while back to get out a pdf version of the Solar System - it's been taking me a while due to all sorts of ancillary delays, but I now have relaid it in landscape format and am in the process of adding some bookmarks and such.

I haven't published anything in PDF format before, and I'm not much for buying pdf stuff myself, so I want somebody with solid experience in being a pdf customer to look at my file and tell me if they find it somehow lacking - whether there is some usability issues, or something more I could do with the file to make it better for its purpose. There could be any sort of stupid flaws that I can't be arsed to check for myself starting with missing pages and ending with some sort of font problems, so it's best if such an usability tester would bother to outright read the booklet through from the pdf, or at least most of it. That way you can also tell me if the font size and landscape layout work in screen reading, or whether I need to improve the basic solutions somehow before starting to sell the file.

If you want to help out, let me know your email address, and I'll send the file over once I've finished tinkering with the bookmarks. I'll be happy with 1-3 different people looking this over, the rest of the possibly interested folks get to pay me for my bother later - I'll make the file available once I've been told that it works fine.
