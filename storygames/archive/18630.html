Hey guys, I just wanted to share my latest creation Disaster Strikes!, a story game that allows you to play through a classic disaster movie. Think The Towering Inferno, The Poseidon Adventure, Jurassic Park or Aliens.

You set up a threat, escalate it to boiling point and then struggle to get out alive. Your Protagonists are the only ones brave, competent and heroic enough to save countless bystanders from their doom!

If you like the sound of that, go check it out. It's free to download.
