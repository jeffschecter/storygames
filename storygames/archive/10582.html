\n One of the designers/writers is going to be at CharCon in Charleston W.Va. at the end of October and I am planning on doing an interview. It sounds a lot like an old skool D&D style game, but with a percentile system for skills, mostly uses d20 for opposed rolls (you roll, I roll, see who is higher), has some insane number of hit locations for combat and spends a lot of time talking about armor.

That's what I got from the Web site. Can anyone tell me more?
