\n Go Play Manchester is a monthly meetup for RPGers in Central Manchester UK.
\n
\nThe thinking behind this is that as nice as browsing the internet, posting on rpg-forums is, at the end of the day Roleplaying games are a face to face over a tabletop pastime.
\n
\nSo if you've dropped out of the hobby or haven't got a group it would be a chance to meet people in existing groups and get in a regular game.
\n
\nIf you have a regular game that is looking for new players it's a chance to play with potential recruits and check that you both get on, before inviting them to your home.
\n
\nIt's also a chance to socialise and play different games. I'll be encouraging GMs to run one shots rather than campaigns, because I don't want the meet up to become closed to new comers as people come a couple of times get into a cliche and sit with the same people, playing the same campaign everytime. Personally I also like the idea of running one shots because I've lots of games that I haven't time to run with my home group.
