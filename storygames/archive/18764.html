\n This may be a bit off topic, but I thought many of you might be interested. Our Kickstarter features novels and short stories by many RPG authors that you're all likely familiar with. There's only a few days left.

It's full of wonderfully weird tales from many wonderful writers: Richard Pett, Clinton Boomer, Dave Gross, Elaine Cunningham, Erik Scott De Bie, Shanna Germain, and more.

Richard Pett creeps you out with Crooked, weird horror set in a pseudo-Victorian and phantasmagorical fantasy world. It's equal parts Lovecraft and Conan-Doyle. Steampunk? Maybe, but I prefer to think of it as corpsepunk.

And then Clinton Boomer takes you on a retro-post-apocalyptic adventure in Soapscum Unlimited and unveils a superhero mystery (with co-writer Stephen Norton) in Questions.

Then there's the wonderfully twisted dark fantasy anthology By Faerie Light, featuring many of the authors you already love: Dave Gross, Jennifer Brozek, James Sutter, Elaine Cunningham, Erin Hoffman, Shanna Germain, Cat Rambo, Lillian Cohen-Moore, Erik Scott de Bie, Ed Greenwood, and more!

Erik Scott de Bie, author of five D&D novels, has joined the crew with a new novel stretch goal. His Scourge of the Realm is a twisted fantasy world. The villain lies defeated and the heroes have ascended, but what happens when evil lingers and turns the champions of light into something far worse?

Plus, there are more goodies planned for Clinton Boomer's fan-favorite urban fantasy tale The Hole Behind Midnight: illustrations by Hugo Solis, an audiobook, new stories by Boomer, and more!

Let us keep you stocked with awesome reading throughout the next year!
