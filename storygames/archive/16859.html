\n There are a fair number of folks on Story Games whom I presume will be interested in the following:

I am interviewing the game scholars who worked with Bill White and myself on the Immersive Gameplay book. They are interesting, erudite people, and many are game designers and players like yourselves.

Every week from July through September, I will be posting a new interview with one of them.

Today's interview: Sarah Lynne Bowman

Look to this thread every week for a new interview.

Upcoming...

Emily Care Boss
J. Tuomas Harviainen
Todd Nicholas Fuist
Jussi Holopainen
Nathan Hook

... and more
