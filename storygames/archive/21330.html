\n I have a simple question that I haven't been able to find the answer to either on forums, reviews and the rule book that I read. A turn consist of three phases: 1. drawing a card & execution its instructions, 2. decrementing project dice, 3. performing an action. If a card says "start a project", during which phase is that project started and does the project count as the action for that player or does the player get to choose an action regardless of what the card instructs?

    \n The card instructions are in addition to your normal Phase-3 actions.

    Also, if you Start a Project because of a card effect, that project does not decrease its die rating during Phase-2 immediately following its placement on the map. This way it just counts as one extra project, and not as one extra and super fast project
