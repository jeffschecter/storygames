\n "Once upon a time..." Telling stories with image cubes by Rory's Story Cubes. Have you ever looked at one of the die and said to yourself "what is that?" Maybe you look at the image and keep coming up with the same idea each time or even draw a blank as to what it could mean. I have.

The below link is a unoffical pdf that I made to help me and you come up with ideas for the images on Rory's Story Cubes Clue Set. This is just a starting point.
https://innerandout.files.wordpress.com/2015/03/rorys-story-cubes-meaning-clue-set.pdf

I want your help. Look at the images. Read my interpretation. Then if you have a new one please let me know so I may include it in clue set document version update.

I am presently working on Rory's Story Cubes Meanings - Original Set. The first version is almost finished.

    \n I have this set!
    For the DNA: Traces -- Evidence -- Body parts -- Memory -- Copying -- Changing
    For the face: Investigator -- Examinator -- Detective -- Carefulness

    I had the same idea once (writing up different ideas for the faces) but I never did it because I came up with another solution:
    Before I roll, I ask a very specific question.

    Like, "What was her mom doing out in the boat?"
    The chalk outline came up, and my brain then went to:
    "She discovered someone's dead body!"
    Yeah, a pretty obvious one but that's fine sometimes
