    \n Right now I'm GMing a game set at the edge of Traveller's Third Imperium using the Sorcerer rules.

    The PCs are three ex-marines who now run a merc company. They got hired to return to a planet where they abandoned rebels at the Imperium's command years ago, this time to put the rebels down for good, but they arrive to find the planet is now the beach head for a religious holy war backed by actual angels promising to bring peace to the stars. It's turning into a cross between Dune and Battlestar Galactica.

    Next up is Don't Rest Your Head, which one of my players is itching to GM -- and given Eric's imagination, I think it's a perfect fit and can't wait to play.
