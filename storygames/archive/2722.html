    \n Replying to myself... running 3.5 hours in the RPG gaming area, at the following times:
    \nThursday 1pm, Sunday 1pm.
    \n
    \nBlurb:
    \n'Toccata and Fur in A Minor'
    \nThere are two means of refuge from the miseries of life - music and cats.- Albert Schweitzer. A new family has moved into the neighborhood, and from the melancholy music you hear it seems that not all is well. Misery and Boggins - always together, and you're just the cats to deal with both. 3-6 players, all ages, family friendly, characters provided, rules taught.
    \n
    \nCat is set in a mythic suburbia where the player characters are the feline pets that are also our secret guardians, keeping all humans safe from an unseen world of gremlins and evil. This is a simple system and a fun game that encourages family play and all age groups' participation.
