\n Ladies and Gentlemen,

We've hit a bit of a snag. Without getting into too many details (mostly because I don't understand all of them myself) the folks that own the lakehouse have had some serious health issues in the family and the lakehouse has changed hands. In other words, someone else now owns the damn thing. I don't know the new owners at all and thus my arrangement with the previous owner is out the window. I didn't get called about this until today or I would have communicated it to everyone earlier. I know this sucks but in my defense I've called to verify that we had the house every few weeks since I first announced the meetup and this is the first time this stuff came up.

Anywho, I'm currently looking at options for us if we still want to get together. There is a pretty awesome cabin in the Smokey Mountains here that is available. The good news is that it is actually a nicer facility than the lakehouse and has more beds. Thus less people in sleeping bags on couches. The bad news is that it is no longer free. The cost divided between all the folks currently signed up would be $35 per person. That would be total... not per night.

I'm trying to find other options also. I might be able to find another place free of charge but I'm not sure. I've only had an hour or so at this point to make inquiries. If I can find another place for free, that'll be awesome but I can't guarantee that at this point.

Here's what I need from people who have signed up for this. Is the $35 an event killer for you? If it is, is there any price under $35 that wouldn't be an event killer (other than $0)?

I'm sorry for this guys but this is something that was way out of my control. I'll keep you informed and see if I can get something else lined up.
