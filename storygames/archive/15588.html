\n I've wanted to play in a Smallville game since pretty much five minutes before I finished reading the rules. Unfortunately, I may be the only person in the Oklahoma City area who feels this way. I was so desperate I decided to run one, sorta got one going with a group of mostly brand new gamers (only ones I could convince), failed miserably at it (learned a lot, though), but can't convince my regular group to give it a go.

So I think getting into a game locally is pretty much a bust. And I don't want to start one until I know I can make it awesome. I feel like playing in an awesome campaign will help with that, especially after seeing how Jason Corley's has come together for him. Lots of meat I can take from a table like that and bring back to my own.

So, to get to the point, anybody running a Smallville game via skype or thinking about starting one? If so, does it have room for one more?
