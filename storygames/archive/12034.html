I've hosted three successful mini-cons in the Washington, DC, area over the last few years, and I've created a Google Group now to help me manage it. If you've come out before, or want to know when the next one is, head over to

http://groups.google.com/group/ice-station-nerdly?hl=en

and ask to join. If you can't do that, whisper me your e-mail address and I'll send you an invite.

If you want to see posts about earlier Ice Stations, just do a quick search on the boards here.
