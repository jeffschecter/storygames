    It uses the set up of MNPR. (You're one of them, you fight stuff, comedy/violence ensues.) It uses a version of the Dogs conflict system.

    Here's a link to his modications, along with NPC Dracula. The main thing he really kept was the rhythm of bringing in traits into a conflict; he added caps to the die types and die sizes, and added a "Weaknesses" feature which are traits that other people can use to get dice.

    So perhaps this is more inspired by the Dogs resolution system at this point, rather than actually being a full port. But I'm told it was enjoyable.
