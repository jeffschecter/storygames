\n
\n A new year, a new podcast. A few friends of mine and I have started a new podcast called Actual People, Actual Play where we discuss our experiences with the game we are playing. We use the term actual play not to mean recordings of play sessions, but more the way the term is used on discussion forums. We put an emphasis on how a game's rules affect our creative process.
\n
\nWhat I wanted do with this show is combine my favorite parts of The Walking Eye podcast and the KTNG recaps from the Podgecast. So, I would like to thank the people on those shows for their inspiration.
\n
\nSince we just started a Burning Empires campaign, the bulk of our shows will be a discussion of Burning Empires. We will cover other games as well. For example, in Episode 3 we will cover The Shab-Al-Hiri Roach.
\n
\nPlease check out our Show. You can find it at: http://apap.libsyn.com/
