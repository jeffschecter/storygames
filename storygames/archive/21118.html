    \n I'd expect the rules of a game about starships to be clear and explicit about starship creation, but if they aren't you should decide for yourself instead of appealing to authority.

    Are 6 modules right for an adequate ship in your game, with the appropriate balance between effectiveness and limitations? What would change with more or less modules?

    Is 4+2 the right balance between obvious common systems and peculiar playbook-specific systems? Would more or less specialized ships offer more fun for some reason?

    Maybe some options or combinations of options should be mandatory or forbidden: what cases of bad starships do you want to avoid? What constraints are the best way to exclude them?
