\n Milton Keynes RPG Club are holding another of their games days on 19 March 2011.
\n
\nWHO?
\nWe're Milton Keynes RPG Club. Find out more about us at http://www.mk-rpg.org.uk.
\n
\nWHEN?
\nSaturday 19 March 2011. Doors open at 9.00am, first games start at 10.00am. We'll finish at 11.00pm.
\n
\n(Concrete Cow 11\xbd will be on 17 September 2011.)
\n
\nWHAT?
\nAny roleplaying or board game. You want to play it, bring it along and run it. Previously we've had on offer 3:16, Amber, Call of Cthulhu, Cold City, Dead of Night, Dogs in the Vineyard, GURPS, Gwenthia, HeroQuest, Hollow Earth Expedition, Hot War, Landston, Mouse Guard, Mythic Russia, Primetime Adventures, Savage Worlds, Spycraft, Tekumel, Trail of Cthulhu, Transhuman Space, True20, Vampire, and some more that I've forgotten. Latest updates will be posted on the Concrete Cow website.
\n
\nA full list of games on offer is on the Concrete Cow games page.
\n
\nWHO'LL BE THERE?
\nWe'll be inviting some Guests of Honour. More details when we have them!
\n
\nLeisure Games and Reaper's Revenge will be returning as traders, continuing their mission to part you from your money in return for the nicest possible shinies.
\n
\nAnd, of course, there's all the fantastic games you'll bring along and run. If you let us know what you're planning to run, we'll put it on the Concrete Cow games page.
\n
\n(Note that for child protection reasons, under-16s will need to be accompanied by an adult at Concrete Cow.)
\n
\nWHERE?
\n
\nThe Old Bath House, Wolverton, in the north-west corner of Milton Keynes. The full address is
\n
\nThe Old Bath House
\n205 Stratford Road
\nWolverton
\nMilton Keynes
\nMK12 5RL
\n
\nMaps are available on MK Web, Google Maps, and Streetmap.co.uk.
\n
\nDirections for drivers are on the directions page.
\n
\nRegular trains to Milton Keynes run from London Euston station. The nearest station to the con is Wolverton (one stop north of Milton Keynes Central), about five minutes' walk away.
\n
\nHOW MUCH?
\n\xa35.00, payable on the door. This'll cover the cost of hiring the hall.
\n
\nFor more information, visit our Yahoo group, email concrete-cow-owner@yahoogroups.com, or ask here!

    \n

    I will have Love in the Time of Sei\xf0 and In A Wicked Age with me

    \n
    \n

    Love in the Time of Sei\xf0 is a low-prep, quick playing story game. This game provides very strong situation and guidance. There are five tightly-interconnected characters, compelling locations and events, and everything you need to get to the good stuff immediately. In a Norse-themed fantasy kingdom on the brink of ruin, who will gain power and who will win love? What price will they pay, and what other plans does fate have in store?

    \n
    \n

    Love in the Time of Sei\xf0 is for 4-5 players, where one of those players is me; GM-less, structured freeform.

    \n
    \n

    In this wicked age... ...Gods, demons and mortals contend with one another for power...Law and civilization are new, and no one is their master... A simple midwife can set in motion the downfall of tyrants and great empires... Your birth is not yours to choose, but your fate is what you make it.

    \n
    \n

    In a Wicked Age is for 3-4 players not including me; I'll be GMing this fast-paced sword & sorcery roleplaying game.
