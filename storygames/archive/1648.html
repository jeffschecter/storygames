I'm going back to Japan in November for the first time in 3 years, and will be hooking up with my old gaming group and showing them some of the games we talk about here. In the meantime, Andy Kitkowski's Japanese blog has inspired me to try writing a series of articles showcasing some of the more interesting parts of story games. These are by no means complete translations (for a number of reasons). If people over there read these and are interested, they can buy a copy of the game in question. Consider these to be extended reviews.

Anyway, if you can read my broken Japanese you're welcome to check them out. Constructive criticism is welcome. I'm trying really hard to be neutral and not push a particular gaming philosophy on people, just show what's out there. (Hence the first article being titled "What Indie Games are to Me" not "THIS IS INDIE GAMING").
