\n The second Geek Girls Rule! Podcast: An interview with Sherry Yeary, Privateer Press President.
\n
\nWe talk about the history of the company, their new product Monsterpocalypse, Hordes, their female characters and minis, and the furor over the Agonizer.
\n
\nhttp://geekgirlsrule.wordpress.com/
\n
\nAlso, Sherry has graciously volunteered to answer any questions you guys might have in a second interview. Just email me your questions, or leave them as comments at Geek Girls Rule! and she will answer them.
