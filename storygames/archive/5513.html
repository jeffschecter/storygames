    \n I'm giving answers, too! My extra favorite questions are underlined

    From one game where my character's name was Meredith:
    Why can I breathe underwater? [because I'm not human]
    Why is my skin turning blue? [because I need to get back to the sea]
    Why can I communicate with water animals?
    Why do I have a picture of a giant octopus in my pocket? [There was something behind the giant octopus: a photo of home.]
    Who is Sean? [answered in play: A friend from 'before' who owns a beach shop]

    My character here was Siddhi Rahda (Named after my neighbor):
    Where is my twin brother?
    Why am I freaked out by mirrors?
    Why is my third eye fixed on the image of an assassination?
    Why am I carrying a severed hand? [answered in play: The hand, with the ring on it, is what They wanted us to get from the store]
