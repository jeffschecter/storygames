\n Hi. I don't know if this has ever been mentioned before, but if you live in the NW US, specifically the Portland OR area, you might want to check out the Go Play PDX forum. This is a nice forum set up by Travis Brown for the growing indie/story/small press games community here in Portland. I had thought I knew everyone who was playing story games in the Portland area, but after just recently meeting so many new people... well, I thought it would be a good idea to try to get the word out.
