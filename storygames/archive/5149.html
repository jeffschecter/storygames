\n To everyone who doesn't believe in or practice Christmas: This is just a goodwill and "hey, let's celebrate the good in life" message, so feel free to swap out the western window dressing and replace it with something that represents the same for you.
\n
\n
\nMERRY CHRISTMAS, STORY GAMES!
\nThis place has been a constant source of friends and conversation and new ideas for me. I've loved participating in design contests, awesome-ofying nostalgic loves, contributing to ridiculosity of all stripes, and just generally hanging out. I call this place home, and I want to say thanks to everyone here for making it awesome (or quite good).
\n
\n
\nWhen I think about Story Games, I think about a cool place where everyone can have themselves a drink and socialize in good company. Even when disparate elements fight or bicker, I know that Story Games is all about the love.
\n
\nRock on, Story Gamers. Merry Freakin' Christmas.
