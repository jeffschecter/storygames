    I think we need a more formal version of this hack.
    \nSo, the Parser removes clothes after the defeat of the Guard and the very end of the game (actually, everyone loses one more article of clothing at the very end of the game). Also after the defeat of the Troll if the Parser is wearing more clothes.

    Everyone else removes clothing whenever they take an item, use an item (properly or not), or die. Also Saving Progress seems to have some amusing effects. :-)
