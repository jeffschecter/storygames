    \nAlso, for anyone coming for the first time:

    Know that you don't need to schedule your games in advance. There's lots of pick-up gaming that goes on. Furthermore, every game session has this cool feature called The Donut, where people without a game get together and get paired off into gaming groups.

    I tend to schedule 2-3 game sessions in advance, and take the rest as they arrive.
