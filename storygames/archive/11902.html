    \n We've been known to dabble. Keep an eye on one-off events, either in London or close to it. There was a SteveCon recently (a mini-convention in a pub). Also, I recommend Concrete Cow, which is only an hour's train journey from London. If you fancy a longer trip, IndieCon, on the South Coast, is excellent.

    If you organise some one-off events, there are various Londoners who might come along, depending on stuff.
