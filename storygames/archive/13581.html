\n At Conception, I'm going to sell games from \xa32 ($3) and give the takings to charity.

One of the games will be Sea Dracula*. Another will be Cthulhu Dark, my rules-light Cthulhu game.

Does anyone else have stuff I could sell for \xa32? Preferably somewhat new (so not, for example, Lady Blackbird, but possibly a special edition of Lady Blackbird with a new character). Preferably also stuff I can print on a laser printer.
