    \n Heya--I just finalized my birthday plans, so I'm sharing them here in case John or any of the cool Portlanders he's palling with are interested:

    1) Birthday Sauna! That's right, the Common Ground Wellness Center is right near my house on about 33rd and Alberta, and I've always wanted to go. A good hour, or two of Sauna and relaxing in the open air is just what the doctor ordered! Come rejuvenate with me--Sauna is a very social affair. Wednesday is Women Only night, so for coed steaming we'd have to play to be out by 7. 5PM or so sounds about right. It's a little pricey, but gets more economical the longer you go. I want at least an hour. Who's with me?

    NOTE: Common Ground is a *swimsuit optional* facility and I'll be taking advantage of that to go "European Style." It's the only way to Sauna, trust me!

    http://www.cgwc.org/

    2) Birthday Beer! And Birthday Music!The St. Pats celebration at Ash St. Saloon begins and ends with Rum Rebellion, and in the middle features Hot Rod Carl, my friend Shanahan's Rockabilly band. Come take in the tunes while I take in some Guinness. Show starts at 8, Hot Rod Carl goes on around 10.

    http://ashstreetsaloon.com/

    Whsper if you're interested. The Sauna requires reservation, but you could just walk right in to the Ash St. Pick one or the other or both! Happy birthday to me!

    Peace,
    -Joel

    PS This being the intarnets--If any activities described here are uncomfortable or wierd to you, please just politely select yourself out of participating. Don't give me grief over it, K?
