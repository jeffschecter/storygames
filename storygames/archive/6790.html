\nProtagonize.com

I recently came across Protagonize, which is a collaborative fiction project. From what I've seen so far, it's pretty cool. You start a story in a genre, and decide whether future installations will be delivered in (linear) Chapters or (splitting and potentially interweaving) Branches.

Then others can add to your story. They can comment on it, add it to their favourites, insert page markers, rate a chapter, and just generally participate.

I'm on there as Joepub. Does anyone else here use Protagonize? Might you start?
I'd love to jump your fiction, if so. Or have you seek out and jump mine, when I put some up.
