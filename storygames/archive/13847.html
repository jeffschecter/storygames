    \n Hi Tomas,

    The deadline for sign up is such that game sessions can be planned: People sign up for e.g. running a number of games and depending on interest, they get to GM some scenarios and to play some other scenarios. Most Fastaval games require a fixed number of players and run with several GMs in the same slot. The deadline for sign up is also very useful for the people organising food.

    You can sign up later - and even just show up in the door - but you will then have to use the reserve queue to get into a game. It is also likely that you can get into a workshop on short notice as they are more flexible wrt. number of participants.

    Best regards,
