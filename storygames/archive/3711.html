\n I've been working on a game for a while, and while it is still in, uh, "closed development" status, it is nearing the point where I will be trying to find others (read: not myself or my close friends) to playtest it and give me some criticisms. We're currently giving it an extended working test by running it through a homebrew campaign setting. I haven't really hyped it, or even advertised it at all, as I'm not quite ready yet to put the expectations down in stone.

What advice can you good folks give me before I open it to external testing? What procedures are generally followed in that stage? What methods of distribution are generally preferred for getting the product to the testers? Are there groups of people who actively seek out and enjoy playtesting as-of-yet-unknown games?

Any and all advice is appreciated. Thanks!
