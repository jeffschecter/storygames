\n So in case you missed it in the other thread, tomorrow evening, my roleplaying web chat show is going to have a livestream with John Wick, designer of 7th Sea, Legend of the Five Rings, Blood and Honor, Houses of the Blooded, the Curse of the Yellow Sign series, the Aegis Project, Shotgun Diaries, Cat, and a ton of other ridiculously great games.

Please attend, it will be at http://livestream.com/pulpgamer at 7 PM Pacific time.

If you want to see some of our previous shows, go to http://pulpgamer.com/outofcharacter.

If you can't make it, but would like to ask John a question or think there's an interesting issue raised in one of his games, PM me or ask it here and I'll ask him.

http://johnwickpresents.com/ is his website, it's pretty great.
