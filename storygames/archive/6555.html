\n Is it just me, or should Poison'd obviously use Crown and Anchor dice?

They're used in a traditional dice game, which used to be played by the British Navy. The symbols are: Crown, Heart and Diamond (which are red) and Anchor, Spade and Club (which are black).

So clearly a red symbol would mean a success and a black one a failure.

Anyway, I find it very disappointing that Vincent didn't recognise this major flaw in his game before now. Considering Poison'd is the second-best game in the world (after Bleakworlds), I expect better.

Graham
