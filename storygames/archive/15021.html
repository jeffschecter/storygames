\n Charles Theel started this last month so let's keep it up!

The idea is to promise to yourself (and others) what you're gonna get done, game-wise, during the month of September. I totally failed in hitting my goals for last month (oath-breaker!), but I'm gonna blame PAX and some other stuff.

My vows for this month:
-- finish the beta draft of Transantiago
-- run at least one session of my periodic near-earth-orbit AW game
-- play something new that I haven't played before (this should be default for every month, really)
-- edit at least two chapters of the anthology I'm working on
-- finish and trade my new AW materials (The Mendicant, +Insight) with some folks
-- write up an alpha draft of a convention one-shot for AW, in the vein of Hatchet City

Next month, Geiger Gamma, hopefully!
