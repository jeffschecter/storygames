What version of Gumshoe can I fraudulently claim to be playing tonight?

\n
\n
\n
\n\nGraham \n\n\n
\n
\n\nNovember 2009 \n edited November 2009 in Story Games
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n We're playing Gumshoe tonight. Gumshoe, as you probably know, is a general mystery-solving system, which has different versions for different genres. Trail of Cthulhu, for example, is a Gumshoe game.

Now, Steve is trying to convince people we're playing Furry Gumshoe. Clearly he's trying to start scandalous rumours of an upcoming Furry-based Gumshoe system. I like the idea of starting rumours, but furries are too much. Nobody will be fooled.

So, what Gumshoe game can I pretend to be playing? It must be something that would be weird as hell, but could conceivably exist. Scooby Doo is an obvious option, but Simon did that for an April Fool's joke already. What else is there? Erotic Gumshoe? St Trinian's Gumshoe? What else?

Graham
