\n Hi guys,

A few months ago I posted here about a game I had in development called 'Revenge of the B-Movie'. It is a card game and as such I was having trouble getting it printed without losing all my money. However thanks to the fine chaps at Guild of Blades this is now going to be out very soon. I will be announcing an actual date along with pricing and order information at the Giant Brain site in the near future.

For anyone else wanting to do a card game, Guild of Blades have been great thus far and their communication has been excellent. I will be putting my order in very soon.

Cheers

Iain
