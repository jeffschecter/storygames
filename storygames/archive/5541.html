    \n Milwaukee to Madison is a straight shot. You get onto 94, and head west.

    When you do get to Madison (typically 90 minutes), 94 splits into three big highway junctions. You want to get on 90/94/39 West. Take the first exit- High Crossing Blvd., and turn left at the stoplight. Go down the hill, and the Best Western is on your right.

    http://maps.google.com/maps?q=4801+Annamark+Dr,+Madison,+WI+53704,+USA&sa=X&oi=map&ct=title
