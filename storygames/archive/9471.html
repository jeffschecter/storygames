    If LJ/Blog, I'd say the most important thing would be to visit the Khepera Publishing booth and write a lot about the Maid RPG.

    OK, btu for real, here's the main things I like to read in a con report:
    * What games are new? RPGs, Board Games, Card Games, etc.
    * What did you (personally) find cool?
    * Attend some big events. There are at least a dozen open/big-looking events. Describe some of them. Take pictures.
    * Take lots of pictures.

    Personally, I'm not as much into interviews. It's all TL;DR unless the interviewee is a friend. But I love to hear a journaled voice as one goes through the con, talks about what he found cool, what he did, etc. Small vignettes.
