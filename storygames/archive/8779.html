    \n Well when you're building a dungeon you roll a die on your paper and where the die landed is where a location turns up. Say a cave or a monster lair or whatever. I'm trying to figure out a way to randomly generate a point without using dice.

    And you know, it doesn't have to be a program or something. I'd really just like a better randomizer than die rolled on a surface. Though if there's a GIMP plugin that randomly generates points on an image that would be awesome.

    \n Bret, in some of the early "HtHAD with Photoshop" threads, we just drew a coordinate system along one vertical and one horizontal side of the canvas and divided it arbitrarily, then rolled dice to target one square in the grid--d12 for the short side and d20 for the long side were popular, I think. (I personally just dropped things on my tablet.)
