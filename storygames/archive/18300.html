\n Arcana Rising is an urban fantasy roleplaying game powered by the Hulks and Horrors system.

AR takes classic old school fantasy, marries it the successful Hulks and Horrors system, and then hurls the lot forward into the modern day, into a world where magic has once again awakened after millennia of slumber, and supernatural creatures once again threaten mankind.

AR features 7 classes both classic and with a modern twist, rules to level 12 and beyond, a new skill and day job system, arcane magic and divine prayer to level 5, an extensive collection of classic fantasy and folkloric monsters, and other goodies.

    \n The magic system is sort of a marriage between a skill-based system, and 3e casting.

    Wizards carry a spellbook which contains all the spells they have learned, and can cast them by making an INT check and spending a certain amount of Will points (based on WIS and a scaling value from class). CHA provides a bonus to damage from spells as well, and they also get a select number of slots to memorize spells that they can then cast without their spellbook. Clerics are similar, but are based almost solely on WIS, and have a limited number of prayers learned per level.

    The spells themselves will include all the D&D classics, as well as a few adapted from D20 Modern's Arcana, my own Heaven's Shadow, and some original stuff.
