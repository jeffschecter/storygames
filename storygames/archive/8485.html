\nThis post got me to thinking.

IS Vincent Baker a Chessex operative? I mean, DitV uses tons of dice. Storming the Wizard's Tower requires tons and tons of dice.

I think he's even recruiting his neighbors. We played (Julia Ellingboe's) Steal Away Jordan the other night and I almost ran out of d6s. I almost ran out of d6s, and I'm the guy who keeps the local model train store and the local comic store in the dice-selling business.

I know Vincent has his hand in Lego's pocket.
