    \n I've heard Jared Sorensen interviewed by other indie rpg gamers and game designers before. None of them ask him about his work on the D&D Online or the Lord of the Rings MMORPGs. None of them ask how working on these types of games influences his RPG work or how it contrasts to his RPG work.

    I'd love to hear interviews with people like John Tynes or Greg Costikyan -- two great designers that had innovative games in the RPG industry who have both left RPG development for new storytelling spaces. Have they heard of or played any of the newer storytelling RPGs that have come out since they left? Do they still game?
