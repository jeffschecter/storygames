    \n Well, there are lots of different screencasting tools, and I'm not really an expert.

    These are some useful links:
    Making good screencasts with linux. The output file can then be converted in less esoteric formats like divx, or uploaded on googlevideo/youtube. I recall also that there's a free app to record from skype calls on linux (on other platforms is for-pay only, afaik).

    A good alternative would be Wink (also free) which allows you also stuff like overlayed notes, buttons to go next/back, and to mix bits of movie/animation with static screenshots taken from your preferred app. AFAIR the linux version is a bit old and does not do sound internally, but the windows one does.

    In general, look for info about "video howto screencasts" for inspiration.
