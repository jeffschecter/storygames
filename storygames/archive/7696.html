\n Since I'm redoing the Range Sheet thingie for 3:16 to include my preferred way of handling Domination (see this thread), I'm gonna set my own stamp on it, I figured, and add some 3:16 fan art. I'll probably do it in colour, so I'm forced to ask, seeing as the illustrations in the book are all black and white, what's the colour of a MandelBrite Suit? Is it white? You run the risk of it looking like a stormtrooper, but any other colour, and you're in space marine territory, anyway (and as long as it's filthy, it won't look stormtroopery, I imagine). Or maybe it varies with rank? Like, when you hit captain, you get a blood red piece?

Something in me wants to make it orange. I'm not sure why.

    \n Well, I ended up making it white, anyway. It was mostly a question of "I'll make it white now, so it'll be easier to change it later" and never changing it. Though, this being the digital age and all, I could probably still change it and try out a few versions. Maybe a camo one, if I can find a good and free texture. Anyway, here's the result, for those who are interested:



    I really did look a lot at the images in the book, and yet, now that it's done, it looks nothing like the fucking originals! Eh, it was good practice, anyway. I've never done a full picture from scratch before. I usually do photomanipulations, but this one is all graphics. Not sure if I'll use it on the range and Dominance sheet. I'll probably end up modding the existing one, after all. Meh, at least I get some nerd cred for making fanart. I've never done that before, either ...
