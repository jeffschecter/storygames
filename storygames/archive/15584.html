    \n I'm enthused to attend this second meeting. We just moved here, and I haven't yet located any of my RPG books in all the boxes (except Burning Wheel Gold, which somehow ended up with pots and pans). So not sure I could bring anything except something free that I printed out.
    \n
    \nLady Blackbird? Danger Patrol?
    \n
    \nLong term, I'd love to try Mouse Guard, although I recognize you probably don't want to run it two weeks in a row. I'm personally fascinated and obsessed with Diaspora, which hits almost all of my buttons, but I've never been able to play it yet. And of course I'd be game for any of the current indie darlings like Fiasco or Apocalypse World. Actually I love AW concept and it looks awesome, but again it's something I haven't played. I have played Polaris, which can work really well as long as you take it seriously, but it is total crap with any hint of smugness or sarcasm. Other favorites are Spirit of the Century-- any FATE game really. Not a Dresden Files setting fan, but I can appreciate the beauty of the game design.
    \n
    \nWhat about practical matters? Mark says maybe not the coffee shop this week. If not, then where? And can I add a request to keep it in Selah? That's a bit of a shorter drive for me coming from Ellensburg. Also, what time? I'm looking forward to meeting you guys!
