\n I've been fascinated by the recent conversation about just that- the webcomic model of regular serial content.

So I'm trying it on my blog. Every week, I'm going to have a post of some content- either a snippet of something I'm working on, a chapter from Shattered Vistas, or something else. But something, every week, damn it.

To start the ball off rolling, I've got the introduction and core mechanic forShattered Vistas, which is pretty much a fantasy heartbreaker. Why not share it with the world?

My other big things I'm working on are War For the Throne, which is gearing up for its playtest, a card game, and a gonzo D&D setting. So subscribe to my webcomic blog.

    \n So, it's been four months since I made this claim. And I've posted every week, more or less.

    So, thus far the projects that are really taking off are D&D Nexus and Future Fantasy, a gonzo planar D&D setting and a cyberpunky action sci-fi game done using the D&D 4th core rules. I'm still blogging about other stuff too, but those seem to be the projects that 1) come most naturally to me and 2) people seem to be getting excited about.

    So check it out. Also, I often mention my updates on Twitterr, if that's your thing.
