I launched a Kickstarter for Goblinville. It's a four issue zine, with the core system in the first issue. Thanks all who helped in the development and improvement. There's a quickstart of the latest version you can download and check out now.

https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/michaeldunnoconnor/goblinville-gazette

I'm also happy to answer questions and talk about the theory behind it!

Special thanks to: @Paul_T @DeReel @Hans_c-o @hot_circle @Aaron_Griffin @Guy Srinivasan @davey
