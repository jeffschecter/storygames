\n Wizards of the Coast did a GREAT job running RPGs at NY Comic Con. How?

There had a plethora of options that took into consideration attendees busy schedules. Including...

- 1 hour games that were not scheduled, run constantly on demand.
- 2 hour games run on a schedule, every 2 hours at even intervals (10am, 12pm, 2pm, 4pm)
- 4 hour games run once per day, towards the end of the day (avoiding late attendees and giving people time to see other con events first)

I saw lots of young players trying RPGs for the first time. It was very cool!
