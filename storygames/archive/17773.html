\n So, I'm working on this board game called "The Wizards Tower" (no relation, definitely stole the name), where you play a mage, run around a castle collecting element cards and powerups and dropping fire and earth walls everywhere to slow down your opponents.

It's coming along. The core mechanic is stabilizing and it's pretty fun.

Is there an equivalent community to this for board game design? Like, where people would be willing to draw out a board on a battle map, give the game a spin, and provide some feedback? I also need lots of education on bootstrapping/marketing/producing a game (definitely second to getting the game right)

I'd also love to post it here if anyone is interested in trying it out. Thanks!
