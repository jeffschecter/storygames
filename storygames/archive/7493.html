    \n The price seems mad, but when you see the pdf, there is a method in his madness. It's a really bare-bone pdf, and printing it would cost a lot. So if you read (or play) it and you like it, buying the book instead would be probably a better option. And you would have paid only $5 over the usual price for the book+pdf.

    So, as a marketing move I think it's sound. Let's see if it works (in any case, it should push HotB in the bestseller list on IPR)

    For european buyers is even more a steal: with the current exchange rate, it cost around 3.5 euro, less than a comic book or a glass of beer...
