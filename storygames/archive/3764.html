\n So for the ENnies ceremony, they want us to give them 20-30 second musical selections to play if/when we win, while the recipient is walking up to the stage. Candidly, I'm not expecting to win an award, but I've spent like 2,300 hours thinking about what music I want to submit.

I can't decide if I should go:

cool/apropos: e.g., "New Day Rising" - Husker Du

cheesy/apropos: e.g., "I Will Survive" - Gloria Gaynor

me just likey/apropos: e.g., "In My Room" - Weezer

over-the-top: e.g., "Flight of the Valkyries"

nostalgic/apropos: e.g., theme from "Battle Beyond the Stars"

annoying: e.g., anything by Laurie Anderson

What are other ENnie-nominated publishers using? Thoughts? Suggestions? Anybody want to compose an original piece? ;-)

    \n Stay away from orchestral.

    Consider something "catchy" that will get the audience singing along, clapping their hands, laughing their ass off.

    I didn't choose in this direction so much (it's not orchestral, but it doesn't have the hip-catchy-fun angle to it), but I do recall the guys who chose songs from the "Pulp Fiction" soundtrack last year as being the most fun to have win.
