    \n There's a DC Game Day (also on Facebook), and The Northern Virginia Comic & Gaming Con.

    Absolutely do not miss Camp Nerdly (Prince William Forest Park, Virginia -- just south of DC).

    Do you consider central and northern Maryland to be the "Washington, DC, area?" If so, there's Charm City Game Day.

    There's 1d4con (Martinsburg, West Virginia).

    If you're willing to drive a few hours, you should get up to Morristown, New Jersey, for one of the Double Exposure events: Metatopia (November), Dreamation (February), and DEXCon (July). These are some of the best events on the East Coast, for story-gaming.
