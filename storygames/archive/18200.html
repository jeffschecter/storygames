This proved to be a bit of a challenge when Lilith spent longer in the fairy realm than I expected, lapping up all the attention as befits a princess (admittedly entirely predictably)!

(I wanna meet EVERYONE in Fairyland! -- Okay, we cut to another player, cut back for one encounter in Fairyland, lather, rinse, repeat as makes sense, fr'ex.)
