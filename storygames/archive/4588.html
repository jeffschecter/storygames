I'm no good at writing these press release-type things, so I'll just repeat what I posted on my blog. :P\n
I'm happy to announce that Evil Hat Productions will be publishing Do: Pilgrims of the Flying Temple!

Evil Hat's been an unstoppable juggernaut of small-press awesome for the past few years, so it's an honor to have this project included in their stable of excellent creations.

Now I can focus on the game itself and let Fred, Rob and da boyz skillfully handle the commercial end of the development process.
\nMan, I've been keeping that news hidden for weeks now. It's a relief to be out in the open.
