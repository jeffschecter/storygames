    \n I have an excel grid I did up as a cheat sheet. Basically it looked something like this:


    Passion Tokens Action Tokens Magic Tokens Power Tokens
    Sacrifice when?
    Commit when?
    Spend when?


    And so forth. The Passon, Action, Magic and Power Tokens were color-coded to match the poker chip colors that we used for each. The whole thing fit on about a third of one sheet of paper so that, combined with a cheap copy of Rob D's player-screen origami (he posted a link in the Forge forum), I could produce quick player-screens for concealing Action point allocation that had the grid shown above on the inside as a reference for everyone.

    I don't have it here, but I can post it somewhere if there's interest. Pretty simple to recreate if someone has the book, honestly.
