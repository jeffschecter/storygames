\n Hey so a few of you expressed interest in taking this game for a whirl awhile back so I thought I would post here. I am looking for people who are willing to commit to playtesting my game in development, The Final Girl, sometime in the next three months. The Final Girl is a horror movie RPG where who will survive to make it to the end is truly in doubt.

The commitment required is very small. A single session of The Final Girl typically plays in 1.5 - 2.5 hours and requires at minimum 3 or 4 people.

If you're interested and can commit to doing this, let me know.
