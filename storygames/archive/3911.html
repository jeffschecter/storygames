\n As some of you might know, we've had a lot of trouble getting Lulu's European printer to spit out working copies of Beast Hunters. It appears that we have fixed the issue now, so anyone who would be served by the European branch now can order a copy of the game there. That's usually much cheaper for shipping purposes than IPR.

That said, I've had a report of a bleed issue, a little line of white along some of the outer edges. Until we've confirmed and fixed that, we've slightly reduced the price to make up for that issue. If that's a concern for you, don't order it yet.

Thanks, and here's the Lulu link for the European version:

http://www.lulu.com/content/991004
