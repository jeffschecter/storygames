    In Darkness is the one worth seeing, methinks. I'm going to maybe take a look at it and Skyflower (up at Secret Wars but in serious need of an overhaul) and see what I can take out of them.

    \n

    None of them are in active development right now, because I'm working on Out of Izumo, which is sort of about the heroes and regular people of an alternate-history, shamanistic Japan. Some of the stuff I learned from the hero games is in there. Whisper me some contact info and I'll show you some things.

    \n

    Here's a snippet from Izumo that I particularly like, some of the text on Titles:

    \n
    \n

    How do you know how useful a Title is to you? Every Title occupies a particular position on your Fame list. Its position represents its prominence in the audience's mind. The higher up on the list a Title is, the more benefit you get from it. In other words, you only get a Title's benefits when the audience remembers that you have them.

    \n
    \n

    It's a big exploration of how we can accomplish imperfectly-reliable narratorship in roleplaying.
