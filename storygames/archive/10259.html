\n We've got a good online version of Games on Demand going on the irc.magicstar.net server, in the IRC channel #playnow.

For those of you who don't like IRC and for whatever reason, want to use different software: that's fine! That's cool. That's encouraged. But in order to get the greatest pool of potential players, we need one specific meeting place, and for that, there's #playnow. Feel free to show up and announce "Hey! Who wants to play Mouse Guard on Skype?" No one will get mad if you poach players onto a different venue. The point is to generate play, period.

Resources:
stryck's guide to gaming on IRC
MIRC, an IRC client for Windows
Colloquy, an IRC client for OSX

Also, we started a Twitter account for organizing online games on demand, so feel free to follow @playnow_IRC. If you want posting access for organizing games in the #playnow channel, whisper me, or ask anyone in #playnow, and we'll give you the password. (We just don't want to post it publicly for obvious reasons.)

So far, #playnow has been the home to a rousing sci-fi game of The Pool, and a late-night crime drama spoof in It's Complicated. There's been some talk of Mist-Robed Gate too, but it hasn't happened yet. Want to play something? Come join us and make a demand!
