    Posted By: Z-DogKnow what I want? I want someone to explain Krazy Kat to me. 'Cause I love the artwork. But man, I do not get it.
    \nOK. So there's this cat, right? And he loves this mouse. And the mouse hates his guts and throws bricks at him. And the Cat interprets all these biffs to the head as signs of true affection, and his devotion remains secure.

    That's it, right there. Oh, and there's this dog cop, who's always trying to put a stop to the abuse, but the cat doesn't recognize this as help. That, and the formalist stuff. But if the formalism makes your head spin, just concentrate on those three characters and their dynamic. If it helps, substitute the words "cat", "mouse", and "dog" with "person." Or with words for more specific roles.

    At least, that's what I get out of it.

    peace,
    -joel
