    Damn you, Dreamation, for stealing our nerds! Not that Troy NY exactly has a nerd shortage...

    Yeah, the con is still going strong after so many years. I have to say it's one of the better-run conventions I've been to, especially given that it's run by overworked engineering students.

    Clay-o-rama is a huge favorite amongst the long-time con-goers. I played in a game of it a few years ago. Most people had pretty standard Play-Doh stuff, like a dragon, or a dog, or a spiked wheel of death, or a boot. Doug Hoover, on the other hand, made this bizarre thing, just a flat day-glow orange blob, with a bunch of little day-glow orange balls next to it. We all just looked across the table and wondered, "What the fuck does that... thing... do?" And Dan DiTursi, the referee, just gave the Evil GM Laugh and whispered in Doug's ear...
