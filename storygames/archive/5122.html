\n I saw this the other day in the local paper:
PAYING FOR A CRIME SHE CAN'T REMEMBER

It seems like a great premise for a Story Game. It also reminded me a novel, Fortress on the Sun, that starts off with premise of a prison in space where the inmates don't know anything about their crimes except their nickname and their bodycount. The protagonist was formerly known as the "Annihilist" and has a body count in the billions. As always, the book doesn't live up to its premise, but a cool idea nonetheless.

Anybody else seen headlines that would make great games or campaigns?
