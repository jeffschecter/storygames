\n I'm only getting partial and fragmentary reports here ... the bits on other forums that people feel like either praising or kvetching about ... but a lot of what I'm hearing about 4ed sounds really good to me. I'm not alone in that, am I?
\n
\nLike, fighters getting limited-use abilities puts them into the same sort of strategy-resource-game world that magic-users have always been in. Do you use your "Mighty Smack-Down" move now, to clear out an entrenched goblin ambush, or do you save it for the big fight later? Same as balancing the Sleep spell (which, for those in the know from GenCon? We should totally have saved for the big fight later).
\n
\nThat balance sounds good to me. It sounds massively good to me if they're also balancing the other end of the scale: Giving spell-casters something effective to do in combat that costs them no resources (the magic equivalent of "I swing my battle-axe, motherfucker, what do you think?")
