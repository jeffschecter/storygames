UPDATE: I am working on the final draft now. If you have played the game or read the text, I would appreciate your thoughts and critique, feel free to email me ross[at]heartofthedeernicorn[dot]com

short pitch:you can kill the gm and take his stuff.

long pitch: We pick a King, give him a Kingdom. One of us starts off as the King and is in charge of the Places, Threats, and Supporting Characters, in the Kingdom. The rest of us are important Characters who have the ability to take that power from the King. We play a series of scenes explore what happens to the King, his Kingdom, and the effect of power on the powerful.

So... I'm making this game and I need your help. I am making the beta version available till April 7th(edited so I have time for Game Chef!). Please download it, read it, play it, and tell me what you think. You can leave feedback in this thread, or in the comments on the development page Serpent's Tooth Development Page

thanks!
