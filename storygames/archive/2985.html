    \n Anemone: I think I figured it out.

    Every once in a while (like 1 out of 10 posts), especially if it's a long post, when I hit submit I get a little red box that says something to the extent of "There was a problem processing your credentials". I think this is wrapped up in the standard PHP 20 minute timeout thing (like, you begin a post, and write a lot, and don't hit submit for 20-30+ minutes) and other minor site glitches.

    The good thing is that normally, your contents are still there. Just hit the Submit button again and they're posted, no problem.

    However, you seem to use "Preview Post" a lot, which has different behavior. It does not appear to save that data in the same way that Just hitting "Add Your Comments" does, so I think if you hit that error that normally shows that red box, with Preview Post it's just dropping the text, kicking you in the butt.

    I'll see if there's a new version of the Preview Post extension. However, in the interim I'd suggest perhaps doing as I do: Using "Add Your Comments", and if you see a mistake simply Edit your post?
