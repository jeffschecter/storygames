Game Chef - Chef Game?

Mark_Causey
May 2006 edited May 2006 in Story Games
Think about a game with five people and a five course meal. Each individual would be responsible for one of the five courses. There would be a deck of custom cards in the middle of the table. Eat your food and discuss things as normal. When the course is done, the cook reveals the top card which has a condition on it "First to compliment", "Last to finish", etc. The person who met the condition would get 3 points. Then, at the end, the food would be judged out in the open, and ranked from 1-5. Award the inverse amount of points to each player, and the highest point total wins.
