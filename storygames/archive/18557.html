\n Hello! My short game Monster Box is now available for free download from DriveThruRPG.

Monster Box is a role-playing game of kids with bonded pet monsters (in the style of Pokemon or Shadowstar). You play as a kid (white d6), with one monster buddy and 2 monster pets (colored d6s). The colors of your dice should match the monsters you draw on your character sheet. Each of your monsters has a different mechanical power (+1 on every roll, roll 2d3, reroll once, 6s explode, etc.). You can describe your monster, and the effects of this power however you want.

Please download the game, read it, play it, and tell me what you think. I would love to hear any questions, suggestions, or actual play experiences, but my specific questions are:

Are the rules clear?
Is there any other material you would need to start playing this game right away?
I am waiting for the game to be accepted at Indie Press Revolution. Are there any other sites where I should consider hosting this free game?
