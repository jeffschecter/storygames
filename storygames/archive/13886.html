\n Hello Everyone,

So a date has been decided on for Nerdly Beach Party VIII. Twice a year a bunch of us gather at the San Simeon State Park Camp Grounds for a weekend of gaming, grilling, and exploring the great out doors in middle California. This is a bit of a self-motivated community organized event and participation is a bit of a multi-step process.

First you can go here to register:

http://nerdlybeachparty.org/

Second, please join the google group here:

http://groups.google.com/group/nerdly-beach-party-viii

Finally, add yourself to the who's coming document located here:

http://groups.google.com/group/nerdly-beach-party-viii/browse_thread/thread/745cc2dcf8b278a5

I will be handling game organization and the sign up process. In the past this has been a mix of people requesting games and people pitching games they'd like to run. I'll have an space setup for beginning that process soon.

Hope you can make it!

Jesse

    \n Technically, there's no limit. What happens is at some point based on how many sign ups we have, we commit to a certain number of camp sites. We usually get 3 to 4 spots. I've never not seen room to pitch and extra tent or two should someone have decided to go last minute.

    Another factor is that Breakfast and Lunch are provided and someone has to go shopping a couple of days before the event. It's hard to shop if we don't have the exact numbers.

    Finally, there's the game sign up process which is multi-stage. People request games both to play and run. Then a pre-schedule is made and sent out to eveyone who ranks their choices by preference. A final schedule is made. So if you're not part of that process then you are kind of playing roulette with the game schedule. That's not the end of the world as there are A LOT of pick up games and spot trades and stuff like that that happen on site.

    So, no, it won't "fill up" but the longer you wait the more you miss out on the organizational process.
