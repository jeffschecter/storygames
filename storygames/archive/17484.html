\nStory Games

Saturday, November 10, 2012
10:00 AM To 6:00 PM

Giga-bytes Cafe
2197 Roswell Rd Ste 100, Marietta, GA

Click here

Meet up and play some new games. There are people new to town who would like to meet people interested in new games.

So far 4 GMs have offered to run:
- Dungeon World, FATE, PDQ
- Monsterhearts, 3:16, Dirty Secrets, Hero's Banner, Spione, Remember Tomorrow, Fiasco, Penny for My Thoughts, Solipsist, Smallville, While the World Ends, and Universalis.
- Shinobigami, Ryutama, or some other JRPG
-Microscope, Fiasco, Robin Law's new Drama System

Don't forget to bring your favorite game. We love looking and trying out new games.

Come and have some fun. Try out new games. Meet people interested in Story Games.
