\n Hey everyone! Hope you're having a great day. This is actually old news, but it's new to me, so I thought I'd share it:

The Gamers movies are on YouTube in their entirety! For those of you who don't know, these are two movies made by Dead Gentlemen Productions, a video production company in Washington state. The Gamers movies are about, well, gamers who play their adventure out with dice at the table, interspersed with cuts to what their characters are actually doing. They also call in all the things we've seen at the gaming table: the rules lawyering, the Munchkinism ("you want to back stab him with a ballista? With a f**king seige weapon?!"), the random killing of NPCs ("if this is about that farmer, I totally thought he was a demon") the angry dorm-mate telling them to STFU, etc.

The movies are hilarious and are now on YouTube, so yay!
