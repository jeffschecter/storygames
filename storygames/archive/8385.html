    \n 2 months? not much. You could probably cover that by going back three pages on the main forum and skimming the thread titles. 2 months is a blip. If it was a one year break, I'd probably have a lot more to say.

    Personally, here's some stuff I thought was cool:
    JD's given me great advice for running a Vampire: Requiem game. HIs advice is currently manifesting in maybe about three threads on techniques to start campaigns, I'll hav to write those eventually.
    The thread on Player Packages set off some lights in my head.
    Oh, and I bookmarked the thread on Generating Culturally-Rich NPCs.
    Prepping for one shot play is a thread I've been meaning to write for a few weeks, and Jason beat me to it. My thoughts soon.
    Oh, and lest it escape notice: Mike Montesa has been writing a great series on Military-themed Role-playing over on his Livejournal. Go back a few entries and follow forward. Great stuff.
