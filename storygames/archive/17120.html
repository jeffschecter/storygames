    \n I have a pretty good idea already, but hey! The Sprawl! Where you at?

    As for Slingtown (my Apocalypse World/Technoir hack), I have moves mostly settled, the starting playbooks written, the die mechanic nailed down, and an outline for the MC's playbook. It's been through two rounds of multi-session playtesting and a one-shot during GenCon, and it's holding up well.

    Solid, barring last-minute tweaks:\n
      \n
    • Die mechanic
      • \n
    • 12 starting playbooks
      • \n
    • Moves
      • \n
    • Outline for the MC's playbook
      • \n
    \nNow it's a matter of hashing out a few more sub-systems, and fleshing out the MC playbook.\n
      \n
    • Gigs, a system for dynamically generating jobs/runs, when players go looking for work. I am going to steal from The Sprawl, Leverage, Blowback and Night's Black Agents SO HARD.
      • \n
    • Technoir Transmission equivalents for regions in the setting, designed to hook into Gigs
      • \n
    • Rackets, allowing PCs to add a 3rd playbook and become the equivalent of Technoir Connections when they have accrued all the pre-requisites. Think Risk legacy.
