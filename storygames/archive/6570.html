\n In August of 2006, I put out Perfect. Perfect is a game about normal people driven to crime by a dystopic Victorian empire. It's a game about passionately rocking out against the system, while the system attempts to track you down and stick needles in your eyes and reprogram you.

About fifteen months after releasing it, I stopped producing copies of Perfect. I felt like the system had holes, and like the physical product wasn't as fantastic as it could ultimately be. I had an awesome game that lacked polish.

I want to share with you the current version of the game, which I feel is significantly close to where I want it to be. The writing isn't pretty and complete in the playtest version, but I don't feel like it necessarily should be. It's what you need to play, and to play as I envision the game working. I'd love critique, feedback, playtesting or questions! You all rock.
