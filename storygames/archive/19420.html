    \n "far, very far, very very far" is absolutely adorable.

    Purely in formatting, it would probably help to have the Backfire text in the Point Blank Shot move too since it's only in Advanced Musket right now, and if you don't have that you'd have to be scanning the whole sheet looking for it.

    My initial concern for a young player's move-writing would be that it would be overpowered, but I don't see any of that really. In fact, I see the opposite: I feel like Hidden Shot doesn't do much for you. Especially if you're not The Thief, an attempt to conceal your gun in public would probably already be a Defy Danger roll.

    Super cool. Love hearing about stuff from your games.
