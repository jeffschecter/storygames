\n I have a bit of a problem with Gay Recruitment Squad. At the moment, all the characters have Neuroses (e.g. "Must wash his hands after touching strangers"), which are lovely character pointers, but don't affect the game much.

Now, I've just been reading Emily's playtest draft of Sign In Stranger, and it's giving me ideas of how to fix this. In particular, it has things like Neuroses, but which actually affect the game.

I particularly like the way that, in SiS, a character sometimes gets overwhelmed by the alienness of their environment. And they basically run round, waving their arms, going "Aaaargh! Aaaargh!". (I paraphrase).

Similarly, I'm thinking that Gay Recruitment Squad Members should sometimes, if they lose a challenge and get scared, spend a turn going "Oh my God! Oh my God!" and flapping their hands until another Squad Member rescues them. That should be an attribute of the character:\n
Panic Attack: Flaps hands and goes "Oh my God! Oh my God!"
\nAnd maybe they should have a similar reaction to their seduction attempts being rejected.\n
Rejection Reaction: Gets blind drunk and pours heart out to strangers.
\nAnd then you should have relationships between Squad Members. They're in Sign In Stranger too. That strikes me as a way to have something like Neuroses, but which point towards the other Squad Members in some way.

I don't know. How does this sound? Help me kick ideas around.

Graham
