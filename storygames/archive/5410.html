\n This came out of the thread on gaming the Presidential Election. I changed it to the primaries because it seemed to flow better. Besides what you see below, the rest of the game pretty much stands on its own. Thoughts?

The Shab-al-Hiri Roach: Presidential Version
or: Are you willing to swallow a soul-eating telepathic insect bent on destroying human civilization?

No?

Even if it will get you your party's presidential nomination?

Character Generation:

Background, Enthusiasms, Relationships, Reputation: No changes

Standing:
\tSenator: slight edge in matters of power
\tGovernor: slight edge in practical matters

Expertise (pick one): Civil Rights, Crime, Defense, Education, Energy, Environment, Foreign Policy, Free Trade, Gun Control, Health Care, Homeland Security, Immigration, Jobs, Social Security, Tax Reform, Welfare


The Events, In Order:

I. \tThrowing Hats in the Ring

II.\tThe Iowa Caucus

III.\tThe New Hampshire Primary

IV.\tNationally Televised Debate

V.\tSuper Tuesday

VI.\tThe National Convention



Play:

Personal Die:
\tLuminaries (Ex-Presidents, Billionaires, TV Anchormen)
\t\td10 for Status; d4 for Everything Else
\tSenators
\t\td8 for Status; d6 for Everything Else
\tGovernors
\t\td6 for Status; d8 for Everything Else
\tRegular Folks (the voting electorate, cub reporters)
\t\td4 for Status; d10 for Everything Else

The Roach: No changes. It just all makes sense in the end.

The Cards:
\t\u2018Letter of Reprimand\u2019 becomes \u2018Something-Gate\u2019
\t\u2018Influenza\u2019 becomes \u2018Laryngitis\u2019
\u2018Journal Article\u2019 becomes \u2018Best-Selling Book\u2019
\u2018Receive University Award\u2019 becomes \u2018Receive Humanitarian Award\u2019
\u2018Receive Academic Appointment\u2019 becomes \u2018Receive Presidential Appointment\u2019
\u2018Earn Tenure\u2019 becomes \u2018Win Nobel Peace Prize\u2019
\u2018Uncover Plagiarist\u2019 becomes \u2018Uncover Draft Dodger\u2019

Endgame
Highest Reputation who is not a slave to the Roach, gets the party nomination for President.
Highest Reputation who IS a slave to the Roach (or next highest if none are a slave), gets the nod as Vice-President.

    It went swimmingly, btw.

    My event were :

    1 First Caucus
    - Arthur Ferguson , Major campaign donor
    - Reverend Talley , Televangelist
    - Tom McMurdock, Election coordinator

    2 Stump Speech in smalltown USA
    - Jimmy Smelt, Speech Writer
    - Bob Kensley, Hotel Security
    - Carlos Huggins, political detractor

    3 The Hotel and the Hooker
    - Regina Sutton , Campaign staffer
    - Reverend Talley , Televangelist
    - Janet Marco, photographer

    4 Getting the Nomination/Gala Ball
    - Amanda Dundermuffin, long lost girlfriend
    - Vito DiMarco, Mafia Don
    - Janet Marco, photographer

    5 The Zealots and the Smear Campaign
    - Carlos Huggins, political detractor
    - Reverend Talley , Televangelist
    - Anna Collins, housewife

    6 Election Night
    - Jimmy Smelt, Speech Writer
    - Arthur Ferguson , Major campaign donor
    - Amanda Dundermuffin, long lost girlfriend

    I didn't do anything with the cards, since I was running short on time, just removed the few that didn't fit.

    Session Two was a pick up game, which ended with the campaign headquarters gardener blackmailing the elected president with incriminating photos, and going back to live in South America (not sure why that kept coming up...).

    The Third was a midnight game, that went completely off the rails with an army or Roach animated undead hookers marching on Washington for equal? rights.
