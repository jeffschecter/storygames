\nJust imagine it! They'd be hanging out in their drafty iron rooms playing surf music on 8-track tapes to pass the time on their quick jaunt to Saturn. Instead of sending little tin cans with camera on a ballistic flight past Saturn, we could go there with a hundred men, a half dozen landers, and hang around there for a couple of years before coming home. Imagine what we'd learn! We might even decide to set up space colonies on some of the larger moons of Saturn. It would be economically feasible with something like an Orion for transportation.
