\n In a discussion with a non-gamer friend of mine, he pointed me at This American Life and highlighted The Fiasco Episode as the one to start with. I immediately pointed him at Bully Pulpit Games.

So, (a) I wonder if the Bully Pulpit Minds knew/heard about this episode of TAL (originally from '97) when they built their game, and (b) whether the meat from the first part of the episode (a small town theatre production of Peter Pan) might make an interesting variant Playset for Fiasco! the game...

(Not a big-scale discussion, I grant you, but a strange "woah, the world is connected through the interwebz" moment, I guess...)
