\n Hey y'all,

My local group is running a day with jeepform in the morning and a really sweet Norwegian Larpfactory larp about high school students in the evening. We have room for about 30 players, and the venue is an easy commuter rail ride (plus a short walk) away from NYC.

The larp we're running, Playing the Cards, is a script from the new anthology of Norwegian larpscripts that I'm helping edit. The hard copy is due out in late October.
