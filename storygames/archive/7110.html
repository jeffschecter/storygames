    \n Three hours should be perfectly adequate for your first Battle Mode session. Outdoors works fine, as long as there isn't much wind (wind + cards = bad).

    In a convention setting, I've got my SoL games down to two hours, but that's because I'm very, very well-practiced with setting up a game and kicking it into gear. Amusingly, after two hours of high-octane SoL, my con groups never want to just jump right into another two-hour game to fill out the four hour session. They have to catch their breath!
