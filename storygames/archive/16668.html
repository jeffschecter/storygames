\n Hello. I have an opening for a player to take on a role in the game of play-by-post Primetime Adventures I am running on RPoL.net, 'Paradise, Alaska'. The show is dark, with a 'Twin Peaks' type of feel, including some hints of weird, possibly supernatural goings-on.
\n
\nWe have had a highly successful Pitch session and now the game is in its pilot episode, and a player has had to drop out. The character you will be playing is Trooper Hudson, a small town cop in Paradise who struggles to command respect in the community due to an error they made early in their law enforcement career that left a local man crippled. Trooper Hudson's Issue in this season is 'Atonement & Respect'.
\n
\nThe character has only made a tiny onscreen appearance so far, so they are still open to be played how you wish. There is no pre-existing character development. Even the details of the character such as name, gender, appearance, and personality are still open to change if you wish.
\n
\nThe character will be an integral part of the storyline, with an instant hook of investigating a murder to get you into the game.
\n
\nOf course, if you want to create a brand new character that would be fine too, as we are only 6 scenes into the Pilot Episode.
\n
\nWe have a cast of creative, mature players, and I hope that you would be able to complement this group. The posting rate is pretty casual, once a day when your character is involved in a scene.
\n
\nIf you are interested and want to play, please get in touch, either here, through RPoL.net, or at paulspraget@gmail.com
