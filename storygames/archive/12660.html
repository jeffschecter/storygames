    \n I take it you guys are working the dealer hall and couldn't start a little later? The reason I ask is that this is the second year of the "Orc Stomp" fun run. It's only a 5K, so a bit shorter than you're planning, but it's a great way to meet other runners at the con. It meets later on Thursday (8:00) than you're planning and earlier on Friday (6:00). The official event is full (20 tickets each day), but at the website they make it clear that folks are more than welcome to drop by and join in casually without tickets. I did it last year and met some nice people. I'm planning on running with them again Thursday. Don't know about Friday (it's probably too early for me -- my sleep deficit is going to be bad enough as it is).

    Anyway, I thought I'd also add that if you guys decide you want to do any afternoon/early evening runs during the con, let me know.
