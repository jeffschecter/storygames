We're just released our latest Kickstarter for Era: Survival, called "Expansions into the Unknown": https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/shadesofvengeance/era-survival-expansions







You can get the basic game, in the form of the Rulebook Primer, for just \xa31.

We're also offering all expansions we unlock (initially 3, with 1 more unlocked by each Stretch Goal) for \xa310.

We have loads of ideas for the various expansions, including:
- an Infected Manual, which gives 100 different biomorphs for the Mutants and Shamblers which threaten
- Source Books for each of the Factions which are trying to control Gaia
- Tales of the Outlands, the region beyond Human civilisation, which let players encounter the weird and wonderful!
- The truth about what lies within the mysterious desert at the centre of Gaia, known as Fallout.
...and much more!








We hope you'll join us in exploring Gaia...
