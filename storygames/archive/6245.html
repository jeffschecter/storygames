    Posted By: Alex FFor the technically naive, can someone pit Wiki vs. Sharepoint in terms of use for Uni as well as online games in general?
    \nHere's my two cents on SharePoint customization and development, as I've done a bit of both.

    SharePoint makes a certain basic level of customization pretty easy through the UI. If you're a non developer looking for some basic features, this is pretty easy.

    Deep customization is possible, but I've always found it a bit painful. For reference, I'm a mid-level developer. It's not my job, but I do a project or two every year just to keep my skills up.

    I don't have any Wiki customization experience, and I'd love to hear from some who does. Blognomic uses a custom wiki to support their online gnomic game. It's got some similarities to Universalis. They use a wiki to track their ever-changing ruleset and to store an ever-changing array of facts and resources related to playing the game.
