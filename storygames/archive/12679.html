    \n I don't know that a list has been posted, but the five Judge's Spotlight winners are:

    CW - Mindjammer
    Jeramy - Fiasco
    Mark - Trailblazer
    Megan - Chronica Feudalis
    Jay - Ancient Odysseys: Treasure Awaits

    I'm glad that this award is being handed out now as it gives each judge a chance to draw attention to a game that really struck a chord with them (even if some of these were being considered by multiple judges ).

    As for Diaspora's gold, CW and I were talking about this during intermission and both of us agree that the system creation alone was worth the nomination. These guys hit a home run with their rules and I can't wait to see more.
