\n This story, submitted for the approval of the Midnight Society, is the usual mess \u2013 but with some rather cogent RPG-related discussion mixed in! I blame the Professor.

River talks about having just discovered Mario Kart (and the seedy, money-grubbing underbelly of video games!), Miles gushes on some media he consumed, fever-dream-style, on his sick day, David and Kaycee love Injustice: Gods Among Us, and once again we talk to you about a recently Kickstarted game after the campaign is over (relevance!).

Once we get down to it we talk about in-character choices and how to get them to feel really meaningful. This topic owes a lot to Rob Donoghue\u2019s article on \u201cChoice Triangles.\u201d Also here: a mini-review of Elizabeth Sampat\u2019s awesome Blowback.
