\n About six months ago I realized that around the internet I repeat myself, a lot. I came to the conclusion that there are some really core things I value in role-playing. So I decided to start a blog about it. The result is\u2026

Play Passionately

Frankly, I don\u2019t think I\u2019m saying anything particularly new. In fact, I\u2019m pretty sure I\u2019m revisiting a lot of old stuff. I\u2019m just saying it in my own words and putting it all in one place. Indeed, I recommend starting with the Concepts From Elsewhere page where I\u2019ve quoted the writings of others that really speak to the principles I\u2019m driving at.

I have more articles planned for the future and you can see some of the topics I\u2019m planning on address on the Scratchpad page.

Announcing this while everyone is away at GenCon is probably a really bad idea. However, I couldn\u2019t resist the humor in having my own \u201cpublication\u201d for GenCon. Maybe it will at least offer those of you not at GenCon something to do.

I hope what I have to say proves useful to some of you. Enjoy!

Jesse
