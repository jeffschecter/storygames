\n I'm really interested to see which games the players at Story Games are enjoying the most. I mean, what was the best session of roleplaying you played last month? To really help me pick out which game to run next, could you answer these two questions:

1. What was the best session of roleplaying you had in January 2010 (which game)? (Not one of your own designs)
2. Which aspect of the game made this session so good?

At the end of the month I'll collate the results and see if I can't find out the current trends in awesome gaming.

    \n 1.) Mouse Guard, a pick-up session at NxNE for people who didn't get into other games.

    2.) The players were incredibly enthusiastic, but really what made the session so good was how easy it was to run the game.
