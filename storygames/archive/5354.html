    \n Gorillaz = the animated band

    This was all based on stuff in their songs, videos, and backstory.

    For example, in a few of the videos Noodle (the japanese girl & guitarist) is flying around on an island with a windmill on it. According to the band fiction she was delivered to Kong Studios in a box.

    Russell (drummer) is possessed by the ghosts of his former gangsta rapper crew, most importantly Del ("who you really think is kickin' tunes?").

    and so on.
