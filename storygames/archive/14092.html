\n It's a character-led mecha game with Norse myth chrome.

It re-uses the basic tenets of Rivers and Lakes, but with many parts of the mechanic adapted to better suit the theme. It has weapon special abilities, a cute way of bringing character traits into combat, a pacing mechanic for the GM and lashings of the descriptive combat you might know and possibly love.
