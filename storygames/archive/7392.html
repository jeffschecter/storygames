\n At Gencon, when you bought certain games, you received 4 copies of buttons with crazy symbols on them. On the button packaging, it said if you collect all 4 kinds of buttons, by sharing or buying certain unspecified games, you would win or unlock something.
\n
\nI saw quite a few people wearing them. What's the deal? Did anyone collect all 4? What did you win? What was the game about? Anyone have pictures?
\n
\nI blame Jon Harper and Matt Wilson!
