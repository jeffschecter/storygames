\n Hey hey. I've totally slacked on promoting this, but here it is: Living Dungeon World will be making its triumphant return (two conventions in a row!) to Gamex, next weekend at the LAX Sheraton in Los Angeles. The format's a bit different, and things are scaled back a bit in terms of the sheer volume of games, but we've got some really cool stuff cooked up and it promises to be a lot of fun again. You can find more details here and here.

If you played in LDW at OrcCon in February and still have your character, feel free to bring 'em with you! We'll be using the current Beta 2.2 rules, so you'll have to fill out a new playbook and stuff, but whatever. Small price to pay for a sense of narrative continuity, if you ask me.

Questions? Comments? Complaints? BRING IT.
