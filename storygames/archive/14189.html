\n To all of our friends in the gaming universe,
\n
\nWe are very excited to unveil Double Exposure's latest project and newest convention:
\n
\nMETATOPIA: A Special NEW Event For Developers and Publishers!
\nNovember 4 - 6, 2011 at the Morristown Hyatt & Conference Center in Morristown, New Jersey
\n
\nDouble Exposure has been a leader in promoting developers and publishers, large and small, for many years at DREAMATION and DEXCON. We have developed a new convention geared specifically towards publishers, in which the main goal is to playtest and develop new games and new game concepts in an atmosphere different from a gaming convention.
\n
\nWe expect at least 30 developers and publishers to join us, with new concepts, new products, and general tweaking of existing role playing games, story games, board games and wargames.
\n
\nBut METATOPIA is NOT just for developers - it is also a place where players can come and enjoy working with and testing with the developers. And to be a player at METATOPIA costs only $20.00 for the weekend, which includes the world-famous Double Exposure Con Suite!
\n
\nWe hope many of our regular gamers who attend DREAMATION and DEXCON will enjoy this new experience of being a part of the new products and games which will debut in 2012 at DREAMATION, Origins, DEXCON and GEN CON. Please read the METATOPIA web page for more information and register today to become a part of gaming history at METATOPIA 2011!
\n
\nhttp://www.dexposure.com/m2011.html
\n
\n>>>Vinny
