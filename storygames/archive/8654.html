    Posted By: bankueiHey Clinton,

    Can you tell me about The Basic Fantasy Rpg?

    Chris
    \nIt's a retro-clone of classic D&D, with a few changes to make it make sense to a human living in 2009: race and class are separated (race doesn't matter that much in it) and AC's ascending. When I'm not using the original D&D books, it's my first choice for classic D&D games.
