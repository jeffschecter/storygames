    \n My Life with Master? With just two players? Intense!

    It works really well with good friends, because then you know how dark you can be. Grim commands from the Master can spur some great tension in the fiction, if they don't get too absurd and pull people out of the game.

    But why not also throw Kagematsu into the mix? With just two, it would get a lot more personal, a little less competitive, and a lot more desperate. And you have the fun of having two layers to the game: the layer that the dice handle, and the secret layer of "Love or Pity."

    Either way, I'd love to read an AP on it.
