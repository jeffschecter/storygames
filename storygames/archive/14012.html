I'm not trying to find the word for "street" necessarily, unless that's the way that word would be used. If anyone hear drives a rig out of Mexico City, hit me up.

    "Migrante" conveys the idea about someone who moves to some other place with the intention of staying there.

    In any case, I'd use something like "la vida n\xf3mada", which refers to someone who is not interested (or capable of) settling down in one particular place.
