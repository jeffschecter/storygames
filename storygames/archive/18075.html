    \n Portray the character as an authentic individual.
    Acknowledge that you will make mistakes, and look to learn from them.
    While playing, think about stuff like status.
    While playing, wonder about the details of this character's life, and reject obvious answers.
    Have fun and enjoy playing the character.
    Reject stereotypes. Reject exotifying the character.
    Portray the character as an authentic individual.

    (spoken as a twenty-something white male, hoping not to negate the voices of others who might have more than me at stake in these questions.)
