    \n I tend to use the bum rush, or give the aliens some sort of ranged attack. By bum rush, I mean fast like a freak, in, strike, out, before the Troopers can effectively target them. Of course, in the fiction, they're probably getting some of them, which are accounted for in their kills. This is generally much easier when you describe the aliens as a mass, with lots of confusion making it hard to count them.
