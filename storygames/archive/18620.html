After DW last year, here is our new project in-a-box, How to Host a Dungeon

fr.ulule.com/dessine-moi-un-donjon/

There's a presentation in english and spanish, thought the french one is more complete. An italian version will come.

It was hard to gather enough money for DW "red box" on french market. So we decide that this time we'd make a -euro version rather than just a french one: the material A2 board, token, etc...) is text-free. The rules are in french only inside the box BUT we will provide italian/espanish rules for download (via QR-code inside the game) if we reach around 20 pledgers in each of these languages. Unfortunately, we didn't found any german partner, but all other european pledgers can get and engish pdf of the rules.

A big thanks to Tony for his support on this. We do not ship the game to North america for the same reason (expect for the tee-shirt).

Despite all this (the box is only 35 euros), i think the campaign is going to be hard.

Wait and see...more pictures to come soon.

Questions, propositions, critics, comentaries, welcome.
