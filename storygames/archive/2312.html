    I listen to Mark Kermode's film reviews. Kermode is passionate about movies, appreciates genre cinema, and is generally good at finding obscure or interesting movies in a sea of pap (which he's not afraid to call as it is). He's opinionated and often very funny, occasionally going on head-throbbing rants that are breath-taking to behold. Further, he often attempts to provide context for his taste, often referring to similar or superior films to the ones he's reviewing, which makes him a useful resource for finding films to watch.

    I do caution folks, he has a taste for some of the grislier aspects of horror cinema, but is often quite open about this. If horror isn't your bag, take his recommendations about it with a grain of salt (I tend to go back and forth, The Descent was amazing, but Hard Candy seemed like it would be a bit too much for me).

    Movies are one of my main inspirations when it comes to gaming, and Kermode helps me find good movies. Just to tie this back in.
