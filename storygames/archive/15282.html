\n1) When people Push, and finger-voting results, it says you can vote for two different ideas if you want with both hands. In that case, can you still give a full 1-5 for each, or do your votes have to total 5? (Can I vote 5 fingers for my favorite idea and 3 fingers for the other, or do I have to do 4 for my favorite and 1 for the other, so I only have 5 total votes?)

    \n Cy is exactly right. It's definitely not clear in the rules text.

    When judging Scenes as Light or Dark, the discussion is actually far more interesting than the final decision. If you can't agree, you should definitely take your time and let everyone explain why they see it differently. That's the meat.
