    \n A number of people seem to have trouble with the roleplaying aspect of 3:16 - just what the characters are supposed to do besides kill abstract Threat markers. Poison'd provides both a theme and mechanisms for all the things that would happen that are not covered by the 3:16 combat rules - particularly in a universe where military "discipline" is unheard of..

    You could literally just switch back and forth between the systems as you wanted, using 3:16 for campaigns against the alien menace (or whatever poor humans you want to subjugate) and using Poison'd for the intra-unit and inter-unit conflicts among humans.
