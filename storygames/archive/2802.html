    \nAs a suggestion to really make the "contexualized story" element really sing, I think that you would need some metagaming element wherein the players act as 'Obsessive Lost Fans' and, at the end of every session compete to make the best 'explanation' of the story (possibly in the form of a forum post) based on the 'clues' provided in that session. The winner would get to declare one 'inaliable' plot element that has to be included in every future narrative.
