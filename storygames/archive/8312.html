\n Just a thread out of curiosity, to see how many nationalities there are on the forum! (Have we done this before? I don't think so.)

Simple rules:
- In your post, highlight the name of your country. (You can write other stuff if you want, but focus on the country's name).
- Every country posts only once. So if someone from your country has already posted, don't post a duplicate!
- Please resist the temptation to treat every state, county, borough, commune or sub-sector in your country as a separate nation. So if someone from Western Beerburgh has posted already, don't post a new entry for Eastern Beerburgh.

    \n I once lived in the Soviet Union.

    That was a huge, monstrous empire of a place that became a world power mostly because of the work ethic it inspired.

    The said work ethic was so powerful that it led many Enterprising and Intelligent people (such as I knew) to decode the serial numbering system so that they could figure out, by looking at a serial number alone, at what time of day a given product was Manufactured.

    That done, they would proceed to buy products only manufactured before 11'o'clock in the morning.

    Why? Because 11'o'clock was the time when the liquor stores opened.

    Thus, it was of Prime Importance to be sure that the product you were procuring was produced well before that time.
