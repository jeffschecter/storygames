\n If you're in the Triangle area of NC, hit me up if you're interested in hanging out this weekend.

Matt S (ennui) will be in town, coming up from Atlanta to stay for the weekend. We'll be hanging out and playing various Japanese RPGs, between my place and Mark C's place.

Currently plans are in the air, save for:
* Doing something Friday night at my place (Matt's arrival time is currently unknown)
* Saturday: Two rounds of gaming, likely either at my place or Mark C's
* Sunday: Likely Dim Sum, gaming if there's interest

On deck for potential play is:
Tokyo NOVA: Think Akira/Ghost in the Shell, Japan's biggest cyberpunk RPG and literally the first Japanese RPG I've ever played
Meikyuu Kingdom: Cute Dungeon Crawling
Tenra Bansho: Hyper Asian theater game
Shinobigami: Fiasco with Ninjas and German Board Game style mechanics
Ryuutama: Hao Miyazaki's Oregon Trail

Not all the above will be played, we're still deciding what's up.

Think of it as a kind-of-disorganized house con with a theme of Japanese RPGs.

Anyway, all are welcome, just either hit me up here in this thread, or over on my G+ account (Andy Kitkowski) if you're in the Triangle and want to try out a never-played before Japanese RPG.
