    \n I've heard rumors - just vague reports from upriver traders and native guides - of a crazy kickass scene in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, USA. They seem to have a very intense core of players who will try anything, and a strange ability to assemble a dozen or more people for parlor larps. That's all I know - the last researcher we sent up there didn't return.
