\n So, I finally finished a decent Alpha draft of broadXcast, and have updated the download link on the loudXclear thread. You can also download a bookletized version here.

I'm pretty happy with the final result, but it's still relatively untested. I played a few solo games (my multiple personalities help), but I would definitely want to hear from people who give it a chance, to find out what they think works or doesn't.

I especially want to thank Daniel Solis for his inspiring games HBR and DO, which I keep having to re-purchase time and again because I keep giving them away to my friends; also to Fred Hicks, since he came up with the original 5x5 game concept. I hope I'm doing their ideas some justice with broadXcast.

EDIT: Fixed a very minor (yet game-hindering) error. While you keep one of the dice that you roll on your turn, I needed to clarify that the rest of the dice are returned to the bag. Otherwise, it'd be a short game, indeed!
