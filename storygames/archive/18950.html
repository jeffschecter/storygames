\n As part of my Patreon series of games, in September I released Rookvale. It's an action-adventure game about fighting demons, capturing them in shards of crystal, and summoning them to help you win battles against even bigger demons. It's basically genderqueer grimdark pokemon.

The game is free to download, available at: http://buriedwithoutceremony.com/rookvale

It's based on the Anima Prime engine, with lots of cards for the character setup and demons you fight. The book is rich with layered backgrounds and pretty art. It kind of cries out for pretty play materials. And now they're available! You can get a deck of printed cards from DriveThru RPG, and a printed book through Magcloud.
