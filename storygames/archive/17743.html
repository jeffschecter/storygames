\n Your challenge, should you choose to accept it: Painter Mike Davis creates art that iO9 describes as "fairy tales that haven't been written yet". With that in mind, pick one of his illustrations and design a game around it. Make sure you include the name of the painting you used and/or a link to the image. I have confidence you all can do this, you consistently amaze me.
