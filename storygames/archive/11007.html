\n Hey, gang!
Any fans of Guardians around? I just picked up a few decks for cheap and I'd love to play.
If you aren't familiar with the game, it's a wacky fantasy battle game, with board-game elements ("Shields" of creatures move across a board made of face-down cards), a very low emphasis on rare or chase cards, and high potential for a "complete" deck right out of the box.
It's got an entry at Board Game Geek, if you wanna check it out.
