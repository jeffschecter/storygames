I have a new dice game called Bombs, Away! It's a quick "hot potato" game with a nice amount of push-your-luck in an elegant little package. I thought it was just a simple way to pass the time between more substantial games.

Buuuuut it could also be used in a Dread-style horror game. Replace the tower falling with the bomb going off and you're set. I think you'd have to introduce competition between the PCs though, just so people have reason not to pass. Also, a simple difficulty measure, again to tempt players not to pass.

Anyhoo, it's a fun game either way. Hope you dig it!
