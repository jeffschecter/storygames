\n Hey,

So you can take a trip to Hawaii and play D&D. It's an 11-day campaign that takes you from level 1 - 30, plus a day or two of sightseeing. Although I'd prefer getting out and exploring Hawaii, the concept is really appealing to me.

But it makes me wonder -- they're positing a 11-day campaign. 8-hours per day. You're gaming like it's your job.

Is there any RPG you would play on a schedule like that? I'm a little hard-pressed. Nobilis or Amber maybe. I'd be really interested in doing Ars Magica or Pendragon -- there's now a chance to see all that generational stuff come into play. Actually, 88 hours might be enough time to play out 2 rounds of HERO combat (I kid!).

What would you play for a week?
