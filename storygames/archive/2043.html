Just picked up the Girl Genius Omnibus Volume 1 graphic novel. More because I like Foglio's art (at least in moderation) and was looking for something more upbeat than most graphic novels lately. And it would make a most awesome alternate setting for a SotC game.

Giant mechanical creations that sometimes run amock - check
Larger than life characters - check
Floating cities of zepplins and support ships - check
"the spark" - a quality to set the players off from the NPCs - check
Ruthless dictator in a sort of 1900s alternate Europe - check

It would be way cool.
