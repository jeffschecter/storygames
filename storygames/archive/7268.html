    \n My Game Chef 07 game was (deliberately) limited in scope. And while it was a lot of fun to play in the three or so playtests, it was never worth putting in the effort to develop further. Actually, getting three fun sessions of play out of one of my games makes it extremely successful. But the limitations that the game naturally imposes makes me less interested in doing anything more with it. Maybe at some point I'll steal some of the ideas therein for other games.
