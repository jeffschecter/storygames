[Little Idea] d20 Fitness

January 2006 edited January 2006 in Story Games
A little idea I had at some point or other.

Basically, it's a meta-add for d20, where you basically try to get in shape. You create a physically sound exercise routine, and check it off. Maintaining your routine, increasing your reoutine (ie "going up to 85 on the upright row"), eating homemade cooking and meeting weight goals all result in experience bonuses of varying types. You bring your "weekly sheet" to your GM and they check it off, adding those XP to your character.

You get stronger in-game for getting stronger out of the game.

Reason I didn't pursue it: It would take too long to write (I'd be writing about fitness rather than getting back into shape myself), plus the concerns that:
* You can't tell if people are cheating. I personally don't care, but some might.
* You'd have to balance all the activities to make sure that you couldn't "min-max" by hitting all upper body weights and not doing cardio, etc.

    Here's how you do it.

    You partner up with a health club (yeah, everyone in the game has to have enough money and time to go to a health club, it's not for everyone, tough noogies) and everyone sets, with the help of the club staff, exercise goals for a 3x a week exercise session; cardio, weights, whatever.

    Everyone gets game currency (XP, whatever) for finishing their workouts that week. After the last workout (and the concomitant showers, of course) everyone retires to the club lounge to play that week's session. Since everyone is exercising together, everyone knows who's been good or bad.
