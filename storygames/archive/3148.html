\n Hey strangers,

I know I haven't been around much recently, but I figured no-one would mind if I made a little announcement. After much too long of a break, carry. a game about war. is back in print. It's a short-form roleplaying game about the trials and burdens of a squad of US Marines in the Vietnam war, if you don't know. Anyhow, it's available again. As my "sorry!" for taking so damn long, if you order it directly from my website, I'll include a printed set of character cards with your order. It's also available from Indie Press Revolution, but sans the cards.

If you have any questions about the game or anything, feel free to use this thread to ask!
