\n Some of you might remember that in this thread, I talked about producing a limited run of hand made dossiers for Cold City. These would give documents, letters and so forth, providing game material, inspiration and so forth in a slightly different format from the norm.

At the time, it was also suggested that we produced a PDF of the finished article. Well, the fruits of our labours are now available for all to see. The physical dossiers will only be available at cons (including, if we have any left, Gen Con), but the PDF package is now available through Indie Press Revolution. It's available here.

As a physical item, it's was a lot of work. However, from my own standpoint, it wass immensely rewarding to produce something which was an artifact that could be used in-game and also something that would become more 'authentic' through use and abuse. So, the outcome is very pleasing.

I should specially thank Jason Morningstar for kindly sending me some authentic Soviet documentation, which was really great stuff.

If anyone has any questions about the process used to create the dossier (advice: use tea), comments on the work that goes in to such items or feedback on the finsihed article, please do let me know.

Cheers
Malcolm
