    \n Sorry I just was looking for the game The Mustang,

    Could not remember the name, and found this site on my google search, and was amazed at the concept.

    \n I have long standing plans together with a few friends to infiltrate such a board.

    We'd start by being Queen Starmane and her court who just arrived to Valley X being driven from their homeland by violent, brutish horses. Queen Starmane is pregnant, the foal being rightful heir to their homeland. The queen and her court are light, swift and beautiful horses that will try to make many friends in the community. They will be generous and friendly but always closer to each other than anyone else.

    Then comes Bloodhoof the Stallion and his gang of heavy set draft horses. They are blunt, no nonsense and out to stop the foal ever returning to the homeland. They will speak frankly about the unjust aristocracy that was led by Starmane and her court and how the majority of horses were forced to seek grass in the badlands, the green pastures reserved for the royals and their flunkies and the scraps left for those who enforced this hierarchy. They will try to gather friends too.

    Several times these groups will meet and just stomp their hooves and insult each other and talk about the showdown. When sufficient other horses are gathered the showdown happens suddenly! Bloodhoof and the rest attack Starmane just as she is about to foal and a fierce battle is fought! Horses are trampled, crippled, blood and dust is everywhere. Starmane's best friend Silverbloom has both her legs broken and begs a player she has befriended to put her out of her misery. Bloodhoof is killed or driven off but not before mortally wounding Starmane as she defends her foal. Starmane whispers faintly to the player she trusts the most and asks her to adopt the foal and raise him. Then she dies.

    \n To do anything roll one pink dice and one sparkle dice (1dP+1dS), mostly stuff just happens with out consequence or continuity.

    PINK DICE

    1=Yay! your action passes, and other ponies must help.

    2=Yea! your action passes.

    3=Nay! your action fails, try again.

    4=Neigh! you must talk a solution for your action to pass.

    5=Hay! you must use a bribe for your action to pass.

    6=Hey! you must get support for your action to pass.

    SPARKLE DICE

    1=Canter! a lively action.

    2=Prance! an even livelier action.

    3=Dance! a show-tune action.

    4=Sing! a show-tune action with singing.

    5=Sparkle! a magical action.

    6=Blush! aw shucks.

    *ta-da*
