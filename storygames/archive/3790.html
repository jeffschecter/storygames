\n Little idea time

You are the Exiled, the lost Children of the Gods. You are exiled due to your rebellion - you did something that didn't meet the approval of your heavenly sponsor. You still reap the benefits of this privilege - vitality, health, maybe some cool powerz, but most importantly, you retain the smallest of divine sparks that allows you the honor (onus?) of being able to slay the Children of the Gods. And only they can slay you, truly.

Lead your rebellion against the Gods that you spurned. Their Children have access to powers you once held, but, they too may be able to be convinced to rebel ...
