\nDescription of the game:
\n
\nThe game is held in Hoffman Estates, IL. It runs SAT noon on. Yes, there is a game going on this weekend. Many of the players show up once per two weeks or more.
\n
\nThe game year is currently in the early 1930\xe2\u20ac\u2122s. The characters are a wide mix of people who work at a private investigator office \xe2\u20ac\u201c doctors, theologians, archaeologists, world war one vets and, of course, PI\xe2\u20ac\u2122s. Their cases run anything from missing persons to stopping Various Evil Things from doing...evil. The campaign is a mix between realism and the bizarre.
\n
\nThis game uses the Heroic Cthulhu system (skill based system).
\n
