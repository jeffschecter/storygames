\n So, my game is a step-die system and I've been looking for some way to have a communal dice bowl but keep the various dice separate. Something useful for Dogs or Agon as well. I found this:



It's one of those veggie trays with the spot for dip in the center. I've been looking for one for a while now but they're elusive. I finally found one at a party supply story for about 4 bucks. Supposedly you can get them for cheaper at a dollar store, but I wasn't able to find one there.

With the 5 outer sections, I can keep d4's through d12's and the spot in the center for tokens for Ardor Points or Fate Points. Easy for anyone around the table to just grab what they need.

The only drawback is that it doesn't work to transport the dice. If I could get something like this with a cover that sealed off each of the sections (and had 6 peripheral sections), I would be in accessory heaven.

So what dirt-cheap solutions have you found for game nights?
