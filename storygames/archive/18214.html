    \n .............And, here it is! Yes, the proof copy of the book wot me and my gamer buddy Dan Marriott wrote. For some reason the pdf I sent to the printers had some errors but we're sorting those out, and I'm doing another proof-read of the original doc in order to weed out any typos that might still be lurking there, and then we're all set. Sadly, the first print run won't arrive in time for Conpulsion next week, but we\u2019ll have the proof copy there and we'll almost certainly have it in time for Con-Quest, and definitely for UK Games Expo.

    Note: This is an ashcan version only: we're awaiting professional illos and cover art, and in the meantime we'll sell copies of this version at more or less cost and probably give folks who buy it a discount on further products like the pdf, e-book or merch. But my goodness I'm excited .
