    Indeed it will be fantastic!

    As far as the Indie Games Track goes this year, we're expanding it out to include 10 one hour demo games and 4 two hour games. Plus there will be 15 minute demos from the various Collective Endeavourers there.

    Games being run as part of the IGT include:

    Best Friends
    Contenders
    Covenant
    Dead of Night
    Dust Devils: Revenged
    Mob Justice
    Piledrivers & Powerbombs
    Shock: Social Science Fiction
    Spirit of the Century

    The IGT also includes playtests of:

    The Gay Recruitment Squad Wants You!
    Grey Ranks
    Reel Adventures
    Up Go The Heads (an as yet unseen football RPG by Gregor Hutton)

    Other games running at the con include:

    Discworld
    D20 Modern
    Buffy The Vampire Slayer
    Hollow Earth Expedition
    Loads of others and a couple of local homebrews!

    So, there is a hell of a lot on. Plus there is the bar. Which means drinking. A lot. Plus the Grand Charity Auction hosted by yours truly and Gregor Hutton, Club Conpulsion, the always entertaininf pub quiz, artist round tables and so on.

    Cheers
    Malc
