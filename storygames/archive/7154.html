\n So, here's the thing. My original plan was:

1. Fly into New York.
2. Get a ride across to Indianapolis.
3. Go to GenCon.
4. Rent a car in Indianapolis.
5. Drive lazily back through the South to New York.

However, the one-way rental fees are so crippling, that it's cheaper to:

1. Fly into New York.
2. Rent a car in New York.
3. Drive to GenCon myself.
4. Drive lazily back through the South to New York.

So, I wondered if anyone would like to come with me on the outward journey? It's just for company, really, and you'd need to pile into someone else's car on the way back, but it'd save you squeezing into a packed car for at least half the journey. I won't pack my car out: I'd be happiest travelling with one or two other people.

Ideally, I'm looking for a beautiful woman with loose morals, but, failing that, I'll take anyone who's nice to talk to. I should warn you that I drive on the left and I'm not changing that for anyone.

Anyway, do let me know if you're interested. It'd be nice to have company.

Graham
