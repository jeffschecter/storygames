    \n I was totally expecting something else. The people in my pocket, as I've noted elsewhere, normally organize in bands. But just in the last few days, I've noticed that they have begun to federate along larger clan lines, to protect their traditional lint foraging grounds from alien groups. I figured you had seen some critical breakthrough in marsupioethology in your own studies \u2014 maybe a tiny conquering clan using force to put captives to work scraping virgin liner fabric areas for lint, pointing toward an expanding division of pocket labor?
