Splitting Up A Game

Levi Kornelsen
February 2006 edited February 2006 in Story Games
Here's something I'm considering doing.

Imagine if a game was written as a series of free downloads; a couple stand-alone 'introductions', and then a few build-up 'supplements' - here's a demo; if you want to add character advancement, grab that continuation. If you want character creation, get that one, and for situation builders, go over there.

Not to say there wouldn't ever be a complete book. But that you could play with all this stuff, buzz could be built, playtesting done, and so on, as it came along.

It's what I'm already doing in a few ways for my game; I'm thinking of taking it up a notch, and making that my actual strategy.

How does that strike you?
