    \n There's potential here, but you'll want to be careful, I'd say. Games always have a chance of spiraling out of control in the early stages. Enough of mine definitely have.

    If you haven't heard of the Power 19, I'd definitely say it's worth checking them out. They're 19 Questions that directly relate to how your game works. I really think they've helped make my own work stronger as well.

    I'd personally say (as I did in another thread) try to make sure everything that goes into your game directly supports the original concept of it. That'll be a good place to figure out where you need to start scrubbing and adding content!
