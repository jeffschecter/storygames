\n One of the developers of Tabletop Network multiuser game table, Tim Fowers, was kind enough to demo the beta for me tonight. Some of the cooler features include easily created custom card decks and dice (just upload the image and define it as such), and dragging directories onto the gameboard (useful for lots of minis).

Though the project is temporarily on hold, he's interested in any feedback we can give him. He enthusiastically asked anyone who is interested to email him (tim@fowers.net) for a beta account.
