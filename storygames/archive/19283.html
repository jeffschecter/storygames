\nFall of Electricity: The Return of Grunge

a story game by Jackson Tegu.


"An echo of a former age swims into view: a second Seattle out in the great dust, cloyingly nostalgic. So the band piles into their car to find it. Two humans, two instruments, like four blood cells being pumped through the heart of the American dreamland."

What is this?

FOE:tRoG is a tabletop story game by celebrated designer and dear friend Jackson Tegu. In it we explore the lives of a rock band on a surreal tour through the American dreamland. The game borrows mechanics and ideas from Life on Mars and Fall of Magic.

Rad, How do I get a copy?

FOE:tRoG is offered as a part of the kickstarter for Fall of Electricity's new album. (Yes it is a real band) Backers at the $5 level and up will receive a pdf of the game along with the rest of their rewards.

Thank you Jackson!
I'm so excited about this! Be sure to check out Jackson't website photographsoflightning.com for more amazing games.
