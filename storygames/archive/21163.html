\n I need summer reading. I have restarted my podcast, I need games to talk about. Please pitch awesome stuff on Lulu, new or old, which I should totally get for my collection.

Myself, I have published a couple of games there:

The Daughters of Verona is a Shakespearean comedy game. It was sold out, but with this crafts project edition you can assemble your own lovely copy, without having access to a color printer. http://www.lulu.com/shop/wilhelm-person/the-daughters-of-verona/paperback/product-23193558.html

While the World Ends was illustrated by Claudia Cangini earlier this year following a crowdfunding. It has never been this pretty before. http://www.lulu.com/shop/wilhelm-person/while-the-world-ends/paperback/product-23102594.html

OKULT is a small booklet which describes a horror game of great scope, set in your own hometown. Investigate a mystery as a returning adult, a mystery that evokes memories of things repressed that happened in high school. http://www.lulu.com/shop/wilhelm-person/okult/paperback/product-21356612.html

The Academy is a game about youths at a boarding school. Play Harry Potter, play Ender's Game, play Tom Brown's School Days, play whatever, as long as it happens at a boarding school. :-) http://www.lulu.com/shop/wilhelm-person/the-academy/paperback/product-23105599.html
