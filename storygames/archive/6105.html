\n Part 1 of 2. I went and hung out with Ben on a saturday night and we recorded a good two hours of content. Some of it rambly, some of it over-excited, much of it funny, and most of it inciteful.
\n
\nI'm proud of this one. It actually tries to have focus. It fails, but in a great entertaining way. We talk about Mutualism a bit, and Ben tells a great story about his first time at the Forge booth.
