\n I am "working" on a system that is quite simply a way to randomly generate themed words (In this case for a Horror Storygame) to tell a story. The players randomly select a word via some method of randomization (I recommend the classic method of writing them down on scraps of paper and mixing them up) and make up a part of a story. The next player then picks it up and continues. It is meant to be a really simple game but I was wondering if anyone had ever played a similar game and if so how did it go?

    There is no competition in this game..you know what, let me just post what I have so far.

    "Horror Storytelling" is a placeholder name.

    Horror Storytelling is a storygame that is designed to be played by either a group of people or used as a story-writing aide by one person. The game works by having a list of Horror related words that are picked randomly be each person, each person must then create a section of a story using that word. How each word is randomly picked is up to the players. Writing each word down on a strip of paper (You do not have to use all of them!) and drawing them out of a hat can work.

    Horror Words
    Morgue
    Corpse
    Haunting
    Graveyard
    Night
    Forest
