\n Hi. Go Play SE Portland #7 is on Sat, October 6th. I just wanted to make sure that everyone who lived in the portland area knows that they are invited. You can find all the details on our Live Journal.

GPSEPDX is a small monthly game night and community dinner hosted by myself and Charles Seaton at our house in SE Portland. In the past we've facilitated up to 3 games and 19 guests. This time we're going for four games. Games on the menu include Polaris, Dust devils, Shock, TSOY, Beast Hunters and Project CHAIR.

Consider this an open invitation. Feel free to email me for more info or directions.

Hope to see you there!
