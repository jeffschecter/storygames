For the benefit of those people who don't get over to the Forge much, I figured I'd start a thread here for people participating in Troy's 24 Hour Blackout Challenge to post and talk about their games. And when Andy puts up a page for the games, he can post the link here, too.

\n

My game is called Comedy Blackout. Although I really needed to add more examples and discussion of the form, I'm still somewhat pleased with the result. We'll see how I feel about it later, after it's set for 24 hours and chilled in the fridge.

\n

The game includes minor usage of "create or roll the dice" techniques and also should be very good for starting play on the spur of the moment. It might even be a decent RPG for people who hate RPGs. Maybe I'll test that theory.
