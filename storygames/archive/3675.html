\n Why aren't you working on your game RIGHT NOW! Yesterday I stumbled across something called Chore Wars, a site where you create a character, then gain experience points, gold, and treasure for completing chores. So I created a Story Game Chore Wars party where you can level up for writing, designing, playtesting, and generally working on you game. Here's the invite code:

Join the Story Game Motivational Project

My first level fighter will Owzor you!

So how it works is you create a character, then join the Story Games party. Every time you work on your game, you send your character on the relevant adventure, with a chance of gaining gold or treasure, or meeting a wandering monster. As you work on your game, your character levels up; sort of a slightly more interactive ProgressQuest.
