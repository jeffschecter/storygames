    \n
    \n After a year of compiling ideas and concepts, I'm finally close to releasing a alpha core book of my game. So this year, my goal is to playtest the hell out of it. Now, getting people on this site interested is probably a difficult thing. But I have several people in my local area interested in playtesting and recording data to help refine the system. Good good stuff.

    On another note, the legendary Fred Hicks wished me well on it. I feel like a an angel blessed my work and it makes me want to work harder and make it even better. I get so fanboy over these game designers.

    Meanwhile, a local DM has allowed me into his 3.5 pirate game. For the first time in about 8 years of being "THE Game master" I actually get to prop up my feet and play someone else's game for a chance.

    If I'm lucky, I might even get to play something I've been dying to like FATE or Marvel Heroic.

    I feel like 2013 will be the year I make enough money that I might be able to afford eating more than one meal a day again. Here's to hope.
