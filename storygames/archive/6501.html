\n Hello all
\n
\nI am not trying to spam, but wanted to get the word out since I have paid out of pocket to create this for free for gamers to read & use:
\nI want to announce the release of \u201cNelafayn Quarry\u201d, a new free pdf download available for immediate download at http://www.lythia.com
\n
\nWhile intended for Harn, the quarry may be easily dropped in ANY feudal kingdom in another gaming world/setting.
\n
\nIntroduction:
\nNelafayn Quarry is a hillside limestone quarry just west of Abriel Abbey in northwest Kaldor near Dynes and Gedan. \u201cNelafayn Quarry\u201d is also known as \u201cAbriel Quarry\u201d or \u201cHerlan\u2019s Tor\u201d and all three terms are used by the locals. The site is located two and a half leagues southwest of Olokand on the north side of the Iver Stream in Nelafayn Hundred.
\n
\nDetails:
\nNelafayn Quarry contains 10 pages:
\n1pp \u2013 Introduction and background
\n3pp \u2013 Quarrying, history, area info, feudal info & quarry residents
\n1pp \u2013 Color PC map
\n1pp \u2013 B&W GM map
\n1pp \u2013 Description of the local map
\n1pp \u2013 Interior scale map & detailed description of the lodge and adventure hooks
\n2pp \u2013 Detail in \u201cfriends, foes & followers\u201d format of the quarry\u2019s key NPC
\n
\nNelafayn Quarry is intended to be used with the Nelafayn Hundred Project: http://www.lythia.com/nelafayn/ and the project\u2019s other articles.
\n
\nEnjoy!
