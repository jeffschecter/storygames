    \n Brennen: I used to work in call centers and we focused on eliminating those pause words to sound more competent. (Their use better lies in masking internal dictionary searches when speaking a foreign language, but I digress.)

    I found that the best way is that, once you've recognized you're using it a lot in a conversation is to simply pause during that time instead of saying um. You'll sound all William Shatner-y for a while, but once you realize that all you're doing is slowing down your speech naturally with sounds, you can learn to pause normally and then push forward again.

    Let me know if it works. I found that it worked for me but I'm not sure if it works for everyone.
