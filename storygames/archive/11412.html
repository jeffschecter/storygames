\n [Possible spoilers, FYI]

So, my wife and I just finished the final episode of "Dollhouse" a couple hours ago, and my brain is buzzing. That the show started out a Westworld-meets-Stepford thing and then ended up about a psychological zombie-ish apocalypse is just so, so cool. Anyway, I naturally am now jonesing to figure some sort of RPG take on this that I can run for an upcoming Gameday.

Any ideas? I have no clue what system I would use or how I would scale down the premise so that something meaningful could be accomplished in 4-5 hours.

The one gimmick I thought up would be to give the pregen characters either no names or just code-names (alpha, whiskey, echo, etc.). Then, I could have an equal number of cards that have the PCs identification tattoos (e.g., "I am Sophie Miller"). As the players discover the tattoos on each other, the cars get dealt to them randomly, so you end up with female personalities in male bodies, and so on.

Beyond that, I dunno. I figure they PCs will be dolls that have been imprinted randomly as part of some post-apocalyptic failsafe (or eve error), all awaking in some tech-filled bunker.

Thoughts?
