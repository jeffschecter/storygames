    \n Well, we did not scribble in the book, but entered some pages at the appropriate places.

    The coolest in my opinion:

    Gettismane
    Once an oasis, this place was the stage for the betrayal of the Damsel Messiah. Now the water has doozed away and alll that remains are dead trees like ribs of the corpse that Marr'd is. Some speak of delicious fruits as sweet as wine, but Oghma believes this not. As Oghma, Son of Oghma was at the place he pressed his ear to the ground and heard screams from inside the earth. He flew.

    Gettismane Type: Object
    Stamina 4
    Will 5
    Lore 6
    Power 7

    Desire: Gratification
    Need: Plant it's seeds all over Marr'd
    Appearance: A bloodred bed of a former lake surrounded by riplike dead trees.
    Telltale: Screams from below the surface.

    Powers:
    Vitality
    Hold
    Special Damage
    Vitality
    Hint
    Travel
