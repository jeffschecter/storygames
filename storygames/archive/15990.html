    The larp-scene in Norway is far from plastic swords, Joachim. The Larp Factory is indeed far removed from that kind of larping. They make the most wonderful small larps, with a whole host of different themes. You really should try it out!

    As for beer and role-playing: drinking don't mix well with rpgs for me, and certainly not heavy drinking. I've had a great number of sessions with "jolly" players, and have observed that those games seldom are as fun as they could be. I prefer the better games made by sober players high on enthusiasm.
