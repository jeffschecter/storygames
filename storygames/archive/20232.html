\n I was heavily influenced by the Barbarians of Lemuria rules but I realized I wanted something a bit different, with some different emphasis, but without giving away too much of the quick jump in, play and have fun of that game.

Here's a text doc with a quick sampling of the rules I'm thinking and a couple of simple examples of the mechanics in action: https://cloudup.com/cz0fkM8_oXp

Any and all feedback is welcome! Thanks in advance if you have a chance to give it a look.

    \n The dice I'm doing differently. It's a lot of dice but I'm hoping to minimize the amount of rolls. Combat, for instance, I'd like to be able to resolve, one way or another, in a couple or three rounds-- fast and brutal, emphasizing the importance of choosing when and where to fight and striking first.

    No play testing yet. I'm still spitballing rules around at the moment.
