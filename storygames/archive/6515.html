    \n Hey, I'll try to rack it up for this weekend.

    Also, the RSS feed will push out whatever page you're looking at. So if you're looking at all discussions, that's what will be fed. If you want a feed that captures every single new post across the forum, go to:
    Search.
    Check "Comments"
    Leave the search field blank, and hit "Search".
    When the results post (it will be all of the comments on the site, as they are created), click on the Atom link to generate that feed.
    You can do the same for topics, etc.

    Hope that helps!
