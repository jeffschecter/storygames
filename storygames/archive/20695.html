\n This might be of interest to folks here - I interviewed game critic Austin Walker about his excellent Actual Play podcast, Friends at the Table, for the critical/academic gaming blog First Person Scholar. We cover a lot of ground, from RPGs and collaborative storytelling, to fandom and nostalgia, to political media and critical theory.

http://www.firstpersonscholar.com/the-power-of-imagined-worlds/

(And if you haven't listened to Friends at the Table yet, you really should. It's outstanding.)
