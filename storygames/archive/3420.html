    \n Justin, if you're near a college town, you should call around to screen printing shops for estimates. I just called a local store specifying a one-color full front print on black (100% cotton) for a dozen shirts (this is what that Ashcan Front shirt looks like) -- the total came to just about $10 a shirt, pre-tax. That's a $45 screen charge and about $6.35 per shirt. If you want more ink colors, your cost would go up.
