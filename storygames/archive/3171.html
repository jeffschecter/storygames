\n The 9th episode of the Canon Puncture show features an interview with Remi Treuer of the Durham 3 podcast. We talk about how D3 started and Game Chef, but spend most of the time discussing Improv theater.

You can find Canon Puncture on iTunes by searching for canonpuncture in podcasts of looking in the Other Games section, or you can subscribe here.

Give us a shot. With only Sons of Kryos, Durham 3, Have Games Will Travel, The Master Plan, Dragon's Landing Inn, House of the Harping Monkey, The Game Master Show, Theory from the Closet, Voice of the Revolution, Rolemonkeys, Villain Talk, All Games Considered, Gamer the Podcasting, and 2d6 Feet in a Random Direction in the RPG podcast arena... it is obvious to me that the market is not saturated at all.
