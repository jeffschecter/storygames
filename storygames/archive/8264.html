    \n I don't know much. I own the original books but I never played TROS (apart from a convention demo). But some time ago I got curious about what happened to the game after the closing of the forum at the forge (after the selling of the game from Jake to another publisher, that did make the game "no more indie" for the Forge definition) and did read some threads of these forums.

    As far as I know (but I invite the people who know more about this to correct me) the new owner had some familiar and personal problems and pretty much disappeared from public view and from publishing. No more books were published and the old ones got out of print. The forum got closed for a time, and the game seemed dead.

    Now some people in the new forum is selling pdf copies of the books, for a limited time, with the promise to give the money to the rightful owner when (and if) he resurfaces. This at least is what I did read, I already have every book on the line and didn't pursue the matter further.
