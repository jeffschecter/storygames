    \n Hey all,

    Game Chef 2014 will definitely be international. The dates are fast approaching (May 10-18) and you can expect competitions to run simultaneously in French, Russian, Italian, Brazilian Portuguese, and English. Those are the teams I have confirmed so far.

    I'd love to see a Korean team organizing GC in their language community this year! For that to happen, someone steps up to serve as the Korean coordinator. That person gets two additional volunteers to help them organize the event. They submit their team details via this form. I then follow up and help them get everything ready.

    Since Game Chef is a mere eighteen days away, that should happen as soon as possible. Yes! I'm excited about this possibility. Korean Game Chef 2014!
