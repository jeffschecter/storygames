    \n The book is unclear on this due to a bad editing pass. The intended rule is "The relationship is broken and cannot be used during missions or interludes unless it is first restored. Additionally, each pilot involved in the relationship takes twice the relationship's intimacy value in Bliss. If the relationship is between a pilot and an anchor, there are other rules that apply in the mission section."

    At the pilot player's option (either one if both are pilots), there can be a denouemont scene which sums up the end of the relationship. My recollection (I don't have the book with me right now) is that this scene has no judge and no mechanical result.

    I wavered a lot between "twice" and "three times" the intimacy value in bliss during playtesting.
