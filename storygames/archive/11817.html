\n Gone! All gone! Some of it will be available at the next NerdNYC Recess, thanks to Tavis!

I'm getting-rid of a big pile (5 milk crates) of 1980's rpgs and other stuff.
There's more than the list below, but you get the idea.
It's all in Ft. Greene, Brooklyn.

The games have seen a lot of use. The comics are pristine.

I want it gone by Sunday. Otherwise it goes to HousingWorks bookstore, I guess.
I want somebody to take it all-- or big chunks (like, just all the games-- or all the paperbacks).
But I can't deal with people coming for just a few items.

Post or whisper here if you want it, or know a perfect place I should donate it to.

    \n I am now the proud possessor of a bunch of Todd's crates worth of awesome stuff! My wife is aghast that I've brought all this into our tiny apartment, but even she had to admit that the handmade rotary analog computer designed to calculate how far Car Wars vehicles moved per phase at different speeds, back when dot matrix printers and ball-point pens ruled the day, is made of win. (Also cardboard and a brass stud).

    If there's anything you were lusting after, look for it to appear on the giveaway table at a coming Recess or NY Red Box game, or whisper if you don't want to wait that long!
