\n Once upon a time, someone (I think it was Ivan Ewert, but I don't remember) started a Yahoogroup for Primetime Adventures play by email. It had become a pit used by spammers. I ended up with ownership of the group, and have cleaned it up; anyone I could positively identify as a spammer was removed, and I deleted all of the spam messages. It's also set up that new members need to be approved, and new members are moderated. There will be no more spam there if I can help it.

So, if anyone wants to make use of it as a resource, it's there.

Oops, almost forgot. The address on the web is http://games.groups.yahoo.com/group/ptapbem/
