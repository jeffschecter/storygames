    \n I like how they reinforce the tension between your capacities in the real world, and the capacities you need to shift to the alternate world. (Character stats, I mean.)

    The Real World sheet seems easy enough, and allows a clear organization of what the reality cops do and what they should expect.

    The Operation sheet gives me a clear idea of what the struggle is at any point in the operation: Pump up the Pressure before the Tension gets too high.

    The Character Sheet needs more character. But without knowing more about the game, I'd ahve no idea how to pretty it up so that it's suitable for any campaign. I could suggest make it look like a typical employee's dossier, but what does that look like from one Real World to the next?
