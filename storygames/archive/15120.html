\n Want to introduce new people to RPGs?

People have proposed:

- Make RPGs educational and sell them to schools.
- Make RPGs into Magic the Gathering. To prepare an adventure, build a custom deck!
- Make RPGs family friendly, put them in boxes, market them as a boardgame and sell them at Barnes & Nobles, Target, and Wallmart.

Forget that!

D&D is becoming cool. People are forgetting how much parents hated D&D and how D&D was evil and taught you demonic spells and other naughty rebelliousness. Bring back the controversy but in a commercially friendly, sell out way!

Package D&D as a satanic bible and sell it at Hot Topic in malls.

I told you it was a terrible idea!
