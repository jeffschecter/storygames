    \n Well, in general in Japan people are far more afraid of fire than in the US. Sure there was the Chicago fire and all, but for the most part, if someone's house catches on fire, they're out one house. In classic Japan, if someone's house catches on fire, that could mean the end of that longhouse, block, ward, district or worse.

    So, you'll see quite a few scary monsters that have the properties of fire or smoke. Kinda cool, actually.

    -Andy
