    \n If it's under 10 Megs, and you don't mind it being on my (new) site, go here:

    http://www.amagi-games.org/tiki-upload_file.php?galleryId=4

    And just select creator, then hit "browse" and "upload". It'll upload, and then give you a link you can paste for downloading.

    You don't need to register, at present, to upload to that section - However, it can't show you as the file creator unless you do; if you're not in a registering mood, just select "admin".

    \n Here's another fun Google Docs trick (figure I might as well share it here). If you want your doc to appear in the "page view" mode, add this to the shared URL after the DocId:

    &hl=en&pageview=1&hgd=1

    This will display the content in page view and also suppress the GoogleDocs footer. See a sample of the effect here:

    https://docs.google.com/View?docID=dhp42mj_98cnm9h2g4&hl=en&pageview=1&hgd=1
