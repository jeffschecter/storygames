    \n Hey Chris,

    Extreme Vengeance might be worth a look. It's by Tony Lee. It's one of the unknown classic indie games. The hero has to earn his Guts up to a point where he can confront the main villain. Have you seen Commando? The big bad can't be defeated until the hero does the work of dealing with the minions to earn up his Guts score. The only issue with the game is that Tony's writing style is quite goofy. Lots and lots of dumb jokes in the text. Some of the names of the character archetypes are goofy. But the game mechanics are great.

    Paul
