    \n This is probably more on the general side, but I enjoy listening to Fiasco podcasts, especially when the group "has the system down," and the story just flows. It's reminds me of listening to old time radio shows.

    But what I find somewhat fascinating is listening to groups who play it for the first time, and how some players just seem so bewildered to the system. Maybe it's just because I come from a board gamey background, where the culture is a strong structure to rule and gameflow, as opposed to freeform reactions to GM descriptions.
