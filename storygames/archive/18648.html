    \n Nice art! If you launch in the fall, keep in mind the tax implications of receiving the kickstarter funds in one tax year and incurring expenses the following year (consult your tax professional), and factor in the typical large rate hikes for USPS and UPS that have become common at the start of each year. If you're getting everything ready so as soon as the kickstarter finishes you go to print (maybe after a PDF preview feedback period) those issues may not be as big a concern.

    I've put together a bunch of links to RPG Kickstarter Tips at http://www.flick.com/~cdr/rpg/kickstarter.html .
