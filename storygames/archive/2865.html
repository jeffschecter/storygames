    \n x-posted with everyone before me, and probably king ghidrah too, jeez

    The text is good and compelling, but the scentence "For 63 days in the summer of 1944, the city of Warsaw stood free before her defenders, betrayed, were crushed by the Nazi war machine" is a little akward. Something about the tenses. I would fix that to be "For 63 days in the summer of 1944, the city of Warsaw stood free UNTIL her defenders, betrayed, were crushed by the Nazi war machine." Aside from that editorial I think its a very clear paragraph that quite accuratly brings players up to speed on history and what gameplay will be like. Its not to wordy, its descriptive, evokative. Quite good.

    The word Grey in the title gets a little lost in the building. Really the whole title needs to pop more. Right now the Kids are the first thing that jump out at you and then you might wander up to the title. Push the title a bit more, its tone is to close the the background.

    I like the font for the title a lot, its accurate to the source material, in spite of being very differant from the preconceived notion of what the source material looks like. It would have been so easy to go for a very americanized propoganda font, but instead to go hand drawn, erratic, and above all polish, is exciting.

    I personally don't really like borders, i think they waste space and don't lend much to the work. I'm not sure how i feel about the use of one here. My first instinct would be to fill the page with art and drop more type directly onto it, but that my opinion as a designer, and might not reflect your personality or what you want from the game. I do think that would help it jump from the page.

    The font in the headline and the italics font are doing nothing for you. they are bland; especially in comparison to the rocking hand drawn title. They need to be punched up a little more.
