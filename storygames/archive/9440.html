    \n If you have 3-4 hours to showcase a game, prepare all the bits that are not necessarily fun and leave the bits that are. That's my advice. There's nothing worse than spending half a convention session making characters or whatever.

    That said, I love to help set up the situation and fill in the details if the game supports it and it is fun. Just not, you know, for two hours.
