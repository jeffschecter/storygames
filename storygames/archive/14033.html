    \n Let's see, answering some questions:

    At Gamestorm, the tickets are available at the Indie Hurricane booth, which is about 50 giant steps as you stride purposefully into the dealer's hall.

    Since we're in a giant warehouse, our cap could be huge; factoring in our cramming-it-full-of-art and a little thing i like to call relative-realism, we're putting our cap at 120. Translated, this means you have lots of time to figure things out, but as that we need a bit of up-front capital, those who know they're coming - well, please grab your ticket when you know you can make it.

    The four AW playbooks are packaged in physical form with all full-pass ticket purchases, so no worries if you're actually at the con or not. If you're thinking that you might want to come from far away - let me suggest rolling it into a longer trip, driving out to the coast, exploring the giant forests. Olympia itself has a lot to offer, too.

    Alberto, even if you're not actually able to come, don't admit that to yourself yet. If you wish, allow yourself a little more time to dream before the alarm goes off.
