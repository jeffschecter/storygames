    \n Since playbooks are for separating out character archetypes, having a single playbooks seems a bit odd as I can't think of any reason it's actually different from having no playbooks. While I can't think of any single-playbook hacks, there are several without playbooks at all (an example is Ghost Lines, though I'm sure there's more).

    Basically I think you should be safe to just go for it.
