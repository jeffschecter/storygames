\n In preparation for the next set of playtests of The Naked City: a game of Film Noir, I created lists to be used during collaborative character generation. The group takes turns adding new information to the relationship map. Each person gets to do one of these things and then pass along until twice as many characters as players have been created:

-Add a first-last name to a space on the R-map.
-Add an adjective to a space that already has a name
-Add a job to a space that already has an adjective

Ex:
Donnie Walker, Frustrated Composer
Joan Laurel, Unfaithful Torch-singer

I could use some help culling through the lists to weed out any lame or redundant ones and add in other good ideas, especially Jobs and Places. The latter are used for scene construction and also as a sort of 'Personal Set" ala PTA. The name lists are from baby names from the 1920s (who would be the right age in the 40s/50s. The other lists have mostly been painstakingly been taken from plot synopsizes of actual Noir films.

Check the lists out here.
