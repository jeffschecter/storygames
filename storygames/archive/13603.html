\nINC Book 2011: PLAYING WITH EMOTIONS

After last year enriching experience, we want to leave some tangible sign of
players ponderings.
In accordance with this year "dionysiac" convention theme, we'd like to give
life to a volume collecting articles about emotions in games.
Give us your thoughts: if you think emotions in games are something
advisable or to avoid, how to achieve it, what elements help or hinder it
and, generally speaking, any consideration or experience about this matter.
If you already wrote articles, post on forums or blogs which, a bit touched
up, could be suitable for this year volume, they are welcome.
The one criteria to keep in mind presenting your articles is that, as usual,
the INC Book is meant to promote the diffusion of games.


HOW THE INC Book IS BORN
InterNosCon 2011 organization will gather the most interesting articles for
the book that will be:

1) freely available on line through a pdf downloadable directly from the
InterNosCon website;
2) a part of INC'S participants Welcome Kit in printed version;
3) on sale at a symbolic price (probably aroung 10 Euro) in printed version
for everybody else.
Please note: INCbook is meant to promote a wider knowledge of games and not
for profit; in the unlikely event that the income from sold copies exceeds
printing costs, the outstanding will be gifted to Doctors Without Borders.


HOW THO CONTRIBUTE
Here's how to proceed if you wish to take part in this project:

Abstract: Before the finished article, we would like to receive a short
abstract, a brief summary of the arguments you'd like to discuss in your
article. You can send abstracts in ".rtf" format to the address
staff@internoscon.it. Once you receive confirmation for your abstract you
can start to work on the finished version of your article.

Deadlines: Abstract within January 31st, finished articles within February
28th.

We are aware the deadlines we propose are quite close. Anyway we hope that
this initiative will find ample support among players. We trust in your
desire to spread the knowledge of the hobby we love.


CONTACTS
For any doubt or clarification needed you can contact us at
staff@internoscon.it or we can discuss on the GenteCheGioca forum
(www.gentechegioca.it)

InterNosCon website: www.internoscon.it

If you feel like letting someone else know about this Call for Papers we'll
be grateful. Please know that posting this on any forum, site, blog, portal,
whathaveyou would be much appreciated.
