\n About 16 months ago, I wrote a game called Dostoevskyan Murder Ballad.
I played it several times and it was uproariously fun. A delightful romp through the pathos and pettiness of what I assume Dostoevsky's writing is all about.

I rewrote the game today, and think it's tighter in both design and writing.
It's available here.
