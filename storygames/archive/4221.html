    \n 1 xp any time you make a decision influenced by that character (or you could use the standby of "any time you are in a scene with that character", but "influenced by' is a pretty easy draw).
    3 xp any time you share intimacy with that character, touching or greater.
    5 xp any time benefits are shared.

    I want to fit in this somewhere:
    Any time the subject of your relationship, or a display of the deeper undercurrents of the relationship, become revealed to a third character.
    Examples can include holding hands or other sensual touching (back massages), making plans in public to "hang out" which sound suspiciously like "dates" or "rendezvous", etc
    (maybe for the 3xp option?)
