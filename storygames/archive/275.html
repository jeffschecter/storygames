[Whedon] Relationship Map for non-Relationships

DevP
March 2006 edited March 2006 in Story Games

So there's all that talk about Firefly/Serenity being about Family and stuff. What if you drew up the characters' relationships as if they WERE one big extended family, then treat that as a metaphor for their relationships. Parent-child relationships reflect relations of protection/overprotection and respect/rebellion. Siblings would share.rivalries/loyalties, and perhaps marriage would be defined as a combination of both.

  • Book: Grandfather ot Mal
  • Mal: Married either to Zoe or Inara
  • Kaylee: Child to Mal or Inara
  • Wash: Possibly married or sibling or even child to Zoe, depending on how you want ot spin it.
  • Simon/River: Definitely married.

Take these metaphorical ties, and overlap that with the actual ties of blood/love/loyalty that the characters acknowledge. What do you get?
