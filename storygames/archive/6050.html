\n (Obviously inspired by Matthijs' "You're going time travelling. What do you bring?" thread, but since I'm not exactly sure what he's trying to do, and to prevent collision, I've split my idea into a new thread.)

So, here's the deal:

You begin by listing a series of items that you, the titular time traveler, bring with you to the time machine to stop those damn aliens. Your list can be eminently sensible, like a significant majority of people's responses to Matthijs' question, or nonsensical, like mine. If it's nonsensical, you can handwave it by saying that this isn't your first attempt in time travel, and you're a dirty cheater who doesn't give a damn about paradox.

Someone else, in their reply, basically narrates what you did with the items that you brought to stop those damn dirty aliens. If it's a sensible list, obviously some of these items don't get used. If the list doesn't really make any damn sense, then the respondent must use every item at least once. When does it not make sense? Dunno. Player judgment, I suppose. Maybe 'sensible' things are optional, and non-sequitur items are compulsory. The player then posts a new list, and the cycle repeats.

Anyway, I figure using my list would be terribly unfair, because I was just banging out things in random with no thought to making any sense.

Therefore, here's that list again:\n
Posted By: T-BoyA towel. Three toothpicks. A Qwardian Power Ring. A 17th century musket, loaded. A bathrobe. A colony of matter-transmuting nanorobots, suspended in a gel solution, good for six uses only, and programmed to stop once the colony encounters mica. Some slippers. A packet of beef jerky, vacuum-sealed. An empty glass. Three quarts of Glenlivet (1972) in a tumbler. An ice tray. A backpack. Two pairs of thongs. A box of condoms. The Emergency Retrovirus Injection Kit, with three spare needles. A skeleton key, date of manufacture circa 1882. A broken kukri, date of manufacture 1941. A vinyl LP (Rick Astley, Whenever You Need Somebody). A quill pen. Some rubbing alcohol.
You have aliens to stop. Here's your manifest. Go!
