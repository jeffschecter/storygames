    \nPlaying? At night, when there are few distractions (kids, etc). Best environ is at large table, either sitting or standing. I sometimes play on couches and the like, but it tends to make my mind wander. I get too relaxed.

    Planning for the Next Session as GM?     Either at night, or regularly during my lunch break the day I'm supposed to be running a game.

    Designing or Writing? At night, anytime between 7PM and 1AM. When I'm on a roll, I actually disdain dinnertime, getting it over with as fast as possible.

    \n I've had to train myself to work when I've got the time. It's hard at first, but it can be done.

    My most productive time is from 9-11 AM. I do game design at that time, because then I'm under pressure to get my paid work done by the end of the day. If I do it the other way around, I slack off on the game work and it never gets done. Then I've got another creative burst from 7pm (child goes to bed) to 9pm (time to watch some TV before bed).
