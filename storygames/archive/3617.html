\n (I hope I'm not covering old, tired ground here. This idea was inspired by multiple streams of awesomeness I've seen floating around (cheifly the regional meetups and group design blogs) that I find it hard to tap into. I think there's a good chance may not be alone in this.)

I'm looking for game designers and players who identify with the following for various reasons (geography, health, schedules, finances, etc.):\n
    \n
  • Don't get to attend Go Play-type events or conventions.
  • Don't live near an active community of gamers and designers.
  • Have limited opportunities for playing a variety of games or play-testing their own games.
  • Find conversation more fruitful than participating in message boards, text chats or design blogs.
  • Want to meet regularly via Skype or some other voice-chat format regularly or for special events in any attempt to remedy those issues.
    • \n
\nIf you find yourself nodding your head as you read that list, let's see what we can work out. If there's something like this already going on, point me to it!
