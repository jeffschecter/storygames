\n Okay, so, as it turned out we DID need badges and they were done well. In fact their use of space allowed many of our attendees to customize their badges will all sorts of things. If you can scan it in, that's even better. in case you can't tell on mine they gave us poker chips with an owlbear on one side (THE AWESOME) and CN 01 on the other side.
