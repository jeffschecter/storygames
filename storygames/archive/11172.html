The Keys point the characters in the right direction so on the whole you can just sit back and go with the flow.

    \n I ran Lady Blackbird not log ago, and am running it again tomorow. It's a great game, I really dig it.

    One thing that came up in my first session was the strong Star Wars context. The players were constantly referencing events/characters/scenes from the original Star Wars trilogy to describe stuff in the game.

    Which sounds like it might be annoying, but I enjoyed it.
