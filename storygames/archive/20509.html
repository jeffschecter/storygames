The Score

\u201cOur operation is small enough not to be noticed...which is advantageous for everybody.\u201d
\u2013Lando Calrissian, The Empire Strikes Back

Cloud City has always operated on the fringe of the law, but its tibanna gas mining has kept its Baron Administrators wealthy and powerful enough to preserve the city\u2019s autonomy. Even Palpatine\u2019s overreaching galactic empire has kept its hands off of the city\u2019s not-strictly-legal operations, making the Bespin city something of a haven for ambitious entrepreneurs, gamblers, and miscreants.

However, rumors have begun to spread of Imperial transports landing in the city. Some even report Darth Vader himself has been meeting with Baron Administrator Calrissian. Tensions are running high, and the city of six million waits on edge. Is it time to pick up and leave the planet, or is there time for one last score?
