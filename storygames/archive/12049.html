\n Episode 18 Maid in the Great Outdoors http://apap.libsyn.com/
\n
\nOn this episode we discuss the Japanese roleplaying game Maid. We talk about the game's extensive use of random tables, the favor/stress economy, and the controversy surrounding the games sexual content.
\n
\nThis episode was recorded at the campsite for Nerdly Beach Party in San Simeon State Park. As a result there is some background noise from the campgrounds and wind.
