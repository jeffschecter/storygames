\n Simon's thread about Small Things kicked me into gear on a website to hold all the games that I've made for contests, etc...

Right now it has all my Game Chef games: Pinnacle Empty Quiver, Pilgrimage, and Blake Borrow. It also has these games from other contest: Iliamna Unknown, Foe Destroyer, Blackout On the Elevator, Burning Monsters Now! with Stakes

Out of all the games, Pinnacle Empty Quiver, Blake Borrow, and Iliamna Unknown are the best. Although, Burning Monsters Now! with Stakes is a fun distraction of a board game.

I also have Q&D Plastic Soldier Warfare online, which is my plastic soldier war game. It was published in Space Gamer Fantasy Gamer back in the day. The Book of Graph is also online. It made the rounds as far east as NH in the early 90s.

My D&D hack, Taverns and Dungeons, is also there. This is a new one. I like it. I have a few other games that I don't really like, so I'm not posting them, unless anyone can suggest a value in doing so.

If anyone wants me to link to your site, let me know. I've linked to Simon's site. I also need a new picture for the site. I'm taking suggestions. Anyway, I thought I'd put this out there in case anyone wanted to know where the latest PEQ is resting.

www.emptygamebox.com
