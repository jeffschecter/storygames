    \n (Florida, folks)

    There's a pretty good FLGS on... some road (heh). Googling now....
    Map to several stores
    Looks like there's three RPG game stores, maybe four.

    War Dogs on Beach Blvd is the one I've been to. Pretty big gaming room with lots of loaner minis terrain, decent stock (with LOTS of old stuff being sold off, last I was there), decent enough sales folks. And I once found Zendo there for $10! (MSRP $50, out of print, eBays for over MSRP) I quietly went to the register and bought it; gave it to a friend for his birthday.

    As for bars, you're at Jax Beach--pick the one with the most gorgeous folks and the biggest beers! When I'm down there, I'm in town, so I tend to hang out in either the River City Brewing Company for froo-froo, brew-geek scene (and dance parties) or Sherwoods (dive bar, pool, watching football)--both around the San Marco/Downtown border).

    If I were you, I'd at least be sure to check out the Landing in Jax City. It's sort of mallish, but it's often got some kind of shin-dig going on of an evening (open-air band or some-such). And Hooters, if you like orange hot pants on college chicks (who doesn't, really, c'mon?).

    Brace for the smell... the paper plants aren't ALL shutdown and gone, and if the wind is JUST right... *barf*

    \n Hey Kevin!

    I live in Jax and David is right, War Dogs is a nice FLGS (not much of the indie flavor, but nice). I also suggest Borderlands, which is one of the stores on David's link. If nothing else, Borderlands is the cleanest and most organized gaming store I've ever seen.

    The Jax Suns have some home games if you want to watch some minor league baseball in a nice park.

    Other fun things in Jax?... uhm.... well... the stuff David mentioned. Its the biggest small town I've ever seen, honestly, not much going on outside of football season preparation.
