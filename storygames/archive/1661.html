Folks,

It is with great pleasure that I reveal my latest Sekrit Projekt. As those of you who have listened to the latest episode of 2d6 Feet in a
Random Direction know, I've started up a second podcast called The Voice of the Revolution. My co-host is Brennan Taylor of Indie Press Revolution, and we're going to talking about the indie RPGs that IPR represents. I'm really excited to be doing a non-solo show, and I'm excited to talk more about indie games. (My intention is to talk a little bit less about RPGs on Have Games, Will Travel so that I get back to talking more about board games.)

Our first episode is up at http://www.thevoiceoftherevolution.com. Take a listen, let me know what you think, and spread the word.

Thanks!

--Paul
