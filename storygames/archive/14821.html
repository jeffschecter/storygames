    \n\nMarkT \n\n\n
    \n
    \n\nOctober 2013 \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n There was much fun to be had at RinCon. Seriously, it's so worth checking out. The staff is awesome, the players are consistently really great and friendly. It's a great place to be. While I was there, I...

    - ...ran a LongCon for Eternity with Marissa that took 10+ players through 3 sessions of our Vast's stories. It was an awesome, intense run through the full rules for the game, and I'm feeling really confident that my 2+ year journey with the game's design is coming to a close.

    - ...sat on a few fun panels, including Building an Inclusive Table, Talking About Kickstarter, and an RPG Design Panel. Woot.

    - ...ran Jason Morningstar's Story Games Dungeon to explain a bunch of cool Story Game techniques (like fishing, descriptive traits, etc)

    - ...ran two sessions of Firefly using a new adventure that I'm working on. While you can totally make your own Firefly characters with the rules, I had folks play the Main Cast as pregens. Brian Poe, one of the organizers of the Con, was the best Wash I've ever seen in a game.

    - ...watched a few minutes of Marissa Kelly's awesome Apocalypse World hack Epyllion, which is sort of a mashup of Lord of the Rings, My Little Pony, and Spyro. Yup, you get to play baby dragons who work with friends to defeat the Darkness that threatens everything through the power of friendship.

    - ...spent a ton of time arguing with Jason Corley about how he is wrong about everything ever except when he is right which is, let's face it, most of the time.
