\n I'm working with Jennifer Rodgers and Joshua AC Newman on the art and layout for my game (Misspent Youth). For those not familiar, it's a near-future sci-fi game about friendship, rebellion, and the fight for freedom from an oppressive Authority. I need your help!

The layout and design conceit of the book is that it's going to be punk zine style, such as Maximum Rocknroll (which looks like this). I am ordering from Jenn a set of 10 little black-ink mini-illustrations to use as icons/plug-marker thingies between articles. These are meant to be flavorful and neat. This is the list I have so far. I need more! Any suggestions?

A skateboard
A slingshot
A pack of cigarettes, or a lit cigarette, or maybe a joint
A taped-up baseball bat.
A bike chain.
Googles
A gas mask

    \n Aaron is Aaron Cometbus. Also goes by Skrub. He's kind of an East Bay mainstay. He's been in bands with just about *everybody* (serousliy, he's been in something, like 100 bands who's members are also in/went on to be in Green Day, Fleshies, etc.) and Cometbus was one of the formative punk zines in the area (maybe even the country, really). He's a swell guy and damn good writer (both in *what* he writes, and the physical act... 99% of what he's published over the years is hand written in a very distinctive style, but you'd swear it was some machine-made "hand written" font).

    Be forewarned, though, reading his stuff will make you pine for the days when you were cool and make you wish you could relive them (but also thankful you don't have to).
