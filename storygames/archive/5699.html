\n In order to avoid the actual work I'm supposed to be doing, and to give myself a kick in the butt, I've started writing my attempt at an play Procedure for the Red Box Hack... in public. This way, if anyone likes it, they can bug me to finish it, and I won't forget about it.

I'm putting it up on a wiki, here:

Adventure Creation Procedure

About half the mechanics are still missing, but I'd like to see what people think so far. I've only tried something like this once or twice before, so I don't really know how it will turn out. So, parts you like? Parts you don't like? Parts you'd like to see more of?

If I get the rest of it up soon, any of you who are playtesting RBH are encouraged to give it a spin!

Note: It doesn't *have* to be used with RBH. Just something RBH-like.
