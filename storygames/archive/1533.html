  • dotan
    October 2006
    2300AD is a Hard SF space RPG from the 80s by GDW, the creators of the classic space opera RPG Traveler. If Traveler was generic 70s space opera with a sprawling galactic empire with traders, mercenaries and early Niven-ish aliens, 2300AD is more "near future", with various Earth nations have space colonies spread across star systems an astronomer would recognize. There's a selection of really fascinating aliens to interact with, and a hostile species that Humanity finds itself at war with, allowing for all sorts of "Aliens 2" style space marine military ops.
    The system is 80s-style crunchy sim with roots in 70s Traveler, and the setting is state-of-the-art 80s SF, pretty much like Transhuman Space does 90s SF.
