I'm pleased and excited to say that Shock:Human Contact, a hard SF RPG about people, is ready for pre-order! It will be going to press in days, and that means that, if you want it, you can send me money and I'll send you a copy!

\n

To keep things simple, I'm not taking PDF-only or hardcopy-only orders right now. If you want either of those, you'll have to wait until I have time to work that out.

\n

I'd also love to answer any questions you have about the game!
