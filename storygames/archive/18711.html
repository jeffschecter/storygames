    \n I've had a chance to playtest this game (and that's me in the videos with Matthew). It's a blast to play. We played a lord of the rings-style, get to a city before time runs out campaign in 3 hours.

    The rules are really simple and the resolution system works well for both combat and non-combat encounters, and allows for a lot of creativity. In many ways, it reminds me of OSR-type games in feel, but the resolution system is very different. Most combat and encounters take at most 5 minutes, allowing us to spend most of our time roleplaying and exploring the world. The resolution system reminds me a little bit of Fiasco, but instead of rolling the dice at the end of the game session, you roll them at the beginning, and apply them to situations and encounters during play.

    It's super fun, and a great way to play a D&D style campaign in a very short time.
