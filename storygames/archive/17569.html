    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n It has more differences than I originally intended, but nothing that isn't a logical extension of 1E. Most of the changes are subtle but aimed at functionally extending the comic book feel of the game.

    Monologues are more powerful but are called upon less often. Banter replaces the common usages of Monologues. (Doing what they did in 1E.)

    Stress Events are now called Fallout and they stick around making powers like Regeneration more sensible.

    Character Generation rules, More Powers, and a better setting. I've got a huge outline The rules elements are after the setting elements. Albeit it was an early plan, most of it has remained intact.


    I'll get a playtest version put together over the weekend.
