\n Just finished reading the Clan Burner... hot!!!
\n
\nIt breaks down and helps you define the core conflicts in the setting. It does so in an extremely well organized and easy to read way. And after each entry, it makes suggestions on how to tie your characters into these core conflicts. Take a relationship with... write a belief about... and much more!
\n
\nThe Clan Burner helps you build situations that the all the players buy into, understand, and become directly plugged into.
\n
\nHot!
