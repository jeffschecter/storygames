\n Hello! It's been a long while since I last posted (busy busy!), but considering all the stuff that's happened, I felt that the community of Story Games deserved my most sincere thanks for being such an awesome place. Thanks to you folks, I was able to make massive amounts of headway in designing Uncharted Worlds during the early days of design. The feedback and discussions helped immensely, allowing me to fine-tune my game.

As of this week, 300+ Kickstarter copies of the book have made their way all over the world, and several hundred more pdfs and print-on-demand books have been sold through DriveThruRPG. I owe Story Games a huge amount of gratitude for helping to get me over that initial design mountain (and right into the terrifying swamp of publishing, but that's another story).

So. Yeah. You guys rock. Once I'm done with the stretch goals and such, I have a couple of new projects in mind, so it's likely I'l be slinking back in the future.

-SG

(Edit: I suppose this is a Directed Promotion? I guess? Heck if you want to pick up UW, I wouldn't say no)
