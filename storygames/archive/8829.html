\n I'm probably going to GenCon this year (yay!), and when I look at the registration info, they have this thing you can buy called a VIG badge.

It's fascinating. On the one hand, it looks pretty cool - you get free beverages and a special lounge. But, um, it's 500 bucks. It looks like what you're actually paying for is a badge saying "I paid lots of cash for this badge". That's kind of silly... and a little disgusting to me.

Am I missing something? Do VIGs get hotel rooms included in the price or something?
