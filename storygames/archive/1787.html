The holiday season is rapidly approaching. That means lots of differant things to everyone, but to me it means giving preasents. I love the holidays. I celebrate christmas, but only in a capitalist non-denominational way. You know, trees, santa, stockings, various nogs, but no nativity. I want to spread this seasonal joy to you, my dear friends.

So here is what i'm going to do. I have whipped up a short game. It's an interactive board game, sort of like an art experiment, sort of like a quick, little, one-shot-play-it-as-soon-as-you-get-it-game. This game is also going to be my holiday greeting card. That means i'll be sending it out free to everyone who wants one.

Here is how you get on my holiday mailing list (and thus recieve your free game): Send a self addressed stamped envelope to...
