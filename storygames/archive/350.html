Josh Roby
March 2006 edited March 2006 in Forum Discussion
So the Lussumo Vanilla forum software is an interesting departure from phpBB, and on the whole I think I like it, or at least approve of it.

However, the smug-ass "upgrade your browser" message when I try to do something that only works in Firefox is starting to really piss me off. I use, for reference, Safari (at home) and Opera (at work), both of which are very good browsers, sleek and efficient and secure. The fact that they don't have whatever side-effect functions or whatever that Lussumo uses out of the Firefox function library does not mean that I need to "upgrade" to Firefox. It means Lussumo needs to take a moment to think about accessibility.

It's like government sites that offer official documents in MSWord format, since "everybody uses Word, right?"

Mutter. Mostly this is just griping.
