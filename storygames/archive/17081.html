\n What does Mystic Empyrean and a greasy, half eaten Chinese takeout box have in common, Find out! The Rolling Intentions Crew along with some special guests to play this masterpiece system. In a world covered in fowl aether, emotions run high as these Eidolons open their eyes to a new world, they are First To Wake.

First to Wake is a test module by Aaron Friesen which brings characters upon a scenario style character creation. These Immortal Demi-Gods first begin to recall their memories of who they they were and literally take shape by their emotions as they come to surface. (This is a play test)

The game doesn't primary use dice and in this session we used colored glass beads for the balance system.
