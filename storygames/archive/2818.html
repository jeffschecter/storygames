\n Hi!

We're having the next installment of JiffyCon fun in Boston--well, really in Cambridge, MA. It will be on Saturday April 28th, at Pandemonium Books. Pandemonium is a great sci fi/fantasy and gaming store which has moved recently and now has a space set aside for gaming. The Boston SG crowd has been meeting there on Wednesday nights, so it will be fun to have a bigger shindig with them.

We're looking for gms/facilitators. Let me or Nathan know if you'd like to come and run something. There's more info at the website here:

JiffyCon Boston

We'd love to see you all! The next JiffyCon will be back in western Mass, sometime in June.

all the best,
Emily
