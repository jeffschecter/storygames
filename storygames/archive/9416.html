    \n...*sigh*...

    I honestly keep intending to get the book back in print, but it just hasn't happened. In the past, I've had more than one printer simply flake out on me. Currently there are a number of edits I want to make, and I feel that should be done before re-printing. (I'm not "changing" anything, I just want to make certain passages more readable.) But life circumstances have been rather distracting as of late, and the re-writing process keeps dragging on...

    I *want* to say the book will be available again within a couple months, but I think I said that a year or two ago. I don't want to seem like I'm stringing people along, so all I'll say right now is that I'm confident the book will be back in print *sometime this year*.
