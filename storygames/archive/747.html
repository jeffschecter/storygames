  • rossum

    Mad props to the Fiend Folio here. Years later, having never run any of them, monsters from that volume stand out in my mind:

    Throat Leech: looks like a small twig, floats in water. You fil your water jug, drink, zang, a leech on your uvula. Manual tells you to run a hot poker down your friend's throat while he's suffocating from the bloated leech.

    Penanggalan: Crazy flying vampire of a head and guts of a beautiful woman. Creepy and seductive and disgusting and a vampire. I pulled out one of these in a playtest of Stranger Things.

    Yellow Musk Creeper and Yellow Musk Zombies: A freakin' carnivorous plant that makes zombies. How awesome is that? People (or demihumans, whatever) walk by, get entranced by the smell, lie down and die... the plant then sticks a seed in their head, they rise as zombies to bring more victims to the mother plant.

    There was another one that was a semi-sentient biped formed on insects. Don't remember the name, though.

    Great. Now instead of prepping for a game tonight, I'm going to read through the damn Fiend Folio. Can't use monsters in Cyberpunk! Hmm...

    MDK
