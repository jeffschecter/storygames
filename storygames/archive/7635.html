    \n Another serious answer for you Kevin- 3:16 is about space marines that uses a simple set of mechanics that are easy to pick up and play in an evening. It also rewards long term play* through some elegant nuances in the system, such as character development mechanics and progressively-difficult challenges in the form of alien (and human) opponents.

    Some folks have expressed a general disinterest in the most obvious theme of the game, which is killing the hell out of all other lifeforms. If that makes you uncomfortable, or if the very idea of space marines doesn't hit your buttons, the game probably isn't for you.

    * I say this having only run 2 sessions so far, but I'm confident that it's true based on reading the game.
