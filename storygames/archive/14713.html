\nVoting has just opened for the Ennies, so let's remind each other of what's on offer. (I am hardly unbiased. I have two nominations. Do speak up for stuff you like.)

In Best New Game, I'd really appreciate votes for A Taste For Murder. Do vote for The Dresden Files and Happy Birthday Robot, too, which are well-deserved nominees.

In Best Writing, I'd appreciate votes for The Dance In The Blood, which I'm really proud of. And vote for Dresden Files again there.

Do consider voting for Hell For Leather: Free Edition for Best Free Product. It's a British indie game, which Sebastian Hickey has worked very hard on. It's the one where you knock piles of dice over.

Also, note that Happy Birthday Robot and Dresden Files are both nominated for Product Of The Year, so vote for them there too. (I haven't listed all the categories that Dresden Files and Happy Birthday Robot are in, but obviously, support them where you see them.)

What have I forgotten? What are your favourites?
