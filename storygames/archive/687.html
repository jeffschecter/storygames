    Thunder God,

    1984 Prime is a game set in an alternate past: In late 1962, the Cuban Missle Crisis escalated into World War Three. Nukes flew and the world as we know it never happened. The U.S. government had set up a huge underground complex, the HAVEN, as a precaution- never expecting to populate it. Over the last twenty-odd years, the HAVEN society has fractured into factions along family lines. Players take roles from the first post-war generation, never knowing the pre-war surface. The game is about trust and betrayal, exploration and renewal.
