    Posted By: fnord3125Posted By: Tim BoserOr is this a secret preview of a new game about train conductors wrestling monkeys?
    I'd play it!

    I... might be working on this game. See, the monkey's on a train. And there are passengers and conductors. Each player has one passenger and one conductor.

    The passengers tell each other stories about themselves to pass the time. Gameplay here is very lose - Baron Munchausen type stuff. Players build up tokens at this phase based on the stories told.

    At each stop, some passengers get off and new ones get on. The other passengers get off, stretch their legs, and travel the city. We don't focus on them right now, but their time in the city may be later told as a story.

    But instead we focus on the conductors. The monkey tries to escape at each stop, and the conductors have to keep him on. Players can spend their tokens here to help out.

    At the last stop is the zoo. The Monkey makes a final attempt and all the players use their tokens to try to be the individual conductor that gets the monkey off the train and into the zoo.

    This conductor is lauded as a hero and makes the paper. He retires on a reward from the wealthy zoo owner. The others... continue conducting.

    Erm... should I actually continue developing this? Would anyone play it?
