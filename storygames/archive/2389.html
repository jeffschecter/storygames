    Ok, here is a revised introduction to the character sheet (I've got both parts done now).

    New pdf pages: Explain Status! / Explain Programming!

    I changed it from portrait to landscape, and used a font with greater clarity, Swiss721. Let me know how that works for you Bryan!

    Now about the premise. To sumarize as a question: How do you handle existing in an extremely inhuman and dangerous world? You are a mechanical being with one of six sexualities which can change over time, seiged from a controlling AI which wants nothing more than to turn you into an actual machine, and have to cooperate with a handful of other units in your Node (something like a family- also the playing group). The AI part of the game borrows some roach-like aspects, like AI instruction cards. A lot of the game is based on surviving, which is an issue of earning and conserving energy (survive = success generally).
