\n Hi!
\n Basically, the game is about graduating from a school of magic in a Harry Potter-like world.
\n There is a good mystery mechanic and conflicts are resolved with dominoes. One side determines who gets clue points and the other determines who narrates.
\n If you want to play it on line, you can use this software:
\nhttp://www.vassalengine.org/download.php
\nThis is free, java-based, multi-platform and games are hosted on their server, so you don't have to much with firewalls and ports.
\n
\nAnd this module:
\nhttp://www.vassalengine.org/wiki/Module:Piecepack
\nThis module will let you use text chat, dice, dominoes, tokens and play them anywhere on the virtual table.
