    \n Thanks very much, everybody.

    So combining all the feedback I got and assigning half a vote to each when people chose two equally, the result is:

    HAND + GUN: 2
    PURE 4.5
    BABY DADDY 5.5
    GUN 5.5
    HAND 7.5
    LINEUP 20 21 (thanks, Renato!)

    Hand and Baby Daddy got more split votes than straight votes. Lineup was my first try at the cover.
