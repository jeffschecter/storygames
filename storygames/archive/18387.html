\nAll Rolled Up offers an innovative and compact way to bring your gaming to the table. Not just a dice bag - when you're All Rolled Up you're ready to game without needing to worry about finding all your gaming gear.



Why should you need to worry about whether you can fit your dice, pencil, pens, eraser, beads and everything else into a dice bag only really designed to hold dice? Or struggle to jam everything into your dice bag, but end up carrying a separate bag for your pens and index cards?



All Rolled Up gaming and dice pouch offers a compact and flexible solution for your immediate gaming needs - whether you're a role player, war-gamer, or a trading card game fanatic.

The adaptable tie means that the roll can even hold a pocket-sized rulebook as well as dice, etc.





The dice bag element has the capacity to hold more than 100 16mm six-sided dice. I've also stowed trading cards, miniatures, beads, counters, tape measures and other gaming components inside. The dice bag has a box cut base so that you can have it open in the middle of the table for everyone to share dice, and the depth means you can see what's inside without peering in or groping around for the die you want.



While we have a web site and will sell through an on-line store, we're also keen to meet gamers face-to-face and show what the product can do. Each All Rolled Up is handmade in the UK and designed by myself and my wife - and we're keen to spread the word as long time gamers.

You can see a (relatively brief) presentation on using the All Rolled Up at YouTube.

YouTube Channel

It will cost \xa315 for the basic All Rolled Up, with the option for custom fabrics and specials to be negotiated on a case by case basis; prices range \xa320+ for custom designs.

We have about 40+ different fabrics at the moment that we're making the initial ARU from - and when we have taken pictures they'll be going up, along with the site. We will have about 100 ARU with us, so there should be extras of a few designs.
