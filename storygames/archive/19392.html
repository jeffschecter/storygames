I have begun a Patreon project to help independent game developers afford high quality illustrations for their games... and have recently revamped it to make it even more affordable.

These are now packs of 5 similarly themed RPG illustrations created from your nominations, votes, and submitted character descriptions and start as low as $5 each. With pledges of that level and higher comes a license to use the illustrations on any number of projects... indefinitely.

I'll be creating several packs a month and you get to select your choice of as many packs as you supported that month.

Unlike Kickstarter, you don't pay until the art has released. Check out the specific details at www.patreon.com/jeshields.
