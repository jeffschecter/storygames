    \nHere's the video I took of Dr. Lisa's trauma surgery. Patient came in with four gunshot wounds to the left chest and shoulder.

    The way the trauma game works is this - somebody actaully mangles the procedure sheet and assigns a relative difficulty (in this case, Joel went at it with something sharp and poked holes, rating it a six I believe). Then the doctor is timed as she rapidly debrides and sutures the wound. There's a card draw every ten seconds, and red cards are complications. It's silly but strangely fun and intense.
