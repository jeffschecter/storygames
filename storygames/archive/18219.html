    \n I have run a full campaign of BE before, and I ran a campaign of BW that used the Kingdoms mod (adds the meta-plot mechanics into BW) for about 6 months before I had to end it to do time constraints.

    I have also created a very simple re-creation of the same mechanics for Smallville using the Doom pool, with episodes and seasons breaking it up. It worked well, had the same feel, and was significantly less complicated.

    And this sort of thing has been done many times with Fate. I believe Shadow of Anglerre has plot mechanic already added into it, but you could probably find more by looking around for something called the Fate Fractal.

    It all just comes down to how detailed you want the rules to be, and what you like.

    The biggest thing is make sure your players are on board with the concept. A lot of people just really do like the concept of abstracting a meta level of the story through mechanics.
