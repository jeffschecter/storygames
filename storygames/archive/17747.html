\n I only noticed the appropriateness to making this skin while writing this post, so I suppose in a way this is kinda my own little Christmas present for StoryGames. And if Christmas doesn't do anything for you, I hope you still enjoy the playbook.

The Reverent is a skin for a special teenager who has a powerful connection to the Almighty and can channel holy power to protect the damned and condemn the innocent. Ooops, I meant that the other way around. I'm a little worried about overlap with The Angel but I think it's different enough to work alongside it. This is still VERY much in an experimental state and feedback and comments would be very much appreciated. I am happy to modify or add to what I have here to better suit everyone's vision. So without further delay, here it is...

The Reverent

Stats
Add 1 to one of these:

Hot 1, Cold 1, Volatile -1, Dark -1

Reverent Moves (Choose 2)

Martyr
When you take harm because you were trying to help or protect someone, mark experience and take a String on whoever you were helping or protecting.

Eye For An Eye
If someone gives you a condition, give them a condition in return. If you already going to give them a condition, take 1 forward against them instead.

Irreproachable
When another PC tries to turn you on or shut you down, you may spend a String to have their roll be shifted one category lower. (10 up is 7-9, 7-9 is a miss)

Stigmata
When someone inflicts harm in your presence, you may spend a String on them to have them take the harm as well.

Guide My Hand
Whenever someone you have Strings with is in trouble (your call), you may spend a String you have with them to have you already be there, with or without any clear explanation why.

Sanctuary
Whenever you consecrate a safe place for another, if they spend a night there with you, both of you may remove a condition.

Your Backstory
\u2022 You watch everyone, practically unnoticed, waiting for them to slip up. Gain a String on everyone.
\u2022 Someone knows your greatest sin. They gain 2 Strings on you.

Sex Move
When you deny someone sexually, gain the condition 'tempted' and take a String on them. When you have sex with someone, you lose all your Strings on them and remove a condition.

Darkest Self
The judgement of the King of Heaven is upon them all! You see sin and corruption all around you and it\u2019s your divine mission to stamp it out, to burn the wicked and cast down the dirty whores. No longer will they laugh at you or put you down for your beliefs, now is the time to show them what true power is. You\u2019ll create a hell on earth to save their miserable souls, whether they want it or not. You escape your darkest self when you repent and someone forgives you of your wrongdoings.

Advancement
Take another Reverent move.
Take another Reverent move.
Take a move from another Skin.
Take a move from another Skin.
Join a Bible Study Group
\x81Add 1 to Hot (max 3).
\x81Add 1 to Cold (max 3).
\x81Add 1 to Volatile (max 3).
\x81Add 1 to Dark (max 3).

    \n Anyways, I reworked it.

    The Reverent (I'd have called it the Faithful)

    They call you a Jesus-freak and a bible-thumper, but they have no idea what faith is. They don't understand that blessed grace and holy charisma that make your soul a firebrand. They don't have the humility to commit themselves to anything beyond their petty wants. That's okay, you'll show them. You'll preach to whoever'll listen and save their little souls whether they want it or not.

    Your commitments wouldn't be meaningful without the temptations of the flesh and vice. Your body screams at you to betray your sacred promises. Your friends turn away from the righteous path. Your faith gives you strength but it's not always easy to stand up for what's right. It's so hard to be strong one all the time. Something's gotta give.

    We all need forgiveness for something, right?

    I'd have made the Stats like this:
    Hot 1, Cold -1, Volatile 1, Dark -1.

    It's not a temptation if your no good at it anyways. Also, I\u2019ve spent more than a few summers at bible camp. It's hot and volatile. Also, I'm mean like that.

    So, five out of six of the moves are harm related.

    Martyr is fine, a bit boring but fine.

    Eye for an Eye: If someone gives you a condition or deals you harm, give them a condition. If you already going to give them a condition, take 1 forward instead.

    Shield of Faith should do something totally different.

    Maybe like this:

    Irreproachable:
    When another PC tries to turn you on or shut you down, you may spend a string to have their roll be shifted one category lower.

    Bless this House Sanctuary
    Whenever you consecrate a safe place for another, if they spend a night there with you, both of you may remove a condition.

    Purity Ring
    Whenever you publicly and ritually indulge your virtues and make a show of you faith, make a promise to your Higher Power, and write it down. Until you violate that promise (or the end of the session), you may take advantage of your own Conditions. Whoever witnesses you violate any of your accumulated promises, gains a string on you.

    Sex Move

    This should be a pretty big deal. Like, marking XP isn't all that tempting.

    When you deny someone sexually, gain the condition 'tempted' and take a string on them.
    When you have sex with someone, you both lose all your string on each other.
    Lost the condition 'tempted' and gain the condition 'guilty'

    As for the Darkest Self:
    You should escape it only when someone forgives you for your own bullshit.

    Also, you (hopefully) grow out of your self-righteousness not into it. That season advance should be 'make someone feel beautiful.'

    \n I like a bit of tension in playbooks. Just being good at the expected stuff, or doubleplus good at it, isn't usually that interesting for me.
