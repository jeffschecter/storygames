\n Hello everyone,

I'm back for a second round of looking for playtesters of unWritten.

unWritten is a game designed to create the types of stories you get from film and literature. It uses the 5 act structure, the hero's journey, and improv techniques to create a story and generate play. With it I've played Greek inspired mythic tales, games about escaped lab rats, pirates in space (with drunk russians), etc. It's a game designed to tell a certain type of story. And, it does it very well.

If you have more questions let me know. If you want a playtest copy just ask and I'll send it to you. I'm not posting much on my blog cause it's messed up and I can't fix it right now. I would love any feedback!

Thanks Alex

PS! Josh Roby will be at Gencon running it at the games on demand booth! Go find him! If you can't play he will have some copies of unWritten to hand out.
