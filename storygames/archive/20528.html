    \n I've recently moved out but spent several years in Amsterdam; the community I recommend is NRG, it is a monthly meetup which runs a variety of games, and over the years I have tried a wide range of games there, with weird birds like (in no particular order) Fiasco, s/Lay w/Me, Panty Explosion or Paranoia on one side of the spectrum and pretty mainstream behemoths like Dragon Age or Mutants & Masterminds. Well, we even playtested D&D5 on one occasion! (It was called D&D Next back then) Most of the time I was quite satisfied, with some minor mishaps like a Planescape game ran without cant and factions (perhaps I expected too much from the blood who ran the show), and each time it was a relaxing break from my own games where I am always stuck at being the GM.

    They did run some tiny storygame-oriented cons (conDamned James mentioned above was one of them, I still have their orange d6 somewhere), but way less often than they should. The community is quite pleasant and open to suggestions as well, so I'm sure you'll find a lot of options open with their help. Good luck!
