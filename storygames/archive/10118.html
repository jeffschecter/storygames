\n Malcolm Sheppard and I launched my modern fantasy hybrid \xc6ternal Legends a couple of years ago, but external commitments prevented us really capitalizing on the launch. So we're re-launching.

What I'd love to do is hand out some free copies of the print edition at GenCon. Unfortunately, I can't be there. So instead we're giving away the PDF until the end of GenCon. Anyone who gets the PDF can get the print edition for $11 (nearly \xa38) off the normal price. This offer's only on over GenCon, in place of me being there.

I've done my best to get some proper support online, as opposed to how the original release happened. My site has an introductory character pack and introductory adventure, and links to both the two reviews on RPG.net and a collection of things people have said about the game. I've been opening up the design process over on mobunited.com, in preparation for the release of the game's second supplement.

I've learned a lot from the first launch and what came after. What I'm currently having a hard time getting straight is what level of online support people want/expect when getting into a new game. So I put it to you: what would you like to see, and what do you expect?
