    \n Do you mean thematically, mechanically, or playstyle? There are categories like Story Now or Old School Renaissance. There are categories like GM-less, or Story Game, or Hexcrawl. Sometimes games are discussed as "procedural mechanics" or categorized by "GM Agenda."

    There are just a lot of ways of categorizing games. What are you interested in? Do you want to try out as many gaming styles as posssible or do you want to categorize a particular game, or what?
