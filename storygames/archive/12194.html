    \n I ran a quick session of Danger Patrol today.
    \nThis notion on how to interpret happiness in two ways, of experiencing self and remembering self, was still on my mind.
    \n
    \nThere was a point during the fight where I came up with some quirky, funny spot-on dangers for the game\u2014and my experiencing self, what I once considered my Flow, was happy.
    \n
    \nGiven that I spawned too many low level threats, the combat sort of petered out. The big end, was putting out a small fire. So, now my remembering self, as I recall the game, isn\u2019t super jazzed about it. It was fun play, but my remembering self wouldn\u2019t say it was super-fun, happy play.
    \n
    \nI think, had I paced the threats better, give a good big bad a larger threat, then the end would have happened more dramatically. And that type of intense ending would have a better chance of impressing my future remembering self.
