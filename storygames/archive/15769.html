    \nTainted Love is by Soft Cell and not New Order. Their Sex Dwarf is also another hit, but not really MH. Bizarre Love Triangle would be a better New Order track; I'd probably go with Age of Consent, though.

    New Ladytron is way too good and the older stuff a little too straight forward electroclash, but, man, Seventeen is a great track for this, or like Minors at Night (Still Sick) by ADULT. if low-fi electro is the thing you're going for.

    Personally, I'd probably put on a LCD Soundsystem, even more likely, or a Placebo album. Nothing really reminds me more of teen angst than Placebo. With tracks like Because I Want You, Flesh Mechanic, Pure Morning, Every You Every Me and the whole of their self-titled first release, they're dead ringers for this kind of thing.

    Although, depending on one's tolerance for terrible teen music, you might want to try like a Good Charlotte album or something. Anything hooky with profound seeming and emotive but ultimately melodramatic lyrics.
