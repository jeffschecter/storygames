\n It's one week till PAX! And of course you want to play and run some awesome games there, right?

WHERE
Rooms 305 and 306. Twice the space of last year!

WHAT
Hot indie game action. Games start on the hour. Be ready.

HOW CAN I HELP?
Come run games! Particularly around dinner time, since that's when the con hall closes and everyone wants to play games. It'd be helpful if you'd let us know when you're likely to be around, but feel free to just show up too. Just check in at the main table and we'll add your games to the board.
