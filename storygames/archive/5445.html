    \n The Chinese "word" dice are hilarious, considering that you can't spell out "words" for the numbers in Chinese. Those are just the homophones they use when writing on checks or other official documents so that the numbers can't be changed later by adding a few strokes. I'm not sure why you'd ever want those on dice. It would have made more since to write the numbers out in Pinyin (the romanization system). The other dice are very cool, though.
