    \n We've got a good-sized group around here. There's nearly always gaming to be had of the non-"white wolf or dnd" variety (though I'm sure that's going on, too).

    Like Harry said, stopping by Red Castle on Wednesday nights and Rainy Day on certain Fridays (check the GoPlay forum) are your best bet for pickup gaming and getting to know people.

    The next big thing we're trying to do is a Diana Jones Award game day, on July 23rd.
