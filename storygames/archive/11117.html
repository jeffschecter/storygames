    Using two dashes -like so- is what it uses to strikethrough, and *two asterisks* makes something bold. For this reason (the dashes, really) I usually spell out "plus, blank, minus" or "success, failure, botch" - we've occasionally had problems with it interpreting minus signs as strikethroughs.
