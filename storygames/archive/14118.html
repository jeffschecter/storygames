\n I like Lady Blackbird. I'm not alone in liking it.

I am cool with there never being an expanded print version of Lady Blackbird. In fact, I kind of prefer it that way. The thing that John made should not, in my meaningless opinion, be bloated into a common gaming tome. That would ruin the core awesome of the thing.

However. I think that I'd like to buy something related to Lady Blackbird. Like an art book. Or a poster. Possibly a hat or t-shirt. Or maybe an art book. Did I mention that I'd like an art book? Basically, I'm saying that one could sell me artwork that helps inspire scenes, characters, traits, tags, equipment, etc without hitting me on the head with lists.

Mostly because I'd like to give John money for his killer fun creation. But also because I'd love to have a game that is more inspiration than text.

If you feel the same way, make a bit of noise with me. Maybe John will hear that noise and make it happen for us.
