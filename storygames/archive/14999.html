    \n When your character interacts socially, roll three Emoticon Dice:
    You pick one result as your character's feelings after the interaction, and the GM picks one that determines what the other person's feelings are...and then role-play how it ended up like that (so, you're happy and their pissed...did you just insult them and they couldn't think of a good comeback?)

    If you roll any doubles, that automatically becomes the result (you roll double mehs...you both discussed the weather...)

    If you roll triples, that automatically becomes the result, and some event occurs because of it (you roll triple angries...both of you are so pissed off, do you end up starting a gang war, defame each other on FaceSpace, or something else?)
