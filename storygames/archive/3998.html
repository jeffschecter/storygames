\n This was my first (non So Cal) Gen Con. I had a great time and recommend everyone go if they can. Terrific amount of energy across the board. I ended up ditching out of most of my reg'd games because Games on Demand was a vastly superior alternative (and because of the ballrooms where the regular games were held, noise for G.o.D. was less and ventiallation a hundred times better).

Some comments:

Having read Bliss Stage, I now have a man-crush on Beh Lehman. It hits so many tropes of the teens 'n robots genre that I've been interested in playing. Wow!

I'm already scheduled to run Grey Ranks at my local con but after witnessing the great demo, it's clear Jason's game is shaping up to be something amazing.

Judson Lester ran a Poison'd game for us and I loved it (and I'm pimping it over at rpg.net)

I didn't get a chance to demo Steal Away Jordan so I can't comment. To be honest, I think Julia dressing on Thursday in the garb of the setting and period made me uncomfortable enough not to want to play the game. It was a decision that I regret now upon hearing that the game rocks.

Romping around with Don's Burning Blossoms game with Andy K. was a blast (and Andy -- you're a pretty cool fellow, awesome to finally game with you!) Don -- you're my new fave GM; short actual play but you made a great impression.

Malcom Craig ran Cold City for us and he really is a rather great GM and world-traveller. What a nice guy.

Jonathan Walton is so creatively smart it scares me. We shared cherry juice.

Mike Holmes rocks. Period. Ditto Joshua Newman. Ditto Fred Hicks.

Playtesting Paul Czege's Niche Engine was a terrific experience. The dude is from another planet and I want to live there.

Mountain Witch, PA was a fantastic twist on a classic game. The meat packing plant on the side of the mountain has caused nightmares.

I regret not worshipping at Greg Stolze and Vincent Baker's feet and not meeting Judd; I kept on missing the dudes. Damn.

Loved hanging with Devil May Care and Alvin Frewer all con long.

I'm missing people and games...and I'm sorry to exclude anyone or anything. I had no problems with anyone and all my games were extremely fun.

I bought loads of stuff, ashcans included, and I can't wait to play them.

It was very awesome meeting so many of you folks -- truly a great community.

I will see you next year!
