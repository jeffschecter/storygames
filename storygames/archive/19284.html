\n Mel and I recap some of our Dreamation 2014 games, with AP excerpts from Dungeon Crawl Classics, Ashen Stars, and Trail of Cthulhu as well as a discussion of using in media res and flashbacks as devices for the GM in designing convention scenarios; we come to no conclusions other than it can be done, and it helps draw players in and get to the action quicker. The new episode of the podcast is here.

    \n This is great news. VP was one of my favorite listens. One question, I noticed that the podcast seemed to move away from a format where you would talk about some topic and then play a short snippet of actual play spotlighting that topic, toward much longer recorded play examples. Was this a conscious change? I ask because I found the discussion mixed with short clips to be both unique to VP and informative/enjoyable.
