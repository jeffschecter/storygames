\n Hey all!
The community at Salgan al Sol, (the game design forum for spanish speaking nerds!) is taking a shot at translating Clinton Nixon\xc2\xb4s excellent game The Shadow of Yesterday.
The work is being done in the forum, but the resulting translation is being hosted in this wiki.

The community is formed by people from Argentina, Chile, M\xc3\xa9xico and Spain right now, and we\xc2\xb4ll do our best to deliver a neutral Spanish translation of the game.

If there\xc2\xb4s any problems with the idea that we are not aware of, just let us know.

And of course, thanks Clinton for your art!

Cheers!
Guido
