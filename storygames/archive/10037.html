\n I am working on a game for my father as a gift - it's based on the old cliffhanger serials of the 1930s and 1940s, particularly the Western serials.

What I'm looking for right now is a list of cliffhanger endings - you know those bits that end each episode to get the audience to come back for more (and pay for the privledge).

An example to get things started:

* The hero(es) ride into a canyon only to have the villain(s) trigger a rockslide on top of them.

    \n
    \nThis person or thing is trapped in an environement made dangerous by (pick one or more): A bridge out ahead, Lit dynamite, an Indian Raid, Bandits, Flash floods, Burning building, Forest fire, Avalanche, Ghosts
    \n
    \nOur hero has a solution, but may not be able to implement it in time becase of (pick one or more): Distance, Unconsciousness, Exhaustion, A fight he's in the middle of, Being (literally) tied up, Ignorance of the danger, Fallen rocks, Extreme weather
