\n So I bought a PDF copy of Dead of Night and am quite pleased with it. I dropped the physical edition on my wish list and hope someone grabs it for me this Christmas. Fingers crossed. Anyway, in different places in the book it points us toward www.steampowerpublishing.com to get various supplemental materials. The problem: they aren't there.

Or maybe I'm not looking in the right place? There are supposed to be printable versions of the scenarios' pre-gen characters available at the site, but all I find are blank character sheets. Not the same thing. Has this stuff just not been posted yet, or is there some wrinkle in the URL I haven't learned about?
