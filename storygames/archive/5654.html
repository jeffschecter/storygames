    \n Well, for one, Saturday is a big night for parties and for all-nighters (LARPs in particular). As such, I've found that the Sunday slots are... slow, to say the least. The first time I demoed there for Looney Labs, I think I played a TOTAL of 10 ten-minute games in eight hours. The AM crowd is sparse; and by afternoon, folks are starting to roll out. YMMV, though, as Dogs only needs three or four sign-ups to be playable.

    Anyhow, good luck with it, and be sure to do a little promoting at the con beforehand, to pump up interest.
