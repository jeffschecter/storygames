\n The Galactic Empire was in a period of peace. The Emperor ruled benevolently from his crushed velvet throne on Galactia. Wars were a nearly forgotten myth.

One man knew better. President McKinley's Ghost had shown him the way. He saw the weak underbelly the Galactic Empire was frighteningly exposed. And he had a time machine.

This man, now known only as Lord Stryfe, recovered the brains from history's bad guys. The later cloning techniques used by our heroes in the Super Galactic Time Patrol were not yet perfected, so his first attempts to "resurrect" the villains were based around fusing the brain with other life forms or robotic bodies.
