\n I present to you the new Crowdfunding Collection Nr. 52, which includes every tabletop project started between 09.04 till 09.19.

This time around presenting more than 50 tabletop RPG related projects.
In regards of the foreign language products I consider Human Punishment of broader interest, as the social deduction game is done in German and English.
As for the tabletop RPGs, I do consider Tiny Dungeons 2e my personal highlight. The previous campaign for Mecha & Kajius went like a charm and this one looks cool as well. Someone Has Died might be on the brink of being considered a tabletop rpg, yet I am deeply fascinated by the topic as well as the sensitive approach, thus I think it deserves a mention. Fans of Monte Cook will probably already have heard of Numenera 2, as the project rockets on. Afterbomb Madness offers a \u201efallouty\u201c look and SURVIVE THIS! a call back to Stranger Things.
Forest of Fate is the game book project that caught my attention the most. The mix of choose your own adventure and board/card game is quite intriguing. In regards of Larp, I really do hope the Rocky Horror Larp finds enough support.
Among the adventures I am most tempted by In Darkness Delve, as I know the auther, as well as by Burial Plots thanks to its theme.
The Constellation Dice do still look totally rad with their newest project.
As for the various projects, I do fancy the FAERIE ENCHANTERS GRIMOIRE a lot, it does portray faeries in a fashion that I imagine changelings might look like. Well, and among the other games, there\u2019s The Expanded Lunatic Hybrid Maze Puzzle which tickles my fancy.
There are many, many more cool projects. Take a look for yourself:
