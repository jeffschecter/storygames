    \n Alright, that's six if I can count. So let's cut off there, all of you are in.

    Phase One: Making Mix Tapes

    Everyone pick a theme for your mix tape. I'm doing one right now that's "love is circular in nature". You could do "fuck the establishment" or "lonely Texas highways" or "blowjobs" if you were so inclined.

    Make a playlist of between 10-20 songs. Then, post your theme + tracklist. If a song has a Youtube video, feel free to link it! You must make sure that the first two songs on your playlist have either lyrics or Youtube videos available, so that we can partake in them (make sure to provide those links for the first two songs. Any after that, feel free to do whatever).

    Your songs don't have to stick rigidly to your theme. But they must all have some connection to it.
    It's best if your first song on the playlist has a strong sense of narrative, but whatever you want to put first is fine.

    Post before Tuesday. Cheers.
