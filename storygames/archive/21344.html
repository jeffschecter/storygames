\n Storm Hollow is live on Kickstarter! Storm Hollow is a tabletop storytelling adventure game that mixes traditional roleplaying with boardgame elements. It is beautifully illustrated, and is accessible to new players while still giving veteran roleplayers plenty to jump into. It's on Kickstarter through November 9th!
