In the book Impro, Johnstone has a game where everyone stands in a circle and adds the next word to a story.

I want to try it here, since it is a long weekend in the states and I didn't have anything cooler to read than what you wrote.

The rules are, I will start the story off with a word, if you are next, add a word that should follow it in the sentence and add the best next word that you can think of, then let someone else add the next word. If you cross post go back and read the sentence again and change your post accordingly. You shouldn't post again till at least two other people have put new words in. Try very hard not to direct the story but provide the best next word and see where it goes.

The story will probably be very funny and it might be dirty, if you are offenden by that sort of thing please look elsewhere.

If you want to talk about the story let's try to do it in another discussion. Good Luck!
