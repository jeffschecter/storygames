    \n The difference between pre- and post- release is that before you let your darling go, you and perhaps a few others are the only ones totally focused on it. After you release it, suddenly there are many, many people looking at it, asking questions, posting APs, and creating excitement. It's a different kind of work after that point, but I think it's a lot of fun.

    For what it's worth, your web site looks as good as your game, so your hard work is paying off.
