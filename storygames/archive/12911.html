\n I'm not sure if people know about the projects the guys behind this website are working on:
Volumique

Have a look at their concepts for a Pirate board game and a zombie book (Night of the Living Dead pixels).

They combine paper with smartphones. I think it is a really interesting development that could also be used for roleplaying games.
