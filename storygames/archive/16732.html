\n1000 years ago, the Social Wars ended the storied Age of Wonder. Today, your tiny village is a point of light in Gamma Terra, beset on all sides by vicious marauders, sinister robots, and terrible creatures. But if you are bold enough and wise enough, today might be the dawn of a new age of glory!

Start with Pocket Danger Patrol and then add in:

Threat-O-Matic:Style
1 - Zombie / Robot
2 - Ghost / Ancient
3 - Immortal / Winged
4 - Mutant / Alien
5 - Insect / Tentacled
6 - Telepathic / Snake

Threat-O-Matic:Role
1 - Marauder / Parasite
2 - Empire / Nomads
3 - Slavers / Hive
4 - Cult / Vessel
5 - Predators / Racists
6 - ? / ?

Threat-O-Matic:Strength or Weakness
1 - Action or Daring
2 - Cunning or Guts
3 - Battle or Power
4 - Technology or Insight
5 - Roll or pick two
6 - None!

Threat-O-Matic:Opportunity
1 - Wealth
2 - Romance
3 - Fame
4 - Expand your Village
5 - Alliances / Friends
6 - Technology

I'm imagining a three-scene campaign game, the first session played at the Heroic tier, the second at the Paragon tier, and the third at the Epic tier, with new threat-o-matics for each tier. But for now this is my idea.
