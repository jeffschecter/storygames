\n Hi folks

Just to let you know about a IndieGoGo Campaign for a new game coming from the UK.

What You Wish For (WYWF) is a structured freeform roleplaying game for exactly 3 players. In it you explore the happiest memories and greatest hopes of everyday people, just like you and I, who have become stuck in a rut, dissatisfied with their lives and directionless.

You will reflect upon your characters past, dream about their possible future and then make a positive move forward to change their lives for the better.

Games of WYWF are pacy, taking around 1 - 2 hours to complete, but they produce satisfying and emotionally poignant stories.

As someone who participated in the playtests I can attest to both the brevity and emotional depth of the game. My most recent experience was very moving, coming out of seemingly trivial input about a person's supposed life. Ash, the designer is launching the campaign primarily to fund a quality print of the Inspiration deck used in the game.

Thanks for your support.
