    Jason,

    \n

    How important is the collections of images and related sort of content management? Is it more than just uploading a file or providing a link, or will you be storing the collection of images on the server.

    \n

    If collection management is important, you may want to look at a framework like Rails or Django to make the that side of the system easier. I don't know much about the Ruby/Rails ecosystem but I think Python/Django should be able to do what you want fairly easily. There are a couple of PDF libraries available for python, and while i haven't used it myself i have heard positive thing about ReportLab toolkit.

    \n

    If its just a quick script to upload an image and fill in some text, something like a straight PHP script is going to be less overhead to learn and set up and will do the job just fine.

    \n

    Hope that helps
