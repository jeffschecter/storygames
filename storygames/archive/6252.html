\n I just noticed in slash-dot this morning a story about Amazon's POD offering, BookSurge, and their aggressive behaviour towards other POD publishers trying to list through Amazon. Has anyone looked into BookSurge? What are the advantages/disadvantages for an Indie publisher using the service? (On the face of it, getting distribution through Amazon would seem to be a "good thing" for a publisher, but I'm sure there are gotchas involved -- what might they be?)
