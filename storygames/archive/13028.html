\n I'm running a Chronicles of Amber themed Fiasco playset at AmberCon NW this year. You're all nigh-immortal princes and princesses of Amber, born into great magical power. As you'll soon discover, however, having a lot of power just means that you're capable of screwing up in completely epic ways.

I'm playtesting this soon - Saturday evening, if all goes well - and I'd appreciate any feedback on improving the playset before then. I'm sure that there are entries that could be made better, redundancies that could be removed, etc. Since my audience is primarily composed of Amber players, I used a lot of Amber proper names and concepts. I don't know if I'll keep those in the final draft or not.

Here's my beta version of the "Fiasco in Amber" playset. Let me know what you think!

* * *
A Fiasco In Amber

RELATIONSHIPS...

1. Family...
* Big / little brother or sister
** Twins
*** In favor / out of favor
**** Raised in court / sheltered
***** Illegitimate / legitimate half-siblings
****** Noble lineage / uncertain heritage

2. The Past...
* War veterans
** Shared scandal
*** Prankster and victim
**** Courted the same man or woman
***** Nearly killed one another in a duel
****** Blood pact

3. Friendship...
* Shared interest
** Manipulator / victim
*** Bitter social adversaries
**** Drinking buddies
***** Friendly rivals
****** Enemy of my enemy

4. Kingdom...
* Captain of the guard and huntmaster of Arden
** Admirals of the navy
*** Spymaster and informant
**** General of the army and diplomat
***** Master or mistress of revels and proteg\xe9
****** Lawmaker and enforcer

5. Treason...
* Attempted fratricide, sororicide, or regicide
** Generals of a secret army
*** Conspirators with the enemy
**** Kingmaker and puppet
***** Wielders of dark magics
****** Anarchists

6. Soul...
* Bitter rivals
** Special bond
*** Near strangers
**** Mistrust and fear
***** Not on speaking terms
****** Unhealthy obsession


NEEDS...

1. To Get Power
* ...through becoming King or Queen
** ...through being the strongest, smartest, toughest, most dangerous, etc.
*** ...through mastery of Pattern, Logrus, Trump, Sorcery, etc.
**** ...through marriage
***** ...through destroying everything and starting anew
****** ...through deals with sinister powers

2. To Get Even
* ...with everyone who ever slighted you
** ...with the person who took something precious from you
*** ...with your father
**** ...with an ex-lover
***** ...with your own shadow
****** ...with the whole damn universe

3. To Get Free
* ...of the castle dungeons
** ...of the blood curse that lays upon you like a shroud
*** ...of your father\u2019s influence
**** ...of your siblings\u2019 machinations
***** ...of the unbridled emotions that rage inside you
****** ...of your destiny

4. To Get Respect
* ...from your father
** ...from your siblings
*** ...from your enemies
**** ...from your lover
***** ...from your child
****** ...from yourself

5. To Get the Truth
* ...about your heritage
** ...about what others really think about you
*** ...about the nature of the universe
**** ...about your dead or missing family
***** ...about the real traitor
****** ...about your own nature

6. To Get Laid
* ...to produce an heir
** ...to get inspired
*** ...to manipulate
**** ...to feel powerful
***** ...to cuckold a rival
****** ...to cement an alliance


LOCATIONS...

1. Castle Amber
* Library
** Hedge maze
*** Main hall
**** Secret passage
***** Dungeons
****** Pattern room

2. Amber Environs
* Arden
** Grove of the Unicorn
*** Lighthouse
**** The beach
***** The Steps of Kolvir
****** Noble\u2019s vineyard

3. Realms of Power
* Rebma
** Tir na Nog\u2019th
*** Crystal cave
**** Primal Pattern
***** Keep of the Four Worlds
****** Courts of Chaos

4. Shadow Earth
* Private hospital
** Well-appointed manor in upstate New York
*** Dangerous mountain road
**** Diner in Berkley
***** Bar
****** Las Vegas

5. In Shadow
* Texorami
** Ygg
*** Avalon
**** Along the Black Road
***** Wonderland
****** Kashfa

6. Amber City
* Church of the Unicorn
** Bloody Bill\u2019s
*** Wine bar
**** Jeweler\u2019s
***** Dark alleyway
****** Guard station

OBJECTS...

1. Untoward
* A family members\u2019 head in a box
** Love letters from a sibling
*** A mausoleum for a still-living family member
**** A demonic servitor
***** A secret shrine
****** Stash of rouge-powder weapons

2. Transportation
* Morgenstern
** Riding wyvern
*** Mysterious trumps
**** \u201876 Corvette
***** Floating rock
****** Shadow path

3. Weapon
* Sword
** Strangling cord
*** Rifle
**** Grenade
***** Brace of knives
****** Bow with silver arrows

4. Information
* Invasion plans
** A calendar of upcoming events at the castle
*** A conspiracy overheard via the Trumps
**** Formula for rouge-gunpowder
***** Witnessing the shapeshifting of a Chaosian spy
****** Visions of the future from Tir na Nog\u2019th

5. Items of Power
* Jewel of Judgement
** Silver arm
*** Spikard
**** Full deck of trumps
***** Dworkin\u2019s book of secrets
****** Greyswandir or Werewindle

6. Sentimental
* A family member\u2019s head in a box
** A treasured rug
*** A loyal servant
**** A beloved pet
***** The last memory of your father
****** An old dueling scar

    \n Quick followup: I ran it twice at AmberCon NW in 2010. Both sessions were zany and unfocused. We had a lot of fun and laughed like crazy, but I'm thinking that Amber doesn't really make a good Fiasco playset. When anything can happen, anything does. It might be better to have a "real-world" playset with a tight focus, that heavily borrows Amber themes.

    \n That's a good lesson to have learned (keeping things focused, providing enough constraints), and it's one that has stalled one of our other projects in development. Perhaps you could add a disclaimer to the playset or write some custom rules that help keep things focused? You really just need agreement among the players not to get too crazy, but sometimes people work better with mechanics to keep them in check.
