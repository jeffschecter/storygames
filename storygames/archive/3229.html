\nRECESS X: NERDNAROK
\n
\nSunday June 10th | Downtown Brooklyn
\n11AM - 10PM | Games start at noon
\nOnly $15 ($7 children) for a day of gaming!
\n
\nThe Nerds of NYC call you the battlefield!! Come play games of all sorts for even the most discriminating undead palate - RPGs, Board & Card Games. Nerd Book & Media Swap.
\n
\nWhat we have for you:
\n
\nRPGs
\nWant to get back into a classic dungeon crawl? Looking for something new? Play Burning Wheel, Dread, Mortal Coil, With Great Power, Masters and Minions, Over the Edge, Call of Cthulhu, Dungeons & Dragons, Pulp Era!, Army of Darkness...
\n
\nTabletop & Card
\nNo Monopoly or Candyland here. Just the "new" classics such as the Power Grid, Battle Lore or St. Petersburg. Don't worry if you don't know the rules, we're more than willing to teach people on the spot.
\n
\nBook/Media Swap
\nBring any old nerdy books that you would rather see on someone else's shelf. We've also seen people bring comix, VHS, video games and the like. Please make sure you bring home what isn't picked up. The last thing we need is six copies of Moreta: Dragonlady of Pern.
\n
\n--
\n
\nRecess will be held at:
\nBRICStudio
\n
\n57 Rockwell Place.
\nBetween Dekalb and Fulton
\nBrooklyn, NY 11217
\n
\n
\n
\nOn-site event registration begins at 11:00am on Sunday June 10th
\n
\nPlease note: the 2 train is not running the weekend of Recess. Plan ahead!
\n
\nIt's located on the 2nd floor of 57 Rockwell Place, between Fulton Street and DeKalb Avenue, around the corner from the BAM Harvey Theater.
\n
\nSubway:
\n2, 3, 4, 5 to Nevins St, walk 1 block east along Fulton to Rockwell Pl;
\nB, M, Q, R to DeKalb Av, exit at Flatbush & DeKalb (front of a Brooklyn-bound train), walk 2 blocks east along DeKalb to Rockwell Pl, turn right;
\nG train to Fulton St, walk 3 blocks west Rockwell Pl
\n
\nBus:
\nB25, B26, B38 or B52 to Ashland Pl/Rockwell Pl, walk 1/2 block
\n
\nParking:
\nStreet parking is limited but there are several parking lots located within a one-block radius of the studio.
\n*If you have any questions, please email us.
