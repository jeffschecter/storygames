    \nJUVELINE

So the game's about their attempts to reframe a pretty ugly reality to fit with a more comforting world view in which they have power and meaning, but it is also a game about doubt, because we might be wrong and they might be right.

    \nLuddites of the Railyard

    Since yer paw's day, them city folk would always be jawin' on 'bout how their doo-daws and fancy contrap'ins would change the world, make things better fer e'rybody. Sure they did, but them city folk are also damned dirty liars! Ya read yer histories, y've been taught yer lessons, and ya know the truth o' it all. They refine steel, n' make deadly weapons that kill innocent folk. They mix that black powder, n' make deadlier weapons that kill more innocent folk. Now, sure, they're sayin' these new steam engines n' mechanical auto-mo-whatevers and clockwork brains 'r gonna make a better life for e'ryone too, but ya know by know these e'ryone 'r only the city folk!
    It's up to good, ol' fashion folk like yerself and yer feller Luddites to take 'em city folk down a notch 'er two. Show 'em that the innocent folk ain' gonna sit by and get killed no more, not gonna let 'em get stomped on by no walkin' metal men, burn'd by no cannons on tracks, r' shot t' pieces by them balloon boats. No siree.
    All's you got is yer wits, yer trappins' in a leather satchel marked with the Luddite's blood-red Cross o' Revenge, and a pair of pistols with steel-piercin' rounds.

    ...may yer paw forgive you fer' that one lil' sin.

    \nHolmes Against Holmes

    Chicago, 1893. Charlotte Holmes has decided to kill her brother, H. H. Holmes. Charlotte is a New York-based detective who uses her powers of deduction to solve criminal cases, and she has discovered that her long-lost brother is a fugitive criminal in Chicago. Arriving during the height of the 1893 World's Fair, she uses her detective skills to scour the underworld. One player is Charlotte, the others are all Honours -- GMs who take turn framing scenes and controlling NPCs. Conflict is resolved through rounds of Whist.

    \nContra-Zeitgeist
    In which the last imaginative humans destroy all memes.

    The dreams of humanity have been enslaved by otherworldly creature who thrive on the raw psychic energy of imagination. These creatures spread their influence through mind-numbing memes and inducing a communal hive mind across the unimaginative masses with things like nyan-cats, references to pop-culture elements of our youth and general subversion of the true form of creative output.

    When they took steampunk it was a line too far.

    To subvert the plans of the otheworldly creatures, a rogue band of imaginative dreamers have infiltrated the recent memes and communal zeitgeists; using the tropes of their enemies as cloaks of stealth, while bringing anachronistic and countercultural contraband into the realms of the collective meme.

    A game where playing to stereotypes can get you close to your enemy, but breaking those stereotypes makes you truly dangerous.
