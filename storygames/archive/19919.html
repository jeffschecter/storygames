\n The 2014 London Indie Darling Awards were held last Saturday.

The London Indie Darling Awards are won, not based on a popular poll or a panel of experts, but solely upon the games run at London Indiemeet. So if folk want a particular game to win then they have to come, pitch it and find others willing to play it.

Congratulations to the winners!

Indie Game Darling (the game played most at London Indiemeet)
Fiasco, by Jason Morningstar

Indie Designer Darling (the designer played most at London Indiemeet)
Jason Morningstar
(Fiasco, Love in the Time of Seid, Night Witches)

Indie GM Darling (the member who facilitated the most games)
Rob Carnel
(Abnormal, Becoming, Death of the Gilded Age, Home, Houses of the Blooded, Odyssey, Protocol, Section 44: Conspiracy of Automatic Fear, Slammin', Vast & Starlit, Wield)

All categories ran from Dec 2013-Nov 2014

Some stats:

We had 12 regular monthy meets in the period.
We played 139 sessions
We played 96 different games
69 games were only played once
We had 40 different facilitators

And in addition to that (and not included in the above stats as we didn't have complete data), we had 9 Midweek meets and 9 Extrameets (3 Playstorms, 2 Longmeets, 1 Newcomers meet, 1 Blasts from the Past, 1 Monstermeet and 1 Tabletop Day meet). Overall more than 160 sessions of games played over the year!
