\n So, I wrote a game for Game Chef and I thought I'd share it here. I know I don't post a great deal, mostly sitting back and absorbing the collected wisdom of this community, so consider this me giving something back. If you want to give it a look over and tell me what you think, I'd be more than grateful!

World Riddle is a Journey towards Enlightenment. It is a game of two worlds and of questions. World Riddle is a koan to which each play group must find their own answer. World Riddle is about the search for the City of the Real within the Desert of the Mundane.

Most importantly, World Riddle is found here.

And the playaid (the cards) is found here.
