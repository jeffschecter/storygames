\n For the past while, I have been including mutagenic substances in my regular D&D game, for which I have mostly used the random mutation tables in the old Warhammer Chaos books, Realms of Chaos. This has been a big hit, and most players find it hilarious. I also played some Mutant Future recently, and rolling up a mutant character was super-fun. I rolled up a midget who has perfect balance, can see radiation, and can shoot blasts of radiation from his hands.

Since I've been having fun with these, and inspired by Jeff Rients' Random Mutation Table Table, I've been compiling a list of all the mutations and mutation tables I can find in rpgs. Now, I have a pretty wide range of knowledge about rpgs, but it's nowhere near complete.*

So I am appealing to you, story gamers. Tell me about random mutation tables, lists of mutations in games, or just possibilities for mutations. They can be physical alterations, psychic powers, super powers, or even magical abilities. I've included stuff from the Warhammer Chaos gods, stuff from superhero rpgs, and the classic mutant games like Gamma World and Metamorphosis Alpha. But please, give me some more material!

Thanks in advance.


*Like, for example, I know nothing about Torg, but it looks like a line that would include random or not-random mutation tables. I played a few superhero rpgs as a teenager, but don't generally pay attention to them now, so which ones should I look at for lists of powers?
