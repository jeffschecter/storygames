\n Hey all!

We're on new hardware, and Vanilla 2.1 (2.0, which we skipped almost entirely, is being deprecated at the end of the year).

We should be faster, and some of the edges a bit shinier. We have a dice roller, if that's your thing.

There are also no more Whispers. The plugin hasn't been updated. All your whispers have fallen out of conversations and into your inbox. If the plugin gets updated, we'll consider putting it back in.

If you run into problems, let me know!

We're also going to be experimenting with new themes over the next few days. Feel free to let me know what you think.
