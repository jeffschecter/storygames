    \n\nJanuary 2008 \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n Character generator: pick X ASCII symbols or letters (technically, "glyphs").
    Conflict generator: the GM (fixed individual or rotating role) draws a challenge using any glyphs he or she wants.
    Conflict resolver: you must draw how you surmount the challenge using only your PC's glyphs. Other players vote if it works well enough (or how it falls short).
    Reward mechanism: access to new symbols for surmounting challenges.

    Done... if you play this game, send $5 via PayPal to david.artman@gmail.com.
