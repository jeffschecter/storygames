\n From 1 to 4 November has been held the main comic and games Convention in Italy in the beautiful medieval town of Lucca. This year, for the first time, some wacky indie games were presented (PtA and DitV) and we\u2019re quite happy with the results: in time we\u2019re gonna catch \u2018em all!

To see what an italian gaming convention is like, take a look here

Actually the convention was held in many different tents sparsed in the streets and squares of the town, but I had the time to photograph only the gaming section tent.
