    \n Shit, I had to go and move away from Austin.

    The University of Texas has a gaming group, but I never really got involved with them while I was there. I suspect they're more traditional game/Vampire LARP oriented.

If you want to put him in touch with me, I can introduce him to some of my friends in the area, and I'd love to play some story games when I come home to visit (usually once every two, three months)
