\n I don't quite get it.
\n
\nThe players look like the wussiest wusses ever in their armor and helmets. Like, can't they take it like a man?
\n
\nLikewise, I can't understand why the hell they are hitting the ball with a frickin' stick and running around in circles. The field looks kind of funny, too! Where are the goals?
\n
\nAlso, it seems like it takes forever before the audience can finally start the slugfest. How do you know which side wins if all the audience does the whole time is watching some silly players chasing the ball? Why would anyone pay for that in the first place, if there's no slugfest?
\n
\nSo, I don't quite get it. Where was the fun, again?
