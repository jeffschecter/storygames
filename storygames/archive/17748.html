\n I thought I should collect my stuff in the one place so that it's easier for myself and others to find. Some of it goes way back, and a lot of it is rubbish \u2026 but I hope you find something to your fancy here. I'm still fond of the Redbox Hack 2 classes and the Polaris Illuminated Monks.

This Dropbox folder should be accessible to everyone and contain the most up-to-date version of each of the following games/game supplements. Enjoy!

The Fading of the Land is a solitaire story game heavily based on one of Gizmet's game poems. It's about boys and girls fighting monsters as their coming of age ritual. All rights reserved. PDF and DOCX.

Oblivious is a game poem about four people so distracted by their own lives that they haven't noticed the monster destroying their city. CC BY. PDF and DOC.
Starting Simple provides quick start rules and two sample settings for Chris Gonnerman's The Simple Game System.

Dungeon World and World of Dungeons
Sanglorian's Dungeon World Stuff is fourteen pages of diverse content I created for Dungeon World. CC BY. PDF and ODT.

The World Sequences is a Chrestomanci-inspired World of Dungeons setting. All rights reserved. PDF and ODT.

Hackbirds
Darkening Skies is an unofficial sequel to Lady Blackbird, with a crew exploring a curiously empty spaceship. CC BY-NC-SA. PDF and Scribus.

It Came from the Wide Blue Yonder is a medieval superhero hackbird. CC BY. PDF and ODT.

Man and Ubermensch is a World War II superhero hackbird with a fascist plot to take over the US government. CC BY. PDF and ODT.

Libre Editions
4C System Libre Edition corrects errata and removes non-public domain content from 4C System. CC0. PDF and ODT.

Otherkind Dice
A Strange Charm is a Doctor Who-inspired 24-hour RPG. CC BY. PDF and DOC.

Ghost/Echo Whispers takes an idea from the development of Do (since discarded, I think) and applies it to Ghost/Echo. All rights reserved. JPG.

You Are Badass is a game I wrote in 6 minutes about having tremendous powers \u2013 and what you might give up when you use them. CC BY. ODT and PDF.

Polaris
The Cult of the Sun was my shot at providing an alternative to the Stellar Knights. All rights reserved. ODT and PDF.

Redbox Hack
The four Seeing Red supplements offer new classes for Eric Provost's Redbox Hack 2. CC BY. PDF.

The Haunt is an itinerant necromancer.

The Grim is a grizzled mercenary.

The Moon is a rabbit-eared martial artist.

The Seraph is a whirling alchemist.

Storystory
Last year I gave some thought to how you could roleplay in prison, and these are some of the results.

Hearth & Hunt is a quickplay adaptation of The Fading of the Land (above), with the players taking the role of storytellers bickering over previous battles against the monsters. CC BY-SA. JPG and PDF.

The Rule of Three and Breaking the Rule of Three are the simplest versions of the Storystory system I envisaged. I submitted The Rule of Three to the Thousand Year Game Design Competition. CC BY-SA. PDF and ODT.

The Life Domestic is a set of cards (like an oracle) for The Rule of Three. CC BY-SA. PDF.
