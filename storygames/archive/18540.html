\n As I mentioned in a previous thread, I'm currently doing an open beta-test for my new game Abstract Dungeon.

The main rules came with one sample adventure, but I figured we should have a little more, so we've put out another called "The Orcish Baker."

When an orc moved to Morvan's Hallow the neighbors got restless. When it opened a bakery they got confused. Convinced that it's part of an orcish invasion plot, a concerned citizen begs the heroes to get rid of the orc. But is this good Samaritan really worried about the public good, or is there an ulterior motive?

You can download the Orcish Baker for free from the Abstract Dungeon Page, or get is as part of the Abstract Dungeon playtest from RPGNow or DriveThruRPG.

Of course feedback is always welcome.
