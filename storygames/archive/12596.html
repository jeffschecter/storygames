\nEpisode 30 Drifters Escape Redux: Drifting Through Time and Space

\nThe week we revisit The Drifter's Escape from, TAO Games, but with a twist. We modified the rules to play Doctor Who. We talk about how we adapted the rules to play Doctor Who, playing in an setting with established canon, and the challenges of keeping focused on the companion when The Doctor is such a dominate character.
