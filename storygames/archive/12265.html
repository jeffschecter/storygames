    \n Showdown will eventually come out, and it's definitely two-player.

    There are rules for two-player "Happy Birthday, Robot!" - a game which is, I think, on its way to the printer even now. I have what I think is a finished PDF in my hands, at any rate.

    There's also an interesting edge case in Sleep is Death, the video game that plays (only) like a tabletop game.

    On the "games that work well for two, even if they were designed for more" note: Would you believe that Puppetland is a great two-player game... and so is Power Kill? You should, because both of those things are true!
