\n Streetview the Driver promised Slicers Gang (a Hunting Pack) to go Hunting with them using his car. This is a great opportunity for him to impress them to get their trust.

I was thinking of this as a move, any considerations?

If you go hunting with Slicers Gang roll+hard (no shit driver does apply here i think)
On a 10+ choose 3, on a 7-9 choose 2
*Your car is undamaged
*You hunt enough food for the hardhold
*You impress the Gang
*You get back safely
On a miss you are caught in a bad spot.
