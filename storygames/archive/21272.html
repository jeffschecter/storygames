    \n Hey there!

    I'm a tabletop RPG streamer primarily focusing on indie content. I run Once Upon A Game which is a story game variety show where we play one shots of tons of (mostly GMless) games. I try to have a fun, mostly watchable video of actual plays of some of the hundreds of amazing games out there. You can find them on YouTube.

    I still do Once Upon A Game and stream it on Twitch Tuesday nights pacific time.

    I keep open sign ups for my Once Upon A Game. Registering for them is done through my primary community platform: Discord.

    I tailor my content into three main areas:
    1.) Once Upon A Game: weekly, fun story game one shots.
    2.) Burning Crowns: my 30+ session Burning Wheel epic campaign
    3.) Quarterly special 5 session miniseries: Currently I'm doing a fast and the furious on Mars as an anime game called Olympus Overdrive using The Sprawl RPG. Previously I've done Under The Fallen Maple which was a samurai mouse guard game and this October I'm excited to run Keep on the Borderlands using Torchbearer.

    I also muse a lot about games on my Twitter.
