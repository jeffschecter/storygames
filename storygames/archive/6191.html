\nSteal Away Jordan: the LARP
Event Description: Re-live the antebellum South in this one-of-a-kind live-action roleplaying event. Players portray slaves and slave owners living and working on a plantation in rural Georgia. Come see the auction (open to all con-goers) and "vote" for your favorite slave or farm for rare and unique items in the fields. Bring your own period garb! Burnt cork will be provided.

This event will run from sunrise to sunset and is recommended for all age and experience levels. Those with medical problems such as heat stroke sensitivity and back pain should not attend.
