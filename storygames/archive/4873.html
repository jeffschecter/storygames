\n Guys,
\n
\nPlanning for the first Indiecon is just getting under way. There's a whole year to go until the event, so there's plenty of time for things to be decided and whatever.
\n
\nIt's not me organising it, but I live close (ish) to the venue and know the guy who's doing most of the work.
\n
\nIt's aimed as small(er) press publishers and gamers who want something slightly off the mainstream.
\n
\nWhen: Friday 7th November till Monday 10 November 2008 - 3 day event with on site accommodation.
\n
\nWhere: Holbourn/Naish i.e. same venue as Conception. This is a great venue and pretty ideal for this sort of thing.
\n
\nPrices are TBA, but if Conception is anything to go by it's likely to be of the order of 200 for a really nice 4 room (6 bed) lodge or \xa3100 for a 2 room (3 bed + sofabed) chalet. (Having said that, those are the conception prices which are for 5 nights. Might be less for just 3?) Plus of course, whatever if costs you to get to Dorset on the south coast.
\n
\nSo, if you're in the UK (or want to travel to the UK for this event) please come along!
\n
\nIf you're a publisher from outside the UK, this might be a good time to think about if you'll be represented there and how. I mean, you can probably get a fan to turn up and demo your game if you ask nicely enough. You've got a year to plan though, so don't panic.
