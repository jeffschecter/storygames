\n This is my flier for Games on Demand at a convention this weekend. I'm supposed to ask for feedback.
Here it is, completely unformatted.


Games on Demand III
Would You Like to Play a Game?

What It Is: We bring a stack of some of our favorite games. You sit down and tell us what you want to play. We run it for you! All rules taught, all supplies brought, and all skill levels welcome. Here's some of our favorite games:

3:16: Carnage Amongst the Stars: The 3rd Squad, 16th Brigade returns, to wipe out all alien lifeforms in the universe! How many kills can you rack up? Military-Satire Sci-Fi With Kill Counting.

Awesome Adventures: The game of Awesome Adventures! Over the top, gonzo cinematic action, with larger than life heroes. Pulp roleplaying for a variety of settings.

Escape from Tentacle City: Play the bottom rung of society, trying to escape the tentacle menace. Laugh uproariously as you die horribly. Dark Comedy Survival Horror

Extraordinary Adventures of Baron Munchausen: Join us around the table, where we recount fictional (and entirely implausible) tales of adventure and daring-do! Victorian Competitive Storytelling.

In a Wicked Age: Swords and Sorcery roleplaying, with fast, easy situation creation. Consult the Oracles, make your warrior, and carve out a name for yourself! Swords and Sorcery at its best.

MAID: You're a randomly generated maid in a randomly generated mansion dealing with randomly generated events. Ridiculous caper anime. With maids.

Misery Bubblegum: A roleplaying cardgame of romance and self-doubt. Can you overcome your own inhibitions to achieve your dreams? Anime highschool romantic drama.

The Mountain Witch: A small group of ronin has been hired to kill the evil Mountain Witch. One wrinkle- they don't trust each other one bit! Samurai Blood Opera.

Mouse Guard: Everything's more dangerous when you're the little guy! Heroic fantasy. With mice.

Shab al-Hiri Roach: Are you willing to swallow a soul-eating insect bent on destroying humanity? No? Not even if it will get you tenure? Dark Comedy Lovecraftian Academic Intrigue

Sign in Stranger: As a human colonist on a strange alien planet, nothing is what it seems. Try to deal with unpredictable and often zany situations. Comedic Sci-Fi

Zombie Cinema: It's the zombie apocalypse! Get your survivor, and try to make the best of things.
Dark Comedy Survival Horror with Boardgame Elements.
