\n Hey, I was thinking about holding a contest for D&D; "solitaire" play (i.e., building an adventure). I'll post details about it in a bit, but I had some questions first:
\n
\nWhen is Iron Game Chef 07? I don't want to compete with that madness.
\n
\nIf you're making a D&D; adventure thingy, dudes really ought to play it. Is there any way to recruit playtesters for such things? Like, groups of playtesters, each doing one of the submissions? I have no idea how many people have an active D&D; group goin'.
\n
\nI'd also like these things to be hosted somehow, but I have no web-hosty magic...
\n
\nMaybe this was a bad idea, but in principle I think it might be a fun contest, and I'd like to see what all the SG-heads do, when they're all grinding away at something Gamist like D&D;, which is like the chicken noodle soup to gamers.
