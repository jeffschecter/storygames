    Posted By: Adam DrayGo for it! I considered a wiki and other things but I want the people here to see it and not have to go elsewhere to find out. Does Google Calendar have opt-in email notification?
    If you've got Gmail, yes. Or SMS. You can subscribe to any calendars you want, (including the Master Con Schedule) and get notifications when stuff is coming up. The owner of a calendar can also designate other users to do updates to the calendar, so if you, Adam, were to set up a Con Schedule, you could delegate to a couple other people so that more Cons could get added.

    Not sure about if you don't have a Google account, but you can always get a Gmail account and set it to forward to your normal email.

    Myself, I'd subscribe to this in a heartbeat.
