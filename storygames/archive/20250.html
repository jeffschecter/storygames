    \n Someone needs to propose the next episode's theme and tell everyone in advance so they can daydream up some scenes they might like to call.

    Other than that, don't prep anything. Except maybe, as you're the GM, scribble down some character names in case you need them in a hurry.

    \n Here's a variation we are using in my DS-based game at the moment:

    - Whoever has the most Drama Tokens at the end of the session gets a point of Axe' (the thing we have instead of bennies)
    - Whoever is GM for that episode may give a point of Axe' to the player they think brought out the theme best. So far no GM has chosen to do so.
