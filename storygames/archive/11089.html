\n Just a quick update to say that Uml\xe4ut: Game of Metal is now available directly from me in print, via the Lord of The Pies website.

The book is priced at \xa312, which is about $20 or \u20ac13.50, with P&P to UK addresses at \xa31, Mainland Europe at \xa32 and US/Canada at \xa33.

All orders placed before Christmas receive a free PDF!

(If you've already bought the PDF from me, please email or whisper me and I'll give you a special "upgrade" url)

Imagine Spinal Tap, Guitar Hero or Metalocalypse as a GM-less story-telling game, and that's Uml\xe4ut. Each player creates and controls their own Heavy Metal band. Simple, card-driven mechanics let you chart their career as they rehearse, play gigs, fight among themselves and pull crazy publicity stunts. Will you make it big, or are your records destined to languish in the discount bin of obscurity? Can you hold your band together when the going is rough, or will you self destruct in a drug-fuelled clash of egos?

A sample, giving an overview of the game and it's table of contents can be downloaded here.
