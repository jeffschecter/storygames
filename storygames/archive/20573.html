\n Hey friends,

My new game, The Skeletons, is now available!

In this structured freeform tabletop roleplaying game, you play undead guardians of a tomb. As time passes for your characters - decades, centuries, epochs - time passes in real life as you rest in silent darkness for seconds or minutes. It sounds weird but it is surprisingly affecting. Plus you get to draw your badass skeleton dude and collaboratively create - and eventually ruin - your ancient tomb. I hope you enjoy it!

    \n What if we knew going into each scene what our Glory Fades and/or Gods Laugh result would be rather than selecting it after we did scenes that answered our questions?

    In other words, rearranging steps of play from:

    1.) Select intruder
    ---a.) Answer Intruder related questions
    2.) Make Scenes answering questions. Slayers may answer two as usual.
    3.) Choose from Glory Fades or Gods Laugh

    to

    1.) Select an Intruder
    ---a.) Answer Intruder related questions
    2.) Choose from Glory Fades or Gods Laugh
    3.) Make scenes that answer questions. Slayers may answer two, as usual.

    We may also want to consider the Time Devours/Worlds Crumble tables in lieu of Glory Fades for ideas for backdrops of scenes.
