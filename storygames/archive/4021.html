\n I was going to whisper this to someone. Then I realised other UK-based gamers might not have seen this either, since it's been a while.

The Collective Endeavour are UK-based indie game designers. They have a forum where you can talk about games.

On that site, you'll find not just the cream of British game design talent but also Andrew Kenrick!*

That's it, really, but they're the designers of some bloody good games (Cold City, Dead Of Night, Contenders, Best Friends, Mob Justice, Covenant, all the new ones I've forgotten).

Oh, and they'll be at Dragonmeet in December. If you're in the UK, come to Dragonmeet. It is good.

Graham

* Sorry for the cheap shot. Andrew is actually a very good game designer and Dead Of Night is a very good game.

We're an open group, so if you want to join just come along and start posting.
