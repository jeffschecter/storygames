    \n Sure!

    Mechanically the game is quite simple. Each turn you move a counter, representing your crew member to a different part of the ship and execute one of the protocol listed on that space. Each turn an additional counter, representing the ship, moves from earth to mars.



    When we reach mars, the board unfolds revealing a mars colony, with new kind of protocol, and additional places to go. The rover activates the third area on the back of the board where you and another crew member explore something together.

    That is the game in a nutshell, the limited version of the game just had the rules you see printed on the board and nothing else, so it is pretty simple. Orion is developing an actual play video which we will be releasing in a couple of weeks. So you will be able to see it in action.
