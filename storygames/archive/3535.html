\n I'm throwing down some PTA next week and I want some help. Basically I'm looking for isolated situation elements teased out of these relationships:

Bad Boy Crips (led by Pin "Titty" Chu), mostly Vietnamese vice lords. Strong ties to Crips. Hate the Tiny Rascal Gang.
Tiny Rascal Gang (led by Billy Phommasanh), mostly Laotian vice lords. Strong ties to Bloods. Hate the Bad Boy Crips.
Flacas (led by Leonardo Fern\xc3\xa1ndez), Central American thugs. Loosely affiliated with Bad Boy Crips.
Unit 88 (led by Chris Whitley), a skinhead meth ring out of Ludlow, Kentucky. Loosely affiliated with the Ohio unorganized militia, third battalion. Hate RENU.
The corruption-shattered and reformed Regional Enforcement Narcotics Unit (RENU) (led by Captain Frank Trang). Informal obligations to the Bad Boy Crips.
Cincinatti police department's Mid-Level Enforcement Unit (Led by Lt. Lowell Evans), known for ruthlessness and lack of corruption. Enemies of everybody.

Example: A Mid-Level Enforcement Unit undercover agent has gone missing. A rogue Unit 88 member knows where he is, but wants something in return for the information.

Go!
