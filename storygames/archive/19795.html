\n This episode mainly focuses on inclusivity. In the aftermath of controversies such as \u201cgamer gate\u201d and \u201cconsultant gate\u201d, and recent moves by mainstream publishers to reflect greater diversity in their games, how can we attract under-represented groups into our wonderful hobby? We talk about best practice from a number of conventions, especially the UK\u2019s Nine World\u2019s Geekfest, and explore how we can all make our spaces safer and more welcoming for everyone.
