\n Alas like last year going to Gen Con is totally out of the question for me financially, and I'm sure I'm not the only one. (Though I've been avidly following This Just In From GenCon and other such news sources about it.) A few times in the past we've had a thread on "non-gen con awesome," for everyone who's not going to post about whatever cool stuff they've got going on. I say it's time for the 2010 edition of that!

I'll go first:

This weekend I'll be doing a playtest of Slime Story ("teenagers hunt cute monsters for spending money"). Last week we made characters and brainstormed a town, and this time around we'll get into properly playing it, with all the neat new stuff (MMO-style quests, better interlude rules, Awesome Points and Suck Points, etc.) I'm really looking forward to trying all this stuff our and hopefully getting the game really whipped into shape.

A friend who is on vacation in Japan was kind enough to pick up some Japanese RPGs for me, including Meikyuu Days (which transplants the already awesome "cynical pop dungeon fantasy" of Meikyuu Kingdom into a quasi-modern setting), Double Cross 3rd Edition, and Ryuutama. He won't be getting back until next week, but it's awesome all the same.

I got the new Doctor Who RPG in the mail finally. It looks like a really fun game, especially for a Doctor Who fanboy like yours truly.
