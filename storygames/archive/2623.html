\n We are now accepting preorders for Beast Hunters, the Roleplaying Challenge Game of Blood, Tattoos and the Hunt. Check out the preorder page for details. People who preorder receive the PDF for free right away, and the hardcopy will be sent out at the end of this month or the beginning of April.

The Berengad Games homepage has received a complete face lift as we've moved from a blog to a CMS interface. You can now download a sample chapter (character creation) and the character sheet. There are also sections for actual play reports and the art preview, and we are going to post a demo of the game toward the end of the month.

If you have any questions, feel free to post them here!

    And you're right, it's not competitive in the sense that players win or lose against each other. It's challenging, and it allows the person controlling the adversity to use all their resources to oppose and thereby challenge the other player(s). And if you have more than one Hunter, they tend to work together against the adversity (though there are certain ways to leverage inter-Hunter conflict to gain advantages, but I'll leave those things for the players to figure out).

    I think Fred might have called it competitive by accident at one point, based on a cursory reading of the text while laying it out.
