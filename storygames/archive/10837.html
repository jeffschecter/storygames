\n I'm nearly ready to submit my competition entry, Hell for Leather.

Hell for Leather is a violent, GM free, team vs. team, roleplaying game for adults only. It uses a physical resolution system, sort of like Jenga meets marbles, and tells the story of teams of contestants on the run from a ruthless, murderous game-show called The Gauntlet. It's thickly inspired by R. Bachman's novel, the Running Man.

It needs to be ready for the 22nd November, and although I'm happy with how the playtests went, I have no idea how a fresh audience is going to react to the text. In that, I need your help badly. Really badly.

Until I get the draft uploaded, you could click here if you want to help or if you don't like using forms, the mail addy is info *.at.* cobwebgames.com. I don't know what I can give you in return, except an honourable mention and a copy of the release version.

Cheers guys,

Sebastian.
