Anonycon 2013

\nGames So Good They Turn Undead

December 6-8
Stamford, CT
http://www.anonycon.com/


Anonycon, now in its 13th year, is a gaming convention featuring table-top RPGs, LARPs, board games, and more. We are particularly proud of the high number of quality original scenarios created for the Con each year. So what are you waiting for? Join the campaign for better gaming now!

Questions: info@anonycon.com or http://www.anonycon.com/
