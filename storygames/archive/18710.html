\n Hey everyone,

Each year, I try to collect news about Gencon for those of us (like me) who can't attend. If you know anyone who will be posting blog posts, announcing news on websites or twitter that would be great! While Gencon isn't quite the place it once was for game announcements, it's great to read peoples reflections about games they've tried, seminars they attend and pictures they take of all the goings ons at Gencon. Thanks everyone! I'll try and keep this thread updated with anything good I find as well.

Here's a some links to some places for news that I've found so far:

Boardgamegeek has a list of all the new boardgames releasing at Gencon this year.
This Just in from GenCon has some great interviews with people, designers and publishers at Gencon each year. They've already started posting interviews.

Thanks everyone!
