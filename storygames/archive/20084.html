\n Hi everybody
I am going to pick your (smart) brains, and look for some design advice, and perhaps even some playtesters, for this little work of mine.
I wanted to give it a try with a Fate Accelerated hack and I used the FAE system to address, in parallel, a little design experiment where rather than creating the typical fantasy classes like warrior, wizard, rogue etc., the characters have a combination of class/narrative archetype.

So the game has a hero, and a wizard who's also the guide of the group, a warrior who's also in charge of the hero protection, and so on. The game contains a little introduction with the basics of a fantasy setting (of course, they're just an inspiration and not mechanically tied into the game).

You can have a look at the preview files here:
- Introduction
- the Hero playbook
- the Ranger playbook

The feedback I am looking for, at the moment, is about the advancements system built-in in the playbooks and the character creation system which is also contained in the playbooks.

If someone would like to see more, please write me here in story-games or in mail ( davide.pignedoli@gmail.com ) and I'll be happy to share more with you (the full set of characters and additional instructions on running the game).

I don't need people to be 'FATE-gurus' to comment, but I am not posting -yet- the full package. I'd rather give it to 10 people and receive 5 comments than have 100 downloads and maybe 1 response :-)

Of course, feel free to comment on whatever is posted here if that's enough to tickle your curiosity! (I hope so...)
