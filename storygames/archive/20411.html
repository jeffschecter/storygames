\n November 22nd, 12 - 5pm, is the second mini-convention for one-shot games in Leeds, Yorkshire, UK. If you can make it (or even if you can't) there'll be chop-socky kung fu action in Feng Shui 2, the rolling of 20 sided dice in D&D 5E, terrifying cosmic horror in World War Cthulhu, and a heady combination of Action! and Science! in Atomic Robo.

Brewdog pub, near the Corn Exchange, website at goplayleeds.uk or the facebook pages you'd expect to find on a search.
