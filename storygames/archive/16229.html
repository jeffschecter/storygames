\n Short and sweet.
\nIn the last few days We've had some good advice from this forum community about our kickstarter project, so I thought I'd pose a question.
\n
\nBefore getting to the question, a quick explanation of the project is in order: Aeranos RPG is a mobile web app that tracks stats for the GM and the players, automates combat for speedy resolution and allows for remote tabletop gaming. It's packaged with an original fantasy setting, but is customizable for any setting.
\n
\nHere's the link:
\nhttp://tinyurl.com/aeranos
\n
\nWe are discussing coding for the D20 3.5 and for our own Aeranos system to start. Beyond those, we're interested in what other systems you'd like to see the app function with, either with stretch goal funding or as a future Gamer's Cortex project. Of course we're also always interested in any feedback, but the main question remains future systems.
