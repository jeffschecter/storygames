    \n The ultimate inevitability of death is one that sometimes keeps me awake, too.

    Spiders.

    Public embarrassment.

    The suspicion that I've forgotten something important.

    Heights. Not the idea of falling off it, specifically, but of being possessed by the sudden uncontrollable urge to jump.

    Mind you, none of these are proper phobias, to the point that they significantly impair my quality of living or anything, but they're things that worry me from time to time.

    \n Creepy children.

    Like, any sort of TV show, movie, video game with evil demon children things.

    Silent Hill, the school scenes just... ::shudders::

    The Supernatural episode with the children who'd been replaced by little soul-sucking dopplegangers.

    There are many others. They really, really make my skin crawl.

    Oh, any sort of faceless humanoid things. Reference Silent Hill again. Mirrormask, too; They had faces, but they were.. less featureful.

    Suffocation. Like, I don't have any real fear of it, but I figure it'd have to be one of the worst ways to die, and tend to freak out if my breathing is restricted, beyond the natural panic reflex.
