    \n I've got two here by the PC. They are pearlescent with black lettering, convention freebies. I quite often roll them when I'm waiting for for things to load. My scheme is that if both dice roll under 10, I multiply the result, if not I add. 81 is the highest score possible but I don't often get that.

    Just now, I rolled a 2, the first prime, the number of each species of animal in the arc and the atomic number of helium. 2 is a severe number which won't allow for any messing around, although it does occasionally let slip its veil and show a glimpse of fun. It is red.

    How do I feel? Spooked that you predicted my number. Otherwise, interested in the number 2 as indicated above.
