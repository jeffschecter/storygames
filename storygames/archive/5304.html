    \n Well, there's a few ways that could work:

    One, paranormal investigators will be implied by several elements of the Oracle.

    Two, if you and your players want to make sure those investigators are in each episode, they should gravitate towards them and protect their places on the Owe list.

    If you do this the worst case scenario in terms of missing investigators is that they'll be missing for the first session or two. That would mean that the first two sessions are like "Issue 0" of a comic, where you're seeing the paranormal elements playing with each other and the hapless citizenry before the investigators start showing up in Issue 01.
