\n Dragonmeet is this Saturday 28th November, at Kensington Town Hall.

Who will be there? I'll be on the Pelgrane stall, running demos of Trail of Cthulhu and other Gumshoe stuff. So will Robin Laws, making sure we're running Gumshoe right, and stepping in when we're not: "Let me show you how to do that properly".

Play Unsafe will be on the Pelgrane stall. You can get \xa31 off if you can persuade Robin Laws to sign it; \xa32 if you can persuade him to tell you why he wrote it. (This is because people generally ask Robin to sign stuff, so I'm wondering if he'll sign stuff he didn't write.)

Who else is there?

Graham
