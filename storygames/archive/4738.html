\n No solid pitch yet, but here's my initial thought that I'll be building on.

Down and Out in Bareknuckle Hospital.

Play a genetically engineered supersoldier fighting in a war that nobody can remember how it started. A war so deadly that a battle will never last more than three minutes. Enemy casualties are 100% Yours are only two percent lower. In all likelyhood, thirty seconds of combat will put you in hospital for a year.

Try keeping the greatest army ever created in bed for a year.

Down and Out in Bareknuckle Hospital, an irreverent and blackly humorous roleplaying game about the horrors of war, medical science and bedpans.

I'm thinking of a series of 30 to 50 second YouTube ads with music, statistics and sardonic commentary. Something funny and eye-catching that'll be spread as a thing in and of itself. The game itself I'm thinking would be presented first as an induction and training video for the fictional military, then as an in-depth infomercial for Bareknuckle Hospital aimed at military personelle, sold as a DVD. One of my artifacts is going to be the DVD case and label. If I get time I'd love to do one of the YouTube spots.

Most of the budget would go towards the the video content, paying for actors and sets/props. Here are some style references -

Portal Trailer
Portal Credits
Starship Troopers
Reservoir Dogs

That's all I've got so far.
