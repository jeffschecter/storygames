\n On Monday I played in a special Halloween one-shot in Dan Bayn's Urbanimus setting for Secrets & Lies. We play silicon shaman and street-speakers who can talk to the spirits that inhabit the infrastructure of the city: the net, the grid, the mains, and so forth.

My character, portmanitou, is a blogging investigative reporter who is out to expose the witch-lawyers who literally steal people's identities. In game, we joked that she's twittering updates on the murder mystery she and her friends are investigating.

So, we are actually in the process of twittering out actual play highlights of Monday's session, in character and vaguely in real-time. Please follow @portmanitou in your favorite twitter client and learn of our harrowing adventure over the next couple of days.
