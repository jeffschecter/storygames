Questions:

Let's say I were to make Pool Refreshment via Debriefing only. That would certain push the paranoia aspect and keep the game dark, but will it remove any instance of reaffirmation of humanity scenes that my previous refreshes would have provided? I think it will. So, if it were to act as an addition to the normal kind of refreshes, would it get used? Should I set up specific ways to call on this Debriefing?
