    \n I grew up playing Rifts. I've thought about running in crazy hippie indie style, but I think it'd probably just hot swap the system for TSOY or PTA. Actually Shock: might be the best choice, because there are clear shocks in Rifts:

    - magic returning to earth
    - dimensional portals opening all over the place
    - beings from other dimensions invading
    - the rise of neo-fascism against inhuman things

    Plus, Shock: has a lot of the dirty hippie techniques that you mention too (world burning, fanmail, etc.).

    But it would be cool if you could keep some of the gun-nuttery, power creep, and 80's metal cover vibe that is at the core of the game's appeal. Like, playing Rifts is fun because you can get Paired Pistol proficiency for your 5th level Psy-Slinger who shoots people with energy bolts that erupt from the tips of his index fingers. Bang!
