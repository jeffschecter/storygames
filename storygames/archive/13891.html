    Todd, man, we gotta hang out more.

    \n

    Kira, I think you're confusing Cyborg with Cyborg Commando. Be sure to include them as separate items in your bibliography.

    \n

    Jason, I propose a panel comparing and contrasting Cyborg and Cyborg Commando at CyborGon.

    \n

    Patty, I had a great time playing with you! Let's do it again sometime!

    \n

    I can't express just how happy I was at the Human Contact events. We had five tables and every one produced a fascinating and weird world. Academics were idealistic, catty, selfish, and confused in equal parts, while colonists were the same. Worlds were weird (a planet of 8-year-olds, a humanoid alien race that had been built out of space visitors a few hundred years earlier by the majority residents, an incomplete Dyson sphere being attacked during a schism between an astroturf union movement and a sincere one). Minor though it was, we even had a space battle! Thank you, everybody, for making it so much fun.
