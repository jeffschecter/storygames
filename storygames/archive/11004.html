\n Hi all,
The BBC recently had a report on self-publishing in the UK with a discussion on National Novel Writing Month and a new POD service being offered by Blackwells on Charing Cross road. You can have your own PDF printed there and then and will print paperback books between 4.5" x 4.5" and 8.5" x 11". I'm not sure of the cost, but the website says that for books printed from their catalogue the price is the same as if purchased off the rack. Anyone used the service yet?
John
