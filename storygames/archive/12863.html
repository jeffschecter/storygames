\n OK, I should really have playtested this at least one more time before I put it up, but I just couldn't wait. I think I've got something pretty cool here. I made a game specifically to play with my non-gamer wife, who turns out to be a natural at this kind of thing.

It's a game that's intended primarily to tell stories like Twilight or True Blood, supernatural romances where someone is dating a magical creature outside of their own species, but it's flexible: you could do super-hero with a crush (like in Superman or Spider-Man); something like Wall-E; hell, even Fight Club is fundamentally a love story. Really, anything where the boy and the girl get together in the end. (Or you could do boy and boy, if you wanted to do something like Brokeback Mountain - just give it a tragic ending.)

It's inspired primarily by *Breaking the Ice* and *S/Lay W/Me* and, strangely enough, *Geiger Counter* - but it's pretty different.

AND - I decided to camp it here on the Story Games Codex Wiki. So any of y'all can just go in and Make It Better without even asking me first. It's an Open Source game design. Let's see how it goes.

My Supernatural Romance

Please give it some playtesting, let me know what you think - or just put your feedback up on the Wiki.
