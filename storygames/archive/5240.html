\n For a long time, I've wanted a forum where people who work with (or just want to talk about) educational role-playing games can meet for discussion. Now, with the help of my employers, I've set up Educational Roleplaying.
\n
\nOur aim is to create a community of people who want to share and discuss their experiences, for personal or professional reasons. On the site, you'll find not only discussions, but also links and references to books, articles, institutions and researchers.
\n
\nI hope you'll come visit, whether you're a teacher, a student or just someone with an interest in the field!
