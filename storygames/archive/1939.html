    One of the things that stands out for me about the Dresden books (which I'm very much enjoying - also Codex Alera, which would make a fantastic RPG) is that Jim Butcher's system of plot development is of the "bad to worse" school. Harry Dresden basically goes through the whole story getting beaten up worse and worse, and just when you think that it's going to take a deus ex machina to get his sorry ass out of this one - Butcher pulls out something that he totally foreshadowed but I hadn't picked up on. I love it.

    So, roundabout way of saying: I like the way you're attempting to model this in your game system by giving scope for repeated beatings, and I'm watching with great interest.
