    \n You should obviously sort the cat names into multiple chapters, based on gender, nationality (of the owners, at least), breed, fur, social class and favorite brand of food. A separate chapter ought to be reserved for "classic cat names" or "historical cat names", for those times when you need a feline plausibly named in a game set in the 1930s or earlier.
    Luckily, "The Story Games Names Project Supplement I: Cats" doubles in utility as a resource for naming Apocalypse World NPCs, further widening its appeal and user base.
