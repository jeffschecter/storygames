\n Just bought the PDF of Dungeon World - missed the kickstarter so have been looking forward to picking this up.

My friends and I normally play through Google Hangouts as we're pretty far apart geographically, but while everyone's back to see family over Christmas there's a good chance we'll be able to do an IRL game or two. As we all used to be pretty big World of Warcraft addicts back in the day, I thought it might be a laugh to do a one-off based around World of Warcraft, using our old characters. I'm pretty sure the rules will work as-is but if anyone knows of a mod for DW - maybe something to include professions, or a few fronts (along the lines of Poor Tristram) I'd be grateful. Thanks!

    \n Haha no worries! You've both got me thinking though. I wanted the Druid to be similar to the Wizard but with a bit more 'responsibility' - first they have to be one with or somehow cajole an animal spirit for power, and then they can't just sit back and let a powerful entity do the dirty work - they become the entity instead. Then again, for the sake of gameplay I didn't want to make it too impractical - guess that part needs work!

    Maybe it could be more of a 'constant concentration' kind of effect. Like the Druid could shapeshift back and forth without delay or constraint, but needs to pass a CON roll once an hour to maintain their new form instead? Being in a place of nature could extend this to once a day, or even indefinitely (might be overpowered, but on the other hand it might be bad news for a druid who's shifted into a bird or fish, then fails a CON roll...)
