\n Hey, folks. I want to make sure I'm as on top of the design community's conversations as possible, and I'm dipping my toe into using Google Reader. Right now, I have an active LiveJournal account and do a lot of my reading of designer's LJ's there, but while LJ supports feeds, it's not as elegant for that as I'd like. Thing is, I've sucked at keeping track of which designers have blogs out there that AREN'T on LJ.

A quick search didn't show me such a thread on story-games yet... So, let's get one started, eh?

My name's Fred Hicks, and I design games.

You can find my LJ here: http://drivingblind.livejournal.com/ (rss: http://drivingblind.livejournal.com/data/rss)

The Dresden Files RPG official blog's feed's here: http://www.dresdenfilesrpg.com/news/index.rdf

... can't think of any others, just now.
