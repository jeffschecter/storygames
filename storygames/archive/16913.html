    \n OD&D! Especially Mentzer/Moldvay BEXMI D&D (see also Labyrinth Lord, LotFP):\n
      \n
    1. Randomly generate six Abilties.
      2. \n
    2. Your Abilities considered, pick a Class. \u2190 only 1 true mechanical choice!
      3. \n
    3. Everything else follows from your Class.
    \n(I'm deliberately omitting the roll for starting money and buy equipment step - still significantly informed from Class - and the choice of Alignment.)

    \n Generating Abilities, with the traditional "roll 3d6 in order" method, was no choice at all: you got the scores you rolled. Those scores then informed and constrained your choices of class, in that it was not a good idea to be a Fighter if you had low Strength, for example (but you could still make the sub-optimal choice); some classes also had Ability score requirements (like needing Constitution 9+ to be a Dwarf). But the only one choice you had was picking a class, and that generated (HPs, THAC0, STs\u2026) or tightly constrained (equipment you were allowed to use\u2026) everything else.
