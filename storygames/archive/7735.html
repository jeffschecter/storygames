\n Inspired by Tony's thread, I've got an idea for reducing intensity In A Wicked Age. Bring in refreshment scenes from Solar System.

Basically, let things brew with a scene that has no die rolling, and increase one die by one size. Have a hard limit of stats can't add up to more than 48, and no more than one twelve-sider.

The only thing I'm having a hard time with is defining which kind of things would line up to which dice get refreshed. So far I'm thinking:

Covertly ... Observe events without being involved
Directly ... Tell someone exactly how it is / declare your intentions.
For Others ... Take care of someone
For myself ... Enjoy your gains
With Love ... Connect emotionally with someone
With Violence ... Deal with / receive / inflict pain outside of conflict

    \n I think this could be a neat idea but I think it has potential to really remove the negotiating with a big stick aspect of exhausting or injuring someone.

    I would use the above proposed rules but only allow each character to have a refreshment scene once per chapter. This way negotiation is only very slightly skewed (only one of the two stats lost would "heal") and the player would have to choose wisely when to use it. Additionally, it would still ensure that the session went by quick and was not bogged down with refreshment scenes between EVERY conflict.
