    the Infected is available for free at my website and for $10 at Lulu. The free version is in HTML and might possibly be a pain in the ass to read. I'm not much of a web smith.

    \n

    I have certainly sold fewer copies than I would had I not put the free copy out there. However, I would not have done things differently to sell more copies. I'm not confident in the awesomeness of it enough to ask folks to drop money on it blindly. But, since it is out there for free, and my Lulu page directs folks to the free version, I can feel good that no one should be surprised at what they get for their money.

    \nThe SRD for Spirit of the Century, which does not include the examples (we consider those product identity), is here:

    http://www.faterpg.com/dl/sotc-srd.html

    We sell PDFs and print versions of the product.\n
    If so, how's that working out for you?
    \nReally well. I know for a fact that the SRD has made several sales for us.

    I think, though, that it really helps -- on the PDF end at least -- that we aren't giving the entire thing away for free. The SRD has allowed people to say "I read the SRD, and I have these questions" and for the purchasers to respond "The examples in the book really help explain that, you should pick it up."
