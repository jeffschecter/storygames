\n This is the Perfectly Boring Substructure - character-specific rules like d20's feats (but not exactly d20's feats) are meant to go on top of this.

I'd like the Substructure to be reasonably balanced; that is, there should be good reasons to take any of the 4 stats: Might, Agility, Insight, and Will. That way when I add the feat layer (superstructure) on top of it I know I'm adding to something that's at least halfway decent.

Here it is in a 4 page .pdf, could somebody give it a once over and see if the abilities are decent?

    \n Looks like a good start.

    A couple of minor things:
    - You mention that a player can ask the GM to increase the difficulty but there is no hint about why you might want to do this.
    - Initiative - this is a normal check i.e. 2d6 + stat. However, that will cluster a lot of characters on 7+average stat - maybe a linear roll would be better in this case? This works against the idea of using the same roll for everything, of course.
