    \n Hmm...

    One thing is that you're going to have different definitions of "scaling well". I mean, there's a difference between "this is designed to go from 1-hr sessions to year-long games, and actually _works_ at all those scales" and "this game says nothing about scale, so I can use it to run short sessions or long ones."

    That said, the Pool is definitely great as far as being Scope scale flexible (if all the players are on the same page).
