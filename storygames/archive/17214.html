In this episode, we discuss our playtest of Epistolary Richard's polar steampunk adventure game Anarktica: Fate of Heroes and then give Alex a half hour to pitch his new Indiegogo project We'll Send It To Them.

Antarktica conversation ends at minute 52. Yes, we went real in depth with this game, so you guys who love to talk and listen about game design will probably enjoy it quite a bit.
