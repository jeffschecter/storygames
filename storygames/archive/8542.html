\n So I've been away from SG for a long while now, partly because Jason asked me to comment on Fiasco, and then I never did (or, I did, but the file was on my laptop for which I lost my charger and ... never mind), and I felt ashamed and went into hiding. Yeah, it's not the first time I do that. I'm such a wuss.

Also, I finally got my game published. It feels good, let me tell ya. Of course, it's in Swedish, so it might not do much good to promote it here, but you never know. If you do happen to speak Swedish, you can read about it here and buy it on lulu here. Also, on the first of those two links, you can see what it looks like even if you don't speak Swedish.

Basically, it's a universal GM-less game engine, where you first create the world, and then game in it. It's not like Universalis in that you all have PCs, and the game mechanics are set up to simply make a good story gamey story -- you get points for banging other players and for setting up storylines for them based on their goals and values. It's a good system.

Anyway, that's all I wanted to say. Please carry on with what you were doing.
