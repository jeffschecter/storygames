Which D&D races would you shag?

Graham
June 2006 edited June 2006 in Story Games
In Magestrike Ultimus -- Everything Changes!, we were talking about the various half-races in D&D.

Now, I don't understand half-orcs. Because who'd shag an orc? Do fighters hang around taverns, thinking "Well, no-one else is going to shag me, I might as well try the orc woman"?.

Or do you get orc fetishists? Are there some people who really get turned on by warty skin and bad teeth?

You see, I can understand half-elves. Elves sit all cool and silver-haired at the bar. Loads of people would shag an elf. And, what's more, you can understand the elf getting sick of antiseptic, sterile elf sex (scented candles, satin sheets) and thinking "Screw it, I fancy a bit of human tonight".

Equally, I understand why there aren't half-dwarves. Because, if a human has sex with a dwarf, the disparity in heights makes it likely they'll engage in certain sexual practices that won't get the dwarf pregnant.

But half-orcs I don't get.

Does anyone have any insightful comments about interracial dating in D&D?

Graham
