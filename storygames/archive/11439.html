    Carnivale is interesting to me. It was my inspiration for my current game, Dark Days, when it comes to themes and the supernatural element. Sadly, I consider it a failure on my part that I decided to emulate Carnivale-style powers with a ''spellbook'' approach (a list of powers with specific effect), rather than a more interesting create-your-own approach (like Wild Talents or Savage Worlds or even DRYH).
