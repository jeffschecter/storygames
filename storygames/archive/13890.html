    \nWhen you talk to ghosts, roll+weird:
    On a 10+: They talk back. Ask them up to three questions, but you must answer their questions in turn.
    On a 7-9: They speak in maddening whispers, choose 2:
    -\task one question, but answer two of theirs
    -\ttake -1cool forward from their chilling presence
    -\ttake -1sharp forward as their whispers echo in your head
    -\tthey cling to you like cobwebs

    On a miss: They scream\u2026 as do you\u2026 take s-harm\u2026 and be loud.

    \nWhen you are afraid your past has caught up with you, roll +cool.

    On a 7-9, choose 1. On a 10+, all 3.
    - You'll get a fair warning before the past comes to haunt you.
    - You're ready for whatever is coming your way. Take +1 forward to dealing with your past.
    - The MC will tell you now what incident from your past this relates to.

    On a miss, your past has already caught up with you some time ago, if you only cared enough to notice.
