I've got an outline for my Solar System entry for the setting challenge. Here's a teaser.

Henry VIII is dead. A frail youth ascends to the throne. Six Queens seek to claim it for themsleves. You are all that lies in their way.

The Revenant stretches an undead hand from her icy castle, seeking to close all England in its embrace.
The pope's submarines prowl the coast, seeking to deliver their cargo of amphibious steam troopers led by a former Queen of England.
Henry's jilted half-faerie bride mocks the realm, but her magical attacks are becoming more sinister and more deadly.
The Silent Queen; the Invisible Queen. Her minions are everywhere and nowhere. They strike from the shadows. They are the shadows.
John Dee and his crack team of combat astrologers follow the dictates of a Queen who leads from beyond the grave.
Some say she gained the throne by witchcraft. Others say she will do so again. Her forces can tip the balance either way.
