    Sadly, the propagandistic efforts of the planetary forces do not match up to reality.

    Today, the sadly enslaved and holiday free peoples took their first step towards freely available consumer durables and mandatory coffee breaks. Yes, the victorious forces of The Combine conducted a stunningly effective operation against a major city of the oppressors. In fact, one of the most hated symbols of the anti-mercantile forces was reduced to nothing more than rubble during the offensive.

    Speaking of rubble...

    The pathetic forces of the oppressors were comprehensively flattened by the superior technology and economic power of The Combine. Sadly, we must report the loss of the famed Colonel Blackjack as he single handedly destroyed five enemy tank squadrons in a heroic defense of civillians who were being used as human shields by the despicable planetary government.

    The streets are now full of citizens rejoicing at their chance to purchase a variety of quality goods from The Combine!

    The Chairman of the Board

    As promised (and who cannot say that The Combine does not live up to its promises), we can now present photovisual evidence of the true nature of the recent battle. I think that any untainted observer will agree that the battle was fought, on our side at least, according to the highest standards of human decency and financial probity.

    Exhibit 'A':



    In this image you can clearly see the steps taken by Combine forces to avoid civilian casualties. Indeed, it was the enemy themselves who needlessly and carelessly placed their own people in the firing line. In the right centre of the photograph, you can see a Combine 'Gunslinger' mechanised unit capturing a valuable enemy communications post. Centre left shows Combine 'Jaeger' and 'Titan' class units advancing on the cowardly defenders skulking behind one of the ruined aircraft.

    Exhibit 'B':



    As is well known, The Combine does not flinch from telling all sides of the story. Here we see a still from a captured enemy gun camera. In the centre we see the 'Jager' mechanised unit advancing fearlessly towards the enemy, yet taking careful steps to avoid injuring civilians who have flocked into the streets to welcome their liberators. To the right we see the noted and characteristically brave leader of the elite Orbital Insertion Battalion hurrying to the scene, eager to take on the enemy and save celebrating women and children from the guns of the hated oppressors.

    Exhibit 'C':



    Spy drone footage taken near the conclusion of the battle shows (bottom right) the remains of the mechanised unit piloted by the heroic and selfless Colonel Blackjack after defeating, single handedly, massed waves of enemy tanks. Bottom left, we can witness civilians eager to celebrate a notable victory. In the top of the frame you will witness members of the elite Orbital Insertion Battalion moving in to finish off the remaining cowardly defenders who, until the very last, were steadfastly stuck in their redoubt, refusing to venture out on to the field of combat and face justice for their war crimes.Centre right shows the destruction wrought on that most hated symbol of the regime, the dastardly secret police headquarters.

    Exhibit 'D':



    Finally, we can show the awesome power of the liberating Combine forces, the twisted remains of the planetary regime forces lying scattred around their pitiful defence works like so much confetti after a particularly well-attended wedding.

    Any right thinking person will surely agree that these photovisuals demonstrate conclusively that The Combine fought a fair and dignified battle in the face of extreme provocation. We are guilt free and confident in the future success of this campaign.

    Lt. Col. Nitze, 20th Information Dissemination Battalion, 3rd Special Services Wing

    I suppose if you are the decrepit and embattled leader of a failing state, a state where thousands of citizens are flocking to the protection of their liberators, then yes, I guess this might be the spin you put on it.

    But not us.

    As always, The Combine is willing to give you the full and unvarnished truth, bereft of propaganda and simplistic xenophobia. Again, we present photovisual evidence of how the battle actually turned out.

    Exhibit 'A':


    First witness the large number of civilians who had come to one of our Forward Repair & Supply Stations to escape the predations of an increasingly frustrated regime. Notice how they are welcomed at our commissary, given food and warm clothing and invited to see the technological wonders of The Combine. Unlike the hated regime, we have no secrets.

    Exhibit 'B':


    Note the men, women and children who are all being cared for by The Combine, completely free of charge. Behind the stout walls of our base, under the watchful eyes of our mechanised unit pilots, they felt safe from harm for the first time in many days.

    Exhibit 'C':


    Unlike the despised and brutal regime, we want all citizens of The Combine to see the fruits of our technological advances. The now former refugees were able to get a close up view of the elegant machines that now protect them from their former oppressors and their devious Farquarkistani allies.

    Exhibit 'D'


    But as we all know, this kind of benificence enrages the the failing and impotent Great Leader. Hence, the regime forces and their Farquarkistani allies launched a cowardly sneak attack intended not to damage a military installation, but to brutalise civilians. Luckily, there are many heroes in The Combine and the pilot of one of our 'Gunslinger' class mechanised units immediately sprinted into action, heedless of the massed forces which she faced. This selfless act of outstanding bravery and gallantry undoubtedly saved many lives and contributed to great destruction visited upon the attacking forces.

    Exhibit 'E':


    And here is that destruction, for all the world to see. Note that despite minor, superficial damage and the ranks of deluded enemies, our brave pilot pressees home her attack, only thinking of the men, women and children whom she has vowed to protect!

    Exhibit 'F':


    And as it to be expected, we find the so-called 'allies' of the regime, the dastardly Farquarkistani, skulking around, hiding from the guns of The Combine. Despite their (self made) reputation, these poltroons contributed nothing to the battle.

    Exhibit 'G':


    Finally, I think this satellite photovisual says it all.

    Proof positive that this sneak raid was beaten off by The Combine and the lives and spending power of numerous citizens were protected.

    Col. Nitze, 20th Information Dissemination Battalion, 3rd Special Services Wing
