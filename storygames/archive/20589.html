\n I've hacked the dice from the Betrayal at House on the Hill boardgame to produce two different games with different mechanics on the Rerolled feature of my blog.

In Lumen De Tenebris, the players are tough space marines being dropped in to deal with crisis situations on colony worlds on the fringes of human explored space and uses a variant of Otherkind dice.

The Psirarchy is a game about an emergent conspiracy of psychics covertly taking over the world, where the players take on the role of agents of one cell in the conspiracy acquiring assets to aid their cause; the game uses a point-pool system.
