\n

\n\nGraham \n\n\n

\n

\n
\n I am currently thinking about packing for GenCon and wondered how much awesome I should bring.

Firstly, bear in mind I'm travelling from the UK. There is a strict baggage allowance, so I will not have that much room for awesome.

Secondly, I am sharing a room, and I assume that everyone else except Jason will bring their awesome too. I am not sure how much storage space there will be.

How much awesome are you intending to bring to GenCon? How much should I plan for needing over the course of the fourt days?

I generally do not bring much awesome to games, so it is possible I can get away with bringing a lower amount of awesome than most people.

Also, I note that there are many new games, which, I am told, will bring the awesome. If the games bring the awesome, this might mean I have to bring less awesome myself.

Any advice appreciated.

Thanks

Graham

      \n Actually, when it comes to beer, Scotland has ruled the roost for quite some time IMO because they produce some wonderful stuff. Belgian beers - yup, good. I had some cracking brews in a pub in Seattle as well (and one that was rancid). Czech lagers - yummy. In the middle of Cyprus in 42C heat, even their beer tastes good. A world of beer goodness.
