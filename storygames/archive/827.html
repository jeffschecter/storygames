    I have Barbarian Prince, which I used to play alot back when much of my gaming was solo. You die alot, but the system is rich enough that you build good stories about what is happening to your character. Be prepared to get lost and starve to death though.

    I also played alot of Star Viking, which is a great little assymetrical space warfare game. We had it rigged up so that the forces in each system were kept in the wells of a couple pill boxes so the game could be hidden in a desk and played during class ;)
