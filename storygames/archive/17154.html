\n Hi all!
I'm excited and scared to death. We just launched our first Kickstarter! The Artifact is a free game, so why would you want to support it? Because we'll be helping a bunch of artists pay their bills and best of all, you'll get a cool game out of the deal.

By being a backer you get recognized on the back cover of the book and you'll get early copies of the new rule set and get to speak your mind about the design.

Please check it out, ask questions and please consider backing a labor of love!

    \n Ten days to go and a long way to go. If you like Sci-Fi games, stop by The Artifact's Kickstarter. We need your help to make this happen.

    There's a gap opening up in the Kickstarter field. It's easier to back projects from well known names but is that what you want? A few super successful games and new voices unable to tell their tales? Do you want to tell the same old stories that everyone's been telling? Or do you want to dive into unfamiliar territory and expand your horizons?

    Come and back The Artifact and keep supporting the new worlds that could be your next new favorite.

    Interview by Game Knight Reviews
