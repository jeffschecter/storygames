\n I recently watched an Actual Play of The Quiet Year, in which the players mapped out a solar system instead of a typical terrestrial landscape. Their original community is a collection of starships on their way to colonize an area and their subsequent decisions where based on the narrative that they were discovering a planetary systems, asteroids, rival species, etc.
It inspired me to try a similar tactic, but instead i'll be using an underwater setting. I wondered, has anyone else out there tried some unique settings and what kinds of decisions did you have to change in order to fit that setting?
