\n The sequel everyone has been waiting for! If you like Story Games, and can be in the South Bay on the appointed Day, this is the perfect mini-con for you. Like before, the event will be from 11AM to 9PM at Game Kastle in Santa Clara, CA, 26 Feb 2011. We'll use a Google docs spreadsheet for signups again, which you can find at http://bit.ly/f4NQWT .

There will be two slots, a shorter one running 11-2:30, and a longer one from 3:30 to 9. GMs/facilitators, please add games to the sheet as soon as you can, and feel free to add more tables if we need them; everyone else, please feel free to add yourself to any game that still has open slots.

This thread will serve as the "official" announcement venue - there's a Facebook event at http://www.facebook.com/event.php?eid=141126522614898, to help make sharing easier - but I'll also be posting at rpg.net. If you know any other forum whose readers would enjoy hearing about the event, please feel free to post there and let us know here. Let everyone know that it's free and a ton of fun! We had a dozen kinds of awesome last time, and I'm confident this one will be even better. Spread the word, and I'll look forward to seeing folks there!
