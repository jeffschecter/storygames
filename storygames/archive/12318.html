\n I'm getting ready for Origins next week in Columbus Ohio.

I'm wondering what story games will be run there?

I'm planning on running story board games - "Thunder Hamsters and the Temple of Cheese", and "Mongolian Goat Rodeo" - through the Rogue Judges area in the board game hall. I'm also going to be showing off the first draft of one of my Gen Con releases "The Temple of Doom" a half hour dungeon game and a sample of the tile placement games I want to put out next year. I'm ready to go.

If I get a chance I'd like to try out other people's story games or at least get together and talk. ('ll be hanging out some at the War College and in the Retailer's Lounge.

Chris Engle
