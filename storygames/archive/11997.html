\n One of my friends is participating in a very old-school AD&D game I am running. She is not a "gamer" by any stretch (nor is much of the group) and really, we're mostly just goofing off and having a good time drinking beers and pretending to be elves. While discussing D&D today, she said this...

"Basically the whole game is just you being a jackass and trying to kill us and then we get experience points when you FAIL. Which you always seem to do."

That pretty much sums up my D&D experience in a nutshell. Thought you folks might find this funny.
