    \n
    \n SoK was rad. I liked that it was smart people talking about playing and GMing and different game mechanics from the perspective of playing and GMing. I think it's awesome that there are a ton of podcasts where people talk about game design and interview designers and all that, but I miss the really insightful stuff about actually playing games that was on SoK. Paul Tevis's podcast, "Have Games Will Travel" was great for this, too. I think part of the what also made those podcasts great (and possibly led to their demise) is that those guys really prepared ahead of time. Personally, I think Canon Puncture, and especially the new "This Imaginary Life" episodes are the best thing still in production now. (Keep it up, Orklord!)
