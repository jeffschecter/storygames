One of the stretch goals for Microscope Explorer was to put the Oracles online so that anybody could use them whether they had the book or not.

They\u2019re up and running, so if you are sitting down to play Microscope and need an idea, just hit the link and the site will generate a starting concept for you automatically. Check it out, and try not to wile away hours clicking over and over again. Including all five Oracles, there are about 250,000 possible combinations\u2026
