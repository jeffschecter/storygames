\n Hey everybody. My name is Stewart, and I'm one of the friendly hosts of Live by the Die, die by the die. We're a sometimes regular podcast and we play many different independent games (Burning wheel, Mystic Empyrean, Shab al-hiri Roach, etc) and provide humor, rules discussion and most likely offence to those of you easily offended.

You can get all our updates on iTunes by searching for "Live by the die" or on our website: http://livebythediediebythedie.blogspot.ca/ and you can email me and get it read on air at: lbtddbtd@gmail.com.

Enjoy!
