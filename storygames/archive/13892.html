    \n Sorry I seemed to have missed this discussion the first time around!

    RPG Geek has never had a policy to discourage uploading of pictures of actual play, miniatures at the table, groups of friends playing, etc. Those images are part of our image policy and have been for quite a long time now. No threads about this were deleted (and if they were, I'd be pretty pissed about it). We do have galleries which we employ on RPG Geek to categorize pictures into Covers, Play (the game in play) and Creative (painted minis, props for games, etc). All three categories bubble up to the front page - nothing is filtered. You can, however, change the front page default behavior to only show Covers or only show Play or only show Creative. I half remember a discussion about the endless supply of cats in box tops and girls holding boxes at chest height - and those tend to get rejected by the user-guided moderation but that's nothing to do with play pics or creative pics which we encourage and generally sail through the geek-modding process. Right now, the community is focused on database entry and so the vast majority of images are cover shots - but you guys are free to upload whatever play pics you want.

    I'm sorry that anyone got the impression that such images were not allowed or discouraged. It's my fault for letting this perpetuate here for far too long.
