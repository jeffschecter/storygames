\n Heya,

Yesterday on just a whim I put up a list of acronyms used in gaming circles to see if it could be solved before I left work. It was a ton of fun and really surprising which ones were difficult and which ones weren't. I really enjoyed the participation in that thread. However, the moment I posted it I thought of twenty more I could have included. So, I decided to do a follow-up game today. Again I've listed them from easiest to hardest (in my estimation). Anyway, here's the List:

2d6 two six-sided dice
GM Game Master
ODnD Original (or Old) Dungeons and Dragons
IAWA In a Wicked Age
TSOY The Shadow of Yesterday
IC In Character
L5R Legends of the Five Rings
GP Gold Piece
SJG Steve Jackson Games
GNS Gamist/Narrativist/Simulationist
WEG West End Games
ADRP Amber Diceless Roleplaying
DFK Drama-Fortune-Karma
FASERIP Fight Agility Strength Endurance Reason Intuition Psyche
GAMA Game Manufacturer's Association
WH40K Warhammer 40,000
DPS Damage Per Second
PBeM Play By E-mail
TPK Total Party Kill
FR:AA
PBP Play By Post
RM2 Rolemaster 2
C:tL Changeling: the Lost
GDS Gamist/Dramatist/Simulationist
FatB Fortune at the Beginning
BASH Basic Action Super Heroes
RTG R Talsorian Games
BTRC Blacksburg Tactical Research Center
GORE Generic Old School Roleplaying Engine
DL:HoE Deadlands: Hell on Earth
HARP High Adventure Roleplaying
TWERPS The World's Easiest Roleplaing System
DP9 Dream Pod 9
NBG Need Before Greed
BB/LG Bend Bars/Lift Gates
MT Empty
CDG Collectible Dice Game
KABAL Knights and Berserkers and Legerdemain
IWNAY I Will Not Abandon You
NBGH Nobdy Gets Hurt (also abrievieated NGH)

Let's see if we can get them all. Enjoy!

    \n Okay, you guys are doing awesome! Let's do a brief round of hints:

    FR:AA- used in or on a TSR Product
    NBG- a solution to a common argument in fantasy roleplaying games with more than one player
    BB/LG- used in or on a TSR Product
    MT- lamest abreviation ever, somewhat related to NBG
    CDG- an entire genre of games similar to RPGs
    KABAL- comes from the 1980's I believe
