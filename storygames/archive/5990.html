Adam Rogers will be talking about his article, Geek Love in which he goes over Gygax's influence on geek culture. The show should be on live, if your NPR station carries it, at Noon PST.

Why am I bringing this up in its own thread instead of stuff to watch for March?

Well, it's a call in show. And I figure RPGs will be touched upon in a sizable way. One of the things he mentions in the article is how D&D "taught the right lessons". And it might be opportune for story game folk to call in and discuss how RPGs have evolved in the shadows of D&D marginally mainstream limelight, and shine some light on the variety of story games that currently exist in a public forum. For a variety of reasons, I can't listen to live broadcasts at work, so I'll have to settle for the recording they put up hours after the show ends. But you can, and you have something to say. So give a listen and give your input.
