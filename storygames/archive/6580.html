\n So what do you call those thingies where you have 2 different realities overlayed with each other? Subjacent realities is one name that I have found. Affecting one affects the other in some fashion and you can move between them. Like the Gloom in Nightwatch, the Umbra in Werewolf: The Apocalypse, and the astral plan in D&D.


Now for the more important question what can you do with them in a RPG?
My motive for asking this question is my Game Chef Entry Ocean has one called the Ocean. About all it does right now is turn you into a fish and allow you to influence the flow of events in the real world.


So to start the list of things to do in a sub-reality:\n
    \n
  1. See things that are normaly invisable
    2. \n
  2. Travel to other locations
    3. \n
  3. Pass through to other worlds.
    4. \n
  4. Become lost
    5. \n
  5. Disappear
    6. \n
  6. Lose thins for a very long time
    7. \n
