\n I am starting this out with a link to my blog post (where I keep these things). Check out The Reality Vault.

Now this is not the full review. This is just the characters and relationship map. I am going to finish the post this afternoon but before I do I am interested in hearing some feedback about the characters. Namely, for those that played this game before, do these look like characters that share similar qualities mechanically as one would expect from this game?

The setting is that we are all heading to a colony world in a space ship looking for a new start. When we get there the planet is rubble. Completely destroyed. Our endgame is when we dock with the space station in the same system.

I thought the characters were way cool!
- Don
