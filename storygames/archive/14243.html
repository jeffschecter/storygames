    \nMinistry of Entertainment aka "minient". It's a combination of blog and news site, kinda like Kotaku for tabletop.
    http://www.minient.net/

    Parkablogs a blog from a guy who owns a Contemporary Art Book store (read: art books focused on video games, sf/fantasy illustrators, comics, etc) in Singapore, and does visual video flip-through reviews of all the books that come his way.
    While not RPG focused at all, the books previewed here that I later bought inspired lots of RPG campaigns (Ookami, 18 Days, Monster Hunter, etc).http://parkablogs.com/

    Rob Donoghue's excellent gaming blog...
    ...which unfortunately for me he updates every single day with a HUGE article, which I unfortunately never have time to read... But every once in a while there's something awesome enough there (usually relinked/reported by friends) that I make the time to read. If you have the time, though, it is pretty awesome, and choc full of content.
    http://rdonoghue.blogspot.com/

    \nGiant Fire Breathing Robot is one I've come across recently. It's a general "geek culture" blog, but with dedicated tabletop bloggers who do reviews and cover conventions. It's a general mix of press releases, reviews, and "hey I played this game" talk. I'd like to see more in-depth analysis over there (which may be coming, cross your fingers), but it's a good blog that, upon discovering, I was surprised I'd never heard of before.
