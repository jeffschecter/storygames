    \n Advice:
    - Wash your hands frequently. Use germ killer. Do it. The KP plague is a very real thing.
    - Go with the flow. Don't plan.
    - Know as little as you can about anything before you go. Talk to everyone. You'll get confused by status phenomena soon enough, avoid them by having no idea who you're talking with.

    \n Use the extra chair policy to your advantage (folks leave an empty chair for someone to join a group, whereever it might be). Do like Matthijs said, everyone's got great stuff to share. Just dive right in and it's amazing.

    Also, I hope you get to play some games. The conference is mostly made up of panels, but the games run are usually very high quality. And if you get to go to the "week of" before hand, that's a great opportunity to break the ice with other participants. Take advantage of the tours and events offered.
