\n I know there are a lot of spy and espionage systems, but they typically lean in the direction of the James Bond/Mission Impossible/Jason Bourne, with high flying action. I wanted to go in a more realistic direction - is there a game that focuses on the CIA and their more unique problem solving efforts? Cracking deals with terrorists, smuggling weapons, starting revolutions. Any ideas on what a good system for that would be?

    \n What about Ron Edward's Spione? It's a tight and focused RPG about real espionage (although in a specific locale and timeframe), or at least about the more realistic kind of spy fiction (including works by former spies and spymasters), and the CIA is a prominent player in the Berlin spy game. Even if that doesn't end up being the exact game you wish to play, it looks like a good starting point.
