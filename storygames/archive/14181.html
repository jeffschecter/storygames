\n Inspired by the "custom moves make the game" thread, I made a site for collecting custom moves which works a lot like urbandictionary.com.

It's still a bit rough -- you can't edit moves, and moves aren't taggable, searchable, or connected to any kind of login.

Still, it's working so I thought I'd share it: http://hardholder.com.

I'd love any feedback you might have.
