    \n A quick whack:
    1 XP Whenever you mention a superstition
    3 XP Whenever you are driven to act irrationally due to a superstitious belief
    5 XP Whenever you get into trouble or are harmed due to adherence to a superstitious belief

    BUYOFF: Publicly reject as ludicrous a deeply-held superstitious belief.

    EDIT: I am basing this largely off the Key of the Troublemaker, which I played in a 1st Quest Game and had a lot of fun with. You just have to be excited about digging yourself a deep hole of craziness.
