    \n I'm looking to recruit a cohort rather than join an existing cohort. If it's already in progress, it's harder to get on the same creative page.
    \n
    \nI love GMless games. If the story is "told" only by one person at the table, whether that person is me or someone else, I get bored pretty quick. So lately I've been enjoying games like Archipelago. That said, I can also enjoy games with GMish figures, like Dogs and PTA.
    \n
    \nStyle/setting-wise, I love a good fantasy. Or western. Or space shoot 'em up. Or hospital drama. Or Victorian mystery. Or urban nightmare. Or ... anything the group comes up with. As long as there's character motive and obstruction to said motive, resulting in entertaining character arcs, I'm sold.
