\nRECESS is Saturday September 15th!

What is RECESS?
- RECESS is an all day gaming event hosted by NerdNYC.
- Join us in making friends and playing games!
- No experience needed, any rules will be taught.
- No advanced registration is required, just show up and play!

Who can attend?
- Anyone 18 or older (bring a valid photo ID).

Free Friday night RECESS party?
- It's true!
- Details coming soon!

Coming to RECESS?
- Let us know here: http://goo.gl/ntxJ1
- Or let us know on our forum here: http://goo.gl/zQWLg
- Use the \u201cInvite Friends\u201d link on the top right of Facebook to tell friends who also want to play games!

How do I register for Recess?
- There is no advanced registration. Just show up, signup, and play!
- Details here: http://goo.gl/224qc

Admission Cost:
- Admission is $20 per person.*
- Want to attend Recess free?
- Sign up to run 4 hours of games and attend free: http://goo.gl/zqhct
- *Admission buys you a 1 day membership to NerdNYC\u2019s nonprofit to attend RECESS.

Schedule:
- 11am DOORS OPEN!
- 11:30am Signups for First Session (12-4pm)
- 12-4pm Game Slot 1
- 4pm Food Break
- 4pm LARP (2 hrs)
- 5pm True Nerd Trivia
- 5pm Open gaming both days
- 6pm Signups for Second Session (6:30-10:30om)
- 6:30-10:30pm Game Slot 2
- 10:30pm Clean-up

Location:
- 440 Lafayette Street
- New York, NY 10003
- 3rd Floor
- Subways: 6 (Astor Place), N,R (8th Street)
- Directions: http://440studios.com/how-do-i-get-there/

Rules for Awesome Gaming:
- http://bit.ly/yWOqI1

Swap Table:
- Want free nerd-stuff?
- Got cool nerd-stuff to share?
- The Swap Table will be on the 3rd Floor, room White Box.
- http://goo.gl/XabIJ

For the latest information:
- http://goo.gl/FutQY

Questions?
- Email us at recess@nerdnyc.com
