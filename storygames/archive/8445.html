Darius Longfort

Player: Paul T.

Aging knight, former King's Champion. Darius is well past his prime, and
considered a relic by many.

Details:
-Long, gray hair
-Hooked nose, aquiline features
-Wiry, tough, skin like leather
-Old hauberk and armour, stained with the blood of wounds from decades past

Qualities (I think I am...):
-Honourable to a fault
-A protector of the innocent
-A veteran and strategist: I never miss a detail on the battlefield
-Weary of battle and killing

Weapon: Asglund, ancient claymore, now rusty from disuse.


Criock

Player: Seth Ben-Ezra

Details:

--black spiky armor
--a booming voice
--a missing eye
--long white hair

Qualities (I think I am...):

--honorable
--loyal
--determined
--a leader of men

Weapon: a black two-handed sword with a skull-shaped pommel

Rules Note: A Detail is something that would be obvious about your character if you looked at him for a few moments. This is purely for color purposes to assist the players in visualizing the characters. Qualities convey what a character is like...or, as the text notes, what a character thinks he is like. Qualities are narrative constraints. Neither Paul nor I may declare anything that would contradict a Quality.
