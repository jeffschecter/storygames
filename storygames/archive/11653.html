\n Look at this:

http://www.castlemagic.com/color.html

They will build a castle for you. No shit. Just costs a few million dollars, but it's comfortable, warm, cozy, and as authentic as can be. You could have one in your backyard. If you have a really big backyard.

If your remote-controlled portcullis breaks down, no problem, they say, just use the "secret castle entrance".

I can't figure out how much they charge to actually stock the castle, though. How much for a room of goblins? Traps? Treasure? I'd imagine the upkeep costs would be prohibitive for me.
