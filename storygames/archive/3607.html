    \n The best way to format your posts is to select the HTML option. If you don't "speak HTML", a good cheat is to use something fairly WYSIWYG such as the Semagic LiveJournal/Blogger client even if you don't blog. It's basically an HTML editor that sends your text to a blog. So type and format your text in Semagic, including indents and any formatting, and preview there to make sure everything looks peachy; then paste it here, preview, and post (instead of uploading to a blog page). Other LJ/blog clients will likely work equally well, as is any HTML editor.

    \n You can do whole paragraph indenting in BBCode by surrounding the paragraph with ulist tags. And the result you get looks like this:
    \n
    \n[ulist]Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetuer adipiscing elit. Etiam fermentum. Etiam condimentum, augue at tincidunt consectetuer, tortor massa ultricies felis, vel consequat sapien lorem sed lacus. Sed velit quam, dignissim eget, dictum nec, rutrum sed, eros. Maecenas adipiscing sem venenatis nisi sollicitudin elementum. Cras auctor nisl a orci viverra lacinia. Curabitur dignissim ipsum eget magna. In elit sapien, bibendum dignissim, mollis in, suscipit in, est. Duis dolor. Praesent non diam nec lectus lacinia posuere. Nunc fermentum turpis vel diam. Duis rutrum lorem ac felis.[/ulist]
