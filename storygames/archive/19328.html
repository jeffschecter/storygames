\n Hello Stalkers!

The lock Down Zone is a PBF of its own kind, PBF will have it's own YT channel, on which radio broadcasts will be uploaded cosisting of the most interesting events in the Zone.
The game is based around the events of the chernobyl Accident, thus it has elements from Stalker: Shadow of Chernobyl, bits of Metro 2033 and Fallout 3.

"As a man of some sort of a past, you had the misfortune to be in Ukraine (in Alpha version only in Pripyat) when the entire country got screwed up. After a mysterious disaster, from which came out a whole wave of strange and unexplained anomalies, hordes of zombies and overgrown mutants hunting for those poor souls that had the misfortune survive.Your whole past has became useless, the most important thing now is just to survive another day, the rest of it is up to you.

What will you choose, will you be trying to escape from the Lock Down, and maybe with a bit of luck you will, or will you stay put, and decide to take some good position in the new reality, for an example bandit chieftain, or an agent of the KGB intelligence, or perhaps on your own or with other players in the group you will create Hanse. Which one you will be is your own choice, terrorizing villages, or will you try to unravel the mysteries of the disaster?

In this game it is you who are making choices, but remember every deed brings some consequences!"
