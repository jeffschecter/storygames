\n Minneapolis is hosting a thing!

A giant warehouse of gaming goodness.

One weekend, 16 hours of gaming, meals provided!
The twist? all 16 hours will be the same game.

Get several months of gaming in the space of one convention. Bigger story arcs, deeper play.

Attendance is scale-able between a few dozen and a hundred, so registration will close mid October so we can get food, tables, and supplies in order.
