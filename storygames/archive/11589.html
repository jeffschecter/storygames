    Where do I go? What do I do?

    \nPrivately miracle yourself up some serious cash, or use your deity talents for seriously gainful employment. Hire an advertising firm, the best you can. Be sure you get ones who understand viral marketing.

    Then, when a disaster occurs (by your own maneuvering or by waiting for one), swoop in and save people.

    Lather, rinse, repeat until viral marketing transcends itself and becomes a religion. Since it's done freely, it avoids the "demand" stipulation and gets you that sweet, sweet worship.

    Now, here's a question: Are deities in this scenario empowered by worship, or do they have the power and consider worship a simple perk?
