    \n Hey Carl,

    Some pretty big progress on the mechanical front. I've been playtesting, taking playtest feedback from others, and making mechanical refinements since doing up the ashcan a year and a half ago. A playtest I did with Ed Heil, Joe Porrett, and Matt Snyder at Gen Con provoked me to make a few big changes that got confirmed for me by a playtest I ran with Ed, Joe, and their friend James in October.

    The short answer is that the October playtest was the first time in three years of development that the game felt like I wanted it to, with fluid and natural character play and no lunging at specific conflicts (specific stat outcomes) by players. It remains a game about advancement, but scene framing now has players focus their attention on specific terrenes rather than types of NPCs, and the advancement thresholds are obfuscated from the players.

    A few mechanical changes could use some additional validation. I'm currently working to achieve the right writing voice for the text.

    Paul
