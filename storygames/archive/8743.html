    Posted By: MelinglorMaybe if I had a SGish reason to and a posse of peeps
    \nThat's my thing: I love anime-themed gaming. Some of my friends locally are trying to grow a gaming corner at the big convention over here, Animazement (and it's bigger this year than before, having moved to the new Raleigh convention center).

    Anime conventions are typically places to meet with only friends, see people talk about anime you've already torrented, buy anime you already have, watch anime you've already seen... it's very passive, and the few "actual DOING THINGS" activities like Cosplay, Karaoke and DDR tend to be mobbed hardcore (esp karaoke). I figure another outlet of Things To Actually Do would be awesome, so that's what some folks are doing locally.

    But yeah, if you're not that into anime, there's really no point in going. I'm seeing if there are anime-diggin' S-Gers who'd be willing to run a session or two of Maid or Other New Anime-Themed RPGs, etc.

    Thanks!
