Hey, everyhuman! The Game Design Studio will open up for its first critique in just five more days! The deadline is Monday, Sept. 14th.

\n

If you have a game design you're working on that you've playtested meaningfully, a text that needs work, a layout that needs a direction, or illustrations that need to be better, put them in as good condition as you can and come participate in the first round.

I look forward to seeing people's hard work!

    If you ask specific questions, you'll get concrete answers. You feel like Blood Red Sands is almost finished, right? If your questions are, "What do you think?" then you'll get broad answers \u2014\xa0perhaps answers about things you don't want to work on. Maybe it moves toward different themes than you want, or maybe the mechanics are balanced funny in unusual circumstances. But you don't want to talk about those things, right? You want to talk about later development stuff. So ask about later development stuff.

    \n

    UNLESS, and this is important, unless you will take seriously critique that will send you back to the drawing board.
