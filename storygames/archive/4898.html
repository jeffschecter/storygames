    \n Using a mission chart is a nice way of having a bunch of ideas sitting there so you can work them into the game.

    It's kind of like writing an adventure without the full adventure there - just a list of some specific things you want to happen. Because they are surreal weirdness, you can just drop them in. Meanwhile, watch for what the players find interesting, and just improvise in that direction.
