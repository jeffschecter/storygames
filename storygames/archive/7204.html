    \n I'd rather not be branded; and it's a fact that a LOT of folks in "our scene" use other presses and distribution methods and have other associations than either of those. As I don't generally wear ads without compensation from the sponsor, I wouldn't want one (let alone ten) publisher's buttons all over me. Hence the reason I suggested something that's not only non-branded but also relatively genre-independent, for folks who might be in costume one or more days (which make a "We Put The Story Back Into Games" T-shirts equally unappealing, to me).

    Maybe some form of Go Play sign, be it T-shirt, pin, button, cardboard sign. Equilateral triangle green triangle, pointing to the right, in any form, would be our "high sign" (or is it "hi sign" never written it before now....).
