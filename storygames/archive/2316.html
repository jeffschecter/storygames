the shadow of yesterday is getting another translation!

oliof
January 2007 edited January 2007 in Directed Promotion
Clinton R. Nixon's pulp fantasy game The Shadow of Yesterday is in the process of being translated to german by yours truly. Frequent snapshots of the work in progress can be found here. The book will expand on the original text, as I got permission from various contributors to the game by way of adaptations, add-ons and new gifts and secrets to include them in the translation.

So, I welcome any advice and criticism regarding the state of my work (besides "it isn't finished yet" and "why isn't there an english version?").
