\n Google Sites & Campaign Bible Wiki
I was curious if anyone out there had used Google Sites as a gaming forum?

It seems to have several features that would be awesome for a campaign.
I've been using wikis for a LONG time as my "campaign bible".

I found Google Sites about a year ago... I'm honestly not sure what's different about them than a wiki site (they seem to have the same functions)... ARE they different? Or is that just Google's name for their wikis?

I love Google Sites. I learn new functionality every week.
Here are a few of my efforts:

IRON KINGDOMS: http://sites.google.com/site/fullmettlefantasy/
FORGOTTEN REALMS D&D4: http://sites.google.com/site/hereinliethelostlands/
CALL OF CTHULHU: http://sites.google.com/site/alphagreengov/

What do you think?


One thing I'm looking for NOW is how to have a "forum" within the wiki. I've always created/used Yahoo Groups with each game group I gather to post questions, notes and (most importantly) schedule game sessions.
I'd like to drive players to the wiki sites more - so I'm looking for ideas on how to ditch the Yahoo Groups and have the same thing function on Google Sites.

I've tried "Google Groups" - BUT
a. I'm not sure if/how that connects with Google Sites
b. I don't like the format/functionality of Google Groups compared to Yahoo Groups.


Any thoughts or suggestions?
