Alright, so all of the discussion in the other thread got me thinking, and especially when User Clone suggested a thread for discussions I figured this was definitely go. Let's run through A Quiet Year. Assuming folk are at all interested, I've got a draft of ground rules here:

1) I keep the map and deck on hand. I'll draw a card and post the options at least a couple times a day. First come first serve on what happens with the card, but I don't choose unless the card lays fallow for at least a few hours.
2) I figure a prechosen action rotation is best Action choice is left up to whoever gets to it.
3) Discussions will account for the first four responses. Crossposting, whatevs, but if you see four responses, don't pile on more.
4) For project length, since it's a sort of micro-discussion, first three responses suggest, with the fourth response deciding based on previous submissions.
5) Whatever your contribution is, tell me what to put on the map and where to represent it. I'll take regular pictures of the map and post them to my G+ stream, and link them in a post.

Anyone interested? Should it be a Fleeting Year? Any suggestions for the ground rules? Lemme know
