    \n I don't know, I think the game's pretty straightforward. Be aware it could end pretty quick, so don't take too long deliberating about details, go with your first instincts (goes for both players). Also, along the same lines, and really important: don't soft-pedal when playing the ghosts! Make them really scaring and menacing and murderous. (The game gets weird if you just try to make your ghosts merely *creepy* when the game says that the ghost is actually about to inflict violence on the explorer. So don't hold back!)
