\nShinobigami (God of the Shinobi) is a contemporary-set story game of warring ninja clans in modern day Tokyo (think World of Darkness, but with ninjas instead of vampires). There are six clans, each with their own goals. Four ninja must work together to overcome a dark challenge, and yet their goals will be at odds with each other. There will be dramatic tension and even conflict/combat, but not "Muy Macho I stab you in the face and burn you to death!" style hardcore PVP. It's a Japanese RPG currently being translated for publication within the next year. The feel is anime-ish, but semi-serious as well. Not no-joking GrimDark hardcore world of darkness, and yet not silly Maid or slapstick-like play either.
\n
\nThe rules will be explained as we play. It's a simple 2d6 skill system, but there's a lot going on with character secrets and the rules for relationships and how those secrets get revealed. The focus is on narrative story, but there are some cool light combat moments and the like as well. I've wanted to play it for evars. Matt's run it several times, the rest of us have never played it. Plus, while I know Matt and Alex, no one else has really met anyone else here, so you're not walking into Old Friends' Land or anything like that, new folks are totally 100% welcome.
