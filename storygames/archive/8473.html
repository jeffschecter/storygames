\nOut There - Beyond Club SG
\n
\nThis is a supplement for Out There, created pretty much entirely by Mad JP (the writer). Out There is an awesome game with strong rules that support collaborative play and direct the imagination. However, some players may feel daunted by the pure amount of creative input the game demands. But no more! Now these players can try out the game using Beyond Club SG, a FREE supplement that provides a creative kickstart. How does it do this you ask? Good question, here are the ways in which it offers this creative boost:
\n
\n- provides a firm starting situation to spark the imagination.
\n- has three decks of cards to kindle the creative impulse: characters, places, events.
\n
\nBeyond Club SG does not supersede the rules of Out There, but offers new supplementary rules that enhance the ability of the game to direct creativity and kick it up a notch.
\n
\nBeyond Club SG is a game of Out There taking place on the campuses of Kent Ohio, where a clandestine organization of self-professed geeks and weirdos known as Club SG operates in force. People have gone missing, and now it is your job as rookie FBI agents to solve the mystery of what is happening beyond club SG!
\n
\nWithin all this fiction is metafun! The character cards in the deck are truly bastardized and shamelessly taken from online Story Games (SG) members!* The places and event cards are twisted versions of those seen in other popular games!
\n
\nBeyond Club SG is a free PDF supplement for the Out There RPG. Possibly there will also be an ashcan kind of boxset with actual cheaply produced cards, club sg signature story tokens, dice, and other bonus material depending on interest.
\n
\nComing Soon, an official supplement to Out There from Indie Game Challenge Company #2... (You may check out Out There right now though, for a minimal 1$ fee)
\n
\n*Obviously this is all in good fun, but if the characterizations truly offend anyone they may contact me to make changes or be removed from the deck.
