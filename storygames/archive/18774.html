\n Chicago Gameday 36 is coming up on October 19th, events starting at 9:30 AM.

Chicago Gameday is a day of free RPG gaming held three times a year at Games Plus in Mt. Prospect, IL. We have two event slots of 4-5 hours each, with up to eight games in each slot. The focus of the event is RPGs, but we also welcome wargames and long-form boardgames.

Upcoming dates of note:

September 9th @ 7 PM: We begin the event planning process; a thread will be started on ENWorld in which GMs can post events they'd like to run.

October 7th @ 7 PM: Player sign-up begins; agains, this is handled via a thread on ENWorld.

October 19th @ 9:30 AM: Gameday 36! Feel free to join us for breakfast at 8 AM, if you're up that early.

For more info, you can check out our Gameday Facebook page or take a look at the Chicago Gameday FAQ.
