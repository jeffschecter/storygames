\n Dear all,
\n
\nI was pleasantly surprised by "Seven Leagues" from Malcontent Games. I like its simplicity, and I think it could be exactly the right thing for my group: they didn't like the Wushu conflict mechanics too much, but they liked the embellishment stuff. Anyway, I think the game might work pretty well for us.
\n
\nExcept that it's written for the fairy-tale background. Which is not bad, per se. But I'd like to use the system for all kinds of settings.
\n
\nHas anyone here used Seven Leagues for any other genre?
\n
\nBest,
\nNorbert
