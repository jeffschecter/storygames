\n I got Grey Ranks a while ago, but started reading today. A nice thick book. 300 pages of WWII angst. 300 pages? Yepp, 300 pages. My copy has two full copies bound within one cover! It's the double plus good Grey Ranks!

One of the batches of While the World Ends was a bit larger than the others. The printer didn't trim the books down after printing. I called them "De Luxe", and sold them at the same price as the regular size books. It was a popular option while they lasted.

What exciting extras have you received from Lulu?
