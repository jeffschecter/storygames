\n I've also grown tired of running games where violence/war/political scheming is the main mode of resolution. As a fan of hard sci-fi, I've always wanted to run that game more in tone with Arthur C Clarke; where the the focal point is exploration, and by using technology and a bit of intestinal fortitude you can reach for the stars or divert a disaster.

Problem is - how the hell do you do that in an RPG and make it fun, grand, and not too super technical?

Any suggestions?
