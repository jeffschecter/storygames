\n Has anyone experimented with drilling down the default Microscope elements (Period/Event/Scene) to something smaller in scale?

I think this could work great for movie/tv series. Just by switching to Act/Sequence/Scene to collaboratively play through a movie, or switching to Season/Episode/Scene to play through a tv series.
