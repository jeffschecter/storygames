Today the company you are working for is laying off 1/3rd of its employees. You can't afford to loose this job. What are you willing to do to keep it?

Will you sell out your fellow co workers?
Will you get a promotion?
What if you are let go?
Will you sue to keep your job?
Will you get a huge severance?
Will you finally do that naughty thing you always wanted to do now that it doesn't matter?

Beyond the above I have no or very few preconceptions. I don't care if this uses any random elements; dice, cards or whatever. I don't know how many people playing at the same time this will support. I don't know if there will be a character sheet or a GM. I don't know if players will play 1 or multiple characters. Maybe everyone plays the same character. Maybe you each play different parts of the same person's personality with different agendas. What kind of company? Great question. I'm not sure. Maybe as part of character creation, players will have guidelines to create the company together. Maybe the company is a player. I have no idea. But whatever it is, it will rock!
