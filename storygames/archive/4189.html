\n There's two conventions coming up and I've got too many games to choose between.

The games will need to work well for a convention one-shot. Also, I don't think I'm going to buy a new game, so it needs to be one I've got already.

Firstly, there's games on my review list:\n
  • Dirty Secrets
  • Dread: The Game With A Hastily Redone Cover
\nThen there's my game, which is pretty solid and could use a playtest:\n
  • A Need To Kill
\nThere's Paul's ashcan, of course, which I ordered...\n
  • Acts Of Evil
\n...but, although I'd love to play it, a convention playtest doesn't seem quite right.

What else? The books on my shelf:\n
  • Lacuna
    • \n
  • Contenders
    • \n
  • Polaris
    • \n
  • Best Friends
\nOr there are those hacks I always wanted to do:\n
  • Back In Time For Tea: that children's hack for Bacchanal.
    • \n
  • The dystopian hack for My Life With Master I always wanted to do
\nI don't know. Any opinions on those? Any other options?
