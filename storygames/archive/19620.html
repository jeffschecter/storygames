\n Hi everyone! There's a new edition of The Analog XP, an online magazine in english about the brazilian analog game design community! I'm publishing it on my patreon page, and I'm counting on you to support it: http://www.patreon.com/encho
These first three editions will present some of our history on analog gaming and some of our current creators. Then we will finally start discussing some game theory on future editions.

In this issue:
+Indie v Mainstream
Authors and Publishers from many generations talk about it. What changed? How do the big publishers help?

+Interviews
Marcelo Rodrigues- TAGMAR
Marcelo Cassaro - DRAG\xc3O BRASIL, TORMENTA
Ant\xf4nio S\xe1 Pop - REDBOX, OLD DRAGON
Thiago Edwardo - UNZA RPG, UED
Danilo Kobold - KOBOLD'S DEN, PULSE

+Full Game: BRIGADE OF FOUR
A four player story game about military teenagers dealing with their sexual and social urges while trying to survive as a team

http://www.patreon.com/creation?hid=745601



Please tell me about your impressions on each issue, especialy on the English writing. I'm training a lot and I hope to get everything right.
Thanks a lot for your attention!
