I am thrilled to announce the launch of my Kickstarter for Urban Shadows, an urban-fantasy rpg powered by the apocalypse. The reception for this game on our community here has been just awesome so far. Thank you all. Mark Truman and I have been working on the project for over a year and are certain that it's ready to rock and cannot wait to get the book into players' hands!

Without further delay, here is the link to the site. Please take a look and if you could share the link with friends who might be interested I would really appreciate the additional coverage.
