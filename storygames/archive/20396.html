\n Working on a sword-&-planet RPG, and emphasising the pulpiness of the genre. As a jumping-off point, however, we're creating a world-building game. It uses as its basis Microscope, combined with Dawn of Worlds and Diaspora.

The aim is for the group to collaborate on a history of a sword-&-planet world (or a region thereof) in order to have a better handle on the world and genre before adventuring in it. Is there anything you'd like to see (or not see) in such a world-building game?
