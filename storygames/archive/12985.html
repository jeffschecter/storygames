    \n Heya Christopher,

    That blog has a lot of neat articles on it! Psychogeography is just one thing there. I am really interested in environmental narratives and how to build a dungeon crawl (or perhaps design a whole new game) using environmental narratives.

    So it is valuable
    To me yes. I can look at a dungeon (or map) and very quickly see the environmental narrative which would hopefully in turn led to a different play experience armed with this knowledge.

    I think I answered your second question in the first part.

    I would love to see a claustrophobic quotient map. It's a little difficult with 10x10x10 dungeons
