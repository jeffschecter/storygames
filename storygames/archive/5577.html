\n While working on some oracles for IAWA in the dentist's office this morning, I think I hit onto a brilliant idea.

When people post their AP reports for IAWA, they should state which oracle they used and give us one or two oracle elements based directly on the AP for their game. We could then collect them and have oracles spawn from gaming itself.

Any problems anyone could see from this? So long as they were kept thematically linked, I think AP spawned oracles could keep new ideas flowing for years.

    Posted By: Mark CauseyScott: I guess you don't have to write up an AP, but at the least, which Oracle did you start the game with for those results?
    \nFrom "God Kings of War"

    A speaker for the ancestors, carrying secrets and warnings.
    A brutish and tyrannical warlord and his uncouth thugs.
    A youth or maiden, the reincarnation of a great hero, whose soul remembers glory.
    The human servant of a mighty and unspeakable demiurge.

    There were three players. A brutal king, his daughter and a merlin like figue. The king was the tyrannical warlord and the servant of a mighty and unspeakable demiurge (a black sword - shard of a dead god), the daughter was whole cloth but implyed by the pregame discussion of the oracles, and the magician was the speaker for the ancestors.

    The king raped a daughter (not the player) against the warnings of the magician to satisfy the lusts of his magical sword. The players daughter made a deal with the magician to take the child as her own. She ended up with the child in her womb undergoing a dramatic and painful labor. When born there was a prophesy he would bring the king down. The last scene had the king forced into killing his daughters by the sword while the child hid under the bed. We will roll new oracles next time but the child and sword will probably play prominently.
