\n We're going to make up "character seeds", which are cool ideas for characters.

I'm going to start, by writing a single-line aspect of a character.

To play:
1.) copy the bottom "aspect" of the last person's "character seed".
2.) Add a second "aspect".
3.) Hit post.

Thus, each post is two lines long, and gives us a cool concept for a character.
And, each post contains one line that was copied/pasted (from the previous poster), and one line that is original.
Therefore, it's like a chain letter kind of thing.

If you want to talk about a given character seed ("man, that'd be an awesome character for X!"; "if I were playing that character, I'd also make it Y"; "That combination reminds me of Actor A in Movie B"; etc), do that in an entirely seperate post which is written entirely in italics, so as not to disrupt the flow.
