\n Have you ever wondered what happens when bloodthirsty barbarians face off against vile alien invaders from beyond the stars?

Barbarians Versus... is a tongue-in-cheek roleplaying game that packs all the rules you need to loot, pillage, maim, and party. The easy-to-learn, fast-paced mechanics are centered around descriptive roleplaying and imagination.

Choose cool abilities and awesome items! Fight opponents with descriptive, brutal action! Use random looting tables to find shiny things! Explore ancient barbarian wisdom! Includes Dwarf and Zombie Meat alternate settings! Available in PDF with Softcover pre-order.
