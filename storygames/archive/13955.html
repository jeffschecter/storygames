\n Based on an idea by Robert (komradebob) from this thread.

So my proposal, give us your games about vampires. No competition, just commentary. Just post a link here. If we can get 30 (or some other reasonably high number), maybe there'd be someway to get it all together for publishing. Ideally these would be short form "story gamey" games about whatever interpretation of vampires strike the author as the most fitting.

    \n Here's what I'm thinking:

    The book will always be available, and whenever there is $100 or more in a dedicated pay pal account I'll make a donation to the Red Cross. The donation will be made every Friday that the balance exceeds that $100 dollar minimum.

    Also, I plan on soliciting art from the Deviant Art and Concept Art communities.

    I have some layout work done and a cover design I'm trying to put the finishing touches on. When they're done I'll post them here for public scrutiny, so that if they're shite or if someone else would like to take up the bat we can do that instead.

    My idea right now is to aim at 200 to 300 page perfect bound soft cover black and white book (U.S. letter 8.5 x 11) that will sell for 12.50 or 15.00 dollars, meaning that 2.00 will go to charity with every purchase for a 300 page book. Each submission will need to be 10 pages or less, and the extra free space will be filled with art, fiction and essays on the subject matter as available.

    Theoretically contributors will either supply text, which will then be placed into a default layout and padded out with whatever art is available and most suited to their game or that contributors can do all the layout/art stuff themselves and have their document (in the form of an editable PDF) be included as is. All art, layout and text needs to be CC-A licensed.

    Next weekend I should have a working version of my own game, which is something like Toni Morrison's Beloved or the opening Dybbuk sequence from A Serious Man. I'm also doing research for an intro/essay type thing that if it doesn't totally suck might make a nice Preface.

    Any comments, suggestions or corrections to the above would be greatly appreciated.
