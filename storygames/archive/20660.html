\n We're doing a thing (again, this is year two) in Minneapolis, MN.
It's based on the LongCon model, where you sign up for the same game for a whole weekend.
Friday night, all Saturday, and Sunday morning.

Meals provided.

(From the site description)
September 23 - 25, 2016
Minnesota Long Con is a small gaming convention where gamers get together and play one game with the same table over the course of a weekend.

Session 1: Friday 9/23 6:00PM-10:00PM
Session 2: Saturday 9/24 9:00AM-1:00PM
Session 3: Saturday 9/24 2:00PM-7:00PM
Session 4: Sunday 9/25 9:00AM-1:00PM\ufeff

https://plus.google.com/u/0/communities/108970759442542364640

----------------------------------

Last year we had 5 tables! Including:
Dungeon World
Project: Dark
PrimeTime Adventures
The Mountain Witch
Worlds without Master

There were some other games played in and around the other sessions:
Zombie Cinema
Remember Tomorrow
and others (my memory is failing me at the moment)
