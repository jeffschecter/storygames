\n Hello, I\u2019m writing 101 adventures ideas; because you need things to do went you sit down to play. Hopefully, at least one of them will be of interest to you to be. They are not system specific, so you can adjust them to fit you play style of choice. This for me is an exercise in generating premises, so I\u2019m looking for any feedback you can give me about that. The rest of the adventures are going to depend on my free time, but hopefully they will be up by the middle of December. Thank you for reading and I\u2019m looking forward to any feedback.
\n
\nNick
\n
\n First Ten
\n1.\tThe Bureau of Fake People
\nDescription: There are people who should not be but are . They maybe children who parents who wish them into existence, or office workers who pop into existence one rainy afternoon. To deal with this problem the government appointment investigators to look into all matters relate to the issue.
\n
\n2.\tThe Ruin Runners
\nDescription: The good news is there is no monsters hiding in these ruins , and there is plenty of treasure for the taking. The problem is the reason is the ruins has very active defense system, and the people you are working with are not your friends in the slightest. You may end up wishing that the monster was there to rip your throat out, because at least then you know it coming.
\n
\n3.\tThe Jinn alliance
\nDescription: People spend their lives and fortunes seeking jinn in the deserts around the Eternal City. So went one come to you willing it was the dream of a life time. However, this Jinn needs your help to find it\u2019s lost love. So by the end of the day you gained an airship and an a group of allies . However, at the end of the day every treasure hunter in the desert was looking for you , and the cult of the Blue Flame is sharpening it knives to stop you. You see the lost love of Jinn is there source of power , and anyone who tries to
\n
\n4.\t Camp Hope
\nDescription: Welcome to a world where paranormal and enhance people exist and commit crimes and act in socially inappropriate manners. Federal law mandate their rehabilitation as they are citizens and human beings .Public opinion depends that the government has a four bombers with nuclear weapons on standby in case something happens at one of the bigger facilities
\n One of the bomber\u2019s targets of this bombers is Camp Hope. It is the largest rehabilitation facility in America located on an American air force land in Central Nevada. Here you work as employee of the American Federal Government Paranormal Rehabilitation Service. You may actively work to prevent violence from those living in the camp. Or you may by help them learn to exist in a world that may explode if they get too upset. Can you help change the lives of wire up cyborgs , supervillian clones, and hormonal pyrokinetics for the better?
\n
\n5.\tThe Last Crusade
\nDescription: The Universe is dying slowly but it course is set and nothing can change it . However, word has come to the tribes that exist within the bodies of their self-created demiurges , castles of black night, and decaying world ships . There is a way to shape the universe to come, but blood must be spilled. Is it a trick to hasten the end of creation or there is a chance that someone will have a chance to shape the creation to come?
\n
\n6.\tThe Old Magi\u2019s Home
\nDescription: The night after the old magi\u2019s death claimants to began to cross the barrier separating his land from the town. Somewhere simple treasure seekers who knew the magi had a stack of gold somewhere. Others where more knowledgeable about the secrets that the magi possessed, and more sinister because of it . However, the magi has a collection of oddities from years of study that had wills and minds of their own . These beings decide to mount a defense of their home in an attempt to keep treasure seekers away.
\n
\n7.\tOption A
\nDescription: The first choice for disposing of hostile aliens, temporal anomalies and ancient monsters is unit of artificial entities and their maintainers known officially as United Nations Special Problems Team. The public knows them by their media nickname Option A , because they are always the first ones to response to a major problem . Of course some of U.N.S.P.T members would tell you that the world\u2019s government prefer U.N.S.P.T get bang up before they send in one of their own military assets.
\n
\n
\n8.\tThe Children Crusade
\nDescription: Time and progress has done little to temper humanity cruelty towards each other. That why the post-human intelligences in what is humanity\u2019s system of birth became the target of raiders from the million worlds that humanity now called home. These raiders looking enslave the powerful intelligences for use in projects ranging from war-making to exploration care little for the damage they did. The intelligences that they damage care very much. Now for the first time in eons in the mother system war is being discussed as a solution to a problem. To help fight the war post-human intelligences are creating a special combat capable beings. These Champions being created in theses cr\xe8ches have been told only two things. Find our stolen children and bring an end to the raids . Everything else is irrelevant.
\n
\n9.\tGod-Killers
\nDescription: Across creation there are beings of divine power, who actions make them dangerous and unpleasant. Some are malice filled dark lords who poison everything they look at. Others are so absolute in their moral righteousness that they threaten everything just as much as any would be dark lord. Some are just too chaotic or quirky to fit into the reality well. To help combat these beings and ensure a balance of sorts a coalition of entities have come together under the banner of the God Killer to ensure order. You have been recruited for your skills or perhaps simply your muscle to help them.
\n\tPerhaps you will serve as a scholar/spy to learn what you can about the entity . Maybe you will attack subtly with rumor and imported magic. Perhaps you will come out as a champion to slay the god. Maybe you will use the subtle knife itself and bring an end to the problem for all time.
\n
\n10.\tThe Twisting Building
\nDescription: Making magic is about capturing the intangible and making it real through ritual and action . One aware mystical practitioner a successful architect during the day is attempting to do this will a new building, and some unaware participants. Maybe this will be for the best for all of them or maybe it will kill them all. The architect does not care for this an experiment. However, those inside will care very much by the end.
