\n Back in February, at Dreamation in Morristown NJ, I played in a slot of Kevin Allen Jr.'s Trouble For Hire, a high-octane GM-less RPG where the adventure revolves around a 1970's action movie-type macho antihero with a fast car and a knack for going from bad to worse. It was a blast, and I play excerpts from the session that Kevin ran to show how and why in the latest Virtual Play, Episode 66: Trouble for Hire.
