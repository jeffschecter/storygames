\n This game is about the losers of the apocalypse. They're not cool, hard, hot or sharp, though they are usually pretty weird. They get stepped on, insulted, beat up and humiliated. Their only chance of surviving is in helping each other, comforting each other, and trying to avoid starving or getting killed by all the violent bastards in this hellhole.

Their only chance of getting to hang with the Battlebabes and Gunluggers for a few minutes is to join them in their bullying of the other losers.

This is their game.

Stats! There are five: Stupid, Ugly, Weak, Scared and Weird.

Playbooks! There are \u2026 a few. Here are some of them: the Cripple, the Deluded, the Broken, the Object, the Moron.

Moves! Here are some of the basic moves.

Stand up to someone
When you're trying to stand up to an NPC, roll+Scared.
On a 10+, you cry, run away and/or piss yourself (your choice).
On a 7-9, you have a choice: back down or be beat up and/or humiliated (Bully's choice, take Harm as established).
On a miss, they actually respect you for it, and leave you alone.

Whimper
When you beg an NPC for help or mercy, roll+Ugly.
On a 10+, they get angry and decide to punish you for your insolence.
On a 7-9, they're willing to give it to you, but you're going to have to do something demeaning and humiliating to get it.
On a miss, you are actually treated as a human being.

Run away
When you're trying to get away, roll+Weak.
On a 10+, they catch you.
On a 7-9, you drop something, leave someone behind, or are hurt trying to escape, or the Bully can offer you a hard bargain.
On a miss, you get away.

Manipulated
When you want something from someone, say what you're willing to offer in return and roll+Weak.
On a 10+, they take what you're offering and offer nothing in return. If you refuse, take Harm as established.
On a 7-9, they take what you're offering and give you something back. It's not what you wanted, but it's something. If you refuse, take Harm as established.
On a miss, they give you want you want and take what you offer.
If the other person is a PC, they're free to disregard the instructions, but can choose to enforce them if they so wish.

Pretend to be cool
When you're trying to fit in and be accepted into a group, roll+Weird.
On a 10+, they pretend to like you.
On a 7-9, they give you a demeaning job to do.
On a hit, they let you hang around them and maybe even listen to you sometimes.

Keep up
When you're trying to understand what's going on, roll+Stupid.
On a 10+, the Bully gets to make a Hard Move.
On a 7-9, you get to ask one question.
On a miss, you get to ask two questions.
Who is going to get hurt here?
What are they really after?
Who do I have to pick on so they won't pick on me?
How can I make this stop?

Get a clue
When you're talking to someone and trying to figure them out, roll+Stupid.
On a 10+, the Bully gets to make a Hard Move. They probably get angry.
On a 7-9, you get to ask one question.
On a miss, you get to ask two questions.
Why are you so angry/sad?
Why are you being nice to me?
What are you going to do?
Are you telling the truth?
How can I make you feel better?

Help/Interfere
When you're trying to help or interfere with another character's move, roll+Pretense.
On a 10+, they misunderstand OR they understand exactly what you're doing OR the Bully makes a Hard Move.
On a 7-9, they get either a +1 or a -2 (your choice) AND they EITHER misunderstand OR understand exactly what you're doing (Bully's choice).
On a miss, they get either a +1 or a -2 (your choice).

Combat!
Haha, no. Unless you're the Moron, take Harm as established.

Gangs!
Better stay away from those.

Experience!
Experience is hard earned, but you're not going to get any better and you're not going to retire to safety. Sorry.

Coming later.

    The Delusional

    \n

    You're pretty damn cool. People look up to you and respect you, and you've done some pretty badass things, and are capable of doing even more. At least that's what you tell yourself and, above all, others. In reality, nobody takes you seriously. You're a fucking joke. People laugh at you, behind your back and sometimes in your face. But you just can't let that sink in, because that would be accepting that you're just another loser. Another nobody. And that would destroy you.

    \n

    Stats

    \n

    Stupid+2, Ugly+1, Weak+0, Scared-2, Weird+2
    Stupid+3, Ugly-1, Weak+2, Scared-2, Weird+1
    Stupid+1, Ugly+1, Weak-1, Scared+0, Weird+2

    \n

    Nobody calls me a chicken

    \n

    When somebody insults or makes fun of you, roll+Stupid.
    On a 10+, you respond with violence (physical or verbal).
    On a 7-9, you set out to prove them wrong. +1 ongoing until you''ve convinced yourself of it.
    On a miss, you keep your cool.

    \n

    Fuck move

    \n

    When you have sex with someone, roll+Stupid.
    On a 10+, hold 2.
    On a 7-9, hold 1.
    Spend your holds when pretending to be cool by bragging about boning this person. You gain a -1 to your roll when you do so.
    On a miss, the person gets to ask you any question about you, out loud, and you have to answer honestly.

    \n

    Posturing

    \n

    When you're all presenting your characters, put down Pretense+3 to everyone. You put up a pretty tough wall.

    The Broken

    \n

    You're not well. That's an understatement. Something happened to you and it broke you. Maybe you were once cool. Maybe you were once a gunslinging badass with an attitude. Not anymore. Now you're just broken. Some people break and they get all sorts of superpowers and shit. They're weird and crazy, and people are afraid of them. Nobody is afraid of you. You're just a pathetic wreak.

    \n

    Stats

    \n

    Stupid-1, Ugly+1, Weak+0, Scared+1, Weird+2
    Stupid-2, Ugly-1, Weak+2, Scared+2, Weird+2
    Stupid+0, Ugly+2, Weak+0, Scared-2, Weird+3

    \n

    Triggerunhappy

    \n

    You have triggers. When some things happen to you, you lose it. You can't control yourself. Whenever a trigger occurs, roll+Weird.
    On a 10+, the Bully chooses one of your responses and you act accordingly.
    On a 7-9, you choose one of your responses and you act accordingly.
    On a miss, you keep it together.

    \n

    Triggers

    \n

    Choose three:

    \n
    • Crowds
    • Tight spaces
    • Animals
    • Nudity
    • People touching you
    • Violence
    • Loud noises
    • Vehicles
    • The dark
    • The outdoors
    • The indoors
      • \n
    \n

    Responses

    \n

    Choose three:

    \n
    • You start screaming
    • You get violent
    • You start drinking or take drugs
    • You get seizures
    • You start spouting nonsense
    • You flee in panic
    • You harm yourself
    • You freeze up, unable to move
    • You start crying uncontrollably
    • You vomit
      • \n
    \n

    Fuck move

    \n

    Oh, yes, sex is definitely a trigger.

    \n

    Posturing

    \n

    When you're all presenting your characters, ask the others one or more of the following questions:

    \n
    • "Which one of you prevented me from taking my life?" Put down Pretense-1 for that character.
    • "Which one of you thinks I'm pretty cool and edgy?" Put down Pretense+3 for that character.
    • "Which one of you am I ashamed to show my responses to?" Put down Pretense+1 for that character.
    • For all others, put down Pretense+0.

    The Object

    \n

    Unlike the rest of those disgusting freaks, you're actually pretty ok to look at. You're not hot, of course, but you're \u2026 fuckable. Not that this brings you a lot of joy. Actually, it's quite the opposite. But it's the only life you know, and you've been forced to get used to it.

    \n

    Stats

    \n

    Stupid+2, Ugly-1, Weak+2, Scared+1, Weird-1
    Stupid+1, Ugly-2, Weak+2, Scared-1, Weird+3
    Stupid+0, Ugly-2, Weak+1, Scared+3, Weird+1

    \n

    Up for it

    \n

    When someone wants you to perform a sexual favor, roll+Weak.
    On a 10+, you either do it willingly, or suffer 1-Harm messy loud and be forced to do it anyway.
    On a 7-9, you either do it willingly, or suffer 1-Harm messy loud for refusing.
    On a miss, it's up to you.
    If the person is a PC, they get to choose to respect your refusal instead, regardless of the outcome.

    \n

    Fuck move

    \n

    Whenever you and a character have sex, whatever the other person's fuck move is, it doesn't happen.

    \n

    Posturing

    \n

    When you're all presenting your characters, ask the others one or more of the following questions:

    \n
    • "Which one of you wants to sleep with me, but is to afraid to ask?" Put down Pretense+1 for that person.
    • "Which one of you has slept with me on numerous occasions?" Put down Pretense+3 for that person.
    • "Which one of you shares a hobby with me?" Put down Pretense-1 for that person.
    • For everyone else, put down Pretense+0.

    You lost me at rape moves
    \nThis is fair, and though I've so far not commented on the game, I feel I should clarify a few things.

    1: This game is not intended as comedy, and does not seek to trivialize rape. Quite the opposite.
    2: The move is in a playbook which is chosen by a player. The group will obviously need to be consulted to make sure that everyone is okay with this. It is thus not a necessary part of the game, but a part that can be chosen by the player who specifically wants this for his or her character.

    I can still respect that you might find the game problematic. In the interest of understanding, and to do better in the future, I'd be thankful if you'd clarify your objections a bit more. Does the problem stem from:
    * including rape in a game at all,
    * using game mechanics to deal with rape, or
    * the particular way this move is structured/described?

    I will postpone the posting of the next playbook, and I would appreciate other people's input on this, not just Jason's.
