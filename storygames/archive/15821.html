\n ENWorld Chicago Gameday is a day of free gaming held at Games Plus in Mt. Prospect, IL. You can learn more at the Chicago Gameday FAQ or on our Facebook page.

We're currently in our event planning phase. The focus is RPGs, but most other games are welcome as well. If you'd like to run an event, post your idea in our event planning thread over on ENWorld:

    \n This is the official announcement about player sign-up for Gameday 31.

    * Player sign-up for Gameday 31 will start at 7:30pm on Monday, March 5th. *

    At that time, I will create a player sign-up thread on ENWorld and post the URL to the email list, on Facebook, the Gameday website, and various other social media.

    To sign up for an event, go to the sign-up thread mentioned above and post the event(s) in which you\u2019d like to participate. Sign-ups are processed in the order they are posted in the thread. It\u2019s a good idea to keep an eye on the thread in case the event you originally chose fills up before you are able to post.
