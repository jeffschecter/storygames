    \n Awesome! I'd love to hear about any game you end up running.

    Regarding having one GM: the reason there are two in the rules is that I found during playtest that games would heavily favor inside or outside action. The two GMs are there to act as advocates of their particular focus. You could do a single GM, but he would need to very carefully keep his role straight and make sure neither inside nor outside became too dominant. That can be really hard, especially when the action is starting to heat up.
