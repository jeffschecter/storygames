\n I want to take my Game Chef game, Final Hour of a Storied Age: A Roleplaying Game of Epic Fantasy, to the next level. In order to do that I need people to help me playtest it over Skype. It's a GM-less no-prep game with an interesting situation generation mechanic (inspired by In A Wicked Age, but with less pre-authored content) and an attempt at a story structure that mimics the source literature rather than a "D&D adventuring party" structure.
