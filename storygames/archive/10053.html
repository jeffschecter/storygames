\n So, the Story Jam Guidebook is moving along, and right about or a little after it hits the market I plan to simultaneously publish a few Story Kits (like Story Jam supplements, probably three to start with).

These are what I'm thinking, and I want to get this community's input.

Please tell me what interests you most and share any cool ideas you want me to consider.




Breaking News
It\u2019s 11PM, and the cameras are rolling. The pressure is on as fresh news pours in over the lines, and the city\u2019s finest news team must deliver.

Dune Sea Prophecies
Remembrance on the Sands.

Wizards\u2019 Tower
One Tower, deadly magics and every great sorcerer in the land. Who will claim the Wizards\u2019 Tower?

Red-Rimmed Eyes v2.0
A new edition of Joe McDonald\u2019s award-winning story game about a monster.

Team Patriot
You are the most awesomely epic special forces team that America has to offer. Play them to the hilt. Oh right, between their awesomely epic scenes, also narrate the ramifications of their actions.

Fiore dei Gato
A game about cats in renaissance Italy. In an age of finery and silent murders, who will be the fanciest cat?

Saint Mary\u2019s
Creeping superstition and teen drama at a private preparatory academy twenty miles north of Salem, Massachusetts. Will the children survive, or crumble?

Biosphere
An educational game about die-off, evolution, and biological technology.

Dungeonfail
A Dromedy about Gamers who just want to Roll with the Dragons.

Reich: Concerning Genocide
Why did this happen?

Reaper
A character tale of Death and those he comes for on one fateful night.

Coming of Ages
One person\u2019s life story, told through the fictions he creates from infancy to elderhood.
