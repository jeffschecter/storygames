\n Hey everyone!
\n
\nI just finished work on an online lifepath browsing tool for Burning Wheel. I designed this thing to make it easier to select lifepaths during character burning. You can browse lifepaths, add them to a temporary collection, and hover over skills/traits to instantly look up their descriptions. No more page flipping!
\n
\nHere it is: Burning Wheel Lifepath Browser
\n
\nIf you don't have the books, take a look at the Orc demo. If you do have the books, you will be asked for a username and password, based on clues from the Character Burner.
\n
\nHope you all enjoy this. In the near future, I'll be adding the Magic Burner, Monster Burner, and perhaps lifepaths from Burning Empires and the Blossoms are Falling.
\n
\n-Chris
