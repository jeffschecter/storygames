\n Until the end of the month, The Unspeakable Oath is half-price and all proceeds go to the Red Cross' Japan fund.

The Unspeakable Oath is an astonishingly well done magazine. For me, it's a model of how a modern RPG magazine can be: beautifully written, beautifully illustrated. I saw the print copy in January and was blown away.

It's a Cthulhu magazine, but even if that's not your thing, it's readable enough to be worth your while. For 5 dollars, really, it's definitely worth it, especially as the money goes to charity.
