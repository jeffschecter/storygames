\n My latest RPG-in-a-month is now released. That's not the point of this post. The point is that I find writing to such a short deadline so much of an adrenaline hit that I was wondering if anyone else would care to join me this May?

The contract is simple- 30th April, all you'll have are a few loose ideas for a game. 31st May, you'll make a complete RPG available. Any other terms are entirely up to you- a 2-page oracular game or a 120-page crunch-gasm.

Who's with me?
