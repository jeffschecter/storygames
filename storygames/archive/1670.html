    If you're on a Mac, you should have GraphicConverter. Although it is shareware, if you choose not to register it the only drawbacks are (a) having to wait 30 seconds after launching it before you can start using it, and (b) not having access to the batch filter function. The advantages? GraphicConverter opens damn near anything, converts to many other formats, has a good set of tools, and is constantly being upgraded and improved.

    I don't know anything about GIMP except for what I just read above and for a very brief glimpse at its introduction page, but I'm fairly confident that GraphicConverter would put up a strong challenge to Brendan's claim that "you won't find a better image editor" than GIMP.
