    \n I read somewhere the scenario is based on the first chapter of The Polish Officer, a novel by Alan Furst. This may be entirely inaccurate, but I hope the information helps.

    However, having read Last Train Out of Warsaw I believe it contained sufficient detail to run without further furnishings. Enacting the story is the important thing here, not the history. At least, in my opinion.
