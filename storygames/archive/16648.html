    \nBLUE O'CLOCK

    Between last call and sunrise is the time
    when customers are escorted from the bar,
    doors are locked, No Smoking laws are suspended,
    and staffers breathe a sigh of relief.
    The barback hasn't cased the empties.
    The soundman hasn't stowed the gear.
    And they won't until the azure light on pre-dawn streets
    announces that it's Blue O'Clock.
    If you liked Parking Lot The Movie
    or Marvel's The Avengers' 'Schwarma' scene,
    you're ready for this.
