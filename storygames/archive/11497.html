\n So, I've been working on a game called Heart of Ashes.
If you've missed every single pitch I've ever made:

Heart of Ashes:
You're kids, and you stumble into another world, where you can do magic. Only, most of the people there can't do magic - they lost that power long ago. And so you're too useful to let slip, and the various adults and their various factions scramble to recruit you to their causes. Because a war is brewing, and the times are not what they used to be. Heart of Ashes is a roleplaying game about adventure, danger and making hard decisions. It's also a game about kids and adults.

Inspired by: Pan's Labyrinth, Coraline, Harry Potter, Ink, Through the Looking Glass, The City of Lost Children, His Dark Materials, Disney's Robin Hood.

Anyways, I have an artist who's hella enthusiastic on working on the project with me.
I have put together a pretty detailed style guide, and wanted to share it with all of you: http://joepub.fileave.com/Heart%20of%20Ashes%20SG.pdf

I don't know how to write/compose style guides, and I didn't really research it, I just made some assumptions about what was necessary and included that.
So! If you want to criticize what I've included or omitted, please do so.
If you want to suggest other artists/pieces that I might want to include later on, please do so.
If you want to ask questions about the game, please do so!
