    \n Here's how I see this working. Any advice or suggestions are welcome.

    * The context will kick off Sunday night. Graham and I are working out ingredients. The requirements will be broadly similar to Game Chefs.

    * The contest will run for twn days, finishing Wednesday the 24th. Everyone mails me their entries.

    * I missed the feedback element of Game Chef in Sight & Sound. So I'd suggest everyone reviews one design each. I like the conversation and connections feedback provides. So the reviews would go up here. This should be done by Hallowe'en.

    * Once all the reviews are up, every participant should read every review. I'm figuring we'll get a dozen entries, so this won't be too onerous.

    * Then every participant gets, say, three votes to cast on the games.

    I'd also like to see, as Joe P suggested above, an aspect of actual play or playtesting. How can this be achieved?
