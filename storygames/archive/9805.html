\n Here is the rough draft:
Wanderer

So keeping with some advice I have internal deadlines only. After I go through this copy myself once more it's off to the kind folks who are volunteering to proofread. It's all OGL. Once the rest of the books are done I am going to put together a limited number of box sets (yippie box sets) to hand out. The mechanics are solid. It's a complete rewrite of the first Wanderer I posted awhile back. It uses the Traveller SRD but stripped down to it's basics to it resembles the LBBs.
