\n The best thing I did, at GenCon, was decide to be a retailer. So, on Sunday, I went round looking for products I wanted to sell when I returned to the UK.

After a few hours, I'd purchased three roleplaying games, two game accessories and one comic book. (Actually, the comic book was sold out, but I'll order some more when she gets them reprinted).

My favourite thing at the convention, predictably, was Apocalypse World. I bought 10 copies. If you'd like to buy one, they're $28. If you're in the UK, I can ship it to you for $3. If you're in Europe, I can do it for $5. Or, if you're likely to see me at a convention, you can collect it from me and save the shipping.

(If you're in the US, buy the book directly from Vincent instead).

This is the GenCon special of Apocalypse World, which comes with a special extra character class, The Faceless. I'll try to get Vincent to sign them, too. (I'm in Western MA at the moment, so he's not too far away.)

Here's a big picture. The book itself is shinier than this.



If you'd like one, go to my website (whimsically called Thieves of Time) and click on the obvious stuff.

(And I'll put the other products on the website shortly. I'm very excited about them.)
