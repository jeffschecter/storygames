\n Hey guys,

Today I'm launching the beta playtest for my new game, Unity Underground. It's a traditional action/adventure rpg set in a near future dystopia. Sort of like D&D if D&D was inspired by dystopian fiction rather than pulp fantasy. It's also strongly influenced by my early days playing Rifts. I like to think of it as Rifts for the first-person-shooter generation. In a lot of ways it's the spiritual successor to Empire of Dust.

Anyway, I'd love to hear what you make of it. If you're interested in checking out the beta document, you can join the Red Moon Medicine Show google group or email me personally at redmoonmedicineshow at gmail dot com.
