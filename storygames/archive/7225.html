\n That line never gets old!

I've been in a playtesting frenzy lately and last night we had a fun and productive session. The melodrama was turned way up and we had a great time chewing the scenery.

Our staff:

Dr. Byron Regal, the pompous and degenerate Chief of Surgery at Sweet Lady of Mercy Hospital!
Dr. Milly LaGreco, ambitious and sexy surgeon eager to knock Dr. Regal off his pedestal!
Dr. Magnus Crisp, brilliant, obsessed, and quadriplegic!
Dr. Dirk Blackledge, young, beautiful, and determined to find his wife's killer!

There was a fire in the OR, the previous Chief's mysterious walk-in humidor, an evil twin disguised by cosmetic surgery, and the usual flurry of machinations and treachery. Dr. Regal saved a cheerleader's life with a well-timed lobectomy, but lost another patient on the table in an abdominal operation gone wrong. LaGreco rocked a stereotactic ablation of a young boy's brain tumors. Blackledge performed a textbook lymphectomy, and Crisp - well, Crisp got the dirty job.

I am really happy with the development of this game and can't wait to show it off.
