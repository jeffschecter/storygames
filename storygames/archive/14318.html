    \n I designed one once, put it on the Forge, and several groups played it. Turns out that the adversarial parts take over, leaving RPing behind. That's because you can only base PvP on mechanics; if you try to bring in fictional positioning, it devolves into disputes (that's my experience with "freeform" chat-based combat), and without positioning the mechanics and the fiction are not connected enough.
