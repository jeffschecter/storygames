In the vein of The Power 19, the The Six-Bang, and The Talky N, I have created my own very refined list of exactly one question for would-be game designers. This single question is intended to get to the heart of matters in the quickest way possible with a minimum of fuss for all involved.

The question is, in its full glory:

                    "What the hell do you want from me?"
