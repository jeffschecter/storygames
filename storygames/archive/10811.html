\n The Purple Pawn blog posted this video of a Bakugan training event held in a mall. With all the talk about video training demos for indie games, this seemed particularly relevant. Seeing this, I could imagine a bizarre parallel universe where people with head mics, trained staff, cool toys and prizes were holding training sessions for Princes' Kingdom or Mouse Guard. :P

Granted, it's easier to teach a card/action figure game with an animated series and a devoted cult following among kids, but it's fun to imagine.
