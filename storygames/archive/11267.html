\nBut I thought about this: the Ophirian Rogue needs the help of the Nordheimer Axe-wielder in order to sneak inside the Temple Area, without getting hurt by the Darfarite Cannibals (kept at bay by the Nordheimer).
\nThat would mean that the Temple (outside/inside whatever) is "occupied" by Darfarite Cannibals so that the Rogue must be Supported by the Barbarian to get there.
\nSpecialists have to follow their err... specific role: the Occupied Temple is a Sneak-Inside Area for the Thief, the Vacant Temple is a Go-Thru Area for Anybody and the Occupied Tribe Lands make a Fight-Thru Area for the Fighter-Type.
\n
\nEdit: there's a way to treason. So it could be a mix of cooperative-antagonistic behaviour \xe0 la Highlander or House of the Blooded.

My own impression is that this might be because ultimately you're going to have to choose whether you're going to respect the essentially wargamey setup of Diplomacy, or your own creative aspirations as a roleplayer. It'd be interesting to see the two harmonized, certainly.
