    \n I was hanging out at Gencon with Klaus Scherwinksi, Franz Vohwinkel, and Imelda Vohwinkel (all German). We were in the art show talking when Klaus looked up and saw the Reaper banner for

    REICH OF THE DEAD

    complete with faux-swastika and all the accoutrement. Now. Thanks to Indiana Jones 3, all Americans view Nazi-related source material in our entertainment as "wacky bad guys." So. To us, the banner isn't shocking at all.

    But to Germans raised on the atrocities of a previous generation, the banner was apocryphal. To say the least.

    We had a good chuckle once the original shock had worn off. But it was interesting to hear their take on anything "Nazi."
