\n Hi guys! This month I'm really excited to be adding 3 brand new games to the Cel*Style label!

The Tulip Academy's Society for Dangerous Gentlemen lets you join the most handsome young men in the world on their ridiculously daring adventures! It's a cross between Ouron High School Host Club and Cassanova, with mechanics centered around the three pillars of a classical education: Art, Science and fencing! This is by nick Smith (who also co-wrote Sea Dracula), with full color art by Kayla Mayer!

G X B (Girl X Boy) is a shoujo dating game designed with brand new players in mind! It's cute, fun and made to play in just 2-4 hours using simple card mechanics, pre-made characters and a rotating narrator role. Heather Aplington supplied all the art, including the 3 pages of instructional manga that help you learn how to play the game!


Panty Explosion Perfect is a complete brand new edition of the original psychic schoolgirl game, rebuilt from the ground op to be 100% better than the original. It's full color and packed full of instructional illustrations and manga. Panty Explosion Perfect is totally awesome and you're going to love it, especially if you liked the original game!

All three books are available for pre-order now at the Cel*Style store and will be shipping after the first of May. Pre-order each book individually, or save $7 and get all three as part of the Cel*Style pre-order bundle

    \n For me this was pretty much my dream character sheet. I'm not a character sheet guy either. I'm not good at designing them and i'm always the guy that shows up without his. So I wanted to make a character sheet that was really cool looking but was also useful:



    It\u2019s postcard size, and the back has a little rules summary. As you can see, the front iS full of helpful stuff, including:

    - Your Student\u2019s name goes in the word balloon. Which I think is really cool!

    - Mark your Student\u2019s Popularity by checking a box. All three Popularity ranks (and their rules) are listed here, both because your Popularity will sometimes change during a session and because it\u2019s just good to know what everyone has to roll.

    - Write down the names of Your Best Friend and Rival. There\u2019s a description of what their job is, which is handy since you\u2019re also someones Best Friend and Rival!

    - Write down both of your Details. In case you\u2019ve forgotten, the Student Record is ready to remind you that Details let you add +1 to your rolls during Challenges.

    - There\u2019s plenty of space to add your Goal as well.

    - And finally, a check box for when you decide to reveal your psychic powers. Until that time the box will stay checked \u201cNo\u201d.

    There's a few versions of the cards. The one above is the generic student, and there's a blank version as well. There's also a pack of four pre-made characters (like Hiromi from a few posts up) that I've been handing out at conventions.
