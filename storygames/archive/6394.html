\n I'm moderating a big pan - San Francisco Bay Area game of Bliss Stage, Ben Lehman's game about teenagers experiencing love, war, and giant robots for the very first time (with next to no adult supervision)!

RIGHT NOW, the moment you are reading this, all but the entire adult population of the world is falling into the eternal sleep known as the Bliss.
Two years from now, the aliens responsible for the Bliss will chase the child survivors of that war down with robots spewing searing rays and noxious smokes.
Five years from now, resistance groups led by surviving adults will defeat an alien drone and reverse engineer it into a weapon against the aliens.
Seven years later, you will strike back.

THIS IS HOW.

Players will be teenaged mecha Pilots and the lovers that Anchor them to reality, using the bizzare Alien Numina Inversion Machines to project themselves into a dreamworld and form giant robots to defeat the alien menace! The A.N.I.Ma turns intimate relationships, which can be either love or hatred, into weapons and armor against the alien threat. But beware... these weapons are only as tough as your trust in those relationships, trust that can be shattered by alien attack.

GM's will need a copy of Bliss Stage to play, available at Indie Press Revolution as the Interim Stage pdf. x Then, look at community.livejournal.com/bay_bliss_stage for the submission guidelines for Authority Figures, Pilots, Anchors and other characters.

The Telegraph by West MacArthur Resistance is 5 Pilots and 5 Anchors strong - but will it be enough to defeat these alien nightmares? Join the Bay Area Resistance and rep your part of the bay, and we can drive off this menace for good... and have a lot of fun telling powerful stories while we're at it!

I hope to see you there.
