\n Hey everybody, I'm new around here.
\n
\nSo, as a big fan of Apocalypse World and several shows of the modern monster-hunting type, I was extremely pleased to read about Monster of the Week. So pleased, in fact, that I emailed Mr. Sands about getting my hands on the playtest version a while ago. I played it and loved it (expect an Actual Play post at some point in the future). When I found out about the Indiegogo drive, however, I was quite disappointed to find myself unable to back it at that time, which also left me somewhat LE playbook-less. But! I then remembered that the Playtest version came with two LE playbooks of its own: the Divine (which I think some backers already received) and the Camera (a reporter-type hunter, looking to get rich and famous off of the discovery of monsters, which I didn't see offered elsewhere). I'm willing to hand out the ones I have, but let's make this just a little bit more interesting...
\n
\nI will offer the Camera or the Divine (your choice) to anyone who does one of the following:
\n-Trades me the Hard Case, Summoned, or Snoop.
\n-Posts a picture to this thread, specifically a snapshot of some strange, supernatural, cryptozoological, or inexplicable phenomenon. Obvious fakes are completely acceptable for these purposes, but it must be a picture that you took personally.
\n
\nThanks!
