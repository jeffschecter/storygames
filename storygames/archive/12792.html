\n The first in the Parsely microgreens series is online!
FLAMING GOAT!

Microgreens are small (1-3 room) Parsely games, usually based around one puzzle, and are meant to be played in under ten minutes.

    Posted By: Jared A. Sorensen#6 is tentatively titled "STEAM RACE!"
    \nI'd love to see this series play around with sizes and folds. For example, what about doing this with an accordion fold and then partially cut the paper twice. This will create three accordions attached along one fold like a giant folded "E" - one accordion pull-out for each vehicle. That way, you first decide which vehicle and pull the appropriate accordion.

    I could see doing a large one that is newspaper themed (perhaps an intrepid young investigative journalist and her hard-boiled detective...). It would be fun to have a newspaper sized Parsely game to sit behind with the game on one side and a giant Parsely emblem or fake newspaper on the other . Alternately, a Parsley Poster could work (for a mega dungeon/piece of art). Finally, I could also see doing a long dungeon using an accordion fold where you glue the first and last faces together to make a Rolodex strip that you flip around (that way you could play in a car/plane w/o having a giant sheet). Alternately, Return to Space Station could be a long sheet that you glue to make a mobius strip that you side-scroll through.
