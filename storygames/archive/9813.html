    \n Owlbears generally hate water. Being landlocked should increase the likelihood of a Nerdly event near you.

    If you live in Ohio, and still have some soul left, you can find your inner owlbear and organize one yourself! There are some awesome campgrounds in southern Ohio, I think. There's also YMCA Camp Fitch up on Lake Erie, in Springfield, PA (basically northeast Ohio, but just over the border). I went to camp there as a kid, whee, so I know it has large cabins and a decent mess hall. There's also the site that SCA uses for Pennsic, just east of Youngstown. I don't think they have cabins, so it'd be a roughin'-it camping experience, which might be challenging.
