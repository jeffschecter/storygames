    Guy,

    I believe the tickets for events go on sale at a later date, so no True Dungeon worries yet.     When searching for rooms this year, I could only get a Westin room if I booked on the 16th or later. Considering that I'm flying in on the 15th, this was a problem. So I booked for the 16th, and booked the 15th at another hotel -- second year I've had to do that. It's probably a bit late to give that bit of advice for this year, but it's not as bad of a hassle as it might sound.

    Here is some handy advice that I discovered last year -- if you arrive earlier than check-in, you can still store your bags with the hotel. In doing so last year, I started by saying "It's probably too early for check-in, but..." and was stopped with "Perhaps not, let's take a look." In short, by round-about asking, I was able to check into my hotel at 9am. Hopefully that knowledge might help someone else out.
