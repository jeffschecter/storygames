\n Session one of Monsterhearts: we meet our characters, Seth the gypsy Serpentine, Daniel the shy outsider Ghost, Logan the fixer Infernal (who didn't use any bargains, boo, hiss), Emily the undateable Chosen, Marcell the damn sexy Vampire that everyone wants to go to Homecoming with, and Zola, the murdered Ghoul. Who will take whom to Homecoming? Will that snotty cheerleader Mindy get what's coming to her? What will the new coach gym teacher, serial killer and rapist Randall Flowers bode for Redbank High? What's going on with that creepy curio shop, Something for Everyone? What foul menace does the Lord of the Slime plan?

    \n The Lord of the Slime is the Chosen's nemesis. I asked Shari (the Chosen's player) what she wanted, and she said 'some sort of demon or devil' thing. The Lord of the Slime is intentionally not the same as the Infernal's patron- this way he has two potential 'bigger, nastier things' to turn to- the Lord of the Slime, and Winston Q. Black, proprietor of Something for Everyone.

    Playing with six players means there's a lot of juggling. I ran an Apocalypse World game for *eight* players, which was insane, but I do like getting to have massive plots and zaniness. Tim's Serpentine, Seth, didn't seem to get a lot of screen time, his character being an outsider and not really socially assertive, but we found out he has a goth senior tutor, Roxanne, who has purple hair and a boyfriend with a totally cool motorcycle, and she likes his writing but laughed when he asked her to Homecoming. There will be vengeance for that! Also, creepy inbred gypsy dragon trailer park.
