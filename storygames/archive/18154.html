    John Harper's Lady Blackbird is what opened my eyes to games that don't have to be a textbook covering every single tiny little element of play. From there, I was much more open to non-traditional games.
    \nA couple of years ago I was asked to run a game for the bar staff who'd been working at the site of a big UK Con for the previous 5-6 years or so and were curious about what gaming was all about. I chose Lady Blackbird (partly because nothing I had with me to run fitted and I was asked after arriving on site) and it went very well indeed. I'd happily run it for novices again.
