\n Howdy All,

Now that KublaCon is over, I wanted to remind folks that there will be a story games room at the upcoming ConQuest SF. The room is being sponsored by EndGame, and I'm coordinating its, er, stocking on their behalf.

I'm looking to fill 4 and 6 hours slots. There would be three games going on in the room, or at least that's the plan.

The slots are:

Friday
2 pm - 8 pm / 4 pm - 8 pm
8 pm - 12 am

Saturday
8 am - 2 pm / 8 am - 12 pm
2 pm - 8 pm / 12 pm - 4 pm and 4 pm / 8 pm
8 pm - 12 am

Sunday
8 am - 2 pm / 8 am - 12 pm
2 pm - 8 pm / 12 pm - 4 pm and 4 pm / 8 pm
8 pm - 12 am

Monday
8 am - 2 pm / 8 am - 12 pm

Running a single 6 hr timeslot or two 4 hr slots gets you free admission to the 'Con.

Folks can contact me directly to coordinate their events. I'm happy to take a generic sign-up to reserve the slot, and a fuller game description can be provided a little later.

I'd love to get a wide variety of games booked. If we can get some GMs signed up early, we can do some early publicity. It also helps folks like EndGame and others stock, as games which are demo'd tend to move a little more.

Additionally, support materials from any publishers out there would be appreciated. Anything from fliers through schwag. Podcast interviews and seminars are also options for designers / publishers as well.

Feel free to drop me a line at brian -dot- isikoff -at- gmail.com.

Thanks all!

Brian
