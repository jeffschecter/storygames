Excitement. Adventure. Disco. (Leisure suit not included)

Spectrum Games is proud to announce the release of the Lightspeed Adventure Manual (LAM), a \u201cpay what you want\u201d PDF that offers a stripped-down, but fully playable, version of the Retrostar system. Retrostar is a roleplaying game that faithfully emulates the science fiction TV shows of the 1970s.

Lead designer Barak Blackburn says, \u201cThis isn't your father's ironic, polyester game system. Retrostar has been crafted to be a fully-integrated game designed specifically for 1970s sci-fi, though, Spectrum is looking into adapting it for some other genres. Inside, you will not find a generic sci-fi system, instead you will find a game that captures all the coolest moments from favorites like Battlestar Galactica, The Six Million Dollar Man, Land of the Lost and every other uniquely groovy sci-fi TV series from the far-out past!\u201d

In addition to all the rules needed to play, the LAM contains a ready-to-play series, complete with setting information, pre-generated characters and a full episode to kick off your adventures with! The series is called R\u014dgun. In this setting, the earth has been ravaged, her resources depleted. Noble warriors defend a mobile, solar-powered fortress, traveling from town to town. These rollerskating Samouri stand against the Warriors of the Night and cybornetic Scroungers.

Despite the fact that the LAM contains all the rules you\u2019ll need in order to play Retrostar, it is not the full rulebook. It is a quick-play PDF designed to let new players jump right into the action and, as such, is available for absolutely no cost to you. However, we have made it a \u201cpay what you want\u201d product, with any money paid going toward increasing the production values of the full rulebook.

You can download the LAM here: http://rpg.drivethrustuff.com/product/141873/Retrostar--Lightspeed-Adventure-Manual-QuickPlay
