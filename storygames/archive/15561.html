\n Coming of Age is a quick, easy-to-learn, and uplifting tale-telling game for 1 to 6 (or maybe more) players. In Coming of Age you play, in the course of 2-3 hours, how your characters discover their potential and learn to be more than who they thought they could be. It comes with setting sheets (not unlike Fiasco's playsets), including Space Police Cadets, Squires of Sword and Sorcery, and Scions of the Superspy.

I've finished the design and development, and I'm in the midst of planning out the publication. If you'd like to try out this game just whisper me your email and I'll send you a copy.

- Mendel
