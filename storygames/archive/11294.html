Hi there,

I'd like to run a playtest of my game Delve at Dreamation. I'm prepared to tell Vinny that his "must run it 3 times" rule doesn't apply to my non-famous game and my non-famous self. However, before I pick that fight, I figure I'll inquire if anyone is in the mood to try running a trad-style game they've never played before.

I'll give you a bunch of scenario elements and the backbone of a scenario. Once you get some feedback from your players at the beginning of the session, you'll have to assign particular elements onto the backbone to create the final scenario. I find this easy, but others may not.

The game is devoid of fiddly mechanics to learn*. Running it is mostly about (a) imparting enough info to propel the PCs from one task to the next, and (b) remembering to evolve the scenario according to the Steps of Doom I provide. Plus, color is key -- if you're good at playing NPCs and/or describing environs, that will add a lot.

I'll happily try to return the favor in whatever way works best for you. I am generally happy to playtest stuff.

Ps,
-David

*Each combat will require a one-time calculation off to-hit target #s. That's as heavy as it gets.
