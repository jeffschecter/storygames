    \n Interesting. I'm looking into both of those suggestions.

    Jason, The Roach and Fiasco are probably my favorite one-shot games I've ever played. Because of those games, I always think GM-less when I imagine a one-shot. My first thought was, "How could I make Attack the Block a Fiasco playset?" But then I realized that conflict within the group of main characters is not really part of ATB... unless the players can be the drug kingpin guy, and the nurse, and... not sure. Still, it seems like the alien invasion, coming from outside, goes against the spirit of Fiasco, where the characters are generally the source of the crises.
