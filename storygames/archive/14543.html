Andy has been nice enough to give us a new category.

\n

Make Stuff! is for threads when we ask anybody and everybody to create.

\n

Some examples are:

\n
    \n
  • Ogre's Describe an image, ask a question
    • \n
  • Mcdaldno's Help! Rat-men are invading.
    • \n
  • Alan's Help me create a Conan Oracle
    • \n
\n

If you know an old thread that should be filed under Make Stuff! please whisper me with a link to that thread and I will get it re-categorized.

\n

Note: Make Stuff! is not the place for "here's this rule/supplement I made, please critique it!" - that's still Game Design Help.
\nNote 2: same goes for "I wrote this game, come play it!" - that's still Directed Promotion.
