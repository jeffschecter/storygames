\n What's that smell? Is that the smell of a smoldering playtest? Why yes it is.

Kingdom just started its fourth and (hopefully) final playtest round. There was a thread here for the first playtest round a ways back, but if anyone new wants to jump in and participate now's the time to stand up and be counted.

Fresh eyes are particularly useful. Speaking from my own experience, when you've played multiple versions of the rules it's hard to put the changes out of mind and just play the game as it is now.

Seventy-seven people have playtested it so far and while that's a mystically charming number I think it could be higher. I like to playtest until the rules melt from furious fun. As always I'm looking for people who can play the game not just read it. If you're interested you can post here or email kingdom-playtest at lamemage.com.
