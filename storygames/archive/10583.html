\n Well, it's always technically been available in print via my lulu store. But it is now available for direct purchase from the Unstore for 12.00 + shipping and handling. It will be up on IPR as well within a couple of weeks.

Quick overview: the game is a fairy tale fantasy played on a chessboard. Your Lady, through a series of waltz's, will pick one of the Lords of Ice to marry - but is he your True Love? More info Dev's site. Questions and comments on this thread are always welcome, of course.

I will have a print + PDF option up fairly soon, probably for 14.00, in case that's a consideration for some.
