\n I'm going to post here about the world creation (and later, the AP vignettes) for the first game of Misspent Youth that I'm going to run as an entire series. I'm pretty psyched about this. In the group are Jer, Phil, Rachel, and me. I'm playing The Authority. Here's the world document:

The Authority:

The Shepherd: Protects humanity from Infiltrator aliens. The Shepherd is a single alien entity, and people talk about it but think it's a conspiracy theory. It's the last of its race and the city that the colonists live in on this alien planet is a city of its people. The Infiltrators are the species native to the world who are able to make themselves look like anyone.

Vice: Fear
Visage: Personal
Victim: Humanity
Need: Wipe out the infiltrators and make humanity love him.

World:

Rating: R

Themes: Sex, Violence, Class

Motifs:

\u2022\tThe Shepherd can communicate instantaneously everywhere in the colony. (Auth)
\u2022\tThe Shepherd controls access to the only definitely-safe food sources. (Auth)
\u2022\tSafe travel throughout the colony is restricted to a rail system with easily-controllable choke points. The Shepherd has many agents at such locations. (Auth)
\u2022\tAn Infiltrator faction wants to ally with humanity. (YO)

Clique: The night shift at a "burger" joint.
