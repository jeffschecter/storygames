    \n I've got the ashcan and have played it a couple of times. Each player plays the captain of their own ship, plus one crewmember on each other ship. You rotate ships, as you all try to grapple with the terrible scourge that threatnes the galaxy: visiting planets, becoming entangled with their affairs, and uncovering information and resources to help battle scourge. There was a lot of coolness in it. There were also some issues, which you would expect from an ashcan.
