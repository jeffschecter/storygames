\nHere's some work I've done on the setting for Scion.
\n
\nEverything on the main page with an asterisk after the name is something I haven't finished yet.
\n
\nThe stuff under Prelude is the vague plan for the campaign I'll be starting some time in the next few weeks. In keeping with the lower key nature, PCs will start off as mortals, but rise to greater stature as their ties to the Gods grow.
\n
\nIt starts with the Amnesiacs with character sheets idea I bantered around here for a while, and then proceeds to the protection and media-whoring of Paris Hilton.
\n
\nThere's more work to do still, but some of it will have to wait until I know what the PCs are going to be.
\n
\nThanks to everyone that's chipped in ideas to make this possible! Keep 'em coming if you think of something.
