    \n There are a lot! They also use cards in very different ways from each other (what The Quiet Year does with them does not at all resemble what Primetime Adventures does with them).

    Am I the first to say Murderous Ghosts? (Now also available in a new "party game" revision. Anybody has a link to the original 2-player version?)

    Questlandia is one of many games that use playing cards in an ancillary role to dice - in this case, for character creation.
