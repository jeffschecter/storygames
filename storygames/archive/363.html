Hi!

I'm in charge of the Indie RPG Explosion at GenCon this year. The Explosion is an organized group of scheduled, ticketed events, focused on independent RPGs, run in the convention gaming area. We had a good start last year, and are hoping to offer more games this year, along with the debut of Games On Demand--helping indie players and indie GMs find each other.

If you'd like to run some cool indie games, meet new people, and possibly get your GenCon badge paid for, come join us. I'm doing most of the organizing on this thread on the Forge, but I'm also glad to take questions here.
