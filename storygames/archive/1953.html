[Grey Ranks] Radio Lightning

Jason_Morningstar
December 2006 edited December 2006 in Play Advice
So I just had this really fun epiphany that I want to share with y'all:

Prior to each of nine scenes in a game of Grey Ranks I'd always imagined the group reading this one-sheet with some background on what was going on. It would provide a little context, some interesting facts, and get the pump primed for the scene. In playtest it was a little dry. So I was trying to think up a way to make what amounts to a spoken word information dump sexy and exciting. Then I start listening to Troy Holaday's little OTR podcast and it hits me - radio. The Home Army had a radio station operating in Warsaw throughout the Rising. So I'm going to provide a frickin' script! Like this:

SCENE SIX: MONDAY, 7 AUGUST. GERMAN TANKS AGAINST HOME-MADE RIFLES.

"War news from Radio Lightning follows. Radio Moscow reports that the Russians have advanced into the foothills of the Carpathian Mountains, near the Slovak, Hungarian, Russian borders. There is no movement in central Poland.

A message from BÃ³r, the Commander-in-Chief; I read his dispatch verbatim: Poles! The armed struggle for the liberation of the capital has begun. Three days of battle with the occupying forces have brought us great tactical and moral successes. The forces of the Home Army have conquered the larger part of the capital, broken the enemy's resistance, and at the same time set free in every Polish heart enthusiasm for the struggle and faith in its success. All workers, peasants, laborers and the intelligentsia alike are fighting together for a democratic Poland, a Poland of social justice; a Poland of the people. Long Live Independent Poland!

More news from the streets: Here in Warsaw, the German forces have brutally murdered wounded and sick people both men and women who were lying in St. Lazarus Hospital in Wolska Street Number 18, and Karol and Maria Hospital in Przejazd Street. On Jerusalem Avenue yesterday, when the Germans were bringing supplies by tank to one of their outposts, they drove 500 women and children before them to prevent the troops of Home Army from taking action against them. Many of them were killed and wounded. The same kind of action has been reported from many other parts of the city. One tank was destroyed by means of petrol bottles owing to lack of anti-tank guns. Warsaw has excellent chances and the greatest part of the City is in Polish hands. To our listeners in liberated Europe and across the English Channel - please send us weapons and ammunition as soon as possible."

I think one player will be assigned announcer duties for the whole game.
