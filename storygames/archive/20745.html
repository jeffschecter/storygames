\n i didn't win the popularity contest, so. this game is going to struggle on kickstarter for the next 30 days. it is, however, completed. and really good. but. i'm not a celebrity like some people. so. you'll probably hear about this once. and never again. you'll forget about it. and move on to something popular. which makes sense. quality can only be found in the hands of people who are well-known.

like kanye west.

and scott baio.

so. check it out. or don't. it won't matter. it's a solid new way to play gmless games (with five finished games in the system). but it's likely none of your friends have heard of it. so. neither will you.

thanks for your time.

https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/218255739/king-of-storms
