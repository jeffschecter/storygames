\n And a fine afternoon to you folks!

We are Beryllium Productions, just your standard group of gamers turned webseries producers. You know how it goes.

Longtime Lurkers, and Lifetime gamers, we wanted to mention our premier Webseries, The Nighthawks to you folks here.

The Nighthawks are a group of gamers, playing in a Steampunk RPG. We'll follow not only their real world adventures as gamers, but also their in-game adventures as their characters. It's half sitcom, half adventure, with years of gaming experience to draw on.

We hope you folks will check us out; the Indiegogo is just to defray costs on the pilot, (maybe pay some people), which is being shot at the end of this month. Otherwise, keep an eye out for our pilot to launch later this summer! If you have any questions of a Non-Spoilery nature, feel free to ask, I'll answer as best I can!

Click here to see our Indiegogo!

Thanks for your time!
Kevin, Director of Beryllium Productions.
