Hey, maybe some of you have noticed that there's this new topic here called "Game Design Help".

Basically, I don't want S-G to turn into a design journal, a collection of boisterous folks using this forum as their personal design blog. Also, I want people to come here and talk about gaming first and look for game design help second; not to come here looking to recreate The Forge in these forums.

However, I totally don't want to put a lockdown on the folks that DO happen to be working on games and stuff, and need some solid advice. Some really cool stuff has come out of these boards out of collaborative help from the members (RPG ideas, LARP setups, etc) that wouldn't have emerged if I shut down all gaming-related discussions.

So I decided to finally create this topic category. It's for folks that are actively working on stuff, and want/need help, or a push in some direction. It's for folks who need some solid feedback, and will act on it. Doesn't matter where in the game you are, you can feel free to post questions/help requests here.

Little Ideas will still remain for awesome bursts of interesting ideas that you happen to have, be it about a game design, gaming in general, playstyles, etc.

Finally, this topic category is "Hidden" to outsiders. Only S-G registered members can see discussions under this this Category and respond to it (like the Little Ideas, Meetups, Links to Gaming Discussion categories). It's to keep the ideas in kind of a "safe zone" where they're safe from outside, until you're ready to share them.

Thanks!

-Andy
