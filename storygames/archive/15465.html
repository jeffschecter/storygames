    \n One of the great things about Fiasco is that you can use it for just about anything as long as the basic pattern of spiraling badness and final collapse is applicable. Steve's got it right: as long as expectations are properly lined up, you're golden.
    \n
    \nEvery Fiasco playset includes a "Movie Night" list. If the sorts of Fiascos you're interested in are like 21 Grams in terms of tone and theme, taking the idea of Movie Night literally would be a good idea. If your group responds well to poignant and melancholy cinema, it should translate just fine to Fiasco.
