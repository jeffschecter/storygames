    \n Even as a long time Gloranthaphile and I found HeroQuest magic a turd in the punchbowl so to speak. Too much of Greg Stafford's emerging theory of Gloranthan Magic and Philosophy and not enough straight forward yet powerful magic system. In HQ 2 there's straightforward advice on how to run a whole bunch of powers and Gloranthan magic (which is also much simpler) is split into a separate appendix.

    Mythic Russia has a much better implementation of HQ system (or HQ1.5) integrated with a lovely setting. I'd recommend any fan of medieval history and fairy tale whimsy check it out.

    Saying that Graham's Wordplay is the Win. Lots of fun with piles of D6s. Its flexible and fast, but has enough sense to nail down some of the areas HQ 1 left flapping in the breeze, such as the outcomes of some of the contests. I'm writing one of the themes for it, Infinite War, I'm so tickled by it.
