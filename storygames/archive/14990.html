\n Hey Folks,

The next JiffyCon is coming in western Massachusetts on Saturday September 17. We should be all cleaned up from the flooding damage that Irene brought.

We'd love to see you there, and have got some great games lined up:

3:16 Carnage Amongst the Stars
Misspent Youth
Tales of the Fishermans' Wife
Swashbucklers of the Seven Skies
Battle School (an Ender's Game version of 1001 Nights)
Freemarket
Psi Run

We'll have open board gaming, and a Board Game Library.

Register by emailing us at jiffycon[at]gmail.com with the information listed here. And if you'd like to run something, the deadline to submit a game is Sept 7.

Best,
Emily

    \n The Not-a-JiffyCon no-frills playdate will be held at Modern Myths on September 17th starting at 11 a.m. (when the store opens). If the attic space on the 2nd floor isn't renovated, then the landlord has agreed to let us use an alternate venue for free.

    So, what does "no frills" mean?

    Well, as opposed to the normal JiffyCons, we will not:

    * be charging an entrance fee.
    * formally organizing GMs into events. Sign-up sheets might happen
    * pre-determining slots beforehand. We'll do that on-site, eyeballing the length of games, etc.
    * providing childcare (though we wish we could!)
    * providing snacks beyond rudimentary offerings from that of generous private donors (i.e. everybody who comes)
    * organizing other outside fun things such as a banquet, a designer's breakfast
    * having vendors present besides, of course, Modern Myths

    We're talking about a space, some tables, some chairs, some great people and some great games, all for the cost of just showing up on Saturday.

    The next official JiffyCon is, as stated earlier, postponed until next year, when a suitable regular venue presents itself to the organizing committee.

    Hope people from Story Games have had patience with us and can make it!
