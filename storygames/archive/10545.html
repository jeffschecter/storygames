\n Inspired by the Genre-Fiction Generator, I made an origin generator for Do. If you take out some of the specific parts of the setting and the mention of the Flying Temple, it might work for any young hero.



Wanna help me come up with new bits for it? I need more ideas for the origins of young heroes in coming-of-age adventure stories.
