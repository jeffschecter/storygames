    \n Well, i gotta say that i hope you (we, really) all DO get down to it in the next couple of months - i've got other exciting stuff on my plate too, but i have that familiar feeling... "This thing i made has so much potential. It's not fully realized, but i could really make it sing with a little more time..."

    how about, "i will not abandon you, game chef entry."

    as for me, I'm gonna play silver & white in a little while, hopefully get some good plays with it locally and then play a few games in seattle or portland early in the year. If you're in one of those parts and want to get in on it, whisper me & we'll make it real. After that, it's re-writing time!
