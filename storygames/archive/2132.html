Building Alternate Earths

tony_dowler
January 2007 edited January 2007 in Story Games
I ran across this site today that I thought some story-gamers might kvell to. This is the site of an artist who designs alternate Earths and life-supporting planets, maps them, then sculpts globes of them. The planets are designed in considerable science-style detail and include: tilted Earth with the poles in the seas, tilted Earth with the poles on land, inside-out Earth, Earth where aliens stole 90% of our water, terraformed Earth and Mars, and a few even stranger things. There's plenty of space for awesome-i-fying and brain stimulating here (along with a health dose of "wtf?").

Be warned that if you wander away from the planets, you may find disturbing images on this site.
