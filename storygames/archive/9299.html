\n This morning I was in my car listening to Theory From the Closet, the one where Clyde talks with Mike Mearls about D&D 4E. My three-year-old son is sitting in the back, and suddenly says "Is that Tony?"
"Who is Tony?" I said
"The guy talking! It's Tony *unitelligible*!"
"Tony?"
"You know! Iron Man!"

So there you have it. Clyde Rhoer is actually millionaire Tony Stark, otherwise known as Iron Man. And my son figured it out.

Makes me wonder what other supers are lurking on these boards...
