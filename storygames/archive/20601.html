\n Greetings Citizen! You have sufficient clearance level to listen to You Don't Meet In An Inn!

Today we begin playing West End Game's Paranoia Fifth Edition. Unfortunately this edition is no longer available for purchase online. Mongoose Publishing, who currently hold the Paranoia license, had a recent successful Kickstarter to make a new edition!

Our \u201cheroes\u201d this week have been assigned by Friend Computer to retrieve and deliver NutriLiquid 1000-1-50-10 to a Yellow clearance citizen. Their personal ambitions quickly disrupt the mission's efficiency.

You can find the podcast on itunes here, on not-itunes here, and our RSS.

You can follow us on twitter at Notaninn or email us at YouDontMeetInAnInn@gmail.com

You can also like our facebook page or subreddit for regular updates.

We hope you enjoy listening!
