    \n Neato! The group that I formed when I moved into The Cities eight years ago coordinates our efforts here. You and anyone else who might read this should swing by and see if we click!

    Over the life of this group, we have played at peoples' houses and at game stores. Right now, we mostly play at The Source in Roseville (just north of St. Paul) every other Sunday or so. Most of the regulars are also in other groups that play other games.

    ETA: We play a lot of stuff, most of it indie or alternative or small-press or whatever-the-hell. We just wrapped a six(?) session 13th age game and before that played three Apocalypse World campaigns interspersed with Fiasco and Carcosa and Diaspora and stuff. We've played Sorcerer, My Life with Master, Universalis, Capes, Donjon, D&D (3 & 4), In a Wicked Age, Dogs in the Vineyard, Nobilis, Polaris, board games...I'm sure I'm leaving out more than I'm including. (Right, like Burning Wheel, Mouseguard, 3:16, The Mountain Witch, ...)

    This area also has a very rich set of board game groups if that interests you.

    There are other people playing and writing story games who aren't hooked up with us, too.
