\n I'm not going to be at Camp Nerdly 2 this year - we're moving continents in April, from a home 30 miles from Camp Nerdly to one thousands of miles away. There's no way for me to make it back for Nerdly. Which is sad enough, based on my memories of the last 2 camps, last year in particular. It was amazing, and I've every reason to believe this year will be just as great.

So, as long as I'm wondering, why not give me a few more reasons to regret not being there? Tell me about what you're going to run or play that has you buzzing. Tell me about your plans for CD-swapping or drink-swapping or whatever it is you crazy kids do these days. Tell me about all the amazing food Derek Treuer is going to make for you. Make me wallow in woe that I won't be there.

If you aren't going and you already know why not, that's fine, but please, this thread's not for you. Those of you who aren't going but DON'T know why, you're allowed to ask questions and stuff. You're welcome here and, if you decide to go, I want to hear about it. Those of you who are going, lay it on me.
