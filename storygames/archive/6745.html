    Posted By: Peter AronsonOne thing else. Last time I ordered from you, you sent a couple of your current promotional D6's, which was nice (if mildly amusing since I had orderedRoma Imperious True20, which doesn't use D6's, but still, thanks!). One thing about them that struck me as a bit curious was that the logo replaced the 6. All the other dice I've seen that replaced one face with a logo (dragon dice, cat dice, pirate dice, etc.) replaced the 1. Any particular reason you selected the 6 to replace?
    \nMostly because I want to have folks happy when the logo comes up. This is usually when you roll a six. Yeah, depends ont he mechanic but once upon a time, initiative in Iridium (the system Roma uses) used d6. Higher was better so we went with the 6. Strangely, my rep says that is not all that common.

    As to why d6s...well, d20s (what the mechanic mostly uses) and d100s were not an option.
