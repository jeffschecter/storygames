\n So, I'm moving continents next year and trying to get rid of some stuff. One thing I have a lot of is books that I think are worthwhile but that I'm unlikely to read ever again. I need to get rid of a few, and maybe you have lots of books that you think other people should read, too.

So here's an idea. The first 10 people who whisper me their real, meatspace address will get a book from me. Probably not a roleplaying book, probably just a book. A book I've probably read, and probably enjoyed. It's a free book!

Well, almost free. You have to give at least one book away. So, when you get your book from me, post in this thread that you're ready to give away some number n books (where n>=1). Then ask n to whisper you an address, and send them a book. Then they'll post here, and offer to send m of their own book(s) on down the line. To participate, you have to be willing to pay the postage to whereever your n people might be. Okay?

So, I have 10 books for now, maybe more later. I'll post when 10 people have whispered to let folks know this round is closed. Go!

edit: Warning: some of these books are awesome (or quite good if you prefer), and I would sell them except I'd rather they go to people who I like. A few of you may get a "Bonus Book" out of the few remaining 2nd Edition D&D books I have sitting around that will never be used again!
