\n Minions, Inc. is an Apocalypse World hack for playing henchmen in the costumed aggression industry.
When it comes time to choose a costumed aggression placement firm, choose a name you can trust.
Choose Minions, Inc., the world leader in Henchplacement.

It is a game that is inspired by the Venture Bros., The Tick, The Incredibles, and just about every bit of evil overlord/super villain/mad scientist pop culture that I could get my hands on.

Get the PDF here.
Get the (Landscape) PDF here.
Get the character sheet here.
edit: Updated on 11/13/2010 with copy-edits, clarifications, and the like.




The PDF has a setting guide, starting situation (Meet your new Employer), 9 new playbooks, and lots of guidance for the MC.

Let me know what you think. It was a lot of fun putting all of this together and I can't wait to see what sorts of crazy "Employers" everyone comes up with.

You can also post ideas, actual play, and questions on the Minions, Inc. Forum on the AW site.

    \n Dave: Good catch, thanks. I've updated the character sheet and the PDF. It still amazes me how I can stare at something for that long and not notice the little things.

    merb: I appreciate that. AW is a great game. I hate to say it, but you might want to try out some AW first, just so you get a feel for the game in its "natural" state. I hacked a lot of AW, but it is still written with the assumption that you already know AW. This is why it doesn't explain a number of mechanical/procedural things that it normally would. In many ways, I almost see this as a setting book in the style of Forgotten Realms or Dragonlance. It is mostly the same, but here is setting and here are some new takes on the rules you know.

    Three big notes.

    Michael... I am amazed at your little illustrations. They fit the tone in style so very very perfectly in that Venture Bros. way - without aping that show too much. I would love to see your images cleaned up and colored. Then again, they have a "charm" in the sketchy B/W. I would also love to see you put together a gallery so that these images could be shown in large scale (for folks like me that love collecting fun art like this for our home games!).

    Brandon... I would note, I looked at your neat little RPG idea PDF and think it's a fun little inspiration. It makes me want to read it more, figure out the rules (since I was unfamiliar with the hack) and get a one-shot game going. So your game drew my attention. THEN, Michael's illustrations dropped and THEY kept my attention and caused me to post this note! Great stuff, both of you!

    Lastly. Brandon. If you are not in talks with Michael to snap up his inspired work here, as well as designing many many more minion illustrations. If you're not beginning to work with Michael to redesign "Minions Inc." (perhaps even updating, changing, or designing your OWN game system for the game) using Michael's style exculsively.... then I think you're missing a big awesome thing here!


    I dig the idea of Minions Inc. I have a hard time "getting past" the Venture Bros. photo and the computer (3D?) generated art of the PDF. I think this idea could SOAR with Michael's work and a new design for the trade dress. Focusing less on Venture Bros. specifically and riffing more with "original" (and loving) send-ups of various minion gangs such as Michael has shown us!
    Heck.
    I'm a graphic designer. I'd toss my hand in the ring to help design an updated rule PDF with you, if all of the above came together!


    What it comes down to (for me) is that the original fun idea + Michael's original illustrations have MADE me want to cancel my regular games for a week or two and GM this gem for my pals. Because I KNOW they'd get a kick out of it with their quicky senses of humor!! LOL
