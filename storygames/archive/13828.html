\n Overnight, the organisers of Gen Con Oz announced that they're not going ahead with the con this year for the same reasons that they cancelled it in 2010. You can read all about it on their facebook page, since the non-facebook site for Gen Con Oz is a little out of date - it still shows the announcement of last year's cancellation.

So, you little sub-group of Australian gamers, what are your convention plans for 2011 now? Where are you going to get your convention fix for the year, or are you going to organise your own convention?
