    \n There's a long standing tradition in Call of Cthulhu of writing "Tales of Terror", which are short little blurbs made to begin a scenario. Just few paragraphs, enough to inspire a story, and a few possible explanations of what is really going on, and few to no mechanics. You should be able to find dozens of the things with a quick Google search.
