Been a while since we had one of these threads, but they remind me why we're here. So let's see what's going on!

I'll post my regular group stuff, and then get into Holiday Specifics.

Monday Weekly game (Durham Three): Tonight is the last night of our Primetime Adventures "Jew Qabbalah Pirates of the Carribean" game. It's been a real exciting game, filled with both Swash AND Buckling, not to mention insane Dutchmen captains, qlipoths, underage touching, Spanish Fireball Amulets, and a living island demon controlled by a voodoo priest. I'm Playing in this one.

Wednesday Bi-Weekly Game: We just finished up a Star Wars d6 two-parter that could be best described as "Feng Shui meets Republic Commando". We were all ARC troopers, which is like a super-super-super Green Beret in the Republic Army. Mad fighting, driving, and hacking. Most of the time we would roll the dice (the ARC troopers have abilities at starting levels that I never reached playing d6) and determine if we "succeeded OK, or succeeded AWESOME", and then got into it from there. Seriously, just like Feng Shui. I even roleplayed out Star Wars computer hacking as exciting as any gun battle. In two weeks we will meet for some boardgaming, and then reconvene in January with new players (Mark Causey rejoins the team!) to play a Unisystem-based Fallout mini-campaign. I was a Player in the Star Wars game, and will be a Player in the Fallout game.

Thursday Weekly Game: My Thursday Slacker Cru is loving the additional slack that the holiday season invokes. We spent the last two sessions in a holding pattern preceeding a Big GURPS Firefly(ish) campaign, and just played Battlelore, Settlers of Catan and Shadows of Camelot. Le Chiffre went back to France for the holidays for three weeks, so we'll have three weeks of probably one-shots and the like. I'm totally going to run a Nativity-themed game, either using Legend of the Five Rings or Warhammer. At the beginning of January, we're rolling with our GURPS/Savage Worlds mixup for a year or so, and it's looking to be interesting. I'll be a Player for the start of this GURPS gig, but will later GM in turns.

Other:
There's a few upcoming lazy weekends rolling into view, where most likely some Battlelore, or Tenra Bansho one-shots, will go down. Already planning a lazy event sometime around the holidays where a Jew and an Atheist are coming over to this Shingon Buddhist's house to celebrate Christmas with board games and Guitar Hero 2... and to walk into a bar.

-Andy
