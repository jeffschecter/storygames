    \n Swarms of aliens is the general consensus for my games. Most of them are at Far, but some of them get in close.

    Fast moving aliens (with or without the special ability) can dart in, strike, and back out before the Troopers can react (or maybe any kills the troopers do are actually from those, whatever).

    Ranged attacks also work. Anything from thrown projectiles to actual guns, to acid spittiness.
