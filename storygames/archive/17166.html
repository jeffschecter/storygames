    \n Curse the Darkness is a post-apocalyptic game that ran a successful kickstarter (http://kck.st/Jr4BBf) that explains the game well. In brief, someone declared that all ideology, including political, national and economic systems, are forbidden on pain of death. That someone had the power to enforce those rules and opened up portals to a shadowy realm of horror that destroy the rule breakers.

    The game is a neat hybrid of a story game and the best of the OSR, in that character death is expected and inevitable. You make replacement characters during play from the ranks of the NPCs. As players, you gain currency by recounting memories of characters who have already died during play, which helps you decide to continue fighting for a better world or accepting the new reality.
