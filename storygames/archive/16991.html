\nWhat's that on the viewscreen?



I am looking for victims volunteers to help me playtest a new RPG-story-game-thing called ViewScream. It's a horror game, set in space. It's designed for Google+ Hangout play. However, if enough people are interested, we can do it via Skype, albeit without Tabletop Forge's excellent built-in dice roller -- for Skype play, you'll just need 2d10.

Any takers? This game would be 2-3 hours long, a one-shot, schedule negotiable (if a lot of participants are in different time zones, I can play in the middle of the night, or first thing in the morning, or in the afternoon, whatever). No prep required.

Note: It would be cool if you've watched some Star Trek, or Aliens, or something like Event Horizon. But that's not a requirement!

Another note: This is a game where you're in-character from the moment play begins. We're going full-tilt Stanislavski on this one.

Final note: For a game like this, we've got to keep it tight and focused: no eating Doritos on-screen, no hemming-and-hawing as you try to figure out what your character should do, no screaming dogs or barking babies in the next room.
