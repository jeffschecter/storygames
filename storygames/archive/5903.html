\n Howdy folks,

So some people may need a place to crash for Go Play NW (May 31-June 1 Seattle). We won't be including rooms in the cost of registration this year. You can get rooms at the Watertown Hotel or other venues within walking distance. There's an accommodations page on the wiki where you can get more information.

However, we don't want anyone to miss out on GPNW because they couldn't afford hotel space. This thread is for people who may not be able to afford a hotel stay, but still want to come down for GPNW. Post in this thread if you're looking for a space to crash for the convention, or if you've got room to spare for people to crash in.

To make it easier, enter "Space Available" or "Need Space" at the top of your post.
