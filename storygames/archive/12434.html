Having just finished my degree and moved back home to London for the foreseeable future, I'd quite like to continue roleplaying. As I only really got into the hobby at University, I'm totally unaware of the gamers who must be about in my pseudo-local area.

So, if anyone is up to setting up some sort of game - either something long term or even just something shorter for the summer, let me know and we can sort something out.
