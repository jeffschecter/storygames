    \n It, like most rpgs, takes a lot of authorship, not represented within the text.

    The first sentence should be "Hey, you're gonna need to know a lot about people and story, if you plan to use this to collectively tell a series of events, that if looked at from afar, kinda, sorta resembles a story. You'll need so much talent, that you might not need to use a random-ass sorta-game to tell a story"

    Let's be honest. We use sort of games, to tell sort of stories.

    To me, the feel of the original series, is something quite different than the feel of what I watched of the new series. I like my murders to have plot purposefulness, not just color.
