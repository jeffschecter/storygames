    \n Here is what I am bringing as part of the games organized by TAG (Toronto Area Gamers)

    "The adventure begins at the entrance to the dungeon" wrote Gygax. All sorts of cool games take off from that point of departure. Try out some of the many different ways to hack and slash with your choice of quick dungeon delves (running 1 to 2 hours). A variety of games will be offered on an "on demand" basis: low-prep player-centered games like the improvisational Donjon and the satirical Elfs, Old School Renaissance games like the light-hearted Adventure Party and Neo-Classical Geek Revival, or the weird fantasy of Lamentations of the Flame Princess, the rules-light narrative Heroquest and the crunchy Burning Wheel. Explore new paths to adventure.
