    Posted By: tony dowlerWhat's Vassal?
    \nVassal is a free, cross-platform java application that allows you to play boardgames real-time or play-by-post. You can also scan in or create boards and pieces (and dice and cards), attach code to those pieces, chat, et cetera.

    I created a module to allow my weekly Skype group to play PTA, and a module for Jason Morningstar to demo Grey Ranks online. It's really easy to learn how to make modules for different systems.

    It's much better suited for story games with weird mechanics than MapTool or Gametable or Fantasy Grounds. Those are pretty good for sim-heavy games.

    \n On PNG and GIF: there is no technical reason whatsoever to use gif, basically. PNG does everything GIF does, but better.

    And yes, pngs not only support transparency, but support 256 levels of transparency, for each pixel. So you can make shadows, glows and other effects and they will "blend" with the background.
