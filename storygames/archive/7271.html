\n Hello everyone,

My name is neither Frank, Nina or Carlyle.

I am not a gymnast, despite my mother enrolling me in classes many times during my childhood. I have never, and will never, join an army. I do not do parkour, flatland BMXing or breakdancing (while on the subject, I do not understand how to appreciate toprocking).

I have never written a novel. I've never had a poem published. I do not listen to country music.

I have never been married, I have never been divorced. I am not currently working a minimum wage job.

I have never written a game about historical France or the ways in which society supports its patriots. I have never written a game where you roll stat + skill. I have never written a game with an hourglass mechanic.

I have never played AD&D (or anything pre-3.5), GURPS, HERO, FUDGE (except Fate). I have never played in a campaign that lasted longer than 5 sessions. I have never cried during play. I do not feel thoughtful catharsis during play as often as I want to.


So. Who aren't you?
