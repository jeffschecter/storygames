    I'm not Harald, but I wanted to add a few things as well.

    Instead of "Verstand" for Reason, you could also use "Vernunft". Verstand is closer to intellect, cleverness and also (in some cases) sanity. Vernunft is in the area of being reasonable, logical and insightful.

    Also, instead of "Derzeit" how about "gegenwÃ¤rtig" (which is a literal translation of "currently").

    StaatsangehÃ¶rigkeit could also be StaatsbÃ¼rgerschaft, which is citizenship. I don't know enough about the game, to tell which might be more appropriate.

    As for Trust: "Er vertraut mir." "Ich vertraue ihm." are literal translations of the english phrases.

    Hidden Agenda: National is a tough one, though. "Staatlich" means governmental. "Nationale Geheiminteressen" would probably fit best stylistically, and means: "national secret interests". You could also take Geheime Nationalinteressen, and turn it into "secret national interests". Personally, I'm leaning towards "Geheime Interessen: National", which is "Secret Interests: National". Ziele are goals, meaning something tangible and clear-cut. I'm not familiar with the game, but if these Hidden Agendas are supposed to be a bit more vague, I'd go for "Interessen" instead, which covers that better.

    cheers,
    Georgios
