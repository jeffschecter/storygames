\n I guess this is directed promotion because it's got my name on the cover ... something which comes as a big surprise to me I can tell you Any way the SOTC-based Starblazer Adventures is out soon, and a PDF with a selection of pages from the book is available for download now at

http://www.cubicle-7.com/starblazer/stardownloads.htm

From a purely selfish point of view you can see some of my Art on page 38 (613) and some of my writing on page 32 (495) Yes the book really is over 600 pages long, and crammed full of artwork from the Starblazer comics as well as a wealth of background, setting, and rules for representing just about everything using FATE rules.

    \n I can give some answers... basically stuff that Fred and JoystickJunkies (dunno the real name, but it's the main author) posted here
    http://forum.rpg.net/showthread.php?p=9131953
    (look at the last 2-3 pages)

    The comic had several unconnected storylines, ranging from planetary fantasy to full out space opera, with world eating monsters. Cubicle7 is working on a deal to republish some of the comics, but it's not yet done.

    The book will be 632 pages, hardbound... the biggest corebook of the history of rpg, I gather.
    It will be printed by the same firm that did Hero 5th edition... they know how to deal with huge books

    The PDF will be available shortly.

    And, for you lucky americans, there will be a bunch of advance copies at GenCon: be sure to get in line, because they won't last much. ^___^
