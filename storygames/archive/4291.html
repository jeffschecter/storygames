\n Hello, everyone!

I'm super excited to announce that I've gotten really far into my translation of my favorite version of D&D, the arcade game Shadows of Mystara, into a tabletop experience! So far, in fact, that I'm ready to see if what I've written will work at other people's tables as well as it has at mine. If my wife can play and enjoy this game, then I feel it can have a broad appeal.

Please check out my blog and the Google Doc version and see if this is the kind of game you'd want to try out and help develop. All I ask for are some good actual play reports. If you want to do this but don't feel that you want to report an AP publicly, that's fine! You can send them to my email as well.

Playtesters will be given some sort of real-world compensation depending on how the final product is released. You also gain Playtest Debt wherein I return the favor and play your game for you (if such a situation arises).

Questions about the game can be asked here or at my blog. Previous discussion can be found on a previous thread on Story Games, see below.

Thank you!
