\n Hi!

The italian publishing house Narrattiva (alias "Claudia Cangini and Michele Gelli", partners in life, crime and publishing...) released two days ago LA MIA VITA COL PADRONE, the italian adaptation of "My Life With Master" by Paul Czege. As for the previous Narrattiva edition of "Dogs in the Vineyard", there are two different edition: a "standard" one, and a "deluxe" one, with a WOOD cover. Claudia posted some images of the illustrations and the layout of the books on her deviantart page (http://claudiacangini.deviantart.com) and I wanted to show them to everybody ;-)

This is the STANDARD edition (click on the image to go to her site and click again to see a bigger version):



and this is the DELUXE edition, with the cover made of wood (the inside is the same of the standard edition):
