\n In this thread, Andy's challenge made this thread occur to me.

What character concept have you been meaning to do for a while, but have not yet been able to pull off. Either pull off satisfactorily, or maybe not even been able to attempt.

For me, I've wanted to take one of these hippy swine games and make a character with a major physical handicap be a serious story-advantage. Now, I don't mean a Blind Swordsman type (that was one of my failed attempts), but rather my being blind gives me story weight that I can use to win narration (PTA, Dogs).
