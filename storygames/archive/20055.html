\n For those who still play ERA Epic Storytelling, here is a new Character Sheet & Scene Tracker Sheet:
https://drive.google.com/file/d/0B0kstQO9Gc1deWVWLW14M1UzNzA/view

I've adapted the naming to my taste. The Elements are now:
Fire: Fight, Physique, Ferocity, Endurance
Water: Charm, Presence, Seduction, Negotiation
Air: Movement, Speed, Agility, Stealth
Earth: Wisdom, Knowledge, Intelligence, Research
Spirit: Unknown, Mystery, Magic (if applicable), Will

I highly recommend this for a light 1-on-1 (GM vs PC) game. It has a lot of good indie ideas going for it (like distribution of authority) and the scene structure ensures you have a variety of challenges.
It's not supported anymore, yet it's available for free (pay what you want):
http://rpg.drivethrustuff.com/product/118877/ERA-Epic-Storytelling-Game
