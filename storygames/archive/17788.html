    \n In 1979 I knew there was a game called "Dungeons & Dragons" but I knew nothing about how it actually worked. The entire role-playing thing, but more specifically I had no idea how you knew what happened. I talked to some (very unhelpful) other kids who I knew played:

    Me: How does it work?

    Them: Well you might be walking down a hallway and get attacked by a gelatinous cube!

    Me: (thinking, is this a board game like Monopoly?) But how would you know that? Do you draw a card or something?

    Them: (gleefully) You'd know because your flesh would start to melt from your bones!

    Clearly I was into rules analysis from the get-go.
