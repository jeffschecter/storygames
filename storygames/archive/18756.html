\nHaunted is Nathan Paoletta's little game about ghosts and haunted houses. I commissioned it a while ago. It is short and wonderful.

It's $2 and we're giving all the money to Shanti Bhavan, a wonderful and small charity run by a friend of ours.

Do support them by getting the game. Thank you!

    \n A couple small updates:

    If you prefer using Paypal, the game is now up at DriveThruCards (even though it uses standard playing cards, the OneBookShelf folks want to keep everything card-based over there, apparently)

    If you'd like to contribute more than $2 to Shanti Bhavan, you can use the Give More, Give a Game setup on my website. You can pay any amount you want, and I'll send a copy of the PDF to anyone you want to send a gift too. You get it yourself, of course, and all the proceeds will get rolled into the annual donation. The info and donation button are now up on the Haunted page.

    Thanks to everyone who's picked it up so far, I'm very pleased!
