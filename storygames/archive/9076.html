\n I just made a post over on Spielweise outlining our "unsupported" patch for Beast Hunters. You can check it out here:

Beast Hunters Patch 1.5.

In short, I'm issuing an optional "patch" that incorporates a bunch of changes to the game. It's optional because it's mainly based on my changing preferences since the game's inception almost three years ago. The original game isn't broken at all; in fact, I played it as recently as last week and it worked great. But my play style has moved a bit from intense up-front creative investment to a more spontaneous playstyle, and this patch reflects that.

I've posted a System Reference Document (SRD), which consists of the complete rules sections from Beast Hunters (How to Play, Character Creation and Development, Challenge Negotiation, and Conflict Resolution) in updated form. Half of this information is already contained in the free sections and the demo on our web site (based on the original version), but this is the first time that all of the rules become publicly available\u2013which also is more in line with my current preferences. That said, I haven't fixed some layout issues (replacing quotation marks, mainly) and typos yet, but it'll be cleaned up shortly. Here it is:

Beast Hunters 1.5E SRD.

The SRD, being simply a rules document, doesn't include the setting materials, beasts, tattoos, art, examples, play advice, etc.

So, there you go, hope you enjoy it. If not, just ignore it
