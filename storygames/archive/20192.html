\n We played our first session with World of Dungeons on Sunday! One of the characters had both Skirmish and Slay. The player asked if that gave him a +3 for melee damage, and we weren't sure. For that session, we went with +2.

How do you do it? Do the bonuses stack, or are they intended for different fictional situations?

Sorry if this is obvious, but we weren't sure what the intended meaning was. \ufeff

    \n I'd let it stack. Skirmish means that you always deal +1 damage, whether ranged or melee. That's what skirmishing means--you're able to switch between the two effectively, acting as harassment. Slay gives you a buff to melee damage specifically. So it's +3 damage in melee, +1 damage any other time.
