\n It seems to me that there are a lot of great gaming blogs out there, but many of them are quite infrequently updated, and many of them are quite hard to find. This seems like the ideal situation for an RSS aggregator.

Does something like that already exist? I mean a site that automatically updates with all the recent updates of a whole host of gaming blogs, and keeps them all in one handy place for people to peruse.

I was going to go through and set up my own Google Reader account to pick up a bunch of gaming blogs (using RSSPect for blogs without RSS feeds), but I figured I wouldn't waste my time if that already exists somewhere, and if it doesn't exist, then it seems like it would be a cool thing to make.

So does it exist? Has it existed in the past? It's slightly outside my technical capabilities to build something like that, but for the next few weeks I've got plenty of time to learn. So, advice?
