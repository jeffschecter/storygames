    I'd have the Excellent Prismatic Spray as one of the spells. A lot of Dying Earth is about status, master and slave, the led and the leader, the chief archaeologist and his diggers, so perhaps some more of that in the relationships. Also, you might like to make some of the things more generic or new. Certainly to habitu\xe9s of this world, the Grue, the Pelgrane and Deodand are not unknown.

    There are some useful downloads on this page: http://dyingearth.com/downloads.htm This includes a character name generator and there's a random quote generator here: http://dyingearth.com/random.htm (if you download the script it includes the list of quotes).
