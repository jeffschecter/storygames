    \n Well, you said "media' instead of pop-culture.... so I find that Indianapolis has always had really positive articles in newspapers and the morning tv (when I'm at the gym at the hotel) covering Gencon. I've been very surprised by how knowledgeable people like cops, firemen and wait-staff are (I've talked to all three) and I remember when it was in Milwaukee. Now. Obviously, Gencon brings a lot of money to Indy and there is a reason to be nice to the Geeks and Freeks. But still, Indy is always a nice warm welcome in my experience. disclaimer: haven't been to Gencon in 2 years, will miss this one.
