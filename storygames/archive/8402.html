    \n I use it anywhere I need a range between +/- infinity.

    Any ties add one to the action pool, and whoever wins the round either adds those points to their action, or subtracts them from their opponent's. This means that winning on the first round leads to an empty action pool, and a result of 0.

    One nifty thing about this method is that you can allow more than two players to participate, and they can join in at any time. The more players involved, the longer the round takes, and the greater the chance of a larger action pool.
