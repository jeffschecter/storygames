\n Tongue-in-cheek subject line for what is, at time, sort of an issue for me (and maybe others? Surely I'm not the only one?).

Is there any convention here for designating a thread as "serious" or otherwise tagging it with "please don't come into this thread with nothing to add but your cock?" Maybe a new sub-forum called "But Seriously, Folks" or similar?

I think it is a BIT of an issue, at times, because folks who might have otherwise participated (or worse, already contributed) to a serious discussion become (a) frustrated, (b) disgusted, (c) insulted, or (d) some combination thereof. They leave, the thread gyres for a bit while the jokes peter out (no pun intended), and an otherwise potentially valuable exchange dies out.

On a more general level, is there any provision for actually moderating a thread, by the OP? Is there any "contribute or be silent--no threadcrapping" policy, here?
