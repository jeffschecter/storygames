\n This thread is to post and discuss small scenarios, 10 words at most (title and game not included).

As an example I post a scenario for any fantasy-game.\n
Clowns in power

- Magical artists
- Comradeship
- Preparations

- Travelling
- Peasants
- Performance

- Shadows

- Angry clowns
\n10 words in total, simple and evocative.

Have a go!
