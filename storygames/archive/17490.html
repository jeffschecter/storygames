In this episode, we interview Steve Mathers, creator of Ingenero. We also set a new Mom's Basement standard for disjointed rambling.

Main topics: The struggles of our romantic language and interpreting intent versus flavor, the number one thing people forget to include in their books, and the backlash against generic systems.
