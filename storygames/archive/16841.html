    \nIt's Complicated.
    Mist-Robed Gate has a reference genre, but it's broad, and you make/choose your own setting. Well, basically like Fiasco.
    Microscope is all about making your own setting in play.
    Universalis, a classic.
    Geiger Counter: again, clearly defined genre, freedom of setting.
    Shock: ditto.
    Remember Tomorrow: you could say it's got a "setting", but I contend it's so broadly defined it's rather a "genre".
    Contenders\u2026? Well, maybe this doesn't hold: set it in whatever time or place, but your actual setting is the boxing ring.
    Ravendeath (warning: it's got separate player roles, but none which you could call "a GM").

    That's what occurred me so far.
