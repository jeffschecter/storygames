\n A small chain of events has inspired me to a simple idea for a setting I must share with people. The chain of events is as follows:

1. Last night, I watched the movie The Warriors on DVD. It is cool, and remains such.
2. Today, during my lunch hour, I watched the Ryan vs. Dorkman shorts on Youtube. Very cool stuff, you should check them out ASAP. Lightsaber combat at its finest.

Out of this, I suddenly had this idea for this take on Star Wars set in modern (or recent) times, based somewhat on The Warriors. The Jedi would represent the Warriors gang in this case, the Rogues would represent the Sith (or the dark side), and the Gramercy Riffs or some sort of corporate interest would be the Empire.

Just the thought of back-alley lightsaber brawls in the Bronx makes me smile. Anybody else think this is a good idea (or have any ideas to expand on it with?)

Tim
