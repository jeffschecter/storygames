\n Yes! We made another issue! And it's even better than the first one!

In this one, you can read stuff like:
- The Con Man: Scamming the scammers
- Sex Moves of Apocalypse World: An interview with Vincent Baker
- Taking Care: What roleplayers should learn from BDSM
- The passion of St. Petersburg: Shakespeare and neo-punk in Manchester, Russia.
- Outside the Comfort Zone: Doing educational larp in Belarus

...and heaps more. Including some surprising pictures of Russian larp sex techniques.

Our web page is at: http://playgroundroleplayingmagazine.wordpress.com/
Subscription info at: http://playgroundroleplayingmagazine.wordpress.com/subscriptions/
