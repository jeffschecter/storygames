\n This may just have to be an adventure idea for another game, but I've been pondering a short game (ala Poison'd) where the players take on the roles of "ugly" characters. Not necessary physically ugly, but real villainous, cancerous people. The debutante the destroys lives in her social circle, the psychopath that collects human fingers, the war profiteer, the world bank v.p., and so on.

It's just an idea at the moment. I don't have anything concrete yet, but the game could be able either redemption or damnation, as the character's lives spin slowing out of control. I know Mark V. would love this game.

As I said. Just a kernel of an idea at the moment.

Feel free to tell me how stupid it is.
