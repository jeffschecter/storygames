\n Hey,

here is a game idea i would probarly like to run but i am not sure what kind of a system would work with this best.

In the game you play normal everyday people that got the ability (from somewhere*) to hack reality. This could mean changing a traffic light, mind controling people, wishing stuff into existence etc.
The interesting question for me would be what do people(and players) do with this kind of power.
Absolute power - zero responsibility?

This could probably work with Sorcerer (since power and the use of it are a topic in it) but i am not sure. I don't even know if "hacking" would require any test at all. It should allways be the easy answer and only hacks that change other peoples hacks would be interesting on a mechanics level so the game would be really freeform i think.
What game could work?
