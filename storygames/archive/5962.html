    \n Here is the diceless system I came up with for the various issues I had with Star Trek story lines.

    D.R.A.T. System

    I have played LUG Trek, Dec Trek and FASA Trek and they all have their issues. The biggest theme that runs through them all is that they supply mechanics that can be used to do things outside the intention of the setting. For example, disintegrator transporter beams, phasers that level buildings and phenomenally overpowered tech that can solve problems for you.

    My personal preference is between LUG Trek and FASA Trek.

    \n I think it's depend of what you want to keep of the show (as alway, with an adaptation). For example, if you like the "a new planet, a new social problem to solve every episode" aspect, DitV would be a very good fit. For someone who value more the prime directive and the image of that perfect future world instead, that would almost be a offensive perversion of the show.

    What I mean is: you can't please everybody. What would this game need to please you?
