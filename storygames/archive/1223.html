Okay, no, not really, but...

"The hardest thing in the world is to be good and clear when creating anything. It's the hardest thing in the world. It's really easy to be obscure and elliptical and so fucking hard to be good and clear. It breaks people. Because you don't often get encouragement to do that, to be good and clear."

If the process of writing frogger (which if nothing else made me appreciate the 'full time' designers and the wonderful work they do -- I don't know why fiction is easier for me, but it is) and the discussions ongoing about Stakes and the like do nothing else, they illustrate this point.
