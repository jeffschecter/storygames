\n This past weekend at Dreamation I co-ran a three-table, four-session Bulldogs! campaign. It was great fun. Brennan did a nice job shortening the stress tracks so that combat and social conflict moved reasonably quickly (as compared to SotC). However, fights against tougher foes still seemed draggy. Thing is, you *want* some fights to be tougher, without being grindy. What solutions have people found for this problem?

Matt

P.S. I haven't read any of the new FATE Core stuff\u2014does it address this issue at all?

    \n FATE Core stress tracks are either the same, or pretty close to Bulldogs!

    Spending FATE points for the bad guys is a key fight-pacing mechanic. To make a fight tougher without prolonging it, spend a couple of FATE points in the first round to get surprise, or a hard hit in. This will put the PCs back on their heels and make them feel like the opponent is really tough.
