\n We've just released a new Page XX. The article of most interest to Story Gamers, I would imagine, is a new career for 3:16 - space pilot. Written by Gregor Hutton and with new art, I found it inspirational - I'm already brewing up a Battlestar Galatica style carrier group.

For publishers, Aldo's article on fulfilment is an interesting read. His company, Impressions, is the best way I've found to reach large numbers of retailers, at the cost of very low margins. It suits me, though, I want to maximise number of sales so I can do high-run litho printing and get as many people playing as possible. This isn't necessarily the same as making as much money as possible.

This month is the final one for the Dying Earth RPG, and we are selling off stock and PDFs at half price. I don't think we could really do much more with it. I do want to do a GUMSHOE fantasy game which has Vancean elements, possibly wth Napoleonic era magicians, and we might do a vanilla release of the rules system.

So, my questions to Story Gamers.

What would you like to see in the next issue of Page XX?
What would you call a vanilla version of the Dying Earth rule system?
