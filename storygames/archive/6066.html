    \n I'm not going. I was involved with the indie room a couple of years ago, but not so anymore. The guys running the room are a friendly bunch, and I'm sure you'll find a game or they'll help you.

    \n I wish! GothCon was my first con experience and an introduction to so many cool things that'd come to permeate my gaming life... but sadly I haven't been anywhere near Sweden during con season for a decade now.

    If it helps you make up your mind, I didn't go with anyone I knew the first time I attended GothCon either. I met a lot of cool people that way! And money - pah, I say! Food and shelter is overrated.

    Go! Shoo, shoo!
