\nSynthetic Paper is Synthetic.

I got a promo pack of the stuff a few years back, it feels like the midpoint between paper and a plastic bag or X-Ray slides. Looks to be about $.10 per page for 40 pound.

Only problem is, then you have to find a printer that has the inks that will work for printing on Yupo.

But when you're done, you can take your game into the swimming pool or tub!

-Andy
