\n Tonight my Monthly Pick-Up Game buddies and I played Fiasco over Skype!

We used the recently released Google Drawing tools to track the notecards alongside Graham Walmsley's awesome die roller.

We picked the Alaskan playset and ended up with the following characters\n
    \n
  • Chet the old school nerd-hacker who was that nice guy nerd who has secretly hacked some passwords with a key logger
    Brett, Chet's buddy who smuggled rare penguins and was part of a love triangle with a girl named Maureen, with Todd on the other side of it
    Todd, the wild stoner who had a C-130 full of pot and just trying to smoke it all
    and Duff, the sleeze trying to get some easy cash from wherever he could before he lit out of town on the C-130
    • \n
\nThe hacked passwords were supposed to be used for blackmail.
Maureen was supposed to keep both Todd and Brett on a leash (along with a young Inuit teen)

But things went ugly, the passwords were lost, Maureen was killed when someone shot the Inuit teen boy and the bullet went clean through and hit her, too. Then the cops showed up and arrested Chet for storing dead penguins without a license.

Brett got out scott-free and Duff weaseled his way into a decent outcome as well, while Chet ended up doing 5-7 (in a warmer climate, luckily) and Todd got a broken arm and shot in the ass (he kept his gun down his pants with the safety off).

Madcap fun was had.
