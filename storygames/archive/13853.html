\n A while ago, I wrote a Trail of Cthulhu character generator. It's an online application that lets you create Investigators, then outputs them as a PDF. It took me a while. It's pretty slick.

Anyway, this has been sitting on ice, for a while, because we couldn't think how to release it. It seemed too nice to release for free, but neither me nor Simon wanted to charge for access.

So we're ransoming it. It's a Kickstarter project, basically, except we can't use Kickstarter because we're foreign, so we're using the thoroughly reputable IndieGoGo instead. Here's the link.

Trail of Cthulhu character generator ransom

I'm particularly proud of the perks, which you'll see if you scroll down the right hand side. We'll kill you for $30, destroy your hometown for $60 and let you survive the end of the world for $150.

Or, you know, you can just donate $10, that's good too. But, seriously, end of the fucking world.
