\n Big Bad Con debuts this October7-9 for a weekend of awesome gaming and awesome gamers.

Some awesome you'll find at the con:\n
    \n
  • Nearly 50 games listed on the roster already and more than 20 more expected from folks like Carl Rigney, Ryan Macklin and Leonard Balsera.
    • \n
  • 1,248 square feet of open gaming for pick up games and games "on demand"
    • \n
  • Big Bad GM - A showdown of four awesome GMs to see who has the cops to be called the "Big Bad GM"
    • \n
  • The Band - Yep, the The Big Bad Can will be rockin' out nerd songs to tickle your fancy like Re: Your Brains, Conventional Lover, etc.
    • \n
\nSome links:

For more information check out Big Bad Con

Sign up (paid attendee): $40 - Weekend pass

Run a game: GM (RPG or LARP)

Staff volunteer: Join the Wolf Pack

Book a room (Important, the con needs it to cover the hotel expenses): Oakland Hilton Airport. Code BBC
