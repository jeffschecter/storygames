\n It just occurred to me that DOGS is an anagram of GODS. I started looking for hidden meanings in full anagrams of "Dogs in the Vineyard." So far, nothing that strong.

Deny their vain gods (my favorite)


Thy god nears divine (strong, I think)

Vanity reshined God (ooh, what does THAT mean -- or reverse it, God reshined vanity)

Vanity hid God sneer (hrm)

Vanity ne'er hid Gods (hrm, or reverse God and Vanity)

I deny th' vainer gods (oh, for an extra E)

They invaders in gods (weak!)

Thy sin[n]er invades God (missing an N to pull this off)

G-d invades their irony (huh?)

Day never thins God (so what?)
