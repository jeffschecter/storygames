\n Hi all!

I'm designing a crap ton of games- they're falling out of my fevered brain faster than I can scoop 'em up and start working on them. I've got several in the pipeline currently, of which I'd ideally like to get 2-3 published this year if at all possible. And it will only be possible with the co-operation of you wonderful peeps from the RPG community!

Please visit my G+ design lab to get some idea of what I'm about in terms of games and gaming. A word of warning straight off: I'm on what is commonly known as the 'indie hippy' end of the spectrum, so don't expect tables of armour types, spells, races and classes etc. OTOH we're not talking searing emotional bleedfest either - many of my projects are enjoyably non-serious (but with a dead serious attitude to the designing of the mechanics).

I'm based in London and, although I can travel some distance to nearby locations, for f2f games I'd prefer to meet up there, on days to suit my hopefully growing army of playtesters. However, I'm also interested in running playtests over G+ Hangouts, so that's an option for non-UK peeps.

What's in it for you? The chance to get in on the ground floor of some interesting, innovative, and (I hope) in years to come much played and talked about games. Plus, some sort of deal whereby playtesters get access to discount deals or cheap merch etc. when the games come out.

Interested? Let me know - I look forward to gaming wi' yez .
