\n Inspired by watching Garth Merenghi's Darkplace and reading about the history of television:

1. Start PTA series about the behind-the-scenes production of a TV show, starring the producer, crew, network executives, actors, etc.

2. Start a second PTA series with the same group based on the TV show being produced in the series described above. Everyone plays the characters in the show; the player who portrays the producer character acts as the producer for this second series.

3. Alternate between episodes of series 1 and series 2. Plot elements and developments mentioned in series 1 become the foci, agenda, locations, problems, complications, etc. in series 2. Behind-the-scenes tensions are reflected in series 2 as it evolves over time.

4. ???

5. Profit.
