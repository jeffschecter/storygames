\n I think it could use an in fiction glossary, sorta like the game Dictionary of Mu or the novel Watership Down.
I also think a "No Really" phrase might not be too terrible.
I get the whole importance of "yes and" and "yes but", but the game does have "no and" and "no but", why not a phrase to match that? I mean, you're just throwing off the responsibility of possibly saying no with a few cards. What's so terrible about a a phrase that codifies that? Maybe only allow it to be used a few times a game? Like you're only allowed three "Hell No's" during one sitting? I'm just thinking. I think if someone is really invested in something the way it is, why force something different outta them? Sometimes it's okay not to go with the flow, ya know? It shows something. No's can be powerful stuff.
