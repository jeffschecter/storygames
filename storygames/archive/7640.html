    \n Your guys are warriors and ass kickers, right? Suit them up in full battle rattle. Have them slice slits in their tongues and run ropes through them as penance, and let the blood splash out as an offering to the harvest and fertility gods. The contest here is one of endurance and fortitude - any display of pain or weakness disqualifies them for the awesome part that comes next (and dooms the harvest).

    Once they are all roped up and maybe cruising on endorphins (and pre-contest entheogens, probably), throw them in an arena filled with enemy captives armed with fake wooden weapons and armor made out of flowers. After some speeches and a prayer the battle begins, and the guy taking the most heads wins. This isn't a martial contest but a purely physical one - the goal here is to pace yourself, shepherd your resources, and decapitate as many captives as you can as quickly as you can. Some will run, some will carry on, most will put up a fight, so the winner is the guy who can be methodical, fast, efficient, and choosy.
