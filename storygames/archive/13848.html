\n I just finished watching Percy Jackson & the Olympains on Netflix, and the basic premise is rather like Scion - gods get frisky with mortals and have kids, unlike Scion it's not that a war has started and the kids are getting recruited to fight, but instead there's a war that might happen and it needs to be prevented. I rather like that take on it, and think it gives a more sunny tone to Scion. Playing as the son or daughter of a god, when you're just kicking around and having fun, getting involved in minor skirmishes has a certain appeal to it that isn't realised by being thrust into the final world ending conflict.
