\n As of late there has been a derth of Conspiracy of Shadows actual play reports. I know there are people out there who own the book and I know that there are even people who have played the game. And since that number is relatively small I am offering a bounty on Actual Play reports.

If you play Conspiracy of Shadows a few times and post some Actual Play either on Story Games, the Forge, theRPGsite, RPG.net (or all the sites in question even), I will create a custom illustration for you drawn from the Actual Play (my choice of what I put together). The world will get to see it, but only you and your group will receive prints, signed and numbered, for each member of your game group. I think that is a pretty fair deal.

All you have to do is notify me with a link of the Actual Play via email (ksenkowski at bobgoat dot com) and get me an address to ship to. It could, depending on when I hear from you, take up to a month to get you the prints, so be forewarned. You can see/read/print the entire game free here.
