\n Latest effort. Anybody fancy playtesting it? You need very little in the way of materials, and it's very low prep. High emotional content though- mature themes an' all.

And yes, you're right, I'm writing a new game even before I've published the two previous ones, well spotted. What can I say, the creative brain never sleeps, and won't be told what to do.
