\n Troopers from five warring factions stranded on the barren world of Tundari, cut off from your mother ships - will you survive? Can you regroup? Will you forge a truce with troopers from the other factions who are trapped on this planet with you? And what of the ancient secrets of the Xen race which once inhabited Tundari? This exciting new RPG from the creator of traditional fantasy RPG Omnifray uses a simple mix-and-match character generation system, five core stats and a d10+stat v. d10+stat core mechanic. Don your all-environments carapace and your hover pack, adjust your energy shield settings, pick up your plasma blast rifle, stride forth, explore and do battle!
\n
\nSundered Space is in its alpha playtest edition (version 1.1) following its recent debut at Indiecon. Its release via Lulu.com today marks the launch of version 1.1 and the first chance for the general public to acquire a copy of Sundered Space apart from Indiecon itself.

    \n Very minor adjustments really:-
    \n
    \nSimplified the in-game process of keeping track of the charge left in your Power Unit by making weapons activate for 1 minute at a time instead of 1 shot (similar for energy shields); got rid of rules on power-down for NPCs' Power Units (now it's ref fiat); speeded up in-game stealth rolls by merging the modifier from camouflage settings of your all-environments carapace into the main stealth table; new rules on guns in hand-to-hand combat, dodging grenade blasts, anatomical precision; added some minor details and corrected some typos / minor errors.
    \n
    \nOh, and changed the colour of the front cover and spine slightly too!
