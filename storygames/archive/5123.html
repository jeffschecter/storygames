    \n Project Bearclaw is a BIG game that Jason Morningstar and myself are working on. It's a game about epic undertakings, community, the terror of the unknown (and unknowable), and what you are putting your faith in.

    Jason has managed to very eloquently describe it thusly: "Project Bearclaw is like caveman Damnation Alley with blood-drinking machine theists."

    I like to describe it as what you would get if Cormac McCarthy wrote a book of the old testament after receiving a vision from the archangle gabriel that consisted only of the last 10 minutes of the original planet of the apes.

    It is named Project Bearclaw because within the indie community secret collaborative projects are named after pastry. That's just how it is now.
