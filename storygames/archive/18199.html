\n Let's only consider non-violent options.

The best one I have is for the secret Diana Jones committee to just give Jason Morningstar a "Lifetime Achievement Award" pre-emptively, so they can go ahead and get that out of the way. I mean, your games are fun, important, and groundbreaking, Jason, but can we just take that as a given? Plus, after a while, you'll be forced to design a non-narrative online massively-multiplayer freeform larp-spiel about the intestinal processes of hyenas in order to shock us anymore.
