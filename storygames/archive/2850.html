    \n I'm not Amanda, but I'm her dealer... er... I invited her. We've been kicking around the idea of writing our own game for a while, and this seemed like an excellent opportunity to give Amanda a total-immersion introduction to Indie gaming. Also, it meant I'd have at least one person there that I knew, and I had company for the drive/someone to share the room.

    To say nothing of Amanda being a fantastic role player. We're in a weekly game together, and she plays in our club's bi-monthly MSU Shadows game.

    Now I'm hoping that the two of us can forge some Indie play locally (pun intended).
