\n In this episode, I speak with Elizabeth Shoemaker of Dissolute Games/Two Scooters Press about her game of quirky dysfunctional relationships, It's Complicated. Elizabeth is a good friend of mine, someone I feel lucky to be sharing the Play Collective booth with at Gen Con Indy 2008. It was a pleasure to talk to her about this game, which I played and quite enjoyed.
