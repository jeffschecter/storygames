    Photos - Recess Party, January 13 2012\n
    50-70 NYC nerds rocking it out to video game soundtrack remixes, playing boardgames, making friends (at least 2 gaming groups formed), D20 Burlesque, Action Castle, with a bit of stand up improv while people rolled a giant D20 to see who could roll a natural 20!
    \nPhotos - Recess Gaming, January 14-15 2012\n
    A weekend of 170-200 people making friends, playing over 80 games, learning how to paint miniatures, competing for freshly baked pies at True Nerd Trivia, learning how to run games and find people to play with, and swapping cool nerdy-stuff (comics, games, toys, books)!
    \nDetails here: http://nerdnyc.com/wordpress/recess-success-january-2012
