Piecepack Competition

MikeRM
September 2006 edited September 2006 in Story Games
While browsing round I came across an announcement of the 7th Piecepack Game Design Competition, which is currently on (3 Sep - 3 Dec 2006). The challenge is to port an existing game to use the piecepack.

The piecepack is a public-domain board-and-card game system. There's an active community that might well have congruences with the indy RPG/storygames community - here's your chance to build a bridge.

Just in case anyone is interested.
