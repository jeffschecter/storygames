\n I'm working on a lighthearted cops & robbers game called Keystone Crimewave. It's inspired by a variety of crook/caper comedy and cop comedy movies.

My list so far includes:

Disorganized Crime: (1989) This is the key movie that inspired the original concept for the game.
The Big Hit: (1998)
Police Academy: (1984)
The Naked Gun: (1988)
A Fish Called Wanda: (1988)
Nuns On The Run: (198_)
The Ladykillers: (2004) The 2004 remake of the 1955 original is a better fit for the style of comedy I'm aiming for in KC.
Hot Fuzz: (2007)
Super Troopers: (2001)
Blue Streak: (1999)
Kindergarten Cop: (1990)
Fun with Dick and Jane: (2005) Haven't seen the original 1977 version yet; but hunting it down.
My Cousin Vinny: (1992) (Tangential) This movie is really a lawyer/courtroom comedy rather than a caper/cop comdey; but the style of comedy is that aimed for in KC.

Most of these sources involve a single major caper, or a single major mystery/crime being solved, as opposed of a bunch of story arcs (as in Snatch, or Pulp Fiction). I've also tried to stay away from the overt mob/mafia comedy movies (such as The Whole 9 Yards, and such).

I would, however, like to expand this list some more if possible.

So, if you know of a crime/caper comedy or a cop comedy, be it a movie or a tv series; let me know so that I can check it out and expand my inspiration.
