    \n Seriously, I'm so deep inside this mentally that I'm having a hard time articulating it.

    Default IAWA Character

    Covertly: 1d12
    Directly: 1d10
    For myself: 1d8
    For others: 1d6
    With Love: 1d6
    With Violence: 1d4

    IAWA-with-cards Character

    Covertly: \u25b2
    Directly: \u25b2
    For myself: \u25ab
    For others: \u25ab
    With Love: \u25ab
    With Violence: \u25bc

    Say we start with a default: When you go into an action sequence in IAWA, you pick two forms, draw 2 cards, and take the highest one as your value.

    For each of the forms you picked:
    If it has a \u25b2, draw an extra card
    If it has a \u25bc, draw 1 fewer cards

    If this would mean you'd be drawing 0 cards, draw 1 card and take the lower of the pip and the numeral.

    \n Damage knocks only 1 form down a category, not 2 (since the hit is doubly powerful with the coarser scale of \u25b2\u25ab\u25bc rather than 12-10-8-6-4-0)

    Particular Strengths by default are "ring-high" so when you draw a 0 you can treat it as 10.

    Potent adds a \u25b2
    Doubly potent \u25b2\u25b2
    Broad: Same
    Consequential: Hits 2 forms instead of 1 (incl. named). This makes Consequential pretty bad-ass that might be a problem.
    Far-reaching: Same
