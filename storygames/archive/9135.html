    \n ProFantasy offers an annual subscription for digital content useable with CC3, called the Cartographer's Annual with monthly releases. At the end of each year, we bundle the content up and sell it as a stand-alone product. A majority of subscribers resubscribe, and sale for each follow a very similar volume and pattern. It's very successful, and subscribers give us very good reviews. It enables us to try new things and play with genres we wouldn't otherwise touch. A year is $39.95.

    I'd certainly consider a subscription model for Pelgrane, maybe short adventures for a particular GUMSHOE system with us able to provide better material the more subscribers there were.
