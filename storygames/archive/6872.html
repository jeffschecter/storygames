    Posted By: David ArtmanPosted By: Saint&SinnerCouldn't this be set up in Lulu pretty easy?
    Yep, assuming that (a) it's in a page dimension that they support and (b) he properly embedded fonts. He'd probably also have to provide the cover as separate (I haven't setup in Lulu, but that's REAL common with other print providers, given that covers print on heavier bond and are often laminated).

    The print version of the game has a number of very nice full color interior plates. In such a case, does Lulu charge full color print costs for the whole page count of the book, or just the full color page rate for the color pages and the black and white rate for the rest? Because a full color rate for the whole book could put it out of a sellable price range. Also, the print version of the game came with a small set of cards.

    For these reasons, I don't believe there's a quick and easy solution for bringing The Mountain Witch back into print. My understanding is that print costs, the color plates and the small set of cards made retailer discounted IPR sales of The Mountain Witch very thinly profitable for Tim.
