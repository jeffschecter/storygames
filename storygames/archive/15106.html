\n Hi everyone --

A couple weeks ago, I launched RoleJack.com. It's a new group blog where each post is a fun game setting, custom rules for a favorite game of yours, scenarios, game hacks, neat name lists, etc.

The first post is Ralph Mazza's swashbuckling scenario generator, and now I'm no the prowl for more fun contributions. All game types are welcome -- this isn't just an "indie" (i.e. creator owned) blog. Conventional and indie more than welcome.

The emphasis is on things players and GMs can use, rather than essays, theory and so on.
Articles are posted for free. But, I'll be putting up a tip jar later on to "keep the lights on." From there, I'd figure out how to share the love with contributors.

If you're interested, shoot me an email to matt -at- rolejack.com.

Meanwhile, check out RoleJack.com!
