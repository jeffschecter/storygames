\n Completely new players (and inexperienced facilitator) managed to pull some cool, dark, weird, but ultimately hopeful communities. One sort of standard--that bore a strong resemblance to the area around Hoover Dam--and one that was basically post-fantasy--imagine the floating islands of Avatar or Gorillaz video.

Joe, expect at least three or four sales! Really great game for a short-slot (2.5 hour).
