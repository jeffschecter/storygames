    \n Hi Julia. Enjoyed both podcasts. Loved hearing the reception that you got from the Spelman College crowd. Listening to their fantastic roleplaying while you left the room was a blast. Gave me a chuckle every time you came back into the room to see if they were ready for you to come back yet. I was impressed with how quickly they jumped into the thick of it, given that they were mostly non-gamers and you weren't there to coach them.

    I would love for there to be more of this kind of healthy discussion with non story-gamer types in general.

    The Theory From the Closet interview was engrossing. I liked hearing more about your design motivations. I also appreciated the thoughtful self-reflection on your Gen Con experiences and the sometimes difficult reception that the game received. Listening to the podcasts has definitely put the game on my "Must Try" list. Thanks.
