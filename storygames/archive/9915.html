\n Apparently they are creating panels and upping the exposure for Indie games at DragonCon this year. There's a thread over at the Forge that discusses it here. The plan is to have panel discussion Friday Sept 4th at 10am and Saturday the 5th at 1pm. If you're coming to the Con might be a good way to increase exposure and spread some education to people who haven't seen these sorts of games.

The guy who's leading this goes by Certified and has a developed a game called 6th World. I'd drop him a line to get in on the ground floor.

I hope to see some of you there!
