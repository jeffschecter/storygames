\n I'm so old school, my first characters were printed in woad

I'm so old school, my first characters weren't historical reenactments, they were just contemporary reenactments

I'm so old school, that when I said I was going to hang out at the forge for inspiration, I meant hephestus's forge.

I'm so old school, that I sneer at stone tablets at the table, real roleplayers dont need mechanical aids.

I'm so old school, I thought that Papyrus was the cutting edge for the future of the hobby, and I was right.

I'm so old school, when Socrates asked Glaucon to image the Beautiful City, I was like, Wait let me go get my platonic solids.

I'm so old school, that I rolled my eyes when osirus died and came back as the king of the dead... Get a new character newb.

I'm so old school, that on the 7th day, the lord did rest for verily my d20 action heroes game had kept him up for most of the night.

I'm all like 2000 and great and sadly your all like 2000 and late.

Boom Boom Pow
