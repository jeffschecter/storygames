\n Folks,

Double Exposure's newest convention, METATOPIA, has evolved in an incredible new endeavor!

We have 49 publishers and designers coming to show off their latest and greatest products, to playtest their current crop of ideas, and to run focus groups to flesh out concepts that haven't even been written yet.

The Complete Schedule of 164 Events has just been posted, and reservation seating begins immediately!

http://www.dexposure.com/m2011.html

Highlights of METATOPIA include:

Kenneth Hite, our special guest of honor, playtesting numerous new games and running a Saturday afternoon panel on game design.
Kickstarter, a group that has been all over the news lately, giving a Saturday afternoon presentation on their company.
Darren Watts and the entire IPR store, selling dozens of Independently Published games.
Stephen Buonocore, bringing back mystery prototypes fresh from Essen to show off and evaluate.
Four LARPs run by established Double Exposure groups.
Midnight Karaoke and Rock Band with our giant LCD projector and Bose speakers.
Non-stop gaming action for ALL interests and the Double Exposure Con Suite.

Pre-Registration is open until Sunday, October 30th at 11:59PM. Don't miss out on the fun!

Vincent Salzillo, President
Double Exposure, Inc.
Gaming the way it was MEANT to be!
http://www.dexposure.com
