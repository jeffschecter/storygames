    \n There's no extension for account pictures: They're enabled by default. However, each Theme/Style has different settings for the account page: Some styles will do a variable-width shrink of the picture you choose, others will only display it in full to a certain size (x by y pixels, starting from the top left corner or the center of the pic), and others just don't have account pics altogether.

    Hope that helps!
