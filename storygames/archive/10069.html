\n So, if you're going to GenCon, and intending on bringing any games with you to play, what games would those be?

I'll be spending a significant amount of time playing the Shadowfist CCG, and I'll be bringing MAID, which is probably my go-to pick up game these days. Also Mountain Witch, though who knows if I'll actually play it or not. And Race for the Galaxy- it has the advantage over my other boardgames of being compact.

(And of course, I'd be amiss if I failed to mention my own games, Awesome Adventures and Escape from Tentacle City.)

    \n I spent hours last night getting my prepped-games folder together, but it is a thing of beauty. I have one of those flexible plastic vertical-file organizers with 10 or so divided pockets. Each pocket holds a different game. I can fit my whole repertoire in there, including the rulebooks. Let me show you at GenCon.

    That took a half hour to get together. I print out character sheets and cheat sheets for the rules for several of the games.

    What took 4-5 hours was D&D prep. If I don't end up running Metrocalypse at GenCon, at least it'll be ready for next time. I made custom pogs for it. I printed character sheets. I tuned my adventure and made a custom monster using the Monster Builder. I printed monsters from the same app; that way, I don't have to take my Monster Manual.

    I'm psyched. Let's play something.
