\n I've had my nose to the grindstone for the past few days, and finally, the first item in the Mist-Robed Gate DVD Library is done.



Forbidden Tiger Manual is ten pages of new rules and recipes, adapting Mist-Robed Gate to kung fu action movies. It's $3 USD, and it's free to anyone who preordered MRG, or purchased the PDF in the month of October. Enjoy!
