    \n Hey Rich,

    Thanks. I enjoyed doing this interview with Chris quite a bit.

    I think we recorded it back on April 11th, so just over a week prior to Clyde interviewing me for his Theory From the Closet podcast. And y'know, I think maybe the conversation flowed a bit better in this one. It was pretty late when Clyde and I started that interview, so maybe I had a bit more trouble finding the words for what I wanted to say. But the other difference is that Chris emailed me an outline of his questions in advance.

    Hmm...for those who listen to both interviews, is the flow of conversation noticibly different between them? Or not so much?

    Paul
