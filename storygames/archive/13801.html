\n Heya,

I've had some folks approach me with an idea for RPG Crossroads. They've asked if I could set up forums for their play groups or RPG Club so they can have an online home. I think it's a great idea and exactly the type of thing RPG Crossroads is meant to be. It's my hope that having these forums on there will introduce gamers to each other and get some cross-polination going on.

If you would be interested in having a forum for your group, please post in the sign up thread under the Social Forums heading. I hope I get to see you there!

Peace,

-Troy
