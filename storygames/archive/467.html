Just by way of passing along information, I was reading on another forum about this guy who has a cafe in Portland, and his interest in getting some gamers to come out and take over his place once a month. Here's his idea:

I've got a cafe. I really want to host roleplaying games, perhaps one night a month. I used to play Rifts, but that was years ago... any suggestions on who to contact, how this might play out, the whole RPGer scene?

I think this would take place on a thursday night. We would, in a sense, theme out the place - complete with my staff and I dressed up. I'd like multiple games taking place at a time, each run by a GM, and have it be kind of a social scene. Like, if everyone played GURPS then the players could intermingle and intergame. I would imagine this would take some pretty good GMs, and that each GM (and storyline) would be assigned to a table. I've got space for probably 7 or 8 games to go on at a time.

I used to roleplay in living room. This could be alot of fun.
To be clear, I myself am not in Portland, but I thought I'd pass it along for those of you who are, since it seems like this could be an interesting challenge and a good venue. It doesn't sound like he really knows what he wants, but my guess is that a group of indie gamers would be better for his business than a bunch of Warhammer fanatics...
