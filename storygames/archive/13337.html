    \n Heh! Funny. I asked a very similar question at another forum. My intention is to use it for playtesting, with the possibility to use it to find a new regular group in the long run.

    My take:

    Personal stuff (name, nickname, age, what they do for living...)

    For how long have you been gaming?

    Which 3 games you played the last?

    Which are your 3 favorite games?

    What aspect of roleplaying interests you the most?

    What kind of stuff allow you to leave the table feeling the most satisfied?

    What games have you ever GM'd (or would like to)?

    What are your lines and veils while gaming? (taboos, preferred explicitness and dislikes)

    Which Creative Agenda you prefer your group to follow?

    Which are your personal sockets?

    Non role-playing hobbies and activities you enjoy

    Preferred fiction genre

    Preferred Author or Director
