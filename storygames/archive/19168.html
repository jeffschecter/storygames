    \n Oh, how did this slip by? I could've sworn I wrote something here.

    Portland has a really nice community for Story Games. A good person to talk to would be Tayler (@quincunx), and you should check out the Play Out Loud page on Facebook (LINK) and the Portland Gamers Meetup page (LINK) to look into Ready Set Game PDX, the regular meetup of story gamers to try cool stuff with new people. I THINK it meets every other Tuesday, and is on hiatus until February 4th (don't quote me on this schedule), and it's a great place to go if you want to meet the local scene.
    Similarly, that same Portland Gamers Meetup page seems like it's a great place to hook some people together if you want to set up a game of your own or get in on the ground floor.

    If cons are more your scene, Gamestorm is happening in March just across the bridge in Vancouver, and the story-game-centric Indie Hurricane will be there. Here's a LINK to that too for good measure.

    Hope there's something in there for ya! I'm up in Tacoma, WA, but hopefully I'll see ya one of these trips down.
