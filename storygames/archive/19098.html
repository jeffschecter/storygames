\n Hi folks. I just released a new freeform game called Amidst Endless Quiet. It is about the last moments of the interstellar spaceship Elios. You play Elios, and you must make a horrible life-and-death decision about your five passengers, with little time and less information.

It is for one to six players, and takes roughly 2-4 hours.

Like (almost) everything I design, you can pay what you want or download it for free. It's fairly short.
