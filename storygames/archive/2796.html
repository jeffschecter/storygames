\n I hope this is the right category... I stumbled across an announcement on Gamegrene about Ziggurat Con - a gaming con some soldiers in Iraq are putting together. They're direly short of all kinds of materials. They've asked for pretty much traditional games, but I also think they'd be receptive to Indie games, if some of you designers wanted to send them along.

For more information, contact SPC David Amberson at the following address: david.amberson (at) iraq.centcom.mil

Donations can also be sent to SPC Amberson directly at the following address:

SPC David Amberson
A Co 86th Sig Bn
APO, AE 09331

I've got a longer story about it at my LiveJournal

I really want these men and women to be buried in gaming materials. And it would be a totally awesome opportunity to introduce them to Story Games and the whole Indie gaming scene.
