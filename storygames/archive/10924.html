\n So,

I love slasher movies. And I've been devouring them over the last month (Scream, Scream 2, Scream 3, Nightmare on Elm Street, Behind The Mask, I Know What You Did Last Summer). And about a week before Halloween, I got inspired and wrote a game.

It's called Slashed to Ribbons, and it's available right here. It's written assuming you already know how to play Ribbon Drive, the same as the other hack I wrote, Radion-Accelerator Drive (my Firefly-via-Ribbon-Drive game).

Enjoy!
