\n A while back we played Remember Tomorrow adapted to swords & sorcery. It worked as great as expected: outcast warriors wandering deserts to avenge their shattered clan, sorcerers of dying races seeking forbidden lore, all that good stuff.

But for speedy pickup inspiration I want tables for character identities and faction types so you can roll or pick from the list. Identities are professions or roles for the main characters and factions are the groups or organizations that make their lives a living hell. The default cyberpunk lists are:

IDENTITIES: Activist, Courier, Dealer, Hacker, Insider, Operative, Outsider, Tech, Torpedo, Worker

FACTIONS: Agency, AI, Corporation, Entertainment, Gang, Group, Idle Rich, Military, Pharma, Ronin

I think the standard motivation table is fine as is. Envy, Pride, Freedom, Revenge, etc work just about anywhere. You mix the two together to get the whole character (like a hacker motivated by revenge, a corporation motivated by pride, etc).

I need ten entries for each. Single entries sometimes combine similar ideas, like torpedoes which are any kind of hitman, bodyguard, etc. Off the top of my head\u2026

CHARACTERS
barbarian
sorcerer / seer
bandit / rogue
legionnaire (any soldier)
beggar / slave
noble

FACTIONS
cult
clan / tribe
guild
circle of magi
noble house
den of thieves (includes bandit bands)
warband (mercenaries, armies, pllaging hordes, etc)

Got ideas? Shout 'em out.
