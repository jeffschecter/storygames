\n Just wondering if anyone has encountered a game that uses dominoes as a randomising mechanism?

They have 2 ends of numbers on them, and traditional play involves them being laid out into patterns where matching number from each end allow dominoes to form a chain.

I'm seeing some potential for something really interesting.

I've got a couple of ideas for something I've been working on, but I'd like to avoid reinventing the wheel (or...if I'm going to improve on the wheel, I'd like to make sure I get a good look at it first).
