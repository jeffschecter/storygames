    Posted By: rossumSo what does "Entombed" mean?
    It's kinda like banned, but banned is hard to do here. It basically takes away all your posting and most of your viewing privs. I equate it to being entombed alive in the walls, like in that Edgar Allen Poe story. IIRC, only one user was Entombed, and that was because:
    1) After being really generally argumentative here and on other RPG forums about the act of roleplaying (and discussion), I discovered that
    2) He was pretty burned out on games (he stated that he hated gaming, and soon after was dropping the hobby entirely), and
    3) Hadn't actually played in years, perhaps even a decade or more
    So I decided nothing good was going to come of that. Turns out that he's out and about again, and may one day get back into gaming, decide it's for him, and be chill. Then he'd be welcome back. But until then, having long-term non-gamers discussing gaming furiously is pretty contrary to the site's "gamecraft & fun" mission.
