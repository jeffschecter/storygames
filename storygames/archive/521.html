Hey folks,

Another one day con in the MD/DC/VA area just come across my radar screen. I went ahead and signed up.

TERPCon 2006 - May 6, 2006
http://www.warhorn.net/terpconspring2006/

This is just a big one day con, full of gaming (and little else). It's actually a nice place to have a con, as the student union features a little "mall-like" eatery, and there is a darn good variety of games to play. At the one in October, I ran two demos of Eldritch Ass Kicking (to rave reviews) and played in one session of Weapons of the Gods. It was nice.

I haven't decided what I will be running/playing in yet, but I know there will be at least one more section of Eldritch Ass Kicking. Maybe Barbarians Versus? Maybe the Land Beyond?

I bet if we approached the organizers, they might even make way for smaller rounds to be introduced, so we can plan two or three shorter sessions of various indie games.

Anyway, that's my blurb. Watcha think?
