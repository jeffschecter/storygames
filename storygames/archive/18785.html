\n Did a Playtest of the fast card combat game I recently mentioned.

The players have a hand of attack and defense cards. Each turn an attacker throws down an attack card. If the defenders plays a defense card the attack is stopped. The word descriptions on the cards create the action account. The player with the highest number on the card makes the next attack. With ties the attacker retains the initiative.

We played in the Ten minutes before we played another game.

It was fun and made a narrative. I can't ask much more. Now I'll make a Wargame deck to run a miniatures deck. Probably a Seven Years War deck because I can use it at the miniatures con I attend in March.
