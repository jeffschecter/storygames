    \n I'm going off memory. Maybe Julia, Meg, or Vincent will come here and read this and elaborate. Anyway:

    You roll the number of dice you have to, plus one, each time, and drop the lowest.

    Unless you're hurt, and you roll only the number of dice you have to.

    Unless you're hurt again, and then you roll the number of dice you have to plus one and drop the highest.

    --

    If they catch up to you, a new "overlay" goes on there that says "capture." The numbers determine how many of you are captured (I don't remember the specific number-values). I think there's another overlay after that, too (as in if you're hurt, and hurt again, something worse happens).

    --

    If you're trying to awaken a new psi talent, you replace the psi table with one we created. Going off memory: 1-2 = GM-defined psi talent; 3-5 = other-player-defined psi-talent; 6 = you-defined psi talent.

    --

    I'm sure I got some of these wrong and someone can correct me or elaborate, but these have made the game much more fun for me. Having being wounded and caught matter, and having the element of choice that the additional dice present, is awesome.

    \n Unharmed: roll an extra die and throw away the lowest. About 30% all good, but 30% pretty bad including 6% super catastrophic.
    Harmed once: roll the right number of dice. 12.5% all good, but 50% pretty bad including 12.5% super catastrophic.
    Harmed twice: roll an extra die and throw away the highest. Only about 6% all good, but 70% pretty bad including 30% super catastrophic.
    More harm = even worse.

    (Those %s are for 3 categories, 3 or 4 dice. More categories - in Psi-Run, you can roll as many as 6 - is generally worse. Rolling 7 dice and throwing away the lowest, you're still very likely to have a couple 1s or 2s in the mix. Rolling 4 and throwing away the lowest, you're far less likely to have any 1s or 2s left.)

    When the chasers catch up, we take out the chase category and put in this:
    Who gets captured?
    5-6 nobody
    3-4 somebody (GM's choice)
    1-2 everybody

    When somebody's captured, we replace it for them with this in turn:
    Do you disappear forever without a trace?
    4-6 no
    1-3 yes

    -Vincent
