    \n I don't have the actual sheet, but from what I remember, you wouldn't go wrong by just writing all the characters' names in a circle, and then drawing lines from one to another as you name Best Interests.

    The main advantage is that you can see when someone is "left out of the loop" or getting too mixed up in things, or whatever. The visual is helpful for framing scenes, too.
