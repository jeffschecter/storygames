\n All,
\n What follows is an incomplete list of some of the major players of the day in alphabetical order.
\n
\nLegend:
\nVampire origin: This reflects whether the group's leaders have an idea of the link between Rom and Vampires. The entire group may or may not know and individual members may always be an exception.
\nVampires: Most people in the world of SPC know about the existence of vampires. This description generally indicates if the group is opposed to the existence of vampires and/or their agendas.
\nRom: Some groups harbor a prejudice against the Rom and their associates. Again, individual members may prove to be an exception.
\nGaze: This is the term Rom use for outsiders, meaning non-Romani individuals. This entry indicates the group's general attitudes towards non-Romani individuals.
\nAllies: This is a list of groups that are allied with or at least compatible with this group. Again, some individuals may stray from the group's general preferences. Allies are listed from strongest, truest alliance at the beginning of the list with weaker alliances and mere compatibilities listed last.
\nEnemies: Again, the most hated enemies are listed first while the end of the list might just be a group that is incompatible with this group.
\n
\nBoomers - Prospectors, Claim Jumpers and Entrepreneurs. Men and women that have felt the winds of change and adapted accordingly.
\nVampire origin: Ignorant
\nVampires: Opposed
\nRom: Dislike
\nGaze: Interested
\nAllies: Wild Bunch, Royal Society, Republicans, Cabal
\nEnemies: Rail Barons, Church of England, Pobratimo, Illuminati, Mafia
\n
\nCabal - Group of Sorcerers and advisers. They trade in knowledge of magic, and aid each other in times of need.
\nVampire origin: Know
\nVampires: Opposed
\nRom: Dislike
\nGaze: Interested
\nAllies: Wicca, Sindo, Trailblazers, Boomers
\nEnemies: Mafia, Drakes, Regulators, Rroma, Rail Barons
\n
\nChurch of England - In this era, the Anglican Church is aggressively expanding. People feel like they need help and the Church is there to provide it.
\nVampire origin: Know
\nVampires: Not opposed
\nRom: Accepting
\nGaze: Interested
\nAllies: Rail Barons, Trailblazers, Sindo, Drakes
\nEnemies: Wild Bunch, Boomers, Rroma, Regulators, Wicca
\n
\nDrakes - A group of Vampires sworn to protect ordinary people. Even though they have turned, they try to use their powers to defend those weaker than themselves.
\nVampire origin: Ignorant
\nVampires: Not opposed
\nRom: Accepting
\nGaze: Interested
\nAllies: Mafia, Republicans, Royal Society, Church of England
\nEnemies: Wicca, Cabal, Illuminati, Pobratimo, Wild Bunch
\n
\nIlluminati - A Secret society dedicated to discovering the true nature of creation.
\nVampire origin: Know
\nVampires: Opposed
\nRom: Accepting
\nGaze: None
\nAllies: Sindo, Wicca, Rail Barons, Rroma
\nEnemies: Republicans, Regulators, Drakes, Boomers, Trailblazers
\n
\nMafia - Organized Crime Syndicate. The mafia is cultivating their influence into every town that is benefiting from the new economy.
\nVampire origin: Ignorant
\nVampires: Not opposed
\nRom: Accepting
\nGaze: None
\nAllies: Drakes, Regulators, Rroma, Rail Barons
\nEnemies: Cabal, Wicca, Sindo, Trailblazers, Boomers
\n
\nPobratimo - Vampires dedicated to feeding on the weak. Some feel that it will end the curse sooner, but most just revel in the thrill of it all.
\nVampire origin: Know
\nVampires: Not opposed
\nRom: Dislike
\nGaze: None
\nAllies: Trailblazers, Rail Barons, Wicca, Regulators
\nEnemies: Royal Society, Rroma, Boomers, Drakes, Sindo
\n
\nRail Barons - Monopolistic conspiracy of industrial leaders. Of course, this does not just refer to rail tycoons. But all industrialists who are making millions off of the sweat of poor people.
\nVampire origin: Know
\nVampires: Not opposed
\nRom: Accepting
\nGaze: None
\nAllies: Church of England, Pobratimo, Illuminati, Mafia
\nEnemies: Boomers, Wild Bunch, Royal Society, Republicans, Cabal
\n
\nRegulators - This is a group of Ex-military personnel, Bounty hunters, Mercenaries & hired guns. They find work however they can, legal or otherwise.
\nVampire origin: Ignorant
\nVampires: Not opposed
\nRom: Dislike
\nGaze: None
\nAllies: Republicans, Mafia, Wild Bunch, Pobratimo
\nEnemies: Sindo, Illuminati, Cabal, Church of England, Royal Society
\n
\nRepublicans - Inner circle of the Government. Works to serve their own agenda at the expense of all others.
\nVampire origin: Ignorant
\nVampires: Not opposed
\nRom: Dislike
\nGaze: Interested
\nAllies: Regulators, Drakes, Boomers, Trailblazers
\nEnemies: Illuminati, Sindo, Wicca, Rail Barons, Rroma
\n
\nRoyal Societies - Inventors, Scholars and Scientists. These groups have existed for over 200 years. It is a meeting of minds that probe into the mysteries of the universe.
\nVampire origin: Ignorant
\nVampires: Opposed
\nRom: Accepting
\nGaze: Interested
\nAllies: Rroma, Boomers, Drakes, Sindo
\nEnemies: Pobratimo, Trailblazers, Rail Barons, Wicca, Regulators
\n
\nRroma - Group of Rom who are trying to affect changes in order to adapt in this new world.
\nVampire origin: Ignorant
\nVampires: Opposed
\nRom: Accepting
\nGaze: None
\nAllies: Royal Society, Wild Bunch, Mafia, Illuminati
\nEnemies: Trailblazers, Pobratimo, Church of England, Cabal, Republicans
\n
\nSindo - A group of Rom who are trying to preserve the old ways.
\nVampire origin: Know
\nVampires: Opposed
\nRom: Accepting
\nGaze: Interested
\nAllies: Illuminati, Cabal, Church of England, Royal society
\nEnemies: Regulators, Republicans, Mafia, Wild Bunch, Pobratimo
\n
\nTrailblazers - Elite Military Officers working to create and maintain a Military Industrial Complex. They just want to safeguard their homeland with the best men, training and technology.
\nVampire origin: Know
\nVampires: Not opposed
\nRom: Dislike
\nGaze: Interested
\nAllies: Pobratimo, Church of England, Cabal, Republicans
\nEnemies: Rroma, Royal Society, Wild Bunch, Mafia, Illuminati
\n
\nWicca - A nature worship cult imbued with magic powers. They believe in the equality of all beings.
\nVampire origin: Know
\nVampires: Opposed
\nRom: Dislike
\nGaze: None
\nAllies: Cabal, Illuminati, Pobratimo, Wild Bunch
\nEnemies: Drakes, Mafia, Republicans, Royal Society, Church of England
\n
\nWild Bunch - One of several gangs terrorizing the open road. These bandits jump any travelers that appear weaker than they are.
\nVampire origin: Ignorant
\nVampires: Opposed
\nRom: Dislike
\nGaze: None
\nAllies: Boomers, Rroma, Regulators, Wicca
\nEnemies: Church of England, Rail Barons, Trailblazers, Sindo, Drakes
