\n I need help coming up with situational modifiers for these six stat-like-concepts:

Directly
Covertly
For Myself
For Others
With Love
With Violence

These situational modifiers can be quite specific, but general is awesome.

Here's a few easy ones:

With Violence
Superior weapon +1
Higher Ground in Melee +1
Surrounded Opponent +3

Covertly
Unseen +2
Unheard +1

Directly
First to move +1

and so on.

Can I get help with some more?

(Assume this is going to be used in some way, but maybe not the direct way you think.)

    \n Suddenly I realized I"m unclear on the difference between "For Others" and "With Love". I now realize "With Love" must mean Eros, because "For Others" is really Agape.

    So how about:

    With Love
    Object of love is intoxicated +1
    Object of love drank love potion +2
    Object of love is lonely / hard up +1
    Object of love has taken vow of celibacy -2
    Object of love involved in monogamous relationship with someone else -1
    Object of love hates you -2
    Object of love dislikes you -1
    Object of love already likes you +1
    You are not object of love's type: wrong personality, look -1
    You are not object of love's type: wrong race, gender -2 (Edit: not trying to come off as racist here, I'm thinking could be anything from elves & dwarves to Montagues & Capulets, or more conventional race in a game/story where that's thematically important)

    Very half-baked ideas here:

    For Myself
    You are alone and surrounded by enemies +1
    When betraying someone's trust +2

    Directly
    First to act +1
    You've informed your enemies of exactly what you're about to do before you started doing it +2

    For Others
    Someone you care for is hurt or being threatened +2

    Lots of room to make some interesting statements about... everything, here!

    Not sure how this is going to interface with other parts of the system, depending on what this is for, exactly. I hope you'll share some more thoughts on this front, Ry, before this gets wrapped up!
