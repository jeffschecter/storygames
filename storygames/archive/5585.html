\n Heya all, I'm going to eventually create a FAQ for this sort of thing, but every once in a while I see this kind of message. It happens to me, too, like if the server is a little flaky (kinda rare), or when my network connection is having issues (more likely with my wireless, etc).

PROTIP: If you ever post, then get that message like "The Server Could Not Be Found" or whatever, hitting Back on your browser I've found to be a 50/50 game: Sometimes your message will be cached there, sometimes it won't.

INSTEAD, just wait about 10-60 seconds or so (or use another browser tab to make sure your internet connection is solid), and hit your browser's REFRESH button. In firefox (as I'm sure with other browsers) it will give you a message, something like "Do you want to repost information?" or something to that extent. Hit Yes. If the connection to S-G comes back, it will post your info as usual.

Again, just need to wait a minute and hit F5, then Yes. It's worked for me each time it's happened: Here, The Forge, RPGNet or other forums.

Thanks!
-Andy
