\n Yesterday, in just under six hours, I designed my first card-based role-playing game.
\n
\nI blame Jason.
\n
\nBasically, one person plays Casanova and the other players can either play the women he's trying to seduce (you need at least one of those) or people who will either help Casanova or his Intended. The goal is for Casanova to seduce the woman/women without being trapped in marriage. The goal for the woman/women is to get him to marry them BEFORE he seduces them. And the others can help or hinder either party as they see fit.
\n
\nI'm using a tarot deck as both a mechanic and as a sort of oracle for story telling.
\n
\nWe're gonna do a bunch of playtesting here, but soon I'll be wondering if anyone else is up for playtesting it for me as well.
\n
\nNot really asking for help just yet, just kind of crowing about it.
