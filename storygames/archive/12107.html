\n Friday, 21 May, Umstead state park.

If you are interested in camping out and playing roleplaying games, let me know! You could probably just show up and game as well, but you'd get chased out when they close the gate at dusk. It is looking like there will be a good supply of tents and the weather will be, if nothing else, warm. This whole thing is turning out very casual, but there will be high quality gaming and nice people.

Anyway, holler if you are interested, especially if you want space in a tent! The park is easily accessible from I-40 and I-70 near Raleigh, North Carolina. If you live near DC, you should be going to Ice Lifeboat Nerdly instead!
