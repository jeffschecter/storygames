\n This is kind of a poll. Hopefully, it'll help me expand my gaming horizons.

I'd like you to post statements in the following formulation.

If (situation X occurs), we play (game Y).

Examples :

If there's only two of us, we play Showdown.
If we want science-fiction, we play Shock:.
If we want superheroes, we play Capes or With Great Power...

    \n If we are two, we play Find a third, then we proceed as follows:

    If we are three to five players, and have but one hour to play, we play Snakes on a Plane RPG.

    If we are three or four players, have four hours and are in a sci-fi mood, we play While the world ends.

    If we are three players, have four hours and are in a fantasy mood, we play Bl\xe4ck. (Not yet available in English.)

    If we are four players, have at least four hours and are in a fantasy mood, we play Polaris.

    If we are four to six players, and don't know how for long we'll be able to play, we play 1001 Nights.

    If we are four to six players, have three to four hours, we play Zombie Cinema.

    If we are four to six players and have at least five hours, we play test whatever revision of The Cadet Game I'm working on.

    If we are five to six players, have four hours and I haven't met the players before, we play "Ett Hj\xe4rta av St\xe5l" for Itras By. Brilliant game and a brilliant adventure, and I have played it with everyone I know already.

    \n Real world and rules of thumb.

    If we're two we play Mars Colony, Hearts & Murders or s/lay w/me.

    If we're at loss for what to play I'll probably pull out something like Lady Blackbird, Ghost/Echo, In a Wicked Age or something like that.

    If we're three to five and it's more casual, we'll play Fiasco.

    When we're in four or more and geeks who want fantasy we'll play Burning Wheel or some edition of D&D, WHFRP if neither seems right.

    If we're gamers and friends, three to five, and want to get right to the story, we'll play Apocalypse World or one of its hacks.

    If we're not in a mood to roleplay or if we're at least 6 we'll play Twilight Imperium.

    If the roleplaying mood is low we'll also play the Battlestar Galactica Boardgame or Game of Thrones boardgame.

    If we're feeling nostalgic we'll play a fake Magic the Gathering draft.

    If I want to see you kick ass, I'll pull out my prints of Danger Patrol.

    If we want something darker, I'd suggest Montsegur 1244 or Grey Ranks.

    If we're not in a gaming mood we go for food and watch a movie.
