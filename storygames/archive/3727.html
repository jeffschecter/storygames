    \n Similar to Deviant Art, Elfwood (www.elfwood.com) - like DeviantArt it's not public domain, you need to ask permission from the artists, but it's likely to be given if your project is non-profit. Because the standard differs widely, I'd search for "modpick fairy" - 'modpick' means it'll only bring up pictures that've gotten a Moderator's Choice award ie ones that are fairly good.
