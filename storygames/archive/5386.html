\n I'm taking preorders for the spiffy revised edition of Steal Away Jordan until Wednesday, January 23rd. I will have some at Dreamation, so if you pre-order and will be at Dreamation, let me know and I will bring your copy with me.

New stuff in this edition: The rules haven\u2019t changed much. I cleaned up the text, redesigned the layout to make it clearer and prettier, added more graphics and information on playing. It\u2019s a little over 70 pages. The price will still be $20 (plus shipping).

If you have the first edition and are interested in this new one, I offer a .pdf version for free, or a hard copy for $15. Email me for more information.

If I have any left after Dreamation, I will send them to IPR. Otherwise I will restock there sometime in February. I'm waiting for the proof from Lulu, and it should be available there after Dreamation. My Lulu page is set up, but *don't order from there* till after Dreamation!

Check out the preview at Lulu, (but don't order it from there just yet!) Pre-order from me.

    Posted By: Rich StokesI'm interested, it sounds like you're going to put this on Lulu soon, but you've had some printed elsewhere in the meantime. Is that correct? What are the reasons for that? I'm just curious about the process.
    \nThat's correct.
    I wanted to try out lulu to see if was a feasible option for increasing the availability. I also really like lulu's preview function.
    I wanted to have printed copies available at Dreamation, and I need to restock IPR. The nice thing about lulu is that if I run out of copies and it will be a while before I can print another batch, I can send people to lulu to buy a single copy. It's still cheaper to print it in batches locally and send them to IPR than it is to have lulu print them and send them to IPR.

    Bottom line, I want to make it available at more than one venue. I think IPR sold out in October, which was okay while I worked on revisions, but now that revisions are done, if I sell out one place, I want it to be available other places until I can restock.

    And in all this, I'm still trying to decide on whether I'll sell SAJ in pdf form.

    \n Posted By: PartheniaAnd in all this, I'm still trying to decide on whether I'll sell SAJ in pdf form.
    \n
    \nMy hard and fast rules about selling in PDF:
    \n
    \nDo sell in PDF form if one of your goals with the game is to make money.
    \n
    \nDon't sell in PDF form if the physical artifact of the book is an important part of the experience of the game.
    \n
    \nLook at a PDF sale as a potential print "upsell" opportunity.
    \n
    \nLook at giving out complimentary PDFs as advertising.
