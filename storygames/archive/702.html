Hey all, after a long hiatus I'm going to begin the long process of reconstructing my www.j-rpg.com site (basically a site which talks about all sorts of weird and cool Japanese tabletop RPGs in English).

I figured before I begin (I'll probably introduce one game with a review every 1-3 weeks) I was wondering if anyone had interest in reading about games from other countries.

If so, cool, I'm not looking for encouragement so much (cause I'm doing it anyway), btu what I am looking for is... "why". That is, why are you interested? And what I mean by that is that I'm totally trolling for questions that you might have about games from Japan.

Anything you want to know about games in general? Japanese gamers? Gaming culture?

I'm looking to create a kind of FAQ of sorts, or at least a few one-page articles where I talk about an aspect of the games or gaming in general, but being used to the games it's hard for me to think of things that might interest other folks looking in.

So, if the idea that "Some guy is gonna have a website where he introduces some cool foreign games", and that strikes an "Oh, heck I'll check that out for a few secs to see if there's anything interesting there" bone with you, what sorts of things do you think you'd like to hear about?

Be as specific or general as you like. There are no dumb questions. Probably.

-Andy
