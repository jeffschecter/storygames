\n Hey there!

My partner and I are looking for some more folks to play games with. We're anglophones, but since my french is about the level of maybe a two and a half year old to four year old, perhaps it would be fun to play Clover in french! We've got at least one housemate who is getting pretty into playing, but often has a rather prohibitive schedule, who might jump in from time to time. We're a super inclusive bunch, queer-positive, and very friendly.

To give you an idea of the kinds of games we're into: Dogs in the Vinyard and The Shab-Al-Hiri Roach are perennial favourites around our house, but we're looking to branch out. Hot Guys Making Out, Kagematsu, 1001 Nights, and Polaris are of particular interest right now, and I'm still waiting for the right group to play Poison'd with. Apocalypse World and it's descendants aren't super popular here, sadly. We've tried a bunch of times in recent memory and the system is just not hooking us.

We're also really open to playtesting new games, so if you're local and got something in the works you should definitely get in touch. I'll probably swing by with something every once and a while myself.

If you're interested, please give me a shout!
