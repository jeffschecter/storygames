    Classes Of Warfare
    Set in a Stalinist State at war.
    The players control characters from different spheres of power. The Dictator, the Politician, The General, The Kommisar, The Soldier, The Worker, The Prisoner and The Child all are options.
    Each scene centers around one of these characters and the decisions and outcomes of the scene influence the next. Winning the war might take the sacrifice of other characters life and freedom and terrible decision might need to be made to fulfill orders.

    The Cloud Factory

    \n

    Suddenly, it happened. After an almost fail free run, people were now rushing to their posts. They all heard the loud voice: "There's been a sabotage, and I know who did it." God is merciless. You all now that, and one of you has used it to their advantage. The Cloud Factory is a game about jealousy in one of God's creations, and the only way to get rid of someone is by framing that person. Each day start with the Plan of the Day where the players place resources to make the factory work. By smart placement you can steer the game to make someone loose, and who's your target is decided by the social interaction that takes place during the rest of the days. Someone is eventually going to loose in Paradise.

    Gas Day

    Your city is under capitalist gas attack! A blanket of death has been draped over your city and only you and a handful of comrades have your masks handy. Rally your city against the assault by searching out groups of survivors in tall buildings or sealed shelters and search for stashes of masks or rip them right off the faces of the enemy! But beware! Not all comrades face adversity with bravery and not every enemy is so brainwashed that he will die for his master.

    White Knights
    War is at hand! Tanks roll across the countryside flattening everything in their path. Fighter planes and bombers fly overhead seeking targets to devastate on the ground below. The horrors of war have summoned you the White Knights, ghosts of battle ready to strike vengeance on the enemies of the Motherland. But even all your spectral powers amount to nothing in the face of the horrors of modern warfare. What does a ghost of war do when even they are scared by the acts of men?

    \nStomp the District



    Exploring the under-examined intersection of soviet realism and modern dance blockbusters like Step Up, Stomp the District is about two rival marching bands in early 1930s Minsk. The city boomed, energy was high, and residents attempted to adjust as quickly as possible to their modernizing world. You explore how these rapid changes impacted youth culture and inter-district tensions, as well as exploring the cutting rivalries and tremendous feats of instrumentality & grandeur present within the soviet marching band scene.

    Das Lotterie

    It is time once again comrades to prepare for the lottery. Who will be chosen to work the mines? Who will wait in line for bread? Who will be first? Who will serve as the commissars' concubine? Fate is left to the wheel, but chance goes to those who know the value of vodka, jeans, and a pack of cigarettes. Trade your contraband in the black market, and bargain for a better place in line\u2026 or a new spin of the wheel.

    REDNECK
    A story game of heroic workers resistance for 3 to 5 Komsomolets or interested comrads.
    Set in Capitalist America this game strinkingly shows the necessity of a solidarity between all workers by putting the players in the role of the exploited american coalminer.
    Experience the poor life of the american worker! Get organized! Join the fight for justice against impossible odds! Dice and your friends decide about your sucess while the evil mine owners try to bring you down.
