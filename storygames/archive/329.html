    Disasters I have seen:

    The most recent thing that really made me scream is Vampire: The Requiem with the "Lancea Sanctum". This is nonsense, as any pupil would recognize after 4 weeks of Latin.

    The German distributor corrected it to "Lancea Sancta", which means "holy lance", only to look up a Latin word for "Blood Potency" (the literal translation in German doesn't sound nice) in the dictionary. Of course they chose"potestas", which means power - but only "political power". They should have renamed the Clans to Conservatives and Social Democrats, too.

    If you like the old Mage: The Ascension be assured that almost all the Hermetic names for the Spheres are wrong.

    In Nobilis there are several mistakes, too. (Like "Valde Bellum" = "very war") OK, there is a page where Rebecca corrects the mistakes, but I haven't really understood, why she wouldn't use the right words in the first place.
