\n Hi folks,

The Wake of Zenas Quantum is now available! Written by professional good guy Jared Axelrod and featuring a new original illustration by Jennifer Rodgers, this is a superhero-themed Guided Play Scenario for Annalise.

The blurb:

When Zenas Quantum, the Superpositioned Man, dies in Oubliette Asylum, the Heroic Agency breathes a collective sigh of relief. No longer do they have to protect Saga City from a man who can be everywhere at once. But not long after Zenas\u2019s death, strange goings-on remove any ease the heroes might have. Is it a copycat, a clone, a government plot, an alien? Or merely coincidental? Is Zenas Quantum dead after all, or are the members of the Heroic Agency merely jumping at shadows?

Guided Play Scenarios are short, free supplements for Annalise that give the group an established setting, situation and cast of characters with which to play the game. They are intended mostly for one-shot, introductory or convention play, but are also suitable for a longer-term game, depending the groups level of investment in the material.

You can download the 2-page PDF directly, or get it from RPGnow or lulu.com.

The Wake of Zenas Quantum joins Dracula Reloaded and The Voyage of the Auspicious in the Annalise Guided Play Scenario library.

Thanks!
