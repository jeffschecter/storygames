\n Is it Albert Pike? (thanks, KAJ)

Is it Garibaldi? (thanks, New Yorker i bought for a plane ride)

Mazzini connects them, but isn't quite as interesting.

I am thinking of a Morningstar-style TSOY one-shot that brings together Garibaldi and Albert Pike and some others, probably set ~1850 when Garibaldi was working in a candle factory in Staten Island and Pike had just finished up the Spanish-American war. Or maybe about 1870, when Garibaldi was participating in the Franco-Prussian war and Pike was at the height of his Masonic career.

But I need maybe 3 or 4 more amazing personages of the period for this little LXG-eaque experiment. Who else fits the bill?

    \n I'm a big fan of Augustus Pitt Rivers, who was not only a successful officer in the Crimean War, during which time he also became a prominent scientist. He is also one of the forefathers of modern archaeology, ethnography and anthropology as a result of inheriting estates that had lots of ancient barrows on them and taking an interest in digging and cataloguing them properly, and eventually became Britain's first Inspector of Ancient Monuments.

    Me and the missus are naming our firstborn son after him (seriously).
