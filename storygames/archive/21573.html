\n I've gotten back into experimental short games again; some of these hit the mark, to the point where @Rafu has been playing a 13 episode campaign of one of them! Anyway, here's the most recent two:

On the Way up is an exercise in the technique of 'playing to lift', inspired by this great article on the subject by the wonderful Elina Gouliou.

I've also had magic on the brain for the last couple of months, looking at different ways it can be included in a game: Why Don't You Use Magic? proposes that casting spells is just about the only way to get anything done, just as an experiment in seeing what that would look like in game play.
