\n The Solipsist 15 minute demo pack got it's first test drive at UK Games Expo. I wasn't there to see it used (Gregor did the demos) so I am keen to get feedback from anyone who read it, watched it, or most of played it, to see what I can improve for the next version (which we will have on the website as a download).

So, did anyone here play in a Solipsist demo? If so how was it? Did you like it? Hate it? Understand it? If you own Solipsist or have played it elsewhere did the Demo do a good job of communicating an interesting part of Solipsist play?

Specific questions\n
    \n
  1. Did the rules presented in the demo (confronting the Shadow, changing reality) make sense as provided?
    2. \n
  2. Was the situation (attacked by dog-demons) interesting?
    3. \n
  3. Did you succeed or fail at making the change and overcoming the attack?
    4. \n
  4. If you failed were the consequences (or non-consequences) of failing clearly explained in a way that made it clear that it was a minor setback at most and fun was still to be had?
    5. \n
\nAlso if you played through and made an interesting change of reality, please let me know what it was.
