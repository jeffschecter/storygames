    \n I'm pretty divided on Avatar. On the one hand, it's got this really detailed, deep, and superbly realized world, but as everyone has pointed out, a cliched and dumb story. With a more complex, nuanced story, would Avatar still have been the box office smash it was, or would audiences have tuned it out for more simple fare? Someone said somewhere that if you're going to risk 350 million dollars of Hollywood's money, you sure as hell aren't going to take any risks with the script - just give the audience something that goes down easy and doesn't require much thought, and distract them with the pretty pictures.

    SciFi movies can be intellectually stimulating, but when they are, they usually don't do so well at the box office. Look at Blade Runner.

    I don't hate Avatar but I don't love it either.
