\n Games Galore is an analogue game design challenge to design a game by the end of every month you are able over the course of a year. This is a challenge, not a competition. The only one you will be competing against is yourself - which is certainly enough. Hitting each month is not a requirement, just a goal which you can get as close to as you are willing and able.

This year, Games Galore will have:\n
    \n
  • Monthly Themes with \u201cingredients\u201d or other seeds to inspire your games
    • \n
  • Design Challenges to encourage trying something different
    • \n
  • Encouragement to get feedback on your designs and refine your games over the course of the year
    • \n
\nI will be posting the first theme on January 1st. I hope you\u2019ll join me for Games Galore this year.
