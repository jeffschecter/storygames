    \n
    \n Sessions are scheduled from anything from 2 to 8 hours, with the average being 4-5 hours. However, the relative amount of game and chatter is very variable. If people are tired or haven't seen each other since a long time, chatter gets important. Sometime, after, 1 to 1.5 hour of chatter only, we declare that the session is a non-gaming one. We still talk about gaming, but do not game. (we play to have fun\u2026 if it seems we'll have more fun by not playing, we unschedule the game).

    Sometime, a guest drops in and shatters the game. This is what happened last time: a gamer's girlfriend came and we could not play at all (she's all nice and friendly, but she's very loud and won't give up any topic until you agree with her).
