\n In the spirit of Judd's library thread, I'd like help coming up with ways to make injuries gained from noteworthy combats more interesting and lingering, instead of just some lost HP to be healed.

Here's an example:

The Stumbling Berserker

By the time we finally brought the dragon down, it had clamped it's jaws tight around my leg. We had to pry it's mouth open with a spear. The cleric tried the best he could, but somewhere inside my knee there's still a tooth lodged. It wakes me up at night with visions, sometimes, especially when there are dragons about. It's also causing something foul to seep into my blood, slowly, not enough to kill me dead, but sometimes, in the heat of battle, my knee burns and I have trouble walking, but a blind rage boils up inside of me and I can't stop until everyone else is dead.
