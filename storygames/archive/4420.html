\n I'm taking October off work, and building a game up to 'ashcan' levels. It build off a lot of my other games (microcosm, exchange, dustbowl prophets, pulse, and several others). Much of the work will be done in public, over here:

http://hoard-rpg.blogspot.com/

I've wanted to try this for a long, long time. But I am, frankly, utterly terrified by actually doing it. I'm not sure if I'm trying to shout "Look at me!" or "Oooh, fuck, geeeeeeeeeeronimo!", here. Both, probably.

    Posted By: Mark CauseyCould you enable your feed on the blog?
    \nUrm.

    I've deliberately disabled it, at present, because the posts won't necessarily be in a useful order. In many ways, I'm treating it more like a Wiki than a blog. But I really, really like the blog software, and don't want to drop time from my month to get good at Wiki.

    The left-side navigation will be updated (and sections will be built) right on the spot; if I need to post items with "wrong dates" to get proper placement, I happily will. So, sure, I could enable the feed, but I have no idea if it would be useful, or just a pain in the ass - and I really don't want to effectively prompt people to subscribe to something that might be a pain in the ass.

    Uh, if that even makes sense...?
