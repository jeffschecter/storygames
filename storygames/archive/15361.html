    \n Im trying to get the names of the traits for the characters right. How does this sound?
    \n
    \nHeat
    \n- When you use violence or threats of violence you roll heat.
    \nGuts
    \n- When you act under fire you roll guts.
    \nPresence
    \n- When you lead or manipulate others you roll presence.
    \nLuck
    \n- When you act on impulse you roll luck.
    \nAcuity
    \n- When you try to assess the situation you roll acuity.
    \nVigor
    \n- When you need to be resilient or strong you roll vigor.
