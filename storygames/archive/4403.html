\n I'm making a random car chase generator and need some random stuff I can toss in. what I've got so far:
\n
\nTurns: Left, right, U turn, travelling with or against the flow
\n
\nStreets: One-way, two-way, with or without median, freeways, residentials, business district, tight, wide lanes, winding, hilly
\n
\nTraffic: empty, light, medium, heavy, jammed
\n
\nRandom events: pedestrian, cross traffick, wreck ahead, police join the chase, car trouble for a participant
\n
\nWhat else can I toss in?
\n
\nThanks.
