\n I'm prepping some stuff for a TSoY game.

I've got this NPC, a creepy Eastern Europe-inspired kind of noble with all sorts of twisted and disgusting appetites.

This noble has been in prison for years and years, and is finally being released--ready to resume their position of power as the Lord of a small mining town.

I need a name!

Here's the thing:

This noble has really kind of messed themselves up. The details aren't important, but their personal appearance is really kind of unnatural (thinking along the lines of unsuccessful surgical procedures, like medieval plastic surgery).

No one knows whether this NPC is a man or a woman!

So, I need a good, evocative name. One that does not reveal the NPC as male or female.

A moniker, like, "The [...]" or even the "The [...] of [...]" is also an option.

    \n Keys and secrets you say?

    Key of the Creepy Guy
    1 xp whenever you act generally creepy in public.
    2 xp when you act creepy even though it might be problematic
    5 xp when you act really creepy and it'll probably have some serious consequences.
    Buyoff: Give up your creepy-guy image and become a normal, upstanding citizen

    Secret of the Thriller
    Your character's dancing skills can wake the dead. By making a successful dance check, you can summon several zombies as back up dancers. However, they can't actually fight, only dance. At least you'll look cool, and you might totally freak some people out. On the other hand, people might dance along with you.
    Cost: 2 points of instinct and several hundred dollars licensing fees
