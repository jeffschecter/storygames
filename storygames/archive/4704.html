\n They're still in beta, with the kinks not all worked out yet. But I'm excited enough that I need to share.

So, let's say you want to get results from the Jason Morningstar Game Generator every so often in your favorite rss aggregator. Here's what you should do:

1) figure out the name of the page you want results from, in this case "Jason Morningstar Game Generator"
2) change any spaces to + signs: "Jason+Morningstar+Game+Generator"
3) figure out the name of the subtable you want to call from the page. On most pages, "main" is the most useful, but you could do a specific subtable if it suits your needs. Append this to the name of the page after a period: "Jason+Morningstar+Game+Generator.main"
4) append your page/table combo to this url: "http://www.random-generator.com/feed/index.php?tablename="
5) add your feed to your reader of choice: "http://www.random-generator.com/feed/index.php?tablename=Jason+Morningstar+Game+Generator.main"
6) (optional) if 10 results at a time is too many or too few, you can append an iterations variable: "http://www.random-generator.com/feed/index.php?tablename=Jason+Morningstar+Game+Generator.main&iterations=5"

The feed will give new results every time it is polled. However, Google Reader, for example, caches results and only checks every so often. Last night it was checking the ones I subscribed to about every 3 hours, but that may change over time. It should also be simple to add a feed to iGoogle or Yahoo. Here are a few fun feeds to try out:

http://www.random-generator.com/feed/index.php?tablename=ClothingFemale.FemaleOutfit
http://www.random-generator.com/feed/index.php?tablename=Fantasy+Town+Event.main
http://www.random-generator.com/feed/index.php?tablename=Oblique+Strategy.main&iterations=1
http://www.random-generator.com/feed/index.php?tablename=TheoryDiscussions.main&iterations=3
http://www.random-generator.com/feed/index.php?tablename=Wine+Review.main&iterations=3
http://www.random-generator.com/feed/index.php?tablename=Swords.main&iterations=5

As always, if there's something you'd like a generator for that isn't there, please add it!


Also, I'm looking at ways of making javascript widgets for people to put on their own pages that would allow them to embed results. I'm thinking, for example, that a widget that made Pilgrim Names on a page for "Do: Pilgrims of the Flying Temple" could be quite useful. If any of you have experience making little widgets and want to be a part of that development, please let me know.

I'm open to ideas on how to develop this new functionality to make it more useful, so please chime in if this is almost-but-not-quite-useful to you.
