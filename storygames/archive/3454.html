    \n We did 'Arabian Heists' last year in a very stylized, not very accurate Aladdin-type setting. Not with eleven people (the most I've ever had at a Fastlane table was 8 and . . . holy crap, um, never again.) but it was a fantasy world heist, full of flying carpet getaway drivers, fire-mages instead of explosive specialists, and so on. I'd be happy to do something similar this year.

    Heck, if my surgery gets more people to actually play Fastlane (which only got 1 play last GenCon I was at, although I brought the wheel in almost every day), even more of a bonus.

    My one worry right now is that plane ticket prices have gone up since Daniel first asked me. I'm honestly too scared to look until the disbursement happens. *crosses fingers*

    Edited to add: Oh, and since I didn't actually say it - THANK YOU ALL once again. You guys will probably get sick of hearing my thanks by the end of GenCon.

    So, thank you, all of you who contributed, and all of you that even thought about contributing. Hopefully next year I can make it there on my own!
