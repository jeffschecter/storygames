I know many of the regulars for Nerdly Beach Party don't hang around here much any more but I don't know how many new West Coast-ish people may have joined since the last time this event was advertised here.

Nerdly Beach Party is a gaming and camping trip held in San Simeon State Park about half way up the coast of California. It's held generally twice a year (once in the Spring and once in the Fall) but occasionally falls through. Anyway, if you're interested in attending this years Fall event the registration is now open:
