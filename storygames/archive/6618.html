\n So I can't go to Gen Con this year. wah wah wah. I'm a sad panda.
Since I can't go, I'm planning on trying to have a little gathering of friends to hang out, eat some food, drink some drinks, play some games.

I attempted something similar last year, around Labor Day, and it was fun, but we only played card/board games. This year I want to play some rpgs.

I'd like suggestions from you fine folks on what to try playing/running. I need stuff that: is one-shot friendly; can be picked up and learned really damn fast; is relatively light and fun; will not be disrupted by the consumption of alcohol; and that will work for people that a) haven't played an rpg before, b) haven't played any recently.

currently thoughts i have include Tribes, The Committee for the Exploration of Mysteries, Psi-Run, and The Sons of Liberty.
