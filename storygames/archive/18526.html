\n Games on Demand at Origins went really well! But we want to make it even better for next year. If you played with us, please consider taking a minute to fill out the feedback form, located here.

The basics: we ran 87 sessions of 40+ different games (IIRC), sat 361 players and overflowed our room four times. I personally had a blast running Vesna Thaw, World Wide Wrestling and Hell 4 Leather, and playing The Quiet Year, Dog Eat Dog, InSpectres, Monsterhearts, Dread, and Saga of the Icelanders. Man.

If you didn't get a chance to play with us, you should really think about it for next year! Origins is a great "destination" con, in my opinion. And next year is going to be even more fun, if you can believe it!
