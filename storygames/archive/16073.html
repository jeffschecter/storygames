\n Hello! I have recently published my first full-length Savage Worlds Fan World, called Wings and Tails. It's fully illustrated!
\n
\nWings and Tails is a high adventure, steampunk roleplaying game about the clashing worlds and dangerous politics of the frontier Mouse Kingdoms.
\n
\nThis game is written as an expansion to Savage Worlds Explorer's Exdition, and includes optional rules to integrate "Wings of War" WW1 air combat boardgames rules into your roleplaying adventure.
\n
\nIn addition to an illustrated world history, six playable character archetypes, and 19 pages of printable game resources, Wings and Tails contains a complete 4 chapter adventure, fully scripted with branching story lines. Designed to be run in 4 evenings, the adventure is perfect for novice Game Masters, and has enough depth to thrill veteran adventurers as well.
\n
\nEnjoyable by furries and non-furries alike, the animals enable the GM to present the difficult theme of colonialism and racism in a way that players find fun and approachable. Think Animal Farm. Also, it's adorable.
