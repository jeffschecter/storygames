Not quite an ashcan, but interesting. I love how the character creation and situation creation mechanics seem to be one and the same. So while the patient is suffering from some weird tropical disease (that turns out to be incidental to the real problem), the doctors can investigate each other's ailments and oddities. Not sure about one thing: the House always wins, but sometimes the patient dies. Is this GM fiat or not?
