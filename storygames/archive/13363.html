\n I'm looking for some mental fuel... recommendations of games that already do what I'm thinking about...

Which is: my abandoned Game Chef attempt was (sort of) a "last human fleet" type story based around the idea of players controlling "factions" within the fleet. The (unrealized) idea was that the factions don't have stats of their own; instead, the fleet as a whole has certain traits, and the factions have ways of using and manipulating those traits.

There must be games out there that work on a similar notion of "shared traits". I'm keen to see how they worked the idea. What games are they?
