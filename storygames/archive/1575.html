On my way out to work, but I had a quick idea in the shower and wanted to toss it up here for your consumption:

After a mission is discussed and planned (according to Wilderness of Mirrors rules), combat scenes are established and resolved entirely through military-style hand gestures (similar to Mridangam), following a structured conflict flowchart (similar to Polaris) that may or may not lead to dice rolls.

EDIT: And yes, I'm fully aware that this is utter blasphemy. :P
