    \n First, Ben and everyone else participating - I think it's awesome (I gave to MSF before the drive).

    Zac's post put me in mind of a general point.* Part of the controversy over the Red Cross after 9/11 had to do with funds donated for 9/11 being used for other operations. While people should get what they agreed to in general, I would urge people, whatever organization they give to, to donate general funds and not earmark them specifically for Haiti. There will be other crises, and preventative measures are undertaken by many of the same organizations doing response now. Especially when there is a big, well-publicized crisis like Haiti, there can often be so much donated that it would be better if some of the response could be devoted to other operations.

    * This really isn't a cryptic way of trying to start a fight with Zac - if I wanted to do that, I'd do it in private.
