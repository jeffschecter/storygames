\n Yeah, so there was this thread a little while ago, about how DWeird was playing some microdungeons games with a resolution mechanic Benhimself came up with. Anyway, I liked it a lot. So much so, that I formalized the rules and added a bunch of stuff to them, in order to flesh them out a bit. cuz i r gaim ziner soz i ritez a gaymz.

It's called Enter the Dungeon.

Check it out, I hope you like it.
