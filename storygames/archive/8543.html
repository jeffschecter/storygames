\n Mortal Coil Revised will be coming out soon! I am finishing up final edits to the layout now and will be sending it off to print in the next week or two.

To celebrate, I'm hosting a contest. Anyone who publicly posts about a game of Mortal Coil they have played and then links to it on the Mortal Coil forum or notifies me of the post (send an e-mail to madhatter at galileogamesdotcom) will be automatically entered (one entry per person). A random drawing will determine the winner when the new edition comes out. If you win, you get a custom set of Mortal Coil poker chips in four colors along with a sweet metal carrying case.

If you already posted about your game online, don't worry! You can just point to your old post and be entered into the contest.

Void where prohibited.

    \n Be happy to, Daniel! I have adjusted the conflict rules to reflect the actual process I go through during play, eliminating a few things that I never use and streamlining some things that were confusing. I've also added two chapters, one of tips for players, the other of tips for GMs. These should help even veteran players get more out of their games. I've completely rewritten all of the examples, and I've included seven full conflict descriptions with example players doing a conflict from start to finish to demonstrate various permutations of the conflict system. All in all, the page count has about doubled. I also got two brand new pieces of art from Jennifer Rodgers for the book, and had Jonathan Walton do the layout. I have to say, he's done a better job than I did in the first edition.
