    \n Jackson, I am your Vancouver (BC) connection, right here. Let me hit you across the face with the info you request.

    First of all, join Random Acts of Story: raos.naos.org/smf. It's small, and it's only starting, but its Vancouver's online indie gaming community. There you can post about design, the playtesting sessions we do, plan out game sessions, shoot the shit, and get a leg up on what's going on in Vancouver. Membership is based on approval, but you'll be approved.

    Games we're doing soon: Zorcerer of Zo (Polish, post-war children flee to The Woods to escape the realities of their homeland, and in doing so enter a mythical land of adventure and uncertainty), Cheap (noirporn psychosexual drama, with an independant system I'm creating for it), Kin playtesting (it's a game of family, reunion, and identity. Super fun).

    Games we're open to: basically anything.

    Second of all, join the Vancouver Gaming Guild: www.vancouvergamingguild.com. Their forums are pretty d&d 3.5 geared, which is why we created our own community, but...
    a.) It is the largest Vancouver gaming forum, and...
    b.) they organize Vancouver's cons.

    I think Findplay was the site you are talking about, but I also think it's defunct.

    My email is joepub88@hotmail.com if you want to talk more.
