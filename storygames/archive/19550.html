\nDonjon is a cool game that was released under a Creative Commons license over a decade ago. (and now I feel old)

Have there been any games that have picked up the baton and run with it? I like the way that it works with defining facts with successful rolls but being very gamist at heart, but I'd like to see other takes on it, or things written after lessons were learned.

So, does anyone know of anything that is like Donjon, builds on Donjon, or is influenced by Donjon?
