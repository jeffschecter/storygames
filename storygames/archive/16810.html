\n So I'm going to be running a Monsterhearts one shot set in the Dresden universe. I'm coming up with a few custom playbooks (most of the originals will also be available.)

The APPRENTICE is mostly a hack of the Witch; where the Witch works as a self trained hedge magician, the apprentice tends to be someone trained within the White-Council style of magic, and has powers more in line with what the wizards in Harry Dresden do.
(Also, Witches can learn up to 2 Wizard Spells, and Wizards can learn up to 2 Witch Hexes, so there can be some overlap.)

I considered making Soul Gaze a move, but instead, I think it's just color on one of the basic moves: if a wizard locks eyes with someone, they both Gaze into the Abyss.

THE APPRENTICE

So you're a wizard. You would think that would be fun and games, except you've got an overbearing mentor making sure you learn magic the right way, a whole council of overbearing wizards just waiting for you to slip up and prove to them you're evil, and you're not even allowed to use your spells for anything fun.

Well, maybe it's time to change things up. You have a power coursing inside of you, and woe be to those who stand in your way.

Names:
David, Elisabeth, Harriet, Jonathan, Maximus, Minerva, Prudence, Roland, Severus, Victoria

A powerful name, a virtuous name, a complicated name, an ancient name.

Look:
Nervous, scrawny, old-fashioned, stubborn.

Cunning eyes, powerful eyes, soulful eyes, eyes that contain the universe.

Origin:
Child of a wizard, stumbled into arcane secrets, trained by a dark mentor, otherworldly patron.

Stats
Hot -1, Cold -1, Volatile +1, Dark +1 (choose and add +1 to one Stat)

Apprentice Moves:
You get Spellcasting and Sympathetic Tokens, and choose one more:

*Spellcasting
You can cast spells. Start with two. To cast a spell, spend a sympathetic token during a secret ritual, or meet the target's gaze and chant at them in tounges. Then roll with the appropriate Stat for the spell. On a 10+, the Spell works. On a 7-9, it works, but choose one:
*The casting drains you, take -1 forward to Spellcasting until you have a chance to rest.
*The spell has unexpected side effects.
*Trigger your Darkest Self.

*Sympathetic Tokens
You gain power from sympathetic tokens: items of personal significance you take from others. A Sympathetic Token counts as a string and can be used to power ritual magic.

*Blackest Magicks
Whenever you violate a Law of Magic, gain xp. (To violate a Law of Magic, Kill with Magic, Conrol the Minds of Mortals, Raise the Dead, Summon Demons, or Transform Mortals).

*Toil and Trouble: When you have time and access to a proper work area, you can brew up a potion. Name a condition, such as Angry, Forgetful, or Madly in Love. Whoever drinks the potion gains that condition for a few hours. Player characters must Hold Steady to resist acting in accordance with the condition. NPCs will have their sense of self dominated by that condition.

*Unbidden Summoning: When you whisper the true name of an entity, roll + Dark.
On a 10+, they come to you, and you gain a String on them.
On a 7-9, they come to you, and will listen to you.
On a miss, they come to you, and are angry for the interruption.

Wizard Spells:

*Elementalism (Volatile)
You conjure fire, cold, or lightning. Elementalism deals 2 harm to one target, or 1 harm to everyone in an area.

*Hexing (Dark)
You cause a piece of technology to short out and malfunction for a few minutes. Cameras cease to record, guns jam, computers blue screen.

*Memory Wipe (Dark)
You cause the target to forget a few minutes, up to an entire hour. On a 10+, you can also put alternate memories in their place.

*Transmute (Volatile)
You turn something or someone into something else. A pencil becomes a knife or crowbar. A person a frog or statue. A vampire a lawnchair. Eventually, the changes wear off. You can't create life from nothing, but you can turn living things into inanimate things.

*Veil (Dark)
Veil hides you behind a web of illusions, making you appear invisible. As long as you concentrate, the spell persists.

Backstory

You start the game with two Sympathetic Tokens. Decide who they came from, and what they are.

You have a Mentor who keeps an eye on you. They gain 2 strings on you.

You wish you could be friends with one of the others. They gain a string on you.

Advancement
*Take another Apprentice move.
*Take the remaining spells.
*Learn two Witch hexes. You cast them with the Spellcasting move.
*Collaborate with the MC to create a new Spell.
*Take a move from another skin.
*Take a move from another skin.
*You belong to a Wizard's Cabal.
*Add +1 Hot (max +3)
*Add +1 Cold (max +3)
*Add +1 Volatile (max +3)
*Add +1 Dark (max +3)
Sex Move

When you have sex with another character, you both erase each other's highlighted stats, and then highlight Dark and choose another stat to highlight.

Darkest Self

You tap into a font of true power, and the time for action is now. You will make your will known. Your spells become supercharged, more powerful than you can probably responsibly wield, and they all have unexpected sideeffects. You escape your Darkest Self when someone you care about confronts you and talks you down.

    \n WHITE COURT VAMPIRE

    Let the blooddrinkers think they're the real vampires. You have what counts: beauty, wealth, power. There's a force more powerful in this world than blood; love.

    Love is what you feed on until there's nothing left.

    Names:
    Athena, Electra, Joachim, Kennedy, Lucretia, Manuel, Persephone, Tanner, Taylor, Vittorio

    A foreign name, a mythological name, a sensual name, an androgynous name.

    Look:

    Imposing, Intense, Noble, Radiant, Statuesque

    Beautiful Eyes, Cat-like Eyes, Shimmering Eyes, Sympathetic Eyes

    Origin:
    Favored Scion, Orphan, Political Pawn, Unwitting Heir, White Court Outcast

    Stats
    Hot +1, Cold +1, Volatile -1, Dark -1 (choose and add +1 to one stat)

    White Court Vampire Moves:
    Choose two:

    *Inflame Passions
    When someone else rolls to turn someone (not you) on in your presence, roll + Hot.
    On a 7-9, add +1 to their roll, and you gain a string on either them or their target.
    On a 10+, add +2 to their roll, and you gain a string on each of them.

    *Inspire Lust
    When you roll to turn someone on and roll a 10+, they must give themselves to you or roll to Hold Steady.

    *Manipulator
    Whenever you roll a 10+ to Manipulate an NPC, gain a string on them.

    *Physical Paragon
    The first time in a scene you would roll to Lash out with Violence, instead roll + Hot.

    *Plots within Plots
    Mark xp when you achieve your goals indirectly, by having others solve your problems for you unwittingly.

    *Sensual Touch
    When you have time and intimacy with someone and touch them, choose one of the following:
    You gain +1 forward, and they take -1 forward.
    You heal 1 wound, and they take 1 wound.
    You gain a string on them, and they lose a string on you.

    Backstory

    Let's face it: you're just that awesome. Gain a string on everyone.

    You actually care for another person. They gain 2 strings on you.

    Advancement
    *Take another White Court Vampire move.
    *Take another White Court Vampire move.
    *Take a move from another skin.
    *Take a move from another skin.
    *You have Mortal Admirers.
    *Add +1 Hot (max +3)
    *Add +1 Cold (max +3)
    *Add +1 Volatile (max +3)
    *Add +1 Dark (max +3)

    Sex Move
    When you have sex with someone, choose all three options from Sensual Touch, even if you don't have that move.

    Darkest Self
    People are things. Toys. Snacks. You take what you want from people, use them up, and they love you for it. You leave your lovers empty shells, and they still moan for more. You stop being your darkest self when you are confronted with evidence that your actions have harmed someone you genuinely care about.

    \n And here's the last of the Dresdenheart playbooks, the Red Court Vampire:

    RED COURT VAMPIRE

    Under your skin lurks a monster. A ravenous, blood drinking monster. You wear this mask of flesh to walk amongst your prey, to lure them into a false sense of security, and then you feed. The blood runs deep, and you lap it up.

    Names
    Antonio, Barbara, Bruce, Delphine, Desdemona, Enrique, Isabella, Ivan, Jezebel, Vladimir

    An aristocratic name, a foreign name, a poetic name, a name associated with slaughter

    Look

    Gangly, Hits the Uncanny Valley, Pale, Too Perfect, Uncomfortable in Your Own Skin

    Animal Eyes, Deceptive Eyes, Devious Eyes, Feral Eyes, Hungry Eyes

    Origin
    Sire's Plaything, Red Court Rebel, Recently Turned, Vampiric Lineage

    Stats
    Hot +1, Cold -1, Volatile +1, Dark -1 (choose and add +1 to one Stat)

    Red Court Vampire Moves
    Choose two:

    *Claw and Fang
    You can manifest a physical change to develop natural weaponry. You deal +1 harm in such a state.

    *Feeding
    You feed on hot blood. If this is the first time they've ever been fed upon, you both mark experience. When you feed, choose two:
    *You heal 1 harm.
    *You carry 1 forward.
    *They don't die.

    *Like a Bat Out of Hell
    You're fast. If someone tries to Run Away from you, you can give them -1 to their roll. You will always outrun fleeing NPCs.

    *Narcotic Saliva
    When another character ingests your saliva, roll + Hot. On a 10+, they are beholden to your commands. On a 7-9, the Narcotic Saliva works, but choose one:
    *They realize you hypnotized them somehow.
    *They screw up your commands.
    *Their sanity is unhinged.

    *Nightmarish Form
    While you are your Darkest Self, use Volatile instead of Cold to Hold Steady or Shut Someone Down.

    *Shedding Skin
    When you would take harm, you can instead choose to become your Darkest Self.

    Backstory

    You've got some plots going on. Take strings on two other characters, and say how they're involved.

    Someone else has seen your true form- and lived. They gain a string on you.

    Who sired you? They gain 2 strings on you.

    Advancement
    *Take another Red Court Vampire move.
    *Take another Red Court Vampire move.
    *Take a move from another skin.
    *Take a move from another skin.
    *You have Mortal Pawns.
    *Add +1 Hot (max +3)
    *Add +1 Cold (max +3)
    *Add +1 Volatile (max +3)
    *Add +1 Dark (max +3)

    Sex Move
    When you have sex with someone, trigger your Narcotic Saliva move, even if you don't have it, as if you had rolled a 10+. In addition, gain a string on them.

    Darkest Self
    You rip free from your human disguise and show your true form: a demonic, bat-like monster with slavering jaws and webbed wings. You crush those who threaten you, leaving them broken and bleeding. You stop being your Darkest Self when you are able to hole up for a few hours and regrow your second skin.
