\n Now that the Shatterzone Classic Reprint has been available for some time, and two of its supplements, Brain Burn and Shadow of a Black Sun are also out, I'd like to know which supplement you guys are looking forward to. Zeenarchs is probably next, but tell me what your next choice would be.
\n
\n
\n
\nHere are the supplements to be reprinted...
\n
\nArsenal (gear)
\nHardwear/Softwear (cyber)
\n
\nContact (aliens)
\nFleet: A Survivor's Guide
\nFringers Guide (outcast groups)
\n
\nTech Book: Ships (advanced ship combat and construction)
\nStock Ships (new ships)
\n
\nThrough the Cracks (adventure)
\nCrosshairs (adventure)
\n
\nGrimsyn Sector (campaign)
