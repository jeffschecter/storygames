Like the difficulty that the GM sets and the strength of the players' desire to succeed become the critical parts of resolution. Less random and more arbitrary, but with the arbitrariness shared between GM and players. I'm not sure how much that would effect the events that occured in play, but it would surely change the feel of resolution.

    \n Ha! I guess it depends how you interpret the OP.

    Did he mean "play the rules as written, rolling 3dF, but add + or - tokens instead of bonus/penalty dice", or did he mean "play TSOY diceless?" I thought he meant the former.

    In regular TSOY, it doesn't matter how many bonus dice you have, you'll never transcend unless you're already a Grand Master. In this variation, if you were rolling dice, it could happen on many more rolls. For example, if your skill is 2, anytime you spend two or more tokens, you risk Transcending.
