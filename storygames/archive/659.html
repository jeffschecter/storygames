    Hey Jason, thanks!

    Bearing: It's pretty good. Nothing making me want to destroy the site and set it on fire while playing the fiddle in the ashes.

    Moderation has been easy. Administration is easy sofar. People have been pretty rational. The nonrational have not been allowed in. Anyone on the road to self-destruction have been shown the door.

    Serverwise, it's all good: we're currently at 1/2 to 5/6 of the daily traffic of The Forge, and well under the soft limits that I set.

    Applicationwise, Vanilla is pretty slick and still doing what I want it to do. The conversations are still numerous without being confusing, and the software with extensions and all seems to work for now.

    Looming decisions: As soon as Vanilla 1.0 comes out, I'm totally throwing it up here. Aside from that, if conversations get far too complicated to navigate with the interface without being able to straighten it out, like in a year or whatever, then I may consider rolling to phpBB or something. But that's just a nascent thought.

    Other than that... er... full speed ahead?

    -Andy
