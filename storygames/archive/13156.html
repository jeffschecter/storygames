    \n I have used it. As I mentioned before, the thing I like best about it is that it's got a very focused skill list. As GURPS has known for years, the choice of a "what you can do" list really focuses a game, which is the main struggle of Vampire for anyone, regardless of experience level. Actually, the system used in the computer RPG Vampire: Bloodlines is quite similar. (Bloodlines is available on Steam, if you can get it running, I highly recommend it.) The focus of the quickstart is what makes it good for both Vampire player noobs and GM noobs. It basically says "okay, so you want to learn this crazy Vampire game. Here's an example of a Vampire game about these things. Try it out, see what you think. Do you like it, do you want to change it? Cool, you're ready for the big toybox."
