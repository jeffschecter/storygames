\n
\n An old tradition carried over from the Forge and the Gaming Outpost before that is to ask this set of questions:

What are the last three games you've played?

What are the three games you've enjoyed the most?

What are the three games you'd most like to play next?

I request no cross-chat about others' choices, or further explanation of your own choices. Generally, the act of thinking about this will spawn other threads, which is great.

Here's my answers:

What are the last three games you've played? Swords Against Fate, Archipelago, Mouse Guard

What are the three games you've enjoyed the most? Trollbabe, Primetime Adventures, classic D&D

What are the three games you'd most like to play next? Dust Devils, Swords Against Fate, Archipelago
