\n I know I've made noise about this game before, but: the Bliss Stage VN is quickly approaching a presentable, playable demo (with the first two Breifing-Mission-Interlude cycles, two weeks of in-game time): I think it's about time to start getting a faq together.

This is a Ben Lehman-approved (I hesitate to call it 'official') adaptation of his game Bliss Stage to a computer-game format, a visual novel and RPG where you, as the teenage boy Joshua Castellano Preston, must fight the aliens you call the Nightmares with love as your weapon and trust as your armor.

Here is a Formspring for the Visual Novel. Ask your questions here and there: any questions I answer here I will repost to the Formspring.

Thanks in advance for your questions!

I am also aware that I haven't been commenting on the forums at large as much as I could have, but that's simply because I've been working on the game and going to school and that's been eating up my time. Now, i can relax a bit and start forumhopping again.
