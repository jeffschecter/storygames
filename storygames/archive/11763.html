\n Jason Morningstar and I have made a new game! It's about vikings, love, sex, magic, violence and intrigue, and... look, let's just show you the intro. \n
The old King has no male heir, and his kingdom is crumbling. To the East, the neighboring land of Gardarike seems to get stronger and richer every year. The two kingdoms have never been the best of friends, and tensions are building along the border. Now, the King has only one chance to ensure the survival of his kingdom - marrying his daughter to the Earl of Gardarike...
\nLove in the time of Sei\xf0 uses the Archipelago rules, coupled with a ready-to-play set of characters, locations and events. It's specifically designed for high replay value, and is an excellent quickstart kit for those who want to just get right into the game. Highly recommended for teaching story games to new players!

We've played it with a few different groups, and are having consistent fun, so it's time to unleash this puppy and move on to external playtest. If you'd like to be in on the playtest, drop me a note at matthijs1000 (at) hotmail (dot) com, or just whisper me your e-mail address in this thread.
