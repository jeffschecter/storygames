New Identities "Witness protection program" now in english!

Witness Protection Program is an introspective role-playing game about people who, having found themselves \u201cin the wrong place at the wrong time,\u201d are forced to relinquish all that is dear to them and to start a new life shrouded by nostalgia and fear.

The game is meant for 3-5 players, one of whom will have special tasks and will serve as a mediator for the others. He will be referred to as GM in this book.

This project was born to help translate other games. We are not a very big game publisher. We are just a group of friends producing their own games indipendently and promoting RPGs all around!

You can, of course, download this game for free, but by donating the price of one coffee, you give us the possibility to translate our other games, Deus Opera, Holy Truth, Robot Commander and Dream of Me. All of these games have been published or are ready to be published in Italian.
