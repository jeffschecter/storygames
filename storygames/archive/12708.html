    \n Due to Gen Con run-about-ness, I didn't realize till after the ceremony that John refers to the non-winners as "runnerups", not 2nd, 3rd place like I announced. Because of the point system, there's ties for those positions in some cases, and I was just reading down a list. So, apologies to the following:

    Jason L Blair AND Neil Gow should be recognized for what I called "third place" indie RPG of the year, for Little Fears Nightmare Edition and Beat to Quarters, respectively.

    Danielle Lewon, Jason Morningstar AND Bill White should be recognized for what I called "third place" most innovative indie RPG, for Kagematsu, Fiasco and Ganakagok, respectively.

    Apologies!
