    Posted By: Luke Wheel
    Well, I liked these interviews because they had a focus -- the question set -- and because I was interested in the interviewees.

    What would be a good next set of questions? Who would be good subjects?
    \nThese are possibly the best questions I've ever heard asked on a gaming podcast. It's fascinating to hear designers have to compare their first and latest designs and to talk about what they learned from designing their games. And the last 3 questions really are gold--Ron had my favorite answer to them.

    I think you should ask this question set again, Luke, just to different people (Fred Hicks, John Harper, Ben Lehman, Emily Care Boss, Jake Richmond, etc.)

    If you asked a new question set, I'd love to hear questions focused on what people think their games say about them (Sorcerers are the ultimate in arrogance, for instance, and MLwM is about abusive/poisonous relationships--what do Ron and Paul think that says about them or where they were when they designed those games?).
