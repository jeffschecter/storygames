    \n Go Play SF Bay meets every 3rd Thursday of the month 6:30pm at Endgame in Oakland (next one is 7/19). GPSF has occasional game days Saturday at Game Kastle in Santa Clara. Both get announced on its twitter account @goplaysfbay.

    Endgame (twitter @EndgameOakland) runs three 1-day minicons/year (typically January, April, October) and hosts the Good Omens minicon in July, which is coming up 7/7.

    BigBadCon in October is by far the best multi-day RPG con in the bay area, but Kublacon and Celesticon also have some. There's also DunDraCon, which caters more to traditional RPGs.

Could be a game of Microscope where anything goes (from epic space opera to World War 2 drama to fantastic) and the focus shifts to points of interest, or perhaps a game of Fiasco where the fun is in watching the story unfold in unexpected ways. Imagination and communication is key!"
