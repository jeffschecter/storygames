    Posted By: HaakonPosted By: shreyasplanning a book of translated Norwegian indie games.

    Tell us more!

    \nWell I'll let Matthijs tell the details, but it's a project to collect a lot of the small games that has been made by people on the rollespill.net forum. You may have seen some of those before and there might even be a couple of articles but little is set as of now.
    (Did I say too much Matthijs?)

    That's about it, yes We've collected a batch of 15-20 small games, ranging from the gross tactical horror of "Zombieporno" to the art larp piece "New Voices in Art". This winter, we'll be editing and translating, aiming for a Lulu publication this spring.

    The aim of the book is to make some of the Norwegian gaming culture accessible to English-speaking countries. We've been stealing your ideas for ages, and it's time to give some back.

    The working title is "N\xc3\xb8rwegian Style".
