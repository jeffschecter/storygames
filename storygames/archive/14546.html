And, to start it off, I present Zannen Gakuen, somewhat in the style of one of my favorites, Ouran High School Host Club.


And I apologize right now if it may be a bit twonky, since literally I put this thing together in about four hours, three and a half of which was tinkering with the cover.

    \n While I have a chance, I'm just going to type up where my imagination is taking me with other things:
    Other Relationships: based on Teammates, Relatives, and Clans
    Needs: I'm thinking of To Get Revenge..., To Rescue..., To Destroy..., To Explore..., To Gain Respect..., To Avoid...
    Locations: hmmm... perhaps Iron Temple, Village of Nozomi, Caverns of Doom, Blood Citadel, The Nine Hells, Fields of Battle
    Objects: Armament, Charms, Artifacts, Subsystems, Trophies, and Spells, maybe?

    At least, the way I'm seeing this right now, is some planet devastated by war, waged between the demon hordes spewing forth from their fiery pits, and the human clans, armed with mighty Iron golems, powered by steam generated from a combined fire and water elemental field, held in check by the clerics of the Iron Temple. A flavor that mixes Mobile Suit Gundam, classic Robotech, and Record of Lodoss Wars.
