\n Hello,

A few fellow story gamers and I were excited to try out the new game by Jason Morningstar.

We played at the Story Games Seattle meetup and played with 6 players. The entire length of the game was approximately 3 hours. We took a single break in the middle. After character selection and map construction, we chose the following options for play:

Age 1

1.) Spirit of Place (Ghosts of children)
2.) Tomb Robber (An impressive dirk-wielding poacher)

Age 2

1.) Giant Tomb Spiders (seeking to repair glyphs protecting the Horror)
2.) A Family Seeking Shelter (and Orc Family)

Age 3

1.) Necromancer (seeking to free the Horror and control it himself.)
2.) Descendants (religious leaders of the church of the Eye looking for this tomb!)
3.) Army of Plunderers (who did us in!)

My thoughts on the experience can be found here on my blog.
