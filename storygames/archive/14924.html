\n I just received delivery of both Burning Wheel Gold and Bulldogs! here in Japan on the same day (08/15). Here is a shipping breakdown.

BWG
Sent from: Staten Island NY
Date Sent: 08/08 (8 days)
Type of shipping: Priority (Flat Rate Envelope, 4lbs. Max.)
Shipping weight: 2lbs
Cost: $13.25
Condition upon delivery: Perfect (2x bubble wrap, handwrapped)

Bulldogs!
Sent from: Washington NJ
Date Sent: 08/06 (10 Days)
Type of Shipping: Priority Mail
Shipping Weight: 2lbs
Cost: $30.31
Condition upon delivery: Lightly damaged (Bottom edge pinched/crushed/torn in one spot where the bubble wrap was thin. Unlucky.)

What have we learned?
The biggest difference in the two books are their dimensions, BWG being smaller). Bulldogs! was a Kickstarter "purchase", so the shipping wasn't out of my pocket. If ti was, There would be a Bulldogs!-sized gap on my bookshelf.
Recommendation:
Smaller form factors: I don't believe Bulldogs! would've suffered from reduced, BWG-sized dimensions. Of course, I'm being pretty me-centric about this, as someone living in the USA wouldn't be all that fussed. Still, if you are concerned with your overseas presence, producing a product that fits comfortably in a flat rate envelope can't hurt.
