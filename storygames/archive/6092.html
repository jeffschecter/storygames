    \n There is a section "playing with detail" that says that whenever you're called on to do something, add a detail.

    So "Mannak dodges the blow, dropping his head like a hanged man and then whipping his hair around under the blade."

    "I drop the statue on the ground, and a piece of its nose chips off as it bounces off the ground."
