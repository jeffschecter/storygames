\n hey there, story-games.

my story/puzzle game for one player is sitting pretty with about 30 copies remaining
in the first print run of the pre-final edition. as i travel around canada and the continental US,
my ability to mail them out to any interested parties may be impeded by such factors as
1) finding post offices
2) the hypothetical rain
3) my willingness to haul these things around
4) availability of internets where i'll be spending my time.

in light of all these things, i ask you to order a copy now if you forsee wanting to order one in the next couple of months.
since i want to keep it as a physical artifact, PDFs shall not be available. Please give me this chance to do well by you.\n
this is a card game that one plays with one's self. draw cards off the top of a normal deck of playing cards and look up their meanings in the
book. some cards are rooms, some are items. some are people.
discard is to the bottom of the deck, so encounters are in the same order. you never shuffle.

you are permitted to read parts of the card write-ups depending on what items you are carrying and who your companion is.
other parts are initially unexplored.
\nmore details of the game are discussed in this earlier thread


i am now asking for $12.50 within the continental US & Canada, including shipping. paypal siempre.
please contact me at

and much thanks.
