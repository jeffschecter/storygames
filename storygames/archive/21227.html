\n As mentioned before, I'm looking for freeform/semi-larp games to be played with 10-12 teenagers. Game sessions are 2 and 3 hours.

One game idea is to play people stranded on a deserted island after a ship-wreck or plane-crash.

Characters
- Captain, Child, Athlete, Technician, Pregnant Woman, Farmer, Elder, Priest, ...
- Each has strength/weakness
- Each player can choose a personal item and an item of general use that they have with them.

Scenes
- Survival (Where are we? Who is in charge?)
- Danger (animal attack, weather, ...)
- The Wounded (What to do with the wounded?)
- Natives (build relationship or fight?)
- Escape (rescuers or pirates?)

We also play with two facilitators, so we could bring in additional escalations or play additional characters (i.e. natives).

Any help with brainstorming or other suggestions (perhaps there is already a game for this?) is welcome!
