\n Which I was rather pleased to see actually, nice to see an rpg outside the FLGS ghettos as it were. My local comic shop, Gosh!, is a pleasant clean and well lit space with helpful staff and a mostly adult clientele, it's a lovely shop actually of any variety.

And it has the Mouse Guard rpg, I didn't look at it I'm afraid as the topic doesn't interest me, but I figure those of you who want to know what's in it by now anyway, but I did think it was good to see rpgs reaching beyond the usual outlets and thought some of you might be interested to hear about it.
