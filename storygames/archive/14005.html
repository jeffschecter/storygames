\n Indie Games on Demand, Bi-Weekly ONLINE game
PLEASE EMAIL ME IF YOU ARE INTERESTED IN THIS EVENT!!!!!!! {sethblevins through gmail}

This is something that I do on occasion for special game day events at our local store, and I am looking to try and do it more often, so I am seeking players online.

The idea is simple, you let me know if you are interested gaming, and we can discuss what game and at what time.

Current schedule of what I am looking at is something Bi-Weekly, from about 6-11pm EST on Saturday evening, or another day during the week, or Sunday afternoon, whatever.
Exact times and days are open to discussion for player needs.

Maptools, Skype, broadband internet connection, webcam, and a microphone are required.
If you do not have a web cam, get one, they are $10 on ebay!

You will at times be joining a live group at my table.

PLEASE EMAIL ME IF YOU ARE INTERESTED IN THIS EVENT!!!!!!!
