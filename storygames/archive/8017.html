\n I REALLY don't want to have to design a game to play this. I want to play this. Here's what I'm looking for:

-A game that recreates the feel of high, high end superhero stories. I'm talking a little bit about The Authority, but closer to The Monarchy (do you remember that one?) or maybe some Planetary stories. There are bits and pieces of this in lots of superhero comics like the JLA, JSA, or various Marvel stuff but it's always a small element. Gods, myths, and these huge ideas are what excites me. There's some Vertigo and ABC stuff that goes down this line.

-I'm not looking for four color as much as I'm looking for a more serious and slightly scary tone. The Invisibles and Unknown Armies are big influences. I like subtle characters and insane/ironic concepts.

-A large amount of room for lots of different character concepts and cosmologies. I know that more focused games work better, but I'd love a game that lets one player be a regular human with good common sense and leadership abilities and another be the universe B Christian God come to kill his brother. And I'd love if they could be teammates.

-Low prep, if possible. I'd probably be running something more old school if I didn't hate doing pre-gaming gaming.

-A simple system, yet crunchy enough to have engaging fights! I want a fun fighty game without miniatures that moves fast.

-A GM required

-Still fun, if not more so with just two people.

I've got a few on my shortlist, but they aren't really hitting it all the way, and I'm not a big fan of modifying games...
Darkpages, Nobilis, Marvel SAGA, UA, IAWA with a different oracle, Don't Rest Your Head, Solipsist, Capes, Truth & Justice, etc...

I guess what I want is a superhero version of Nobilis that has Sorensen-style prep and a Baker-type conflict system.

Any thoughts, or should I just bust out the notepad?

You could also tweak the existing descriptions of what Ranks can stretch to or even add a couple more brackets (I'm not especially keen on that personally, but if you grab the PDQ# rules freebie Chad's put some in there).
