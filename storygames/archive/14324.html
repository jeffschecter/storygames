MY LITTLE PONY
Diminutive equines, built for adventure, powered by the magic of friendship
Ability Scores: +2 Charisma; +2 Strength (pegasus) or +2 Intelligence (unicorn) (earth ponies may choose either bonus)
Size: Small
Speed: 8 squares
Vision: Normal
Languages: Common
Skill Bonuses: +2 Athletics, +2 Diplomacy

Cutie Mark: You get a further +2 racial bonus to your highest-rated skill.
The Horse\u2019s Mouth: You can only wield one weapon at a time, though it can be medium-sized, and you treat it as if you were holding it in two hands.
Gallop: You gain a +3 racial bonus to bull rush and charging attacks.
Horseshoes: You have two foot slots, but no hand or arm slots.
and choose one according to your breed \u2026
Earth Pony\u2014Workhorse: Encumbrance does not affect your move speed.
Pegasus\u2014Flutter: You ignore difficult terrain.
Unicorn\u2014Magic Horn: You gain one mage cantrip of your choice. You can also wield a second weapon, shield, implement, whatever, telekinetically.

Friendship is Magic: You have the friendship is magic power.

FRIENDSHIP IS MAGIC ~ My Little Pony Racial Power
You used to wonder what friendship could be, until your comrades shared its magic with you.
Encounter \u2726 Arcane
Free action\tPersonal plus one ally
Trigger: You take an aid another, aid attack, or aid defense action.
Effect: Both you and the beneficiary of your aid action can either use your second wind, or make a saving throw. You decide independently which to do\u2014you don\u2019t have to take the same benefit.
