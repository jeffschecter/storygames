    RPG.net and EndGame bring you the April Fool's 2006 Mini Con.

    But really, this is no Joke.

    Saturday April 1, EndGame along with our sponsors, RPG.net presents you with a full day of Role-playing goodness. We are currently on the hunt for GMs willingto run their favorite games in 4 hour sessions. The only criteria we have is that 1) The game must currently be in print, and 2) we are really looking for games with more of an Indy bent to them. I personally would love to get a session of Dogs in the Vineyard or Polaris in...But, if you like D&D and want to share it with others, that's just great too!

    The sessions will run at 10am-2pm, 3pm-7pm, and 7pm-11pm. There are 10 tables available per time slot, so email now to sign up to run a game! Players, check the event listing on EndGame's event's calendar. As we get GM's signed up, we will be posting game's and their time slot. It will be first come, first serve. Players, there will be a $3 fee per time slot, with GM getting store credit for running the game.

    There will be door prizes and generally a whole lot of role-playing fun. Contact Chris Hanrahan to sign up as a GM now, or for any questions you might have. See you then!
