\n I will be running a game in which the PCs will be traveling down the River Styx.

(specifically while riding on "Demonwing" the vessel from the classic D&D module "A Paladin In Hell")... In the adventure the PCs watch from the sterncastle as 30 spectral trolls, 3 skeletal pirates and the glabrezu ship captain sail the river down.


The PCs will be traveling from the 1st layer of Hell down to the 6th layer of Hell.
What strange, creepy, neat, interesting events and spectacles could they witness?

Give your best "narrator" (or movie voice!!) view of the shores and waters and skies, to read aloud monologue while they travel down.
What Lower Planar sights will the PCs to see as they sail?!??


EXAMPLES (from the module "A Paladin In Hell")
- Vast herds of larvae driven on by demons (or devils) and yeth hounds.
- A galloping trio of nightmares racing along the terrain and up into the sky.
- Winged mephits bearing ill missives.
- A curious night hag watching their vessel Demonwing float past.



Looking forward to the sights!
