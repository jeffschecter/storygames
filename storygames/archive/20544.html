    \n That's a very impressive document! Beautiful design and excellent writing. I hope someone can translate this into English (and other languages)!

    I didn't read the whole thing, just skimmed through, but it looks like an excellent sourcebook on traps.

    One thing that struck me (and, again, I only skimmed through, so don't take this too seriously) is that so many of the moves include a "you lose an object" clause. My first impression was that the adventurers would leave the dungeon naked and unarmed! However, now that I think about it further, it might be just fine. After all, you've got weapons in hand: how likely are you not to drop them when you're trying to avoid a trap?

    Perhaps a more general clause, like "you leave something behind" might be good in some cases, however.
