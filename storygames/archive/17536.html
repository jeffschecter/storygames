\n Hello Story Games Friends,

I am the lead on organizing the Indie Games Explosion at Dreamation this year. I started by sending out an email to those who have gmed in the past. I want to reach out to everyone I can who might be interested in running games for the Explosion.

Please send me an email to let me know your interested. katswhim @ gmail.com put "I want to gm" in the subject line. I do not need the game details yet, just a simple \u201cI want to gm\u201d would be great.

My Goal is to get as many games as I can and put up a nice pre-schedule of games so that changes can be made before anyone is committed to a time slot.

Running two Indie Games entitles the GM to badge coverage and free meals in the staff suite. If you need crash space let me know, as occasionally a room has been made available.

Dreamation is easily my favorite convention of the season, and that for me is because of the efforts of the volunteer game masters who bring the fun and life to the party, so thank all of you for your efforts in the past and I look forward to seeing many of you again in February.

In a week when I get a head count I will send out another email to those who wish to gm, letting you know what information I need to get you registered and pre-scheduled.

Thank you again,

-Kat Miller.
