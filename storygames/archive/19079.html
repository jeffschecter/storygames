\n The 2013 London Indie Darling Awards were held last Saturday.

The London Indie Darling Awards are won, not based on a popular poll or a panel of experts, but solely upon the games run at London Indiemeet. So if folk want a particular game to win then they have to come, pitch it and find others willing to play it.

Congratulations to the winners!

Indie Game Darling (the game played most at London Indiemeet)
Intrepid, by John Keyworth

Indie Designer Darling (the designer played most at London Indiemeet)
Jason Morningstar
(Fiasco, Love in the Time of Seid, Durance, Carolina Death Crawl, The Sha Al-Hiri Roach, We Are Here To See The Evil Wizard Korm\xe1kur)

Indie GM Darling (the member who facilitated the most games)
Rob
(One Night Only, Final Girl, Nanoworld, Vast & Starlit, Itras By, Blood & Honour, Monsterhearts, Psi Run, The Quiet Year, Nemesis!)


All categories ran from Dec 2012-Nov 2013

Some stats:

\t\u2022\tWe had 13 meets in the period.
\t\u2022\tWe played over 139 sessions
\t\u2022\tWe played over 79 different games
\t\u2022\t54 games were only played once
\t\u2022\tWe had 41 different facilitators
\t\u2022\tThe facilitator name most requiring an initial was James (thanks James G, James J, James M, James T, James W!)
