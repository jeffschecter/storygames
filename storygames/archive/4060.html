\n As part of the group organizing the role-playing section of the next edition of ConQuest Northwest in February, I'm excited to report we're planning to have a story game lounge at the con. We've approached some of the people who organized Go Play NW -- namely John Harper and Tony Dowler -- and they're interested in joining forces. We're picturing a room dedicated to story games and hosted by SG folks, working the same way the two GPNW rooms worked in June. See you in the lounge!

    \n I'm going to give this thread a bump because the Story Game Lounge and ConQuest NW are in just a week and a half. To recap: as part of organizing the role-playing section at ConQuest NW, February 15-18 2008, we managed to get a Story Game Lounge that will be hosted by Go Play NW.

    The SG Lounge will be run on the same model as Go Play NW: people show up at the beginning of the time block (morning, afternoon, evening) and GMs make a pitch for games they'd like to run; interested participants form groups and start playing. Tony Dowler is the primary host.

    Head on over to the Go Play NW wiki to list the games you want to run or play!
