\n In my other thread, we talked about the 'shape' of the discussions here.

Levi brought up Marginalia. Everyone loved that suggestion. Then Levi was more specific. Everybody loved the more specific!

So I made up some mockups (just in MSPaint) of how this might look on Story Games.

Here are the mockups:


Mockup 1: Showing Marginalia



Mockup 2: Click "Add Marginalia" in the top post



Mockup 3: What happens when you click "Let's talk about this post (new thread)"



Mockup 4: What the Original thread looks like after forking (difference from Mockup 1 highlighted in Red).



Mockup 5: What a moderator/admin sees


Mockup 6: What a moderator/admin clicks on the edit link


Mockup 7: A well-liked first post.


Edit: Last comment, # of comments, and # of new comments need to all include Marginalia. Users have to see them "respected" as much as posts so there's no disincentive to deciding "this little post is marginalia." Basically, marginalia can't be the sad little brother of "real" posts.

Andy is amenable, but we'd need to figure out how to get it coded. Maybe for Vanilla 1. Maybe for Vanilla 2. Maybe one of us codes it. Maybe pay some other developer to do it on the Vanilla forums.

Thoughts?
