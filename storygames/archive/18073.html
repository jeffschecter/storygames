\n I put together some superhero-themed history seeds to make it easier for people to quickly sit down and play Microscope at Emerald City Comic Con. There are some quickstart seeds in the setup chapter but I wanted ones with that fit the comic book theme of the con.



Based on the games I ran and the reports I got from other facilitators they were a definite success. I actually ran two games using the same seed ("the greatest superhero on Earth is gone\u2026") but the histories were still *vastly* different even from the start.

One of the games was with the most awesome 10 year old ever, but that's another story.
