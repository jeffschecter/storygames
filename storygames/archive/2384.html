    Posted By: Mark CauseyWhat would happen if Keys fed into Drama Points instead of XP?

    Also, how bad would it wreck the system to have characters grow rapidly?
    Hmm, good question. For some character types (the non-White Hats in Buffy/Angel), each Drama Point is actually worth 2 XP so in that respect they're getting even more value. However, DP are more transient and are intended to flow back and forth.

    I think Keys tied to DP would help a couple of things with respect to Cinematic Unisystem. First being that some conditions for earning them are somewhat setting-specific. Example, in Buffy and Angel, you're rewarded for making witty quips and snappy come-backs, as to reflect the tone of the television shows. Putting this into a Key instead (Key of Smart-Ass) could fit better into certain settings.

    Second, it puts more power into the hands of the players to determine when they should earn DP and takes a bit of burden off the GM. For instance, the last Fallout session we ran, I missed several opportunities for awarding DP because my brain was too distracted by planning what was going on and what would be happening next. I ended up awarding them at the end of session, which is not what I want to do and it's not what I plan to do in the future. Hopefully. By giving players more clearly laid out conditions for earning DP, and by making them much more customized and individualized, you're helping them think more about how their character should act instead of relying on the universal DP rules which reward everyone the same.

    As for rapid power growth, I don't know. I haven't run a long-term CU so I don't know that I can comment on that. Certainly you can always scale the power or number of their opponents, but that might be satisfying only up to a certain point.
