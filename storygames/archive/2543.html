\n nerdnyc presents:
\nRecess IX: THE UNDEADENING
\nMarch 11th, 11am-10pm
\nOnly $15 ($7 children) for a day of gaming!
\n
\nThe Nerds of NYC want your BRAINSSSSSS! Come play games of all sorts for even the most discriminating undead palate - RPGs, Board & Card Games. Nerd Book & Media Swap. Homemade delicacies.
\n
\nWhat we have for you:
\n
\nRPGs
\nWant to get back into a classic dungeon crawl? Looking for something new? Play
\nBurning Wheel, Dread, Mortal Coil, With Great Power, Masters and Minions, Over the Edge, Call of Cthulhu, Dungeons & Dragons, Pulp Era!, Army of Darkness...
\n
\nTabletop & Card
\nNo Monopoly or Candyland here. Just the "new" classics such as the Power Grid, Battle Lore or St. Petersburg. Don't worry if you don't know the rules, we're more than willing to teach people on the spot.
\n
\nBook/Media Swap
\nBring any old nerdy books that you would rather see on someone else's shelf. We've also seen people bring comix, VHS, video games and the like. Please make sure you bring home what isn't picked up. The last thing we need is six copies of Moreta: Dragonlady of Pern.
\n
\nRecess will be held at:
\nBRICStudio
\n57 Rockwell Place.
\nBetween Dekalb and Fulton
\nBrooklyn, NY 11217
\n
\nfind out more at: www.nerdnyc.com/recess
