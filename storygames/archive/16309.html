    \n This is pretty interesting. Two things stood out to me.

    This: Industry 64 (68.09% of successful) 14 (25% of unsuccessful)

    and the frustration of the fact that they used averages instead of mean or 'typical' numbers. A single Order of the Stick or even a Mobile Frame Zero that gets a big boost from outside media will totally skew those overall numbers. I'd love to see some numbers that include the top end, bottom end, and mean/typical numbers for some of these.

    Another piece I just read on ICv2 made the point that whether your project makes its way to the front page and/or gets mentioned in their Twitter updates has a big effect. The author described this as KS 'playing favorites', not sure if that's a fair assessment or not.
