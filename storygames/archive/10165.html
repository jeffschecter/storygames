\n Because I love psychological horror so much, I bring you this, Eight Days A Stalker The Mask of Joy, an Unknown Armies game. (Not in the standard UA universe, more's the pity).

I'll be holding it one day a week (which day is To Be Determined) over Google Chat. I need 4-5 players before I begin.

Create your own characters, with the standard Street Level stats (and without magic. Yet). All the PC's are friends from college. One must be a young-ish woman, and another player, that girl's lover.

A girl and her lover (player characters) receive threatening letters from her exes... at the same time that bizzare and clearly supernatural things begin to happen. It cannot be a coincidence, and you must investigate before something terrible and horrifying happens. And what is the connection between the stalkers, the supernatural attacks, and an artifact called The Mask of Joy?

For more info, post here. I'll whisper interested parties with my G-mail address.
