\n I have been so bold to create a fansite for Gregor Hutton's 3:16 game. It is called Carnage Amongst The Web and (for the moment) can be found here:
\nhttp://316carnage.com/
\n
\nIt is still a work in progress.
\n
\nI was very pro-active and I've added all the fanmaterial I could find on the net without asking permission first. If you find some of your stuff on the site and want to have it removed or changed then let me know and I will do so asap. I have not included material that can be found on Gregor Hutton's Boxninja site or that was published in an online magazine (like the starship rules).
