    \n From what I've read, the 4E community seems to be divided between:

    1) those using the pre-errata'd p42 DC numbers including the footnotes that indicate to add 5 for Skill checks
    2) those using the errata'd p42 DC numbers (w/o the footnotes that indicate to add 5 for Skill checks)
    3) those using the errata'd p42 DC numbers (but re-incorporating the footnotes, as a sort of compromise)
    4) those doing something else entirely like what John Harper or Stalker0 have come up with.

    It's sort of a mess, really, and a stain on a system that I am otherwise pretty happy with.
