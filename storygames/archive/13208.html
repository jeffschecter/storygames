\n
\n
\n
\n
\n I mentioned a design contest. The question, then, is who will win the coveted Golden Satsuma?
\n
\nPart one of the design contest - I would like a Golden Satsuma logo for the design contest. Please make sure it looks like a satsuma and not any other small citrus fruit.
\n
\nWhisper your entry on this thread, and I'll narrow the field to three if necessary, then poll for a favourite. The winner will surely be immortalised.
\n
\nI will buy a $25 MSF bundle, emailed to the artist, for up to five entrants, in order of submission. I will at my own discretion ignore entries I think are just a naughty attempt to get a bundle, and I reserve the right not to award a prize if nothing makes the grade.
\n
\nIn addition to immortality, the winner will receive $50. It will constitute work for hire (otherwise it's a pretty useless logo).
\n
\nIf I don't get a suitable winning entry, I will sulk, and take my design contest elsewhere.
\n
\nEntries close 25th October.
\n
\nLet me know if I need to clarify any of this.
\n
\nEdit: If you prefer, you can whisper your entry.
\nEdit: You can submit up to three entries, but will only be eligible for one bundle.
\nEdit: I've been alllowing late entries - I'll give it til Monday. If any existing entrants object, do let me know.
