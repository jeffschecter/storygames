\n Hi,

I'm looking for a copy editor for my game Other Worlds. If you haven't heard of it, that's cool, I've been working on it for ages and all the buzz has pretty much died down at this point. It's a universal, trait-based rpg in the HeroQuest mould with a heavy focus on collaborative worldbuilding, genre emulation, player empowerment, and other storygame stuff. You can read more about it here and here

Here's the thing though: you must be British. I had been working with an American editor and, though he was very good, I think that the language barrier proved a bit bigger than either of us had expected. I mean, my book's not written in cockney rhyming slang or anything, but there were enough slight differences that we weren't always on the same page about things. I want to see how it works with someone closer to my linguistic wavelength, as it were.

So... any takers?

Mark
