\n Ok well, I've been working somewhat feverishly on this game for the last week or so. If any of you spotted my thread on Social Combat, this game is why it came up. Fair warning, this uses very little of what was discussed in that thread, I think to do it any justice I'm gonna need to build a game based solely on those ideas. At any rate, here it is. Let me know what you think.

Deadshot

Elevator Pitch: Players play as half-human/half-constructs in the service of the Empire in a victorian steampunk setting. They seek to route out Sedition and Insurgency while trying to reconcile their new state of existence as not quite human creatures.

I set out some weird goals for myself in creating this one. Probably not good design practice. I wanted to make a small system that:
A) Had a method for determining Hit Location in combat
Had a method for character advancement
C) Was 10 pages or less.

I failed at the last goal, it clocks in at 13 pages not including the title page, but I did accomplish the other two. I just don't know how playable it is. After writing it up, I realized I probably wouldn't want to play it, but I do like some of the ideas in there. Oh well.
