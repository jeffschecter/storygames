    \n The Game Design seminars (GDS) are all worth listening to. The GDS have been one of Gen Con's highlights for me and have gotten better each year.

    The podcast seminars are likely more hit and miss. I don't like the four people per seminar format, and the lack of prep for making sure important information is communicated. Some of them are just too stream of conciousness. I include this criticism for the one I was on, until we get into an argument... that part might be interesting.

    Edit: Besides the podcast seminar I was on this year, my criticism is based on listening to last years, as I didn't go to any this year based on last year, as the format hasn't changed. Next year I doubt I'm going to even bother volunteering, if the format is the same.
