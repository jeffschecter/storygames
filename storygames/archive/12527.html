    \nCritical Hits is mostly D&D focused, but has some general RPG type news stuff. I've mostly been using forums for my 'news' recently, and the blogs I check (and the one I write) are all design focused. Here I thought I had a golden opportunity to plug my own stuff, then I actually read your post

    Lame Mage Games Blog is for a particular developer (Ben Robbins) who mostly does D20 type stuff, but he also does shout outs for interesting things going on in the gaming world, and his other blog, Ars Ludi, has some really great GMing and design advice.

    Oh, and yeah, Michael's list was very helpful to me as well.
