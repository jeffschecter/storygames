Outies!

Rob Donoghue
January 2007 edited January 2007 in Story Games
So Ken Hite has listed his winners for the best games of 2006, and it's as interesting as ever, and there's a lot of love on it for folks around here.

Just so people have some context, look at the best game category (and go read it to find which magnificent product is the winner) and see who is up for consideration:

Questers of the Middle Realm
Faery Tale
Esoterrorists
Spirit of the Century
Promethean: The Created
Don't Rest Your Head
Mortal Coil
Zorcerer of Zo.

How fantastic is it that the White Wolf product is the exception on that list?

-Rob D.

    I'd speculate that Ken has had his Liscenced Game category for years now, simply because it's hard to compare something from a big Liscence to some something without. I mean, a lot of what goes into liking a Liscenced game is how much you like the Liscence, and how well you think it was treated.     You want to reward X for originality, and you want to reward Y for encapsulating the license. . . which are two very different things.
