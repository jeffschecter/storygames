\n Well, my Lacuna thread over @ RPG.net drifted down a few pages and I don't feel like playing necromancer, but I can't imagine a more suitable site than this one to get a couple very small mechanical issues resolved:

* Is there a limit to the number of times you can push yourself via a reroll? (This is relevant while you're in your Target Heart Rate: If it's an unlimited # of rerolls, there's no reason not to simply roll one die each time -- presumably you could also add in an appropriate training die each time as well. But that seems sort of slow and lame; it also means actually failing any given effort is outright impossible as long as the player's willing to deal take the HR hit.)

* If you can push more than once, are you considered to have failed the roll each time? (This is relevant in determining lost dice during risky actions: just the one for failing the first roll, like when you gain Static, or do you lose a die every time you roll and don't succeed?)

I think that's it. I've run the game several times now and it's come out a little different every time, which is awesome (like really great fish-and-chips awesome). But these last couple issues continue to kind of bug me.
