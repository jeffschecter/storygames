Crapify: The Shab al-Hiri Roach

Graham
November 2006 edited November 2006 in Story Games
There have been many threads about "Awesomifying" games. But there haven't been any about making awesome games worse. So let's try with the Shab al-Hiri Roach.

First, the Roach itself needs hit points. It's crazy that the Roach can't be killed - it's just a bug, isn't it? - so let's give it some hit points and a high amount of armour.

Now, since the Roach can be killed, we need weapons to kill it with. So let's give Pemberton University a weapons research lab. Perhaps they use alien technology to build awesome weapons. So we're setting the scene for a final confrontation between the various professors and the Roach, a la Starship Troopers.

What else can we do?

Graham

    The real problem here, people, is that there is nobody playing The Roach itself. I recommend that whenever someone chooses to obey the order of the Roach, the person to their left tells them exactly what they must do. The original player must then narrate doing that.

    During character creation, you will create both your Pembertonian and the Roach that is destined to take the Pembertonian to your right. That way, when it's time for you to start issuing commands, you can properly take on the persona of your Roach. Anything else would lead to metagaming.

    Also, there are different kinds of Roaches, even if they all look the same to the mundane eye. Clanbooks will be forthcoming.
