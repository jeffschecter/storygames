\n Hey everyone. My game, School Daze was mentioned in the Stuff to Watch: January 2012, and it's in its near-final build. Here's the deal: I need to see how it handles. I've gotten a few playtests in, but I need more. The community here at S-G is great for things like this, so I'm hoping that you'll help.
\n
\nSchool Daze is a narratively driven RPG that uses high school as a vehicle to tell fun stories. Think The Breakfast Club, Brick, Glee, or Buffy the Vampire Slayer. It's not about re-living high school, it's about making a caricature of it. It plays somewhat like Fiasco, but there is a GM (the Administrator), and there's a light conflict resolution system.
