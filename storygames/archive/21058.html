\n In Everway setup as written each player selects some vision cards as part of character creation, five I think (don't feel like taking the box from the shelf to check). How about if players together with their neighbor also select a couple of vision cards? Instead of five each, you'd have X amount for yourself, Y amount in common with your left neighbor and Y amount in common with your right neighbor for a total of 2Y+X. I'm thinking 4+1+1 looks like a good number but 2+2+2 might be better.

    \n Depends on what you're aiming to develop/accomplish with the vision part of character creation, I suppose. In my current GM-less adaptation of the game, a large part of initial vision has been shaping the kind of Realm each individual Spherewalker comes from - which makes me think, sharing vision cards would be great for player characters who share a common origin. What's your aim? Are you trying to weave together player characters into a group or a common starting situation since the very beginning or character creation?
