\n I'm about to start a campaign with a japanese/chinese flavor. It will not be historically correct in any way; we'll be making it up as we go along, with rice fields, dragons, misty mountains, pale maidens with big black hairdos, struggling peasants and harsh warlords.

Does anyone have recommendations for some fitting background/theme music? I'm looking for stuff that gives an eastern vibe - it can be from wildly varying countries and time periods.

    \n Heya: Download purchase the entirety of the Twelve Kingdoms (12 Kingdoms) soundtrack set (there's about 4-6 CDs). The "piano accompanyment" isn't that great, but the rest is shit hot, and exactly what you're looking for.

    From moving, to dramatic, to subtle, to fun: It's all there. Japanese music production company and orchestra produces faux-Chinese fantasy anime music. It is the cream of the crop.
