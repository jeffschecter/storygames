\n We just released our first standalone module for Deeds and Doers!

It is called m1: Storming the Dead City, by Marshall Miller. It has a pretty neat re-imagining of both dungeon maps and random encounters, and should be fairly challenging even for advanced players.

You can download it on the main Deeds and Doers page.

    \n Busy weekend! Marshall and Ara have each released second modules in their series, which can also be played on their own.

    In "Mountain of Swords," the characters travel across the broken lands towards Mt. Saif, where they will cast the cursed bronze bones into the fires.

    In "A Desperate Gambit," the characters attempt to stop the siege of Chorus, led by that thrice-cursed traitor, General Czot.

    You can download them on the main Deeds and Doers page.
