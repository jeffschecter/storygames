    \n The main rulebook gives you a good historical feel and the rules are fairly easy to follow. The supernatural element is a little underwhelming in the first book, but the creator assures everyone he was saving some of the choice bits for a second book, which will delve more into the dark nature of the 13 Colonies.
    \n
    \nSome people have griped about the game system, which is based on two D12s, but when I played I didn't seem to have any issues. The book feels like a good start to a game that needs more development, but the setting idea is really cool and I'm looking forward to seeing what they do with it.
    \n
    \nI did a review on the game you can find over at RPG.net. There are some other reviews as well that differed in opinion from mine, but brought up some valid points, so you'll want to check those out as well.
    \n
    \nThe high point, I believe, is the idea behind the setting, but that also is the low point because it doesn't get fleshed out nearly enough.
