\n Hey there,

The next JiffyCon in Greenfield, MA is coming up. It's on June 21st (and 22nd!). We'd love to see you there. It is also, apparently, Free RPG Day. I wasn't familiar with Free RPG Day, but I hear it is a growing annual event. How do other FLGSs celebrate it?

One of our local gaming stores was disappointed that we'd scheduled it that day--they wanted to invite us in to do stuff for the big day. In lieu of this, we're going to take part at Jiffy & in cross-pollinating ways with the local stores. I invite everyone to bring any free games they have to Jiffy--got a playtest version you'd like to circulate? Or a mini-game or adventure? We can put them out for give aways at the con, and also bring them to the stores. They are hungry for new stuff.

In general, this seems like a great way to bring indie stuff into new hands.

best,
Emily
