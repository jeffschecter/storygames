\n So, I was on my MySpace page checking recent comments, and I saw an advertisement for Shock: in the Sponsored Links banner.

I own Shock: already, but I had to click through the ad link anyway.

This is the first time I've seen promotion for a small-press game from a person I know on SG out in the wide, wide Intarwebs.

It needs to happen more. I would have bought the game this morning if I didn't already have it.

Josh? Rock on.
