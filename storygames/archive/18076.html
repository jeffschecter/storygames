\n Hey y'all!

As you may know, John Wick has spent the last year working on a set of Pathfinder supplements called Wicked Fantasy (and releasing them via DriveThruRPG). Basically it's a setting guide centered around innovative takes on the core Pathfinder races. He's done 10 races in all, covering traditional races like humans, elves, and gnomes, and some less common races like orcs and goblins. The whole project is designed around Pathfinder, but John's done a lot of work to tie the mechanics to the setting in ways that stretch what Pathfinder can do.

Early this year, John asked me to help him set up a Kickstarter to bring all the smaller PDFs into one 300+ page, full color, hardbound book. I helped him with his last Kickstarter for Houses of the Blooded's supplement called Wilderness, so I was totally eager to take on a bigger project that would do something for Pathfinder.

One of the first things John wanted to include was a Dungeon World conversion for the setting. I thought that was awesome, and I started poking around to see who might be interested in handling the conversion. I wanted to find someone who was really experienced with the material, and when I asked the illustrious Mr. John Harper if he knew anyone who might be a good fit he said "Well... Adam and Sage, of course."

I finally got the courage to ask Adam and Sage if they were interested in doing the conversion themselves only about a week or two before John's Kickstarter launched, and they said yes! Which is so awesome!!! I think Dungeon World has the potential to host a lot of cool supplements and settings, and I'm thrilled to see that Wicked Fantasy is going to be one of them. And it's so cool that the creators of Dungeon World will be helping John Wick make that happen. (This is like my DW/HotB fanboy dream mashup.)

John set the Dungeon World Stretch goal at $17,500. Today the Kickstarter is only a few hundred short of that goal with nearly 3 weeks left to go, so Adam and Sage are almost certainly going to be working on an awesome DW supplement. Go check it out and let us know what you think!

http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/2006204732/wicked-fantasy
