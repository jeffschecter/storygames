    \n ...or split into three groups of three, also great and probably more appropriate for a party, since it will go faster. Play the same playset and give a general briefing at the Tilt - "The Chicken Hut is now on fire ... shots fired out by the paper bag factory ..." and let that stuff inform the other two games.

    Playing one game with nine won't be any fun.
