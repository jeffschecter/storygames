\n A friend of mine has started up Demon Thesis, which is about half interactive webcomic and half tactical bit-sized web-based game. (If you like games like Final Fantasy Tactics, the gameplay is in that vein, though perhaps a bit simpler.)

Of possible interest to the SG crowd:

* Delivering content at a webcomic pace and style.

* Bite-size, casual-friendly gameplay with a few tasteful social game features.

* Integration story with gameplay, and (soon) integrating audience feedback into both. (I believe users will soon get to vote on what new equipment / powerups are available to the party.)

* Built in pure HTML5/CSS3/JS/BBQ.

There's a leaderboard and everything to show off how you do in the battle, but you'll see that my results ("locke61dv") are shamefully poor. Q_Q
