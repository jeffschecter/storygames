    Posted By: northerainThat's the part I liked. I'm not comfortable until I read a blurb though
    \nThat means I need to work on my blurb, I suppose.

    In A Penny For My Thoughts, you play amnesiacs helping each other recover their memories through an experimental therapy. Conceptually, it lives in the same space as The Extraordinary Adventures of Baron Munchausen and 1001 Nights. You tell your character's story, but with the other players having a huge amount of say over what your character did or said. And there's more than little bit of my experience with improv that's worked it's way into the design.

    The rules and structure are very simple, and they can easily be adapted to other settings and genres. In addition to the "standard" setup, which focuses on loss and personal drama, we've included one inspired by Call of Cthulhu and another that's basically The Bourne Identity.

    That enough of a blurb for you?
