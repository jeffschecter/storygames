\n Surprisingly, there is not a lot of games with this kind of setting. Agon, and Hellas, Worlds of sun and stone ( about ancient greece / mythic greece). Nothing yet in the roman empire.

So, which games would you hack to do so and why ? Khe kind of stories i'd like to play would have more to do with I Claudius/Massada than Gladiators. I'm not against fight and tactical elements, but social combat/relationships and intrigue would be the core of the story. And we could act at large scales.

Any sugestions ?
