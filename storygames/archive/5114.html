\n My players want to take the roles of fantasy characters who gather resources in order to solve problems and defeat threats, and earn rewards for doing so. They're also interested in doing some collective worldbuilding and getting excited about some Mortal Coil-like passion mechanics, stake setting, benny, and scene framing mechanics. So far so good.

We did a worldbuilding exercise and it was kind of a flop. It created a great big mesh of threats, problems, assets, and rewards, but we could do that forever without knowing where the game begins or what it's about. I realized that we needed to start with the PCs and work our way out from there.

How do I start with the PCs and work out from there? What kind of guidelines do I give to the players, or conversely, what kind of targeted questions could I ask them to get them to create a bunch of characters that fit into the mold?

(Coming into the holidays I'm broke so I can't buy say, Burning Wheel and try to use parts of that.)
