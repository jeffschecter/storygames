Okay, I'm interested in finding a play-by-post or play-by-email game.
I'm interested in it because it's something I can do for thirty minutes on an idle weeknight. I'm interested in it because I like writing. I'm interested in it because I like the character relationships that develop in pbp.

I have this idea for a perfect game. I'm either looking to find it or create it. First, and prettiest, step is to search for it.
Below are the criteria for the game I want to play.

Forum-based (I am open to email, but it is less desirable.)

Free-form or a game with mechanics - Either works for me, but if there is a system I don't want it to have dice/chance, and I don't want the system to trip up the story.

Genre: Something between post-apocalyptic, cyberpunk, modern. I want a "gritty", streets level game, with violence, chaos, budding romances, and the stuff you care about coming into crisis a lot. Romanticized, gritty, pre-apocalyptic tragedy.

Stuff I want the game to include: street gangs (think a mix of Necromunda, Mad Max bikers and your typical cyberpunk-y gangs), guns and crazy cyberpunky weapons weapons (like "hyper-cryo-knife" and "FQ-117 Arm Cannon" and "cyber-enhanced power gauntlet"), romantic tension between characters (preferably in an environment/group that is open to having wisps of queer sexual tension in there), occassional gore (I want a game with thugs and razorwire and chainswords and violence as a political tool).

Things I'd be open to: mecha (power-suits, armoured walkers and cyborgs and stuff are all pretty cool. Not ultra cool, but pretty cool), supernatural elements (angels, occultism, psionics, divine protection and general eeriness are all pretty cool), mega corporations and commercial monopolies , katanas (and all the tropes and self-centered bad-assness that accompanies them inevitably).

Things I'm not at all interested in: a laundry list of supernatural beings (12 gothic races! three different magic systems! Vampyres! Wytch Covens!), senseless and boring violence (I want my blood splatters and power weapons to be accompanied by heartbreaking tragedy, damn it!), fanfic universes (I don't want to play something directly set in a TV/Movie/Comic/Anime/Book universe. I am totally cool with playing something "largely inspired by", but I'm not interested in writing shared fanfic, or in getting into authenticity debates), games where posts are regularly 1,000+ words of flowery prose (If the phrase "the skies a dark and tiresome ebony, our tragic hero casually but briskly stepped along the dark and mottled cobblestone walkway, musing about the many colours that a darkened sky can encompass in its awesome expanse" then I'm not interested in reading on) general wankery (I don't like childish people. This includes logic nazis and mods who feel the need to stifle stories with their policing).

So, yeah. Teenage wasteland, cyberpunk, robocops and arm cannons, anarchy and corporate control, young love, chemically enhanced warriors, biker gangs, emotional trial, gang wars, and thinly-veiled gay love stories. Any reccomendations?

If not, I'm seriously going to have to start a forum-based game for this. I really want to play one!
