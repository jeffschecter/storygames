\nI played drinking Jungle Speed with non-gamers at a block party and I learned a few things:
\n
\n1. Don't expect drunk people to follow the rules.
\n
\n2. Forcing people to drink when they break rules will only make them more forgetful.
\n
\n3. "All grab" is confusing to drunk people.
\n
\n4. "Flip outward" and "put your losing cards under your deck" are the first things to go after a few drinks.
\n
\n5. No matter how drunk someone is, "colors" is really exciting!
\n
\n6. Your cards will get alcohol on them.
\n
\n7. End disputes over who has the totem when 2 people grab immediately as it may escalate.
\n
\n8. Drunk non-gamers match shapes better than my non-drunk mom.
\n
\nTerry pointed out that starting 2 threads named "Gencon on pain killers" and "Drunk Jungle Speed" may send the wrong message.
