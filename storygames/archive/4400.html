\n OK, so I'm ready to get off my duff and start actually planning this thing. I'm shooting for, say, February to make it as palatable as possible for the Northerners to enjoy some of our nice weather. Per the initial spitballing thread, we've narrowed it down to Orlando, easily accessible and ripe for tacking on a day or two to visit local attractions.

Uhm, so now what do I do now? Tips from others who have organized such things would be greatly appreciated. Should I start a separate blog/wiki/site? Do I just pick things, e.g., the date, the location, etc.? Or do I posit several options and open them up for comment and voting? Thoughts on scouting for a location? What types of places should I consider?

(One bonus: The Florida real estate market is so dodgy right now, I found several places where I can rent a mansion for peanuts, i.e., an amount I'd be willing to foot entirely by myself.)

    \n OK, so I was laying low working on a few things. Notably, I was finding myself forced to decide between going to Dreamation or hosting this thing. Much as I would love to get to Dreamaiton, I am compelled to finally put my money where my mouth is and make this thing a reality.

    Then, I had to try and firm up some dates for me personally. I've got my childcare shored up and permission from the wife.

    I am looking at February 22-24. I am not aware of anything of note over that 3-day weekend. Please chime in if that's a deal-breaker for you or if you know of a reason why it would be a sucky weekend to do it.

    I am scoping mansions, but obviously I can't book until I see some semi-firm commitments from some interested parties.

    In short, if you're interested and like 50+% sure you'll attend, please post here confirming if those dates are good for you.
