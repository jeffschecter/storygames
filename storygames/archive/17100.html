\n Jason, Dave, Matt and Megan play Dungeon World using the Campaign Fronts of "Poor Tristram" by Sage LaTorra and Adam Koebel. This was one big nostalgia trip and brought me back to when I was 15. Not just because the setting is based off of a prequel to Blizzard's Diablo but the play style of the game.

In this podcast you will hear familiar names from the series and even new stuff that the players themselves brought into the game! I have to say it has been a fun night and I cannot wait till next week. This was suppose to be a test run but the session was so fun I had to post it.

Just remember to "Stay awhile and listen!"
