\n In a fit of madness, I've launched a new blog exclusively focused on all things subterranean, ruined, or dungeony.

Subterranean Design

But I don't want to go it alone. I'm hoping it'll be a multi-person effort and am looking for contributors. If you think you're up to it, whisper me your email and I'll add you as an author.

    Posted By: tony dowlerWhat are your thoughts as to the aims of this blog? Are we looking to provide GM advice, or is this to be a celebration of all things dungeony whether linked to play or not?
    \nThe latter, at least in my posts. All things subterranean, cool, and especially both. Without particular regard for any roleplaying system, but acting as if there were really a corpus of mad dungeon-builders out there looking for advice and ideas. I'm also not opposed to including urban exploration stuff and other ruins, because that's all in the same genre to me.

    Or, as I put it in the intro post:
    "I want a blog full of underground wonder, urban exploration, ruins both modern and ancient, maps, traps, speculation, fantasy, and inspiration. Like BLDGBLOG but exclusively in a subterranean domain. I want a subterranean design blog."
