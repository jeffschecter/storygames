\n I made this last night and thought you guys might be interested. If you don't know what a virtual tabletop is, you should definately look into it.

Pixels & Polyhedrons is like myspace or friendster for roleplaying afficianados who use virtual tabletops. Hook up with new players or old friends, organize your play sessions, and discuss your games.

EDIT: I'd like to point out that you don't have to use Virtual Tabletops to join.
