    \n I have been to this one, it is a decent size, been around forever and is near where you were asking for:
    Game Town

    3954 Harney Street
    San Diego, CA 92110
    Phone:(619) 291-1666

    Great location in Old Town San Diego. They carry a lot of different rpgs including D&D, d20 and White Wolf. They have a few gaming tables that are in the back room.

    \n I'll mention that the game stores in San Diego are not great for rpg/story games outside of the usual big names: D&D, pathfinder, shadowrun, SW, WH40k RPGs.

    We use to have a game store called Gamers Torch that was great (lots of story games)but they had to close shop.

    We did just have some new ones open including Villainous Gamers Lair and At Ease Games. There is also Pair a Dice up in North County.

    Ran Fiasco at a meetup and someone asked where they could get, in San Diego the only option is online.
