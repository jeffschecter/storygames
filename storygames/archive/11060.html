\n Oh, man, most of you guys totally won't get this, but I'm so excited about it.

When I lived in San Francisco back in 2000/1, I played a Vampire LARP. It was one of the best games I've played: ridiculous levels of intrigue and some great characters. Completely old-school, of course: if you were using the mechanics, you were doing something wrong. The point was to play your character.

Anyway! One of the old storytellers is starting up a game on Google Wave. It's old World of Darkness Vampire. If you'd like to play, you're welcome to join us. Do a search for "Veni Foras" and you should find it. If you don't, tell me, and I'll find a better way to link to it.

Graham
