    \n Another name to the list: Pilgrim Waru the Western Wind.

    Interesting results so far: The lack of time sensitivity is nice for a public participation thing like this. The 140 limit hasn't stopped people from just posting multiple tweets. The last name, Waru, didn't get much response but I dunno if that's because I posted it in the evening or if it just wasn't an inspiring name.

    Today I'm flipping the game around, asking for people to come up with new pilgrim names for me to riff on, using the pilgrim name generator as inspiration. We'll see how that turns out.

    \n I've just suggested it by Twitter, but I expand it a bit here : Malu the Moving Maze
    \n
    \nMalu helps people by never letting a problem quiet. She tackles the problem form every possible angle, always changing her approach, always trying new ways to solve it.
    \n
    \nShe gets into trouble because people are unable to understand her. With here, the shortest distance between two points isn't the straight line but a complicated set of ever-changing curves, that got her the "moving maze" nickname. Her trouble comes mostly from the people she tries to help, who cannot see how what she does can help them.
