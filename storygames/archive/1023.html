  • rrr23
    July 2006
    Posted By: Clinton R. NixonFor my money, the UA rules have an insanely high whiff factor.
    I also feel a bit like this... but it's a feature not a bug apparently.

    The rules are that you never roll checks unless the character is in significant peril or it's a really intense and scary moment. The rest of the time you just pretty much "say yes" if the person has the skill.

    The point is (according to the book) that it's a horror game, and in those really dark and scary and perilous moments your brain is going "OHFUCK...OHFUCK...OHFUCK" and so your Firearms (or whatever) skill is just statted at ~15% to model that.

    Whether or not it's a design feature you particularly enjoy is another matter, of course. But it is like that on purpose apparently.

    Drew
