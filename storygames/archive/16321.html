Here is a Kickstarter entry for a project (not mine) to complete a browser-based InterNet videochat facility for RPGs with less emphasis on the boardgame/wargame-like interpretations and more on improvisation and personal interaction.

Roll20 virtual tabletop gaming that tells a story

I'm not sure that the project as planned offers me a lot that I don't get using just plain InterNet videoconferencing such as Google+ Hangouts. I'm not heavily into the map. But I chucked the guys a bone anyway.

The project as described doesn't seem to include any facility for exchanging virtual tokens, which would be really useful for Fate Points in Spirit of the Century, other FATE builds, and I guess a fair few other games. Perhaps if a few story gamers got into the closed beta and made a suggestion they would consider adding the capacity.

Regards,


Brett
