Shock:1.2 will be joining us at this orbit in the next couple of weeks. When that happens, the "Buy Shock:1.2, get a free PDF" promotion will end.

\n

I've been hesitant to sell PDFs in the past. They don't really satisfy my objectives as a publisher. But I get asked for them enough that it will obviously be worth it. So I haven't figured out a price yet, but you'll be able to buy PDFs of Shock: I think the deal is, the best deal will be to get the book/PDF combo. Then the next best deal will be to buy the PDF, see if you like the game, and buy the book at a reduced price that will total more than the combo price but be less than if you bought them separately.

\n

I'll be pricing them in such a way that they won't cannibalize my book sales, though. I really like selling books. So if you want a PDF on the cheap, the current book/PDF will certainly be the best deal for the foreseeable future.

\n
