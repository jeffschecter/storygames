\n That's right! I know people did a lot of talking at GenCon this year and other people had recording things on hand! Don't lie to me, I knows it!

So now that it's over, please post links to recordings and podcasts of stuff that happened at GenCon 2011, so we can all listen to them. Podcasts about GenCon and recaps of GenCon and reviews and stuff from people after they get home are fine too.

If you could write a short blurb for links as well, so I sorta know what order to listen to (or watch) them in, that would be super. But don't argue here, take it outside.

Thank you!
