    \n Welcome - Tucson is a very relaxed town and very fun. We have a local con which is coming back this year, and we just had a GM's Conference there at Amazing Discoveries. Hat's Games, down on Alvernon and 29th, is the other major game outlet other than the one in the Park Place Mall. Other events routinely get posted on the meetup group, which is where you should be looking for upcoming "small things happening" since you're not in the market for a regular group yet.

    Tucson is a great city, I really enjoy it. The best Sonoran hot dogs are at Guero Canelo. Buy them with Mexican Coke, not American. Done.
