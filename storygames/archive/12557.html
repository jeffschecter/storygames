\n So, Chad shared some sales figures, and those included a game I've long been curious about, Zorcerer of Zo. It's sold nearly 500 copies from IPR alone, and yet I feel like it missed the boat on indie hype. In searching for mentions, I found:

Story Games: pre-release, Fred writes about doing layout, thread goes on for about a month

Story Games: in 2008, one dude asks if it's good and 3 people say "yes"

Story Games: a few days ago, someone sums up a first game

The Forge: zero Zo-focused threads, and a only a handful of mentions in threads about conventions and other games

I'm a little surprised that a guy who has plenty of friends in the indie community sold a game on a site that caters to the indie community without needing to grab the internet ear of the indie community. I'm tempted to think that either there was more buzz elsewhere, or indie buzz isn't very important to sales, or both.

Or does 500 sales in 3+ years just kinda happen by accident, and I'm a dork for being impressed?
