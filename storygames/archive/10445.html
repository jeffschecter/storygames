\n Hello and welcome from the ENnie Awards!

As you know, our main goal at the ENnie Awards is to allow the fans direct access to the publishers and their products. In order to continue to do that, we need your feedback in the form of the ENnie Awards Fan Survey. This survey will help the staff of the ENnie Awards learn what areas they are strong in, what areas need improvement, and how we can better market the Awards. Please be as honest and thorough as possible when answering.

http://www.ennie-awards.com/blog/?p=323

Note: Not all of the questions require answers but we would certainly appreciate it if you would take the time to expand upon your answers to certain questions.

Thanks for all of your input!

Tony Law
ENnies PR Coordinator
