\n My random car chase generator is up and running. I'm still short a few images but everything else is working. Most of the pictures I already have, but forgot to upload before I left work yesterday.
\n
\nIt's designed for use with Scion, and a minor modification to the chase rules in the core book, but can work for generating scenery and cause-and-effects for any modern setting.
\n
\nCheck it out and let me know what you think. The turn sequence (top left) has a link to my chase rules, and at the bottom right is a link for peeking behind the curtain that will open a new window with some tables of all the possibilities in a chase.
\n
\nFeedback is greatly appreciated. You can either use the links on the pages themselves, or post it here.
\n
\nThanks!
