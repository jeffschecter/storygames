    \nI have all of the Fight On!s except for the latest, I believe. Each issues has had some gems for me.

    The Dungeon Alphabet is definitely the best $10 I have spent on old-school material. A must-buy, especially if you enjoy Erol Otis-style art.

    I have Swords and Wizardry, but as I am starting to play in a Labyrinth Lord game soon, I'll be picking those books up.

    I have hesitated on getting LotFP because of the cost (not sure when I'll run it) and because of the subject matter. Seems a little on the horror/weird side? I'd like to peek at the books before buying.

    Posted By: Caesar_XBut what is the cream of the crop of the small press stuff on RPGNow?
    \nOf the stuff available on RPGNow (which, unfortunately, does not include Stonehell, which is probably the best old school megadungeon product I've seen in a long time), I'd recommend:

    Death Frost Doom
    Hammers of the God
    Labyrinth Lord: Advanced Edition Companion
    Labyrinth Lord: Original Edition Characters
    Swords & Wizardry: White Box
    The Dungeon Alphabet
    The Majestic Wilderlands

    There are a number of other excellent products that you can't get through RPGNow, as I said above, but the ones in my list above are definitely the cream of the crop.
