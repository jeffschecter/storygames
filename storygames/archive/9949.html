\n BOY (maybe ten, holding a 4e Player's Handbook): Dad, have you heard of ... Dragons and Dungeons?

DAD: It's a game. I've never played it but I knew people who did when I was a kid...

MOM: It's like a board game. You make characters, like different sorts of warriors. Some people ... used to get very obsessive about it.

(BOY puts D&D book back on the shelf)
