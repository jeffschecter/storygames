    \n Inspired by this thread I rolled into our redux Session 1 tonight.
    \n
    \nThe player of Hooch the chopper declared with a smirk that his entire gang were gay men. I'm pretty he did this for chuckles, but I decided to roll with it and turn it on him when I could. I named his lieutenant Balls and played him as a matter-of-fact biker who said what he thought and generally backed up Hooch's leadership. But when Hooch blew his roll to force his will on the gang and Wisher tried to take him out in the middle of a charged situation, Hooch blew a roll and I chose to have him miss something vital. The thing he missed - Balls' reaction to Wisher trying to take over the gang.
    \n
    \nWhen Hooch's player said there was one girl in the gang, a butch lesbian, I named her Joe's Girl. I played her very low key, she was subdued but nice to Melody the Battlebabe and asked her lots of questions without expressing much of an opinion on the matter.
