\n I've been working on a web-based virtual tabletop for "rules lite" gaming, and it's finally in Open Beta.
\n
\nFabletop is a bit different from the traditional VTTs in that there is one built-in system (trait-based, special d6 dice pools). This freeform approach works well for casual chat-based gaming, and should make it easier for players to join any game on the site.
\n
\nEvery user has their own "table" page, where they can GM games any time. Anyone can join for free.
\n
\nFabletop
\n
\nI'd be happy to hear your feedback after trying it out. Thanks!

    \n Hi Gremlin, there isn't much much grittiness so-to-speak. You can read about it at the Help page I linked to above, but here is a summary:
    \n
    \n---
    \n
    \nRoll a number of d6's equal to your rank (usually 1 to 4) in the most relevant trait, plus any bonus traits. There is a 1-in-3 chance a Star (aka success) will come up on each die. You typically need 1 Star for basic tasks and 2 for hard ones. For attack rolls, each Star does one damage.
    \n
    \nThere is also a 1-in-18 chance that a Moon or Skull will show up on the first die, which can add an good or bad twist to the outcome.
    \n
    \nPower points are like a combination of Hero points and Mana. They also let you improvise powers or stunts based on your traits, and add one Star to the result.
    \n
    \nCurrently, the base traits are Might, Agility, and Wisdom. Characters begin with two bonus traits: a major trait (like Warrior +1) and a minor trait (like Nature +1).
