\n So, my players (some of whom are new to the whole RPG thing) would like to have examples of characters on hand when we sit down for our SoTC Character Creation. No problem, I point to pages 395-398 of the book that shows the sheets for Jet Black, Mack Silver, Sally Slick and The Grey Ghost. They say these are fine, but some of them are also new to the Pulp genre, and I point to the Quick Pick Stunt Packages (p. 373-392).

What I'm looking to do is provide some examples of popular Pulp characters so they could see how they would be put together in SoTC. I realize that these are trademarked and copywritten characters, and I'm not looking to do anything other than show my players that A) they already know pulp, and this is what kind of character you can make with this system.

The ones I would like help with are:
Dr. Henry "Indiana" Jones, Jr.
Marion Ravenwood
The Shadow (Lamont Cranston)
Margot Lane
Mata Hari (Margaretha Geertruida Zelle)
Tarzan of the Apes (John Clayton, Lord Greystoke)
Sheena, Queen of the Jungle
The Phantom (Kit Walker)
Mandrake The Magician
Zatanna Zatara
"Doc" Clark Savage, Jr.
The Rocketeer (Cliff Secord)
Axewell Tiberius (from Monkeyman and O'Brien)
M-11, The Human Robot (from Atlas Comics and Agents of Atlas)
Venus (from Agents of Atlas)
Namora (Aquaria Nautica Neptunia from Marvel Mystery Comics and Agents of Atlas)
Captain Joe Sullivan (from Sky Captain and the World of Tomorrow)
Polly Perkins (from Sky Captain and the World of Tomorrow)
Commander Francesca "Franky" Cook, RN (from Sky Captain and the World of Tomorrow)

If you would rather I ask the FATE Yahoo Message board or just drop it altogether, I'll understand.

Thanks!
