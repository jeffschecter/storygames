\nIn this thread I casually mentioned a Diana Jones Award game day, and Jason asked me about it:\n
Posted By: Jason MorningstarPosted By: hans ottersonDJA game day
\nNew thread, tell us more, so awesome.

So, to indulge him: Last year I got the idea of getting a bunch of people together for a game day where we'd play all of the DJA nominees. It didn't come together, but Harry reminded me about it this year when the nominees were announced. He got in contact with a bunch of people, offered his house up, and organized the whole thing. We're not going to be able to do it until after the awards, but it should be super fun and I for one think it will be kind of fun to play Kagematsu, Montsegur 1244, and Chaos in the Old World all in a day and look back and try to judge which I think is best.

Hey, let's make this more of a topic: Have you held small game days like this, where there was a theme or focus, or gone to one? How about an Awards game day?

I think it was also Harry who had the idea to get together, watch one or a couple Coen Bros. movies and then play Fiasco. After this DJA day I really want to make that happen.
