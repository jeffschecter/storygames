Secret StoryGaming Santa

James_Nostack
December 2006 edited December 2006 in Meetups / Conventions
...What it says.

If anyone's interested, please whisper to me, James_Nostack, in this thread, and I'll try to match people up. The goal is to (a) build community and trust, and (b) get stuff.

1. I need your name, shipping address, a requested item, and an e-mail address (in case there's unexpected problems). Also, please indicate if you're cool with paying international shipping costs. Otherwise, I'll try to match you up with someone from your same country. (WARNING: If you come from a small country with little SG representation, like Djibouti, you may be stuck paying international shipping.)

2. The requested item can be StoryGame related, or not. I recommend a price range of around US$15-25--roughly the price of a game, CD, DVD, fancy book, or graphic novel.

3. You do not have to request a specific item. You can say, "I'd like a copy of your favorite movie." Or, "I'd like a good book." Or even, "I don't care what I get, surprise me!"

4. By participating in this, you understand that your Secret Santa may be a Secret Slacker, and can't get his or her act together in a reasonable time. Please be patient.

5. There's also the possibility that your Secret Santa is a Secret Scam Artist. I don't see any easy way to address this problem, other than to (perhaps) post a list of those who didn't comply. If anyone sees another way around this, let me know. In the meantime, by participating, you understand that there is some risk that you won't get anything.

6. When you give, or receive, a gift, please post.
