\n Hey guys! This isn't strictly about gaming, but I picked a couple brains to make sure it wasn't wildly beyond the pale to post this here.

I released my first short story called Arcanoir: Hob Lesatz for Hire. Arcanoir is a blanket sandbox that's more or less the 1930s but with magic and fantasy creatures having returned to the world at the turn of the century. It's an excuse to do really hardboiled stories but with some nonsense mixed in like spells or werewolves. The gaming tie-in is in that my fascination with genre mash-ups definitely began at the tabletop with things like Shadowrun.

Nobody is as shocked as I am, but the story is hovering between 60 and 80 on Amazon's top 100 fantasy>historical ebooks. That's not bad for a first-ever effort. It's got some really excellent reviews, so I thought I'd link to it here and see if any of you guys might be interested in such a thing for a thin greenback. If you like or love it, think about doing a review. If you hate it, please follow mom's advice of not saying anything at all.

Thanks for the look, fellas! You can find it here.
