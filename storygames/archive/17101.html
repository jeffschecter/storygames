\n Greetings,

today i will play my first game of Fiasco (3 Players, Town and Gown) but thinking about the game yesterday brought something to my mind.

If you have seen the movie "Run Lola Run" it is about the small changes in your actions that can lead to very different outcomes.

Could you recreate this in a long night of Fiasco?

I was thinking you do it like this:
Do SetUp as normaly
Play as normaly but the first player has to establish and you note the die color he got

Then
Play again and the first player establishes the same scene but get's a different die
Note the scene the second player get's and his die too

Then
Play again and Player 1 establishes again and get's the die color that had lead to the cooler scene THEN
Player 2 establishes and get's a different die color

you can repeat this as often as needed but i think even the 3 game isn't really needed.
Thoughts?
