    \n\nAugust 2011 \n

    \n A rabid raccoon??

    Our Dragon Slayers game at Ropecon had nothing whatsoever to do with dragons, but it had a skeleton warrior called Skeletor (his former master had a weird sense of humor), a corrupt cleric of peace, a paladin and two elves. In the ensuing tragedy my nice, Lennier-like elf wanted a beautiful princess for himself and lost everything; the other elf was responsible for a nuke-'em-from-the-orbit of an elven slum, in effect a genocide. Skeletor won himself an army of the undead.

    I've long hated elves and would never have assumed that playing Fiasco would make me feel really sorry for an elf character of mine.
