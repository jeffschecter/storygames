\n Hello,

here is the contest we are opening, until september :

http://www.narrativiste.eu/2011/vieux-pots/

it's a about redesigning old RPG into sometning more "narrative" / "indie", or if you prefer, using old settings with a more solid design, in the way of "the system matter".
And yes, it's clearly inspired from the excellent Game Chef, as there is nothing yet in french like this.

The purpose is to:

1) Make french (speaking) "traditionnal" RPG gamers know about indie design (a lot of work to do here)
2) Make new generations (and people from other places) know about some of the goods 80's RPG in french language

Que le meilleur gagne !
