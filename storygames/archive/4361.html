\n Scorn: The Second Book of Pandemonium will feature two covers (much like Dread before it). Though the book won't be out for a little while, I did want to show off the cover artwork, courtesy of two insanely talented artists:



This is an angel, illustrated by Tariq Raheem. Tariq has worked on several video games, including Fallout: Tactics and Heavenly Sword. He also worked as a concept artist on the TV series Farscape. to see his other illustrations, please check out http://www.tariqart.net (please note that the site contains artistic nudity).



This is a demon, illustrated by Rob Thomas. Rob has executed artwork for tabletop RPG developers like Fantasy Flight Games and Mystic Eye Games, as well as various covers for Dragon magazine. For more of his work, check out http://www.mindsiphon.com (please note that the site contains artistic nudity).
