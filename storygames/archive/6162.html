    \n I went with this:

    "In a Wicked Age" is the latest game from Vincent Baker of "Dogs in the Vineyard" fame. It has an Anthology Engine, and leads to vicious, gritty gaming with quick character creation with a multiple chapter format that follows in the footsteps of Conan the Barbarian and Fafhrd and the Grey Mouser. Look for fantastic adventure, wicked sorcery, and killer fun.
