    [Totally Random]

    In this RPG, the PCs are intrepid heroes in an Italian opera who, with vague threats, fight to control their damaged aircraft for cash under the table and nothing will ever be the same.

    Stats

    In this game you have 2 stats:

    * finesse
    * wit

    You also have skills. To make a character, you get 45 points to spend on skills and stats.
    Task Resolution

    Roll dice when the GM tells you to, using one stat. Find the sum when you roll a number of d6s equal to your stat.

    A skill may also be relevant. If so, roll extra dice equal to its rating.

    The GM will arbitrarily set a target number, which you must beat in order to win. The GM will then say what happens.

    Experience

    The GM will give you experience points during play or the end of the session. He will award up to 375 experience per player, based on how much you deserve them. You can spend these to raise your stats and skills. A single raise requires 75 points.

    [Going through until they fit]

    In this RPG, the PCs are inhuman missionaries in a beseiged castle who, with vibroblades, fight back tears for a shiny quarter in a dark future.

    In this game you have 3 stats:

    * acuity
    * alacrity
    * acumen

    You also have skills. To make a character, you get 40 points to spend on skills and stats.

    Task Resolution

    Roll dice when the GM tells you to, using one stat. Find the highest die when you roll a number of d6s equal to your stat.

    A skill may also be relevant. If so, add its rating to the total.

    The GM will arbitrarily set a target number, which you must beat in order to win. The GM will then say what happens.

    Experience

    The GM will give you experience points during play or the end of the session. He will award up to 875 experience per player, based on how much you deserve them. You can spend these to raise your stats and skills. A single raise requires 175 points.
