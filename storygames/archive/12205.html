\n I might be way off topic for this forum, but I think this also has something to say on what people appreciate in gaming. Tell me if I'm out of line here.

Anyway.My sister and I have been talking about playing the old classic Heroes of Might and Magic III over the internet together, but I'm thinking maybe there is something that better suits us.

What she is into in HoMM is collecting stuff, building stuff, improving your hero and fighting creep. She is not as interested in taking enemy cities and vanquishing other players, though she appreciates that there is a clear and reachable goal.

I want something I can run on my netbook, something you can play sessions of when you feel like it, something which doesn't demand huge tactical immersion but I don't mind if it allows for it. Something casual that still has the potential to grab you and immerse you, in the same way as the HoMM games does.

I'm thinking something like cooperative tactical squad-crpg:s or something. What do you think?
