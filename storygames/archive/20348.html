\n Hey everyone. LARP Scouts is something my partner Natalia is spearheading; it's sort of like a smaller-scale, LARP-only Camp Nerdly. See below for details about our first event.

Looking for 6 more players for our next Nightvale-themed game, Picnic in the Dog Park, on October 23-25! 1.5 hours from midtown Manhattan in New York City - accessible via your Metrocard.

Registration is $50 if you want us to provide food and lodging for you. (Free if you want to provide your own food and lodging!) Register via Conplanner, but feel free to RSVP on the Facebook page for updates.

https://www.facebook.com/events/1659480787622919/

If you can't make it for this day, but would like to be informed of future events, go ahead and join the LARP Scouts NYC group here!
