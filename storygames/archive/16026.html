Hey, my Storygamely friends, I've just fired up the Kickstarter for Mobile Frame Zero: Rapid Attack!!

\n

The 10th anniversary edition of Mechaton includes all of the stuff Vincent and I have been doing since 2006, but we haven't written down. Rules for spotting and communication are simper and more central, Hand-to-hand combat is balanced better (that is, it's devastatingly effective if your guys can get there), cover takes more hits now, and there are some nifty tweaks that are too complicated to explain.

\n

The book will also have building instructions designed by our own Lester Ward (divnull) around designs by Soren and illustrations by Richmont Gan (below). The new rules are, of course, by Vincent and his son (my godson) Sebastian is our apprentice game designer. Thor Olavsrud of the Burning Wheel is our editor.

\n

I'm really excited about the project.

    \n\nlumpley \n\n\n

    \n\nMarch 2012 \n edited March 2012
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n I can answer.

    R. Talsorian, owner of the trademark "Mekton" ("Mechaton" in Japan), asked* me to change the title.

    It seemed to me that the most fun way to do so would be to publish a new version under a new name. That didn't happen for a while. Fortunately, they allowed me to sell through my existing stock instead of destroying it.

    Then J was like, "hey, 10th anniversary edition?" J and I have been fiddling with the game together for years. Since he's the one with the time and energy to make a new edition happen, he's the one making it happen.

    I'm wicked excited!

    -Vincent

    * "Asked" may not represent the precise reality of the situation.
