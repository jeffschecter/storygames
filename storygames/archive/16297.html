\n Hey everyone! I have been preparing a Marvel Heroic Roleplaying campaign. One of the things that I'm psyched about but don't "get" that much is Unlockables. I love them so much but I have a hard time writing them. I'd loooove to do them for more personal milestones (or power-related milestones, or whatnot), although they're much easier to write for Event Milestones. Does anyone have any thoughts on this?

Specifically, how would you write an Unlockable for (say) a Milestone about joining/being accepted as a member of a team?

I wrote some more Unlockables for the SHIELD Super-Agent event Milestone in the main book, see what you think: The post. (The tumblr itself is not quite up to speed yet.)

The main question I have is whether you think it's kosher to have an unlockable Asset or Complication be in addition to already-existing assets and complications without spending a Plot Point. In other words, normally you get to roll 1 Asset for free. With this Unlockable, I give you the ability to roll 2 for free for a limited time, costing XP instead of Plot Points.

    \n I suspect that the system is robust enough that anything you do is unlikely to break it, especially if they had to work for it in the first place, and doubly so if it's just for a limited time.

    No ideas for a team unlockable mechanically, but I envision something with calling in backup, or forcing the team to follow your lead.

    \n I wrote a shitload of Unlockables - I think they're way more interesting than the other things you can do with XP (other than shift Affiliations around...that one I find really cool. "Look, Wolverine is learning to be on a team. AGAIN.")

    So for a Redeem A Villain set of Unlockables:

    5 XP - A former victim of the villain comes forward to forgive them.

    5 XP - The authorities agree to a period of temporary parole in your custody. The parole officer provides a Scene Distinction of Got My Eye On You to any scene of your choice.

    5 XP - The villain agrees to aid the forces of good. They will become a Watcher Character to assist you in an Action Scene of your choice.

    5 XP - The villain takes concrete steps to make amends for a past evil deed.

    5 XP - The villain provides concrete assistance in bringing down a former associate. This assistance is more than just information and can take the form of a d8 Asset or Complication when used against the former associate.

    5 XP - The villain publically apologizes for past misdeeds and says they are going straight, despite the personal risk. They gain the Distinction \u201cGoing Straight?\u201d It complicates things when former criminal pals show up\u2026

    5 XP - A significant political movement develops in favor of the villain getting another chance. The Political Support can be used as a d8 Asset or Complication when appropriate, for an Act of your choice, including this one.

    5 XP - The media provides favorable attention to the villain\u2019s efforts to reform. The Media Support can be used as a d8 Asset or Complication when appropriate, for an Act of your choice, including this one.

    10 XP - The authorities issue a pardon for the villain\u2019s past crimes, a long-term suspension of sentence, or other indefinite change of legal status.

    A Milestone about underground mutants, a la the Morlocks or the "Omegas" from the movies:

    5 XP - Because of your actions, local authorities, agencies or organizations, begin to assist Morlocks/Omegas with food, shelter and other supports.

    5 XP - A significant Morlock leader who has not previously done so comes out against mutant extremism and violence, and supports co-existence with humans.

    5 XP - Omega Ally. You gain a mutant underground ally as a Watcher Character to assist you in an Action Scene of your choice. They have one Power Set and are taken out on a d10 of Stress or more.

    5 XP - Evil mutants find it harder to recruit among the Omegas due to your efforts. When you face an enemy with the Mutant limit who is a Brotherhood member or mutant criminal, you can impose on them the d8 Complication \u201cJust Kinda Threw This Together\u201d for the remainder of the Act.

    10 XP - The authorities announce an aggressive policy of protection of the Omegas like any other citizen.

    For a milestone about clearing the name of someone who was wrongly accused:

    5 XP - A major media force (cable news pundits, AM talk radio, newspapers, high-end bloggers) takes up the cause of the imprisoned hero. You can assign the \u201cMedia Attention\u201d Distinction to any single scene you choose related to the hero.

    5 XP - The authorities agree to temporarily suspend the hero\u2019s sentence while further investigation is conducted, but still restrict his movements and actions.

    5 XP - A witness comes forward contradicting the official story. They provide an Eyewitness Account, a d8 Asset you can use in an Act of your choice, including this one.

    5 XP - A figure complicit in the hero\u2019s railroading panics and attempts to cover up the situation clumsily - you learn about it and gain Incriminating Evidence, a d8 Complication you can use against that figure or others allied with them.

    10 XP - The authorities issue a pardon or otherwise set aside the accusations against the hero. He becomes a playable character in the next Event.

    10 XP - The hero makes a major public sacrifice in an upcoming Action Scene, regaining (or gaining for the first time), popularity and fame with the public and the Unjustly Accused Distinction.

    From a few team-focused Milestones like we discussed above:

    5 XP - The team is recognized by local authorities, giving them Police Backup, a Scene Distinction, in a scene of your choice.

    5 XP - The team comes together. Double your Team Affiliation in an Action Scene and a Transition Scene of your choice.

    5 XP - You are doing better on a team. Step up any single Power Trait when you are rolling your Team Affiliation during an Action Scene of your choice.

    5 XP - People who use their Team Affiliation in conjunction with you double that Affiliation in an Action Scene and a Transition Scene of your choice.

    10 XP - You and a teammate of your choice gain a SFX that activates when you and your teammate take action together (i.e. both using the Team or Buddy affiliation). This can be a step up, an additional effect die, or any other SFX effect. Both of you gain the benefit, even if you are using another SFX in the same action.

    10 XP - The team is recognized by worldwide or national authorities, giving them more dramatic backup in the form of Scene Distinctions or Watcher characters for the Act of your choice, including the current one.

    For Mutants Sans Fronteres:

    5 XP - Gain the NGO Observer Distinction for the rest of the Event. It can be used when persuading, applying leverage and protecting the oppressed. It can also complicate things as a normal Distinction when dealing with politically conservative, nationalist or tyrannical leaders or their agents.

    5 XP - Gain an Asset: Political Support d8, for one Act of your choice (including this one). You can use this Asset without spending a Plot Point.

    5 XP - You become too High Profile for anti-mutant agents to directly attack. If you do, your High Profile becomes a d8 Complication against their attacks, as they have to either make it look like an accident or in some way stymie your fame.

    5 XP - Refugee Ally. One of the mutants you aid joins you as a Watcher Character in an Action Scene if your choice. They are taken out on a d10 or higher of Stress and have one Power Set with the Mutant Limit.

    5 XP - A former \u201cevil mutant\u201d (Brotherhood member, criminal) shows up to aid you in an Action Scene if your choice. They are arrested or disappear at the end of the Act.

    5 XP - A major media outlet (cable news, newspaper, top-tier bloggers) takes up the cause of oppressed mutants. You may put the Scene Distinction \u201cMedia Attention\u201d on any single scene you choose.

    10 XP - A locale declares itself a mutant safe haven, as a result of your organization and protection of mutant refugees.

    10 XP - A locale with oppressive anti-mutant programs gives up its position (though not necessarily its bigotry) due to international attention raised by your deeds.

    From an "I'm the cosmic adviser of the team" milestone:

    5 XP - A single prediction you make (or made) comes true, figuratively, literally or hilariously literally. You choose the prediction. If it makes sense, you will have a free I Knew It! d8 Complication to use on the opposition in the scene where it comes true.

    5 XP - An occult or cosmic faction or force recognizes the wisdom of your guidance and assists you in an Action Scene of your choice. This can be anything from a witches\u2019 coven to an alien demigod. They are considered an Expert character, have a single Power Set and are taken out at d10 stress.

    5 XP - A municipal or local official agency or organization recognizes your wisdom and will follow your guidance for the remainder of the Event. This may result in Scene Distinctions for locations within their influence.

    5 XP - Other occult practitioners recognize (and perhaps covet) your knowledge. Gain the Recognized Occultist distinction for the rest of the Event.

    5 XP - Your wisdom leads to a cosmic bond with another hero. Your Buddy Affiliation, and theirs, is doubled when you work together, in a scene of your choice.

    5 XP - You may provide your wisdom to another character, who gains it as a Cosmic Wisdom d8 Asset in one of the following areas (your choice): attacking, defending themselves, or recovery, for one scene. They keep this asset even if you are not present in the scene. They choose the scene to use your wisdom in.
