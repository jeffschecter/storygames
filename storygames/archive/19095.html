\n Hi! I've recently taken part (and humbly won) in an one-page RPG contest, and I'd like to share my submission.
It's a little GMless story game called I Hate You!, in wich the players represent a group of characters that deeply hate each other but have to join forces for whatever reason.
A word of notice: my English is quite rusty, so I'd appreciate any advice on typos and that kind of stuff.
