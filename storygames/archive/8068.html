    \n I've not done it.

    However, my gut goes with Ryan. There is a lot of things said and unsaid and resaid in a IAWA game around the dice that relies on some quickness of medium, some ongoing editing of intent, and some reflexive back-writing to keep smooth and even. All of those things are hard to do in PBEM, which often benefits from a more straight fiction-forward approach.
