\n The voting period for the Indie RPG Awards has officially begun for games released in 2008. After a week of voting, I'll add up the totals and announce the winners in time for Gen Con.

This year we have 42 games and 4 supplements registered. That's short on supplements, but still a wide range. You can check out the full lists (in order of registration) at Indie RPG Awards 2008. Regardless of whether you're voting, it's an interesting list of indie RPGs.

All of the voters should have received links to the new voting form that I set up -- please contact me via info@rpg-awards.com if you haven't received your link. Also, the emails I had on file for the following people bounced:
  • Bill Corrie
  • Christopher Helton
  • Jeff Diamond
  • Darrick Dishaw
  • Gary Pratt
  • Philip J Reed
  • Evangelos Hugo Paliatseas
  • Keith Senkowski
  • Derek Stoelting
  • Ed Cha
  • Jasper McChesney
  • Jason Blair
  • Ethan Greer
\nIf you have any info on how to contact them, please let me know.
