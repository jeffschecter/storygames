\n As I've mentioned before, my book Stealing Cthulhu contains hidden things.

One of these things is an encoded geographical location. As far as I know, it has never been found. I'd like someone to find it.

If you tell me the location before 31st December, I'll donate $251 to charity: half to a charity of my choice, half to a charity of your choice. Please email me the location: my email is Graham, at thievesoftime, dot com.

(If you go to the location, you may find something there. I put it there two years ago. I hope nobody has moved it.)

You're more likely to find it if you work together. If you'd like to use this post to discuss theories and avenues to explore, you're very welcome to do so.\ufeff And I'd be grateful if you shared this widely.
