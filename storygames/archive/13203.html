In honor of the 100th backer of Human Contact's Kickstarter project, I'm offering as a kickback the biggest donation I've gotten: the Quarantine playbook for Apocalypse World!

\n

Vincent Baker wrote and designed the book, then donated it to the Human Contact project. It's only available to Human Contact backers at the listed levels (from the PDF up), and will only be around until the end of the Kickstarter in 19 days. Then he'll go back to be frozen underground, isolated from the psychic maelstrom until a new age dawns.
