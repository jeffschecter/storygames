OK, so in Crime & Punishment, conflicts between players are resolved by bidding currency against each other until someone backs down. Like this:

P1: X Happens.
P2: No, Y Happens (bid 1)
P1: Nu-uh, X Happens (bid 1 to see and one to raise)
P2: No way, no day, Y Happens (bid one more to see one more to raise)
P1: Hrm. OK, Y Happens.

I was vaguely putting this in auction terms, but I realize it's misleading because at the end of the conflict P2's bid of 3 and P1's bid of 2 both get discarded. So it's not an auction because all bids are real.

Any suggestions on what to call this? The bids are assertions of investment and will, but I don't want to call them investments, because I'm using that somewhere else.
