\n If IAWA was a game you could win, I think this is how I\u2019d score it, when you were wrapping up all the games you were playing in the same setting. \xa0Each of the following bullets is worth 1 point:

Variants
  • Played with Use it, Risk it
  • Played with Partial Victory
  • Played Oaths (promise-keeping house rule) once and used an Oath
  • Played Oaths (promise-keeping house rule) three times and used Oaths
\nOracles
  • Added at least 1 card to an Oracle
  • Added at least 1 card to each Oracle
  • Added 10 cards across all Oracles
  • Removed 5 cards from the Oracles
\nGMing
  • GM\u2019d the game
  • GM\u2019d the game for 3 sessions in a row
  • GM\u2019d the game for 3 sessions of closely linked continuity
  • GM\u2019d the game for new players
\nPlaying
  • Played in 5 sessions in a row
  • Played a genuinely good character
  • Played a believably evil character
  • Played a character who had sex
  • Played a character who was acting directly against their best interests
  • Played a character whose best interests were dependent on another character
  • Created a detail that someone else incorporated into a later character
  • Played a character that recurred
  • Played a character that recurred a second time
  • Played a character that recurred a third time
  • Played a character that recurred a fourth time
  • Played a character that recurred a fifth time
  • Played a character that recurred because you struck them from the Owe List
\nI'm sure I'll think of more later. \xa0Any suggestions?
