    Reveal an unwelcome truth
    1) It turns out that the treasure you carry was stolen recently and is now widely sought.

    Use up their resources
    1) As you flee the beast, your last torch is snuffed out, leaving you in complete darkness, deep underground.

    Offer an opportunity, with or without cost
    1) The hanging bridge might be able to support your weight... but probably not as you are, laden down with armour and provisions.

    \n Reveal an unwelcome truth:
    2) Unfortunately, the bandit is not alone. In fact, his fellows have you surrounded!

    Show signs of an approaching threat:
    1) You feel the ground shake, and then a flock of birds flees from the trees around you. A horn sounds in the distance.

    Deal damage:
    1) As you turn around, her mace hits you in the face. Take a d8 in damage.

    Turn their move back on them:
    1) So you peer out from behind the bushes in order to discern realities, and then you lock eyes with the bandit queen. What do you intend to do next? She knows, but you take -1 forward if you change your mind.

    Separate them
    1) Avon, in the confusion of the fire and smoke, you lose sight of Valeria. She knows the way to the gate and you don't.

    Give an opportunity that fits a class' abilities.
    1) (fighter/thief) The guards push you into the cell, lock it, and walk away.

    Show a downside to their class, race, or equipment.
    2) Thiliel, you've been away from your elven forests for so long you've grown listless and weak. You can't bolster until you raise your spirits.

    \n Present the druid with animals from their land in city market, being sold to the gladiatorial arena.

    Present the ranger with fresh tracks in the woods, and cries for help in the distance.

    Present the thief with a treasure room full of legendary traps.

    Present the cleric with a prophecy of a war in the heavens, a great battle between the gods.

    Present the wizard with a colossal and ancient tome, written in the language of magic.

    When they return from looting a dungeon, present the bard with an overzealous tax collector... in a common room full of dour and miserable villagers, sick of hiding from monsters in their homes.

    Present the paladin with evidence that the local sheriff is in league with demons.

    Present the fighter with a steam-powered war tank full of bloodthirsty goblins.

    \nUse up their resources

    Cave in! By the time you can dig through all this rubble, you'll be out of food/water!

    Firestorm! By throwing yourself to the ground and rolling, you can avoid significant damage from burns. Your flammable equipment, however, may not be so lucky. Arrows, bedrolls, backpacks, purses, look out.

    The monster's skin is corrosive! Even as your weapon tears through it to wound the beast, the weapon rots.

    Lake of acid! To cross to your objective, you'll need to sacrifice something you have with you, so that you can run across it instead of melting your feet off. (Note: this only costs actual resources if the party doesn't have easy access to the corpses of their enemies. Yeah, you see how my group solved this one.)
