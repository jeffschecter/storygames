\n Collective Consciousness will be held on Saturday, July 12, 2008, at All Fun & Games in Apex, NC.

This one-day mini-con features 9 tables, each of which seats 4 people. There will be two four-hour slots: an 11-3 session and a 5-9 session. It's not a huge con, obviously, but you've got to start somewhere!

Registration begins in late May (exact date TBD).

For more information, please visit http://collectivecon.wordpress.com.

Hope to see you there!

-- Rafael

    \n Here's how I'm going to handle registration:

    Interested GMs will be able to email their session descriptions to me on Sunday, May 18th. I'll "open the doors" at noon with an initial post in this thread, and any emails received after that will be added to the database. However, there are only 18 slots (two sessions, each with 9 tables). So, it will be on a first-come first-served basis. I'll need a title for the session, the name of the system/game being used, and a short synopsis of the scenario.

    I'll post these session descriptions to the site, and then a week later, on Sunday the 25th, I'll open up registration (via Warhorn) to all interested parties.

    Warhorn: http://www.warhorn.net

    CollectiveCon: http://collectivecon.wordpress.com

    If you have any questions, post them here. Thanks!
