\nHow would GM Fan Mail work?

Meaning, when the players love something the GM does*, they hand the GM a chip (representing Fan Mail).

Added twist, how can we make it so that GM Fan Mail can't be used directly against the players? In most games hacks I've seen where players hand the GM resources which can then be used against them... the players will hesitate because of fear of how that reward will come back later to bite them (even if that's part of the fun for some players). If you have ideas to circumvent this behavior, please share!

Rock,
John

    \n Why not come up with a way to have players spend the chip to potentiate something that the GM has done or give him a bonus. The catch is that if the players don't use them by the next session, the GM can use them as he/she sees fit.

(gotta admit, I know very little about the game) Why not let him spend them to charge other people with writing brief commercials for the show to be performed at the next session (or a poster/radio spot/mock actor interview/etc.) to entertain everyone. Is there research that the players could do to ease GM burden? What if the GM can use them to do asides where he can steal the spotlight for an NPC for a while.
