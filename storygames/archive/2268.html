Hey,

Any of you who are coming in super-early, I would like to invite you to sign up for the playtest of my game, Misspent Youth: A Game of Juvenile Delinquency and Being Awesome. This would be the third playtest and I think the game is humming pretty well right now. It's a sci-fi game that focuses on rebellion, friendship, and the temptation of using The Man's tools against him. It's R101 (the first roleplaying game on the schedule!), and I hope to see you there. If you have any questions about the game, let me know. Here's the blurb:

R101: "Misspent Youth: A Game of Juvenile Deliquency and Being Awesome" by Rob Bowell. An INDEPENDENTLY PUBLISHED GAME from Dog-eared Designs - Part of the Indie Games Explosion! It's 10 years in the future and The Authority is out to crush you, everyone you love. The adults won't do anything about it, the other kids are sheep. Only you and your crew will stand up. Misspent Youth is a story game including collaborative world creation, fluid and easy-to-remember rules, and kickass adventure. NOTE: This is a playtest of a forthcoming game. Thursday, 8:00PM - 12:00AM; One Round; All Materials Provided. Beginners Welcome; Fun, Under 18 Requires Parental Clearance.
