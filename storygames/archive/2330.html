So, lately the community has been running a lot of contests, which I think is awesome. It's challenging to keep up with, though! So, if you have a contest planned and upcoming, post a (tentative if necessary) contest date and schedule here, and link to it when it starts. I'll keep this post updated with a roundup.

My hope is that this'll help participants to regulate what contests they're getting involved in (I know this is always a tough decision for me) and help organisers avoid stepping on each others' toes, you know, spacing their events out in a comfortable way.
