    \n Finished my first dungeon, the age of villainy lasted only 3 turns, but it was fun. I like how the dungeon come alive with his own history.

    I notice that the age of villainy is not necessary the most interesting age to use for dungeon crawling since many monsters group dissapear and a lot of monsters territory competition is resolved. (next time I think I will use the option of keeping to roll for new monster groups).

    Once the cave giants get a good population, with their population being doubled in combat, they quickly get the uper hand in any encounters. Could be interesting to have a rule that splinter large breeder group into rival groups once they reach a certain population level.

    Could be nice to be able to turn a dominating monster group into a "horde" villain, maybe giving them a special leader.

    \n Hi,

    I used a Intellivision Emulator, Nostalgia 5.0 to get screen shots. Then with photoshop I took the spites I needed from the screen shots and edited them when needed. But I was kind of sloppy and I dint work with a bitmap images in a 1:1 pixel scale. So my pixel blocks where too big (my smaller squares dint equal 1 pixel by 1 pixel) and sometime they got blurry or deformed when I needed to modify their size. But as you can see on the blog, I was still able to get presentable images.

    Ideally, doing again I would start with a map where the resolution give me a 1:1 pixel scale, so that my smaller square in the image can be 1 pixel x 1 pixel.

    I will post this week the sprite sheet I used on the blog.
