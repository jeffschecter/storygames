So, who ISN'T going to GenCon?

Andy
July 2006 edited July 2006 in Meetups / Conventions
I was just curious who wasn't going to the Big GenCon (Indy) in 2 weeks?

This year I'm not going for a few reasons. They are pretty much, in order:

1) Want to save Money
2) Want to go to other cons/meetups to meet the gang, and can't afford to do that AND GenCon (see above)
3) I want to wait until next year when I have a game of my own to pimp
4) Sometimes you need a break
5) (weird, but) I want to get into better shape before I go back. This is kinda irrational, but after being around the GenCon masses last year, I decided that I wanted to get back to my year-2000 weight (40 pounds less, gained through six years of computer work and no martial arts save Wing Chun for a year), and GenCon is kinda going to be a Reward for making progress towards that goal.
6) I've got too much going on in my head between personal gaming projects and full time work stuff (some stress, some planning, and lots of thought about the future) and I want to get my shit in gear to stop the raging storm in my head. In other words, I can't afford the time to go to GenCon, see and play tons of new games, get tons of new ideas, and get caught up in even more cool projects.

Next year, though, I'm totall there.

So that's my deal. I was just curious who else wants to go but can't for some reason?

-Andy
