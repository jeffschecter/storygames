    I'll be able to steal my old domain name back from the cyber squatters.
    The person laughing the loudest at these predictions will be me.

    Industry:
    Story Games will get a shout out at a major games award ceremony.
    Adamant's Buckaroo Banzai will be a complete sell out at Gencon.
    Adamant will sell the movie rights for Far West, but nothing will happen with it.
    The next mega hit for the iPhone will be an RPG.
    Someone will make a combination LARP and critter battle game for the smart phones, probably the Android.
    Japanese pen and paper RPGs will show up in a major gaming blog and NOT be ridiculed.
    The number of people that play role playing games via Skype or Google+ will double (not that I have a way to measure that.)
    A non-industry convention earlier in the year will have a Legionaires Disease scenario and it will drive GenCon attendance down.

    World:
    Obama will be a one term president, but it's more due to 'vote for the other guy' than whatever the GOP can put on stage.
    The lame duck administration instead of rolling over will raise a shit storm of agenda based legislation and executive orders.
    Israel will get hit hard and the will US sit on its hands offering humanitarian aid but no support as an ally.
    The Higgs Boson will not be found.

    Way out there:
    Using Heim Drosher theory, some eggheads will make a rotating superconducting ring that will generate .001 of a G. It will prove that anti-gravity can be generated, but that it's practical application are next to nil. The money and resources for a 'full scale' test will not appear.
    More PHDs will choose to deny the results rather than accept them since it contradicts their pet theory(s).
    A 100kg meteor will impact in an Asian city. More people are killed in the panic than from the actual impact.
    Mayan calendar ends, nothing happens.

    John Carter will be better than the geek crowd expects. Prometheus will be worse than the geek crowd expects, but better than the non-geek crowd expects. Both will inspire new games.

    The Hunger Games will be exactly what people expect. It will not inspire games, but anime in the "high school students killing each other competitively" genre will.
