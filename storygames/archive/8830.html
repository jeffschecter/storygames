    \n Ben, having just spent a few days in NYC I can confirm that the Top of the Rock is a breathtaking...if rather indulgent, way to spend $20.
    \n
    \nIf you're a fan of dinosaurs and evolutionary patterns the fourth floor of the Natural History Museum is probably the single best designed, best laid out, best organized tour through the paleozoic era anywhere. Just make sure you start with the intro movie and then go through the halls in the proper order...we went backwards and it while it was still great, the beauty of the layout would have been much clearer.
