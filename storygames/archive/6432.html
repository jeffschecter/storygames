\n I wanted to remind folks that May 9th is the deadline for submitting events to Gamex (May 23-26) at the Raddison LAX Hotel so there's only a few days left to get your events on the site and available for web registration. If you're interested in running a game at Gamex please go to www.strategicon.net, click on Events on the left-side menu and then choose "Run an event".

Game slots for FOUR hour games are:

Friday 3pm, 8pm and midnight
Saturday 10AM 3pm, 8pm and midnight
Sunday 10AM, 3pm, 8pm
Monday -- 10 AM

Game slots for SIX hour games are:

Saturday noon and 6 pm
Sunday noon and 6 pm

Game slots for TWO hour games are:

Saturday noon
Sunday noon
Monday 10 AM

As before, it takes 48 player-hours to earn your badge to the entire con. Player-hours are determined by the # of player slots in your game times the # of hours of the event. e.g. A 4 hour game with 6 players earns the GM 24 player-hours.

Thanks for everything and you can always contact Denys, RPG Department Head, at strategiconrpg@earthlink.net if you have any questions.
