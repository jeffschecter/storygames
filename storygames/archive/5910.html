\n Once upon a time there was a game called Great Knights. It was really fun and ran 30 sessions of epic storyline, but it had 4 problems:

1) Movement and distance were GM Fiat.
2) Damage was GM Fiat.
3) Characters grew by getting uniformly better at everything and couldn't be customized
4) There was no magic system.

Red Box Hack, or at least, Red Box Hack isms, have solved all four and look like they grafted gracefully onto what was once Great Knights. My game currently has no name but I really just wanted to publicly say that Red Box Hack has

1) A brilliant Arena systems
2) Awesome equipment and damage systems
3) fantastic character options
and
4) Capital-M Magic

each of which has solved a major issue for me and my game. I have an absolutely terrifying number of drafts of my game where I've tried different designs, going back and forth for 5 years or so (more like 8). With Red Box Hack as a seeing-eye dog I've been able to put those drafts to bed.

Thanks Eric.

PS: Story Games deserves a nod here as well - if it wasn't for the AP I wouldn't have looked at Red Box Hack for long enough to see where it linked up with what I had been trying to do all these years. You guys are awesome.
