\n What would be a good ending for an event where people design games? (By "event", I mean anything like Game Chef, RPG Solitaire Challenge and so on).

We know two answers: someone gets declared the winner. Or several people get awarded things.

What else can we do that's a suitably fitting ending?

    \n Off-hand, some things that would get me squeeing: A printed copy of your game (more appropriate for some events). Actual playtesters playtesting your game, with feedback provided. A paired contest where a group of artists compete to design covers for your book. The right to set the theme/add to the restrictions of the next contest in the series...

    These are all random ideas that are intriguing to me at the moment.
