\n Today the DEXCON registration went live! This year there are 7 new signature events that you can register for today. DEXCON is from Wednesday July 4th - Sunday July 8th at the Morristown Hyatt & Conference Center, Morristown NJ.

The signature events are\n
    \n
  • The $5000 Interactive Puzzle Challenge
    • \n
  • The $1000 Team Space Marine Championship
    • \n
  • 2012 Iron GM Reginional Semi-Final
    • \n
  • The $1000 Beat the Mob! Game Show
    • \n
  • The $1000 Night's Black Agents Team Championship
    • \n
  • The $1000 Space Cadets - Live! Experience
    • \n
  • The $500 Summer Poker Championship
    • \n
\nIf you are interested in being a GM under the Indie Game Explosion AND you want to register right away for the signature events please contact me at jennifew@gmail.com. We can get you registered today and you can submit your game proposals by June 1st. Also, if you wish to submit an indie Larp note the submission date is May 6th.

If you are ready to submit your game proposals please use this proposal form for IGE games ONLY. If you are interested in running a different type of game (wargaming, board games, card games, panels/discussions) please submit your proposal through the dexposure site.

Let me know if you have any questions and pass this on to any GMs who are interested in Dexcon 15!
