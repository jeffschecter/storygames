    \n Back when 3.5" diskettes prevailed, I would print a sheet of Avery disk labels and slap two of them on an index card. I remember having to do an emergency print run for a LARP at Archon. Needless to say, that was a LONG time ago.

    I think you can buy sheets of printable index cards now. Large nametag sheets might also work for you.

    -Grant

    I just bought some 2.5" by 3" that fit nicely on index cards, as mockups for a card game.
