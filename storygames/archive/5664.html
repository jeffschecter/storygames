\n I was working on Twisted Rhymes, Nursery Crimes earlier and the thought occurred, "What happened to the other Sight and Sound Design Challenge entries?". This one started out as "Don't Take A Nap" and has morphed hugely since the initial entry and I'm quite pleased so far. I have a good start on background and setting, working mechanics that are not too derivative, and ideas of what is next.
\nSo, where do the others stand, if at all? Some were quite good.
\nAnd I open it up to any other challenges of 2007. What is the status of your game(s)?
\nHow about an update?
