\n This is for Heart of Ashes.

The other skills are named: Talk, Argue, Explore, Sneak, Save, Attack, Prepare.
This last skill... I can't figure out a good name for it.

The examples list for the skill is:
(scale a huge wall; leap from a cliff; lasso a giant spider; stamp out the fuse before it goes off)

It's basically about athleticism, and doing tricky stunts, and physical feats that aren't about combat.
The skill name needs to be a verb.

Possibilities: Move, Act, Maneuver, Perform.

Do you have a good idea for what to call this skill? Thesaurus is givin' me nuthin'.

    \nHey James,

    I'm being pretty careful about introducing "vocab words" into the game.
    I really want a game that will be reader friendly to kids (aged 10+).
    That said, I do want to introduce challenging words.
    Magic cards introduced me to words like "tectonic" and "seismic" and "cadavers" when I was 8, and I felt proud to have this secret knowledge, when years later it came up in science class.
    But... "fend" seems like an unusual word that might trip people up, and yet isn't very exciting to prove mastery over.

    Sense?
