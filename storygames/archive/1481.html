Hey all, I'm running this contest over at Lussumo.com, sort of like Game Chef but without ingredients and with a money prize. Basically, "Make New Styles by Octover 1st":

http://lussumo.com/community/discussion/3624/vanilla-community-new-styles-contest-cash-prize-ends-october-1st/

So the new styles are starting to roll in, and for testing and giggles I threw most of them (the non-sucky/unbroken) ones on this site.

You can check them out by going to Account->Change Stylesheet.

I'm digging this "PurpleHaze" one. I thought I'd hate it, but it's quickly growing on me.

Check back next week, too, I should have more.

Eventually, what will happen is that I'm going to test them all out, and then once the contest above is over, I'm going to dump all the styles save a few (maybe like 5 max or so). From there, I'm going to slowly edit together some new styles like a Frankenstein monster made up of the older styles, and again reduce everything to about 5-7 styles or so. Eventually, I'll create the "Ultimate Bomber First Class Style" and that will become the Site Default for folks who haven't chosen a style, and leave the 5-7 others as options. I'm just saying this up front so there's no confusion when styles like "Great Concept but Fucking Bothersome Orange" disappear, etc.

Have fun!

-Andy
