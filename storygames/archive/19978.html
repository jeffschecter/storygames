    \n I really like this. Unlike a lot of the L&F hacks I see this embraces that the "Lasers" and "Feelings" stats are encouraging two different flavors of play. Guns and mojo distill the spy genre pretty well.

    I do have some notes on the scenario table:

    -For "Wants to," "Bond with" doesn't really seem like a genre appropriate phrase for espionage. James bond is larger than life but the scifi is more down to earth. At most he has crazy gadgets and weapons. Dominate or control might work better? Though that is similar to occupy.

    -I feel like "kidnap celebrity" is the most boring "Which will" on a really impressive list. Consider some thing with bigger stakes like "enslave the earth."

    Again, really solid work. I'd love to play this with my friends.
