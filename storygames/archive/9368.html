\n Ryan Macklin and I are running The Upgrade! at Go Play Northwest, so I went back and took the English language version apart and put it back together. The result is here. I'm hoping it is a little more practical and accessible - I edited a bit and organized it into double-sided handouts - one for the hosts, one for the cast, one with scene suggestions for everybody, and one for each character.

Check it out and let me know what you think.
