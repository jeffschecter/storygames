The Sprawl is a game of mission-based action in a gritty neon-and-chrome Cyberpunk future. You are the extended assets of vast multinational corporations, operating in the criminal underground, and performing the tasks that those multinationals can\u2019t do, or can\u2019t be seen to, do. Deniable, professional, and ultimately disposable.
\nFor those at the intersection of Cyberpunk Fans and Apocalypse World, I've released an updated version of The Sprawl.

If that sounds like you, please take a look! Comments welcome wherever.

    \n Ooh!

    I read the alpha before, and I particularly liked the world-building idea of having each player define a corporation. I borrowed it for my own scifi hack (where the PCs are the rulers / powerful / influential instead of lower-class / exploited). I had the players also define an existing relationship between their character and each corporation the other players had invented, which was something I thought I had also borrowed from The Sprawl Alpha, but I just had a quick browse through again and couldn't find a mention of it.

    Anyway, it worked amazingly well. So thanks for that!
