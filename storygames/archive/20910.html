\n I finished my latest design, just in time for Hanukkah!

Maccabees and Menorahs is a one page RPG played with a dreidel and gelt. Designed to run over 8 short sessions, one for each night of Hanukkah. Feel free to share broadly!

This might be my fastest project yet, from idea (Sunday) to final pdf (last night).
