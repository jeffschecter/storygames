\n Yesterday, I came across the "National Gaming Day @ Your Library" website.

In short, it's a way to get people to visit their local library and have fun with video and tabletop games on one day. I decided to quickly call in to the massive library in Santa Clara, Ca (which I'm practically next door to) to volunteer my services. The librarian I spoke to hasn't heard about this program, and she said she'd discuss it with a few other people after I directed them to the website and gave my phone number and credentials. Here's hoping things go well.

Still, this might be a great way to introduce people to story games and show it's not all slaying monsters by the metric ton.
