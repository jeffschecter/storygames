\n Since there's a nice 30% sale going on at Lulu right now (codes: REDEMPTIO is for the UK. DELIRITAS for the US) and since these sales always send me scrambling through my brain to remember what did I want to buy at Lulu?!, I thought a thread like this could be useful to many.

What I think is good:

SUPPLEMENTS:
The Metamorphica - Ah, so good. Random mutation tables like you wouldn't believe! Could also be used to make interesting alien races. I already have the free PDF, but this is so good I need it in dead tree.

Story Game Name Project - This book has names for everything. It's available in a zillion different formats now, including a smartphone app, but I derive comfort knowing this is in my game bag.

Play Unsafe - Improv techniques for roleplayers to make gaming less work and more fun.

GAMES:
Twenty-Four Game Poems - Cool, small games to get your brain running in different directions.

Monster of the Week - AW-style game for playing badass monster hunters.

Hollowpoint - Bad people killing bad people for bad reasons - as fun as it sounds!

NOVELS:
Hexcommunicated - I think Rafael's summary of his book says it best: Hexcommunicated is an urban fantasy thriller set in a world of cybernetic werewolves, undead spies, and Lovecraftian WMDs.
