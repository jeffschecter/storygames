\n My sister \u2014 you know, the sorceress-consort to the Court of Pestilence? \u2014 has decided that the whole 'mistress of the underworld' schtick isn't her thing. We're all really proud of her and think she's very brave for walking out on the Dread Lord and abandoning the path of evil. Of course there's a hitch \u2014 she's been a public figure for so long, her name and face are indelibly inked in the people's consciousness. Add to that, she's sort of hell-bent on joining that damned Coalition of Light and taking down the Dread Lord once and for all, so simply hiding her in some far-flung frontier province isn't an option.

She's been talking to some of the small gods and eldritch powers, trying to find a way to abandon her old identity. However, she's run into a bit of a snag. Harkun the Stormlord will scour her old persona into something new at the cost of her memories, but then she wouldn't remember her reasons for fighting the Dread Lord. On the other hand, Anstass Willow-Breaker will give her a total body reshaping, but wants her voice in exchange, and without her voice, there will be no summoning of spirits of air and darkness against her ex. She knows better than any of us that no great work of magic comes without a price, but the prices she's finding are not just inconvenient, they're counter-productive.

So she asked me to go online and do a little comparative shopping. Does anybody have some leads on fresh identities at a price that won't interfere with my sister's revenge plans?

Thanks so much!

    From the site rules:
    Partial list of items for sale and services the advertisement of which is not permitted on mageslist:

    (item 8) * Obscene spells or magics that concern necromancy through force (sacrifice or murder).

    Please refrain from suggesting humanoid sacrifice.

    Also, why not just go to the bizaar at NeuEier and pick up, say, 20 Obfuscation Scarves in bulk? Each one will last a few weeks: Makes it almost impossible for anyone to recognize you (unless they were in love with you, or you them, of course), you don't have to deal with anything permanent or disfiguring, and they're cheap to boot. Like what, 10 gold each? I mean, you can't claim an identity while using them (they simply make you unrecognizable or simply not interesting enough to notice for long), but there's no need for all that soul-swapping and the like.

    Plus, the added bonus: She can make a big reveal after defeating the evil dude, and have the receipts to prove that she was in fact working for the forces of good this whole time.

    See, there's plenty of magic to be had by repurposing inexpensive tools; no need to rely on expensive sorceries or wizard consults.

    \n Drugs are all the rage where I live, and while many will make you forget who you are, the really strong ones are so good that they'll make everyone else forget too. That's because of the devastating effect they have on the human body, naturally. Marikot, in particular, is highly efficacious after only a handful of doses (taken just beneath the lower eyelid, by injection). It tends to stain the eyes as black as night and leave the skin a deep shade of eggplant, as well as causing the nose and cheeks to sink in a bit, which is only natural since the drug is harvested from certain demons in one of the unfashionable circles of hell. I've been told that some people find the appearance quite becoming. However, her voice is going to get a bit huskier. Do the spirits of the air really mind?

    It does have certain psychological side effects, which you might consider a plus, since they tweak the personality a little. She'll be a little more irritable, and desire warm blood with every meal (but this is only so that she doesn't die of malnutrition, which I suppose isn't really a psychological problem).

    The downside is, of course, addiction and a distinctly different appearance. Marikot dealers are also prone extracting ridiculous prices for the drug once the addict starts "showing", since by then they're generally hooked for life. Users are also sterile (mercifully), and somewhat light-sensitive. Many of the worst side effects can be remedied by quitting the drug, a process which is is only fatal about half the time.
