    \n Oh, and let's not forget Canadian musician Final Fantasy, who's 2006 album He Poos Clouds, which won the Polaris Music Prize (given to the best full-length Canadian album) that year, was loosely inspired by the 8 schools of wizardly magic in D&D.\n
    WikipediaEight of the ten songs on He Poos Clouds are directly related to the eight schools of magic in the tabletop game Dungeons & Dragons[2]. The school of magic correlating to the song is in parentheses[3].

    1. "The Arctic Circle" \u2013 4:24 (Abjuration)
    2. "He Poos Clouds" \u2013 3:31 (Illusion)
    3. "This Lamb Sells Condos" \u2013 4:39 (Conjuration)
    4. "If I Were a Carp" \u2013 4:03 (Necromancy)
    5. "\u2192" \u2013 0:57
    6. "I'm Afraid of Japan" \u2013 3:56 (Enchantment)
    7. "Song Song Song" \u2013 4:31 (Evocation)
    8. "Many Lives \u2192 49 MP" \u2013 2:56 (Divination)
    9. "Do You Love?" \u2013 3:03 (Transmutation)
    10. "The Pooka Sings" \u2013 5:25

    \n As far as I know never recorded, only in print, but there is the Warhammer Career Song.
    \n
    \nA small adaptation to the current edition...
    \n
    \nI started a Commoner attending the local church,
    \nBut I wanted Observation so I could better search,
    \nI stumbled on the poor box, labelled \u201cwar relief\u201d
    \nSo I took the cash, spent XP, was now a Thief.
    \n
    \nI joined a Gang of Thugs, working on the sly,
    \nBut I started to feel guilty about the other guy,
    \nSo now I am an Agitator, ranting against riches
    \nWhen I'm not rolling toffs penniless into ditches.
    \n
    \nWhen the lord of the city by posted writ my death decreed,
    \nAs I ran, I thought after all this time I should learn to read,
    \nSo I became a Student with a change of name,
    \nStill I ended up fighting cults and mutants all the same.
    \n
    \nI\u2019m a character in Warhammer, reprobate and rake,
    \nI like to raise my stats up and I don\u2019t care what it takes.
    \nI\u2019m a character in Warhammer, take a look at me,
    \nIf it\u2019s got a skill I want to learn, then that\u2019s the job for me.
    \n
    \nAs a Pit Fighter I learned Resilience, a very useful skill,
    \nRaised up my wounds so I was that much harder to kill,
    \nResigned to a life of violence, I took Specialist (Fist),
    \nSo that I could go on in life as a living protagonist.
    \n
    \nIn this career I found myself oft smacked upon the head,
    \nIf it wasn\u2019t for my Fortune Points, I\u2019ld probably be dead.
    \nFor safety I donned some chainmail I had managed to acquire,
    \nThen convinced a captain as Mercenary me to hire.
    \n
    \nI\u2019m a character in Warhammer, my fortunes rise and fall,
    \nThere\u2019s a few trades that I\u2019ve mastered, but I\u2019m jack of almost all.
    \nI\u2019m a character in Warhammer, my stats will someday get maxed,
    \nMy weapon skill is specialized and I\u2019ve an extra die on all attacks.
    \n
