\n So the Voodoo Western RPG has been in the works for two years. I've run a couple dozen playtests and it's been through a number of revisions and it's ready to be released into the wild.

I've been using "active player" to designate who was in control of the primary character for a scene.
While protagonist is technically correct I'd like to name the player's role something a little more evocative without being kitschy. Hero is okay but there is a bit of a "good guy" connotation and I'm uncertain that's appropriate.

Any suggestions?

Thanks,
~Don
