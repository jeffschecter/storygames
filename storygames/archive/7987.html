    Posted By: jenskotI believe If someone is being rude or passive aggressive, they should be ignored. But that doesn't often work.
    \nIt actually appears to work about 99% of the time. That other 1%, you should whisper/email me (or Jason, now, until Sat/Sun or so). But usually, after that I rip into the thread and... ignore the person as well. Sometimes I give them a whisper, which helps. Most of the time it ends there.

    A lot of people find solace in whispering their friends/acquaintances with stuff like, "Dammit, what the hell is with this guy?" etc. It actually does help take the edge off a bit. Even for me.

    But yeah, I'd argue that you should do this, in order:
    1) Don't read too much meaning into a poster's post, if you find it aggravating to you. A lot of the time, it was just a miscommunication. Or a general feeling that the reader may be reading too much into.
    2) Otherwise, try to ignore the person. Most of the time it works.
    3) If the person continues to be in that thread what we call in the forum admin biz, "A Dipshit", then you probably want to whisper/email me with the details.
    4) In most of the cases, I "keep an eye on the thread". In fewer cases, I actually do something, most of the time a whisper to the offender requesting that they take it down a notch.

    You can try to whisper the offending party directly and continue the conversation further, however I often see that lead to a mess. But, it never hurts to try, especially if you empathize with the person's point without trying to lecture them on their behavior like a schoolmarm. Again, just an observation. Do as you wilt.

    -Andy
