    \n Roll20 can be spotty in performance at times; this afternoon the group I played in was not able to see a map the GM was sharing with us, so we resorted to dropbox for that. Another option would have been to use skype's shared desktop. If you all have good machines with a lot of RAM, google+ hangouts can handle the video chat plus text chat plus document sharing, and then you can load the roll20 app inside the hangout.

    ETA: There's another cool hangout app called "DiceStream" that does exactly what you suspect it does.
