Since some folks here mentioned they hadn't previously heard of Truth & Justice, here's a list of resources:

Freebies (game preview, errata, characters sheets, etc.) from the ASMP website:
http://www.atomicsockmonkey.com/freebies.asp#tj

The T&J LiveJournal community is located here:
http://community.livejournal.com/tandj_asmp/

I try to post something there every week: an NPC write-up (usually with HeroMachine art, since it's free and online), an interesting thread, a call for actual plays, etc.

And the T&J Yahoo Group mailling list is here:
T&J Yahoo Group

I'd really love it if the mailling list follows the lead of the Unknown Armies mailling list, and becomes just a cool thing to read (that usually has game useful material, but not always). So far, we've had a few cool "general super-hero" discussions and such, which is tres cool.

I think all three are decent uses of the tools at my disposal for supporting and spreading the world about the game, but I find myself wondering if I'm missing any major resource niches.

(The only one I can think of is "a forum" or "bulletin board" -- but I'm not sure how much that gains anyone when I already have the mailling list and LJ running.)

Feel free to look around and comment!

CU
