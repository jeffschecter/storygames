    \n just wrote a bunch of things down last night.
    \ni haven't gotten In a Wicked Age yet...but i think i get the idea.
    \nso here goes...i thought about calling it the Zimmer Oracle or Jewish Cowboy or Baker on Blonde, something like that...anyway
    \nthis is just from Visions of Johanna..
    \n1. the night plays tricks
    \n2. Louise holds a handful of rain, tempting you to defy it
    \n3. lights flickering from the opposite loft
    \n4. a room where the heat pipes cough
    \n5. a country music station playing soft
    \n6. Louise and her lover entwined
    \n7. visions of Johanna conquering my mind
    \n8. an empty lot where ladies are playing blindman's bluff with a key chain
    \n9. all night girls whispering of escapades on the d-train
    \n10. a night watchmen tests his flashlight and questions his sanity
    \n11. the ghost of electricity howls in the bones of her face
    \n12. visons of Johanna have taken my place
    \n13. a little boy lost, taking himself too seriously
    \n14. inside a museum infinity goes up on trial
    \n15. voices echo talks of salvation
    \n16. Mona Lisa with the highway blues
    \n17. a primate wallflower freezes in place
    \n18. the jellyfaced women all sneeze
    \n19. a person with a mustache who lost their knees
    \n20. a mule carrying around it's neck jewels and binoculars
    \n21. a peddler speaks to a countess how pretends to care
    \n22. Madonna hasn't shown herself
    \n23. an empty cage, corroded
    \n24. an empty stage, without curtain
    \n25. a fiddle player approaches the side of a road, writes on a fish truck
    \n26. harmonicas play the skeleton keys and the rain
    \n27. visions of Johanna are now all that remain
