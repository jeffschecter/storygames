    \n If you're looking for encouragement to get the thing, I'd say it's worth about... $20, to me anyway. I read it cover to cover, but only played a few of the games. Pagoda is awesome but has some holes which you will find in play. Pretender is the strongest game in the book (it's basically WoD but with Otherkind/Psi Run "fortune in the middle" mechanics), and I had a lot of fun with it. I never made the connection that "WTF?" was written by THE Daniel Solis, above, until now.

    Also, in the year(s?) since it's release, I think I've done more promotion of the book than any of the authors. That was kind of a sticking point for the producer, but overall it was a good experiment in group publishing.

    -Andy
