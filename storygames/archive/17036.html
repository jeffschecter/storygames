\n Some of you may remember seeing my game, School Daze on here some months back. At the time, I was Kick starting it. Now, the fruits of that funding have paid off. School Daze is now for sale on DriveThruRPG: http://rpg.drivethrustuff.com/product/104650/School-Daze

The game is rules-light, and uses a high school setting to tell improvisational, player driven stories. If you want a chance to check it out in person, and will be at GenCon this weekend, I'm running many sessions of it at Games on Demand. I hope to see you there!
