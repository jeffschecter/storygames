\n So, I'm heading off tomorrow to Connecticon 2015! Woo!

While I'm there, I'm going to be doing a panel on roleplaying game design called Cancel your Fantasy Heartbreaker: How to Actually Write that RPG, where I hope to go into a bit of detail on the techniques I use to write games and why incorporating lessons from nontraditional RPGs into your projects is a good idea. I've been writing it a while and I've very excited; this'll be my first panel presentation!

I'm also going to be bringing my current draft of PATROL along and hopefully playing some test games with whoever is interested.

Is anyone else going?
