\nHeroine is a storytelling game about a girl who has a chance to be heroic in the Other World. It is inspired by Labyrinth, Alice in Wonderland, Wizard of Oz, and the Chronicles of Narnia. The game is written by Josh Jordan, and I am Kickstarting art by J.R. Blackwell and layout by Daniel Solis.


Seeing as how the game has benefited from the playtest feed back I received on Story Games, I would be a fool not to announce the Kickstarter here. Please take a look. We are over halfway to our initial goal and we have some fun stretch goals available.

Thanks!
