\n Hey, folks, it's time for another installment of What We're Playing at #playnow. On the table today: "Unhallowed," a scenario for Dead of Night, with room for five players.

The premise:\n
In the war between good and evil, good lost. The world lies in smouldering ruins, ravaged by the war between heaven and hell. The surviving remnants of humanity have been herded into great camps by the demonic servants of Lucifer.

In this scenario the players are a small band of survivors who are holed up in the crypt of a church, still protected by mystical wardings. Necessity drives them out into the open, rumour of another sanctuary leads them to the centre of the city where they discover other survivors fighting back, the fate of the last angel and the Devil himself.
\nRight now we're trying to figure out the best day and time to play, so if you're interested, please stop by the channel and put your two cents in. New faces welcome!
