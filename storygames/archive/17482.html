    \n Hi guys here's the update as far I know,

    The virtualized guest that is burningwheel.org lives in Chicago. About 10 of the parent hosts had their administrative pages hacked. It looks like the hackers used their access to delete the guests and burningwheel.org was one of those that got dumped. The hosting provider is restoring everyone from backups but if that fails I've got backups from last week that I took before BurningCon.

    Stormsweeper and myself are going to give them a bit longer and then I'm going to restore everything from my own backups.

    Cheers!
