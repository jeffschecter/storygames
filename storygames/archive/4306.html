\n Hey there,

You're invited to Greenfield, MA for the next installment of the indie goodness that is JiffyCon. We've got Dogs in the Vineyard scheduled, as well as Steal Away Jordan, 1001 Nights, Shock:, Mechaton and new to the scene: Giants and Acts of Evil. Also, we'll play two new structured freeform games that Jonathan Walton and I are working on.

The website is up, go check it out:

http://www.jiffycon.com

One of our friends from the area commented that he really liked the format we have going. Something I'm proud of is that we provide kid care for parents who come. It's really varied how many kids we have there, but it makes a huge difference for the folks with little ones. It means Meg and Vincent can actually play, instead of taking turns chasing the boys around. And since so many of us are past a certain age, and some of us are doing our share to keep the species going, it just makes sense to do it this way.

I'm glad to report a lot of folks are coming from near and far this time around. Let me know if you can make it. We'd love to have you.

Plus, if you're lucky you could play Steal Away Jordan for yourself, and see what all the fuss is about.

best,
Emily

Edited to add: It's on November 17!!!!
