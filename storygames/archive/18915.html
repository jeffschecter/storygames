\n Here is a link to the Lone Warrior - the journal of the Solo Wargaming Association

http://lonewarriorswa.com/

The SWA has been around for over thirty years and is based in England. It's put out a journal that whole time which is full of great game ideas. Some of the games use miniatures others don't. There is a wargame focus but that is not all they do. I wrote Engle Matrix game articles for them 20 years ago. I think you might find their stuff interesting. Makes me think I should start writing for them again.

Chris Engle
