\n People of Story Games,

I am about to embark on the adventure of a lifetime. I cannot take all my role-playing games with me, however! So, I am going to sell those which I've played and loved and have PDFs of. You can see a list of my games for sale with their prices on Google Docs.

I will be charging shipping, as many books are priced very low. I'll just be charging Media Mail prices + $1 (for a box) to the US. If you're overseas, we can figure something out.

If you are interested in something, whisper in this thread. First come, first served, and I'll try to update the list when something's sold.
