\n I want to know your food habits while gaming. Just a survey really.
\n
\nDo you eat while gaming?
\nIs it a meal?
\nOr is it snacks?
\nDoes the host provide?
\nDo the players provide?
\nIs it byo?
\nIs there a favored style of meal?
\nIs there alcohol?
\n
\nI usually eat and drink something whenever gaming, unless that's specifically not ok with the group, and even then I usually step out and eat some snacks from my pocket to keep my blood sugar even.
\nAt our regular sunday game there's almost always dinner, and I usually cook. Lately I've been favoring casseroles and baked pastas that I can prepare before people get here. At various other games we go to it's usually "snack luck" with people each providing a little. That works well.
\nSometimes people chip in for the sunday meal, but i'm ok with feeding people, and beverage is usally brought and left, so that makes it even in my book.
\nSometimes we have a few beers or wine with dinner. once in a great while we have some whiskey. We used to smoke green stuff, but that was back in college, and the games were more casual in tone then.

    \n Weekly game: People eat beforehand or bring their dinner, and we eat in the 30 minute pre-game chat session. Then the rest of the evening we gorge on nasty ice cream and candy corn. I've been meaning to, like, NOT, but it's hard. Alcohol is always within reach, though we don't always engage.

    Bi-Weekly Game: Folks eat beforehand, or else bring dinner and eat it when they can near the beginning of the game. Sometimes there is beer. Usually no snacks, but we all drink lots of water and take lots of bathroom breaks.

    Weekend gaming: Usually snacks, though I'm trying to be a little bit healthier with the food choices.
