So, I had the best of intentions to pay attention to how our games went, attendence-wise, and I did so well for 2 slots. And then it all went to pot.

I mean, we did well - we had a full room of games, overflowing to other rooms at times, for pretty much the whole con. But, I think it would be a useful thing to take a more detailed look at the info.

So, people running IGE games, post your timeslot and game, whether it went off, and the number of players (other than yourself) you had. I'll update this post as info comes in.

EDIT: please post pickup games and such, as well. I'll keep filling in the slots on this post. Also, I'm filling in stuff as I see it mentioned on blogs and other posts. Feel free to post corrections and clarifications. Thanks everyone!
