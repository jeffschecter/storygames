\n As we know, Matt Machell is a very self-effacing guy and doesn't like to toot his own trombone (unless in the privacy of his own home). However, he recently did a little add-on for Covenant which I happen to think is very cool and deserves more people getting involved.

The Covenant Codex is in familiar Wiki format, but has got me very excited about adding cities, factions, conventions and truisms for use in Covenant (which is a game I'm also very excited about).

You should take a look at it and maybe add some stuff. It deserves expanding and deserves more attention.

One thing I'm thinking: are collaborative things like this the way to go for collatig information for a game like Covenant? I think yes, they are very useful (as they are for so many things).

Cheers
Malcolm
