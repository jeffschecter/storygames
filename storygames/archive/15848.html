\n So, we've flung a lot of general enthusiasm around, we've brainstormed some cool game facilitation mojo, and we've come together on a short, snappy docket of awesome panels. All good!

Now it's time to talk about the actual schedule of games. A lot of folks have submitted games, and that's great! A lot of folks, also, have volunteered for Open Story Gaming circle to pitch games in a great big anarchic gameathon. Super-great.

The events will be open for player signups February 20, which is NEXT MONDAY. So while there's still time to submit after that, this makes a nice target date to have our lineup rounded out. So let's look at where we stand!

A great lineup so far. What I notice is, we've got a lot of scheduled afternoon games, especially Friday, and scant few evening games. If anyone is looking for a hole to fill, I'd ask you to concentrate your fire (that is, fire from your .50 CALIBER HOLE-FILLING GUN) on Saturday night, and maybe raking your full auto over Thursday and Friday night as well. If you've still got action points left, squeezing off a few rounds toward Friday or Sunday morning would be cool. I'll leave it up to your judgment; I don't know how the autofire rules work in your hack.

If anyone on the Friday Afternoon docket feels a strong urge to move to a different timeslot, it wouldn't go amiss, for that matter. It looks like we're butting up against our table limit i that slot.*

We can also use a few more scheduled Open Gaming facilitators. I'd like at least three for each listing, and four wouldn't hurt. Right now we have three on every slot but Friday Afternoon, which has one. (I had Ross there, but moved him down to Fri Evening so he could man the booth in the afternoon.) Any takers?

Beyond that, use this space to talk about games you're interested in running, or which you'd like to see people run! For instance, I'm intensely interested whether anyone will be bringing Psi*Run to the show...?

Peace,
-Joel

*We have four tables in Room Birch, and three tables outside it. There are a couple of seating areas nearby that folks have been known to use as unofficial playspace, and there're overflow tables in another room, but only during certain hours. If it looks like we're going to go over space (and Open Gaming slots can potentially be 3 or more games!), I'm certain I can make it work, but I need to know as soon as possible so I can investigate and plan the overflow.

    \n PS and by the way, everyone, the Gamestorm website is a bit tricky, so a tutorial on setting up your GM profile might be in order.

    FIRST:

    Go to the Gamestorm Main Page, and under the LOGIN section on the upper left, find the little button that says "register" when you mouse over. Click it, andfill out the form to register.

    SECOND:

    Go to the Gamestorm Schedule Site, and Login if necessary. Click Edit Profile. Fill in all the required fields, including first and last name.

    You should now have a GM account, which uses that first and last name, and are able to submit proposals, which I will then approve! Be sure to indicate which slot (Friday Afternoon, Saturday Evening, etc) you want the game in.

    The actual scheduling system will not recognize you until you complete that last step, though you may have an account on the mai page.

    Hope that troubleshoots everyone in advance!
