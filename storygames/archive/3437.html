\n I have a few players literally begging for a Jonathan Strange and Mr. Norrell game, and I'm having a hell of a time deciding on a system. Risus is a bit too open, and Fudge just doesn't seem to fit, but I guess it could work. Forgotten Futures is too restrictive in my book. Sorcerer could do with some major shoe-horning-- with demons defined as fairy servants as well as negative personality traits (Strange's arrogance, Norrell's paranoia and greed) Just looking for a few suggestions as to how I can get this off the ground.
