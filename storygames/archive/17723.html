\nAmerican Disasters! New rules for chaining together multiple Fiasco sessions in Trainwreck mode, as well as three tight playsets that work together perfectly. We've been squirreling these playsets away just waiting to use them as Trainwreck examples. They are really good!

You can pick this up at Drive-Thru RPG right now.
