I have been thinking about the game books that I really like, as objects--things to read and keep. There aren't too many of them just now (but that collection is increasing).

They have a common thread; they have really strong, distinctive, natural-seeming voices. Weapons of the Gods is an interesting study in mixed authors with sharply contrasted voices...

I don't know where I'm goin' with this, just wanted to bring it up. I really like when an author has a voice.
