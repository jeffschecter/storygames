MonkeyCon One is a virtual convention for roleplaying games. It is a celebration of the
Monkeys Took My Jetpack podcast and homage to what they and the GutterSkypes have
done for role playing games via Skype.

As of the time of this writing, MonkeyCon One will be held as a two day event on
October 19th and 20th, 2012. The event is ticketless and free. You'll need to sign up
to attend activities such as games in one of the six time slots.

All activity times are listed as US Central time.

Joining at the last minute? Check out the IRC for real-time help...
http://www.sorcery.net:9091/?channels=MonkeyCon&uio=d4

You can follow the discussion at:
http://mtmjforum.sitex.us/index.php

Sign up to run and play games at:
http://www.conplanner.com/MC1

All the way up to the last day we'll be accepting new events and players.

Subscribe to the RSS feed or look for MonkeyCon activity reports on fine fora such as:
Monkeys Took My Jet Pack, the Gutterskypes, Fear the Boot, and Story Games.

    \n Well, there are no tickets and everything is free as in free beer. You should be able to sign up and either create new activities in the previous mentioned slots or join an existing activity. Monkey Con is essentially a good excuse to get together with friends and strangers to play favorites or something new. Need to do a play test session with new people? Make a pitch and create an activity.

    I encourage people to record if everyone is OK with that and share it. Last year we have people from five different actual play podcasts present. [Yes, MonkeyCon is counting from zero.]
