\n The game scheduling forum is now open for Go Play NW 2017!http://www.goplaynw.org/forums/viewforum.php?f=49

If you're coming to Go Play, consider adding a game to the schedule.

Go Play NW is a small indie tabletop gaming convention in Seattle, WA. This year it will be held July 7-9. Go Play NW features a mix of scheduling gaming (through the forums) and ad-hoc gaming (through a pitch session for each gaming slot). We are all about playing games and meeting people. Check out the web site for more information: http://goplaynw.org/.
