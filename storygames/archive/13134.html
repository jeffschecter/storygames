\n Don't know if any of you will be in West Virginia later this month, but our annual (and only) convention dedicated to games will be held Oct. 22-24 at the Charleston Civic Center.

Indie games still don't have a huge presense, but sometimes you can find games of The Mountain Witch and some other titles being played. There also are a lot of homebrewed-style games which are actually pretty fun. Plus tons and tons of board and card games.

If anyone would be interested in showcasing their game at the con, I easily can get you in with the organizers. They are a great bunch of game enthusiasts and big supporters of self-published games.

www.charcon.org
