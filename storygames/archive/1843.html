    There were three sessions of Spirit of the Century.

    The first was the chargen session run by Paul Tevis, with myself, Nancy (faerieloch), John Wick, Ryan Macklin, Laura BishopRoby, Alejandro, and his son Julian.

    The second was a scenario run for us (minus Laura) by Paul the next night. After landing an exploding airplane, we tracked down a mobster archaeologist turned giant viking by an ancient mystic axe.

    The third was a scenario run for us (with Laura, minus John and Julian) by Ryan. We chased the evil clockwork minions of the twisted Baron von Robot to his hidden castle in Deepest Bavaria and had to rescue two-fisted detective Dick Awesome (Ryan's character from the previous night) from the baron's clutches.
