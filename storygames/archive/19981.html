\n These are games I'd like to play, but need to really have at least a few sessions of to feel like they're worth the commitment. Many of these are games I learned by playing a one-shot of them, but feel like I understand on that level about as well as I'm going to, and so I want to get deeper with them. (Except maybe to demo for other people, like at a con or something.) Add other suggestions in the comments!

    \n I've always thought that the strength of Forgite indie game design was not the one shot (as compared to the "trad" long-term campaign), but rather something in the middle:

    The ability to design and carry-out a really solid medium-term game, with a beginning, middle and end.

    Somewhere in the 2-session to 10-session area, depending on the game. That's what a lot of these games are made for and the context in which they shine.
