Last night, I found out that Google now offers SketchUp for free at: http://sketchup.google.com/ I had previously used a demo version of SketchUp for a project in the past, so I was pretty excited to get a copy for free. After I downloaded it, I discovered that Google has set up a design library that users can upload and download from, so I browsed it and found a couple of nice castles and the like, but I couldn't find any dungeons....

About five hours later, I uploaded a very nice 3D dungeon to Google. It's single layer with one level two room. If you want to check it out, you can download SketchUp and then go to the Warehouse view from the Google --> Get Model menu.

If you're doing any mapping for your games, this is a sweet deal.
