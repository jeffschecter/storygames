\n Hi everyone.

Sufficiently Advanced is getting to the stage where I need artwork. Ergo, I am looking for artists.

I'm going to be looking not just for good art, but for a specific feel. Clean lines, a feeling of elegance or optimism, and beautiful vibrant color - the whole book is going to be full-color. Some of the stuff I've already made for it (and Daniel Solis' beautiful cover) can be found on my DeviantArt site, but that's the extent of my artistic talent - I have no ability to draw people.

My pay is typically $25 per quarter-page, and I do usually buy quarter-page illustrations. Since this is probably going to be a 200-page book, I'm going to need quite a few pieces. My checkbook quails in terror, believe me. Artists will get to keep the rights to their work, including the ability to resell it to others.

If you know anyone whose art fits those criteria and is willing to work for that price, please let me know.
