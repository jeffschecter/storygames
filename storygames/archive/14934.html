\n An alternative.

When creating your initial three-node mission seed, roll six dice at once and allocate them, column & row, to preference.

This ensures, for me, that I won't get a result that I'll find difficult as a jumping off point, i.e. two objects and a location, while still retaining the fun pick-up-and-play random generation nature of the tool.
