\n I stumbled upon the polyhedral images category on the ever-reliable Wikipedia. It's chock full of inspirational stuff!

The flattened truncated isocahedreon and flattened truncated icosidodecahedron both look like board game maps, or character sheets, or both.

Who wouldn't want a cockeyed augmented dodecahedron rolling around? It's faces wouldn't land reliably and some would be favored in interesting ways.

Finally, there's this Insano 600-cell cantitrunculated nonsense, which is crying out to be a book cover or something. Recent Game Chef people using Memory, Palace, and/or Drugs take note.
