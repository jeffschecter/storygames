\n I've designed and posted two free games on my site, both made in about an hour.

Everyone Wants to be Sartre is a game about emo kids and their internal conflicts.

Wounded is an experimental game is more of a prep for building characters in a traditional rpg. I think it can stand alone as a fun 2-hour distraction.

Both games are rough and in need of some playtesting. But I'm posting them here for you to enjoy, while I prep for and attend GenCon.
