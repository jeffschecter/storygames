\n
\n Last week I finally finished translating all of Maid RPG, which includes a full sixteen adventure scenarios. The overall approach to scenario-writing in them is really interesting, because there are several of them that actually introduce new bits of rules to fit the situation. There are quite a few where the PCs have a given amount of time to work on something, and they get a specific number of actions/scenes they can do per time interval. There are also ones that add things like giant robots, world conquest, detective stories. (Some more on that here).

I've also been trying to brainstorm new scenarios, and I've come to a realization:

Maid RPG can be about goddamn anything.

All you really have to do is contrive an excuse for there to be a Master and some kind of cute maids to be present. Andy came up with the idea of having maids serve Dethklok. Not a few people hear about the concept and say, "Is that anything like My Live With Master?" and I can't help but think that "My Life With Goshujin-sama" would be very easy to do. Another friend of mine wanted to do a Maid RPG version of Dark Heresy.

So, what game, genre, or title would you pervert with maids?
