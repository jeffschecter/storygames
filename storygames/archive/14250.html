\n The Pulpy Primer is a trifold pamphlet I made to help communicate what I love about sword and sorcery fiction to people who may be less familiar with the genre. It takes things you're familiar with from "standard" fantasy, like taverns, knights on horseback, wizards, and orcs, and gives you a list of alternatives with a more "pulpy" flavour.

To celebrate (and, to be honest, advertise) the release of my game, On Mighty Thews, I am making the Pulpy Primer available for free:

The Pulpy Primer

Enjoy!
