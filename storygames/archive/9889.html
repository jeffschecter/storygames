\n So I have this idea for a Sorcerer game that centers around the old pith helmet explorers. Guys with moustaches exploring Africa and the Himalayas and whatever. Places mysterious and strange and dark.

Sorcerers are explorers. People who leave the 'civilized' places for faraway lands.

Sorcery would be expeditions.

Demons would be things you brought back with you that don't belong here. Strange magicks, powerful artifacts, unnatural beasts. These are the ones you kept to yourself.

Humanity would be civilization and adherence to civilized customs. Going to classy parties and acting proper and upright. Hitting Humanity 0 represents just losing your patience with "civilized society" and leaving and not coming back.

I'm imagining the default setting for this would be late 1800s England.
