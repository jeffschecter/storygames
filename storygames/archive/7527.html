\n This emerged from our game group on Tuesday night: stripping as a game mechanic! No, we didn't try it out, but we did imagine what it might be like...
\n
\nScott Dorward (Sitting at the head of the table, wearing nothing but a thong): He doesn't want to give you that unless you sacrifice your sister's ife...(Scott stands, his thumbs reaching into the waistband of all that conceals What Man Was Not Meant To Know) Want to make it a conflict?
\n
\nPlayer: GOD, NO! NO!! He can take my sister, jesus, just please sit down again!
\n
\nChorus of Other Players: God, my eyes! They burn! Aieeeeeeeeeee!!
\n
\n
\nGraham, Scott expects you to have the mechanics drawn up by the end of next week; I said you'd probably dig them out of something you wrote ages ago.
