\n Specifically, does anyone know of an app for tablet or smart phone which simulates the chance mechanism where colored stones are placed in a bag and then one drawn at random?

There are a few story games that use this, and I also use it for karmic luck "rolls".
While GMing, as a character does nice guy/dick moves, white and black stones are put in the bag, and when looking for inspiration for the character, or when we hit a lull in the action, I draw a stone at random, and riff off of the light or dark nature of the "luck".

I'd like to use this in improv sessions with my friends, which we do sometimes without access to dice and game props. I have a dice roller, but no app for drawing stones.

    Another thought:

    Pulling stones from a bag is easily simulated with a deck of cards. (e.g. with red cards being white stones and black cards being black stones)
