    Posted By: Jason NewquistHi Nathan. I think you meant the question a little more seriously than it's been treated so far
    \ni'd just like everyone to know that my answer was TOTALLY SERIOUS.
    every game is different.
    every game that has ever caught my eye has had something about it that made me go "Ooooh! That's AWESOME!" sometimes it's a really funky setting (Noumenon is a great example of one like this that i haven't gotten yet) and
    sometimes it's a really interesting mechanic or rules set (Polaris is still my favorite example there).
    that's all i look for.
    i don't look for a certain thing out of every game. on the contrary, i want all games to be different, to do different cool things and to do them as well and as awesomely as possible.
