\n In going over the Dreamation 2010 schedule last night, I noticed that there were no sessions of the new Warhammer Fantasy RolePlay 3rd Edition. That really surprised me, to be honest. I know that there are a lot of people here on SG that are interested in the game and haven't had the opportunity to see it get played. I've run a couple of sessions of the game at home, and I definitely wouldn't mind running a pick-up session of the game sometime at Dreamation.

If you're interested in seeing what WFRP is like, just speak up here and let me know. Let's try to organize a time to meet up and get a quick session of gameplay under our belts. I'll be there for the whole of the show, and I get along fine on late nights.
