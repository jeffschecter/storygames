    \n You mean a second meaning on the card, or another card in the deck with an opposite meaning? We do have the reverse versions of a lot of cards in the deck already:

    Wealthy vs Poor
    Large vs Small
    Quiet vs Loud
    Lucky vs Jinxed
    Etc

    If you can think of an opposite from our card list that isn't already included let me know.

    I'm hesitant to put too much on a single card though, to keep a nice clean look to each card.

    \n I like the idea that expansions would provide descriptors for things other than people (whereas the ones in the main deck all work for people). Some fun expansions might be descriptors for:
    - items
    - groups (factions, organizations, govts, etc.)
    - locations
    - relationships
    - situations (probably easier to build these out of multiple relationships, but just throwin' it out there)
    - spans of time (covering events and processes)
    - forces (weather, earthquakes, gravity, fate, momentum, etc.)

    Separately, I clicked into this thread titled "Short Order Heroes" thinking it was a Superhero RPG. You might want to change the thread title to mention the fact that it's story cards.
