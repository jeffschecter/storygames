    \n
    \n Casual gaming is exploding with coolness.

    Super Energy Apocalypse: a new take on the old resource gathering model, where you also have to dispose of your pollution and trash. Most indispensible unit in the game is the garbage truck.

    Zilch: showing that there are always new things to do with dice

    Onslaught2: looks like a straightforward tower defense game but slowly reveals depths of potential strategy

    Portal: the Flash Version. 'nuff said

    Mud and Blood 2: looks like a wargame, but is really a sandbox

    Also, The Redistricting Game shows that having an agenda doesn't mean your game HAS to be bad.
