Beast Hunters Art Preview

Christian_Griffen
October 2006 edited October 2006 in Directed Promotion
I've put an art preview up on our site: Berengad Games

The tattoos came out the way I envisioned them (a cross between modern tribal and Pacific Northwest Native art), and I'm very happy about that. The sketches of actual scenes are meant to be more evocative and moody rather than detailed and stark. I.e., this is not a photograph of something that happened; this is the image you get in your mind while the beast hunter describes her latest hunt at the tribal gathering. I think Joanna captured that style beautifully.

We're still on track with getting the art and text done by the end of this month. We've got 8 tattoos finalized so far (there will be a total of 15), as well as a couple of scenes. Playtesting is underway; I've made some changes and some neat additions that I'm really excited about.

Comments are more than welcome.

As a side note, I'm having issues with Wordpress right now: you might need to click "refresh" for the page to actually show. I have no idea how to fix this, but I'm trying to figure it out.
