\n Hey there,

As you may have seen from reading this thread, there will be another Indie Games Explosion at Dreamation 2008. If you don't yet know about Dreamation, consider coming. It will be held Jan 24-27 in East Brunswick, NJ and has a great scene for indie gaming.

For gms taking part in the IGE, they are waiving some requirements since we are coordinating it as a group. They ask that each gm run at least 3 games with 4 or more players during the weekend. Perks this gives are access to a staff suite with food, a free room we can share and free admission to the event.

To register as part of the IGE:

1) post (or whisper to me) information about your 3 or more games to this thread, answering the questions listed below.

2) join the IGE google group and add your games to the schedule there on the Games List.

If you want to register the games yourself or don't want to post the info here, email me at keirgreeneyes+at+yahoo+com

Thank you! I'll post the info needed next.
