    \n Got to see Paprika the other night, and really enjoyed it. It's one of the better "dream war" type films I've seen, and does a great job of not only playing with "real (movie) world vs dream world" but also plays a little with "real (movie) world vs real (real) world". And fabulous animation. It's a bit more upbeat and light than the mood I get from Lacuna, but there is certainly inspiration to be had.
