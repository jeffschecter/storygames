\n I'd like to circulate a rather fun experience I had recently playing online, with Elizabeth and her game-in-progress It's Complicated. The game is about telling interesting stories about the interactions that come about between people of dysfunction and oddity, something alla Pushing Daisies. I wrote an entire review of the game and experience here: Complications & Mess. Its not totally flattering, but this is only playtest #3, and I think its rather fair. Overall I had a good time, and hope the game will rock.
