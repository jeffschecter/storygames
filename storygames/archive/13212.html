    Posted By: viktor_haag

    See, that's my chief worry about the Quarantine. While I think playing one would be cool ("this strange fellow wearing a yellow jumpsuit, and with a bleeding forehead, just staggered into town: he's jabbering in a language you don't understand -- is it even a language? Might be Russian..."), I rather suspect that, given the "only one player per playbook" groundrule, it solidly cuts out any notion of Morrow Project/Project Phoenix/etc type scenarios...
    \nI mean, it's fine to create a scenario where everyone just came out of quarantine, but only one is The Quarantine.

    Like:

    1. Timber was the first to emerge, followed shortly by Dusk and Uppers. I mean, they didn't go in with those names, but when they thawed, the old names didn't seem to fit any more. I mean, what kind of a name is "Jared Sorensen" in a world with mutant cannibals and psychic maelstroms? Soren's dead, along with his sons. Now, Ricky... Ricky's auto-dethaw didn't work like it was supposed to. And so, while the others have been out for a little while now, setting up their lives, Ricky's still been stuck in cryo. Finally, Uppers (the Savvyhead) has figured out a manual bypass solution involving a couple car batteries and some green sludge. Ricky's freeze cell pops open. His friends are there to greet him. And he's scared as fuck.

    2. Some stuff survived the apocalypse. Cars survived, guns survived, and fickle human passions survived. So, when your squad came out of quarantine, they fit right in. They grabbed some gear, and named themselves after whatever they could find. Luke became Impala, but he was still a Driver. Marty became AK, but he was still a Gunlugger. Amy became Absinthe, but she was still a Battlebabe. But what about you? Rank didn't survive the apocalypse. Law and order didn't survive the apocalypse. The entire fucking chain of command failed to survive the apocalypse. There's no place in the apocalypse for an officer like you. You emerge to find an alien world into which you don't belong. You're just that guy, the other-place guy, The Quarantine.
