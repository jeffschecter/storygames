    \n To give more information:

    Have Games, Will Travel Q&A and The Roast of Paul Tevis

    The event will be a hybrid of a roast (an event where people mock and poke fun at the guest of honor) and a Q&A session. I'll be the MC & Roastmaster for this awesome event.

    If you have a question you would like to ask Paul, you can either ask in person at the event, or email Paul at havegameswilltravel@gmail.com. We might be able to do some audio there, so feel free to record your question or comment.

    If you want to participate in roasting Paul, email me at MasterPlan@hmfy.com. Some folks have already talked to me about material, and I want to make sure that we aren't just telling the same jokes over and over. I also think it would be a disservice to Paul if he knows what we have in store for him.

    We're looking forward to seeing ya'll there!
