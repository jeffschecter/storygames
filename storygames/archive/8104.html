\n From the 1979 reprint issue of White Dwarf. Yes, this was actually published.

The Pervert

The prime requisite for the Pervert is Charisma, which must be below 9. However, both Strength and Constitution must exceed 12. Alignment can be heterosexual, bisexual, or homosexual. Preverts are armour class 7 when wearing black leather armour and using an unsheathed weapon, but suffer a 50% chance of loss of sight per melee turn if weapon is hand held. Suggested Artifact: Elven dirty mac.
