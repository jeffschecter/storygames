    \n Got my pdfs. The Serpentine looks awesome.

    Failing Dynasty
    In the age of serpents, your family was powerful and prolific. Now, they live in a shadow of their former glory. They want to regain (choose one): their political clout; their old wealth; their failing beauty; their web of secret allegiances. Whenever you are convinced to do the bidding of a family member, carry 1 forward to doing it and that family member gains a String on you. Whenever you help your family regain some of their former glory, mark experience.

    Such a great hook. I want to play in a Veronica Mars-esque Monsterhearts game with a Serpentine from a "Kane-family-in-Neptune" clone.

    Flipping through the Skins, I get so many ideas for TV series. I like the idea of pitching a concept like a PTA pitch session, picking 5 Skins that fit that concept and *then* having players pick from among those few Skins. They could always add moves from the other Skins later.

    All my ideas so far have one or two Mortal characters - that seems like a really critical archetype to me. "Straight men" to make the monsters more monstrous, more forbidden.
