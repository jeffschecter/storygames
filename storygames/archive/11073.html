\n Epimas, as you well know, is the traditional day of game giving. Every year, on December 24th, friends and family gather round an exchange games, which they then play well into the night. Often making new friends or settling old scores. It's a most magical time of the year.
\n
\nDig a Thousand Holes, npd design, and Black & Green Games want to do our very best to make this the perfect Epimas season for you and yours. So we've gotten together and came up with this special offer: from now until Epimas (Dec. 24) if you purchase one of our PDFs for a friend, you can select any of the PDFs involved in the offer for yourself, for free. The gift PDF will be e-mailed to your friend (or loved one or arch nemesis or random stranger with a valid e-mail address) on December 24th, but your free PDF will be sent to you as soon as we can get to it. Details are all right here.
\n
\nGive the gift of giving to yourself this Epimas season.
