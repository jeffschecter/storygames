Caper! A game of Pros and Cons.

Just in time for a new Fall tv season which features shows like 'Smith', 'The Nine', and 'The Knights of Prosperity'...

I have written a role playing game called 'Caper: A Game of Pros and Cons.' It is a shared narration storytelling game in which the players take turns telling the story of a heist or a con in the tradition of a Hollywood caper movie. It went through several rounds of playtesting, and I got some great feedback-- one of the playtesters thought it was "the most fun he's had gaming in forever." I have now published it and am looking to get the word out!

Caper uses a mechanic I've created called the 21 System, which uses playing cards and poker chips. I set out to create rules that allow the players to create the twists, turns and reveals of a caper flick in a game, and I think the rules do this very well indeed.

I am selling Caper on Lulu.com, but I have come here to make an offer-- I will offer up a free copy of the .pdf rulebook to the first three people who agree to run a session and write a review and a summary of game play here on Story Games.

Please email me or drop a comment here if you are interested.

Thanks!
