Eric Provost
December 2006 edited December 2006 in Play Advice

For the first time in nearly two months I found myself inspired to work on the Infected. I think I have something awesome for it, but before I get some feedback on it, I need to fill in a blank. It's a word I'm missing. Or maybe two words.

Some diseases are sexually transmitted.

Some diseases are airborne.

Some diseases are spread through bites via saliva (especially zombieism* and lycanthropy!)

I want a step in the game to ask; What is the X of this disease? And I want the answer to be: The way that the infection is spread from one person to another. But I want one or two words to say that. I swear there's a word or phrase out there that means what I need it to mean, but it just won't come to mind.

(* Zombieism is so a word)
