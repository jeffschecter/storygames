    Steampunk ain't an easy genre to illustrate, I have come to find out. This project knocked out six of my eight illustrators. It's a tricky thing to span that gap between crisp scifi and gothic Victoriana. I really like what Lem's pulled off, and Kirk's contributions are of a different style entirely -- sort of woodcut-ish / engraving-ish. They've both found a different place on that spectrum (Kirk's badguy is wearing a little face-mask like radio drama robbers and a bowler hat). It would have been nice to have more points along that spectrum represented by other artists, but I am happy with the pieces I did get from my two steadfast artists.
