    \n One thing that would be important if you wanted to replicate Soviet speculative fiction would be how power and scarce resources would be developed and distributed in a space environment. Actually I am thinking Dogs' system might be a good start here. (Not knowing Burning Empires myself.) Instead of a moral situation, though, you would (might?) want to come up with a scientific, explorational or even mystical one.
