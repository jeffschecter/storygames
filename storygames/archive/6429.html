    \n Well, there's the Complete Strategist on 33rd St, right near the Empire State Building for starters. That's your gaming goodness right there and they have a lot of older product still on the shelves if you like that sort of thing.

    The Cloisters is quite cool if you like medieval stuff. It's a bit of a subway ride but worth it.

    Wandering around Chinatown is always fun.

    Oh, and go to Katz's Deli on Houston and get a pastrami sandwich. It's the gold standard.
