    \n I think you're right. The "colour pizza" effect was my first venture. Once I realised that significant tones did the same job of differentiating without the messiness, I played around with tonal variations. Red seemed to suit the layout. I think I'll give people the option to choose from the three (for download and home printing), but for the high-res print I'll probably go for this reddish tonal option. Maybe I'll drop in a black backdrop, spray the whole thing was some Pollack style blood drips and bump up the saturation for the commercial print.
