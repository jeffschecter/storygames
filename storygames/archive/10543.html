\nDate: October 24th-25th, 2009
Location: HQ of the American Federation of Teachers (AFT), 555 New Jersey Ave, N.W., Washington, DC 20001
Quick Description: Regional mini-con in the DC metro area with a good mix of trad/indie gaming, supported (in name only really, as expense and organization is handled by local organizers) by ENWorld.org; any and all welcome.
Link: DC Gameday Forum on ENWorld (login for ENWorld required, free to register)

17 game sessions currently scheduled, with systems including Dread (Jenga NOT d12), D&D 4e, Moldvay Basic D&D, Savage Worlds, Hollow Earth Expedition, Desolation, Time & Temp, and Lady Blackbird.
