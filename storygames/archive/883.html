The Mortal Coil Character Sheet

You stack coins/poker chips (for resolution) on the sheet itself. I've been working loosly on the same kind of dealio on The Ghost Killers for a while, and if I put 100% of all my effort into making it easy to understand and gorgeous to the eye, I still probably won't get to the level of that sheet.

I haven't really been following the development of this game too-too closely, but man, I've been sold on a glance at the character sheet. Lame but true!
