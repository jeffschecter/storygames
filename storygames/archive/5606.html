    \n Cute.

    What does the questioner do? Reading this it looks to me like the Questioner is mostly passive. They may be allowed to choose the order of questions other than the first question (this isn't clear to me). They may ask extra questions to get more detail. But they can't seem to influence the stories, and I get the impression that the game would play out pretty much the same regardless of who the questioner was, and even if there was no questioner at all.

    Also, who are the questioner and storyteller? Are they both dragons? Is one a dragon and the other a human? Two humans? Something else entirely?
