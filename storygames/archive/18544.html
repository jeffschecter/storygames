\nSelf-Critical Hits 039 - Damn it, Nerd Culture

Because we are just the types to not be satisfied with an apology (no matter how much money is thrown after it), we\u2019re still mad about the crap Gabe from Penny Arcade spewed out on Twitter last week.

To be fair, however, this was recorded last Friday night \u2013 mere hours after the apology was issued \u2013 and is only coming to you now, at this late date, because editing is hard and sloth is enticing. Included here are our thoughts on where some of that patented knee-jerk nerd defensiveness may be coming from, how to address issues of exclusion in the context of PAX (boycott cons? or get political at them?), and River\u2019s dramatic reading of Gabe\u2019s apology itself.

We also talk through some happy stuff \u2013 fond reminiscence about the original Total Recall, trepidation about World War Z, true excitement for The Conjuring and GoPlay NW, and some neat games \u2013 The Upgrade and Seven Grand Steps.
