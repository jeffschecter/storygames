\n One of the beautiful things about lists is they help shore up our imaginations, either by stepping in where they fail or by helping us to see where few have gone before. Being able to have a list of settings for The Quiet Year to refer to time and time again will likely be as invaluable as Fiasco's constantly updating playsets. No need to post your maps (unless you really want to), just the settings you started with.
