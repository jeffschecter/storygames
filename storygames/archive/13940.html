\n Heya,

Do you guys remember back about five years ago we did a thread called Dark Sun gets Awesome-O-Fied? It, IMHO, was one of the greatest threads ever here in Story Games. Well, after working on it, putting it aside, working on it again, putting it aside again, I am overjoyed to present to you Dreadsands!

I have two links to PDFs. A Fancy Version and a Simple Version. Both are the same, it\u2019s just that one is less graphics intense.

Anyway, I\u2019ve gone through that thread probably fifty times to pick out the ideas I liked best. Maybe some of yours are in there! There were so many good ideas, I\u2019m not going to recount all the ones I used in this thread. I want you to read the game, of course

If you do read it, I would love your feedback. What did I leave out of the Setting? What do I need more of? When it comes to the rules, are they understandable? Are the complete?

The text is fully ready for playtest, and I\u2019ve done a few myself. It\u2019s really fun! I can\u2019t tell you how excited I am about this game. It\u2019s the biggest one I\u2019ve done since Ember Twilight back in 2002. I hope you like it, but tell me if there\u2019s anything you don\u2019t. I can take the criticism.

Peace,

-Troy
