\n
\n
\n I have access to a lakehouse on Cherokee Lake in the Tennessee Valley region of Tennessee for a weekend this summer. There's a place for a big campfire, swimming and boating. Plus there is a nice area for gaming in the great room of the house. There's a full kitchen and I have a nice grill. Basically its a nice place for a gaming weekend get away. I've rounded up 3 or 4 friends in the area for the fun but there is a lot more room than that in the house. I figure there is bed and couch space for 10 people easy and another 10 could be accommodated if they bring sleeping bags and don't mind the floor or the loft.

So, consider this an official invitation to Go Play SE. I'm tentatively making it for the weekend of July 27-29. That can be changed if another date seems to work better for everyone. However, I do need to set the date more permenantly within the next couple of weeks so if anyone is interested in coming we need to talk it over and decide.

This brings us to cost. The only thing it will cost people to come is gas to get to Tennessee and whatever food you bring. The house is within easy reach of some grocery stores and restaurants, so replenishing supplies isn't a huge deal. Staying in the house for the weekend is free. So, it's as low cost as I can manage.

Finally, a word on organization. I'm going to keep this kinda minimalist. I don't want it to turn into too ridgid a system but I also don't want complete chaos either. Thus we can discuss how to organize stuff between now and then. People can request games be run or request to run games. Food and activities around the water can be arranged very easily. Basically, I'm looking for this to be a fun, informal get together for gamers (and family if there is room) to play games, swim, boat, ski and generally have a good time.

So... I'll bring the Guiness if someone will run some TSoY for me.
