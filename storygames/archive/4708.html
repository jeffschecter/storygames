    1) We contact the Dreamation staff, and let them know that we'll have a subsection of the tabletop rpg called Indie Game Explosion

    \n Here's the GM guidelines for Dreamation:

    http://www.dexposure.com/d2008gm.html

    Here' s the pertinent information:\n
    Free Membership - Double Exposure will give you one free Complete Membership per 96 man-hours of planned GM/Director time. Note that we are trusting you to be honest in your appraisal of the number of players your game can handle. A man-hour is defined as entertaining one player for one hour. So a 4-hour game which is planned for a maximum of 8 players will count as 32 man-hours towards your free Membership. If you have multiple GMs working with you, we will negotiate the number of players who will actually be entertained by your game. The more often you run your game, the more generous we will be.
    \n96 hrs = 4 games with 6 players each, or some combination therein.

    Now I wonder what the flexibility with this is. Cost of entry to the con is minimal, $45 for 4 days, and the free con food--while making it much cheaper, is very minimal so I think many folks make alternate arrangements anyway. But having a free room to crash in, as has been provided in the past, has been a really good deal.

    I wonder if people technically could sign up to run, but just do less games and pay. 4 games is asking for burnout for many of us (I'd imagine). It would be nice to not feel so locked in.
