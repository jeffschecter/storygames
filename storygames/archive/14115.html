\nDC Gameday VIII -- May 14th, 2011

The eighth semi-annual National Capital Area Gameday will be held May 14th, 2011 on the ninth (9th) floor of the AFT building. The AFT building is located at the corner of New Jersey Ave and F St NW (555 New Jersey Ave, N.W., Washington DC, 20001), two blocks from the Union Station stop on Metro's Red Line.

Join fellow adventure, storytelling, and roleplaying gamers here in the nation's Capital for a fun-filled day of games. The event, held in conjunction with ENWorld, one of the world's largest RPG fan communities, is free to all participants thanks to the generous support of the gameday organizers.

Game masters will be able to start posting games on April 8th; player signups will commence at 7pm, April 22nd.

Please visit DC Gameday forum at ENWorld for more information. If you\u2019re not already a ENW member, you\u2019ll need to sign up for a free account.
