\n This is a simple survey to see whether SG'ers in general fit into the gamer stereotype. You can also use it to improve your stereotypicality, if you want! Ideally, you should be able to answer "YES" to all the following questions.

Are you a chubby guy with a beard?
Do you prefer to wear T-shirts if given a choice?
When you're in a public place and talking with your friends about games, do you use a loud voice and laugh too much?
Do you have more dice than you need?
Do you regularly do one or more of the following:
- Play awesome games?
- Participate in an awesome campaign?
- Have an awesome gaming session?
Do you work with computers?
Are you interested in martial arts, fencing, swordsmanship or guns?
