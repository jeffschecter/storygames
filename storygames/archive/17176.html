\n Hey folks!

Were doing it again : \u201cMoatCon 3 - There and Ditch again..\u201d

And now you've been invited.

We take over a hotel, and a big room with 6 awesome tables, provide fresh fruit, and gaming. Lots of gaming. It's like a party at someone's house, if you could fit 30 people in your dining room.

We have a Google group for discussion. Ask to join and someone will add you.
MoatCon 3

Its starts Friday October 26th and goes through Sunday morning October 28th, in Raleigh NC at the Wake Forest Holiday Inn Express.

We got an excellent room rate. Doubles/King are $70/night and hot breakfast is included in the room price.

MoatCon Reservations

We'll be using a ConPlanner to organize games.
http://www.conplanner.com/MoatCon3

Attendance last year was around two dozen. We had between two and four games per slot.

Last year we had:
13th Age
Black Crusade
The Dreadful Secrets of Candlewick Manor
Dead of Night: Bugs in the System
Panty Explosion Perfect
Ghost Echo
Fiasco
Swords without Master
Stalker
Leverage
Hell for Leather
Mist Robed Gate

... and a few pickup games on the side.

Beyond games we have other activities planned, including Friday dinner out together, and a book/game swap on Saturday night. (Yes, the return of Operation: Blind Drop!)

Drop me a line/whisper here, or just go join the group. But please let us know if you plan on coming. We need to estimate attendance so we can provide appropriate space and refreshments.

We have to take money to pay for the conference rooms, which cost us $300 for the weekend.
Use conplanner to buy your tickets. The whole weekend is $20. Friday and Saturday only tickets are available at $10 and $15, if that matches your schedule better. We need 15 people to commit and buy tickets by the week before or we\u2019ll need to cancel half of the conference space.

If you want to pay at the con, that will be an option, but we really need folks to commit ahead to know how much conference space to get.

To start we're limiting each person to only running 1 game during the Con. The ideas is to have the GMs bring their top game. And we really want people playing excellent games. We generally end up lifting this restriction later if the schedule is looking thin.

So join us for MoatCon 3.
