\n Hi everybody. I have been working in Indy the past few months and have eaten basically everywhere. I'm sure y'all have heard of/been to these places, but just in case not I wanted to give big ups to:
\n
\nBazbeaux Pizza - favorite pizza outside of Brooklyn or Chicago (15 minutes walk from the con center)
\nCladdagh - pretty legit Irish fare (2-3 minutes walk)
\nGrecian Garden - this is a new-ish Greek place that has had impeccable service every time I go in.. food is good, but special GenCon honors for being quick (2-3 minutes walk)
\n
\nPalomino and Rathskellar are also all totally awesome but a little painful for gamers who want dollaz to spend in the dealer room. I'm going to get burned at the stake but the Weber grill is actually better than St. Elmo's as far as steaks go, at least in my opinion.
\n
\nI'm sure I missed some favorites.
