October 2006 edited October 2006 in Directed Promotion
Some of you may recall I ran the "Game for Hire" contest in November 2005. I solicited game design ideas, and resolved to publish the winning idea as a full game, as well as issue the game under a Creative Commons license.

John Harper's idea, 44, was the big winner. The game is finally coming together. Right now, I'm writing the text on a my wiki "workshop". Since the game is creative commons, I don't see much point keeping it under wraps any longer. Feedback is welcome. Understand, though, that it's still very much in-progress as a text.
