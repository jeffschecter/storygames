    \n Hey, Josh. You were right, I do read this forum. The game was an absolute hoot, man.

    Just to throw in my 2 cents, Josh did a STELLAR job of writing beliefs and instincts for each character. One character (John Turner) had an instinct "Always throw out any unattended alcohol" or something like that. Put him on a ship with Jack Sparrow and watch the sparks fly!

    Using pre-gen characters with entangled Beliefs and Instincts like that turned out to be an excellent recipe for a good con game. I never considered it before, but any game like BW, with clearly stated character motives, can be sauced that way.

    Great game, Josh.
