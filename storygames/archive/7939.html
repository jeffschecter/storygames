\n ACHTUNG! Spooky Halloween Games whee!

InSpectres is back in stock in limited quantities. Anyone who buys InSpectres (and really, you have no good reason NOT to own a copy) also gets a free PDF of the Darkpages: Sketchbook that debuted at GenCon 2008. InSpectres is $20 and also comes bundled with the PDF version (PDF: $10 by itself). Sale ends Halloween night!

The Sketchbook is also available one sale by its lonesome as a PDF. It costs $6.66.

I also have copies of octaNe kicking around. It sells for $20 ($10 PDF) and the print version is bundled with the PDF version.

Purchase InSpectres and octaNe and receive both books, plys 3 PDFS (Darkpages: Sketchbook, octaNe and InSpectres) for $45 (includes $5 shipping).

Visit www.memento-mori.com for details.
