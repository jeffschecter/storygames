\n I'm looking for feature films that dramatize the lives of doctors, set in hospitals. Know of any really good ones? I'm also looking for television, although I've got a start on that list. These are all shows I've either seen episodes of or have seen name checked in media studies texts like Jacobs' Body Trauma:

Angels (BBC, 1975-83)
Cardiac Arrest (BBC, 1994-96)
Casualty (BBC, 1986-present)
Chicago Hope (CBS, 1994-2000)
Doogie Howser, MD (ABC, 1989-93)
Dr. Finlay's Casebook (ITV, 1962-71)
Dr. Kildare (NBC, 1961-66, also ten feature films 1937-42)
ER (NBC, 1994-present)
Gideon's Crossing (ABC, 2000-2001)
Gray's Anatomy (ABC, 2005-present)
Holby City (BBC, 1999-present)
House, M.D. (FOX, 2004-present)
M*A*S*H (CBS, 1972-83)
Marcus Welby, MD (ABC, 1969-76)
Medical Center (CBS, 1969-76)
Riget (DR-1/Zentropa, 1994)
Scrubs (NBC, 2001-present)
St. Elsewhere (NBC, 1982-88)
Your Life in Their Hands (BBC, 1958-64)

Medical-themed soap operas other than General Hospital (ABC, 1963-present) and The Doctors (NBC, 1963-82)?
