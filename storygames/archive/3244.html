    \n Thank you all for your kind comments.

    Eric, 'Revenge of the B-Movie' is going to be printed in 60 card decks from RapidPod. The game will be full colour, both front and back, you can see the back design on the website. I am seeing my web guy tonight and am hoping to put up a couple of fronts of cards to give people an idea of how things will look (it is kind of hard to describe). It will come in printed tuck box with rules as well, as this is kindly provided by the folks at rapidpod for free.

    As an interesting aside, the number of cards in the deck is actually a design deciscion enforced by cost. The way rapidpod works is it charges for the first 50 cards and then a cent amount for each card over that. Therefore the 80 card deck, which you may have played at Gencon last year Eric, has been cut to 60 as it is simply cheaper. I have had to increase the way the rate the deck cycles to accomodate this change but it is certainly something to consider if you are thinking about putting one together.

    Stitch is very much a work in progress at the moment, but I am going to put some information up on the site about it soon. I am very excited by this project ater the spodley playtest and look forward to the challenge of writing this game.

    As another aside, how do I add a signature in these forums?

    Cheers

    Iain
