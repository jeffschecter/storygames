    \n Interesting project, that. Glad to see it's found its funding. I particularly like how countercultural that whole project is in terms of the Official Imprimatur so often emphasized in rpg culture: it doesn't particularly seem to bother Ron & the team that they're not creating the next official edition of the game here, but rather just putting down one self-consistent vision about what it can be. This is significantly more interesting than a 7th edition (or is that already a thing?) of the Hero System would be, with all that implies.
