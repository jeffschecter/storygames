\n Hey there!

My Kickstarter for six fresh new Skins for Monsterhearts is well underway, and in fact has reached its funding goal! I'll be releasing The Sasquatch, The Wyrm, The Unicorn, The Cuckoo and two as-yet-secret Skins to the wilds of the capitalist market in both PDF and print, thanks to many wonderful backers. And I am super, super excited about that!

And because I enjoy making things more complicated and more useful, and grouping other cool things together with the cool things we already have, my community and I have got some wicked cool stretch goals all lined up and ready to roll.

http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1571708637/monsterhearts-second-skins

Ahead of us, we've got the promise of more insights: how to write a good Skin for Monsterhearts, and how to win at it (winning...?). We've got a pair of Monsterheartsy songs, some art and microfictions to add to the Joke Skins, a revised Shut Someone Down, a handful of variant situations for Monsterhearts (called Skeletons) and...

Ross Cowman's The Giant!

and Orion Canning's The Firestarter!


So, as they say, "Unlock and load"
...wouldn't it be great if people actually said that?

    \n Hey there NeonChameleon!

    Yeah, sorry about that $17 shipping. Not my fave either. As to your question, What Big Teeth You Have will include:

    Under The Second Skins (MC advice for providing support for each Second Skin).

    What an MC should consider when deciding which Skins to field.

    Three ideas for making Monsterhearts more attractive for people new to gaming.

    How to run an awesome Monsterhearts game in one sitting of 4 hours.

    Joe Mcdaldno's Safe Hearts: Boundaries and Vulnerability in Monsterhearts.

    Jackson Tegu's How to write a good Skin for Monsterhearts.

    Morgan Stinson's How to win at Monsterhearts.

    Insightful annotations for the included articles by Morgan Stinson, Ross Cowman, Renee Knipe, Robert Bruce, Hanna Schiendelman, Orion Canning, Carl Rigney, Kira Scott, Fred Kraii, and Joe Mcdaldno. ("Articles" include all of the above except maybe not Safe Hearts)

    Four new pieces of art by Joe Mcdaldno.

    A get-wound-up-and-go scenario (aka Skeleton) by Jackson Tegu called Great Lakes of Iowa.

    A Closetful of Skeletons: a selection of additional get-wound-up-and-go scenarios by Jackson Tegu.

    And then, should we hit The Abyss $10,000 level (which it looks like we're going to), we'll get Amy Fox's A Spectrum Of Shadows, a short supplement on filling out the range of gender and sexuality; for MC's and players, for Monsterhearts and beyond.


    All that stuff will be in the book. I'm not sure the page count, but that's not a short book. That is one long-ass book we're looking at there!


    The stuff which will be PDF exclusive includes...

    Ross Cowman's re-written version of the shut someone down basic move (laid out into a nice player reference sheet with the other basic moves),

    Two songs inspired by Monsterhearts by Jackson Tegu,

    The Firestarter by Orion Canning

    The Giant by Ross Cowman!


    And then all of the stuff from The Abyss, (excepting the article by Amy Fox) will be PDF exclusively as well.


    tl;dr The book What Big Teeth You Have will be huge and gorgeous and filled with awesome goodness.
