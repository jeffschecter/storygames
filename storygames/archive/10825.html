\n This was the interview I started the entire podcast for! Simon talks about his design approach and goes into some detail about the history behind Barbarians of Lemuria. I also learn that there are rules-light and rules-heavy wargames just as there are with RPGs.
\n
\nhttp://www.therpghaven.com/podcast/?p=191
\n
\nSimon is also one of the earlier independent designers doing a distribution deal with Cubicle 7, who has their fingers in a lot of interesting pies this year. We discuss how that came about. I am very curious (and cautiously optimistic) that Cubicle 7 will open up channels for small-press designers and expand upon some of the work IPR has already done there. They are also distributing 3:16. Very interesting.
