\n Ability Scores: +2 Strength, +2 Intelligence

Speed: 6 Squares

Vision: Low Light

Languages: Common, Tengu

Skill Bonuses: Athletics +2, Perception +2

Tengu Weapon Proficiency: You are proficient with all Simple and Martial Light Blades and Heavy Blades. You may reduce the Dexterity requirement for the Two-Weapon Fighting and Two-Weapon Defense feats by 2.

Keen Observer: You gain training with the Perception skill.

Tengu Battle Cry: You may use Tengu Battle Cry as an encounter power.

Tengu Battle Cry

Encounter * Move Action * Close Burst 10

You and all your allies in the area may Shift.

Play a Tengu if you want\u2026

*To look like a Raven.

*To be fight with two swords.

*To be an enigmatic servant of the Raven Queen.

*To be a member of a race that excells at the Fighter, Ranger, Swordmage, and Warlord classes.

If there\u2019s enough demand, I\u2019ll do a whole article, with feats and monsters, & stuff, and sell it as a cheap PDF. This is a teaser to whet your appetite and gather some feedback.

    \n Are your Tengu a medium or a small-sized race?

    "Tengu battle cry" is powerful stuff. I'd suggest making it a daily power, really. Either that, or reducing its range to close burst 5 or smaller.

    "Tengu weapon proficiency" is broken good, fundamentally overpowered in comparison to similar abilities. I'd suggest narrowing it down to a specific set of 2-3 weapons (such as "shortsword, scimitar and glaive"), chosen according to your own "visual concept" and "cultural sketch" of the race.
    Also, drop the whole "reduce the Dexterity requirement for the Two-Weapon Fighting and Two-Weapon Defense feats" thing (it's quite byzantine and not typical of the system). You'd rather design a racial feat that works like a slightly better TWF, or TWF+TWD, with reduced prerequisites but only available to Tengu in the first place.

    Racial feats go a long way toward defining the niche for a given race. Extrapolating from what you have designed up to now, I'd go so far as suggesting a racial bonus to DEX instead of STR, and a set of racial feats that let Tengu use DEX in place of STR when using melee attack powers with Light or Heavy blades (a la 3.5 Weapon Finesse).

    Minor nitpick: the "To be a member of a race that excels at" line traditionally lists only 3 character classes, not 4.
