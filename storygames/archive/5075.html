\n In the last week I've put together a portable accordion file thing of character sheets and quick-reference material for ~20 different games that I want to run. Where appropriate, I hope to eventually include supporting material like pictures, pre-gen characters, kickers and other situation-building grist. In most cases, I've read through the rules at least once but don't yet feel comfortable enough to run that particular game. But I've already got all the download stuff ready to go for when I do feel I'm ready.

Anyhow, I've got four "D" slots: Dirty Secrets, Dogs In The Vineyard, Don't Rest Your Head, and Dust Devils. The file only came with with two printed D tabs so I made up two blanks. At three games, "C" is almost as bad.

If you're working on a game and you've got two or more titles you really love, you might take a gander at the IPR site and see what letters are crowded. Where are the "K" games!?! Have pity on the OCD afflicted among us.
