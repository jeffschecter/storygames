    \n Thanks Fred!

    Having run a game at Origins last year, I'm pretty comfortable with partially prepping characters and running new players through the Novel stages of char gen (to give them a feel for char gen). Likewise, you've designed a great system so actually running a scenario is a breeze once I've prepped notes on setting, NPCs, and the like.

    I should invest in Fate dice, but telling people to count 1's and 2's as negatives and 5's and 6's as positives seems to work really well. I should also look at the various rule summary pages various folks (including me) have done and pick the best of the best.

    What would be really cool is if there are any promo pieces you could provide as handouts. I know you included bookmarks in the pre-reg edition of SOTC (I got one yay!) - are there any left? Or maybe you have something else I could pass out and leave in the store before and/or afterwards to drum up more interest.

    Again, thanks for the support. First Saturday Indie feels like a win-win-win. I get to run the games, Jeff/Alley Cat gets some more buzz, and the games get more buzz.
