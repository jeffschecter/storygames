\n This is an unedited raw tape for an interview I've done just now for the Norwegian roleplaying webzine imagonem.net, with Tony Totino, who worked with Keith Johnstone in the 70s. It's the raw, unedited version, I'll do a text version and maybe an edited version of the sound sometime within a couple of weeks (w/ photos and all of that), and post the link in this thread. The sound is a bit crap, and the conversation is kind of meandering. We get into RPGs from 37 onwards (Play Unsafe, RPGs and improv etc). So this is not meant as a "check out our finished product"-post, but more of a "yay, I had this very nice conversation today, and if you're really, really interested in impro and RPGs, you can listen to the unedited version here: http://www.filefactory.com/file/af14f89/n/IMPRO_wav "
