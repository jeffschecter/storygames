\n So yeah, having a bit of brain block about what to play..and have this feeling that this has to be a design space that others have looked at..
\n
\nI guess what I am looking for are games like "breaking the ice" or "KAZEKAMI KYOKO KILLS KUBLAI KHAN".
\n
\nWhat else is out there? I mean there are plenty of games like fiasco, or nobilis, or Exalted, or Maid, or any game with a well deveulped seduction mechanic, where romance is part of the deal..but I am really looking for games where its an strong focus, what the game is _about_.
\n
\nSo whats out there?

    \nHot Guys Making Out is a game by Ben Lehman, emulating yaoi manga. In his own words, it's "a yaoi role-playing game, set in the Spanish Civil War, in which a tormented nobleman and his young ward attempt to resist their forbidden love for each other, and fail."

    Monsterhearts is a game I wrote, about the messy lives of teenage monsters. Messy love triangles, pubescent awkwardness, monstrosity, sex and body horror.

    It Was a Mutual Decision tells the story of a break-up (where one of the characters might be a were-rat). The players all divide into two groups: the men all play the woman in the relationship; the women all play the man in the relationship.

    Bacchanal is a lusty game about the sexual and social madness that comes with the bacchanalia. Players take turns describing vignette scenes, using a wine-glass full of dice to provide inspirational cues along hte way.

    Under My Skin is by Emily Care-Boss, the author of "Breaking the Ice." It's a game about overlapping romances amongst a group of friends, and all the drama and confusion that can emerge from such a situation.

    It's Complicated is a game about quirky people in messy relationships.

    S/lay w/Me is a game by Ron Edwards, for two people. It's billed as "from-the-heart phantasmagoric roleplaying" with strong sexual themes.

    \nTales of the Fisherman's Wife is about a husband and wife. It's not a porn game or a game about tentacle sex. Okay, sure, it can be about tentacle sex, and you can make it as dirty and awkward as you want. But under all the layers of demons, ghosts, tentacles, and fishing, it's about two people in a relationship who encounter other people, issues, or their pasts (in the form of yokai and yurei), how they are transformed by those things, and whether they're able to stay together after the transformation.
