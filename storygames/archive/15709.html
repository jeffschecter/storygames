\n So I was browsing this list right here...

LIST!!!

...and because I'm kinda' weird like this, I started toward the bottom.

Well, I instantly recognized Mr. Harper's name, and I had to check out the link right away. Needless to say, I'm a bit blown away by Wildlings. My design skills can often be less than innovative (or I discover my "breakthrough" was something created long, long ago), but I will proudly admit that I am a grammar maniac who is obsessed with every esoteric in and out of the English language. I think I can ultimately combine my desire to create with my love of grammar. I feel that "Wildlings" has inspired me to play with my designs in a new, not so esoteric direction: Adverbs.

I love how the game treats adverbs as the outcomes of an action!!! I'd love to launch further into this, but I need some help. Because I am a total Story Game newb, I need some adverbs that you folks feel are especially relevant to Story Gaming.

So I beseech everyone - tell me your favorite adverbs and how I can use them!!!

(p.s. I have several settings I could run with, but for now, I'd like to leave them out to avoid "adverb bias.")

Thanks!
~Klaus
