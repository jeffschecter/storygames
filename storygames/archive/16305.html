\n At Pax and Comic Con people wait on line...

...to get into seminars
...to get into the exhibit hall
...to get badges / passes
...to get into the con itself

...for hours!



Can we bring Games on Demand to people waiting on line?




At Pax East people waited 2-4 hours to get into the Mass Effect panel!

In that time you could have made a character, leveled up, fought dragons, fell in love, died, and get resurrected!
