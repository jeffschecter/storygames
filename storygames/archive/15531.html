\n I love Christmas specials, and I love gaming. I think most gaming groups tend not to meet too awful close to the holidays; people are busy, shopping, wrapping, traveling. One effect of this is that holiday gaming doesn't happen very often. (Am I wrong? Tell me about it!) I think this is a bummer. I'd love to see more games with a Christmas (or Hannukah/Festivus/Wintermas/Kwanza/whatthefuckever) theme.

Have you ever done any kind of a holiday one shot? Maybe a special holiday edition of your ongoing campaign? Tell us about it!

If not, but you're intrigued, let's brainstorm ideas and discuss how we can slip a little holiday cheer into our dungeons and our horror movies and our investigations into things man was not meant to know.
