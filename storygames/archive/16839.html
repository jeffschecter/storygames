\n A few people at GPNW 2012 asked me about the visual aids I built for the Classroom Deathmatch game I ran. I wrote them all in a language called processing. The visuals were projected on a screen during the game. If you want to check it out, I uploaded the script I wrote and a copy of processing too. Just extract the files, launch processing, Open.. the file "CDWheel/CDWheel.pde" then hit the play button.

The controls are:
'i' toggles the intro screen.
spacebar toggles the random item picker. hit 'z' to hide the random item.
'm' toggles the map. 1-6 toggles the deathzone indicator on the map.

The game was Classroom Deathmatch at Disneyland, the happiest place on earth to die.
