\n Figure it's a long shot, but why not:

Leftahead, my roommate, and I are currently planning a Night of Blood and Dice!! for next Thursday, December 22nd, into Friday, December 23rd. At the current moment it's looking like we're going to be trying to get through the 4E version of Tomb of Horrors! (Or at least a good chunk of it.) This will be an all-night affair at our my place near Columbus Circle! We are looking for any interested gamer who is free from Thursday at 6 PM onwards. We figure due to the proximity of the holiday there will be someone who can heed this call...

Respond here or via PM or email (same as my screen name at gee mail).

Matt

P.S. In accordance with tradition, there will be Chinese food.
