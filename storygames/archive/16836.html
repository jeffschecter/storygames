\n My friend Tyler's sci-fi book book store in Boston, Pandemonium Books & Games, needs GMs:\n
Pandemonium is looking for enthusiastic GM's willing to show off their favorite systems Tuesday nights starting in August. We have space for up to three RPG tables that we'd like to fill with games of all sorts. If you have an awesome idea for a campaign and want to run it in the store, email events ATSIGN pandemoniumbooks DOT com and we'd love to set you up with a table and some players.
