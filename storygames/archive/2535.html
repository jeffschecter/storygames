    \n Oh shit, yeah: I got so caught up in other stuff recently that I forgot about that.

    So, basically, I intended to 1-2 times a year have a "cook off" for what people thought were the 5-10 best threads (totally subjective). Then have a voting portal. Then take the winners, and dump them in that category.

    Basically, the others made it there because I figured they fit there, and pushed them there. I might knock the reverse mantras one out unless it's voted in, but the others (especially Who Are You) seemed to fit right in there.

    So, please, keep going on and linking the threads that you think should go in that category to keep them fresh in peoples' minds (I'll throw a few of my own in there too), and we'll hold that voting sometime in March.

    Andy, I'll throw out a thought. If you want to do a "Best Of..." thread for threads, you could run it like the Oscar awards and use catagories. You start with a phrase like, "Best ________ Thread" and fill in the blanks with different catagories like:

    Game Design
    Design Exercise (awesome-o-fy)
    Actual Play
    Social Commentary on RPGs
    Publishing
    General RPG Theory
    Tools For Play
    Promotional (sell me on\xe2\u20ac\xa6, check out my new...)
    Site Discussion
    Con Report
    Stuff To Watch (which month)
    Community Building

    So you would take nominations according to each catagory, and then select the "Best Game Design Thread" or the "Design Exercise Thread" or the "Best Con Report" thread. Then the winners are linked in the Best of Story Games forum. I dunno, just a thought
