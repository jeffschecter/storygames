\n So I've linked to the Urban Tarot Deck Kickstarter in a stuff to watch thread, and tossed out the idea of talking about ideas for using the deck in games. (The first pieces were built for an "modern magic" RPG that did not come to be, but if you look at the deck, it simply screams inspiration.) So, some thoughts:

Existing games it would work great for (as inspiration, if not as part of a more concrete mechanic hack): Mage, Mortal Coil, Don't Rest Your Head.

I also look at the deck and think of revisiting my Mage Heartbreaker (with four factions inspired by the tarot suits, and a focus on fortune-telling and predictive/coincidental magic). That concept will take a bit more simmering, however.
