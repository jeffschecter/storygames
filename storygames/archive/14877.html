    \n Welcome to Story Games!

    There's a lot of play-by-forum stuff happening at RPG Geek so you might want to check that out.

    There's a Nice play mat using Google Draw Renato whipped up (let me know if you can't view it), which works great with Skype.

    Not to be a shill but we talk about distributed play (synchronous and asynchronous) in the Fiasco Companion as well. Lots of people are doing it and it works fine, you just need to be very clear and intentional about the handling bits, like awarding dice when you Resolve.
