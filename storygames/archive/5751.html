\n Because that's what the topic really is, I just didn't quite put my finger on it until some recent threads:

"This is discussion geared towards improving the gaming experience as a player (or GM), independent of system. In short, Making Gaming More Fun. Tips, tricks, processes, 'craft', spotty ideas and random musings are the order of the day. System-specific tips and tools are kosher."

Yep, that smells like Play Advice to me.

Play Advice is about giving play advice to others.
It's about telling stories about stuff that worked in play.
It's about telling stories about stuff that sucked in play.
It's about looking for help with running or playing a game. Getting advice, as well as giving it.

Yum!
