    \n One option would be something similar to Cthulhu Dark. Let the PCs roll 1 to 3 d6 when attempting an action. One d6 if the action is humanly possible, a second d6 if they put their physical well-being at risk, and a third d6 if they put their mental well-being at risk. If the 'physical' d6 or the 'mental' d6 have the higher result, then that characteristic risks increasing in accord with Cthulhu Dark's rules for Insanity. Once the d6 reaches 6, the PC is either mortally wounded (physical) or totally stressed out (mental). This is a very simple mechanic that might be perfect for a one shot and has the benefit that the d6s themselves show the current level of physical or mental stress.
