\n Holy crap.
\n
\nIf you have the chance - get your hands on this and read it!!!!
\n
\nI repeat
\n
\nRead it carefully. Then use it in your next game
\n
\nIt is I think the BEST advice on running a compelling character driven game. I like character driven games. Very clear instruction, great examples on what to do, and easily ported to pretty much any "world" as long as you want the game to be about the characters and the repercussions of their actions.
\n
\nI just spent the last weeks having a marathon watching of "full metal alchemist", which basically boils down to - people chase dream, make bad shit happen, spend rest of time trying to "fix" and make more bad things happen.
\n
\nAnd its compelling.
\n
\nI am so energized to go an run a campaign now after reading through the apocalypse world MC book and using it in a one shot.
\n
\nMr Lumpley - the moment you finish that game, I will order a dead tree version and a pdf.
