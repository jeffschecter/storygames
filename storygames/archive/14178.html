\n It occurred to me that DitV would be a great vehicle for a Detective Drama RPG, like The Wire, Lethal Weapon, or maybe even the Dirty Harry movies. Two players plus the GM is ideal--partners. The supernatural is dialed way down. Demons are things like booze and drugs, guilt, hatred, etc--the things that drive people to break the law. Escalation can remain exactly the same. The point of many of these shows and movie is the moral dilemmas in which the detectives find themselves. It's something I'd like to try, I think.
\n
\nHas anyone done this? Are there any threads about it?
