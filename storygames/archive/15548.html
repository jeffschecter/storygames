\n Just to share with you the board we are making for the french version of Polaris form Ben Lehman. It will be around 70x70, printed on vinyl with erasable PC sheet

(like the board we've made for the Commitee...)

Here is the work in progress :



The artwork is from Monica, a Spanish artist who 's also illustrating the book...

The idea is that the board may give the players a sum-up of the rules (this part is still draft in this version as you can see), bring them the names of stars, and make the process of erasing and writing easier ..apart from bringing "immersion" to the game...

Suggestions are welcome...

The final version of the board will have to get the agreement of Ben anyway
