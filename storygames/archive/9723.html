\n Even though it's near completion, Principia could use a bit more playtesting. I'm looking for groups willing to throw down a session or two.

Principia is a game of alternate Renaissance, where you control the actions of the great heroes of the Florentine Renaissance. You will play Machiavelli, Leonardo da Vinci, Caterina S'Forza and others as they decide the fate of their world. Maybe in your world, Leonardo makes flying machines that really fly, Lucrezia Borgia opens a gate to another world, and Machiavelli assembles an army of vampires. Through the War of Ideas mechanic, your actions can contribute to resolving great questions that your pose, like "Is lasting peace possible?", "Should art bow to religion?", or "Does Florence deserve to be destroyed?" When you resolve these questions at the end of the game, you narrate how they change the world in the epilogue of the story you just told.

Here's a recent AP: Steam Tank vs. Marble Warriors

If you're interested, please whisper to me with an email address, and I'll add you to the playtest list.

I'm looking for people to try the game out and make sure I've explained it clearly. Secondarily, I want to make sure the pre-gen characters sparkle for people, and that all the mechanics serve a useful purpose. I'm happy with the game, and it's working very well in my playtests.
