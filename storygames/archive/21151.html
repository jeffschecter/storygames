\n Hello, Story Games! It has been a while.

Here is the Kickstarter for Cthulhu Dark, a roleplaying game of intense Lovecraftian horror in a beautiful 200-page hardback book.

Have a look at the rewards! You can just get the book. You can get a signed book or a leatherbound edition. You can get the Season Pass, in which you'll get each section of Cthulhu Dark as it's finished. And, whatever level you back at, you'll get the preview version, Cthulhu Dark Zero, just after the Kickstarter finishes.

At higher levels, I'll send you mysterious letters about Cthulhu Dark, from throughout space and time. And one backer will have their copy of Cthulhu Dark hand-delivered by a malevolent faceless stranger.

If you're new to Lovecraftian horror or unsure whether to back, have a look at the samples of the book, which you'll find on the Kickstarter page. I think you'll find Cthulhu Dark a perfect introduction into a world of terrifying and terribly human stories.

Thanks for your support! I'm excited to share the book with you. And please do share this Kickstarter link widely.
