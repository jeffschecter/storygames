\n When I was a kid, my older brother left my a garbage can load (literally -- we kept them in a galvanized tin garbage can!) of comics. Most of them were Marvel.

Nestled among copies of the Invaders and Avengers and a few X-Men were a handful of Marvel Team Ups. You know, Power Man and Iron Fist. AWESOME.

What team-up (2 or more!) of designers would you want to create games?

Alternatively, who would you love on your own team?

    \n Oh right, we can say people we'd like to work with. Man, I dunno. I'm pretty collaboratively promiscuous; I'll start a project with anyone who's not a jerk, basically. One of my favorite things to do is to make stuff with my friends.

    As a result, I've collaborated formally and informally with like a half-dozen people on various games. There are a couple people I'd love to design with that I haven't collaborated with before, but I don't know them very well and I'm shy. Ish.

    Another voyeuristic mashup I'd love to see: Justin Achilli and Nathan Pao.. wait, no, doubt it could be better than Annalise is on its own.
