  • joshua neff
    April 2006
    Oh, and another great HQ extended contest was when Mike's Darjiini diplomat was trying to find the villain causing all this trouble in a temple. The villain was another PC, played by our friend Ryan, who had this terrific ability--"Who, Me?"--that he used to evade Mike's detective work. The two went back and forth, Mike tracking down clues, Ryan using misdirection to escape detection. Finally, Mike's character assembled all of the main characters into one room for the "detective sums everything up and fingers the guilty party" scene. He totally beat Ryan at the extended contest--"It was you! You did this all!" It was another great sequence.
