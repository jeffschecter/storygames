\n Say you're fourteen. You've just come up with this awesome and realistic idea for a Skill Web, wherein you get to pick three categories of skills, then one sub-skill from each category, then three sub-sub-skills within each sub-skill. (If you are actually fourteen, by the way, you are probably not allowed to participate because you are already way cooler than I was at your age.)

The goal here is to make up little lists that demonstrate enthusiasm overtaking self-restraint. Also to indulge in some lonely fun. The theme is (sort of) your standard pseudomedieval fantasy onanism.

I'll start!

Skill Category: COMBAT

Sub-Skill - Weapons
Sub-Sub-Skills: Sword, Giant Sword, Three-Ball Morningstar, Bo Staff, Nunchuks, Quadruple Axe, Crystal Sword, Energy Lance

- Martial Arts
Aikido, Karate, Krav Maga, Mantis Style, Jujitsu, Super Saiyan

- Guns
Arquebus, Six-Shooter, Steam-Powered Gatling Gun, Desert Eagle, Sniper Rifle, Rocket Launcher

- Death Metal
Drums, Keyboards, Guitar, Electric Guitar, Bass, Electric Violin, Two-Neck Guitar, Sword Guitar, Laser Axe Lightning Guitar



Does that give the general idea? Other possible categories: Arts, Exploration, Wilderness, Social, Healing/Buffs, Tricks/Curses, Athletics, etcetera.
