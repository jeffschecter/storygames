    Posted By: MatthijsAmazing games.

    Hey, John, Norwegian Style is from 99. Can anthologies be in the contest?

    Archipelago II - are second editions allowed?
    \nBased on the precedent of Andy Kitkowski's handling 2004's "No Press RPG Anthology" - I guess Norwegian Style should be judged as a single product in the category of "games".

    Second editions are generally not eligible according to tradition from Andy. They are great and should be recognized, but the Indie RPG Awards are focused on what's wholly new. From his FAQ: Second editions of games published in earlier years, or upgrades to a PDF RPG (like 1.0 to 1.5, 2.0, etc) do not count for the purposes of these awards. However, this can be a grey area. If an RPG's second edition were to be a complete overhaul of the rules to a completely new system, or it includes 50%+ more original material/design that completely reforms the game, it may be considered for these awards.

    This is regrettable when there are new editions of games that were not previously considered for the awards - like Archipelago II, Little Fears Nightmare Edition, and BASH Ultimate Edition. However, I'm not going to go changing the award rules at this point in the process.
