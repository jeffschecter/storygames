\n So a couple months ago the best friend I've never met convinced me to join LibraryThing. I did and attempted to add an indie rpg book. Didn't have much luck. I felt that adding it from scratch was more work than I wanted to do at the moment.
Today, however, the daughter of one of the women that works at my library came to visit. She works for LibraryThing, and she gave a presentation, and that got me interested in it again.
And I started to think... I can't be the ONLY indie rpg enthusiast on LibraryThing! There are bound to be some SGers on there that have already added stuff.

Anyone care to help me out? If you're a LibraryThing member and you've got any indie games in your library, if you don't mind telling me what your name is there so i can check out your library to add stuff to my library, i'd REALLY appreciate it!
