    Britain Fantasyia is in ruins as lords and chieftains fight for supremacy. From this chaos will rise Arthur the SUPER PRINCESS and a golden age of chivalry love , joy and wonder - and a dark time of betrayal and bloodshed. Take the role of a knight battleprincess at court and make a name for yourself. Can you save Camelot? SPARKLE PALACE Will you make your own legend?

    An Epic Roleplaying Game in King Arthur's Britain
     land of magical creatures and fierce princesses!

    * A complete roleplaying game of Frilly might, adventure and glory - players portray battle princesses who grow in power and reputation. Play sessions occur between winter seasons of character "downtime" meaning a campaign can span the entirety of the SUPERPRINCESS era.

Players will tell the stories of three generations of characters - assuming their battleprincess survive the brutal realities of pastel fantasy life.
