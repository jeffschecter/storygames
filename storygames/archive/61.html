What good are our dead ideas?

Jeph
January 2006 edited January 2006 in Story Games
So, I'm sure that all of us here have numerous dead projects. Stuff that we got all inspired about, worked on feverishly for a few days or weeks (or months or years, in some cases), then just kinda dropped. Maybe the spark went out, or we realized we were on the wrong track completely, or we got excited about something else and switched our attention over to the New Thing.

So, what good are all those dead projects we have floating around in our minds, notebooks, and hard drives? What uses can we find for our abandoned babies?
