\n Many of my friends have come to me wanting to know why I GM and how I keep things fresh and challenging after almost three decades of gaming. I would often give them crazy Story Games answers, talking about such things as Social Contract, Player Driven Conflict and Agressive Scene Framing. Recently, however, the truth of my success has come under great scrutiny and I fear another party has come upon the answers folks have been seeking. Before it becomes public I thought I should reveal to you my dirty little secret so at least you did not get the news from some stranger.

Click on the link for full disclosure...
