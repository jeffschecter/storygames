\nSign-up thread for ENWorld Chicago Gameday

ENWorld Chicago Gameday XIX is a day of free RPG'ing at Games Plus, in Mt. Prospect, IL. Though the Hero's Banner event has just filled up, we've got spots for 1001 Nights and Squirrel Attack!. In addition to these small-press offerings, there's plenty of D&D, Paranoia XP, Unisystem, and even some Ghostbusters RPG.

So, if that sounds appealing, go grab yourself a seat.
