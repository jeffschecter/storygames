    \n Good response.

    I'm looking at the teddy bear as being a hero.

    But that's the obvious take on the picture.

    Perhaps the child will grow up to be a mass murderer, the teddy bear is an evil avatar sent by demons to ensure the child fulfills a certain dark destiny while the "monstrous creature" is a benevolent spirit sent from the future to prevent dark events from occurring in the world by destroying children such as these before they've had the chance to cause their destructive activities.

    I'm still thinking of that first option though. A game about childhood protectors, keeping their charges safe from the things that go bump in the night. Usually successfully.
