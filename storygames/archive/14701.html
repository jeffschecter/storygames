    \n Tokkou (\u5fb3\u884c) is basically "virtuous deeds". Like as in "Stuff you do that is (alignment) Good"
    Kudoku (\u529f\u5fb3) is "pious deed".

    I'm thinking either TOKU \u5fb3 (the straight-up word/kanji for Virtue), but more likely TOKKI (\u5fb3\u5668), which is a rarer word but basically means "your talent/virtue" or ITOKU \u5a01\u5fb3, which comes out to "virtue/influence".
