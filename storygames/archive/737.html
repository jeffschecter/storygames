Gamex - LOS ANGELES this weekend

Josh Roby
May 2006 edited May 2006 in Meetups / Conventions
Hey, I get to do a meetups post!

Gamex is this weekend at the LAX Westin. It's got a negligible admission fee (like, $25 or so) and is basically just a pile of games to be played.

Paul Tevis, Jesse Burneko, and I are running a full slate of indie titles. At any given time, one of us will be running something, whether it is Dogs, Capes, PTA, Mountain Witch, Sorcerer, Roach, Nine Worlds... and maybe something else I've forgotten. There's also traditional games and board games and I will personally beat your ass at Puerto Rico.

The Strategicon cons are really nice for locals to just swing by and play -- I don't even grab a hotel room, it's a nice short drive down the 105 from home. Come on by and play some games!
