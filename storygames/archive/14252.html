\n I'm having thoughts about running a variant game of 3:16 set in the Warhammer 40,000 universe. But its 10,000 years earlier during the Great Crusade when the Emperor of Mankind sent out the Astartes Legions to conquer the galaxy as part of Man's Manifest Destiny. (so, not too far from the 3:16 concept)
\n
\nPlayers are Luna Wolves in service to the great Primarch Horus Lupercal - the Luna Wolves dont seem to have Chaplains or Librarians, but do have Assault Marines, Terminators, & Devastators. Initially loyal, they may join warrior lodges between missions (+1 NFA in some circumstances) or shun them as the Emperor commands or perhaps they will befriend the Iterators and Remembrancers, Tech Priests or Army personnel that accompany them. The worlds they will battle across include Ullanor, New Terra, Murder, the interex, Davin, and perhaps all the way to Istvaan? And in their final Weakness, will they betray their Warmaster and father or their Emperor and maker?
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\nRanks
\nRank 1: Brother Marine - Bolter & Grenades
\nRank 2: Special Weapon - Pick one of Flamer/Meltagun/Plasmagun
\nRank 2: Devastator - Pick one of Heavy Bolter/Missile Launcher/Las Cannon
\nRank 2: Assault Marine - get Jump Pack, Chainsword & Bolt Pistol
\nRank 3 Sergeant - Access Powerfist, Plasma Pistol, Vortex Grenades. Rhino or Drop pod.
\nRank 4 First Company- Can requisition Terminator Armour. Power Sword. Storm Bolter. Thunderhawk
\nRank 5 Captain - Orbital Bombardment, Forcefields, Land Raider
\n
\nWeapons -
\n(1H - can be held in 1 hand. Marines assumed to be ambidextrous - can use either weapon in round without changing if carrying two 1 handed weapons. 2H - 2 handed - change weapons as normal)
\n
\nBolter = Close: 1 Near: 1d10 Far: 0 2H
\nGrenades= Close: 1d10 Near : 1 Far: - (hit PC's at close range who fail roll. Auto Switch to it)
\n
\nHeavy Bolter= Close: - Near: 2d10 Far d10 2H
\nMissile Launcher = Close: - Near: d10 Far: 2d10 2H
\nLascannon = Close: - Near: 1d10 Far: 1d10 (Ignores Armour) 2H
\n
\nMeltagun= Close: 1 Near: 1d10 Far: - 2H (Ignores Armour)
\nPlasmagun= Close: 1 Near: 2d6 Far: - 2H (Ignores Armour, Gets Hot (take Kill on Max damage)
\nStorm Bolter= Close: 1d6 Near 1d10 Far: - 1 H (vambrace mount)
\nFlamer= Close: 2d6 Near: 1 Far: - 2H
\n
\nBolt Pistol= Close: 1 Near: d6 Far: - 1H
\nChainsword= Close : 1d6 Near: - Far - 1H
\n
\nPower Fist = Close : 1d6 Near: - Far : - (Special - Ignores Armour. Auto Switch to it: 1 H
\nPower Sword= Close 1d10 Near: - Far: - (Special - Ignores Armour) 1 H
\nPlasma Pistol = Close: 1d6 Near: 1d10 Far: 0 (Ignores Armour, Gets Hot (take Kill on Max damage) 1H
\n
\nOther Equipment
\n
\nTerminator - 2 Armour - can only move forward.
\nJump Pack - Can change range by 2 areas on a NFA change range roll
\nVortex Grenade - as TPK bomb
\nScream "Lupercal!" or "For the Emperor!" - as Combat Drugs
\n
\nVehicles
\n
\nBikes - 1 extra armour per planet.
\nRhino- APC (Crippled / Destroyed)
\nStormbird/Thunderhawk - Dropship
\nLand Raider- APC with Armour / Armour / Crippled / Destroyed
\n
\nWeakness: if using a Weakness to avoid death, may be interred in a Dreadnaught sacophagus. 3 Armour, but wounds dont heal on-planet.
\n
\n
\nAlien Powers
\n
\nOrks - Enrage or Swarm
\nEldar - Ambush & Flee
\nMega-Arachnids -Ignore Wounds or Swarm
