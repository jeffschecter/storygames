\n Chicago Gameday 38 is happening on June 14th (yup, same weekend as Origins) and needs some people to run games.

We currently have five events in both of our time-slots (a.m. and p.m.), but can accommodate up to eight in each.

If you'd like to run an event for us, post in our planning thread over on ENWorld.

Chicago Gameday is a day of free RPG gaming held three times a year at Games Plus in Mt. Prospect, IL. Check out our FAQ page or the Gameday Facebook page for more info.
