\n
\n

\n\nDarren Watts \n\n\n
\n
\n\nSeptember 2010 \n edited September 2010 in Directed Promotion
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n It's been suggested to me that IPR hasn't been as communicative as some would appreciate recently. This is probably a fair cop, since we've been spending a lot of time in the last few weeks building financial reporting spreadsheets, learning the vagaries of the store reporting, and getting through a backlog of titles that had piled up in the store and warehouse during the transition. Nevertheless, it's clear that I'm insufficiently aware of where discussions regarding IPR and what it does are happening around the webz, so I'm making a formal request here- where should I be looking online for IPR discussions? I was just introduced to Vincent's blog today, for example. Anyplace else I should be aware of that you guys or other indie creators are hanging out?
\n
\nOf course, if you have any questions, concerns or just want to shoot the breeze, you can reach me directly and fastest at darrenwatts AT yahoo DOT com, or even leave me a note here. Many thanks! dw
