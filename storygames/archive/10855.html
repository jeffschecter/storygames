\n Hey all,

I want to point you over to the 79th episode of the Canon Puncture show. In this episode, we try something a little different, and I'm pleased with how it came out. This episode is about the original Little Fears RPG (not Nightmare Edition) and the interviewee is not the designer, but a gamer who has played the game heavily. We call it a Game Advocate episode because of this.

This is a play-centered interview about a game, it is about the experience at the table. Tell me what you think about this episode, guys and gals. I'd like to do more of this, but I'm curious if y'all want to hear more of it.

Linkage: http://www.canonpuncture.com/2009/11/canon-puncture-79-game-advocates-little-fears/
