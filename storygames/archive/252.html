You know, my facetious comment about the superiority of blue page Polaris leads me to another idea - a game that supports different learning styles based on versioning.

"We like Gorgon Hunters: Red because there's no fluff, just numbered rules."

"We like Gorgon Hunters: Blue because it's basically a big comic book."

Or how about dialects of play, where the actual rules change subtly from version to version in interesting ways, and intentionally combining elements from different versions creates awesome mega-power synergies?
