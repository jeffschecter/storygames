\n What if Obi-Wan hadn't been lying? Some folks in London re-imagining the Star Wars saga with Microscope:

Star Wars under the Microscope: What if Darth Vader wasn\u2019t Luke\u2019s father?



"A last alliance of secessionists, Rebels (including Leia and Chewbacca, her one true love) and former Imperial commanders (including Luke and Darth) combined to take down Palpatine, and in the midst of an assassination attempt by Palpatine\u2019s spy, Vader revealed that (even though he wasn\u2019t Luke\u2019s father) he really was Leia\u2019s father. As expected, Leia did not take it well at first."

    \n Here's a totally different but equally interesting approach: it's a step-by-step account of using the world-building procedure from Microscope Explorer to adapt a history they played to make the backstory for a Dungeon World game.

    World's Collide: Microscope With Dungeon World And A Cinder Queen



    What's particularly interesting (I think) is that they're using an existing Dungeon World module, but just making slight adjustments to the module and the Microscope history to make the two work together.
