    \n I believe the font you are using is called 1942 Report. I could be wrong, but it looks like the one I used on Cookie Jar.
    \n
    \nEither way, I love the cover. If you'd like some feedback, here is it (feel free to ignore it if you don't like it).
    \n
    \nYour page numbers are running too close to the edge of the border. Pull it down about 1/4". Also, page numbers should not be the same font as the body of the book, but rather should match the headers.
    \n
    \nYour cover font is Initial Caps, but your Header Font is Small Caps. Choose one or the other, but do not mix and match.
    \n
    \nMonotype typewriter fonts do not look good in italics. Since your examples of play are all done in a contrasting font, it is not necessary to italicize them.
    \n
    \nYour border is solid black (top and bottom). Consider using a texture or a 80%. Right now, this is overpowering and dominates the eye.
    \n
    \nAlso, they should be the same width on every page.... pages with full pictures have a different size for some reason and this is jarring.
    \n
    \nIt's okay to lose them on PICTURE pages, but not change the size.
    \n
    \nIt seems inconsistent.
    \n
    \nThose are my initial thoughts.
    \n
    \nLove the photos. Good value and range. Excellent color correction on those.
