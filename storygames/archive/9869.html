    \n in case anyone is interested, here's a little feedback on my first Jeepform session
    \n
    \nafter quite a bit of discussion, the four of us played "Hostage"
    \n
    \nwe really took our time to understand what we were doing
    \n
    \nconsequently, we didn't have much fun, but it was very interesting
    \n
    \nI believe we now have a pretty good idea of how to play a Jeepform scenario
    \n
    \nif I can compare the experience with something, I'd say it's like trying story games for the first time, with no one to explain how you're supposed to go about it
    \n
    \npersonally, this experience also showed me that:
    \n- a lot of things that we usually take for granted in LARP (such as costumes, decoration, secrets) are just habits;
    \n- I can pretty much apply what I understand of GNS / the Big Model roleplaying theory to LARP.
