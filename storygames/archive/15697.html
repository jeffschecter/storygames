\n If you do, you better volunteer to facilitate Fiasco at Dreamation! The Indie Game Explosion bosses (Brennan and Jenn) are looking for GMs, and you know as well as I do that being a Fiasco "GM" is secretly a free ticket to playing a fun game with no pressure. Also - 3 games, free badge.

Anyway, if you are going to Dreamation and want to Fiasco it up let me know! We'll have some fun surprises for you to share. Send an email to brennanrtaylor@ gmail.com and he'll get you set up. You have a few more days, the deadline is February 1.
