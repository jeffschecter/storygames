Interlacing Games

shreyas
April 2006 edited April 2006 in Story Games

So, I was thinking about City of the Moon and Snow From Korea. They've got reasonably compatible structure for this effort, I think; it's particularly felicitous that each game's main characters will be at best supporting characters for the other game.

I want to play these at once, and have them work together and connect. Sometimes you might be playing a scene where only City's mechanics are in effect, or where SFK is ascendant, but also there can be scenes where they're both in place and interacting.

I don't know how to do this yet. But, does it sound like a good idea? How might I do it?
