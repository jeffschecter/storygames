\n A brief chat this week with game designer, Olympian, and awesomely beautiful man Ross Cowman. The main thing here is his currently-Kickstartable story game Life on Mars \u2013 we talk about what play his like and how the game works to evoke the sense of a long journey to an unknown place.

Additional bits follow, including: some discussion of Heavy Water, the post-apocalyptic game (about feelings and relationships, naturally) that Ross is currently working on, and hopeful noises about the future of the great Fabricated Realities gaming con / art show.

Hosts: Ross, Miles
