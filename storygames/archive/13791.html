\n Inspired by the other thread, yeah, the first time I read the AW text I was creeped out by the sex moves. It made me think of a freeform game I played once about homosexual tiger gods that I never want to play again...

But what the hell, right? I don't have to use them. In fact, I played a Maestro D', whose special doesn't require sex.

But then, in play, an NPC has my armor (+1 leather pants) and I want to get it back - and the Seduce move can totally do it for me - and I just go for it. And, hey, it's not so bad, we take it off camera, and now the story's in a more interesting place!

From "Play Unsafe" this is one of my Unsafe areas - so, according to "Play Unsafe", I should go there. And AW makes it easy, giving us game mechanical incentives. It's like, "I'm not a pervert, I just want game resources, yo."

And now that I've broken that ground I can do love relationships in In a Wicked Age (or the Venetian Tragedy, last weekend) and stay with it, do what the story wants rather than retreat to my comfort zone.

How do others feel about the sex moves / Seduce move?

    \n I found that when we played, sex moves followed the paradigm of 'to do it do it' more so than other narrative 'action'. If the sex isn't part of the narration then it didn't get mentioned.
    \n
    \nI mean, its a move, and the players wanted to 'try out' moves, but in the end they didn't have to, unless they wanted the prescriptive and descriptive reward of the move - their characters just had sex / went to the dunny / ate food / played games in the background and we didn't mention it.
    \n
    \nWhen we did narrate the sex move, I modeled Jared's rule of three with my (unnamed) MC moves. The player's caught on. Thus just enough description was hinted to spark the imagination without it being so graphic that the assembled players left the room blushing. I mean, folks are usually quite happy to describe emotional abuse, arterial bleeds and dismembered heads with aplomb. Sex isn't all that icky is it? Heck, its meant to be fun, or at least funny and enjoyable.
    \n
    \nSo, our sex moves became: 'Bullet takes her roughly, yet with joy and abandon.' or 'Nicotine is a lazy screw, she works her paramour in sinuous delight.' or most recently; 'He's a fumble, all thumbs and forgotten contraceptives. His hazy recollection after is of a most mouth-watering kiss'.
