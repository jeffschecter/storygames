\n Hello Ben,
I write this post because I would start a petition, hopeful that others will join it, in order to have Microscope distributed on Leisure Games (http://www.leisuregames.com/ or anyways, outside of IPR).
The reason for this post is that I badly want to own and play this game, but if I buy it from IPR, I am going to need to add $29,25 for USPS priority mail shipping. The shipping would be therefore meaningfully higher than the product itself, which is sold at $19,99.
I'm sure I'm not the only european roleplayer who might be interested in having your game distributed in Europe without having to pay such ridiculous shipping costs. In addition to the fact that were I to buy it from Leisure Games, the cost would be \xa33 (roughly $4,82), I would be able to receive the product in less than one week, while with IPR I once had to wait as long as one full month.

I am sure, Ben, you'll see how these factors are a major discriminant factor between buying and not buying the game.

All the best
Tazio

    \n Tazio, everybody else-
    \n
    \nWe (IPR) currently offer 20 active USPS shipping methods (out of a potential of 68) that customers can choose from. Internationally these include the following:
    \n
    \nExpress Mail International Flat Rate (EMS) Envelope (the most expensive)
    \nPriority Mail International (the second most expensive, and what Tazio seems to have selected)
    \nPriority Mail International Flat Rate Envelope (we offer 5 different varieties, all of which are rather reasonable and usable for a book like Microscope.)
    \n
    \nI just added USPS First Class Mail International Package to the selection bringing the number up to 21 (frankly, I didn't know that I'd left it out). I think First Class International would probably be the least expensive and, again, is usable for a book like Microscope, as it is under 16 ounces.
    \n
    \nAs always, I'm easy to reach through the website and am more than willing to do whatever I can to drive shipping costs down on IPR's products. In fact, I have done so many times for many different customers, working around the website's software when nothing else works. However, there haven't been many requests for me to do it since we increased the international USPS shipping options from 8 to 20 two months ago.
    \n
    \nI would also have been more than willing to refer Tazio to Leisure Games, or even the publisher of Microscope, had he contacted me directly.
