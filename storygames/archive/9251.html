\n A few months ago there was a topic on The Forge where someone said they'd love to see a game written on a single page, front and back.

The suggestion was offered that the game could be trifolded for easy display in a stand.

I'd been thinking of a couple of quick ideas, for games and started to develop a couple of them, the one that worked best was called Guerilla Television.

This was all done before I started seeing the influx of "oracle games", and it was written before we saw the "Harper Award" in little game chef, so it's a bit rough but it's basically written as a complete system on the front and back of a page. If you saw my little game chef entry, Erzulie ge Rouge you'll note that this game was an evolution of the concept (but perhaps got a bit confused along the way...).

There's an updated version I'm working on based on the results of a dozen playtests (quite a few at a recent con).

But for the moment, I offer you...

Guerilla Television Mk. 1

I'll post Mk. 2 shortly.
