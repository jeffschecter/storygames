\n What are your playing (gming included, natch) goals for the next year, the next three years, and the next five years? This doesn't include design goals, though it may include playtests. Mine are,


1 year:
-single Lady Blackbird,
-single Maid,
-2 complete games of S/lay w/Me,
-at least 2 more games of the Dreaming Crucible,
-a complete game of My Life with Master,
-at least beginning a long-term game of Apocalypse World or FreeMarket.

3 year:
-the other of the Apocalypse World/FreeMarket game,
-a long-term game of Burning Wheel,
-a long-term game of Dogs in the Vineyard,
-more than one session of InSpectres,
-a complete game of Polaris,
-a complete game of Grey Ranks,
-a long-term game of Sorcerer,
-a couple sessions of Moldvay Basic d&d,
-another game of Penny for my Thoughts

5 year:
-another long-term game of Burning Wheel,
-a longish-term game of Mouse Guard,
-a whole game of Burning Empires,
-a few sessions of Spirit of the Century,
-a whole game of Lacuna,
-some Drifter's Escape,
-some more FreeMarket,
-some more Apocalypse World.

This obviously doesn't include whatever new games come out that I have to play immediately.

I have no idea whether these are achievable or not, and I'm kind of wondering why this feels like something worth posting to me. I suppose I want to make clear to myself what it is that I want out of gaming, and to be able to look back and see if I've gotten it, and whether it's still satisfying either way.

you?

    \n I can totally get this reasoning; I've started doing this sort of thing in my head a LOT. Like you said, helps me focus my thoughts and figure out what I really want. 5 years is pretty far out for me, though. I'm gonna project a mere 3 years, which still might be stretching i1 yeart:

    1 Year:

    Playtest a shitload of the Dreaming Crucible, with anyone who'll get together with me for a 1-2 hour stretch
    Playtest one long-term game of Spectre of the Beast
    Play a long-term game of Sorcerer
    Play a long-term game of Apocalypse World
    Run a few Shock: one-shots at Guardian Games, where it'll soon be in stock
    Run one or two Capes one-shots at Guardian Games, where it's in stock
    Run a few Solar System one-shots at Red Castle Games, where it's in stock
    Play a few games of Clover, starting with my wife
    Play Wildlings with some kids I know
    Play a full game of My Life With Master

    2 Year:

    Keep playing Dreaming Crucible here and there
    Play a full game of Bliss Stage
    Play a long-term game of Capes
    Play the new Panty Explosion
    Play Heart of Ashes when it comes out
    Play a game of Misery Bubblegum
    Play a multi-session game of Lady Blackbird
    Play Grey Ranks
    Play Steal Away Jordan
    Play a full game of My Life With Master
    Play a session of Bacchanal with just the right group of friends
    Playtest my nascent mecha game Metal Angel Opera, assuming it gets to playable condition by then

    3 Year:

    Playtest Metal Angel Opera
    Play a bit of Dreaming Crucible
    Play a bit of Spectre of the Beast
    Play Mouse Guard
    Play whatever else

    I think the list diverges from the probable reality more and more as it moves forward in time. Mainly I just listed games I'm interested in playing, in the rough chronological order that I foresee playing them, and put year breaks where it seemed reasonable. I'd imagine I'll get to half of these games in this timeframe if I'm lucky. But Hans is right, listing them out helps me see the main scope of what I want, and I can pursue the core games while letting the lesser whims fall by the wayside as needed.

    Peace,
    -Joel
