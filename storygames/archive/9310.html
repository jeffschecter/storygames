\n Rob Donoghue posted this cool thing in which you draw three cards to get three random questions that flesh out your character's background. It seemed readymade for making a random generator out of it, so I've posted one on Abulafia here. Refresh that page to get results like these:

Where did you wake up after passing out?
How did you survive when the ship went down?
What did you confess to the character to your right?
