\n I have been working on an Inquiry based ARG/RPG which will be be released at the end of the month for the NaGa DeMon challenge.

The premise for the game is that you (Real life you) have been contacted and told of a world of magic and sorcery hidden below our mundane world. Sessions will be managed by a Magus (myself) who scouts out those with the potential for magic, testing them before fully indoctrinating them into the world of magic. These trials take the form of a tabletop RPG in which you play a mental projection of yourself, with the ability to cast Harry Potter Style magic.

You can find more information HERE, if you are interested.
