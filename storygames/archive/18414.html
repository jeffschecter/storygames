\n I ran InSpace for a couple of friends the other night. One had never played it before, and was very taken by it - I think he described the session as "the best game ever". But fantasy's much more his thing (and mine, really) than space adventure. Has anyone run Inspectres in a fantasy setting? I've seen UnSpeakable and InSpace, but no fantasy hacks. A web search turns up nothing.

(I read Donjon at some point, and that has some commonalities, but it's much, much more complex and clunky)

    \n It shouldn't be hard to create a hack. What you do need to change? The name of the skills and the assignment table. Example: Technology can be named equipment, Knowledge is lore, the company is a guild, the confession chair is you telling about your deeds for your grandchildren, and the Franchise Dice are loot.

    An [adjective] [employer of some sort] has a map to an [adjective] [place].

    Second phase is to get more information about the place and after a quick visit to the guild's storage, it's time for fieldwork.
