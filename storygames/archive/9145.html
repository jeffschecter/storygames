\n My contribution to the "Players of Games are Rockstars" event that Judd made. I totally approve of an environment which enthusiastically encourages players and actual play over the endless design posts/interviews/podcasts/whathaveyou.

Also, since I'm making my first baby steps with the use of InDesign, I decided to combine both projects into one. So here's a 2-page interview with Jason G. We talk about how he brings his A-Game to the table, his thoughts on D&D, some tidbits about playing a game (one he admittedly happened to be working on, but still play nonetheless), headtubes, etc.

I didn't want to get too bogged down, so I kept the questions light. In fact, the core questions are based on some interviews with pop stars that I found online (50 Cent, Rihanna, etc).
