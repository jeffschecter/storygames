\n Registration is Open for Go Play NW 2011!

Go Play NW is a super-awesome gathering of indie gamers and game designers in Seattle. It's a great time to meet new friends, re-connect with old ones, and play some really great games. This year we're meeting at the Seattle Center Northwest Rooms, under the shadow of the Space Needle and a block away from numerous eateries and tourist destinations; not that you'll have time for eating and touristing with all the great games going on!

Also, we're thrilled to be able to keep our ticket prices the same for the third year in a row!

If you're from out of town, this thread is a great place to plan shared rides, accommodations, or crash space for the con.

Early registration helps us pay our site deposit and plan better, so register today!

    Posted By: sagewill this year be all ages? My brother will likely be in town about then, and he's under 21, while last year's GPNW was 21+.
    \nAll ages is not something we can reasonably facilitate: it means we'd have to provide both space and people facilitating games throughout the weekend separate from the main event. That's not just for separating younger people from potential adult content of some games, but also for the benefit of the majority of the adult gamers, so that they don't have to be concerned about managing their speech and game content so that it's friendly for all ages.

    What we can do is make the weekend 18+. The Friday Night Feast, however, might still be 21+; we're still working on those arrangements, and part of that is determining whether we can have alcohol being served with dinner if there are attendees under 21.
