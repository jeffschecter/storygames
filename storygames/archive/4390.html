Hi all,

You're all invited to Brooklyn, NY on Sunday November 25th for the next installment of NerdNYC's Recess!

This is a special edition of Recess. We thought that it would be nice to do a little twist on Recess and take the gaming down a small notch and push the socialization up a bunch.

We're still going to have lots of games, but we're planning on having a big potluck lunch in the middle where we all stop playing, sit down and eat together.

Save the date! Let us know if you would like to join us. More details to follow!

Click here for the latest information!

Recess will be held at BRICSTUDIO from 11am to 10pm.
