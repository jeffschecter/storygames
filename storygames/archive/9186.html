    \n Yes, we should talk about it here.

    Let's just all remember that Vinny is one of the best things to ever happen to Indie Gaming, and that his requests to us were intended to help both his con and our community thrive, so it is critically important that we read him as charitably as possible, even on issues that he seems to just "not get."

    Personally, I think there are some things where we're not going to want to budge, and that's probably all right. I'm not going to run Polaris or DRYH for six people, for instance, nor do I think anyone else should.

    Most of the best ideas that were tossed around in various livejournals after Dremation had to with the scheduling system. If we could convince Vinny to modernize it a bit (IIRC several people volunteered to do the necessary coding), we could then detect where the need for more GM's is. Then anyone who steps up to the plate to GM a high-demand game would have their

    Issues not addressed by me in this post:
    -designer-only playtest track? (I hate this idea with a hatey hatred, but I get that having unfinished games on the main schedule can sometimes be problematic)
    -giving more people access to the *designers* of hot games (sure, I can run DRYH, but I ain't Fred)

    Matt
