\n I need cool things as prompts for revenge tragedy - think Ford, Webster, Kyd, Shakespeare. If you are not super familiar with the sub-genre, Hamlet is a less-lurid example. The best of it is positively Grand Guignol and I love it. Take the survey!

The idea here is I want prompts that will guide play. So each of these should be slightly open-ended, ghoulish and horrible, and offer really fun possibilities for the player, who will be applying it to someone else's poor dude. Here's my list right now:

Mistaken Identity Leads to Disaster
A Crime Released from the Cage of Lust
A Crime So Black and Foul That Angels Weep
A Lover's Still-Beating Heart
Bitter Tears, Helpless Against Inexorable fate
A Lovingly-Prepared Meal of Bitter Revenge
Arrogant Pride Trumps the Painful Truth
A Savage Punishment, Excessive and Shocking
A Heart Needlessly Broken by Ignorance and Lies
A Weed-Choked Corpse, Reeking of Canal Mud, Returns for Vengeance
A Guilty Soul Foolishly Cries Out for Forgiveness
An Evil Secret is Sunk in the Lagoon Forever
Suspicion and Terror Prompt an Unholy Alliance
Vicious Betrayal Leads to Madness
A Broken Promise Hides a Dreadful Curse

If any of these seem odd, or weak, or too specific, or just off-tone, let me know. And please suggest more and better ones!
I am totally OK with stealing, so if you have a favorite bit from Duchess of Malfi or whatever (hooray for poisoned bibles!) shout it out. Thanks!

    \n These will be on cards and assigned more or less randomly to each player (each card will have two to choose from). They will then use that, as Fate Guide, to drive the character of the player on their right toward that as a thematic goal somehow. If you've seen Love in the Time of Sei\xf0, they take the place of character Themes.

    So if I'm Fate Guide for Cardinal Zago and get to choose between "In A Hidden Chamber Beneath the Palazzo, Awful Justice" and "Bitter Tears, Helpless Against Inexorable fate", I'm going to wait a few scenes to see how things are going, and then either steer Zago toward an appointment with the Doge's torturer or a situation where he sees his fate coming and can't stop it.
