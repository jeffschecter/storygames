\n Hey there...

The Kickstarter just launched today for Story Realms, a storytelling adventure game created by my wife Angela Hickman Newnham and our design partner Julian Leiberan-Titus. It combines the elements of a roleplaying game and a boardgame, and has amazing art! Here is the link:

http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/springboard/story-realms?ref=activity

Please consider sharing it on your social networks. Also, if you are at PAX Prime, consider popping up to the sixth floor to the Game Salute booth and trying a demo!
