\nThis episode begins a three part series on Grey Ranks from Bully Pulpit Games. Grey Ranks is a game about teenagers taking part in the Warsaw uprising in 1944. We talk about the appeal of playing this game, the nuts and bolts of how scenes work, and our standard GMless question of how the game mechanically fills the GM's role.
