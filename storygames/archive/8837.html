\n John and I were talking about the game of Ghost/Echo we played and how the setting didn't really click until we hit upon one key idea. Then the whole setting really came together. As we chatted about this, I mentioned that a Word Cloud with the setting tags would be cool. John mentioned Wordle and the following was born.

This excel spreadsheet takes the Ghost/Echo setting terms and duplicates them a random number of times to create a giant word list. Then you feed the generated word list into Wordle, play with the settings and poof, you have a very unusual setting document for your game of Ghost/Echo.

The spreadsheet can be adapted for any list of keywords. Just replace the words that are already there, hit the button and there you go.



Here is my Wordle Gallery. Not sure when I will do more of them.

You will have to enable macros to use the spreadsheet. It is built in Excel 2007, saved in Excel 2003 format. If you are having trouble with whisper me and I will try to help you out.
