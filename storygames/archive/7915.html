\n I'm trying to find a good name for a mechanic to be included in Eekamouse in Atlantis ... and utterly failing

Eekamouse has abilities which give dice in certain situations. Some abilities can be enhanced with an extra clause that gives a bonus dice in a sub-class of situations, introduced with 'especially' e.g.

ABILITY : Fighting Monsters

ENHANCED : Fighting Monsters, especially when defending others

So it's basically a specialization of the ability, but I don't want to call it a specialization, because that doesn't properly fit the terminology of the game, which is about toys. I'd also thought of 'perk', 'bonus' (but that's used for something else now) and a few others I don't remember now

Any suggestions?

    \n How about "Particulars"?

    Or even better, how about calling them "Especiallys" (or "Especiallies")? As in "My Ability is 'Fighting monsters', and the Especially is 'Defending others'." and "You're gonna fight him? What's your Especially, again?"

    Since it's about toys, I think this kind of made-up word would be cool, as children often do that sort of thing when learning a language. There are many varieties one could use: Especialities, Absolutelies, Evenmores, Biggies, or even the full Especiallywhens.
