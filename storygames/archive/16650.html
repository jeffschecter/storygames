\n Inspired by the impending release of Dungeon World, I read the latest Beta last week and created a document of new/changed/alternate rules to run Dungeon World in my existing homebrew setting. It was very fun and easy to make these rules. I had an awesome time and the rules helped my ideas become moves, rather than bogging me down. It's a very flexible system and so great to make house rules and custom moves for. I haven't playtested any of this yet, but here it is nonetheless.\n

Sarrenor Rules Compendium (Singles)
Sarrenor Rules Compendium (Spreads)

Page where I'll put any updates or additional documents. Also the change log is there.

Seems like the links are working for most people, but here are some alternates (Google Docs) for anyone having trouble downloading: Singles and Spreads.

Here's a brief run-down of the document:

    \n
  • Replacements for the racial moves (mostly tweaked to fit a human-only world) plus two new 'backgrounds'
    • \n
  • Replacements for a few Cleric and Ranger moves
    • \n
  • Tweaked Recover and End of Session moves
    • \n
  • Gods with domains and precepts (also alignments you can adopt, inspired by Sanglorian's Dungeon World Stuff.
    • \n
  • Some world moves for things like (accidentally) summoning demons, giving offerings at wayshrines, and a psychic maelstrom inspired move
    • \n
  • Dreamscape Moves: a suite of rules for operating in a dream world, featuring rules for building your own realm there and using it to fight others' realms
    • \n
  • Tweaked equipment/services/price lists
    • \n
  • Some monsters from my world, including these skeletons
    • \n
\nI hope you enjoy these rules and setting bits. As I said, I haven't used them yet. Feedback is welcome. Once I play with these rules I might update the document.
