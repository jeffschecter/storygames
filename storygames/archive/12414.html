\n Check out our interview with Josh Roby where he reveals the stealth tactics of his design, slipping story elements under the hood of their re-worked Cortex system used in Smallville. We also get into his other games as well with some emphasis on Agora.

You can find it here: http://www.therpghaven.com/podcast/?p=266

Personally, I really love what MWP is doing. With the market power of their licenses and Cam Banks open-mind to design influences on both sides of the spectrum, I think they are doing a lot to break down artificial cultural divides in the hobby. I'm very curious to see how extremists on either side will react to games that mix traditional structures with rules that manipulate the story.
