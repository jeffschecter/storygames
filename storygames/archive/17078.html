    Hello Daniele,

    A little bird told me you ran a game of this but it went badly. Sorry about that.

    My thoughts: Chronicles of Skin is very much a game of exploration and it may not intersect well with canon. Perhaps the Scenario needs to focus on something we don't know about the Star Wars universe. Like, what happened to Yoda's home planet? Maybe he was from a race of despots. What enemy could they have faced? Why, and who won the war?

    Perhaps that's the road we need to take with established franchises.
    \nUnluckily it went not so good. The major problem was the fact in Star Wars you have a canon; you\u2019ll expect Jedis, action, adventure in a Star Wars game. But, how you said, this is not the intent of a Chronicles of Skin game. This is, in my opinion, the explanation of my partial failure.
