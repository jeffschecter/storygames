\n The trailer for a new zombie video game is here. It's probably the most gut-wrenching game trailer I've ever seen. Go watch it for yourself.


I found myself quite moved like I am when playing certain story games. I'd love for the actual game to stir up the same tension/sorrow/anxiety/desperation I felt watching the trailer. I believe I hope for too much.

    \n I miss video game trailers that showed actual gameplay, instead of trying to mimic movie trailers. There are a few developers/publishers that still have gameplay-centric trailers--Nintendo, in particular--but it's becoming increasingly rare.

    I guess I'm irked by the growing trends of cinematic games. I'm tired of games that amateurishly ape film techniques. The writing/style is never up to that of legitimately good films, and it offer means linear, by-the-nose gameplay designed more to enhance the impact of the next scripted event than to deliver richness or depth of gameplay.
