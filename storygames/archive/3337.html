    \n This is pretty slick: These guys came out of left field with their "Witch Girls Adventures" over on the Indie RPG community at LJ.

    Here's a link to their latest post, where they provide a mini-comic, and an overview of the system with a character sheet.

    It's simmy (attributes, skills, yadda), but I love that:
    1) It looks rules-light, and the chargen rules look stupid-simple* and easy to get into play.
    2) They appear to be focusing entirely on anime/comic fans instead of RPG fans, which is healthy IMO.
    3) The art is slick.
    4) Sure, it's simmy, but I noticed on the character sheet there's no big "combat section" or massive "weapons lists", which appears to work for the material.
    5) ...again, they came out of left field. Refreshing!

    -Andy

    * I use "stupid-simple" as a high form of praise. I love games where you can jump right in and pick up the specifics along the way, which this one seems to do.
