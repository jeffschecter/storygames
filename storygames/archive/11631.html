    \n
    \n\nMarch 2010 \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n 1. Still neck-deep in Apocalypse World.

    2. My Brainer got her sixth improvement. This lets you choose experience items from "the special list", aka The Ungiven Future. So I chose to expand three of her basic moves. One of the expanded moves is: "When you open your brain to the world's psychic maelstrom, roll+weird. On a 12+, you see through the maelstrom to what lies beyond."

    She then proceeded to open her brain and I rolled a 13. She saw through the maelstrom to what lies beyond.

    It was fucking awesome.
