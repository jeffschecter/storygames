Manifesto of Manifesting Manifestations of Manifest Magnificence

April 2006 edited April 2006 in Play Advice
I am Manifest! I write out stuff that I think is cool!

I am Manifest! I reduce all things that I do in a game into one or two basic propositions!

I am Manifest! I use simple, declarative sentences and lots of exclamation marks!

I am Manifest! I play all games the same way, according to my one or two basic propositions!

I am Manifest! I repeat myself many times!

I am Manifest! I hope that other people will chime in with agreement and support, proving that I am not the only person who believes these crazy one or two propositions!

I am Manifest! Something else goes about here!

I am Manifest! How Manifested are you?
