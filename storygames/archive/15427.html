\n I\u2019m launching a kickstarter for http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/2088966277/heads-of-state-nine-short-games-about-tyrants.

It's been over seven years since I first posted about this game concept on the forge. It was an interesting series to design and playtest. Editing & layout of 9 games (even short games) has always seemed a bit too daunting to complete. But the Arab spring has inspired me take another look at them and Kickstarter seems like a good way to gauge interest. Heck it even got me back to story games after a couple year absence

I hope you\u2019ll check out the project and consider whether it\u2019s something you\u2019d support. I would also appreciate any help you can give in sharing the link online through twitter, facebook, G+, blogs, forums etc.
Short url: http://kck.st/tyegfn

thanks
Mark
