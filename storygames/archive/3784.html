A somewhat different approach, here.

If you are having problems enjoying a game you think you should enjoy, identify it and why you are having problems with it.

Then someone champions the game and tries to explain either what you're doing wrong, why the game doesn't support your play style, or make suggestions on how you can modify the game to suit you better. Start a new thread with the title "Help me understand [Bunnies and Burrows]" or similar. Don't post in this thread, as it would get messy, but feel free to bump it with a link to your thread so it doesn't disappear off the map.

Thus, critique of the game can be given with your experience as the focus, rather than the game as the focus.

It has been my experience that whatever you're discussing, approaching the subject matter as "this didn't work for me, please help" is much more constructive than "this product is bad, everyone stay away".
