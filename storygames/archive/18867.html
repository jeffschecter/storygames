\n Damn, I need to quit screwing around and get this game going... Anyone care to help?



- My goal is to create a leisure game that is played with friends or family to kill an otherwise boring evening.
- This Is Pulp challenges the players' creativity and helps the group to collaborate in creating a pulp story like those on the silver screen.
- This Is Pulp is a truly pickup and play kind of game. You can even read the rules while playing.
- The game rules are less than three pages long and a session takes around 70 minutes.
- It takes place in an alternative 1930's universe. Sources of inspiration are Indiana Jones, King Kong, Sky Captain and the World of Tomorrow, Tintin, The Rocketeer, King Solomon's Mines, and similar movies.
- It's built for 3-5 people in mind.
- It will be launched at Indiegogo.

If you ever followed my previous threads, those thoughts are what spawned this game. This is the second game that I aim to publish and I need your help to see if people can understand how to play it. I want suggestions on how to make the text more clear and feedback on how it plays. It would be incredible if you could record the session in any way, either in audio or video, but that's not a requirement. You will need to print ten pages, where six of them are characters, three are maps and two are game aids for the game master.

If this sounds interesting, whisper your real name and email address to me, and I will send you a playtest document.

If you want more information, check out my temporary site for the game.
