\n Here\u2019s a fast, fun two-player storygame that will help you while away many an otherwise burdensome hour standing in line to vote, or during long unanticipated road trips to Canada.

It\u2019s easy to learn. One player takes on the role of Candidate Adam, a major figure in US federal politics. The other player takes on the role of the Commentariat. Each round consists of the first player making some remark which in normal contexts would seem utterly monstrous, insane, or just plain evil. Player two then finds away to \u201crepackage\u201d said remark in a way that would make it appealing to the American cultural mainstream.

Examples:

Player One (CA): \u201cY\u2019know what would really improve this nation\u2019s landscape? Setting fire to all the churches!\u201d
Player Two (Com): \u201cLiberal elites want to regulate firearms, fireworks, househould uranium refinement, and seatbelt use in cars, because of so-called threats to \u2018public safety.\u2019 But we say: let freedom ring!\u201d

Player One (CA): \u201cI ate an entire human child for supper just last night, cooked into a delicious ragout.\u201d
Player Two (Com): \u201cHitler was a vegetarian!\u201d

Now you try!
