\n I was wondering if it would be worthwhile to take one of the tables out on the landing level (that the Indie explosion is always on at Dreamation), and designate it as a the Indie Meetup table?

So folks who were looking for an Indie game (either their game didn't run, or its between sessions, or whatever) could wander over and talk to like minded folks.

I'm not looking to replicate Games on Demand, or anything like that, but I have noticed that if your game doesn't run for whatever reason, you're stuck with a 2-4 hour slot and no real resources to find other folks other than just stumbling into something.

Hopefully, this would enable a few more impromptu games to get going, or at least give folks a clear signal that "hey I'm interested in Indie stuff and am not to busy to talk".

I don't think that sticking a sign on a table is overengineering a solution to this, but I'd like other folks ideas...

Jeff
