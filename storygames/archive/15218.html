    \n (From an idea stolen from Pratchett:)

    A game where Siri is confused across timelines. You try to do stuff, she gives you feedback based on what an alternative version of yourself is doing in a parallel universe. Then, the two start bleeding over into each other - as you communicate indirectly with your other self through Siri.

    \n In Soviet Russia, Siri tells YOU what to do.

    Make a database of tasks, tagged by theme, preferences, social network data or whatever. Anyone can enter a task. Or maybe all your facebook or google + friends. When you talk to Siri, she assigns you a task based on what you say. In a loud, mean voice. Maybe she yells and swears at you and call you names if you don't tag the task as completed within a certain deadline. Maybe she makes threats too. And follows up on them. Completing the tasks gets you credits, which you can use to assign tasks to other players.
