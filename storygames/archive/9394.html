\n I needed a cover for A Taste For Murder. For my first attempt, I used this photo:



It was OK. I put the title over the red area, in white Art Deco lettering, and it looked beautiful. But the glass bothered me. It was the wrong sort of glass for 1930s England. For a while, I swore at every stock photographer everywhere, none of whom had thought to photograph a wine glass with a big red spill.

So I talked to Elizabeth, who took a stock photo with two wine glasses and a tiny bit of spillage, did some stuff I don't understand and gave me this:



She is great. The title will go in the same place, over the red area, in that beautiful Art Deco font I mentioned above.

Do feel free to give me feedback on that cover picture, by the way. Whisper it if you need to.

Graham
