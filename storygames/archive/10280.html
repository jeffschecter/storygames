Making Money in the RPG Industry: Gamer Lifestyle
\n
\nTwo of the biggest names in online RPG writing, Yax of Dungeon Mastering and Johnn Roleplaying Tips, have teamed up to create The Gamer Lifestyle Project.
\n
\nIt\u2019s a bit of a funny name, but if you\u2019re interested in having your work published, making money in the gaming industry, and balancing those concerns with real life, check it out. If you sign up for their email list, you get a free PDF that outlines the program and shares all sorts of useful tips. Even if you have no interest in the program, the PDF is great \u2014 I wish I\u2019d had something like this when I started out as a freelancer and blogger.
\n
\nThe Gamer Lifestyle course isn\u2019t cheap \u2014 $447 for five months (payable monthly) \u2014 but I\u2019ve known Johnn and Yax online for years, and met Yax in person. Not only are they good guys, they know their shit. If you\u2019re interested in the course, there are only a few slots left for this \u201cschool year,\u201d and registration closes tomorrow.
\n
\nhttp://gamer-lifestyle.com/
