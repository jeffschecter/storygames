\n The very last episode of the Voice of the Revolution has gone out. IPR has had some changes recently, and they reassessed all their expenses, and sadly the Voice did not make the cut. I totally understand their decision, it was a cost with a very shaky relationship with return. Still, it's sad to be done with the show.

Rich and I did get Paul Tevis and Ryan Macklin on the show to record a goodbye, so check it out. There's lots of joking and reminiscing. I'm going the miss doing the show, and I know Rich had a hard time editing it because it was making him sad.

So, hoist a glass for the Voice! It's more of a wake than a funeral.
