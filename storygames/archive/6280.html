\n Paul asked me how unWritten uses OK. I thought I should answer in a different thread, and give Levi room to do Levi things in his thread.

Paul: What I kept from OK was its conflict squares (I'm not sure what OK calls them) and their basic purposes. I've reduced the conflict squares to three because I realized that the fourth die was redundant for my purposes. I've adjusted the game so that everything you do has a mechanical function that supports the creation of story. Each element of the game is some aspect that is relevant to the "way" stories are created/written in general. Thus, it is constantly reinforcing itself with "what the game is about."

Are there any specific questions that I can answer?
