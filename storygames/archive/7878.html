\n Milton Keynes RPG Club are holding another of their games days on 14 March 2009.
\n
\nWHO?
\nWe're Milton Keynes RPG Club. Find out more about us at http://www.mk-rpg.org.uk.
\n
\nWHEN?
\nSaturday 14 March 2009. Doors open at 9.00am, first games start at 10.00am. We'll finish at 11.00pm.
\n
\n(We'll be doing another on 19 September 2009 at the same times.)
\n
\nWHAT?
\nAny roleplaying or board game. You want to play it, bring it along and run it. Previously we've had on offer Amber, Call of Cthulhu, Cold City, Dogs in the Vineyard, GURPS Banestorm, Gwenthia, HeroQuest, Hollow Earth Expedition, Mythic Russia, Primetime Adventures, Savage Worlds, Spycraft, Tekumel, Transhuman Space, True20, Vampire, and some more that I've forgotten. Latest updates will be posted on the Concrete Cow website.
\n
\nA full list of games on offer is on the Concrete Cow games page.
\n
\nWHO'LL BE THERE?
\nWe'll be inviting some Guests of Honour. There may even be a trader. More details when we have them!
\n
\nMembers of the Concrete Cowncil, MKRPG's convention gaming arm, will be in attendance to run all sorts of funky games.
\n
\nWe may get some official demo people along too. Again, details when we have them.
\n
\nAnd, of course, there's all the fantastic games you'll bring along and run. If you let us know what you're planning to run, we'll put it on the Concrete Cow games page.
\n
\n(Note that for child protection reasons, under-16s will need to be accompanied by an adult at Concrete Cow.)
\n
\nWHERE?
\n
\nThe Old Bath House, Wolverton, in the north-west corner of Milton Keynes. The full address is
\n
\nThe Old Bath House
\n205 Stratford Road
\nWolverton
\nMilton Keynes
\nMK12 5RL
\n
\nMaps are available on MK Web, Google Maps, and Streetmap.co.uk.
\n
\nDirections for drivers are on the directions page.
\n
\nRegular trains to Milton Keynes run from London Euston station. The nearest station to the con is Wolverton (one stop north of Milton Keynes Central), about five minutes' walk away.
\n
\nHOW MUCH?
\n\xa35.00, payable on the door. This'll cover the cost of hiring the hall.
\n
\nIf you want to know more, email concrete-cow-owner@yahoogroups.com, visit our Yahoo group, take a look at the Concrete Cow forum or ask here!

    \n
    \n It may soon be Christmas, but the gaming world stops for no deity! I've got a couple of exciting announcements to make about who'll be attending Concrete Cow 09 on 14 March 09!
    \n
    \nWe have our first Guest of Honour! It's Gregor Hutton, the man behind Box Ninja Games. He's the author and publisher of those excellent games, Best Friends and 3:16 Carnage Among the Stars. He's also a member of the Collective Endeavour, the UK indie-publishing coterie. There's be more information on him when he sends me a bio! He'll be chained to a table and forced to run games of Best Friends and 3:16 all day.
    \n
    \nWe also have a trader confirmed! The Cubicle 7 Entertainment / Leisure Games roadshow will be rolling back to Concrete Cow 09. There will be all sorts of Cubicle 7 games for sale, such as Victoriana, StarBlazer, SLA Industries, and Qin: Warring States. There may even be some news on the forthcoming Doctor Who game.
    \n
    \nThe Leisure Games end of the stall will have a variety of stock, but will concentrate on things such as the Indie Games, the HP Lovecraft Historical Society T-Shirts, DVDs and various paraphernalia.
    \n
    \nIf you have something in particular you'd like to buy at Concrete Cow, either contact Leisure Games directly, or PM me and I'll pass the message on.
    \n
    \nMake sure you bring plenty of cash to give to the nice people with the shiny books!
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \nMake sure you keep an eye on the Concrete Cow website for more updates when they happen.
    \n
    \nLook forward to seeing you all in March. Have a good Christmas and a Happy New Year!
    \n
    \nNeil.

    \n An update from Concrete Cow central.
    \n
    \nWe're pleased to announce Bob and Reaper's Revenge will be at Concrete Cow. Bob will have all manner of sparkling polyhedra along to tempt you.
    \n
    \nHe'll be flanking the hall with the Cubicle 7 / Leisure Games stall, so bring plenty of money to give to the nice gentlemen. If there's anything in particular you want, either contact the stores directly or PM me and I'll pass the message on.
    \n
    \nNathan Baron is offering a Battlestar Galactica game:
    \n
    \n
    Battlestar Galactica: Hard Point
    \n
    \nA four player scenario for Chaosium\u2019s Basic Roleplaying sytem
    \n
    \nTwo weeks prior to the Cylon attack an elite squad of operatives known as Hard Point are sent on a vital mission to infiltrate Arch Angel, an abandoned space station, which has become the base for a new religious movement. Hard Point\u2019s mission: to assassinate Gabrielle Schindler, a religious agitator, and locate two government agents that infiltrated Arch Angel but never reported back. Schindler preaches the word of the one true God, a doctrine that is seen as dangerous by the current administration. What they discover will lead to a terrifying revelation in the future, when the last of humanity is fighting for its very existence.
    \n
    \n
    \nYou can find details of games on offer at the Concrete Cow games page. Trouble is, that page is looking rather bare. Tell me about that wonderful game you'll be offering and I'll put it on the website for all the world to see and get excited by.
    \n
    \nHope to see you all on 14 March!
    \n
    \nNeil.
