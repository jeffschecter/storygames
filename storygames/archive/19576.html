\n In Dead Man Talking, you play prisoners on Death Row reviewing their lives and crimes in the days leading up to their execution.

I've substantially re-written the doc following the last playtest. Revised draft here.

By way of a pitch, here's the frontispiece blurb:
Can you, in your final moments, come to terms with who you are and what you\u2019ve done, or will you go to meet your maker haunted by your unresolved past? Death is easy, living is hard.

Any o' y'all fancy it, please let me know.
