    That's, more or less, the Spirit of the Millenium idea, which is sort of my notion of SOTC 2025...

    In my head, SOTC's the first in a trilogy, though I doubt the other two will see light:

    Toward the end of the last century is Shadow of the Century. I can say it all by saying what the cover would look like: An aging Mack, Sally, and Jet are backed up against a bullet-parked brick wall. Guns point at them. There's a campaign poster on that wall. It reads: METHUSELAH * PRESIDENT * 1984. The Century Club is now an outlaw organization, and the Shadows are winning.

    Then there's the full-on supers-plus-near-future-tech thang of Spirit of the Millennium. Methuselah's been in power for decades, and as a result the technological advances have been on a constant fast-forward. But behind the gleaming facade of advancing humankind, is the Earth's doom at hand? Here, we have the elderly Spirits of the last century guiding and protecting the new spirits of the present age ... and the everpresent question: are these new, powerful people Centurions, or Millennials?

    That sort of gig. Very rambly and not edited for punchiness or clarity.
