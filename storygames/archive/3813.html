    \n George --

    I've taken down Ganakagok from Lulu for the time being because I want to make some rather substantial changes to it in terms of layout, rules explanations, and GM advice and options. I've been troubled by the idea that people might regard the game in its current incarnation as "half-baked" in the way that that term has been bruited here on Story-Games.

    See, if you've ever seen a game of Ganakagok being played, you know how it works. But I'm not sure the written explanations of the rules do all they could to give GMs an understanding of how to run the game. So I want to try again.

    I still have a few copies of the current version, though, and so if you whisper me a mailing address, I'll send it to you. Once you read it and hopefully play it, you can send me your feedback and a check for whatever you think it's worth.

    -- Bill
