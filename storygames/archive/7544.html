\n You know when you are fighting and you have to poll everybody about who failed, and who succeeded, and at what level? In our game Graham suggested a simple hack that made assessing and synthesizing all this quite easy:

Thumbs down if you failed, the number of fingers equal to what you rolled in a success otherwise. So in the photo, Isbo and I succeeded with a one each, Graham succeeded with a six or something, and a couple of people failed.
