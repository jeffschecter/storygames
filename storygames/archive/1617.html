Does anyone want to help me enter those events here?

Entry Format

I have attempted to standardize the entry format for maximum searchability, cuz only crazy people want to read the whole list. More likely, you want to find all the cons in California or all the Gaming cons. Here's a quick guide to my format.

Sample Entry:

Spiel Essen
October 19-22, 2006
Essen, Germany
Gaming
(Mostly boardgames but some RPGs, strong trade angle. Huge.)
Discussion Thread

Key by Line:
  1. The title of the event, linked to the event's web page (or an email address if that's the only contact.
  2. Date of the convention. Month spelled out followed by day range and year. If the convention spans two months, Spell out both month names (e.g., October 28-November 1).
  3. Location of the event. City (spelled out, e.g., Los Angeles), State (spelled out, e.g., North Carolina), Country. Use "USA" for events in the States. Leave off hotel information, in general.
  4. Type of convention. Current categories are Gaming, System-specific Gaming, Fandom, and Writing.
  5. Optional. Other notes about the convention, in parentheses.
  6. Optional. Link to the discussion thread on the phrase "Discussion Thread".
Looking up all of this information and putting it in the right format makes my job easier and saves me time. Thanks!
