    \n The playtest series is a way to separate episodes we do on playtest versions of games from our regular episodes, since the aspects of the games we talk about may change between the time we record and the games release. We don't currently have any other games to playtest scheduled. Our selection criteria is the same for any game we play. That usually means one of gets excited about a game then sells the rest of the group on playing it.
