This is Reflections. A game of Dueling Samurai.

Reflections is a two person short-form RPG where players take on the role of samurai that have come to one final confrontation. The game begins as these samurai meet to have a final duel. Before blood can be drawn, they relive the events of their lives and the circumstances that brought them to this moment. They will use their past memories as weapons to help them prevail over their former ally. Two samurai who started as friends, ended as bitter rivals, and only one can survive this duel.
