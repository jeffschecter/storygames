\n Hi!

I've been asked to compile a couple of pages of LARP/RPG-related news for the new European Larp/RPG-magazine "Playground", and I was hoping you could help me out. I'm looking for important/experimental/different/interesting/peculiar news about events, publications, Larps, organizations, projects, methods, happenings, media coverage, etc.

The editors of the magazine are mainly from the Nordic Larp-scene, but it also covers table-top/pen&paper RPGs. The focus is European, but interesting news from all over the world are welcome. The news will probably be presented in a fairly short format, like three or four sentences.

The vision for the magazine is to come across as "Sexy, Art/Design, Weird", so that might give you some pointers as to what I'm looking for.

Tips about Larp/RPG-related crossovers into other cultural areas (computer games/ARGs/performance, theatre/theatre sports/street theatre/whatnot) are also most welcome!

So: what's going on? Don't assume I know everything you do, please post anything you think might be of interest!

Any help or hints would be greatly appreciated!

I'm especially interested in things that's happened the last couple of months, and things that will happen within the next half a year.

Best regards,
Ole Peder Gi\xe6ver
