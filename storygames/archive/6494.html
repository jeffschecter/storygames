\n June 16th - 20th at RPGnet is Fantasy Week, and as part of our big review event we're offering a lot of awesome prizes. The big change this time is that the judges are only rating the top 5 reviews - all other prizes are being distributed through random drawing among the remaining entries. This way the reviews we consider to be the very best get first pick at the prize pool, but everyone has an opportunity to walk away with PDFs from White Wolf, RedBrick, and Evil Hat Productions.
