\n Hi all

I'm contemplating a trip to Fastaval, a Danish gaming convention in April. It's one I'm very keen to attend, as a sizeable chunk of my storygaming/Nordic larping homies will be there, including many luminaries from that scene. Trouble is, I'm very short of funds atm, so I'm thinking of producing a set of custom dice to finance my trip. The dice will be manufactured in the indyhippo company colours, to whit grey with pink spots (there's a reason for that particular colour scheme which I won't go into here, unless folks ask), and I\u2019ll probably sell them in sets of 20 for \xa35. Does that sound reasonable? I\u2019ll need to sell around 60 sets of 20 dice, which seems quite ambitious, but I\u2019m hoping it will be achievable.

Can folks signal interest here so I can get a sense of likely numbers, and whether it\u2019s doing at all?

Thanks
Leo
