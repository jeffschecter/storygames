    \n Yes that's all legit -- that's material that's from the last round of collaborative writing before we gatewayed the product. It varies from the final product in a few ways:
    \n
    \n* there is no copyright statement so you should take it as read that it is not OGL but rather trad. copyright by us just like anything (vs. SRD and book)
    \n* flavour text, examples, and fiction have not been cut (vs. SRD)
    \n* it predates about a year of playtest and editing, so many rules vary substantially (vs. SRD and book)
    \n* it could cease to exist at any moment as it's not an official resource -- no plans to remove it though as I like the historical document of our design process
