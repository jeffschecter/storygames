\n Because it's a great meme, and because Andy hasn't said no, and because I want to play with it, damnit...

The Tom Waits Oracle

Suggestions: let's keep it in that 20th Century continuum, running from film noir to Kerouac to, say, New York in the late 70's. Late nights and rainy days. Punchiness is a virtue.

Diamonds:
A: A red barn in winter: inside the blood is just beginning to cool, but outside the footprints are getting filled in with snow.
2: A wounded man in a hotel room, too drunk to take off his blood-stained shirt.
