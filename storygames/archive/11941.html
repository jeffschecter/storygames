\n Some of you have heard of a line of games called "Parlor Larps", produced by Shifting Forest Storyworks. They're well-constructed live-action games designed for 1 room, 1 evening, 4-8 people. They've been hard to find for the past few years; after appearing on the scene around 2005 and getting a decent amount of word of mouth, the creators disappeared from the face of the internet.

Now, all of the Parlor Larps have been released as freeware.

On March 25, 2010, our lead playtester Matt Brown died unexpectedly of medical reasons. In his honor, we are making our collection of Parlor Larps free to the public. Matt was a brilliant and avid larper. His passion inspired us and his faith motivated us always. He will always be foremost in our minds in every game we ever design, run and play.

Matt, here's to being the protagonist. When I conquer the world, I expect you to come claim your half.

-- J Li
March 26, 2010
