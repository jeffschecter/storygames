Your good friend Kan-chan died once.

She was once the princess Kwan-Yin of China,

Who defied her father,

The King, who's heart was held by a Hungry Ghost,

And was beheaded for it.

-

But she regretted her defiance.

And her regret made her Highest Hell a Lowest Heaven,

Made her the goddess, Compassion Herself,

And sent her back to the Highest Heaven,

Where she has ever remained.

-

This is is the story of how you,

A bishoujo shinigami, a Reaper Beauty,

will face and hopefully Reap the Hungry Ghosts

that craves the lives they once had,

armed only with your friendship with Kan-chan.

-

This is Reaper Beauties,

a Shoujo Manga-Style Storytelling Game

Written by Elliott Belser,

Copyright 2007 Perpetual Motion Games,

Now accepting official playtesters.

-

Playtests every Saturday at 2:00 pm until 6:00

at Green Griffon Games,

1640 Martin Luther King Jr. Way,

Berkeley, California.

-

I look forward to meeting you.
