\n I have an irregular Skype group made up of friends we gamed with before moving to Canada. We were reminiscing about WGP last night and wishing that there was some way to play it online since it's one of our favorite systems ever. But the multiple decks and the card-stacking mechanic makes things far too difficult to try to navigate using online tools that we know about. So I thought I'd throw this out to Story-Games: does anyone know of an online tool that already exists that would allow for playing With Great Power over Skype?

If such a tool does not exist, then I know we would all be willing to throw money at someone who could make such a tool.
