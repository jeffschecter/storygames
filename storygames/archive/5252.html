    \n If you roll over/under your Praxis-scale according to your stated intent, you lose. Keep in mind that both Protagonists and Antagonists can lose. This yield a number of different - and interesting! - conflict outcomes.

    If you strongly want to succeed as Protag/Antag-player, choose more d10's when you roll. If you want to be able to affect the outcome of the opposition, rolling more d4's will help you do so.

    You always only keep one die of each, though (the one that best helps you with your intent).
