\n Ok, there's this thing in my game that I am right now, unsatisfied, calling a "cool chit." MY is a sci-fi game about teenage rebellion, friendship, and growing up.

So the thing is: if I make someone look cool, I take The Thing. Until someone else makes me look cool, I am the leader, in-fiction and in-rules. People are going to look to me for plans, The Authority is going to attack me and the things I care about, I have some scene framing responsibilities, etc.

"Make you look cool" is fluid and interpretable. In fact it may be a strategy to tell someone, "Oh you made me look cool, here you go."

Anyway, got any ideas for the chit? Some perverse part of me wants to call it the conch. Other ideas:

The Rock
The Potato
Flow

Any others?
