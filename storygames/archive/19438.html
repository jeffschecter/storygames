\n The brazilian game design community is growing increasingly, following the many new games published here in portuguese. We gathered many authors to discuss and push analog game development forward and many incredible initiatives started to appear all around our country. So now I decided to write about all this creative movement... in English.

The Analog XP will be a free online magazine about all that is happening around here, with articles, event coverage, interviews, and every issue will contain a full game translated to English. We have tons of material to show you, so this is going to be really fun! To fund all the work involved in creating this content I started a Patreon: patreon.com/encho. So if you really liked this idea, I'll be honored to have you as my patron.
