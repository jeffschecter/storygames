So, Hell for Leather is now available from IPR. I think they ship from Indianapolis... So, I guess that means cheap P&P for the North American gore hounds. If you don't know about Hell for Leather, here's the sales blurb (yawn!)...

Hell for Leather is a darkly gory game of pursuit and savage violence. On the world's bloodiest gameshow you must kill to survive...

* Actual Play: What do people do at the table?
* Examples: What are the stories like?

Hell for Leather creates anxiety using a stack of dice. To overcome challenges, players try to land a ten-sided die near a tower of six-sided dice (see video) without knocking it down!
