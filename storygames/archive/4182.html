\n Howdo
\n
\nFurnace is a small cosy con (max 70 attendees) run by Roleplayers for Roleplayers. We've got a great line up of games over the weekend, including (but not limited to);
\n
\nHeroQuest, Burning Wheel, Shadow of Yesterday. Call of Cthulhu, RuneQuest, Qin and Savage Worlds.
\n
\nAlso the UK Small Press group Collective Endeavour will be running all weekend various indie games (Cold City, Dead of Night, Contenders, A|State, Mob Justice Covenant etc).
\n
\nMore details, inc online registration at www.rpgfurnace.com

    \n You forgot to mention the best bit Newt! The con is based in an old 19th century garrison and barracks, and the games themselves take place in the old cells!

    I had a blast year, and it was very indie friendly - I think the sole game of DnD that was running felt very left out. I played a great couple of games of Burning Wheel and Burning Empires, and hope to find the time to do so again this year!
