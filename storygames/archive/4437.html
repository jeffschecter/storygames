\n
\n
\n
\n\nBen_Johnson \n\n\n
\n
\n\nOctober 2007 \n edited October 2007 in Directed Promotion
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n Dithered a bit about whether I should post this here, but what the hell. I'm proud of my work.

The game I've been working on for the past year, The Simpsons Game, is coming out this month on 360, PS3, Wii, DS and PSP. It's probably the best game I've ever been paid to work on, and easily the most fun I've ever had making a videogame. I'd like to think it shows.

There will be a demo available via Xbox Live and PS3's online service on October 8th. I did a little work on the level in the demo, but it's mostly invisible stuff that just makes the gameplay better.

My primary task was running the design on Level 6, which I won't speak in much detail about, because it's one of the plot twist levels we haven't been showing the press.

I'm really pleased with this game, and hope that any of you with an interest in videogame platformers with cooperative multiplayer will at least check out the demo, and hopefully buy a copy of the game. Preferably a new one each time you wish to play it.

There are a bunch of trailers for my game here.
