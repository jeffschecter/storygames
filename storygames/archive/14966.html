    \n It's pretty much just a D&D 4e skin over vanilla Solar System. I didn't have to rename things (Backgrounds = Keys, Feats & Powers = Secrets, Defenses = Pools, Levels = Advances, etc.), but it helped camouflage things. The check-boxes on the Abilities was mostly to make the list feel more like a 4e character sheet. The ability to choose more than one is just a coincidence.

    Secrets are always kind of fuzzy, power-wise, but yes, for the D&D-minded, spending that "level" on Speak Dwarven ought to come with a bonus die to make it feel worth the cost.

    I didn't give a whole lot of thought to the skills. Yes, I kind of assumed that everybody had the same base D&D 4e skill list (plus "melee" and "ranged"). If I really got into this there would probably be some race/class/culture -only Abilities as well.

    Unfortunately, I saved over the PSD.

    It's still "blank", but making any changes to the basic layout will be a nightmare.

    Thanks, everybody, for taking the time to look.
