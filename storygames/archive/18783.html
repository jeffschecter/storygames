Hi fellows! Here is a free pdf RPG (prototype) we're developing at Cia Imperial L\xfadica! From Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

The Early Days RPG is a card-based zombie RPG. The players adopt the role of zombie apocalypse\u2018s survivors. The game is inspired by the tales "The Early Days" and it is based on the use of cards.

The Early Days RPG was designed to simplify and assist the construction of an adventure tale, saving time of the Harvester and survivors \u2013 life is short! All the necessary information for a session are in the cards of Characters, Items and Sceneries.

Narrative Construction or How to Play: Scenerie Card + Characters Cards + Items

Game features:

* Simple and direct. About 20 to 30 minutes between reading the rules and starting the game session.
* The use of Sceneries, Characters and Items cards based on the Early Days tales and made to facilitate the game\u2019s dynamics and narrative. Also, the use of cards simplifies the release of expansions as new tales are publicized.
* Keywords: Use of Keywords to inspire the narrative.
* The Harvester dice\u2019s game mechanism use to incorporate the rising despair of zombies encounters on the apocalyptical scenery.

Please, comments and feedback are always welcome! Thanks!
