In order to prevent environmental waste this year, as well as give people a reminder of the event, we're using buttons as name badges at Camp Nerdly. I'm pants at graphic design, though, so I turn to you.

\n

LORD OWLBEAR'S BUTTON-OFF CHALLENGE

\n

Using a Photoshop template or an Illustrator PDF template, design a 2" x 3" rectangular button. Use a separate layer for the button, and post a link to the finished PSD or PDF here (or email it to Clinton at crnixon at the gmail com). The winner will win a valuable prize! It may be more valuable if you come to Nerdly, as I won't have to ship it to you.

\n

What I'm looking for: something relatively simple, even rustic. A owlbear, even (especially) crudely drawn, is a big bonus. The event's name is pretty essential, but you might have a crazy idea, so go for it. What is definitely essential is a place for an attendee to write their name with a Sharpie. If there's a bigger blank place, they could write more stuff, so leaving plenty of space is great.

\n

Deadline: Thursday, May 7, 2009
