\n Being excessively hype for Apocalypse World 2.0, I made a silly procedural-generation tool, Apocalypse Fuel, to generate concepts (and stats, if you want 'em) for NPC gangs, hardholds, cults and the like.

Here's one of the more basic outputs:\n
Lady Crud Tower is a hardhold. It's a regular size for a hardhold - perhaps 75-150 souls. It has a powerful fleet: at least 7 combat vehicles, and as many utility vehicles. It owes protection tribute to a more powerful group. The population are lazy and drug-stupored. Custom drugs may be obtained at Musk Wren's.

It is a hierarchy: social status is determined by advancement within an institution - in this case, a religious order - with a leader appointed by senior members for a fixed term. It marches under the inexperienced leadership of Tyrant Kim.

Its posse is poorly-armed, small (10-20 violent bastards) and disciplined.

Notable members:
Midge: unnaturally lust-transfixed on Dieter; smartest wayfinder you know
Luis: always horny
\nIt's meant as an MC tool for coming up with weird concepts quickly. Or just for barfing forth a whole bunch of AW-flavoured names, if that's what you need. Or if you just want to snigger at weird procedurally-generated results.

This is the first public release, so suggestions of any kind are very much welcomed.
