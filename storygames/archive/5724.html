    \n I just got mine!

    I was so excited, until I noticed the package was all mangled.

    Then I got worried. I opened it up and the book was trashed. It wasn't just crumpled, or had a ripped cover, it had water damage -- and a slight yellow stain.

    I was cursing Canada Post and the sled dogs that delivered it to me, when I noticed that Vincent had written a personal message in the book.

    It said:\n
    Brand,

    Please do not blame the post office for the condition this book arrived in. When I realized this was the book I was sending to you, I took it into my back yard and stomped on it for half an hour. I then had my dog, Joshua Newman's cat, and a passing raccoon take turns pissing on it.

    That's what you get, you mother fucker, for criticizing games in public. Know your roll, bitch, or the next copy of Dogs you order will be -really- unpleasant.
    \nSo... I'm thinking I can get some real money for this on eBay. Vincent's dog's piss? What Storygames geek doesn't want a piece of that action!
