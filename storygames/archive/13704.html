\n We are extending the schedule for our feedback and awards for the RPG Solitaire Challenge. We expected to get about 15 games, but the judges are working hard on the 33 games that were submitted.

In the interests of sanity, and also to be sure that the feedback we give to folks are considered and worthwhile, the final date that all the feedback and challenge winners will be announced is February 10, 2011. We will, however, start posting the feedback on February 1, and will post the reviews in batches throughout the week until we have all online on the 10th.

The Challenge Favorites and the Overall Favorite will be announced on February 20, 2011. The Awards, a post by the judges on each of the winning games, will be written during the rest of February and early March.

We're really enjoying reading the games! Thanks for your patience as we work through them. We look forward to getting to share our thoughts on the games. Feel free to post here if you have any questions about what is up.

All the best,
Emily
Challenge Host
