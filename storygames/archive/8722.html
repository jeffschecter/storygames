Hey all you Owlbears!

\n

This year's Swagmeister is your's truly. It's my repsonsiblity to make sure everyone has a cool momento of the weekend. In previous years that duty fell to Remi (a big round of applause if you please). Remi did an awesome job, in part, because Remi is an awesome artist. I am no artist. No one wants a blurry stick-figure to remember Camp Nerdly by.

\n

So that's where you come in!

\n

I'm calling all SG artists to submit their ideas for this year's Camp Nerdly logo. You don't have to be planning to go to Camp Nerdly and you don't have to have gone before. You just have to have some skill with line and color.

\n

This year, instead of a t-shirt, the Nerdly swag will be a sew-on patch, like this:

\n

\n

or maybe like this...

\n

\n

or possibly even like this...

\n

\n

I want you to come up with your coolest idea for a patch and email a scan of it to me (at the email addy in my profile, just click on my name). I'll post the entries here for everyone to look at. Come mid or late March, I'll get together with the organizers and we'll all agree on our favorite entry and make that into the offical Camp Nerdly patch.

\n

The winner gets a handful of patches. Five runners-up will get one patch. Everyone else gets a big hug.

\n
\n

Guidelines

\n
\n

>

\n
\n
    \n
  • The patch should be no less than 1.5" on it's smallest side and no more than 5" on it's longest side.
    • \n
  • Artwork should include the camp's mascot, the Owlbear.
    • \n
  • Scans should be as high-resolution as you can manage.
    • \n
  • Original artwork made by you only!

    Good questions.

    Those are, in fact, all the specs. The shape thing is very customizable, so that's not an issue. As for color, if you make your art work look like a patch, then that won't be an issue either. I mean, don't get too attached to your colors. You might paint aqua with a hint of cerulean but the patch will likely turn out light blue.

    \n

    Let's say... oh... March 31th as the deadline to submit something. Yeah. That'll work. I was gonna say the 18th, but then I was like; what the heck. March 31st.

    Don't sweat the details. You draw some awesome patches and I'll do the rest.
