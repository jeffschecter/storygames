\n So, this is for my game based on Castlevania. I need a term for the Big Bad, which is always going to be a malevolent supernatural entity in an unassailable position. The GM or the group as a whole make one up before play.

In my head, I've been calling it "the Dracula." That ain't gonna fly in the rulebook, so I need something else.

    But this issue of gender non-neutral terms doesn't really bother me that much.
