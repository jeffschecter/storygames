    \n By removing the hero, of course! The player is now the GM - for the monsters!

    Play starts with a "wandering adventurer" but when there is a fight, the victor is determined randomly. The player continues the adventure, GMing for whichever monster survived, and is required to justify it's continued existence within the story, which will give that monster an advantage in combat. The monster whose existence is unjustified is soon defeated! The "wandering adventurer" never gains this bonus, because murder and cruelty to animals by people is never justified.
