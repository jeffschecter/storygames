    \n I couldn't answer from question 10 and beyond. I'm one of those guys who likes it all, changes game style and groups from time to time. I sometimes prefer playing one type of games with friends and another with strangers. I'm both logical and creative, at least according to tests about which brain-half I'm using. I'm equally into competition and collaboration (but not in the same game). I play, and had played, all sorts of characters. That's one reason why I don't like character immersion anymore; it feels like I've played everything.

    The only thing I could see in common was that the characters I enjoyed playing was when I was serious, but I don't always play games for character immersion. I never do it today so I had to think ten years back at least, and I couldn't place any of those characters I really enjoyed before anyone else.

    So it was both hard and weird for me to answer.
