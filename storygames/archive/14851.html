\n just a quickie:
\n
\nI'm about to run Agon and I have 1 simple short question: how do you handle defense against multiple opponents ?
\nAt the beginning of an exchange, you choose to put dice in your left hand for defense. And you roll them when you're attacked. That's OK for a 1 to 1 confrontation.
\n
\nBut what if you fight against 2 opponents simultaneously ? You can't use the same defense roll or roll the dice each time you're attacked ! That doesn't make sense: you could easily defend against 10 ennemies at a time without any malus for facing multiple opponents.
\n
\nSo, anyone has a take on that ?
\n
\n(I also asked on RPG.net boards)
