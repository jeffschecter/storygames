\n I was so taken with Ben's description of running Paranoia using Otherkind that I had to extend on his brilliant idea using Ghost/Echo as a model:

Paranoia

Roll dice when\n
    \n
  • You act under pressure
    • \n
  • You infiltrate, hack, or steal
    • \n
  • You suffer or try to avoid injury (or just punishment by The Computer)
    • \n
  • You shoot, attack or attempt to destroy something (commie, mutant, traitor, R&D equipment, etc)
    • \n
  • You manipulate or resist someone
    • \n
  • You access information above your security clearance
    • \n
  • You use your mutant power
    • \n
  • You contact your secret society
    • \n
  • You use special equipment
    • \n
  • You use drugs
    • \n
\nWhen you roll dice\n
    \n
  • After you have identified the goal and how many dangers there are, take that number of dice.
    • \n
  • When you\u2019re especially well-prepared for the action at hand, gain a bonus die. Example include:\n
      \n
    • Appropriate Skill (e.g. Management to suck-up to briefing officer)
      • \n
    • Relevant Service Group (e.g. R&D to reprogram a bot)
      • \n
    • Special knowledge (e.g. Blackmail material on PLC clerk)
      • \n
    • Special equipment (e.g. BFG when firing on Commies)
      • \n
    • Appropriate drugs (e.g. Happy Life to pass Happiness Officer review)
      • \n
    • Secret Society aid (e.g. Calling in an IOU from Death Leopard to blow a power relay)
      • \n
    • Mutant Power (e.g. Using Adhesion to climb a sheer wall)
      • \n
    • Whenever the GM feels like it
      • \n
    \n
    • \n
  • When something seriously compromises your effectiveness, gain a penalty die. Examples include:\n
      \n
    • When injured (e.g. climbing with a broken arm)
      • \n
    • When drugged (e.g. Standing guard OD\u2019d on Sleepy-Time pills)
      • \n
    • When lacking crucial information (e.g. cutting the red wire while color blind)
      • \n
    • Whenever the GM feels like it
      • \n
    \n
    • \n
  • Bonus and penalty dice cancel each other on a one-for-one basis
    • \n
  • Roll the total number of dice
    • \n
  • Assign a die result to the goal and to each danger to find out what happens
    • \n
  • If there are any bonus dice in the pool, assign any dice of your choice first and discard the extra
    • \n
  • If there are any penalty dice in the pool, assign all the lowest dice first and discard the extra
    • \n


Goals and Dangers
Goals And Dangers are always based on the specifics of the action. You decide what your goal is.\n
    \n
  • Each action has a required danger (the default is Treason unless otherwise specified).
    • \n
  • The GM may define a second danger.
    • \n
  • You may define an additional danger and roll an additional die.
    • \n
\nWhen a danger remains, write it on a card and put it in front of you. During any future dice roll, you or the GM can add that danger to the roll (instead of the danger you would normally have the opportunity to add).

When your goal could be treasonous or is accomplished by treasonous means (e.g. use information above your security clearance, lie to Friend Computer, use a mutant power, etc), add the danger \u201cTreason.\u201d
When you use your Mutant Power, add the danger \u201cMutant Power Mishap.\u201d
When you request help from your Secret Society, add the danger \u201cSecret Society Mishap.\u201d
When you use special or experimental equipment, add the danger \u201cEquipment Mishap.\u201d
When you use drugs, add the danger \u201cMedication Mishap.\u201d

    Goal
    1: \u201cCitizen, You have been fined [INTEGER OVERFLOW] credits for damaging Computer property.\u201d
    2: \u201cPlease cease all activity until you have an approved R-U-A-T8R form. Have a nice day.\u201d
    3: \u201cI am concerned about your limited success, Citizen. Please request appropriate Service Firm assistance.\u201d
    4: \u201cYour effort seems unenthusiastic Citizen. Would you like some mood elevators before continuing?\u201d
    5: \u201cExcellent work in the service of Friend Computer\u201d
    6: \u201cSuspiciously impressive, Citizen. Are you certain you wouldn\u2019t like to register any Mutant Powers?\u201d

    Danger: General
    1: You suffer the effects of the danger, and some unexpected mishap also befalls you (and maybe bystanders)
    2: You suffer the effects of the danger
    3: You suffer partial effects of the danger, and the danger gets worse
    4: You avoid the danger for the moment, but the danger remains
    5: You avoid the danger
    6: You avoid the danger and gain some unexpected benefit, like choosing someone else to suffer its effects

    Danger: Treason
    1: \u201cBe advised that a Termination order has been issued for this Citizen. Please render assistance.\u201d
    2: \u201cCitizen, please report to the nearest Confession booth for a bonus mandatory interrogation. Thank you.\u201d
    3: Your actions draw immediate suspicion, gain 1 Treason point or suffer egregious fine
    4: Your actions draw unwanted attention
    5: No record exists of your treasonous activities
    6: Implicate someone else for your Treasonous action and given them 1 Treason point (yours or a fresh one)

    Danger: Mutant Power Mishap
    1: Your power works - too well! Power rages out of control, injuring you and possibly others, and is instantly visible to all witnesses, then shorts out until you jump start it (drink toxic waste, irradiate self, overdose, etc)
    2: Your power works in some useless but visible way and refuses to shut off until you are injured
    3: Your power fails to work, but maybe it just needs some brisk exercise or some quiet time alone
    4: You power works in a partially effective or limited way
    5: Your power works
    6: Your power works better, longer or more effectively than normal - but roll again when you try to shut it off

    Danger: Secret Society Mishap
    1: Your request is denied and for mysterious reasons your own or an enemy society has marked you for death
    2: Your request is ignored, forgotten, or denied and your IOU is wasted
    3: Your request is denied, but you may try to cash your IOU again in the future
    4: Your request is addressed in a sullen, incomplete, slow, blindly literal, or haphazard fashion
    5: Your request is fulfilled
    6: Your request is fulfilled with suspicious speed and efficacy as it randomly aligns with society goals.

    Danger: Equipment Mishap
    1: \u201cActivating anti-communist self-destruct failsafe in three...two...Have a nice day, Citizen\u201d
    2: \u201cTerminal antivirus failure. Shutdown in progress...Hear Oh Alpha Complex. The Computer is Our Friend!\u201d
    3: \u201cLow battery. Please re-charge me at a Qik-N-Go charge station for only [INTEGER OVERFLOW] credits per minute\u201d
    4: Equipment functions in completely bizarre but partially effective way. Maybe it\u2019s the other button.
    5: Equipment works as designed (note, this is not necessarily as expected)
    6: Equipment works in miraculously effective fashion, exciting enviously predatory glances from all bystanders.

    Danger: Medication Mishap
    1: Bad batch - drug causes violent allergic reaction, hallucinations, tremors, blindness, itching, or all of the above
    2: Past expiration - drugs causes only intestinal distress and halitosis
    3: Slow burn - drug does nothing until random event triggers its effects (usually at an inconvenient time)
    4: Weak sauce - drug seems to work at first but its effect fades rapidly. Perhaps another dose will help?
    5: Drug works as designed (note, this is not necessarily as expected)
    6: Drug works amazingly well with pronounced effects that exceed the norm. Immediately addictive.

    Creating Troubleshooters
    Choose one skill to receive a bonus die to all actions. Choose a different skill to receive a penalty die to all actions. Then for each remaining skill choose a two different specialties, one to receive a bonus die and one to receive a penalty die. Note everyone receives a bonus die for the Violence specialty Energy & Projectile Weapons by default (but you may still choose it to receive a further penalty die or bonus die, canceling or doubling its value; it also adds or cancels if the Violence skill is chosen to always receive a bonus or penalty die).

    I'm not going to post any more details on Troubleshooter creation (skills, mutations, secret societies, service groups, etc) because those details are easily available in any of the legacy or current editions of Paranoia.

    Other types of Danger it might be fun to create lists for
    Bureaucracy
    PLC Request
    R&D or Service Services
    IntSec Interrogation
    Vulture Warriors
    Accuse Traitor / Report Treason
    Hygiene
    Happiness

    I'm planning on running this soon but I hope it proves useful to others as well.
