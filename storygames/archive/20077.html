\n Hello Everyone,

For the past year or so I have made a number of small print and play game and now I am try my hand at creating my first printed game. This game Started out as an Entry for National Game Design Month (NaGaDeMon) but since it's release it has undergone a numerous reworks in order to make the game really shine.

Trials of the Magi is a simple, tactile RPG designed for introducing the hobby of story-games to new players.

If you are interest you can find more information about the game at the following LINK

This will be my first time running a Kickstarter campaign and I would appreciate your support and feedback!

Thanks a lot! Without the great people on this forum, Trials of the Magi never would have made it this far.

-Patrick Lapienis
