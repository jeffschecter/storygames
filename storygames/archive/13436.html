\n I thought I would start a new thread.

Background: Since a couple of weeks ago, I've been running an experiment where I let people pay whatever they want for Polaris and Bliss Stage, including $0, in the style of Radiohead or 2-d Boy. I had said I would try it until the end of the month, and then see where we stood.

So, in all, I've pulled in just under $500 with this experiment so far, including one very generous donation of $100. But even discounting the philanthropist, this is a pretty amazing intake, about 5x what I make in an ordinary month (and this was only about two weeks!) In terms of volume, I've moved well more than 5x my usual (I've moved somewhere in the realm of 70-80 copies). In terms of engagement, this deal has resulted in several play sessions that I know about, a few AP reports, and so on. Basically, it's been a huge success on every metric.

I will definitely be continuing this into December. I guess if anyone is looking for a cheap Christmas* gift, you could put some PDFs onto a flash drive to give to a gamer friend.

I will re-assess again at the New Year, but I think that I will be keeping this style of pricing as a semi-permanent business model.

Questions? Comments?
