On November 1st at 9:00PM or thereabouts, we made a move from one server to another, to test out... well... what a new server would be like.

Here are some known issues:

1) The Codex (WIKI) has not been moved yet. That will be done on Sat/Sun.

2) UTF-8 Migration was entirely unsuccessful. That means that anything that was ever posted to this board in native characters in Japanese, Chinese, Russian, etc is busted. Too bad. Next time a migration occurs, I'll spend more time investigating how to make that not happen.
Tangentially, this means that if your user name had some UTF-8 characters in it (like umlauts, etc) it will be busted. Please email me and I'll fix that.

Other than the above, things should be ok. If you can't seem to log in or are having other issues, please email me at my gmail.com address, "storygames".

Please post in this thread your experiences with the forum over the next few days. Namely, huge amounts of slowness. We're on a grid, so as traffic increases the server will adjust and hopefully move/migrate. Which basically means you might see a lag here or there for up to 2 hours, then adjustment, then another lag, then adjustment, until it settles in 2 days or so.

Next week we'll hammer the server through our normal activity and see how much it can take.

Thanks for your time, everyone!

Also: There may be some slowness with New Members applying for the forum in the next few days, until all the nameserver issues are resolved.

-Andy
