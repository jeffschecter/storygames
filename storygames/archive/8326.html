    \n Dude - Beer is in the same place Carbon used to be.

    That means that instead of organic molecules having Carbon rings, they now have Beer rings. Beer rings where any unlabeled node is assumed to have Bacon attached.

    I also noticed that the highly-polar Lithium Fluoride gets replaced with Batman Piratate. Common table salt is now Chuck Norris with Zombies.

    Do not mess with Baco-Zombic acid.

    I'm still stumped as to the trend in column 13, though. This table needs more vertical trends.

    No, I haven't been re-learning chemistry so that I can teach it this year, why do you ask?
