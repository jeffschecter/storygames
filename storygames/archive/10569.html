\n ...or, "Der Wille Zur Spiel!" In Portland, Oregon.

(cross-posted to goplaypdx, in case anyone is here who isn't also there.)

Here's what's going down: I want a weekly game. Bad. So I'm going to hustle this thing and see if something doesn't happen.

This is going to happen on Tuesday nights, at 6:30pm. We'll play at my house (details on the place to come later, in a personal message or email), unless someone else wants to volunteer to host.

What we'll play: most anything. I've never had a good experience with a traditional game, so I'm open but unexcited about that option.

Ideally, if we start playing a game I'd like to play it for at least 3 sessions (barring games that are just good one-shots). I'm open to rotating games a lot, with a few sessions of each, or latching onto one and grooving it into longer play.
