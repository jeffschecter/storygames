    I've been interested in checking 13th Age after all I've read but I've always been confused about one thing. Why are they funding and releasing a supplement before their core rule book has been put out?
    \nThe supplement will not be released until after the core book is out. Funding the supplement allows the original team to stay together. Rob has been working solidly on the final draft of the core book and we hope to have it out in December.

    The Kickstarter ended with both of the Robin Laws goals met, so it will be quite some supplement.
