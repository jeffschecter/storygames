\n We start our interlude into Inspectres tonight. We had intended to jump into Torchbearer. But Jeff is running the game and had some conflicts so Todd is running a few sessions of Inspectres. This is a Ghostbusters inspired game where the players form a paranormal investigations franchise. We figure up characters pretty quick and then launch into franchise building. This includes coming up with tech and vehicles and all sorts of stuff.
