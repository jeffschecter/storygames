\n Hello there! As Khimus commented here, the first Runica Publishing games are now available. Among them you will be able to find Nanodungeon, a nanogame that I wrote and I would like to share with you all.

But, what is Nanodungeon about?

It is a free nanogame which attempts to reduce the dungeon-crawl experience to its bare components. You will find in it random setting and event generation, typical character classes like warrior, thief and magic user, and an archenemy you will help define.
But then again, it's a Story Game! The game does not require GM, does not have a traditional character creation, and it's mechanics focus more on the party's internal conflicts than on individual actions. It is not about telling if the warrior manages to defeat the orcs in combat: it is about defining if the other characters sacrifice themselves to help him or if they betray him to benefit.
Anyway, that is a brief summary of the game, which will be available to download here.

So, what do you think? We would really welcome your feedback, and I hope you enjoy your next Nanodungeon session!

    \n Thanks for your help @Paul_T! I will answer your questions in order:

    1) It takes approximately 20 minutes per player, with scenes of about two and a half minutes. On the number of players: it has a minimum of three participants (to cover three types of character), but it doesn't necessarily have a limit. I have played it with six people, and it worked fine.

    2) The pieces used in the final battle are only pieces of equipment. And every time you bid equipment pieces, those pieces will be consumed during the turn of the fight against the Necromancer. If you find a better wording for this, we'd love to read it!

    3) In case of the death of one of the characters, you have to add one to the total required. If defeating your enemy took you 9 equipment pieces, it will now take 10. If it took 12, it will now take 13. And so on.

    Finally, proofreaders are always welcome in Runica Publishing. If you want to help us and join our team, please du contact us.
