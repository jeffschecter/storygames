\n I know there are some New Englanders kicking around this forum, so this could be of interest to some of you.

The Gamer Maker's Guild is starting an RPG playtest meetup in the Boston area. The first one is May 10th (not coincidentally the same day Game Chef launches). It is co-organized by myself and Meg of Brilliant Gameologists. Designers and playtesters are extremely welcome. We'll be focusing on new RPG systems developed by members. The plan is to run "scenes" in as many of the alpha game systems as possible to get the games in shape for an open beta (PDF) in the near future.

It costs nothing to attend, and nothing to become a member of the GMG. Hope to see some of you there!
