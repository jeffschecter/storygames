\n Episode 8 Fighting for What We Believe http://apap.libsyn.com/
\n
\nThis week we are joined by Christopher Kubasik and Patrick Pilati, so we have the whole Burning Empires crew together to discuss the game. We talk about the Artha cycle and the role beliefs play in Burning Empires, the need to engage the game as a game i.e. playing to win, and Jesse's experience running other games after having spent time running Burning Empires. This episode is a little longer than normal. We had a lot to say on these topics.
