\n I'm looking for an ecommerce solution that:
\n
\n- handles both digital download and physical product sales
\n- integrates with a payment processor that can handle US, UK, Canadian and other currencies as well as paypal
\n- runs on PHP or JSP
\n- supports multiple custom front end designs
\n- supports multiple stores that share the same inventory
\n- allows you to categorize your products and tag them any way you want
\n- allows you to upload multiple photos of the same product and display some sort of photo gallery
\n
\nThis seems like an ok solution:
\n
\n- Software: http://www.ecommercetemplates.com/
\n- Features: http://www.ecommercetemplates.com/help/ecommplus/about.asp
\n- Cost: $115 to $200 depending on template chosen
\n- PHP and ASP versions
\n- Digital Download plugin ($30): http://www.ecommercetemplates.com/help/digital-download.asp
\n- Works with many payment providers, multiple currencies (including pound), paypal, google checkout, and more: http://www.ecommercetemplates.com/payment_processors.asp
\n
\nThe above makes me nervous because of the lack of examples and lack of activity on their forums.
\n
\nAny suggestions?
