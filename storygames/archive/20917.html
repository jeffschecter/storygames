    \n It's a silly game, but I am 100% serious about playing it and running it in the near future.

    It came out of @Deliverator and I talking about a D&D pitch I'd had kicking around for a while (roughly "Entourage with the meme-ified hard partying version of Thranduil from the Hobbit movies"). We decided it would work better as a one-off or highly episodic thing than an ongoing game and that a Lasers & Feelings hack could nail the right feel.
