\n Full disclosure: I haven't been on the Oracle generator in close to a year. I found it after playing IAWA, and I found myself adding to and creating Noir and the Western oracle.

But wow, this page has blown up since then. There are literally hundreds of different oracles there now, all user-created!

Sure, there are the obligatory ones like the Jason Morningstar Game Generator and the Mr. T Facts oracle.

But also some nice gems like Police Dispatches and the Diaspora (nee Traveller) mission generator.

Any favorites that you've been using (or want to use)?
