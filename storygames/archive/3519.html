\n Calling all podcasters: I'm looking for one of our esteemed voices to attend and cover my Dawning Star VIP Game to benefit the Shriners Hospitals for Children. The event is being held at the Alcatraz Brewing Company at 7:00 PM on Friday, August 17th at Gen Con. You'll get free entrance to the event and all of the other goodies we're giving to other attendees (free food and drink, free limited edition publication, etc.). You'll also get to cover this unique event, record audio, whatever.

If you're interested, drop me a line at justin[at]bluedevilgames[dt]com. Space is limited; please let me know as soon as possible. (For scheduling purposes, it is the same time as the ENnies awards ceremony.)
