\n Of course, it's months away.

Of course, it will be here before we know it.

The love fest continues, but while it's fresh in our mind, anyone have any observations, ideas, and so forth on the RPG / LARP end of things?

Here's my first note:

I know the con is moving to a new hotel. However, one thing I'll miss is the lobby. The reason is this: It was open and easily accessible. It was the kind of place people would want to gather when they didn't know where to go next... because other people were there.

This is as opposed to, say, a suite that's been set up as the lounge, because a suite that's been set up as a lounge is -- well, exactly like where we've all just been for four hours. Whereas the lobby at the Westin was anything but like a hotel room. It was a break.

I know that the Radison lobby is one story high, not as big, and accessible only by elevator. It doesn't have any of that, "Let me take a peek over the railing and see if anyone is down there charm."

I also know it has that cool bar with the 360 degree windows -- for example. Which might serve great for Larps, but also might serve well as the place people gather up.

Gather up. That the term I'm looking for. So, I know the convention planning people are only just starting to deal with the new hotel (if you've ever started at all!), but if you could keep an eye out for a great, intuitive attractive in some way place for people to Gather Up, that'd be great.

CK
