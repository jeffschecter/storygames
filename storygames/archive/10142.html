\nRed Moon Medicine Show (formerly KNRPG Productions) is proud to announce the MECHA Quickstart Guide, a free PDF preview of MECHA, our upcoming role-playing game of giant robot anime.

We'd love to hear your feedback! We'll be using feedback from the Quickstart to help improve and refine our final game text.

P.S. Speaking of refinement, if you nabbed a print copy of the Quickstart from the Design Matters booth at Gen Con, we've got a small but important piece of errata for you.
