OK, the deadline has passed and we've got an extraordinary collection of high quality name and resource lists. In addition, we've got a number of ads that will be placed in the manuscript, most of them accompanying relevant lists.

At this point, unless we've chatted, submissions are closed. Thanks again to everybody who contributed.
