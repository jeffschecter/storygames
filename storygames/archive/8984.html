    \n Many years ago, I was GMing a game with a single player. His character encountered an owlbear, which crushed him to death - a totally pointless end which I imagine would not happen in a story game. Neither of us wanted it to happen, but we looked aghast as his character failed in the futile struggle to break free of its grip. His brother (a remarkably similar character) swore vengeance on all owlbears. If it happened today, I'd hand him the stats for the owlbear, and start an owlbear based game of bloodlust, hooting and honey - bears who hug too much.
