    \n When I was at Whitman ('05-'09), there were plenty of story gamers (and rpgers) in general, but they didn't network too well (or necessarily broadcast their gaming preferences). If you want to start networking to meet non-college folks and find some games, drop a note by Book and Game Company downtown (they are very gamer friendly! Learn to love them...) and think about dropping by the Magic: The Gathering meetup on Whitman's campus-- you'll find lots of college-age gamers there, many of whom also play pen and paper rpgs. Good luck! And if you need any other WW advice, don't hesitate to ask.
