Agon: Ancient Greek RPG is on sale now!

John_Harper
August 2006 edited August 2006 in Directed Promotion
My new game of ancient Greek heroes, Agon, is on sale now in PDF form. Check it out at the Agon website where you can also download a 29-page PDF sample.

In brief: The game is competitive, in the style of Rune or Capes. It features a fast and intuitive battle system, a solid challenge-creation system, and support for short- and long-term play.

I'll also have printed books at GenCon, but they'll go fast. Come by the Forge booth early if you want to snag one.
