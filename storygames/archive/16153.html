    \n The script of MW was on the game's website as far as i remember, and it's not part of the game (the site seems to be down but I've got a copy...)

    It's 5 pages and begin like this:


    Script: Intro



    GM: \u201cThis here is THE MOUNTAIN WITCH, which could be
    described as 'AKIRA KUROSAWA meets RESERVOIR
    DOGS' the RPG . You each play a dirty, grubby, lowlife
    ronin who---when hard-up for cash---is hired to kill the
    mythical Mountain Witch of Mt Fuji.



    Script: Scene 1: Wolf Attack
    GM: \u201cThe adventure usually begins at the bottom of Mt Fuji as
    you slowly start your climb up to the Witch's castle. Let's say
    of the mountain. But you're not alone. There are other
    creatures---servants of the Witch maybe---that roam these
    woods.\u201d

    GM: \u201cI'll also tell you that these creatures are looking for you.
    Let's roll to see if they find you, or if you find them first.

    We all roll together, you all against me.


    GM: \u201cWe now take the highest die that you guys rolled, which
    is a {whatever} , and compare it to my roll,
    which is a {whatever} . Since {my/your}
    roll was higher, {I/you guys} win.\u201d

    -----------------
    I know pretty well APFMT as we've translated it. It's a good (the best?) exemple of what can be achieved with a "script" method. I had it in mind

    IRegarding Silver and white, i didn't know about it, and i'd be very interested to have a eye on it...
