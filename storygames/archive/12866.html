    \n Agreed 14thWarrior.

    There are many box sets, even gorgeous ones, that come with substandard components inside or don't come with everything you need to play right away. And even if the physical components are amazing, it doesn't mean the game is great or has replay value. And even if the game is great, doesn't mean it is for everyone. Without knowing the details, it's really hard to judge value.

    I'm 100% biased as I helped playtest the game and am friends with Jared and Luke. So I wanted to show what the game comes with so people can judge for themselves. And share my excitement!
