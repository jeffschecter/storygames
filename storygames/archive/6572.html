1 GM, 1 Player, No Prep. Does this RPG exist?

\n
\n
\n
\n\njessecoombs \n\n\n
\n
\n\nMay 2008 \n edited May 2008 in Story Games
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n I'm really digging the no-prep games like Primetime Adventures, In a Wicked Age, and maybe Mortal Coil. Is there anything out there for just two people? Schedules can be a bitch and sometimes stuff happens, but I still want to play something. The game that I can think of that would work the best for me would be Don't Rest Your Head, as there seems to be no NPCs to stat up really, aside from pain dice. Plus the setting is fairly inherent and flexible. As awesome as Beast Hunters seems, the setting isn't really my thing, and it looks like there is some prep time needed.

...Or should I start designing my own game?
