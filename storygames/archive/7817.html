    \nFREUDIAN CLASH

    A scenario for two groups of players. The scenario cuts between scenes, alternating between the two groups. Group one is attempting to find an entrance to a cave, opening the entrance, going in to find a magical treasure that will bring personal bliss. The other group starts deep inside a cave, tries to find their way out, experiences pain and struggle in the narrow passage. It becomes more and more clear with each scene that the scenario is about thinly-veiled Freudian symbolism; the first group is (metaphorically) trying to have penetrative sex with a woman, the second group is actually experiencing birth trauma.

    When the groups finally meet, they fight in a Freudian way.
