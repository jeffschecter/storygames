\n Hullo, Story Games. Like so many other folks seem to be doing these days, I'm writing a hack for Apocalypse World. I figured you guys might know a thing or two about that.

When Greg Stolze started a thread on RPGnet about the possibility of a 2015 reboot for Unknown Armies, a few people said "hey Greg, you should make it into an Apocalypse World hack." And at first I laughed. But then I realised, if there was an AW hack that could capture the feel of UA - the obsession, and the panic, and the occult bullshit - I'd play the hell out of it.

And then, I started tinkering with the idea, and with the help of some clever friends, it's all falling into place. It hasn't been playtested at all, but here's the RPGnet thread where I've posted the moves and playbooks.

If you've got any feedback, advice, or contributions, I'd love to hear 'em!
