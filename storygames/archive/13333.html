\n I decided not to be subtle with the title. And I decided to start a thread to address a specific issue and thing for me. At the moment I'm in a bit of a gaming desert. Actually that's not true, I've never had a really gaming-rich period yet. I grew up in a small town, moved to a slightly bigger town where it was hard to get a regular group, and now I'm living back in the original small town. I'm planning on trying to get some one-on-one gaming going with my Fiancee, and then there's my friend who's tangentially interested, but it's not enough.

I considered playing some games in the play-by-post or play-by-email format. I still might, but to be honest I dislike the lack of immediacy and I find it hard to get into them. I don't know if I have the attention span. What that leaves is Skype gaming, which is the next best thing to being there in person. There are still troubles with this though, which mostly come down to timing. I live in Australia and I'm at GMT+10, which means the time zones don't match up very well with most of you. The other thing is that I work a weird roster, where half the time I'm away from town. So scheduling something as the same time every week just doesn't work. Hassles, basically. There are some images of my roster on my tumblr which is right here.

The games I have and am currently interested in playing:
- Polaris
- carry
- shock:
- Mouse Guard
- Burning Wheel
- Fiasco
- Dust Devils
- Ganakagok
- Apocalypse World
- In a Wicked Age
- Archipelago II
- Dogs in the Vineyard
- Panty Explosion
- 3:16
- Dungeonslayers
- Reign
- Eclipse Phase
- Unknown Armies
- Mutant City Blues
- Rifts
- Changeling: the Lost
- Werewolf: the Forsaken
- Wraith: the Oblivion
- Demon: the Fallen

Now just so I don't feel entirely selfish, if anybody else has a schedule that doesn't match up with mine but still wants to get some Skype games going, feel free to use this as a recruiting thread and post what your times are and what you're interested in playing.
