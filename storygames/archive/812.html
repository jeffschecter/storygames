    Brennan, what art instructions did you give Jennifer, I'm sure others are also curious.
  • Brennan_Taylor
    June 2006
    I asked her to create art in the style of Art Nouveau, and gave her a list of basic concepts in one or two sentences. We went through the list together and picked the seven ideas that we both like best. She then started on the pencils, which I would approve or ask for changes in, then proceed to color proofs for a second round of approval, then the final piece.

    I tend to prefer light direction, and I changed as little as possible, but there were times when I made some major changes to the pencil sketches. On one of the last pieces, we made had a major change. We realized that the tone of the book was getting too dark and we switched to a different concept that was a little less horrifying.
