\n First off, I\u2019m terribly sorry for such a long delay, a year in fact, but unfortunately life and its many obstacles take precedence over RPG\u2019s. Currently, Empire of Dust is out of print; however I am pleased to announce I will be re-releasing it at Gen-Con 2009 exclusively at the Design Matters booth. I\u2019ve set up a blog specifically for the status of EoD and other games in the works (namely Paralysis).
\n
\namy m. garcia art
\n
\nAgain, I am very pleased to hear that there is still an interest in the game and want to work towards making sure that everybody is taken care of. If you are interested in purchasing a copy of EoD, I would be happy to put you on a pre-order list. When the game becomes available a copy will be held specifically for you. Feel free to email me with any requests or questions. You can get into contact with me through my email address: Amymichellegarcia@gmail.com
\n
\nAmy M. Garcia
