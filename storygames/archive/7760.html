    \n Most French games do tend to be more traditional than other indie gamesbut Charognards has some interesting quirks in the mechanics. Stardrakkar is possibly more what you're after.

    The backpack system works pretty well. You need to be very careful with what you carry but the key ingredient is probably the danger dice. It's hard to get very high success without using them but if you do you leave yourself open to greater consequences if you fail. Admittedly this mostly applies in combat.
