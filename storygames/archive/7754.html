I saw that there are a few Brazilians in this community, so perhaps I'm not the only one interested in promoting story games (and indie games) in Brazil. I'm thinking about creating a online group (to gather all of us) and starting an organized effort to promote these games (e.g. GM/facilitate sessions on conventions and other events). I intend to do this anyway, but i would be much more effective (and fun) to have more people doing this.

\n

Also, if there's sufficient people in the same area, it would be great to have a group to meet from time to time, even do some mini-cons or just get together to talk, play or drink. BTW I'm from S\xe3o Paulo.
