\n March 22-30, 2008

www.iconvention.org

This is the second year of iCon, a Virtual Tabletop convention held by the guys at www.fouruglymonsters.com.

There are a few Story Game evangelists on fouruglymonsters.com and I played a game of Inspectres with three of the regulars at the site this week. It was their first Story Game and they LOVED it.

Guys and gals, this is a great opportunity to demo your game or games you love to an audience that's interested but never really been exposed. I've already got several of them interested in the Lacuna game I'm going to run during the con. Waldo Pepper is preaching collaborative RPGs to them and they are intrigued.

Several for-pay Virtual Tabletops will be running demos or have free licenses during the con. The con, by the way... is free.

    \n Yeah, there is no 'formal' registration per se. If you are registered on the FUM forums, you are set for the games, but you do not have to, you can contact the GMs running the games and sign up for their games, or participate in the chat (and possible round table discussions) all without formally registering to attend. Our goal really is to get this information out to the public on a wider basis than it is now. Too many people are still using AIM, IRC, or even old versions of OpenRPG to run their games, and to be frank, when you see what some of these virtual tabletops can do, you will be surprised, I promise.
    \n
    \nRyan, if you could send your PM to me (snikle on FUM) I will get you set up later today if it has not already been added. We do ask that all GMs register on the forums because we communicate with them regarding set up and scheduling (PMs are easier with the forum).
    \n
    \nTo check all games that are planned right now: http://iconvention.org/schedule.html (just skip to March on the calendar).
