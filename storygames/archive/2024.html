Credit to Mark W and Joshua BishopRoby: something that emerged from the "De-Geeking Gaming" thread-- the idea of a collection of a couple of ready-to-play PTA series: the premise of the show; characters with issues, edges, connections; maybe a sketchy outline of where people saw the series going (with alternatives) so that if people wanted to jump in at a specific episode, they could. (Maybe that's too deterministic.)

Jon ran a great game for a few episodes set in Serbia, weeks before the eruption of civil war. The premise of the show was, if I recall, how a community can get ripped apart for all the right reasons pulling in all the wrong directions.

The characters, as I remember 'em:
* Ludmilla, a working mother; issue: suffering
* Danijl, an underworld fixer and connections guy; issue: self-respect
* Alexei, a firebrand university student; issue: humanity
* Statia, a war heroine turned chief of police; issue: responsibility

There were some great secondary characters, too, like Statia's sadistic, ambitious lieutenant; Danijl's latest client, a clueless American businessman ("It's so great to see people discussing ethnic identity so passionately!"); Alexei's hero-worshipping deputy, Ludmilla's son; the elderly, dimwitted widower next door who keeps trying to propose to Ludmilla...

I think the flow of the series was that we see increasing economic unrest, leading to political disruptions, leading to reprisals and counter-reprisals, until all hell breaks out in the fifth and final episode.

Who else has good stuff for PTA? There was an awesome Farscape-y thing that Shreyas, Jonathan Walton, and Thomas put together one time... and I'm a big fan of Deep Blue and Moose in the City...

    As Mark W says. Something like:

    Secret Supernatural Investigations!

    The protagonists are aware of supernatural threats in the world but no one else is. They've banded together to stop the supernatural from victimizing the whole world. No one believes them (they may be kids), but they save the world every week.

    Characters
    The Skeptic
    Issues: Skepticism (duh), Faith, Fear, Uncertainty, Science
    Edges: Faith, Reason, Parent, Know-it-All
    Connections: Police, Teachers, Politicians, Rich Parents

    The Baby Witch
    Issues: Self-Esteem, Addiction, Recklessness, Secret Crush
    Edges: Magic (duh), Occult Lore, Telekinesis (or other simple power)
    Connections: Familiars, Demons, Magical Mentors

    And so on.
