=================cross post===================

Meet other local Roleplayers for adventures and fun! Come to a Meetup to explore fantastic worlds, clash with dark monsters, spin lore and commit heroic feats while playing the greatest small press RPGs around! I've set up a page to facilitate getting together as an Indie Games Group/Club/etc. http://roleplayers.meetup.com/481/ This is on the third Saturday of the month at 5pm so this will not conflict with the major holidays. We will be meeting at the Joe Muggs Newstand/Coffee shop at the Summit with options to game at length at Legion Comics/Game store or at a meeting room nearby.

If your coming please join the Meetups and RSVP.
