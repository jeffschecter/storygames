\n On Bastille day we (the VSCA) released Hollowpoint. Yay us! Thank you, thank you. Now as soon as we released I had a number of people ask me about downloads and my question back was, "like what?" Of course the answer was character sheets. Hollowpoint is far too austere a game for character sheets though -- part of the premise is that life is cheap and you might reasonably make up two or three characters in an evening, so they are easy to create and therefore their representation is very simple. My own character card, for example, assumes an index card:



Now it's pretty obvious that if you erase all the character specific stuff and leav just the guides and hints for a blank card, you have practically nothing. No character sheet necessary.

So a fan comes along and does this and blows my tiny mind:



Toe tags for character sheets.

Anyway that's my long way around announcing that Hollowpoint is here. Get it before it gets you. Also, talk to me about how awesome fans are.
