    \n
    \n For those who have not been to Dreamation before, here are the basic details:
    \n
    \nhttp://www.dexposure.com/d2009.html
    \nThursday, February 19 - Sunday, February 22, 2009
    \nEvent schedule will be posted in January, 2009.
    \n
    \nMorristown Hyatt Hotel
    \nhttp://morristown.hyatt.com/hyatt/hotels/
    \n3 Speedwell Ave, Morristown, NJ 07960
    \nHotel directions.
    \n
    \nPre-registration is $45 for the entire convention.
    \nFree Parking for hotel guests
    \nHotel Reservations are around $109 a night.
    \n
    \nThere is a train via NJ Transit on the Morristown Line 2 blocks away.
    \nIt's about an hour trip from Penn Station in NYC.
    \n
    \nKevin Allen Jr. says the location rocks.
    \n
    \nI would imagine that overall attendance will be down due to the change of location, date, the economy, and the Wicked Faire going on at the same time. But I suspect the local RPG community, especially the indie / small press crowd will have a fairly strong representation. And the new hotel looks great!
    \n
    \nAnd there is always lots of socializing to look forward to (especially if you work Gencon and don't have time to hangout there), playtesting of new games (it often feels like a preview of what will be out for Origins and Gencon), you can check out Jared and Luke's new game Free Market, and as always, there is the infinitely helpful indie small press game design round table.
    \n
    \nI can't wait!

    \n
    \n I can't speak to Newark. But from JFK you are looking at 2 hours with public transportation.
    \n
    \n1. Take the Light Rail / Sky Train from JFK to Jamaica. Leaves every 5 minutes. 15 minute ride. Around $5.
    \n
    \n2. Get off at Jamaica Station / Sutphin Boulevard.
    \n
    \n3. Buy a ticket for the Long Island Rail Road from Jamaica to Penn Station. It's $7.25 peak (business rush hours, weekday trains arriving in NYC between 6 and 10 AM or depart NYC between 4 and 8 PM) or $5.25 off-peak. Buy your ticket before you get on the train! If you buy your ticket on the Long Island Rail Road, it will cost you double.
    \n
    \n4. Take the Long Island Rail Road to New York Penn Station. Leaves every 20-40 minutes. 18 minute ride. You will actually pass by my apartment! I'm off the Forest Hills stop!
    \n
    \n5. Get off at New York Penn Station. It's the last stop on the train. You will actually be near my job! I'm 3 blocks from Penn Station!
    \n
    \n6. Buy a ticket for NJ Transit from New Your Penn Station to Morristown Line towards Dover. I'm not sure how much it costs.
    \n
    \n7. Take the NJ Transit to Morristown. Leaves every 20-40 minutes. 52 minute ride.
    \n
    \n8. Get off at Morristown.
    \n
    \n9. Walk to 3 Speedwell Ave, Morristown, NJ 07960. 10 minutes.
    \n- Head northwest on Lackawanna Pl toward Lafayette Ave/New Morris Ave - 325 ft
    \n- Turn left at Lafayette Ave/New Morris Ave - 384 ft
    \n- Turn right at Morris St/RT-24\t- 0.1 mi
    \n- Slight left to stay on Morris St/RT-24 - 0.1 mi
    \n- Turn right at E Park Pl/RT-24/RT-53/US-202 - 184 ft
    \n- Slight right to stay on E Park Pl/RT-24/RT-53/US-202
