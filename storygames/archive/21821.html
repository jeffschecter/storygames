[Kickstarter] Itras By: German edition (weird, rules-light, small-press)

olepeder
October 2018 in Directed Promotion
Vagrant Workshop is kickstarting the German edition of Itras By.

At the time of writing, we’re entering the final 48 hours. The book is fully funded, most stretch goals unlocked.

It’s a rules-light game with a surreal-light setting.

I’m the co-author, together with Martin Bull Gudmundsen.

Originally a self-published Norwegian game, it has previously been translated to English, Finnish, Catalan and French. See itrasby.com for extracts, reviews etc.

I’ll be around to answer any questions you might have.

Best,
Ole Peder
