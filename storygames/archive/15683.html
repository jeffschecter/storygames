\n The weekend just past was Kapcon, New Zealand's biggest RPG con, which includes a non-trival Games on Demand presence which attracted 25-30 players in every session I attended (three slots on Sunday). There were a lot of awesome games on display including Monster of the Week, Dungeon World, 3:16, Fiasco, Freemarket, Monsterhearts, Microscope and more that I didn't see, many run and played in by Story-Gamers.

I ran Dungeon World three times, played in it once, and ran the first playtest of my own hack, Cyberworld and my reports are here: Day 1 and Day 2.

If you're contemplating a trip to NZ in the future, leave the northern winter behind and come to Wellington in January!
