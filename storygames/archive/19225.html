\n Hello, everyone.

My name is Igor, and I am the translator for Monsterhearts, Dolls and other games here in Brazil. I also was the coordinator of Game Chef Brazil, which elected Pulse, and it turned out to win the Game Chef World.

As some of you may know, or imagine, Brazilian game designers have been through a thriving process: lots of ideas happening, not many of them getting published or turning into products. There are several initiatives and many people excited to produce, many more to consume, new ways of playing. And I happen to be in touch with several of them.

It occurred to me that game designers would benefit enormously from English-translated version of their work, and discussing with some friend, the idea of a Patreon for translating all these cool mini-games could help other people to come into contact with a glimpse of the kind of work it's been done here and help them release other projects in the future.

What do you guys think? Would this be an interesting project? Would you pay for something like that? How much?
