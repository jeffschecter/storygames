\n Hey all, I've been following this site for a few weeks now.

So if you know me, you know I love RPGNet. Still do! But while I use them to answer some questions where I want lots of feedback, I can expect a bunch of crap as well simply from saturation of people.

Basically, TheRPGHaven is pretty much like RPGNet, but with a much smaller crowd. There's fewer members, no fighting, pretty much straight, generalized "chat about games while you're at work" talk with a smaller pool of people.

Anyway, I've been digging it a lot. It's a great place to talk about all sorts of games. Sure, it's going to be more trad-games focused, but if that's your thing, it's something worth looking into. Check it out.

-Andy
