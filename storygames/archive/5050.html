\n So I wanna make a relationship map of game design influences. This is inevitably going to end up indicating favoritism and such and being a big mess of egos and hurt feelings, but I think it would also be really helpful at getting the lay of the land as far as design influences go. So... try to tuck your ego and your feelings into your pocket and...

For each game you have in a published or finished form (your call), list the games (in no particular order) which you feel have had significant influence on it. For most folks, these would be the games you credit somewhere in the text.

With the results, I'm going to try to create some kind of map that shows how each game is related to every other game. Anybody with great ideas or software for doing this, thoughts on that would be great too.

Crediting unfinished or unpublished works is fine, but don't just credit "this thing Ben Lehman said one time." I can't really graph that. And try not to have more than 5 credits per game, though do it if you have to.
