\n Really, Tycho being an old tabletop grognard makes Penny Arcade so much more appealing to me. This and this three-frame strip sums up so much.

What's especially intriguing is that in my head, Gabe takes on the role of both D&D-style "rules lawyer" (I wrote that in the most self-deprecating, sarcastic tone possible, if you couldn't tell) and hardcore "System Matters" Forgist:

Player: Hey, heavy-handed DM dude, don't you go getting your freeform narration bullshit all over my game mechanics!
DM: Um, I'm just trying to make this exciting for you... asshole...

Thought provoking stuff.
