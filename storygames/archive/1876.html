What's up with the Story Games Name Project?

Jason_Morningstar
November 2006 edited November 2006 in Directed Promotion
Hereafter referred to as the SGNP.

It's been a long time coming but it is going to be well worth the wait. We have fabulous art, including a beautiful full color cover by Leo Lingas. We have lists - I lost track after 100. We have an introduction by a gaming industry dude you know and love. Layout is the big holdup at this point, and Joshua BishopRoby is stepping in to address that. We have ads - in fact, sound off if your game are not on this list of paid advertisers and it ought to be:

Fastlane
Super Console
Crime and Punishment
FLFS
Grey Ranks
Brendan Adkins mystery ad
1001 Nights

So things are going well, picking up steam, and I have a question for you - will you buy a copy? It'll be on sale through Lulu, and I'm wondering how to package and price it. What would you pay above cost, knowing that anything over cost goes to the Carter Center? They've already received US$200 from the ads but they deserve lots more. Would a .pdf be attractive for a product like this? The lists are already freely available. I'm imagining a thick, sporty little 6x9" book will have a lot of utility. Give me some feedback.
