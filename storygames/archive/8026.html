\n Today is my 32nd birthday. I'm a horrible "finisher." I juggle multiple projects at once, bore easily, let things languish for months then work on them in a feverish manner only to be forgotten the next morning. Quite frankly I'm tired of having my projects just sit on my computer. So today, as a birthday present to myself, I put them out for public consumption in their imperfect and unfinished form.

Bloodthorn Press

Basically, there you'll find the latest drafts of all my RPG pet projects. I will continue to work on them and update them there. If any of them actually reach any kind of final form I will simply remove the drafts and place a link to where the final version can be found. Until then, enjoy and as with everything I put up for public consumption, feed back is welcome.

Jesse
