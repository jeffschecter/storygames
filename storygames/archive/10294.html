September 14th is the first deadline for the Game Design Studio critique.

\n

If you have a game design you're working on that you've playtested meaningfully, a text that needs work, a layout that needs a direction, or illustrations that need to be better, put them in as good condition as you can and come participate in the first round.

\n

I look forward to seeing people's hard work!
