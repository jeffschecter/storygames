\n Everybody! Everybody! My awesome girlfriend, Natalia, has published a game. Ghost Ship Enyo takes the idea of an "out-loud text adventure," as seen in the Parsely line of products, to a new level of depth, world-building, and referentiality.
This game has special sentimental value to me, since it was written as a sort of birthday/holiday gift to me this past winter, but it is legitimately awesome and you should buy it and play it.

Dead Tree, $10

Kindle, with full hyperlink support, $5

Also, support her Patreon!

    \n So, it's basically a Battlestar Galactica game, with the serial numbers filed off. It is specifically a reference *to* a reference within the BSG 'verse... saying any more would give it away, though. ;-)

    It's quite tough and demanding on the players. You have to keep a good map and inventory list, and there are some genuine decisions to be made regarding NPC interactions and the fate of various NPCs.
