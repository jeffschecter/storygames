    \n It's really good. The central conceit is this memory-like mechanic where you repeatedly reveal playing cards looking for matches, and it absolutely does what it says on the tin. Each session is infused with oracular elements that enjoy a dark, off-kilter Kevin Allen Jr. sensibility, simultaneously creepy and funny. The book is beautiful, like a glossy magazine. Recommended!
