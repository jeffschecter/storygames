\n Just started watching Burn Notice and remembered that I had bought Blowback a while back. After my initial read through of the rules I have a question.
In the Blowback section of the rules (page 30) it reads: "If the number of times you rolled a skill is greater than the number of points you have in that skill, your nerves are shot"
but on page 19 the rules say "Every time you roll a skill, fill in one of the small circles that drop down underneath your skill rating. If the number of times you rolled a skill is equal to your expertise in that skill,
your nerves are shot and you can\u2019t use that skill for the rest of the job"

So the blowback section seems to indicate you can roll against a skill more times than your rating, but then the other rule states that if they are equal you can't roll against that skill for the rest of the job. Does anyone have any clarification on this mechanic?

Followup question:
The URL listed in the book no longer appears to be valid. Is there a FAQ or Errata posted somewhere for Blowback? Character sheets etc?

Thanks!
