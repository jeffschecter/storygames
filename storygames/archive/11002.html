    \n So, I want to make it clear up front that I love this Tom Gauld stuff and think it's very cool and I'm only adding this for background, but, if you didn't know the reference, these images are done in the style of Ed Emberley (Ed's home page , Ed on Wikipedia), who is an illustrator of childrens' books, and published many books with his step-by-step method teaching kids to draw. These books, especially Make a World, but others as well, were some of my favorite things when I was growing up.



    Of course, Ed Emberley's belief in publishing these books was that anybody could learn to draw, so, again, while I think the Tom Gauld stuff is rad, I would say that if you want to have illustrations like these in your game book, you should get some Ed Emberley stuff as reference and do your own illustrations.
