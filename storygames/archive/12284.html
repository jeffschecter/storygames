\n I had a lot of fun doing the artwork for Ron's S/Lay w/Me, in time for GenCon, after he contacted me on July 22 last year. I delivered 5 full-page (for digest-sized pages) illustrations by the 25th.

And doing a full color cover for Paul Czege for Thy Vernal Chieftains, when he asked me on Jan 8 '09. I delivered the first sketch that evening, the first color version of it on Jan 11, the final version on Jan 12...

And man, I love these projects. Sitting down (virtually) with an impassioned designer and an improbable deadline and saying "OK, we're going to DO this." Sometimes with a flurry of art direction and feedback (as with Paul); other times with just a "you see the kind of thing I want? Do you get the vibe? Great! Go draw something!" (as with Ron)!

So if there's anyone out there, panicked, needing to get some illos done ASAP, and looking for the style that I deliver (heavy on figures and monsters and other organic forms; focusing on expressiveness and life, never quite achieving precision and neatness, but coming close at times), I'd love to hear from you. Anybody out there? Or did everybody who's hoping to debut at GenCon actually get all their art needs taken care of well ahead of time?

(see also: deviantArt gallery...)

Drop me a line here or at edheil at-sign fastmail dot fm.

And other artists also in the same position as me of being open to some work with a deadline at this point in time -- chime in in the thread!
