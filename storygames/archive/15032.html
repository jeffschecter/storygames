\n Here is a thread to post oracles that you write, so others can mind the cool ideas for their games.

Use whatever format you like, but I prefer to use this one because constraints can be fun!

-No title, someone should be able to figure it out based on the content.
-Four lines, each with different content from the same genre.
-No more than 10 words per line.
-Hint at the awesome.

Here are a few to get things started:

A space relic, with a slowly pulsing blue light.
An alien king, trapped inside a cave-in.
A broken-up free trader, orbiting an airless moon.
A crate of obselescent but unstable chemical weapons.

==

A restless medina, where a thief is on the loose.
A cuckolded general, out for revenge.
A monkey who sees all and communicates with one.
A girl of some beauty, but little wealth.

==

A bas-relief, full of grotesque creatures and killing.
A fiendish chute trap, leading to a cesspool.
A mad dwarf, risking all for the Gem of Light.
A large cavern, hiding many nooks and crannies.

==

A lonely ranch-house, occupied by brother and sister.
A crack-proof safe, at the bottom of a pond.
A bearded stranger with a gold-plated Peacemaker.
An Indian burial ground, being excavated by hired coolies.
