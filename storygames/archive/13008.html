    \n When I went, J\xf6rg Bours and his buddy took me out to eat, to a comic and then a game store. No real stores focusing on 'story games', but really nice people and interesting food. Eat the Parisian Macaroons or you will regret it. I met up with him from RPGNet. Will give you his contact info privately, but a "Hey, anyone up for meeting in Zurich" post over at RPGnet went a long way.

    While the university-going crowd like Joerg and his buddies love any kind of RPG, they're really into it for the long run: They love long campaigns. But if you bring a one-shot, I'm sure they'll like it.
