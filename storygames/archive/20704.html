\n Hey everyone!

Yesterday I launched the KS for my latest game, Meridian. It's inspired by stories of fantastical journeys like Labyrinth, Alice in Wonderland, The Neverending Story, Mirrormask, and so on (but also takes inspiration from darker journeys like Ink, Silent Hill 2, and Demon's Souls). It's a boxed set, because the game is based on evocative prompt and location cards. If you like imaginative exploration full of quirky characters and fleeting moments of wonder, and enjoy games like Fall of Magic or Everway, there's a good chance this one's for you.

https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1344826513/meridian-a-story-game-of-journeys-wondrous-and-fan

Thanks! And let me know if you have any questions.

Christian
