    There is in fact speaking, but only in the meta-game sense, where the players can say to the GM "I go check out the big logs" But in game, IC, it's wild gestures and grunts and emergent language for 'Hey look! Grubs we can eat!' As a person interested in language, this game is awesome. It is very much a GMed game, and the GM can pull weird stuff on you. My best bit: when Kevin described a sound we'd never heard - "Imagine that all you've ever heard is water, wind, animals, other people, and fire. Maybe a rockslide. That's it. Now imagine suddenly hearing the sound of metal scraping against metal, like griding gears." We were some creeped out cavefolk!
