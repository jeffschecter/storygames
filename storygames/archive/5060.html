\n Last night we experimented with world creation using a bit of Jeph's Map/Codex/Web idea plus a coin currency, and the rule that everything we added had to be a TRAP (a Threat, Reward, Asset, or Problem).

It rocked.

The world is very much on a macro level right now, it's full of demon lords, angry gods, opposing factions, and so on.

There was a ton of energy at the "table" (done via Skype and maptool) and we were all adding notes and finding connections between things. It was sweet, and the players are excited about doing some more (drilling down on particular areas to make starting-PC-level stuff). They also "get" how we'll be able to trot this method out later on if the game starts to stall, so the GM isn't burdened by the prep.

Anybody want to see the rules? I recorded it but haven't typed the transcript yet, I can talk about some AP stuff too if people are interested.
