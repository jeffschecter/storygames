\n So, I wrote a game, and it's a bit of an unusual format.

It's a two-player short form game, where the player develop the backstory of two characters who have known each other for a long time through recalling memories. It is based on the game "Summer Song' which is a game from the J. Walton Indie Mixtape #3 collection, but goes off on it's own direction.

Anyway, so here's the format thing. Instead of using cards, or tables and dice and whatnot, it's entirely mobile only. Each player uses their own tablet or smartphone and each player get's their own "secret" prompts for developing the story. So it's still a "two player across a table" kind of thing, but the devices handle all of the randomizing, explaining the rules, and general "stuff". Below is an image from the game.

so.....is anyone interested at poking at it? I'm really looking for any kind of feedback before I put it to bed and fully submit it ( I was looking for short project to get my feet wet to better understand the submittal process for mobile apps ).

I can provide a link to an android package, and I *finally* got approval via apple for external beta tester via iTunesConnect for iOS users.
