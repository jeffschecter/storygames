\n So story games are those which create a story that all the participants had a hand in. I played Micoscope and it built a setting as well as a story. I read Catty Big's SkipJack and saw that it'd make a person.

I'm trying to make a game that will produce a plan for a building as you play it. It's currently too shallow to be at all educational for the roles the different parties play in the construction industry which was my original intent.

What other games make something more than a story as you play it?
