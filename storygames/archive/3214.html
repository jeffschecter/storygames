\nEmbroidered patches are easily within our reach. Everybody wants a MASTER ROLE PLAYER merit badge, for Pete's sake. Let's see an Illustrator genius whip one up. Let's see somebody volunteer to print a mess up and sell them at Gen Con. An especially savvy person might also make some JOURNEYMAN ROLE PLAYER patches in a different color scheme.

You will be on your way to becoming a MASTER ROLE PLAYER yourself by volunteering to shepherd these patches into reality. Do you not want that prestige for yourself? Do you not feel shame that you have not thrown yourself at this task already?
