    \n What I would have said to Jason is: Jenn Rogers looked at me from the art corner the Indie Alcove and called out to the register corner that I was working, "Rob, give me something to draw."

    I could only think of greedy, selfish things that would get free work for my game. I was stumped. Then I had inspiration. "Russell, name a card!" I said. I did that to a few other people. I picked the Ace of Spades myself--because, Lemmy--and gave Oracle elements for her to work with.

    Russell pointed out--accurately--that people are going to be planning weddings and Bar Mitzvahs with Oracles soon.
