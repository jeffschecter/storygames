\n Our local independent toy store just moved to a new location about 5 times the size. We were down there today chatting with the owner, and I started talking to him about Go Play Northwest (trying to get some free swag, since Seattle U is just around the corner). It turns out that he's a role-player from way back, who once designed a D&D hack called Punks & Posers (about fighting your way through a music festival gone mad).

He has a big space for public events, and he wants to get more gaming into the store. He knows about Catan and Ticket to Ride, and stocks the Haba games for kids under 10 but not much else. I'm not knowledgable enough to help him out much.

The store is currently targetted at the toddler set, but he also wants to branch out into games for pre-teen crowd and games that kids and parents can play together. I told him I'd ask around and collect some input for him. Do you have any ideas for events or items to stock?
