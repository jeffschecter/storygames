    \n If you have all day, you might consider just taking a cab from La Guardia into Manhattan (about $30 to midtown) to someplace you'd like to see (the Metropolitan Museum! the Compleat Strategist!) and then taking the subway up or down to Penn Station when you've had your fill of the city for the day. Apparently, you can also take the M60 bus from the airport for $2 to 116th St. & Broadway (right in front of Columbia University), from which point the A or C subway (blue line) heading downtown will take you to Penn Station at 34th St.

    \n I REALLY REALLY REALLY suggest taking a cab. It's a little bit more expense, but it's way faster and you get to see some of the city. You can also take the subway directly from laguardia to Penn Station. You hop on Air train (a shuttle within the airport) to the A (blue) line. Ride it into manhattan and in about 45-60 min you'll be at 34th st. Penn station.

    Both of these solutions are super easy.
