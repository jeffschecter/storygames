      Posted By: Mr. TeapotWhy do you care? More importantly, why should I care? Does it matter when a game was released?
      \nI personally care because an understanding of design chronology because it informs my understanding of the games in question. Our play & design culture riffs off and is inspired by collective wisdom. If Mist-Robed Gate had been published after In A Wicked Age, then it would have camped the term "best interests," and might have taken a page from forms.

      Thinking about timelines and chronology and release dates is a component of thinking about how games inform playcultures, which is something I dig.



      You should care for your own reasons. If you don't have any, you shouldn't care.



      It doesn't matter when a game is released, no. I mean, for sales and buzz, it does. For play, though, of course it doesn't.
