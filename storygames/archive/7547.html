\nProject Playlist, for the uninitated, is a free music-sharing website that allows you to create custom tracks from, er, songs on other people's custom tracks and linked to from Teh Intarwebs.

Hands up, anyone, who thinks that this is a gaming / marketing godsend.

So, I propose the Story Games Project Playlist Project - post a playlist for your campaign or the game you have written!

Getting started in my role as TBBSFBitU, here's my unofficial Bliss Stage soundtrack. Note the following songs in particular:

Pilot theme songs - Pilots in general would march to battle singing a This Sacrifice by The Cruxshadows - "In the fury of our darkest hour, we will be your light - you've asked me for this sacrifice, and I am Winter-Born!" For a hopeful game or a Rising Hero, has to be It's Not My Time by Three Doors Down.

Anchors - A Driven or Kind Anchor would whisper the lyrics of Endlessly by Muse: "Hoplessly, I'll love you endlessly - Hopelessly, I'll give you everything - but I won't give you up. I won't let you down. And I won't let you fall, if the moment ever comes." One who has seen far too many pilots die on her screams for "Bring Me To Life" by Evanescence: and there's not an anchor in the world who wouldn't relate to Polly Scattergood's "I Hate The Way."
