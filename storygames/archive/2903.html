\n Inspired by the Regional Soda Exchange, I thought we'd maybe try another 'barter-only' event: the CD swap-meet!

Pledge your compilation here and we'll do this all as part of the Nerdly Bazaar on Saturday Morning. Unless its just sad little me with my stack of sea shantey CDs.
