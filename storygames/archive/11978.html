\nThe original post with musings about my Mouse Guard hack.

I've been noodling away on my not-quite-The Wire hack for Mouse Guard, called Down in the Hole.

One of the major decisions I've made so far is to limit player characters to be police officers involved in the same, slowly evolving case. I'm not ruling out the possibility for players to play both police and criminals in the same case, but MG's team-based structure fits one or the other.

Anyway, I've a character sheet and am ready to playtest.
