    \n I would love to offer a long-con Torchbearer game. I think that'd be insane fun. Character gen is a little slow, so I'd probably have to use pre-gens. A lot of the fun of the game is bringing in relationships with twists -- like having your nemesis hunt you out to the dungeon 10 miles from anywhere -- so it behooves the GM to know the characters well.

    Getting people -- including me as the GM -- to commit to an entire day of Nerdly for one game is hard, though, even if it is the best game I played last year.
