\n Use a Prima Collector's Official Game Guide: http://goo.gl/CkU2K

Take their Skyrim book:
http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0307891402/

- 656 pages
- interactive world map
- Large map Poster
- Details on all items, spells, locations, characters, quests
- Dungeon maps

I would run Dungeon World with this.

Give the players the large poster map and let them go anywhere.

Then use the book as a guide. Place stickies inside the book to keep track of all the changes the players make to the world.

Done!

And Prima makes hundreds of books like these. Endless content for most campaign ideas!

No prep! Just play!

    \n Haha. That's gold. I have this book and the art book to match. I've used both as 'prompts' for the players in our DW game.

    "So [wizard] you're spouting Lore about the Dragon's Maw caves? Cool, find the map the old prospector gave you in this book. Oh and [cleric], you said one of your brothers had returned from a pilgrimage in the mountains where the caves are found? Choose one of these sketches that show all of us what they look like from the pictures in this book..."

    Fill in the blanks, grab a few factions filled with dangers that have obtuse instincts to the players bonds... Works a treat
