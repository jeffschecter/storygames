    For Graham (and myself, turning this in my head):
    Wallace and Grommit pretty much IS Jeeves and Wooster, if you look at it a certain way. The obstensible master spends the story prancing about and having life made very difficult, either as a result of their actions or not coping well with what life thrusts at them. The obstensible servant is smarter and cooler and spends the story trying to fix things up. The game is about making a mess that seems impossible to fix, then setting about to fix it.
