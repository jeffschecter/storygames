\n Hey! So, I'm working on a game currently called "Gun n Fuck," which is an action movie game based loosely on Crank.

A lot of the game is driven from adjectives describing the central character, the main bad guy, his friends, girlfriend, and foes. The groups I've tried this with have all come up with some neat ones, but I wanted to see what a few dozen heads would come up with.

Here's the deal: there are five different roles:
* The Badass, the main character. Your Jason Statham type of dude.
* The Motherfucker, the central guy who is responsible for condemning the Badass to die.
* The Girlfriend, the person that the Badass doesn't want to drag into all this shit.
* The Friend, the person (or people) that help the Badass. (The doctor and the kid that he calls in Crank would be in this role)
* The Other Motherfuckers, random people that get in the way of the Badass

I'm looking for adjectives that could be used in either awesome or funny ways. In the last game, we came up with for the Badass: Vicious, Drunken, Pissed-off, Hungry, Foreign.

What other adjectives would you find awesome or funny for the Badass? For the other roles?

Thanks!

- Ryan
