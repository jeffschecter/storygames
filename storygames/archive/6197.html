    \n True story, and I am a little embarased to say this, when we first moved to chicago I was working at the Tower records down on Wabash. Since I had another job and wasn't one of the cool kids I worked cash register every morning. The sole benefit of working the front was that I got control of the stereo. We had lots of music to choose from and I being of an adventurous taste tried to play the music I had never heard first. One day amidst the new arrivals was a CD by a girl that used to be on You Can't Do That On Television. I put the album on and instantly fell in love with the music and kept it in a heavy rotation. It would be a year before Alannis Morrisett was everywhere but it was my fault as much as her's.
