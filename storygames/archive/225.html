A note of explanation

Bruce Baugh
February 2006 edited February 2006 in Story Games
I've started a couple of these query threads, and have some more in mind. But I realized that I should explain what I'm doing.

When we argue about concepts, particularly at the distance of the net, it can be easy to lose track of being organic beings with histories, natures, and tastes. I'm hoping that looking at some of the human dimensions that don't deal with the concepts at all, but with matters of impulse, pre-rational inclination, and the like, I will come to some fresh perspective on who I am as I look at the concepts. I'm also, admittedly, slightly voyeuristically interested in who the rest of you are apart from the syllogisms and all, and love the unexpected sympathies and strangnenesses that can emerge of such exchanges.

So this is me trying to understand those who game and theorize, as distinctive from games and theories.
