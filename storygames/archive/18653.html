\n Remember when you all failed to back Ben Lehman's Kickstarter for his inevitably radical game SKEW? Yeah, boo on you (Hey, I'm kidding. Sorta. I was a backer & I wanted that book to exist!)

Well, thanks to Patreon, SKEW is now free. It's a game about making science fiction stories where a protagonist struggles to understand a world beyond understanding. Ben consistently makes games that do brilliant things that make you think they're obvious in retrospect. Check out Ben's page, click on over to the "Just Content" tab, and there's SKEW!
