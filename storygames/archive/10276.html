\n So first, I was reading this thread and the gaming parlor discussion got me wondering again about what it would take to buy a house cooperatively, outfit it as a game space and then just have a gaming coop. Or maybe it would be a mixed-use building and be a game-space coop as well as a FLGS. This would be neat.

Then I got to thinking about discussions of gamer communes that I've been in and decided to search. This has been talked about before around here. What would it take to make you actually consider buying into such a thing? Are finances the biggest deal? Or the move? Or what? What would you expect to have to compromise on? What would you not be willing to?

E.g. on that last bit, I wouldn't live in a building where people could smoke. But I'm a vegetarian and am kind of squicked by meat -- but I wouldn't have a problem sharing a kitchen with (tidy) carnivores.
