Candamir as a gateway game.

Levi Kornelsen
August 2006 edited August 2006 in Story Games
Candamir is one of the spin-off games from Settlers of Catan. In it, you have a character (playing piece-style). You adventure. You get and spend Xp. You fulfill quests, buy potions, fight bad guys.

It's not a story-based game, by any stretch. But still.

I can't help but thing that having a game but much like this that starts simple and slowly stages up towards roleplaying couldn't help but be awesome.

EDIT: Everthing you might want to know about it: http://www.rpg.net/reviews/archive/11/11309.phtml
