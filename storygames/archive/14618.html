\n Hey, gang!

We've got a date for the next Nerdly Beach Party, in San Simeon State Park. From the website:\n
Welcome to the 8th Nerdly Beach Party, at San Simeon State Park
NBPVIII will be from September 23rd to the 25th. It starts that Friday in late afternoon, and breaks up Sunday after lunch. Save the Date! We'll be doing registration soon.
- Campsite at San Simeon State Park Campground (Google Maps link)
- Breakfast and Lunch on Saturday and Sunday (Dinners are BYOMeat and side to share)
- Plenty of gaming to keep you occupied for the weekend!
Follow Ryan Macklin or Go Play SF Bay on Twitter to find out when registration will open!
\nWe'll have registration up soon. Hope to see you there!

- Ryan
