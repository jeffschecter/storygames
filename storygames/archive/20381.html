\n A couple of new short games have appeared on the Groundhoggoth blog this month (what can I say, I'm feeling creative):

Phasmagraph: This is a mini-story game about a seance: it works best with 4 or 5 players, but it can also be a neat 2 player game and all you'll need is an ordinary pack of playing cards (Ouija board is optional.)


Lally Tullet: This short game is about delivering a monologue that recounts a notable event in a person's life: the monologue for each player may be comic, tragic or just a slice of life.
