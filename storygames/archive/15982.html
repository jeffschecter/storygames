    \n It came out through a long discussion about gaming. StWT has a lot of awesome in it, but I will have to sit down tonight and reread it to remember what.

    Besides that what actually lead us to this discussion was interesting dice mechanics. One that we felt would work great for a game like Demons Souls was "Momentum Dice", essentially after every confrontation you get more and more dice to roll. But, eventually you must stop and recover, thus losing all of those extra dice. The mechanic essentially encourages you to push beyond what you really should, because you need those dice to win at many points.

    I think this "could" translate to a game of StWT, but I am not sure if something like Dungeon World, or even something more traditional would be best.
