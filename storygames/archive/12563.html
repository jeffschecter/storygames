\n Girls of Mars, my new dreamy indie rock band,
will play in portland on the 25th of this month, aka this coming sunday!

aka SUNDAY THE 25TH!!

Here's the blurb from another website:

on july 25th, kusikia, fabi, forest park, like a villain, and girls of mars will play a tour comeback/joint record release/label showcase show at In Other Words Books on williams and vancouver in north east portland at 8pm. kusikia, fabi, and like a villain will all be releasing albums!

Here's our myspace:
girls of mars

come down/up and check us out, if you're free!
