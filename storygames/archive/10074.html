    Posted By: Simon_PetterssonThough you can say Polaris if you think it worksbetterwith three players than with four.
    \nActually, my gut tells me that Polaris might work better with three -- with four, it's easy to feel like you're playing in two different games with each Heart-Mistaken pair, but if you make that a triangle, you're engaging with each other.

    For other games...
    Don't Rest Your Head I think is equal with three or four (counting one as GM) -- the nature of protagonist power and the focus of the character creation as solo story leans me in that way.

    I can't think of any others off the top of my head.

    Posted By: Simon_PetterssonMendel: What is Coming of Age? Tell me more!
    \nComing of Age is a young adult fiction focused RPG system I've been developing for a few years. My current publication plans for it involve focusing on several of the more interesting settings in accessible, fairly inexpensive format:

    Scions of the Superspy - The mysterious superspy known only by a number has foiled many nefarious plots, and broken many hearts. Each of you comes from one such dalliance. But something has gone wrong the superspy is missing, the world is in peril, now it\u2019s time for you to live up to your heritage.

    Magic Academy - Build an academy of magic, then save it.

    Monsters in Love - Isabella is wondering if her boyfriend, Dessie, will ever ask her out to the Soph Hop. Her friends tell her he\u2019s a vampire, but that doesn\u2019t bother her too much. She hasn\u2019t told them she used to be an Incan Princess before being mummified a millennium ago. And anyway she\u2019s more worried about how she\u2019ll beat that werewolf and her dark warlock date to become queen of the hop.

    The system is GMless, built for heroic style fiction where the characters will learn from early difficulties to discover the potential within them, and ultimately save the day. The entire game plays in about 3-4 hours, character creation included.
