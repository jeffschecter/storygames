\n This week You Don't Meet In An Inn presents Beam Saber: The Cenotaph!

Beam Saber is a mech/vehicle focused Forged In The Dark game by Austin Ramsay!

In this episode the players gather for their session zero where they build the setting, their squad, and their characters.

You can find the podcast here, our RSS, and our itunes.
