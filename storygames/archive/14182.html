    \n Agile always strikes me as rather bland. I like Nimble better. And it might carry enough sexy overtones to cover Hot.

    I can't think of anything in fantasy terms that applies better to both males and females. Most of the terms I'm thinking of are rather gender biased. Heck, it took a while before Hot was used for males, as I recall. I think the phenomenon of using the same sexy terminology for both men and women is a rather modern construct, which makes it difficult in pseudo historical settings. Most of what I remember of older literature involves a very different vocabulary being used to describe attractive men and women.

    But Jack was nimble and swift. And nimble sounds sexy for a babe as well. So that might do the trick. Plus it has a nice broad scope.

    But if you want to laser-focus on the attractiveness part, then I agree with Charming.

    And I like Mighty. It has a barbaric feel to it that I think covers the violence tendencies you are looking for. You just need to say it with a growl in your voice, and you're fine. To me it covers both the fighting and the intimidating concepts really well.
