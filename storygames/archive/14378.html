\n Our little con is growing up. We've been putting on JiffyCon in western Massachusetts since 2006. It's grown from a dozen folks running games for one another and having crazy sleepovers, to fifty or so adults and kids, with local stores and businesses sponsoring, but keeping our indie game focus and friendly vibe. We anticipate growing to 60 or 75 folks in the coming year.

So we'd like to ask you to help us continue spreading the word and share our con with a growing community. We've had many looks over the years, but we'd like to create a logo for JiffyCon that we can use to tie together all of our outreach. Cons like Go-Play Northwest with their Yeti, Camp Nerdly with the Owlbear and communities like NerdNYC with their burning d20 have great logos. We're opening it up to the community to see what folks might be inspired to create for our con.

The themes of our JiffyCon are: local, independent, community and seasons. And values like innovation and being family friendly are important to us! Our motto is "Great games in a jiffy." You can see our current site, and there are links to all our past websites here:

http://jiffycon.blogspot.com/p/past-events.html

Post your ideas here, or at RPG Crossroads. The deadline is June 30, 2011. We'll bring the designs to our con committee and make a choice. For the designer of the chosen logo, we'll have a package of games, we'll publicize your work at the fall con and on our website, and (if you're anywhere near us) free admission to JiffyCon in September.

Let us know if you have any questions!

Best,
Emily
jiffycon.com
