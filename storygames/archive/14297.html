\nThis, my friends, is quite possibly the best music video of all time. My exact reaction upon first seeing it was, "I never need to play fantasy RPGs ever again. I can just keep watching this video."

Of course, we all know that's not true! So... what RPG would best capture the essence of this masterpiece?

Lula proposes Swords Without Master, which sounds great but with which I am sadly unfamiliar.

My idea: Use Burning Wheel, but change the rules as follows:

1) Failure consequences are still failures, but they up the amplitude of the awesome in some way. Fail your sword test? Ship explodes! Fail your Speed test to get in the lifeboat? It's now night-time and you swing on a rope into the ocean!

2) Stats and skills and such go up by 1 every time they are failed, and every two times they are used successfully.

3) Intents must be suitably kooky and awesome, like "I throw my dagger at the witches to make them do an awesome spin-dance," so that success also ups the volume.

It would probably last all of about an hour before the players had completely conquered the world for all time. But it would be totally awesome.

What are your ideas?

Matt
