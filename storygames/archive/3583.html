\n Hrm, this is a bit shill-y, but its coming from my heart. I think I'm just a big Google fanboi.

Anyway, I was wondering about y'alls experiences with Google Checkout.

I've been using it to take direct orders from my website, alongside Paypal, and I gotta say....as a vendor, I really dig it. Once someone makes an order, the interface has a whole convenient process that I can click through to update my customer as to the status of their order. Not to mention that it doesn't take a cut of my sale, at least for this year.

Are there horror stories out there about it that I don't know? Or is Paypal just so widespread that it's not worth the time to set up an account with Google checkout?
