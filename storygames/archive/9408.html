\n Apologies if this is inappropriate, but I just read this RetroRoleplaying blog entry from Randall Stukey, the creator of the Microlite74 retroclone, who's under a severe financial burden from medical expenses, and is giving PDF copies of his old, old gaming zine from the 70s, Grimoire, to anyone who donates any amount to help him through the month. Thought y'all might want to take a look and see if that's the sort of thing you want in on.

Disclaimer: I don't know Randall from Adam, I just read his blog, saw the plea, and thought I'd like to spread it around, cause I strongly empathize with his situation.
