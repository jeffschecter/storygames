    \nBoth of these are very acceptable systems for Star Wars. I would second the idea that, if you want to use the FATE system in SoTC, getting "Starblazer Adventures" is a very good suggestion, as it is FATE tuned to handle Space Opera from the same time period that the original series came from. The feel will be there.

    The advantages Solar would offer is that the players are more focused on the advancement and reward structure than you have to be and the system's "Bringing Down the Pain" mechanic (called Extended Conflicts in the Solar text) lets you run fast and loose until it is important to the players - then slow down and make it count.

    I would also, for my part, suggest a peek at the PDQ# rules from Atomic Sock Monkey. There is a free download of them at their site and they could be easily used to run Star Wars (as mentioned above, any good game can be - it is because good games and Star Wars both tie into that Mythic element that makes adventure stories so darned good).

    All of the above games will make relationships important to play and take the focus off of combat tactics and put it more in terms of cinematic action. You should be well served by any of them.
