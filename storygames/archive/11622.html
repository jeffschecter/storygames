    \n Paul,

    It's random, so you can't predict too much. I can share my experience, though.

    As a previous purchaser, I can confirm that there are a *lot* of dice in there. The pack I got, and one that got shared out, both seemed to have a bias towards d20, d10 and d6s, but still a fair number of d4s, d8s and d12s. I needed to go get some more of these for playing games that require a lot of those dice. Aside from that, I haven't run out of anything since buying the pound-o-dice.

    Plus, I think one bag had a d30, or something bizarre like that.
