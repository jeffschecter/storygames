\n Well, in three weeks time, I leave Scotland for a 10 month, round the world trip extravaganza. So, i thought it would be nice to meet up with some of the good people from Story Games along the way!

So, anyone like to meet, have a beer, maybe have a game, just chat or show me the outstanding sights of their local area?

The trip goes something like this:

23rd July: Arrive in Atlanta for a week (activities already sorted there, with mountains and the IHOP!)

1st August: Arrive in Montreal to stay with friends: any local SG people like to meet?

There is then a period of meandering through New England and upstate New York, then travelling to Gen Con with the redoubtable Sons of Kryos!

20th August: Leave Gen Con for Vancouver and Seattle

27th August: Arrive in San Francisco

2nd September: Fly from LA to Tokyo

Spend a week in Tokyo, then travelling about Japan until...

2nd October: Fly from Tokyo to Wellington, New Zealand

Then it all gets a bit vague.

So, if anyone would like to meet up along the way, then pleae let me know! I'd be delighted to meet some friendly faces.

Cheers
Malcolm
