The Game Design Studio is opening up its doors for the third time! Get your drafts together, figure out how to present them according to the minimum (or better) standards, and come discuss in a focused, supportive environment!

\n

The doors will be open for a couple of weeks, or until it looks like everyone's gotten everything they need, then I'll shut the door.

\n

There are a couple of people clamoring to get in, so I'm opening the doors immediately, rather than giving a deadline, so that means you have less time than usual to get your stuff together. Sorry about that!

\n

I'm also doing an experiment: there's someone with a game design idea that they need to work out. I'll be moderating very carefully to help get this project on track as efficiently as possible. I don't in general like talking about ideas, but if this works well, I might have figured out some good policy on the matter.
