    \n I would have told you that it's available from IPR, but for some reason it's not. I thought that Jim said at one point that he's shipping a bunch there, but apparently he hasn't.

    The second-best option is to buy from retailers. Noble Knight has it, as well as many other OSR products (it seems to be something of a flag-bearer for the OSR scene, that store). The price is very fair, too.

    As for local stores, I wouldn't expect any particular one to have it. You know how it is - indie product, too little volume to justify major distribution (although I think Jim's working on that, too, busy man that he is). Some stores, certainly.

    In general Jim does a pretty good job of keeping track of his world conquest at his blog - there's a list of places you can order his stuff from, for instance. A couple of American webstores in there.
