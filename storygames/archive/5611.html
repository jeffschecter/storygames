\n Recently, Steve Dempsey posted this over on The Collective Endeavour:

"I imagine that if the Crown had kept control of the American colonies then agreement with other colonial powers would have lead to a North American continent with at least three countries, the Hispanic in the south and west, the Russian to the north west and the British, maybe even another for the French in the south east. These national tension may have made independence less attractive. Perhaps this would have been the theatre for world war rather than Europe.

I can see a game of Cold City set in post-war Salt Lake City, a city divided between the Russian, Spanish and British Sectors over a local Mormon population. What demons lurk?"

I find this idea tremendously exciting, as it hits so many buttons for me in terms of setting, background and so on. It could be a wonderful alt-history game. The combination of Imperial/colonial politics, the aftermath of a destructive war and the nature of the place itself just cries out for a game.

I think I'm going to need to do some work on this!

Cheers
