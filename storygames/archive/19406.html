\n Has anyone played Bugs of Venus Bugs of Venus by Benjamin Osborn yet? There's not much on the web yet, so starting a thread here seemed as good a place as any to pool thoughts and info on it.

It is "a hack of Jason Morningstar's Death School (which was a hack of John Harper's Lady Blackbird). Fight and kill bugs in the year 2XXX, make sure humanity stays alive and carry very big guns." (From the Story Games weekly email).

I think it looks like fun. What I am missing from it is an initial situation - a bang to get things moving. I guess the thing implicit in the text is "go deep into the Ruins of Denver to find the critical supplies we need to continue to survive" - and then you can let things develop from there. Still, anyone have any cool hooks?
