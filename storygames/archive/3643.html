\n Hi all,
Well after much faffing about and prevarication, NerdLDN is live and needs London gamers. We're small in number at the moment, but your input can help shape the route we take in the future. We are aiming to spread the Nerd community, first seen in the USA, to blighty and create a community of and for London's disparate gamers. All are welcome, including clubs, groups and individuals so that it will be easier to find games and gamers in the big smoke. We'd also like to carry on the, wonderful, NerdUSA tradition of bringing gamers together in meetups and gatherings, all in a way which works with rather than at cross purposes to those clubs and groups already in London.
Come over, say hi and let us know what you think.
John
