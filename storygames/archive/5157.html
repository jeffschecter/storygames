    Posted By: tomgjdrakeh,
    Have you played Tannhauser yet?
    If so give us a brief review.
    tomg
    \nYes, just last night. The production quality is gorgeous and the gameplay is great, though the Reich forces seem to have a distinct edge over the Union forces where balance is concerned. Also, despite claiming that it's meant to be played by 2 to 10 players on the box, the truth is that it's only designed to be played by two players but can be played by up to ten (with each player taking on an individual character). Applying the two player rules to multiple player situations is left entirely up to the consumer. Aside from those two issues and the HtH Combat gaffe addressed in errata (which is really not so much an error as it is a confused presentation issue), the game is very solid and I wholeheartedly recommend it.
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
