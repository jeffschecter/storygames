Hello Story Gamers!

\n

JiffyCon is returning to the Boston area on Saturday June 25. We're back in Davis Square, and we'll be gaming up a storm.

\n

Games Planned:

\n
    \n
  • Burning Hoopleheads (Burning Wheel adapted for Deadwood)
    • \n
  • Combat Diaries
    • \n
  • FreeMarket
    • \n
  • The Last Von Hoern (Cthulhu Dark)
    • \n
  • Microscope
    • \n
  • Polaris
    • \n
  • Swords Without Master
    • \n
  • Vampire Hunter: 2031 (Loom)
    • \n
\n

More details over at the JiffyCon website. We're still accepting games, so if you want to GM something, let us know.

\n

Hope to see you soon!
