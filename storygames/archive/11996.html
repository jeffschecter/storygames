    \n I recommend Spellbound Kingdoms. Its a FRPG with a lot of unique mechanics, ideas and executions. The game stresses Scene Order Play which I think is absolutely perfect for a one-on-one game. If the both of you like combat, the game is very unique in that respect as well. In fact, you can check out the Combat Primer if you want a great source for information about that specific mechanic. Furthermore, the website contains a few previews of some the game's elements. Spellbound Kingdoms also happens to be eight dollars. Here is the Table of Contents just in case you want to look at it.
    \n
    \nThis may or may not interest you but the game is also easily hacked or added to. You can check out my Homebrew threads for proof of that on the official forums if'n you would like.
    \n
    \nOne final note... Spellbound Kingdoms is due to be updated fairly soon (by about the end of June or so). However, those who purchased the pdf will get the updated pdf for free.
