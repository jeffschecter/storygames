    \n Since we're just under a week from Valentine's Day, I wanted to give this a bump.

    Before Valentine's Day, write a game for someone you love, and give it to them. Make it something you think they'll like. If they're not into roleplaying games, even better: use this to stretch yourself, to write a roleplaying game for someone who's not usually into roleplaying games.

    I know a couple of people are working on their games already. If you'd like to join in, this is an ideal time to start. (If you really loved them, you would.)

    Graham

    \n I'm doing it. I've wanted to dedicate a game to Danielle for a long time. She's been with me through them all. But my damn games are so...unflinching.

    So this one's going to be sweet. (Well, as sweet as I can make it.)

    At this point the mechanics are worked out in my head. And about a third of the text is written. It'll have a cover illustration and one or two interior illustrations by me. At this point the cover illustration and one interior illustration are roughly pencilled. The title of the game is Honeydew.
