    I don't think it has to be tremendously crafty or clever; for ailurophiles, the idea pretty much sells itself.
    Something like, "You've been a good cat, at least by cat standards. You've protected your humans from the invisible beasts and even caught a few mice. But what you saw last night is different. It'll take a whole alleyfull of cats to handle this one."

    Just get across that this is a game where you're a cat, and you live in cat's world that humans can't appreciate or even see.

    Also, my favorite standby after the game blurb (in fact, the above looks grand):
