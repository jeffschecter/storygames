\n On Friday night, me and some friends (5 or 6 of us, probably) in my local game group are gonna head out to a bald in the National Forest to enjoy the moonless dark and watch the Perseids. There has been some interest in playing a scary one-session game. I'm not super interested in doing a Lovecraft thing, and the circumstances kind of rule out Dread... something semi-larpy that doesn't require a ton of referring to sheets or fiddling with dice would be ideal. Any recommendations?
