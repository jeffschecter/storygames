\n I'm helping edit the Norwegian Larpfactory Book Project, a collection of 20+ ready-to-play short larps created by the Norwegian Larpfactory.

Games about time travelers! Murder mysteries! Formal experiments about silence and more!

You can pre-order a hard copy of the book over on our Indie Gogo page:
http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/the-larp-factory-book-project/x/4806749

Alternately, if several people in a central location want to team up and order together, you can send us an email at larpfactorybookproject@gmail.com with the number of copies you want and a shipping address, and we will send you an invoice and ship all the books there when we have received your payment.

The book is due out at the end of this October, and will be accompanied by a website that contains all of the game materials, plus video demonstrations of all workshop techniques used in the larps.
