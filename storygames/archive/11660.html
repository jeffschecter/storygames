    Posted By: Jason MorningstarWhich two of these people seemleastinteresting? Why?
    I'd say B and E, because they just don't hook me. Two dudes, one smiling slightly, one frowning slightly, in vaguely period clothing. E makes me wonder, "What's up with the snowy trees?" as it's the only one with any sort of background or "scene." But that's naught to do with the character (other than "Why's he standing in the snow?").\n
    Which one looks like the most interesting, challenging or fun to play? Why?
    That's three questions:
    Interesting - G. I mean, seriously... that guy could be anything from a Russian oligarch to a football coach from the 50s. And that fur coat is OUTRAGEOUS, in any modern context (e.g. PETA).
    Challenging - All the women, but A in particular. First, because I don't presume to know how to portray a woman in anything but a tomboy-ish (or even more stereotypical) manner. Last, because her nudity suggests vulnerability, and her hands suggest prayer (or placation?), and her face suggests inquiry and traces of sorrow or disappointment: that's a LOT of nuance to reconcile into a coherent character (even assuming she's gonna eventually put on a shirt!).
    Fun - G or the former, for the same reason as the Challenging answer above; the latter, because he looks like "the very model of a modern Major General," and I could play that stiff upper lip to the hilt. (Wait... that didn't come out right. You get what I mean, though, I trust.)

    \n B & F are the least interesting to me.
    G is by far the most interesting.

    In the case of B, I see a man who is perhaps virile, perhaps clever, and perhaps a narcissistic socialite.
    Well, if he is the first, then why isn't he in uniform, strutting his stuff, being a man of decisive action? D > B
    If he is the second, then why isn't he putting his cleverness to work, perhaps as a detective or a double agent? E > B
    If he is the third, why isn't he reveling in his power, being showy for the hedonistic sake of it? G > B

    No matter what B is trying to be, someone else is doing it better.

    In the case of F, I see a woman who is maybe youthful, maybe lost in the world, and maybe resigned to domestic life.
    Well, if she is the first, then why the fuck isn't she getting naked and winning the hearts & loins of young men everywhere? A > F
    If she is the second, why even try to fake the smile? C > F
    If she is the third, she clearly hasn't been through enough of the wringer yet to come out interesting. H > F

    No matter what F is trying to be, someone else is doing it better.

    Now, G is absolutely amazing.
    G is this dude who's just asking you to try fucking with him.
    He's a badass. He's also wearing a frou frou coat and hat.
    He's going to dress pretty, and if you mess with him, he's going to destroy you.
    Not with weapons, like all those filthy commoners, but with words, with friends, with pure unadulterated power.
