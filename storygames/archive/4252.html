Indie Game Mash-Ups, e.g. Cold City + Geiger Counter

\n

\n
\n This idea struck me when I was reading Jonathan Walton's latest Geiger Counter text when he mentioned that, among others, Cold City would make a fine setting for Geiger Counter. So the idea struck me. What if you were playing in a Cold City campaign and, for one night's session and one encounter, you used Geiger Counter to play it out? So the RPG squad finds themselves trapped in an abandoned Nazi research facility with one of the Alternatives on the loose. Next session, you play out that scenario using Geiger Counter. That sounds awesome to me in a Reese's you-got-your-peanut-butter-in-my-chocolate kind of way. It would probably take some tweaking (e.g., so all but one or two of the RPA square aren't killed off with Jonathan's intentionally lethal ruleset), but otherwise fairly doable. Bonus: You get to play more/different games without abandoning your regular campaign.

Thoughts? Other games that might work well in conjunction or as intermittent one shots. Panty Explosion + Best Friends? Agon + Committee?

    So yeah...

    Dogs in the Vineyard + Stargate SG-1 = Do: Pilgrims of the Flying Temple
