\n'The Giant Brain' is delighted to announce that \u2018Revenge of the B-Movie!\u2019 is now available from the fine folks at Indie Press Revolution.

\u2018Revenge of the B-Movie!\u2019 is a fast playing card game about making ridiculous B-Movie titles. Players compete to make the best(worst?) film titles and sell them to Hollywood for vast riches. After all who wouldn\u2019t want to see 'Killer Tomatoes from Outer Space vs. The Chainsaw Wielding Bikers from Hell?\u2019.

You can read a review of the game here.

All the best

Iain (The Frontal Lobe)
