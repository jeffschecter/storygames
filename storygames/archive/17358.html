    \n This was a pretty interesting podcast and I think it probably aplies to a lot of StoryGames readers -- being Joe Nobody, working in a vacuum and getting stuff done. Since I was featured in this podcast, let me answer the questions presented in the podcast as they relate to me and my game

    What helped me immeasurably in actually putting something out there was other people coming on board. Michael Wight/ (StupidGremlin) did some very inspiring artwork and four others wanted to contribute with scenarios - Wightbred's Goblin World, Daniel Hoffman's Bogeymen, Allan Farr's Singularity and Taleweaver's Nights of the Crusades.

    Having these guys contribute made all the difference. So get together with like-minded folks and collaborate! Even if you cant get playtests, or feedback, or whatever, at least you are being ignored together

    On a related note, I think you guys should offer a compendium of your MT games.
