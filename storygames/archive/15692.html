    \n So, the question(s)of the game is this; how does the parking lot shape them, and what will they do after the parking lot?
    What made the little interconnected vignettes work? Would this work in a rpg? It's a story that's told over years, with one thing in common, the place(Microscope?). Is the owner a player character? Do the other players play patrons?
    This was such a neat story, but it might not make an easy game.
