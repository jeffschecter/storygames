    \n Also if you are running it at a club you should know a couple things:

    1. It takes about an hour per Event; cut the number of Events accordingly, picking the ones you like.

    2. It can go to some really dark places, so use safety mechanics (I recommend the X-Card and "The Door Is Always Open") and have a little pre-game chat.

    \n My pitch:

    "One safety feature of our game today is that the door is always open. Take care of yourself - if you need to use the bathroom, please do, if you need to step out for a moment, that's fine. If you want to stop playing, that is totally OK. You won't break the game or cause a problem, and nobody will ask you why you left."
