    \n Hey Ben, I went ahead and started another thread to call for volunteers, so if you're interested, jump in there. To answer your question, we're looking for about 12 hours of your time over the course of the three day show. That will be a combination of playing host (greeting people, pitching and arranging games) and running your favorite games for enthusiastic strangers. It's a lot of fun, and you should still have time to see the show and catch some panels.
