\n So, I hadn't checked my Google Analytics for Abulafia in a while, and logged in there today. What I found was quite odd - usage of the site is up, but an amazing 26% of all pageviews were for the "Fantasy Barbarian Names generator. Over 3000 pageviews (so, like 60000 barbarian names generated) in just the last month. A few sites referred 20 or less viewers, but the vast majority come straight from Google searches. Here are the top searches that have led people there:

1. barbarian names 3,403
2. fantasy barbarian names 56
3. barbarian character names 49
4. cool barbarian names 36
5. barbarians names 19
6. random barbarian names 13
7. "barbarian names" list 12
8. "barbarian names" 10
9. good barbarian names 8
10. babarian names 7
11. female barbarian names 7

So, why the sudden glut of searches for barbarian names? None of the other generators is experiencing this - usually when I get a big spike it's associated with a forum somewhere or some other referring site - this one seems to just be a zeitgeist thing. Any theories? I wouldn't think it would be 4E's release, as there aren't actually Barbarians as a class in game. But I guess that could be a factor. Any other guesses?
