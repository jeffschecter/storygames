\n Has anyone here played Corunea? It bills itself as a "Role Card Game", and the website is kind of poorly translated from French. It looks like a very high-production-value take on an idea that's been floating around for a while - a trading card role playing game. If this was good, I'd be really excited by it, but looking over the few cards that are online, I see a lot of big numbers and crunchy-looking gameplay, which is the opposite of what I want from this kind of thing.

Does anyone know any more than me?

Cheers,

    \n There are a few reviews of the game on BoardGameGeek.com that mainly talk about it as a card/dice battle game. I think one of the main ideas is your deck builds your character the same as numbers and words on a character sheet. There are expansions that take you through specific challenges/areas of Corunea, kinda the same way the Raid Decks do for WoW TCG.

    I've requested a review copy from the company in the hopes of looking at the game more from a story-games perspective. I will let you know if I hear back from them.
