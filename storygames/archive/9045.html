    \n Hey, Brendan and Zach...

    I'm trying to toggle a few things based on the playtest observations I had at Gamestorm and Weathertop.
    Expect a new, updated version to be released within a week or two.

    Keeping the opposition points balanced is a tricky thing to do, which is part of my current thinking.
    I'm considering having the GM bank +x points per crime cycle, where x is the round. So if this is the third round of crimes, I bank an additional 3 points.
    Might help some of the rapid growth issues?

    Anyways, there'll be an updated version soon.
