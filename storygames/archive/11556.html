Okay, I'm running 44: A Game of Automatic Fear for ENWorld Gameday on Saturday and i have a few questions.

"You may grab any available Resistance dice to add more dice to your conflict roll. And, any other player with a main character may assign any available Resistance dice to you."

I don't understand the bit about other players assigning Resistance dice. The active player can take as many or as few Resistance dice as they want, right? Why would they need another payer to "assign" them more? I don't get it.

"If the main character you assist wins the conflict roll, place a number of dice in the Trust Fund equal to the total of all triggered Anxieties."

What's a Trust Fund? The term isn't used anywhere else in the text.

Lastly, I wanted some more clarification about Secrets. Are Secrets public knowledge before they are revealed? I.e., does the Director some out and say "Okay, the two Secrets we'll start with are that Section 44 drives black sedans and that the machines never blink." I know Mr. Snyder said that 44 is not a "discovery game," but I wasn't sure if one keeps this info out in the open or not.

Thanks in advance. I'm sure I'll have more questions as I prep for the game during the week.

    Posted By: buzzI don't understand the bit about other players assigning Resistance dice. The active player can take as many or as few Resistance dice as they want, right? Why would they need another payer to "assign" them more? I don't get it.
    \nGood question. The reason lies at the heart of the game. Given limited resources, which of these people (the PCs) deserves help? Is it always your character alone? What kind of person are you that you take all the available dice, to the detriment of others? Alternatively, will you help others at the risk of hurting yourself, given the option?

    It's about the people playing the game much more so than the characters they're imagining.\n
    Posted By: buzz"If the main character you assist wins the conflict roll, place a number of dice in the Trust Fund equal to the total of all triggered Anxieties."
    \nDamn typos! The Trust Fund was the former name of Resistance dice. So ...

    "If the main character you assist wins the conflict roll, add a number of Resistance dice equal to the total of all triggered Anxieties."\n
    Posted By: buzzLastly, I wanted some more clarification about Secrets. Are Secrets public knowledge before they are revealed?
    \nI have always played with secrets as open knowledge among players. It tends to add to the suspense factor. But, I suppose it would be workable that players start to figure it out in the first couple scenes, particularly as you set up each scene with things like "And they're in a black sedan ... "
