\n I've been working on Space Opera style game for a little while now, and I hit a bit of a creative block, so I figured I'd toss it here. The game lies somewhere between Traveller, Star Trek, Firefly and the Vorkosigan series. The primary driving themes are "The galaxy is full of deadly beauty" and "Everyone has someone pulling their strings". The game is divided by the web of inter-faction politics in civilized space and the beauty and danger of 'wild space'. Lots of exploration and space travel.

Careers:
- Military
- Commercial
- Scientific
- Industrial
- Personality
- Clandestine
- Scoundrel
- Cybernetic
- Explorer
- Starfarer

Characters are made by combining two careers to make their "class"; a Military/Clandestine might be an infiltrator or sniper, a Scientific/Scoundrel could be a drug-dealer or performing illegal research, etc. Long story short, each career has 5 skills/abilities to choose from. BUT I'm having trouble coming up with the Starfarer's 5th skill.

Starfarer is all about interstellar travel, especially through civilized space. It is the mirror of the "Explorer" career, which focuses on exploring the unknown and is rather terrestrial-based. Here are the skills that I have so far for the Starfarer
- Cosmopolitan: You know a little bit about every civilized planet
- Opportunity: When in civilized space, you can always find exactly the kind of job you're looking for.
- Pilot: You have a small vehicle (shuttle, fighter) and a hangar bay for it.
- Zero-G Training: You never suffer penalties for being in zero-g.

So, I'm looking for a skill or ability that compliments that career. Something fitting for a Space Opera style game, possibly even inspired by popular sci-fi. If anyone has any suggestions or ideas, I'd really appreciate it.
