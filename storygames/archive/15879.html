\n This is a revised Shock character sheet I whipped up for the zillions of Shock games we play at Story Games Seattle. We've been using it for nearly a year now but I kept forgetting to share. My bad.



I like it better because it helps a player keep track of both their own character and their adversary (protagonist or antagonist). So really there are four characters on that sheet. I also recommend just folding it in half and flipping it over depending on what turn you're playing: hide the stuff that isn't relevent.
