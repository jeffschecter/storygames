\n So... what are folks bringing to GenCon that's not finished? Let's throw down and help each other finish some things up.

I'm running Giger Counter, my survival horror rpg/boardgame (originally a hack of Vincent's Afraid), using Clint's Roanoke as the setting. It's actually running as an IGE slot, since's it's 80% solid, so that's not really an open playtest. But I'd be glad to throw down with other folks, assuming anyone's interested. Zombies, aliens, slashers, ghost vikings... it's all good.

Daniel Solis and I have tentative plans to swap playtests of Do: Pilgrims of the Flying Temple and my freeform Avatar: The Last Airbender game. If anyone wants in on those games, they should track either of us down at the show.

Whatcha got?
