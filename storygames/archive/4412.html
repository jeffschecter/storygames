    \n Allow a former Dukie to reminisce:

    18" sub from Li'l Dino's, wings from Satisfactions (and a Girl Scout Cookie if I'm feeling plucky), cookie from Ninth Street Bakery, deep-dish pizza from Ricci's, and a gourmet meal from Nana's or Magnolia Cafe.

    Can I assume that none of these places exist anymore?

    And, when I was there (graduated in '95), there were no decent game stores of which to speak. I remember having to buy M:tG cards from KB Toys. Has that changed?

    And I once won a life-sized pink pig stuffed animal at the NC State Fair. So top that!
