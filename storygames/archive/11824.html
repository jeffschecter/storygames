\n I've got a very rough and unedited draft for playtesting here: Derelict Delvers
\nI'm looking for people willing to try it out and respond. It is very traditional in most ways, however:
\n
\n
\n1) Does the game capture old school concepts without being bogged down by rules?
\n
\n2) Does the heroism point mechanic work as described? (Does it work as Hit Points have always been described?)
\n
\n3) Should directed (that is class specific) experience be added to each class?
\n
\n4) Anyone have ideas they'd be willing to share on keeping the "D&D" experience as well as creating a more modest item economy?
\n
\n5) Should starships be treated more as another character, or as a tool?
