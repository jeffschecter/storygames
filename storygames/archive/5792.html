\n In the spirit of open, honest criticism, I will, in this thread, give RPGs scores out of ten. I'll tack on to that a one-sentence review.

The Shab Al-Hiri Roach: 9/10 (Gives an offensive portrayal of academics)
Sorceror: 5/10 (Particularly recommended for academics)
Shock:: 8/10 (Too orange)
Lacuna: 6/10 (Last few pages are unreadable)
Spirit of the Century: 7/10 (Book is a bit too thick)
Contenders: 10/10 (Too violent)
Best Friends: 3/10 (Written by a man who is not very feminine at all)
Covenant: 10/10 (Square shape doesn't fit well on my shelf)
Dogs In The Vineyard: 7/10 (Over-Mormoned)
My Life With Master: 8/10 (Generic portrayal of Europe is offensive)
Bacchanal: 8/10 (Completely inappropriate for children's parties)

There you go, Mr Consumer! You're welcome. Hopefully that feedback will lead to better games in the future.

Graham
