    \n Thinking a little further:

    The more information there is written down on these pages, the more frightening it is for the GM to improvise more information: you risk contradictions arising.

    I think I almost wish it was more impressionistic: something like In a Wicked Age...'s oracle elements might be a good inspiration. And connect it with a system that helps bring it to life, like how IaWA's Best Interests help you take any set of oracle elements and bring them to life.
