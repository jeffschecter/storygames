    \n To me, the perfect soundtrack to 3:16 is slower, sludgier and darker than most symphonic metal. Bolt Thrower is a great choice - Warmaster or IVth Crusade are great, chugging slices of molten metal that one could kill aliens to. Other than ye olde Thrower, I'd go for Neurosis around Through Silver in Blood. In a totally different direction, I think that Megadeth's Rust in Peace would make a nice backdrop for carnage among the stars.

    \n Bolt Thrower - check, they definitely own the 40K identification.

    If you sprinkle a bit of comedy, awesome costumes and a dollop of bad taste bad taste you get GWAR.

    On the mean metal side, with an eye on conquering and a very light touch of opera we have Emperor. If you push the opera dial a little further we find Cradle of Filth. And if the battles are meant to be fought in the snow I'd go with Immortal.
