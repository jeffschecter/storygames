\n So, imagonem.org has been putting up stuff daily-ish for a little more than a month now, and it looks like I can keep it up for the time being.

It's mainly Norwegian, with a bit of English thrown in - translations whenever someone can be bothered. The blog takes over from the old webzine at imagonem.net - so far, it covers local gaming events and news, with a bit of reviewing and whatever comes along thrown in. I feel that it hasn't really found its form yet.

The paper magazine hasn't seen print in a while now, but I'm hoping to put one together for the big summer event, Arcon. The theme is zombies, but we'll bite anything.

If you, like the majority of the human species, do not understand Norwegian, google translate might give you the general shape of the content. Got any advice and/or opinions on where to take the project?

If you'd like to contribute to the zine, or have any news you think should go on the blog (I'm watching story games and this category, but I suspect a lot of cool stuff zips right past me) feel free to mail GKraag@gmail.com!

...aaand there's a small gift card prize up for best contribution to the paper edition.
