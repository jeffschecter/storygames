\n TLDR*: SMW, role-playing gamer, seeks 1 hour comedy or fantasy game for good times, running at cons. Must be open to non-exclusive relationship.

TSNC\u2020: So I'm planning on running a bunch of short games during this upcoming con season, basically two ~1 hour games with a break in the middle and maybe, depending on slot size, room for an extra session of whichever one we enjoyed more. For example, I'm planning on running Hot Guys Making Out and Gun Thief as a "sex and violence double feature," because both games pack a lot of great sex, violence, and action into a pretty small timeframe.

For another, I want to run something with Mud Dragon (a goofy comedy game in the spirit of Dragon Magazine backup features). It should take about an hour to play, and ideally either be hilarious or be fantasy at least light-hearted enough to handle being cross scheduled with a game where, I'm serious, laughing so much you can't talk takes up about 1/2 the gameplay time.

Suggestions?

    \n My game about animated sit-coms, Bad Family, is in beta and you can run an episode in under an hour. I could flick you a copy if you'd like.

    I haven't figured out how to pitch it yet, but here are some of its qualities:\n
      \n
    • GM-less
      • \n
    • Pre-generated characters
      • \n
    • The only resolution system is a simple succeed-or-fail mechanic
      • \n
    • Players are rewarded for being quickest to create horrible complications for a spotlighted character
      • \n
    \nI've used it at conventions to create games that feel like Family Guy meets 24, and American Beauty meets American Pie.
