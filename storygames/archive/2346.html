Well I've been working on this game for a long time, and now that I've nailed down play, I need a little more creative juice.

In short, You play an android in the far future trying to be human without any examples. This leads to the creation of a rather odd society of self-labeled 'units' where:

- The next step in escalation from argument is a dance off.
- You are known on occasion to 'touch each other' in order to swap charge.
- Spiders lurk around most corners, which are devolved units ready to eat you. (Eating your comrade is only ok when in extemis)
- Your pocket is actually an energy matrix outside of space and time, from which you compile and decompile your 'stuff'.

And I could continue, but I'm getting carried away. You can see some of the stuff for the game in the directories here: http://fab.wishray.com

So I need help on creating the basic styles of music that each unit calls their own. So far I've got: Power Rock and Electric Funk. I'm looking for styles that grab you, things that you might go: "Hey, I'd want to play a unit with that musical style!".
