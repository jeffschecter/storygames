    \n The Game of Thrones Fiasco playset works incredible well if you play it with a serious GoT-like tone, no need to get Cohn or gonzo at all. Always play it from the main character\u2019s perspective, with the \u201cgood\u201d white-die being equivalent to the main character getting what they want in a given scene and the \u201cbad\u201d black-die being equivalent to the main character not getting what they want in a given scene. The current main character should always want something out of the scene (even if it\u2019s horrible for them in the larger picture) and the white or black die determine if they get it via player vote. It works very well.
