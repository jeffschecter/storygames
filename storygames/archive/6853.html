    Posted By: Callan S.Can you get the actual stats for monsters? Or is it not like 3E, where they were freely available?

    On reflection, what really helped launched D&D3 for me was that someone made a dungeon generator which also gave the stats.
    \nNo, the 4th ed SRD doesn't contain any actual rules. Just info for third party publishers on what they can and can't use in their products.

    (The 3.5 rules being freely available made WotC at least a couple hundred dollars out of our playing group. None of us wanted to but the books to upgrade from 3.0, but we started using the 3.5 rules for the fixed ranger and Bard, and it eventually convinced us all to buy the actual books. This wouldn't happen with the 4th ed SRD.)
