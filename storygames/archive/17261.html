\n So I've been screwing around with inklewriter, a free, web-app-based Interactive Fiction authoring and reading tool. It uses a choice-based interaction style, and it's main selling point is how extremely accessible it is: no registration required to mess around, just start banging out paragraphs. It's hard not to get sucked in.

Judging by the Stuff to Watch threads, there's been some marginal interest in computer-based story gaming around these parts, so I thought you guys might like it. Thoughts?

(By the way, you can find a quick rundown of inklewriter by prominent IF author Emily Short here)
