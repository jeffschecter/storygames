So My Games Name Sucks

ndp
May 2006 edited May 2006 in Story Games
So I've been told on good authority that Carry is a sucky name for my game. My argument ("But I love the name!") is easily countered by these naysayers ("Thats why you need to think about changing it. Sacred cows, blah blah blah") Basically, it's an awesomely evocative name once you know what the game's about, but if you don't, it's completely meaningless.

Basically, as GenCon promo season starts to warm up, I don't want to be selling a product with a different fucking name then the "buzz," such as it is.

So, wonderous minds of Story Games, help me find a better name. If you want to take a look at it, the last-updated public version is here. It may be easier to look at the actual play links at the top of this page.
