Has anyone heard of the Die RPG?

WarriorMonk
March 14 in Stuff to Watch
It's soon to come from Kieron Gillen's, the creator of the Die comic. Dunno how many here are into comics but he does a great work on this one in terms of rpg experiences seen through time and age. The pitch for the comic at the Image page (the publishers) goes like this:

DIE is a pitch-black fantasy where a group of forty-something adults have to deal with the returning unearthly horror they barely survived as teenage role-players. If Kieron’s in a rush, he describes it as “Goth Jumanji.” That only captures a sliver of what you’ll find in oversized debut issue—where fantasy gets all too real.

Here's an article in Polygon about it.
