\n Thinking about using the card idea, plus an even softer Tilt and Aftermath, to use Fiasco with younger kids, possibly in a teaching setting. The playset would implicitly be based around the Diary of a Wimpy Kid books and movies, with the serial numbers filed off.

So far the idea I've got cooking is that each card has a "Think about it!" section. So, for instance, a card might look like this:

-------------------------------------------------------------------
Relationship: Friends

Best Friends

Think About It!
* How long have you been best friends?
* What do you like to do that your friend doesn't?
* How do your parents feel about your friendship?
* Does one or the other of you try to get you to do
things together that you shouldn't?
-------------------------------------------------------------------

... It's a start, which is why it's here in Little Ideas.
