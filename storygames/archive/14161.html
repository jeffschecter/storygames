    \n There is, of course, a Dark Wizard.

    But they didn't do either of those things.

    The Dark Wizard in question is a dirty little secret of house Ravenclaw - a wizard specializing in fire and healing, who has been alive for hundreds of years, using a Phoenix as part of an evil spell of renewal. He (she?) is going to the school to steal Fawkes, the familiar of one Albus Dumbledore.

    The Dark Wizard is pursued by a member of Ravenclaw, who has been tracking him invisibly, along with a frosty pet to counter the fire magic. Unfortunately, our intrepid hero has fallen on the wrong side of the school, and they assume that there's only one bad guy.

    Naturally, our young heroine will meet this misunderstood do-gooder, and if all goes well, the story will be explained. Should that occur, the do-gooder, of course, will be caught and imprisoned shortly after, leaving our heroine as the only one who understands the truth.

    As it should be.
