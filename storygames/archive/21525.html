\n I am currently designing my Dream RPG System and I have a problem I haven't been able to solve. I've been flipping between a game that uses a straight Token auction to resolve conflicts and another mechanic that is almost the same, but it uses Cards.

The only real difference would be that the Tokens will be able to make it easier to play online, while the Cards will add some randomness to the game. So please, reach out with your feelings and tell me which one is more attractive to you.

If you have any questions, feel free to post them here.
