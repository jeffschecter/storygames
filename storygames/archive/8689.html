\n Hey folks,

I'm organizing the 6 pm Saturday Indie Party at Dreamation this year. Mendel's girlfriend is going to cater it for is, so, awesome! On the down side, we're not allowed to have "real food." Kat went back and forth and we got this definition of unreal food: "as it's not meal-ish, we're good. so fruit tray, cheese tray, we're good." This is apparently a hotel restriction.

So, the food will accommodate vegans and vegetarians as well. Mendel's girlfriend figured it'd be $7 per person. Last year, 80 people showed. What we're planning on doing, therefore, is to ask people to Paypal Mendel whatever money you're comfortable with doing so that she can buy the stuff she needs up-front (and so that we're minimizing the money we collect on-site). We thought having 20 "oops I didn't know, can I pay now to get in?" slots where people would pay when they got there would be sensible.

I'm going to ask Mendel to post here when the monetary requirements ($560) have been met. I'm sorry this is so ragged and last-minute. There were lots of coordination problems happening unfortunately.

So, in brief:

1) Paypal Mendel (at his psu.edu email account, which he is mxs485 at)
2) Tell your friends
3) Spread the word (including reposting this post)
