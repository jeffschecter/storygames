Shadows is now under Creative Commons

Zak Arntson
August 2006 edited August 2006 in Directed Promotion
Well, not a huge deal, but I figured I should announce this somewhere. And being a constant lurker here, I figure this is the best place.

I've released Shadows under the Creative Commons Attribution License. I'm hoping this can breathe some new life into the game, what with its newfound ability to be printed and disperesed.

Clinton, I know you've released your games under the Creative Commons. How have you seen it affect your audience (if at all)?
