\n I know this isn't really Story Games fodder, but pretend this conversation is taking place furtively out back by the dumpster.

Suppose I had a stack of review copies of a really excellent European indie board game that had yet to make an impact in the States. If you were in this situation, who are the people you'd contact about reviewing it? Board gaming podcasters, luminaries, reviewers, and so forth. You can assume this scrappy game is roundly excellent and innovative and fun, which is why, theoretically, I'd be helping them out as a rabid fan.
