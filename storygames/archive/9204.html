    \n I wouldn't call it the best NBP we've had yet. The wind was harsh, and we had fewer people than usual who Brought Game as opposed to just wanting to join game. But it was still a good time, and I'm looking forward to the next one in late September.

    Yeah, I went up to Hearst Castle on Saturday morning. That place is insane. Imagine you've got an art history degree, a good architect, an unlimited budget, and lived in an era where people were much more cavalier about selling their cultural heritage. Now go build your dream house.
