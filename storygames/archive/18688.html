    \n I absolutely have. Apart from the fact my 9-year old daughter regularly GMs me in a 1-on-1 freeform game, there's one story I'm quite fond of from several years before.

    A friend of mine was wanting to play a "good" character for a change (having played a Ratman gang member for some time). I'd always wanted to play a spider, so that got me ruminating on the idea of a "good" spider. I imagined one that was had feelings of longing and protection for a village of humans who were either unaware or suspicious of it.

    Later, I was giving my daughter a bath, and I explained the concept to her. I said, "If you were a frightening spider and you wanted people to love you, what would you do?"

    She said instantly, "I would cover myself in pretty things." I thought that was brilliant.

    Thus was born 'Shirat of Arton Village'.
