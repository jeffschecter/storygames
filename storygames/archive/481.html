The Epicaric Virago Manifesto

Mo
April 2006 edited April 2006 in Story Games
I am an Epicaric Virago! I don't fear the pain, I eat it up! I don't need you to bring it to me, the whip is in my hand and singing my Geisslerlieder like a Wagnerian opera, I will bring it on myself! Can you say the same?

I am an Epicaric Virago! I do not need to beat you in order to feel like I have won. I find my challenge by lifting you up and allowing you to be weak so that you can bring on and savor your own pain. I know that strength is tested at the foundations, and so I build groups that can not and will not abandon each other. Can you say the same?

I am an Epicaric Virago! I play right out on the precipice where the ground gives away. Love, death, violence, power, sex, betrayal and the foul rag and bone shop of the human heart: I want it all! Vividly! Viscerally! Nothing is verboten because I trust that we can recover each other. Can you say the same?

I am an Epicaric Virago! I am no stoic. I am open and affected and emotionally engaged by and with this: I breathe, I scream, I hurt, I fuck, I fight and I cry along with it before I step back and marvel at the exquisite badness that we have wrought together. It is not something I watch, it is something I AM for a time. I know that it can change me just like I can change it. Can you say the same?

I am an Epicaric Virago! I'm no wallflower or a shrinking violet: I hunt down the pain and joy, the sound and the fury. I am the Mistress of the tragic flaw. Can you say the same?
