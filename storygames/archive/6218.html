    \n I am going to go against the grain here and say that you could distribute the master to whoever isn't in the scene. I think that this would have two advantages. First since you have all agreed on who and what the master is, it would give you a chance to see that the aspects you most enjoy keep being hit. The second is that it keeps the other players completely in the game even when it isn't their scene.

    Remember that the master has to be completely cruel but if you do it to your friends they can do it back to you.

    \n I think that the whole "If you are cruel then your friends can do it back to you" would -radically- change the dynamic of the game.

    Part of the point of the Master is that he has all the power, and you have no recourse. It's meant to be abusive and unfair.

    I'm not saying that you couldn't have a fun game distributing the role, but I think that people are going to enact it and react to it in fundamentally different ways.
