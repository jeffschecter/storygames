\n JiffyCon returns to Western Massachusetts on January 23rd, 2011, and we're looking for folks to run games.

We have a fabulous new space in Thornes Marketplace's "Dynamite Space" in beautiful downtown Northampton, Massachusetts.

In past JiffyCons we've featured locally grown games like Time and Temp, Shooting the Moon, Misspent Youth, 1001 Nights, and Shock:, games in development that such as Tales of the Fisherman's Wife, and board games like Mechaton. We love both the old school and the new fangled. We offer games for kids as young as 8, games for a general audience, and games strictly for adults. Our attendees have come from far and wide throughout New England and the Middle States, and our attendance has steadily increased over the past three years. We couldn't have done it without the kind folks who GM, DM, and facilitate GM-less goodness.

I don't have a deadline set for GM signup, but it will likely be Deadline will be mid-December--12/15, in fact. Signup for the con itself will begin in November. If you email me, I'll keep you posted on either date, and I'll update the information here.

So bring on the games--the fully completed and published games, the ashcans, and the 5 page playtests! If you have any questions, please check out the JiffyCon Website, JiffyCon Facebook Page or email Julia at stonebabygames at gmail dot com.

Hope to see you in January.
Julia, JiffyCon planning chair and GM wrangler

I had a really terrible experience 2 or 3 JiffyCons ago where I basically lost out on an entire slot of gaming because the GM had brought a game that was in such an early playtest stage that play broke down within 5 minutes.
