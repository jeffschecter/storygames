    \n Okay, so, I use CMapTools.

    From the just-finished campaign:
    After three sessions
    Some later point
    After seven sessions
    After eight sessions

    I wouldn't, of course, have bothered to change it if it wasn't for the ease of click-and-drag, and bothered at all not so much for keeping track of changing relationships so much as having an "OK, so who are the significant NPCs?" visual aid that was more glance-catching than just a plain list. (Thus also the lack of relationship-nature-specifications in later versions...)

    (Yes, it's under bork bork bork-encryption...)

    A bit fiddlier than using graphviz, but experiments with the latter has shown me the results tend to come out... not quite ideal for visual reference. :-)

    (ed: These are not proper r-maps in the strict edwardsian sense. Conflict webs?)
