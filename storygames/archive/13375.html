\n Saw this linked over on RPG Haven: As a bag fan of the West End Games d6 system from back in the day (my copy of the WEG "d6" generic book was worn to tatters from use) I was expecting (for free, as that's what the PDF is) a pile of scattered home notes, maybe a crude illustration or two.

But actually, it looks *really* well done: In rules (d6 but No Quite), in layout and illustration (the art is awesome, actually), in sample support (a kind of Staw Wars-ish setting hack used as an example)... it's really pretty cool. If generic games are your thing, or you liked d6 back in the day, it's worth a glance.

http://antipaladingames.com/minisix.html

-Andy
