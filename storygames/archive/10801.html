    \n Played session 2, very satisfying. I'm really starting to like Cyrus Vance, and in fact I came within inches of re-purchasing my secret longing for Lady Blackbird. At the last second I really needed a medic, though, so there I went.

    We struggle a bit with conditions (besides tired and injured), and with keeping track of stuff we made off-hand in-game. Sometimes we were pushing and it wasn't clear how much things should push back, so it felt a little like I was pushing a helium balloon.

    Mike (veritascitor, GM) is singularly talented at taking names we make up while we talk in character and turning them into characters / places / things that are highly entertaining.

    We had a couple of refreshment scenes and I loved them.

    Mechanically, Peter noticed that it felt like it did most of A Fairy's Tale better than A Fairy's Tale did. I think that's true except for the fact that A Fairy's Tale defined enemy stats and challenge ratings, and strangely I felt I missed that.
