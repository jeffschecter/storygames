\n About a year ago, I was working at second-hand bookstore. I was browsing through a commemorative volume depicting the adventures of the Palestine Exploration Society, a group of mostly German-Jewish archaeologists that was established in the early 20th century, and was active during the time of the British Mandate for Palestine (1919-1948). I found one photograph particularly appealing: I want to make a short scenario of archaeological pulp ripe with ancient demons, secretive cults, trap-infested ruins and a dastardly power-thirsty villain based on the guys in that photograph.

You can view it here (large-ish file). And here the expedition members are numbered, for easy reference.

And here are my questions. Feel free to make up as many outlandish details as you deem necessary:
1. Which of these guys looks like he would be fun or interesting to play as protagonist? What can you tell me about him?
2. Which of these guys looks like he would make a good villain? What can you tell me about him?
3. Can you make out any interesting relationships between any of these guys?
4. Would you add female characters to this group? If so, who would they be, and where are they when the photograph is being taken?
