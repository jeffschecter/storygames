\n Want to learn how to market your small press game on a small budget?
\n
\nAwesome! Click here: http://a.nnotate.com/php/pdfnotate.php?d=2008-08-19&c;=e5QBokLB
\n
\nAt Gencon Luke Crane (creator of Burning Wheel) organized an all day Small Press RPG seminar covering the basics from creating a game to writing, designing, publishing, marketing, and financing. Luke Crane is a beautiful man and in the spirit of Luke's enthusiasm and willingness to help others, I'm making my marketing notes available for free.
\n
\nBut this is only the beginning.
\n
\nNot only are these notes available but you can add to them. In fact, I encourage you to! Leave comments and feel free to reply to other people's comments. Please add:
\n
\n-\tQuestions.
\n-\tClarifications.
\n-\tCorrections.
\n-\tAdditional solutions.
\n-\tRequests for more information.
\n-\tAnd anything else you feel would be helpful to the indie RPG community.
\n
\nPeriodically I will look through your additions and incorporate them directly into the document as well as expand on any requests for additional information.
\n
\nIf you would like to me to keep you updated, email me at jenskot AT gmail DOT com with the subject [Marketing] how to...
\n
\nEventually I may expand this into a blog or book which will also include detailed tutorials. But I don't want to make any promises! At the very least I wanted to start here and get something out to all of you that can be immediately useful. Enjoy!
\n
\nGet started here: http://a.nnotate.com/php/pdfnotate.php?d=2008-08-19&c;=e5QBokLB
\n
\nBest,
\nJohn
