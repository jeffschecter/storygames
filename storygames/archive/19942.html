\n I was looking at our huge collection of actual play threads from our meetups at Story Games Seattle and thought other people might want to take a look. It\u2019s nearly 500 game reports, going back to 2010.

I hate it when you hear about a game but can\u2019t find info about how it plays. If that\u2019s you, take a look because we might have taken it for a spin.

What We Played @ Story Games Seattle

We focus on GMless story games, so that\u2019s the kind of games you\u2019re going to find. There\u2019s a search feature in case you trying to hunt down a particular game instead of just wading through story after story after story\u2026
