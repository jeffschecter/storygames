    I might suggest something like looking to price a POD at about 2.5 to 3 times its printing cost, minimum.   • Luke_Wheel

    Joe, typically the print runs for these games are very small -- less than 1000 copies for the life of the game. And you'll be printing in gulps of 100 or so. Therefore, you've must factor your costs for art and editing into your base production cost for the book.

    Take the cost of production, art and editing and add 'em all up. Divide by 100, 200, 500 and 1000. Find a number which makes you comfortable -- that's your "break even point." Take that number and multiply by 4. That's your MSRP.

    And that's my jaded, tired and short answer for the day.
    -L
