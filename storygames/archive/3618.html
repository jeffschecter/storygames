    \n That's typically (as I understand it, and feel free to correct me) a combination of material printed on and coating.     \n Heh - no. Aside from the thickness issues, the PVC would act like a great big magnet to printer toner once it was hit with the laser. Almost all ID printers are thermal based films.

    I have to second (third? fourth?) the local printer. I've been looking for someone to print DEAD cards online for about 6-8 months, and I haven't found anyone who can give me a better price than I can get right down the street, with the added headache of handling proofs.
