    Posted By: chearnsAndy could tell you for sure, but I think you have like twenty minutes to respond from the moment you open the page or you get an error when you click Add your comments.
    \nYeah, that's a regular thing. PHP (the code) has a timeout value of 20 minutes. So if you have a session open for 20 minutes, you'll get that error that says "There was a problem with authentication" or something. There's no problem there, though, all the text is right there, just hit Submit again to push your comment through and stay logged in.

    This was actually the reason I removed the "Preview" button: If people hit Submit and get that PHP session timeout, all their text is still there. With the Preview extension, it would eat your text, which was monstrously frustrating.
