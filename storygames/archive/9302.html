\n We were playing AD&D 2e Ed last night. It was Dave's film noir scenario of a dame caught in a tight spot between the mage's guild and a necromancer, and someone had killed her husband. We were the poor saps dealing with it. Except unlike Marlow, we'd rolled most of our stats on 3d6 so were a motley crue of social misfits, thieves to a man.

At one point Simon, Graham and I had a discussion, in character, during a fight, about the noise that the bad guy's sword made when the Swashbuckler disarmed him and it flew into a wall.

Simon's wife, who is fairly used to our brand of madness, and happened to be passing said 'What on Earth are you doing?' and burst out laughing.

I think my "Thud-ud-ud-ud-ud" carried the day.

And then I punched the wizard in the face, cos I'd had the training and that's what you do. Punch the wizard ... in the face.
