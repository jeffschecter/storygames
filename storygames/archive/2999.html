    \n A buddy programmed this die roller:
    \n
    \nhttp://www.math.tu-clausthal.de/~madow/DIESERVER/
    \n
    \nYou can use passwords to protect your dierolls, while still be able to see 100 lines of past dierolls. Players can use theirs or their characters names too. And they can write a comment to each roll to identify, what it represents.
    \n
    \nThe die roller supports even weird dice like FUDGE dice, it can do "exploding" dice, it can drop dice and can count successes.
