\n Hey everyone!

Exploding Kingdoms has a new version ready for your gaming pleasure. It features full rules, pregen characters, and an adventure ready to go (Search for the Puce Apocrypha), as well as rules for making your own characters and playing a campaign in Kingdom Mode.

Exploding Kingdoms has all the tactical goodness of the most recent editions of games like D&D, streamlining some things that suck (like attack rolls), and adding things that are awesome (like Pixie Explodomancers). It takes the genre of metal fantasy and turns it up to 11.

Go download it already! Why aren't you playing Exploding Kingdoms right now?
