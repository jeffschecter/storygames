Okay, so I've been talking with a close friend and regular player/GM in my group. Specifically, (since I've been watching a couple zombie movies and re-reading The Walking Dead) about zombie genre stuff, and other geeky stuff like Battlestar and what not. You know how these conversations wander around.

What came out of it was the realization that we both kind of like stories where:

* The world gets turned upside down.
* Civilization crumbles.
* You face the threat of oblivion, both individually and as a culture/species.
* The survivors try to retain their humanity and their lives, sometimes one costing the other.

Which, interestingly, are elements we discovered at the core of both good zombie stuff and BSG.

So... now I'm sitting back and thinking about which games could really capture the kind of setting/story we're talking about -- as seen in BSG or The Walking Dead or 28 Days Later or whatever, and picking your brains for input.

Feel me picking? Pick pick pick.
