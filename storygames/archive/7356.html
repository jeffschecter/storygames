\n
\n After the successful release of Thou Art But A Warrior at this year's GenCon, Tasty Bacon Games is moving on to a new project - an RPG of truly epic proportions. Many RPGs have promised open-ended design, and failed to deliver. But Tasty Bacon Games will deliver where other companies have failed: STAB! STAB! STAB!!. STAB! STAB! STAB!! delivers truly innovative and ground-breaking game design to an indie games movement that has seen only stagnation and standardization.

STAB! STAB! STAB!! is in the final stages of development, and should be available through IPR before Christmas. For a limited time, a special preview edition is available here. Details concerning a pre-order will be announced soon.

If you have any questions or comments, feel free to contact me on my blog.
