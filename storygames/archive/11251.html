    \n He does the second. Also 3.5 hours of audio creates a 2gb recording so this might not be a great solution if hard drive space is a problem for you. There is a skype call recorder that you can download as well. They will let you demo this program for a week for free as well. It worked really well when I first used it. I wouldn't recommend using Ambrosia's Wiretap program however. I tried it for a little while, but it doesn't record the audio coming from the person recording the call.

    \n I used Pamela Pro and HATED it because the incoming and outgoing audio would record out of synch and sometimes the channels would echo onto each other at a reduced volume so you would hear me on right channel and then a half second later, you would hear me again on left channel, but quieter, and vice versa. It was a nightmare to edit.

    I bought PowerGrammo based on Ryan Macklin's recommendation and I'm extremely happy with it, been using it over a year now.
