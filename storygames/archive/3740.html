\n I got a call from Greg Stolze this morning with the news. Apparently, Thomas had a sudden seizure in his mother's kitchen and died shortly thereafter. Still awaiting further details, but I just thought some folks here would like to know.

Thomas illustrated tons of stuff for D&D, Feng Shui, Legend of the Five Rings, Vampire, Promethean and Unknown Armies.

You can find more of his work on his site.

    \n Thomas was a good friend of mine and we worked together on 40 different occasions. He loved film and we talked often about directors, colors, sets, and independent film making. He was amazingly creative, moved to paint by other artists in all mediums. His work reflected a darker tone and a love for more monstrous monsters. I watched his work evolve over the last 8 years and now I'll never get to see him paint again.

    I miss him already.

    Anyone interested in mailing condolences to the family can contact me off list.
