\n Hi.

Don't get what it is with tsoy, but that game just forces me to fiddle with it. When having a scene that does not work out right, the response is no longer "hmm. I should play it out differently next time" but instead "Wonder if I can make rules to help me out here?".

The scene in question was a refresh scene where a character goes ring-fighting after an insult. And gets promptly beaten up. Now what? If I was a good gm there would be consequences which the player or I could visit later. What I got from that is just one NPC and some harm on the player, which seems a bit flat.

Also, I've noticed another peculiarity of tsoy: bonus/penalty dice are distributed unevenly. It is not surprising to have some 5+ bonus dice at one go, but cases where you have 2 penalty dice seems rather rare. I think that there is room for bit more.

What I thought might be nice is "harm cards". Do away with the harm track and replace with harm cards. These are names for the bad stuff that happen to you and a mechanical effect which can be invoked by any player or gm. There are basically two kinds: ones that give you either bonus or penalty die, depending on the situation and the ones that give you penalty die and if you invoke them yourself you get 1 XP. There is a buyoff condition like the keys. You don't get xp but you get rid of the harm. Or you can keep it and invoke it for xp or compassion dice as long as you like.

Design idea is to provide tool for inspiration and including pains and hurts in the narration. They each cover a smaller area with penalty dice, so you can have several without getting totally unusable character.

(Invoking = including in the narration and tagging for mechanical effect.)

They are a freeform tool in sense that some are worse than others and it is totally left to players and gm to decide what are included in stakes. You can even select a few and then have the player pull out predefined number of them.

You drop out of btdps if you get five. You can include them in btpb stakes when changing goals.



As an afterthought, I added death card as well as death of companion. I'm doubtful of these, though they have certain oomph! in them.
If you'd ever do cthulhu tsoy (why!) you could probably steal various insanities as well.. Though.. Insanities would be very cool for my Conan campaign with necromancers.. hmm.

What do you think? Got more ideas for hurts and pains? I would need a bunch, especially social ones. (If you do I might make a printable cards out of them for everyones pleasure.. Naturally with better quality text than these proof of concept ones. )

    \n To expand on what Mark is saying, I would like to see an economy behind these cards so that you can generate them on the fly and then color them as appropriate.

    I am thinking that you start with a name and description of the penalty imposed.
    Create a cost for the harm by deciding how steep the penalty is and how difficult it is to buy off.
    Then you "pay" for it by adding a Cost to Place and/or 1 or more XP conditions.

    This ends up giving you a well rounded mini-key and as you play with them you both create a menu of harms that can be picked from or you can still create a new one.

    Format would be something like:
    (the numbers are just an example and only vaguely follow any sort of rules)

    Name: In-The-Face
    Description: You got it in the face this time .. It hurts .. and it shows.
    Cost to Place: 3 Vigor Harm (-3 pts)
    Penalty: 1 Die to All Social Interactions (+2 pts for 1 Die, +1 pt for Social, +2 pts for All)
    1 XP: When you mention how bad you look (max 3 per session) (-1 pt)
    2 XP: When your injury could get you into more trouble (-2 pts)
    Buy Off: 2 Reason (+1 pt)

    Does this seem like the best way to go? If so, what sort of formulations should go into the menu?

    \n Thanks for input!

    I agree on making them all pretty much uniform 1xp/1 penalty with some special ones thrown in the mix.
    I am dubious on two things. First is formula to generate these, which atleast I am not qualified to make. Even if you'd give a price, the 'balance' would entirely depend on how the play advances. It is the same with current harm tracks as well. Sometimes having it hurts and other times not so much.

    Second is the XP bonus. Harm does not cost you a thing (*) and you can be forced to take some. I really don't want these to be things you like to collect but something you perhaps flaunt for few scenes and then try to get rid of. Ie. A real disadvantage but with a little something to encourage you to show it up. Would you really start collecting these while playing?

    (*) Actually getting a wound costs you the time in play to get in a fight.


    And to summarize what I've got so far:
    * 1 XP (max 3 per wound? per session?) for incorporating the wound into narration
    * Each wound can be tagged by the opponent/other players for a penalty, whenever.
    * Five of these is way too much! Either print out empty cards that count towards limit if pulling these from a deck, or give them out when you progress in harm tracks
    * They seem like good stakes for non-bdtb tests.
    * The buyoff seems to be the interest point of these: How do I get rid of this wound? (No, you don't get anything extra for buying it off)
    * No playtesting yet, since I want to get feel for tsoys wound mechanism before altering it.
    * Require a physical card to work.

    Can't really see how they work as refreshes.. But that gave me an idea: Require refreshes to
    get rid of these. Ie. Going to the doc who sews you up is a refresh (Do I make things too easy for the players? Will every refresh scene be one with a doctor?)

    I've got the following planned, but I'm still uncertain about pricing. (Can produce a pdf if someone wants):
    Rumour
    There is a nasty rumour going about you..
    Buyoff: 1 invocation.

    Afraid
    You're scared..
    1 Penalty die for dealing with object of your fear
    1 XP for invoking
    buyoff: 2 Reason OR confront the fear

    Fear
    It grips you like steel! Cannot run.. Cannot
    breathe!
    1 Penalty die for dealing with object of your fear
    1 XP for invoking
    buyoff: 2 Reason OR confront the fear

    Shame
    You should have done better! Feels like you cannot show your face again..
    1 Penalty die for dealing with the topic which you incurred the shame
    1 XP for invoking
    buyoff: 1 instinct

    Loss of Face
    You've embarassed yourself beyond forgiveness!
    1 Penalty die for dealing with the people who know of your shame
    1 XP for invoking
    buyoff: 3 instinct

    Bad Reputation
    It is no longer a rumour, it is a reputation!
    1 Penalty die for dealing with strangers
    1 XP for getting hit by the rumour
    Buyoff: Sufficient change of location

    Bad impression
    Someone has a really bad impression of you
    1 Penalty die for social dealings with him
    1 XP for invoking
    Buyoff: Refresh scene with that person.

    Wounded relationship
    One of your relationships is becomes hurtful and you cannot think straight anymore..
    1 Penalty die to deal amicably when the 'other' is involved
    1 XP to invoke
    Buyoff: 1 reason

    Infected relationship
    One of your relationships is becomes hurtful and you cannot think straight anymore..
    1 Penalty die to deal amicably when the 'other' is involved
    1 XP to invoke
    Buyoff: Let go of the relationship.

    Broken arm
    You could hear the crack of bones when violent wave of pain washed over your arm..
    1 Penalty die to work or combat
    1 XP for invoking
    Buyoff: 1 Vigor AND a one invocation.

    In-the-leg
    Instantly you find yourself down on your knee. The wave of pain comes later..
    1 Penalty die for things including moving around
    1 XP for invocation
    Buyoff: 1 Vigor AND a one invocation.

    A Cut
    You are slashed open and bleeding profusely. It is not the searing pain..
    It is seeing the life drawn out of you..
    1 Penalty die for anything
    1 XP for invocation
    Buyoff: 3 Vigor or Refresh scene

    Punctured!
    You're bleeding and hurting.
    1 Penalty die for anything
    1 XP for invocation
    Buyoff: 2 Vigor or Refresh scene

    In-the-Head
    You suddenly see dazzling lights and feel nausea. The world spins.
    1 Penalty die for reasoning and combat
    1 XP for invocation
    Buyoff: 1 Vigor or Refresh

    Fever!
    You tremble with cold and shiver with heat. 1 Penalty die for anything
    1 XP for invocation
    Buyoff: 2 Vigor

    Deathly Fever!
    You tremble with cold and shiver with heat. You look terribly pale.
    1 Penalty die for anything
    1 XP for invocation
    Buyoff: 5 Vigor
