\n Principia (my Renaissance war of Ideas game) is going to include larger-than-life pre-generated characters of historical figures of the Renaissance: folks like Machiavelli, Leonardo, John Dee, and Sir Francis Walsingham. But I am finding few female characters to draw up. Anyone have some suggestions? My focus settings are Florence in the time of the Medicis, and England under Elizabeth I, but I'm open minded to other eras and locations as well.
