\n Resolutions are the cliche of Jan. 1st. People resolve to lose weight, stop smoking, use their time more productively, etc. I'd like to see a game where making resolutions/causing people to drop their resolutions/you assign resolutions to other players is the focus. Maybe you are mental states personified (Doubt, Willpower) and you play to influence the resolve of a person. I'd love to see what you come up with for this idea.

    \n Gather a group of players. Find the two who are most equal in weight. Assign one of them to be Higher Nature and the other to be Base Nature.

    Everyone else roleplays a character with several resolutions. Whenever an opportunity arises for a character to either uphold or forsake a resolution, they yell "Fight!" The two Nature players then wrestle until one of them is pinned for 5 seconds.

    As they wrestle, the character player interprets each shift in advantage and describes the shift in their character's inner monolgue. Rather than counting down 5-4-3-2-1, the character player counts down AND-THAT'S-WHAT-I'LL-DO! The desire of the winning Nature is then enacted by the character; the resolution is upheld or forsaken.

    Play continues until one Nature player is too sore or tired to continue.
