\n They call it the elevator pitch.

Here's the idea, since we are all strapped for time/attention and sometimes people may miss a project they would otherwise be interested in and frankly there are a lot of games in development.

Lets create a list of current games in development that are requesting input.

The Rules:
-Your entire post must be100 words or less.
-No other posts allowed. If you want to follow up on a project, follow their provided link.

Your post should Include:
-A brief description.
-What input you are seeking.
-A link.
-A deadline.
