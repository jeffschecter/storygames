\n All you left-coasters who are coming out to Nerdly Beach Party at San Simeon State Beach Park, now's the time to register.

Registration for the inaugural Nerdly Beach Party is $50. The registration price gets you:

* Campsite space at San Simeon State Beach Park
* Nerdly Beach Party Teeshirt by Remi Treuer
* Food: Friday lunch and dinner, Saturday breakfast and lunch, Sunday breakfast and lunch (Saturday dinner is BYOM)
* All the gaming you can shake a stick at

Any extra cash will be paid forward to the next Nerdly Beach Party to defray costs, provide "gamerships," and make things even better next time around.

And just one Nerdly party-goer to another, your registration fees immediately turn around into the capital we're using to set up the event, so the sooner you register, the less we stress out.

You can register here.
