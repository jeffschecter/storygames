      Posted By: DenysDead of Night really interests me as I've no experience with it. That you're running it raises its stature in my mind....
      \nThat my opinion carries that much weight with you is flattering.

      Dead of Night is part of a family of games I've taken an interest in lately that I feel get ignored. These games are not particularly socially or politically "edgy" nor particularly mechanically unique and so get ignored by the "Story Games" crowd. However, they are also not wide open, YOU CAN DO ANYTHING, games where a GM can just pastes his preferred techniques over generic mechanics and so are equally ignored by the "traditional" crowd.

      These games have vision, focus and purpose. They just aren't particularly challenging about it. The fact that they get so little love, makes me sad.

      Dead of Night is a little machine for churning out horror movie stuff.

      I'd put Dread (the non-Jenga one) and Spite in this family as well. Both of which are basically kick-ass survival horror video games on paper. I'd run one of these games at the convention as well but I lack the confidence to learn them well enough in time. One game I haven't played before at a time.

      Jesse
