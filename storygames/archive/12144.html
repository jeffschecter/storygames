    \n 1. All fronts start with a Scarcity: I think there can be at least one or two types of scarcity in the town. Someone needs something and that threatens the PCs. What is it? Who is it?
    Maybe have one scarcity on the outside, like a pack of hungry cannibals, keep it simple.
    Maybe the mutant hyenas. Throw in a Breeding Pit.

    2. Look for things that are already shaping up:
    -Who or what is the Crimson Kite? Gang? Grotesque? What does it do?
    -How does Kettle feel about being stared down in front of his gang?
    -Who steps in as the new hardholder? How does he feel about the Gunlugger?
    -How do the previous hardholder's lackeys feel about the situation?
    -etc.

    Not all of these need to be fronts but can be parts of fronts or simply on the home front.
