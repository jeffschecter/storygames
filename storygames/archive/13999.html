\n Hi there folks,

We all know books and movies are the most common source of inspiration for games. But have you ever had the urge to play the very story depicted by a single picture? A piece of visual art so moving or evocative, you couldn't just leave it alone? And I mean - the story on the picture, not behind it.

I've almost had it with an icon we all know - Edvard Munch's Skrik. Sligtly less - with the Gauguin's Tahiti cycle. But the cliche factor kept me from doing anything with it. Today I've stumbled upon a picture which made me like: "I WANT TO PLAY IT NAO!". Seriously.
By the way - does anyone know who is the author of this illustration?
