    Posted By: GrahamSee that pub? That's my pub! I wrote about it and then Jerome did a picture of it. You've no idea how happy that makes me.

    Graham
    \nBeing involved in a cycle of art is really cool! I love that feeling.

    Part of the reason that I wrote Ribbon Drive is because I was inspired by the stories that Jackson told me about his travels.
    I then asked him if I could include Towards Oblivion on the mix CD that Ribbon Drive is sold with.
    He said yes, but also wrote a new song about Ribbon Drive. I use the word "about" loosely, but it's about road trips and friendship and the end of the song is literally "you have a name, a compliment of traits, two futures - to choose, or to refuse."

    Art inspiring art inspiring art, this back and forth between friends as they create something. Yeah! I love that feeling.
