\n Along with working on my first comic book mini-series (Conspiracy of Shadows: Betrayal, available at GenCon bitches!), I have been steadily writing up and posting to my wiki, what I can only describe as a broader version of the rules for Conspiracy of Shadows, which I call the The Descriptor System.

My goal for the project is to take some of the things I have learned about game design, elements of my favorite games, and mix them into a system that scratches me where I itch. The whole things is going to be free online and once done, put under the Creative Commons Share Alike License, just like Conspiracy of Shadows. Every Tuesday I post a new section of rules and would love some feedback if folks have got the time.

    \n I'm a big fan of pregs for one shots. To personalize it I would let each player define their own Doom (and maybe their Drive) and do the Group Kicker together to tie everyone's investment in the game. Also I would give everyone time to read over and choose the character's they are most interested in. This helps to eliminate the growing pains you can get when not everyone is on the same page at the start of a game.
