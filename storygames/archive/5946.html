\n Ed over on theRPGsite had a really good idea, and we're trying to get the word out about it:

As a way of honoring Gary Gygax we will have a \u201cGary Con\u201d across the globe this weekend.

Get your old gaming friends together, or invite some new players to join you for a game. We\u2019ll all play either Dungeons & Dragons, or some other game but with a classic D&D type dungeon in it.

This is a global event for new and old fans of Gary\u2019s game to get together, roll some dice, and have some fun. If you\u2019re on Facebook, we\u2019ve setup an event page for Gary Con 2008.
