\n (this be joke)

Ask someone if they want to play a game. Once they have agreed, tell them that the game is Hugs For Jesus.

The person who initiated Hugs For Jesus plays a Beloved. Come up with a suitable image of what you look like, and you can either share this with the other player or not.

The Beloved asks the other player how much they love Jesus. Regardless of answer, begin hugging them now. After they have been embraced in your hug for a while, ask them how much they love Jesus. If the answer is greater than their previous answer, release the hug. If not, keep embracing for a while longer.

It is not necessary for either player to be religious to play this game, because it's a ROLEPLAYING game, and you are ROLEPLAYING characters.

le fin.
