\n SCORN: THE FIRST BOOK OF PANDEMONIUM



\xa1Feliz D\xeda de los Muertos!

Scorn is available now as a free PDF from DriveThruRPG.

The Last War has begun. Demons walk among us, feeding on the anguish of their unsuspecting victims. You are a Disciple, a demon hunter armed with repugnant magics and superhuman abilities.

Scorn is a fast-paced game of splatterpunk action in a world of violence and decay. The demons you face are nothing like the comfortable horned-and-hooved devils of monotheistic lore; these tentacled horrors feed on our worst impulses like a cancer, galvanized by lust, murder, addiction, and despair.

Wielding bizarre magics like Death Panel, Photobomb, Sexpletive, and Acid Reflux, you must hunt down these merciless predators and rescue innocent civilians without losing your grip on the tattered vestiges of your humanity.

How far will you go to destroy evil? Will you sell your soul? Will that help?

(SPOILER ALERT. You're going to fail. This is a one-way trip, and no one comes home in one piece. You'll either become a demon or die in agony. Sorry. Nothing personal.)

    \n Well, the broad strokes include: five character classes, new spells, new demons, and some changes to the core mechanics. Instead of rolling #d12 against the DM's roll, you now roll against a target difficulty for everything.

    Also, instead of hit points, you've got Blood and Decay. When the blood score drops to dangerous levels, you must resort to cannibalism in order to use magic. When the Decay score gets too high, you become a demon and your teammates must execute you. If the Decay score gets too low, you become a normal human.

    Calculating your dice pool now involves adding your core attribute (Violence, Traction, or Instinct) to a skill, and then adding extra dice for vivid descriptions and the like.

    In addition, everyone has a few Cabal dice, which they can hand out whenever they see you do something cool.
