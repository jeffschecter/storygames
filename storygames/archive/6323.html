    \n For turning PDFs into books cheaply, get an A5 notepad (make sure it's wire bound), pry the spine apart and remove it, printed double sided onto the pages then put the binding back together. It's not quite as good as a regular book but it doesn't cost much. I have a few games printed off like that and they are fine. Takes a little over 30 minutes to sort out the printing and removing/replacing the spine.

    \n To get the shipping cost down, inquire with Indie Press Revolution for the cost of shipping all the book you need together, or e-mail directly the authors (the few ones that still sell books directly and don't sent everybody to IPR or Lulu usually have very good international shipping costs, sometimes even less than the real cost, because they still get more money selling directly)
