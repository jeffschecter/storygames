    Posted By: Jason MorningstarIs this the one Andy Hopp goes to? It looks really fun.
    \nYea, that's all Andy Hopp's artwork on the website, and I believe it's his shindig. When I've gone I've seen a good variety of gaming happening of all varieties, and the RPG selection has a big Savage Worlds representation because of the locals. Travis and I have run Unknown Armies, Dogs in the Vineyard, Monsters and Other Childish Things and Unhallowed Metropolis (2 years ago that was I think). I've seen Dread, Paranoia, White Wolf, playtests of all sorts, and some general oddities happening there as well, so there's a good mix of the types and styles of RPG's being played and most everyone is open for everything! There's also great artist representation and activities... the Iron Artist awards are a blast to watch. And it's also pretty family friendly with lots of kids activities and gaming as well.

    Thought I'd throw this out there for people looking to get more of a Convention Fix this year. Con on the Cob happens every year mid October.

    \n I have wanted to go to this for years, even written about it before for the local newspaper, but it falls right about the same time as our big state convention CharCon in Charleston, W.Va., so I usually have to choose between the two.

    Con on the Cob is especially great if you are a Savage Worlds fan as they have their big Gathering of Savages event there every year. Plus the after parties are supposed to be excellent.
