Like most of my crazy ideas recently, this came up in a conversation with Annie Rush:

Secret Ingrediant (example): GIANT INFLATABLE CRAYON!

"Each Master Chef has an hour to lead their team of Su Chefs in creating a game that requires a giant inflatable crayon! You will teach it to the judges and then we'll play it together. Go!"

Of course, it might be too late to implement it for this year, but... THIS MUST HAPPEN!
