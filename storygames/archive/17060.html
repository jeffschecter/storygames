\n I'm just about done with a Fiasco playset, set in an imagined Kurosawa film (especially the Shapespeare clones like Ran or Hidden Fortress) or 13 Assassins, or Onmyogi, or ... .. ...

If this kind of thing interests you, please let me know. I would like to provide you with a link to help me fill in a few holes in the oracle.\n
The Score

(Catchy Subhead)

Duty, honor, structure. It binds us all together towards our divine purpose and keeps order, but it
rules a man from birth until death, and it favors some while demanding from others.
If the rules could be bent for just this one time, if the gods could shift their affections only
slightly, we could find some small measure more than fate has set aside for us.
That is, of course, if everyone else stays in their place afterward...

Movie Night

Ran, Rashoman, 13 Assassins, Kagemusha, Onmyogi, [insert every movie ever where people thought they
could manipulate the system, or where they drove towards duty while secretly hoping for some other
reward].
