\n Hey folks! I'm putting together a mini-con in Vancouver, in the vein of nerdnyc's Gotham Gaming Guild. Rather than a bunch of one-shots crammed into a single weekend, like most cons, the mini-con takes place over six sundays in October and November.
\n
\nWe aim to have a handful of two- to six-session mini-campaigns, plus a bunch of one-shots (Perfect, Lacuna, Inspectres and Don't Rest Your Head are already lined up). The goal is to give like-minded players and GMs a chance to get to know each other better than at a one-shot game, while still offering one-shots for those who are short on time or commitment.
\n
\nIt's a great chance to run that new game you picked up, get to play if you're a perma-GM, sit in on a longer game if you're between groups, and find people who share your gaming interests to form a new group after the con.
\n
\nThe sessions are from 2-6pm, October 7, 14, 28 and November 4, 18, 25 at Drexoll Games in Kitsilano. We've got four tables that can handle between 4-6 players plus a GM.
\n
\nI'd love it if some of the local story gamers would run some games or even just sign up for your favorites once our schedule is finalized.
\n
\nIf you're interested or just want to be notified when sign ups start, either respond to this thread or email d6minicon at gmail.
\n
\nThanks!
\n
\nChris
