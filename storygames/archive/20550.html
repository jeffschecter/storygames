\n Rumour has it that there is nothing better than a custom move for Apocalypse World.

Not too long ago, Story-Games.com gave birth to a beautiful abomination called "On a miss, wolves".

But now the second edition is coming out, and we no longer have wolves. Now we have a bear. Just one. But the trick is that you fall on the bear. You see? It's pretty spiffy.

So, please tell us: what move ends with "...on a miss, you fall on a bear", and why is it the best move ever?

    \n When you pilot your boat across the bear sea, roll +cool.
    On a 7-9, choose two; on a 10+, all three:
    -no bears crawl on board
    -no bears clog the engines
    -no bears were harmed in the making of this voyage
    On a miss, your boat capsizes (and you can guess what you fall onto.)

    When you plead for your life before Baron von Berenstain, roll +hot.
    On a 10+, the Baron is moved to mercy. On a 7-9, he may instead choose one:
    -banish you from the barony
    -send you to the cells
    -demand a pound of flesh
    On a miss, the trapdoor under you feet drops away. What's down there? Bears.

    When you're just a little black rain cloud, hovering under the honey tree, roll +hungry.
    On a 7-9, choose one; on a 10+, both:
    -you grab a tasty smackeral
    -the bees aren't bothered
    On a miss, still choose one above, but you fall on your bear bottom.

    \n When you survey from on high, roll +hard. On a 10+, you cling expertly to the tallest treetop; the MC must describe what's around you for a distance of 4 miles in every direction. On a 7-9, you can choose either a quick glance in all directions (1 mile, general description only) or a steady glance in one direction (4 miles, detailed) before you slip slightly from your perch and take 1-harm from crashing through tree branches before you can halt your fall. On a miss, you fall on a bear.

    \nWhen you swing on the high trapeze at the St. Petersburg Circus, even though you really really shouldn't be up there, roll and add 1 for each arm you're using to hold on.

    On a hit, you swing high over the crowd and amaze all newcomers. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.

    * Even the people who have seen this show many times before are astounded.
    * You manage to impress the circus performers, as well.
    * You won't be berated or punished by the quartermaster, afterwards.

    On a miss, lucky you! You fall on a bear. The MC will pick one, two, or three:

    * The bear is wearing a clown costume.
    * The bear is riding a unicycle.
    * The bear is juggling.
    * The bear is on the ice (wearing skates).

    Be prepared. Bears don't like having people interrupt their hockey game.

    \n When you're doing the New Years shift inside the reality apparatus at the core of The Zoo, and trying to bring a peaceful Spring for all the animals, roll+weird.

    For every point you roll above 6, you can subtract a month from October and start the new year then. Hopefully there's enough time to grow food this year. On a 10+, you can also choose one of the animal populations who are particularly happy with your work. On a miss, you (drop) Fall on a bear. Good luck.
