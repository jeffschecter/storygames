    Posted By: Nathan H.Is there a reason for picking two Details, other then to add further complications?
    \nTwo seems to be the right number of new inputs at the game's mid-point. The necessity to include new things into a situation that's already well-established is a deliberate application of creative pressure. You are tilting the platform you've built.

    That said, if you and your friends have tried two and find it to be a distracting amount of new stuff, one won't break anything.
