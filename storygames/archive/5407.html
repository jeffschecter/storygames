\n I'm not a fan of politics, but like many I find the elections every four years to be morbidly fascinating. How would you story game them? What rules could you use?

Off the top of my head, I wonder if Contenders could be a good start. "Battles" or debate between two candidates at a time as they stump for votes. Connections with important partners or "super-donors" and dirty tricks to shut the other candidates down.

Put the partisan stuff aside for the moment and think about the realities of presidential politics, which is all about funding and vote-mongering.

Who is with me here?
