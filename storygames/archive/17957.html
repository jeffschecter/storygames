\n Ahoy maties!

We've just launched a kickstarter for Conquering Corsairs, a deck building card game in which you take the role of pirates in a fantasy archipelago trying to gain gold and prestige to become the most feared and famous ship to sail the Silver Seas.

The game is for 2-4 players, takes 45-60 minutes and combines deck building and strategic map movement. All of the art we commission for the game will be donated to the Prismatic Art Collection.

Check out the Kickstarter page!
