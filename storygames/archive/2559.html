\n This is, well, hardly related to Story Games at all, but of interest, I suspect, to about half the folks on this board.

A group of brave young punks have put out the first issue of Steampunk Magazine. It is a self-published, creative-commons-licensed, bimonthly magazine. I don't know if these guys are very ambitious, very foolhardy, or both.

However, the first issue looks pretty good -- and it's available for free download.
