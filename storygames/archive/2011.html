    Burger King and Chick-fil-A are right there. There's a chinese-food place. Some pizza place I've never tried as well.

    Red Hot & Blue is medium-priced I would say. I like Danny's BBQ which is about a half a mile away. Their logo is a cartoon pig smearing BBQ sauce on his belly so that you can eat him--how can you not adore that? There's a Quiznos in the adjacent Wal-Mart shopping center, but they made me mad last time I ate there. I think there is a pizza place in that shopping center too. And I think McDonald's is in the Wal-Mart.

    A few miles east down Tryon is a Subway. About 2 miles west there is a Wendy's, Perkins, Taco Bell, Schlotsky's, Jersey Mike's, Bojangles and an inexpensive BBQ place called Dixe's BBQ that I like. There's a Chili's down Kildaire Farm road as you head northerly. I don't imagine too many people would go to these more distant places unless you really hated the choices in the above paragraphs. There's plenty of food in walking distance.

    As for the Whole Foods, you can get a really good salad there and they have soup as well. Take 10 on a disguise check before you go in and I'm sure you'll be fine.
