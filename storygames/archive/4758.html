\n I just revisited The World's Unknown page at Abulafia, and got a killer story:

THE SEARCH FOR CAPTAIN BAKER

CHAPTER ONE: HUNTING THE ELEPHANT WITH SPEARS
CHAPTER TWO: BAKER PURSUED BY AN ENRAGED ELEPHANT
CHAPTER THREE: BAGGING A LOT OF ANTELOPES
CHAPTER FOUR: A MONKEY AND AN OLD REPROBATE
CHAPTER FIVE: ACQUIRING THE LANGUAGE
CHAPTER SIX: CAPSIZED BY A STORM
CHAPTER SEVEN: THROWING VICTIMS OVER A CLIFF
CHAPTER EIGHT: PLENTY OF CATTLE, BUT NO MEAT
CHAPTER NINE: A DREADFUL PLOT DISCOVERED
CHAPTER TEN: THE POWER OF SORCERY

That's pure fun, right there. I like the deus ex machina hinted at in chapters one and two, and again in nine and ten.
