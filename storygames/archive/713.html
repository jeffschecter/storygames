  • J_Walton

    Now, that I've actually looked at the site...

    Frames are WAY out of style. Everybody hates them now. Be a rebel if you want, but...

    iframes are only really viable, I think, if you can fit the entire frame within the normal viewing window when a browser initially loads a page. If I have to scroll down the page to read everything in the iframe and then pull the scroll bar on the iframe to see the rest of the text, that's no good. Too much scrolling. Or you could make the iframe much taller, so that you don't have to scroll the frame, that might make things better. But, in either case, just say no to lots of scrolling (especially any scrolling that's not the normal right hand scrollbar).
