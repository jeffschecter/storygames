\n
\n
\n
\n\nBrennen Reece \n\n\n
\n
\n\nOctober 2008 \n edited October 2008 in Story Games
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n My mom was attempting to explain these weird games that I play to her best friend. She didn't know if she did a very good job of it, so she called me to ask if she'd gotten it right. Strangely enough she had done a pretty good job, and in the process got herself and her friend really interested in playing. You know, just to see what it's about.

They're both pretty eclectic. They're both in their mid-50s. They're both nurses. My mom is a big fan of weird movies and listens to lots of Stephen King audio books.

I need something for a one-shot. I'm looking for low-commitment, easy on the rules, high fun, engaging story, and possibly a bit macabre.
