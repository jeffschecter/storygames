\n I've heard from multiple sources that tremulus is a weak PbtA game and a weak Lovecraft game. From glancing over the basic moves, it seems like rolls are too "easy" in a way that doesn't promote the themes of the game. For instance, "Poke Around" generates benefits far too easily for a proper Lovecraft game (and it seems like the majority of the move should be handled on the GM-side of things, where clues are left in plain sight).
