\n I've drafted a new playtest version of HVE Water. Let me know if you're interested in trying it out.

the pitch: HVE Water (pronounced "Heavy Water") Is a rogue-like storygame about love and survival at the end of the civilization. In it we play fictional versions of ourselves in a procedurally generated world where survival is uncertain and memories of the past are our only link to humanity.
