I'm making a thing called World of Pilgrims (hack/expansion/remake of World of Dungeons), which has an absurdly big (compared with the rest of the rules) illustrated bestiary. I'm taking requests of what should draw next on my blog here:

http://www.daylands.blogspot.com.es/2015/09/taking-monster-requests-for-world-of.html

You can find the rest of the rules and the monster creation basis there too, in case you are curious about it; and if you have this idea of a monster you'd really see drawn and stated, feel free to ask for it (If you don't mind me putting it into the book once it's written!). Use the blog or this very forum for your requests, I don't mind!
