    \n The Great Luke Ski, Carrie Dahlby, Tom Smith, Worm Quartet, Possible Oscar, Jonathan Coulton, and Rob Balder all do assorted fan/geek/gaming-related music, mostly comedic filk and parody stuff. Check out wwwthefump.com, the 'Funny Music Project'. It's a clearinghouse, and from there you can link through to most of their websites, etc. Good stuff.

    Also check out the Cemetery Surfers. Not gaming related per se, but definitely geek music.
