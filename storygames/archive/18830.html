    \n Grimm was reworked as a bigger game with its own d6-based system -- that's the edition I have and it's pretty cool. I've never read the original d20 one. The version I have already has something like play books in the form of different kid tropes like the Bully and the Nerd, that could adapt well to AW.

    Anyway, reading list: Two essential books of dark fairy tales are Angela Carter's "The Bloody Chamber" and Tanith Lee's "Red As Blood". Also Neil Gaiman's story "Snow, Glass, Apples". And of course his novel "Coraline".
