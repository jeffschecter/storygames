\n I'm looking to see who would be interested in helping me assemble a new Saturday gaming group. I'm looking to explore some new avenues of gaming, mostly involving different perspectives on storytelling that involve more than "go on an adventure and kill things" and such traditions. I'm seeking to actively playtest some new RPG mechanics designed to facilitate such a focus. I have a game system in mind, and am looking for folks who would be up for trying something new.

As such, I'm considering starting up a new gaming group on Saturdays. My hope is to get a good four-to-six hours of gaming each session, starting in the early afternoon (speculatively around 2 PM or so), ending around 8 or 9, and followed afterwards by the group hanging out, getting some drinks at a nearby pub and talking about gaming and stuff. Making new friends is most definitely a plus, but I'm more interested in working together to establish a solid gaming relationship.

Me: I'm 27, been gaming since my single-digits, manage the creative affairs of a small-press gaming company, and am accepting of people of all different walks of life. We'd probably be gaming at either my house or Guardian Games, and my only requirements for gaming are that you be cool, open-minded, have a sense of humor, and bathe regularly.

If you're available on those hours, find my proposals intriguing, and are open to "indie" styles of gaming, drop me a line. I'm just fielding interest at the moment, to see who else might be down for this idea. Thanks!

- Nathanael
