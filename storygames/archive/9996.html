\n I came across this little equation today:
\n
\nUsing a representative sample passage of about 100 words reading difficulty can be assessed using the following formula. 206.835 \u2013 (1.015 x ASL) \u2013 (84.6 x ASW) where ASL = average sentence length (number of words divided by the number of sentences) and ASW = average number of syllables per word (number of syllables divided by the number of words). The higher the result the more easy the text is to understand. A result of 60+ represents a good level of comprehension.
\n
\nI took a random sample from Radiance and got a result of 90.94.
\n
\nHow readable is your text?
