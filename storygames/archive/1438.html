    Posted By: joepubAny suggestions?
    Make sure you understand what each "+3 pain" increment does in terms of its effect on dominance and what players must do to be on an equal footing (this is implied in the guidelines on setting pain for opponents).

    Search the RPG.net forums for DRYH threads. There's a number, and they are full of awesome.

    Make sure to look at the evilhat wiki on DRYH, which includes a link to a FAQ that may be useful: www.evilhat.com/wiki I believe.

    If you're doing this as a one-shot, consider starting people at 1 Exhaustion (or 2, if it's going to be tightly paced).
    Should the GM try and steer the story, or be reflexive?
    Depends on your group. I'd try to have a story in mind as a backup.
    What tone makes the game the most kickass?
    The one that matches the tone of the players' answers to the character questionnaire.

    Seriously. I've seen one that's been all wack-ass hijinks with madness powers like "Can summon the rock band Scorpions to help me out," and ones that are dark, sober, and somber.
    Experiences with it?
    Wrote it.

    Ran it a couple times, folded the ideas into the text.

    Have been a player in a two-session game, and ended the second session by Crashing, while playing my guitar (I was playing Diesel Remmick, the sample character) in an effort to break the evil Nightmare casino's bank, using my 'Break Things' exhaustion talent in concert with my 'Rhythm Nation' madness talent.

    It was good.
    General tips?
    See the above.
