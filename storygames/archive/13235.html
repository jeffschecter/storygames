    \n Spreadshirt offers flex printing, which looks great and is very durable. But it is pretty expensive as a POD option.

    We just started working with a local screen printer, Flytrap, and they are excellent. They also do fulfillment for cost plus some very small fee. This isn't POD, but if you can gauge demand it is a much better product for a much lower price.
