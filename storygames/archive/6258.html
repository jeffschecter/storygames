    \n I think Paul is right. There's something really neat for me about being involved in a game during the creative process. Whether it's buying the ashcan, participating in a play test, giving feedback on a forum or something else. This is something I've really enjoyed with video games as well. It's a neat experience, and I think just being involved gives us a kind of sense of ownership over the project. We want these games to succeed because we have a small but meaningful stake in them.

    I'm telling you stuff you've already thought of, I'm sure.
