\n Neoplastic Press is proud to announce Grey Ranks: The Second Book of Pandemonium, a tabletop game of horror-action.



Your government has surrendered to the combined forces of Hell and the German army. Demonic Nazis roam the streets of Paris. You are a teenaged member of the Confr\xe9rie de Notre Dame, a secret agency devoted to the overthrow of the Nazi regime, and to hunting and killing demons. As a member of the French Resistance, you must wage a losing battle against the infernal forces of the Reich while trying to navigate the doldrums of adolescence.

Grey Ranks uses profanity in an attempt to look edgy, and there are several pictures of demon-girls with titties. The game is powered by the Disciple 12 system, which requires you to buy large quantities of 12-sided dice that will never be used again, once you've played Grey Ranks twice. They will lurk at the bottom of your dice bag like the keys on your keychain that you can no longer identify, but which you fear to part with, because one day, you might be in some horrible situation, and you'll need to escape, but the door will be locked, and like a fool, you've thrown away your keys, and now the demon-possessed Nazis are going to get you.
