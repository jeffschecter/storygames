\n So, I have a new thing to think about.

I need:

a. Some morally dubious job offers. "I want you to kill this guy", "I want you to smuggle this". Most of these will invove a choice : at the least, do it, don't do it or report it to the authorities.

b. Some disputes that might arise between two factions: "The big company is poisoning the farmers' land", "The hardline criminals don't want to work with the guilds", "The priests are censoring the communist's pamphlets".

Thank you in advance!

Graham

    \n Disputes:

    One company wants to cover up the fact that their product is contaminated. Their minority shareholder wants to announce it. (Based on a true story.)

    The large conglomerate wants to open a megastore in the small town. The local small businesses oppose this. (Based on another true story, frequently repeated.)

    The political reformer has popular support, but the entrenched powerbrokers want to "turn" him. (We may see soon whether this is a true story or not.)

    Some people on campus have an unpopular opinion which falls outside the generally accepted parameters of "what you can say around here". They want to propagate their opinion. Others consider that "free speech" means they get to speak and other people get to listen. (Yeah, true story.)

    Hundreds of years ago, the conquerors planted people of a different religion among people who take their nationalism and their religion very seriously indeed. Their descendants consider that they belong here. The nationalists don't. (Mmm-hmm, mmm-hmm.)

    \n Job offers:
    This large box of [food, medicine, building materials, money] needs to make it to those poor people on the other side of this [dangerous forest, barren desert, worm hole, ocean]. Take it, please?

    Reversing that:
    This large box of [stuff] needs to get past those poor people to the rich people on the other side of [places listed earlier].

    Yeah this new model car has a few problems, but is anyone really going to ever be going 55, with the radio volume set to this setting, while the passenger-side seat warmer is on low? Anyway, get the first shipment out by Tuesday.

    Slave traders will be passing by tomorrow. Take your team, steal the slaves, and bring them back to me.

    The drug mule will be here tomorrow. Pick her up from the airport and take her to the doctor to get the bags removed.

    Rape this woman to prove you're one of us.

    Disputes:

    Z Street has just been moved, rehabilitated, and rezoned for businesses. It used to be the DMZ between rival gangs. Whose is it now?

    A large bank is in danger of collapsing, and is willing to accept suitors for a buyout. Two other large banks are interested.
