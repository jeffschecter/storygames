The initial playtest document is now complete, and I am looking for energetic and committed playtesters to help guide the development of the game. I have set up a quick survey to serve as an application for people who are interested. You do not need to be an experienced role-player or have ever read any of the John Rain novels to participate. In fact, we are looking for people with a broad range of experience to provide us with a depth of feedback. Likewise, the playtest document is designed to permit very quick playtest sessions (under 30 minutes), and it only requires two people to play. Even if you only have one friend to play with you and a couple of hours available over the next month or so, you can still provide me with critical input.

So, if you are at all interested, I encourage to complete the survey. Please click on the link below to get started:

Tokyo Rain Playtest Application

I will be in touch with each applicant within a few days of their submitting the application. Thanks in advance for your time and interest.
