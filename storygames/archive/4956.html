\n

\n\nMatthijs \n\n\n

\n

\n I can't get started with podcasts. I have no idea when to listen to them.

Sometimes when I'm surfin da netz, I click on a podcast link and listen for about... one minute, max. But I'm staring at a blank screen, listening to a bad recording of someone mumbling or talking in a geeky voice about something I could just read somewhere in half the time. Still, I feel I might be missing out - that somewhere in there, there's a gem of brilliance, some new idea or something that will make things click for one of projects.

When and how do you listen to podcasts?

    \n I'm on dial-up at home. And my workplace has throttling on mp3 downloads. So I have this slick little shell script I found that accepts a list of urls and pulls them over to a remote server, wherefrom I can SCP them down to my mp3 player.
