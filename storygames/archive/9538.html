      \n Hi Paul,

      I can attest that the Compleat Strategist Thursday game night is filled with friendly, welcoming people who will have you playing a game within 20 minutes of your arrival, Zac D. being an prime example. (Sadly for me, I haven't been in quite a while. Glad it's still happening.)

      Also, keep your eye out for DC Gameday; I think there is one coming up in a couple months. It's a weekend convention in the District with a good mix of old-school and new-school games. I'm sure someone else could drop by and give better information about it, but it's also a good way to meet some friendly local gamers.

      There's also a MeetUp group called The Great Hall that covers the greater DC area. I've never been to any of the games, but they seem to have a few hundred members, and regularly post open slots (via email) for all manner of RPGs, usually at Labyrinth, a game store near the Eastern Market metro station.

      That's just the stuff I know about, and I don't even get out that much. GO FORTH, AND ROLL TWELVE-SIDED DICE.
