    \n Wilhelm: fold in half, then in half, then in half. On each fold the first page should be visible. I'll put that in.

    Simon: I didn't think of ties initially. I suppose that you can either reroll or have a compromise; I'll put that in too.

    I really hadn't thought of the idea of spending Hero Points for things you're not involved in. That's a great idea. I think I'd limit it to your personal stash of Hero Points rather than the ones you get for class, species, or equipment. I might also say that your character has to be in the scene, but I'm not sure.

    Setting info. Adventure hooks. Enemy stats. Got it. (Some of the enemies should definitely have Villain Points.) If I had two games that thematically went together I'd weld them onto the same paper, but I think I'll just expand this one a bit.

    Thanks for the positive comments. I should totally do this with Console.
