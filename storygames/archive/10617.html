    \n It's pp. 144-151. Quick summary:

    You're a monster and you fuck shit up.Rarr.

    Your stats are now Power, Finesse and Resistance. Each stat covers mental, physical, and social.

    No Abilities. Your skills are now the seven deadly sins. One of them must be zero. Roll Stat + Sin in dice when you do stuff.

    You get one Virtue, and a Weakness stat that starts at one. Use your Virtue, Weakness goes up, it gets to 10, you're done.

    You have a few resources, like Herd and Wealth and stuff, and the disciplines are a bit toned-down and less magical.

    And there's some advice on how to play this version. It's more about being a kick-ass crazy monster, less about angst.

    \n The unexplained joke is that you're not a vampire but you are a monster. The other joke is the titles of the piece ("Monster Garage") was done in jest and never intended as the published title. No red lines on this sucker, for better or for worse.

    I tried to give the disciplines a heightened reality, but still mundane.

    The major change in my version is that Humanity = weakness. You don't want Humanity. And if you have any, you definitely don't want to hold onto it or (worse) display it.
