    \n Yeah, what they said.

    There's absolutely no doubt that switching to nWoD Storyteller from D20 Modern is going to dramatically change the game. Just translating the characters into a new system is going to change what they can do and how well they do it, and that's before you even consider the very different ways those games handle combat.

    But change doesn't have to be bad. In fact, when you're talking about a game, the only time that change is always bad is when it's unwelcome. So the only people, and I really mean the ONLY people whose opinions matter in this case are you and the other players. Nothing anyone else has to say will be meaningful or helpful.

    If you guys like how your game feels after the change, you're all set. If you hate it, you'll need to change again, or just abandon that game and start a different one.
