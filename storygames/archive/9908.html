Last night:

Zap McHenry, Atomic Detective, is explaining the power of the atom to a classroom of inquisitive Rocket City youngsters. He opens his chest panel.

ZAP: And as you can see, my atomic pile keeps me fit as a fiddle.

A BOY raises his hand

Yes, you there in the back. What's your question, son?

BOY: So you don't have a heart, Mr. McHenry?

ZAP: Not as such, no.

BOY: Does that mean you can never love again?

ZAP bursts into tears and runs out of the room
