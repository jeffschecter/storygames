    Posted By: renatoramYeah, Capes got mentioned as one of the rare gmless games that last several sessions (and that tends to seem to break in the long run).
    \nCapes actually works BETTER in the long run. The issue is that there's no way to stop. The game does comic book soap opera continuity very, very, very well which means you'll never have a neat tidy ending. There will be arcs that develop and resolve but there will always be loose ends to pick up and keep running with. When something big and climatic happens you have to be willing to call it, "Good Enough" and stop.
