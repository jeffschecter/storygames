    \n "Is aspect X relevant to the current situation?" is an operation that looks at the clouds and leads to the boxes. When you make a roll, and want a +2 or reroll on your result but don't see any aspects that currently apply, it's often a prompt from the boxes to contribute to the cloud (e.g. add some richer detail to your narration so that one of your aspects will apply).
