\n Hey everybody,
\n
\nWe started our game of Misspent Youth last week! This weekend I edited and posted our first session:
\n
\nSession 1: 2 hours 30 Minutes
\n
\nWe're having a lot of fun with Rob's game. It was a lot of fun and was our first attempt at collaborative setting creation, and we were nervous our idea was way out there. Fortunately feedback from our #1 Independent Insurgent put those fears to rest. As Rob put it: "Great shit. I fucking love it. I'm a little envious I didn't come up with something this fucking cool." Awesome!
\n
\nHere's the basic setting info we came up with:
\n
\nAuthority: Trans-dimensional corporation that steals History to sell it to other cultures. Historical events that are stolen are not remembered by adults. Buildings lose their significance, culture fades, progress stagnates. Only children are unaffected by this.
\n
\nTheme: Violence, Class, Innocence
\n
\nClique: History club. \u201cHardcore Historians\u201d (Nerdy Athletic Miscreants) \u201cTo preserve and promote the glories of our civilization through understanding of our history\u201d
\n
\nCharacters:
\n
\nJen Kerzon 15yr old Loud and Angry, naive and pretty, stern leader
\n
\nMistreatment: Abused Motivation: Thrills Means: Bad
\n
\nSchtick: Fast Talkin\u2019 Hard Brawlin\u2019 Party Girl Glitch: \u201cCares too much\u201d
\n
\nCJ Nix Ranz 15yr old Silly But Serious, sex crazed teen
\n
\nMistreatment: Spoiled Motivation: Optimism Means: Cool
\n
\nSchtick: Can Hot Wire Anything Glitch: Virgin
\n
\nDan 12yr old small kid with big attitude, gratuitously emo
\n
\nMistreatment: Neglected Thrills: Pride Means: Tough
\n
\nSchtick: Sherlock Holmes wannabe Glitch: Little Man Syndrome
\n
\nKevin Celedor 17yr old shy and bookish, wandering dreamer
\n
\nMistreatment: Sheltered Thrills: Altruism Means: Smart
\n
\nSchtick: \u201cCatch me if you can\u201d Glitch: Caught up in the Details.
