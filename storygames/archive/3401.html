\n So Medical Hospital has scenes where you do stuff, at the table, like cut up bits of paper and post-it them back together. These are called tactile scenes. They are not bereft of roleplaying, but it's roleplaying with scissors.

And it has scenes that are exactly like the sort of scenes you see in Prime Time Adventures or whatever. And I'm not sure what to call these. My shorthand is "talking scenes", because you are not doing arts and crafts, but that doesn't sound right. And they aren't "roleplaying scenes" or "action scenes" any more than the tactile scenes are. They are what we do all the time and would normally just call "scenes".

Any thoughts on what to call these?
