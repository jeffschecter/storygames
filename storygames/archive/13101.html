\n So, I woke up excited last night. Could we get a few folks together for an online convention? Maybe no, because someone would have to be in charge, to schedule the events, post information, and manage the thing. Or would you even need anyone in charge? I thought about it, but in the end I couldn't find a good reason why I couldn't help to start it up. So here I am.

I am able to get Sundays off work, so I thought I would set it up on a Sunday. Perhaps the 17th of October (EDIT: Or maybe in November\u2014see posts below)? Jonathan said that the games should be played in October, and that's the only Sunday I have free that isn't Halloween. So, here's my call out:

ChefCon 2010
A GameChef Playoff Convention
Date: Undecided
Get a chance to playtest one of this year's GameChef entries (it doesn't matter if it's not a finalist). You don't need to critique, you don't need to AP. Come along and play. And meet people with funny foreign accents.


If you'd like to join in, participants should indicate:

(a) a game(s) they can facilitate
(b) a game(s) they can play
(c) their availability online (EST)
(d) their internet-talky software (Skype, VoIP, etc.)
(e) how many session they'd like to participate in.
