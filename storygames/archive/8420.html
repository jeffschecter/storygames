\n I'm beginning to admit to myself that I probably won't be going to GenCon 2009. It's not that bad. I've been to 2006, 2007, and 2008. With my money situation what it is and the fact that everyone I go with (and to be with) except me is sick of GenCon, it's probably for the best. We'll replace it with some other kind of get-together. However, I want to hit me some cons! I've been meaning to get into the local scene more, and this looks like a good escuse. Also, as a hungry freelancer and designer, the networking is important to me. Can anyone point me in the direction of a comprehensive schedule of San Francisco Bay Area rpg conventions?

Thanks.
