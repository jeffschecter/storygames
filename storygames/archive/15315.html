    \n In Violence Future, characters have Soul and Fate traits. When Fate exceeds Soul it is endgame for the character and special mechanics come into play. The character becomes more effective in conflicts, but also takes double damage from all successful attacks against him. And if that doesn't result in the character being dead by the end of the current scenario, then the character dies at that point.

    \n Annalise is divided in 4 phases, played with different rules.

    Dirty Secrets has an endgame when the witness can't move anymore

    Apocalypse World open new advancements after thw 6th one. Two of them "open" the common traits to superior results.

    Characters in Spione are played differently in alternating phases. Mechanics in the game open new options.

    These are the first 4 games on my shelf, I think you need to specify better what you are searching for, or the answer is "almost every game".
