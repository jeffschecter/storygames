\n One-of-a-kind autobiographical comic book writer Harvey Pekar died yesterday.
I found out about Harvey while reading a story, written by him, in an issue of Chester Brown's Yummy Fur.
He had such a unique voice, 'specially in the medium of comics.
What got you to read some of Harvey's comics?
What are some of your favorites?
If you've not read anything by him, give 'em a try.
Might I suggest Our Cancer Year?
