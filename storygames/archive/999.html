If you're designing a game, and you've got forum or blog-space for it, do create a sticky thread to brainstorm good soundtrack songs. It's a neat way for the players to interact with the game designers about themes from the game, and it creates a good basis for helping players and GMs generate Color for game prep and game play.

...This is really aimed at Matt Wilson, because I've got a few CDs here ready for the _Galactic_ soundtrack.

From my experience so far, active music during games has been hard to work out, but soundtrack music can be great for GM prep / pre-game warmup.
