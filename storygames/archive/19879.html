\n Another nano-game on the blog as I get back into my writing groove: Vox Populi is about what happens after the heroes overthrow the Evil Overlord and find themselves tasked with forming a Republic! This a simple, light debating game in which policies are proposed, discussed and voted on from the perspective of archetypal fantasy RPG heroes.
