\n This new RPG StackExchange thing has a lot of potential to be an amazing resource.

I'd say it's like a forum, but with a tighter focus around expertise and Q&A.

The 'incredible' part for me is the reputation system, which has completely hooked me on the site. I'm already seeing good answers getting more and better attention, and refinement. I kind of love it.

There's only a few indie correspondents jumping in right now, but I hope to see more soon.
