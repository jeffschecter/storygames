So we've heard the after-action reports from Camp Nerdly 0. Now we, as good left-coasters, can crib off the east coast's notes and do our own version, possibly with movie stars.

Our tentative-to-firming-up location is San Simeon State Park, halfway between LA and SF.

Our first broad time slot possibility is between March 15 and April 30. There would be very little temperature variation through this period, partially thanks to the ocean. We'd be looking at 60s and 70s during the day and high 40s, mid 50s at night. Rain is a totally unpredictable possibility. Easter will fall on April 8th, so that weekend is probably not feasible. Remaining weekends include: March 17-18, March 24-25, March 30-April 1, April 14-15, April 21-22, and April 28-29.

The second broad time slot is May and June. According to Ye Olde Almanac, the temperature doesn't really rise so much as the possibility of warmer temperatures rises. So we're looking at 60s to 80s during the day and in the 50s at night. Rain remains a totally unpredictable possibility. The weekend of May 25-26 sees KublaCon in SF, Celebrations in LA, and potentially Gamex in LA. Remaining weekends include: May 4-5, May 12-13, May 19-20, June 2-3, June 9-10, June 16-17, June 23-24.

We could go later, but we will start running into lots more people (school vacation starts) and it would be harder to stake out 'our' area.

I think our first step is knocking out weekends that absolutely don't work for folks and maybe highlighting which weekends do work. The weekends I have listed are just Saturday/Sunday; if we like, we can subsume a Friday and/or a Monday as well.

Me personally, I like sooner rather than later, and March 30-April 1 amuses me because it's April Fools. But at this scheduling range, I'm pretty damn flexible. I'd love to hear from other folks interested in coming.
