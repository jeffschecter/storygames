\n Anyone considered running a game set in the Wild West, I'm thinking like Deadwood for inspiration, with the Apocalypse World engine?
\n
\nHere's some class conversion ideas:
\n
\nThe Angel - The Doctor - Doc Cochran
\nThe Battlebabe - The Sheriff - Seth Bullock
\nThe Brainer - The Reverend - Reverend Smith
\nThe Chopper - The Gang Leader - Mr. Wu maybe? Or, some kind of outlaw gang leader... Can't think of anyone off the top of my head.
\nThe Driver - The Rider - Calamity Jane or maybe Silas Adams
\nThe Gunlugger - The Gunslinger - Wild Bill Hickok
\nThe Hardholder - The Proprietor - Al Swearengen
\nThe Hocus - The Politician - Sol Starr or maybe E.B. Farnum
\nThe Operator - The Henchmen - Dan Dority
\nThe Savvyhead - The Professional - A.W. Merrick
\nThe Skinner - The Whore - Joanie Stubbs
