\n I'm learning Spanish by listening to Spanish-language podcasts twice daily. It's working, to my surprise, but I don't get to practice with other people. One solution to my problem would be to, you know, speak to some Spanish speakers. That is not the solution I am here to talk about.

I am looking to play an RPG in Spanish. Preferably, the book would be in Spanish. I am sure that old school versions of D&D were printed in Spanish, but I can't find any to save my life. I've been searching for an hour. If anyone knows where I can get my hands on some classic D&D - like red-box D&D - in Spanish, let me know.

Also, if you know how I can get a copy of El Juego de Rol del Capitan Alatriste, let me know. I found the publisher's website, but it looks old and I can't read it well enough (yet) to know whether to give them money or not.
