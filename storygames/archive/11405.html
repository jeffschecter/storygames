\n In this thread you can vote for the characters you wish to see included in the Montsegur 1244 Expansion Set. See the project outline here.

Vote for up to 3 characters by replying to this post. Feel free to include comments with your vote. The vote ends on February 14th 2010.

Here is the shortlist of characters you can vote on:

1. Joscelin
Adult male. Troubadour. Travelling entertainer and bringer of news, trapped in Montsegur by the siege.
\u2022\tWhat is your real name?
\u2022\tWho in Montsegur doesn\u2019t recognise you?
\u2022\tWhat happens to the hero Ogier in your most popular song?

2. Aude
A young lady of noble birth. Heiress of the Fanjeaux fortune. Perfect.
\u2022\tWhy did you give up the life as a noble to become a perfect?
\u2022\tWhat item of great wealth do you carry in secret?
\u2022\tWho helps you leave Montsegur on certain moonless nights, and to what purpose?

3. Eloise
Middle aged woman. Recently widowed sister of Corba.
\u2022\tWhat do you wish for your sister?
\u2022\tWho do you know in the crusader camp?
\u2022\tWhat is it, you cannot tell Bernard?

4. Etienne
Young man. Perfect. Brother of Arsande. Arrives to Montsegur during the siege.
\u2022\tWhy were you not in Montsegur when the siege began?
\u2022\tWhich wrong do you wish to make right?
\u2022\tIn what way are you not perfect?

5. Guilhelm
An old man. Blind. Fought in the Fourth Crusade as a young man 40 years ago.
\u2022\tWhy are you not a perfect?
\u2022\tWhat is it that only you can see?
\u2022\tWhy are you so fond of Garnier?
