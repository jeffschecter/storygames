Problematic Character Virtues?

DevP
November 2006 edited November 2006 in Story Games

The seven deadly sins are generally problematic in interesting ways, while the seven heavenly virtues seem somewhat... less helpful.

But there are interestingly problematic virtues out there. Such as:

  • Mercy. You are willing to let the guilty go free, even when they aren't truly remorseful or reformed.
  • Patience. You are too willing to wait for a problem to pass, rather than confronting it directly.
