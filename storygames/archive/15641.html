\n I want to run a scenario at Dreamation 2012 that mashes together heroes from several different TV shows/Movies/Books/Comics etc.
\n
\nThe general theme is "Saving the world from stuff no one believes in."
\n
\nSo far I'm thinking of taking characters from Buffy:TVS/ Angel, Men In Black, Ghostbusters, Doctor Who/Torchwood, Warehouse 13, The Dresden Files by Jim Butcher, and The Laundry Files by Charles Stross.
\n
\nAre there any other IPs you would want to see in a game like that? Any particular characters that would be the most fun to play?
\n
\nSystem will probably be FATE, somewhere between SotC and DFRPG. I plan to pre-gen more than enough characters for any one session, and multiple characters from the more popular properties. I'd leave a bit of each character un-finished to allow customization at the table.
