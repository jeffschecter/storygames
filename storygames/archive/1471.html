2 weeks from tomorrow.

The cabins are paid for. Remi's getting the shirts. I have the dice. I'm thinking about nametags. I'm trying not to pee my pants.
Let's talk about logistics. What to bring and how to make stuff happen right.

Packing List
-First aid kit
-flashlight
-clothes & such
-toiletries
-shower flip-flops and towel
-sleeping bag or sheets & blanket
-fleece or other chilly-fall-weather gear
-rain gear
-snacks & candy to share
-a musical instrument if you play one
-mess kit if you have one
-$5 per car entry fee to the park
-a couple copies of any game you want to sell
-dice,paper, pencils, index cards, y'know: your game kit
-??
