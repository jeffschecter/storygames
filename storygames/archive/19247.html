\n Hi Guys,

We played Jason Morningstar's game "The Climb" a few weeks ago and had a ball! I have pictures that I will get around to putting up one day.

The question is, what should we play next?

Our priority in play is to have something with more established character backgrounds than the characters in "The Climb" but otherwise, mechanics and structure like "The Climb" is good.

I want to go straight for the bleed and darker topics but I think with some of my other friends, they will need to be brought into a more personal play style in a gradual fashion.

Any suggestions?

Stu.

    \n There's some interesting stuff here - http://www.great-murder-mystery-games.com/diamond-geezers.html and the link specifically points to Diamond Geezers, which is great. Don't let the fact it talks about murder mystery games put you off, the site is designed to be fairly popularist. They can point you to the more involved games quite happily.

    Also consider Intercon http://www.interactiveliterature.org/NEIL/ or if in the UK Consequences http://www.ishtari.co.uk/consequences/ as other places to play.
