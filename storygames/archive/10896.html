\n Just thought, since the beta started today, I'd start up a thread to go along with it. Anyone have any thoughts about FreeMarket? I'm just working my way through the PDF at work and so far it's some pretty standout stuff - I love the setting and keep coming back to the whole discussion about an RPG being about friendship and creating stuff and having a good time of it vs. violence and blowing shit up and being morose. It's interesting. I want to create a baking MRCZ and do a game about an Ace of Cakes-style competition. Good clean fun. Plus nanites.

Mechanically, I feel a little daunted by all the custom chits and cards and stuff but I have a feeling commenting on those is going to have to wait until I can try them out.

Lastly, I'm really digging the commentary stuff at the top of each page and the clever little bits of reference throughout. Anybody know who the Originals are meant to be references to, if anyone?
