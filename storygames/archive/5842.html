\n Hello, comrades,

This July 12th, Collective Consciousness will be held at All Fun And Games in Apex, North Carolina.

CollectiveCon is an all-day gaming event for gamers and game designers. Hopefully, it's a chance for people from the Raleigh-Durham area of North Carolina (and beyond!) to meet, play games, and have a good time.

For more information, please check out http://collectivecon.wordpress.com.

Hope to see you there!
