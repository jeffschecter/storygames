\n Hey there!

After reading through Monsterhearts (and greatly enjoying it), I came across this implicit rule: every Skin favors 2 out of 4 stats. I wanted to inspect how many Skins exist for what combination, and also I took the bait to graphically solve this system. How do you display the "2 out of 4" rule visually?

Well, here's how!

You need three sectors and a triangle. At the six intersections you will find the unique combinations (HC, HV, HD, DV, DC, CV).



Anyway. Maybe it helps someone to identify their favorite combination? (Well, if you disregard themes and Moves, but you get the picture).

Cheers!
