    \n My charity is the National Theatre. The arts are under great pressure, at the moment, and need support. The National Theatre produces some astonishing theatre and I'm proud to do a little to support it.

    So, I'm going to read the H.P. Lovecraft story Dagon and release it as an MP3.\xa0If you donate $5, I'll send you a copy. If you donate $10, I'll credit you at the end of the audio recording. And, if you donate $15, I'll thank you effusively at the start of the recording. If you donate more than that, I'll also read something of your choice: ten words for every dollars you donate.

    If I raise more than $250, in total, I'll release the audio recording publicly.

    Please donate as much as you can. It's for a superb cause.

    \n My charity is Reading Is Fundamental. Reading has been a huge part of my life and I really consider myself fortunate to have grown up in an environment where materials were not only easily accessible, but plentiful.

    So...if you donate $10 to my charity...I will cover one song (of your choosing) on youtube. I can promise nothing in terms of quality, but I will try my darndest to do it justice. I will also give you credit for your generous donation. If you donate $20, I will write you a short song (on the topic of your choosing, if you like) and post it on youtube. I will of course, give you a shout out as well.

    $10 will buy one book for a child in need; $20 will buy two. Donate as much as you are comfortable with bearing in mind that the more you donate, you can make that much more of a difference.

    Donate down here:
    Donate to Reading is Fundamental

    And then whisper me the amount that you donated and any other sort of information you want so I can craft/cover a song for you!
