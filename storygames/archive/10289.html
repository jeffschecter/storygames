    \n Well, the 10am Luke Crane panel didn't actually have any Luke. Or, more accurately, look just got to stand up and take a bow after the hosts pimped BW and MG. Fun panel, but not much new for anyone acquainted with story games.

    Oh, based on that panel, Luke's a "rockstar GM" and Jared almost is.

    There were some good times, with Jared running a huge game of Action Castle that had Steve Jackson, among others, playing.

    I was also not the only person trying to get some indie games into Gabe and Tycho's hands. Someone else gave them Carry Away Jordan and some unnamed other indie game, and I gave them all John Harper's recent stuff, at John's request. I may have given them a copy of the 2-page version of my game too, but I'm not quite sure if I took it out of the folder with John's stuff or not.
