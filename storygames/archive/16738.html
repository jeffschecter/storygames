I didn't know this place was here until Lizzie Stark told me. Thanks, Lizzie (buy her book, Leaving Mundania, right now--if you have a copy, buy another for a friend or relative).

I am asking for donations to help bring larps to the classroom. A playtest of our second larp, Hit Seekers, is just about over. But other schools would like to get the kit/box, and we need help to make that happen. If you can't help with money, maybe mention this or post it around? We're in an LAUSD school whose funding was cut. The graduation rate of LASUD is 55%. That's high school graduation, not college admission. LAUSD is in a death match with UTLA, the teacher's union. I don't want to pick sides, I just want kids to learn. So please help.
