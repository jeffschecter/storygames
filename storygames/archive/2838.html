    \n
    \n I go to Knox College in Galesburg, IL which I think is in the Quad Cities area, although my knowledge of the local geography isn't that good as I'm originally from California. The gaming scene is pretty good, here. We've got a LARP that draws people from as far as Peoria, a thriving weekly board game/Magic night, a bunch of active tabletop games, and spontaneous/annual large gaming events seem to happen fairly often. Sigma Nu fraternity (which I'm in) is known around campus as "the gamer frat." All in all, it seems hard not to find gaming opportunities here.
