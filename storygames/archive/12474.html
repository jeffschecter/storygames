\n This year the Annual Gen Con EN World RPG Awards are pleased to celebrate their 10th ceremony, and would like to invite you to participate in a VIP cocktail reception on Friday, August 6th from 6:30 to 7:30. We will be serving cheeses, antipasto, and a light assortment of hors d'oeuvres at the Westin Grand Ballroom. Join Peter Adkison, Industry Guests of Honor, and your favorite RPG publishers. Hobnob and take advantage of the opportunity to bid first on items in our annual ENnies silent auction.

Book early to get the discounted price. After the July 23rd, the price per ticket will go up.

Remember: If you win the Dream Date Auction, a ticket to this party is included. I will keep a running list in this thread of industry people who will be there as I get them in.

If you want to purchase tickets, fill out the form here: http://ennies.wufoo.com/forms/2010-ennie-awards-vip-cocktail-party/

Industry Participants
Peter Adkison - Big Wig
Erik Bauer - Gaming Paper
Maragret Weis - Margaret Weis Productions
Matt Forbeck - Game Designer/Developer/Editor
Steve Ellis - Game Designer and Consultant
Brian Lewis - Former Vice President of Business Development & General Counsel for Wizards of the Coast
Jeff Tidball - Gameplaywright
STAN! - It's STAN!
Rob Trimarco - Pantheon Press
John Reyst - d20pfsrd
Greg Vaughn - Necromancer Games
Chris Pramas and Steve Kenson - Green Ronin Publishing

Tony Law
ENnies Assistant Business Manager
tony@ennieawards.com
