\n EndGame will be hosting a series of pick-up games starting this month and continuing throughout (at least) June.
\n
\nFrom the EndGame site:
\n
\n
We walked out of the last MiniCon with a total warm and fuzzy feeling for Role-Playing. It was one of our bigger MiniCons in a while. There were a buncha people who got to try games that were brand new to them, and that got us noodling...
\n
\nWe are pleased to present the EndGame RPG Demo Series. What is that, you may ask? We have worked with many of the same GMs you see at the MiniCons to bring you short, but sweet 'demo sessions' of games you may not have tried yet. Consider the whole series a very long wine tasting...but with dice! The schedule, and how to sign up are ready, up, and raring to take sign ups. Join us, it will be a hoot.
\n
\n
\nVisit EndGame's mini-site to peruse games, and sign up to play or to run.
\n
\nKeep an eye on that page, as Chris will be posting more games as they come in.
