\n While not a story-game, I figured people here might find this interesting.

Audatia is a two-person card-game that models medieval / early renaissance longsword fencing out of armor:

http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/audatia-the-medieval-swordfighting-card-game/x/92517

There's a reasonably in-depth review on reddit by someone who tried the demo at Ropecon:

http://www.reddit.com/r/wma/comments/1j3nud/guy_windsors_audatia_the_medieval_swordfighting/cbci3cz

There are also additional comments on some of the design goals and decisions:

http://audatiagame.com/home/blog/
http://guywindsor.net/blog/2013/08/5-reasons-why-audatia-matters/
http://guywindsor.net/blog/2013/07/games-and-swords-digital-v-analogue/

Disclosure: I'm not directly involved, by I know some of the folks behind this, and I'm funding this on Indiegogo.
