    \n We played the EoD setting using straight-up Apocalypse World rules. There was very little actual conversion. We wrote a custom move for "when you take dust" that hooked straight back into the EoD rules. We also wrote up an AW profile for some of the weapons and stuff (like the Storm Engine in particular). I think there were a few more custom moves like "when you attack god" and so on. We didn't need to do much. EoD basically provided an awesome Apocalypse World setting.
