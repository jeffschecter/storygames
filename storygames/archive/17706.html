\n Give the gift of game! Buy a PDF for a friend or loved one and get that same PDF for yourself, for free! Your recipient will have their game delivered on Epimas (December 24th) of this year. You, of course, will receive your PDF once we process the order, so you can read up on the game ahead of time and play it with your friend or loved one on Epimas--as is tradition.

This year Epiclaus has made some lists that he's checked considerably more times than twice. The games have been sorted into Naughty and Nice lists, so you can avoid embarrassing holiday moments like playing Under My Skin with your mother-in-law.

The Naughty List
1,001 Nights by Meguey Baker
Bliss Stage by Ben Lehman
Final Girl by Bret Gillan
Fortune's Fool plus "Grimm Tales" supplement from Pantheon Press, LLC
Hyakumonogatari Kaidandai by Julia B. Ellingboe
It's Complicated by Elizabeth Sampat
Pickets and Blinds by Kevin Allen Jr.
Under My Skin by Emily Care Boss

The Nice List
Apocalypse World (with Extra Nice Material) by Vincent Baker
Clover by Ben Lehman
The Cat of Excellence by Frida Ullenius
Daughters of Verona by Wilhelm Person
Dread House by Emily Care Boss & Epidiah Ravachol
Human Contact by Joshua A.C. Newman
Misspent Youth (with Extra Nice Material) by Robert Bohl
Time & Temp by Epidiah Ravachol
Victoria by Daniel Hodges

Buy the games one at a time, as Naughty or Nice bundles or as the Whole Tofoose. Or mix and match your own custom bundles!
