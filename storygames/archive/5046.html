\n Just curious what games folks are giving as gifts during the Holidays?

I've ordered a copy of Evilhat's "Spirit of the Century" for an old gaming friend. I also hooked him up with some FUDGE Dice. I was pretty happy to be able to order the book and the dice in one spot at IPR.

I just ordered a copy of Eric J. Boyd's "The Committee for the Exploration of Mysteries" and Emily Care Boss' "Shooting the Moon" for a trio of friends who have a background in traditional games, but are pretty hip to new and interesting things.

I'm pretty confidant that my old gaming buddy is going to get a lot of use out of SoTC, but I honestly don't know if my other friends are going to cuddle up with The Committee and Shooting the Moon, but even if they don't run the games, I think it's worth the effort just to expose them to something new and cool.
