    \n Nice April Fool article.

    Single Skill Design:
    Instead of having a range of non-combat abilities, characters now have a single skill called Dude. This skill does not have a value because it is a roleplayed skill. Whenever the player encounters a non-combat obstacle that they do not see an obvious way to bypass, they say "Dude" in a nasal drawn-out whine. The pitch and duration of the whine, and it's corresponding annoyance to the GM, will determine success or failure.

    It was the "in a nasal drawn-out whine" that clinched it.
