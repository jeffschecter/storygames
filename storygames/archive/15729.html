\n Hey story games,

Just got a new post up on my blog. One of the things I'm working on is a re-skinning of Kirin Robinson's Old School Hack for Shadowrun. It's for my once-in-a-blue-moon gaming group, should we get together any time soon.

Here is the first draft of the Street Samurai character sheet. They're currently in 11x17 format, but I'll do them in 8.5x11 too once I finish with all the classes.



Quick PDF Export

If anyone is feeling up for it, I'd love to hear some feedback. I think a full on hack for Shadowrun would be pretty boss. I'm up for the challenge, but it could be hours and hours and hours of work. If anyone on SG would like to see a full hack, I'd love to know so I'm not spending time on something only my group will play with. Otherwise, I'll just do the character sheets and wing the rest probably!
