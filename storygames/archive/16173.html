    \n Besides Breaking the Ice, Shooting the Moon can also work for romantic "comedy" of a sort, albeit more action-y one maybe. Those two games are as light as you (collectively) want them to be.
    Elizabeth Sampat's It's Complicated is also very good for the genre, but requires 3 players at a minimum.
    Maid RPG can also work for certain sorts of romantic comedy, I guess.
    I like what I hear about Cel-Style GxB (not 2p, I think) and the not-as-new-and-shiny Misery Bubblegum (also not 2p, I think).
    And if you ever succeed in bringing a large group together, here's The Upgrade.
