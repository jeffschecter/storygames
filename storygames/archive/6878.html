    \n The article is a long and detailed description of the Motivation/Relationship-map invented by Kaare Berg, a Norwegian GM/game-designer. He's been developing this tool for some years now. This article seems to sum it up very nicely, giving good advice on how to use the map and what effects to get from it. I am impressed with his presentation of it, and the way he both describe the theory behind it, and show how it functions with actual-play examples. A very nice article on a very useful tool for classical games!

    \n Sorry Chris, I do not know of any English version. Contact Kaare and ask him. I believe he will be more than willing to send you an English version if he has one. You may go to the Norwegian RPG-forum and post a message to him there:
    www.rollespill.net
    When registering:
    navn = name
    passord = password
    epost-adresse = mail-address

    The most central themes (you may post in English):
    Rollespill generelt = general RPG-talk
    Norske rollespill = discussion of specific Norwegian RPGs
    Utenlandske rollespill = disussion of foreign RPGs
    Spillskaping = game design

    Good luck!
