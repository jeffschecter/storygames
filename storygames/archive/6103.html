    \n I like the idea of taking a game text (FATAL?) and having a bunch of people take it home and try to hack something legible out of it.

    I bet you'd see all sorts of funky shit as people try to one up one another. I'm thinking of stuff like the "Untitled" rpg, various different approaches to writing game text, etc. Could be interesting!
