\n Hey people.
\n
\nI've started a blog called Buried Without Ceremony.
\n
\nIt's a blog about story games, shared narrative, communication, intentional living and exploring our experiences.
\nIt's going to be tied into some experiments about impromptu community building, anti-consumerist living and guerilla theatre.
\n
\nIt's meant to be a fairly weighty platform for discussion, hopefully.
\nI'd love it if you all read it and joined in / challenged me to rethink stuff.
\n
\nCool.
