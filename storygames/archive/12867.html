\n I'm finding that somehow, if the universe has intention, it's driving me towards WEG's D6 system. I was given a bunch of Star Wars D6 books, for free, while a friend was clearing out his extra copies, and conveniently some other D6 based systems (Men In Black, a couple others) have had supplements on sale for 99 cents, making it too good to pass up. There's nothing about it that really pulls me towards it, but the price just does it.

Have any of you found something similar, situations that just seem to make a certain system more prevalent to your gaming collection that isn't because you're seeking it out?
