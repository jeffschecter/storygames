    \n Shreyas,

    While I also like robots, I think the basic structure of Bliss Stage can definitely be drifted to any number of different applications. In your pharaoh example, what would be the interaction between the pharaoh and his family left behind? Since the breaking and building of Trust and Intimacy are core elements of the way the system works, how would we rationalize this in the context of Egyptian mythology?

    One way I think this could work is if all the Interlude Actions were actually flashback scenes detailing the interaction between the Pharaoh and his court. The power of your scepter failed you at a critical moment? That's because the former chief priest-embalmer sabotaged your final sacraments as a consequence of his humiliation at your hands.

    Also, let's not forget the importance of Anchors, any ideas of how they would translate into this premise?

    \n So, I imagined more of a highschool drama dreamworld/Matrix thing. Imagine the pilot, living a hardscrabble life, goes into the submersion tank and wakes up in a "normal" teenage life. School, friends, parents, homework. And maybe their life is just a little bit sweeter than they might otherwise expect. But some of the people they meet over there are aliens, trying to seduce the pilots into detaching from the real world completely.

    Now imagine the anchor, watching their lover and tracking the vitals. How does it feel to talk the person your sleeping with through the seduction of an enemy agent? How does it feel to finally watch them drift off and leave you behind?
