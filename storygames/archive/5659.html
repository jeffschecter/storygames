    Posted By: Jason MorningstarRussian bad-ass lady pilots
    \nThe Night Witches? We were talking about them at work the other day. Wait, there was a thread here about NWs recently. Need to read that.

    I was told that part of made the Night Witches so deadly (other than their utter disregard for their own safety) was the fact that they didn't track their targets the way their male counterparts do. Now, I know little about such things, but apparently, men fixate on targets, which can result in successful enemy flanking maneuvers. You're so focused on shooting this one guy, who's just out of range, that you don't pay attention to this other guy coming up behind you. He shoots you, you die. But female pilots perceived moving objects differently, and were not just focused on a single opponent at a time. This allowed them to avoid such entrapments.

    Maybe you've read something similar...?
