    \n The primary goal of this meetup is for gamers in the DC Area to meet and have a chance to game together, so the actually game doesn't matter much to me.

    That said, I'd prefer the opportunity to try new indie games, as that's the best way to learn their nuances.

    Last time, we played Arkham Horror (a board game), Grave Robbers from Outer Space (a card game), and Spirit of the Century (a role-playing game).
