    Posted By: boulet[Off Topic]

    Just a question though. In session 3 sister Chase "escalates" to talking though the conflict started as physical. It kind of contradicts the way I understand how players can escalate conflicts in DitV (talking -> physical -> fists -> guns). Did I read the rules wrong?
    \nYes, you did read them wrong. The rules don't give a "direction" to escalation. You can escalate from shooting to talking, or from punching to moving. All you need is to change to another arena of conflict.

    But for this to work, you must remember the rule about ignoring the raise. If the raise can be ignored, isn't a valid raise. So you can't just start talking during a gunfight to get more dice, doesn't work that way. You have to yell "If you don't stop shooting and you don't surrender, I will go to town and I will shoot you children and then their mother!" or something like that.

    (a talking raise that won a conflict with a sorceress, done by a player during one of my games: "the Lord of Life forgive you")
