    \n\nNeko_Ewen \n\n\n
    \n
    \n\nApril 2013 \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n Thanks guys.

    I never doubted that it would get funded. I would not have guessed that we'd be at over 200% in less than 24 hours. The Kickstarter last month for the card game I designed barely squeaked to 105% on the last day, though it had dramatically less buildup.

    Juggling what's sane for us to attempt for stretch goals and what people are clamoring for is trickier than I realized. OTOH it's already turning up some really interesting stuff, like Allen Varney (of Paranoia fame) has offered to write something for us!\n
    Will you approach regular game distribution companies for this? I'd really like to be able to direct people (not in my immediate vicinity) to stores where they can peruse the book before buying it. Online stores don't usually have that option.
    \nWe're going to be looking into a variety of distribution methods. I want to get it out to lots of people in lots of different places, but we're just starting out as a part-time two-man venture (albeit with lots of awesome friends helping us out), so there are limits to what we can do just now.

    I have this adventure in mind that would require the henge to go to the big city to comfort a child who must visit her sick grandmother in the hospital.
