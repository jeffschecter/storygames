\n Just wanted to let everyone know how my Patreon project is evolving.
I'm still developing games at my snail-slow pace, but in the meantime a couple of talented Italian artists are going to help me produce amazing full-color illustrations for my World Deck projects.
This means 60 cards for each game setting... starting with Dreamwake.

The cards (image+setting info) will be freely available from my main www.UnPlayableGames.tk website.
The card-seized picture will be licensed under CC, completely free to be used even in commercial projects.
On my Patreon you will then be able to get access to the high-quality/high-resolution version of the illustrations (again, with unrestricted use licencing)

Here is the first preparation sketch for a Heretic dwelling in the dark and damp backstreets of Babele, the main bastion in the Sea of Mysts.
