    \n\nJiituomas \n\n\n

    \n\nJuly 2009 \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n In contrast, I was really disappointed with Dominion. Both me and my son found it a solid, interesting mechanical concept, which soon degenerates into repetitive group solitaire unless special precautions (card selections) are taken beforehand. This was our shared opinion after both a dozen test runs with groups of various sizes and some mechanics experiments. I have heard, however, that the expansions make it a lot more interactive, so I am holding my final judgment for now.

    Then again, I would have found 4th Ed innovative two decades ago, but now I think it's just over-appreciated crap running on nostalgia. So I may be biased against certain kinds of play systems. And I think one of these products that I dislike will end up being the winner anyway.
