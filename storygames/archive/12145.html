    \n
    \n The rulebook editing is widely criticized by its fans (see FFG forum).
    \n
    \nFor a summary, though not on one page, try universal head's text one
    \n
    \nhttp://www.headlesshollow.com/freebies_games.html#wfrp3
    \n
    \nOther fan resources at hammerzeit
    \nhttp://74.53.20.230/hammerzeit/index.php?option=com_phocadownload&view=category&id=2:utilities&Itemid=54
    \n
    \nScroll down to the GM Sheet 2.0 for a more simplified visually appealing summary of key points though not enough to understand it all, enough to remind/key in.
    \n
    \nThe Players Guide (emirikol's) is a compromise of two (text and more info but not as dense of Universal head's) and is more intended as a guide to walking through players.
    \n
    \nSome official errata pdf's at FFG site.
    \n
    \nThe rules are actually simpler than the game text makes them seem. GM has to grok that "really we're giving you guidance not pretending to tell you how to run things exactly, you want an "act" to last 3 days or 3 minutes, fine".
