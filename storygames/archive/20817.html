\n We just started a Kickstarter for my game Ghost Court. It is Story Games adjacent, being, essentially, a low-commitment social parlor larp in a box.

You might be interested! It is very silly and fun, but it builds on what I've learned making tabletop games, structured freeform games, and live action games and games specifically for teaching and learning in different ways.
