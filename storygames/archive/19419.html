    \n Friends of mine have played my game Enter the Avenger during a road trip, and reported it worked fine.
    I advise you use folded bits of paper instead of the playing cards, for ease of handling. In terms of required attention, I wouldn't assign the Avenger role to the driver, and maybe have them skip the Suspect role as well (but rotating roles mesh well with taking turns driving).

    \n I love playing games like the "Groundhog Day" movie: I come up with a time-loop story that keeps repeating (a day, an hour, 10 minutes). My player dies over and over again and has to figure out what's going on and how to get out of the loop.
    Perfect for roadtrips.
