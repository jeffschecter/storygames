    Posted By: Ben LehmanJohn: Bliss Stage was released, for real, in 2007.

    If not having art with your text disqualifies you from a real release, I dunno.
    \nSorry, I have not followed the release in detail. My impression had been that there was a release in 2007 that was more of a playtest or ashcan release. Currently, the web page for Bliss Stage says "Bliss Stage will shortly be available at Indie Press Revolution. If you want it before then, please paypal $25 to taogames@gmail.com, extra for international shipping." So it seemed like the release was still in process.

    Among other games, authors have not generally been submitting ashcan editions, presumably because they would prefer to be judged on a later version of the game. The Indie RPG Award policy has always been that new editions aren't generally eligible, so if someone submits under an early edition, then they can't get in under a later version. Also, if a game hasn't been easily available, then people aren't likely to know it well enough to vote on it.

    It is very late, but I can put the 2007 edition on the list if you want.
