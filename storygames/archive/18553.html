\n So one of my fond childhood memories is of Tales of the Arabian Nights (though at the time it was the WEG edition). Two open questions:

First, are there any similar games? The ones I know of are Agents of Smersh, Always/Never/Now, and Arkham Horror if I squint.

Second, does anyone else like this style? Is there an audience for them? They're a little more uhh confining by nature than most games, but for someone like myself who has a bad imagination...
