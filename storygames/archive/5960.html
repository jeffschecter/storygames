    Posted By: Kuma

    Design a roleplaying game where the characters are all trees. Not trees that can move and talk. Not Ents. Trees.
    \nThis game already exist:
    PiGiTiNDi, cio\xe8 il "gioco di ruolo delle piante grasse telepatiche nel deserto"!

    (for people who don't know italian: "the role-playing game of the telepathic cactus in the desert"

    In this game you play a telepathic cactus in the desert. This simplify the game system a lot. There is no need of conflict or task resolution (you can't do nothing, and there is no conflict), there is no movement rate, nothing of the sort.
    You play it simply by immersing yourself in "cactusness of being a cactus", feeling what your character feels, exploring this role-playing experience.

    This ground-breaking game, called "the first diceless, ruleless, masterless, playerless e lifeless rpg" is not a new indie game, not by a long shot: it was produced by the unparelled italian creativity applied to rpgs in early 2000, well before the creation of the Forge, and it's on the verge of it's 9th year of acclaimed play worldwide!

    (for people who don't like fantasy, there is a variant called "C\xe0SNeD, il gioco di ruolo del cactus schizofrenico nel deserto!" where the cactus is schizophrenic, not telepathic, and you play one of his personalities)
