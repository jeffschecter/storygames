    Hm, how is Mouse Guard for those new to RPGs? I was mulling over pulling my cousin, his girl and her brother (all avid Video-game nuts but table-top cherries) into an evening's one-shot of something to share the joy. I was thinking of hitting them with the playtest version of Messers Holter & Morningstar's Love in the Time of Sei\xf0, but perhaps M.G. would be a more legit and surefire thing?
