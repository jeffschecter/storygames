\n Dragon Con is coming this Labor Day Weekend to Atlanta. Will any o' y'all be in town? I'll be running my first official tabletop RPG for Dragon Con on Friday at 0900 (Bully Pulpit Games' The Skeletons). Beyond that, I'll be around to run games-on-demand Saturday from 0930 til 1600 (and offering this menu: Union of Magical Childcare Professionals, Into The Odd, Fiasco, and octaNe.)

The monthly, one-shot tabletop RPG event I run at Manuel's Tavern and/or Joystick Gamebar started two years ago at Dragon Con. We're always looking to meet new gamers in the area, or just game with folks from anywhere, so come around and say hello!
