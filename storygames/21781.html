    \n Jeff, David_Berg and I play so games online semi-regularly. We might be able to bring you in for something at a future date.

    That said, I think "Hey, I'm interested in playing if anyone wants to invite me!" buried in a larger thread is unlikely to result in too many results.

    A more specific pitch - a time, a concept, a particular game, etc - is much more likely to work for you. (e.g. "I'm going to facilitate a session of such and such a game on this date and this time. Anyone want to join in?")
