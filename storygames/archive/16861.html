    \n Warrens and War Machines

    During those grim times, the great lion king R'Arore became increasingly paranoid and charged his felines with ensuring that all the animals stay in plain sight where they could not plot against him. The lapin, however, could not tolerate the open spaces and left in droves to inhabit the space below.

    In Warrens and War Machines, you and your friends tell the story of the Lapin Secession. Using a clever plot building mechanism, the key events of the struggle are laid out and played out using blank cards. Just as history is said to repeat itself themes, characters, locations, and technologies that are introduced to the history make their way into the oncoming struggle. As you stand at the tipping point, the decisions you make will eventually come to define both the cause of the king's paranoia and the outcome of the war!

    \nSteampunk Cats of the Apocalypse - The RPG

    Long after the fall of mankind conflict once again rages across the shattered world. The Cats, with their infernal steam driven technology seek to subjugate all the other animals to their will. Only you and your kind - the free animals - stand against them. They may have machines of wonder, but you are mighty warriors who draw you power from the Earth itself.

    Pick-up your Spear of Might Telluric Warrior and fight against the tyranny of the Cats in this easy to learn and fun to play game based on Barbarians of Lemuria system. Features all new flaws and boons for various animal types and career options unique to the world. All you need to play are some friends, paper and pencils and 2 six-sided dice!
