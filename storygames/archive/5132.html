\n I'm looking to have a minicon right here in the beautiful city of Peoria, IL. The date is February 23, 2008. This will be a roleplaying and boardgaming event. The long-term goal is to try to develop a group similar to nerdNYC. We'll see if the area can support a group like that, but, in the meantime, we'll have fun gaming with each other!

For more information, and to begin preparing for the con, please go to Go Play Peoria. I'll also answer questions here.

Seth Ben-Ezra
