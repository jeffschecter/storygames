Judd Karlman, Tony Lower-Basch, Malcolm Craig, Emily Care Boss, Vincent Baker, Rob Bohl, and I will be joining our robot lions into a giant game designer at Gen Con this year. We're keeping a blog of various fun things over at Playcollective.org, where you can see us in our various Lion forms.

\n

I'm the Magenta Lion. I form the butt.

\n

We're getting our stuff together for our booth, including some ashcan games as well as our proven darlings. We'll probably even have a flag!
