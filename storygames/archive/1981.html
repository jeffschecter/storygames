    Just as a hint, be sure to schedule a substantial chunk of time for a one- or two-shot chat game to make sure you can finish it. Chat games are notoriously slow, maybe around 20% the speed of face-to-face games. In the game I play with my wife, it took us three hours to finish one scene this week, and we're very focused and in tune with each other. In the Dogs game that Thomas ran on the Foundry, we managed to do one or (rarely) two conflicts during each three-hour slot.
