[Names Project] The Story Games Name Project is On!

Jason_Morningstar
January 2007 edited January 2007 in Directed Promotion
Hey! Long time, no see! The Story Games Name Project has been clicking and whirring in the background for months, and now it is nearing completion. The art is done, we have a beautiful Leo Lingas cover and wonderful interior pieces by indie comics guys and dingbats by Shreyas. The layout is (almost) done, the inimitable Joshua BishopRoby has tackled that beast.

Here's a sample .pdf.

I'd like to refresh everybody's memory that this project still exists, and offer a last minute chance for you to buy a little ad.

$25 gets you a 1.5" by 4.5" ad wherever we have space, typically on the second page of a set but at the Josh's discretion.

$50 gets you a 8.5" by 5.5" ad, wherever we want to put it. We have space for exactly one of these.

Your ad will be inserted exactly as you give it to us. If you lack the ability to produce an ad, we'll be glad to just add some text you provide us in a distinctive font inside a border, but it won't look good or fancy. Consider asking a designer friend to help you out.

Submit a camera-ready greyscale .tif file, ideally at 600 dpi, either as an attachment or a URL we can grab, along with a copy of the electronic receipt from the Carter Center indicating your donation of the desired amount (or more!). Feel free to buy an "ad" that promotes something other than a game - like, say, story-games.com for example, or your favorite podcast or whatever. If your ad will make the book look stupid, we'll reject it.

If you want to include an ad at this point, it's late in the game and we'll need it in about a week - we realized we had space so we thought we'd try to wring some more charity out of you Yeggs. Hooray!
