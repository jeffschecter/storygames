    \n Jeff, I saw your most recent post just before I was going to respond! Hope you enjoy it!

    The Short Story edition makes the free stuff much more playable. It actually has a process for creating starting situations and scenes. It also simplifies the free version's rules down to just the fun stuff that actually works.

    I'm currently working on an expanded edition, so you can play multiple Authors and it'll work smoothly. I've also simplified how the Weird works and made the process of creating supporting characters more interesting. I'll be doing another playtest of that next weekend.

    Because of the work I'm doing on that, I don't think I'll be printing the Short Story edition because:

    - I'm still a bit uncomfortable with the whole printing process, and
    - I want to minimise the confusion between the various editions once this new one's published (I'm anticipating a problem here).
