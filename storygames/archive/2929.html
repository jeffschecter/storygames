\n Ever since I started gaming, I've been annoyed at the shitton of stuff that one has to carry arround to game. Tons of heavy books (I started with Shadowrun, so go figure... ^_^ ), often loads of dice, stuff to write on, maybe chips or other tokens...
And that's without going into battlemaps, miniatures or snacks.

For a long time, this conflicted with my "No matter what happens, I'm prepared" style of packing my bags. (Prepared not in the survival sense, but in the "Whatever strikes my fancy" sense. I hate it when I want to listen to some music but don't have a CD player with me, or want to draw but find myself without a pen or sketchbook.) Dragging all stuff I need/want to game with me would break my back, on the other hand I've lost count how often I forgot to bring dice or whatever.

For a while, I satisfied the urge with just carrying around a few dice, mostly d6 (For Wushu or SR.) and a bunch of others, but they were somewhat bulky, the bag had the tendency to open, spreading my dice through my bag and it annoyed me that I couldn't really take any rules with me. (And this became worse when I saw the cool thread we had here about the ways other people here transport their stuff.)

BUT NOT ANY LONGER!!!

For now I'm working on my perfect gaming pack. It started when I found this Folder for cheap in a local store:


Out of the box, it just included a notepad (On which you can see my test character for the "Book of Nine Swords" d20 rules.) but I've already added a CD-R/W with my digital game collection (Lots of Indie games there, but also Tristat dX, d20 SRDs, ...), the first trial printout of my personal Wushu Core and a few pens.





Next Steps:

- The various file-holders will be labled, probably "Systems", "Characters" and "Various", or whatever...
- One of my LGS has TINY six-sides dice, like 2-3 mm, which I'll buy a dozen or so of and put them into the bag next to the CDs, in case I forget my other dice when playing Wushu or SR.
- I'm planning to print business card or something similar for my austrian SG forum, so don't have to write the address down whenever I give it to people.
- I THINK Engels Arcana cards could fit into the business card slots, but I haven't tried. Could be usefull, though.

But what other kind of stuff can I put into this nice thing? Any ideas?

Aaron
