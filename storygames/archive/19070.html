    \nIPR sells the book (plus PDF) for 25 bucks, or the PDF alone for ten bucks.

    Not cheap, but not crazy expensive for a nice-sized book. Also, it's a great game! You will dig it.

    It hits a great spot in that it's got fun, newfangled mechanics, but the material is all stuff that every gamer-nerd is down with (eg - D&D stuff).

    I think there's a pile of free supplements floating around, but I can vouch for Grim Portents, which is free, heavy on the OSR vibe, and has lots of good bits.
