So I mentioned awhile ago that I would spearhead collecting the Owlbear entries into a book and sell it through lulu for charity.

So. First off. Does anyone have a wildlife-related charity that they would like the proceeds to go to? If not, I'll pick one based on about 20 minutes of googling and how much I like their website.

Secondly. I think landscape (9x7) would be cool, mainly because I would like to do layout for that size because it sounds fun. We could also steal from Push and do some kind of designer commentary on the entries. Strong arguments against? Once format is settled, you can either lay it out to spec yourself and send me the file, or send me the text and I'll lay it out trying to adhere to your preferences.

If we could get some art from volunteer artists, that would be teh awesome. Remi, would you be interested in doing some artist wrangling? Anyone else?

Once we see how long the book is and how much it will cost through lulu, I'll set a price (probably in the neighborhood of $15).

I will be using these by permission with whatever copyright (creative commons, standard copyright, whatever) you want. Assuming you give me permission, of course.

I have no deadline. This is as time permits.
