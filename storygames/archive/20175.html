\n In the latest episode of our podcast Virtual Play, my brother Mel and I talk about resolving actions with cards, using excerpts of actual play involving the Deck of Fate in a Fate Core game I ran, and playing cards in a game of Daniel Cruz Chan's God-King in a game Mel ran. We try to answer the question, "Why not just roll dice?"
