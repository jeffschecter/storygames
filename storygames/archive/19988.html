\n Hello!
I'd like to share my first attempt in the making of a full English version of a game that I created, launched through Patreon: My Favorite Plaything:
https://www.patreon.com/creation?hid=1572925&rf=67465

MFP is a story game about childhood, innocence and overcoming fears. It is a lot about nostalgic feelings too. It uses domino tiles as \u201cmechaphors\u201d for cognitive connections in the attempt to overcome this fears.

My first published game, Violentina, was the first crowd funded Brazilian game, sometimes described as "Fiasco with Tarantino movies instead of Cohen Brothers", and will be eventually translated to English too.

So before I go, let me drop here a few more links, in case you are a little bit more curious about this game or my work:

If you like to see a video about the DIY bookbinding process of the copies granted to the Maecenas, or the \u201cZine\u201d version for sale, you can click here:

There is also this Steiff Ad that I found, that would be a perfect Book Trailer for MFP, and it hepls to catch in one minute the core feeling of a session:


And if you are curious about that Violentina stuff, you can get the free PDF here: http://www.secular-games.com/loja/?product=violentina-pdf

I'd love to hear your thoughts about My Favorite Plaything!

    \n This is very exciting. It's a setting I find very interesting and I was thinking for a while about finding a game that is even more rules-lite and more story driven than Little Fears. Thinking about it, I find it interesting that Little Fears is not referenced at all as a source.

    This video Mean Teddies is also a perfect inspiration for your game in my opinion:




    There is also a German teddy-bear based game called "Pl\xfcsch, Power & Plunder":
    http://www.ppundp.de/Einfuhrung.htm

    Little feedback on replaying dominos with dice:
    A work-around with the blanks would be to roll two d8s:
    1-6: number
    7: blank
    8: re-roll
    This way, you have the same distribution. (I need to buy a set soon...)

    I love the design of "My Favorite Plaything" and hope to get a chance to play it!
