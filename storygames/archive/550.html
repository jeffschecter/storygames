    Which did you do first, sex or roleplaying?

    I started gaming when I was eight . . . but I wouldn't say that I did anything but roll dice and kill orcs until I was much older. Still, I'd have to say roleplaying came first.

    You can have sex with your partner or be on time for roleplaying. What do you do?

    I GM with my partner, so we'd just lock the door and make our players wait outside.

    Have you ever faked: (a) an orgasm (b) a scene?

    a) No . . . sounds like a great way to sucker yourself out of a good time.
    b) No . . . also sounds like a great way to sucker yourself out of a good time.

    Have you ever gone to a game because of the cute guy/girl there? Even when it sucked?

    Yes . . . then I dated him . . . and now we've been together ten years.

    The game we met in was pretty awful, but we both dropped out as soon as we realized we could just date and play games together.

    You know when you find yourself saying the wrong name in the heat of the moment? Has it ever been an imaginary person's name?

    . . . no. Though I've never said the wrong person's name at all, so I'm not sure it's relevant.

    Do you turn out the lights when your characters have sex?

    Depending on the game, it can be anywhere from "fade to black" to, well, um, not fading to black.

    Do you ever imagine your character naked? Do they have genitals?

    I don't really imagine my characters naked . . . but then again, I'm not a visual person, more of a verbal/auditory one. I have described my characters naked, when relevant, and yes, they have genitalia.

    Do you like your 'conflicts' hard and fast or slow and easy?

    All of the above!
