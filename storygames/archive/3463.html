\n So we created a design collective in the mode of Master Mines, called Splitting the Party. The title references the fact that we're from all around the world. I intend to do work on Misspent Youth.

Also working there are Mike Reeves-McMillan (who is working on Errantry, City of Masks, The Pentasystem/Underground Railroad) and George Cotronis (working on Fallen).

I'd appreciate some eyeballs if you get the chance. Specifically the Master Mines folks, letting us know how we could be making better use of things.

EDIT: I am no longer a part of this collective, but I wish the guys well.
