\n Anyone else have this? I bought it from Jim Crocker's booth at Dreamation and it is definitely one of the best gaming investments I've made in a loooooong time.

There are a few ways it doesn't translate super-well from Internet to print. A few of the dungeons have hyperlinks, for example. :-)

It includes both the contest winners and other submissions. I haven't read the entire thing, but the winners overall do seem stronger. But there is goodness all around.

    \n I didn't know that they come in a book. What I do is, I take all the individual PDF files and then compile one big pdf of them for my own use. Almost as good as a book.
