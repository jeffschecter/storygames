\n A few months ago I started a Monday night game group at Time Warp Comics & Games in Cedar Grove (about 10 minutes south of the Willowbrook Mall). We meet (almost every) Monday from 7PM to 10:30.
\n
\nOver the past few months our numbers have declined due to work and moves, and we're looking for interested players and GMs to join our merry band. We're currently spending a few sessions playing a number of games, looking for something that really sticks.
\n
\nWe are currently playing:
\nDungeon World
\nDogs in the Vineyard
\n
\nWe have recently played:
\nApocalypse World
\nMouse Guard RPG.
\nLeverage
\nPoison'd
\nFiasco
\n
\nJust let me know if you're interested. We're very easy to get to for Essex/Passaic gamers, and reasonably easy for Bergen/Morris.
