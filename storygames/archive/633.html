    Cool thread!

    Are you planning on releasing a game this Gencon or sometime soon?

    I hope to release Carry by DexCon (July 17th-ish). If it doesn't happen by then, it should be out by Gencon.

    What's it about?

    It's the way-better version of my Game Chef 2005 entry. It's about a squad of Marines in Vietnam, their issues, and how they deal with the neverending pressure of war. It's about narrative and memory. It's heavily inspired by the book The Things They Carried and the movies Platoon and Full Metal Jacket.

    How close to completion are you?

    I'm finishing up playtesting and figuring out the layout right now. I'd say the rules are 95% done, the prose is 85% done, the layout is 50%, and all the accoutrements I want to be in the final product are 10% done. The book itself is going to be very much in the style of a military field manual, and balancing that with playability and usability is turning out to be a mite trickier than I thought.

    How, and with whom, are you publishing?

    Self-published 6x9 perfectbound book printed through lulu.com initially, unless I finish it early enough to get a short print run through a digital printer by the date I want. I haven't decided whether I want to offer a PDF or not. I also haven't decided if its going to be color or not. I haven't talked to Brennan about it yet, but I don't see any reason why it won't be available through IPR.
