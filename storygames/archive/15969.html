    \n I've wanted to facilitate it forever. I often have a copy on me and a couple of times I've even had a flask of whiskey on me to pass around and to add to the immersion (& tastiness).

    My intent has always been to ask the players to make sure we make it last a minimum of one hour (probably not much more though) to really inhabit the characters and learn about them.

    edit: I'd settle for 30 minutes if I had three other willing players and we had a half an hour 'til midnight...
