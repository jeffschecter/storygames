    \n Sign In Stranger is Emily Care Boss' game in development about humans who get sent to alien planets and explore a truly alien culture.

    I participated in a playtest session at Go Play PDX II, about which I posted at the Forge.

    I don't know if the playtest rules are posted somewhere, but you could ask Emily for them.

    The game would work well because it's all about the unknown being explored, strange things happening, and flashback scenes that relate to these events.

    \n Hey there,

    Thanks guys. It has mad libs world creation rules that would really make it fit for the Lost: The Game. You pull a random word and describe the aliens and other things on the alien planet based on them. Like a rorschach's blot world. The juxtapositions you get end up making it really funny oftentimes.

    And you know, I actually have thought about the flashback sequences in Lost a bit while working on the game. I've only seen the first episode of lost, but they are a striking example of this kind of storytelling.

    There are a couple playtest drafts/samples of the game online. The original booklet I put together for Gen Con last year is here. And the more recent, and much more complete playtest draft version is here.

    I can't wait to play with you, too, Jason. Yay Nerdly!

    best,
    Em
