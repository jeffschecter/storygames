\n The issues were resources and democracy.
The shocks were ubiquitous and accurate lie detectors and the plastic plague (hive minded sentient plastic)
The minutiae included the President of the World, the Delaware Hive Mind, lie detectors in voting booths and marriage no longer related to romance.

The protags were:
Davek (played by Dave K), a playboy billionaire intent on bringing love to the world. Antag: the President of the World
Empyrion (played by Simon), a biologist intent on making peace with the hive minds. Antag: the Humanists, antiplastic human purity protesters
Arty Weinburger (played by John), owner of OmniTruth (TM) manufacturer of the lie detectors, intent on controlling the hive minds. Antag: Pope Janet 23rd
Jane17 (played by Steve), secret identity of the allergic to plastic sister of AW, intent on freeing Chicago from the world democracy. Antag: "Plastic" Paddy McGuire, mayor of Chicago.

Scenes:
Davek's love fest in Libya was a success but police helping out with an earthquake nearby (due to the plastic stealing the oil) turned up to put a stop to it.

Empyrion doing some lab work finds some Humanist inflitrators and persuades them to join him, giving them plastic enhancements, however hir data is exposed.

Pope Janet takes OmniTruth to court which finds in hir favour and they are forced to remove the "truth" from their name.

Investigating Chicago's amenity supplies, Jane17's bots are destroyed but not before grabbing the plans

Davek's "kiss a baldy day" fails in a wave of bad publicity for him and The President who was attempting to have his resources signed over whilst in a drunken haze.

Empyrion manages to communicate with the Delaware Hive Mind but finds out that it want to kill hir.

Omni***th uses Jordan "the Plastic Messiah" to integrate the Shanghai Hive Mind, whilst Pope Janet's bid to create bad publicity fails.

Jane17 DNA bombs Chicago through the water supply making the entire population allergic to plastic however her materials are traced back to Arty and Omni***th.

Davek's psychopathic sister is turned into a sex zombie in a failed bid to get ecstacy into the water supply and sie is ruined and put in prison by the President of the World. (story end).

Empyrion gets a law passed to make everyone allergic to plastic but it spirals out of control and the hive minds are declared enemies of the state (story end)

Janet 23rd's Plastic Crusade kills Arty, the Shanghai and Delaware hive minds but the plastic rises up in war against humanity (story end).
