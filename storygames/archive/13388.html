\n Who are the most famous heroes from ancient times? My criteria:

1) Near-universal identification (not just name recognition, but actually knowing what they were famous for) among English-speakers.

2) No pure Gods. That is, sitting upstairs and ruling stuff doesn't count. Adventuring and risk is required.

3) Needn't be entirely virtuous, but no villains.

4) "Ancient times" really just means "before guns", and can be known from any source, from millenia-old myths to a movie that came out last year.

If you can state what they were famous for, that's extra cool.

I'm wondering how universal this is. Try not to read each other's lists before posting!

    \n By "fictional" here, I mean known to be invented and by a particular person at that. Achilles and Hercules/Heracles fall into the mythical/legendary. They may have been someone's invention or they may have a kernel of historical fact, but it's impossible to tell either way.
