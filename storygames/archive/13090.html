\n So we all remember the Your First Love thread, the thread that skyrocketed me to Story Games fame and fortune.

Okay, so we have all these games that we loved back in the day, right? Some of them were great, some of them we gritted our teeth and endured playing striving towards this dream of what the game could offer us that we bashed our heads against the wall tryin to make happen. So!

Your first love - if you were to play it now or next week or soon, would you use the old system? Would you hack it and if so what hacks would you give it?

Or would you retool some other system for it?

    \n My first love was Planescape, over the years I have prob thought about hacking each and every game I have to run Planescape with.

    If I was to run it today, depending on the feel I wanted for the game I would run it with either Burning Wheel or a hacked FATE/Dresden Files.

    Burning Wheel would be the harder hack, but would be the most rewarding for long term play and would definitely be a BW game first. I would probably make it a very human-centric Planescape too, not just for ease of hacking but it would limit the magic down to just Faith and Sorcery, which really fit well. Maybe give each plane or location its own Belief that anyone could follow as if it was one of their own for artha and traits.

    Conversely FATE/DF would be a real simple hack, drop the Trouble Aspect and replace it with a Belief Aspect, tinker a few stunts and powers and blammo a nice action packed adventure version of Planescape. I think it would also feel very close to what the game was back in the day, and less of its own thing like BW would.

    - Colin
