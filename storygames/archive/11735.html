Who all is going to be at PAX East in two weekends? I'll be there, demoing the shit out of my shit with Luke Crane and John Tornutlukin.

\n

But John is demoing something that shoots awesome out its asshole like a Saturn V that just ate at Chili's: Super Gatchapon Fighter

\n

If you're gonna be there, come to the Compleat Strategist booth to Burn some Wheels, Shock some Humans, and HOH RYUUUUKEN!
