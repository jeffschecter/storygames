    \n I can answer this is two parts. First is the scenario we are playing. It is a re-write of the first prequel cos I didn't like what was in the theater.

    All the action of this game takes place on Tatooine. The gist of this movie is to introduce characters, like Anakin Skywalker, and to introduce the villan \u2013 the clones. In this story the future Emperor is a good guy, Anakin is a good guy, heck! everyone is a good guy. The purpose of the movie is to get you to like these guys so you will feel bad when things go cockeyed.

    The characters are (in order of appearance):

    Lord Palpatine: A Jedi politician. Commander of the mission.

    Queen Padme: Politician, young unmarried woman, very important. This is her mission.

    Obe Wan Kenobi: A young Jedi. Loyal follower of Palpatine.

    A mysterious cloaked bad guy: You will learn later in the movie that he is a clone and that clone are evil\u2026

    Anakin Skywalker: A hot shot farm boy.

    Owen Skywalker: A not hot shot farm boy.

    Sand Raiders: Bad guys

    Jawas: Plot device guys

    Sneaky Bartender: Plot device guy

    Old Republic Storm Troopers: Bad guys because they are replacement clones!

    The story starts on board a space ship entering the the Tatooine star system. Palpatine and Padme are on the observation deck. Obe Wan will enter the scene with a message of a strange ship behind them.

    The scenes

    On the observation deck

    \u201cWe\u2019re under attack!\u201d Introduce cloaked villan.

    Crash landing: Introduce Anakin and Owen who see it happen

    To the rescue! Farm boys go to help. Sand Raiders introudce first fight.

    We need your help. First meeting of Padme and Anakin.

    There is a plot afoot. Who can you trust?

    Jawa transport. Time for Anakin to talk to Obe Wan.

    Intrigue in Mos Eisley. Cloaked figure gains control of the local storm troopers.

    The bar scene. \u201cI\u2019ve got a bad feeling about this\u2026\u201d

    Running street battle

    Capture a ship.

    Clone troopers are defeated. The enemy is revealed.

    Anakin decides to run off with Obe Wan to become a Jedi.

    End credits\u2026

    \n The next part is how this EMG works (there are actually many different Matrix games of varying complexity - this one is the simplest).

    The way this game works is that one player starts a scene by making up a little of what happens next. The other players may accept this (in which case it happens without a roll) or challenge it and say what happens instead. Everyone may challenge an openning action and may ask the host if they can challenge a challenge. The two players roll 2d6 and the high roller wins. The losers action will get a line drawn through it. Players add or challenge action by making a comment on this thread. If you challenge then roll the dice for both players to find out who wins. We will trust one another to play fair with the dice. Once a turn is resolved another player can jump in with the next beginning action. This builds on the scene. The host will say when we move on to the next scene. The scene are pre-laid out so don\u2019t go trying to change that!

    I will open a new blog post for each scene. All you have to do to play is to jump in.

    I will take this game slowly so I don\u2019t expect to get through a scene in a day. It may take a couple of days per scene. Enjoy the creative part. Take it slow. Make it fun.
