\n Drip is Kickstarter's new community support platform and we're one of only a few game-related organizations currently using it.

We're asking for your help in making more, better, weirder games. Supporting us via Drip works much like Kickstarter - we pay the fees and you, as a subscriber, get charged only what you pledge. I think it will be a good deal for everyone and we've got a lot of really interesting ideas for how to use the platform, and what to release through it (also I wrote a Fiasco playset specifically for Drip supporters that is, I think, Very Good). Please check it out!

https://d.rip/bullypulpitgames
