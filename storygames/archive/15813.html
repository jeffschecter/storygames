\n Hey all, I'm not normally all "Lookit mah blog!", but I wanted to share and there's only one day until Valentine's Day.

This was a cute thing I got from the blog of a large (for Japan) game store owner in Tokyo. To pass the time and decorate his store a little bit, he took his New Products area and made a bunch of cards basically ranking the games on their Valentine's Day Qualities (like "heartthrob" "romance" "makes you cry" etc):



The full translation and more details on the blog, but I wanted to share here. Also, open it up for discussion if you think this has any legs in the US.

Or, if you want to rank the Games You Play by arbitrary romance-themed categories, this is definitely the thread for that.

http://j-rpg.com/?p=30
