\n Hi Everyone,

I'm very happy to announce that registration is (finally) open for Go Play Northwest. You can find more information and a registration link here:
http://goplaynw.wetpaint.com/.

Go Play Northwest is an informal convention of game designers and players set in the heart of Seattle's Capitol Hill District (i.e. plenty of good food and beer within walking distance). It will be held the weekend of June 23-24. This is a convention in the style of Forge Midwest. The emphasis is on getting together, meeting your fellow story gamers, and playing some games. You can find more information at the Web site link above.

And yes, this is the first time we've run a convention. We take suggestions, complaints, feedback, and kudos happily, so send us email at goplaynw@gmail.com.

    \n Heya Caesar, the closest option is the Silver Cloud, about a block away:

    http://www.scinns.com/14home.htm

    It's quite nice, but I'm not sure how cheap it'll be. There aren't very many hotels on Capitol Hill.

    In downtown Seattle, there's the Green Tortise, which is a hostel. I don't know much about it:

    http://www.greentortoise.net/

    Whenever my father-in-law comes to town he stays at the Moore, which he says is quite nice and reasonably priced:

    http://www.moorehotel.com/

    You can get from either of these to the venue by taking a #2 bus.

    I hope that helps!
