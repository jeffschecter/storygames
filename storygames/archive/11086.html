    \n I'll make my ubiquitous plug for Long Live The King, as a fun game for just that many people (eight, counting you). Seven roles, plus the King (GM), and plenty of minor roles that the less-ambitious players can fill on an as-needed basis. It's as much of a line-action game as you want it to be--from full costumes and always acting in character, to little more than a strategy game you play mostly while standing up. And the price isn't too bad, considering that it comes with everything you need to play and that stuff is actually handy (lots of cards; "character sheets" for every role, complete with basic strategy; gold tokens).

    Mike Young also has a number of short, one-shot LARP games that are generally well received by those who play them.
