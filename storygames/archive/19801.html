\n Well, it's been a few weeks now that Kaleidoscope has been causing silly laughter out in the wild,
and it's past due for me to tell you about it!

Kaleidoscope lets you make ridiculous art-haus films with some friends and a stack of index cards.

I took Microscope, put it into the windtunnel of comedy and trimmed off everything that made it drag. Then I bolted on a couple of springs to get you airborne and an engine of wait-no-actually-you're-REMEMBERING-the-movie-not-producing-it with a couple of propellers to help reincorporation and forward momentum - and voil\xe0. Strap on your eyeballs! Here we go!

http://www.photographsoflightning.com/kaleidoscope/
