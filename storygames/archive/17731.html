\n Was wondering if there are any story games or systems that would lend themselves thematically to a campaign based on the works of writers like Flannery O'Conner, William Faulkner, or Tennessee Williams. Think deeply conflicted, dark religious overtones, moody bayous, and larger than life grotesque characters.

    \nMortal Coil might do, with its emphasis on novelistic exploration of theme via character-driven conflict. There's a supplement called More Things In Heaven and Earth which includes a Faulknerian "campaign frame" called That Which Rises. It's set in a small town in Alabama during the Great Depression, and it's got hellfire preachers, hard times, and a hint of voodoo.
