    At the risk of a tangent, I think this is something that people who talk about making games 'for IM' or irc seem to overlook. I remember being pretty disappointed with Code of Unaris precisely because of its assumption that RPing via text message would be fast and involve a lot of short declarative sentences -- the exact opposite of my experience in text-based roleplaying. Hacking a single word in what the other person said is a lot less punchy if they just wrote two paragraphs of description, and it seems like the 'fast and furious' approach is trying to overcompensate for the fact that text communication is slower than talking. Like I said, it's a tangent, but people have been mentioning Unaris and similar types of (theoretical) games in other threads, and so it's been on my mind.
