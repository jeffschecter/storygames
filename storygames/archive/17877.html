Monsterhearts Session 1

--- Explict --- This session contains a lot of adult subject manners.

Scenes and subjects might be offensive to some. Please be advised.
The actors are on the stage and they brought no script. The damned run loose in this session of Monsterhearts! Come and listen to how what seems to be 'everyday' teens unleash their darkest self and chew off more then they could ever handle. The Queen attempts to hold on to what is rightfully hers. The Witch's mouth strikes a deep wound into a friend heart. The Infernal, still learning his powers yet understand what hellish nightmares lurk in the darkness. This is Monsterhearts.

The Burning Wheel Revised Session 1
With the making of a story, enter the characters. Tensions run high as the elves begin a movement against the oppressing humans. The Orc and Dwarf, run into to some troubles of their own. The streets of the town are painted red and the player characters are in the middle of it. What will come of them when their luck runs out?
