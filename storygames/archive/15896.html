\n I've talked about it a bit here before, but I recently wrote a rules-light, highly collaborative RPG called School Daze. The playtesting has gone well, and I've decided to take the steps to publish the game in the best form that I can. To that end, I've launched a Kickstarter to see that happen. If it gets funded, I'll not only have the funds I need to run the business side of this project, but I'll have Daniel Solis doing the layout, and Brian Patterson (of d20monkey.com) doing the art.
\n
\nYou can find the Kickstarter here.
\n
\nI'm relatively new to this community, but I know that some great things happen here. And, without the help of some people who call this place home, School Daze would never have gotten off the ground. Thanks for taking a look.
\n
\n~Tracy
