\n I was thinking last night about what game I would make based on:

"Save the Cheerleader. Save the World" (one of the tag lines for the TV series Heroes)

if I were explicitly not going to make "Heroes: the Game".

I ended up in a mid-20th century sci fi themed "Mars Needs Woman" sort of thing, perhaps mixed with a liberal amount of "Sailor Moon"-esque Manga/Anime:

"Tentacled (or otherwise) Aliens from Outer Space want to conquer Earth. To do so, they need to capture one special young woman from our planet to serve as the template for thier ultimate warrior race.

You play special Black-Wearing operatives armed with shiny rayguns and other 'advanced' technology trying to save the young woman in question.

Unfortunately, neither you nor the aliens know which young woman it is. You do know it's a cheerleader, somewhere in the mid-west. And you know the fate of humanity rests on your ability to protect her.

Save the Cheerleader. Save the World."

Now it's your turn. Here's your tag line to work with:

"You can't handle the truth!" from A Few Good Men

Create a game concept unrelated to "A Few Good Men" and the propose a new tag line or quote for someone else to riff off of.
