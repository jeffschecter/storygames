\n I'm planning on running a game on the night before my wedding this Saturday and I've decided on Lady Blackbird. The only thing is that there's an outside chance I'll end up with six players instead of five, so I've decided I should make a sixth character just in case. But what kind of character?! What fits into the existing group, doesn't tread on anyone's toes (in a bad way, positive story-producing toe-stepping is great) and will make it more fun not less?

The quick one-line ideas I've had so far:
- An Imperial defector
- The Owl's Doctor
- A pirate that was already in the brig when they're thrown in there

What would you add? Is this even a good idea?
