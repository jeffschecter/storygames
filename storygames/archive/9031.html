\n Hi all -
\n
\nNo doubt most of you know this already, but I'm putting in one final plea. Fight On! currently stands in second place behind the National Hot Rod Association and in front of One Glance Ministries in the Lulu Author Contest. We really want to finish in the top three so that we can get interviewed on the lulu website and support not only our own little niche but the RPG hobby in general. So, I know some of you have no interest in our products, but I also know that some of you do, and if there was anything you had ever wanted to buy in print from us, now ould be the time. You can check our wares at
\n
\nstores.lulu.com/fighton
\n
\nand look forward to the day after tomorrow when I'll resume my former existence as a schlumpy quarterly fanzine editor, grognard, and occasional kibitzer on matters fantastic.

    \n Well, first of all, only buy it if you think you might enjoy it. :-)
    \n
    \nThe individual issues cost less to buy (even all four of them) and get us more print sales. So from the point of view of the contest buying multiples of those helps us more.
    \n
    \nOn the other hand, the FO! compendium is a temporary product, and will be gone forever tomorrow. So if you think you really want one, now would be the time to try. I was not able to order a proof copy of this though due to the emergency situation and some of the margins may be tight. Should still be cool though, and may wind up being one 'o them pricey collector's items you can sell on Ebay down the road - who knows?
    \n
    \nThanks for your interest, Graham!
    \n
    \nAlso, any of you artists, I hope you will enter the Erol Otus Art Challenge. We have some good subs already but we could use a LOT more to send on to Erol. Still more than a month to go on that, but we're hoping for a big turnout.
