    I'm a sucker for imagery of Rosicrucian/Hermetic esoterica in fiction and RPGs. You could quite easily add "Rosicrucian Symbology" as a motif or convention in Covenant and then go an play with them as a conspiracy... You could even pick particular symbols if more than one of the group was interested in them.

    For cool fantasy literature with a Rosicrucian overtone, I recommend 1610: Sundial in a Grave by Mary Gentle.

    As it happens, I played a hermetic philosopher in a LARP who'd constantly be drawing up diagrams in this style.
