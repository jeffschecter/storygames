So I've been running a Firefly game recently using the Dogs in the Vineyard system lately. I had gotten a lukewarm reception to DitV when I proposed it, and a few moans of "Sheesh, not ANOTHER western!" So I switched gears and made it Firefly. The game has been going well and I've got some cool characters to work with, but feel like the game is starting to lose momentum after a few game sessions. I was hoping a few of you gaming virtuosos might be willing to toss a few ideas my way, help me flesh out a game I feel has some cool potential.

The game is set about a year after the events of the movie Serenity. The Alliance Senate is in chaos over the "Miranda Incident", and has withdrawn military support from the border worlds in order to maintain stability in the Core. Left to fend for themselves, the border worlds are falling prey to piracy, famine and infighting, and a second Succession War is on the brink of breaking out. A group of idealists have formed a political faction called the Independent League, moved to Miranda, and have begun distributing supplies from the abandoned cites on Miranda to the impoverished border worlds. A group of volunteers called the Rangers haven taken up the task of distributing the supplies and helping out the border communities as a sort of freelance police. The players are Rangers.

The game is based around the crew of the Pegasus, a firefly class transport much like the Serenity, only newer and in better condition.
