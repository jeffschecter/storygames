    \n When pitching the game to Italians, what I usually do is first explain (as concisely as possible) what a "drifter" is. Second thing, optional, I talk about the role of the Devil and supernatural in everyday contemporary American culture (like, that no negligible numbers of people still actually believe the Devil to exist as a real individual being, not a metaphor). Sometimes I also add something about playing poker with the Devil and the Man.

    Overall, it's a game I usually have no problem finding players for: there's always at least somebody around who's interested in the subject matter.
