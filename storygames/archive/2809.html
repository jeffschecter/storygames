\n I'm cruising forward with development of my storytelling game of pulp exploration, having incorporated the feedback of some awesome external playtesters (such as the games discussed here). The game is GM-less, no prep, and can be played in one-shot mode or over several sessions. And it lets you embrace full-on pulp awesomeness with freeform narration and pulse-pounding timed conflict resolution.

The plan is to get the game finalized and ready for publication this summer. Time is short, and I could really use another external playtest or two to evaluate the current rules. If you're interested, please drop me a line via whisper or e-mail. If you do a playtest for me, I'm willing to offer feedback and editing services of your own design in return, possibly even a reciprocal playtest if I can gather up some local folks. Thanks.
