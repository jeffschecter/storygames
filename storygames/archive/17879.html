\n I was cleaning up my office today and found my copy of Do: Pilgrims of the Flying Temple. I've only played the game a few times, but it's such a joy to hold. I don't know exactly what makes it so perfect (some combination of the art/size of the book/etc), but I really love it.

What story games do you cherish in their physical form? Is there something special about the art or printing that makes you love a game more for how it showed up on your doorstep or felt in your hand at the FLGS? Is it a function of the way it looks or something more?
