\n First things first, let me just say that the Indie Developers' Roundtable was incredible. I want to talk about it a bunch. But that's later. I'm posting this right now because I feel like an asshole over something.

Someone came up to me just after the discussion rapped and asked me about my game, Criminal Element. He was really enthused and asked me for the website and stuff. I gave it to him, then I was interrupted by a friend that needed to tell me something. I turned back around and the guy I was talking to was gone.

Hey, guy! If you read this, please whisper to me! I want to talk to you, ask you a couple of questions, and get the chance to actually introduce myself. I want to make up for the fact that I was pulled away mid-conversation.

-m
