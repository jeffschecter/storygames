\n Hey,

Disclaimer: I haven't played Monsterhearts yet or even bought the book (pending on if i find people for it soonish) but i looked at the Skins and am listening to an AP right now and i had a Skin Idea that does not want to leave my head.

The User/Web/Geek/Internetjunkie

I think the internet and social media is an important part of teenage life right now. This skin is about being lost in a different but "real" world and struggling between online-chat, facebook-stalking and real live.
It's about being the best hacker around, cybersex and the delusion of power you can have on the net.
It should also be about stalking people until you have enough leverage over them to make them do real world stuff for you since you are not confortable enough without your laptop.

A few things i was thinking about:

Backstory:
You are stalking someone on *Facebook* for quite a while, gain a string on them
Someone you look up to has denied your Friend-request multiple times, they gain 2 strings on you

Darkest Self should be about locking yourself in with your cyberstuff and venge digital revenge on everyone. It should require someone to come to you and get you out to the sunlight again.

Sexmove:
If it is online remove a condition and get -1 forward
If it is real they get a string on you but you get +2 forward

(the idea here is to make online sex a thing that you do but it doesn't really make you feel good. Real sex is awkward but hey you did it)

Move ideas:

Everyone leaves a cybertrace/hack their cellphone
When you search for information about someone roll+cool/dark (?) 10+ choose 3, 7-9 choose 1
And some kind of "read the sitch" move where you can find out stuff about them from the internet. Potentially everyone could do that i guess but you are better than they. This should give you information/strings etc.

Erotic-Chatting
basically a move to allow you to easy trigger your cybersex move with something interesting on a 7-9

Hack their *Facebook*
Allows you to put conditions on them

Maybe a Move about being good at science/math
Allows you to get strings on teachers but may put "bullied by the jocks" condition on you or something.

This is what i have for now. Can anyone help me with this? Any ideas?
