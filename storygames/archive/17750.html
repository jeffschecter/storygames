Who accompanies the dungeon boss?

\n
\n
\n
\n\nChristopherWeeks \n\n\n
\n
\n\nDecember 2012 \n edited December 2012 in Story Games
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n If there's a dungeon (in the most liberal and sandboxy sense) and the dungeon has a boss -- could be anything, but maybe an obese, one-eyed, obsidian-skinned harpy (or maybe just some guy, a bandit lord), who is in that boss' company?

I'm thinking stuff like: servants, slaves, concubines, porters, advisors, jesters, doctors, witches, pet humans, victims, oracles, priests, cooks, etc.

Thanks!
