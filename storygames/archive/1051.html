Dear game creators,

I like games. Especially the type of games that people talk about here. I also like to talk about games. In fact, I have a whole podcast devoted to it. Perhaps you've heard of it: it's called Have Games, Will Travel, and you can find it at http://www.havegameswilltravel.net.

Now here's where you come in. I'm going to be at GenCon, but I'll be working in a booth for pretty much the whole time, which means that actually hearing about your games during show hours is going to be hard. So I'd love to figure where and when I can get together with you in Indianapolis so you can convince me that your game is cool and that I should convince my 1000+ listeners* that your game is cool and that they should buy it. Sound good? So, who's first?

If you'd rather set things up by email, that's cool too. Drop me a line at ptevis AT gmail.com

Also, I'm younger and hipper than Ken Hite. Or at least younger.

--Paul

*Not an exaggeration. My last three episodes have averaged closed to 1100 listeners each.
