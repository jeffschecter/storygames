\n In the game i'm working on for Game Chef (Posted here: GC'10 Eric's Game) I'm working up a list of Humours that will work as Stats/Aspects. The mechanic is that you are essentially an adequate sailing man from the 18th/19th Century but when things are "Go" you draw on some of your most colorful character traits. Once committed, you'd draw on that flavor for the rest of the act.

I was hoping for your help to build on phrases or couplings that sum up some colorful and often unflattering behaviors from the period.

My list so far (and please feel free to improve upon anything i have already):

black and most foul
crazed
maudlin\t
cruel and cold\t\t
with bloodlust
morbid and distant
free from society\u2019s rules
saucy and unbridled\t
violent drunk
lost in laudanum
wicked and stringent
ablaze
clinically cold
intrusive braggart
gruff but concerned
jolly
informed paranoia
quiet nobility
weakness for children
faith in god
