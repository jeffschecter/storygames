\n Here are some custom lair moves that I have been working on. They could be used generically or they could be tied specifically to one of the Employer's Trusted Advisors.
You can get a copy of Minions, Inc. from this topic.

The Surgical Suite (The Doctor)
When you visit the sick bay to get your injuries healed, roll+persuade. On a hit, the Doctor will heal 1 injury per Dx spent or given. On a 10+, choose 3. On a 7-9 Choose 1.
\u2022\tThe process does not take time. If you don\u2019t have time, then you must choose this one or forgo treatment.
\u2022\tSpend 1 less Dx for the entire treatment.
\u2022\tAllow the MC to choose one of your moves. That move is now unstable.
\u2022\tYou mark the experience box.

The Armory (The General)
When you want new equipment, roll+evade. On a hit you can buy something new with Dx. On a 10+, choose 3. On a 7-9 Choose 1.
\u2022\tYou don\u2019t take 1 injury from \u201cfriendly\u201d fire.
\u2022\tIt costs 1 Dx less.
\u2022\tAdd both an FX tag and the unstable tag to the item.
\u2022\tYou mark the experience box.

The Kitchens (The Monstrosity)
When you visit the Kitchens for a snack, roll+break. On a hit, choose a move. Gain +1DS forward when you use that move during your next mission. On a 10+, choose 3. On a 7-9 Choose 1. You may only use this move once per mission.
\u2022\tThe Monstrosity does not inflict 1 Injury (Wound) for trespassing.
\u2022\tYou don't lose 1 Reputation for your \u201cBravery\u201d.
\u2022\tChoose a 2nd move to gain +1DS forward on.
\u2022\tYou mark the experience box.

The Library (The Lawyer or The Librarian)
When you visit the Library to gain knowledge, roll+analyze. On a hit, you gain the effects of investigate a person with a 10+ or read a sitch at a distance with a 10+. On a 10+, choose 3. On a 7-9 Choose 1.
\u2022\tThe process does not take time. If you don\u2019t have time, then you must choose this one or forgo your research.
\u2022\tYou do not gain 1 Injury (Nightmare) from the Lawyer\u2019s current \u201cproject\u201d.
\u2022\tYou do not owe them 1 Dx.
\u2022\tYou mark the experience box.

The Laboratory (The Scientist)
When you want something improved, roll+improvise. On a hit, you may either add a standard tag to an existing item or add an FX tag and the unstable tag to an existing item. On a 10+, choose 3. On a 7-9 Choose 1.
\u2022\tYou do not gain 1 Injury (Suspicion) from talking to the Scientist.
\u2022\tYou may remove the unstable tag from 1 thing that you own.
\u2022\tYou gain +1 Reputation.
\u2022\tYou mark the experience box.
