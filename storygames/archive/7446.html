\n All right: I've been waiting until the post-Gencon frenzy subsides a bit before unveiling. But I can wait no longer! I present to you my new blog:

Story by the Throat!

It's an ongoing discussion of the pursuit of Story in all areas of life, with roleplaying at the fore. It's about relentless narrative, passionate engagement, and storytelling as a human act. It's intended to be conversation, not dissertation, so that's why I need you all! Please drop by and join the fun! The latest post has some lively discussion bubbling up. Join in now and wealth and popularity will be yours.

Also, if you've got any opinions about the format of the blog you can post 'em here, but if you've comments on the content, please add them to the site. thanks!

Peace,
-Joel

PS. Yes, Jesse Burneko's doing something too (though a little different, I think). I swear, I just had the idea at the same time.

PPS. This WAS inspired by this thread of Jesse's. Got me thinking, and I want to share it.

PPPS. Seriously, folks, conversations. Let's have 'em.
