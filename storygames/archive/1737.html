November 2006 edited November 2006 in Story Games
I'm a total scab, this took way to long for me to process. Paul is a champ for doing it faster and in a much more organized way. My final results were slightly different from his. I will simply name the top six rated games, they are:

Dreamcatcher, by Ashok Desai (sheet by Mike Sands)

Something Rising, by Graham Walmsley (sheet by Mike Sugarbaker)

Architects of Aztlan, by Adam Dray (sheet by Mike Ramsey)

Chimera, by Fred Hicks (sheet by Adam Dray)

The Court, by Troy Holaday (sheet by Aaron Caskey)

Growing Pains, by Andrew Kenrick (sheet by Joe Prince)

And there you have it. The next bunch of games after the top six all placed very close to each other (I was going to do a top eight, but i would have had to pick and choose). A lot of these games look great, and I'm really hopeing some of them are brought to the next stage.

The superlatives were rather inconclusive; There were three three-way ties with only three votes each, the other two catagories tied out with one vote each. So I won't post those findings as they seem kind of meaningless.

I enjoyed this, but it was a big task, and one that came at a particularly inconvienient time in my life (i just started a new job). I have some ideas for contests and i may run more in the future, but for now i have to get back to my games, and see what i can do with them. Thanks for playing, everyone. It's been really cool.
