    \n Hi folks,
    \n
    \nI don't know the details of the Forge's hosting, but since they aren't involved in advertising and therefore not making money on the sites traffic I would bet they are using shared hosting. The way that works is the service provider promises more bandwidth and space than the average person will use, and then stick lot's of folks on one computer. If you use a lot of your promised bandwidth you likely get moved to a server with other folks who are doing the same, which means your performance degrades, as the users on the computer you share are more active, thereby using more resources. Folks who are making money likely take this as a sign that it's time to upgrade to a better service. Folks who aren't making money just have to deal. I know some folks who got some results by complaining to dreamhost by getting moved to a less active server.
    \n
    \nOne tip to help you. Don't use the Forge's search, you can get much faster results by using google and adding\n
    site:www.indie-rpgs.com\n
    \nto your search terms.
