\n So several moons ago my computer crashed and died. Then my backup computer and backup drive crashed and died. Life was suck.

Now I have a shiny new Christmas laptop, and I need to rebuild my library of links to internet goodness.

So, I'd like to ask you, the kind folk of Storygames, to help me. Give me 1 to 5 (no more please, else I'll get snowed under) of the links you have in your library that you think I absolutely must have. Gaming links are cool, but non-gaming links are just as good.

So please, show me the best the net has to offer me.
