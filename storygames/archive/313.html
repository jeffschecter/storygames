So I'm trying and failing to fall (hehe) asleep, and for some reason this pops into my head:

Drowning and Falling...In Love!
The story-telling game of telling a story about falling or drowning in love.

[From the back cover]

Drowning and Falling...In Love! is for those of you who are tired of the ho-hum drowning and falling that you've been doing for all of these years. It is for those of you who want to tell stories in the best tradition of story-telling - people who tell stories about drowning and falling...in love!.

This telling-stories game will give you all of the tools you need to tell stories about your characters. There are no "stats" or "hit points" or "levels," but there are the most innovative and original rules for telling original and innovative stories about falling in love, or drowning in it.

Only play this game if you really want to drown, or fall, in love!
