    \n I think that the very act of roleplaying/story gaming transforms our relationship to the source material quite meaningfully.

    For instance, I have no interest whatsoever in teenage drama. I have no interest in supernatural teen drama, and, in particular, werewolves and vampires. None.

    However, I *love* playing Monsterhearts. That game is great fun to play, and, in that game, the supernatural and teenage stuff is really powerful.

    A similar thing happens with potentially "boring" subject matter. Playing it out can be a very different experience, and is worth trying.
