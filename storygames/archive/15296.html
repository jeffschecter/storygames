    \n Hi guys! Ben is not around, but I wrote some of the content, so I think I can answer your questions.

    Matriarchy is a free to play browser-based social game, similar to Echo Bazaar in form factor and monetization practices. Anyone may play for free. There are two reward tiers which allow you earlier access to the game; one to be part of the closed beta, and one giving you access to nightly alpha builds.

    Since the game is browser-based (and not Flash!), the basic functionality for mobile platforms will be there from launch. Plans to make it prettier/faster on mobile devices will come later.

    Ben, please correct me if I am wildly giving out erroneous info!
