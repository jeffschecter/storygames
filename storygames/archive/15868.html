\n So I've just rolled my nine Rory's Story Cubes, four Writer's Dice, Adventure Dice, Monster Dice, and a Body Part D12. The results are as follows:

Rory's:
-Alien
-Fountain
-Planet
-Compass Rose
-Tree
-Turtle
-House
-Block with letter "L"
-Book

Writer's Dice:
-But (1)
-Or (4)
-If (6)
-If (6)

Adventure Dice:
-Sunny (weather)
-Door
-Trapped
-Poison Gas
-Scroll

Monster Dice:
-Octopus
-Orange Triangle (Medium Difficulty)
-Bees
-Mage
-Berserk Attack (reaction to encounter)

Body Part:
-Right Hand

Using one to three dice results, construct a sentence to be strung together into a short narrative. No changing what's already been established in the fiction, unless you use a Writer's Die result and make it believable somehow. Tag the die results you used after your sentence to make it easier for everyone to see what's been used. Results can be re-used, but once each result has been used in at least one sentence, the story comes to an end. Play fast and loose with the meaning of each image or other result. Have fun and make something cool! I'll start, and hopefully someone will jump in:

Liberty literally tearing itself apart as it went screaming through atmo, she assessed the situation; it quickly became clear that she'd have to abandon what was left of her ship if she was going to make it out of this alive. ["L"]
