\n I've been waiting for a batch of books to arrive from the UK. In the meantime, I went to Morocco, got the sh*ts and fled to Spain, watched a fine blues trio in Madrid, came back to Ireland and got new filling at the dentist. Really exciting stuff.

All that excitement ended this morning as I slotted books into envelopes and trusted their "do not bend" packaging to hands of the wilful post cohorts. That is to say, pre-order time for Hell for Leather is over. Anyone who'd like to play the game before they buy it can get the PDF for free . Or else they could see the info page for a brief overview. Or both. I hope you like it.
