Hi everyone.

I have an idea for some cross-promotion. I have some games that I'd love to have reviewed publicly, such as at RPG.net, gamingreport.com, 20by20room, or other often-viewed locations. I'm sure nearly everyone else does, too. So here's the idea:

List, in this thread, something you made that you want reviewed. (Posting an informational link is a good idea.) You send a free copy to the first person who responds and swears to review it. That person writes a review and puts a link here (via edit) when the review goes public. Once you promise to review something, you can post something of your own for review. It's a "round robin" sort of thing, so it's not just mutual back-scratching.

Anyone interested? I'll be willing to write the first review.

--Colin

P.S.: If you want to open up your product to multiple reviews, you are welcome to do so, but we don't want to force people with popular hardcopy-only products to ship a dozen of them. Also, anyone who falls through on their review is a meanie and gets... um... kicked in the shins or something.
