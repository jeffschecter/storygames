\n Call it Doc Strange on the cheep. I'm tinkering up a M&M3E game for a friend and what I'm considering is a setting that plays up a marvel 1970/90's lower powered supernatrual setting. So more Blade and Darkhome but with Strange's more light hearted air.

Part of the process is dreaming up a city (lets call it Tradewinds City) that features secret socities, arcane power, demons and angels fighting over people...but still features the spandex you'd expect from such a game.

What inspirations do you think would be good design sources? (I've read Dreaming Cities, and Dresden natch...but I want to stand in both the urban supernatural and 4 color universes.)
