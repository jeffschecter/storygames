\n The stars are right and Groundhoggoth has arisen!
\n
\nAt Groundhoggoth you can download a variety of RPGs in various stages of completeness and scenarios for games like Dead of Night and Best Friends, with more to come for Cold City, Hot War and Over the Edge to name a few.
\n
\nHere are some of the complete RPGs available for free download now:
\n
\nWhen Bad Things Happen to Good Magicians: A turn based, competitive RPG that crosses the Fellowship of the Ring with the Jerry Springer show and asks the question: who takes the blame when the quest fails?
\n
\nNever to Die: An entry and finalist for Game Chef 2010, this is a game about a typical British lads' night out that ends in a tragedy.
\n
\nSawmill: A mini-game inspired by films like Saw, Cube and The Collector, in which a group of strangers must expose their darkest secrets in order to escape the death maze of a psychopath.
\n
\nMystery of Life: A mini-game where an Angel & Demon investigate a case of apparent suicide and the evidence they collect is then used by the celestial court to determine the fate of the deceased.
\n
\nPyramid: A romping mini-game of pseudo-Victorian exploration, where players brave the traps & guardians of the tomb in order to claim the treasure.
\n
\nThe Curse of the House of Wretchwick: A full size RPG set in the Gormenghast-like 'House of Wretchwick', in which the PCs are dissolute nobles seeking ways to fill the emptiness in their over-privileged lives. A very tongue-in-cheek game of Gothic suffering & despair, where death is no guarantee of release.
\n
\nDead Man's Chest: Full on pirate adventure RPG.
\n
\nMore games and scenarios will appear every month and constructive feedback on all articles is welcome.

    \n More updates for November; the first is a new mini-game, 101 Zombies!
    \n
    \nThe dead have arisen to feast upon the living... again. You and your team have been dispatched to the site of a major zombie outbreak with instructions to contain and eliminate the threat. Using your brute strength, agility and ingenuity, see if you can score the most zombie kills without becoming their lunch!
    \n
    \nThis is a friendly-but-competitive, GM-less, rules-lite RPG which starts when you roll 101d6 to represent the 101 Zombies that must be destroyed before they overrun the site and force the PCs to retreat or die.
    \n
    \nI'll also be posting a series of updates from Indiecon for the next few days, including news from the con and Actual Play reports for each game I run.
