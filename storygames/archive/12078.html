\n What would you want in an In A Wicked Age boxed set?

In A Wicked Age (original book)
Perfect dice for In A Wicked Age (6 different sets)
Oracle Decks for the original 4 Oracles.
Blank cards for writing Oracles
A large, fold-out map without anything on it.
A few unlabeled maps of towns, overgrown courtyards, ruins, palaces, and temples
Character sheets
NPC sheets
Particular strength sheets
Chapter Sheets
Blank We Owe list

Anything I'm missing?

    \n I've made a IAWA boxed set. I've been really strict about keeping only what the game itself tells you to include. I have included blank paper for the maps, notes, whatever, but I didn't bother separating them into different categories. I do use all the different types of sheets, with labeled envelopes for keeping them in. I'm not including the oracle decks, as it makes the deck of cards required in the rulebook useless, and I found a really cool deck of cards that fits the game. I also wanted to keep it small and compact enough to fit in my backpack. So, I guess what I'm saying is that I would cut out a bit of that stuff.

    You did forget pencils, though.
