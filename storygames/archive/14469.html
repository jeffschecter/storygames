\n This year\u2019s Emerald City Gamefest, Seattle\u2019s foremost free, family, fun gaming event will be held on July 2nd from 10:00 am \u2013 6:30 pm in the undercroft of the Church of the Redeemer, 6211 Northeast 182nd Street in Kenmore. This year we are pleased to announce that we will be offering our gamer garage sale at the same time, so if you are looking to sell games you don\u2019t want, or give a good home to someone else\u2019s unwanted games, come on by and check it out!

We always have a lot of story games at ECG meetups. As always, we are looking for people to run games at our event. If you want to run a board, card, miniatures, or roleplaying game at the gamefest, see the information on the ECG Website.
