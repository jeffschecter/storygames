\n My hard drive is full of gaming projects I got started on and either never completed or completed but never playtested: this is one of those, from 2010. It would probably make a good nano-game if you're looking for a break from the norm.

Back to the Start is a game that begins at the end of a story; whilst the game moves forwards in time, the story is revealed backwards, starting at the end and working towards the beginning.
