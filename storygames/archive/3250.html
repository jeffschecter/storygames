\n I just came across The Generator Blog, a collection of links to online generators - some of them enable you to put your own words into a picture, like the Hollywood Sign or a can label or something, others of them do more-or-less random text.

(OK, I was looking for a generator so I could put lolcats on my blog. You happy?)

Two thoughts:

1. I'm sure that a community like this one can come up with many, many uses for these.

2. The guy probably needs to know about Abulafia.
