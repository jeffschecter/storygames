    \n Witch Quest. http://yarukizerogames.com/2010/02/11/witch-quest-book-ii-release

    You play cats and witches. An old Japanese game that has been fan translated into English. Copes wonderfully with large groups.

    The Daughters of Verona. https://wilhelmsgames.wordpress.com/the-daughters-of-verona

    You play romantic comedies. Chaotic, loud, hilarious. If it is suitable for 10 and 12 year olds depends on the kids, and their parents I suppose. I have played it with some rather young players at conventions.
