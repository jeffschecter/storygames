Way cool dice cup and dice

forlorn1
January 2007 edited January 2007 in Story Games
I showed up to my group on Sunday and got handed a late holiday gift of a Pirates of the Caribbean dice cup and 5 6 siders with a skull and crossbones as the one pip. Apparently they come in set of four cups and can be had at Walmart for something like $12. I haven't checked but 4 cups and 20 pretty cool looking dice for $12 seems pretty reasonable, and very easily divisible into most gaming groups.

The cup is plastic, deliberately molded to look misshapen with a couple of barnacles on the outside. The dice are black, but not jet black, more like a powder finish black if that makes any sense.

The cup works great for rolling dice in and slamming down on the table. Especially appropriate for pirate themed games (we were playing 50 Fathoms/savage worlds). Lots of Arrrrs and such going on.

We felt obliged to play a round of liars dice. Two round later we had 8 people total, 4 playing with random game store cups and 6 siders. And I have to say that liars dice with 8 people is crazy bluffing fun.

Cheap and awesome is always good.
