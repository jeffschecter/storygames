    \n I played in the game at Gen Con last year with the designer. It was hilarious. I would say that he might have meant for it to be serious, but with added mechanics like the "Dance Off" I am pretty sure it was meant to be a bit humorous. It is also not racist if it is written by someone with at least a bit of Indian heritage. There were 3 different eras of play Historical, Modern, and Future, each one with a little extra flavor to distinguish it. Overall all though it was not too "Different." Just more romance, spiteful lovers, and exaggerated drama.
