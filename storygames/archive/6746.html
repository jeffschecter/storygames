\n I haven't gotten D&D 4th yet (although my son has enough legoes that I'm now eying it), but a thought just hit me that I'd like to share. Take the tactical combat of D&D 4th (the board, minis, and how the powers interact), but move it to a board filled with abstract ideas (much like WotG's Great Game has the Elephant Board).

So social combat could be plotted out and detailed in the same way that more physical combat is with Straw Men as minions and two skilled courtiers literally dancing around each other for moral "high ground" (that gives a situational bonus for whoever is in that square). It would be like Burning Wheel's Duel of Wits with miniatures!

Just a random idea for your day
