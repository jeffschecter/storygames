\n Well, another Nerdly Beach Party come and gone, and another awesome time come and gone. This time, our campground was a bit harder to get to, but the weather was amazing. Mostly clear but cool during the day, not too cold at night. Couldn't have been better!

The gaming was great, too. A mostly-comprehensive list includes:
Ninja Burger, Fireborn, Houses of the Blooded, Mouse Guard, Shock:, Mythender, Happy Birthday Robot, Maid, Weapons of the Gods, Penny for My Thoughts, Dominion, and Once Upon a Time. It was a good time for all!
