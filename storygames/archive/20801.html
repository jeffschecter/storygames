\n I've got a few more short game designs to drop; first up, and most recent, The Elephant in the Room is a short little absurdist piece deriving some comedy from the juxtaposition of mismatched elements.

Prior to that we have Mouthpiece, a slice-of-life chamber LARP inspired by the ventriloquism of Nina Conti.

Last and by all means least, there's Saving Throw Down, which answers the question, what would your favourite RPG scenarios be like if they were porn movies? Do I need to add that this last one is NSFW or, indeed, NSFA?
