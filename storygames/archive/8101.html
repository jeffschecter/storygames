\n Hey computer gamers, as always I am eager to shoot you in the face.

Me and my local peeps have an annual pre-Thanksgiving game day where we play the hot games from a few years back on our cobbled-together hardware - this year it'll be Battlefield 2 and some HL2 mods (CS:S, DoD:S, probably Zombie Panic). We'll have a LAN up in our secret base and lots of friends around the world joining our mysterious bootleg private servers. It's typically an all day thing, probably noon to midnight EST. If you'd like to play, let me know and I'll hook you up with the relevant details. We'll also have a "dress rehearsal" or two to make sure we're all patched and in general alignment for the big day.
