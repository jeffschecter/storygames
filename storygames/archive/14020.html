\n So we're running a competition to celebrate the release of Cthulhu Apocalypse. Whether or not Cthulhu games are your thing, it should be fun: you can test your knowledge of postapocalyptic literature and, hopefully, win some of it.

It goes like this. In the Pelgrane office is an unopened box of 13 postapocalyptic books. Nobody other than me knows what they are. Tomorrow, at 4pm GMT (12 midday EST), we'll publish 13 numbered clues to the books. The first person to get each clue wins the book.

It's hard. Steve, who knows everything, only got about 4 of them. Beth, who's read a lot of postapocalyptic stuff, got 6. We're expecting liberal use of Wikipedia and Google. And, to b ehonest, I think some books will go unclaimed.

Anyway, it's quite fun and I'm looking forward to it. Tomorrow at 4pm, then.
