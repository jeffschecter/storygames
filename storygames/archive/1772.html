    I ran this with five kids at ConQuest 2006. I include a description of the event in my convention report,

    ConQuest 2006 Report

    I thought it went very well. It is aimed at having a grown-up run games for kids who are in roughly the 5-10 range. The dice mechanics are pretty easy, and the Essence gives a simple currency for paying for things that they want.
