\n As I have warned, I have made a Ghost/Echo hack for Men In Black called Project:Black.


You may have seen the Randomizers I was making earlier. The ones included here are mildly updated, and include a Requisition Chart as well (which was contributed largely thanks to my girlfriend's MIB obsession).

I have another version that I'm working on that prints as a single, double-sided 11x17 sheet with bleeds, but I figure the booklet version will be easier for everyone else to print on their own.

And now that you have seen this, please watch the flashing light.
