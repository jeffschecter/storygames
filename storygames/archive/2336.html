Denise, who is a nice person and quite serious about growing the ENnie awards, lays it down over at the Forge. She's been trying for several years to increase non-D20 entries and participation in the ENnies, and the next logical step was to look for judges from outside ENworld.

One of the requirements is that the potential judge not be affiliated with any game company. The complete criteria are here.

    Y'all have until 10 February. I'd suggest contacting all of these excellent people and see if any of them are interested. Here's the relevant rules bits:
    • not have any professional relationship with any RPG publisher during the period from January 1st, 2006 to July 31st, 2007. Such relationships include unpaid work and work on free products;
    • not maintain a relationship with any publisher (including EN Publishing, except for the Ceramic DM line) in which they receive money, product, or special consideration in exchange for their services;
    • If you have a relationship any with a publisher, even as a playtester, it is important that you notify the ENnies board of this so that they may determine whether you are eligible to seek a judge position.
