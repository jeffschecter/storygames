To that end, anyone who supports the XXXXtreme STREET luge 5th Anniversary GenCon Edition Tournament Trophy campaign and also contributes to Monsterhearts: Second SkinS (at the $5 level or higher) will get the first ever* totally official* supplement for XXXXtreme STREET luge!

It has rules for playing bitch-ass awesome monster racers.

    \n Hi!

    So just to clarify. This is a supplement for both Monsterhearts and XXXXtreme STREET luge. It has rules to add haunted go-kart racing into Monsterhearts (a scenario, six moves, some agendas and advice for integrating it into your campaign) and rules for monster racers in XXXXtreme STREET luge, including who thinks you're famous if you're a frankenstein and don't have a mom.

    You can get it by donating at least $5 to both Monsterhearts: Second Skins and XXXXtreme STREET luge 5th anniversary gencon edition. If you have already donated to both you'll get it automatically, you lucky dog.

    Thanks to Jackson for all his help and Joe and Arnold for putting up with my shit.

    It will contain a picture of Dracula.

    P.S. (I don't get any money from this, I'm just doing it because I like both of these projects a lot and want to support them.)
