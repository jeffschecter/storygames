\n I just got halfway through typing up the list and hit "home" by mistake. Boo. Anyway, John has just emailed all the peer voters and publishers, so I hope I'm ok to announce the winners here as I know there'll be a lot of interested folks out there? I'll just post up the winners and runners up, not the points or the funky comments.

Indie Game of the Year

Winner: Spirit of the Century
1st runner up: Burning Empires
2nd runner up: Zorcerer of Zo

Indie Supplement of the Year

Winner: Dictionary of Mu
1st runner up: Push volume 1
2nd runner up: Steampunk Musha (which i edited - yay!)

Best Free Game

Winner: JAGS Revised
1st runner up: EABAlite
2nd runner up: Mexican Standoff

Best Support

Winner: Burning Empires
1st runner up: Spirit of the Century
2nd runner up: Zorcerer of Zo

Best Production Values

Winner: Burning Empires
1st runner up: Spirit of the Century
2nd runner up: Dictionary of Mu

Most Innovative Game

Winner: Lacuna Part I. The Creation of the Mystery and the Girl
from Blue City (second attempt)
1st runner up: Don't Rest Your Head
2nd runner up: A Thousand and One Nights

Congratulations to all the winners and the runners up. 2006 was a very, very strong year indeed. The full list, complete with snippets of commentary and reviews is a good read - I'm sure John will post it upon his return.
