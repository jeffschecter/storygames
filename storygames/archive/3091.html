So my girlfriend Kristin teaches highschool level TV production to a group of kids that are usually total jerks (they stole the ipod i saved up for months to get her for x-mass). One of her classes is strangely obsessed with me and my game Primitive. They've never met me, and they only know as much about me as my website tells them (i don't think they are even aware of this blog, oddly). I guess they aren't the best googlers around. They know i make a "game" called Primitive, but what that "game" entails is beyond their understanding.

Anyway they made this video as a class project. Its suposedly about me and Primitive. It features the worst Jay Leno impersonation ever. Watch it because it's weird, watch it because it's actually a little bit funny, watch it because it's barely about gaming, and is the best advertisment i could have never paid for.
