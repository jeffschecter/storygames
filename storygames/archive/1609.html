SPIRIT OF THE CENTURY - SIGNING PARTY

When: Sunday, October 29th, starting at 3pm (feel free to show up a little early if you're willing to get drafted into the bustle), officially running until at least 6pm, after which we'll happily hang about and shoot the breeze into the evening.

Where: Fred Hicks's house (directions will be sent when you respond).

Why:
  • Pick up your copy of Spirit of the Century two weeks early!
  • Meet and hang out with the authors Fred, Rob, and Lenny, and possibly other independent publishers as well!
  • Meet some of the players from the original Spirit of the Century campaign
  • Participate in a whole-party Spirit of the Century character creation (we'll have plenty of books...). Characters created will be compiled into a Who's Who in the Century Club web extra, to be downloaded soon afterwards.
What:
  • We're a small press! This party is very much BYOE!
  • We'll have a grill fired up on the back deck, so bring your grillables and snacks/drinks what go with grillables!
  • We'll hand over signed books by 6pm to folks who have already preordered and RSVP (see below) -- no sales will be made at the party, so preorder now if you haven't!
How:

In order to be sure to get your Spirit of the Century book at the party, you must RSVP! Fill out the RSVP form (below) and let us know you're planning to attend, and we'll make sure to have your book(s) set aside for you. When you RSVP, we'll email you directions to Fred's house.

If you're interested in joining us for the Spirit of the Century signing party and getting your book a couple weeks early, and are able to attend, go to this webpage and RSVP now!
http://www.evilhat.com/spirit/rsvp.php
