    \n Bret, I've superused sessions for three or four users, but also ran two sessions for a friend with just one user. There was some play reporting here (although I never added a full run-down of the second session). It's easy on the superuser and hard on the user. Gaining flow without being able to engage in group challenges is a significant hurdle to overcome, but it can be done (e.g. friending, gifting). It was fun, however, and I'd do it again if the circumstance presented itself.
