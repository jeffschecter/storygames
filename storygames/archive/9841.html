\n Right, so we had RinCon last year, the first gaming convention in Tucson in a long time. Now we're going to have it again, Columbus Day weekend!

We had a ton of support from various publishers/stores and so had a big table of cool prizes to give to people that helped organize, run, and sometimes even win, games!

We want to do that again. I would love to put in some story games!

Here's how it works:

1 - You arrange to get me a copy of your game. This can be a code for a PDF download, a hardcopy, or whatever. DON'T just send me a PDF. This is a promotion for you, I want to drive the person to your website/download site so they can see how great you are.

2 - If it's a PDF download code, I put it in a nice envelope, if it's a hardcopy, it goes into the RinCon Stack of Justice. Either way, I PERSONALLY my own self pimp your game to passers-by and urge people to pick it up and give it a go.

3 - You tell me how much your game currently costs, I will send you (or your business) a receipt because donations to RinCon are tax fuckin' deductible, due to us being registered as a nonprofit and such.

4 - You are great.

Ask questions or send PMs!
