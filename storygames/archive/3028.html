Massive thanks to everyone who came. It was fantastic fun and very productive. I got some really excellent feedback on the games I was testing and enjoyed pretty much every minute of the weekend.

Some of my personal highlights were:\n
    \n
  • Finally getting out of my car after the two-and-a-half-hour drive.
    • \n
  • Andrew nearly running over a badger.
    • \n
  • Reel Adventures "High Roller". 80s direct to video action movie goodness.
    • \n
  • Waking up to see the fat cock.
    • \n
  • Rolling the required 6 and successfully summoning William Shatner to defeat The Eye Of Lashnak with a 2-fisted sci-fi punch in James' Doodle Dungeon playtest.
    • \n
  • Seeing Malcolm slap his meat.
    • \n
  • Claire and Iain's enthusiasm about Stitch. Even though I didn't play it.
    • \n
  • Ninjas. Ninjas are always good.
    • \n
  • Getting fucked by scary people in Okhrana. Like all the games I played over the weekend, this needs a lot of work but it's surprisingly solid considering the early stage it's at.
    • \n
  • Long Buckby: definitely high up the Ministry Of Quaint's watch list.
    • \n
  • Just seeing Memories and Madness in play. It looked really pretty, but actually there were some pretty vile things going on in that game...
    • \n
  • Umlaut was riotously funny. Unfortunately that game was also so disgusting that I can't really talk about it in polite company.
    • \n
  • Seeing Scott carry away two bulging carrier bags of unopened beer back to his car. That was actually a tiny fraction of what he'd brought with him though.
    • \n
\nGreat fun.
