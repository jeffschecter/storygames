    \n Unfortunately I'm about the worst person to ask about gaming in the Crescent City right now. I am however available for general ideas of other stuff to do and see here( and I can always use some practice giving tours, especially of the two main tourist attracting cemetaries in town).

    Not SGing, but the D-Day museum sponsors a free to play WW2 themed wargame day one Saturday a month, so you might check their site out.

    Also, one of the local comic shops was selling copies of My Life with Master, and from talking with the counter clerk, it is apparently at the behest of the owner, who'd like to see more of that stuff played. The shop was Crescent City Comics on Freret St. (contact= comix@crescentcitycomics.com ). It was the only thing other than MtG cards game related at the store.

    There's apparently a decent minis gaming scene down here, but my job has precluded easy engagement with it. Katrina kinda wiped out the businesses and regular gaming meet ups I was familiar with when I lived here in the 90s...
