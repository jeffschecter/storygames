    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n
    \n I'm way more interested in the honey than the vinegar on this one. I don't have anything to prove to anyone except myself. If the ideas suck, and people don't like the books, then it will have been a big waste of scarce time. On the other hand, if the ideas work, and people like the books, I think it will be time well spent. And hopefully, I'll start to get some visibility on what's happening to the player network to match the data I have on the player market.
    \n
    \nPlus, I'd like to have those three games on my shelf to read & play. They sound Real Fun to me. :)
    \n
    \nRyan
