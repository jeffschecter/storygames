    \n Fear goes up by one if an Innocent dies (GM determines who is an Innocent and is optional).
    Reason temporarily increases by 1 in the presence of an Innocent.
    Weariness goes up when you fail at Violence.
    Self-Loathing never decreases.

    The only score that can ever decrease in My Life with Master is Love. You lose all Love points associated with a connection if that connection dies.
