    \n
    \n Hi chaps, thanks for the love! I am glad you like the blog, it seemed somewhat crazy that someone hadn't done so before me. I hope you all find something there that ignites some interest.
    \n
    \nI am indeed a 1KM1KT Admin. So, the blog sits somewhere between rip off and supporting interest! ;-) 1KM1KT is by far the best repository of free RPGs, as they seem to fall of the net with alarming regularity.
    \n
    \nFor those who just love their links, I've also set up a directory via delicious:
    \nhttp://delicious.com/thefreerpgblog
    \n
    \nIf you like a game, please do leave a comment on the blog as the designers of the games read their reviews and comments make their hard work (for free) all the more worthwhile.
    \n
    \nFinally, if you know of a game that you really love and I haven't reviewed it yet, please let me know. PM me here or on the blog.
    \n
    \nCheers!
    \n
    \nRob
