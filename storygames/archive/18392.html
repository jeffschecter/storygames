\nCartooner RPG
Hello everyone, I am happy to announce Cartooner RPG, my first RPG in English (I'm Italian)!

Let's start with the beautiful artwork, because we are talking about Cartoons! The artwork is approached with the Ecoline technique for the background and characters are digitally rendered! It is our modern interpretation of the unmistakable style of animation!

We took equal care also in the manual and the text, making Cartooner suitable for all fans of the genre Looney Tunes and Hanna-Barbara cartoons are popular for. But the rules are flexible enough to stage your own cartoon stories born from the fruits of your very own imagination.

In this ambitious project, we have focused heavily on simplicity, allowing the player to focus more on creating an environment of play suitable for the style of Cartoons! The result is a streamlined manual, full of funny quotes, and the only things that are missing to play are: 4 to 6 players, 2 six-sided dice, a carrot and 40 minutes.

Cartooner was conceived as a modern retelling of the TOON RPG!

    \nNew update: for English Edition! The new file can be downloaded from your Drive or DriveThruRPG.

    A curiosity about Cartooner. Cartooner is a role-playing game based on a system (of our creation) called Experience Engine. The engine is just a way to get Fiction, each game is deeply custom but are based conceptually on the same mechanism.

    There is three Game based on the same engine:
    Deus Opera: A role-playing game to create Epic stories, like Odissesa and Ulysses, it is the most played game produced by me. Deus Opera use all the possibilities of Experience Engine and i'm going to publish the game in italy (playtest is over)!
    Sitcom passion! (Sitcom che passione!) is a very different version of the Experience Engine but it is still based on the same engine, the game perfectly recreates Sitcom: Master-less, Dice-less, Laughing+full!
    May The Force Be With You: Star Wars fan game!

    The Experience Engine base is very versatile and easily costomizzabile and of course suitable for certain types of fiction!

    I wonder if one day I can translate someone else of my games based on the same system. Especially Deus Opera would be nice ... but i think translation cost is impossibile!

    \n Cartooner is profoundly different from TOON RPG, everything it's based on the three laws of physics cartoons: ill-logical, Sketch-happy and Stuffed-up, the characteristics are of the Player (Cartonist), not the character! The characters are created by the group as a CAST, this allows you to facilitate the interactions of Charcters! It is a choral game: the goal is not to win but to make a comic cartoon and everyone around the table contribute to this (the game is gm less).
