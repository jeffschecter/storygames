\n With the holidays and all, my Skype group is short a couple players for the next week or two, reducing us to three and putting our normal game on hold.
To fill the gap, I've offered to run Dungeon World. Does anyone have any experience with running DW for only two PCs? Any advice or anything I should be aware of? I'm especially wondering how well Bonds work with only two heroes.

    \n I've done it a bunch of times, and it's super fun. Very much lends itself to Fafhrd and the Gray Mouser style swashbuckling adventuring partners. In the current/final version of the rules, Bonds are pretty much fully compatible with two-player games - there just won't be a fast turnover of bonds (probably every few sessions, rather than every session). If you want more bond-based XP opportunities, you could incorporate NPC bonds as well. But yeah, the game sings even with just a pair if players.

    \n I ran a 2-player DW game on Christmas, and we used one bond written between the characters, and one bond written between each character and an NPC. Worked pretty well, I can't say it ran much differently than playing with one to five players, besides allowing/asking for NPC bonds.
