    \n Hey all,

    gentle reminder that preorders go on sale a week today. 10th August.

    Why preorders?

    Simply down to the fact I have no idea how many we (Pompey Crew Design) will sell. I have had a number of copies printed without breaking the bank but if orders exceed this number (fingers crossed) I can use the money made from preorders to have another print run.

    If the demand is there I will also be looking to send some stock to IPR so any folks in the US wanting a paper copy will get a cheaper P&P option.

    Cheers

    Kevin (Toad)
