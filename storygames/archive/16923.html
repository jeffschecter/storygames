\n My Android app for the Story Games Names Project is now available to be installed from Google Play - it's called StoryNames.

Thank you for the feedback in this thread. The changes I've made since the initial version are:\n
    \n
  • More sets of names added, using a neutral background picture (although not all of them, yet)
    • \n
  • Optional male / female signs next to the names (thank you rafial for the 'using unicode symbols' idea for this)
    • \n
  • Optionally display three names at once
    • \n
  • You can shake to get new names (actually, you could do this before, but it's not very obvious)
    • \n
\nI haven't changed anything around the aspect ratios problem for some images yet.
