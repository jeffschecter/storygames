    \n Paul: I assure you, I used every one of my allotted words! It's been some time since I wrote that, but I remember having to drop some things that I wanted to include to fit my 200-word limit.

    That does seem pretty steep, now that you all mention it. It is something that only comes up when the player disagrees with everyone else about something involving her character. I suspect my motivation at the time was ensuring that one player overruling everyone else was pretty rare.

    I recall an earlier version where it was a contested roll, the person who disagreed rolling against the current narrator. In that instance, the traits coming into play had a much larger effect. I think that came out of the draft when I shifted away from one person telling the story at a time to everyone adding in story until someone disagreed. At that point, a contested roll no longer made sense.

    As is, changing the condition for success to rolling a five or six changes the probability to a range from 1/3 to ~80% with all three traits. That's a bit more mid-line, I think.
