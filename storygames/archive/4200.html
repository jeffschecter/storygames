\n I've been fascinated by WYRD for a long time. It's extremely evocative and the unconventional (especially at the time) rules mesh with so well with the feel he's going for. It changed the way I look at roleplaying, no matter what game I use. I lost track of the game for a while and found it again recently. I've also noticed it does get mentioned here, to my surprise.

I've always wondered if Scott Knipe planned to continue working on it. IIRC, he seemed to allude a few times that he had refinements in mind.

Does anyone know?

    \n Hey Consonant Dude! I know, because I'm actually Scott...name changes are confusing, I'll explain some other time.
    \n
    \nAnyway, I did allude to fixing it several times. I more or less know what those fixes would be even. Frankly, my life is a mess right now. Lack of energy and participation in the rpg community have forestalled any game design efforts on my part over the last couple years, but it could happen. I'm not making any promises, but I also never say never.
    \n
    \nAlso, Paul has prohibited me from launching new game design ideas at him until I finish at least one project on the table. That more or less means WYRD, Kagematsu, or maybe something goofy, like Human Wreckage. So maybe it will happen!
    \n
    \nAt any rate, I'm glad it continues to garner a little attention here and there. It's actually a fun,if imperfect, game just as it is, and I like that it's still floating around out there. Your kid words are very much appreciated!
