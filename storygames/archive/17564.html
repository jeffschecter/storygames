Howdy! This begins a new segment called, Not Safe For Mom's Basement. In it are topics that don't relate to our normal group discussions, but want to still discuss. It's the equivalent of Not Safe For Work, without the nudity.

In this recording, Alex gives his take on making town concepts in a speedy manner to decrease prep-work for your games. He gives a formula that includes four questions if used in order, could be the right thing you need to flesh out the town your players will interact with.

[Time Stamp]

Minute 1-14: Describes the four questions and examples given

Minute 14:30-21: Actual use of the technique to show how fast and effective it can be to make a town with this method.
