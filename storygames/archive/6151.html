\n I'm trying to get into In A Wicked Age.

One thing I've always thought was missing from In A Wicked Age was an enormous, over-arching, and highly random meta oracle. So over at Abulafia I've created an In A Wicked Age Oracle Oracle that references every available In A Wicked Age oracle and can draw situation elements from any of them. It is a convenient way to get cowboys and magic swords in your police procedural.

Fans of In A Wicked Age, fans of oracles, fans of In A Wicked Age oracles, let me know what you think!
