    \n These guys are just amazing. I first learned about them when I was doing a bit of audit work at England's Arts Council. I remember thinking what a splendid thing it was that there was a place people could go to get financial support for their plans to build giant puppets and march them through the streets of London.

    I think the best thing about giant clockwork elephants (beyond the obvious) is how incongruous they are in our world. So I think the best way to use them in a game is in a modern urban fantasy game in the Neverwhere/Don't Rest Your Head/Mage kind of vein. I just love the idea of anti-authoritarian elephant riders fighting back against The Man and his forces of oppression.
