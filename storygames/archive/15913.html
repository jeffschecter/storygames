\n One of the Roo Sack Gamers has crafted his first game... a Fiasco playset. For those who don't know, Fiasco is quick (2 hours or so) game for 3-5 players. Each playset has lists that help you chose how screwed up the game is going to get. Because of the variability in the list, the dice, and the player input, no two games of Fiasco are alike.. even when using the same playset.

Roo Sack Gamer Joe based his playset, Hell's Highway, on The Road Warrior. On the latest episode of the Roo Sack Gamers podcast, Jeff, Todd, and Roy test out the playset and have an absolute ton of fun.
