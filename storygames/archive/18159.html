    \n Wait, THE Zak Arntson?

    The stores with indie games in Seattle include Gamma Ray, Gary's Games, and a small selection at places like Card Kingdom (a great place to play games!), The Dreaming (mostly largely a comics store), and sometimes at Green Lake Games.

    SGSeattle is great, especially if you're into GM-less or collaborative games. GoPlayNW is a must. If you can make it down to Olympia for Fabricated Realities I highly recommend it.

    Or just shout if there's something you want to play and we could try to arrange a meetup. We could even break out some Shadows (a classic)!
