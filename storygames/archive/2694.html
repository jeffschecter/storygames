\n I was searching through the RPG event entries for Kublacon 07 in the SF Bay Area in May and found this entry:

Your characters are consultants from the Davidson-Oligmueller Group, a firm that defines and drives managemement, process, and organizational principles. The consultants - known as DOGs - travel to subscribing companies and do what's necessary to ensure they are operating smoothly, following the DOG standards. This can mean turning out weak middle managers, confronting rogue operators, challenging the behavior of corrupt bosses, and so on - you are those three-piece-suited consultants they fear!

I don't know the GM (Wilson Zorn), but this DitV mod sounds pretty damn funny, and reminds me a little of Jason's copier repairmen game. Especially if you replace the Steward with Terry Tate, Office Linebacker.
