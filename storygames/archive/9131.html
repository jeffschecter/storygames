\n
\n

\n
\n
\n
\n
\n -Sometimes I haven't eaten Thai food in a long time and although it feels lame to order Pad Thai it's what Thai food means to me and I don't want to take a risk on something else my first time back.
\n
\n-Sometimes I think I dislike a broccoli but it turns out it was because my parents overcooked it but when I have it at my favorite restaurant it's awesome.
\n
\n-Sometimes who makes the food is more important than the food itself.
\n
\n-Sometimes no matter who makes it I don't like the food.
\n
\n-Sometimes the recipe is so easy and good it comes out great every time!
\n
\n-Sometimes the recipe sucks but the food is amazing I just can't replicate it with these directions.
\n
\n-Sometimes I'm not allergic but the way I act may make you think I am!
\n
\n-Sometimes I think I don't like something but it's because I associate that food with a bad memory.
\n
\n-Sometimes I want to experience new things. I've chosen a restaurant I love and try a different item on the menu every time I go there to see what I may be missing.
\n
\n-Sometimes I'm so hungry I just don't care!
\n
\n-Sometimes I'll eat something I don't like because that's what my friends are eating and we are hanging out.
\n
\n-Sometimes I'm a professional cook and very picky about food due to my overly intimate knowledge or overexposure.
\n
\n-Sometimes I want quantity!
\n
\n-Sometimes I want quality!
\n
\n-Sometimes I own a restaurant and am biased on my food tastes because I depend on them.
\n
\n-Sometimes I have a political or morale agenda that affects what I choose to eat.
\n
\n-Sometimes I just don't have the time to cook at home.
\n
\n-Sometimes I can't afford certain foods.
\n
\n-Sometimes I'm a nutritionist and I just know too much I can't eat certain things!
\n
\n-Sometimes I've only eaten burgers, fries, hotdogs, and chicken fingers and didn't really ever consider eating anything else.
\n
\n-Sometimes people try to break down why we like certain things and I just want to eat!
\n
\n-Sometimes I'm a cook and I can take something most people hate and make it taste awesome!
\n
\n-Sometimes people keep getting confused when they try to make it on their own and it sucks.
\n
\n-Sometimes I like to measure my ingredients.
\n
\n-Sometimes I wing it!
