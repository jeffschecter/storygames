    \n\nSeptember 2010 \n
    \n
    \n

    \n
    \n I helped with last year's "games on demand" Story Games Carnival, It was amazing. We played PILES of games; Starblazer Adventures, Dread, Dogs in the Vineyard, Spirit of the Century, Hellas, Swashbucklers of the 7 Skies, Fear, Grimm, (and a bunch I am sure I am forgetting) it was a blast. This year there is going to be a repeat of the awesome! :-D There will many games available. We find interested people and then run a game! We can run a game for 2 hours in between other activities, or sit down for an all night story games bender. It really is a great format to try out new games and play your old favorites.
    \n
    \nWe are looking for GMs/DMs/Story Tellers!! you can email me directly at trechriron10 **at** yahoo **dot** com. Depending on the game you want to run, I will get it to the right coordinator (including myself :-D). If you run scheduled games or volunteer to man the Story Games Carnival 4 slots (16 hours), you get free admission!
    \n
    \nCreative U is going to be so amazing this year! Come out and meet game designers, industry professionals, and publishers and listen to them talk about the business of games and creative pursuits!
    \n
    \nThis year NeonCon is going to be amazing! I highly recommend you come out to sunny Las Vegas, NV and spend 4 awesome days of gaming fun!
