\n Hey everyone! We're organizing a KristaCon event in NYC, at the same space as Burning Apocalypse Con (Studio 440), from May 24th - 26th. It'll have six games, three of Marvel Heroic, one each of DW, AW, and Monsterhearts. Each game will have 12-16 players, 3-4 GMs, and 3 sessions connecting to each other, forming a kind of miniature campaign. KristaCon is meant to provide a level of character growth and depth that you don't normally get in one-shot con games, and that's exactly what we're aiming for.

This'll be the first time that we've run the *W games in this format, and we're all pretty excited!

We'll be putting up a Kickstarter soon so people can get tickets in advance (and we're going to be throwing a few other goodies in there, too). We're looking for players, of course, but we're also looking for anyone interested in helping out as a GM. GMs will get lowered costs, along with endless admiration and adulation. Huzzah!

If anyone has any questions or is interested, let us know! Hope we'll see a bunch of you at the end of May!
