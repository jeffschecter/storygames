\n Dear friends and acquaintances.

I am seeking art for the upcoming release of the High Quality Roleplaying White Box. The setting is a fantasy world of great heroes and horrific monsters, but you don't play the heroes or the monsters. Rather, you play ordinary people caught up through recklessness or foolishness into adventures that are way over your head and will likely kill you.

In play, the adventures are way over your head and usually kill you.

Anyway, art. This is the sort of stuff I want. Think pre-Elmore D&D style. I strongly encourage amateur-level work. Black and white, and inked please.

Things which are awesome:
1) Cartoons of any sort.
2) Art you've already done which is at all appropriate.
3) Dungeon maps.
4) Maps of any sort.
5) Characters who do not have swords or armor. (characters are poor and generally unarmed.)
6) Anything in the monsters section. (complete list follows)
7) Characters interacting with things in the monsters section, ideally not in actual combat (if you're going to show them in combat, show them losing), but cowering, running away, pulling a trick, etc.
8) Locations: creepy old forests, houses, abandoned castles, catacombs and their entrances, tombs and graveyards, trackless deserts, etc. ideally with characters entering them, leaving them, or just present in the picture for a sense of scale.
9) Magic signs and symbols, including just random patterns and shit.
10) Fancy items and treasures.
11) Art which you do not want to be paid for. (in which case, please let me know what I can do in exchange.)
12) Art which you want to be paid for. (in which case, please let me know how much money you want.)

--
