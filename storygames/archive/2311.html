I have just posted the first of two official threads on Forge Midwest 2007.

Forge Midwest official thread, part 1 of 2

Last year, the first annual Forge Midwest was a fantastic hit. People came in to the Chicago area from all over the Midwest, but also all over the US and Canada (go James Brown!).

ANYONE is invited. We'll create an evite once the plans are finalized, and use that as "registration." This isn't a formal con in the traditional sense -- no committee, no guests of honor, no reserved block of hotel rooms, etc. Just let me know you're coming, pay up your share of the cost, and have a great time!

What we do is charge a small fee (last year it was around $20 for the whole weekend) to rent meeting rooms at a hotel. Everyone's responsible for his/her own lodging and food (we'll probably have some refreshments again this year). And, then we all get together and have a HELL of a lot of fun playing RPGs and other games (read: Jungle Speed).

If you've ever been to GenCon and enjoyed nights at Embassy Suites playing games in the lobby, this is basically an excuse to do that for two and a half days nonstop.

It's beyond awesome. So! Get to that link above or chime in here with questions, comments and concerns. Part 2 will come in less than a week from now with final details for the event.
