    My Scenario -- Others Are Welcome to Submit Their Own
    This is what I'm thinking of running, if I run. If others want to run, I'm cool with that too.

    Maldor
    Maldor is the Forgotten Realms meets Road Warrior, a post-apocalyptic fantasy wasteland, ravaged by unceasingly by War, Famine, Pestilence, and Death. It's a dreadful, awful place, but life goes on for its dirty, illiterate, (literally) godforsaken people, who scavenge for life's necessities amid corpse-crows and mercenaries.

    Absolon's Bastards
    Maldor's politics are a mess. Each powerful nobleman fancies himself a lineal descendent of Absolon, the Sun King, destined to reunite the kingdom and bring peace to the land. This messianic concern for the common good justifies perpetual war against pretenders to the throne, and receiving lavish tributes from the region's impoverished villagers.

    After decades of this treatment by the aristocracy, the people are torn between devotion and hatred; revolutionaries and blasphemous cultists skulk in the shadows, waiting for weaknesses in the class system, and rival armies tromp across the broken lands, hungry for conquest. It is no place for the weak of heart.

    But...
    What happens when a major aristocrat, leader of a recently vanquished war machine, trained from birth with the belief that he is the son of a god, realizes that he isn't the messiah? What happens when the guy with the most power, realizes he doesn't deserve it? What happens to his family's ambitions? What of the priest who filled his head with lies, or the scoundrel wizard who showed him the truth? What happens with the army marching on his kingdom? What happens to the serfs and soldiers who devoted their lives to him?

    It's a little before 1 AM my time, I gotta go to sleep. But the idea here is, everyone in the game will be a Maldorite, and is involved in this situation. I'll post a few more details on Tuesday, if not before.
