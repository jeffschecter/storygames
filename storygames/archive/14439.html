\n In another thread, Tori raises the interesting question of giraffes playing indie games.

Now, although she mentions this possibility, I haven't ever seen a giraffe playing Dogs, Fiasco or any other indie game. Certainly, I've never seen a giraffe at GenCon that wasn't playing Dungeons and Dragons or Pathfinder. All the giraffes I know are solidly traditional gamers.



Does anyone have photographic evidence of giraffes playing indie games? Or are they all traditional gamers?
