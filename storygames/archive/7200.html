    \n Edit: In reference to this movie:

    Aye, yes! Instead of it being all about 'the awesome' the character reflects on things which are just not awesome at all. The characters reflection is the payoff moment, rather than 'awesome stuff happens' being the payoff. Though a side order of invisi cloaks and glowing bows is nice. Even then the glowing bow becomes part of a greater question of kill or not kill. The glowing bow isn't supposed to be whats actually fun with this sort of focus - it's just a side order. I sometimes wonder how many people only get excited about magic bows or motorcyles with flaming wheels or whatever stuff, then confuse that feeling of fun with actually having made story. It's like they think if they have fun with an RPG, they must have been having fun making story.

    Anyway, that vid strikes me as being spot on, which reminds me of how many actual play accounts I've seen which are supposedly aimed at story but end up being about the glowing bows and such.
