I am looking for help tracking down a copy of Jedi Blackbird. I plan to use Lasers and Feelings to introduce RPGs to my kids, to help get them comfortable with adding the structure of story games. Star Wars is a well-known and loved in our house so using it as the backdrop for the game removes the need to explain what's possible for the characters.

Once we get a few of those down my plan is to shift to Lady Blackbird - again in Star Wars - to get into a more sophisticated system (phase three is to move into more traditional Lady Blackbird and play out a story along the lines of Moby Dick, another favorite). That's the plan anyway.

I could build Jedi Blackbird from scratch but if anyone has access to the previously-created version I'd appreciate the head start. The link from John's site is dead so this is my last try. Without it I might jump straight the classic Blackbird. Thanks in advance for any help.
