\n Hi guys,

We are looking 1-2 for new storytelling games fans to come and join us

When: Every Monday night 18:30->22:00
Where: The Queen's Head Picadilly (though we might go back to the Black Horse off Oxford Street if there was room again)
What do we play? There is nearly always a HeroQuest game going. We usually alternate that with something else. In A Wicked Age is popular. In the last 12 months we have played: Dying Earth, My Life With Master, Spirit of the Century, Sorceror and Burning Empires. I'm keen to get a Dogs game and a Houses of the Blooded game on in the next 12 months. We are fairly broad-minded, though some have more tolerance for crunch than others.

How do you get in touch: Mail me at ian_hammond_cooper@yahoo.co.uk or via http://groups.yahoo.com/group/londonherowars/

Cheers,
