\n The Endgame Minicon is just a week away. It's a good thing I signed up early, because there turned out to be so many good choices, I would have had a tough time deciding.

http://www.endgameoakland.com/minicon/

But if you are in the area of Northern California, come and check it out. There's still a few spots open (including a couple of GM spots, though you might have trouble filling the seats with only a week left). Still room in InSpectres, Dark Heresy, Sword & Wizardry, Labyrinth Lord, Burning Wheel, and a couple of games run by good friends, Montsegur 1244 and A Penny For My Thoughts.

Anyone going? Aside from the two games I'm running, I'm playing in the morning Spirit of the Century game as well.
