      Some interesting stuff with the Atom feeds, too.

      The feed will generate a dynamic feed for whatever you are currently doing.

      So, at the site level, if you click the Atom button, your feed will get updated every time there is a new discussion (and perhaps every new post as well).

      If you go into a discussion and click Atom, the feed will get updated when new comments are added.

      If you go into a category and click Atom, you'll get updates on posts/comments in that feed.

      If you click on SEARCHES, then search for something, then click Atom, you'll get updates on posts whenever that search term comes up again.

      For example, here's an Atom feed that will update each time a comment mentions "owlbear":
      http://www.story-games.com/forums/search.php?PostBackAction=Search&Keywords=owlbear&Type=Comments&btnSubmit=Search&Feed=Atom

      Funky.
