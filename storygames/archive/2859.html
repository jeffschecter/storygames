\n (Spun out of the Quality Control thread.)

This thread is for people who are interested in getting some serious, hard-hitting criticism of their game. Not anything insulting, of course, but not pulling punches either.

To help others in this thread, start your posts with one (or more) of these tags:\n
  • Game Up: "I have this game I want you to look over." You MUST include one of the next two tags, and should give a description of your game.
    • \n
  • Critique: "I will read your game, make characters, consider the rules critically, look for loopholes and possible abuses, advise on the layout, artwork, and writing style, and generally scour the whole thing. I do not have (time or a group) to playtest right now."
    • \n
  • Playtest: "Me and my pals will playtest your game for at least one full-length session, preferably more, and we will play rough with it. We will take notes." The "critique" tag is implied in this one.
    • \n
  • It's Mine: "I'll critique Game Blah from So-and-so."
    • \n
  • Kibitz: "I'm on this thread to hang out and talk." You CANNOT include the Game Up tag unless you're also critiquing or playtesting.
\nOnce you've written your critique, send it to the person who wrote the game. Don't post it around without their permission - this is intended to be private commentary used in revision, not a review for public consumption.

Suggestions on the rules are welcome, and those incorporated will be reflected in edits to this post.
