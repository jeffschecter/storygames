\n A propos of my obsession with crossing over between different AW hacks, I've signed up to run a one-shot MotW/MH mashup whose premise is, "Imagine that Mulder and Scully were sent to hunt down Buffy and her friends."

So... how do I make this work? I'll have six PCs, and I'm thinking 3 from each group, but I could see an argument for 2 MotW and 4 MH.

I have some other, semi-detailed thoughts already but I want to see what other people think first.

THREAD RULES: Please only post if you are familiar with both games or at least 3 different AW-family games.

Matt

    \n I'm thinking about rules crossovers: strings can probably retain their use, and operate as written on MotW characters. I don't think there's really anything else that will "push over" into the foreign rules like that.

    You should think about mapping the stats, too, for when a MH character needs to roll Charm or a MotW character needs to roll Dark or whatever.

    It will be interesting to see how having characters with different sets of basic moves interact. I'd love to watch (or take part in) this game!

    In terms of numbers, I'd go 2 + 4 as well. If there are even numbers, the monster hunters will find it easier to just kill the others. You mention Mulder and Scully - are you going to specify the playbooks? That suggests Flake + Professional to me, and I think that will work well. You probably want to discourage the more combat-focused hunters as well.
