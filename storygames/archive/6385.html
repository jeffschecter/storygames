    \n Yes! Notably, Daniel (of Highmoon) and myself. I'd love to meet up, Ryan. I'm always complaining about the desert that is South Florida. When are you coming down?

    Game store-wise, the best is The Adventure Game Store (not the best name, I know--formerly, Dragon's Lair). They just moved into some new digs. I haven't been yet, but the photos look very promising--sort of a poor man's Endgame with the open space and lighting. The owners and staff are very nice and knowledgeable (though I think Daniel and I need to start ramping up the indie side of things). In fact, Daniel and I have discussed a GoPlay/Game Day event at the store for some time this summer.
