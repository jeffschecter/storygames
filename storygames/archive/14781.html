    \n I think Kings' have an RPG society also but they might be a bit trad.

    Speaking as the "Organiser" of London Indie RPG (we are actually a cake-eating anarchosyndicate) I can heartily recommend us. Plenty of friendly, talented folk attend - we had last year's Game Chef winner and runner-up amongst our attendees last month. It will be our first Birthday in October and there will be more cake there, plus some sort of event we have yet to dream up.

    We have a forum on UK Roleplayers. Feel free to post comments and questions there.

    Also Concrete Cow is happening the week after our September meet and quite a few London people go to this. Milton Keynes is about an hour on the train.

    Look forward to seeing you in September

    \n London has a number of groups, some trad, some indie, and some somewhere in between.

    Gameforce is a Sunday session run by the Cubicle 7/Leisure Games team: http://gameforce.livejournal.com/

    London Gamers meet in the Black Horse. Mixed trad with some indie: http://games.groups.yahoo.com/group/LondonGamers/

    The Monday Nighters (us) http://mondaynighters.net/ We're mixed. Right one group is running a HeroQuest game (we got together through Hero Wars), and Apocalypse World looks next up (prior games include Diaspora, My Life with Master, Houses of the Blooded...) The other group just switched from 4e to Fate...

    There are more. Try searching here: http://www.ukroleplayers.com/forum/viewforum.php?f=6
