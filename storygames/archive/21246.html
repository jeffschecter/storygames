    \n I'm a big Clockwork: Dominion fan.
    I was impressed by Vanagard.
    Pythos does some interesting stuff, as does Dreamchaser.
    I've missed Castle Falkenstein and was glad I got a chance to play that.
    Dweomer by Gaslight is a mashup of Fate and Hero for system and Victorian setting Dresden for setting.
    Blue Rose -- I'm trying to figure out what is feeling a bit off for me tone-wise, and I shall have to read the book before I answer. I mean, I've played twice and had a blast each time. And, each time, I was surprised by the combat -- how much there was in the first game I played, and by the nature of the combat in the second. But, I need to actually read the book before I'm sure whether it's the scenario or me.
