    \n Hi Evan,

    There are scattered expat gaming groups in Hong Kong, along with some local groups. Some of those folks may have some interest in story games. I'm out of the loop -- I moved out of HK in 2007 -- but you may find people. The best way to do this is to take a scattershot approach. Try whatever gaming meet-up sites are currently active. Try RPG.net. It's big. Try other sites like ENWorld. If you don't limit your gaming to "story games," you'll get the best results. There's also one gaming store in HK that sells RPGs. It's on Nathan Road in the Mongkok/Prince Edward area. I've never tried recruiting there, but you might run into someone. Unfortunately I don't remember the name of the store.

    Also, as it happens, I'll be visiting HK myself for work from May 17th to July 2nd. If the timing is right, we could potentially get together for a game.
