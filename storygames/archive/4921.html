\n I'm afraid that trying to tack this on in the prior planning-and-logistics thread will leave it nigh-invisible to some. So I'm giving it it's own thread.

Please post here if you have some interest in attending a Go Play Florida event. It will likely be held in Orlando (though I am also still somewhat considering South Florida/Fort Lauderdale/Miami). I am presently looking at the dates of February 22-24. I will likely be renting a luxury mansion for the event, which means nice rooms, a full kitchen for attendees to display their cooking prowess (I'll personally bring stuff for one nice meal), and even a heated pool and hot tub. I am trying to identify how many likely attendees we will have so I know how big a mansion to get.

When you post, please let me know: (a) the level of your interest, i.e., the likelihood you will actually attend; (b) which of the two locations you would be willing and able to attend; and (c) if the dates are good for you. So, a post might look something like this.\n
I am definitely planning on attending. I am able to attend at either Orlando or South Florida, but I prefer South Florida. Those dates are fine for me.
