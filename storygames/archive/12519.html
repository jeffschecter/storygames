    I just came up with this extra rule while walking around today:

    When you roll Architect during an operation, you can use it to combine multiple actions (including improvised actions) into a single action, pooling any Prep Dice assigned to those actions into a single roll. This basically lets you cut through the dream, by altering to suit your purposes. However, each time you do that, the Agency gets to make a free move, as if you failed, since the projections in the dream become aware of your intrusion and turn against you.
