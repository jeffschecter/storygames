    \n Ooh! Ooh! Pick me! My current design Showdown is GMless, with no setting (indeed, it is genre-neutral), for two players, and is intended to be played in full in 60-90 minutes.

    The concept: you and your fellow player play two characters in a duel to the death. Intercut with your duel are a series of flashbacks, where you tell the story of how these two arrived at this final duel.

    Only one will survive.

    High melodrama.

    Whisper me if you're interested. I'd *love* some playtest feedback.

    Seth Ben-Ezra
    Great Wolf
