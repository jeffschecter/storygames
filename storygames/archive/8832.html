    \n If you want something that's not tied to your geography and one vendor (kindle) or a single drm'd vendor with an awful reputation (sony) you can always buy a standard compliant ereader from elsewhere:

    Cybook
    BeBook
    (both seem good, price is almost the same, both support pdf zoom in their latest incarnation)

    iRex
    (lots of cool startrecky features like touch and writing on pages, but awfully expensive)

    I think they all support books bought from any store that sells .mobi books. Obviously if you value the amazon integration none of them is a competitor to the Kindle, though.
