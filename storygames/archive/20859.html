    \n I never find anything on Google (other than millions of results) but surprisingly often in this forum's searchbar.

    Just be sure to include quotations marks (") if you remember several words or use plus signs (+) to give some words higher priority. Using ...
    "hello world" birdcage +piper
    ... searches for pages (read: forum threads) with either "hello world" and birdcage but pages containing piper has a higher priority.

    That's how I'm guessing the search engine works. So searching for +indie +hack lists all pages containing those words but lists pages that contains both words at the top.
