\n Inspired by this article, I decided to whip up an interactive Apocalypse World scenario. It's an application of the rules and principles of Apocalypse World (Successfully I hope) into a cRPG form. The scenario can be download here and requires Chatmapper to run. This little vignette is loosely drawn from a game I'm currently writing that takes place in post-apocalyptic Columbian Plateau. The final game will hopefully be less violent, but I figured this scenario would be better suited for a short stint like this.

Feel free to let me know about your thoughts of adapting AW to a cRPG form and any bugs or criticism you might have of my vignette (I'm pretty drunk right now so there will doubtlessly be a few bugs and typos)!
