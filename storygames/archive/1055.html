Challenge: Game ideas for non-rpg magazines

Matthijs
August 2006 edited August 2006 in Story Games
(This was inspired by komradebob's thread "Submitting games to non-rpg magazines").

This is a little challenge to get the ideas rolling. The rules are as follows:

- Check out the last post in the thread. It has the name of a magazine.
- Write a one-to-four sentence idea for a RPG that magazine might publish.
- Finish your post with the name of a new magazine. (Provide a link to the magazine if possible).

So, for example, a post might look like this:
"Soldier of Fortune: A solo RPG with heavy tactics, based on getting behind enemy lines in a warzone in the Middle East. Choice of weapons a major factor of game mechanics.

Next: Barbie International Club Magazine."

    When Planning is Not Enough: One player is the Planner, the other is the Chaos Factor. The Planner sets up a goal - paint the house a different color, refurnish the living room, redesign the garden - and divides it into five stages. For each stage, the Chaos Factor introduces unplanned things that might go wrong, and they role-play it out. The aim of the game is to help players understand and master the unforeseen.
