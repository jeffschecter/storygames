    \n You can officially download a 30-day trial version of Vegas Pro. That's plenty of time for a good project.
    \nAlso. older versions are quite affordable. If you don't need support for HD-filming stuff you won't really see any big differences between them. Just don't pick an older version as Vegas 5.
    \n
    \nEdit: And I think it's PC only. Also if you plan to get it, don't bother with "Vegas Movie Studio". Go straight for "Vegas Pro".
