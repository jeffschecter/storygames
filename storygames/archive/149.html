Okay, I really dig on the idea of loresheets; and while playing with different ways of arranging traits for my game, I've been picking at the idea, in a way.

Basically, each of them would be a skill - with an attached need that has to be fed. And a bit of stuff about how that works in the setting, maybe even a 'ficlet'.

So, for example, if you buy into trap-making, you'll also have a need for security, and should read up on 'home defense after the pulse'. If you buy into leadership, then you also get a need for approval or confidence, and should read up on 'governments after the pulse'. If you buy into seduction, you get a need for intimacy, and will want to read up on 'sex after the pulse'.

...Something like that.

How does that sound? Any thoughts?
