\n Are you a fan of Miike Takashi movies? I totally am not, yet for some reason watch them anyway: They've had a pretty big impact on me. Some, I've found kinda formulaic in their own perverse way, and I was kinda thinking that once the formula is broken down, you might be able to turn the game into a multi-session RPG?

    Posted By: Antoine FWhat's your advice ?
    \nWow, that's some pretty weird luck!

    Well, See, with the Oracles you first create explicit characters, then implicit characters.

    Like the Box (Diamond 7), there is no character spelled out. So we have to infer some implicit characters: Who made the box, whose it is, who it was meant for, etc.

    So, with the above, you have to think of implicit characters like:

    Whose semen is it?
    Which (presently not assaulted: yet) callgirl's apartment balcony is covered with semen spent from the dark hero perched on the railing?
    -Who is that callgirl's yakuza boyfriend, who will somehow inadvertantly lead to her assualt?
    -And who is the dark hero, from whose busted nut came the oracle artifact?
    Which yakuza gang leader has a laboratory, complete with 20 test-tubes filled with the semen of his enemies?
    -Who is trying to get their hands on that collection to sell on Yahoo Auctions Dot JP, and why?
    Whose (or, "which cop's/yakuza's") bowl of ramen noodles had a thin scum of semen floating on the top of it, as well as two 9mm bullets, to serve as a warning?
    -Who made the bowl of seme-ramen, and for what purpose?
    There, eight characters, 4 implicit, 4 further inferred from the implicit characters.

    Finding the implicit characters here might be challenging, but there is more freedom in that as well.

    -Andy
