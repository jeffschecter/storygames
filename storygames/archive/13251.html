    \n I have played it fairly extensively now, though I had to take hiatus recently before we could incorporate the translations from German for their new potions and spells.
    It's an easy enough game, and if you go through their forums, you can find plenty of information on building your own monsters and such. The dice-rolling is actually pretty cool, allowing for some heroic abilities that aren't 100% criticals (which I kinda like). For something that's simple, fan-supported, and best of all, free, it is probably one of the better ones out there, in my opinion.

    My group and I had been working on a hack of it, since we've been moving further and further away from things like talents and experience points, but we really don't have a polished version yet.
