    \n Still pretty much ludicrous, but there aren't many options otherwise. The website for the hotel says that their in-house airport shuttle is $40.00, or $70.00 for a car. Judging from some maps that I dug up on google, you can take the train from Newark Airport down to Morristown. The train ride would take you about an hour and a half to cover, with one switchover. On the plus side, the station seems like it's only about two miles away from the hotel. Maybe a quick cab ride from there won't hurt the wallet as much.

    \n Newark airport connects by train to Newark Penn station (not to be confused with New York penn station, see below). From there NJ transit trains that will connect to morristown, the hotel is like 3 blocks from the train station, no cab needed.

    JFK and laguardia you have to take NYC subways to penn station then get on a NJ transit train to morristown, again no cab needed.

    It sounds a lot more confusing than it actually is.
