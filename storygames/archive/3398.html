\n Howdy!

I've put up my interview with Matt Snyder & Paul Czege of the Ashcan Front on my game design podcast, Master Plan. If you're interested in hearing Matt & Paul talk together about ashcans, the Ashcan Front, and what you can do with ashcans outside of GenCon, check out Master Plan #9.

My interview is sort of a response to Paul's interviews on Theory From The Closet & Canon Puncture. I found myself asking some questions and wanting to also hear what Matt had to say, so here's what I got out of that. The episode is just under 30 minutes, and most of it is our interview. Hope you enjoy!

- Ryan
