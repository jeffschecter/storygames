\n People who make games, I need recommendations for someone that you have worked with who can make good maps.

1. Must be someone you worked with -- on a game you made.

2. Will need to make some modern-day maps of various buildings. No overland maps or rural areas or anything like that -- just someone who can take my crude sketches and use them to make cool maps of modern-day locations (such as schools, churches, or office buildings). Modern-day dungeons. Top-down black-and-white stuff.

3. Note: must be comfortable working on the kind of game that I make (many of my games -- like Spite and Scorn -- are free, shouldn't be hard to find out that I make R-rated games full of gore and pentagrams).

Recommend away, s'il vous pla\xeet!
