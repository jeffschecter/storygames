\n I'm putting together the final touches for my new edition of Guerrilla Television, a reality bloodsport storytelling game. But I'm looking for some shows/movies that might make a decent glossary of atmospheric pieces to get players into the mood.

So far I've got...

The Running Man - A man forced to fight his way through a glitzy game show after being accused of crime he didn't commit.

Death Race - One man forced to fight for his life in a deadly car race.

Battle Royale (1 and 2) - Most notably 2, because the basic map laid out for the game is Hajima Island, where Battle Royale 2 was filmed.

Dead Set - In which zombies are prevalent outside the "Big Brother" house.

The general genre constraints are "the more violent, the better", "the more desperate the participants are, the better", "the more decadent the game show, the better", comedy/action/drama...I don't mind, TV shows/movies/other games...I don't mind having a variety here either. I'm going to start a thorough search for movies and TV shows of this genre, but I thought the SG community might have some good ideas.

    \n A few video games worth checking out, some of which might be tough to find:

    Mutant League Football and Mutant League Hockey (Sega Genesis)-- last I checked, there were emulators online.

    Dead Ball Zone (Playstation One)-- kind of a weird mix between lacrosse, american football, and soccer. With chainsaws and grenades.

    Blood Bowl (PC) -- the rulebook for the minis game is available for free online, and might be worth paging through

    Twisted Metal (Playstation) -- any of these games capture the mood, really, but I prefer the earlier ones.
