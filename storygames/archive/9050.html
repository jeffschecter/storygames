    \n We are full with 6 players. The campaign will have a Sword and Sorcery vibe set in "Tol-na-Grondh" a massive ancient city set on the coast and ruled by some of the last of the Sorcerer Kings. Think Fafhrd and Gray Mouser fun, with a little Elric (high magic) thrown in, but not as morose.

    We have:

    "Black" Alice - a spooky widow that speaks to her dead husband, on a quest to learn about his death and to find her children

    Graham Bannion - a playwright, actor, dilatante, with a secret association to the Lamplighter vigilantes

    Bane - an ex-noble, weather warlock, rogue scholar, with way to much morals for the desires of his heart

    Czarina - Bane's sister, a noble, temple knight/guardian, trying to salvage her brothers name

    Chompontli - disgraced lesser sorcerer prince, made outcast by killing his brother and his uncle's guile, now back to prove his righteousness.

    Kostya - Northern barbarian, last of his tribe, repaying an oath of protection so he can get on with an oath of vengeance

    If interested check it out at The Burning Wheel Forums
