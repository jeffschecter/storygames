    \n Just caught this thread now, for some reason.

    I see absolutely no reason not to make the hold lasting - there\u2019s no rule that says it expires at the end of the session (moves which do specify this).

    Keep in mind that, with \u201chypnotic\u201d, it\u2019s the MC who spends the hold (at least most of the time), not the player.

    It\u2019s your job as the MC to try to spend the hold freely and basically as much as you can - it\u2019s not something to hoard. Give the player (and the PC) their due, and keep being a fan.
