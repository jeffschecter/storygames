\n I thought I would invite any Story Gamers who live close to SLC, UT to our Meetup this week.

Where:
Demolition Games
85 West 3300 South, Salt Lake City, UT

When:
Thursday, October 13, 2016
6:00 PM to 9:00 PM

Our group gets together and plays different Indie RPGs and Story Games. The groups also play-tests each other's RPGs and Story Games, which are in development. The type of games we could play, are any and all types of Indie RPGs and Story Games, but here are some examples: Apocalypse World, Fiasco, Dungeon World, Burning Wheel, Lady Blackbird, Fate Accelerated Edition, Kagematsu, Dogs in the Vineyard, Don't Rest Your Head, Fate Core, Microscope, Kingdom, Primetime Adventures, Urban Shadows, Blades in the Dark, Mouse Guard, Monsterhearts, Archipelago, Swords Without Master, Polaris (the "indie" one), and many, many more.

(This week we will be playing Follow by Ben Robbins (which is in the late stages of development but very playable).

Here is a little bit about the game we will be playing:

\u201cFollow is a game about working together to achieve a common goal. Slay the dragon. Cure a disease. Overthrow a tyrant. Get your candidate elected. We\u2019ll pick a quest together to decide what kind of game we want to play.
Can we stay united and succeed or will our differences tear us apart? We\u2019ll play and find out.
But even if we do everything perfectly, our quest may fail. As players we can push for the outcome we want to see but we cannot guarantee it. Our story may surprise us, and that\u2019s part of the fun.\u201d
-from Follow

Here is the link if you would like to RSVP:
https://www.meetup.com/wasatch-rpgamers/events/234740626/
