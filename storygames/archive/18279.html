    \n Hey Jarrod, I think I ran that Dungeon World. You played the druid and y'all got up to all kinds of mischief in Blackmoore.

    In case you're interested, there's annual roleplaying convention in Canberra called Phenomenon. It's always on the Queen's Birthday Long Weekend, this year's that's 7\u201310 June. I'll be running games on demand. You can find details here: http://pheno.org.au/

    Pheno has a strong roleplaying community but their games are a little different. They're typically tabletop freeforms, with very few explicit rules and are competitively judged. I don't really grok this style so I prefer running games on demand. But there's usually over a 100 attendees by my reckoning, so plenty of folk clearly like this style of game.
