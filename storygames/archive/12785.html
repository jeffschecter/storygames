    \n The loudspeaker continues to repeat the same dull message. "Testing, testing. Ignore." Those same words echo over and over again, their cold repetition a suitable soundtrack to the abandoned penitentiary. The floors in the East Wing are wet, slimy mold growing in some locations. The walls here are hewn of stone, rather than the uniform poured cement that you've seen elsewhere in the compound.

    As you continue on through the East Wing, you feel a damp breeze on your neck. The origin is unclear to you. "Testing, testing. Ignore." The words are still present, but they're fading as you move deeper into the East Wing. The breeze feels stronger, with each step you take.

    "Testing, test..."

    There are no loudspeakers in the East Wing. It's just you and the uneven stone, here.
    That is, until you hear a voice. What does it say?
