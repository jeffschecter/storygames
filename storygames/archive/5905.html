\n Hey everybody!

Pre-registration is open for Go Play NW 2008! Here's the registration page. Also, here's the Go Play NW 2008 Wiki.

There are a few changes worth noting this year:

First of all, our space is limited to 75 people. That gives us some space to grow this year, but it's probably still a good idea to pre-register.

Also, if we get enough pre-registrations, we won't need to raise the price at the door very much, so that helps everyone too.

You can order T-shirts with your registration. The theme is "robo-sasquatch". John Harper is doing the design again this year. We don't have a preview yet, but it should be pretty cool.

This year we're launching the Go Play NW Scholarship Program. The purpose of this program is to help folks come to Go Play NW who might not otherwise be able to afford to attend. If you know someone who qualifies, please email goplaynw@gmail.com! You can donate to this program on our registration page. If by chance we have money left over, we'll be using it to improve Go Play NW in some other way.

We're not booking accommodations through our Web Page this year. Go to the accommodations page on the wiki to see what options are available.

edit: a couple of minor clarifications
