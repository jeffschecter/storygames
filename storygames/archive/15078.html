    \n I think The Pool would work well for the talky, light ensemble drama. When a character Says Something, roll to see how it is received by the others. Then narrate the reaction. Then roll for the reaction to the reaction.

    Often on Northern Exposure, characters vied to win the community's support on a specific topic. One's reputation would rise or crash, like a dice pool.

    Also, the character sheet matches the way Northern Exposure characters start as sketches, then accrue shading and depth as we get to know them.
