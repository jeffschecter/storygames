    \n To those that have been suggested who do not yet have books in stores,

    1) Do you consider anything you have done complete and "published?" Your own definition of publishing will suffice and obviously does not have to include paper publishing.

    2) Is it done? Is there a significant chance that there will be serious revisions to it?

    My show is about game design and I try to conduct interviews about games which are believed to be done. While I'd love to interview "awesome players" or artists or such folks, it's out of theme for the show and I'm trying to stay pretty strictly to my self-imposed limits.

    PS: If I'm monopolizing the thread, tell me so.
