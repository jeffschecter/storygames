\n Hey! I've just finished my new game, here, have it!
It's called Launch Day!
Well, to be more precise it's called Launch Day, Part One: Asphalt Walkers.
It's a sleek 12 pages, 3-4 hours, 4-5 players.


Pitch? Yeah, got it right here:
A group of suburban homeowners build a rocket ship in a garage so two of them can visit a strange settlement on top of a cloud. This game explores one side of a peaceful cultural exchange and examines assumptions about the others and about how we wish to be known.

The link is on my website!
http://www.photographsoflightning.com/launch-day-part-one/


I bet you're wondering about that "Part One" thing.

Well, I'll be kicking off my Patreon page on October 23rd. Would you help me spread the word? Though this game is fun on its own, it does have a continuation game for a second session... and yeah, I'm pretty proud of it. That'll be my first Patreon release, exclusive to my Patrons the day I make my page public... Launch Day!\ufeff
