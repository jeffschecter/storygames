\n Full disclosure: I've never played this game. Most of what I know about it comes from the newest APAP. That said, what I heard in that podcast suggests to me that this game would make a great LARP, albeit one that's more structured that the standard live-action experience (something moving toward the "live action strategy" of Long Live the King, but without the competitive elements or fiddly bits).

Has anybody tried it? How did it go?

    \n Montsegur 1244 is well suited to be played semi-live: Scenes are framed by the player with narration right and are then played out. You will have to add LARP techniques to resolve actions. However, most scenes revolve about what the characters want to do more than if they can do, what they attemp to do. This approach will give a longer game and requires more space to play.
