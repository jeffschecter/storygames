    \n I've written and played so many versions of that damn thing... can't ever seem to get the tone and mechanics juuuuust right. Exemplar started out as a stepping stone for my friends along the path of D&D; -> Feng Shui -> ????? -> ya'll hippies, but kinda busted out an Anschluss on my brainspace. Gonna take my sixth attempt at it for a spin this summer, hopefully.
    \n
    \n'N jeeze, guys, when I see dudes with swords and space ships and sword-ships, I don't want your pansy exploration of gender roles -- I want dudes who fight each other! With swords and space ships! I want to hurl burning frigates from the sky onto the glass towers of Sha and match blades against my brother in the ruins of shattered arcologies! I want massive, bloody boarding actions! I want to fight my way to the bridge of the flagship to cut down the beloved lieutenant turned House traitor! I mean, yeah, no reason to Eagle Claw someone in the face unless they've got something going on with your Intended or whatever, but let the fuwafuwa play second fiddle to the gatagata just a bit, ya know?

    \nAt this point, the things I need to get done before I even begin to think of putting it all together are threefold:

    1) Playtest, playtest, playtest!

    2) Kill my babies!

    3) Write down the setting!

    Duh for the first. The setting, though -- mostly a guide to who has what cool powers and why they are likely to declare jihad against everyone else -- I want to do it through a bunch of little verbal sketches, but given that I am not Shreyas, damn, making those things not suck is hard.
