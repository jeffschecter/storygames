\n The most recent two articles in my blog concern hacks for a couple of my favourite games:

In Dead of Night: Hacked Up, I look at why DoN is so hackable and I introduce three variations to the way the game can be played, to support other styles and agendas.

Spectres is a quick & dirty hack for InSpectres that changes it into a different game, where you play ghosts trying to defend their shared residence from external threats.

Also, ICYMI, there's my irregular but ongoing series of articles in The Black Catalogue, where I describe unusual artefacts that could serve as plot devices or gimmicks in a variety of storygames.
