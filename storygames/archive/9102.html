    There's a certain point at which you can no longer suspend your awareness of the fact that the entire thing is basically a viral meme ("Get XP for recruiting people!") with a tiny cash-making advertisement slapped on it. ("Also, get XP for clicking on the page four or five times a day to run through all your fights! Even if you lose!")

    Although giving you XP when the people you recruit level up IS clever. Most of these sorts of things just go for rewarding bulk recruitment, instead of rewarding people who recruit actually active participants. It's nowhere near as effective as "spam yourself a zillion sdknsrigtn-style pupils", mind you, but that would be fairly easy to prevent if they really cared about it.
