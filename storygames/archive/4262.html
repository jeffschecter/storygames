\n I was reminded by this thread that I've wanted to do this for a long time.
\n
\nA lot of people like The Shadow of Yesterday. It's not rare to see the virtues of it's system praised.
\n
\nBut hey, ever so often it's setting get's dismissed. "I'm not that hot on the setting but TSOY rawks!"
\n
\nI think that's a shame. The World of Near is a thing of beauty. A truly rich and amazingly presented setting.
\n
\nLet's sing it's praises in this thread. What parts of the book get you all hot and bothered? Talk to me of how you've changed the world. Tell us all of how you've taken the palette and worked it to tell your twisted stories. How did you do this using The Solar System?
\n
\nNo negative content in this thread please. If you've got a problem with Near or hold it in low opinion, stay away, or share the things you do like.
