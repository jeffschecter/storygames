\n Hi all,
\n
\nI will be running Lady Blackbird at our local meetup in Amsterdam in March.
\n
\nAfter looking through the material I believe it's a perfect choice for me.
\n
\nHowever, there was some ideas which I did want to run by others as well.
\n
\nFirst of all I have made up character portraits by shamelessly re-appropriating pics.
\nPlayers will pick their characters based on the portraits.
\nhttp://dl.dropbox.com/u/14273541/Lady%20Blackbird/Character%20portraits.pdf
\n
\nSecondly, I was thinking on expanding by having several questions to the players on what the game world is like. This way the game world will be consensual and everyone will have an understanding is this science steampunk or magical steampunk. Is this more steam or electric...
\n
\nI want them to think on these aspects so that the world is clear for them, so when I start asking them further questions they already have become comfortable with this way of consensual storytelling.
\n
\nIt will be fun to run. :)
