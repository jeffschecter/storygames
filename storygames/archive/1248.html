    Whoops!
    Very fast, Andy. I had no time to post about it myself !!

    It is not intended for this kind of forum, of course. But for a play-by-post dedicated one.

    Lxndr: In a comment you may include as many calls to the dice-rolling function as you want. Something like #dice(3d6), and #dice(1d8). Each call may roll as many as 50 dice, but only of the same type (mixed types is still future work).
    The dice type could be anything from 1 side to 100 sides. I have also added d0, which means ten-sided dice but numbered from 0 to 9, instead of from 1 to 10, which is what you get with d10. The results may be sorted, you may see the sum, etc.

    The comments with dice-rolls cannot be edited to prevent people modify the results or the context of the rolls.

    And that's it! I hope it will be of help.
