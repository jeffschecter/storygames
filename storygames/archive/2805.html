\n At Camp Nerdly Zero, I brought my Amber ruleset but never actually got to run it. It just never won out over the other things in play.

I really really want to do this, more than anything else on my schedule.

So what I'm asking for here, is four people who are willing to say, "let's find a timeslot when we can play this game." I'll rearrange my schedule to make it happen.

    \n What does the timeslot look like?

    I remember last year we had some folks interested, but at Nerdly 0 the game was written for no fewer than 8 people, for no fewer than 6 hours: No one could or wanted to commit to that amount of time for one single game last year.

    Has it been reimaged to fit a "4 hour" or so timeslot?

    \n Yeah, I'm rebuilding it for a smaller "social footprint". I'm dropping the "flag" rules (as much as I like them) in favor of a choice of set-piece goals.

    This time the game is written for between four and twelve participants (nine through twelve being NPC's) and a strict time limit... at the end of the time slot, Julian picks the new Warden and the game is over.
