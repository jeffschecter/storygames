---======= /UPDATE ======---

:: puts on top hat, tails and grabs a megaphone ::

Calling all story gamers, indie kids and theory wonks! (OK, you dungeon crawlers, too.) If you like games and you're in the Philadelphia area, this is the post for you!

Looking for some new blood for your table? Or maybe some new tables to get your blood going? Want to try out that new ashcan but don't have any guinea pi... ahem... open-minded friends to try it out on?

:: ok, ok, enough with the megaphone ::

Then let's get together for some drinks in Philly. I'm looking to meet folks and I'm sure you are, too.

If you're interested, don't be shy. Leave your venue vote here! The more people that show interest on the board, the better time it'll be.

Seriously. Leave your vote. Don't make me call cousin Vincenzo from down South Philly.
