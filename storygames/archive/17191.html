\n If you're an RPG or board game designer, HammerCon provides a low-effort, low-barrier-of-entry way for you to get publicity and get your game into circulation. You owe it to yourself to take a look, I think. Over the past few years we've already done wonders for the promotion and play of story games and other independently designed and produced games in our area (Hamilton-Toronto with surroundings), as you can see from our current schedule-in-progress. We also host the Protospiel North design / prototype track, and already several games have been fine-tuned, found partners and collaborators, and reached the market (via crowdsourcing or otherwise) through HammerCon.

Take a peek and let us know what you think!
