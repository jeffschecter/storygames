Back in November, I announced a new set of awards, the Playmakers, for those who do the admin and scudwork to create space for others to have fun playing tabletop rpgs.

Through January, I'll be announcing the winners on this website. If you're interested please subscribe or add it to your rss reader.
https://playmakerawards.blogspot.com

Thanks to OneBookShelf for their contribution to the prizes and the Story Games G+ admin for their endorsement.
