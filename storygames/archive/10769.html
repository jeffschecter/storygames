\n Well, Wordplay has been spoken of favourably over here, so I just thought I'd let everyone know that it has actually just been released. It's been described as 'a bit HeroQuest like but with lots of D6'. The site describes it as:

[blurb]

Wordplay is a roleplaying game where everything that can be described has the power to affect the story that you and your friends will tell together.

Describe a thing, assign it a number of dice and roll them to see how it influences the story that you are all telling.

This book can turn any place that your imagination may care to take you into an absorbing and creative storytelling adventure game.

At the heart of Wordplay are characters. Become a pivotal character in your story, described using your own written words, giving them form and purpose.

Easy to play in any setting you wish, Wordplay is a rules light game with endless possibilities.

All you need are pens, paper and lots of six sided dice to be ready to play. [12mm 4x9 d6 dice block, probably the most powerful dice block in the world and could blow your gaming table away..]

[/blurb]
