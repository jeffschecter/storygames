    Posted By: Thunder_GodTroy, are your older games "Shoot and forget" or will you revisit them for issues of Layout/Typo errors?
    \nI'm not sure if I understand your question completely, but I will try to answer it as best I can. Each new printing of my older games will have the layout, covers, and typos fixed. It would be irresponsible IMO to not correct things like Cutthroat's somewhat blurry cover. So, for each new printing, the books will receive basic editing and fixes. This would not be something like a second or revised edition. I won't be changing any of the mechanics. Does that answer your question?
