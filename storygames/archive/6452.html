\n We've moved:

DATE/TIME: Saturday May 17th 5pm
LOCATION: Birmingham, Alabama, Legion Comics/Game Store behind the Summit on Cahaba Heights Rd (map at the meetup below).
EVENT NAME: Indie Games Meetup
QUICK DESCRIPTION: Meet other local Roleplayers for adventures and fun! Come to a Meetup to explore fantastic worlds, clash with dark monsters, spin lore and commit heroic feats while playing the greatest small press RPGs around! Although we're scheduled to end at six, we usually keep playing until we're done.
LINK: http://roleplayers.meetup.com/481

Currently playing alpha playtest rules Dresden Files RPG (v1.1) If your coming please join the Meetup and RSVP.
