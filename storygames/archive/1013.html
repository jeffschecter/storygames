    Actually, if you don't mind giving a miss to the background, then games like Beast Bind, Alshard etc aren't too hard to translate: Just break them up into their power bite-sized-chunk pieces and roll from there. But if you want the background, or it's a game where the background is really meaningful to play ( a few pages from Doublecross, or A LOT of Tokyo Nova or Tenra Bansho), then yeah it's a biiiiiiiiiiitch.

    BTW, I hope that when Tenra is on its way out, that we can get folks organized into little teams or something for slow-paced, casual fan translations of other games. Mark Causey was interested in being the point man for Alshard, for example (and he's really novice, but having a really fun time reading and learning, so it's all good).

    -Andy
