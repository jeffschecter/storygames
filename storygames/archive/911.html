    Sorry, Joe, my vote (in the current form) is "nay". It's very complicated and it uses D12s, both negatives for a "gateway" game.

    I think it could be a great game, evocative and fun, but the mechanic needs to be simplified. Have you playtested at all? My gut feel is that with D12s and a success only on a 12, there will be huge expenditure of points to "nudge up" the dice and it will quickly come to a grinding halt as people run out of points to spend. But I may have missed something.

    I briefly playtested it with my family who had never role-played before! They found it fairly easy to pick up, that's what got me wondering whether it could be a gateway game.
