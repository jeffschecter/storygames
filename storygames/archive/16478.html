\n I'm working up a one-shot packet for Sagas of the Icelanders, and want to include little countdown generators/kickers/situations/whatever for each of the six positions on the wheel-O-Gods. The idea would be that you'd fill out an r-map based on the player's playbook and relationship choices, and then look to see which of these fit with unused playbook archetypes and an impulse toward general fuckery. Please help me fine tune these!

ODIN
A great hero doesn\u2019t want to go a-viking
A woman defies the Landv\xe6ttir and digs in a mound on her farm for treasure
An orphaned child seeks to prove himself through a suicidal test of courage
A professional holmganga duelist cast his eye on a rich farmer

THOR
A Go\xf0i must decide between two ugly wrongs
A witch curses a family for a trivial slight; the effects are far-reaching
A terrible storm rises and the wreckage of a local knarr is found, but no bodies

FREYA
A man is obsessed with a forbidden woman
A woman offers a man herself in exchange for the betrayal of her husband\u2019s rival
The ribbon of rainbow light in the winter sky, the nor\xf0rlj\xf3s, is as bright as the sun - what's up with that?

LOKI
A visitor under hospitality is not what he seems
Two neighbors, male and female, form a friendship that a returning husband cannot countenance
An earthquake topples a boulder home of the Hulduf\xf3lk, opening a path to an important road

HEL
A selfish Go\xf0i amasses a great treasure at the expense of his people, who simmer with rage
A community foolishly squanders their larder on the word of a charlatan soothsayer
A friend returns from a-viking with a terrible disease and demands hospitality

NORNS
Summer ends too early amid grim portents
A prophet of doom and delight speaks
A terrible crime from the past is vomited up by the Earth

Some of these are better than others. Help me make them all super juicy. Thanks!
