Hello, folks! This is the monthly reminder of upcoming conventions. See the Convention List on StoryCodex for lots more.

December was a dry month and I didn't post this in time for people to go to Dragonmeet (Dec 2, London, England). Sorry about that.

I know about four cons in January:

* Double Exposure, i.e. Dreamation (Jan 25-28, East Brunswick, NJ). We're talking about it here.
* Genericon (Jan 26-28, Troy, NY)
* Vericon (Jan 26-28, Cambridge, MA)
* Concepticon (Jan 31-Feb 4, Bournemouth, England)

Looks like the weekend of the 26th is gonna be a busy one, but I hope lots of people from the northeast can make it to Dreamation. I'm told that the Dreamation staff treats the indie crew like gods. I'll be there! Someone save some ambrosia for me.
