\n Vinny's filled 3 slots of my 4-player game at 8pm Saturday. For the 4th slot, I am hoping to find someone who:
1) digs this sort of game,
2) digs game design, and
3) will chat with me about it, either right after playing or (preferably) at the indie roundtable.

I am happy to return the favor.

Delve dreamation blurb:
Armed with new tools of augury and powers of perception, novice adventurers choose between dangerous puzzles to unravel. One village is plagued by an inexplicable drought and sightings of strange creatures. Another is missing some children, while village elders lose their sanity. A third seems normal on the surface, but mystic signs point to a hidden, evil power. Uncover supernatural secrets, gain the esteem and allegiance of the powerful, and help those who need it most. Challenges customized to player taste. Play focused on character POV.

More about Delve:
"Old school" or "trad style" game with a single GM (me). Intended for sandboxy campaign play.
Bits and pieces available for perusal at my website.

Thanks!
