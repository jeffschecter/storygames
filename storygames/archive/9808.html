\nNerdly Beach Party V: The Budget Strikes Back now has an official time and place.

Save the weekend of Sept 25-27 for the West Coast's premier indie gaming camp-con. State Park availability in September is still in doubt due to California state budget woes, so we're moving shop to Plaskett Creek Campground in Los Padres National Forest, 35 miles north of our usual stomping grounds at San Simeon.

Registration is $35 per person, and covers the campsite plus breakfast and lunch on Saturday and Sunday. Due to reduced campsite availability, there will be a hard cap of 24 spots available for NBPV. Registration will open in less than a week.
