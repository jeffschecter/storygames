\n I'm starting a game of Nero at the MK RPG club next week. It's pitched as the game of HBO's Rome series, complete with all the sex, violence, and intrigue. As the name suggests, it's set during the reign of the mad Emperor Nero. It'll also be completely mundane, with no superpowers or anything similar.

I've written up details of chargen on the Nero page, if anyone wants a look. It's got new Lifepaths and Distinctions, suitable for the game.

If you have suggestions on how to improve things, please dive in! In particular, some of the new Distinctions could do with improvement.

    \n The Nero game is now really catching fire. (We had a bit of a rocky start with PCs that their players didn't like, and a lack of interaction between them, but that seems to have resolved itself now.) Last night featured an epic fight between two large groups of legionaries. It started off as a complication and was quickly resolved as another complication. I'm glad to say that the skirmish didn't detract from the main event, a discussion between Lucidus and Julia, his impetuous 16 year old sister-in-law who fancies the pants off him and who just tried to kill Nero's latest wife. In a later scene, we discovered the need for a new Distinction, "***k like your life depends on it" as Drucilla convinced Nero that Vitus really was a Christian traitor.
    \n
    \nVitus and Vebis reconciled their differences (Vitus had previously disowned Vebuis) and then staged a coup against Nero. Things didn't quite go to plan, with Nero escaping, Lucidus (a Nero loyalist) captured by Vebius, and Vitus mostly kidnapped by Drucilla.

    \n I think the new Distinction needs a broader remit, so perhaps this:
    \nTake One for the Team
    \nSometimes you need to get your hands dirty; sometimes, you just need to get dirty. You're not above doing what has to be done, if it gets you what you want. Roll this Distinction's die when you carry out an unpleasant task as a means to an end.
    \n- d4: You can Give without a penalty when rolling Take One for the Team if you Choose to be publically exposed & humiliated by this task.
    \n- d8: Increase your opponent's Insecure or Angry stress pool against you and they are Stressed Out as if they had lost the roll by 5 or more.
    \n- d12: Spend a Plot Point to add all of your opponent's Stress dice to your pool when rolling Take One for the Team.
