Google Maps for gaming

Jason_Morningstar
December 2006 edited December 2006 in Story Games
Google Maps has a pretty open API and you can use custom tile sets - so there's no reason you couldn't make beautiful maps of your lil' fantasy realm complete with all the features (riding directions!) that make Google maps so cool. I've just started exploring the possibilities. I have some beautifully rendered period maps of Warsaw that I'm going to tile up when I get a chance.

Does anybody see any useful applications of quick and dirty GIS for gaming?
