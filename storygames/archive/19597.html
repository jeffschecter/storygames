\n Sooner or later, you're going to need more story! Generate NPCs, plot seeds, and more, all with one deck of randomizer cards. Kickstater.com has a nifty set of cards called "The GameMaster's Apprentice" by Nathan Rockwood.

The GameMaster\u2019s Apprentice is a deck of 60 double-sided cards. They started out as a way to quickly randomize names for NPCs, and then became a way to also select a bunch of random loot, and then a way to generate random encounters...and it just kept going. I love rolling on random tables, but I find the cards easier to manage, especially with so many different fields on them. And, because there are 120 unique card faces, if you use them to generate an NPC, drawing just three cards yields up to ~1.7 million different characters.

Here is a link to the Kickstarter, so you can see for yourself. It has several demo videos for you to view.
https://kickstarter.com/projects/376399785/the-gamemasters-apprentice
