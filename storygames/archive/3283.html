\n OK, so I'm preparing to play Dogs tomorrow, and thus took all my D12s and D20s out of my dice box to make room for more sixes, excepting one D20 for emergencies. Said emergency came up, when yesterday I went over to some folks' place to play D&D. I was gonna just take the dice as is, but then I thought: "You know, I always rotate through my whole stock of D20s trying to find the best 'luck,' I better take 'em all."

So I did. And I kicked ass. Rocked the knowledge check pre-combat so I could be all "Allips! Guard your minds, they cause madness!" And I wailed on the bitches, gambling on Power attack and WINNING, tearing up those fuckers even with the 50% incorporeal miss chance. Every 20 I own rolled me good numbers, usually in excess of 15. I was proud of all my babies.

It was glorious.

Peace,
-Joel
