\n I'm currently designing a game called Wash:

Genre: Expressionist Dark Fantasy; Epic Sword and Sorcery; Mythic Post-Apocalyptic.

Wash is a game about defending the last community that matters in a fatalistic and apocalyptic world. It's equal parts: bargaining with the supernatural, crisis management, community development, epic confrontation with a dark and stormy world, and risk assessment in a world of dwindling resources. It's meant to be intense, mythic sword and sorcery set in a time of change and destruction.

Hollywood Pitch: It's like Age of Polaris, set in the Warhammer world.
Alternate Hollywood Pitch: Dark Sun meets Don't Rest Your Head.

I'm going to be documenting the development of the game, as well as eventually providing the game playtest documents, here: http://washdevelopment.blogspot.com/
I'd be super excited if people bookmarked it and gave feedback to the posts.
