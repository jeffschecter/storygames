\n My creative process: let me show you it.

I'm enjoying keeping a blog record of the evolution of my current game design, the Pentasystem. I've never kept a journal of a creative project before, but I now see why people do; it gives you a lot of insight into your own process. I generally find that I need to make something to find out what it is, and now I can watch myself doing that.
