\n For the past few years around my birthday (June 29), I have hosted AdamCon, a gaming bash at my house. This year, I'm planning AdamCon for two days -- Saturday, June 28 and Sunday, June 29. Anyone who isn't a complete psycho can come. Yes, you (unless you're a complete psycho)... you are invited. Even if we have never met. All you have to do is let me know you want to attend and I'll give you my address and directions to my house, which you could probably get off the net anyway.

Everyone is expected to find something to play, even if it's a card game or something. This lessens the hosting burden on my wonderful wife, who doesn't really game with us.


Location

This is a house con. I have many areas suitable for gaming and can probably manage three or four games at a time. There's even space outside on the patio, and I might set up a small pavilion tent to block the direct sun.

Oh, yeah. I live in Owings Mills, Maryland. That's northwest off the Baltimore Beltway (695). Driving time from various locations:
    \n
  • Baltimore city -- 20 minutes
  • Maryland-Delaware border -- 60 minutes
  • Philadelphia -- 90 minutes
  • NYC -- 3-4 hours
  • Laurel, MD -- 45 minutes
  • Bethesda, MD -- 1 hour
  • Annapolis, MD -- 1 hour
  • Washington, DC -- 75 minutes
  • Alexandria, VA -- 90 minutes
  • Northern VA (Chantilly, etc.) -- 90 minutes
  • Ohio -- 6-7 hours
  • Raleigh-Durham -- 6-7 hours
\nDistances do not include bad rush hour traffic. I-95 and 695 can suck in rush hour.


Games!

In the past, we've had loosely scheduled "slots" for games. They generally run 4-6 hours and start at noon, 4 PM, and 8 PM. Things tend to run late, so don't set your watch by the schedule. There will be three slots on Saturday, and two on Sunday. We'll start kicking people out around 10 PM Sunday, unless people need to crash overnight till Monday morning (please pre-arrange with me).

We need GMs! Please let me know what you want to play and if you can run something.

Games I am pretty sure I will run: D&D 4E, Verge
Games I am able to run: Dogs in the Vineyard, My Life with Master, The Shab-al-Hiri Roach, Polaris, Shock:, carry
Games other people may run: Misspent Youth, Trail of Cthulhu

We play role-playing games, beer & pretzel games like Munchkin, board and strategy games like Carcassonne, card games like Magic, and occasionally Wii or PS2 games.


Room and Board

We have a guest room with a double bed, plus two double-sized futons in the (finished) basement area, and two regular couches. We have lots and lots of floor space. People are welcome to stay Saturday night. Some folks are coming in Friday night for religious reasons or traffic reasons; just let me know if you want to arrive early.

We'll have some food, but please do bring something to share. We might just take a collection and order pizza or Chinese, too.
