\nVIP Attendee Tickets are now available at RPG Now (and DTRPG and EN World).

To refresh: Friday night from 7:00 to 11:00 I'm running a special Dawning Star game at the Alcatraz Brewing Company. Eight players -- including our VIP Guest-of-Honor JD Wiker -- will suffer the devious schemings of the vaasi at the hands of DS Designer and GM extraordinaire Lee Hammock. Attendees will get free food and drink, a limited-edition publication, a limited-edition hat, and will get to run enemy combatants including the vaasi.

All the proceeds of the event will go to the Shriners Hospitals for Children. OBS has generously agreed to waive their comission on the sale of the tickets. Between the player and attendee tickets, expected on-site donations, and pledges to date, I'm now shooting for a total donation of $2,000!!!

So come down from your indie "ivory tower" and "slum" with a kick-ass d20 game for one night. ;-)

Only 8 tickets left and counting....
