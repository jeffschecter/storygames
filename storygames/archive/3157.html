\n In the wake of some threads lamenting the demise of forward momentum at a once popular design/theory forum, I realize that I have unfairly criticized others for not taking any action to provide an alternative, while I myself have stood largely still. Inspired partly by the open atmosphere here at Story Games, I've decided to open my own site up to aspiring game designers/theorists (let's face it, I wasn't using it very much at the present time, anyhow).

Here are the forums. I've finally managed to nail down a decent, ad-free, forum host and upgraded the main site with a snazzy CSS frontend here. While I'm certain that it won't be The Next Big Thing, all game theorists and game designers are welcome in the spirit of open discussion. I'll host files on the main site's "Free Games" page and designer/imprint-specific forums by request.

Suggestions for improvement by registered posters are, of course, welcome. Before you ask, Andy, no -- my primary server doesn't support Vanilla

The rules (see "Welcome" threads in each forum) are loose and sensible. If you act like a human being, you'll be spared banning. If you act like a sociopath, you'll be treated like one. If you like to design/write games (even if they're just for you and your play group), by all means, drop by!
