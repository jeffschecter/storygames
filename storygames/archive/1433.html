  • Mike_Holmes
    September 2006
    What I find ironic is that everyone seems to forget that indie is to do with not being caught up in commercialism. That is, not debasing your game based on what somebody else in the business thinks will make more money. Basically the more independent you are, or so goes the argument, the more likely you are to have a product with integrity.

    Worse, while I see a lot of people say, "I prefer Indie games," I've never seen one say, "Indie games are all superior to non-indie games." So while I think indie, yes, is to do with quality, it's a fallacy to dismiss this because of some notion that somebody might mean it as intrinsically superior. Nobody does that I know of.

    Mike
