\n Since Chris H. is much too shy, I wanted to pimp the upcoming RPG Minicon at EndGame in Oakland on Saturday, April 7, 2007. This will be my fourth minicon and second one running a game. They do a really nice job with these quarterly minicons and they are a really nice alternative to planning for an entire extended weekend. Typically they will schedule slots at 10am, 3pm and 8pm. A few games you might have heard of are:

Dogs in the Vineyard
Spirit of the Century (x2)
The Princes' Kingdom
Mortal Coil
Esoterrorists
Primitive
Best Friends
Carry
Prime Time Adventures
The Shab-al-Hiri Roach

So thanks to the whole EndGame crew for all the indie love!! If you are near the SF Bay Area, by all means check it out.

http://www.endgameoakland.com/minicon/
