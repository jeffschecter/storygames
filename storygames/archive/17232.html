\n\nSeptember 2012 \n edited September 2012 in Story Games
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n You may know that Matthijs Holter's roleplaying game Archipelago is among my favorites. I like it so much I grabbed the second edition manuscript and laid it out all pretty, with custom cards, which prompted Matthijs to go back and tweak his rules a little, and all of a sudden we had a very accessible new version of the game! I then solicited bits of advice from veteran Archipelago players to include, and we're very pleased with the result. Enjoy!

If you are unfamiliar with Archipelago, it is a simple, elegant structured freeform game that is extremely versatile and full of surprises.
