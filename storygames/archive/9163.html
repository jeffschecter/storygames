\n Hi!

I'm doing an mms street art project in Oslo. The images are published on a Creative Commons-licence at Flickr. I work as a journalist in a Norwegian Online, but this is a more or less private project.

If you'd like to look at some of my stuff, you can see the tagged images here: http://www.flickr.com/photos/77272877@N00/sets/72157616858288626/

Here's a feed for stuff I'm just finding around and haven't bothered sorting: http://www.flickr.com/photos/77272877@N00/

Here's an explanatory article (alas in the Norwegian, but you might be able to use Babelfish at Altavista or some Google translation service): http://www.abcnyheter.no/node/87161

A big inspiration for my project is obviously the great science fiction/thriller author William Gibson, but there are also others whom you may, or may not, have heard about.

This invitation goes out to a very select group of forums and individuals. If you believe that words like "disco tantra" have any kind of meaning beyond the metaphorical level, It may or may not be a good idea for you to peruse this stuff. But it's obviously up to you.

I would strongly encourage every potential street artist to consider local rules and regulations, and preferably play by them.
