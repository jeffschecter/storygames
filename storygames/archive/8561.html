\n One of my favorite podcasts is called the Mediocre Show. It's a weekly, call-in style show broadcast out of my adoptive home town of Philadelphia that covers all kinds of crazy topics, from current events, to delicious beers, to how much one of the hosts hates manatees. Think of a morning radio show, then get rid of the annoying "zoo crew" canned joke crap and you get the Mediocre Show.

I'm a very irregular caller on the show, but on the most recent episode I called in and talked with the guys for a couple of minutes about my upcoming trip to Dreamation, my game, and Jason Morningstar's Grey Ranks. One of the hosts is a huge history buff, so I figured that might appeal to him more than a little bit.

If you want to check it out you can find the show here, or just search for mediocre show on iTunes. I come in at about the one hour, ten minute mark. If you listen, you'll also get to hear a bit about my home town and how weird it is.
