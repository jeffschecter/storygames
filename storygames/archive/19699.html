    \n I don't know of any existing system that handles the specifically.
    But if you were to make one, I think a key structural ingredient would be the tension between getting all the resources you need (backing from various parts of the community, weapons, communications, foreign support, etc.) against the need to avoid being discovered and stopped by the current regime. Procuring resources would make you more likely to succeed but have an attendant cost in time and exposure.

    As a side note, this thread has made me think about making a game that recreates the post-Cultural Revolution narratives of Chinese scar literature. Something along the lines of My Life with Master , probably.

    (I re-read the eponymous "Scar" yesterday - depressing, though perhaps not in the way the author intended.)
