\n Camp Nerdly is a weekend long d.i.y. gaming event where we inhabit Prince William Forest Park, cook and eat meals together,

We've only got room for about a hundred people, but there's a month left to register.

We make it a point to:
- be welcoming for families, parents, children and all
- get together to make the camp work, through chores, eating together, and pitching games together
- try and engage the space, through a mix of outdoor activities or larps, sure, but mostly by turning our phones off (since they don't get reception anyhow)
- try an build a community together, with a mix of familiar and new people each year

More information here:
campnerdly.org

with a bunch of links to social media and such on the bottom right of the page.

You can apply here if interested:
Application

Special priority will be given to underrepresented minorities, some priority for new campers in general. Applications are not first-come first-served, but you've only got until the end of March to apply.

We'll do our best to get everyone notified who's in, and who's on the Wait List within a week (so, by April 7).
