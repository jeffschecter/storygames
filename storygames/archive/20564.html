    Escalation comes in two forms though neither offers the mechanical impetus of timed threats.

    The first is the accumulation of Danger tokens. The hero takes a token for every Miss and the Scribe gets to make a \u2018soft move.\u2019 These moves have no real mechanical penalty and are purely descriptive in nature, but the growing stack of Danger tokens provide a very tangible reminder that the hero may fail

    The second is when the hero concedes (player invoked fail) or is Defeated (scribe invoked fail). Both of these are triggered by amassing Danger tokens. So in effect the accumulation of tokens (and the narrative moves made by the Scribe) acts as a rudimentary escalation mechanic.
