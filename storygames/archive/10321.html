    \nZendo is designed to be played with Icehouse pyramids, but you can also play it with any objects which have more than a couple of attributes: playing cards, different-sized Legos, even matchboxes (my go-to choice for playing at the pub). It gets tricky the drunker you get, but lawyers seem to love the "figure out the rule" element. Be careful not to make rules that are too complex--if you need more than a couple of adjective, or if you use a conjunction, it's probably too hard.

    Nomic might be too much like their day jobs, but it's very interesting. The possible drawback being that it takes rather a long time to play (some games have gone on for years, but that's at email/BBS pacing).

    Everything else folks listed above is good, too--these just occurred to me because of the lawyerly element and because they cost basically nothing (assuming you own a deck of cards for Zendo or index cards/paper for Nomic).
