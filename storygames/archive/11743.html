\n Dear Story Games, I have a mission for you.

I'll preface that mission with some context. I'm trying to pull together a Go Play style convention in Vancouver, BC (in mid to late Fall). The title is tentatively Go Play Van. There's some great logo opportunities there - riffing off the Go Play NW sasquatch, I pictured a couple sasquatch smoking outside an old VW-style van.

One possible venue has a single weekend that might work, but there's a burlesque troupe that has the Saturday night booked. They said they're potentially interested in sharing that weekend with us, and merging a gaming-themed burlesque show into our con. Which is an idea with some legs. Zany, but it could be pretty awesome. The burlesque group is named the Screaming Chickens.

So, my inquiry:\n
    \n
  • instead of sasquatches, maybe we could put a different monster smoking outside a van. Ideas?
    • \n
  • and if we wanted to riff off the "screaming chickens", any chicken-inspired monsters out there? I know of the cockatrice. Are there others?
    • \n
  • maybe this "smoking by an old van" logo idea is played out. Any other ideas? Strip-teasing owlbears?
