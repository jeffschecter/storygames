    Looks like it's pretty much just a basic installation of something called Kodemall. There's plenty of not-necessarily RPG-themed hosted e-commerce sites out there, with variable pricing schemes.

    \n

    One I used at one point to sell T-shirts is called BigCartel; the developers have a companion site called Pulley for automatic digital fulfillment. I haven't used Pulley, but BigCartel is nice and easy to use and can obviously be used to sell physical and digital goods alike.

    \n

    There's also the very robust Shopify.

    \n

    I'm not marketing expert, but as a shopper I much prefer to buy indie-type goods from a modern storefront that the creator obviously controls.

    \n

    edit: in other words, if someone's taking a 20% cut of your sales in exchange for running a white-label Etsy clone\u2026you'd do better sticking with Paypal buttons.
