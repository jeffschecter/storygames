\n The first issue of the Duty & Honour Almanac is now available for download from the D&H; site - http://www.omnihedron.co.uk/dutyandhonour
\n
\nThe Almanac is a free .pdf periodical that I will be producing to provide ongoing support for the game. The aim is to provide practical materials and tips about the game as well as new rule options and historical reference.
\n
\nThe first issue includes 'The Enemy Within' - four fully realised allied NPCs for use in any campaign, along with guidance on how to get the most out of their dastardly natures - and 'London Gazette Extraordinary' - a piece on using online newspaper archives as inspiration and reference in your games.
\n
\nhttp://www.omnihedron.co.uk/dutyandhonour/downloads/almanac-1.pdf
\n
\nAlso added to the site are a set of editable .pdf character, mission and regiment sheets, thanks to Shane McClean.
