\n When reading the blog for Do: Pilgrims of the Flying Temple I came across Daniel Solis' idea for a Pilgrim Generator. It's an idea that really interested me, I guess because it - like Oracles in IAWA - provides enough details to prod the story along without dictating things to the players.
\n
\nSo when I discovered Ghost/Echo a couple of days ago, I had the idea to write up a generator like Daniel's for that game. I've done the first of about four sections - this one is about your character. You can view the work in progress on Photobucket. I tried to follow the format of Daniel's generator and John's product.
\n
\nI'm interested in your feedback about my generator, but more broadly about whether generators in general are a good idea for games. I think in one-off games they could be a nice compromise between time-consuming character generation and potentially restrictive pre-gens.
