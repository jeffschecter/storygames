    \n Essentially, it's a story game in its shortest form: minimal rules, playable in 5-30 minutes. Some of them are little more than parlour games or improv exercises; others are micro-versions of larger games. I've put rather a lot of them on my blog, but there are plenty of other sources for them too... which I can't seem to find right now, but I'm sure someone will leap in with the big list shortly. :-D
