\n The HELLAS: Wine Dark Void kickstarter is now live!

Please stop by and have a look and see what the book has to offer.


HELLAS is an epic space opera in the style of ancient Greek culture and legend. Every player character is a Hero in a story that you will help create, with the power to influence the settings and elements of the game. HELLAS spans multiple generations, allowing for the continuation of the epic story even after the death of a Hero, in the style of the epic poets of old.

The 128+ page book includes:
Full color illustrations of 40+ detailed ships.
Ship modification and construction rules.
Slipspace phenomena. An in-depth look into the wine dark void known as the Panthalassa; the cosmic all sea
Slipspace encounters. A detailed look at the strange denizens that ply the slispspace trade routes preying on the weak
A detailed look at the planet Korinthos. A mega city sprawl that covers the entire planet.
New Metousia rules. A clear look at the players ability to change the world around them.
Planetary Consumer/Trade scores for all the known worlds.
A detailed look at the goddess Aionisia and her temple moon.
Vehicle space combat and chase rules.
