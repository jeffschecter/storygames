I have this blog, the nicely-named 10by10room. I'm quite fond of it, and I'm looking for some more co-bloggers.

10 by 10 room

It tries to be a tumblelog (something of a random linkblog with minimal and sometimes absurd commentary) about gaming in general and storygames in particular. At its best, it's not about gaming per se, and not even about linking to cool new gaming things.

Rather, it should link to cool things that, in some roundabout fashion, relate to gaming / gamers / whatever. Ideally, I'm mostly linking to things outside the normal game blogs and fora. So, links to non-gaming sites/blogs/fora, to clever online videos, random AIM and IRC quotes, and like whatever. Particularly, I'm looking for folks who pay attention to cool things outside of normal gaming, and are willing to stitch them into this gaming blog.

This can be a low-key blogging thing - you don't need more than a link and a pithy title, really - but I'd really love some more co-conspirators, since I've apparently been a little slow on the bloggering lately.

Let me know if you're interested!
