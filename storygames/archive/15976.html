\n Okay, so my Dreamation experience wasn't as great as it could have been, due to circumstances beyond my control, but that time has passed. On to the future! And what's next on the horizon? Dexcon 15, scheduled for July 4-8.

I understand that Dexcon is different from Dreamation somehow, though I have no clear indication of what those differences are. Looking at last year's site it seems to be a gaming convention with war games and board games and roleplaying games, which seems to be exactly the same. It's one day longer, I guess?

Anyway, I wondered who's planning on going and what they plan on doing when they get there. I'm also trying to decide if I should make the trip or just wait until next year's Dreamation.

If I do go, this time will not be like last time. That much I know for sure.

    \n Dexcon traditionally was the lynchpin convention and Dreamation was the satellite convention. Dreamation used to be a big early-in-the-year launching pad for new indie games (less so the past few years.) There is some difference between the two. Dexcon is definitely more robust on the non-indie side. However you would recognize about 70-80% of the same indie rpg folk. some like it better/not so much.

    Considering your positive reaction from a tiny slice of Dreamation; i would whole heartedly endorse you trying Dexcon out.

    ps. Vinnie sent out an announcement that he's going to reinvest into Dexcon this year so there will likely be some changes and ... ? anyway. check it out!
