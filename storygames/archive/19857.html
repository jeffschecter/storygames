\n This is my latest game, and is something I've been working on for 4-5 months now. It's a little experiment into fluctuating and changing characters. I wanted to create a game where characters are incredibly flexible, and can switch playstyles and focuses mid-combat.

E.G. the Archer could drop his bow, draw some knives, and go to town without needing to multi-class into knife-dude.

Also, I've written a video-game like tutorial where mechanics are slowly added in over the course of a fight until players have a better grasp of the core mechanics. I've never seen that done before, and am curious as to how well it will work

Here's what I've got. It's still pretty rough, and the biggest problems are the Classes, which need a LOT of work.

But I finally have something I'm proud to present, and am eager to refine. So critique away! Am I completely off base here?
