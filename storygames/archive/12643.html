\nEpisode 31 Lady Blackbird: Still Flying
\n
\nThis week we discuss Lady Blackbird from One Seven Design Studios. Lady Blackbird is science fiction fantasy game, sort of a cross between Firefly and Final Fantasy. We talk about the game's somewhat unique nature, the role keys play during play, and how the GM constructs the scenario from the framework provided.
