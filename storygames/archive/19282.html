    \n Interesting concept, Paul. I playtested it solo this morning. I think the dice mechanics might make it too easy to win unless the PM allows only very small actions, so it all seems to be about how just large an action the PM will allow. Perhaps I need to see some more examples of what counts as an "action".

    About the dice mechanics: You say "Highest roll is the only one that counts." If I'm reading you correctly, this means it's to my advantage to always roll all three dice because it will only count as a 1 if I roll triple 1's. The chance of rolling triple 1's is a little less than 0.5%. If I can get another obake to donate a die to the roll, the chance of losing a die drops to less than 0.1%.

    About the narrative mechanics: If I roll a 6 the rules say I can specify three actions instead of just one. Are these actions all pre-specified? IOW, should I say what all three actions might be before I make the dice roll, or do I just say one of them, and then come up with the other two after the roll?
