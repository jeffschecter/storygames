    \n Nick, what has happened at GoDs past is this:

    There's a friendly host who facilitates for a certain amount of time. Ideally this person isn't involved in games, but helps match people up and put sessions together. This might be "Who can run some Dogs for these people from Kentucky?" or "You guys like sci-fi? Let me find you somebody to show you 3:16"

    There's one or more guys who have a bag full of games they can demo or run short sessions of on a moment's notice. These people run games based on their expertise and enthusiasm, tempered by people's interests. "I can run the following games and I am shameless about it..."

    There are other guys who come in with a specific game in mind, and the facilitator tries to make a love connection. "That lady over there is running Inspectres in fifteen minutes, how does that grab you fools? Why am I asking? Go sit down."

    So it's a little loose, a little structured. That's my experience, although I'm not the boss of this at Origins. But I imagine regardless of the setup you could be any of those guys, Nick.
