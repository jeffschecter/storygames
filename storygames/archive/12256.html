Sign up for Gameday 26 over here on ENWorld

ENWorld Chicago Gameday 26 is a day of FREE gaming held at earth's finest game store, Games Plus in Mt. Prospect, IL.

Gameday 26 is June 12th, starting at 9:30am.

To participate, simply post to this thread with your intention to play in one of the following events. Event sign-up is first-come, first-serve. Be aware that the event schedule may be subject to change until the last week before Gameday.

Our current schedule:

Slot 1: Morning events from 9:30am to 2:30pm
1. Kingmaker
2. FUDGE, "Ghost Facers! - Miskatonic Valley"
3. D&D 4e, "Secret Of The Slavers Stockade: The Hill Fort"
4. Cinematic Unisystem, "Mutant X: Use The Rod, Save The Child"
5. Star Wars Saga Edition, "Things can only get better, right?"
6. Call of Cthulhu/Dogs in the Vineyard, "Delta Green in the Vineyard"

Meal break from 2:30pm to 3:30pm.

Slot 2: Afternoon events from 3:30pm to 8:30pm (or later)
1. Supernatural RPG, "Black Night, White Salt, Silver Moon"
2. D&D 3.5, "Revenge is a Dish Best Served Hot! (with a side of pineapple!)"
3. BattleMasters
4. Paranoia XP, "The Yellow Clearance Black Box Blues"
5. The Shadow of Yesterday, "Khas Fara: Village of Fear"
6. Danger Patrol, "The Maniacal Moonnapping"
