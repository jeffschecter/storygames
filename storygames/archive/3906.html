\n I sometimes review stuff for RPG.net. I haven't done so for a while, but I'd like to write a few reviews again.

Have you written/published a game you want me to review? My reviews are based on a thorough reading, and sometimes a playtest. I only review books I receive in print - no .pdfs. I point out positive and negative aspects of the game; some reviews have been negative, some positive.

Here's what I've posted on rpg.net so far (do a review search on my name, Matthijs Holter, for links):

Capes (Muse of Fire Studio)
Conspiracy of Shadows (Revised Edition) (Bob Goat Press)
Dawning Star: Operation Quick Launch (Blue Devil Games)
Dissecting LARP (Knutepunkt)
Dogs in the Vineyard (a lumpley game)
Faery's Tale (Firefly Games)
Fastlane (Twisted Confessions)
Pax Draconis (Technicraft)
Sex and Sorcery (Adept Press)
Universalis (Ramshead Publishing)

If you're interested, whisper to me in this thread.
