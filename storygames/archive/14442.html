    \n Inspired by this thread, I've printed out a pile of Cthulhu Dark, Ghost Echo, and Lady Blackbird. We will be giving them away on Free RPG day, along with the official free stuff and the random stuff I've added to the mix. I discovered that out printer could do a cool automated double sided printing thing where it flipped the paper over, and all I had to do to make little booklets from Lady Blackbird was fold them. I tried stapling them, but I got one in the finger and there was blood. LB should be one of the games we have running in the shop on the day.

    I'd would be very happy to have random people add random stuff to be given away, but I'd have to see it in advance... not to judge it, just to make sure that I knew what I was giving to people and that it was somewhere around the Certificate PG zone.

    So, I've given Free RPG day free blood, and free sweat... but no tears... yet. They will arrive if I don't get a chance to play anything, or if people that never shop with us turn up at 9:00am just to claim free stuff and then leave.
