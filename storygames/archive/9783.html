    \n Practical game design is fun. Luke and Jared ran that panel for the first time at Origins. In the first half of the panel, they had the crowd throw out a game concept and then they developed a game right then and there, walking through their thought processes. In the second half, they turned the tables and gave the crowd a game concept and had them develop it.

    I think it was really eye opening for a number of the people there.

    With Paul up there too, it should be even more interesting!
