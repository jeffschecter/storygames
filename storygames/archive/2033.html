    If your network connection is real slow, the text of the page will load before the stylesheet completes. It's almost always a product of the computer network (or host computer) that you are using. Every time it has happened to me (save once, when there was some genuine forum/server issues a few months back) it's been because the network between me and S-G was slow. Usually it indicates to me that it's a good time to reboot my home router, as the memory/ip tables get full regularly (it's kind of a crappy router, manufactured before the days of glorious Peer to Peer sharing considerations).

    Hope that helps!
