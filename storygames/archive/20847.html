    \n It really doesn't matter, does it? Since you have to gather them anew for a subsequent roll, you can do whatever you like.

    Dice you roll based on Traits, Tags, and so on are one "type". Let's call them Trait dice.

    Dice from your personal pool are another "type". Pool dice.

    When I play Blackbird, I like to have two different colours of dice (or types!**) for this very reason.

    The dice you gather for your Trait (+one by default) are temporary - you're just rolling them now, and you don't keep track of them.

    Pool dice, though, those need to be counted and tracked. You give them away or keep them depending on pass/fail.


    ** You'll note that since LB's dice are binary, you don't HAVE to use d6s. You could use any kind of die, or even playing cards. So, if you have lots of dice, you could use d6s for Trait dice, but d10s for Pool dice. Or use dice for your Pool, but draw cards for your Trait dice.
