\n I just released a sourcebook for Dread. Specifically, it's a collection of canned adventures (thirteen, plus a half-dozen adventure hooks). I have to say, I was really happy with the artwork this time around. If there's one thing that I regret about Dread, it's that I included some artwork that I wasn't 100% enthusiastic about. If I ever do a third edition (unlikely, considering how many projects I'm working on right now), I'll definitely make sure that every single page looks exactly like I want it to look. But anyhow.

Pent was a lot of fun. I learned a lot about layout while working on it, and playtesting all of the scenarios was a hoot. Even if it did take about five years.

Here's an Exhumilat demon, by Jason McLellan:



And this is a Remarec demon, courtesy of Earl Geier (who worked on CoC and Whispering Vault):
