    \n Just to manage expectations:

    On August 26, 2008, biologists at Prince William Forest Park, a unit of the National Park Service located in Triangle ,VA, instituted a temporary restriction on bringing outside firewood into the park for camping. Firewood not collected in the park may contain non- native insects such as Emerald Ash Borer and Asian Longhorned Beetle which carry with them diseases that threaten park resources and the health of our forests. Wood that is dead and downed, and less than 6 inches in diameter can be collected inside designated park areas for use by individuals camping in the park.
