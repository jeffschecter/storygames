This morning, as I was heading off to work I noticed a piece paper on my desk. "VAMPIRE JETS" was written on it in brown marker in my 4 year old son's unmistakable monospaced printing.

"Are you making a story called Vampire Jets?" I asked him.

"No, that's the game I'm making for you." He said.

"That's a great name." I said.

He responded, "I should have called it Battle Bats, huh?"

(Kids are awesome)
