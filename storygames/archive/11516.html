Julia Ellingboe and I are going to run a new larp hybrid we wrote, at Dreamation. It is called What to do about Tam Lin? It is inspired by the Child Ballads, a collection of folk ballads from the British Isles, which are chock full of murder, betrayal, incest and crossings between the Fae and Mortal worlds.

Tam Lin Preview

Child Ballads

The songs are so full of crimes of one against another, we decided to let these characters take each other to court! The game takes place at the Court of the Faery Queen, placed at the crossroads of Carterhaugh, which is where the Tithe to Hell is to be paid in the ballad of Tam Lin. Tam Lin and his human lover will try to save him from this dire fate, in and amongst the cases of characters from other well-known ballads such as the Great Selkie and Barbara Allen prosecute eachother and defend themselves from the great wrongs that happen in these songs.

It has been are brand new experience writing a larp. So much character background to be created, and creating such a specific world and setting. We did steal some techniques from the jeep tool box, using flashbacks as the testimony from witnesses in the court cases themselves. For mechanics, we took a page from some larps we've played (including the Everway larp I was in that Kat and Michelle ran at a Dreamation long ago and made them simple(!) to resolve. Drawing cards from a deck made up of your attributes, with two or more "Success" cards mixed in there that if pulled, allow the action to succeed.

We're looking forward to getting to play with folks! If you have any questions about the game, please post here or email us. And if anyone who is experienced with running larps has any general tips for us to keep in mind, we'd be grateful to receive them!
