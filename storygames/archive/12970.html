\n I thought Google Scribe deserved a bigger conversation than the one in Stuff To Watch.

I'm fascinated. It's a wonderful tool for helping you avoid cliche. Write something, then see whether Google predicts the word you're thinking of. If it does, you're probably writing cliches.

Also, as Colin suggests, you can simply give it seed words, then see what it generates. It's a beautiful thing.

The horror is released from the hospital, where doctors were unable to detect any abnormalities in the fetus. Shortly thereafter, millions of people worldwide suffer from some type of physical damage.
