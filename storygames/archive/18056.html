\n I've had a short-form hack of World of Dungeons sort of half-finished, floating about my head as brain crack for a while now. It's driving me nuts having it sitting on my computer, but I have too many other priorities right now to polish it or give it any sort of functional graphic design, so I figure it's best that I just cast it to the winds and see what happens.

World of Superspies is a superspy-themed hack of World of Dungeons. Take control of one of three agent classes -- Soldier, Operative, or Tech -- and stop the madmen of the world from releasing their latest superweapon on the world. If you decide to give this a whirl, or re-hack it, or whatever, let me know what you think!

    Semi-related: I've never run a spy game; if I did my main concern would be that I wouldn't be able to improv the setting details of a typical globe-trotting action-spy film. If anyone has any tricks or resources that might help (besides going to the library and researching Turkey, etc.) I'd love to hear about them. Maybe some spy RPG at some point featured a chapter of half-page flavor descriptions of various countries? Or maybe my problems would be solved by a used copy of Rick Steve's Guide to Europe...
