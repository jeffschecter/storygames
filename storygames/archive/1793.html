Help me come up with some ideas for Dreamation

Andrew Morris
November 2006 edited November 2006 in Meetups / Conventions
I'm going to be running three or four sessions of Unistat at Dreamation, but I haven't figured out what they'll be yet. I'm tempted to run the One Wing Dipped in Blood scenario again, because it was so awesome last time, but part of me doesn't want to "cheapen" what was an amazing gaming session.

Also, I want to highlight the flexibility of the system. Last convention, I ran the One Wing scenario along with a Firefly scenario. I probably want to do at least one session in a popular IP, because that's one of the strengths of Unistat -- you can just pick an IP and go, and you'll likely get something with a similar feel. I also want to do a crazy, out-there, over-the-top, world-changing power level one.

But I'm really at a loss for what to do, exactly. I'm sure I'll come up with some ideas, but I'm asking for SG-ers help here to feed my creativity. So here's what I'd like:

1) An IP that has mass gamer appeal, preferably one that doesn't have a licensed game.

2) A crazy-cool, wild concept. We're talking angels, gods, world-devourers, etc. here.

3) A scenario so bizarre no game system can handle it. Because Unistat can. Underpants gnomes trying to make a profit? No problem. Warring memes attempting to control the human thoughtspace? Easy. The Obsidian setting done as a comedy? Did it a few months ago. Seriously, try and stump me -- I dare you.
