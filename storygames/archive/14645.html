\n Hi all,

Okay, I am putting the last minute touches on my Alpha Playtest for DexCon and could use more help. I need quotes from Lovecraft and Lovecraftian sources, namely fiction before games and movies but I'll eventually use it all. I need quotes that are evocative of the situations characters are put in. The themes, storylines and situations that I am using are listed below. Please grab quotes and post them here and your name will appear in lights - okay, not lights but print The quotes should be creepy (not humorous at all) and give the players the proper feel for the situation. These are pointedly vague so as long as it fits the theme, storyline and situation then it's fair game.

To list your quote simply reply with:

Theme
Storyline
Situation
Quote
Citation

For example:

Inevitability
History Revealed
Beyond the door lies truth and madness
"The horrible conclusion which had been gradually obtruding itself upon my confused and reluctant mind was now an awful certainty."
- The Beast in the Cave, HP Lovecraft

Thank you for your time and effort in advance,
- Don

    \n Steve's right. Trawling for quotes is work.

    Start with the Complete Works of Lovecraft on one page, which is much easier to search.

    Then, here are some pointers. Secret Societies: The Call of Cthulhu (search for "Castro" and "Mountains") and The Shadow Over Innsmouth ("Esoteric Order of Dagon"). Knowledge is Power: The Dreams In The Witch House. Harboring Monsters: The Dunwich Horror. Inevitability: The Colour out of Space. History Revealed: The Shadow Out Of Time. Forbidden Experiments: Herbert West, Reanimator. Dead God Lies Sleeping: Dagon/The Call of Cthulhu. Beyond the Ken: The Shadow Out Of Time.
