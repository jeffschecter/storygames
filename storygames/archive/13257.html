    \nGregor and Jamey and Jeff were first. This whole thing is for a friend who's in grad school and can't game with us, so he's in as well. That makes 4 players. I think that's all I want to handle, time-wise. If someone drops, then someone else will be added. There will also be posting requirements, which we'll figure out later. It's won't be three times a day or anything, though.
    \n
    \nI've thought about an AW West Marches, but I'd do think in the RL, if I was going to do it.
    \n
    \nWe've got a local board that's all set for PbP, complete with die rollers and all that. I've put off setting up a forum PbP after Google murdered Wave, because I've been hesitant about how it would work. Some of the built in "When"s for some moves are broken by the PbP format and will have to be rewritten. Any help with that would be appreciated. I'm specifically talking about "end of session", etc. Moves that are triggered by events at the table, rather than by events in the fiction.
    \n
    \nAnyway, here's the board, for now: Apocalyptic PbP
    \n
    \nThe board is local and is slowly undergoing a lot of work in moving towards a reboot. But I own it, so it's not going anywhere. Feel free to register for a user name. Send me an email at theparableengine at gmail when you do. I get a boatload of spambots every day and I don't want to reject anyone real.
