You played it till your thumbs blistered, now write it up RPG style.

It occurs to me that many people today who browse the 1KM1KT website have only played computer games and have no (or very little) experience with tabletop games.

This http://www.1km1kt.net/24hourrpg/ 24 Hour RPG contest is about introducing our computer gaming audience to the world of tabletop gaming!

Adapt a computer game into a tabletop RPG in 24 Hours! Any computer game will do: Doom3, PacMan, Zaxxon or World of Warcraft (for the uninspired).

The goal is to breech the gap between hardcore computer gamers and the awesomeness that is tabletop role-playing. Read that: "Try and make your content accessible to new gamers."

The submissions will be judged by the folks over at 1KM1KT.net and the winner will have their game heralded in the 1km1kt official newsletter, bumped to the front page, and they will receive a fabulous 1KM1KT fun pack! The fun pack consists of an official 1KM1KT T-shirt and T-shirt packing material!

The Rules:

1) Games must be based on PC or console style video games.
2) Games must be completed within a continuous 24 hour period.
3) Games must be submitted by September 20th, 11:59pm CST.
4) 1KM1KT staff will judge all entries.
5) Scoring will be based on originality, adherence to the theme, coolness, layout, playability and technical stuff like spelling and grammar.

Authors love it when you read their stuff. If you read it, please let them know what you think: http://www.1km1kt.net/community/forumdisplay.php?f=19

Good luck!!

    Forbidden Forest - The RPG (created in 300 seconds starting... now!)

    You're a guy with a bow. In a creepy forest. And giant spiders are charging you! Not to mention the huge snake that gave me nightmares. Can you make it through the... Forbidden Forest?

    Character:
    You don't need a sheet. You're an archer guy. You've only got a bow and an arrow. And you're damn good with it.

    Preparation:
    Play at night. Turn down the lights. Candles only. Creepy unsettling music. The player gets fifteen six-sided dice. The GM gets four six-sided, two ten-sided and one twenty-sided dice.

    Gameplay:
    GM and player look at each other through the dim candle light, sitting six feet apart, while the music is playing. The player describes how he's walking around the forest. Out of nowhere during the player's narration, the GM yells what kind of creature comes running and throws forward an appropriate die (and by yelling, I mean YELLING). The player has to throw down any number of six-sided dice before the GM's die stops rolling. If the player is too slow, the character dies. If the sum of the player's dice beats the GM's die, the character survives. If not, again, the character dies. Used dice are discarded immediately. The game ends when all the GM's dice are used up. Then you get to trade off.

    Monsters:
    SPIDER! (D6)
    SNAKE! (D10)
    DEMON! (D20)
