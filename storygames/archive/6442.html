\n Hi folks,

We'll have our next Jiffyness in Greenfield in June. We want to see you! Who can come?

JiffyCon.com

We've got the space for Saturday June 21 (the solstice, as it happens) and also for part of the day on Sunday the 22nd. We'll have scheduled games on Saturday and then playtesting/playstorming on Sunday. Playstorming is a mix of playtesting and brainstorming--coined by the pioneers over at the Imagination Sweat Shop. Take a game idea you've got and help give it form by developing it in play. It's design in motion. Got one you'd like to work on?

best,
Em
