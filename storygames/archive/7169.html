\n I know that the current buzz is all about GenCon, but we're still pressing on with our Go Play Peoria minicons. The next one will be held on August 30, 2008 at the Samaritan Ministries building from 9 am to 9 pm.

Turnout has been decent for the last two, although the roleplaying side of things is a bit slim. So, I'd really like to see more roleplayers showing up and strutting their stuff. So, if you're going to be in the area, please join us on August 30.

Admission is free, though we're asking for a $5 donation to help cover the food cost. If you'd rather donate food to the food table, just get in touch with me and we'll work it out.

Seth Ben-Ezra

    Here's a special MRG rule for you (note that it's untested):

    \n

    The Standoff

    \n

    When you receive the unbloodied knife, you have an additional action: Standoff. With a second knife (this is the only way to invoke the second knife), make a counterdemand of the same extremity as the one you were given. This immediately initiates a Wirework sequence with special rules.

    \n

    The rules for performing Wirework are unchanged, but the victory options are:

    \n
      \n
    • Calm the knife. Put both knives down and calm them. (The knives are always both in the same state.)
      • \n
    • Take the initiative. The knife you possess remains; the other knife is put down. Continue.
      • \n
    • Injure your opponent. Choose one of his Displays and mark it as an injury - from now on, he cannot use this Display to gain chips, and other characters can describe it to gain chips for themselves.
      • \n
    • Subdue your opponent - extract a demand.
      • \n
    \n

    Only one player may choose to calm the knife, but the other victory options may be chosen twice.
