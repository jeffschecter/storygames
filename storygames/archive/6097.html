\n In an effort to break some of the boundaries I see on the game forums and game communities namely

the division between gamers and GMs
the division between indie and traditional gamers
the division between game designers and non-designers

in an attempt to make a forum centered on play and how to make it more awesome

and to give some space up for Play by Post

I have in a fit of insanity, created There is No Screen

With the kind permission of Andy, I now throw it out to all you S-G.

Come on over and talk about your play.

Jeff
