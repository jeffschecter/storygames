    I've always viewed IIEE as saying "hey, these are the phases you need to consider when you are putting together your resolution mechanics," not "this the process you must always go through to resove something."

    So, like, you need to deal with Intent, Initiation, Execution and Effect. But, Jon's talking about Intent->Effect, skipping the other two (or Initiation->Effect, or whatever. I'd need an example to pin it down). Polaris starts with Initiation. And so on. Just because the acronym goes IIEE doesn't mean that by "using" it you need to go in that order, or anything.
