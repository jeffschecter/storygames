\n I thought I'd told everyone about this, but Jason didn't know, so I'll mention it again.

For the last few months, I've been blogging about roleplaying and improv. I've expanded the posts, added a load more stuff, and turned it into a book:



It covers telling stories, playing characters using status, being good to play with and lots of other things.

It'll be on Lulu before Christmas: the price will probably be $18.

If you're interested in the blog, that's still going, too, although many of the old posts are now hidden. I think I'm about three weeks away from finishing what I've got to talk about: I've just started the section on Playing With Others. (I must change that title, but the only one I can think of is Being Nice To Play With, and I can't use that.)

Graham
