    \n Signed and numbered copy, ideally with my number in the single digits.

    I also like the idea of appearing somehow in the credits (Special Thanks?).

    Designer's Notes is also cool, if it's just a section you add (Appendix) that easily removed for the "full production run" or what-not. I don't think I'd want them as a supplement or insert--too likely to get stuck in a pile, if I play it regularly, and forgotten. Also, while insightful (sometimes), they are very much a "read once" item; and I like a high reference:fluff ratio in my game books. All that said, I know such insights often make one a better GM... so maybe they shouldn't even be optional/special; maybe not in the book, but DEFINITELY on the game's web site somewhere.

    HTH, and congrats!
