  • David Artman
    June 4
    MF0: Rapid Attack (mechs) allows for squad-based tactical combat of 1-N units, each with 0-4 systems (things that provide dice other than the 2d White a basic unit gets). That variability in squad makup is a big part of the 'initiative' and scoring rules. (Not so much, with Mechaton, which is a bit simpler than MF0:RA.)

    "Reskinning" to any other genre is simply a matter of changing the 'theme' of the Legos that you use for systems. For fantasy:
    * Melee attack = obvious, given all the clubs, swords, etc that are available for minifigs
    * Ranged attack = Bows, crossbows, etc
    * Artillery (long range) attack = Magical or BIG bows/arbalests
    * Single-shot long ranged attack = As above (these don't count against 4-system-per-unit limit)
    * Defense boost = Armor
    * Movement boost = Skill (agility) or magical or fanciful (mechanical DaVinci wings?)
    * Sensing (improves results of ranged attacks) = Magical or maybe telescope?

    For craft-based combat rules, consider reskinning MF0: Alpha Bandit
