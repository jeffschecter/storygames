\n Hey all,

We've done this in the past, and it was super fun.
I'd like to do a Secret Santa gift exchange again.
I'm willing to organize it.

Deadline to enter: Dec 1.

If you want to participate, whisper me the following information:
Your Real Name
Your Address
Your Email Address
optionally, Gift Parameters (like, if you like games, books, DVDs, CDs, buttons, comics, handmade stuff, etc)
optionally, Stuff you'd like to give

As an example:
Mike Garbin
123 Example Lane
Generictown, Texas
13441
USA
example@server.com
I'd love to receive a game or a book or something handmade.
I'd love to give a book or comic. I've got great taste in comics.

The gift parameters thing is totally optional, but it'll help make sure that people get gifts that they are genuinely enthusiastic about.

So whisper your participation here.
Talk out loud about the event.
I'll email you your "secret santa assignment" on Dec 1. You'll have a narrow window to find a present and mail it, if it's to arrive by Christmas time.

Thoughts?
