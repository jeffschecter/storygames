\n I'm in Minneapolis/St. Paul all week visiting my in laws. Could anyone recommend some local indie games friendly gaming shops for me to visit while I'm here?

    \n Phoenix Games (corner of Lyndale & Lake, Minneapolis) is long-established, but is going out of business this month. There used to be a teensy indie section tucked away in back, I don't know if that's sold out now. They're right across the street from Dreamhaven Comics, which you should visit despite their lack of gaming stuff. Beware the roadwork in that area.

    The Source Comics and Games (corner of Larpenteur & Snelling, St. Paul) is huge and has been known for picking up a pile of indie stuff at the GenCon Forge booth every year. We've played indie games at their gaming tables too.
