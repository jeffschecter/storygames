\n I'm working on a game set in World War 2, but I need some more Weird War stuff for the Nazis to be working on. The game is very much based on two-fisted WW2 comic books, so every German soldier is a Nazi and every American GI is a gung-ho hero. So far, what I've got are:

Nazi werewolves
Heads in jars
Giant Nazi robots
Ubermensch (evil Captain Americas)
Lightning guns
Crazy occult rituals
Giant walking machines
Zeppelins

But I need more Weird War stuff! Any suggestions?

    \n You can pretty much do it by riffing of eugenics and the fear of the Nazis developing a nuclear bomb. So...

    Cross-bred human apes
    Humans with a third eye
    Genetically engineered Nazi vampires
    Bombs which tear a hole in the fabric of reality
    Radioactive zombies
    Genetically engineered crumbling Lovecraftian shambly humans

    ...actually, those are pretty lame, but I think genetic and nuclear experiments are a good starting point.

    \nOff topic, this reminds me of one of my Dad's favourite stories. He was in the Air Force during World War II with an Irish guy (my godfather, after whom I'm named). They had interviews for promotion.

    Officer: And what are your views on the war, Sgt Phillips?
    Mike Phillips: The views of a neutral, sir. I'd as soon have joined the Luftwaffe, but it wasn't practical.

    He didn't get promoted.
