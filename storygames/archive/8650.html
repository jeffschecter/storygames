    \n I guess I'm gonna do my usual reply: what genres or fiction interest her? Forget "the simple system for newbs" angle--find something she enjoys, then choose a game which tightly integrates its system to its setting, and roll with it. Remember: you were once a "newb" non-gamer, and you somehow figured out how to play shit like OD&D or Hero System.

    I guess I'm just saying don't be prejudicial. My first serious girlfriend (16 y.o.) back in 1988 learned Pendragon in a matter of hours of actual play, and totally dug the game's focus on really simulating the Arthurian legends and lifestyle. If I met a woman into westerns, I'd be rolling DitV out without a question.

    All that said... Prime Time Adventures is supposedly a good entry game, because EVERYONE knows how TV series play out and pace.

    HTH;
    David
