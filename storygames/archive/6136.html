    \n If it's of any help (you may not need procedural advice), you could look at how we manage the Icehouse Game Design Competition:
    http://icehousegames.org/wiki/index.php?title=IGDC

    In summary: self-nomination (we may begin to require any submitting designer to provide feedback to all other submissions, to be sure "committed" designers only); anyone may judge; ballots are submitted as game rankings; a modified Condorcet Ranked Pairs algorithm is used to determine final rankings.

    [edit] I meant to add that this puts 99% of the work into the hands of participants and the public, with only promotional, reporting, and calculation duties falling on the organizers. Thus, it's relatively little effort to run it--maybe a couple of hour total each comp. [/edit]
