    \n I do this all the time at cons. Here's what I do: Use Fear 3, Reason 2. Give the PCs a couple of starting Love points but this isn't necessary if a few of the players are aggressive with Overtures of Affection. I then write down all the PCs More Than Humans, Less Than Humans, and Connections.

    Then what I do is I give each PC an order from the Master that does the follow: 1) Is impossible for the minion to carry out in a straightforward manner because of the limitations of their More Than/Less Than Humans and 2) threatens a DIFFERENT minion's connections.

    It's very important that "threaten" here doesn't necessarily mean physically harm. It could be an attack on something that connection values or otherwise involves bringing unpleasantness to the connection.

    Basically, keep repeating the above until Endgame.

    Note: Playing this way is not exactly what I call the full My Life with Master experience. It results in a very frantic game I sometimes liken to 18th Century Paranoia. But it gets the idea across.

    Jesse
