    \n Standard colour from DTRPG looks kind of cheap. It's not terrible, but it's noticeably inferior to what you'd expect to buy from a bookstore. Reviews of premium colour have all been good, but I have not seen it in person yet.

    Lulu's colour looks decent, the pages are matte, not glossy.
