\n I just released a new RPG in "playtest edition." It's called The Ladykillers. I'm looking for some feedback and play testing before releasing early next year.

Playtesters get mention in the text and any of my existing games in PDF, including this one.

https://drive.google.com/file/d/0Bzxc8LOtvfOfZGNoeVBOUXh2WVE/edit?usp=sharing

The game
You play women who come back from the dead as assassins.\xa0

From the text intro:
You died before your time. At least for a while. Someone, somewhere gave you another chance. The woman you were has returned to the world. You\u2019re free from darkness. But it all comes with a heavy price. Call it justice. Call it God\u2019s wrath. Curse it if you want. Redemption is out there. Peace. You would kill for that. And you\u2019ll have to.

Some thoughts for playtesting

Do the hitlist targets work out well? Do their number of hits make things to prolonged or too easy?

Are the aspect powers engaging, and can you tie them to the fiction?

Are there parts and procedures that are unclear? How could a summary or guide help out?
