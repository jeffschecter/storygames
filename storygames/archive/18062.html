\n Camp Nerdly will be May 17-19th this year.

Registration is currently closed as I get the web page revamped and finalize costs.

A couple of changes from last year

- We have the camp until 5pm on Sunday. So Sunday morning is a much more viable gaming time slot. I know people with long drives will still have to leave, but hopefully this means that folks Sunday games won't be cut short. Scheduled activities will end at 3:30pm, giving plenty of time for clean up and depature hugs.

- There will be online game sign up ahead of time, as well as pitch sessions before each event. When we get the online scheduling setup finished I'll link it.

So save the dates,stay tuned, and figure out what you want to run...

    So, for anyone who hasn't used conplanner before, don't worry. It's pretty simple. After you create an account (which is totally free) you can go to the Convention page and create Attendees (which are all totally free.)

    But it's those "Attendees" which create games, or sign up for games, or buy tickets.

    So don't create like 5 accounts if you are a family bringing 5 people. One account can create any number of attendees.

    I will create a tutorial if need be, but I think it's pretty straightforward after that initial explanation, and we will definitely be able to help or work around any difficulties people have.\ufeff

    TICKETS CAN BE PURCHASED THROUGH CONPLANNER
    The money goes to the same paypal account as before. If you want or need to pay in some other way, please let us know.

    \n Yes, go ahead!

    I'll try to clarify that on the page.

    If you have something you definitely want to run there, go ahead and put it in a time-slot. But the Pitch Sessions will continue, so the con-planner list is just kind of advertising and appetizing.

    Go ahead and sigh-up for games you want to play, even. However, even if your game is "full up" through ConPlanner, we still ask you come to the pitch-session and tell everyone what you're doing and where, and also let your players hear what else is going on. Be open to new players, or players changing their minds.

    (You don't have to come back for a pitch-session mid-game, if you span multiple slots or something.)

    If you need a special location, let me know and we'll create it for you. Otherwise, we'll just mark you as "Castle" or "Mess" or "Tower" depending on the age restrictions.
