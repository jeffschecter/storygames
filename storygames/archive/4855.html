    \n Not really. The "vs" Engine (as one might expect) is all about physical conflict. You can make it about other stuff, of course, though that is what it is about out of the box. It's not particularly deep, nor is it intended to be. The rules are simple specifically to facilitate a continuous flow of action. Because that's what the games are about. Action. In many ways, vs. Outlaws is the Tombstone to movies like Dances With Wolves, while vs. Monsters is the Van Helsing to Bram Stoker's Dracula, and vs. Elves is the Krull to Peter Jackson's The Fellowship of the Rings.
