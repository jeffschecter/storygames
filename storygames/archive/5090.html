    \n Hello, Peter. Yes, they happened two years ago! The current edition of the game is fully updated and in a state I consider very solid and complete.

    Those actual play reports of Ron's were key in those revisions. I released the "Aristeia Edition" of Nine Worlds at GenCon in 2005. The nice softback edition came out later that fall.

    (The previous version was also softback, but had a comb binding, which is basically a plasic coil-thingy that holds the book together.)
