\n We did it! We finally got Diaspora out the door. It's got strong traditional roots welded to a burgeoning trunk and branches of FATE, so in a lot of ways it's probably not a story game per s\xe9, but it's a cracking good play. I wanted to thank everyone here who's given me ideas, criticism, support, and even the faintest whiff of enthusiasm. I have godawful release-fear and now I'm done, hands washed, and have to watch it live in the wild.

    \n PDF is being carefully considered, but we're not committed to it at this time. The package you get in the 6x9 hardcover is so EXACTLY what we wanted to deliver in shape and feel and all kinds of aesthetics, that it just doesn't seem right to dilute that yet. But it will probably happen.

    As for IPR, when we get some Real Copies we'll send one to the gang there to decide if they want to carry us. The thing about Lulu, though, is the risk for us is zero. The risk if we go to IPR is however many copies we send IPR (couple hundred bucks at least) and then we also lose almost all the profit. So it kind of sucks for us. We're headed that way, for sure (at least in part because it's a distribution vector into the brick and mortar world), but we're not really in a hurry.

    If you're in Vancouver, though, I can get you a copy cheap.
