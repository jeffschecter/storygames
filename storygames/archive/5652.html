    \n Stranger Things, for me, hits all my cool points. Charged situations, demons, lost cities, gods and men. The art is kick ass and the APs have proven to be as fun as I thought the game would be. Hellboy and Lankhmar are two of my most favorite imagined spaces and hold a lot of history for me; the game feels like an extension of both. So, the setting is tres cool. When it evolved past a Trollbabe hack it went in even better directions. The map tiles are intensely awesome and the situation generator makes me feel like it would take years to explore thoroughly.

    As such, when I saw In a Wicked Age, with some of the very same elements, I felt that an Oracle would be the best way to get my fix, for now.

    It also holds a special place in my heart because it was the first indie game in production that I began to follow.
