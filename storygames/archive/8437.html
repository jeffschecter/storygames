\n Hey, help me add to this list. (I'll update this post daily as new items are added) Try to think of as many as you can per post.
I'm looking for Complete Games, and from all walks of life (trad, hippie, whatever)
Game doesn't have to be "for sale for dollahs", but it does have to have come out in 2008, in English, and does have to be complete (as complete as one can expect: A complete game posted on a website/blog is fine, an incomplete series of notes over a few blog posts or self-recognized incomplete "ashcan" is not). Also, just games not supplements.

=====
