\n I just launched a huge update to FiascoPlaysets.com. It has a new design and backend, and now features filtering, pagination, permalink pages, ratings (care of RPGGeek.com), not to mention dozens more playsets, including playsets in French, German, Spanish, Portuguese, and Russian. Coming soon: search, an improved playset submission form, a trainwrecks section, and continual UX improvements. Take a look and let me know what you think.
