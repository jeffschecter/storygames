\n It\u2019s that time of year again! Go Play NW 2011 will be held July 8-10 2011 at the Seattle Center in the shadow of the famous Space Needle.

Over the coming days and weeks we\u2019ll have more news and information. Watch for the opening of registration very soon, and new discussions starting up on our Web site.

Go Play NW is a yearly gaming convention held in Seattle. It\u2019s about getting together, meeting friends, playing games, and having a good time. Last year was hard to top, but we\u2019re going to try! Visit our Web Page for more information at http://www.goplaynw.org.

    \nDowntown! For example, if you happen to stay at the Green Tortoise Hostel at First and Pike, the monorail would be fairly convenient for getting to the Seattle Center.

    Of course, the monorail stop is at 5th Ave downtown and the southeast corner of Seattle Center, while the buses that go right by the Northwest Rooms at Seattle Center run up 3rd Ave, so it would be more convenient and involve less walking to take the bus rather than the monorail. But still, you could totally ride the retro-future monorail to sort of near your destination!
