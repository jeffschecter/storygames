\n Hi there!

We're having our next JiffyCon in June, on Saturday the 6th. It will be at the Hampton Inn this time. A bit of an upgrade, and convenient for anyone who'd like to book a room there. (Reduce rates if you book before May 15th and mention Jiffycon!) There is info about other accommodations and the schedule at the website:

http://www.jiffycon.com

We're going back to it being a one day event, but this time we'll have the room through the evening, until 10pm. Our fantastic food committee is planning a potluck/informally catered dinner affair that we can all share, and we'll play a live game of the Werewolves at Miller's Hollow.

We are would love to have you there, and are looking for GMs too. Can you come? What would you like to run?

all the best,
Emily

    HiQKid, JiffyCon is a smallish con. I have at least two playtests on the schedule, so why not sign up to run your pickup games? If you don't want to commit just yet, I'd be happy to take your game info and either check in with you before I print the final list if games, and/or keep the game on the schedule as a backup game.

    Anyone else want to run something? Email me at stonebabygames at gmail. Give me the name of the game, the GM's name, a brief description, min/max number of players, and appropriate age group: all ages, adult (18+), or youth (7-17).
