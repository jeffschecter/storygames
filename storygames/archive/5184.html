    \n Another thing I REALLY liked about this episode is the huge validation I felt at listening to Judd go Fuck Yeah about being a system hacker game designer. We had that brief conversation him and I at Gen Con and it was great getting an expanded version here. For some reason that I cannot figure out I have had this (possibly self-created, who knows) complex about being a system hacker, about that not being accepted as being a "real" game designer. The reason I say it might possibly be self-created is because, as I look back and try to retrace my proverbial steps, I cannot find any one post or thread that supports that stigma from the greater game design community. So maybe it has been just me projecting my fear or something. Regardless, hearing Judd wax poetic on being a system hacker felt good; maybe I can finally banish those demons within me once and for all now.
