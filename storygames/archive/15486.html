    \n Dave Younce used Google Earth for his AGON/Delta Green mashup. The adventure took place on Nauru, and we had the entire island in absurd detail to plan with, right down to individual footpaths. We used the pan/tilt/zoom features to get a character-eye view of the landscape.

    If you are going for a static presentation, I'd consider using regular satellite views (try rival sources, because image quality varies from area to area between, say, Bing and Google). I'd screencap and mate the results in an image editor. I'm sure there are more elegant ways.

    \n I used screen-shots of Google Earth and compiled them together in Photoshop to make a large (22x34 inches) map of modern Arkham. It took about 120 individual screen-shots, carefully lined up and overlapped, with the extra erased. Looked great, but I really should've printed it as a poster instead of splitting it into four parts.
