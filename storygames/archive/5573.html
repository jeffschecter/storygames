\n Well, I'm going to make my first attempt at strapping Camaraderie onto something... and right now I'm leaning towards Red Box Hack, mostly because I can see how to do the group.

Character creation starts with party creation, where everyone sits down and chooses 1 stat and 1 talent that belongs to the group. Basically, being part of the party means having 1 more level for everyone in the party. A party of Strategists is very different from a party of Beefcake/Cheesecakes, or Yari Wielders.

Once the party is created proceed with character creation as normal.

Now, how to do the players' ante? Awesome tokens for the GM comes to mind, as does giving monters more Powers, Hit Points, and making them worth more XP. I admit I'm more sketchy on that bit.
