Just FYI, y'all. Evil Hat means business when it says customer service is important!

    \n Makes perfect sense to me as a designer and publisher, you don't want to miss having your game played just for a piddly detail like whether it was purchased or whatever. I mean, the ratio of purchase vs. play is so skewed for many games that it would be entirely sensible to just give your game for free to anybody and everybody who'll actually play it. Get your revenue from the curiousity purchases, get your artistic satisfaction from the people who actually dig your stuff enough to play it.

    Hmm, maybe it'd be possible to have a voucher of some sort in the product: the cost of your purchase off any other games of mine you'd like to buy, but only after you've played this one, and maybe perhaps posted pics or something in the 'net. I'll have to seriously consider something like that at some point.
