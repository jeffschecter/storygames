\n Seems like all the Apocalypse World AP vignettes are over on Vincent's forums. Here's one highlighting two cool game mechanics.

Babbel hardhold, The Wake. A large conference room on the tower's third floor above water that the algae farmers call The Pit. It's where they go to drink and blow off steam over the water rationing situation.

There's one faction of the muckers that want to storm the neighboring hardhold of Sagrada and take over their water purification facilities. That faction's alpha is Ricarra. Urging restraint and patience is Max, our gunlugger Butch's NPC dad.

Ricarra's got Max in a corner and is waving a pistol in his face. Kettle, Ricarra's second is covering with a shotgun and is shouting "Just kill him!" Butch has snuck up on the both of them and has seen this whole thing going down. Butch absorbs a last second shotgun blast from Kettle but now has him in a headlock with his pistol pointed alternately at Ricarra and Kettle. Ricarra's got a gun to Max's head. It's a stand-off.

Butch knows Max wants to defuse this whole situation without any killing and abhors Butch's occupation as a gunlugger. It's possible no one has to die. Ricarra's knows the hardholder will, best case, banish him if he kills Max in front of an un-corroborating witness.

But Butch needs to be sure this goes just right. He's got the Battlebabe move, Visions of Death. He rolls 10+ and now gets to name one person who'll die and one who'll live. He's gotta name both. Whatever else happens, Butch says Max is going to survive and Kettle is food for carps.

That's when our savvyhead Linux shows up at another Pit entrance. We last saw her on the garden roof of Babbel, thirty floors up. But she rolled a 10+ on her Bonefeel move at the beginning of the session so she gets to spend her hold at to be in any scene "already there, with the proper tools and knowledge, with or without any clear explanation why."

"Well what do we have here, Ricarra? You know perfectly well you shouldn't be doin' that\u2026"

I ask Linux what the "proper tools" are in this case.

"I'm holding Ricarra's eight-year old daughter in my arms."

Bottom line, the situation is defused. Ricarra puts away his gun, releases Max, and holds his arms out for his daugher. Linux lets her down and she runs to him.

And the Visions of Death? Hmmm\u2026 I decide Kettle is crazed and not willing to let this thing go. He goes for the knife in his belt and stabs backwards at Butch. Butch snaps Kettle's neck with a quick movement and tells Ricarra to take his daughter and get out of his sight.
