\n Last night, Fred Hicks was kind enough to stop by EndGame in Oakland for a meet and greet with the local gaming peeps.

A bunch of people stopped by the store to get their copies of SotC, DRYH and Don't Rest Your Mind signed and to chat with (the super nice) Fred.

My pictures make it look like no one was there, but trust me, besides a bunch of us downstairs there were 20+ people upstairs on the mezzanine doing board gaming, Warhammer and 4ed. It just reminds me of how lucky I am to live near such a cool gaming store!

Thanks to Chris Hanrahan and the EG crew for hosting, and to Fred for taking the time to stop by. If only Carl Rigney wasn't at a sci-fi con this week, we would have had a table full of Mad City inhabitants!
