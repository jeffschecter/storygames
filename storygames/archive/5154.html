    Posted By: jenskotIs there a new bus line we can take from Manhattan?
    \nJohn, according to the hotel's website:\n
    Amtrak Train / Metropark Station is walking distance from the hotel. Exit the train station and turn right onto Middlesex Turnpike. Turn left at the light (Wood Ave South) and the hotel is two blocks up the hill on the left hand side. Total distance is 2.5 blocks.
    \nSo I'd recommend you take NJ Transit to the Metropark station rather than the bus. Though I'd guess there are bus drop-offs there too. You also might want to check with the hotel and see if there are shuttles.
