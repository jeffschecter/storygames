\n Idea I had awhile ago for a Trollbabe hack to play vampires:

You have a number between 1-10. Roll a d10 for conflicts. Roll over it to do supernatural vampire things, and also monstrous things. Roll under it to do normal human things and decent or self-sacrificial things.

And I think if you failed a conflict, you could flip what you're doing. So a failed conflict to do a good/human thing can become a successful evil/vampiric thing. You succeed but you've perverted the outcome, and your number slips down a notch because you've sacrificed a bit of yourself to get what you want.

I don't know what hitting your number would mean. I forget what it even means in Trollbabe at the moment.

Obviously mimicking the WoD stuff but simpler but I think better addressing the struggle with humanity. Oh and let's say you get three Human attachments or skills or contacts and three Vampire powers or whatever.
