\nThe world is about to end. And we dance and we sing.\u201d

Living Shanri is a shared universe created by players of the Houses of the Blooded roleplaying game. Playing in Living Shanri is free\u2026 with one notable exception. We\u2019ll discuss that below. Players take the roles of nobles in the world of Shanri, using their own culture, allies and enemies as weapons to gain political power and prestige.

___

This is organized play for the Houses of the Blooded game. Both a Living Campaign and Organized Play in the same style as the Camarilla Club and other "global chronicles."

More info at the HotB Design Journal.
