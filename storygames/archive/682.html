    So we combine that with Vince Baker's Afraid playtest document we can see how liking cookies too much can really turn you into a monster:
    Name a person with a habit that she enjoys.
    Cookie Monster, who enjoys eating cookies.
    Indulging her habit, over time she becomes jaded and curious. Name her curiosity.
    Cookie Monster becomes determined to find the world's best cookie, and keep it all to himself.
    Because she's jaded, her habit becomes increasingly baroque and ritualistic.
    Cookie Monster increasingly cares less and less about enjoying the cookies, and more and more about selfishly keeping the pleasure of cookie eating from other people. He'd rather crumble cookies to bits in his big felt mouth than let someone else eat them.
    Because she's curious, she becomes progressively prone to extremity. Extremity is measured in human harm - indulging her increasingly baroque and ritualistic habit, she inflicts increasing human harm. Describe how.
    Cookie Monster begins stealing all the cookies from Mr. Hooper's store, leaving the shopkeeper deprived of income and local kids deprived of joy. He goes on sugar high induced rampages, destroying snacks and toys that other muppets enjoy.
    There's some threshold of ritualistic extremity over which she crosses. Some combination of ritual and human harm gives her her first access to nightmarish resources. Name the threshold and describe the nightmarish resources.
    The world's finest cookie recipe, which Cookie Monster discovers in a seedy underworld bakery, creates cookies so delicious that no man, woman or muppet can resist them. Using these cookies as bribes, Cookie monster can create a host of cookie addicts following him, provided he doesn't eat al the cookies himself. Again.
    The nightmarish resources push her and enable her to make her indulgences even more ritualistic and extreme.
    Cookie Monster begins demanding a tribute from all the children on Sesame Street, demanding all normal cookies in the neighborhood for the occasional secret cookie.
    Eventually, she does something irrevocable and life-or-death. She kills, dies, conceives a child, brings someone back from the grave. This transforms her; she's now a monster, a creature all nightmare. She is a danger to humanity: her existence, her influence, threatens the lives, souls or humanity of us all. Name the horror her influence could make of the world.
    The secret ingredient in the world's finest cookie is people!
