\n Hey everyone!

A bunch of cool gamers in Columbus Ohio are having an event in just under two weeks! We're all really excited about it, and even have a kickstarter up to help support the event.

We would love to see anyone nearby out for the event that includes food, boardgaming, indie games, and game design. I'd love to see you out! I'm thinking of running Monsterhearts, Kagematsu, and the Tribunal! Aaron Brown from NYC Nerd is helping organize, as well as Travis Scott, and Aaron Sturgill in Columbus Ohio. It promises to be a really fun weekend.

Here's the Kickstarter.

Join OH Nerd for a weekend of games, food, and fun! We have partnered with local organizations to offer attendees a unique three-day gaming event! OH Nerd aims to create a casual setting for gaming and socializing. Our focus in on small press and independently produced role-playing games, and we're also big fans of contemporary board games.
