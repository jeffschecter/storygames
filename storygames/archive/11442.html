\n Your squad has been together for three seasons, kicking ass and making ratings. Now you're over the ruins of Novosib\xedrsk ready to paradrop into a hot zone under the control of AllOil-sponsored mercs with a force that outnumbers yours by a factor of ten to one. The objective is simple: secure a staging area for the bigger units that will follow by any means necessary, gratuitous violence not only expected, but encouraged.

DMZ TV is on the air!


Have you been over on #playnow? Well, that's where I'll be running War Inc! on a Saturday mid-morning coming soon. I tried once before with a western game but the interest wasn't there. Hopefully it'll be a little different this time.

The game is easy to pick up and play so prep time will be minimal. I just ask that you grab a copy of the game ahead of time and familiarize yourself with what rules there are. It'll be my first time running it, so I don't expect anyone to be an expert (I'm not), but knowing the kinds of things you roll dice for would be a boost.

Total running time shouldn't be much more than 90 minutes or so. It might even go faster than that depending how easily you blow through the opposition. Like I said, without having run this before I only have a vague idea of how it could go. And it might go sideways right from the start. We shall see.
