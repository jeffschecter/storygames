\n So after visiting my pbwiki Notebook for the first time in a while today and besides being surprised about the huge amount of new features, I read their announcement that they added a YackPack plugin to the free wikis. Now, I had absolutely no idea what "YackPack" is, so I googled around for a bit and what I found sounds quite interesting.

From what I can tell, Yackpack seems to be a Web 2.0-Style, voice chat/messaging-based Social Website-thingy where people can open public/private voicechat groups called pack that feature both live chat and saved voice messages for people who are offline.

(Well, and it has tons of the usual Widgets one can put on Websites for stuff like live voice chat or answering machine-style features.)

Now, I only stumbled over it today and I haven't really tried it out yet, but if it works as promised, I could see quite a bunch of usefull gaming applications, especially if combined with my favorite internet technology, the Wiki. But I've been wondering, has anyboy else any experience with YackPack? Good, Bad, Ugly?
