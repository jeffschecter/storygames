    \n Yes! The nonviolent pdf is identical to the spreads pdf, but with the big black images removed.

    The maestro d' fold pdf is the same format as the character playbooks at apocalypse-world.com, for folding into a fun and/or irritating little minibook. Print it 2-sided, fold it in half, then in half, then staple down the center, trim the edges, and fold along the staples to make the spine. Character sheet on the centerfold, big "maestro d'" on the front cover, image on the back cover.

    I endorse John Harper's character sheets instead, if you don't want to make the minibooks. I'm also about to release a more convenient non-minibook set, including the maestro d' for preorderers, but it'll still be fun and/or irritating in its own way.
