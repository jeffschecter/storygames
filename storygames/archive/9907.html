    \n I don't think there's a nice analytical trick to solve it here, it's just going to be a big sum. Here are probabilities for you via brute force simulation:

    3d6: 0.0276807
    4d6: 0.0971648
    5d6: 0.2129348
    6d6: 0.3671087
    7d6: 0.5407696
    8d6: 0.7075797
    9d6: 0.8424222
    10d6: 0.9324779
    11d6: 0.97936
    12d6: 0.9965548

    They should be good for a few decimal places.
