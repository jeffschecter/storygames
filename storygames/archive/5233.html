\n Starting in February, I am going to run one-shot RPG game sessions at Alley Cat Games here in Columbus.

In March, I plan to run With Great Power to correspond with the store's Super Saturdays event.

I don't know what I will run in February, but front-runners are Dogs in the Vineyard, Spirit of the Century, Inspectres, and The Shadow of Yesterday.

This post is to serve a couple of purposes. First, to let you all know in case you are interested.
Second, to get some input on what people would be most interested in trying in March.
And finally, see if I can get one or more people to commit to showing up so that I'm not all alone.

So, what do you think?
