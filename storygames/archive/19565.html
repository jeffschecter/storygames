\n Meet some of the 10,000 San Franciscans who took part in the Jejune Institute, a combination citywide art project and living game, from 2008 to 2011.

You can find "The Institute" under Documentaries on Netflix, and on the film's website: http://www.theinstitutemovie.com/

This is one of the most fascinating game concepts you've ever heard, and it's about to take 90 minutes of your life. You're welcome.
