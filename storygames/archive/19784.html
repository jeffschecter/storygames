Indie+, a community of fans and creators of small-press role-playing games, is having a drive to increase our subscriber base to our YouTube channel. Our channel features lively panel discussions on play and design, as well as demos of exciting RPGs and actual play videos of pick-up games that we host once a month.

We set a goal of 250 subscribers by the end of the year, and we're so close to hitting our number that we are giving everyone a special holiday gift. It's the Indie+ Anthology, a digital download of supplements for the kinds of games we love. If you help us exceed our goal, we can level up. At 300 subscribers, Quinn Murphy will make a system agnostic game supplement that I'm desperate to read. At 350, Shoshanna Kessock will make a mini-LARP! If we blow past that, we have plans for even more!

Please, if you're not already subscribed, head over to the video linked above and join us. If you already ARE a subscriber, thanks! Share with your buddies and help us out, too!

Thanks,
Rich
