    \n System.

    Seriously.

    I had a conversation with a friend last week where I realized that I'm kind of done with expecting a system to help me Tell a Good Story -- pacing, theme, probably anything that falls under "Narrative"/Story Now stuff.

    I don't know where the heck this came from. It's like there's been this coup d'\xe9tat in my brain and the guy who says stuff like "oh you can run that with anything" has taken over. I'm all into generic systems and can't be bothered with anything else, and I can't tell you what the hell happened to cause it. Maybe I've just spent a ridiculous amount of time looking for a Holy Grail of Story and being disappointed again and again -- having these brief romances with the New Shiny Thing that will Solve All My Problems and then becoming disillusioned in the end. Maybe I'm just realizing there's no such thing. Maybe I'm realizing me and my friends can tell a damned good story without help.

    I'm also done with playing with people I don't want to play with. But that's another story.
