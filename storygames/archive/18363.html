\n Hello all,

Thought a few of you might be interested in Limbo, a very short Nordic larp by Norwegian designer Tor Kjetil Edland. I'm part of the team running it in Highland Park, NJ on Saturday June 8, from 2:45 - 8pm.

The game examines what happens after you die, but before you move on, and is a low-stress mechanics-light way to get a taste of this style of game.

We welcome players who are new to larp in general or Nordic larp in particular.

Details available here -- http://www.emmaleighwaldron.com/limbo.html
