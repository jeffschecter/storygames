For those who haven't seen it on the forge: A few brave souls will man the first combined PrO/Forge booth at Spiel Essen. We will be hosting more than a dozen Forge games to the biggest game and toy convention in europe.

The Spiel Essen runs yearly in October. It will be from Thursday 19th to Sunday 22nd this time.

As this is a first-timer for most of us at the booth, wish us luck.

Reports will follow sometime late next week.

Regards,
Harald

PS: This post is a feeble event to get revenge at all those who can easily attend US conventions without needing to fly for eight hours plus.
