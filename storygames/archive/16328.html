    \nI actually did GM a session once while bleeding from a lacerated facial contusion. I'd bumped my forehead on a moveable screen while searching for the light-switch in a dark game room, and like a scalp wound it bled more than you would expect from such a trivial injury. The character-players were freaking out all night and trying to talk me into letting them give me a lift to A&E, and it wasn't until I got back to my room that I realised what they had been on about. The blood running down this dead-straight laceration on my forehead had dripped from my eye-brow to my cheek, run down to my jaw, and dripped onto my shirt. It looked as though my face had been split open from hairline to jawline.
