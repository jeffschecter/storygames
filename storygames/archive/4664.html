\n As a Halloween treat, Precis Intermedia is giving away a free copy of the Unbidden PDF with a purchase of $10 or more. Unbidden is a game of Horrors, Secrets, and Legends in which the characters are forced to live on the streets because they no longer fit into mortal society. They must not only battle the evil agents of the darkness, but also their own personal demons. Armed only with supernatural powers, can they accept their roles in destiny or fight who they have become in a struggle to rejoin society?
\n
\nPrecis Intermedia is one of the first e-publishing RPG companies (formerly Politically Incorrect Games and Spectre Press), and is known for its diverse line-up of clear and concise roleplaying games, as well as low-cost accessories. From diceless, story-based, and rules-light games to paper miniatures, dice, and generic add-ons, Precis Intermedia offers both downloadable and printed materials. Plus, the Affiliate Publishers section of the catalog offers even more exciting, quality titles.
\n
\nJust add Unbidden to your cart and enter coupon code CZHCB382E3 on the payment screen during checkout.
\n
\nFor more information, visit the Precis Intermedia web site.
