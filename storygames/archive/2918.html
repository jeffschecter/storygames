\n The Thursday night GM crew at Endgame in Oakland, CA has a new forum(Thank you Paul!) set up to organize our activities.

You can put your name down for any games that are going to be run, see what's been done before (and discuss it), request games that you'd like to see someone run, and offer to run something yourself, along with general discussion related to the stuff we do at Endgame. Sign up and help us grow the community there! :-)

Old games, new games, it's all good. Mostly it's con-style one shots, with the goal of trying new and different games people may not have had a chance to play before.

Gamers in the East Bay, this is for you!
