\n I know I love the show. That is partially why I am an idiot. Not too long ago I went hunting for Entourage Season 4 on Ebay. I found it, and I placed a bid. But like an idiot, I bid later that day on another auction. Guess who won both? That's right, this guy.

So here I sit, with an extra, unopened copy of Entourage Season 4. Now I could just try and resell it, but that is a pain in the ass. My selling days on Ebay are done. Too much of a hassle. So instead, I am going to make an effort to put it to good use.

See, I want people to try out my game, Conspiracy of Shadows: Dirty Hands. To give it a go. To play it some. I'm not in it for the money. I'm not in it for sales. I give the shit away for free.

Nope, I am in it cause I love the game. I love playing it. I love illustrating for it. I love tinkering with it... Obviously. So in honor of that, I am giving away this unopened copy of Entourage Season 4 for actual play reports. I'll send you a comic and some art prints if you are interested in that, but if all you want is the DVDs, I won't be offended.

Contest rules are below. If you have any questions don't hesitate to ask.
\n

Contest Rules

\nRequirements: Play through a whole Theme out and report on it back to me. Post the report somewhere in English and post a response here with a link to it so I can read it.
Duration: Due date is GenCon 2010 or when I get my socks blown off by some awesome play.
Criteria: You knock my fucking socks off.
