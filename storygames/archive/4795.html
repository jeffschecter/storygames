    \n Ehn. No, this isn't a really quick easy question.

    Ron calls Sorcerer's dice system conflict resolution; I don't really agree. The game does not, to my memory, actually talk about setting stakes, and it also predates the coining of "conflict resolution." The CR/TR divide was something developed online, in forums and whatnot, as far as I'm aware, and not in any specific game.
