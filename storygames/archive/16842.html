    \n I saw it a while back when it had it's (short) theater release. I went with my gaming group and we all enjoyed it. I thought it was good, on the verge of great. It definitely has cult film potential. Some of the scenes are fantastic, others drag a bit. The acting is reasonably good for such a small film and the dialogue is decent. I mostly enjoyed how creative and quirky it was (at times), it made up for a lot of the films flaws in my opinion.

    I am a bit biased in favor of it because it's a local film and I wish it had seen a little more success. It's supposed to be coming out on DVD/Blu-ray and Video-on-Demand this month (July 10th) and I plan on picking up a copy.
