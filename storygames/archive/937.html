Fantasy Storygames?

shreyas
July 2006 edited July 2006 in Story Games
So I'm looking around and I see this weird drought of fantasy[1] storygames. I can only think of a few - BW, TSoY, and Vincent's unnamed game - and two of them are entangled in their own settings. Am I missing prominent ones? I'd really like to play a game where I have the tools to build a setting that works for me and my group, rather than having one dumped on our doorstep.

Am I imagining this drought, too, or is there something else at work?

fn1. That is to say, fantasy in the sense of Robin McKinley or Greg Keyes; something fantastical and culturally coloured, and explicitly set in something like the past of Earth.
