    \n Story Engine was definitely a big influence on Universalis.
    \n
    \nIt predated alot of the internet RPG communities so it never really got the buzz it could have if it had come out 5 years or so later. From what limited actual play reports I've seen people had trouble running it effectively...in much the same way as you see threads today about people having trouble running the Pool.
    \n
    \nBut it went to a pretty radical design place for its day, and seeing that in black and white was definitely a <boink> moment for many.
