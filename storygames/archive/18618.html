Following the 2-word pitch thread and especially this otherdoc's suggestion, I've started writing a game name "Monster Chef". here's the more-than-two-word-pitch:\n
Monster Chef is game about cooking for monsters. Their mission is to satisfy the customers by preparing Roast Unicorn, Salamander Eye Soup, Carnivorous Plant Salads and other bizarre specialities. Of course, anything can get wrong when you're cooking dangerous meals for dangerous creatures.

Could anyone give me pointers to a lexicon or a cuisine-related dictionary or help me out in the english translation?
