    \n I used photoshop since I don't have a good PDF editor. For the fonts I googled transformers fonts to find some free transformers fonts. The images of cybertron come from a comic book transformer guide and the last one is a screen shot from Transformers War for Cybertron.

    ...

    For the subsystems, I wonder to what kind of interesting dangers they could be linked to.

    For the supernatural elements they raise some fun questions to answer: How do this team of transformers can access or travel to the spark world? Do they become ghosts? What are their alternate mode? What are their subsystems? Did Starscream's ghost took over the spark world? Did he created the wraiths (wraithicons :P)? What are the wraiths alternate modes? What is happening with the sparks? What is the loot from the spark world? etc...
    I think it add a little something mysterious that make me want to explore the transformers (so that its not just about robots fighting each others).
