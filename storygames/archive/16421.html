\n I have some family in town. We want to play tonight, but only have 4 hours or so available to make characters and get a game in. We normally play Chaosium's Call of Cthulhu. The players are all very creative and energetic, and we've had a few successful story game experiences. Lady Blackbird was really fun with this group.

We don't really have enough time to get into a game of cthulhu. It takes about an hour to make a good CoC character, and 3 hours isn't enough to finish anything. The players are used to a traditional game-mastered game, but could handle gm-less if it was really fun for people who aren't expert story-gamers.

What would you recommend we play for tonight?

What's the latest state-of-the-art for games that are fun in a single night and don't require much prep?

Perhaps games with pre-generated characters?

Thanks!
