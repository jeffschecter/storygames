    OMG! Holy Munkholt! You are plotting to play w/o me.

    I'll just finish my as-yet-untitled Gourmet Chefs & Gourmets game, the JeepStyle game I began making with Juhana Pettersson at Knutpunkt in Stockholm. Not a campaign.

    I'd like to game with Jonas Harild and friends, though, tap into some of that True imagination of his, in what we call The Emperor Game, for lack of a better name. It is a freely improvised game, with GM duties being performed by the player flock as a crowd -- a self-organizing structure, but which is instigated by a plot driver for the session, who has some ideas of "where this thing is going".

    Content matter: Ancient Rome, ancient China, and perhaps also Viking sagas.

    Style: very open, very "pull" (I read what that means at Story-Games, but I have yet to read any off-forum materials), Nordic freeform style.
