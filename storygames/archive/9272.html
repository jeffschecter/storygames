\n We've been talking, in various places, about a database for RPGs. In another thread, I suggested that I'd like a ""People who liked this game also liked..." feature, similar to the one on Amazon.com.

I think we could add this to the Codex without too much trouble. It won't be perfect, but it'll work. Try this.

1. Go to the Codex page for your favourite game.

2. At the bottom, add a new heading: "If you liked this, you may also like...".

3. Add a link to another game, with a brief explanation: "Contenders, another GMless storytelling game". Choose a game you'd genuinely recommend to someone if they liked the first game.

For example, on the Poison'd page, I've linked to "Lacuna: another strongly improvised game with a grabby setting". What matters isn't the "strongly improvised" bit, which is just to give people an idea of what they're clicking on: it's that I'd genuinely recommend that someone who liked Poison'd should try Lacuna.

Go and do this, for your favourite game, and let's see how it works. Don't go mad, so don't recommend six games on one page, and don't recommend other games by the same author, because there's another tag for that.

That tag, by the way, is [[author::Vincent Baker]]. If you're feeling particularly helpful, change the author's name to that format.

The format of the text you'll need to insert is:

=== If you liked this, you may like... ===

* [[Lacuna]], another strongly improvised game with a grabby setting

So yes. Let's see how it works. I'm interested.

Graham
