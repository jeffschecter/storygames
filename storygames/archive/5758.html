You know what I'm in the mood for right now? A tall strawberry shake. A good one. But I'm also in the mood for a place where constructive criticism of games is the only purpose of the place. A place where fair and honest feedback is not just permitted, or invited, but demanded. A place also devoid of name-calling.

\n

Does such a place exist? If it doesn't, and it suddenly did, would any significant population find it's way there?

    The Owlbear looks groovy, but it's not what I'm looking for.

    \n

    This is what I want (in no real order):
    \n1. Everyone can post an opinion or observation, not just a handful of folks.
    \n2. Readers can rate the value of reviews and reviewers.
    \n3. Readers can find reviews of a particular game very easily.
    \n4. One case of name calling or unsportsmanly behavior calls for a warning and a public apology. A second case results in a polite request to refrain from posting for an extended period of time.
    \n5. Seperate sections for reviews that are open for discussion and those that are not.
    \n6. Subjective opinion is just as valuable as objective criticism. So is griping and frustration over a bad session.
