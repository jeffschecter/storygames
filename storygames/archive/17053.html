\n I've been wondering about this for a while. What could I a person clone using text released under the Open Game License?

Among the available sources are d20/D&D and clones (including Mutant Future), Fudge, Fate, Mutants & Masterminds, True20, Action! System (descended from HERO System and Fuzion), Traveller, Basic Roleplaying/RuneQuest/Call of Chtulhu (via GORE and Mongoose RuneQuest), The Fantasy Trip (via Warrior & Wizard), public domain text from 4C (FASERIP), the d6 System (West End Games - Ghostbusters and Star Wars)... I'm sure I'm missing some.

I guess some of the questions are how much text am I is our hypothetical person willing to add and how far are they willing to go to change dice mechanics.

If someone wanted to clone Villains and Vigilantes or Palladium's basic house system, those seem pretty straightforwardly descended from D&D and thus pretty do-able. What else?

Does anyone know of a family tree of RPGs on the web anywhere?
