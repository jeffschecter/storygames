\n Hi All,
I'm working on a Pocket Danger Patrol playset for fairy tales. I know that there are quite a few other playsets for this game; it seems to be a fairly popular game to riff on. But other than Gamma Patrol, I haven't been able to download any of them, because my current country of residence blocks a lot of websites and services, e.g. Google drive. All the same, I'd like to get a look at some of these other playsets.

It may seem obvious to all the designers out there, but just in case it isn't:

A) Reading Gamma Patrol gave me two great ideas which I'm planning on using; and allowed me to springboard my own thinking into two other design solutions that I think will work for the genre I'm interested in expanding into, as well.

The inverse is also true: I don't want to waste my time or others time (playtester, readers) time walking over ground that someone else as tried and found unfruitful.

C) Conversely, if someone has already done a similar playset that I am unaware of, and I am completely wasting my time, when something good and useful already exists, it would be nice to see and know.

I know there is a French website where the people there are really keen on PDP. They have at least four or five playsets. I also know that there is at least on D&D-esque, swords and sorcery playset. I think there might be a film noir playset, and a cyberpunk playset as well. I'm sure there are others that I don't know about, too. Maybe someone has already done what I am trying to do, but I don't know it. Knowing this is frustrating--I can't download any of them (or trace much of their histories).

I'm loathed to post my email address online because of spam, but if you've authored a PDP playset could contact me through PM, I'd appreciate getting access to these playsets by email.

Your kindness in helping me overcome this problem will be greatly appreciated.

Thanks,
Chris
