Camp Nerdly is many months away and it seems like we have a local gaggle of folk that can get together for some post-IGE action!

I am very willing to host such an event or series of events. So lets get the ball rolling:

Dates

Weekly: I'd love to get a weekly gathering going. We currently meet on Wednesdays and Fridays for BE and The Shadows of Yesterday but they are on hiatus over the next month due to one of my players shipping off for some National Guard duty. So it looks clear. The Wednesday thing looks like MLWM and maybe some playtesting if gainsclockwork is okay with extra folk. Tuesday nights are usually BattleLore for us.

There is a gaming club that meets every Sunday night at the Ethical Society on Rittenhouse Square (19th & Locust). They are good folk and although they do not often play the things we are particularly interested in it's worthwhile to play there if we have a group of us going (4+). I prefer to not do campaigns at PAGE for reasons that I can go into at a later date. PAGE does pay rent so there is a modest set of dues $5 a meeting or $15 a month. The first two meetings are free.

As an aside PAGE also meets weekly in Glenside PA on Tuesday nights. Just ask and I am happy to provide you with more info.

Weekend gathering: Pick a weekend between now and March 11th. We (spring and I) are unavailable the weekend of February 7th and March 3rd.

I live in south Philly (accesible through the rt 29, rt 64 and rt 12 buses) and I am more than willing to have gatherings at my place with a few minor caveats:

- Pet allergies. Although the pets are seperated from the guests they still claim a presence. If there are a number of people with this issue then we can find a more public place to play.

- I can host 15 people very comfortably. If it gets to be too much more then we may want to consider other options.

So chime in if your interested.
- Don
