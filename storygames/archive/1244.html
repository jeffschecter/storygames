    From what I've seen, the theory forum is pretty hostile, the rest though seems like rpg.net without the focus on Exalted.

    Whether that is a good or bad thing of course depends on one's tastes, but I think there is a definite gap in the market for positively discussing trad games and that's what the rest of the site does. Most of the folk there seem to be into historical rpgs, sf, DnD, other fantasy stuff, trad gaming. Rpg.net still isn't bad for that, the Forge is trying to do something very different and I think this place is trying to do something different.

    So I think it's utility will be directly related to your interest in trad gaming. If you enjoy trad gaming, there is useful stuff there. If you enjoy trad gaming and enjoy it all the more when you can make nasty comments about people who don't enjoy it, then the theory forum looks like it is for you. I fall into the first of those groups, but sadly there are many who fall into the second as well.
