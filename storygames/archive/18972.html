\n Hey all! As most of you probably know, Story Games also runs a weekly newsletter (if not, sign up! http://story-games.com/)

But to celebrate getting to 10 newsletters, we're throwing a little contest, with a $20 DriveThruRPG gift card as first prize, and a $10 card as second prize, and we'll donate an equal amount to a mutually agreeable charity.

The contest is simple: post on this thread, or send storygames@gmail.com a Fate Aspect that with just those few words alone, makes you can't wait to figure out and play the character. An example I love from Grant Howitt: "My Mother's Second-Best Sword".

Max 2 entries per person. We'll close the contest by next Thursday.
