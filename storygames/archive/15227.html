    \n Did you see how my short response killed this thread dead? That is meta as heck.

    Anyway, all the promise I thought I saw in the medium back at the time of the original thread was brought to a screeching halt by the brevity issue. That's unfortunate, but pretty much what the book predicted would happen. Cosmic horror wants a larger canvas. Every tweet written by someone who's gone mad from the revelation is pretty much the same*, and people who've retreated into the safety of a new dark age don't tweet.
