\n So Conpulsion 2007 in lovely Edinburgh, Scotland is but a handful of days away now. So, for those who may be interested, here's the schedule for the 2007 Indie Games Track. There's a nice selection of stuff there: from published games (Shock, SotC, Best Friends, Covenenant, Contenders, etc) to playtests (Grey Ranks, Reel Adventures, The Gay Recruitment Squad Wants YOU! etc),there should be something to suit all tastes.

As RPG Co-ordinator for the con, I'd like to say thanks to all the Collective Endeavour guys for volunteering to run games and help out.

If you're in the area on the weekend of the 24th, you should drop in and say hi.

Cheers
Malcolm
