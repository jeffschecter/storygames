\n I wind up having to work with Dragon Ball a lot for my translation work, and while it has its flaws, I find it fun enough that I'm contemplating doing a short RPG thing based on it. I'm specifically referring to the early Dragon Ball. While I don't object to Dragon Ball Z as much as many people do, the earlier stuff seems easier to work with, and would be a much easier sell to my gaming group. I want to do a game about a bunch of friends having wacky adventures looking for magic stones that can grant a wish, in a world with weird martial artists, \u201cHoipoi Capsules\u201d that fit in the palm of your hand but can contain cars or houses, animal people, dinosaurs, magical mysteries, and so on.

I have no idea what system to use though. I want something that makes the wacky characters and occasional wacky powers interesting, and that is good for light-hearted player-led adventures. I want to avoid the excesses of fightiness and power level (in the first chapter Bulma freaks out and shoots Goku in the face, but it just stings), and concentrate on the fun adventure and wonder of the setting.

    \n Y'know. . .this seems like too easy an answer, but I'd go with Solar System. light, simple and easy conflict, with the whole Extended Conflict deal for those intense and climactic duels. . .characters with a variety of personalities and goals can all follow their own Keys. . .the Martial Arts can be developed through some choice Secrets (training sequence!), as can all the other weird shapeshifting and inventing and such. . .you can pump up your power with Pool points (Chi). . .death is rare and doesn't have to be the result of taking "damage". . .yeah, I'm not seeing any way that it wouldn't do Dragon Ball well!

    It would start to get less good for Dragon Ball Z, but I'm sorta convinced that there's no way to do a faithful DBZ for roleplaying. It would certainly violate Solar's dictum that even the weakest can sometimes beat the strongest. Though I could see a Solar Dragon Ball game that ran long enough become rather DBZish as players accumulate greater and greater Secrets (and Goku would totally have the Secret of the Bodhisavatta after coming back from the dead!) But I doubt that'd be sustainably fun. You'd definitely hit the ceiling of the Solar System power framework early on.
