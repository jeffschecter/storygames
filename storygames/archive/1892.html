Hey everybody, in case you are interested in getting cards printed for some project, we're currently working with Alphagraphics to do just that with good results so far. They can shrink wrap, print on whatever stock you specify, round corners, whatever. It isn't cheap, but it's cheaper than your time spent sorting 3200 cards printed at Kinko's.

Anyway, there's a thread with details over at The Forge.
