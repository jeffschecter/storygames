\n My players' characters are facing retirement - holding onto loads of swag gleaned from mucho pillage, but where, oh, where to store the potz-o-gold ?

So they bought the vacant labyrinthine dungeon of Baalzdeep under a big black tower that screams inside the skulls of all who approach. They got the builders in, the gnomes are ready to start, give or take a teabreak or two and random wandering monsters with random RBH stats/powers/hobbies but they need some TRAPS - especially if they mess with arenas/connections.

Any ideas for cool RBH traps ?

Cause my other gaming group are gonna have their characters go a robbin' and....I really haven't wasted enough PCs yet !
