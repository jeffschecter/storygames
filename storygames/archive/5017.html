\n Premise: Repo Man as an HBO series
\n
\nWe had a punk kid going metal who was looking for his real mother and all he had to go on was a make and model of car.
\n
\nAn older repo woman with a steady drug habit and an ex husband who was the punk's probation officer.
\n
\nA smooth womanizing black dude with a fear of commitment and a bad relationship with his ex wife.
\n
\nAnd a macho-man survivalist type with a mustache and a big case of the latents.
\n
\nSide characters included a rival repo operation, a bartender with the same secret crush on macho man that macho man had on him, an apocolypse preaching security guard, the angry girl who works at the porno shop next door and wishes the punk kid would quit hitting on her, and the ex husband/probation officer who's having an affair with the macho man.
\n
\nThere were kidney theives in the pilot!! Goddamn kidney thieves, man!
