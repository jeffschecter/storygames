\n Dreamation 2017 is being held at the Hyatt in downtown Morristown, New Jersey, from February 16\u201319, 2017. That is President's Day weekend, for you USians.

We're hoping to have the biggest, best slate of games available yet. For that to happen, we need you to come and run those games!

Running two indie games through the IGX gets you a free badge. Our Dreamation 2017 hosts provide free nourishment to GMs in the staff suite. Crash space may also be available. Email me if you are in need.

If you're unfamiliar with the IGX at Dreamation, here's a primer

If you'd like to run events for the convention, please fill out this Google Form

We have nearly twenty events already, and more arrive every day. We'll post the current slate of events this weekend at: the IGX schedule page.

If you have questions, please contact me or Kathryn Miller directly.

Please reshare widely. We'd like as many GMs as possible. After a year like 2016, it's good to have something to look forward to.\ufeff
