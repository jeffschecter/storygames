\n We're going to try a little experiment for the Story Games Lounge at ConQuest NW. We're going to have a pickup table with a more-or-less continuous game of In a Wicked Age. We can shuffle players and GMs between chapters, making it easy for people to join in or drop out as other tables get going and end.

Naturally, everybody's invited to come play some Wicked.

Has anyone tried something like this? Do you have any pointers?
