    The Five Elements of Hip-Hop
    \nSetting > Color > {character, system, situation}
    Setting > Character: Beatboxing, B-boying, Tagging
    Setting > System: Beatboxing, B-boying, DJing
    Situation: MCing, DJing

    Setting controls (should control) what kind of character, system and colour that are created. MCing is framing scenes, presenting situations and DJing is controlling the situation, which the system can take care of as well.

    B-boying is the player's response to DJing - when they move to the music - and Beatboxing is when they are moving forwards towards their goal. Tagging is when the characters is making their marks on the world, burning down houses, killing people, falling in love or getting children. That kind of stuff.

    Note how colour is influencing the situation when setting doesn't influence situation at all (besides through the colour). You can play any kind of situation in any setting, but it's always coloured some way.
