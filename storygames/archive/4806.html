    \n It's a gaming wasteland, sadly. I'm registered and (at last count) I knew the other two locals who were registered, as well. Same deal with FindPlay. The RPG Registry seems to generate much more activity (insofar as new members), though the bug-ridden script makes using it to communicate with other members next to impossible. AccessDenied suffers from total lack of maintenance (there are entries the better part of a decade old still archived there as if they were entered yesterday). I'd kill for a gamer database that:

    A. Has voluminous membership.
    B. Is purged of profiles not logged into in more than a year.
    C. Runs on stable, tested, software that works as advertised.
