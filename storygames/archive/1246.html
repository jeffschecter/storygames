The British Museum has an exhibition of The Royal Game Of Ur, which is, apparently, the oldest board game in the world. You can play it online and it's bloody good.

I mentioned this in another thread, but it's worth looking at Ogame, an immensely popular and occasionally compelling online thing. It'd be easy to write something like this - it's just a big database - and I keep wondering how you could do it better.

Looping Louie is a game where a battery-powered plane goes round in a circle. As it goes round, you have to hit it upwards, so that it leaps into the air, missing your counters and hitting your opponents counters. It's fantastic.

Anything else interesting?
