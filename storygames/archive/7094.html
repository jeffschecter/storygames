\n Hey, all. Been a while.

I'm the tabletop head of Con-X-Treme this year - it's a convention aimed at members of the fandom who want to create anime, comics, tabletop roleplaying games, video games, and so on, the website of which can be found here. It features Lots of chances to meet with the Powers That Be, lessons on how to use Flash and Poser, and the like. (As well as silly things. Like Bleach TCG tournaments. And the Sexy Cosplay Competition, not to be confused with the Safe For Work Cosplay competition.)

I would like to invite you all to come and talk about the process of game creation, as well as set up stands to sell metric tons of games.

If any of y'all would be interested, please contact Angel Thacker at angels_store@comcast.net and tell them that I sent you.

It's going to be awesome, and I really would like to give you the chance to teach some new creators the nuts-and-bolts of RPG self-publishing.

Yours,

Elliott Belser
