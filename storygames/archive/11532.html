\n Because I am both insane and obsessed, I was going to try out this pile of shenanigans as a play-by-post game on RPG.net - and I was wondering who else might be interested.

It's Primetime Adventures. It's running the Bliss Stage animated series. Try to keep it above PG-13.

The TV Guide version follows:

Bliss Stage: Love Is My Weapon
Cartoon Network, 11:00 pm
Seven years ago, Earth was devastated by an alien attack from the edges of our understanding. This is the continuing story of the brave teenagers, led by the last adult on the planet, who fought back against the alien Nightmares - with love as their weapon.

There's more to it, of course, which I would be willing to explain. Would anyone be interested? Naturally, the pitch session is going to be a little weird, and I'd want all interested parties in on that before holding a Play-By-Post pitch session in an OOC thread.
