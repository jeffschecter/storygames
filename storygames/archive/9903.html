\n It's happening! The Pirate Jenny booth is a go. We'll be Booth 2023 at GenCon.

Last year at GenCon, Anna Kreider, Julia Ellingboe, Danielle Lewon, Elizabeth Shoemaker and I chatted about how there were so many of us we could totally do a booth full of women game designers. Then it dawned on us, what a great idea!

http://piratejenny-rpgs.blogspot.com/

The full line up is this crew:\n
  • Emily Care Boss of Black and Green Games, with Sign in Stranger (finally!), other Black & Green Games, and also RPG = Role Playing Girl, a zine by and about women in gaming.
    Julia Bond Ellingboe of Stone Baby Games, with Tales of the Fisherman's Wife and Steal Away Jordan.
  • Anna Kreider of Tasty Bacon Games, with Thou Art but a Warrrior and her fantastic original art.
  • Danielle Lewon of Cream Alien Games, with her full version of Kagematsu.
  • Kat and Michael S. Miller, of Incarnadine Press, with Serial Homicide Unit and With Great Power... With Michelle Mishko attending since the Millers can't be there.
  • Terry Hope Romero, long-time gamer and vegan cook extraordinaire, to bring some awesome.
  • Elizabeth Shoemaker and Shreyas Sempat of Two Scooters Press, with Homecoming, one soldier's story, It's Complicated and other Two Scooter Press games.

    Some of us will be there in person, others are helping out ahead of time and from home. But we'll all be glad to join The Forge, IPR, Design Matters and the other small press booths. I chatted with podcasters Rob Bohl of Independent Insurgency and Mickey Schulz of Geek Girls Rule! And I think we're also going to contact the folks at Cannon Puncture, so there should be podcasts aplenty about what we're up to.

    Hope to see you there!

    best,
    Emily Care
    Pirate Jenny
    RPG = Role Playing Girl Zine
