\n So... the standard print sizes at Lulu are not right for my current project, but I'm too lazy to find another printer and deal with the inevitable hassle.

That led me to this thought-chain:

1. Saddle-stitched books are normally bound with two staples, right?
2. What if I took a big paper-cutter and sliced a 6x9" standard book in half, horizontally?
3. Would that create two 6x4.5" books?
4. Would one staple hold each of the two resulting booklets together?
5. Are the staples spaced in such a way that they would be in the middle of the booklets' spines? Or would they be really off-sided?

Anybody have any saddle-stitched 6x9" books from Lulu that they can send me pics of?
