Like Camp Nerdly, I had an idea to do something similar for the west coast types called Donner Party. A place I have in mind is located just outside Truckee, California, up in the Sierra's, very close to Reno, NV.

Before it goes any farther from this point, I'd like to get an idea about how many people would be interested in attending a 2 day (Friday evening through Sunday morning) kind of event. This way when I speak to the people running the place, I can give a rough estimate for possible group rates.

Truckee, Ca is (according to mapquest)...

3 hours, 4 min from San Francisco, CA
7 hours, 22 min from Los Angeles, CA
7 hours, 37 min from Salt Lake City, UT
9 hours, 52 min from Portland, OR
12 hours, 34 min from Seattle, WA
12 hours, 45 min from Phoenix, AZ
14 hours, 30 min from Tucson, AZ

Any takers?
