      \n Burning Wheel - I want to be involved in (ideally, "run") a game that rewards a deep investment in the mechanical underpinnings with deep non-mechanical implications.

      Monsterhearts - I want to play in (not "run") a game where I can explore all of the messy, beautiful implications inherent in Monsterhearts and so much of the inspirational media.

      Anything Free-form/LARP - I want to look in my fellow participants eyes without the constant distracting mediation of character sheets/rule books and just let go.

      Unknown Armies - still on my list, for the last mumble-mumble years. The setting is so amazingly in my wheelhouse.

      Cthulhu Dark - would love to "run" this multiple times to see if I can hone in on my ideal horror GM'ing/scenario style.

      Durance
      Dog Eat Dog

      A Quiet Year
      My Life with Master
      ...basically anything.
