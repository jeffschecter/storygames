  • Rich Stokes
    December 2006
    Just accessories.

    There are three companies I know of that make custom lego figure accessories. I use Lego quite a bit in a lot of RPGs. Players who can't draw can still pick out the elements they want to use for their characters, and they're cheaper than lead minis, can be re-cycled more easily and don't need paining.

    Brick Arms make a range of pretty cool modern weapons. They're really nifty and I need to take some photos of my Mechaton robots sporting AKs and M16s.

    Little Armoury used to make a really awesome range of medieval weapons and a whole bunch of Star Wars blasters. The medieval weapons look very cool, they make an interesting variation on the standard Lego swords and make the PCs look a little more interesting. At the moment they appear to be out of stock of everything except their new Modern range, which is annoying because that means there are now two different competing suppliers of modern weapons. I don't like the Little Armoury modern weapons as much as the Brick Arms ones because they look a bit too realistic, and that jars with the cute, blocky look of the minifigs.
