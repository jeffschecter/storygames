\n What is Reaper Beauties, a Girls' Manga Story Game, you ask?

Let Story Games equal "Let's play X with formal rules that guarantee a rousing story and lots of fun, else your wasted time will be refunded."

Let Girls' Manga equal "Coming of age stories about girls in the style of Japanese comic books."

Let X equal "Shinigami Kagan - the Reaper Beauties, those ordinary girls in a podunk town in Japan with only one Kentucky Fried Chicken franchise to its name - chosen by their friend, the dim-sum delivery girl Kan-chan (who was once Princess Kwan Yin a lifetime ago, but is now the goddess Compassion Herself) to face Hungry Ghosts - who clutch the Hearts of innocents because they have no Hearts of their own to feel the feelings they crave so much - and try to reap them, else they will Suffer or even give their hearts to the Ghosts in the attempt."

I will be holding signings, talks and demonstrations of Reaper Beauties, a Girls' Manga Story Game, across Berkeley and Oakland. To find the nearest signing or find out how to get a copy of Reaper Beauties, E-mail me at elliottbradybelser@hotmail.com.

Namaste.
