I have most of the concept of the Hellboy game figured out, but am having trouble with the characters! I know that character creation is a group event in SotC and I'm not taking that away - but I don't want to have blank slates for a 'con-game'. So, your help is appreciated!

Here's the idea:

In a small town in Massachusetts, the children attend a private high school geared towards the exceptional needs that they have. Arkham High School is well known in the area for its bright but often disturbed teenagers who often move on to the local college, Miskatonic U.

You are one of those teenagers. A senior, in fact. Graduation will be soon - or, it would be, if classes hadn't been halted due to some explosion on campus. And now, the government has sent in a special team to investigate. Except, they're finding out about things they weren't meant to know - things that could keep you from graduating!

I need four high school seniors, unique personalities with something to hide. I'll be leaving some blank room for player decided personality and abilities, but I want something like:

Richard 'Rock' Branson - Extreme athlete, rock climbing is where he got his nickname. He has found something while climbing Mt. Greylock which has been giving him the strangest visions. He caught a glimpse of the team sent to investigate, and has eyes for Liz Sherman.

So, I'll be doing three aspects for the three years of school previous and leaving three for what's 'hidden' about these characters.

So, to continue the example:

[] "It's daring me to climb it!"
[] "What strange and wondrous visions!"
[] Eyes for Liz Sherman

Bonus points for pictures to print out with the character sheets!
