    \n Play Unsafe is excellent!
    I don't know about the other one, but here are other suggestions:

    Play with Intent (also with an alternate layout)

    the "Techniques!" thread (also edited in the form of ebook compendium)

    Sail the Stars (or here) contains a synthesis of the most common "techniques" in the form of 9 handy cards. And it looks like a very nice game (I hope to play it soon)

    the Protocol Primer may be of some interest (and it contains a game)

    the GMZero Introduction Document

    and I think you already know them, but I'll mention Archipelago and Fiction for completeness.
