    \n Sorry, missing links fixed.
    I picked $25 because I figure the average person is going to submit a quarter, or smaller, page sized single character piece. As I have been slowly getting quotes, and actually pieces, for my MicroLite20 project I am working on, this has been the standard price requested by the artists.
    Overall I suppose if people had other terms they would want to consider they would be more than welcome to offer. Other than that knowing how much I am offering draw what you think is worth $25 of your time.
    As far as judging criteria I will be picking the piece I think is the coolest, and would really like to see what ever he artist specifically thinks would be the coolest interpretation. I will also be open to any discussion on the contest entries from folks on the forums and things like that as well to possibly sway my decision.
    As far as medium, that again is up to the artist to decide how best they can portray their vision. I am really just fishing for artistic inspiration on a new fresh take on kobolds.
    I will update the blog post with some additional info regarding these concerns.
