\n Not Lulu vs. other P.O.D. companies.

Let me frame my question a little. I've had lulu make two personal-copy books for me, and I haven't bought anything else from them. They're certainly not of bad quality, but they're not the same quality as, say, any small-press RPG I've bought. So I want to know: If I buy a game from Lulu, will it be of a different quality than if I buy the same game from, say, IPR or my local FLGS?

Basically, I want to buy Grey Ranks, and IPR is out of stock, so I'm thinking of buying it from Lulu.
