    \n As a frequent playtester of this game, I have to say that I love it. The mechanics for creating the kobold village, for dying and bequeathing your abilities to your progeny, and for developing the exterior threats (kingdoms) really take the Dungeon World model and run with it. It is simultaneously simpler and more engrossing than a straight run of DW... and the writing is full of lovely gallows humor.

    I feel it is one of the best games for sitting down with a group and developing a rich, colorful world at the table.
