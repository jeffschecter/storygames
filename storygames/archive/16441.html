\n It kind of got on my nerves just a little as I was browsing Games that it's really become huge lately, and the disparity between Video Game projects and Tabletop Game projects started to become huge.

Anyway, it might have been at user request or something, but now there's a clear Video Game/Tabletop split!

http://www.kickstarter.com/discover/categories/games?ref=sidebar

Okay, so sure RPGs aren't "Board and Card Games" (I would have called them Tabletop, but I'm sure that would be missing something else), but hey it's a solid start.

I do actually go through all Games, but Video and Tabletop, when looking for things to support. But this makes it easier for me: There's some days when I just want to see the new tabletop stuff without all the video games.

Anyway, thought it'd be worth sharing with the other folks, especially those folks like me who have actually had to plan a budget around eventual Kickstarter expenses!
