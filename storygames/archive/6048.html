\n I'm moving, which of course means I'm finding metric tons (tonnes?) of gaming paraphanalia that I forgot I had. Games long out of print, collectable games that never took off. You know the stuff.

While we are waiting for Game Chef for this year, I propose this warmup challenge:

Design a game that incorporates pieces (as in physical artefacts) of games that are either out of print or you have not played in at least two years.

Judging criteria:
Playability
Graphic Design
Use of the pieces (centrality to the game)
Complete use of pieces (using all information on the peice)
Innovation

Games will be DQed for heartbreaking-- Make your own game, don't do Game X, only beter. Each category will be rated 1-5.

Deadline:
3/31/08, 11:59 pm Pacific time, assuming GC isn't going to start before then.

Prizes:
The Golden Spanner award will be given to the overall winner.
Games should be submitted as .pdf's to my email, I'll whisper if you announce your intention.
