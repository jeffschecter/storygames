\n Gen Con is next week, and the Games on Demand crew will be there making games happen! If you'll be at the con, I hope you'll come by and join us. I wanted to make a post for those that have never been and might have questions.

Here are the basics:

The event kicks off at 10am on Thursday, with games running in 2 hour slots pretty much all day through the weekend. The last games start at 12pm on Sunday, wrapping up at 2pm on Sunday.

Each 2-hour slot costs one generic ticket per player. We'll have around 70 GMs running a ton of games, so you're bound to find something you like.

Find us at Indianapolis Marriott Downtown hotel (350 West Maryland St.), 2nd floor, Ballroom 6.

Follow us @games_on_demand for updates during the show.

See http://www.indiegamesondemand.org/ for more details about all of our shows.

What else would you like to know?
