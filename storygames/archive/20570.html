\n We at You Don't Meet In An Inn hope your week has been delightful and that we'll make it even better!

Today we begin playing Viral Games Publishing's Engine Heart!

After the humans left the robots Monkey, Schoolmate, Wallytron, and General Electric Beautifier 5000 have been filling their time as best they can. Now however their community needs them to band together and fix a problem they have been chosen for!

You can find the podcast on itunes here, on not-itunes here, and our RSS.

You can follow us on twitter at Notaninn or email us at YouDontMeetInAnInn@gmail.com

You can also like our facebook page or subreddit for regular updates.

Currently we have contest on for a chance to win an early access key to Blizzard's new FPS Overwatch! To enter share this on facebook with your favourite moment from the show or tweet your favourite moment at us on twitter! Contest ends April 29th!

We hope you enjoy listening!
