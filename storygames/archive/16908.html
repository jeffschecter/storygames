\n For a few years, maybe since late 2009 or early 2010, I have interested in a world-building roleplay. Now I joined a forum, the BattleOn! Forums, maybe about 2008 or 2009, somewhere in the time-frame. So anyway, I was a roleplayer there, and I enjoyed it. One roleplay I was specifically interested in was a world-building roleplay by a user named 'DWeird'. It was "The Island", about players spending 'dice' to create, strengthen, and influence 'power factions'.

Now, DWeird did not come up with this idea alone. He had inspiration from this very place, by Simon Pettersson. Here is where you can view that post:
http://www.story-games.com/forums/discussion/9667/x#Item_0
And here is DWeird's version:
http://forums2.battleon.com/f/tm.asp?m=16333180&mpage=1&key=

It is now 2012, I have not been a member on the BattleOn! roleplay forums for a while. I have tried joining other roleplay websites, only for no one to care for a world-building game. But finally, I came across the PlayDiplomacy forums, and started it. Fortunately for me, it got started and is currently up and running!


So why am I here? To ask if you guys think it is decent, and for any suggestions you may have.

Here is an OOC for it:
http://www.playdiplomacy.com/forum/viewtopic.php?f=59&t=30243
And an IC:
http://www.playdiplomacy.com/forum/viewtopic.php?f=59&t=30253

    \n The game looks like it works and I like sand-boxy stuff. The only problem I have is with some of the terminology. To me, writing out a short story would take a couple days. When you're talking about establishing factions, perhaps it would be more helpful to say something along the lines of "write out a short DESCRIPTION" or a "write a few sentences about the Faction building a city or uniting to avenge a great injustice or discovering the TriForce ..." or whatever. Otherwise, I'd have to play it to find out... and I'm a little behind on my playtesting right now (split shift at "work".) But as soon as I do, I'll let you know!
