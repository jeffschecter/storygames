\n So - I was starting up a Primetime Adventures campaign (which because it's me has the basic premise of Bliss Stage...) and made a TV Guide entry for same:\n
Bliss Stage: Love Is My Weapon
Cartoon Network, 11:00 pm
Seven years ago, Earth was devastated by an alien attack from the edges of our understanding. This is the continuing story of the brave teenagers, led by the last adult on the planet, who fought back against the alien Nightmares - with love as their weapon.
\n...And it occurred to me that it if you could make a compelling TV Guide entry for a game you've made, or want to run, that it would be a very helpful tool for selling people on the idea and/or figuring out what the game is about.

So, what would the TV Guide entry for your game, or your favorite game, look like? What channel would it run on, at what time, and in one paragraph, what is it about?

    Thieves in the Temple
    ABC 10:00 PM
    In a faraway land, a faraway time a ragtag band of beggars, rogues, thieves and mysterious strangers enter the sacred temple grounds they have travelled far and long to reach...but this is just the beginning! What strange things does the ancient temple complex hold, and what do the heroes want? Have they come for redemption or revenge? Will they reach the inner sanctum? Reveal the mystery!
