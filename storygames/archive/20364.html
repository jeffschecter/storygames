After months of designing, i can finally share "The Warring Woods", my first story-game, with the world. Aimed at aspiring writers and actors (but fun for everybody), it's about working together with your friends to create a story that follows the narrative rules. The same ones that apply to most successful stories. Only, you get to do it through the eyes on an animal.

Following this link leads you to a .rar file that contains the following files:

- The manual
- A sequence list
- A character sheet
- A set of 5 different cards that can be used
- A "card overview" file as an alternative for the cards

I'm offering it for free to the story-games community, though i'm considering a "pay what you want" option in the future. That way i can start working on an expansion pack called "The Savage Savannah" that includes animals for the Southern hemisphere. For now, i just hope you enjoy it!

Be sure to let me know what you thought of it. Your opinions can only make it better!

Have fun!
