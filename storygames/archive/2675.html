You'd think after they'd heard my ascerbic voice and heard my enthusiastically crotchety opinions, people would avoid recording anything I said. But for some reason, The Gamestas interviewed me a while back and posted the interview as part of their podcast. We talked a bunch about the hows and whys of Shock: and why rocketboots are a world-changing technology.

\n

Listen to the other ones, too. Keith Senflowski was interviewed more than once, no doubt because he kept calling in the middle of the night to ask what Spike was wearing, and Luke "The Six Million Calorie Man" Crane's on there, too, as well as Clinton R. "The R Stands for Quality" Nixon.

\n

Spike and Tyler run a fun show.

\n

I also tagged along on an interview last night between Emily Care Boss, and Paul Tevis and Brennan Taylor, the Voices of the Revolution. You'll have to wait a bit to hear that one; I don't know when it will be posted on the Voice of the Revolution site, though I bet it'll be next week or something. Emily talks about Breaking the Ice, Shooting the Moon, and an exciting weird upcoming game, while I talk about why Shock: isn't GMless after all and I touch on my upcoming projects.

\n

So now, word's out. Download them while you can, before the ascerbic voice and crotchety opinions keep me from ever getting interviewed again!
