\n Another one of those resolution mechanics I find intriguing, but will never have time to work with.

Dice are rolled by banking them off a wall and onto a target. The location on the target determines accuracy, the number thrown determines power, so that we have an X,Y decision maker. Changing the size of the target space alters the outcome of the task in a predictable fashion, but the die roll remains random.

Is anyone doing this already? Would anyone like to?
