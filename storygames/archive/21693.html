    \nTales of Entropy does an interesting thing where each game is based on a scenario and a pregenerated character, but the others are made up session-by-session. However, all the other characters, first of all, play off the single pregen, and, second, are usually/often based on other characters mentioned in the writeup of the pregen (for example, their family member, their rival, their enemy, their lover). It's an interesting middle ground between a pregenerated scenario and an open canvas to play on.
