    Did you tell your store about this: http://www.bits-and-mortar.com/ ?
    \nMargaret Weiss Productions does not participate in the Bits n Mortar program. This particular game was released in a quick-play version several,months ago that has been in the retail channel for a while, but the full version of the game is months late. It's been pretty frustrating as a retailer to watch people bail on reorders because of MWPs dropping the ball on this one. Smallville had availability issues for a little while as well. We'd really love it if MWP came on board with BnM, anyone who speaks to them pleas let them know you'd buy it locally if they weren't offering us an inferior product to the one they sell directly!

    That said, I am looking forward to FINALLY having it, if only because of how meta it is to stock an RPG licensed from a TV show that the head writer happily admits is based on his DnD games.

    -Jim C (retailer by day)
