    \n
    \n
    \n Spooky,

    There's an experience in FM that concerns spreading, critiquing and killing trends (we call it Thin Slicing). There's also another (Ephemera) that's used to generates memes that can "infect" the populace (basically this experience covers the production of artistic works, as well as handling journalism or oration -- be it philosophical, religious or political).

    Oh, and Mark, we also have rules to creatre your own Stephenson-inspired tech (smart-wheels, katanas, rat-things) and social systems (like phyles and burbclaves).
