\n Skulduggery is a new rule set based on the Dying Earth. It's designed for quickfire games of guile and persuasion usually comedic, and includes four pre-defined settings with pregen characters on cards. Included with the setting are:
\n
\nYes Wing: politics and bureaucracy
\nSkulduggery & Crossbones: pirates
\nCasting Call: high school musical
\nIf Space Permits: interstellar traders
\n
\nAnother setting might be the long con, like Oceans 11, the Sting or Hustle.
\n
\nAny other ideas?
