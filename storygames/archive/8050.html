\n The Guild of Blades Retail Group is hosting a FREE game design and independent publishing workshop on Sunday, November 23rd at our new Game Retail Store in Madison Heights
\n
\n( http://www.guildofblades.com/gobstore-madisonheights-mi.php ),
\n
\nMichigan. The workshop will run from noon to 5 or 6 pm and will go over the nuts and bolts of designing games destined for independent publication, plus a detailed overview of the many aspects of operating a small press game publishing company and attendees can see the GOB Retail POD operation first hand. Hosted by veteran game designer Ryan Johnson, a partner in the Guild of Blades Publishing Group, a prolific small press publisher since 1996.
\n
\nSeating is limited to the first 20 attendees. To register drop by the store at 28736 John R Rd, Madison Heights, MI. For questions, give us a call at 248-430-4980.
