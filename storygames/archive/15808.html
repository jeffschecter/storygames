\n After some severe frustration trying to explain the Interim Stage character creation rules, I hit on the idea of making an actual, physical deck of Relationship Cards for each Pilot Archtype.

So, the Innocent Sweetheart deck had as the top card a picture of Sara Smith and a male Innocent with a big bold Innocent Sweetheart at the top and a note at the bottom saying "Add 1 Trust to any one Relationship you deal," with the number 1 in a diamond. Then there was a card saying "The Innocent Sweetheart has a Relationship with _______, with a {3} Intimacy" - the 3 in a heart, and an explanation of what that means - "and [2] Trust." And so on for the rest of the relationships, and the other Archtypes.

Worked like a charm! Character creation took about 30 minutes, 40 tops, instead of the hours-long slog it tends to be without them. So I made up a quick .pdf of the same. Need to figure out how to host it so people can just download it...

Anyway. It has 6 sheets, each one with one Archtype, it's Relationships, and appropriate Anchor (in that it's a good choice for that kind of Pilot), and one N Pilot C. Print them, cut them, sort them into piles by Archtype and just hand them out to players.

Mention they can't can't deal Relationships to characters they control, that if you give a Relationship to one pilot they can't give one to you, and about the bonus Intimacy die for immediate family and you're good to go.
