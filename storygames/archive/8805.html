\n Spring is making its tentative advances, which means that its almost time for the third annual JiffyCon Boston! JiffyCon is a organized one-day gaming meetup for the purpose of hanging out, meeting new folks and playing fun games!

What: JiffyCon Boston
When: Saturday, March 21st, 9am-7pm
Where: Unity Church of God, Somerville, MA
Cost: 10$ admission, covering cost of space rental, snacks and a pizza lunch.

We need events! If you are interested in running a game, please email me (n-dot-d-dot-paoletta-at-gmail-dot-com) with the event title, a short descriptive blurb, the number of players you can handle, and whether you prefer the morning (9.30-1) or afternoon (2-6) slot. Events will go up on the website as they are received.

JiffyCon is historically slanted towards small-press and independently published games, but we've also had sessions of D&D, Traveller, and other "traditional" games, so feel free to submit anything you want to run.

Preregistration! You may preregister in order to ensure a slot in games that you really want to play - and it helps us out by giving a rough head count for food purposes. Preregister by sending me an email with your name and the game(s) you wish to register for.

More details (including directions to the venue and a list of events submitted) are available at the website: jiffycon.com.

Feel free to send me or Dev (dev-dot-purkayastha-at-gmail-dot-com) an email if you have any addition questions. Also, this thread!

Excited!
