\n Hi there folks,

Myself and some friends are in the process of putting together a "magazine show" styled video podcast. We have put together a very short test reel mainly to experiment with the editing software and distribution channels. While the episode itself is a bit ropey it has given us a whole heap of useful insights for Episode One.

Any feedback and ideas would be appreciated, and feel free to post elsewhere and feed back from other sources. Also, if you are in the UK and would like to take part, either on or off screen, then please contact us!

Cheers for watching and I'll pop up a post once Episode 1 goes live!

Keith.
