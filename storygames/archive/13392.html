    \n I'm going to recommend a couple of shows with misleading names.

    The Jank Cast is an excellent show with interesting discussion of roleplaying topics by some really smart, funny people. They talk about indie games a lot. They are not janky.

    Dice + Food + Lodging is a show where a guy interviews people about games and does so really well. There was an early focus on travel and RPGs I believe, but now it seems to have moved away from that.

    \n I listen to the order 66 podcast, i enjoy the hosts and guests they have. it fairly exclusively covers saga edition star wars. as I dont play that anymore I mostly use it as a source of information about how "main stream" rpg players play rpgs.
