\n Based on a great discussion after the AP at HarperCon 08 last weekend, I wrote up a new version of my UFO-contactee game The Believers that I think will play even better! Thanks to Marcy, Matthew, Jackson and Brendan for some great stories and even better feedback.

It only takes 15 minutes to play, and is a great exercise to get your creative muscles working at the start of a longer gaming session. So give it a try with your group and let me know what you think!
