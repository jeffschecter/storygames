\n "Harnessing innovative technology from the mind of video game legend Will Wright
and storytelling expertise from television hitmaker Albie Hecht, Creation
Studios is looking to revolutionize the way TV is made. And that's by including
you in the creative process of a new television series called Bar Karma--the
first show of its kind. Utilizing Will Wright's StoryMaker Engine, you can
develop the stories and characters you want to see. You'll also be able to vote
on ideas submitted by other members of the Creation Studios community. The most
popular ideas will become contenders for the actual plot developments on the
show, written and filmed by our professional production team. It's not reality
television; it's real television made by real people, including you."
