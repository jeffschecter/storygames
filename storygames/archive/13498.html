\n I was thinking that AW might make a good base for a game inspired by Glee, Election, Breakfast Club, Fast Times @ Ridgemont High, Friday Night Lights, etcetera (and our own sordid pasts)...the high school replaces the hardhold and violence probably results in shame rather than harm...fronts could be prom, big games, academic decathalon, finals...
Coolness under fire -> Coolness under pressure
Go aggro, seduce/manipulate, reading people & sitches all could work as written.
But Weird people shouldn't tap into the maelstrom, obviously - people with high Weird are the loners, and burnouts, I reckon. I'm not sure what the base Weird move should be - maybe "When You Take Drugs"...
Might want to split "sharp" into "sharp" and "smart" - smart would be for knowing stuff, taking tests, academic decathlon and dissecting fetal pigs. sharp would be as per AW.

That said, honestly don't know if I could talk anyone into playing such a thing, and hacking AW seems like a lot of work, so I thought I'd just make a SG forum game out of it in the vein of "Custom moves make the game".

So, if you're game, take someone else's playbook move and define it, and invent a new move. And you can invent new playbooks as well.

Here's a few to get started:

Head Cheerleader: "Peer Pressure" When you manipulate with two other cheerleaders present...

King of the Geeks: "Science!" When you invent something...

Bully: "Swirly" When you go aggro in a bathroom...

Quarterback: "Man of Letters" When you wear your letterman jacket...

Maybe faculty would have playbooks as well...?
