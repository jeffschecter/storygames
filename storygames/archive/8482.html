    \n I'm a bit late with HG,WT episodes. I listen to a variety of gaming podcasts. When I have collected enough of them (close to 700MB), I burn the episoded on a mp3 cd, and then listen to them while commuting in my car. This puts a time gap between the moment new episodes are released and when I listen to them. So, I still have to listen to the twelve episodes of Christmas, and to the following ones.

    But, in general, I much prefer news and reviews over personal musings. Your personal preferences, opinions and thoughts (which I appreciate) show anyway, plus I'm informed on new games and can decide if a game interests me enough to purchase it. The thing I always liked of HG,WT is that it covers (covered?) roleplaying games, boardgames and even light wargames, pretty much the full spectrum of my gaming. That, and the fact the sound quality is very high compared to the average gaming podcast (to a non-english listener, sound quality really makes a difference).
