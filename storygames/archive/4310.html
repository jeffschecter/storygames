\n I don't know if this is old or not, but I've just found c-jump. According to the website it "eliminates intimidation of many kids and their parents, bored by the mention of "computer programming", often associated with visions of geeky guys glued to their computers. c-jump reveals simple programming terms in a cool way!"

But it looks like this:


Now doesn't that look fun? :P

I'm kinda thinking a boardgame challenge coming over me... Is it possible to design a game (board, card, or RPG) that could teach kids some of the core skills of programming and actually be fun and non-intimidating at the same time?
