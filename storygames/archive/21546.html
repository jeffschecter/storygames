    \n It's probably too late now, in a sense, but i think it would be better to continue a single thread with Monsterhearts questions, and to add new questions there. It would make it a great resource for people once it was done, too.

    How do characters take Strings on NPCs? The same way they do on each other - basically, when a move tells them to.

    For example, let's say Fiona the Vampire rolls the "turn on someone on" move against the Principal at her school, Mr. Smithers. She scores a 10+, so she gets a String on Mr. Smithers. Nothing fancy or tricky here.
