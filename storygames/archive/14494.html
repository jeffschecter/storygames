\n My writing partner (Travis Heermann) and I are in an online contest and we have to review two scripts. So I got one called Dinosaur Girl (name has nothing to do with the genre of the script). Below is the logline and it just screamed Fiasco.

Log Line:
A distraught father wants revenge on the unknown head of the drug company who inadvertently killed his daughter, complications arise when the father blackmails an employee to do the deed for him and it turns out to be the unknown man in question.
