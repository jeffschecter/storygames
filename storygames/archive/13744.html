    \n It was kind of a sweet (as in "touching") episode. And it didn't treat gamers as diseased mutants, but I already expected that from the show.

    Chang (aka *Doctor* Ken Joeng from Greensboro, NC Reprazent!!) in blackface was an awesome send-up. I immediately had flashbacks to Chris Chinn noting the fucking whitetards at GenCon in blackface, complete with "a good ol-fashioned drow hangin' " of a mousy white chick in blackface *IN FRONT OF THE FOOD COURT, WHICH IS STAFFED PRIMARILY BY BLACK PEOPLE*. Hoping that Drow Cosplayers take note of that.

    The group not setting Lines and Veils, and thus Annie playing out the seduction, was hi-larious. It actually kinda uncomfortably reminded me of some con games where the lines and veils discussion never happened.

    Not as awesome as the Paintball episode, but very little TV ever comes to that level of perfection.
