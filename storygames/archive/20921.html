Berlin XVIII was a "cult" french RPG with 3 editions between 1998 and 1995. And it's back in 2017, powered by the apocalypse.

It's a kind a low-tech europunk universe, where you play cops in a dirty 80 millions people city, capital of a european federation just keeping afloat while most of the world have collapsed.
