    I'm a little disappointed by this thread. I thought the casting was the probably the weakest part of Game of Thrones. Of course they are books so the casting is never going to be perfect but I thought it was just shocking. Too many white people, they were all way too old. In complete contrast to my impression of GRRMs books the men all look to soft and the women mostly look to hard.

    My impression is that many of the people of the seven kingdoms are various flavours of white. But the Targaryens (sp?) should be more exotic IMHO. And what was with Drogo? He looked like some kind of beefcake in asian drag from the 1950s! That makeup... yeesh. Cerci Lannister is supposed to be gorgeous... and Tyrion is supposed to be ugly and with two different eyes! Is a contact lens that difficult. The Hound, had a bald spot instead of a half burned face /palm, although his helmet was very nice.

    I'm guessing they're going to rough them all up a bit as times get tougher, but yeh it left me pretty disappointed and I'm only talking about the casting here. Ah well. The only way is up (I hope).
