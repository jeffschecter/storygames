    \n I'd be totally up for some PTA. Or some sort of freeform hippie crap. Either one.

    Actually, thinking about it, the best road-playing experience I had was back in college: We were driving from Green Bay to Madison, and on the way we did a totally freeform diceless description-and-roleplay only Shadowrun game. I was an Orc Fixer, by best bro was a human street samurai. It was really slick. The lack of randomizer and total fiat on behalf of the GM ("Toober") worked well, mainly because he was really on the ball and not into dis-empowering us. It was a fun, memorable experience.
