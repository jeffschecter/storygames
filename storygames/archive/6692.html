\n So, in Bleakworlds, you're an escaped criminal, trying to make a new life for yourself in the bleak worlds of the Outer Rim of the galaxy.

But before you do that, there's these things you've got to deal with. For example: "My ex-husband", "The jury who put me away", "My old accomplices", "The money".

Note that they're phrased as things (you need to deal with "my ex-husband") rather than goals ("To kill my ex-husband"), so there is ambiguity about how you deal with them.

What can I call these things? I'm totally stuck. What's a good word?

Graham
