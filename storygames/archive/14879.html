It felt indulgent and I knew I was taking up a table we needed for other games, but we had fun and were able to finish the story.

    \n As an aside, I find what is satisfying for me personally depends on the number of players. Generally I prefer 1 hour per 1 player (not including GM) for many games with GMs. For example, a 3 hour Dogs game with 3 players is my favorite. I find games where conflicts are resolved with a relatively quick mechanic can cut the time down to 30-45 minutes per player.
