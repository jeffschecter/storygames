Hi folks,

My latest little project called 'Reel Adventures' can be downloaded from my blog at RPGlab.

Reel Adventures is a game about the sort of storytelling you get in action and adventure films. Player's take on the roles of the heroes of the story, framing scenes one at a time for their heroes, their allies or the nemesis of the story. Player's spend impact to manipulate the story fighting against the ever growing momentum of the plot as it threatens to overcome their heroes as well as their own personal nemesis.

I would appreciate any feedback you guys could give me, especially on how you think the momentum vs. impact mechanic works. I am also thinking about allowing the players to flesh out the plot nemesis like they do their heroes over the course of the game. Please take a gander and tell me what you think.

Cheers
Iain
