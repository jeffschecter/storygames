Here's the "back cover style blurb"\n
You are a maid, having worked dutifully for the Saionji family for several years. You are also a shy albino princess who works for the yakuza, and has been trained with the three-section staff. Your peer Missy is an outgoing young maid with freckles, with a streak of being greedy for sweets, who also happens to be a military cyborg.

Your master is a kind teenager who lives in the mansion alone, but is a bit of a train otaku. He is also a cursed werewolf. With amnesia.

Somewhere between doing the laundry and preparing lunch, the master is kidnapped by ninjas. They escape through the basement of the mansion, which contains a portal to the Netherworld. It's up to you to get him back before dinnertime.

All in a day's work for a maid!

Maid is an light comedy anime-themed tabletop role-playing game for three or more players. The very easy to learn rules-light system, complete with random events which drive the story forward, will have you playing the game with friends only fifteen minutes after opening the book. Maid is also the first ever Japanese role-playing game to be translated and released in English.
\nAnyway, the Maid RPG is now available for purchase, in both Print and PDF.
