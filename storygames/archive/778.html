Hello, all, :)

So, I'm in need of some recommendations.

A few friends and I are going to go away for a whole week and we plan to take two or three games with us. The idea is to choose two and play through nine sessions of each over those nine days. The exact number of people is still unknown, but it will be either three, four or five players, no more, no less.

These are people who have played everything from D&D to Capes, although we are a tad on the side of tired of "traditional" RPGs. Of the new stuff, we've had the most success with TSoY.

What I'm asking is, if it were you, what game would you want to bring along that you would want to play nine sessions of. Also, what game, if any, have you enjoyed greatly in the past, but would feel would be inapropriate for a nine session stretch.

I know you "don't know me" and "don't know what I like" and all, but I'm asking, if it were you, what would you do.

Cheers,
J.
