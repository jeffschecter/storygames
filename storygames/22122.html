With this site closing, it's unlikely that all the site members will migrate to the same places of the internet.

When Google Plus was closing, Kit La Touche made this cool little website to help people figure out where other users were going to land. It's located here: https://wheretofind.me/

The idea is that you list what internet platforms you can be found on. Then you share a link to your profile with the people you like and trust, so they know where to find you. For example, my profile is here: https://wheretofind.me/@nickwedig

So I thought that this might be a useful little tool for people to figure out where everyone was going once Story Games closed.
