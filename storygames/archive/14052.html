    \n I will be there running the Hamster Press booth in the hall. I'll have a bunch of new board game story games (Jump the Shark - Cthulhu Madness) and my old stuff as well as a nice selection of stuffed animals and hand made hand puppets.

    I have a crew of GMs out in the board game hall running games for me: Mongolian Goat Rodeo, Thunder Hamsters and the Temple of Cheese, Cthulhu Madness, and Jump the Shark.

    I'm looking forward to seeing how the new hall works out. I walked the loading dock area back in the winter and can see how it could be good (or a screaming nightmare) to get in and out of. By not using the west halls (the old dealers area) they are shifting the center of the con closer to the Circle Center Mall and Union Station. The new additions to the convention center look good. I think this will work out well for everyone.

    Chris Engle
