    \n Craft stores like Hobby Lobby or Michaels would have them. Those are probably the artist stores Jason was talking about. I really don't see them being carried at a real artist supply store like Pearl or Utrecht.

    Pet stores might have something similar in the aquarium section.

    Dollar stores might be worth investigating.
