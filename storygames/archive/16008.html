\n This actually works pretty well.

Replace Diversion with Cook. Cook covers the science of drug manufacture, including equipment maintenance, substituting ingredients on the fly, synthesis, etc. It's those last two that are why Walt has Cook 5 and Jesse has Cook 4. (Minor spoiler: Poor Gale has 4, but in a different way.)

Civilians are still normal family members, but Professionals are either Criminals or Cops. They use the same stress tables.

Unlike in normal Blowback, you can go from Civilian to Professional by Breaking Bad. State what you're doing to cross over. This stresses every single relationship you have to within one point of breakage, so narrate how that happens. Switch stress charts. You can never go back.


The Agency is The Cartel.

That should cover it.
