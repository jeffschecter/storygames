    \n Evolution Pulse: Hyonos Character Archetype

    Hekath are the evil, the darkness of human soul and divine wrath that struck on our old world. You travel between LostZones searching for miserable souls to save, and you sacrificed your mundane life for a higher purpose: safety of the last remnants of humanity. Doomed to a life of struggle and suffering, you know your choice was the only one possible, because your sacrifice will allow many others to survive. Hekath are pure, feral and devastating hatred. You don't have any mercy, and you erect yourself as the bastion against the end of time.

    \n Evolution Pulse (coming this summer): Proxy Character Archetype

    The world in the LostZones is lost and rotten , at the mercy of Hekath's wrath. You saw what happens and you saw what the last LostH must withstand. Above all, you saw the world inside the LostZones. You have some memories, everyone has some, but yours aren't true, not yet.
    Nirvana shaped a life for you and you're earning it piece after piece. A life fine-tuned on your genetic abilities and talents. For this reason you travel between LostZones on a mission as an Executor. You feel sympathy for the LostH, who wouldn't? Everyone deserves a better life, or a less cruel ending at least.
    You have a clear goal: your prefabricated memories are the only part of humanity you can afford for yourself, so that someday you will turn it into a real life.

    Evolution Pulse Preview: http://rpg.drivethrustuff.com/product/144528/Evolution-Pulse
