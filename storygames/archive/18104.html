    \n Hah! I was just coming here to make an official post. Thanks, Mathalus.

    So, yes: registration for Go Play NW 2013 is open! That link goes directly to the registration page; also keep an eye on the Go Play NW blog / site for updates.

    Here's the official announcement:

    We\u2019re very excited to announce that Go Play NW 2013 will be held Friday, June 28 to Sunday, June 30, once again in the wonderful Campion Hall at Seattle University! We had a fantastic time in Campion Hall last year and we\u2019re thrilled to be able to return this year.

    Once again there will be overnight rooms available in Campion Hall itself. It\u2019s a really good deal for both out-of-towners and locals as well. Check out the ticketing page for details. Plus the great combination of the ballroom, lobby, and dorm floor lounges for gaming spaces. (And that cool outdoor garden deck area as well, if the weather\u2019s more cooperative this year.)

    Second, as part of our deal with the University, lunch in Seattle University\u2019s Cherry Street Market cafe is included in the price of admission for all attendees. If you\u2019re renting a room on campus for the con, you\u2019ll also get breakfast at the cafe.

    Finally, as with previous years, there will be a Friday night feast in the main hall.

    We urge everyone who\u2019s coming to buy your tickets early. Even though we\u2019re not having an early registration deadline this year, paying early helps us pay our bills and deposits in advance of the event!

    Feel free to contact us with questions or comments at info at goplaynw.org.
