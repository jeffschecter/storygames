\n The first Saturday of June is the "Day of Fudge", and this year it lands on June
\n5th! For those who don't know the DoF is a day where we encourage Fudge fans to
\nrun a Fudge game out in public to introduce others to the game. It was started
\nlast year, and it was great fun for many of us who ran both public and private
\ngames.
\n
\nWhat do people plan to do for this year's DoF? It can be anything that you like.
\nMake Fudge characters and share them with others. Email links to your favorite
\nFudge related web sites to your gaming buddies (no spamming strangers though).
\nBest of all - play Fudge with your friends (both old and new)!
\n
\nI look forward to another great "Day of Fudge", and I am eagerly waiting to hear
\nwhat others are planning to do this year. A word of advice - start booking your
\nevents at your favorite local gaming stores now!
