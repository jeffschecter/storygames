    \n It's about 2.5 hours from JFK...

    1. Take Air Train from JFK to Jamaica Station (Sutphin Boulevard) - 20 minutes
    2A. Take Long Island Rail Road towards Penn Station to Penn Station - 30 minutes
    2B. Alternatively in place of the Long Island Rail Road above, take the E subway towards WOLD TRADE CENTER to 34th Street / Penn Station - 45 minutes
    3. Take NJ Transit, Morris & Essex Line Train towards Dover to Morristown - 70 minutes
    4. Walk 12 minutes to Hyatt Morristown, 3 Speedwell Avenue, Morristown, NJ 07960

    It's also about 2.5-3 hours from LGA...

    1. Take Q72 Bus towards Rego Park / Junction Blvd / Queens Blvd to Junction Blvd / Roosevelt Ave - 20 minutes
    2. Take the 7 subway towards TIMES SQUARE to Times Square - 42 St - 30 minutes
    3. Take the 1 subway towards SOUTH FERRY TERMINAL to 34th Street / Penn Station - 42 St - 5 minutes
    4. Take NJ Transit, Morris & Essex Line Train towards Dover to Morristown - 70 minutes
    5. Walk 12 minutes to Hyatt Morristown, 3 Speedwell Avenue, Morristown, NJ 07960

    All of the above depends on transfers and double check with the MTA (www.mta.info) in case there are any train changes.
