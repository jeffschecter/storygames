\n Are you coming to PAX East? Do you like fun? Let's play some games!

It's time to get organized about Games on Demand for PAX East. The event isn't as well-established as at other shows, so we'd love to have your help pulling things together. Come play games with us!

Luke Crane tells me that the exhibitor and gaming spaces will probably be close together,and that the booths will ring the free play area as they have in the past. This is a great setup, and should really work well to introduce people to games that they can then go buy. If you're involved with a booth, you might consider offering a coupon for playing at Games on Demand.

I'm not sure how many tables we'll have yet, but I know that we need to try to cover them from 10am to Midnight each day of the show. That means we'll need a bunch of people running games, and I'm hoping those people are you!

If you're interested in GMing or helping to organize the event, drop me a line (steve@segedy.com). I'll update here and elsewhere as we get more information and a sense of the schedule.

    \n A few updates:

    We've got a great crew of organizers helping to put Games on Demand together at PAX- Luke Crane got the ball rolling, and now we have Will Hindmarch, Terry Romero and John Stavropolous on board to run games and coordinate the event. We'll have between 6-8 tables in the midst of the Tabletop area, near the Compleat Strategist booth and many others.

    We also have confirmation that we should have a limited number of special badges for GMs who commit to running games on the schedule. If you don't already have a badge and would like one, let us know as soon as possible and we'll get you set up!

    Posted By: e4mafiaIs there a general consensus on what, aside from D&D, constitutes a game that is not "indie" and therefore would not be appropriate to run at GoD?
    At PAX it may not be as big a problem, considering that tabletop gaming is a smaller part of the show. That said, a good general rule is that if the game is big and well known enough that it has an established presence elsewhere at the Con, it probably doesn\u2019t need to be at GoD.

    For PAX, I know that the Burning Wheel crew, MWP productions, and all the gang at the Indie Bazaar are going to have demo tables running all day and night. I still hope/expect to see those games (BW, Mouse Guard, Marvel RPG, Apocalypse World, Mobile Frame Zero, etc.) getting play at Games on Demand.
