[Seattle] RECRUITING New Group: Seattle/Ballard area
================================================

Looking to form a group in the next 1-3 weeks.
Saturday morning/afternoons
Group Size 3-5 players max.
Heavily story-focused.
But system/rules a fun and important aspect of gaming.


REQUESTS:

Commitment.
We all have lives, so missing a game now and again is cool. But someone who is skipping out every couple weeks is probably not right for this group.

Consideration.
Remember childhood sandbox rules, the ones you were supposed to follow? Be nice, share, don't kick sand in other's faces. Gaming is best when everyone's helping everyone have a good time.

Communication.
Be interested and excited to participate in email threads and wiki updates for the campaign.

Creativity.
Have ideas! Dont just be 'I like to play everything. Bring something to the table. Co-create and "believe" in the game fun.



Most likely some people will be invited to some one off events (original D&D Red Box, Supers, etc...) and we can figure out who fits and who doesn't. The goal is to develop the best group dynamics possible, so a couple of sessions might be necessary. Once that is determined, we can plan a campaign.

Also, be open minded. If a game/campaign just isn't working, be open to allowing it to change and try something new. The goal is to have a long lasting fun, but sticking with a sub-par game to maintain longevity is not fun.




CAMPAIGN IDEAS?
We have so many!! But on the top of the list (in brief) are:

- Vampire the Dark Ages (Storyteller system)
- White Wolf Modern Nights: Vampire, Werewolf, or Mage
- Geist the Sin-Eaters (Storyteller system)
- Magic the Gathering (using Pathfinder rules)
- Ars Magica (Ars 4th-5th system)
- Fading Suns (system?)
- Hellboy or Buffy (Buffy Uni-system)
