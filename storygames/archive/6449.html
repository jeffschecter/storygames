\n In Medical Hospital, at the beginning or end of a session, you can choose a special scene type. Where scenes during the rest of the session are either introspective, focused on relationships, or dealing with medical challenges, you can start or end with a big, flashy, attention-grabbing scene. The helicopter full of seeing eye dogs crashes into the opthalmologic wing! A semi full of mining explosives jackknifes on top of a bus full of Russian prostitutes!! It's a huge event that involves every character and sets either a framework for the session's events or a cliffhanger for the next session.

So it's not a relationship scene, or a surgical scene, it's a __________ scene. The perfect word eludes me. Help!
