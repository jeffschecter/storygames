\n Well, I've gotten almost everything set up - a room, vacation from work. I'm waiting on the confirmation for my schedule for gaming (and just noted that the Mist-Robed Gate on Sunday I'm signed up for might not go off, darn it! I wanted to play that game so badly! ), and in the meantime, I'm checking my options on getting out to Morristown.

I'm in Philly, and unless I'm completely mistaken getting out there via train is going to be a pain in the butt. Getting into Jersey via the R-7 to Trenton is fairly straightforward, but checking NJT's site shows that Trenton and Morristown are on completely different lines.

Their suggestion is that I take the NE Corridor Line to Secaucus Transfer, switch over to the M&E Morris Line from there, on yet another train, which will mean 2 transfers between 3 trains total.

That seems a lot of wasted E <-> W travel, when I really just want to be going straight N from Trenton.

It's listing about 2+ hours to get from Trenton to Morristown via that route, and I know it takes about an hour to get to Trenton via the R-7, so I'm looking at 3 1/2 hours-ish. Ugh.

Any New Jerseyites have any advice on getting from Trenton to Morristown more easily? Is there a quicker bus that might go N from Trenton towards Morristown? Or have I already found pretty much the only route?

Thanks in advance for any help anyone can give me!
