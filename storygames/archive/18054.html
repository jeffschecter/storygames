    \nLet the rumor-mongering begin!     \nAfter playing 7 Wonders, I went back and looked at With Great Power: I drew out an alternate resolution mechanic, using basically a print-and play deck of 108 cards (didn't actually complete the cards themselves, just wrote out in a text doc what numbers/effects I'd put on them). Each card has three stats (top/middle/bottom), representing the beginning arcs, middle arc, and last arc. A few cards have five sets of stats, one for each arc.
    Plus, with P&P cards, you can create cards with special effects, a little bit of German board(card)-game injection.

    BTW: If anyone REALLY wants a copy of the game as-is, I have two, and thus would be willing to part with one of the two copies. I'll sell for $10 plus shipping.
