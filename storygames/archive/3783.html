\n my mother sent me some old photographs she had found in an album, including this one of me reading an article about "The Ninja' character class which I believe was in Best of the Dragon, Vol I.

It's probably one of the only records of me role playing, even if its not of play. What's depressing is I've gotten so old I can;t even figure out how old I was in the pic. I suppose I was in highschool and the other books on the floor are my schoolwork. Although it's conceivable that it was taken when I was on break from college, looking over old stuff from my closet.

There was a recent thread on photos of current story gaming. Anyone have any old photographs from your earlier role-playing?

- alexandria, va, circa mid/late 80s
