    \n Hmm, Secret Trees. Something like:

    Secret of Mighty Blow
    Your character can strike with extreme might. Spend as many dice of Vigor as you like to increase the harm of a successful blow in combat.

    Secret of Divine Strike
    Whenever you spend multiple dice of Vigor to increase the Harm of a successful blow in combat, roll your Pray afterwards. You immediately recover (up to your maximum Pool size) as many dice as you have successes. Briefly describe the prayer you made earlier that day for help in battle.

    Yes?

    \n From Warhammer, I always liked the professions best. You could rejig that for TSoY by changing the way that you pick up Secrets and abilities, fairly easily. Just have each profession open up certain options, and keep the idea that you must get all those advances before you move on to the subsequent profession.
