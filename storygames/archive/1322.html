[Abulafia] How would a contest even work?

Dave Younce
September 2006 edited September 2006 in Directed Promotion
So, I posted about Abulafia over on ENWorld, and to my surprise Morrus, the guy who runs things over there, posted it on the front news page (thus tripling my traffic overnight) and said:
Actually, I think this is so cool, that I've had an idea. How would you like to run a competition of some kind to encourage new content? I'll provide the prizes (ENP pdfs) and mention the competition in the news if you handle the specifics of the competition itself.
But, I don't know how I would make such a thing work. Best new table? Best additions to existing table? The whole point of Abulafia is that things are meant to be collaborative, so I'm having trouble coming up with contest parameters that make sense within that spirit.

Any suggestions?

    Abulafia rocks! Is there a complete list of the generators somewhere?

    You could do category based contests, where everybody is invited to make new ones in areas that need to be fleshed out. E.g.: Fall 06 Contest: mythologies and gods. The winner could be chosen by peer review, where everyone who takes part gets one vote for someone *not themself*. You could ask for prizes to be donated before you start to get you through a bunch of contests. I'd be happy to donate a game or two! And winners could choose one among the stuff donated.

    And if you could incorporate some of Abulafia's techniques in the entries, maybe you get extra credit. : )
