\n So, my game of war drama, The of War, is almost finished. Since english isnt my native language there are bound to be some typos, bad grammar and so on. I really need someone to read it through, pointing out errors. Its been proofread several times, this is the last clean up.

Edit: Im looking for proofreaders. If youre intrested, just answer here, and Ill send you a PDF or whatever suits you.

\u201cWar is the realm of uncertainty; three quarters of the factors on which action in war is based are wrapped in a fog of greater or lesser uncertainty.\u201d
- Clausewitz

Uncertainty.

Why are we at war? Why am I here? Who am I? How will the war change me? Is it worth it?

These aren\u2019t the question that comes to mind when you hear the expression \u2018fog of war\u2019 but that\u2019s how I chose to interpret the phrase. Uncertainty regarding the war. What are we fighting for? What are we dying for? Play and find out.

The Fog of War is a roleplaying game about war. In particular it\u2019s about American wars. It\u2019s about the soldiers who fight them and what they need to do and who they need to be in order to be a good soldier, to function as a soldier. The game begins before the soldiers enlist and we follow them through training, through their first mission and through their part in the war. When the story ends we know all the choices they made, we know the consequences of their choices and what the war did to them. There\u2019s no more uncertainty. We have dispersed the fog of war, laid it bear for all to see. Was it worth it?
