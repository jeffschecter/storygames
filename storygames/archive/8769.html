    \n I'm definitely there with Jason and Remi. I played in and ran 8 good games - which I don't regret at all. Besides trying out interesting new games or favorite "classics" and the crazy stories that get told, it's such a joy to see what everyone else brings to the table and watch it all blend. You see creative and sometimes intimate sides of people in games that don't come out as much in small talk. But those games (and my need for sleep) certainly limited my time to get to know and socialize with people who weren't in the same games, so I guess I regret that there wasn't more time. I do think I'll try to leave a slot or two open next time to allow for more social flexibility.

    Also: Montsegur 1244. I regret not getting in on that.
