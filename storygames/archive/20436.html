Hello everyone!
The Kickstarter for Malandros has a week and a day left to run.

It's a game about ordinary and extraordinary people in Rio de Janeiro at the end of the Empire of Brazil. You play characters in a tight-knit community caught up in tumultuous times: gang leaders, captains of industry, fishermen, martial artists, swindlers and more. You all know each other - you're family, friends, rivals or enemies, all living in the same part of town. You all want something from each other. Maybe it's respect, maybe it's love. Maybe it's fear, or something else. But will you get what you want?

This is the Kickstarter page: https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1690011586/malandros-tales-from-the-streets-of-old-rio

And this is an interview I did with Mark Knights about the game: http://rpgknights.com/malandros-kickstarter/

Here's the current stretch goal situation.

Upcoming (in this order):

- Other Borders, modern-day sorcery setting by Tod Foley,
- Gangs of Titan, an SF setting by Stras Acimovic
