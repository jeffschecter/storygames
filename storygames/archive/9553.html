    \n Hey all, sorry I wasn't more clear.

    I was thinking of purely character experiences, emotions, etc. Stuff that happens in epic (or "long") adventures, be it a Native American adventure with magical bows and medicine bags, Neo-french vampire princes in pre-flood contemporary New Orleans, or Post-Chinese laser pirates on thought-powered spaceships.

    I tend to make adventures focus on some elements (high action, friendship, justice) and tend to forget other elements (romance, obsession, community) that would be cool to bring in for an adventure (or character, when playing) from time to time.

    So I'm looking for any emotions/experiences/elements that are somewhat universal (even though they might mean more in other cultures; duty in Japan, community in Eastern Europe, for example) that I can apply to capital-A Adventure gaming (not so much Emo stab-self/mom-in-eye gaming).

    -Andy
