\n I'd like to run a TSoY game in a cyberpunk setting. So, let's come up with some secrets and keys to make it happen!

If you have ideas for other game tweaks, I'd love to hear about them.

Here are a few starters I came up with fast. Some aren't terribly original (Key of the Firefight is basically Key of Bloodlust with guns, for example):

====================
SECRETS

Secret of Sims
Your character has modular programs that allow him to mimic many abilities.
Add 1 bonus die per pool point to any ability roll.
Cost: 3 Pool points per bonus die. The GM decides which pool applies.

Secret of Access
Your character can access the net anytime, anywhere.
Cost: None. May require a Computer ability roll in extreme situations.

Secret of the Burst
Your character knows how to fire a storm of bullets. He can designate and shoot multiple targets.
Cost: 2 Vigor per target.

====================
KEYS

Key of the Company
Your character is a devoted company man or woman.
Gain 1 XP every time your character acts for the company's interest.
Gain 2 XP when those actions result in profit.
Gain 5 XP when your character acts for the company at great personal or financial risk.
Buyoff: Leave the company payroll. Permanently.

Key of the Firefight
Your character lives by the way of the gun.
Gain 1 XP every time your character wins a firefight.
Gain 3 XP for overcoming superior odds in a firefight.
Buyoff: Lose a firefight.

Key of Anarchy
Your character is a rebel who defies authority.
Gain 1 XP every time your character breaks law or policy.
Gain 2 XP every time your character breaks the law and suffers minor harm or hassle.
Gain 5 XP every time your character breaks a major law or policy and suffers serious harm for it.
Buyoff: Obey an authority figure.

    Gain 5 XP when your character replaces a major body part of bodily function with a synthetic/cybernetic equivalent.
    Buyoff: Refuse an opportunity to 'upgrade' one of your biological parts to cyberware.

    \nSecret of Mirrorshades
    Add 1 bonus die per pool point to any ability roll where intimidation or cool is called for.

    Key of the Bounty Hunter
    Gain 1XP every time you pursue a contracted person.
    Gain 3XP every time you turn in or otherwise "cash out" on a contracted person.
    Buyoff: Voluntarily let a target escape.

    Secret of Rep
    Add 1 bonus die per pool point to any ability roll where contacts or trust is called for.

    Key of the Rep Hunter
    Gain 1XP when you act to preserve your underground reputation.
    Gain 3XP when you act to increase your reputation.
    Gain 5XP when you take extreme risks to boost or protect your rep.
    Buyoff: You go back on your word, nullifying your rep.

    Key of NegRep
    Gain 1XP when your negative reputation gets in your way.
    Gain 3XP when your negative reputation draws you into a conflict.
    Gain 5XP when your negative reputation endangers the life of your or an ally.
    Buyoff: Get your rep back with your community.

    Secret of the EmotionHack
    At any time, spend one pool point to abruptly and instantly alter your emotional state to anything else.

    \nSecret of HyperThreading
    You can split off "threads" of your mind to do multiple things at once, provided it is physically possible to do so. Spend 1 Reason for each "thread" you create; this thread disappears once the action is complete.

    Key of the Artificial Memory
    Do you remember that day at the Shell Beach (even though you can't swim) with your family (even though you can't recall their faces) in the summer house on the cape (even though you can't remember the name of the town)? Yeah, I thought you did.
    Gain 1XP when this memory intrudes on your normal life.
    Gain 3XP when you uncover a clue towards the truth of this "memory".
    Gain 5XP when you uncover a clue towards the parties responsible for this "memory".
    Buyoff: You burn the memory right out of your skull, by force of will or worse.

    Key of the Memefected
    You've been infected by a viral designer meme that's been hitting the street hard lately. But what's a meme to do? SPREAD, that's what.
    Gain 1XP when your normal behavior or speech is altered by the meme.
    Gain 3XP when you "preach the good word" of this meme.
    Gain 5XP when you incur risk on behalf of "the message".
    Buyoff: Again with burning the meme right out of your skull.

    ...

    Matt, are any keys going in the right direction? Are some (like mine) too weird or somesuch?

    If you take Stephenson as a good nod to Snow Crash, then these might be good:

    Secret of the Librarian
    Every CIC Stringer needs one of these.
    You have a virtual Librarian. It can collect data from the Library as requested. Once it has parsed the information, it can be engaged in a conversation as if it were an expert on the subject (which it virtually is, as it can read and extrapolate from the actual experts). Conversations with the Librarian can be tedious or lead nowhere at times. Beware of conversational bifurcations.
    Cost: 3 Reason per level of Weapon the Librarian's Knowledge gains you.

    Key of Kourier
    Smooth Move, Ex-Lax
    Yours is the life of the Radi-KS Kourier. You have full access everywhere; especially any burbclave but also onto the grounds of the United States of America (uggh). But that doesn't mean that you have a reason to be there - but that just excites you more.
    1 XP Anytime you make your way to somewhere new using your credentials but have no real reason to be there.
    2 XP Anytime you warrant 'special attention' from someone but evade their BS, either on your board or via legalese.
    5 XP Anytime you make a delivery despite incredible personal danger.

    \n Hmm, *I* think it would be neat if some cyber secrets came with 'drawbacks'. For instance:

    Secret of the Claw
    You've got subdermal retractable steel claws or nanite fingernails or something similar. (Yes, you're goddamn Wolverine. Don't let it get to your head.)
    You have a hideable +1 Weapon you can bring out at any time for melee violence or intimidation; counts as a +2 for it's use in surprise attacks.

    Mini Key of Unperfected Technology: Anytime these claws 'just come out' at the wrong time, gain 1 XP. If they hurt someone dear to you, gain 3 XP.

    EDIT: I realize now that this idea is literally a Secret and a Key bolted together. If you didn't charge an extra Advance for a Secret like this, you'd be unbalancing the game towards these enhanced Secrets. Still, if I were writing a TSOY hack, it'd be neat if some or all of the Secrets had suggestions for Keys to go with them.

    So, how would you make a drawback fun without it being tied to the XP system? I can't think of any way to do it right now. I suppose you could make 'more powerful secrets' that permanently reduced a point in a Pool, but that doesn't sound fun.
