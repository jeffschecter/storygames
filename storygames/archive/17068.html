    \n I picked up Dog Eat Dog, They Became Flesh, Flatpack, Rimward (the latest Eclipse Phase setting book), Leverage, and snagged my pre-ordered copy of Night's Black Agents.

    1st impressions: Leverage is one of the most newbie-welcoming games I've ever read. NBA does a great job of providing dials for telling the kind of story the group is looking for, but is a bit crunchier than something I'd actually run.

    I'm seriously considering dropping Call of Cthulhu as my go to horror game. I really like the cleanness of Trail of Cthulhu.
    \nI played a game of CoC that... well, I am quite sure you could do it as Trail of Cthulhu, but I am not convinced one should. On the other hand, I need a better understanding of the non-investigative system bits for ToC (and Gumshoe in general) than I have. But, keep an eye out for Eternal Lies, which I helped playtest for Pelgrane. It rocks!
