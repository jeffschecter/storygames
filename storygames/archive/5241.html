\n It's getting to the point where we need to start thinking about a Spodley 2008.

For those who don't know what Spodley Grange is, it's a weekend mini-convention dedicated to playtesting.

Last year there were 11 of us locked away at a cottage in Northants. We managed 12 playtest sessions over the weekend. Which was cool as.

It was a blast, I enjoyed it tremendously and drank far more than was appropriate.

Ideally I want to get the same venue again this year, because it's nearly perfect for what we wanted to do. Also I'd like to aim for April, although the first week in May might be possible. Price, like last time will depend of the numbers attending. More people = less \xa3 each. Assume that this is around the \xa350-60 mark, plus whatever it's going to cost you to get into the middle of a field in the middle of nowhere in Northants.

Everyone is welcome! If you have a game you want to playtest, bring it along. If you don't have a game to playtest, that's even more awesome! Last time about a third of the people there didn't bring anything to test and just tested other people's games. I think it's fair to say that they had just as much fun (if not more!) than the designers who came.

So, who'd up for some more Spodding about?
