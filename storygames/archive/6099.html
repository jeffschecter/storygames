\n What is Cheap?
Cheap is a B-grade story that you don't want to be a part of, and it's also the story of how you write yourself out. In Cheap, you have been drafted into a smutty, dirty story, and you need to find and kill your narrator in order to escape. You don't know the rules, and when you learn them you won't like them.

It's a landscape inspired by 80's pornos, Crooked Little Vein, The Cell, Palahniuk, Paprika, Being John Malkovich, and Noir.

I've been working on it lately, and I want to test out my current scene framing mechanics/guidelines. They're focused on NPC creation and situation priming.

If you're interested in helping me with a little exercise, whisper me in this thread. I'll explain everything you need to know. I'll take six volunteers total.
