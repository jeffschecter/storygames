    I'm like two different people when it comes to camping: I love the hard, rugged, "load up a pack, walk 15 miles, set up camp with bear-bags and the works, sleep in your 30-below bag, rinse & repeat" camping. But I also love the "many comforts of home, bring a fluffy pillow, relax and do nothing" style of camping as well. Camp Nerdly is definitely the latter. There are cabins, it's in May so I expect it to be warm, the showers and bathrooms are FAR more modern, clean and non-smelly than I was expecting (and heated, too!). The only thing that was a bother for the last Nerdly was that the temperature was colder than we expected. This time it's bordering on Spring and Summer, so we should totally be good.

    In any case, Nerdly is the big event that I'm looking forward to this year. Even if I end up making it to GenCon, Nerdly is still the big one in my mind. And I haven't even signed up for any events yet (or listed my own).
