\n This year, I have learned a couple valuable lessons. The first is that you shouldn't try to gain social cred by putting yourself in unsafe situations (and I had a fractured pelvis to testify to that lesson learned). The second is that you should keep your Kickstarter/IndieGoGo neat and focused. The second lesson was learned across many months of toiling away at stretch goals and special perks (two new playbooks, a three-adventure campaign book, sixteen zines, four mix cds, handout folders, a suite of postcards, skype gaming sessions...).

All of which is to say, in a much more roundabout and personal way than is necessary:

The Angel is complete. That art is our very own Aaron Friesen. Enjoy.
