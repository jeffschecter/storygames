So...

\n

I had this brilliant idea to make a game world setting based on Candy Land. You know... a dark, twisted, demented version of it. There would be some back story that discussed how it got that way, although I never really could figure out what happened.

\n

At any rate... turns out that Candy Land is pretty well trademarked. I had thought it was public domain, but alas.

\n

Even though I only planned on doing this as an online freebie thing, I wouldn't exactly want to get sued for it. I know it could be construed as parody, but... that can be a hard one to prove.

\n

So I dunno...

\n

What would be a good way to fend off the Hasbro lawyers?
