    \n There's been a tortured history with CthulhuTech and printing/publishing, as I recall.

    It was originally published by Wildfire through Mongoose's Flaming Cobra line of creator-owned games, but that was at the same time that Mongoose had that horrible debacle with buying a second-hand printing machine and trying to print their own hardcover books. The resulting quality was really terrible and, for that reason and others, Wildfire left Mongoose and started publishing Cthulhutech through Catalyst Game Labs (who also do Shadowrun). The new version you saw is probably the new printing done through them, but I haven't seen the new one yet, so I'm not sure.

    I was fairly interested in the game before the horrible printing disaster and, even now, the mechanics don't excite me that much, but I'd definitely give it another look now that the books won't fall apart in your hands.
