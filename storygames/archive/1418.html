Seeking Guides for Old-West-style LARP

eruditus
September 2006 edited September 2006 in Directed Promotion
We are looking for two to three dedicated Guides to help us develop and run our LARP campaign over the next two years. It will be an old west game set in a world not too unlike ours (the names have been changed to protect the guilty). We require that you commit to attending the six conventions/gatherings (three per year) that the LARPs will be run and a monthly meeting (online accommodations are also acceptable). We will be developing table-top games intermittently at other events to add depth to the campaign. Although development will included the table-top games, running them is voluntary.

At what events the games will be run will be a group decision but we are hoping that we can start at PhilCon 2006 in November (to be discussed at our first meeting).

The LARP will use a new set of simple rules based off of the Burning Wheel system. All characters will be made in Burning Wheel (a set of books will be provided).

The group dynamics will be very democratic among the Guides. We have begun a storyline and some character backgrounds but we're open to new ideas. I feel my job stands as a moderator, both among Guides as well as between Guides and the game we create. I retain veto rights to certain sweeping ideas that I may feel detrimental to the big picture or contrary to what we've all developed. The Guides may unanimously out-vote my veto.

Most correspondence will be via an email list.

Please let me know if your interested in joining us in this rewarding role-paying endeavor.

Yours in gaming,
- Don
Philly Game Whore
