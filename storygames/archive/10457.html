\n Hi there storygamers!

\n[EDIT] Please ignore this thread my problem has been solved and my error pointed out! Defatigable is an actual word apparently! [/EDIT]
\n
\nI need to pick your brains. I need a word which is the opposite of "tireless" or "indefatigable".
\n
\nThe idea is that the undead, who cannot feel pain, are utterly indefatigable - they carry on going and going until you hack off all their limbs. We can simply call them "tireless".
\n
\nI need a word for creatures who lack that capacity - for instance, ordinary humans who can become weary or fatigued.
\n
\nIf you hadn't guessed, this is to help me with a project which I won't shamelessly plug here!
\n
\nI expect I will be using the word "Defatigable", which I have just recently coined. But if there is an existing word in the English language which will do the job, I would love to know, and may well use that real word instead of my made-up one. I have searched online thesauruses for antonyms and found nothing appropriate.
\n
\nPlease help!
\n
\nMatt
