\n So, I was reading through the "Ninja Burger RPG: No Honor Edition" and was struck by the fact that even though I really enjoy the silly setting material for Ninja Burger (and absolutely love the card game) the PDQ system didn't sound like it'd do that much for me. Now, I haven't played it, so I could be way off base here. But anyway, I also just read the rules to InSpectres, which had the opposite effect of making me want to play right away (I was warped/shaped as a child by Ghostbusters), and I was struck that the potentially silly feel, mission oriented play, and franchise support of both games would mesh really well.

My only sticking point so far is that 'contacts' would probably see less use for Ninja than normal people, but other than that I think they'd be a great fit. Does anybody have any thoughts?
