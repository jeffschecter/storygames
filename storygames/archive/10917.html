\n After taking nominations and conducting a Qualifying Round of read-throughs, we're ready to start the "MULRAH Mini" Tournament to find the best free GM-less game that plays in an hour or less!

We'd love to have other gamers participate in this tournament by playing all of the games below, as grouped, and posting a brief comparative assessment for each session, choosing which game you would most like to play again. If you do it by December 14, then we'll include your report in the tally determining who makes it to the Finals.\n
    \n
  • 1 Executive Decision vs 4 An Unpublished Game (choose below)
  • 2 Drowning & Falling vs 3 Mexican Standoff
\nUnpublished Games: Pick one to play against Executive Decision in the first match-up.\n
    \n
  1. Grover Cleveland (short version)
  2. Being a role-playing game on the topic of the High-Flying adventures of Beatrice Henrietta Bristol-Smythe, DBE, daring Aviatrix and accomplished Exploratrix, and her Gentleman Companion, who for a Modest Fee, accompanies Beatrice Henrietta Bristol-Smythe, DBE, when the Occasion warrants her an Escort
  3. Court of the Empress


    If you've already played some of these games, you do not have to play them again to include them in your comparative assessment.

    Once we've crowned a MULRAH Mini Champion, we'll move onto match-ups among more involved games, which are still under discussion here.
