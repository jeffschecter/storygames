\n Honestly, it looks as though all I post about is running games at conventions, or trying to organise conventions. I felt bad about this for a moment until I realise I spend more time working on conventions than I do playing games.

If you're going to be in Brisbane, Australia on October 1 and 2 this year, you should get along to Auscon. And if you're not going to be there, you should make plans to be there so you can be at Auscon. I'm helping out again, coordinating the RPG element of the weekend. The full list of events is still in development so you still have an opportunity to get your game registered.

Find out more details at the Auscon site.
