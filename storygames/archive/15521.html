    \n Ralph, that\u2019s the spirit! Great to see that some people still remember the game.
    \n
    \nAndy, I\u2019ll try to give a very brief overview of the rules. There is one basic attribute used for everything: Virility. All player characters have Virility 3 but foes can range from 1 to 5. This is your basic number of dice in a standard d6 pool system. A few things that add a little meat to your character are strengths, weaknesses and bonds (which come in the flavors of desire, rivalry, and oath), which can grant you bonus dice. In addition, there are a couple of situational sources of bonus dice.
    \n
    \nThe main part of the rules is dedicated to fighting and seduction, with systems that mostly mirror each other. The main resources are Dangerous and Attractive, which are used up by taking actions or being wounded / rebuffed. To spice things up, you can buy \u201ctricks\u201d which you \u201cburn\u201d when using them, such as \u201cdouble strike\u201d or \u201celectrifying touch\u201d. Your choice of weapon is also of relevance. Last not least, there are Aggro (gained by fucking) and Horny (gained by fighting), another twin pair of resources which can be used by the player to add dice to a roll. Used-up Aggro and Horny points turn into XP.
    \n
    \nFinally, there is the Skull, which a player can claim if he pulled a really badass macho stunt. The player who has the skull can expect the other players to refill his glass, pass him the chips and generally treat him like the leader of the pack \u2013 until another player claims the skull.
