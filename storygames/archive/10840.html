\n I'm going to be running a marketing campaign on a couple of major sites to promote sales of Campaign Cartographer 3 for new users.

The community here has been very helpful in the past, particularly in suggesting we have better CC3 maps on our websites. Our front page now has a selection of decent, zoomable maps as a result.

So, I want to offer the promotion here first, and also get feedback. This offer is exclusively for story gamers, so please don't redistribute the vouchers.

You have a choice of a half-price CC3 or get CC3 and get a free add-on of your choice. The offer can be combined with other purchases.

To make use of the offer, place the order, then paste the voucher code into the box on the checkout:

SGAMES@CC3HALF for the half-price CC3.

SGAMES@ADDON for the free add-on.

Feedback: Please let me have your comments on offer and let me know anything you think we might be able to do to improve take-up

    \n I've analyzed the results of this offer, and the subsequent one on rpg.net. As you've been so indulgent, and so I though I'd share the results.

    The half price offer here got three sales, the free add-on two. Too small a sample to gain any useful data.

    I then posted a banner-based offer on rpg.net (a free add-on for rpg.net subscribers), which attracted 22 sales. A third of the sales included additional products.

    Talking of offers and sales, I ran a 15% discount sale for Pelgrane on rpgnow.com, and gained about 8 times a month's level of sales in a week, and a large proportion of them were new customers. Definitely worth doing. I've also done the same on e23, and later in the year will do IPR and the Pelgrane mail order site.
