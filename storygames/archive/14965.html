\n Suspended Animation is a story game you play alone by yourself, before and after going to sleep. The rules are simple and allow for a lot of interpretation in translating them into a story. You create a deck of cards based on prompts, starting with 2 cards for each of four categories. Then you write a ships log based on those prompts, as if you were a person on a long space voyage, spending years in suspended animation between each log. So my play ended up being fairly epic and is 24 pages long. Thus I\u2019ll slowly be releasing it in chapters. Enjoy!
