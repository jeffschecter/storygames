\n Tomorrow I'll be at Dragonmeet in London with the Collective Endeavour.

Having been distracted by the whole getting married thing, I came back to discover that a whole bunch of folks from stateside are going to be there too! Awesome! It's got a great storygames contingent in general this year!

Shout out if you'll be there too, and come and say hi in the trade hall.

Edit: Also, as is traditional we'll probably end up in a pub somewhere afterwards. All friendly types welcome.

    \n Sadly I have to miss this year. Moving house has eaten up all of my free time and available cash, so I can't make the trek south for the show. Hope it goes well for everyone.

    Incidentally, if you've not been there before you might want to look into visiting the wonderful old Derry & Toms roof garden ( http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kensington_Roof_Gardens ) if they're open. Quite an astonishing place and practically on the doorstep of Dragonmeet. Not sure of the current access situation, but when I was there a few years ago the gardens were open if you asked nicely and they didn't have any functions on.
