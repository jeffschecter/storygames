\n I've played three games of Darcy Burgess' Black Cadillacs now (with the guy who introduced our group to Lacuna Part 1) and generally enjoyed them. However, I can't help but feel that there is a disconnect between Trooper Creation and game play.
\n
\nThe game includes mechanics to benefit your memories if they link to existing Story or Rumour, but no benefit for connecting to Next of Kin, Background or Last Night. As these are the only three "stats" a starting character has, it would be nice if there was some benefit to bringing them up in play.
\n
\nThat said, I wouldn't want them to provide the same "wild card" bonus as linking a memory to Story or Rumour. Perhaps a re-roll benefit?
\n
\nIdeas?
