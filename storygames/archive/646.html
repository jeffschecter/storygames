Hey! Drowning and Falling is actually in layout, but when it came time to write captions, I realized I only had names for two of the five iconic characters, and one of them was lame. So! A contest!

Name these four adventuring companions of the brave (if prone to falling off the wagon) warrior woman Fyren Cloudfloater.

I'm looking for extra apostrophes. I'm looking for subtle but hilarious. I'm looking for awesome. I'm looking for 13-year-old-Korn-fan. Winners get thanked in the book, as if you aren't thanked already. Deadline is when I have four hilarious fantasy badasses. One set of four per person, so bring me your best, honed to R.A. Salvatore perfection!

If you can't tell, that's: #1 Cleric, #2 Elf, #3 Dwarfling, #4 Wizard.
