\n 'Tis the season for giving and I've decided to give away free copies of my game Defiance to the lovely people who slink through the story-games.com. Please go here to unwrap your gift or check out this preview
\n
\nPlease read the following, before you decide to download it:
\n
\n
Defiance is a fantasy storytelling and role playing game for between 3 and 6 players. The story begins in the darkest hours before the dawn of a revolution. Players will create stories about insurrectionists involved in a heroic struggle against the tyranny of corrupt authorities. Throughout the game players construct and encounter challenges and they discover truths about themselves and the other insurrectionists. Truths about the way the insurrectionists think and feel. Truths about their beliefs, loyalties, sense of justice, and limits of moral courage. With each new choice, a story emerges from the imaginations of the players so that his or her insurrectionist moves closer towards his fate.
\n
\n
\n
The most important things for your 3\u20146 players to have at the outset are time and a great imagination. Each player will also need something to write with, blank paper, character sheets, 15 red beads or buttons (plus an extra 3 per player), 15 white beads or buttons (plus an extra 3 per player). The beads do not have to be red and white, but there each player much have the exactly same two colours as everyone else. One small opaque hand sized bag for drawing out beads and a ten-sided dice (1d10) will also be required.
\n
\nChoose a place for your Defiance session that is comfortable and be prepared to devote between 1 and 4 hours per session. (Experience has shown the ideal gaming session of Defiance lasts 2\u20143 hours).
\n
\n
\nThe "official" release of Defiance will happen in early 2008 and it will contain pretty pictures, a completed index, and a redesigned character sheet. And yes, I will be charging for it.
