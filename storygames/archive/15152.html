    \n "When your danger level reaches the K.O.\u2019d and Vaporized levels, you get a different kind of reward.

    - When your danger level is in the K.O.\u2019d section, you get to roll
    2 trait dice when you take an action, instead of just one.
    - When your danger level is in the Vaporized section, you get to
    roll 3 trait dice when you take an action."

    It's not when your hero is K.O'd or Vaporized that you get additional trait dice, it's when your danger level has reached those sections.

    It's the same Trait die, yes. And the Daredevil gets an even greater boost.

    \n To further clarify what Matthew is saying: being at the Vaporized danger level doesn't mean you get Vaporized. You only get Vaporized if you take a Full hit, and don't have the Power Point to Reduce it, or you've somehow filled up all the lower levels (which would be difficult, since you'd also be Unsconscious.)
