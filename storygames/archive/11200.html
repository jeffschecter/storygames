\n Okay, I thought it might be a good idea to give the #playnow channel on magicstar some love, since more games are going on in it than you might think from a casual lurk. Admittedly, most of them seem to be played by the same group of players, but that's no bad thing.
\n
\nSo, post here when you've run/played in a game on #playnow, and let us/the rest of your group know how it went. And then people can see how games are actually happening there.
\n
\nFor instance, earlier today I ran a game of 3:16 which was almost a complete bloodbath at the end... despite the aliens fluffing all but one of their rolls (like, in the entire planet).
\n
\nI know other stuff has been going on recently, so share your IRC-based tales of quick games with people you've never met.
