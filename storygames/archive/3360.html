    Posted By: chaduQUERY 1:Have I ever looked at a game you've been designing/have designed/have published and offered comments or advice that improved it for the better?
    \nYou looked over Pyramid article about my Unknown Armies magick school, Countermancy. Not the same as a full game, but still useful for me.\n
    QUERY 2:How valuable do you feel those comments or that advice was?
    \nI only remember one point where I disagreed with your comments, about the names of spells being boring, and I had a reason for it. Even so, I have to ponder some time to decide whether my disagreement was valid. I don't remember anything else, I think because when I read the comments I thought "Yeah, that's a good idea" and didn't have to mull over them like I did about the spell name comment. In the final text, you'll see that I even addressed the issue by saying that Countermancers give their spells boring names, if they even bother to name them at all, because magick isn't something for them to hype up like with everyone else. In that respect, you helped me keep from losing something important in the article.
