\n So one of my game group is reading books about smoke jumpers and talking to his dad who was a forest ranger and forest fire fighter back before my buddy was born. It's got him thinking about doing some sort of story game where the enemy is Nature. Maybe it's a Fire, maybe it's a Storm, maybe it's a Drought or a Flood or whatever. The point is, it isn't at thing to kill or master, it's a thing to ride out, help get to an end, or maybe at best survive.

This tickled my memory bank a bit and made me think I'd read something similar here on SG. Am I imagining that or is this already a thing? If it isn't a thing, does anyone have thoughts on what might be good to hack for it? We goofed around talking about Dread but the jenga means you will eventually lose and we felt like there ought to be a mechanic for a clear "win" even if that win is just survival.

Thoughts from the hivemind?
