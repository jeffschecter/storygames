\n This announcement is for everyone who is planning on running something under the Indie Games Explosion banner at GenCon this year. The first deadline rather snuck up on me. The deadline for having event requests in is the end of this month. However, the convention has lost 30k square feet of space due to construction this year so tables are at a premium. Turning in our requests on the very last day is likely a very bad idea. Here's what I need from folks.

1.) What game(s) are you running?
2.) If you want very specific time slots, let me know what they are. If you just know how many sessions you are going to run and don't care about specific time slots, let me know that too. (I promise not to schedule you for 12 midnight or something silly.... unless you ask me to.)
3.) How many players can your game handle?
4.) A small blurg describing your game.

Oh... and I need your name.

If you run 4 sessions 6 players at 4 hours each, then you will get a complimentary GM badge. I'm not sure if that is the same as it was last year but that's the deal this year. I don't make those rules. They come down from GenCon LLC.

I need this information by the 16th of March if at all possible. If we go beyond that, we are stuck on the last round of approvals and our location might suck or not even be available. As I said above, there's a premium on space this year.

You can send me the information at aecooper AT musfiber DOT com. If you need to speak to me on the phone, send me an email and I'll send you my cell phone number and we can chat.

Here's who I have so far...

Jonathon Walton
1 slot Geiger Counter
1 slot The 108 Bravos of Mount Liang

Malcolm Craig
1 slot Mob Justice
1 slot Cold City
1 slot Hot War

Seth Ben-Ezra
1 or 2 slots Dirty Secrets

Green Fairy Games
6-8 slots of Fae Noir, Candy Creeps and other stuff.

If I don't have you listed above, I don't know about you yet. If you are listed above but haven't sent me the information I need, please, send me the rest of the stuff.
