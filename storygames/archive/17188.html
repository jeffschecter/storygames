\n We've played one session so far. My character is the queen. At the start I said, she uses sex as a weapon. Definitely not a Kantian.

She had sex with the Infernal's runner (he's a dealer), whilst he was watching, to show the Infernal who's boss.
She had sex in a ritual with her gang and another PC (to make him part of the gang, it's a cult thing with rituals).
She had a fight with another PC and then turned her on in the middle of the fight but denied her sex and gave her a condition instead. This was in the canteen.

Some other PCs have had sex, although mostly with NPCs, mostly with members of my gang although there was also a priest.

So how much actual sex happens in your games, as opposed to the promise or threat?
