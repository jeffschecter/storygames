    \n If you're the original copyright holder, you can relicense the work to your heart's content. After all, the various licenses derive directly from your copyright, which you don't give away by licensing. So if you could have licensed something with license A before licensing with license B, then you can do it afterwards as well; you can't limit your own copyright with a license agreement, unless that agreement contains specific limiting language.
