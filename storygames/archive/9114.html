\n So saith Ken:\n
I beseech you to spread the word to all and sundry indie game-designers.

If they have a game that will be debuting at GenCon Indy, I need to hear about its coolness so I can try to put it into the "New Hotness" essay I write every year for the GenCon program book.

This year, my deadline is criminally short -- I need to hear back by April 20. I'll be at GTS, if any of you want to buttonhole me on your own behalf or that of some other game that would leave my essay a hollow mockery of a sham by its absence.

Any designer is free to contact me at hit2@mindspring.com, and you guys are welcome to post this to Story-Games or the Forge or whatever virtual smoking section the cool kids hang out in nowadays.

Thanks much,

Ken
