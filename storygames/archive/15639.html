    \n BATTLES IN NIGHTTIME

    You're just an old man in old pajamas. Perhaps a noise draws you from your slumber. Perhaps you were sleepwalking, and just woke up here. Perhaps every night you find yourself compelled to stand in the middle of the street and cry over the memory of your late wife, unable to sleep before completing this morose ritual.

    Regardless of how it happened, you're here. An old man with his pinstripe cane, his nighttime cap, and nothing else. You against the demonkind who have chosen this night to rise up and swallow the earth.

    Using an innovative bartered-memories mechanic and a retro loot-dropping 8bit combat system, you need to fight for the redemption of the earth, the preservation of your memories, and the glory of item acquisition. For 2 players, 20 minutes to 2 hours.
