\n No offense to Game Chef, but one of the things I miss about the competition is how far it has moved away from the Iron Chef style. But such regret can only be over overcome with action, and since no one else seems willing, I will take a stab at it.

In the later half of 2007, I will be starting an Iron Game Design tournament - with head-to-head (possibly head-to-head-to-head) events. Two (three) designers will compete to best express a single theme ingredient in a time limited design battle. The other competitors will act as judges, determining who is the winner of each battle. Battles will be elimination, leading to a final battle which will determine the Iron Game Designer for the year.

This isn't just about finishing your game, it is about the challenge and seeking excellence as a game designer. Do you have what it takes?

- Mendel S.
