\n RandomCon is a new gaming convention coming to Arizona in May of 2009.
\n
\nWe are proud to bring the Arizona public 'mainstream' games as well as 'indie' games.
\n
\nWe are striving to provide you with a fun and friendly gaming environment for everyone involved.
\n
\nJoin in on all the fun!
\n
\nWe especially need people to run games. If your interested in running anything please contact RandomConArizona@gmail.com with the subject line Volunteer.
\n
\nIf your just interested in keeping up to date please check out any or all of the below sites.
