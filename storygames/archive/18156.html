\n I've just discovered that if I navigate to story-games.com and log in, then click a link to any www.story-games.com URL, I will not be logged in there.

This was driving me nuts until I figured it out. So now I share.

If you follow a link to a thread that you want to comment on, but suddenly you're not logged in, you can just add or delete "www." from your browser's URL bar.
