    Posted By: Nathan HI think using the the word Feat is now a bit of a misnomer, since most of the action-y things have become Powers.
    Why not call 'em "buffs" or heck, even "traits"?
    \nWell, first of all, because that's not what a buff is. A buff is an effect that produces a beneficial bonus to a character. Powers are actions. Feats are altered character features. Spells, Exploits, and Prayers are types of powers. Buff doesn't replace any of those as a term. Any of those can produce a bonus, which could be called a buff, but they can also produce penalties (a "debuff" in MMO terms), cause damage, prevent damage, grant free actions, and many other effects.

    But, when using buff in the proper context, bonus is a perfectly useful and applicable term. And it makes more sense. In a video game, you don't see (or need to see) all the numbers in order to use a bonus, so it's called a "buff". It makes you "buff" by some degree. In table top games, it's a bonus, because it provides a specfic bonus that you need to apply in order to make it work. I might be splitting hairs here, but I like the hairstyle.
