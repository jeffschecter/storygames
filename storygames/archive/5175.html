\n As inspired by Ok I get that Cold City is British....

I know this is silly, but I'm fascinated.

Go and get a new-ish roleplaying game book and post what it smells like. Don't joke: things like "my copy of Dogs In The Vineyard smells like horses and guns" will get old fast.

My copy of Hollywood Lives, a Christmas present from Secret Santa, smells like new electrical equipment.

    \n The smell of a book is something I always notice.

    Especially after hearing about the bacteria that cause the smells of old books, which can affect the brain, even only slightly. That euphoria you feel at discovering some musty old tome may not be entirely enthusiasm!

    So this idea has lurked in my mind for many years. It shows up just before 2 and a half minutes into this video.
