In 2011 Double Ninja put together a list of GMless games. I would like to continue to build the list and add GMless games that have since be published; for example, I could add House of Reeds, Kingdom, Follow, Perseverant, Downfall, Fall of Magic, Dog Eat Dog, etc. I also would like to weed out any games from the list below that aren't published on a website, or in PDF or book format. Also, the games should be adequately play-tested and have a decent following.

Why put together a list? I think it would be a good resource.

Want to help? Either suggest adding a GMless game that fits the criteria above or suggest removing a game from the list that doesn't fit the criteria. Thanks

    I don't want to "sour the waters" here, but I'd like to mention that many/most of these games, since they are being created by designers (and often FOR designers), are not "thoroughly playtested" or don't "have a decent following". Even some games which are talked about A LOT - many of them haven't been played all that many times, and likely even some "never". That's the nature of this rather small but extremely prolific community, I think!

    For instance, I feel quite confident saying that a lot of people have played Lady Blackbird. But if I had to guess how many people have played Kazekami Kyoko Kills Kublai Khan, I'd guess it's very few (even though I bet many have read it and have been inspired by it).

    Ultimately, though, it's really hard for anyone to guess these things.
