\n Sparked by the discussion on game acronyms, I was inspired to look for a Vanilla add-on that could handle common acronyms automagically. Based on a cursory search, it looks like Transmogrifier could do the trick.

Essentially, someone (Andy, or someone else with admin rights) would maintain a configuration file with text replacements in it. This list could be generated initially by the community, and edited periodically to add or remove acronyms as necessary. Once this is in place, the text of a given acronym ("MLwM") would get replaced as the page renders with the acronym markup ("<acronym title="My Life with Master">MLwM</acronym>").

Possible downsides include the administrative maintenance, and also the possibility that text would be replaced inappropriately. For example, if we had "DI" for "Dead Inside" (just to pick a game out of the list) we might wind up with "distance" or "dill pickle."

Thoughts? Has anyone tried Transmogrifier before?
