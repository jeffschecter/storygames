\n I'm working on this hero character for my game-in-development MASK.

Her name is Resurrection Mary. She can survive damage that should have been fatal, and she can heal any wounds by sleeping them off (for, like, days) in a sort of coma in which her vital signs are diminished enough that you'd think she was dead unless you hooked her up to an EKG. She fights crime in the bloody bridal dress & veil that she was murdered in.

So, here's what I know about her backstory:
She was murdered on her wedding night, by her husband (she didn't know at the time that it was him). She survived, thus first discovering her power, and set about on a course for revenge, which led her to her husband. So she showed up in the wedding dress, which scared him, then she cornered him and advanced menacingly, despite the fact that he emptied a clip on her, which scared the hell out of him. And she, y'know, killed him.

Here's what I don't know:
Why might a man murder his wife on their wedding night? And how would he get away with it?

How does a woman escape from the grave without being trained by Pai Mei?

How does this character make the shift from revenge against her husband to fighting crime? I imagine that she'd appoint herself as a champion of victimized women, but how does she end up fighting organized crime (which is what the game is about)?
