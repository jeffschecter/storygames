\n My apologies for going from being an occasional silent lurker to asking for help, but I hope it\u2019s for a cause that will be of some interest.
\n
\nI\u2019m making a short film for a school project about story gaming and the folks who participate. This film will be about 5 minutes long and will end up on YouTube most likely. While I\u2019m working on this project, I would like to get enough footage to support a longer film after my class is done.
\n
\nMy aim here is to talk about story games in a positive light to as broad an audience as possible. While I can\u2019t make any promises about how much (if any) promotion my final film gets, I am passionate about the hobby and want to get the word out.
\nWhat I need is some volunteers to be interviewed on video. I\u2019m looking for a diverse cross-section of the hobby, along as many axes as possible.
\n
\nHere\u2019s the bummer part: I need to do these interviews very quickly: in the next couple of weeks. I am also geographically limited: I live in the Seattle area, and can range around western Washington to some degree. The good news is that my schedule is pretty open, so I can most likely work with whatever time constraints participants have.
\n
\nIf you would like to help out and live in (or will be visiting) the Seattle area, please drop me message. The commitment I need from each person is realistically a couple of hours, with somewhere between 20 minutes to an hour of actual filmed interviewing.
\nAgain, sorry to spam the board when I haven\u2019t been an active poster.
\n
\nI hope to hear from some of you, and I look forward to spreading the word about story games.
\n
\nThanks,
\nJay
