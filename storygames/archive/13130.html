\n Enter a world of fantasy noir in Bloodshadows\u2122, where trench-coat detectives swap biting comments with vampires, humans walk the dark streets with demonic breeds, and death - or undeath - waits around every corner! Your weapons are magic and technology - but neither replaces a good head on your shoulders. And you'll have to work to keep it there! Originally published by West End Games, Bloodshadows, along with the MasterBook and Shatterzone\u2122 lines, are now part of the Precis Intermedia family of game products.

Now available at Indie Press Revolution, RPGNow/DrivethruRPG, and the Precis Intermedia website -- includes both the Adventure D6 Core Rules AND Bloodshadows sourcebook in PDF format for only $6.95
