    \nThe Quick and the Dead
    Previous heroes have attempted to come to her aid. Who are they and what was their undoing? Where are they now or where do their bodies lie?

    Hubris & Dark Fates
    You have a relationship with one or more of these previous would-be champions. What is it? How are their failings mirrored in your own weaknesses? How can you hope for triumph when they have failed?

    Things Are Not What They Seem
    The lady is not merely a helpless flower. Why has her potency not been the agent of her own rescue? How has it been subverted by her foes or how is she, herself, the active agent in her own predicament?

    \nDamsel in distress Burner

    A Lady in great danger
    A noble woman is in peril; or perhaps a foppish helpless man of high birth. How did she come to be in this situation? Who is she? What is it about her that inspires our heroes to come to her rescue?

    Vile Beast or Dastardly Villain
    There is always a Beast or Evil man, who are they? Why are they holding the holding the prince/princess and to what end. What does he or she hope to accomplish? What makes the villain dangerous? What makes the villain seductive? What do you find sympathetic about the villain's aims?

    Who are the villain's servants? Why do they serve the villain? What makes them dangerous? Why do you fear them? Why do you pity them? What terrible and frightening beast has the villain wronged? Why does it want revenge on the villain?

    In what forbidden or forbidding place is the villain holding the prince or princess? A tower? A castle? An underwater grotto?

    The Village
    Every story has a village, describe the people of the village and its features. Who are the prominent people? Name a noteworthy location. What is the Heroes relationship to this village? Are they well liked? Feared? Unknown? Take a Relationship with a prominent member of the Village. Or / And define a 1D Reputation involving the village.

    Obstacles on the path
    What dangerous and horrible barrier must you penetrate to reach the forbidden place? A haunted forest? Craggy mountains full of monsters? A deadly desert? A frozen land of ice and terror? Who knows the secrets of the barrier? Why has that person sworn never to enter the barrier again?

    Things Are Not What They Seem
    This lady is not merely a helpless flower. Why has her potency not been the agent of her own rescue? How has it been subverted by her foes or how is she, herself, the active agent in her own predicament?

    Sinners All
    Every Hero is flawed. Each Player chooses a Vice card. Write a belief centered on this flaw, direct this emotion at another Hero. Formulistic beliefs are fine; I am X, to Y\u2019s Z. Thus I will do D.

    The Blackguard
    One of the Heroes\u2019 Relationships has betrayed you all... He as already betrayed you. How have you been betrayed? Why? Another option is that one of the Heroes is not what he seems, he is the betrayer. This option requires everyone\u2019s consent. It should be clear to the players.

    From all walks of life
    Choose your Heroes from any setting. The only requirement is that they seem deserving of the Damsel.

    Vice Cards
    Desire
    Jealousy
    Bitterness
    Envy
    Spite
    Longing
    Resentment
    Discontent

    A good template for these beliefs would be to write them in the form of:

    I think X, so I'll do Y.

    Or if you want to get a little more wordy,

    Because of X I want Y, so I'll do Z.
