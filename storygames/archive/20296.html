\n We offer early sparring for first-time Fastaval writers: the help of an experienced Fastaval roleplaying writer who will offer feedback both generally, in terms of specific Fastaval viability, and with making a good synopsis - experience tells me that what we actually need from your synopses, and what the standard North American roleplaying game pitch contains, differs in a few crucial respects.

Note that while planning to attend Fastaval isn't a requirement for submitting games, it will everything better for you, for your game and for Fastaval if you do attend.
