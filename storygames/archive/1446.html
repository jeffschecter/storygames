[DEAD] Making 200 story cards is kinda hard.

Kuma
September 2006 edited September 2006 in Directed Promotion
So with the rules drafted, I've taken to writing out the decks of cards for the players and the DEAD. The zombie-threat cards are amazingly simple, compared to the cards that the players can draw in their favor. I guess it's just easier for me as a lifelong GM to think of ways to kill players.

So if anyone would like to contribute, here's how:

I've linked to a short sample of the cards that I've made already - Items, Events, Locations and Goals that the players can play in order to help their character survive. One thing to note is that I'd like a fairly large collection of 'narrative' or 'conflict' cards: for example "Make another character so angry that he or she attacks you." The player who successfully integrates this into the narrative gets a big bolus of Recovery points to soothe their character. I've included the file to give you an idea of the things that I've come up with so far. Don't worry so much about whether something will fit in - I can shoehorn it if necessary. I'm just running low on ideas after making 60 of them.

If anyone would like to jot down a few ideas, I'd appreciate the assistance. Contributor credit in the game book will, of course, also be forthcoming.

Thanks in advance, folks.
