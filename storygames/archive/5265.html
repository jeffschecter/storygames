\n A while ago, back in New Zealand, I came across a comic entitled "Killer Robots Will Smash the World". Of course I had to have it. It was a comic told entirely in pictures, by a New Zealand artist, Greag Broadmore (and another guy whose name eludes my google-fu). It's about two giant robots fighting in a near-future city. Their fight smashes through a city zoo, which releases dinosaurs into the city. Soon WWII Germans arrive to fight them. Finally, a futuristic Maori warrior blasts the dinosaurs with a ray gun, before being bombed by a WWI biplane. There's a guy with a cowboy hat riding a yellow digger with a mounted AA gun and with "Unstoppable Fist of Legend" painted across the front. I was sold.

I quickly grabbed anyhting else I could find, which included a poster of a cowboy facing down a T-Rex in the middle of a western town, while being strafed by zeppelins and biplanes, and a postcard of a motor-cross bike jumping a WWI tank in the trenches.

I forgot about the guy until recently, and did some googling. Turns out he works with Weta Workshops now (he did design for King Kong, and some new movies), and he has a gallery online. It yeilded some surprisingly awesome pictures. I thought they might be neat inspiration for people here.

Check it out!
