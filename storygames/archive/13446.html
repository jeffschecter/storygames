\n So say I want an RPG that captures the following elements from How to Train Your Dragon:

1) Fighting monsters, but beating them with cleverness is easier / better / encouraged.

2) Banter

3) Lots of room for error

4) ways to gain info besides direct engagement

5) Winning can change your hometown and your personal relationships for the better

    \n That's not too different from how initiative worked in some early versions of D&D I've seen (I can't remember if it's Moldvay or something else):

    Could be a good fit for this game.
