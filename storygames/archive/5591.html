\n So, I've owned Dictionary of Mu for a while. I've flipped through it and read various entries here and there but I've never read it cover to cover. I mean, it's a dictionary. Who reads a dictionary cover to cover?

Well the other night on a whim I started reading it cover to cover... and I could not stop... and I HATE "setting" material. I still haven't finished it, I'm up to R but I have to say that Dictionary of Mu is probably the single most evocative game text I have EVER read.

I mean it says stuff like, "Oghma would not want to offend honorable 'Q'" and I find myself looking over my shoulder, turning lights on and thinking, "Wh- Why? What's Q going to DO to me? I mean have *I* ever offended honorable 'Q'? I certainly didn't mean to."

Seriously, read this book if you haven't already. It will make you terrified of the perfectly ordinary.

Jesse
