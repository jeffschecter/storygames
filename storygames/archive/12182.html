\n The Boston StoryGamers are hosting JiffyCon Boston, a one day indie gaming event, on Saturday June 26. This is the fourth JiffyBoston. The Boston SGers have created a nice community, with regular gaming at Pandemonium Books over the years. I've really enjoyed getting to play with the Bay area gamers at past cons.

They are looking for GMs right now. Contact Dev at dev.purkayastha-at-gmail.com if you'd like to run a game.

There's more info here:

JiffyCon website

JiffyCon Boston Facebook page

It will be held in the spacious event room at Unity Church in Somerville, MA. We were there last year and it's a great space: a large main area for gaming, hanging out and having fun. It's in easy distance of lots of good food, accessible via the T at Davis Square.

Hope to see you there!
