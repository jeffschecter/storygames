    Posted By: Jason MorningstarWhat do you call a magic horse with multiple horns? My friend Robo suggestsmulticornia(Latincornu, 4th declension noun), which could be abbreviated to the somewhat badass but also breakfastymulticorn. Any other suggestions?
    \nFirstly, u-declension plural neuter is /-ua/, so cornua.

    Nevertheless the correct form is unicornis or (rarely) unicornus. (Compounding is not a very productive process in Latin, so you can't count on the resulting compound to behave in any calculable manner.)
