\n Hey all, my solo RPG contest entry grew out of control until it was simultaneously awesome and ineligible, but it turned into a very straightforward and playable thing. Basically I expanded on the bones of my previous solo game, The Plant, injecting some interesting player scene painting and decision making into the old, reliable choose-from-multiple-paths format. What I arrived at is METAL SHOWCASE 11PM. It's a little 88-page adventure in the very appealing pocket book format over at Lulu. The pitch:

The Metal Showcase is coming up, and you need to win. But first, you need to assemble a band, whip it into shape, and defeat the most bone-crushing lineup the stage at Randy's Show Club has ever seen. This unique half-book half-game allows you to forge your own destiny in contests of skill and luck. Do you have what it takes to win the Metal Showcase - or will your rivals feast on your bones?

Check it out!
