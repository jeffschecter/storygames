    \n Paul is off to Dreamation so I will tackle this thread.

    There is, in fact, an area for Open Gaming. It will be in the massive 2nd floor ballroom, just around the corner from con registration and within line of sight of the RPG HQ. As usual, the third floor will house rpgs in private rooms.

    Albert -- feel free to get yourself signed up for indie games that might interest you; we have a decent schedule of them now though I suspect a number of indie/story games will be added in the next couple weeks. Event registration opens up February 2nd and, since we don't allow all slots to be filled in pre-reg, even if games apparently "fill" on the website, there will be slots open at the con (at RPG HQ) available on a first come, first served basis.

    Alternately, I'm fairly certain there will be a critical mass of GMs and players for pick-up indie/story games as there have been at previous cons. Either way, I think you will have a fun time. Please stop by and say hi if you get the chance. I'll be running games as well as hanging out occasionally at RPG HQ.

    - Denys

    Posted By: Joshua BishopRoby
    As for open gaming -- the Strategicons do not have "east-coast style" open gaming where you can walk in and play a game. You are far more safe in signing up for games or arranging informal games with folks you know. To find indieheads, come to the NerdSoCal meetup at the bar at 7pm Friday, or stop by the IPR booth.     \nJust want to point out that when the con used to be in the previous hotel in the '90s -- which is now the same hotel we've returned to (under new ownership) -- open gaming was GIGANTIC. Hundreds of folks just hanging out and playing pick-up games. Great energy in the ballroom. The layout of the hotel that we've been at for the past few years has discouraged open gaming because there hasn't been a large, conveniently located central space in which to gather.

    So hopefully now that we're back in a space that geographically encourages an open gaming space, we'll see a resurgence once again.
