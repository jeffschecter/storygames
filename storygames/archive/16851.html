\n Since I won't be going to Gen Con, I wanted to find a way to still enjoy the energy of the best four days in gaming. I was planning online games for those days anyway and I figured, why not make an event out of it?



CONcurrent is a gaming con held online (and at local venues) running parallel to Gen Con, from Wednesday 8/15 to Sunday 8/19. The hub can be found on G+ at http://tinyurl.com/CONcurrent-con.

I'm using ConPlanner to organize the con. You can find the con page at http://conplanner.com/CONcurrent. We even have people holding events in their local area as part of the con! You can start Adding Events at http://tinyurl.com/CONcurrent-events.

I love Gen Con, and I love the energy that is generated during that week. With CONcurrent, I hope to be a part of that wave at home while the party's going on in Indy.

Drop by and join in!
