I have just released my new espionage RPG, "Deniable Asset" based on my Power of 12 role-playing system.

I'd love to get a few people to try it out.

To that end I am giving away three free downloads to interested parties.

It is a succinct 45 page book including character generation and rules of play.

I published a free to download micro-version last year, "Hiding in the Mosque."

Also available is my first full mission, "Executive Copier." This mission picks up after the events in "Hiding in the Mosque," continuing the Agents' activities in Uzbekistan.

I can only provide limited authorization codes at this time for the free download. Any more than three would show up on the radar of organizations that I do not want asking questions... or worse.

E-mail to randomeric@randomencounters.net and reference Story-Games. The first three will get a code for a free download. Any e-mail beyond that will receive a cryptic response. If I am still able to respond.

Visit http://www.randomencounters.net/ to see what else I am up to and for links to the download and print versions of my games.

Before it's too late.
