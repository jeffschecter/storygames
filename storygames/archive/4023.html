    \n Thanks for your interest, Ben!

    Playtest report and Another one.

    It's a short-form game about photocopier repairmen. Each player creates a lead, a trainee, and a client, and you rotate going on service calls. On each call, you have to actually fix a copier (the client creates the problem, the Business Solutions techs diagnose and repair it), and also deal with a personal challenge of some sort. On the surface the game is ridiculous, and playtests have been pretty funny, but it has a weird emergent quality of evoking unexpected emotional responses as well. It isn't exactly gut-punching emo porn, but there can be a strong Daniel Clowes sort of vibe.
