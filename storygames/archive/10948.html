\n Once upon a time, as far north as north can go, like well north like past Seven Sisters or some shit, there lived the greatest people that this world will ever know. We cannot look upon them, because the CCTV cameras is all broke and shit.

Behold...\n
  • Shallyce has dropped her mobile down that drain and it is well minging down there
  • Blood, is you stupid or is you just stupid?
  • She is a slag and I is not talking to her.
  • Yeah well your mum does it for a fiver, like for real.
\nDuring play, the players will sit together in a circle, around a lighted cigarette.

In a conflict, the Key Phrases are as follows.\n
  • You'll Never Guess What Happened. replaces Long ago, the people were dying at the end of the world.
    • \n
  • So My Mate [Name], Right. replaces But hope was not yet lost...
    • \n
  • And It Was Like... replaces And So It Was...
    • \n
  • But To Be Honest, I Was Well Out Of It. replaces But that all happened long agon, and now there are none who remember it.
\nAnd the Key Conflict Phrases are:\n
  • Yeah But... replaces But only if...
    • \n
  • And He Was Like... replaces And furthermore...
    • \n
  • You Is Chatting Shit replaces It shall not come to pass
    • \n
  • You Is Disrespecting My Human Rights replaces You ask far too much
    • \n
  • Whatever replaces It was not meant to be
    • \n
  • Innit replaces That was how it happened
    • \n
  • Talk to the hand replaces But it was no matter
    • \n
  • He is not even joking replaces We shall see what comes of it.
\nWith apologies to Ben, innit.

    \n To take it a bit more seriously than we did on the night - and this is just my take:

    These are basically kids getting on with life in a hostile environment. The pressures are on to aggrandize, and belittle others to accrue social worth. This is at odds with their better nature and seeks to corrode it. The end of a tragic story arc would be to confuse rep for reality, and kill or be killed for a meaningless slight.

    Other characters could exit the estate in other ways, by the book, right? I've never Polarised to the end, forget if you all get to the end of the Weariness track.
