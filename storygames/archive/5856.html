    \n DUDE. It's like Crimson Skies... in space! Very cool.

    For system, I would use Danger Patrol (my game in development which has a very similar setting and style). But I'm the only person who has it right now. Not helpful, I know.

    Savage is a great choice, I think. You could use the character creation system to make your ships, too, and then fight them on the battlemat (probably using the helping mechanic so the PC crew can do stuff that matters). Or do ship-to-ship as simple contests and use the mat only for person-scale stuff.

    Something simmy/crunchy but lightweight feels right for this. Oh, duh. Spirit of the Century.

    EDIT: If you want to really story it up, Shock:. Your shock is rocketships! That could be awesome.
