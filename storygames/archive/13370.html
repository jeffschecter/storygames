\n I love crowd sourcing and I love good rpg music.

So, you've tried Grooveshark? It's an online "radio" which lets you listen to any music, free, and it's legal. You can listen to one of its channels, you can search for songs or (and this is where it gets fun), you can search for other people's playlists.

For example, try this Dogs in the Vineyard playlist. Disclaimer: I'm Irish. I don't know anything about this kind of music. I just searched on Grooveshark for all the music suggested by Vincent in the back of his book.

---------------------------------------------

It would be cool if other folks could put together short playlists for some of their favourite genres/themes/games. Because it's free and instant, it seems like an awesome opportunity. If you give it a try, why not post a playlist here, so we can hear it too?
