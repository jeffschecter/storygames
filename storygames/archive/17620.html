    \n Paul, your keen mind reveals to us, as always, the truth of the matter. The monk is testing us.

    But what is he testing us on?

    Do we pass the test by ignoring its existence?
    By connecting some as-yet-unseen set of clues?
    By engaging him despite his strict instructions not to?
    By seizing directorship of the test itself?

    And why is this monk testing us? For what purpose?
