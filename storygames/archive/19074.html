\n Hi Everybody!

I'm Hope and I look after a Story Games collective in London called Storygasm. We're young, inclusive and make some great stories happen. If you live in London and can make weekday evening games around London Bridge or Aldgate East then come down and play with us!

Last night we ran two simultaneous games of Jason Morningstar's The Climb. We created the two base camps in the basement of a pub with a lot of plastic sheeting and red and white stripy tape. With the lights out, relying on (not much) torchlight and the sound track up, the setting was rather atmospheric. Great fully contained pack Jason!

Next up we're doing a Monsterhearts LARP (obviously by the lovely Joe McDaldno) and A Mother's Heart from LARPs from the Factory by Christina Christensen and Eirik Fatland. We also do a lot of Story Tabletop too and inevitably have started to blend the forms.

Add yourself to the FB group below to receive game invites or drop me an email and come play!

https://www.facebook.com/groups/storygasm/

hope.gilham@gmail.com
