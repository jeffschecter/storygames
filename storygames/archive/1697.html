  • Josh Roby
    November 2006
    Oh, Marco reminds me. I had just got GURPS Riverworld (a setting in which everyone who has ever lived on Earth is resurrected on the banks of a planet-spanning river all at once). I wanted to play it, but I wanted to really get the shock and awe of the world across, so I set up a game but didn't tell the players we were going to play Riverworld. Instead I told them that we were going to do a time travel game, and individually approached each player and told them "secretly" that I wanted one of the party members to be a historical figure that the party picked up along the way. So everybody came to the first session with their characters -- Joan of Arc, John Glenn, William Wallace -- and I start the game off with, "You die. You're burned at the stake, pass away in bed at home, are drawn and quartered. And then you wake up. You're naked and there are a thousand other naked people laying in the grass on the banks of a huge river." The looks on their faces were priceless.
