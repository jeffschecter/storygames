    \n The license for Shadowrun has passed from FanPro to Catalyst Game Labs in July of '07 I think it was. (In fact, I am uncertain if the license has been finalized, but they do have a waiver to continue publishing the line from WizKids Games until final negotiations are completed.)

    Since they have been publishing, they got Emergence, Augmentation, and Corporate Enclaves out. The revised 4th edition (first one from Catalyst) hits next week (ish), and the new weapons book, Arsenal is due out in March. So, they have really been picking up the pace since they have been working on the game.

    Hope that all helps.
