\n Hi guys and gals,
some of you might have seen them through my twitter and/or facebook, and stuff, but I figured I'd echo here all the previews we (Janus Design) have done for the Italian edition of Spirit of the Century. It's mostly illustrations, done by our very talented artists. The layout is all-new, also, and features a two-column setup we think is pretty readable; the bigger size of the paper also means less pages... it's only 300 pages long.

The book is coming out tomorrow at PLAY! Modena and it's a sturdy hardcover, 17x24cm book with color and UV coated cover, glossy paper and sewed binding (much like a big, flashy version of our Polaris edition :-D ). Oh, and the price is 35 EUR if someone is itching to learn some Italian ^___^
(all the images are clickable for huge sized versions)
