    \n So this was an interesting experience today. My supplier informed me that we would either have to delay the production of Writer's Dice until after Christmas or produce them on schedule as 16mm.

    I anticipated most people would want them on time, even if smaller, since several prospective backers wanted assurances of that delivery date. I still wanted to keep everyone in the loop before I placed the order, just to see what backers thought. Boy, was I in for a surprise.

    An overwhelming number of backers have said they'd rather wait and get the 19mm dice shown in the video and photos. Goes to show transparency is the best policy.
