    \n Bill, one suggestion on an otherwise very nice catalog:
    It would be good to have some sort of separation between the entries for each product. For my tastes, they run too close to each other and get muddled a bit. It may end up increasing your page count, so if that is a concern, you'd have to play with font and image size, but yeah, something, even a line of white space, between each product entry, would make the page look even better than it already is.

    Nice work.

    And I totally grok what you say about layout. :-)
