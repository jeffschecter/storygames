\n I got this idea from another forum where there's a monthly post involving Miniature Painting Vows.

Basically you post and list what you vow to finish painting that month. My post this month included painting 4 Skaven Clanrats and a Warplightning Cannon.

It's neat because it produces a small amount of accountability as you typically return to the thread and post your results and people often provide encouragement.

I don't know if this is a worthwhile SG endeavor but I thought I'd give it a shot and see if it sticks.

My vows for the last week and a half of August:

-Post Actual Play of my BWG One-on-one campaign on the BW forums. This game is rocking and I've never done one-on-one gaming before.

-Put together a very simple website for my game Charge! and somehow convince a soul or two to playtest it

-Put more effort into prepping our biweekly steampunk game. They attempt to take down a Crime Lord Necromancer on Wednesday.

I have more but that's aggressive enough considering we have a week anda half left of August.
