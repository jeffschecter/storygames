\n Registration is now open for Go Play NW 2009! There are a few improvements and changes this year:\n
  • THREE days of gaming: from Friday night June 26 \u2013 Sunday June 28
    • \n
  • Cool location downtown: Freehold Theatre in the heart of Seattle\u2019s Belltown neighborhood
    • \n
  • Friday Night Feast: food and beer will be available for a reasonable additional charge
    • \n
  • Same price as last year!
\nPre-registration really helps us out, so go register now, or check out the GPNW home page for more information.

Go Play NW is a Seattle convention of indie gamers and game designers where we get together, greet old friends, make some new ones, and play some games.

There are several affordable hotels near the Freehold, and many local gamers willing to offer couches and spare rooms for out of towners. If you're looking for a crash pad or some hotel recommendations, post in this thread and we'll do our best to help you out.

      \n I have a nefarious plan/crazy experiment to bring the OLD SCHOOL (so authentic it's even spelled correctly) to GPNW.

      I'll be running a classic megadungeon, tentatively named "Vergammon's Perch" ALL WEEKEND, using Swords & Wizardry/OD&D.

      Here's how it will work. Players will be free to come and go as they like. If at least two people are signed up prior to the start of a given time block, I will run it for them. (Otherwise I'll wander off and inflict myself on some other game). Returning players may continue an existing character if they choose, or roll up a new one. The focus will be on dungeon expeditions. Players are free to enter or exit the dungeon as many times as they like in a time block, but if they are found to be in the dungeon at the end of the time block they will be subject to the PERILOUS TABLE OF ESCAPING THY DOOM.

      You have been warned.

      \n Hey everyone - GoPlay Northwest '09 is only 2 months away! Is that possible?

      REGISTRATION
      If you haven't registered yet, hop to it! Pre-registration ends and prices go up June 1st. Pre-registering really helps us cover our upfront costs and plan the best game weekend.
      http://goplaynw.wetpaint.com/page/Registration

      GAME SIGN-UP
      We're going to do it much the same as last year using the powers of the the wetpaint wiki, but in three phases:\n
        \n
      • May 1: GM sign-up begins - email your games to us and we'll create pages/threads for them on our site. See the schedule grid and the info we need on the Schedule & Game Sign-up Page.
        • \n
      • May 25: Game Preview begins - This is the week you can see what games are scheduled, make any changes etc
        **New for GPNW '09** - We're going to create an "ad-hoc gaming" slot for each session if you want to tell people your plan is to be available to play or run pick-up games.
        • \n
      • Noon June 1: Player sign-up begins - sign-up via comments on the wiki
      \nMore details including the schedule grid for the weekend:
      http://goplaynw.wetpaint.com/page/Schedule+and+Game+Sign-Up
