\n
\nAlthough the game store is located in Bettendorf Iowa, the Con will be held at the Moline Illinois Holiday Inn (near the airport). we hope to round up some playtesters for PsiRun & expose folks ti indie/Story Games. besides doing our prideful PsiRun promotions we hope to do some games-on-demand of some other Indie-RPGs.

    \n as promised i'm tagging back on how GamerCon5 went.
    \n
    \nit was good. it was small. a one room affair. with about 35 attendees & judges in the room per day at hand at max.
    \n
    \nChris & did run a few full session games. each game making it to the Crossroads [endgame].
    \n
    \nwe make some good conections. we talked w/ the coordinater regarding offering sessions of PsiRun play @ his game store. we got a big "you bet" from him.
    \n
    \nwe opened the eyes of a few people to the existance of IndieRPGs & StoryGames. to play off of a paraphrase of ObiWan~ they had just taken their first steps into a larger world of roleplaying..
