    Excellent.

    I think it would be worth looking at Restoration Comedies. They're basically very bawdy, very vivacious comedies and they use the sorts of archetypes you're talking about. It seems to me to be pretty much what you're doing.

    You could pretty much make a Restoration Comedy by throwing together a Lover, a Wanton (the housewife) and a Cuckold. And probably a Drunkard and a Debtor for good measure. Oh, and a Young Innocent.

    Have a look at this:

    http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Restoration_comedy

    If nothing else, I think it would be worth you looking at the plots of restoration comedies (The Country Wife, Way of the World, The Provok'd Wife), because they seem to be basically what you want. They've all got Wikipedia entries. See what you think.

    Graham
