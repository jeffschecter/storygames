    \n Sorry to bump such an old thread, but...

    I just recently got in to SotC, and have fallen deeply in love

    I'm working on making a fan site which will present a new adventure each month that takes place in the "official" SotC universe. Each adventure will be inspired by (but not, necessarily, "based on") real pulps. Maybe the inspiration will be from the title, or the cover, or the plot of one of the stories.

    To keep up the pulp feel, I decided to make a little cover for each "issue" and most of these will use illustrations from original pulps. I just got done doing the second one when I found this post, so I thought I'd share.





    I also wanted to point anyone who's interested in this kind of thing to BlamBot fonts. Several of the free ones offered there are very appropriate for pulp things, and *all* of the fonts are a good resource for anybody doing game related graphics.
