    \n I got a bunch of people together online and played a spontaneously organized game of D&D 3.5 with 1st level characters. It was a silly little romp involving a quest to find a small local village's stolen Destiny Stone in the kobold-filled ruins, and involved some wacky adventures in miscommunication between characters, and a climactic battle with a half-fiendish baboon, done in by a successfully confirmed critical hit on the part of a player who had failed almost every other roll that night.

    It was definitely fun to game in the New Year. I am happy and content.
