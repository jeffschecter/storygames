Hey guys,

\n

I just wanted to share that Elizabeth and I are releasing MRG under a Creative Commons Attribution license on Monday.

\n

We have been working with Ryan Macklin and Jess Banks to raise money for our daughter Gwen - she is autistic, and since we relocated to California, her school district has failed us at every turn when we tried to get her the special education support she needs to succeed. A number of great authors have donated short stories as a thank-you for donors, and we're so grateful for all the support you've given Fighting For Gwen, that we decided to share a game with you. The plain text will be available on Monday, free for all.

\n

You don't have to donate to get access to the game, but if you'd like to, the link is there(:
