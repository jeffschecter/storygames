\n As mentioned in this thread, I've been playing an on-line version of the classic Werewolf game (also known as Mafia). At the prodding of a few, I got around to collating the materials and dumping them on my site. You can download the eWerewolf zip file, which includes a short rulesheet, a pdf of our role descriptions, and a handy spreadsheet for balancing games. BTW, I lied in that earlier thread, we have around 150 roles now.

I'd love to hear some feedback on the packet. And, as I offered to Ryan, if anyone wants to get in on our next game, let me know. The more the merrier.
