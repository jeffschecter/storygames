    \n Here's what I would need to read Planet Story Games more regularly:

    1) Slightly better layout. The names are too big, there's no seperation between one article and the next (like an bar-tag), I'm iffy on the green but I could keep it.
    2) Variable width columns, or at least something larger than "40% of your screen". The columns are too narrow for my tastes.
    3) I'd love to see a "cut off and link" system with the feeds. Like, if the article is more than X lines (X being 10 or so), it cuts off the article, but allows you to click on a "More" link or the equivalent to read the full article.
