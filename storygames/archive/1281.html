I've been participating on a few local groups and boards: CVGamers boards and the yahoo group for a local Library Meetup group. They've proved somewhat useful for finding game days, local RPGA events, and the like.

After finding that people still aren't assembling groups very successfully, I finally took the plunge and have started a local mailing list, modeled on the Stealth Gamers Forge post.

So, a few things:
First, thanks to James for talking about Stealth Gamers and providing a useful model I could crib from.
Second, if you're in Fresno (or are somewhere nearby) and are looking to join a PTA game, let me know.
Third, if you're not looking to play PTA, but are gaming in the area, contact fga [at] scottrpg.com and I'll add you to a games announcement list, so you'll know what games are available to join.
