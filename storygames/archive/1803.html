BBC4 has started a series on British science fiction.

Whether you're British or not, you can share your recollections of British science fiction at...

http://www.bbc.co.uk/mysciencefictionlife/

If you do live in the UK, I really recommend the documentary series The Martians And Us, which started last night on BBC4. The topic last night was aliens; next week it's dystopias; and the week after that it's apocalyptic sci-fi.
