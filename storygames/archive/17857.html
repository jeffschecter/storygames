    \n Oh yeah, for opposed rolls and such I just make them all static. Such as, this guy is Good with a blaster, so he is at +3 when shooting all the time and only we roll in regards to that. I think that's what you're saying, anyway. This little chart is more for when we mosey into the black market and are wondering if there are any rare seeds that will serve as props when we pretend to be horticulturists. Rather than leave it up to us, we leave it up to Fate (hahahahahaha, oh man, whew!) and roll on this chart.

    P.S. Turns out there were some very rare seeds, which is why we're now merchants instead of mercenaries. Turns out, it's just as dangerous.
