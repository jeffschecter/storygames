\n Rafael Pinheiro Costa wrote a really interesting playset called Planeta Droga and shared it with us, and we wanted to share it further. It's based on his brother's experiences with addiction, and it strips the "powerful ambition" half of the equation out of the Fiasco experience. What remains is something smaller, darker and sadder, but still fun - by some definition of fun. Anyway it is thought-provoking and takes Fiasco in a weird direction and we hope you enjoy it.
