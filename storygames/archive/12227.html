\n Life After People - broke my post-apocalyptic setting
heheh.... not really...

The Discovery Channel's series (Life After People) and other similar documentaries and books) have described the heck out of what is LIKELY to happen to all the structures and such in the future - in a very cool way HOWEVER they tend to *show* that what many post-apocalyptic settings assume CANNOT be the case.

As it turns out many of the structures and such that are often used in post-apoc settings would be GONE GONE GONE completely after 600-1000+ years.


So...
What are some ideas that might "fix" this little scientific issue?

Either through pseudo-science or magic or complete other fiction or a combination of ideas... what are some ideas that a GM could write into the backstory of a post-apoc setting to "explain" how buildings and structures WOULD/could still be around after a long long time in the future?


Any creative ideas??

-kev-
