\n One of my favourite books is called "Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software". I purchased it long ago when I was coding. It features solutions coders have found to typical problems. The most efficient algorithms that repeatably solve different coding problems.

Is there a similar resource for RPGs? A list of house rules, mechanics, game rules, design approaches that offer ways to solve different gaming and game design situations?

This entire forum would be such a resource but I'm looking for a list or linked directory I could easily browse.
