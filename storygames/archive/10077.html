    \n Putting up a sign at the room was what ruffled feathers last year. One of the GenCon staff took me aside and gave me a talking-to. He did threaten to close the room down if we didn't take the sign down. Putting up a sign on a nearby wall, pointing to our room, ruffled even more feathers, so I definitely wouldn't suggest doing that.

    But it might be different this year. I'd suggest going prepared with signs, but not spending too much money on them. Put one up outside the room, but be prepared to take it down if asked.

    Definitely give flyers to the booth staff. If you want someone to stand around and give out flyers, that might be a different thing, and might ruffle feathers.

    Hey, listen, I don't mean to be negative. Do try things out, but do be ready for members of the GenCon staff complaining. I hope it goes well.

    Graham
