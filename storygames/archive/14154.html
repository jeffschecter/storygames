    \n I'm back from GothCon XXXV. I wrote a short report on my blog, mostly of interest to those who have been wanting to see artifacts from While the World Ends in English.

    --

    Other reports from the convention:

    Ken Hite, see the entries for April 22 and 23. He was impressed by the Swedish (trad) games Western and Fallen Reich.

    James Raggi, visited and enjoyed the quiet life that comes with not being recognized by anyone. (The Swedes didn't get into the hobby with D&D, even in the 80'ies, so the OSR is a very obscure phenomenon here. (We were busy playing BRP derivatives.))

    Forum thread from a Swedish forum with reports from the con, for those of you who simply can't get enough of that horrible horrible Google machine translated Swedish.
