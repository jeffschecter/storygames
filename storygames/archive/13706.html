\n I started developing mechanics for superheroes but realized the setting was all wrong for it. After some brainstorming, I decided to set it in industrial age europe (perhaps France). I want to keep a heavy superstitious tone to it with a bit of horror mixed in (Dr. Jeckel and Invisible Man style). Here are some archetypes (theme) [hero/villain type] I came up with so far (with subtypes).
\n
\nInventor (science/logic) [gadget hero]
\n-Electricity (Tesla style)
\n-Mechanization 0DaVinci style)
\n-Chemistry (Explosives, Acids, etc.)
\nAlchemist (magic/logic) [Gadget/Magic]
\n-Enchant Item (essentially making an item magical, hence gadgets)
\n-Control Elements (being able to shape and alter substances of the various elements)
\n-Imbue Self (potions and meditation to power himself up)
\nSorcerer (magic/social) [Magic]
\n-Hermetic Spells (latin phrases, symbols, etc.)
\n-Religious Miracles (priests from any religion)
\n-Witchcraft (Potions/Blessings/Curses) (some nice Celtic/tribal themes here)
\nPower-house (body) [Attribute boosts]
\n-Paragon (the perfect human)
\n-Mech-Warrior (can you say Iron Man)
\n-Monstrous (Mr. Hyde/Hulk, maybe Vampire/Werewolf too)
\nGenius (mind) [Attribute boosts]
\n-Mastermind (Sherlock/Moriarty style)
\n-Mesmer (Hypnotists, super-charismatic leaders, etc.)
\n-Spiritualist/Clairvoyant (super intuitives, they just 'feel' the truth)
\nExpert (physical) [Skill boosts]
\n-Gunner/Archer (typical but with steam punk riffles and arrow-tips)
\n-Burglar (old fashioned cat-burglars, lock-pickers, etc.)
\n-Ranger/Scout (Celtic themed forest dweller)
\nTalent (social) [Skills boosts]
\n-Face/Spy (typical but themed to the era)
\n-Fixer/Trader (streetsmarts, the guy who knows everyone and can close the deal)
\n-Manipulator (stereotypical master manipulator)
\n-Investigator (another Sherlock/murder mystery theme)
\n
\nI felt I kind of ran out of ideas and started getting unthematic near the end.
\nAny other ideas for character types or suggested modifications to the above?

    \n Players can mix and match since, the archetypes will be more similar to character templates and then class options. I plan on having rules to make your own.
    \n
    \nI love what Ascetic archetype. Very superhero and could also fit any time-line. I may convert those ideas into the European theme unless I want to go for an expansion.
    \n
    \nBy the way, I have been doing a lot of work on the setting and it will definitely be in late 1800's Europe, ruled by Napoleon III. So it is a bit of an alternate history bit, though I am only manipulating about 50yrs of political history. From the superhero/steampunk perspective though, I changed all of human history by making magic and superpowers real.
