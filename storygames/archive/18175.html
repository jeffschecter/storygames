Based on the indie video game Hotline Miami. I originally ran a Lacuna campaign based off this vg, but found it wandered more into David Lynch territory which was still awesome. With this hack I wanted the feel of always being close to death and creating a gory bloodbath in your wake, just like the game. Still some work to be done, but would love to hear what anyone thinks.
