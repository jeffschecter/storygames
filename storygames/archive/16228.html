\n I made a web based text adventure game that's played between twitter buddies

Tweet Your Adventure

The way the game works is that one person is in charge of writing short (100 character) story pieces and 3 options for their twitter buddy to choose. The twitter buddy then clicks a choice and the game continues.

I put the game together very quickly so it's more than likely full of bugs, just let me know what you find. I stumbled upon this community and thought you all might enjoy the game.

    \n The only thing I use is the "write to your timeline" priv. So when you use the app it will write out automatically to your timeline your adventure.

    For example...

    If I wanted to play against "twitterPerson" and I wrote the first part of the adventure as:

    "You have encountered a boar. He looks very hungry. What do you do?"
    Option 1: Run away from it.
    Option 2: Fight it.
    Option 3: Sob

    It will write to your twitter timeline:
    "@twitterPerson You have encountered a boar. He looks very hungry. What do you do? http://bitly.com/"

    clicking on the bit.ly link will then let twitterPerson make a choice. Their choice will post to you as:
    "@you Run away from it. http://bitly.com/"

    So on and so forth
