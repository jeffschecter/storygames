\n I have never played Monsterhearts and will be MC-ing a game for a group of people who have also never played. I've read through the book and the Reference Sheets a few times, feel fairly confident about the mechanics. What advice would those of you who have Monsterhearts experience give to someone running it for the first time? Anything I should keep in mind? Something you realized after playing the game that you wish you had thought of at the start?

    \n Ask a lot of questions, especially about strings. Don't let someone just take a string on someone. Find out why they now have emotional leverage. Don't let up until it's clear to everyone why what happened has given one character the ability to manipulate another.

    \n In which case I'd have a think about which moves need other characters to be absent to work well. I found first time out I didn't get to use the Mortal shut down move 'Mess with me, mess with him' because it kind of needs them to be in small scenes in trouble. In the one shot they just didn't seem to come up because we were pushing play along. Knowing that, I chose moves more carefully in the next one shot I played.
