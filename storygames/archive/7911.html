\n We're exploring a planet. We've hit on a theme that the population expresses affection by demonstrating anger. For example, after a successful deal with a market trader, the trader yells at you and tells you to get out of his store. It doesn't mean anything, it's just how things are done.

Later, Tris' character is hitting on this girl. He's an outsider, she's a native of the planet. It's going well, and she's interested, so she yells abuse at him.

He apologises and backs off. She assumes he's not interested (if he was, he would have yelled back).

They meet the next morning. He smiles and apologises. She smiles and apologises too (she's less interested now).

This goes on a while. He becomes sick of it. He demands to know what's going on. She is irritable. He is irritable too. He yells at her. She kisses him.

I really like this world.

Graham
