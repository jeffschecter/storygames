\nBlood and Bronze is not a story game, it's a board game. But it's by James Brown (of Death's Door), so S-G is one of probably two places on Earth where people have maybe heard about the game.

I've finally come to terms with the fact that I'm never going to own the Without Peer!, bronze-hammered bowl edition of the game, so I ask, as it's on IPR, and I know nothing of how it plays: Have you played this game? What's to like about it? Is it worth my $20?

    \n I've played it, it is weird and compelling. I guess I'd describe it as the bastard child of a light-complexity wargame and a Larp. If that description makes your heart race then it is $20 well spent. I'm not sure it has a ton of replay value but it is a singular gaming experience.
