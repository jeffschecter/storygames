    \n If you're willing to go as far north as Seattle there's Dragonflight 8/8-10 in Bellevue WA.

    Labor Day weekend (8/29-9/1) in Santa Clara there's Conquest SF (that I'm going to), and a bigger con in Los Angeles.

    Endgame in Oakland usually has a one-day minicon in October, that's excellent for story games.

    This is the first year for Conquest Reno 2008/11/1-2 so it'll likely be tiny, but who knows?
