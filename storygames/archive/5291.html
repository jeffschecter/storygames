\n So I've been working on this project on my blog for, jeez, over a year now, off and on. It's called the RPG Design Handbook, and every so often when I get in the mood I write another section. I usually write them off the cuff, and the idea is that over time I'll revisit the sections and revise them based on feedback. It may end up as a thing, some day, but right now I think it's a good mental exercise for me, and hopefully helpful for others to see how I think some of these concepts fit together.

Anyhow, I just wrote a new section, and feedback would be appreciated! Also, feedback on anything else is totally cool! It's the magic of the internet!

Thanks!

New Section: Chapter 3, parts 2 & 3 - Lumpley Principle & System Does Matter vs. System Doesn't Matter
