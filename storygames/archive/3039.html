\n So I'm in the final stages of getting my game "The Committee for the Exploration of Mysteries" ready for publication this summer. I'm sure a lot of other folks are doing the same with their new games, or perhaps getting ready to do another print run of an old one. I'd love to swap ad space with any interested parties, particularly any other Game Chef 2006 participants who are taking the publishing plunge.

So drop a reply here of shoot me an e-mail if you're interested. And if anyone else is looking for ad swaps, please feel free to let others know in this thread.
