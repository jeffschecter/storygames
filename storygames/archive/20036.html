\n Just a shower thought that came to me this morning. I remembered an interesting mechanic from the game Underworld that set stakes as a way to determine the outcome of a large battle quickly.

So, I was thinking something along the lines of this\u2026 Each side in a conflict picks three objectives for a conflict, things that they want to see happen. Dice are rolled, and depending on the result, you may get some or all of your objectives completed, and the other side may get some of theirs as well.

Have there been any other games that do something like this?
