\n
\n
\n\nWarriorMonk \n\n\n

\n\nApril 2013 \n edited April 2013 in Story Games
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n So, you like to GM? You like challenges? What if we write a lot of inspiring difficult scenes for you to pick up randomly and frame for your players? Do you think you could pull these up, huh? Oh, players can surely come up with some solution, you can fumble some dice and help them a bit... But I'm talking about scenes that you could find challenging but fun to present, like, I dunno...

-Choose a player randomly. Somehow warn her PC/other PCs that she's gonna get killed soon. Actually GO for it with all your resources for the whole session. Don't make it look like you're picking on the player, but more like the NPCs/monsters are picking on the character for an unknown (yet) reason.

-Portrait a character as a genuine latin-american. Don't use the archetype.

-Barf forth apocalyptica. A neat clean apocalyptica.
