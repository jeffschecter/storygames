\n In honor of the season, we've put together a little coloring book for the favorite ghoul or goblin in your life.


The Dread House Coloring Book is a free pdf you can download from the Dig 1000 Holes website. Also, for a limited time only, a print copy comes with the game. Order by Halloween or while we've got them!

The illustrations were so fun, we wanted to let folks be able to play with them, and we've found kids like to color and makes stuff while they are waiting for their turn. We'd love to see what you come up with to color these flights of fancy. We'll post scans of the colored pages. Whisper us here for the email.

Happy Halloween!

Best,
Em & Ep

    \n Tested on Ingrid Ellingboe (age 5 1/2) and Tovey Baker (almost 5). Ingrid liked it so much that she would not let Tovey color it because it was "special". I tried to explain that I could easily print another one. It took some convincing, but she finally relented, but Mr. Ellingboe brought out a huge sheet of blank paper, and Tovey decided to draw on that. Ingrid was happy that he wouldn't have to use her special coloring book. She liked the creepy pictures, but then again, she's an Ellingboe girl. She's going to color it tomorrow.

    For some reason, the O's on the cover didn't print.
