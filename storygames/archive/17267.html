\n Players are British Lords trying to improve their status in the Royal Court by dropping everyone else down a peg. The best way to do this is to slander the other Nobles\u2019 daughters. Unfortunately your own wild daughter seems to be doing her very best to compromise your family name herself, by performing some very unlady-like acts. Cover up the truth about your own family, while bringing everyone else's indiscretions to light in this party game for 3 or more players.


Slander has been a personal project for the past 18 months, and I've been actively playtesting for the past year. It didn't start as a great game, but I stuck with the concept, refined the processes, and now I think it shines. What do you think?

I need outside opinions to drive it to the next level, and honestly to tell me whether or not I should try to make this into a product.
