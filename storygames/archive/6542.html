\n So thanks to Serenity, I have to design a game about God's black ops angels. Dammit.

[SPOILER WARNING: If you're one of the three Firefly fans who haven't seen Serenity yet, this might be spoiler-ish]



It started with an argument about Shepherd Book, whether or not he would be an angel or a demon. (Hey, it was late) I maintained no way would he be an angel, and my friend comes back with 'you mean God doesn't have a black ops division?'. He then pointed out the bizarre and inexplicable switch from Asshole God to Nice God between the Old and New Testament, and said that obviously God could afford to look nice in the New Testament because now he has black ops to do the dirty work for him and he gets to keep his hands clean.

Needless to say he won. (My friend, that is. Not God.)

So now I have all these irreverent thoughts spinning around in my brain like 'they SAY that David Koresh blew himself up, but really it was the black ops'. Or 'if God has black ops, does that make Lucifer a terrorist or a freedom fighter?'. I think the thing that I'm going to hell for, for sure, is deciding that that means Jesus is God's Press Secretary.

Dammit. I'm going to hell again. But I'm pretty sure I have to try and write this now. I just hope I won't fail as hard as I failed Game Chef.

~Anna

    \n The whole Jesus thing was pretty smart, freshening up the brand, getting a new generation of recruits to do the wet-work. I don't think you really need a black ops squad when you've got a tidal wave of murderous zealots at your beck and call. All the covert agents would be on the other side, going to ground amid a sea of newly-minted Christians and biding their time. So God can crack mountains but lacks precision and subtlety. He owns the west and he's still hungry. His opponents have their own agendas and are capable of inflicting small but measured blows. The polytheists make nice with each other and establish cellular networks. God's own cast-offs regroup and make nice with the spiritual arms dealers and disaffected rank-and-file, literally demonized by the Jesus-run propaganda machine.
