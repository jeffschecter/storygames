    \n I did some art for Evony -- it was kind of funny. They said originally they wanted an attractive female character, and wanted her to have sex appeal, and to have medieval-style weapons, armor, and clothing. They also said they did not want her to be lewd or over-exposed, and wanted her to appear "formidable". So, I gave them two possibilities that I did quickly:





    They selected the second one... and then the fun began. First, they said that they wanted her in a pose where she was kneeling or knocked-down, with an expression like she wants the viewer to help her, and wanted the sword in a separate image, so they could place it as they wanted. I sent them a few pose tests -- telling them that that was what they were, and that I'd do finals later (my contract prevents me from posting any of the images I actually sent them, I'm afraid). They picked two of those, and asked me to produce a new image, with her clothing ripped up and bloodied. I did that. They came back saying they need it more ripped and bloodied, so I started on that.

    Then they let me know that they had gone ahead and produced an ad using one of the test images. Now, these were pose tests, meant to give an idea -- not meant for final use. Here's one of the ads they did, since I can link to someone else's image of it without violating my contract:



    Note the sleeve poke-through, the eyes, the skin tone. These are things I'd planned to fix when they chose an image for a final render... but there they were, in the actual ad they were using. They did pay me, though, and, well, if they didn't care about quality, that just meant I could do the rest of their contract that much faster.

    I went through three successively more ripped-and-torn versions of the clothing, until my poor virtual model was left with basically scraps that approximated a bikini. Thankfully, they never actually used those versions (I think because of the forum posts making fun of the skin tone and eyes that hadn't been fixed), and instead used other images.

    I did find it interesting, though, that they were originally asking for a character who was sexy but competent -- saying specifically that they wanted a Lara Croft type -- then wanted her defeated, with clothes successively more ripped, etc.

    Ah, well... at least I actually got paid.
