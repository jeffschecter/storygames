    \n GoogleDocs is an incredible resource. In fact, you can now host a doc, watch in real time as people view your doc, and then open a group chat window and talk to those people as they read your doc.

    I dunno why, but I found that really cool.

    Sharability is way up there, although who can and cannot access/change data can be a little confusing at times.

    Basically, I think this:

    Is it a PDF (a document or file) that will be unchanged, permanent? Use either DropBox or GoogleDocs.
    Is it any kind of doc that you could imagine editing, or want group comments on? GoogleDocs.
    Most eyes humanly possible with least resistance (creating accounts): GoogleDocs.
    Sharing many, many themed documents with the same consistent group of people (ie your gaming group members) and no one else? DropBox shines in this category.
