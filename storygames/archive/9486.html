\n
\n I was thinking of experimenting with a blog project wherein I take small, "nouns" of fiction such as "Ghosts", "Jerks", "Barbarians", "Serial Killers", "Time Travel", "Dreams", "Gods", etc etc, and examine how I view their incorporation in role playing games, kind of like a Monster Manual for indie hippies. I would probably start with an examination of different variations on the theme, and lead into their usage in my role-playing experience as well as other entertainment sources. Ultimately, I'd want to present examples that I found particularly good or bad with some analysis on the underlying principles on why some things work well in games/fiction and why other things don't.

I'm posting to see if there is much immediate interest in such a blog, and also to test whether people are of aware of projects in a similar vein.
