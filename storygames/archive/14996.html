\n Hello everybody-

\nI'm planning on running a Continuum/Narcissist time travel game in the near future. These are both obscure games, and Narcissist never got a proper release.
\n
\nThere are a few versions of Narcissist out there - v0.5, v0.7 and v0.9. I have both v0.5 and v0.7. The authors of the game have gone on record to mention that v0.9 took a wildly different direction from the others. I have tried to reach them for comment but all I have found are dead email addresses.
\n
\nWhich brings me to my question, and I know it's a long shot. Does anybody know anything - anything at all - about Narcissist v0.9? Or, does anybody know how to reach the authors of the game?
\n
\nThanks for your help!
