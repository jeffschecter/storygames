\n I am, however, making a story game tentatively called "The Archipelago". It's meant to be run by my group (if they decide to agree), and we'll certainly base our private campaign on the Earthsea books. I'm toying with the idea of publishing the game when we've tested it; it won't have any mention of anything from the books - but it will have rules on how to handle the Balance and Pattern, how to create detailed stories with strong protagonists, how to describe places and people you meet in a way that the group can agree on.
