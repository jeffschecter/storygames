    \nReally? Ok, here goes:

    JD, in his aggregate of RPGNow releases, omitted one very obvious one: Sci-Fi Beta Kappa, written by me and a gamer buddy, and uploaded to DriveTHRURPG/RPGNow in October last year. I've also got low cost beta versions of many of my current GIPs coming out over the course of the next few months, the first one (due out very soon) being Where the Heart Is, my game about alternative family structures. If you want to follow the progress of these games, and generally chew the roleplaying fat (it's more appetising than it sounds!), wander over to my design workshop on G+.

    \n I Just finished the playable Beta of Mythic Mortals! It's still free for now, but I'll be putting it up on the store page soonish!

    http://schirduans.com/david/my-creations/mythic-mortals

    In a setting inspired by the Avengers, X-men, Devil May Cry, and God of War; your in-game persona has the potential to reshape the landscape, overcome incredible foes, and engage in exciting fights that span cities.The goal of the game is to do amazing things, feel like a bad-ass, and by the end of the session, have a story worthy of an ancient epic.

    You play as yourself, granted Ancient and Incredible powers
    Card-based mechanics inspired by Magic the Gathering and Hearthstone
    Over-the-top abilities ensure that fights will level cities and inspire feelings of badassery
    A modern setting allows for diverse and familiar combat scenarios
    Built to be modular from the ground up, ripe for community hacks and additions
    Perfect for short campaigns, one-shots, or for when you haven\u2019t prepared a Pathfinder adventure, but you and your friends still want to kick some ass.
