    \n Yeah, pretty much my friends and acquaintances, Joe. What I do is ask people when they and I both are not busy and have some down time, so it is opportunistic and I am just grabbing the available and interested people around me at that moment, usually at the tail end of the show. In this one you'll see some WOTC guy I grabbed at the food court by chance, but everyone else I know and love.

    It isn't like I have a lock on the format; I'd love to see someone take the idea and run with it using better gear, more focus, and a broader reach. When I do get random people they always have really interesting things to say but I don't care enough to personally make it a big deal.
