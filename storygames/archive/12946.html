    \n The way I do it: You can't take a weapon you already have. If you get promoted from Trooper to Corporal, you get either an ECannon or an MG, unless you already have both (in which case you get nothing). When you get promoted from Corporal to Sergeant, you get a Sidearm. For ranks above, see the rulebook.

    \n Hello Everybody!

    I just e-mailed with Gregory Hutton and he wrote that after promotion you can keep your old weapons. Even when you are demoted and they take away a weapon, you keep your kill values with that weapon if you can get hold of one on the planet (that represents your skills). But you can't use a weapon you didn't get, even if you get hold of one (you don't have the skills).
