\n Still working on this; it's basic and slow at the moment. But for those of you jonesing for some maps for your 4E dungeoneering, I offer my own little side project:

SKG Tile Random Dungeon Generator

Happy delving! For miniatures-scale tiles, you'll need to follow the links at the bottom to the SKG online store.

    \n Thanks, all.

    To be clear, the tile art is not my own. Skeleton Key Games is the game company of cartographer Edward Bourelle. I am just a fan, nothing more. I have tried to contact Mr. Bourelle regarding permissions and marketing, but so far haven't gotten any response. So I'm trying to balance the fact that I've done this without his permission by driving business his way. I don't get a cut of any such business. At any time, he could contact me and ask me to stop, and I would comply (and start looking around for somebody to draw me some tiles that I could use).

    I am doing this for my own fun (I have some build-on-top-of-4E rules in mind that I'll share soon), and for your fun.
