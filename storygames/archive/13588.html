\n Are there any story games written or translated into the French Language? Some of my prospective, non-gamer players aren't very comfortable with the English language. So are there any?

The thought has occured to me that I could just translate a game, or several, or even write my own. Be the change I want to see. But I'm a bit of a procrastinator, and besides, I have other priorities. So I keep putting it off.

So what does exist, right now?

(For reference, my favorite games include Hero's Banner, Shock: Social Science-Fiction, Capes, InSpectres, Dogs in The Vineyard, Mouse Guard and Solar System/The Shadow of Yesterday.)

So is Scales, a game which was indie before there was an indie scene (a quick search gave me this site: http://hopitalpsy.fr/ which appears to have the pdf of the original books, well worth a look if you can read French).

I mean I love the game to death, but it's the granddaddy of French aesthetics in RPGs... calling it indie is kinda like calling D&D indie. Feels weird to me, though I don't care either way. It's a great game, whatever the label you give it.

    How does your system facilitate a narrativist agenda? I don't think Fate or Unknown Armies are themselves particularly Nar...
    \nTangent:
    Loosely structured, improvisational and player focused game. Firstly, Player characters each have three aspect-like traits which define the narratively important aspects that define a character's motivations. There are also particular narrative elements of the setting, campaign, story and act. There are mechanical advantages to including these elements and the level of competency of NPC's depends on the number of those traits which are relevant.
    While it is a task-resolution system, there is a narrative interjection capability for adding "yes, and", "yes, but", "no, but" & "no, and" conditions.
    It's narrativist first, gamist second and leaves simulationism behind. I think that is necessary for a decent, clean and useful universal game imo.

    On Topic:
    Very glad that there are a decent number of story games originally produced in french. I had been obsessing about the original French version of Inomine Satanis Magna Veritas as I thought it one the better avenues for improving my french. Very happy there are some story games out there which I could partake of instead. Is there any online retailer which sells some of these games? I doubt they are through IPR at the moment, but I would love to get my hands on them.
