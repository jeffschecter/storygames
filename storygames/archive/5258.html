\n

Ok, so it's not exactly difficult to inspire hatred and bile on the RPG site. But hey, it's my first time! I'm a little excited about this. Having fans for my game? Yeah, totally awesome. Having game designers that I respect give my game praise? Also awesome. But this RBH hate-thread is like the last horse in a trifecta. Now I win.

\n

Big thanks to James K Skach, Architect.Zero, and especially to Sean, who enjoyed the game enough to talk about it at theRPGsite.
