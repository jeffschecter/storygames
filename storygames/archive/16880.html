\n Hi Story Gamers,
I've been reading Dungeon World kickstarter commments, and I played the game once. Since then, I got really excited by the thought of doing a post-apocalyptic hack called Worldapocalypse.
I think I could make a list of simple moves about surviving in the wasteland while you fuck with all of the other asshole survivors.
The harm and supply rules can be easily ported from the Regiment.
And the clan rules from Seven Thunders could be used for setting up your post-apocalyptic holding.
And the advancement rules from Monsterhearts seem pretty solid.

What do you think? Any ideas for moves and character classes? I want really evocative stuff like Sexy Fighting Person of Either Gender or Person of Either Gender Who Hauls Around a Really Big Gun and Driver. Driver's my favorite class so far.

Let's brainstorm!
