\n This is a thread in which we explore other peoples' secret gaming projects. Things that they, themselves, may not even be aware of.

The rules are simple: Write a short-ish description of the Secret Gaming Project of the person who posted before you. So if the last poster in this thread is Billy Bob, you'll write something like "Billy Bob is currently playtesting his Nuclear Fallout LARP with the frightened neighbors he's imprisoned in his basement".

References to real-life projects & persons are, of course, totally allowed.

    \n Matthijs Holter's secret Jeepform LARP, Sn\xf8 Exit, requires the building of snowmen (or snowwomen) as character generation. Play consists of trying to persuade, through clever arguments or moral suasion, the other players' snowmen into melting. No one is allowed to leave the play area until there is only one snowperson remaining. Use of a hair dryer is frowned upon and is considered "powergaming." Who will win? The decorated colonel snowman or the middle-aged banker snowman? There's sno' telling!
