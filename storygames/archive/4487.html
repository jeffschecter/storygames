...so I guess that means I'll have to watch it. (^.^)

In any case, it currently looks mediocriffic, but most reviewers are currently allowing for some time for it to improve. Basically it, like the game, is a half-comedy half-fighty RPG about a modern magical school. Basically, an anime Harry Potter.

But still, as an artifact ("the first ever anime based on an RPG to be released") it's still an interesting milestone.

The game (and the anime) is called "Night Wizard":



Wikipedia Entry

Some interesting tidbits:
* Night Wizard (the game) is produced by my favorite Japanese RPG company, FEAR (which also did Tenra Bansho).
* Night Wizard, the first edition, was the best selling game the year it came out in Japan (later it was taken over by Alshard). In Japan, magical school comedy games have selling power, it seems.
* The anime stars the "canon characters" of the RPG.
* The giant gun-thing in the anime intro (here: http://youtube.com/watch?v=ImcTm5VeiRs) is a "Broom" (jpn: "Houki"). A Titanium "Broom" with GPS, autopilot, auto-targeting, and a rail cannon (tho in the anime it looks like it turned into a regular high-power rifle).
* The anime "Lodoss War" was originally way-back-when based on some tabletop D&D sessions or something. And later, the animes Gundam and Angel Core (pornographic) became tabletop games. But this is the first instance of an actual RPG becoming an anime.




Potential for Story Gaming? "Magical girls eating snacks. Roll your Salty and Affection!"

Anyway, just thought I'd give a heads up to the anime fans out there to check it out, give it a looksee. When it comes to anime, pretty much like anything else, 90%+ of it is total crap. Hopefully it has enough awesome things to not fall into the crap pile..
