    \n I have. I did up a whole 4E game world with it, detailing three or four cultures for each core race. I really like the complexity of cultures you get with Jonathan's technique. They feel much more like real societies than the typical game culture.

    That said, it is quite difficult to use with DDI. You can't input the variations, so you're stuck updating characters by hand. I'm interested in seeing how it can be applied to other game systems.
