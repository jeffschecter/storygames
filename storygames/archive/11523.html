Post by Peturabo \xbb Sat Feb 13, 2010 3:04 pm

Hi

...How does a weekend mini-con, held in the UK in the late summer, and devoted to playing Burning Wheel, Burning Empires, and Mouse Guard, grab you?

If the gritty fantasy of Burning Wheel floats your boat, how about forging a bloody Orc kingdom carved from the weak flesh of the Elves and Dwarves and every other ******* that frustrates your burning Hatred? Or, something else entirely.

If the sci-fi saiko-hora of Burning Empires is more to your taste, let your stentor suit you up in hallowed Iron, scream a prayer to Amilahk down your comms, and step aboard the hammer battleship that's taking the fight right to the heart of the Vaylen. This time the fight goes to the Worm. Or, like, something else that rocks.

If Mouse Guard is more your thing, how cool would it be to play a whole Years worth of Seasons that surely must see the Territories crushed under the triple hammer blow of a cruel winter, ravenous critters, and an enemy within? Really Tiny Tim says to shout "Honk!" if you're keen. Honk! Or, you know, something just as cool or even awesome-er.

It's early days yet, so the project is still in the stages of soliciting interest. I reckon it's a games day as opposed to a mini-con. I know that I'm keen. If you're potentially interested, ...head on over to the Burning Blighty group over on the Burning Wheel forum and join the conversation.

Sho ga nai, cheers

Pete
\nAs Pete says, it's still early days but we'd like to have a small get-together to spread the love that are the Burning X games. We're still discussing dates, venues and numbers, and would love as much input as possible.
John
