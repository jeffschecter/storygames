\n (Thought experiment)

I'm stuck on a desert island, but beautiful weredolphins see to all my material needs. Unfortunately, while they're interested in gaming, my materials are limited.

I have pens and paper, but in limited quantities.

As I scour the beaches, tropical forests, and caves of this island, however, I find packs of cards. They always come in packs of 10, numbered 0-9, and they have some curious symbols on them - frequently they feature little pictures and so on. The pictures and curious symbols don't have any consistent rhyme or reason, but they always come in these packs of 0-9 so I'm pretty happy that they'll be a good randomizer.

A crate full of indie games washes ashore. These games can easily be used with my decks. What does the crate contain?
