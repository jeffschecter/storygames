\n Hi, i thought i post this here as well (it was originally posted to Fate Core's G+ community), maybe some people are interested in Fate.
I created this short Fate Core scenario based on the Temple of Ungu dungeon world demo by Jason Morningstar.

What is this intended for:\n
    \n
  • Con games, when you have only a couple of hours, so you want to start play as fast as possible.
    • \n
  • Introduction of Fate Core to players familiar with D&D like games.
    • \n
\nWhat does it have:\n
    \n
  • Some semi made characters, similar to how the classes are done in Dungeon World. This will get you ready to play in 20 minutes.
    • \n
  • The full Temple of Ungu scenario converted to Fate Core. That means mainly the NPC/monsters converted to Fate core.
    • \n
  • Currently missing, but it will have Treasure cards, just like in the original Temple of Ungu.
    • \n
\nSome texts are directly copied over from the original Temple of Ungu, but I didn't find copyright information in it. I hope i am not doing anything illegal sharing this.
Download here.

How you would do anything different. Also, i am not a native English speaker, so if you find a place where the text doesn't make sense, please also let me know.
