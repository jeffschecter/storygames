\n Hey Story Gamers,

Over the last few months I've gotten involved with a group called Babycastles, an independent arcade based in Ridgewood, Queens installed in a music venue called The Silent Barn. We curate an arcade of video games by small/independent developers, and in addition to our own opening parties, they're available to be played by anyone who comes out to see shows there over the next month. It's been going really well. We were recently featured in the L.A. Times, and are going to be running events at the upcoming NY Maker Faire and the DUMBO Arts Festival.

The really big news is that we're going to be opening a new location for a couple of months in Manhattan at the Chashama space on 42nd Street and 3rd Avenue. From October 4th through December 29th, we'll be setting up new arcades every 2 weeks or so, with a bunch of special events. We'll be featuring games from the Experimental Gameplay Project, The Copenhagen Game Collective, Eric Zimmerman, and others. Plus a slew of exciting musical guests, speakers, workshops, and a couple of other secrets we're not ready to announce yet.

So, I come here to ask two things:
1) If you're near New York City, please come check out the arcade (especially if you can come in the second half of October, which is when my curation, the EGP show, will be up).
2) We've got a kickstarter fund going right now to help us cover some set-up costs. If you can kick us a couple of bucks, I'd really appreciate it, and we'll send you a t-shirt or some of our other sweet schwag.

Thanks so much.
