\n I'm bored at work. I want to go on holiday. I want to do a quiz.

Here's one: game titles as anagrams. Find out the correct titles of these games:

1. Renumbering Psi
2. Beer Dud Then
3. Dinner Owls
4. One Sip
5. The Labia Harsh Choir
6. Be Her Lung Win
7. Deed Trifles Hotel
8. Oral Pis
9. Authentic Moth Win
10. Scone Trend

Send your answers to me in whispers and I'll announce the winners (and real titles, of course) tomorrow.
