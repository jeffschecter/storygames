    Posted By: iagoWhat's the deal with GMing and badge discounts for DexCon? Do you need to run 3 for free? Is there a partial discount to be had if you run less than 3, or is that a binary condition?
    \nFred, here's what their site says:\n
    # Free Membership - Double Exposure will give you one free Complete Membership per 128 man-hours of planned GM/Director time. Note that we are trusting you to be honest in your appraisal of the number of players your game can handle. A man-hour is defined as entertaining one player for one hour. So a 4-hour game which is planned for a maximum of 8 players will count as 32 man-hours towards your free Membership. If you have multiple GMs working with you, we will negotiate the number of players who will actually be entertained by your game. The more often you run your game, the more generous we will be.

    # Registration of Game Masters - Yes, we are generous in giving out free memberships to willing game masters. But as of DEXCON 10, Double Exposure is instituting a NEW policy that ALL game masters MUST INDIVIDUALLY REGISTER at the regular DEXCON 10 Pre-Registration Page - EVEN THE ONE WHO SUBMITTED THE EVENT. Once your game proposal has been accepted, you will be given a GAME MASTER REGISTRATION CODE which will enable you to claim a number of free memberships granted by acceptance of your proposal. ONLY PRE-REGISTERED FREE MEMBERSHIPS WILL BE ACCEPTED - If you have any issues, they need to be dealt with BEFORE the convention!
