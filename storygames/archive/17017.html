\n I will be there!

NYC, 3 days, October 26-28. Same space as RECESS (where NerdNYC runs events). Friday party. Sat-Sun games.

Luke Crane is hosting. Vincent Baker and John Harper are co-hosting.

It will feature games like Burning Wheel, Mouse Guard, Apocalypse World, Dogs in the Vineyard, Lady Blackbird, Burning Empires, In A Wicked Age, Murderous Ghosts, Agon, World of Dungeons, Freemarket, and Burning Empires!

Forum discussion: http://goo.gl/sIYyl

Registration opens tomorrow. Limited Spots. First come, first serve: http://www.burningwheel.org/
