\n So scheduling is starting for Camp Nerdly 3, and I'd proposed running the jeepformy version of Ganakagok outside, by the council fire pit, at dawn (which, according to the astronomers, is 5:46 am). And while a few folks are definitely up for that, others are only interested in playing the game if it "fits their schedule." So what I need to know is, if I schedule it for dawn, before breakfast on Saturday, are there really people who would play? Or is it, like I'd guessed, just plumb loco?
