When a player dies, the world trembles in this dark fantasy supplement full of new classes for Dungeon World and FATE Core

Grim World is a dark fantasy supplement for the Dungeon World and Fate Core systems. It's full of 6 new class playbooks (now 7), tons of death moves, monsters, fronts, compendium classes, cursed magic item & toolkit, locations & toolkit, full color character illustrations, and more!

We just hit our 20k stretch goal and will be adding a new 7th class, Skirmisher! And all text will be released under CC BY 3.0 license early next year!


Check out a free preview of the classes (Backing at any reward level gets you instant access to 6 class playbooks! Check the Updates tab for the download.):

Dungeon World Playbooks

Fate Core Playbooks

We are making an awesome limited edition hardcover, large format, with full color art if you want something non-digital too! Here are some art illustrations and mock ups of some pages from the book:



The physical edition will be a HARDCOVER, full COLOR, 8.5"x11" book with an estimated page count of 150+ pages. This is a limited edition run and every single book will be personalized by the creators (that's us!). Personalization means we'll sign it, doodle on it, write custom moves, draw mustaches on all the art, or otherwise deface it as much or as little as you want.

All backers that pledge at $15 or more, get the digital version of the book. This includes all of the content in the physical book, but in digital formats. You'll also get the revised playbooks improved from all the playtesting feedback we received (version 2 will go out soon). We'll also be doing a bunch of separate PDFs to make important content easy to reference and print. So here's the breakdown of what we have locked down so far. Any time you see a plus "+", that means there's a chance there will be more because of stretch goals or additional community contributions:

7+ Dungeon World class playbooks
6+ Fate Core classes, with theme descriptions, aspects, and stunts
15+ Playable Species with custom moves, free aspect invokes, and portrait illustrations
Grim World setting tips and GM philosophies
The Hunt faction details about their structure, hidden caches, lodges, hunting trophies and contracts
8+ DW style Compendium Classes to flesh out your characters
7+ GM Fronts/Arcs for building a campaign
Grim World locations and the Grim World Location Builder (roll some dice for an instant adventure location)
20+ Monsters with descriptions, instincts, weaknesses, and portrait illustrations
Loads of gear including new magic items, cursed items, and a Cursed Item Toolkit (to build your own cursed magic items)
36+ (yes, 36 and counting!) additional Death Moves, including all standard DW classes, Inverse World classes, and tons of other custom classes (written by Jacob Randolph and other awesome contributors from the community)
ART!!! Full page class illustrations, spot illustrations (for species, monsters, magic items), and additional icons and sketches, all to make Grim World come alive!
