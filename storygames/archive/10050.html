\n As some of you may be aware from Facebook, I spent most of my summer in the hospital with H1N1, which decided to totally kick my ass. Two weeks sedated in the ICU with pulmonary and renal failure ... the whole nine yards. Well, I'm finally back in some semblance of normal life, and I'd like to get caught up on my indie reading. Please direct me to new games or adaptations, free or not, which have really caught your attention this summer.

Let me know what everyone's been talking about, so I can catch up. After all, I just got two whole lungs about a week ago, so I can't run very fast.
