\n I've just submitted a draft of a new Trail of Cthulhu adventure to Pelgrane Press, and now playtesting is the order of the day:\n
Beth Lewis of Pelgrane Press saysPlaytesters Wanted - ToC The Big Hoodoo
We are looking for playtesters for 'The Big Hoodoo' a new adventure by Bill White. Lovecraft visits the home of film noir in this ripped-from-history adventure set in California in 1952.

If you're interested, please contact me at beth@pelgranepress.com, including 'The Big Hoodoo Playtest' as the subject, also saying where you heard about the playtest.

Thank you very much.

Beth Lewis
Pelgrane Press Ltd.
www.pelgranepress.com
\nHere's the pitch:

Lovecraft visits the home of film noir in this ripped-from-history adventure.

The investigators are science fiction writers in 1952 California, mourning the death of a long-lost friend, rocket scientist-cum-occultist Jack Parsons, in an explosion in his garage laboratory a few days ago. The police believe that it was an accident, no more than a careless mishandling of dangerous chemicals.

But as the investigators are pulled in to a charlatan\u2019s web of chicanery, avarice and malice, they must turn all of their science-fictional insights to unraveling its secrets, lest they find themselves ultimately the innocent victims of Parsons\u2019 \u201cbig hoodoo.\u201d

***

I've run the adventure at Dexcon and Gencon, so I know it works when I run it. It's taken me a while, but now I think it's in shape for other GMs to run it, too. But I'd like to get feedback on that. So if you have any inclination to run a Trail of Cthulhu one-shot, do me a favor and get hold of Beth Lewis.

Thanks!

Bill
