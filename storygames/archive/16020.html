    \n Hey Justus,

    Remember that Danger Mountain was written in a week and may be a little rickety, even for a bitter old game nerd. I think if your girlfriend looks it over and is excited to play you'll do fine, though - it is definitely no more complicated than Fiasco. I doubt you'll get much advice since I don't think very many people have played it.

    As a side note, being a n00b will rarely be an impediment to learning and playing a roleplaying game. There's very little to "get wrong", so I'd encourage you to jump in. I have been playing RPGs for decades and I make rules mistakes all the time, even with (especially with!) games I actually wrote. If you get something wrong and have a good time anyway, well, how wrong were you?
