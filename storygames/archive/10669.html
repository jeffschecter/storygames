\n Just thinking that it would be really cool to do a fighting game where you can be an assortment of cool heroes or villains from TV, Movies, Comics, Video Games, Toys, etc. Each character would have special abilities.

A few that I'm thinking of are:

I kind of like the idea of them being the forgotten awesome heroes of childhood (Hong Kong Phooey?), but obviously some (Batman) were never forgotten.

    \nThis is where I run into trouble, I think that there are lots of potential female characters, but I also don't want this to become too obscure. Of the list above I think the average population (in the United States) would only recognize The Bride, Xena, Friday, the Bionic Woman and maybe Jinx. I'm not sure I want it to become a catalog of anime, manga and Japanese video games. I also hesitate to use any literary references outside of the mainstream.

    I think my struggle is: what is mainstream? My mainstream is not the same as the person next-door and it is definitely different from someone from the other side of the country, or even from a different country. I read the above list of heroines to my Canadian wife and she only recognized Mara Jade (which she only knows because a friend of a friend named their daughter Mara because the husband is a Star Wars fanatic), Xena, and The Bionic Woman (but not by the name "Jamie Summers"). \n
