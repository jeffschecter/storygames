\n After years of searching, we finally managed to find Mark Pettigrew, who designed the wonderful "Flashing Blades" RPG back in 1984. I interviewed him for Imagonem, and he has some interesting things to say about game design, publication - and fun.

    \n Really nice interview, thanks!

    Flashing Blades is one of the few old-school game books I've held onto after all these years. I never played the rules as-is, but it was a great sourcebook and inspiration for the GURPS Swashbucklers and En Garde games I used to run/play.

    I remember it being pretty well written and designed, and I have to say I'm floored that he was 16 when he wrote it!
