\n Let me start by saying that I recently got a copy of 3:16 and I loved it. Lots of fun playing it, and it's a great "gateway game" for my group, which generally get antsy about anything less tactical than D&D, although they seemed to enjoy the game a lot. Anyways, I was thinking today that the rules could work surprisingly well as a game about rockstars rather than marines. FA becomes skill, NFA becomes style, kills become sales (of concert tickets or albums), and planets become some major event like a tour or an album. The ranges become the difference between gimmicks and art. Similarly, weapons become musical styles, ranks become degrees of fame (eventually moving the band members into the recording industry), damage becomes stress, medals become awards, etc.
Anyways, just a thought. I'm not sure if I'll ever actually run it, but it would be a fun idea, maybe for a one shot or something.
