Action Squad
A Danger Patrol Hack about Kicking Ass and Chewing Bubblegum*
*Gum not included.


Action Squad is the super elite team, sent in to deal with all the tough jobs no one else can deal with. This hack will ultimately try to emulate all my favorite action movies on the last 30 years. I've been looking for a game that could handle The Bride's fight with the Crazy 88's for quite a while now, I think Danger Patrol might be the perfect system.

Expertise
The first thing a member of Action Squad needs is an area of Expertise. This is role you fill on the Squad.

\tAdventurer \u2013 Animals, Ancient Languages, Cultures, Lore (Indy, Lara Croft)
\tCommando \u2013 Guns, Heavy Weapons, Explosives, Intimidation (Rambo, Snake Pliskin)
\tCop \u2013 Investigation, One Liners, Persuasion, Interrogation (John McClaine, Max Payne)
\tGeek \u2013 Computers, Science, Gadgets (Neo, Shia LeBeouf)
Driver \u2013 Planes, Cars, Mechanics (Transporter, Fast and Furious)
\tMartial Artist \u2013 Kung Fu, Extreme Athletic Feats, Swords (Jackie Chan, The Bride)
\tSpy \u2013 Foreign Languages, Stealth, Surveillance, Deception, Security (James Bond, Jason Bourne)

Motivation
Different heroes fight for different reasons. It\u2019s these Motivations that give them the strength to fight.

\tGovernment \u2013 When a job\u2019s too big for the Army, they send you instead.
\tRevenge \u2013 They killed your father, kidnapped your girlfriend, and left you for dead.
\tJustice \u2013 You always do what\u2019s right. It usually involves killing a whole lot of people.
\tQuest \u2013 Find the sacred artifact of untold power lost by the Forgotten People before they do!
\tGreed \u2013 Can you believe they\u2019re paying you for this? Two days ago, you were doing this for free!
\tDumb Luck \u2013 Why do these things keep happening to you?
\tRespect \u2013 They took your badge away and made you look the fool, but you\u2019ll show \u2018em now!

    John, I'm happy you're happy dude. Danger Patrol now travels everywhere my dice go. It's my go-to pick up game!

    I'm starting to ponder a few different powers. I know there are two different camps developing among Danger fans, for powers and against them. I'm not quite sure what I want to do with Action Squad yet. I see two options:

    1. Port powers directly from Danger Patrol, rewording for flavor, to keep 'continuity' between games.
    2. Develop a few basic powers, using versions of them for each Expertise and Motivation (you get [+] when A, he gets the same [+] when B, etc)

    I think another project needs to get out of my brain first, but I'll be toying with this off and on till it's done.
