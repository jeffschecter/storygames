Hey, designer-humans! Because of the perennial interest in critique, I'm opening up the Game Design Studio for an open critique in one week, starting, oh, let's say Sunday, August 22nd. If you have something you're working on, get it together according to the guidelines and present it.

\n

As usual, the doors will be open until the conversation's over \u2014\xa0usually a couple of weeks \u2014 at which point, we'll shut down for a month or so, or until enough people lean on my to get off my ass and make things happen again.

\n

If you're just curious, please come by and see what folks are making. If you have something to say, please also follow those guidelines.

\n

We've done this three times before, with Apocalypse World being our most well-known alum. I expect On Mighty Thews by Simon Carryer will be the next one. I'll probably be bringing out some stuff for Human Contact, as well.

\n

I'm excited to see what folks are working on this time around!
