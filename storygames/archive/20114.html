Hi all!
Today we are launching a fundraiser with the aim of translating the Swedish fantasy tabletop roleplaying game Symbaroum to English, and to release it internationally. The game has been out in Sweden for almost a year now and the reception has been great, both nationally and around the world. Now we hope to get YOUR help in bringing Symbaroum into the international scene. Please visit the Indiegogo campaign page, and if what you see is to your liking, any contribution is greatly appreciated. Likewise, we would be more than happy if you could help us share this fundraiser to your roleplaying buddies around the world! Thank you all!
