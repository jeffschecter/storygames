\n I've just been made aware of Obsidian Portal, a wiki farm built specifically for organizing and promoting tabletop campaigns. I've signed up for a free membership, poked around a bit, but so far I'm nonplussed. I already use wikidot for this sort of thing, and I can't find any features particular to Obsidian Portal that distinguishes it from that general wiki site. In fact the Obsidian wiki lacks several useful features, such as being able to change the theme and add forums, which I like in wikidot.

Am I missing something? Anyone here experienced with Obsidian Portal who can talk it up a bit?
