    I see that they have Truth & Justice, but not Dead Inside, and wonder if they could order the latter one. DI is something of a subtly different beast from a lot of other RPG's; while it has light, somewhat traditional mechanics (I like them), the real meat is how the overall theme is set up and how it guides you with minimal system interference and strong thematics. In short: you have lost your soul and now you have to do good things in order to get it back. Love it, even with some shortcomings.

    Truth & Justice is not a bad choice either if you're into superhero genre; the mechanics share the good things from DI and are kind of customizable (for the super-powers). It lacks the strong theme of DI, but provides a good toolbox for setting up a superhero game. There's a short example setting of a character-driven campaign with very good advise.
