    \n I want to totally encourage anyone else who wants to play this game to organize their own group of players and start a game. Just send us the links to your videos at thehousethegame@gmail.com or send them to us in a private message on Youtube. and we'll put them into a playlist so everyone can find them and watch them. Even if you can't find anyone to play with, if you send us a pitch video we can put you together with other players for a new season, once we get enough pitches.

    Also it would be awesome if anyone would like to write a write up of the game to help spread the word!
