\n I hope I don't have the wrong idea here - this is my first thread.

A few months ago I witnessed the development of a Homestuck RPG that lacks a proper name (I helped out once or twice but props go to Jonatan Kilhamn for it's creation). The idea behind it was not to emulate Homestuck per se, but to emulate the storytelling style behind MSPA: a story where structured insanity is a guiding principle and nothing is forgotten for long. There was talk of a playtest but it never got off the ground.

Until now, of course!

Things to Know Upfront:\n
    \n
  • The game requires five participants. I will not be acting as GM.
    • \n
  • As mentioned above, this game has not been officially playtested (to my knowledge). This could run great, or it could burst into flames during the first session. We'll have to find out.
    • \n
  • I am usually at a computer between four and ten PM, Eastern US time.
    • \n
  • Please be patient with me. I have no experience organizing an online game.
    • \n
\nThings to be Decided:\n
    \n
  • What's a time frame everyone will be online?
    • \n
  • How is this going to happen? Skype? Forum thread? Pesterchum?
    • \n
\nAnd finally, a the game itself.
