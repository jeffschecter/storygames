\n Player Stance, Author Stance, Director Stance, Designer Stance?

Now that the idea of the GM as a necessary element of an rpg has been done away with, i see one last bastion of authority in RPG's. The designer, the invisible GM at your local game. Anyone tried to get some player participation in this process?

GM: You walk into a 10' by 10' room.
Player1: I want to make a rule about content authority
GM: Sure, roll a 3 or lower on d12
Player1: Actually i was thinking about that d12 im gonna make that a d6

but serious now...anyone done this, if so what were the results?

    We occasionally started blank in the wayback, and built in play. It worked, but not quite as smoothly. Even now, it's not smooth-as-glass, but we fumble along and things get better and better as we do.
