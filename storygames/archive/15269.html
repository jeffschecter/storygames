    \n It's only $20 if you are going to play (and pre-register by this Sunday).

    The panels / seminars alone seem worth the price of admission:

    - Playtesting Best Practices
    - Marketing Your Game
    - Remixology. From Lady Blackbird to Fate to Apocalypse World and more, game design doesn't have to start with a blank page.
    - "Surviving Success" presented by Fred Hicks.
    - "The Nitty-Gritty of Self-Publishing" presented by Brennan Taylor.
    - "An Introduction to Kickstarter" presented by Cindy Au.
    - "The Game Design Roundtable" presented by Kenneth Hite.
    - "The Independent Gaming Roundtable" presented by Darren Watts.

    And a lot more! I didn't realize they were going to have all these panels.

    Full schedule is up: http://www.dexposure.com/m2011sched.html

    I really want to go myself. I'm 50/50 because of some last minute emergencies but if I can, I will be there.
