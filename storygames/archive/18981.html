\n I'm so enamoured by Sweet Agatha's straight forward storytelling system. I was wondering if there were any hacks out there? I think it lends itself to different genres out of the box, but I see no reason a conversion wouldn't be possible (though a lot of work, admittedly). I'm a bit of a graphic designer myself, so I'm quite tempted to go at it alone with a Mythos or Sherlock hack, but I'd be keen to find out what's out there (if anything)!
