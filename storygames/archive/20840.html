    \n Hey, this is pretty cool! I like how focused it is, and how strongly tied to its theme.

    The random generators seem pretty strong, as well. I think the "why can't we leave?" one is the weakest; I'd have trouble explaining a few of those. (Although, given your space restrictions, I'm not sure how you could give more detail - you probably have something specific in mind for each entry, but I don't necessarily know what you mean by each one.)

    The visual design is OK. I find it a little cluttered and overly "in your face" - lots of different colours and fonts and shapes. It's a little overwhelming for me; my tastes tend towards the more aesthetically coherent and toned-down.
