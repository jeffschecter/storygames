\n I've been getting to know and like old blue book D&D (Holmes, -78) over the last two days. This, combined with coursework to be dodged, meant that I started tinkering with a character record sheet when I couldn't find one I really liked. When I showed it off on a Swedish rpg forum a friend of mine informed me that having character record sheets the size of 3"x 5" index cards was all the rage, so I had to make one of those too, especially .
