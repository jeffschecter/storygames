    \n There's no rules difference between the two texts. (ignition and interim)

    There will be slight rules clarifications in the revised text, as well as a few extra options. The clarifications are:
    1) On a relationship breaking, add double the Intimacy score to Bliss, not triple (the book right now sometimes says one, sometimes says the other).
    2) Starting Bliss = pilot age.

    By and large, I'm not expecting any serious rules changes. A lot of rewriting, but no serious rules changes.
