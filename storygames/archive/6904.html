\n Hi everyone,

The Canon Puncture Show is planning on doing a roundtable for game designers who are bringing a new game to Gen Con '08. There are several really sweet games coming out this GC including Clint Krause's Empire of Dust and Rob Bohl's Misspent Youth are two I know off the top of my head and I suspect I know at least one more.

But anyway, I am looking for 3-5 game developers to come on, shamelessly promote their games for a few minutes each and then discuss the joy of getting a game ready for arguably the biggest Con of the year. Do I have any takers? I will be approaching a few of you individually, but I hope there are some designers out there who want to discuss their games.

I would like to hold this roundtable in mid-July with the episode coming out within days of the interview. Given the geographic disparity of the Story Games community, I would like to do a Skype conference with the limit of developers being equal to the limit I can put on to a Skype Conference.

I will be the moderator/arbitrator for the show.
