\n Since the local zombie outbreak, I've been tasked with recruiting a team to deal with the situation.

Partly, of course, I'm looking for people with useful zombie-facing skills. They will also be required to discover the source of the outbreak and eliminate it.

However, team dynamics are increasingly important in the zombie containment industry. I'm particularly looking for people with entertaining character quirks that, although perhaps irritating, will keep their teammates amused in the long war ahead. Quirks strangely unsuited to a world full of zombies are particularly welcome.

Could you help me write some job descriptions? HR requires me to do this in the following format: Role, Psychological Failing, Skills, then Quirks. Each post requires one role, one psychological failing, three skills and three quirks.

For example:

Role: Doctor
Psychological Failing: Uptight
Skills: Science, Medicine, Library Research
Quirks: Making everything tidy and ordered, Figuring out the exact numbers, Insisting on controlled laboratory conditions

Any help appreciated. Thank you.

Graham
