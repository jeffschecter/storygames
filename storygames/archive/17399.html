\n Not that it needed one, but for the sake of conventions, I made a character sheet for Fiasco!

It can be found here as well as folding instructions.

The first few pages of the document are cards that we cut out and laminated (to reuse during the con) and the last page we printed about twenty copies of. That way players had the web-site and game name and info to take home with them, as well as the rules facing them should they need them.

Enjoy.

    \n Before anyone shows up at my place for a game of Fiasco, I write out "Relationship," etc., on the top of each index card with a "C" line underneath (for Category) and an "E" line under that (for Element). Usually, I try to use a different color card for each type of connection (red for Relationship, green for Object, etc.) so someone can easily tell what's what without having to decipher my messy writing from across the table. These should definitely save me some time!
