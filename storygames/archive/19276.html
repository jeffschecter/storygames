\n I run Dungeon World, Marvel Heroic RPG, Psi*Run, the Burning Wheel, and a few other games for middle-school kids, five days a week. Occasionally they do funny or thought-provoking things, so I write about them in my blog, which is at http://dungeon-elementary.tumblr.com if you would like to read it. Please let me know what you think, and tell your nerd friends!
