\n I'm Matt Sanchez, and I have the extreme pleasure of working with Andy on translations of Japanese RPGs like Ryuutama. Now that Tenra Bansho is burning up the interwebs with its hotness, we've got some other stuff on our plate. I've agreed with the Japanese publisher to make the announcement of our 3rd game at Spiel Essen!

I'm extremely excited to bring this game out in English. It's an extremely story-driven ninja game with simple combat mechanics and Japanese scenario structure. Sometimes I describe it as Fiasco with combat and ninjas and secrets and secret missions and ultimate attacks. Secrets, twists, backstabs, and treachery are all part of the ninja lifestyle, and the game only gets more interesting and intense the more PvP happens.

If you're at Essen this year and are interested in checking the game out, I'll be running demos from the 18th to the 20th all day, every day. Come by and say hi, and see if Japanese RPGs interest you. If not, that's cool, it'd still be cool to talk to people.
