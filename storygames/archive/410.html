    Nathan,

    The situation last year was as follows (I hope this will anticipate some other layout questions...):

    Levering Hall contains

    • the Glass Pavilion (dealers' room)
    • the Arellano theatre (directly below the dealers' room; anime plays here)
    • the information/registration desk
    • the Living Greyhawk room

    Gilman hall contains (all the rooms we have are in the basement):

    • non-LG gaming
    • the movie room

    Last year, I believe a dude from Looney Labs (the Icehouse + Fluxx people) did a sales-and-demo thing in the basement; it's not unprecedented. The buildings are right next to each other so it's not too much of a hassle moving things from one to the other, although obviously it'll be less convenient than using only one location.
