\n There was a new stall at the farmers market this weekend. A handsome elderly gentleman was selling some rather odd items and I found his prices a bit disconcerting. He had these out on the table when I stopped by

Locket of safety -- the color of your hair
Faerie light -- memory of a perfect sunset

When you stopped by, what did he have out, and were the prices any better?
