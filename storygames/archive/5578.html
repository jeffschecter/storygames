\n I've created a new character sheet for Ganakagok, motivated by a comment from Chad Underkoffler, who played it at Dreamation. He said that a character sheet that had some clues about how what was on it was to be used might be helpful in allowing players to make sense of the game and its flow.

Take a look and tell me if you think I've got the right idea, or if I'm missing something obvious.

    Posted By: HituroI like it a lot, and not having played I got a lot of impression of rules and so forth from it, except for the bit about sins, there is no indication of how many (if any) to take, other than "you may"
    \nThanks, I'll have to clarify that. The language is correct but probably not terribly illuminating; a player can decide to take his or her initial allotment of "Burdens" as one or more Scars, Fears, Hates, and Sins; the first two are noted in the "Gifts and Burdens" box, while Hates are drawn in as negative links on a relationship map and Sins get tacked up against a specific kind of spirit as shown. Like all Burdens, the GM gets to invoke them against the player, albeit in the case of Sins in a special way (as dice that are "set" to produce Bad Medicine, rather than as shifts on existing dice).
