Game spot lights (games needing players)

01 Morning - PDQ: Golden Gauntlets and Iron Republics \u2013 four seats open
- There's something rotten in the state of the Iron Republic, and the gods are unhappy. But why exactly are they? And why don't they just smite the problem as they usually do? Assassins are running through the streets, and you, yes you, might just get caught in the crossfire and pulled into the problem...
- Behold, a fantasy game run in a homebrewed fantasy setting I'm using in some writing projects. We'll be using the core PDQ rules with Zo's Hero Points added in, and I will have pre-gens available. If you want to make your own, that's great! I should have some world details for such folk up on the MTMJ forums before the con.

01 Morning - The Trouble with Rose \u2013 four seats open
- TTwR is my (TAZ) Game Chef story telling game where players collaboratively make a play by creating scenes and populating them with PC and NPCs. Players earn points by acting according to their character's natures and the play of dominoes from their hand. With secret agendas and limited resources, players vie to score the most points and gain control of the epilogue where they can seal the Fates of player and NPC alike.
- A normal game of The Trouble with Rose begins with the players doing some world building and character creation. To speed that up for con purposes, I've created several play set for the players to choose from; I've got one for a deep space adventure, a western, a magical court, and one inspired by the game Ghost Echo. And, I'd be glad to write up more if you have any requests.
- Here's the rules and play sets that have already been created. The Trouble with Rose folder on Google Docs

01 Morning - Dragon Slayers' FIASCO - four seats open
- A popular Fiasco playset about greedy adventurers.

02 Afternoon - Zorcerer of Zo \u2013 six seats open
- the pain never ends. (Darfo was never that good at marketing.)
- Darfo's the mad-cap GM for the Sunday Skypers when it comes to our Zorcerer game. If you like twisted humor and plots, trivia questions, or a Zo game that likes to jump down the rabbit-hole head first, you should check this out.

03 Evening - Hocus Focus FIASCO - four seats open
- The latest playset from Bully Pulpit, set in the Harry Dresden universe where players are the 'bad guys' with poor impulse control and too much ambition.

04 Night - Faery's Tale - four seats open
- (from a sidebar of the Deluxe edition)
- 'Faery Godmothers'
- Sometimes, when a very special baby is born to humans, the Faery Queen will name a faery godmother--or godfather, though these never seem to get mentioned in faery tales--to watch over the infant and help her as she grows.
- You will play faery godparents to twins (one boy, one girl). Each child can have two godfathers, two godmothers or a mix. Your options are to be a Pixie (with Flying and Pixie Dust gifts), a Sprite (with Animal Friends and Champion gifts), a Brownie (with Household Magic and Invisibility gifts) or a Pooka (with Change Form and Travel Magic gifts).

    \n True, true, and now that we're in the post mortem phase, that's one of the questions that have been brought up. MCZ worked great as a kind of end of Con season thing because people got to play with their new toys from Game Chef, Gen Con, or the Ennies. Most folks had burned through their travel money and gaming money and are now saving for the holidays.

    Will the same thing work every quarter without burn out? Will the event get less attendance if it is done in the middle of summer before the big cons? It's something that the GM and players will hopefully hash out with me and the other volunteers that made this event happen.

    This was called MonkeyCon Zero for a reason, it's a 'test drive' for what we want to do in the future. FYI, most of the discussion is happening on the Monkeys Took My Jetpack forum, if you're interested in following or contributing. :-)
