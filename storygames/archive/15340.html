\n Sit down at a con, play this game, and find out something awesome about the people you are playing with. And maybe when it's done, you become friends!

Like speed dating except in RPG form. Or like how fantasy / science fiction allow us to talk about touchy subjects wrapped in an awesome / safe wrappers, except in game form and replace touchy subjects with friendship.

Level Up: if the game doesn't just reward you for making friends BUT to become super awesome in the game, you need to play it with more people, and possibly make more friends.

Level Down: Of course this might explode into having too many friends and you unfriend everyone and blame Story Games!

Achievement Unlocked: If you can play this game with just 2 players or as many as 6+!

Bonus XP: If the game is appropriate for any reasonable age level.

Why John? You have too many friends, what is your super secret agenda gotcha motive?!? I run gaming events for lots of people, some friends, some strangers. People want to make friends. I want to help them make friends (even though that often means they come to less events because they have too many friends). So help me, help other people.

What game does this?

OR

Who is going to make this game?
