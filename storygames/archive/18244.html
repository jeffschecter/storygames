    \n The DW thread on Something Awful would be the main clearinghouse for new classes and other shiny toys designed for the DW players, since they are like crazed magpies building a tower of babel to fun.

    Specifically, there is The Sharpshooter (beta) and The Marksman.

    Other options, specific to Dungeon Planet:
    a) Make an Engine of Destruction with the Seek and Destroy Unit specialization (so you can Volley with STR).
    b) Choose another class (like Earthling for example) and replace one of their starting moves with A Sniper's Eyes (p. 86) and let them take other moves from the Sniper compendium class.
    c) Make a Mutant and choose monster moves that are all about ranged combat.
