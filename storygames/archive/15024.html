    \n Have them go through Pathways and add them to the Pathways Map, just as you would had they been in the first session. There's not as much of the collaborative fun but the new player gets the benefit of fitting into the existing framework and adding in their own extras and locations as they like.

    The Watchtower Report covers other options, too, such as converting an existing Feature into a Lead. Of course, that book's not out yet, but it won't be too long!

    Cam
